## Verse 6

> يَوْمَ يَقُومُ ٱلنَّاسُ لِرَبِّ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ

The Day when mankind will stand before the Lord of the worlds?

**Linked Hadiths:**

#### Bukhari 6531
Narrated Ibn `Umar:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said (regarding the Verse), "A Day when all mankind will stand before the Lord of the 
Worlds,' (that day) they will stand, drowned in their sweat up to the middle of their ears."

#### Bukhari 4938
Narrated `Abdullah bin `Umar:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "On the Day when all mankind will stand before the Lord of the Worlds, some of 
them will be enveloped in their sweat up to the middle of their ears."

#### Muslim 2862 a
Ibn 'Umar reported Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) as saying: When the people stand before Allah, the Lord of the worlds, each one of them would stand submerged into perspiration up to half of his ears, and there is no mention of the "day" in the hadith transmitted on the authority of Ibn Muthanni.
