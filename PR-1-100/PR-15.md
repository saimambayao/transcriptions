**A RESOLUTION**
**EXPRESSING SINCEREST GRATITUDE TO DR. AMIREL SANDAY USMAN FOR HIS DEDICATED SERVICE TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE AND CONVEYING THE DEEPEST CONDOLENCES OF BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY MEMBERS OF THE PARLIAMENT TO HIS FAMILY FOR HIS DEMISE**

**WHEREAS,** on Safar 21, 1444 | 17 September 2022, Dr. Amirel S. Usman, the incumbent Director-General of the Ministry of Health (MOH) and concurrent OIC-Chief of Hospital at the Iranun District Hospital joined His Creator at the age of 42;

**WHEREAS,** Dr. Amirel S. Usman, popularly known as "Doc Amz", was born on 10-Safar-1400 | 30 December 1979 in Parang, Maguindanao;

**WHEREAS,** his outstanding educational background demonstrates years of excellence, as he obtained a Bachelor of Science in Nursing from Notre Dame University - Cotabato City, a Doctor of Medicine degree from Davao Medical School Foundation (DMSF), a Post- Baccalaureate internship at San Pedro Hospital - Davao City, a sub-specialty in Surgical Oncology at the Davao Regional Medical Center, and a Master's Degree in Hospital Administration from St. Bernadette of Lourdes College - Quezon City;

**WHEREAS,** Doc Amz started his medical career as a resident physician at the Cotabato Regional and Medical Center (CRMC) in 2008, then as a Medical Officer at the Dinaig Municipal Hospital, Datu Odin Sinsuat, Maguindanao from 2014 to 2016, and thereafter as the Head of Disaster Risk Reduction Management - Health (DRRM-H) until he was designated as the OIC Minister of the Ministry of Health - Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (MOH - BARMM) from September 18, 2020 to March 30, 2021, and currently the Director-General of the MOH - BARMM and concurrently OIC-Chief of Hospital at the Iranun District Hospital;

**WHEREAS,** Doc Amz was at the frontline and was very active during the early implementation of the COVID-19 vaccination plan; he led the Bangsamoro Inter-Agency Task Force on COVID-19 in crafting policies and guidelines to address the pandemic in the BARMM; he helped in the establishment of the conventional RT-PCR laboratories in all the provinces of BARMM; he also supported the establishment of cold chain facilities, and was the first person in BARMM to receive the COVID-19 vaccine;

**WHEREAS,** Doc Amz's notable advocacy efforts included the operationalization of the Iranun District Hospital, for which he received support from various institutions and Members of the Parliament, and the modernization of hospitals in the BARMM, which quickly triggered numerous legislation of the BTA Parliament's Committee on Health involving various hospitals in the BARMM, as well as the establishment of Regional Health Units (RHUs) in all municipalities and Barangay Health Stations (BHS) in all barangays of the BARMM;

**WHEREAS,** the demise of Dr. Amirel S. Usman, a true Bangsamoro leader and public servant who devoted his life serving the Bangsamoro people with integrity, strength, excellence, and to the best of his ability, is a major loss to the Bangsamoro Ummah and he will be fondly remembered and revered;

**WHEREAS,** may this resolution, together with the outpouring expressions of heartfelt sympathies and condolences from the BTA Members of the Parliament provide refuge and solace to his grieving family during this difficult time in their lives;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it;**

**RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** that the BTA Members of the Parliament express its sincerest gratitude to Dr. Amirel Sanday Usman for his dedicated service to the Bangsamoro people and conveying its deepest condolences to his family for his demise;

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that a copy of this resolution be furnished to the bereaved family and loved ones of Dr. Amirel Sanday Usman.
