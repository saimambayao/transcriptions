**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MR. KURAIZ ALAMADA ABUBAKAR FROM COTABATO CITY FOR RANKING TOP 8 IN THE SEPTEMBER 2022 PHILIPPINE VETERINARIAN LICENSURE EXAMINATION AND FOR GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS,** the Philippine Veterinary Medical Licensure Examination was conducted at testing centers located at Manila, Baguio, Cagayan de Oro, Cebu, Davao, Koronadal, Legazpi and Tuguegarao last 27-29 September 2022;

**WHEREAS,** on 3 October 2022, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced the result of the September 2022 Veterinarian Licensure Examination where out of the nine hundred twenty eight (928) aspirants, three hundred thirty five (335) of whom passed the said exam given by the Board of Veterinary Medicine;

**WHEREAS,** among the Top 10 performing examinees, Mr. Kuraiz Alamada Abubakar successfully passed and ranked TOP 8 with an average score of 82.82% in the September 2022 Philippine Veterinary Medical Licensure Examination of the Professional Regulation Commission;

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Kuraiz Alamada Abubakar, who graduated at the University of Southern Mindanao – Kabacan at the age of 25, is a young Bangsamoro professional, and an *Iranon* hailing from Cotabato City of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**WHEREAS,** his exemplary performance to become one of the topnotchers is a source of inspiration for the Bangsamoro children, youth and young professionals not only in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region but also in the entire country which then merit this Parliament’s recognition and commendation;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Transition Authority is cognizant of Mr. Kuraiz Alamada Abubakar for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro Community as we aim to stay true to our belief that the youth are not only our future, in as much as they are also our present;

**NOW THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to congratulate and commend Mr. Kuraiz Alamada Abubakar for ranking Top 8 in the September 2022 Philippine Veterinarian Licensure Examination and for giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVE, FURTHER,** to present a copy of this resolution to Mr. Kuraiz Alamada Abubakar for his information and its necessary effects.
