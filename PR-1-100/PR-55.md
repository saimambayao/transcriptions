**A RESOLUTION**
**CALL FOR THE SUSPENSION OF THE PLENARY SESSION IN OCTOBER 2022 EXCEPT FOR THE SESSION ON OCTOBER 17, 2022 TO GIVE WAY TO COMMITTEE ACTIVITIES FOR PRIORITY LEGISLATIVE MEASURES**

**WHEREAS,** under Article XVI, Section 4 of Republic Act No 11054, or the Organic Law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) is entrusted with the enactment of priority legislations that include, among others, the Bangsamoro Electoral Code and the Bangsamoro Local Government Code;

**WHEREAS,** in his speech during the Second BTA Parliament’s inaugural session, Chief Minister Ahod Ebrahim affirmed the interim government’s commitment to enact the Bangsamoro Electoral Code and the Bangsamoro Local Government Code by the first quarter of 2023;

**WHEREAS,** Article VII, Section 27 of the Bangsamoro Organic Law mandates the Parliament to pass an annual appropriations law that sets out the Bangsamoro Government’s allocation of public funds;

**WHEREAS,** the enactment of the aforesaid priority legislations is now at the committee level;

**WHEREAS,** during its first regular meeting, the Committee on Rules, recognized the need to optimize the time and resources of the Parliament to enact the priority legislations within the desired timeline;

**WHEREAS,** the Committee on Rules has resolved unanimously to recommend the suspension of the plenary session in October 2022 except for the session on October 17 to give way to committee deliberations and public consultations;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to suspend the plenary session in October 2022 except for the session on October 17 to give way to committee activities for priority legislative measures.
