**A RESOLUTION COMMENDING MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY – MAIN CAMPUS, MARAWI CITY FOR RANKING 6TH NATIONWIDE WITH A RATING OF 94.85% AMONG SCHOOLS AND UNIVERSITIES WITH 10 OR MORE EXAMINEES AND WITH AT LEAST 80% PASSING PERCENTAGE IN THE SEPTEMBER 2022 SOCIAL WORKER LICENSURE EXAMINATION**

**WHEREAS,** on the 27th day of September 2022, after three working days from the last day of the examination, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) released the results of the September 2022 Social Worker Licensure Examination (SWLE);

**WHEREAS,** with an excellent rating of 94.85%, Mindanao State University-Main campus, Marawi City ranked 6th among Schools and Universities with ten or more examinees and with at least 80% passing percentage;

**WHEREAS,** out of the 97 MSU examinees, 92 of the attendees have successfully passed, and out of the 4,723 total of nationwide examinees, only 2,955 attendees conquered said licensure examination;

**WHEREAS,** MSU system is the only university in the Philippines with a special mandate of integrating the cultural communities, specially the Muslims into the mainstream of the nation’s socio-cultural and political life by providing them with opportunities for quality and relevant public education for their self-development and providing trained manpower skills and technical know-how for the economic development of the Mindanao, Sulu, and Palawan region;

**WHEREAS,** MSU, particularly Iligan Institute of Technology and Main campus Marawi City also secured their spots in the top 10 Performing Philippine Universities as released by the Global Filipino Magazine sourced from the July 2022 edition of the WEBOMETRICS RANKING, placed at 5th and 6th respectively, and also with a fair ranking worldwide;

**WHEREAS,** the Social Sork Department in the College of Public Administration of Mindanao State University – main campus, Marawi City produced manifold topnotchers and superlative number of passers ever since its establishment due to the standard of education, and compassionate support to their aspirants;

**WHEREAS,** for this anticipated accomplishment, and also to the continuous prestige, recognition, and quality & relevant education the MSU is contributing to the Bangsamoro people which emphasizes merits from this parliament’s commendation;

**WHEREAS,** MSU being one of the major instruments why the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao produces an overwhelming number of professionals annually, it is indubitable that with this privilege, a time will come where majority, if not all, of the Bangsamoro community will be abounded by humble professionals and great leaders;

**NOW THEREFORE,** be it resolved as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to commend Mindanao State University – Main campus, Marawi City ranking 6th nationwide with a rating of 94.85% among schools and universities with ten examinees and with at least 80% passing percentage in the September 2022 SWLE.

**SUBMITTED,** Rabi Ul Awal 02, 1444/September 28, 2022.

**MP KHALID M. HADJI ABDULLAH**
