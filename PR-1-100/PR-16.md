**RESOLUTION**
**ADOPTING THE CALENDAR OF SESSION FOR THE FIRST REGULAR SESSION OF THE SECOND PARLIAMENT**

**WHEREAS,** Section 4, Rule XIII of the Parliamentary Rules, Procedures and Practices of the BTA-BARMM states that:

> *“Section 4. Conduct of Sessions. – Plenary sessions shall commence at one o’clock in the afternoon on Tuesdays through Thursdays of the third and fourth week of the month. xxx*

**WHEREAS,** adopting a Calendar of Session will guide the Members of the BTA Parliament in the performance of their legislative and constituency-serving functions;
