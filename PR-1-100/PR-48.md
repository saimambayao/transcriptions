**RESOLUTION URGING THE BANGSAMORO HUMAN RIGHTS COMMISSION (BHRC), PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE MAGUINDANAO PROVINCIAL OFFICE (PNP-MAGUINDANAO), BARMM SCENE OF THE CRIME OPERATIVES (SOCO) AND OTHER CONCERNED AGENCIES TO CONDUCT SEPARATE AND INDPENDENT INVESTIGATIONS ON THE ALLEGED TORTURE OF MR. JOHAIRE MAONGCO DAGALANGIT THAT RESULTED TO HIS DEATH WHILE IN THE CUSTODY OF PNP BARIRA INSIDE THEIR POLICE STATION ON 25 SEPTMEBR 2022, AND TO PRESENT THEIR FINDINGS AND RECOMMENDATIONS TO THE PARLIAMENT COMMITTEE ON PUBLIC ORDER AND SAFETY**

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Johaire M. Dagalangit, was turned over to the custody of PNP-Barira on 25 September 2022 by the Barangay Captain of Barangay Tugaig, Barira after he accidentally bumped on a child in the said Barangay with his motorcycle;

**WHEREAS,** before his detention, Mr. Johaire was accordingly without any injury or wounds;

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Juhaire spent the night inside the PNP-Barira Police Station;

**WHEREAS,** in the morning of 26 September 2022, policemen from PNP-Barira Police Station went to the nearby house of the parents of Johaire to inform them that their son was found already dead when they tried to wake him up and it could have been due to nightmare;

**WHEREAS,** the family of Johaire went to police station to claim the cadaver only to discover the wounds and bruises on the body of the deceased;

**WHEREAS,** following the recovery of the cadaver, SOCO was requested to conduct examination on the wounds and bruises on the body of Johaire;

**WHEREAS,** the family of the victim questions the cause of his death;

**WHEREAS,** among the identified reasons why many people often times put the law into their hands is the failure of the state to give sufficient attention to alleged cases of oppressions and tortures by authorities;

**WHEREAS,** this incident could add to the list of *ridos* in the region if not addressed properly;

**WHEREAS,** fair and impartial investigation to discover the truth is the goal of this Resolution to craft appropriate policies;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), to urge the Bangsamoro Human Rights Commission (BHRC), Philippine National Police Maguindanao Provincial Office (PNP-Maguindanao) BARMM Scene of the Crime Operatives (SOCO) and other concerned agencies to conduct separate and independent investigations on the alleged torture of Mr. Johaire Maongco Dagalangit that resulted to his death while in the custody of PNP Barira and to present their respective Findings and Recommendations to the Parliament Committee on Public Order and Safety.

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that this shall be held in an Executive Session of the Committee and hence, private and not open for the public considering the sensitivity of the case.
