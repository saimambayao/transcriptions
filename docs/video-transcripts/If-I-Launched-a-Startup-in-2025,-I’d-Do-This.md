### **Organized Notes: The Bootstart Blueprint**

**I. Overview of the Framework**
*   **Goal:** To bootstrap a product to paying customers in 90 days or less.
*   **Background:** Created by Ash Maurya (Author of *Running Lean*) based on a decade of experience and battle-testing across thousands of products.

**II. Level 1: Business Model Design (Duration: ~2 Weeks)** **[01:07]**
*   **Focus:** Getting the idea out of your head and into a shareable format.
*   **Tools:**
    *   **Lean Canvas:** A one-page business modeling tool.
    *   **Lean(er) Canvas:** A simplified version focusing on:
        *   Target audience.
        *   Starting point.
        *   Specific problems to tackle. **[01:55]**
*   **Action Steps:** Sketch up to three variations of the idea and pitch them to friends and potential customers to see if they care.
*   **Objective:** Overcome "overthinking" and determine if the idea is worth the long-term commitment. **[03:21]**

**III. Level 2: Demand Validation (Duration: ~10 Weeks)** **[04:32]**
*   **Philosophy:** "Sell Before You Build." Avoid building a product until you know what customers want.
*   **Strategy: Demo-Sell-Build:** **[05:23]**
    *   Build a **demo**, not a working product.
    *   If you can't sell the demo, do not build the product.
*   **Founder-Led Sales:** Founders must learn to prospect, run discovery interviews, and close deals. **[06:16]**
*   **Objective:** Secure tangible commitments (e.g., Letters of Intent, pre-orders) and transition from an amateur to a professional founder. **[07:44]**

**IV. Level 3: 10X Launch and Growth** **[08:17]**
*   **Focus:** Reaching Product-Market Fit.
*   **The Customer Factory Concept:** A systems approach to generating "Happy Customers." **[08:49]**
    1.  **Activation:** Delivering a great first experience/value realization.
    2.  **Retention:** Bringing customers back repeatedly.
    3.  **Referral:** Happy customers providing revenue and word-of-mouth growth. **[10:04]**
*   **Growth Pattern:** Growth is not a smooth curve but a **staircase**, where each step represents a new insight or skill unlocked. **[10:28]**

**V. Educational Resource: LEANfoundry** **[10:54]**
*   A community-based program designed to help founders implement this 90-day systematic approach.
*   Includes tools, step-by-step playbooks, and cohort-based workshops.
