**1. Revenue Growth Loop**

* Hiring sales teams to scale up your customer acquisition factory
* Or hiring a team to do content generation to build up your top-of-the-funnel awareness

**05:06**

**05:13**

**05:22**

**05:35**

**05:41** 
Lean Canvas also benefited from this growth loop where the canvas became a social artifact that when shared, spread even further.

**3. Retention Growth Loop**

**05:49** 
And finally we have number three, the Retention Growth Loop. This growth loop is powered by some derivative asset or byproduct of happy customers that can be used to attract more prospect or users into the customer acquisition factory.

**06:03** 
Think:
* User-generated content in the case of YouTube
* User-curated artifacts in the case of Pinterest
* Or user-generated reviews and data in the case of Yelp and Waze

**06:13** 
The content or data your happy customers create in your customer success factory feed more leads or prospects into your customer acquisition factory, and that's what powers this growth loop.

**Identifying Your Growth Loop: Three Questions**

**06:25**

2. For the Referral Loop, you can ask: what do customers naturally want to share about your product, and how can you can make that sharing easier?
3. For the Retention Loop, you can ask: what valuable content do users create that could attract new users into your factories?
