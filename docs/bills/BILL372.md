**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BULUAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL TO MAGUINDANAO DEL SUR PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.**

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Parliament in session assembled:

**SECTION 1. Change of Name.** The Buluan District Hospital in Buluan of Maguindanao del Sur, is hereby renamed as the **Maguindanao del Sur Provincial Hospital.**

**SECTION 2. Rationale.** The change of name reflects the hospital's expanded role and commitment to serving the entire province of Maguindanao del Sur. It also acknowledges the administrative and geographical reconfigurations that have led to the creation of the province.

**SECTION 3. Appropriations.** The amount necessary for the effective implementation of this Act, including the costs associated with the change of name, signage, and any necessary administrative adjustments, shall be charged against the appropriations of the Ministry of Health. Thereafter, such sums may be necessary for its continued operation and maintenance as the Maguindanao del Sur Provincial Hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

**SECTION 4. Implementing Rules and Regulations.** The Ministry of Health (MOH), in coordination with the Provincial Government of Maguindanao del Sur, shall formulate the necessary rules and regulations for the effective implementation of this Act within sixty (60) days from its effectivity.

**SECTION 5. Separability Clause.** - If any provision of this Act is declared unconstitutional or invalid, the other provisions not affected thereby shall remain in full force and effect.

**SECTION 6. Repealing Clause.** All laws, decrees, executive orders, proclamations, and administrative regulations or parts thereof inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

**SECTION 7. Effectivity.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its complete publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
