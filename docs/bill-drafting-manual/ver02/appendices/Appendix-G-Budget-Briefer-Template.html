Appendix G — Budget Briefer Template

This template follows the Completed Staff Work (CSW) framework described in Chapter 9. It is designed to be filled in for each agency whose budget the MP will review during the annual Bangsamoro Expenditure Program (BEP) deliberation — whether in Committee on Finance hearings or in plenary.

The template covers sixteen sections. For detailed guidance on how to complete each section, refer to the corresponding sections in Chapter 9.

Section 14 — 1-Minute Manifestation for Budget Approval

(Source: BB16 of the CSW Budget Briefer framework)

Prepare a concise 1-minute speech for the MP to deliver during the Committee on Finance hearing or plenary budget deliberation. Purpose: Prepare a ready-to-deliver 1-minute speech for the MP during committee hearing or plenary.

What to include: A 150-200 word manifestation covering position, BDP alignment, 2-3 PPAS highlights with statistics, legal compliance, constituency impact, and vote recommendation; draft in the MP’s preferred language.

Sources: Section 13 recommendations; Section 3 (BDP alignment); Section 6 (key PPAS); the MP’s previous plenary speeches for tone.
