**AN ACT**
**UPGRADING THE EXISTING DATU BLAH SINSUAT DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN MAGUINDANAO FROM ITS PRESENT LEVEL I WITH A TEN (10) BED CAPACITY INTO LEVEL II WITH AN AUTHORIZED ONE HUNDRED (100) BED CAPACITY AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in Parliament assembled:

**Section 1. Short Title.** – This Act shall be known as the “Datu Blah Sinsuat District Hospital Upgrading Act of 2022”.

**Section 2. Declaration of Policy.** – It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) as provided under Section 22, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the “Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to provide for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the individual’s basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services.

**Section 3. Upgrading of the Datu Blah Sinsuat District Hospital.** – The existing Datu Blah Sinsuat Level I District Hospital is hereby upgraded from its present level with a ten (10) bed capacity into a Level II hospital with a one hundred (100) bed authorized capacity in accordance with the national standards set by the Bureau of Health Facilities and Services under the Department of Health (DOH). The existing buildings including landscaping, medical and office equipment, and other facilities, their operating funds and personnel are also hereby upgraded to include the needed construction and procurement thereof in order to meet the prescribed standards to the upgraded level.

**Section 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program.** – The upgrading of the Datu Blah Sinsuat District Hospital shall integrate into its system of operations all pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic Act No. 11223, otherwise known as the Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

**Section 5. Administration, Management, and Organization.** – The Datu Blah Sinsuat Hospital shall be under the administrative supervision of the Ministry of Health (MOH), which is hereby authorized to determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the management and operations of the facility.

**Section 6. Appropriations.** – Funding is hereby appropriated from the Bangsamoro General Appropriations for the upgrading of the Datu Blah Sinsuat Hospital including the construction of infrastructures, acquisition of medical and office equipment and the annual budget needed for personnel services, maintenance, operations and other expenses for the implementation of this Act, and, thereafter, such amount as may be needed for the continued operations of the hospital.

**Section 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations.** – The MOH-BARMM shall promulgate the necessary implementing rules and regulations within sixty (60) days after the enactment of this Act.

**Section 8. Separability Clause.** – If any provision of this Act or the application of such provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this Act shall not be affected by such declaration.

**Section 9. Repealing Clause.** – All laws and regulations under the BARMM government including those passed under the defunct Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) regarding the operations and upgrading of the Datu Blah Sinsuat Hospital not consistent with this Act are hereby repealed.

**Section 10. Effectivity.** – This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following its publication in a newspaper of regional circulation.
