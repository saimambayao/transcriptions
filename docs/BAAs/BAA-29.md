**AN ACT**
**UPGRADING THE BULUAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL FROM LEVEL I DISTRICT HOSPITAL WITH SEVENTY-FIVE (75) BED CAPACITY TO LEVEL II DISTRICT HOSPITAL WITH ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) BED CAPACITY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BULUAN, PROVINCE OF MAGUINDANAO, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in Parliament assembled:

**Section 1.** The classification of Buluan District Hospital in Buluan, Maguindanao, is hereby converted into Level II District Hospital and its bed capacity is hereby increased from seventy-five (75) bed capacity to one hundred fifty (150) bed capacity under the supervision and control of the Ministry of Health (MOH), Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM).

**Section 2.** The existing hospital facilities and professional health care services of the Buluan District Hospital are hereby upgraded and increased to conform with and commensurate to the increase in bed capacity and classification level. The number of medical personnel shall be correspondingly increased.

**Section 3.** Funding for Personnel Services (PS), Maintenance and Other Operating Expenses (MOOE) shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act of the Bangsamoro. The budget shall be apportioned in three (3) tranches, to start in the calendar year 2022 with the following allocation: Year 1 - 40%, Year 2 -30%, Year 3- 30%.

**Section 4.** The construction and upgrading of the hospital facilities and services shall be under the direct supervision and control of the MOH-BARMM.

**Section 5.** The MOH shall immediately include in the Ministry's 2022 programs the initial implementation of this Act, the funding of which may be charged to available appropriations under the Contingent Funds, with subsequent funding included in the annual Bangsamoro Appropriations Act.

**Section 6.** The provisions of this Act are hereby declared separable, and in the event that one or more such provisions or part thereof are declared unconstitutional, such declaration of unconstitutionality shall not affect the validity of the other provisions not affected thereof.

**Section 7.** All regional laws, acts, decrees, executive orders, proclamations and or administrative regulations which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby amended, modified, suspended, or repealed accordingly.

**Section 8.** This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following its publication in a newspaper of regional circulation.
