**AN ACT**
**ADOPTING THE OFFICIAL HYMN OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament in session assembled:

**Section 1. *Short Title.*** - This Act shall be known as the Bangsamoro Hymn Act of 2020.

**Section 2. *Declaration of Policy.*** – In the exercise of its right to self-governance, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao is free to adopt its Bangsamoro Hymn that conveys and preserves the peoples’ identity, history, heritage, and aspirations.

**Section 3. *Adoption of the Official Bangsamoro Hymn.*** – Pursuant to Article II, Section 2 of Republic Act No. 11054, there is hereby adopted an Official Hymn of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, hereinafter referred to as the Bangsamoro Hymn.

**Section 4. *Official Lyrics.*** – The official lyrics of the Bangsamoro Hymn shall be as follows:

*Simula noon hanggang ngayon*
*Iisa ang naging layon*
*Magkaisa at magbuklod*
*Kagitingan ay marubdob*
*Tumayo tayo mula sa hamon ng nakaraan*
*Niyapos ang panganib na humahadlang*
*Pinangako sa puso at paniniwala*
*Ang ginhawang para sa kabataan*

*Bangsamoro ’y tagumpay*
*Bunga ng pawis, dugo, at buhay*
*Kapayapaan, katarungan*
*Ay atin nang nakamtan*
*Alhamdulillah, Alhamdulillah*

*Pagpalain Bangsamoro*
*Bangsamoro, Bangsamoro*
*Lagi kang mamahalin*
*Walang pipigil sa damdamin*
*Mga pangako’y tutuparin*
*Habang-buhay kami sayo’y magbabantay*

*Mananatili sa puso ’t isipan*
*Ang kahapong humimlay na*
*Nagbuwis ng buhay*
*Nasa piling nang maykapal*

*Bangsamoro’y tagumpay*
*Bunga ng pawis, dugo, at buhay*
*Kapayapaan, katarungan*
*Ay atin nang nakamtan*
*Alhamdulillah, Alhamdulillah*
*Pagpalain Bangsamoro*

**Section 5. *Rendition of the Bangsamoro Hymn.*** – The rendition of the Bangsamoro Hymn, whether played or sung, shall always be in accordance with the official musical arrangement and composition. An Arabic, English or any of the Bangsamoro language or dialect version of the official Bangsamoro Hymn as approved by the Chief Minister shall likewise be considered official.

All public and private schools, colleges and universities, public offices, government-owned or controlled corporations, and other government instrumentalities within the Bangsamoro are required to play and sing the Bangsamoro Hymn during flag-raising and flag lowering ceremonies. Other offices of the Bangsamoro that are outside its area of autonomy are likewise required to play and sing the Bangsamoro Hymn.

The Bangsamoro Hymn may also be played and sung in ceremonial and symbolic functions and private activities.

Whenever the Bangsamoro Hymn is sung, it shall always be preceded by the singing of the Philippine National Anthem pursuant to Article II, Section 2 of Republic Act No. 11054.

**Section 6. *Penalty.*** – Any person found willfully violating any provision of this Act shall upon conviction shall be punished by a fine of not less Fifty Thousand Pesos (Php 50,000.00) but not more than One Hundred Thousand Pesos (Php 100,000.00) or by an imprisonment for not more than six (6) months or both fine and imprisonment at the discretion of the court.

**Section 7. *Repealing Clause.*** – All previous laws, Executive Orders, other decrees, and rules and regulations or parts thereof inconsistent herewith are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

**Section 8. *Separability Clause.*** – Any provision of this Act that will be found by a competent court to be contrary to the Constitution or the Organic Law, such provision shall be severed from this Act, and such action shall not affect the enforce ability of the remaining provisions hereof.

**Section 9. *Effectivity.*** – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a regional newspaper of general circulation in the Bangsamoro.
