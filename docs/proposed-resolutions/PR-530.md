**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MS. HANNAH ACOSTA SAKAY FOR PLACING THIRD IN THE FEBRUARY 2024 RESPIRATORY THERAPIST LICENSURE EXAMINATION AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS**

**WHEREAS,** the Professional Regulations Commission (PRC) announced the recent passers of the Respiratory Therapist Licensure Examination given by the Board of Respiratory Therapy last February 6-7, 2024 held in National Capital Region, and cities of Baguio, Cebu, Davao, and Zamboanga;

**WHEREAS,** out of 1,077 passers, Ms. Hannah Acosta Sakay garnered third place with a general average of 91.25% in the said board exam;

**WHEREAS,** Ms. Sakay graduated Cum Laude from San Pedro College in Davao City last June 2023. She completed her elementary education at Al Durah Gems International School, Jeddah, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia with High Honor, 4th Honor in Junior High, and Salutatorian in Senior High School at Notre Dame University, Cotabato City;

**WHEREAS,** Ms. Sakay is a Maguindanaon and a daughter of Mr. Alfonso R. Sakay, a Muslim revert, and Mrs. Nympha Acosta Sakay both a resident of Datu Odin Sinsuat Municipality, Maguindanao del Norte, BARMM;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Transition Authority is cognizant of the feat of Ms. Hannah Acosta Sakay and those of all Bangsamoro passers of the 2024 PAM Certification Examination for registered microbiologists.

**NOW THEREFORE,** be it **RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved, to congratulate and commend Ms. Hannah Acosta Sakay for placing third in the February 2024 Respiratory Therapist Licensure Examination and all Bangsamoro passers.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** To forward the copy of this Resolution to Ms. Hannah Acosta Sakay, for information.

ADOPTED.
