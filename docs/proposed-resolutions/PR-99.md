**RESOLUTION**
**REQUESTING THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS AND HIGHWAYS (DPWH) - NATIONAL TO PRIORITIZE THE MAINTENANCE AND REHABILITATION OF NATIONAL HIGHWAYS AND BRIDGES IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM)**

WHEREAS, the DPWH-National is responsible for the planning, design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure, especially the national highways, flood control and water resources development systems, alongside other public works in accordance with national development objectives;

WHEREAS, **Section 37, Article XIII** of Republic Act No. 11054, or the Bangsamoro Organic Law, provides that the national government shall fund and implement the construction and maintenance of national roads, bridges, water supply services, flood control and irrigation systems, and the maintenance of existing airports, seaports, and wharves in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

WHEREAS, **Section 6, Article VI**, of the same law created an Intergovernmental Infrastructure Development Board, which shall be responsible for coordinating and synchronizing a national and Bangsamoro infrastructure development plan. The Board is composed of members from the appropriate agencies in BARMM, with the Secretary of Public Works and Highways as a member and a representative from the National Government, with his designated officials for the said purpose;

WHEREAS, as of 2019, a total 992.62 kilometers of national roads and 210 bridges (6,724 kilometers) are located in Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, that require regular maintenance and some needs major rehabilitation;

WHEREAS, some of the national roads in BARMM are in deplorable condition, with practically no regular repair and maintenance that make the national roads almost impassable, particularly the Narciso Ramos Highway that links BARMM to major cities in Mindanao;

WHEREAS, the Quirino Bridge, which was constructed during the 1950s, connects the province of Maguindanao and Cotabato City, is now in its critical stage and needs immediate rehabilitation;

WHEREAS, the Simuay Bridge located along the Narciso Ramos National Highway that connects Lanao del Sur and Maguindanao, has been reported to be in deteriorating condition and was grossly affected by the Severe Tropical Storm Paeng (Nalgae);

WHEREAS, last 29 October 2022, the Nituan Bridge located at Parang, Maguindanao del Norte and the Labu-Labu Bridge located at Datu Hoffer, Maguindanao del Sur were both declared impassable as a result of the heavy volume of water brought about by Severe Tropical Storm Paeng that affected the abutment and foundation of the bridges;

WHEREAS, national roads such as the Cotabato-Alabel-Gensan Road with asphalt overlay have an observable number of small to large holes that pose a danger to the public;

WHEREAS, the MPW-BARMM shall coordinate and cooperate with the DPWH-National for the immediate repair and rehabilitation of the national roads and bridges, with priority to the Cotabato-Alabel-Gensan Road and Narciso Ramos Highways that traverse across the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region mainland, consistent with the mandate of the Organic Law specifically on joint cooperation of the national government and Bangsamoro Government on infrastructure development programs and projects in BARMM;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED** by the BTA Parliament, to pass and approve this Resolution requesting the DPWH-National to prioritize the maintenance and rehabilitation of national highways and bridges in the BARMM.
