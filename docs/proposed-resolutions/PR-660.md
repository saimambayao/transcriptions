**BTA RESOLUTION No. 660**

**RESOLUTION URGING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE AND OTHER AUTHORITIES OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO TO EMMEDIATELY ACT AND INVESTIGATE THE BRUTAL KILLING OF BAIWAN BANOG ANGAN IN SITIO KUKOR, BRGY. MANTAO, DATU HOFFER, MAGUINDANAO DEL SUR**

Introduced by:

**MP FROILYN T. MENDOZA**

Co-Author

**WHEREAS**, the 1987 Philippine Constitution under the Bill of Rights provide that, No person shall be deprived of life, Liberty, or property without due process of law, nor shall any person be denied the equal protection of the Laws.

**WHEREAS**, on December 7, 2027, at approximately 1:00 PM in Sitio Kukor, Brgy. Mantao, Datu Hoffer, Maguindanao Del Sur, Baiwan Banog Angan and Cita Manguda Angan went to the river to fish. While there, they were approached by armed men. The armed individuals instructed Cita Manguda Angan to return home. Shortly afterward, gunfire erupted from unidentified firearms, tragically resulting in the death of Baiwan Banog Angan.

**WHEREAS**, The perpetrator allegedly subjected the victim to abuse before his death, forcing him to dig his own grave before being fatally shot to death.

**WHEREAS**, we strongest condemn the vicious killing of Baiwan Banog Angan who is a Non- Moro Indigenous Peoples Leader Maguindanao Del Sur from the *Menubu Dulangan Tribe*.

**WHEREAS**, in the spirit of Justice, it is imperative to bring the perpetrators to the court of Laws for his criminal act.

**WHEREFORE, RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, that the Philippine National Police and other government authority to act immediately for impatial investigation of the brutal killing of Baiwan Banog Angan and bring the assailant to the court of Justice for the criminal act have done.
