**A RESOLUTION URGING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT, THRU BAMGSAMORO SPORTS COMMISSION, TO CONDUCT AN INTER-GOVERNMENT AGENCY SPORTS FESTIVAL DURING THE CELEBRATION OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO’S (BARMM) FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY**

**WHEREAS**, Section 15, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides:

> “*<u>Section 15</u>. The State shall protect and promote the right to health of the people and instill health consciousness among them.*”

**WHEREAS**, Section 17, Article II of the same also expresses that the State shall give priority to sports to foster patriotism and nationalism, accelerate social progress, and promote total human liberation and development;

**WHEREAS**, moreover, Section 3, Article V of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the “*Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao*,” states:

> “*<u>Section 3. General Welfare</u>. The Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the power expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective governance and those which are essential to the promotion of general welfare. Within its territorial jurisdiction, the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure and support, among other things, ... xx... promote health and safety ... xx.*”

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) commemorates its anniversary each year, celebrating the establishment of the autonomous region and its ongoing progress toward peace, development, and prosperity;

**WHEREAS**, the celebration of BARMM’s founding anniversary is a significant event that symbolizes the unity, progress, and cultural heritage of the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, sports and physical activities play a vital role in promoting health, camaraderie, discipline, and teamwork among individuals and communities;

**WHEREAS**, holding a sports festival during the BARMM anniversary will provide an opportunity to unite employees from different Bangsamoro government ministries, offices, and agencies, fostering camaraderie, respect, and a sense of regional identity;

**WHEREAS**, an inter-government agency sports festival would serve as an excellent platform to foster stronger relationships and collaboration among various government agencies within BARMM, enhancing inter-agency cooperation and coordination;

**WHEREAS**, furthermore, such an event will showcase the talents and skills of Bangsamoro employees, inspiring them to participate in sports and adopt healthy lifestyles;

**WHEREAS**, the sports festival can feature a range of activities, including basketball, volleyball, football, athletics, traditional games, and other sports, making it an inclusive event for all age groups and abilities;

**WHEREAS**, the conduct of such activity will give the employees something to look forward to despite hectic schedules and workloads;

**WHEREAS**, organizing an event such as this will demonstrate the Bangsamoro Government’s commitment to promoting health and wellness among its workforce;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to **URGE THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT, THRU BAMGSAMORO SPORTS COMMISSION, TO CONDUCT AN INTER-GOVERNMENT AGENCY SPORTS FESTIVAL DURING THE CELEBRATION OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO’S (BARMM) FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY.**
