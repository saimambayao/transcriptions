**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MR. AL-ZAHEER ADIL ABUBAKAR, A BANGSAMORO GRADUATE OF NOTRE DAME UNIVERSITY – COTABATO CITY, FOR RANKING TENTH (10TH) WITH AN IMPRESSIVE RATING OF 90.40% IN THE MARCH 2023 LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR PROFESSIONAL TEACHERS - ELEMENTARY LEVEL, FOR GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, Section 16 of Article IX or Republic Act No. 11054 or otherwise known as the *Bangsamoro Organic Law* states that education shall be a top priority of the Bangsamoro Government to establish, maintain, and support a complete and integrated system of quality education, which shall be a subsystem of the national education system;

**WHEREAS**, Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and the Professional Regulatory Board for Professional Teachers (BPT) administered the regular Licensure Examination for Professional Teacher on March 19, 2023 in various testing centers nationwide;

**WHEREAS**, the results of 2023 Licensure Examination for Professional Teacher – Elementary Level were released on May 19, 2023 with a 40.76% passing rate or 24,819 passers out of 60,896 takers;

**WHEREAS**, among the 24,819 elementary teacher passers, Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar, a Bangsamoro of Maguindanaon descent, admirably ranked tenth (10th) with an average score of 90.40%;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Abubakar, who hails from Cotabato City, is also a recipient of a Latin academic honor as Cum Laude from Notre Dame University – Cotabato City last 2022.

**WHEREAS**, his exemplary performance as one of the topnotchers is the source of inspiration for the Bangsamoro children, youth, and professionals not only in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region but also in the entire country, and elevated the Bangsamoro’s educational profile among our younger generations inspiring them as the model of scholastic achievement;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority is cognizant of Mr. Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar together with other LET Bangsamoro passers for bringing honor and pride to the Bangsamoro people. He is living proof that the youth are not only our future in as much as they are also our present: **NOW THEREFORE, be it;**

**RESOLVED, BY THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY**, to congratulate and commend Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar, a Bangsamoro graduate of Notre Dame University, for ranking tenth (10th), with an impressive rating of 90.40% in the March 2023 Licensure Examination for Teachers at Elementary Level, for giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that a copy of this resolution be presented to Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar, for information.

Adopted, __________.
