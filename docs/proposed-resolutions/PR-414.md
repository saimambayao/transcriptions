**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MS. AISHAH AMBOR GANDAMRA FOR HER REMARKABLE ACHIEVEMENT IN WINNING SILVER MEDAL IN THE HONGKONG INTERNATIONAL MATHEMATICAL OLYMPIAD (HKIMO) FINAL ROUND 2023 AND FOR GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, the International Mathematical Olympiad (HKIMO) was held from August 25 to 28, 2023.

**WHEREAS**, HKIMO, is named as Hong Kong International Mathematical Olympiad, developed by former Hong Kong Mathematical Olympiad Team Leader Mr Wong Tin Chun;

**WHEREAS**, the objective to develop HKIMO is to promote Mathematical Olympiad all over the world;

**WHEREAS**, Aishah Ambor Gandamra, a Bangsamoro student from Tambo Central School, was one of the representatives who participated in the International Mathematical Olympiad (HKIMO).

**WHEREAS**, she also brought home the Gold Award (Primary 4 Division) in the Thailand International Mathematical Olympiad Final Round 2022-2023 held at Pattaya, Thailand on April 1-2, 2023;

**WHEREAS**, Aishah Ambor Gandamra, 11 years of age, is a young Bangsamoro of Maranao descent and a proud daughter of Abdul Rahman C. Gandamra and Sittie Nur-aine M. Ambor-Gandamra who are natives of the Islamic City of Marawi and the Municipality of Taraka, Lanao Del Sur, BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, her hard work and commitment have resulted in her remarkable achievement of winning the Silver Medal in the HKIMO;

**WHEREAS**, her success did not only showcase her competitive spirit and personal excellence, but also served as a source of inspiration and motivation for all Bangsamoro children to pursue academic excellence and excel in various fields of knowledge;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority is cognizant of Aishah Ambor Gandamraâ€™s achievement in bringing pride and prestige to the Bangsamoro people on the international platform, hence deems it fitting to commend her success as we stay true to our belief that the youth are not only our future, in as much as they are also our present;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to congratulate and commend Ms. Aishah Ambor Gandamra for her remarkable achievement in winning silver medal in the Hongkong International Mathematical Olympiad (HKIMO) final round 2023 and for giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVE, FURTHER**, to present a copy of this resolution to Ms. Aishah Ambor Gandamra in recognition and expression of appreciation of her exemplary performance and hard work that brought pride to the Bangsamoro people.
