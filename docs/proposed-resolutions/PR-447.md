**RESOLUTION**
**COMMENDING AND THANKING THE KONRAD ADENAUER STIFTUNG (KAS) FOR ITS INVALUABLE CONTRIBUTION TO THE DEVELOPMENT OF AUTONOMY AND PEACE AND DEVELOPMENT IN THE BANGSAMORO SINCE 2001**

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution categorically provides that the Philippines renounces war as an instrument of national policy, adopts the generally accepted principles of international law as part of the law of the land and adheres to the policy of peace, equality, justice, freedom, cooperation, and amity with all nations;

**WHEREAS**, Section 7, Article II of the Constitution provides that the State shall pursue an independent foreign policy. In its relation with other states, the paramount consideration shall be national sovereignty, territorial integrity, national interest, and the right to self-determination;

**WHEREAS**, Section 3, Article V of Republic Act No. 11054, or the Bangsamoro Organic Law, provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the powers expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective governance and those which are essential to the promotion of general welfare;

**WHEREAS**, the Konrad Adenauer Stiftung (KAS) is political foundation inspired by the ideals of Konrad Adenauer as the first Chancellor of the Federal Republic of Germany;

**WHEREAS**, KAS is founded on the basic principles of freedom, justice and solidarity and has made significant contribution in supporting people to live self-determined lives in freedom and dignity;

**WHEREAS**, KAS has been actively promoting democracy, the rule of law and a social market economy in its 70 offices abroad and projects in 120 countries;

**WHEREAS**, KAS has been promoting democracy and political institutions in the Philippines since 1964;

**WHEREAS**, KAS has been providing grants, technical assistance and capacity building in the Bangsamoro region particularly in strengthening autonomy, governance and political parties since 2001;

**WHEREAS**, KAS, with the European Union, has been in forefront in promoting the parliamentary and political party system in the Bangsamoro thru the programs **Democratic Party Development Project (DEPADEV)** and the **Democratic Leadership and Active Civil Society Empowerment (DELACSE) Bangsamoro** from 2014 to 2022 that raised capacities of CSOs and emerging political parties including that of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF), the Moro National Liberation Front (MNLF), women, youth and IP groups in developing principled political parties in the Bangsamoro and empowered stakeholders and underrepresented groups to transform their interest groups and operate them as functional programmatic political parties;

**WHEREAS**, KAS is in consortium with PARTICIP Germany in implementing the **Support to Bangsamoro Transition (SUBATRA) Programme**, that is providing technical assistance in building the foundation for lasting peace and development in the Bangsamoro by strengthening the capacities of its institutions to establish an enabling democratic governance environment during the transition period;

**WHEREAS**, KAS has been consistently and sustainably providing trainings and technical assistance to the Bangsamoro policy bodies particularly the Regional Legislative Assembly of the ARMM, the Bangsamoro Transition Commission, and the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in fulfilling their mandates in building effective public policies and institutions in the BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, KAS has brought many key Bangsamoro government and non-governmental leaders in Germany and Europe for study and benchmarking sessions that strengthened international support to the cause of the Bangsamoro people and enriched the discourse and institution building in the Bangsamoro towards effective autonomous and parliamentary system;

**WHEREAS**, KAS has assisted the indigenous peoples in the Bangsamoro through the program **“Recognition of the Rights of the Indigenous Peoples in the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao for their Empowerment and Sustainable Development”** thereby significantly contributing to the promotion of inclusive peace and governance in the Bangsamoro;

**WHEREAS**, KAS thru the annual Kusog Mindanaw Conference, has significantly helped promote the Bangsamoro peace process into the national and international arena particularly in promoting autonomy and federalism as sustainable solutions to the Mindanao conflict; and

**WHEREAS**, KAS and partners have produced numerous publications and policy papers that helped raise awareness and capacities of policymakers, academic institutions, international partners organizations, CSOs and advocacy groups on key political and governance issues in the Bangsamoro.

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament to extends its recognition and appreciation to the Konrad Adenauer Stiftung for its continuing support to the Bangsamoro and for its steady and consistent work for sustainable peace and development in the region, thereby approving this Resolution commending and thanking the Konrad Adenauer-Stiftung (KAS) for its invaluable contribution to the development of autonomy and peace and development in the Bangsamoro since 2001.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that a copy of this resolution be presented to **Ms. Daniela Braun**, the country Director of the KAS in the Philippines on the occasion of her official visit to the Bangsamoro Transition Authority on November 28, 2023.
