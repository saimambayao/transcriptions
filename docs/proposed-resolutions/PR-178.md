**A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST SYMPATHY AND CONDOLENCES TO THE BEREAVED FAMILY OF MR. ADZIS ULANAN GUIMBANG AND RECOGNIZING HIS HEROIC ACT IN PERFORMING HIS DUTIES AS COTABATO CITY TRAFFIC ENFORCER**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority mourns and expresses deep sorrow over the loss of Mr. Adzis Ulanan Guimbang, 53 years old, married, father of a minor child and a resident of Poblacion 9, Biniruan, Cotabato City, who is employed as a Traffic Enforcer of Cotabato City Public Safety Office - Traffic Management Unit;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Guimbang dedicated his life in the service of his family and his community for his untiring public service, having served as Traffic Enforcer from July 1, 2022 until his death;

**WHEREAS**, he exhibited deep sense of commitment and diligence in the discharge of his obligations, worth emulating to his profession;

**WHEREAS**, his passing away is a great loss not only to his family but also to his peers in the service and to the community as well;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority joins the bereaved family of Mr. Adzis Ulanan Guimbang in beseeching the Almighty Allah in His wisdom and compassion to grant the repose of his soul and eternal rest and peace;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it;**

**RESOLVED, AS IT HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to express deepest sympathy and condolences to the bereaved family of Mr. Adzis Ulanan Guimbang and to recognize his heroic act in performing his duties as Cotabato City Traffic Enforcer.
