**A RESOLUTION**
**ENCOURAGING THE DEPARTMENT OF FINANCE (DOF) TO ESTABLISH AND OPERATIONALIZE THE BANGSAMORO REGIONAL OFFICE OF THE BUREAU OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT FINANCE (BLGF) IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION**

**WHEREAS,** Section 3, Article XII of the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) **created the Bangsamoro Regional Office of the Bureau of Local Government Finance under the Department of Finance**, which shall have the authority to coordinate, assist, and monitor the treasury and assessment operations of constituent local government units within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, in pursuance of good governance and local autonomy. The regional office shall be guided by the standards set by the Department of Finance-Bureau of Local Government Finance including the requirements set for the appointment of local treasurers;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro receives an annual block grant and other funds from the National Government, through the Department of Budget and Management (DBM);

**WHEREAS,** the block grant passes through a circuitous process before it reaches the Bangsamoro Government. The block grant released from the DBM is transferred to the Department of Finance-Bureau of Local Government Finance (DOF-BLGF) in Koronadal City, Region XII; from DOF-BLGF Koronadal City, it is transferred to the Office of the Chief Minister (OCM) in Cotabato City; then from the OCM, to the Bangsamoro Treasury Office (BTO);

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region covers five (5) provinces of Maguindanao, Lanao del Sur, Sulu, Tawi-Tawi, and Basilan; three (3) cities of Cotabato, Marawi, and Lamitan; 116 municipalities, and 2,590 barangays;

**WHEREAS,** setting-up a BLGF Regional Office in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, preferably in Cotabato City, enhances and fast tracks the flow of transactions;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Administrative Code identified Cotabato City as the seat of government, unless otherwise provided by a succeeding law;

**WHEREAS,** the national tax allotment (NTA) of the LGUs are directly released by the Department of Budget and Management to the LGUs through the BLGF;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Government, through its ministries and offices, extend services to the communities also being served by the LGUs in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS,** there is a need for the complementation of services of the Bangsamoro Government and LGUs for their constituents in order to maximize utilization of resources;

**WHEREAS,** there is a need to channel these resources in the BLGF Regional Office, which should be proximate to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Regionâ€™s seat of government, in order to fast track the flow of transactions;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the BTA Parliament, to pass and approve this Resolution Encouraging the Department of Finance (DOF) to Establish and Operationalize the Bangsamoro Regional Office of the Bureau of Local Government Finance (BLGF) in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.

**BE IT RESOLVED FURTHER,** that a copy of this resolution be furnished to the Department of Finance and Department of Budget and Management.
