**A RESOLUTION ENCOURAGING ALL OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES OF MINISTRIES, AGENCIES, AND OFFICES, INCLUDING THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS (LGUs) OF BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM) TO TAKE PART IN THE VALUES TRANSFORMATION TRAININGS CONDUCTED BY THE BANGSAMORO DEVELOPMENT AGENCY (BDA)**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government is committed to establishinh a governance system rooted in moral principles and the teachings of Islam to Moral Governance;

**WHEREAS**, moral governance, as defined in Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13. also known as the Bangsamoro Administrative Code, encompasses as framework or set of rules, practices, and processes completely devoid of all the evils of graft and corruption, and explicitly driven by the moral principles of utmost dedication, devotion, honesty, justice and integrity”;

**WHEREAS**, the essence of moral governance extends beyond Islamic principles, aiming to infuse ethical virtues derived from Islam into the functions of government, its officials, and employees within the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, recognizing the role of public servants as stewards, it is incubent upon officials and employees of the Bangsamoro Government, including LGU’s, to internalize the moral, ethical, and spiritual dimensions of their roles;

**WHEREAS**, promoting moral governance requires comprehensive training for officials and employees to deeply comprehend and internalize the values underlying moral governance and the historical context of the region;

**WHEREAS**, the Values Transformation Training (VTT) is designed to enhance participants' faith, self-development, and commitment to serving the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, the Training encompasses a range of topics including, but not limited to, Values Transformation and Personal Values Clarification; Concept of Islamic Values and Development in Moral Governance, Faith and its Articles; The Role of Faith, The Islamic Ibaadaat; The Role of Ibaadaat in Developing Islamic Values; Applying Islamic Values; Applying Islamic Values in Moral Governance, and Islamic Values in Dealing with Non-Muslims;

**WHEREAS**, ministries, agencies, and offices, including LGUs, are encouraged to designate a Values Reformation Officer (VRO) from their respective Human Resource Units to coordinate, plan, and facilitate the training in collaboration with BDA;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to wholeheartedly oblige all officials and employees of ministries, agencies, and offices, including the local government units, within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to actively participate in the values transformation trainings conducted by the Bangsamoro Development Agency.
