**A RESOLUTION**
**REQUIRING ALL MINISTRIES AND OFFICES OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM) TO SUBMIT TO THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) PARLIAMENT, AN ANNUAL ACCOMPLISHMENT REPORT OF 2019, 2020, 2021, AND JANUARY TO JUNE 30, 2022**

**WHEREAS,** Article V, Section 3 of R.A. No. 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) provides that "[t]he Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the power expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its **efficient and effective governance** and those which are essential to the promotion of general welfare";

**WHEREAS,** Article VII, Section 2 of the same law states that "the powers of government be vested in the Parliament which shall exercise those powers and functions expressly granted to it in this Organic Law, and those necessary for, or incidental to, the **proper governance** and development of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. It shall set policies, legislate on matters within its authority...";

**WHEREAS,** Article VII, Section 38 of the same law and Title VI, Section 11 of Bangsamoro Autonomy Act (BAA) No. 13 or the Bangsamoro Administrative Code state that "[t]he Bangsamoro Government shall have the power to create offices and **organize its own bureaucracy.** It may establish other offices and institutions necessary for the exercise of is functions and delivery of social services in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;"

**WHEREAS,** more than three years have passed since the shift from the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) to the BARMM. Since then, several ministries and offices were created by the new autonomous government;

**WHEREAS,** several efforts, accomplishments, and progress have been achieved by such ministries and offices of the BARMM. However, it cannot be denied that there are also some gaps in the implementation of programs and projects of the ministries and office. Thus, there must be thorough records of such efforts and accomplishments of such ministries and offices in order that their progress may be archived and reviewed. Such records will take the form of yearly accomplishment reports;

**WHEREAS,** these accomplishment reports will detail the accomplishments of each ministry and office for 2019, 2020, 2021, and January to June 2022. These reports must include comprehensive physical and financial accomplishments;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority (BPDA) shall devise a report format that contains the minimum physical and financial contents of the report;

**WHEREAS,** these accomplishment reports shall guide the current and future Bangsamoro Parliaments, in order that appropriate legislations may be crafted to fill in the gaps of such efforts or to further the ministries' or offices' progress and accomplishments;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED** by the BTA Parliament, to pass this Resolution in aid of legislation, requiring all ministries and offices of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) to submit to the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament an Annual Accomplishment Report of 2019, 2020, 2021, and January to June 30, 2022.
