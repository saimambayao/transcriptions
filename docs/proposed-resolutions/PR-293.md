**RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING ATTY. ALLAN GANI PANOLONG FOR HIS ELECTION AS THE EXECUTIVE VICE PRESIDENT OF THE INTEGRATED BAR OF THE PHILIPPINES**

**WHEREAS**, the Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP) is the official organization of all Philippine lawyers whose names appear in the Roll of Attorneys of the Supreme Court¹ with a mission to elevate the standards of the legal profession, to improve the administration of justice, and to enable the Bar to discharge its public responsibility more effectively²;

**WHEREAS**, according to the by-laws of the Integrated Bar of the Philippines, a President and Executive Vice President shall be chosen by the Board of Governors from among nine (9) regional governors, as much as practicable, on a rotation basis, and significantly, after the service of the IBP – National President following his or her election, the Executive Vice President shall **automatically** become President for the next succeeding term³;

**WHEREAS**, on 22 May 2023, ATTY. ALLAN GANI PANOLONG, current President of IBP Lanao del Sur chapter and Governor for Western Mindanao Region, was chosen by the IBP Board of Governors as the new IBP Executive Vice President, who shall serve for a term of two (2) years⁴ starting 01 July 2023 until 30 June 2025;

**WHEREAS**, following his two (2) year service as the Executive Vice President, and upon being qualified thereto, Atty. Panolong shall thereafter assume, and finally serve as the new President for IBP National in 2025;

**WHEREAS**, the Executive Vice President-elect is a native of the Province of Lanao del Sur, a former Board Member of the 2ⁿᵈ District of his province, a good son to Hadja Omelkhair Mapandi Gani and retired Major Tomabilang Gunda Panolong, loving husband to Dr. Murshida Alonto – Panolong, and an affectionate and devoted father to Imran Jibril, Raja Fareez, and Anya Zareenah;

**WHEREAS**, Atty. Panolong’s election as the IBP Executive Vice President is a first, not only in the Province of Lanao del Sur, but also Bangsamoro-wide, and undeniably a history in the making, especially for the humble community of Muslim-Filipinos;

**WHEREAS**, as the first ever Bangsamoro lawyer who have climbed the peak of the structure of the country’s biggest mandatory organization of lawyers, the pride and honor brought by Atty. Panolong to the Bangsamoro deserves praise and recognition from the Bangsamoro Parliament;

**NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED, BY THE BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT**, to commend and congratulate Atty. Allan G. Panolong for his election as the Executive Vice President of the Integrated Bar of the Philippines.

**ADOPTED**,

(Signed)
