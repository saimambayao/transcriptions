**A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST CONDOLENCES AND SYMPATHY TO THE BEREAVED FAMILY OF AMELITA A. PIANG, WIFE OF MEMBER OF THE PARLIAMENT RAMON A. PIANG, SR.**

**WHEREAS**, Amelita A. Piang is the wife of Member of the Parliament Ramon A. Piang Sr., former mayor of Upi, Maguindanao, and the mother of Ms. Ma. Rona Cristina P. Flores the incumbent mayor of the said province.

**WHEREAS**, the late Mrs. Piang, Sr. is the President of the Upi Women’s Federation Inc. During her term as president, the organization received a recognition from the Provincial Government of Maguindanao for their valuable effort against human trafficking through its program, “Stop Trafficking and Exploitation of People Through Unlimited Potentials” (STEP-UP).

**WHEREAS**, Mrs. Piang, Sr. is also a member of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Red Cross Cotabato City, Maguindanao Chapter, from February 2020 up to present. Furthermore, she is also the Chairperson of the Committee on Welfare and Services of the said agency.

**WHEREAS**, an active church member, Mrs. Piang, Sr. joined the Couples for Christ Upi Chapter, a religious organization in their sect, and together with MP Piang Sr., the couple became the Chapter Head of such organization.

**WHEREAS**, because of her generosity and commitment to helping people from different walks of life in their locality, most residents of Upi identify her as “Ina ng bayan ng Upi”.

**WHEREAS**, this resolution signifies the intent of the Bangsamoro Government to sympathize with the bereaved family of the deceased, especially with MP Piang, Sr., and with the whole community of Upi, Maguindanao.

**WHEREAS**, on August 4, 2023, Mrs. Piang, Sr. drew her last breath and returned to our Almighty Creator.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament – Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao **A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST CONDOLENCES AND SYMPATHY TO THE BEREAVED FAMILY OF AMELITA A. PIANG, WIFE OF MEMBER OF THE PARLIAMENT RAMON A. PIANG, SR.**
