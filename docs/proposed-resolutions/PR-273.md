**A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING UTMOST CONGRATULATIONS TO THE NEWLY ELECTED EXECUTIVE VICE PRESIDENT OF THE INTEGRATED BAR OF THE PHILIPPINES, ATTY. ALLAN GANI PANOLONG**

**WHEREAS**, the Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP) is the official organization of all Philippine lawyers whose names appear in the Roll of Attorneys of the Supreme Court¹. It is the mandatory bar association for Filipino lawyers whose purpose is to elevate the standards of the legal profession, improve the administration of justice, and enable the Bar to discharge its public responsibility more effectively;

**WHEREAS**, the Integrated Bar of the Philippines is administered by a Governing Board consisting of nine Governors representing the nine regions of the IBP. The Governing Board elects the IBP National President and IBP Executive Vice President from among themselves or from outside the Board²;

**WHEREAS**, under the Integrated Bar of the Philippines By-laws, the IBP Executive Vice President shall automatically become President for the next succeeding term. The Presidency shall rotate among the nine Regions³;

**WHEREAS**, on May 22, 2023, Atty. Allan Gani Panolong, the present President of IBP Lanao del Sur Chapter and at the same time the Governor of the IBP Western Mindanao Region, was elected as the new IBP Executive Vice President, who shall automatically become President for IBP National in 2025, making him the very first Muslim Bangsamoro Lawyer to head the Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP);

**WHEREAS**, the election of Atty. Allan Gani Panolong as the new Executive Vice President of the Integrated Bar of the Philippines brings honor and pride to the Bangsamoro Region;

**WHEREAS**, it has become the policy of the Bangsamoro Parliament to give due recognition to the Bangsamoro people who bring honor and prestige to the Bangsamoro Region;

**WHEREAS**, as a token of appreciation of the Bangsamoro Parliament, it is but proper to pass a resolution expressing utmost congratulations to Atty. Allan Gani Panolong;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it resolved as it is hereby **RESOLVED** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to express utmost congratulations to the newly elected Executive Vice President of the Integrated Bar of the Philippines, Atty. Allan Gani Panolong.
