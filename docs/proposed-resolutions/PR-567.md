**Proposed Resolution No. 567**

**Authored by: MP Suharto S. Esmael, MSc**

**A RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT THROUGH THE OFFICE OF THE HONORABLE CHIEF MINISTER AHOD BALAWAG EBRAHIM TO INSTITUTE A JURIS DOCTOR BRIDGING PROGRAM IN ORDER TO PREPARE AND QUALIFY OVERSEAS AND LOCAL KULLIYAH GRADUATE ULAMA FOR LEGAL PRACTICE AND TO A JUDGE POSITIONS IN THE BANGSAMORO SHARI'AH COURTS**

**WHEREAS,** Section 1, Article X, of Republic Act 11054, otherwise known as, the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL), provides a justice system in accordance with the unique cultural and historical heritage of the Bangsamoro;

**WHEREAS,** Section 8, Article X of the BOL specifies the qualifications of judges in the Shari'ah Circuit, District and High Courts, which include passing the Philippine Bar;

**WHEREAS,** Section 13, Article X of said law specify who can practice before the Shari'ah courts, which include Shari'ah Special Bar and the Philippine Bar members;

**WHEREAS,** Section 16, Article X of the BOL, calls on the Bangsamoro Parliament to creation legal offices, such as a Shari'ah Public Assistance Office, Shari'ah Special Prosecution Service, a Shari'ah Academy and Office of the Jurisconsult of Islamic Law;

**WHEREAS,** the ulama (religious leaders) together with the umara (political leaders), in the Islamic context, is an integral part of Islamic leadership. Thus, a critical stakeholder and sector in the development of the Bangsamoro;

**WHEREAS,** in order to assume their roles and positive engagement in the regional Shari'ah justice system, a bridging program is needed to meet legal qualification of the Philippine Bar and qualify for appointment to the Shari'ah courts within Bangsamoro;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it RESOLVED,** as it is hereby RESOLVED, requesting the Bangsamoro Government through the Office of the Honorable Chief Minister Ahod Balawag Ebrahim to institute a Juris Doctor bridging program as a scholarship grant in order to prepare and qualify overseas and local kulliyah graduate ulama for legal practice and appointment to a judge positions in the Bangsamoro Shari'ah courts.

Adopted.

**Suharto S. Esmael, MSc**
