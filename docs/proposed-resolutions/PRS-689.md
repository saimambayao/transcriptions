**RESOLUTION URGING THE BARMM LEADERSHIP WITH THE CONCERNED AGENCY TO CONDUCT THE DREDGING OF THE RIO GRANDE DE MINDANAO, CALLED THE PULANGI RIVER TO AN APPROPRIATE DEPTH, WIDTH AND LENGTH COVERING ITS PASSAGE WAY FROM THE SPECIAL GEOGRAPHIC AREA TO THE PROVINCES OF MAGUINDANAO DEL NORTE AND MAGUINDANAO DEL SUR, BARMM.**

**WHEREAS**, the occurrence of flood is a problem almost every year that destroys the livelihood of great number of farmers living on a vast portion of the Special Geographic Area, and the Provinces of Maguindanao del Norte and Maguindanao del Sur, BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, dredging of the river is the appropriate solution to prevent such yearly flood, and modern farming with irrigation will flourish. Consequently, it can generate production of hundreds of thousands in volumes of rice, corn, vegetables and other crops in this mainland BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, it will restore the old and pre-colonial state of the riverbeds of the Rio Grande de Mindanao, also called the Pulangi River. It will revive the old capacity of the river to accommodate medium size motor lunch, huge motor boat or pump boat to pass thereon. Then, it will enable the public to transport their products from farm to market through water ways, reducing the overland traffic;

**WHEREAS**, dredging is a maintenance of the natural ecosystem which will enable the disappeared river fish, such as the native clams, the tabong-fish, pegek-fish, etc., to go back to existence in the river, thus reviving the most favorite delicacies of the people;

**WHEREAS**, cleanliness and beautification will be enhanced by giving care to the depth and width of the river. Thus, a compliance to the Islamic principle that cleanliness is half of iman (belief) and the Prophetic saying that "Verily Allah is good (which includes beautiful) and does not accept deeds except those that are similarly good (with beautiful included).

**NOW THEREFOR**, be it resolved as it is hereby resolved to request the BARMM leadership with the concerned agency to conduct the dredging of the Rio Grande de Mindanao, called the Pulangi River to an appropriate depth, width and length covering its passage way from the Special Geographic Area to the provinces of Maguindanao Del Norte and Maguindanao Del Sur, BARMM.
