**A RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE DEPARTMENT OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS TO ENDORSE THE BANGSAMORO PILGRIMAGE AUTHORITY – BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO TO THE MINISTRY OF HAJJ AND UMRAH, JEDDAH, KINGDOM OF SAUDI ARABIA, AS THE AGENCY EXERCISING ADMINISTRATION AND SUPERVISION OVER MUSLIM PILGRIMS FROM WITHIN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION TO MECCA, KINGDOM OF SAUDI ARABIA**

WHEREAS, Section 42, Article VII of the Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known as the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, provides that the Parliament may create other offices such as youth, women, settler communities, disaster risk reduction and management, and planning and development, including a Bangsamoro Pilgrimage Authority which shall exercise jurisdiction over Hajj and Umrah matters affecting pilgrims from within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

WHEREAS, Section 43 of the Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13 (BAA No. 13) otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Administrative Code, provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall have primary jurisdiction over Hajj and Umrah matters affecting pilgrim from within the Bangsamoro. The Bangsamoro Pilgrimage Authority shall act in close coordination with the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos on Hajj and Umrah matters to ensure that the pilgrims from the Bangsamoro are provided with the same level of services as those from outside;

WHEREAS, Section 44 of BAA No. 13 also provides that the Bangsamoro Pilgrimage Authority is primarily responsible for the administration of the annual Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia of pilgrims from within the Bangsamoro. It shall formulate and implement programs, projects and activities for the efficient and effective administration and supervision of the conduct of pilgrimage activities of Bangsamoro pilgrims;

WHEREAS, the formal endorsement of Department of Foreign Affairs to the Ministry of Hajj and Umrah, Jeddah, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia of the Bangsamoro Pilgrimage Authority, as the agency to exercise administration and supervision over muslim pilgrims from within Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, is necessary to carry out its mandate and primary responsibility to administer the annual Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia;

WHEREAS, the authority of Bangsamoro Pilgrimage Authority to administer the annual Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia of muslim pilgrims from within Bangsamoro Autonomous Region is not recognized by the Ministry of Hajj and Umrah of Kingdom of Saudi Arabia due to a fact that there is no formal endorsement from Department of Foreign Affairs;

WHEREAS, without the formal endorsement of the Department of Foreign Affairs, the Bangsamoro Pilgrimage Authority cannot fully carry out its mandate;

WHEREAS, the Department of Foreign Affairs is requested to expedite the necessary procedures to grant endorsement, recognizing the authority of the Bangsamoro Pilgrimage Authority to administer the annual Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia of muslim pilgrims from within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, to request the Department Of Foreign Affairs to endorse the Bangsamoro Pilgrimage Authority – Bangsamoro Autonomous Region In Muslim Mindanao to the Ministry Of Hajj And Umrah, Jeddah, Kingdom Of Saudi Arabia, as the agency exercising administration and supervision over muslim pilgrims from within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region to Mecca, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

**RESOLVED FURTHER** that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to the Office of the President, Department of Foreign Affairs, and Office of the Chief Minister, for information.
