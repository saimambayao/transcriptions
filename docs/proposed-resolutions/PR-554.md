**A RESOLUTION URGING MINISTRIES, OFFICES, AND AGENCIES OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT TO PROMOTE LOCALLY MADE, CULTURALLY RELEVANT TOKENS HIGHLIGHTING THE CULTURE, HERITAGE, AND TRADITIONS OF THE BANGSAMORO REGION IN EVERY SEMINAR AND OTHER OFFICIAL OCCASIONS.**

**WHEREAS,** Ministries, Offices, and Agencies of the Bangsamoro Government are regularly conducting meetings, seminars, and other official gatherings. In its conduct, tokens are usually given to the invited resource speakers and guests;

**WHEREAS,** Article XIII. Regional Economy and Patrimony of Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known as the "Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao," provides:

     Trade and Industry and Tourism

     **Section 24. Trade and Industry in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.** - *The Bangsamoro Government recognizes the private sector as a mover of trade, commerce, and industry. To achieve equity, social justice, and economic development, it shall encourage and support the building up of entrepreneurial capability in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and shall recognize, promote, protect, and support the development of cooperatives and other medium, small, and micro-enterprises. It shall adopt and implement cooperative development policies and programs through the enactment of a Bangsamoro cooperative code.*

     *The Bangsamoro Government shall promote trade and industry in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region by providing avenues through which other countries may learn about its unique industries, economic opportunities, and culture through participation in trade missions, trade fairs, and other promotional activities. It may also organize trade missions to other countries observing the necessary coordination with the relevant government agencies.*

     *The Bangsamoro Government shall also promote domestic trade preference for goods produced and materials sourced from within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and adopt measures to increase their competitiveness. The Bangsamoro Government shall also ensure that Bangsamoro products and services gain considerable access to the markets of its trading partners. Particular attention shall also be given to the markets of its trading partners who have historic and cultural ties to the Bangsamoro people.*

**WHEREAS,** one of the primary purposes of the creation of Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) is the recognition, preservation, and promotion of the Bangsamoro culture, heritage, and traditions;

**WHEREAS,** with the thriving economy in the Bangsamoro Region, the Bangsamoro people are also thrilled to get involved by establishing their businesses, hence the growing number of small-scale entrepreneurs;

**WHEREAS,** to support the success of these small-scale entrepreneurs in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region (BAR), different Ministries, Offices, and Agencies of the Bangsamoro Government should provide avenues through which local goods could be recognized;

**WHEREAS,** the Ministries, Offices, and Agencies of the Bangsamoro Government must patronize and promote local products featuring the culture of the BAR, incorporating them as tokens to be given to the invited resource speakers and guests during seminars and other official occasions;

**WHEREAS,** the support of the Bangsamoro Government and its offices will help showcase the cultural beauty of the Bangsamoro Region, boost tourism, and support entrepreneurs;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to urge the Ministries, Offices, and Agencies of the Bangsamoro government to promote locally made, culturally relevant tokens highlighting the culture, heritage, and traditions of the Bangsamoro region in every seminar and other official occasions.
