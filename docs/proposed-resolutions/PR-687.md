**RESOLUTION URGING THE INTER-GOVERNMENTAL INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT BOARD TO INCLUDE IN ITS PRIORITY AGENDA THE REHABILITATION AND UPGRADING OF THE AWANG-UPI-SOUTH UPI-LEBAK HIGHWAY IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION**

**WHEREAS**, the 12.03-kilometer Awang-Upi-South Upi-Lebak Highway is a major arterial road that connects key municipalities in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region (BARMM), and plays a crucial role in facilitating trade, commerce, and transportation of goods and services within the region and beyond;

**WHEREAS**, the road’s current state has significantly deteriorated over time, posing risks to the safety of commuters, hindering economic activities, and limiting access to vital social services in the region;

**WHEREAS**, its repair and maintenance is within the responsibility of the National Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH), as prescribed under Section 37, Article XIII of the Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the “Bangsamoro Organic Law”.

**WHEREAS**, Section 6, Article VI of the same Organic Law provides that the Intergovernmental Infrastructure Development Board is tasked to coordinate and synchronize the National and Bangsamoro Infrastructre Development Plan;

**WHEREAS**, both the DPWH Secretary and the Minister of Ministry of Public Works (MPW) are members of the Board;

**WHEREAS**, the Awang-Upi-South Upi-Lebak Highway needs the urgent attention of the concerned departments for its immediate repair, rehabilitation, and upgrading that would lead to increased mobility, improved connectivity between municipalities, and enhanced economic integration within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, thereby promoting long-term regional development;

**WHEREAS**, this project is aligned with the Bangsamoro Government’s vision to foster inclusive growth and development by addressing infrastructure gaps and ensuring sustainable infrastructure that can support the region’s socio-economic progress;

**WHEREAS**, with the joint action of the National DPWH and the Bangsamoro MPW, faster decision-making and action can be anticipated for the urgent repair, rehabilitation, and upgrading of the Awang-Upi-South Upi-Lebak Highway within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao jurisdiction;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to urge the Inter-Governmental Infrastructure Development Board to include in its priority agenda the immediate rehabilitation and upgrading of the Awang-Upi-South Upi-Lebak Highway, taking into account the urgency of addressing its deteriorated condition and its importance to the economic and social development of the region;

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the Inter-Governmental Infrastructure Development Board and the Ministry of Public Works, for immediate consideration and appropriate action.
