**RESOLUTION**
**COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING RAYHANAH NUESTRO DECAMPONG FOR RANKING 2ND IN THE RECENTLY CONCLUDED MAY 2023 CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANT LICENSURE EXAMINATION, AS WELL AS ALL OTHER NEW BANGSAMORO CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANTS**

**WHEREAS**, on 30 May 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) released the results of the May 21, 22, and 23, 2023 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination (CPALE);

**WHEREAS**, a total of 2,239 out of 7,376 or 30.36% of examinees passed the CPALE;

**WHEREAS**, along with the release of the CPALE is also the announcement of the topnotchers (Top 10) to which fellow Bangsamoro, **RAYHANAH NUESTRO DECAMPONG** from De la salle University – Manila, managed to secure the second highest spot;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Decampong is a Meranao and a native of the Province of Lanao del Sur, a good and responsible daughter to Norman Decampong and Sharon Nuestra, who also deserve compliments for through their love, support, and cheer, victory is achieved;

**WHEREAS**, her excellent performance in securing the second highest spot in one of the hardest licensure examination in the country is but a pride and honor to the Bangsamoro community and also a source of inspiration to all young Bangsamoro professionals;

**WHEREAS**, the same recognition also goes to all new Bangsamoros Certified Public Accountants who successfully scaled what time and again has proven to be a Herculean battery of exams;

**WHEREAS**, it is imperative that unwavering recognition be given to all Bangsamoro professionals in hurdling their respective licensure examinations to continuously ignite our young professionals’ will and determination to achieve greatness and illustrate that Bangsamoros are of exemplary quality;

**NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED, BY THE BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT**, to commend and congratulate Rayhanah Nuestro Decampong for ranking 2nd in the recently concluded May 2023 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination, as well as other new Bangsamoro Certified Public Accountants.

**ADOPTED**,

(Sgd.)
**KHALID M. HADJI ABDULLAH**
