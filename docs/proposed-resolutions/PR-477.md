**A RESOLUTION URGING THE MINISTRY OF TRADE, INVESTMENTS, AND TOURISM (MTIT) TO CONDUCT TOURISM PLANNING ACTIVITIES IN THE PROVINCES OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM) FOR THE PURPOSE OF CRAFTING A COMPREHENSIVE TOURISM PLAN FOR THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS (LGUs)**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) is endowed with diverse cultural heritage and natural beauty, offering significant potential for tourism development;

**WHEREAS**, tourism is a vital sector that can significantly contribute to the economic growth, cultural preservation, and social development of the BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, the crafting of a comprehensive and sustainable tourism plan for the local government units is essential for maximizing the tourism potential of the region in a responsible and inclusive manner;

**WHEREAS**, the Ministry of Trade, Investments, and Tourism (MTIT) plays a crucial role in the development and promotion of tourism within BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government should strengthen collaborative effort with Local Government Units (LGUs) and key sectors in tourism industry to develop a comprehensive tourism development plan to improve local economy;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, to urge the Ministry of Trade, Investments, and Tourism (MTIT) to:

1) Conduct tourism planning activities, including but not limited to workshops, consultations, and surveys, in the provinces of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

2) Engage with local government units, stakeholders, and communities in these provinces to identify unique tourism opportunities, challenges, and areas for development.

3) Develop a comprehensive tourism plan for each local government unit that aligns with the cultural values, environmental sustainability, and economic objectives of the region.

4) Coordinate with other ministries and agencies to ensure an integrated approach to tourism development that respects the cultural diversity and ecological integrity of BARMM.

5) Provide technical and strategic support to local government units in the implementation of their respective tourism plans.

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that a copy of this resolution be furnished to the Ministry of Trade, Investments, and Tourism (MTIT) for their immediate action and compliance.
