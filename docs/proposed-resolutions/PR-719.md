**RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING THE CHIEF MINISTER TO INVEST IDLE FUNDS, IN EXCESS OF THE NORMAL OPERATING REQUIREMENT OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT, WITH THE BUREAU OF TREASURY**

**WHEREAS**, Section 3, Article 1 of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise knowns as the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that the purpose of the Organic Law is to establish a political entity, provide for its basic structure of government in recognition of the justness and legitimacy of the cause of the Bangsamoro people and the aspirations of Muslim Filipinos and all indigenous cultural communities in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to secure their identity and posterity, allowing for meaningful self-governance;

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article XII of the Organic Law vested fiscal autonomy to the Bangsamoro Government and entitles it to all funds sources enumerated therein, as well as the power to create its sources of revenues;

**WHEREAS**, it is a declared principle of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region that in the exercise of its right to self-governance, it is free to pursue its political, economic, social, and cultural development;

**WHEREAS**, Section 3, Article V of the Organic Law provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the power expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective governance;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in 2019 enacted Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Administrative Code (BAC). The Code was promulgated to prescribe the structural, functional and procedural principles and rules of governance of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**WHEREAS**, Sec. 1, Chapter 1, Title IV, Book V of BAC provides that it is the policy of the Bangsamoro Government to promote, adopt, and implement sound and efficient use of government resources for regional development and for the attainment of economic self-sufficiency and genuine development;

**WHEREAS**, the Ministry of Finance, and Budget and Management (MFBM) is primarily mandated to evolve sound, efficient and responsive fiscal management and utilization of resources. It shall be responsible for the formulation, institutionalization and administration of fiscal policies in coordination with other concerned ministries, agencies and instrumentalities of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, under Sec. 3, Chapter 1, Title IV, Book VI of the Code, the MFBM shall have the powers and functions among others, to (d.) supervise, direct, manage, and control the collection of Bangsamoro Government revenues and (e.) act as custodian of, and manage all financial resources of Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, s, Sec. 12, Chapter IV, Title IV, Book VI of the Code provides for the functions of the Bangsamoro Treasury Office to (a.) receive and safeguard all the revenues generated and collected by the Bangsamoro Government; (b.) act as principal custodian of financial assets and real properties of the Bangsamoro Government; and (c.) develop an investment strategy for investment of funds of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, consistent with the best practice in Public Financial Management, the making of investments of idle funds is one of the main components of a sound cash management of government funds, with the aim of ensuring productive, efficient and effective use of government funds taking into account the amount and timing of cashflow requirements of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, investing the idle funds will provide additional income for the use of the Bangsamoro Government in delivering public goods and services;

**WHEREAS**, in line with the mandate of the Bangsamoro Government to promote the growth of Islamic Finance, preference should be given on Shariâ€™ah-compliant financial instruments in making profitable investments of idle funds, whenever considered feasible;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, as it is hereby resolved, to **AUTHORIZE THE CHIEF MINISTER TO INVEST IDLE FUNDS**, upon the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, and Budget and Management, in accordance with Section 12, Chapter 4, Title IV, Book VI of the BAA No. 13, to invest idle funds, in excess of normal operating requirement, with the Bureau of Treasury (BTr) in the form of treasury bills, subject to the cash requirements of the Bangsamoro Government and approval of the Parliament through the Committee on Finance, Budget and Management (CFBM).

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, to furnish copies of this resolution to all persons and entities concerned.
