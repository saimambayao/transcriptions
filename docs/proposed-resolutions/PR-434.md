**RESOLUTION**
**COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING HEBBAN TALIB TAWANTAWAN FOR BEING THE TOPNOTCHER IN THE OCTOBER 2023 CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANT LICENSURE EXAMINATION, AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS**

**WHEREAS**, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) conducted the nationwide CPA Licensure Exam from September 30 to October 2, 2023;

**WHEREAS**, only 2740 out of 8,734 examinees passed the examination;

**WHEREAS**, mr. tawantawan, a Meranao is a graduate of University of San Jose-Recoletos garnered the highest rank with 91.17% average;

**WHEREAS**, his parents, Mr. Abon and Mrs. Saida Tawantawan are entrepreneurs and residents of Poblacion Salvador, Lanao del Norte;

**WHEREAS**, during college, he is a consistent academic performer and received numerous awards from school accountancy competitions.

**WHEREAS**, placing 1st in the board exam brought pride and honor to the people of Lanao and the Bangsamoro that inspire many young moro students;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, to commend Mr. Hebban Talib Tawantawan for placing 1st in the September to October 2023 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination as well as all Bangsamoro CPALE passers.

**ADOPTED.**
