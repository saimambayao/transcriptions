**RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE HONORABLE CHIEF MINISTER, ABDULRAOF A. MACACUA, BTA-BARMM, WITH THE SUPPORT OF HIS CABINET PARTICULARLY THE MINISTRY OF INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT, THE MINISTRY OF PUBLIC WORKS AND THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE AND BUDGET AND MANAGEMENT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF A STABLE AND CONDUCIVE MUNICIPAL HALL IN EACH OF THE EIGHT (8) MUNICIPALITIES OF THE SPECIAL GEOGRAPHIC AREA, BARMM**

**WHEREAS**, the Local Government Code of 1991 mandates the establishment of government centers with appropriate buildings thereof. Section 12 (Government Centers) says:

> “Provinces, cities, and municipalities shall endeavor to establish a government center where offices, agencies, or branches of the national government, local government units, or government-owned or controlled corporations may, as far as practicable, be located. . . .”

**WHEREAS**, the construction of a municipal hall in each of the eight (8) municipalities of the Special Geographic Area (SGA), BARMM is a foremost necessity and demand of the residents in these communities, being a symbol of their juridical existence as a municipality;

**WHEREAS**, the inaccessibility of stable and conducive municipal halls in the eight (8) SGA municipalities has been creating problem on the housing of their respective municipal executive and legislative bodies and other essential services which consequently affect their local government operations and engagements with the constituents;

**WHEREAS**, an engineering plan, computations, and estimates must be undertaken by competent engineers of the government or private firms to determine the material costs, labor, engineering, and other costs of the construction;

**WHEREAS**, an initial budget for the construction of the municipal buildings must be at FIFTY MILLION (P50,000,000.00) PESOS per building in each municipality, without prejudice to increasing it now or in the future and in any manner; and

**WHEREAS**, we are further requesting the Ministers of the Ministry of Interior and Local Government, the Ministry of Public Works, and the Ministry of Finance and Budget and Management to actively support this endeavor.

**NOW THEREFORE**, we resolve as we herein resolved to request the Honorable Chief Minister, Abdulraof A. Macacua, Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, with the support of his concerned Cabinet particularly the Ministry of Interior and Local Government, the Ministry of Public Works, and the Ministry of Finance and Budget and Management to appropriate funds for the construction of stable and conducive municipal hall in each of the eight (8) municipalities of the Special Geographic Area, BARMM.
