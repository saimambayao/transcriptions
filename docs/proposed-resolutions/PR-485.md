**RESOLUTION STRONGLY CONDEMNING THE BOMBING INCIDENT DURING THE CONDUCT OF A RELIGIOUS GATHERING AT DIMAPORO GYMNASIUM, MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY - MAIN CAMPUS, MARAWI CITY, AND REQUESTING APPROPRIATE GOVERNMENT AGENCIES FOR ITS THOROUGH AND SPEEDY INVESTIGATION**

**WHEREAS,** Section 6, Article IV of Republic Act No. 11054 or otherwise known as the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao provides that, The Bangsamoro Government shall promote unity, peace, justice, and goodwill among all peoples, as well as encourage a just and peaceful settlement of disputes.

**WHEREAS,** on December 3, 2023, an explosion occurred during a religious gathering at Dimaporo Gymnasium in Mindanao State University - Main Campus, Marawi City;

**WHEREAS,** the explosion claimed four (4) lives and injuring other attendees to the said religious gathering;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro government promotes inclusivity and religious freedom within the region and maintains that schools and learning institutions are zone of peace where learners and faculty members must be safe at all times;

**WHEREAS,** this act of violence against innocent civilians practicing their faith has no place in our society and an insult to the Bangsamoro government’s effort to create an enabling and conductive environment built on the foundation of peace, reconciliation, tolerance and acceptance of one’s belief;

**WHEREAS,** Islaam prohibits the likes of these things. And Islaam is at war with these types of treacherous acts. And likewise, Islaam threatens a heavy punishment to those people who do these acts, in this life and the hereafter. Verily the true Islaam is that which has come in the Qur'an and in the Sunnah;

**WHEREAS,** Allah, the Most High, said “And do not kill those souls whom Allah has prohibited unless [justified by] a due [legal] right”;

**WHEREAS,** The Messenger of Allah said, in an authentic hadeeth reported by imam al-Bukhari that “Whoever kills a non-Muslim who has an agreement with the Muslims then he will not smell the fragrance of Paradise”;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament mourns with the victims and their families of this atrocious act of violence as well as pray for the speedy recovery of those wounded in the explosion;

**WHEREAS,** it is the prime duty of the State to maintain peace and order and promote the protection of life and general welfare of the people;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament feels the need for a swift but thorough investigation and action of appropriate government agencies to bring the perpetrators to justice; NOW THEREFORE, be it;

**RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to condemn the bombing incident during the conduct of a religious gathering at Dimaporo Gymnasium, Mindanao State University - Main Campus, Marawi City, and request appropriate government agencies for its thorough and speedy investigation.
