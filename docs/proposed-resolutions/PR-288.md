**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING RAYHANAH NUESTRO DECAMPONG FOR HER ACHIEVEMENT IN RANKING 2ND IN THE MAY 2023 CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANT LICENSURE EXAMINATION (CPALE) AND RECOGNIZING ALL BANGSAMORO EXAMINEES WHO PASSED, FOR BRINGING HONOR AND PRIDE TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, the Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination (CPALE) was conducted from May 21-23, 2023, across 17 testing centers throughout the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, on May 31, 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and the Board of Accountancy (BOA) announced the results of the May 2023 CPALE, revealing that 2,239 out of 7,376 examinees or 30.36% successfully passed the examination;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Rayhanah Nuestro Decampong, a young Meranaw from Bayang, Lanao del Sur, in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), and an esteemed alumna of De La Salle University-Manila, has displayed exceptional dedication and perseverance for not only passing the CPALE but also achieving an impressive average score of 89.33%, securing the remarkable position of 2nd rank in this rigorous licensure examination administered by the Professional Regulation Commission;

**WHEREAS**, the achievements of Ms. Rayhanah Nuestro Decampong and all successful Bangsamoro candidates in professional licensure examinations have not only showcased the competitive spirit and excellence of the Bangsamoro people at a national level but have also become a source of inspiration and immense pride for everyone, especially for our younger generation;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, recognizing the remarkable accomplishments of Ms. Rayhanah Nuestro Decampong and all other Bangsamoro passers of the May 2023 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination, deems it fitting to commend their unwavering commitment to hard work and excellence;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to congratulate and commend Ms. Rayhanah Nuestro Decampong for ranking 2nd in the May 2023 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination (CPALE) and to recognize all Bangsamoro examinees who passed, for bringing honor and pride to the Bangsamoro people;

**RESOLVED**, as it hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to extend our warmest congratulations and heartfelt commendations to Ms. Rayhanah Nuestro Decampong for her exceptional achievement in ranking 2nd in the May 2023 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination, and to recognize and appreciate all Bangsamoro examinees who successfully passed the examination, for their remarkable contributions to the prestige and pride of the Bangsamoro people;

**RESOLVE, FURTHER**, to present a copy of this resolution to Ms. Rayhanah Nuestro Decampong and all other Bangsamoro passers as a token of our recognition and appreciation of their achievement, and for their information as well as for this resolution's necessary effects.
