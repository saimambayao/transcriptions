**RESOLUTION**

**URGING THE NATIONAL COMMISSION ON MUSLIM FILIPINOS (NCMF), DEPARTMENT OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS (DFA), AND THE DEPARTMENT OF MIGRANT WORKERS (DMW) TO EXTEND FULL AND IMMEDIATE ASSISTANCE TO THE MUSLIM-FILIPINO (BANGSAMORO) STUDENTS AND OVERSEAS WORKERS AFFECTED BY THE ARMED-CONFLICT IN SUDAN**

WHEREAS, on 15 April 2023, an armed-conflict broke out in Sudan and the confirmed death toll has passed Four Hundred Twenty (420) while more than 3,700 people have been wounded¹;

WHEREAS, based on the record of the Department of Foreign Affairs, there are 740 Filipinos registered in Sudan as of April 25 consisting mainly of professionals, agricultural workers, students, and health workers;

WHEREAS, on 24 April 2023, the first batch of Filipino evacuees consisting of (49) Islamic scholars and one Filipino worker fled the conflict-torn City of Khartoum via land to the Sudan-Egypt border;

WHEREAS, after reaching the Sudan-Egypt border, there were no personnel from the Philippine embassy in Cairo to facilitate their entry to Egypt;

WHEREAS, the Muslim-Filipino students were forced to spend the night on the side of the road without anything to eat or drink;

WHEREAS, they have been stranded for three (3) days as of April 27 without accommodation and comfort room — repeatedly appealing in various platforms for proper assistance from the Philippine embassy;

WHEREAS, to alleviate the plight of the Muslim-Filipino students and the Filipino workers, there is an urgent need for the National Commission on Muslim Filipino, the Department of Foreign Affairs, and the Department of Migrant Workers to extend full and immediate assistance to those Filipinos affected by the conflict by facilitating their entry to Egypt and providing basic needs such as food and accommodation as well as means to return to their respective provinces.

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the Bangsamoro Parliament, TO URGE THE NATIONAL COMMISSION ON MUSLIM FILIPINOS (NCMF), DEPARTMENT OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS (DFA), AND THE DEPARTMENT OF MIGRANT WORKERS (DMW) TO EXTEND FULL AND IMMEDIATE ASSISTANCE TO THE MUSLIM-FILIPINO (BANGSAMORO) STUDENTS AND OVERSEAS WORKERS AFFECTED BY THE ARMED-CONFLICT IN SUDAN.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY,** that copies of this resolution be furnished to the NATIONAL COMMISSION ON MUSLIM FILIPINOS (NCMF), DEPARTMENT OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS (DFA), AND THE DEPARTMENT OF MIGRANT WORKERS (DMW) for information and appropriate action.
