**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MR. ABDURAHIM MOH. SALI ABDULLA FOR HIS EXEMPLARY PERFORMANCE IN RANKING 10TH IN THE NOVEMBER 2023 NURSING LICENSURE EXAMINATION AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS FOR BRINGING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS,** Section 13, Article II of the 1987 Constitution and Section 13, Article IX of the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) both recognize the vital role of the youth in nation-building and shall promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, intellectual, and social well-being, as well as their role to inculcate in the youth patriotism and nationalism, and encourage their involvement in public and civic affairs;

**WHEREAS,** the Philippine Nurses Licensure Examination (NLE) given by the Board of Nursing took place from November 11 to 12, 2023, at nineteen (19) testing centers nationwide;

**WHEREAS,** on December 2, 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission announced that out of a total of thirty-two thousand two hundred three (32,203) examinees, twenty-five thousand seven hundred sixty-one (25,761), constituting eighty (80) percent, successfully passed the NLE;

**WHEREAS,** among the accomplished candidates, **Mr. Abdurahim Moh. Sali Abdulla** distinguished himself by securing the 10th rank with an exceptional average score of 89.20%, standing as the sole Bangsamoro representative within the esteemed top 10 positions;

**WHEREAS, Mr. Abdurahim Moh. Sali Abdulla,** a 23-year-old of Tausug descent, completed his college education at Western Mindanao State University (WMSU) in Zamboanga City in May 2023, earning the distinction of Cum Laude;

**WHEREAS,** acknowledging his remarkable achievement encourages and inspires the Bangsamoro youth to pursue academic and professional excellence, drawing inspiration from his exemplary performance as a role model;

**WHEREAS,** his success together with all the Bangsamoro passers serves as a guiding light for the Bangsamoro children, youth, and young professionals, resonating not only within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region but also across the entire nation;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to congratulate and commend **Mr. Abdurahim Moh. Sali Abdulla** for his exemplary performance in **ranking 10th** in the **November 2023 Nursing Licensure Examination (NLE)** and all Bangsamoro passers for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.
