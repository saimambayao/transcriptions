**RESOLUTION**
**REQUIRING ALL NON-DEVOLVED NATIONAL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND OFFICES OPERATING WITHIN THE JURISDICTION OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION TO PROVIDE COPIES OF THEIR ACCOMPLISHMENT REPORT TO THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT THROUGH THE OFFICE OF THE CHIEF MINISTER**

**WHEREAS**, during the early operation of the Regional Government under the Republic Act (R.A.) No. 6734, as amended by R.A. No. 9054, there were non-devolved national government agencies that established their regional offices in the autonomous region and are operating under the administrative and supervision of their respective national or central offices;

**WHEREAS**, under R.A No. 11054 or the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, some National Government Agencies and Offices are mandated to establish their regional offices within the jurisdiction of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao under the direct, operational, and administrative supervision of their respective national or central offices;

**WHEREAS**, **Section 2, Article XI**, R.A. No. 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) provides that the Philippine National Police shall create a Police Regional Office in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region which shall be organized, maintained, supervised, and utilized for the primary purpose of law enforcement and maintenance of peace and order in the Bangsamoro in accordance with R.A. No. 6975, otherwise known as the Department of Interior and Local Government Act of 1990, as amended by R.A. No. 8551, otherwise known as the Philippine National Police (PNP) Reform and Reorganization Act of 1998. The Police Regional Office in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region shall be under the operational control and supervision of the Philippine National Police;

**WHEREAS**, **Section 3, Article XI**, states that the National Police Commission shall establish the National Police Commission Bangsamoro Regional Office under its direct control, supervision, and administration in accordance with R.A. No. 6975, as amended. The Secretary of the Interior and Local Government shall support the Regional Director who shall head the National Police Commission Bangsamoro Regional Office;

**WHEREAS**, **Section 5, Article XI** mandates that the Bureau of Jail Management and Penology shall establish a regional office in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region under its direct operational and administrative control and supervision, in accordance with R.A. No. 6975, as amended, and R.A. No. 9263, otherwise known as the Bureau of Fire Protection and Bureau of Jail Management and Penology Professionalization Act of 2004. The Bureau of Jail Management and Penology shall support the regional office in the establishment, maintenance, improvement and procurement of jail facilities, alongside the appropriation for the subsistence of detainees and prisoners, and enact the establishment, maintenance, operation and management of separate detention homes, and designation of Jail Wardens.

**WHEREAS**, **Section 6, Article XI** requires that the Bureau of Fire Protection shall establish a regional office in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region under its direct operational and administrative control and supervision, in accordance with R.A. No. 6975, as amended, and R.A. No. 9263. The Bureau of Fire Protection shall support the regional office in the establishment, maintenance, and improvement and procurement of fire protection and prevention facilities, the enforcement of R.A. No. 9514, otherwise known as the Fire Code of the Philippines of 2008, and other existing laws on fire safety enforcement, protection, and prevention.

**WHEREAS**, **Section 7** of the aforementioned Article mandates that the Philippine Coast Guard shall establish a regional office in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region which shall be organized, maintained, supervised, and utilized for the primary purpose of law enforcement over coast guard matters. It shall be under the direct operational and administrative control and supervision of the Philippine Coast Guard;

**WHEREAS**, **Section 3, Article XII** states that there is hereby a Bangsamoro Regional Office of the Bureau of Local Government Finance created under the Department of Finance which shall have the authority to coordinate, assist, and monitor the treasury and assessment operations of constituent local government units within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in pursuance of good governance and local autonomy. The Regional Office shall be guided by the standards set by the Department of Finance-Bureau of Local Government Finance including the requirements set for the appointment of local treasurers;

**WHEREAS**, one of the major functions of the Bangsamoro Economic Development Council mandated under R.A. No. 11054 is to monitor and evaluate all development programs and projects implemented by the national government, Bangsamoro ministries/line agencies/offices, local government units, and government owned and/or controlled corporations in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**NOW THEREFOR, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the BTA Parliament to pass and approve this Resolution requiring all non-devolved national government agencies and offices operating within the jurisdiction of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region to provide copies of their accomplishment report to the Bangsamoro government through the Office of the Chief Minister;

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that copies of this Resolution be furnished to all concerned national government agencies and offices operating within the jurisdiction of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and the Bangsamoro Government for their proper guidance, information and appropriate action.
