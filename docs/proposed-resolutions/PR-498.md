**"A RESOLUTION REQUIRING ALL CONSTITUENTS OF THE BARMM TO OBSERVE AND COMMEMORATE THE YEARLY CELEBRATION OF THE BANGSAMORO FOUNDATION DAY"**

WHEREAS, on July 23, 2018, President Rodrigo Roa Duterte signed into law the Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the “Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao”;

WHEREAS, as stated in the Bangsamoro Administrative Code, January 21 is proclaimed as the Bangsamoro Foundation Day and declared a non-working holiday in celebration of the establishment of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, which shall be celebrated in recognition of the dreams and aspiration of the people to chart their social, political, cultural and economic destiny in the exercise of their right to self-determination;

WHEREAS, Bangsamoro Foundation Day shall be embodied, adhered, and inculcated in every heart and mind of men and women in Bangsamoro Region to preserve the history of the struggles and the sacrifices of our leaders and the Mujahideen.

WHEREAS, the founding anniversary of the Bangsamoro Government holds significant historical, political, and cultural importance, symbolizing the establishment of new era of peace, unity, governance, and autonomy;

WHEREAS, it is essential to foster a sense of unity, pride and collective memory among all people of the Bangsamoro region, as well as promote awareness and understanding of the Bangsamoro Government’s achievement and aspirations.

WHEREAS, all residents, whether public and private entities, play a crucial role in contributing to the commemoration of the founding anniversary and ensuring its meaningful observance;

WHEREAS, all residents, whether public and private entities, play a crucial role in contributing to the commemoration of the founding anniversary and ensuring its meaningful observance;

WHEREAS, all residents, especially the private sectors, within the jurisdiction of the Bangsamoro Government, are hereby encourage to actively participate in commemorating and celebrating the yearly founding anniversary of the Bangsamoro Government;

WHEREAS, private sectors and entities shall organize or support events, activities, and initiatives that promote the shared values, achievements, and aspirations of the Bangsamoro Government during the commemorative period and are encouraged to display commemorative symbols, messages, or decorations in their premises or public places showcasing their support and solidarity with the Bangsamoro Government;

WHEREAS, all ministries, agencies, and offices as well as all local government units under the jurisdiction of Bangsamoro government shall conduct special activities and services with the participation of private sectors in supporting the celebration and commemoration of the Bangsamoro Foundation Day during its commemorative period; ;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) and the Bangsamoro Government requiring all constituents of the BARMM to observe and commemorate the yearly celebration of the Bangsamoro Foundation Day.

**ADOPTED.**
