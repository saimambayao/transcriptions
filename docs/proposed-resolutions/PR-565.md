**RESOLUTION APPROVING THE SELECTION OF BARANGAY SARMIENTO, MUNICIPALITY OF PARANG, PROVINCE OF MAGUINDANAO DEL NORTE AS THE LOCATION FOR THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT CENTER BY THE COMMITTEE ON THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE SEAT OF GOVERNMENT OF THE BARMM (CESG) IN ITS RESOLUTION NO. 001, SERIES OF 2024**

**WHEREAS,** Republic Act No. 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) provides that the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, in the exercise of its right to self-governance, is free to pursue its political, economic, social, and cultural development. Further, the Bangsamoro Government shall have the power to create offices and organize its own bureaucracy;

**WHEREAS,** Section 1, Article VII of the same law mandates the Parliament to fix by law the permanent seat of the Bangsamoro Government anywhere within the territorial jurisdiction of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region taking into consideration accessibility and efficiency in which its mandate may be carried out under the BOL;

**WHEREAS,** consistent with the BOL, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament enacted Bangsamoro Autonomy Act (BAA) No. 37 otherwise known as the “Seat of Government Act of 2023” on 19 June 2023, which provides for the establishment of the seat of government of the BARMM in the Municipality of Parang, Province of Maguindanao Del Norte, amending Book 1, Chapter 2, Section 9 of the BAA No. 13 or the “Bangsamoro Administrative Code”;

**WHEREAS,** pursuant to the provisions of BAA No. 37, the Committee on the Establishment of the Seat of Government of the BARMM (CESG), which is composed of the Senior Minister as chair and the Minister of Finance, and Budget and Management, the Minister of Public Works, the Minister of Environment, Natural Resources and Energy, the Minister of Transportation and Communications, the Chairperson of the Bangsamoro Commission for the Preservation of Cultural Heritage and the Director-General of the Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority as members, was created to oversee the establishment of the seat of government of the BARMM in the Municipality of Parang, Maguindanao del Norte, including the identification of the location of the Bangsamoro government center;

**WHEREAS,** on 07 March 2024, the CESG adopted Resolution No. 001, series of 2024, identifying Barangay Sarmiento in the Municipality of Parang, Maguindanao del Norte as the location for the Bangsamoro government center;

**WHEREAS,** the same CESG resolution was endorsed by the Office of the Senior Minister to the Office of the Parliament Speaker on 22 March 2024;

**WHEREAS,** Section 5 of BAA No. 37 provides that the CESG’s identification of the location for the Bangsamoro government center shall be subject to the approval of the Bangsamoro Parliament;

**WHEREAS,** the selection of Barangay Sarmiento as the location for the Bangsamoro government center has been unanimous and was thought to be most practical and advantageous for the BARMM;

**WHEREAS,** the identification of the specific site for the Bangsamoro government center is crucial to the establishment of the permanent seat of government of the BARMM;

**WHEREAS,** on April 26, 2024, the Bangsamoro Government, thru the Ministry of Interior and Local Government (MILG), conducted a Community Consultation Dialogue for the establishment of the seat of government of BARMM in Barangay Sarmiento, Parang, Maguindanao del Norte to facilitate discussions with different stakeholders;

**WHEREAS,** the immediate action of the Bangsamoro Parliament on CESG Resolution No. 001, s. 2024, identifying Barangay Sarmiento in Parang, Maguindanao del Norte as the site for the Bangsamoro government center pursuant to Section 5 of BAA No. 37 is imperative;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED** as it hereby resolved by the BTA Parliament, to approve the selection of Barangay Sarmiento, Municipality of Parang, Province of Maguindanao del Norte as the location for the Bangsamoro government center by the CESG in its Resolution No. 001, Series of 2024.
