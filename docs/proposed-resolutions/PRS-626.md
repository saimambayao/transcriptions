**RESOLUTION**
**ADHERING TO EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 64 SERIES OF 2024, UPDATING THE SALARY SCHEDULE FOR CIVILIAN GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL AND AUTHORIZING THE GRANT OF AN ADDITIONAL ALLOWANCE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

**WHEREAS,** one of the priorities and functions of the Bangsamoro Parliament is to act on other matters that may be necessary for the protection and promotion of the general welfare of the constituents of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. The General Welfare clause under the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the powers expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective governance and those which are essential to the promotion of general welfare. Within its territorial jurisdiction, the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure, support and enhance economic prosperity and social justice, and preserve the comfort and convenience of its inhabitants;

**WHEREAS,** in the allocation of the block grant, the national laws and the budgeting rules and regulations implemented by the Department of Budget and Management and Department of the Interior and Local Government applicable to local government units shall apply;

**WHEREAS,** the Parliament shall, likewise, adopt the Revised Compensation and Position Classification System of the National Government as provided for in Republic Act (RA) No. 6758, as amended, and relevant executive issuances;

**WHEREAS,** President Ferdinand R. Marcos Jr. issued on August 2, 2024 Executive Order (EO) No. 64, increasing the salaries of government workers and authorizing the grant of an additional medical allowance to state employees;

**WHEREAS,** EO No. 64, s. 2024 applies to all civilian government personnel, now existing or hereafter created in the Executive, Legislative, and Judicial Branches, the Constitutional Commissions and other Constitutional Offices, SUCs, and Government-Owned and Controlled Corporations (GOCCs) not covered by RA No. 10149 and EO No. 150, s. 2021, regardless of appointment status, whether regular, casual, or contractual; appointive or elective; and on full-time or part-time basis; and Local Government Units (LGUs);

**WHEREAS,** EO No. 64, s. 2024 shall not apply to Military and Uniformed Personnel; Government agencies that are exempt from RA 6758, as amended; GOCCs under RA 10149 and EO 150; individuals engaged without employer-employee relationship and funded from non-Personnel Services (PS) appropriations/budget such as contract of service/job order workers and consultants;

**WHEREAS,** the Department of Budget and Management consequently issued National Budget Circular No. 594 which provides for the guidelines, rules and regulations for the implementation of the first tranche of the updated salary schedule for civilian government personnel under EO No. 64, s. 2024;

**WHEREAS,** one of the general limitations on the use of funds requires that the total appropriations, whether annual or supplemental, for Personal Services of the Bangsamoro Government for one (1) fiscal year shall not exceed forty-five percent (45%) of the total revenue sources of the Bangsamoro Government as provided under Section 6 of this Article XII of the Bangsamoro Organic Law;

**WHEREAS,** Special Provisions under the Miscellaneous Personnel Benefits Fund of the 2024 General Appropriations Act of the Bangsamoro provides that the amounts therein appropriated shall be used for deficiencies in authorized salaries, allowances, associated premiums and other similar personnel benefits of the Bangsamoro Government personnel. It shall cover for, among others, the payment of other Personnel Services as may be mandated by law or as provided in such appropriations act, subject to usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations;

**WHEREAS,** the Ministry of Finance, and Budget and Management shall promulgate guidelines in the implementation of the First Tranche of the Updated Salary Schedule for Civilian Government Personnel under Executive Order (EO) No. 64, s. 2024.

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, as it is hereby resolved, to ADHERE TO EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 64 series of 2024 or AN ORDER UPDATING THE SALARY SCHEDULE FOR CIVILIAN GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL AND AUTHORIZING THE GRANT OF AN ADDITIONAL ALLOWANCE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** to furnish copies of this resolution to all persons and entities concerned.
