**13** **A RESOLUTION CONDEMNING THE TRAGIC KILLING OF BETTY**
**14** **CADULONG LU AND JONARD LU IN POBLACION, PIKIT, NORTH**
**15** **COTABATO EXTENDING CONDOLENCES AND SYMPATHY TO THE**
**16** **BEREAVED FAMILY AND CALLING ALL CONCERNED AUTHORITIES TO**
**17** **EXPEDITE THE INVESTIGATION SURROUNDING THE INCIDENT**
**18**
**19**
**20**       **WHEREAS,** Section 5, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution
**21** provides that, "*The maintenance of peace and order, the protection of life,*
**22** *liberty, and property, and the promotion of the general welfare are essential for*
**23** *the enjoyment by all the people of the blessing of democracy*";
**24**
**25**       **WHEREAS,** Section 11, Article II of the same declares, "*The State values*
**26** *the dignity of every human person and guarantees the full respect for human*
**27** *rights*";
**28**
**29**       **WHEREAS,** Section I, Article III of the Bill of Rights states that "*No*
**30** *person shall be deprived of life, liberty, or property without due process of law,*
**31** *nor shall any person be denied of the equal protection of law*",
**32**
**33**       **WHEREAS,** Section 6 of Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054, also
**34** known as "Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
**35** Mindanao" states that "*The Bangsamoro Government shall fully respect human*
**36** *rights*";
**37**
**38**       **WHEREAS,** on January 09, 2024, the community of Pikit, North
**39** Cotabato was shaken by the tragic and senseless killing of Betty Cadulong
**40** Lu, a respected eatery owner, and her 47 years old son Jonard Lu;
**41**
**42**       **WHEREAS,** Betty Cadulong Lu, is a businesswoman and one of the
**43** prominent residents of Pikit, North Cotabato together with her son Jonard Lu.
**44**
**45**       **WHEREAS,** according to the information gathered by Brigada News FM
**46** Cotabato City, the proprietor of the said eatery, Betty Cadulong, a senior
**47** citizen, was the first one shot while attending at their eatery. Allegedly, one of
**48** the suspects took the money she earned from morning until noon;
**49**
**50**       **WHEREAS,** it is said that when Junard, Betty's son, saw his mother
**51** dead, he chased after the suspect and that was when he was shot;
**52**
**53**       **WHEREAS,** the mother and son victims were immediately taken to the
**54** hospital but they also passed away due to the fatal woundsa they sustained.
**55**
**56**       **WHEREAS,** the suspects in the murder of a business woman and her
**57** son in Pikit, Cotabato, were caught on CCTV.
**58**
**59**       **WHEREAS,** it is reported that four (4) individuals riding two (2)
**60** motorcycles were behind the shooting and killing of Betty and Junard
**61** Cadulong yesterday at noon.
**62**
**63**       **WHEREAS,** despite being captured on CCTV footage, the identities of
**64** these individuals remain unclear.
**65**
**66**       **WHEREAS,** the suspects involved in this heinous act immediately fled
**67** into the direction which the authorities are still trying to determine.
**68**
**69**       **WHEREAS,** the loss of Betty Cadulong Lu and Jonard Lu has deeply
**70** saddened and shocked the community, leaving behind a void that can never
**71** be filled;
**72**
**73**       **WHEREAS,** "Mother Betty", that was what her regular customers called
**74** her, was the sister of Dr. Robert Cadulong, Al Haj, the owner of Cadulong
**75** Hospital in town and a retired municipal physician, was also shot and killed
**76** last July 23, 2021.
**77**
**78**       **WHEREAS,** the circumstances surrounding their deaths are not only
**79** heartbreaking but also raise serious concerns posing a threat to the peace
**80** and security in the whole Bangsamoro Region.
**81**
**82**       **WHEREAS,** we in the Bangsamoro Parliament stand together to
**83** condemn such abhorrent actions in the strongest terms the brutal killing of
**83** Betty Cadulong Lu and Jonard Lu for such acts of violence and crime have
**84** no place in our society;
**85**
**86**       **WHEREAS,** we call upon all concerned authorities to expedite the
**87** investigation into this tragic incident, apprehend the perpetrator, and ensure
**88** that justice is served for Betty Cadulong Lu, Jonard Lu, and their grieving
**89** family.
**90**
**91**       **WHEREAS,** we extend our heartfelt condolences and solidarity to the
**92** family, friends, and loved ones of Betty Cadulong Lu and Jonard Lu during
**93** this incredibly difficult time;
**94**
**95**       **WHEREAS,** that this resolution be disseminated widely within
**96** Bangsamoro Region particularly in the Local Government Unit, in Pikit, North
**97** Cotabato to raise awareness and to foster unity against violence and crime;
**98**
**99**       **WHEREAS,** that a copy of this resolution be forwarded to the local
**100** authorities and representatives as a testament to our collective voice against
**101** such atrocities.
**102**
**103** **NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the
**104** Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
**105** Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION CONDEMNING THE TRAGIC KILLING OF**
**106** **BETTY CADULONG LU AND JONARD LU IN POBLACION, PIKIT, NORTH**
**107** **COTABATO EXTENDING CONDOLENCES AND SYMPATHY TO THE**
**108** **BEREAVED FAMILY AND CALLING ALL CONCERNED AUTHORITIES TO**
**109** **EXPEDITE THE INVESTIGATION SURROUNDING THE INCIDENT.**
