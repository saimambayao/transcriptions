**A RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING HEBBAN TALIB TAWANTAWAN FOR BEING THE TOPNOTCHER IN THE SEPTEMBER 2023 LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANTS**

**WHEREAS**, Licensure Examination for Certified Public Accountants (LECPA) is one of the most gruelling and toughest board examinations in the Philippines administered by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC);

**WHEREAS**, On October 6, 2023, the PRC announced that a total of 2,740 out of 8,734 successfully passed the September 2023 LECPA constituting 31.25% overall passing rate;

**WHEREAS**, Hebban Talib Tawantawan gained an astounding score of 91. 17% securing the top spot alongside Allaine Beduya Collamar of the University of the Philippines (UP) Tacloban;

**WHEREAS**, Hebban Talib Tawantawan is a proud Bangsamoro who graduated Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Science in Accountancy from the University of San Jose- Recoletos;

**WHEREAS**, Hebban Talib Tawantawan has consistently demonstrated diligence through his academic achievements and unwavering commitment to attaining excellence;

**WHEREAS**, one key of upholding progress and advancement within BARMM is to cultivate the culture of excellence among the Bangsamoro youth who are the future holders of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, recognizing this achievement of a Bangsamoro youth will serve as an inspiration and motivation to the entire Bangsamoro community and the student population to always and continuously aim and pursue excellence.

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to **CONGRATULATE AND COMMEND HEBBAN TALIB TAWANTAWAN FOR BEING THE TOPNOTCHER IN THE SEPTEMBER 2023 LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANTS**;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to **Mr. HEBBAN TALIB TAWANTAWAN**.
