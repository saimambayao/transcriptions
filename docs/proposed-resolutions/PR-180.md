**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST SYMPATHY AND CONDOLENCES TO THE BEREAVED FAMILY OF SHEIKH KHALIPHA USMAN NANDO, WA’LI OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro society is deeply saddened about the death of **Sheikh Khalipha Usman Nando**, Wa’li of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS**, the death of Sheikh Khalipha Usman Nando is a great loss not only to his immediate family but also to the leadership of Bangsamoro Government who considered him as their second father;

**WHEREAS**, as Wa’li of the Bangsamoro he pushes for the institutionalization for an alternative system of Islamic Finance that are more just and equitable to the society, such as the institutionalization of Zakat for the benefit of the whole Bangsamoro Society;

**WHEREAS**, being the symbolic head of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, the Bangsamoro Government is mandated to honor the Wa’li of the Bangsamoro’s demise and may hold ceremonial funeral;

**WHEREAS**, his legacy as Co-Founder of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front, the key organization that led to the creation of the Bangsamoro Government as well as his enduring guidance to the leadership of Bangsamoro Government deserves to be honored and remembered;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by Bangsamoro Parliament to express sympathy and condolences on the death of Sheikh Khalipha Usman Nando.

**HON. HARON M. ABAS**
Member of the Parliament – Bangsamoro Transition Authority
