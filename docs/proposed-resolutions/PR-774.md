**RESOLUTION RECOMMENDING THE IMMEDIATE DESIGNATION OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT CHAIRMAN OF THE NATIONAL INTERGOVERNMENTAL RELATIONS BODY (IGRB).**

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Article VI of Republic Act No. 11054 (Bangsamoro Organic Law) created a National Government — Bangsamoro Government Intergovernmental Relations Body to coordinate and resolve issues on intergovernmental relations through regular consultation and continuing negotiations in a non-adversarial manner;

**WHEREAS**, the Intergovernmental Relations Body, have for a time implemented mechanisms to resolve intergovernmental issues, e.g., Technical Working Group to effect the transitioning of Sulu from BARMM to Region IX during Secretary Amenah B. Pangandaman’s chairmanship of the National Government — IGRB;

**WHEREAS**, the resignation of Secretary Amenah B. Pangandaman as Department of Budget and Management (DBM) Secretary also left the Chairmanship of the National Government — Intergovernmental Relations Body;

**WHEREAS**, the vacancy in the Chairmanship of the National Government — Intergovernmental Relations Body have unnecessarily caused the non-convening of the Intergovernmental Relations Body and delayed the resolution of many intergovernmental issues including the Sulu transition;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Parliament in Session to request his Excellency President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr., to designate a new Chairman of the National Government — Intergovernmental Relations Body;

**RESOLVED FURTHER, to ENDORSE Special Assistant to the President Secretary Antonio Ernesto F. Lagdameo Jr.**, a Mindanaoan and one who had been an active participant in the Intergovernmental Relations Body since the start of the Marcos Administration as Co-Chairman of the Intergovernmental Relations Body for the National Government.

**RESOLVED FINALLY**, to submit the resolution to the Office of the President thru the Office of the Executive Secretary, Secretary Ralph Recto.

**ADOPTED.**
