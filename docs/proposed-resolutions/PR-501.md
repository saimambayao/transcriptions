**RESOLUTION COMMENDING PRESIDENT FERDINAND R. MARCOS JR. FOR ESTABLISHING THE SITUMIAYAT WAKHAMSUN ALDHIKRA NATIONAL COMMITTEE (SWANC) IN COMMEMORATION OF THE 650TH ANNIVERSARY OF ISLAMIC PRESENCE IN THE PHILIPPINES IN 2030**

**WHEREAS**, the Philippine Government acknowledges the historical significance of the 650th anniversary of Islamic presence in the Philippines in 2030, a milestone that marks the enduring contributions of Muslim Filipinos to the nation;

**WHEREAS**, Scholars of Philippine Muslim History agree that the beginning of the Islamic faith in the Philippines took place between 1350 to 1380, making Islam the oldest monotheistic religion in the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, the year 1380 signifies the arrival of the Islam religion in the country, which was officially recognized by the National Historical Institute, the predecessor of the National Historical Commission of the Philippines, through the installation of a historical marker in 1980;

**WHEREAS**, President Ferdinand R. Marcos Jr. has demonstrated a keen understanding of the importance of commemorating this historic event by issuing Administrative Order No. 10, establishing the Situmiayat Wakhamsun Aldhikra National Committee (SWANC);

**WHEREAS**, the creation of SWANC is a thoughtful and commendable gesture that underscores the commitment of President Ferdinand R. Marcos Jr. to recognize and celebrate the diverse cultural heritage of the Philippines, particularly the invaluable contributions of Muslim Filipinos;

**WHEREAS**, SWANC, under the guidance of the President, is tasked with planning and implementing programs, projects, and activities to commemorate the 650th anniversary of Islamic presence in the Philippines, thereby fostering national unity and promoting international comity;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, as it is hereby resolves, to express its deep appreciation to President Ferdinand R. Marcos Jr. for establishing the Situmiayat Wakhamsun Aldhikra National Committee (SWANC) in commemoration of the 650th Anniversary of Islamic Presence in the Philippines in 2030;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that a copy of this resolution be duly furnished to the Office of the President for the information and appreciation of President Ferdinand R. Marcos Jr.
