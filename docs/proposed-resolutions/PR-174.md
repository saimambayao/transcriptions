**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE PROFOUND GRIEF OF THE BTA PARLIAMENT ON THE PASSING OF HIS EMINENCE SHEIKH KHALIPHA USMAN NANDO, THE WALI OF THE BANGSAMORO**

**WHEREAS**, His Eminence Sheikh Khalipha Usman Nando, the Wali of the Bangsamoro, has returned to the Mercy of the Almighty Allah (s.w.t.) on 05 February 2023 (14 Rajab 1444);

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro people mourn the passing of Sheikh Nando, a *Mujahideen* who has dedicated his life to advancing the teachings of Islam and the Bangsamoro cause;

**WHEREAS**, as one of the great founders of the Moro revolution, Sheikh Nando played a pivotal role in the Bangsamoro people’s fight for self-determination;

**WHEREAS**, as a member of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front’s (MILF’s) Central Committee, Sheikh Nando helped steer the organization towards the most virtuous and sensible path and to triumph over many trials and adversities;

**WHEREAS**, as the first Wali of the Bangsamoro, Sheikh Nando served as a figure of wisdom, prudence and hope;

**WHEREAS**, as an ordinary man, Sheikh Nando epitomized a true servant of the Almighty Allah (s.w.t.), selfless and wholly devoted to serving The Almighty and the *Ummah*;

**WHEREAS**, the passing of Sheikh Nando is a great loss to the Bangsamoro people and our continuing pursuit for self-determination;

**WHEREAS**, despite the immense sorrow, the faithful find comfort in knowing that Sheikh Nando has returned to his Creator, ***“To Allah (Almighty God) we belong, and to Him is our return.” (Qur'an 2:156)***;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the BTA Parliament, to express its profound grief on the passing of His Eminence Sheikh Khalipha Usman Nando, the Wali of the Bangsamoro.
