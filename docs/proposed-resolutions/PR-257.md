**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING NISHRIEN AMATONDING BARUA, AND AL-ZAHEER ADIL ABUBAKAR FOR THEIR ACHIEVEMENTS IN RANKING 5TH AND 10TH, RESPECTIVELY, IN THE ELEMENTARY LEVEL OF THE MARCH 2023 LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR PROFESSIONAL TEACHERS, AND RECOGNIZING ALL BANGSAMORO EXAMINEES WHO PASSED, FOR BRINGING HONOR AND PRIDE TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS,** on 19 March 2023, the Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers for both Elementary Level and Secondary Level was conducted at 37 testing centers all over the Philippines;

**WHEREAS,** on 19 May 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and the Board for Professional Teachers (BPT) announced the results of the March 2023 Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers where 24,819 elementary examinees out of 60,896 (40.76%) examinees and 48,005 secondary examinees out of 102,272 (46.94%) examinees successfully passed it;

**WHEREAS,** Ms. Nishrien Amatonding Barua successfully passed and achieved an impressive average score of 91.40%, ranking TOP 5, while Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar achieved an impressive average score of 90.40%, ranking TOP 10 in the Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers (Elementary Level) conducted by the Professional Regulation Commission;

**WHEREAS,** Ms. Nishrien Amatonding Barua, 23 years of age, is an honor student being a Magna Cum Laude graduate at the Mindanao State University-Main Campus, Marawi City, and a proud Muslim Meranaw hailing from Malabang, Lanao del Sur of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM);

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar, 23 years of age, is a graduate of Notre Dame University with an academic distinction of Cum Laude, and a proud Muslim Maguindanaon hailing from Cotabato City of BARMM;

**WHEREAS,** the achievements of these outstanding Bangsamoro youth and all successful candidates in the professional licensure examinations have demonstrated the competitiveness and excellence of the Bangsamoro people at a national level, giving us greater hope and a source of inspiration and pride for our younger generation;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Transition Authority is cognizant of Ms. Nishrien Amatonding Barua, Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar, and all other Bangsamoro passers of the Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers for bringing honor to the Bangsamoro, and their achievements merit the Parliament's recognition and commendation;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to congratulate and commend Nishrien Amatonding Barua and Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar for ranking 5th and 10th, respectively, in the Elementary Level of the March 2023 Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers, and to acknowledge all Bangsamoro passers for their contributions in bringing honor and pride to the Bangsamoro people;

**RESOLVE, FURTHER,** to present a copy of this resolution to Nishrien Amatonding Barua and Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar as a token of our recognition and appreciation of their achievements, and for their information and its necessary effects.
