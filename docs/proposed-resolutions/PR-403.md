**RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING FADZRATI D. ASARUD, A NATIVE OF TAWI-TAWI, FOR RANKING 5TH, WITH A RATING OF 88.20%, IN THE RECENTLY CONCLUDED AUGUST 2023 PSYCHOMETRICIANS LICENSURE EXAMINATION ADMINISTERED BY THE PROFESSIONAL REGULATION COMMISSION (PRC)**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority - Bangsamoro Parliament takes pride in recognizing exemplary accomplishments of Bangsamoro professionals which contribute to the region’s prestige and honor.

**WHEREAS**, this Parliament acknowledges yet another outstanding achievement of Fadzrati D. Asarud, a native of Tawi-Tawi, by placing 5th overall in the recently concluded Psychometricians Licensure Examination administered by the PRC’s Professional Regulatory Board of Psychology held last August of 2023.

**WHEREAS**, Fadzrati Asarud obtained a rating of 88.20%, a significant milestone by a Bangsamoro, among the 6,133 passers of the 2023 Psychometricians Licensure Examination.

**WHEREAS**, Fadzrati Asarud, earned her baccalaureate degree in Bachelor of Science in Psychology with Latin honor (Cum Laude) at the Ateneo de Zamboanga University in 2022. Asarud also completed her Senior Secondary Education with Honors in 2018 in the same school.

**WHEREAS**, Fadzrati Asarud graduated as Class Valedictorian from Mindanao State University - Tawi-Tawi College of Technology and Oceanography Laboratory Elementary School (MSU-TCTO-LES) in 2012. Asarud also finished her junior secondary education as Class Salutatorian from MSU-TCTO Science High School in 2016.

**WHEREAS**, Fadzrati Asarud’s recent achievement is a manifestation of her consistent dedication, discipline, and commitment to excellence in her field of choice.

**WHEREAS**, Fadzrati Asarud’s outstanding accomplishment serves as an inspiration to the Bangsamoro youth to aim for excellence in their chosen field of endeavors.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority – Bangsamoro Parliament to Commend and Congratulate Fadzrati Asarud for ranking 5th in the 2023 in the Psychometricians Licensure Examination held last August of 2023 thereby bringing honor and pride to the Region.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That a copy of this Resolution be forwarded to the Asarud, for information.

**ADOPTED**,
