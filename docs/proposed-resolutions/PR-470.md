**RESOLUTION CONDEMNING THE TRAGIC BOMBING INCIDENT AT DIMAPORO GYMNASIUM, MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY, MARAWI CITY**

WHEREAS, on or about 7:00 AM of December 3, 2023, a horrific explosion incident transpired at Dimaporo Gymnasium, Mindanao State University, Brgy. Dimalna, Marawi City, resulting in the tragic deaths of four (4) individuals and leaving more or less forty (40) others wounded;

WHEREAS, this heinous act of violence constitutes a severe assault on the lives of innocent civilians and represents an egregious violation of the principles of peace, harmony, and unity that the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao Parliament is dedicated to upholding;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro people, as represented by this Parliament, express our deepest condolences and profound sorrow for the families and loved ones of the victims who have been irreversibly affected by this senseless tragedy;

WHEREAS, it is imperative for the Parliament to vehemently condemn this atrocious act and to call for swift and thorough investigations by the relevant authorities to identify the perpetrators and bring them to justice;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao Parliament reaffirms its unwavering commitment to promoting peace, tolerance, and understanding among all communities within the region;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED** by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao Parliament to:

1. Condemn in the strongest terms the heinous act of violence that transpired at Dimaporo Gymnasium, Mindanao State University, on December 3, 2023.
2. Extend our heartfelt condolences to the families and loved ones of the twelve (12) individuals who lost their lives and express our sympathies to the more or less forty (40) others who sustained injuries during this tragic incident.
3. Call upon the relevant authorities to conduct a thorough and expeditious investigation to identify the perpetrators of this reprehensible act and ensure that justice is served.
4. Reaffirm our commitment to the promotion of peace, tolerance, and understanding among all communities in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.
5. Urge the community members and residents of Marawi City to remain vigilant, cooperative, and resilient in the face of adversity and to work together towards the preservation of peace and security in the region.

**RESOLVED FURTHER THAT**, this resolution shall be transmitted to the appropriate agencies, the families of the victims, and other concerned parties as a testament to our collective condemnation of such acts of violence and our commitment to fostering a peaceful and harmonious Bangsamoro.
