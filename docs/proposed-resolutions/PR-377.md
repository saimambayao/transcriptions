**RESOLUTION**

**CONDEMNING IN THE STRONGEST POSSIBLE TERMS THE SENSELESS AND BRUTAL KILLING OF MEDICAL PRACTITIONER DRA. MARIVIC TELLO AND URGING THE COTABATO REGIONAL MEDICAL CENTER TO UNDERTAKE NECESSARY STEPS TO ENSURE THE SAFETY AND SECURITY OF ITS PERSONNEL AND CLIENTS**

**WHEREAS**, Section 4, Article II of the 1987 Constitution of the Philippine states in part that “*The prime duty of the Government is to serve and protect the People*”;

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article III of the Bill of Rights states that “*No person shall be deprived of life, liberty, or property without due process of law, nor shall any person be denied the equal protection of laws*”;

**WHEREAS**, Dra. Marivic Tello, an obstetrician-gynecologist practitioner and a wife of former Provincial Police Director of Maguindanao, Retired Police General Agustin Tello, was reported missing on July 23, 2023 and she was last seen at the Cotabato Regional Medical Center (CRMC);

**WHEREAS**, on July 27, 2023 the body of Dra. Tello was found buried in the Cotabato Medical Society Building, inside the CRMC compound and it is believed that she was killed within the hospital premises;

**WHEREAS**, on the same date, her secretary surrendered to the police authorities, and thereupon voluntarily confessed and admitted to murdering the Doctor which happened inside the CRMC compound;

**WHEREAS**, medical doctors’ role in society extend far beyond individual patient health conditions. They play a significant role in improving our health system as a whole. Worth noting that during the height of Covid-19 Pandemic, they sacrificed their time and lives while saving patients;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority; To express its condemnation in the strongest possible terms of the senseless and brutal killing of medical practitioner Dra. Marivic Tello and to urge the Cotabato Regional Medical Center to undertake necessary steps to ensure the safety and security of its personnel and clients.

**ADOPTED.**
