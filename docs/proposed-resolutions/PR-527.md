**RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING ALL PARLIAMENTARY AND STATUTORY COMMITTEES OF THE BTA PARLIAMENT TO CONDUCT MEETINGS, HEARINGS, AND CONSULTATIONS DURING THE RECESS OF THE SECOND BTA PARLIAMENT**

**WHEREAS**, the Second Regular Session of the Second BTA Parliament will adjourn *sine die* on 29 February 2024 | 19 *Shaâ€™ban* 1445 AH per Resolution No. 336 or Resolution Adopting the Calendar of Session for the Second Regular Session of the Second Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, there are pending deliberations on priority codes such as Parliament Bill No. 273 or the proposed Bangsamoro Indigenous Peoples Development Act of 2024 and other important bills and resolutions, and ongoing investigations in aid of legislation before the different committees of the BTA Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, the expeditious consideration of these pending measures which necessitates continued committee meetings, hearings, and consultations during Ramadhan is in the best interest of the Bangsamoro;

**WHEREAS**, the scheduling of the committee meetings, hearings, and consultations shall give due consideration to the observance of fasting during the month of Ramadhan;

**WHEREAS**, Zoom participation of the committee members during said committee meetings, hearings, and consultations will be allowed subject to the discretion of the concerned committees;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the BTA Parliament, to authorize all Parliamentary and Statutory Committees of the Parliament to conduct meetings, hearings and consultations during the recess of the Parliament.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That the conduct of subject committee meetings, hearings and consultations shall not be done on Fridays.

**RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE**, That this Resolution shall take effect on the date of its adoption and shall remain in force until amended or repealed.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, To furnish copies of this Resolution to the Parliamentary and Statutory Committees of the Parliament for their information and ready reference.
