**Proposed Resolution No. 137**

Introduced by: MPs KADIL M. SINOLINDING JR., MD, DPBO, SUWAIB C. ABU, MUDJIB C. ABU, MOHAMMAD KELIE U. ANTAO, BAILENG UTTO S. MANTAWIL, JAAFAR JAMBO L. MATALAM, MICHAEL E. MIDTIMBANG, TAWAKAL L. MIDTIMBANG, ATTY. SITTIE FAHANIE U. UYOD, and BASSER D. UTTO, MPA

**“A RESOLUTION ENCOURAGING EVERY BANGSAMORO TO PLACE THEIR RIGHT HAND OVER THE LEFT CHEST UPON SINGING THE NATIONAL ANTHEM AND THE BANGSAMORO HYMN TO SHOW RESPECT AND SOLIDARITY AND TO STAND AS UNITED BANGSAMORO REGION”**

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Government was established in 2019 under Republic Act 11054 as the legal basis for the existence of the Bangsamoro region;

WHEREAS, with the aforementioned legal basis, the establishment of Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao is the fruit of 40 years of negotiations that opened up new government entity under the Philippine government;

WHEREAS, under the provisions of Republic Act 11054, among all other granted rights, is to name its own Bangsamoro Hymn to be sang in every flag ceremony, official events and special activities within its territory as a symbol of identity, respect, unity and solidarity;

WHEREAS, placing of right hand over the left chest while singing the National Anthem and the Bangsamoro Hymn shall show respect and remembrance to the men and women who struggled for the sake of the Bangsamoro identity, culture and to remind the coming generations of the history of the quest for self-determination and to remember the sacrifices and teachings of the late Shiek Salamat Hashim (Allahu Yarhamu);

WHEREAS, placing the right hand over the left chest shall symbolize our united stand as proud Bangsamoro and Filipino recognizing as one region under one nation.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to passed and approved this resolution, “Encouraging the constituents of the Bangsamoro region to place their right hand over the left chest upon singing the Bangsamoro Hymn to show respect and solidarity, and stand as united Bangsamoro Region”.

**FURTHER RESOLVED,** that the Statutory Committee being the Secretariat of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to furnish a copy of this resolution to Bill and Index Department and all members of the BTA a copy of this resolution.
