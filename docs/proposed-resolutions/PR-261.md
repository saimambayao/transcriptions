**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING AL-ZAHEER ADIL ABUBAKAR, A GRADUATE OF NOTRE DAME UNIVERSITY, FOR BEING TOP 10 IN THE RECENT LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR PROFESSIONAL TEACHERS GIVEN BY THE PROFESSIONAL REGULATION COMMISSION (PRC)**

**WHEREAS**, on the 19th day of May 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced the results of the March 2023 Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers;

**WHEREAS**, with an impressive rating of 90.40 percent, Notre Dame University graduate and alumna Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar obtained the rank of Top 10 of the other 60,896 examinees out of which 24,819 have passed the licensure exam for elementary level after attending the March 2023 examination;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Abubakar completed his college education in Cotabato City, at Notre Dame University with the degree Bachelor of Elementary Education;

**WHEREAS**, he serves as a good example and inspiration for the young Bangsamoro people to emulate and excel in their chosen field of endeavors;

**WHEREAS**, the exemplary achievement of Mr. Abubakar is a source of inspiration for the young professionals and aspirants in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and the entire nation which merits this parliamentâ€™s recognition and commendation;

**WHEREAS**, it is without a doubt that Mr. Abubakar brought honor to our region. His perseverance, dedication, and courage were evident in securing the Top 10 in the Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers. It will forever serve as an inspiration not only to the people of Lanao del Sur but also to the millions of Bangsamoro people across the country;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Parliament, to **CONGRATULATE AND COMMEND AL-ZAHEER ADIL ABUBAKAR A GRADUATE OF NOTRE DAME UNIVERSITY FOR BEING TOP 10 IN THE RECENT LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR PROFESSIONAL TEACHERS GIVEN BY THE PROFESSIONAL REGULATION COMMISSION (PRC);**

**BE IT RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that a copy of this resolution be furnished to Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar.
