**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MS. FATIMA AYESHA JUWAK AMIRUL, A TAWI-TAWI NATIVE, FOR BAGGING A GOLD MEDAL IN WOMEN’S SOFT TENNIS COMPETITION DURING THE RECENTLY CONCLUDED 32ND SOUTH EAST ASIAN GAMES HELD FROM MAY 05 TO 16, 2023 IN PHNOM PENH, COMBODIA**

**WHEREAS**, The Southeast Asian Games, also known as the SEA Games, is a biennial multi-sport event involving participants from countries within Southeast Asia. The 32nd SEA Games is currently held from May 5 to 17 of 2023 in Phnom Penh, Cambodia.

**WHEREAS**, The Philippine delegation to the 2023 Southeast Asian Games is composed of 814 athletes participating in all 38 sports.

**WHEREAS**, On May 09, 2023, the Philippines’ women’s team won a gold medal in soft tennis in the 32nd Southeast Asian Games in Cambodia. The women’s team is composed of Christy Sañosa, Fatima Ayesha Amirul, Noelle Conchita Mañalac, Noelle Nikki Zoleta, Princess Lorben Catindig and Virvienica Isearis Bejosano.

**WHEREAS**, Among the gold medalists is Ms. Fatima Ayesha Juwak Amirul, a Tawi-Tawi native, residing at Bongao Poblacion, Tawi-Tawi.

**WHEREAS**, Her exemplary performance as gold medalist serves as inspiration for Muslim Filipino children to excel in sports and other field of endeavors and bring glory to Bangsamoro Communities.

**WHEREAS**, The Bangsamoro Transition Authority is cognizant of Ms. Fatima Ayesha Juwak Amirul’s achievement of bringing pride and glory to the Bangsamoro Communities;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to congratulate and commend Ms. Fatima Ayesha Juwak Amirul for capturing the Gold Medal in Soft Tennis in the ongoing 32nd SEA GAMES held in Phnom Penh, Cambodia thereby giving pride, glory, and honor to the Bangsamoro Communities.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That a copy of this Resolution be presented to Ms. Fatima Ayesha Juwak Amirul, for information.

**ADOPTED**
