**A RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE GOVERNMENT OF THE DAY TO PROVIDE A FILIPINO TRANSLATION OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMY ACT NO. 35 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS, THE BANGSAMORO ELECTORAL CODE**

**WHEREAS**, as enshrined under Section 7, Article IV of RA 11054, or the Bangsamoro Organic Law, “the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure every Filipino citizen in its territorial jurisdiction the provision of the basic necessities and equal opportunities in life.”

**WHEREAS**, Section 6 of Article XIV of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that, “The national language of the Philippines is Filipino.”

**WHEREAS**, the office of Member of the Parliament Dr. Susana Salvador – Anayatin conducts a monthly orientation of the salient features of the Bangsamoro Electoral Code (BEC). During one of those forums, they find it difficult to understand the BEC, and suggested if it is possible to provide a Filipino translation.

**WHEREAS**, considering that the first Parliament elections is forthcoming, the people should be apprised of their rights and duties as voters and as aspiring candidates. The uniqueness of the Parliament system will be a challenge to our constituents to devise their objectives as a party or as an individual candidate.

**WHEREAS**, this resolution aims to ensure that the legal provisions are comprehensible to our constituents who may not be fluent in the original language of the law, and to encourage those from our grassroots to organize among themselves, a group guided by the BEC, and raise their fervent advocacies to the parliament.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament – Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao **A RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE GOVERNMENT OF THE DAY TO PROVIDE A FILIPINO TRANSLATION OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMY ACT NO. 35 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS, THE BANGSAMORO ELECTORAL CODE**
