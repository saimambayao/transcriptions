**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY MAIN CAMPUS - MARAWI FOR BEING RECOGNIZED AS THE TOP UNIVERSITY IN MINDANAO AND ATTAINING THE DISTINCTION OF RANKING 10TH IN THE ENTIRE PHILIPPINES**

**WHEREAS**, the Mindanao State University Main Campus - Marawi has been duly acknowledged as the premier educational institution in Mindanao, as evidenced by its recent accolade of being ranked the top university in Mindanao and securing the distinguished 10th position in the nationwide ranking according to the latest EduRank;

**WHEREAS**, the said recognition reflects the enduring commitment of Mindanao State University Main Campus - Marawi to excellence in academia, research, and community service, thereby contributing significantly to the advancement of education in the region and the nation as a whole;

**WHEREAS**, the journey of the Mindanao State University traces back to the historic events surrounding the Hadji Maas Kamlon uprising, which precipitated the compelling need for a comprehensive study on the socio-economic and political conditions in Mindanao, ultimately leading to the seminal Special House Committee report of Congress;

**WHEREAS**, the Commission on National Integration (CNI) was created in 1957 charged with the economic, social, moral and political advancement of the cultural minorities to include the granting and promoting local and foreign scholarships;

**WHEREAS**, the foregoing milestones laid the groundwork for the establishment of the Mindanao State University (MSU) on September 1, 1961 by Republic Act 1387, as amended;

**WHEREAS**, with the dissolution of CNI, its mandated responsibility of educational integration and advancement of the Muslim and other IPs of Mindanao, Sulu and Palawan (Minsupala ) was passed on to the Mindanao State University;

**WHEREAS**, the establishment of MSU is primarily to accelerate and intensify the education with a more extensive professional and technical training and instruction of the Muslims and other cultural minorities in southern Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, the remarkable achievement of Mindanao State University Main Campus - Marawi in attaining the top position in Mindanao and securing a prestigious rank nationally underscores the enduring legacy of dedication, innovation, and resilience that has characterized the institution since its inception;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, by the Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in session assembled, to extend heartfelt congratulations and commendations to the Mindanao State University Main Campus - Marawi for its outstanding performance in the latest EduRank, wherein it was recognized as the top university in Mindanao and achieved the notable distinction of ranking 10th in the entire Philippines;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that copies of this Resolution be furnished to the President of the Mindanao State University and Chancellor its main campus - Marawi, the Ministry of Basic, Higher, and Technical Education of the BARMM, and all relevant stakeholders for their information and appropriate action.
