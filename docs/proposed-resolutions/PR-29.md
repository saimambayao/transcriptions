**RESOLUTION**
**EXPRESSING DEEPEST SYMPATHY AND CONDOLENCES TO THE BEREAVED FAMILY AND LOVED ONES OF FORMER MINISTRY OF HEALTH DIRECTOR GENERAL DR. AMIREL S. USMAN WHO RETURNED TO HIS CREATOR ON SAFAR 21, 1444AH/ SEPTEMBER 17, 2022, AND RECOGNIZING HIS CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS,** Dr. Amirel S. Usman was the Director General of the Ministry of Health and the OIC-Chief of Hospital at Iranun District Hospital who served his constituents with humility, sincerity and utmost dedication;

**WHEREAS,** Dr. Amirel S. Usman served as the Medical Officer at the Dinaig Municipal Hospital in Datu Odin Sinsuat, Maguindanao from 2014 to 2016;

**WHEREAS,** Dr. Amirel S. Usman served as the Head of the Disaster Risk Reduction Management- Health (DRRM-H) before he became the Officer-in-Charge of the Ministry of Health from September 18, 2020 to March 31, 2021;

**WHEREAS,** as former Officer-in-Charge of the Ministry of Health, he helped guide the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao through the Covid-19 crisis, and a physician who tirelessly and unselfishly attended to those in need of his service even beyond working hours;

**WHEREAS,** Dr. Amirel Usman was the first to receive the Covid- 19 vaccine (Sinovac) during the Integrated Provincial Health Office (IPHO) Maguindanao roll- out vaccine launching in the region, setting as an exemplary person to brave and promote the vaccination efforts in the region;

**WHEREAS,** he now departs from us but he would always be remembered as a responsible and dedicated man to his duties and responsibilities as a public servant and as a source of love and inspiration to his family and countless of friends;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Government expresses deep sorrow over the loss of the beloved former Ministry of Health Director General Dr. Amirel Usman, who passed away on Safar 21, 1444AH/ September 17, 2022, leaving behind his family, relatives, friends, and his constituents in grief;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED** by the BTA Parliament, as it hereby resolves, to extend its deepest sympathy and condolences to the bereaved family and loved ones of the late Ministry of Health Director General Dr. Amirel Usman, and to recognize his contributions to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** to send a copy of this Resolution to the bereaved family members of Dr. Amirel Usman, for their information.
