**A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING ATTY. ALLAN G. PANOLONG FOR BEING ELECTED AS NATIONAL EXECUTIVE VICE PRESIDENT FOR YEAR 2023-2025 AND SUCCEEDING NATIONAL PRESIDENT OF THE INTEGRATED BAR OF THE PHILIPPINES FOR YEAR 2025-2027**

**WHEREAS**, the Integrated Bar of the Philippines is the national organization of lawyers which is mandated to raise the standards of the legal profession, improve the administration of justice, and enable the Philippine Bar to discharge its public responsibilities more effectively.

**WHEREAS**, Atty. Allan G. Panolong was unanimously elected as Executive Vice President of the Integrated Bar of the Philippines on May 22, 2023 during the election of the 26th Board of Governors.

**WHEREAS**, Article VIII Section 47 of the IBP By-Laws state that the Executive Vice President shall automatically become President for the next succeeding term.

**WHEREAS**, Atty. Allan G. Panolong has shown an exemplary track record of dedicated service to the Integrated Bar of the Philippines as he previously served as Chapter President of the Lanao del Sur Chapter and Governor for Western Mindanao.

**WHEREAS**, Atty. Allan G. Panolong has brought honor and pride to the Bangsamoro for being the first Moro Maranao lawyer to be elected to the highest office of the Integrated Bar of the Philippines.

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority wishes to extend our sincere and utmost congratulations to Atty. Allan G. Panolong for achieving such feat.

**NOW THEREFORE**, be it **RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, to commend and congratulate Atty. Allan G. Panolong for his exemplary distinction and accomplishment.

**FURTHER, BE IT RESOLVED**, that, a copy of this Resolution be furnished to Atty. Allan G. Panolong and the Integrated Bar of the Philippines.
