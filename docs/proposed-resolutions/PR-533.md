**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING MSU - SINING KAMBAYOKA ENSEMBLE FOR WINNING THE PHILIPPINE LEAF OUTSTANDING CULTURAL THEATER GROUP AWARD**

**WHEREAS**, the State shall foster the preservation, enrichment, and dynamic evolution of a Filipino national culture based on the principle of unity in diversity in a climate of free artistic and intellectual expression¹;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government shall preserve the history, culture, arts, traditions, and the rich cultural heritage of the Bangsamoro People²;

**WHEREAS**, the MSU – Sining Kambayoka Ensemble received the Philippine Live Entertainment, Arts, and Festival (LEAF) Awards as the Outstanding Cultural Theater Group at the Metropolitan Theater on February 8, 2024³;

**WHEREAS**, the Philippine LEAF Awards is a prestigious award-giving body dedicated to recognizing and celebrating outstanding achievements of Filipino Artists in the fields of live entertainment, arts, fashion, and festivals. Founded on the principles of integrity, creativity, and excellence, the Philippine LEAF Awards is committed to honoring Filipino individuals and organizations that push the boundaries of artistic expression and contribute to the enrichment of Filipino culture, arts, and society⁴;

**WHEREAS**, the Philippine LEAF Awards offers a diverse range of award categories to encompass the broad spectrum of creative talent and among these categories is Performing Arts: Honoring outstanding contributions in dance, music, theater, and other performing arts disciplines, in which the MSU – Sining Kambayoka Ensemble's award belongs;

**WHEREAS**, The MSU-Sining Kambayoka Ensemble is one of the duly supervised groups of the MSU - Cultural Affairs Office of the Mindanao State University Marawi Main Campus. The MSU-SKE is in the thrust of mounting and remounting quality theater productions based on the Mindanao tri-peoples’ culture and arts particularly the Meranaw cultural community for peace advocacy and educational purposes of the University.
