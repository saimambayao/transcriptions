**RESOLUTION**
**REMINDING THE MINISTRIES, OFFICES, AGENCIES, AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS (LGUs) IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION TO STRICTLY OBSERVE THE ACCOUNTING AND REPORTING GUIDELINES OF CASH AND IN-KIND AIDS/DONATIONS FROM LOCAL AND FOREIGN SOURCES, BASED ON THE COMMISSION ON AUDIT (COA) MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR (MC) NO. 2014-002**

**WHEREAS,** the Commission on Audit (COA) issued Memorandum Circular (MC) No. 2014-002 on April 15, 2014 regarding the accounting and reporting guidelines on the receipt and utilization of the National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Fund (NDRRMF), cash and in-kind aids/donations from local and foreign sources, and funds allocated from the agency regular budget for Disaster Risk Reduction and Management (DRRM) program;¹

**WHEREAS,** Republic Act No. 10121, also known as the Philippine Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Act of 2010, seeks to institutionalize the policies, structures, coordination mechanisms and programs with continuing budget appropriation on disaster risk reduction from national down to local levels;²

**WHEREAS,** the Regional Disaster Coordinating Councils is known as the Regional Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Councils (RDRRMCs) and in the case of Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), it is called the Bangsamoro-Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council (B-DRRMC). The B-DRRMC coordinates, integrates, supervises, and evaluates the activities of the Local Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Councils (LDRRMCs) in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS,** Section 18 of R.A. 10121 provides the mechanism for international humanitarian assistance;

**WHEREAS,** the Intergovernmental Fiscal Policy Board (IFPB), created under the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) has approved the guidelines on the acceptance of foreign grants and donations by the Bangsamoro government;³

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region is prone to both natural and human-induced disasters;

**WHEREAS,** the Philippines, especially the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, was recently hit by the Severe Tropical Storm (STS) *Paeng*, with international name *Nalge*, which claimed hundreds of lives, and millions worth of public infrastructures and private properties;

**WHEREAS,** among the hardly hit areas with a number of fatalities is the Municipality of Datu Odin Sinsuat in Maguindanao del Norte;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro-Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council (B-DRRMC) through its Secretariat, the Bangsamoro Rapid Emergency Action on Disaster Incidence (READi), the Ministry of Social Services and Development (MSSD), and other agencies have facilitated bulk of the immediate response of the Bangsamoro Government to the victims of STS *Paeng*;

**WHEREAS,** several non-government organizations, and individuals have also assisted the affected and internally displaced persons in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS,** several assistance and donation drives have been conducted for the victims through the line agencies, the local government units, non-government organizations, and even private individuals;

**WHEREAS,** it has been observed that some agencies in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region have been extending assistance to victims of calamities and other agencies have resulted into donation drives in order to generate funds for the victims of calamities;

**WHEREAS,** transparency and accountability in the extension of assistance to the victims and in the use donations are quite challenging;

**WHEREAS,** there is a need to remind the ministries, offices, agencies and LGUs in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region on the observance of the COA MC No. 2014-002;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to approve this Resolution Reminding the Ministries, Offices, Agencies, and Local Government Units in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region to strictly observe the Accounting and Reporting Guidelines of Cash and In-kind Aids/Donations from Local and Foreign Sources, based on the Commission on Audit Memorandum Circular No. 2014-002.

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the Ministry of Interior and Local Government (MILG) shall furnish and monitor the implementation of this Resolution to the local government units in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.

**RESOLVED FINALLY,** that a copy of this resolution be furnished to all concerned ministries, offices and agencies in Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
