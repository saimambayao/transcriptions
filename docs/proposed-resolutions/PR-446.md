**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING THE REPUBLIC OF TURKIYE ON ITS 100TH ANNIVERSARY**

**WHEREAS**, on October 29, 2023, the Republic of Turkiye will celebrate its 100th anniversary as a nation, marking a significant milestone in its history;

**WHEREAS**, the Republic of Turkiye, under the dynamic leadership of President Recep Tayyip Erdogan, has made remarkable strides in political, economic, and social development during its first century;

**WHEREAS**, the people of Turkiye have demonstrated resilience, determination, and commitment to democratic principles, fostering peace and stability domestically and internationally;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament, on behalf of the Bangsamoro people, recognizes the vital importance of international friendships and cooperation and is committed to maintaining a strong and enduring friendship with the people of the Republic of Turkiye;

**WHEREAS**, it is timely and fitting to acknowledge the indelible contribution of the Republic of Turkiye to the Bangsamoro peace process by joining the people of Turkiye in the commemoration of the 100th anniversary of the Republic;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it **RESOLVED** by the BTA Parliament to extend the warmest greetings of the Bangsamoro people to the Republic of Turkiye on the occasion of its 100th anniversary, celebrating the remarkable progress and accomplishments achieved by the Turkish republic during this century.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the BTA Parliament commends the leadership of President Recep Tayyip Erdogan for his outstanding efforts for the advancement and prosperity of the Republic of Turkiye.

**RESOLVED FINALLY**, That a copy of this Resolution be forwarded to the Embassy of the Republic of Turkiye as an expression of our solidarity and
