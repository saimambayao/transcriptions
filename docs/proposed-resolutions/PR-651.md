**A RESOLUTION CALLING THE COMMISSION ON ELECTIONS (COMELEC), THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE - POLICE REGIONAL OFFICE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION (PNP-PROBAR), AND THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES (AFP) TO CONDUCT A SPEEDY, FAIR, AND THOROUGH INQUIRY IN LIGHT OF THE INCIDENTS THAT HAVE TRANSPIRED DURING THE FILING OF CERTIFICATE OF CANDIDACY FOR THE 2025 NATIONAL AND LOCAL ELECTIONS IN SOME AREAS IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM), RESULTING IN DEATHS, ARRESTS, AND DAMAGE TO VEHICLES AND PROPERTIES**

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) is a region striving for lasting peace, justice, and progress under the framework of autonomy and governance as provided by the Bangsamoro Organic Law (Republic Act No. 11054);

WHEREAS, the conduct of national and local elections is an essential democratic exercise that ensures the representation and participation of the Bangsamoro people in governance and decision-making processes, contributing to peace and development in the region;

WHEREAS, the filing of Certificates of Candidacy (COCs) for the 2025 National and Local Elections is a vital part of the democratic process, which should be conducted in an orderly, peaceful, and safe manner in accordance with election laws and procedures;

WHEREAS, recent reports from some areas in BARMM indicate incidents of violence during the filing period for COCs, resulting in deaths, arrests, and damage to vehicles and properties, as highlighted in a report by the Philippine News Agency (PNA) [https://www.pna.gov.ph/articles/1235080];

WHEREAS, these incidents raise significant concerns about the security and safety of the election process and the general public, and warrant an immediate and comprehensive investigation to uphold the integrity of the election process and to provide justice to the victims and their families;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament, to respectfully request the Commission on Elections (COMELEC), the Philippine National Police - Police Regional Office Bangsamoro Autonomous Region (PNP-PROBAR), and the Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP) to conduct a speedy, fair, and thorough inquiry into the incidents that occurred during the filing of COCs for the 2025 National and Local Elections in BARMM;

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the investigation shall focus on determining the facts surrounding the incidents, holding accountable those responsible, and ensuring the safety and security of future electoral activities in the region;

**RESOLVED FINALLY,** that copies of this resolution be furnished to the Commission on Elections (COMELEC), the Philippine National Police - Police Regional Office Bangsamoro Autonomous Region (PNP-PROBAR), the Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP), and other relevant agencies for their information and appropriate action.
