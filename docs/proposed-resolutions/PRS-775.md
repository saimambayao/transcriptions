**A RESOLUTION**
**RESPECTFULLY REQUESTING HIS EXCELLENCY PRESIDENT FERDINAND “BONGBONG” R. MARCOS, JR. TO DESIGNATE ANTONIO ERNESTO F. LAGDAMEO, JR., SPECIAL ASSISTANT TO THE PRESIDENT, AS THE GOVERNMENT OF THE PHILIPPINES CO-CHAIR OF THE INTERGOVERNMENTAL RELATIONS BODY (IGRB)**

**WHEREAS**, Section 15, Article X of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides for the creation of Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao within the framework of the Constitution and the national sovereignty and territorial integrity of the Republic of the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, Section 16, Article X of the same Constitution provides that the President shall exercise general supervision over autonomous regions to ensure that laws are faithfully executed;

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article VI of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL), reiterates that the President shall exercise general supervision over the Bangsamoro Government to ensure faithful execution of the laws;

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Article VI of the BOL created the National Government-Bangsamoro Government Intergovernmental Relations Body (IGRB) to coordinate and resolve issues on intergovernmental relations through regular consultation and continuing negotiation in a non- adversarial manner;

**WHEREAS**, the IGRB is mandated to exhaust all available means in a non-adversarial manner to resolve issues brought before it;

**WHEREAS**, the National Government and the Bangsamoro Government shall each appoint their respective representatives to the IGRB, which shall be supported by a joint secretariat;

**WHEREAS**, the IGRB has been co-chaired on the part of the National Government by Secretary Carlos G. Dominguez III during its initial meetings in 2019, and more recently by Secretary Amenah F. Pangandaman, while Minister Mohagher M. Iqbal has consistently co-chaired on behalf of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, following her resignation as Secretary of the Department of Budget and Management (DBM) on 17 November 2025, Secretary Amenah F. Pangandaman is no longer serving as Co-Chair of the IGRB;

**WHEREAS**, Section 3, Article V of the BOL provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise powers expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, and those necessary, appropriate, or incidental to its efficient and effective governance and the promotion of general welfare;

**WHEREAS**, Executive Order No. 1, signed by former President Rodrigo R. Duterte on 30 June 2016, consolidated the offices and functions of the Office of the Special Assistant to the President, the Office of the Appointments Secretary, and the Presidential Management Staff;

**WHEREAS**, Section 2 of Executive Order No. 1 placed the Office of the Special Assistant to the President (OSAP), the Office of the Appointments Secretary, and the Presidential Management Staff (PMS) under the oversight of the Special Assistant to the President (SAP);

**WHEREAS**, the OSAP is headed by an appointee with the rank of Secretary who oversees and supervises improvements in systems and processes to ensure efficiency and effectiveness in management;

**WHEREAS**, in 2022, His Excellency President Ferdinand “Bongbong” R. Marcos, Jr. appointed Antonio Ernesto F. Lagdameo, Jr. as Special Assistant to the President, a position he continues to hold, signifying the continuing trust and confidence of His Excellency;

**WHEREAS**, SAP Antonio Ernesto F. Lagdameo, Jr. earned his undergraduate degree from the University of Pennsylvania and his master’s degree from the University of Asia and the Pacific;

**WHEREAS**, he was elected in 2007 as Representative of the Province of Davao del Norte and served three consecutive terms;

**WHEREAS**, since his appointment as Special Assistant to the President, SAP Lagdameo has actively supported peace-building and community development initiatives involving the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) and the Moro National Liberation Front (MNLF);

**WHEREAS**, SAP Lagdameo possesses a thorough understanding of the complexities of the National Government’s peace process and its implications for Moro communities;

**WHEREAS**, SAP Lagdameo has consistently demonstrated competence, integrity, and credibility, enabling him to effectively articulate and implement the directives of His Excellency President Ferdinand “Bongbong” R. Marcos, Jr. at the intergovernmental level;

**WHEREAS**, the designation of SAP Antonio Ernesto F. Lagdameo, Jr. as the Government of the Philippines Co-Chair of the IGRB will further strengthen coordination and cooperation between the National Government and the Bangsamoro Government in addressing intergovernmental concerns;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, as the interim government of the Bangsamoro Government, deems it necessary to reinforce the National Government’s commitment to the peace process and the effective implementation of the Bangsamoro Organic Law;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to respectfully request His Excellency President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr. to designate Antonio Ernesto F. Lagdameo, Jr., Special Assistant to the President, as the Government of the Philippines Co-Chair of the Intergovernmental Relations Body (IGRB).
