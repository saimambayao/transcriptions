**RESOLUTION**
**INVOKING THE REPRESENTATION OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT IN THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) is a new political entity created on 2019 by virtue of Republic Act No. 11054, or the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL);

**WHEREAS**, the BOL is considered to be the culmination of the decades-long struggle of the Bangsamoro's right to self-determination;

**WHEREAS**, **Section 2**, Article IV of the BOL provides that in the exercise of its right to self-governance, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region is free to pursue its political, economic, social and cultural development as provided for in the Organic Law;

**WHEREAS**, **Section 3**, Article V of the BOL provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the power expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective governance and those which are essential to the promotion of general welfare. Within its territorial jurisdiction, the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure and support, among other things, the preservation and enrichment of culture, promote health and safety, enhance the right of the people to a balanced ecology, encourage and support the development of appropriate and self-reliant scientific and technological capabilities, improve public morals, enhance economic prosperity and social justice, promote full employment among its residents, maintain peace and order, and preserve the comfort and convenience of its inhabitants;

**WHEREAS**, **Section 11**, Article VI of the BOL provides that as far as practicable, the Bangsamoro Government shall be represented in the departments, offices, commissions, agencies and bureaus of the National Government that implement and enforce policies, programs, and projects of the National Government in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS**, as of 2024, more than 150 national agencies, offices, commissions, and agencies of the National Government are listed in the 2024 General Appropriations Act, excluding consulates and consular mission, state colleges and universities, bureaus, and regional offices;

**WHEREAS**, **Section 2**, Article VII of the BOL states that the powers of government shall be vested in the Parliament which shall exercise those powers and functions expressly granted to it in this Organic Law, and those necessary for, or incidental to the proper governance and development of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. It shall set policies, legislate on matters within its authority, and elect a Chief Minister who shall exercise executive authority on its behalf;

**WHEREAS**, transitioning the Bangsamoro region from a conflict-ridden area to a peaceful and prosperous community takes time;

**WHEREAS**, one of the prerequisites in achieving a thriving region is to properly address and deal with the issues that the region is currently facing;

**WHEREAS**, there should be representatives from the Bangsamoro region, as far as practicable, in all national government departments, offices, commissions, agencies and bureaus that have in-depth knowledge of the struggles of the Bangsamoro region, in order to better address the gaps and current problems that the region is facing;

**WHEREAS**, in addition, the representation of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in national government posts is crucial and fundamental to achieving the ultimate objective of a peaceful and prosperous BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), as the interim government of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, performs a crucial role in realizing the aspiration of every Bangsamoro in achieving the long-lasting peace in the region; and

**WHEREAS**, moreover, it is imperative for the BTA to pass a resolution that not just protects, but also promotes the general welfare of every Bangsamoro.

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the BTA Parliament to pass and approve this Resolution invoking the representation of the Bangsamoro Government in the National Government.
