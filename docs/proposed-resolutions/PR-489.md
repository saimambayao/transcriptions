**“A RESOLUTION COMMENDING SAKINUR-AIN MUGONG DELASAS OF TAWI-TAWI FOR BEING DECLARED ONE OF THE NINE MANLILIKHA NG BAYAN BY PRESIDENT FERDINAND ROMUALDEZ MARCOS, JR. FOR HER COMMITMENT TO SAFEGUARDING AND PROMOTING THE SAMA IGAL DANCE TRADITION”**

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) is dedicated to recognizing individuals who significantly contribute to the preservation and enhancement of the Bangsamoro’s cultural heritage;

**WHEREAS,** Sakinur-ain Mugong Delasas, a distinguished cultural practitioner from Tawi-Tawi, has been honored as one of the nine Manlilikha ng Bayan by President Ferdinand Romualdez Marcos, Jr., acknowledging her exceptional dedication to the Sama Igal, a traditional dance of the Sama people;

**WHEREAS,** Sakinur-ain Mugong Delasas’s work in safeguarding and promoting the Sama Igal dance tradition exemplifies her profound commitment to preserving the unique cultural heritage of the Bangsamoro, particularly the rich traditions of Tawi-Tawi;

**WHEREAS,** her recognition as a Manlilikha ng Bayan not only celebrates her individual contributions but also highlights the importance of the Sama Igal dance as a vital element of our national cultural identity, inspiring the Bangsamoro community and the nation at large;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to commend Sakinur-ain Mugong Delasas for her extraordinary efforts in safeguarding and promoting the Sama Igal dance tradition, and for being recognized as one of the Manlilikha ng Bayan by President Ferdinand Romualdez Marcos, Jr.

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED,** that a copy of this resolution be presented to Sakinur-ain Mugong Delasas as a symbol of our highest regard and acknowledgment of her invaluable contribution to the cultural heritage of the Bangsamoro and the nation.

Adopted ________ December, 2023.
