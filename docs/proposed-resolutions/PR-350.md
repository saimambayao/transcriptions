**RESOLUTION HONORING THE LATE GRAND MUFTI SHIEKH ABUHURAIRA U. UDASAN AND EXTENDING DEEPEST CONDOLENCES AND APPRECIATION FOR HIS CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION**

**WHEREAS,** we deeply mourn the passing of Shiekh Abuhuraira U. Udasan, the esteemed Grand Mufti of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, who peacefully departed at the age of eighty-one (81) on July 3rd, 2023, in his residence in Lugay-Lugay, Brgy. Bagua I, Cotabato City;

**WHEREAS,** born on March 3, 1942, in Kitango, Municipality of Dulawan, now known as Datu Piang, Maguindanao Del Sur, Shiekh Abuhuraira U. Udasan pursued spiritual studies in Comparative Religion (Christian-Islam) in Jerusalem in 1966 and acquired an "Al-Ijazah Al-Aliah" degree from the Faculty of Da'wah and Usuluddin, specializing in comparative religion, at the Islamic University in Medina, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA), from 1967 to 1971;

**WHEREAS,** Grand Mufti Udasan served as the Director of 'Ma'had Al-Rahmaniah,' an esteemed high school institution focusing on Islamic studies in the Arabic language, situated in Maganoy, Sharif Aguak, Maguindanao del Sur. Furthermore, he held the position of Director of Islamic Da'wah for the United Sabah Islamic Association in Sabah, Malaysia, for fifteen years;

**WHEREAS,** Grand Mufti Udasan was a pillar of the Bangsamoro struggle and one of the co-founders of the MILF, standing alongside the late MILF Founding Chair Salamat Hashim and Sheikh Khalipha Usman Nando, the first Wālī of the Bangsamoro;

**WHEREAS,** his appointment as the Grand Mufti of BARMM and his leadership in the Office of Bangsamoro Darul Ifta (BDI) showcased his profound responsibility in issuing fatwa (a ruling on a point of Islamic law) and legal opinions concerning Islamic affairs;

**WHEREAS,** during his lifetime, Grand Mufti Udasan authored several books, including "Islam In Practice," "The Wisdom of Jihad," "The Bible of Qur'an," "Islamic Dah'wah," "Allegations Against Islam and How I Refuted Them," and "Back to Qur'an," enriching understanding of Islam;

**WHEREAS,** Grand Mufti Udasan will be remembered as a person of unwavering integrity, dedication, and compassion. His extensive knowledge of Islamic teachings and steadfast commitment to Islam made him an invaluable source of guidance and inspiration;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Transition Authority joins the entire Bangsamoro region in expressing our deepest sympathies to the bereaved family, relatives, and friends of Grand Mufti Udasan. The BTA Parliament stands united with the Bangsamoro people in mourning the loss of one of our greatest revolutionaries and religious leaders and in praying that Allah Subhanahu Wa Ta'ala accept all of his good deeds, grant him forgiveness, and provide eternal solace for his soul in Jannatul Firdaus. Inna lillahi wa inna ilayhi raji'un (Surely, we belong to Allah and verily to Him we shall return);

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to pass and approve this Resolution conveying deepest condolences and sympathy to the bereaved family of the late Grand Mufti of BARMM, Shiekh Abuhuraira U. Udasan, and extending profound appreciation and gratitude for his invaluable contributions to the region;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** to present this Resolution to the bereaved family of Shiekh Abuhuraira U. Udasan, for information.
