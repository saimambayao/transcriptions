**RESOLUTION**
**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MR. AMINODDIN MASBUD DOMADO FOR RANKING TOP SIX (6) AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS IN THE RECENTLY CONCLUDED 2023 MEDICAL TECHNOLOGISTS LICENSURE EXAMINATION, FOR GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, on 8 and 9 March 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and the Board of Medical Technology conducted the regular Medical Technologists (MedTech) Licensure Examination in various testing centers nationwide;

**WHEREAS**, the Professional Regulation Commission announced on 14 March 2023 that out of 6,165 examinees, only 4,714 or 76.46% successfully passed the Medical Technologists Licensure Examination;

**WHEREAS**, the Professional Regulation Commission announced that Mr. Aminoddin M. Domado, who is a recipient of a Latin academic honor as Magna Cum Laude from Far Eastern University - Dr. Nicanor Reyes Medical Foundation, admirably ranked as Top Six (6) with an excellent score of 91.40%;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Aminoddin M. Domado, 23 years of age is a young Bangsamoro professional of Maranao descent and a proud son of late Engr. Alimoden Domado and Bae Mokiin Masbud-Domado who are natives of the Municipalities of Poona Bayabao - Ramain and Poona Bayabao - Masiu, respectively.

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Aminoddin M. Domado has an impressive list of academic achievements in both tertiary and secondary education, including numerous awards in research, academic excellence, and poster and oral presentations;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Aminoddin M. Domado is an active member of the Philippine Society of Microbiology (PSM) and his research experience spans various fields, including COVID-19, carbon credits, nutritional status, and the impact of computer gaming on adolescents;

**WHEREAS**, his exemplary performance to become one of the topnotchers is a source of inspiration for the Bangsamoro youth and young professionals in the field of medical technologists and other allied health professions;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority is cognizant of Mr. Aminoddin M. Domado and all Bangsamoro passers of 2023 Medical Technologists Licensure Examination for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people. They are the living proof that the youth are not only our future in as much as they are also our present.

**NOW THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to congratulate and commend Mr. Aminoddin Masbud Domado for ranking Top Six (6) and all Bangsamoro passers in the recently concluded 2023 Medical Technologists Licensure Examination, thereby giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people

**RESOLVE, FURTHER**, to present a copy of this resolution to Mr. Aminoddin M. Domado, for information.
