**A RESOLUTION COMMENDING THE ESTEEMED BANGSAMORO SCHOLARS WHO RECENTLY GRADUATED IN THE DIFFERENT FIELDS OF ISLAMIC STUDIES AT THE AL-AZHAR UNIVERSITY IN CAIRO, EGYPT**

**WHEREAS**, in the pursuit for academic excellence in the field of Islamic studies, we should recognize and celebrate the accomplishments of our graduates from the Al-Azhar University in Cairo, Egypt;

**WHEREAS**, for academic School Year 2022-2023, thirteen (13) esteemed Bangsamoro Scholars in the different fields of Islamic Studies completed their courses at the Al-Azhar University, in Cairo, Egypt, as follows;

**List of Graduates** | **Course**
--- | ---
1. Abdulmujid Jailani Usman | SARIA WALKANUN MAJOR SARI-AH ISLAMIA
2. Lukman Limanda Hudjatun | SARI-AH WALKANUN MAJOR SARI-AH ISLAMIA
3. Zambas Imamil Sangkula | USULUDDIN MAJOR IN HADIST
4. Abdulkadir kappalahu haddaman | USULUDDIN MAJOR IN HADIST
5. Ali mamokhan Romanggar | USULUDDIN MAJOR IN TAPSIR
6. Asma Pindilehen Lucman | SARI-AH WALKANUN MAJOR IN KANUN

8. Jamal Palao Dimasipun | USULUDDIN MAJOR IN HADI
9. Aslanie Domangcag Jalal | SARI-AH ISLAMIYA
10. Normisbah isah abdulmohaimen | SARI-AH ISLAMIYA
11. Najib sarip noor | SARI-AH ISLAMIYA
12. Abdullah Polao Alang | SARI-AH ISLAMIYA
13. Asfahanie Asim Abutazil | ARI-AH WALKANUN MAJOR IN SARI-AH ISLAMIYA

**WHEREAS**, Islamic scholars are the backbone of Bangsamoro liberation, and the remarkable achievements of these scholars have contributed great pride and honor to the Islamic community in general and to the Bangsamoro in particular;

**WHEREAS**, Islamic knowledge and propagation are greatly contributing to uplifting of human welfare and well-being of Muslims in this world and in the hereafter, and the graduation of these Islamic scholars shall yield further strength and motivation to the Bangsamoro; and

**WHEREAS**, in recognition of their sacrifices, hard work and perseverance, it is but proper to recognize the efforts of the foregoing esteemed Islamic scholars in their quest for eternal knowledge that is indispensable to the moral and spiritual liberation of the Bangsamoro.

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to commend the esteemed Bangsamoro scholars who recently graduated in the different fields of Islamic studies at the Al-Azhar University, in Cairo, Egypt.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to present this Resolution to the above-mentioned esteemed Bangsamoro scholars, for their information.
