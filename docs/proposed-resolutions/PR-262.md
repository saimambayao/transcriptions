**RESOLUTION**
**DIRECTING THE BANGSAMORO COMMISSION FOR THE PRESERVATION OF CULTURAL HERITAGE (BCPCH) TO DOCUMENT THE SIGNIFICANT MILESTONES ACHIEVED BY THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT FROM 2018 TO 2025 FOR INCLUSION IN THE BANGSAMORO HISTORICAL DOCUMENTATION**

**WHEREAS**, the first mandate of the BCPCH is to “respect, recognize, protect, preserve, revive, harness, develop, promote and enhance the culture, customs, traditions and beliefs and practices of the people in the area of the autonomy”¹;

**WHEREAS**, Section 24, Article IX of the Bangsamoro Organic Law states that the Bangsamoro Government shall “preserve the history, culture, arts traditions, and the rich cultural heritage of the Bangsamoro people”;

**WHEREAS**, Section 25 of the same article also outlines that the primary responsibility of the BCPCH is to “write the history of the Bangsamoro people and to establish and sustain the cultural institutions, programs, and projects in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region”;

**WHEREAS**, the BCPCH traces its history from the preceding BCH-ARMM²;

**WHEREAS**, the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) existed from 1989 to February 2019, following the passage of the Bangsamoro Organic Law;

**WHEREAS**, no documentation about the milestones of the transition from the defunct ARMM currently exists, where significant milestones from the defunct ARMM towards current Bangsamoro Government showcases the progress and achievements of the region over the years;

**WHEREAS**, the transition from the defunct ARMM government is part of the region’s history and itself is a historical achievement to be promoted;

**WHEREAS**, the transition period started from the ratification of the Bangsamoro Organic Law and shall end upon the dissolution of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority.

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the BTA Parliament, to pass and approve this Resolution directing the Bangsamoro Commission for the Preservation of Cultural Heritage (BCPCH) to document the significant milestones achieved by the Bangsamoro Government from 2018 to 2025 for inclusion in the Bangsamoro Historical Documentation.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, to forward this Resolution to the BCPCH for information and appropriate action.
