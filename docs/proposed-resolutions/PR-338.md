**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING MINISTRY OF BASIC, HIGHER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION (MBHTE) MINISTER MP MOHAGHER M. IQBAL FOR BEING CONFERRED HONORIS CAUSA BY THE COMMISSION ON HIGHER EDUCATION THE DEGREE OF DOCTOR OF PHILOSOPHY IN PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION BY VIRTUE OF CEB RESOLUTION NO. 093-2023.**

**WHEREAS**, on May 17, 2023, upon the recommendation of the Faculty and Staff of the Cotabato State University and by the authority of its Board of Regents and Commission on Higher Education (CHED), the Cotabato State University (CSU) awarded an Honorary Doctorate (*Honoris Causa*) Degree of Doctor of Philosophy in Public Administration to MBHTE Minister Mohagher M. Iqbal.

**WHEREAS**, “*Honoris Causa*” is a Latin phrase that means “for the sake of the honor” and is an academic degree awarded by a university or any awarding institution for which the usual requirements are being waived and is often conferred as a way of honoring an individual’s contributions to a specific field or to society in general.

**WHEREAS**, according to CHED Memorandum Order No. 19, Series of 2014, the Honoris Causa Doctor of Public Administration is awarded to individuals who have distinguished themselves in and contributed so much to the management and administration of public services issued by a Higher Education Institution that has been existing for at least 25 years that has a well-acknowledged academic reputation and institutional values and mission as adjudged by the CHED.

**WHEREAS**, Minister Iqbal has been so brilliant in both his academic and employment careers as manifested by the different recognitions and awards he acquired as an educator and a public servant, to name a few are:
*	**Most Inspiring Teacher, Republic of the Philippines 2021**, awarded by Saceda Youth Lead School, Inc. on July 22, 2021
*	**Peace Weaver Award**, given during the Mindanao Week of Peace 2012 in Zamboanga City in 2012.
*	**Fr. William Masterson SJ Award**, given by Xavier University in recognition of his service to the mission of building a society of justice, peace, and compassion at Cagayan de Oro City, Philippines in 2015.
*	**Presidential Award for Peace Building in Mindanao**, given by the Ateneo de Davao University on April 6, 2019.
*	**GAWAD AKAP of Rotary Peace Awards (Parangal Kapayapaan)**, given by Rotary District 3830, Rotary International at the Philippine International Convention Center (PICC) on February 27, 2019.
*	**2021 Most Inspiring Teachers of the Republic of the Philippines**, conferred by a prestigious institution, the Saceda Youth Lead, where Minister Iqbal was the first awardee in BARMM in 2021.
*	**Professor ad Honorem / Vittasta Ambassador Distinction Honoris Causa**, awarded by the University of Henry Durant, Republic of Ivory Coast-Subsidiary of UHD Geneva, Switzerland in 2012.
*	**Honoris Causa in Doctor of Philosophy in Public Administration**, awarded by the Cotabato State University on June 17, 2023.

**WHEREAS**, as proof of his academic excellence, Minister Iqbal was also a consistent academic scholar, a Commission on National Integration scholar from 1965-1972, and a scholar of Mindanao State University.

**WHEREAS**, Minister Iqbal has been serving the Bangsamoro people for decades in different capacities while upholding the Bangsamoro cause for peace and quality education.

**WHEREAS**, some of the highlights of Minister Iqbal’s service to the Bangsamoro People are the following:
*	Chairman of the Committee on Propaganda of the Kutawato Revolutionary Committee from 1973 – 1977.
*	Member of the Central Committee of the MILF since 1979
*	Chair of the Committee on Information (since 1979)
*	MILF Chairman for Peace Negotiating and now Implementing Panel since 2003
*	Chairman of the Bangsamoro Transition Commission that crafted the first Bangsamoro Basic Law from 2013 – 2016.
*	Member of the second iteration of the Bangsamoro Transition Commission from 2016 – 2019.
*	Member of the Parliament in the Bangsamoro Transition Authority since 2019.
*	Appointed by Interim Chief Minister as Co-chair of the Intergovernmental Relations (IGR) Body for the side of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.
*	Minister of the Basic, Higher, and Technical Education
*	Worked with the international community and peace-building organizations, especially those part of the peace process architecture.
*	Chaired significant bodies such as the Consultative Committee of the UN-World Bank Facility for Advisory Support for Transition Capacities (FASTRAC).
*	Co-chairs the Education Pathways to Peace in Mindanao Steering Committee together with representatives from the GPH Department of Education and the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade (DFAT) of the Australian Government.

**WHEREAS**, as a champion of peace and academic excellence, Minister Iqbal has written and published several books entitled; 1) Bangsamoro: A Nation Under Endless Tyranny; 2) The Long Road to Peace: Inside the GPH-MILF Peace Process; and 3) Negotiating Peace: An Insider Perspective of the Bangsamoro Struggle for Self-determination written under his nom de Guerre name “Mohagher Iqbal”.

**WHEREAS**, this Honorable Parliament, as justified by the abovementioned facts, must consider the propriety and essence of expressing our utmost congratulations to MBHTE Minister Mohagher M. Iqbal and extend as well our partaking in the celebration of the exceptional success brought to the Bangsamoro society, by this extraordinary man of values and wisdom.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament – Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING MINISTRY OF BASIC, HIGHER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION (MBHTE) MINISTER MP MOHAGHER M. IQBAL FOR BEING CONFERRED HONORIS CAUSA BY THE COMMISSION ON HIGHER EDUCATION THE DEGREE OF DOCTOR OF PHILOSOPHY IN PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION BY VIRTUE OF CEB RESOLUTION NO. 093-2023.**
