**A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING APPRECIATION AND GRATITUDE TO PRESIDENT FERDINAND ROMUALDEZ MARCOS, JR. FOR HIS CONTINUING COMMITMENT TO SUPPORT THE TRANSITION OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM) AS MENTIONED IN HIS 2023 STATE OF THE NATION ADDRESS**

**WHEREAS**, On July 24, 2023, President Ferdinand Romualdez Marcos, Jr. delivered his second State of the Nation Address (SONA) as mandated in Section 23, Article VII of the 1987 Philippine Constitution which states that "*The President shall address the Congress at the opening of its regular session.*"

**WHEREAS**, during the SONA, the Bangsamoro Government's efforts and milestones were recognized by our President as he mentioned particular points such as:

1. the forging of a partnership with the BARMM in relation to energy exploration and development, and its utilization within our territorial jurisdiction;
2. the continued rehabilitation of Marawi City;
3. the issuance of a proclamation granting amnesty to rebel returnees in order to guarantee the full decommissioning of former combatants; and
4. the guarantee of his continued support to BARMM Region's transition.

**WHEREAS**, in view of the foregoing, the Bangsamoro Government is one in celebrating this acknowledgment by the national government of our hard work towards achieving sustainable progress which is anchored on a true and lasting peace in Southern Philippines.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament – Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING APPRECIATION AND GRATITUDE TO PRESIDENT FERDINAND ROMUALDEZ MARCOS, JR. FOR HIS CONTINUING COMMITMENT TO SUPPORT THE TRANSITION OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM) AS MENTIONED IN HIS 2023 STATE OF THE NATION ADDRESS.**
