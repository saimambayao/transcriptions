**A RESOLUTION CALLING ON THE GOVERNMENT OF THE DAY (GOTD) TO ENSURE THE PARTICIPATION OF PARLIAMENT OFFICERS IN NATIONAL-BANGSAMORO INTERGOVERNMENTAL RELATIONS ACTIVITIES**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Organic Law (Republic Act No. 11054) provides for a mechanism for intergovernmental relations between the National Government and the Bangsamoro Government, recognizing the need for cooperation, coordination, and consultation in the exercise of their respective powers;

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Article VI of R.A. No. 11054 mandates the creation of intergovernmental relations mechanisms to coordinate and resolve issues between the two governments and to promote the harmonization of their functions;

**WHEREAS**, intergovernmental relations bodies, such as the Intergovernmental Relations Body (IGRB), the Philippine Congress-Bangsamoro Parliament Forum, the Intergovernmental Fiscal Policy Board, the Joint Body for the Zones of Joint Cooperation, the Intergovernmental Infrastructure Development Board, the Intergovernmental Energy Board, the Bangsamoro Sustainable Development Board, and the Council of Leaders, play a critical role in maintaining institutional linkages, policy harmonization, and programmatic alignment between the National and Bangsamoro Governments;

**WHEREAS**, several intergovernmental relations bodies have conducted multiple meetings and activities, but there has been no comprehensive reporting to the Bangsamoro Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, the Parliament is the primary legislative institution of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), vested with the authority to enact laws, appropriate funds, and represent the interests of the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, the active involvement of Parliament Officers in intergovernmental relations (IGR) activities can foster transparency, mutual understanding, and synergy between the Parliament and the Government of the Day (GOTD) in the pursuit of shared goals under the Bangsamoro Organic Law;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Parliament elects its own officers at the start of every new government;

**WHEREAS**, ensuring the participation of Parliament Officers in intergovernmental activities will strengthen democratic governance in the region and reinforce the institutional integrity and inclusivity of the IGR mechanisms;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, to call on the Government of the Day (GOTD) to ensure the participation of Parliament Officers in national-Bangsamoro intergovernmental relations activities and mechanisms, including meetings, dialogues, and related engagements;

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that copies of this Resolution be furnished to the Office of the Chief Minister for appropriate consideration and action.
