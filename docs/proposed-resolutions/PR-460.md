**RESOLUTION**
**COMMENDING MS. BAI REHANA MARAJAN LAUBAN FOR OBTAINING 9TH PLACE WITH AN IMPRESSIVE RATING OF 84.50% AMONGST THE 2,124 EXAMINEES IN THE 2023 LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR FISHERIES PROFESSIONALS (FTLE)**

WHEREAS, Section 16 of Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 or otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law states that education shall be a top priority of the Bangsamoro Government to establish, maintain, and support a complete and integrated system of quality education, which shall be a subsystem of the national education system;

WHEREAS, On the 6th day of November 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced that 716 out of 2,124 passed the Licensure Examination for Fisheries Professionals given by the Board of Fisheries in N. C. R., Baguio, Butuan, Cagayan de Oro, Calapan, Cebu, Davao, Iloilo, Koronadal, Legazpi, Lucena, Pagadian, Rosales, Tacloban, Tuguegarao and Zamboanga last October 25-26, 2023.

WHEREAS, while passing a licensure examination is a great achievement in itself, more exceptional is obtaining a high rank therefrom;

WHEREAS, with an impressive rating of 84.50%, Mindanao State University – Maguindanao graduate and alumna Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban obtained the rank of Top 9 of the other 2,124 examinees out of which 716 (33.71%) have passed the examination;

WHEREAS, Ms. Lauban completed her undergraduate degree in the Municipality of Datu Odin Sinsuat at Mindanao State University – Maguindanao with the degree of Bachelor of Science in Fisheries Major in General Fisheries as Cum Laude and a Model Student Awardee;

WHEREAS, Ms. Lauban, who hails from Datu Odin Sinsuat, Maguindanao, serves as an inspiration and a great example to her fellow Bangsamoro professionals, and a testament to that hard work and perseverance lead to success in their chosen endeavor;

WHEREAS, Ms. Lauban together with the other Mindanao State University – Maguindanao passers, deserves recognition, as a source of pride for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority is cognizant of Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban together with other FTLE Bangsamoro passers for bringing honor and pride to the Bangsamoro people;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it;**

**RESOLVED, BY THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY**, to commend Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban, a Bangsamoro graduate of Mindanao State University, for ranking ninth (9th) with an impressive rating of 84.50% in the October 2023 Licensure Examination for Fisheries Professional, for giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people;

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that a copy of this resolution be presented to Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban, for information.

Adopted, __________.
