**"A RESOLUTION DIRECTING THE COMMITTEE ON ENVIRONMENT, NATURAL RESOURCES, AND ENERGY TO CONDUCT INQUIRY IN AID OF LEGISLATION ON THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE BANNING SINGLE-USE OF PLASTICS IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM) IN ACCORDANCE TO MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 285 SERIES OF 2019"**

**WHEREAS**, Section 16 of Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that the State shall protect and advance the right of the people to a balanced and healthful ecology in accord with the rhythm and harmony of nature;

**WHEREAS**, it is the responsibility of the Bangsamoro Government for the exploration, utilization, management, conservation, protection and sustainable development of the region’s environment, natural resources and energy sources;

**WHEREAS**, the Office of the Chief Minister issued a Memorandum Order No. 285 series of 2019 which requires that all officials and employees to utilize reusable drinking containers, tumblers, mugs, and/or biodegradable food packs, it is noticeable that compliance among concerned employees is virtually nil;

**WHEREAS**, more than three (3) years had passed and it appears that the order of our beloved chief minister was ignored and openly violated;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament had already made a determination of an adverse effect of the single-use plastic in the region;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority adopted a resolution urging the parliament and all ministries, offices and agencies of the regional government to immediately implement and strictly follow memorandum order no. 285 series of 2019 issued by Chief Minister Ahod B. Ebrahim banning single-use of plastics in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM);

**WHEREAS**, this proposed resolution aims to inform the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament the measures employed as well as the status of implementation of the Memorandum Order No. 285 within the Bangsamoro Region as of this date;

**NOW THEREFORE**, be it RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament Resolution directing the Committee on Environment, Natural Resources, and energy to conduct inquiry in aid of legislation on the implementation of the banning single-use of plastics in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) as provided by the Memorandum Order No. 285 Series Of 2019.
