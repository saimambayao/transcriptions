**A RESOLUTION VEHEMENTLY DENOUNCING AND CONDEMNING, TO THE HIGHEST DEGREE POSSIBLE, THE HEINOUS BOMBING INCIDENT CARRIED OUT ON DECEMBER 3, 2023, AT THE MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY MAIN CAMPUS IN MARAWI CITY**

WHEREAS, it is the policy of the state to ensure the safety and well-being of its citizens. This includes protecting them from external threats, maintaining internal security, and upholding the rule of law;

WHEREAS, acts of violence and cruelty against the citizens, particularly against peace-loving students striving for a better future, have no room in a civilized society and are absolutely unacceptable;

WHEREAS, it is widely recognized that the Mindanao State University, the main campus in Marawi City, stands as a haven for students, providing not just education but also a nurturing environment that fosters growth, excellence, and a sense of community;

WHEREAS, it is recognized that MSU-Marawi is more than just an institution, it is a nest that caters to diverse and dynamic cultures from all over Mindanao, coming together to shape their futures and enrich their educational experience;

WHEREAS, turmoil has befallen the students of Mindanao State University on a peaceful Sunday morning, during a service to commemorate the first Sunday of Advent as the start of the Christmas season, when tragedy struck and an explosion disrupted their holy gathering;

WHEREAS, the investigation has revealed that an improvised explosive device hidden inside a black tote bag was the cause of the explosion that took the lives of four (4) individuals and injured forty-seven (47) others;

WHEREAS, the attack orchestrated by these felons, especially during the celebration of the Mindanao Week of Peace and the start of the Christmas season for our catholic brothers and sisters should be condemned to the highest order possible;

WHEREAS, we are deeply saddened by the loss of lives and injuries dealt with by this tragic attack. An unspeakable tragedy that leaves a scar on the lives of families, communities, and society as a whole which shall be a stark reminder of the senseless violence inflicted upon innocent individuals;

WHEREAS, we share in the grief of Mindanao State University and the Marawi community in this tragic event that transpired. Understanding that in times of loss and tribulations, you are not alone;

WHEREAS, we support and call upon the proper authorities to perform a thorough investigation in order to bring justice to the victims and to hold the perpetrators accountable for this heinous attack;

WHEREAS, any dreadful act with the purpose of sowing fear and terror towards a community, such as this tragedy that struck a recovering Marawi City, shall be denounced, rebuked, and condemned to the strongest degree possible;

**NOW THEFEFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, that the parliament vehemently denounce, rebuke, castigate, and condemn, to the highest degree possible, the heinous bombing incident that was carried out on December 3, 2023, at the Mindanao State University, Main Campus in Marawi city and we are one in solidarity with the whole Marawi community that in these darkest moments, you are not alone.
