**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING THE THREE SIBLINGS DR. AYEZA-SAPHIA TAMMANG MADJID, DR. ZERFA-NISHREEN TAMMANG MADJID, AND DR. DANA TAMMANG MADJID FOR PASSING THE RECENT 2023 PHYSICIAN LICENSURE EXAMINATIONS**

**WHEREAS,** the Professional Regulation Commission released on Friday, November 10, 2023, the results of the 2023 Physicians Licensure Examination where 4,083 out of 6,456 or 63.24 percent hurdled one of the toughest examinations in the country;

**WHEREAS,** in the recent examinations, an extraordinary accomplishment was showcased by the twins Ayeza-Saphia T. Madjid and Zerfa-Nishreen T. Madjid twenty-five (25) years old and their younger sibling Dana T. Madjid twenty-four (24) years old, who demonstrated exceptional academic prowess and commitment;

**WHEREAS,** the Madjid siblings are all Registered Nurses from Jolo, Sulu and they have earned their Nursing and Medicine Degrees from the University of Santo Tomas and San Beda University respectively;

**WHEREAS,** the three sisters have not only individually excelled in their pursuit of becoming licensed physicians but have collectively broken the record as the first siblings to simultaneously pass the same examination.

**WHEREAS,** the journey to becoming licensed physicians is marked by challenges that require resilience, empathy, and a genuine passion for healthcare, and the Madjid siblings have exemplified these qualities throughout their academic and professional endeavors;

**WHEREAS,** their success brings immense pride and honor not only to their family but also to the Bangsamoro region, serving as an inspiration for aspiring healthcare professionals and highlighting the region's commitment to excellence in healthcare;

**WHEREAS,** this Honorable Parliament through this resolution, intends to recognize the significance of acknowledging and celebrating the accomplishments of the Madjid sisters who have not only demonstrated academic excellence but also established the values and qualities essential to outstanding healthcare professionals while showcasing the advantages of having an extraordinary level of dedication and familial support.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament – Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING THE THREE SIBLINGS DR. AYEZA-SAPHIA TAMMANG MADJID, DR. ZERFA-NISHREEN TAMMANG MADJID, AND DR. DANA TAMMANG MADJID FOR PASSING THE RECENT 2023 PHYSICIAN LICENSURE EXAMINATIONS.**
