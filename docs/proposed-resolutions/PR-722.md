**A RESOLUTION AMENDING SECTION 5, RULE XVII OF THE RULES OF THE BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT TO INCLUDE COMMITTEE RESOLUTION AS A TYPE OF BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION**

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Article XVI of Republic Act No. 11054 created the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), which serves as the interim government in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region during the transition period. The legislative and executive powers in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region during the transition shall be vested in the BTA.

**WHEREAS**, the BTA adopted BTA Resolution No. 268, which defines the Rules, Procedures, and Practices of the BTA Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, the Rules, Procedures, and Practices of the BTA Parliament serve as the internal governing framework that guides parliamentary procedures, including the classification and processing of legislative measures and resolutions;

**WHEREAS**, Rule XVII, Section 5 of the Rules, Procedures, and Practices of the BTA Parliament currently enumerates the types of parliamentary resolutions but does not include Committee Resolutions as a distinct classification;

**WHEREAS**, the various Statutory and Parliamentary Committees of the Parliament regularly deliberate on matters within their jurisdiction and issue Committee Resolutions to express collective positions, recommendations, or actions on such matters;

**WHEREAS**, recognizing **Committee Resolutions** as a formal type of parliamentary resolution will enhance institutional clarity, reinforce committee work, and ensure proper documentation and archiving of committee decisions;

**WHEREAS**, this amendment is necessary to further strengthen the legislative processes and procedural integrity of the Bangsamoro Parliament;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament in session assembled:

**SECTION 1.** *Amendment.* – Section 5, Rule XVII of the Rules, Procedures, and Practices of the Bangsamoro Parliament is hereby amended to read as follows:

***Section 5. Types of Resolutions.*** *– There are **four (4)** types of resolutions in the Parliament, to wit:*

*(a) **Simple Resolution.** – A simple resolution is a measure used by the Parliament to take action affecting its own procedures, urgent resolution requiring immediate action by the Parliament, or to express an opinion, sympathy or commendation.*

*(b) **Concurrent Resolution.** – A concurrent resolution is a resolution issued concurrently by both the Bangsamoro Cabinet and the Parliament expressing their sentiments on certain matters.*

*(c) **Joint Committee Resolution.** – A joint committee resolution is a resolution of two (2) or more committees expressing the will or action of the Members on certain matters. This requires the majority vote of all the Committees concerned.*

*(d) **Committee Resolution.** – A committee resolution is a resolution adopted and issued by a Statutory or Parliamentary Committee of the Bangsamoro Parliament in relation to matters referred to the committee and within its jurisdiction, expressing its collective position, recommendation, or directive.*

**SECTION 2.** *Effectivity.* – This resolution shall take effect immediately upon its adoption by the Bangsamoro Parliament.
