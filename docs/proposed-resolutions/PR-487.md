**RESOLUTION**
**EXPRESSING UTMOST APPRECIATION, GRATITUDE AND COMMENDATION TO FORMER MINISTER ATTY. NAGUIB G. SINARIMBO**

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament recognizes and pays tribute to the remarkable service and enduring commitment of Atty. Naguib G. Sinarimbo, the Former Minister of the Ministry of the Interior and Local Government (MILG), a distinguished leader whose multifaceted contributions have significantly shaped the trajectory of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM);

**WHEREAS,** Atty. Naguib G. Sinarimbo, a legal luminary, played an indispensable role as part of the legal team of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) during the historic negotiations with the Philippine government, demonstrating not only his legal prowess but a steadfast dedication to achieving a just and lasting peace for the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS,** Atty. Sinarimbo served as the spokesperson of the BARMM government from its inception, emerging as the voice and face of our region, tirelessly and ardently advocating for the fundamental rights and aspirations of the Bangsamoro;

**WHEREAS,** Atty. Sinarimbo's vision and commitment to political representation for the Bangsamoro found fruition in his pivotal role in the formation of the United Bangsamoro Justice Party (UBJP), where he served as the deputy secretary, contributing significantly to the advancement of inclusive and representative governance;

**WHEREAS,** during the darkest days of the Marawi siege, Atty. Sinarimbo exhibited unparalleled resilience and commitment by choosing to remain in Marawi City, offering critical support to local government officials and demonstrating a profound sense of duty and courage in the face of adversity;

**WHEREAS,** as a consultant to Sen. Juan Miguel ‘Mig’ Zubiri, Atty. Sinarimbo played a strategic and instrumental role in the sponsorship and passage of the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) in the Senate, a historic legislative milestone that not only marked the culmination of years of dedicated effort but laid the foundation for the establishment of the BARMM;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament acknowledges and expresses its deepest appreciation for Atty. Sinarimbo's foresight, strategic thinking, and relentless efforts that contributed significantly to the passage of the BOL and its subsequent ratification by the Bangsamoro people;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** that the Bangsamoro Parliament, with a profound sense of gratitude and admiration, formally expresses its deepest thanks and commendation to Atty. Naguib G. Sinarimbo for his unwavering dedication, exceptional leadership, and multifaceted contributions during his tenure as the Minister of the Ministry of the Interior and Local Government (MILG);

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED,** that this resolution stands as a testament to our collective appreciation for Atty. Sinarimbo's lifelong commitment to the Bangsamoro cause—a commitment that has transcended negotiations, advocacy, political representation, crisis response, and legislative accomplishments.
