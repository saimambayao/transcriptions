**BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY-PARLIAMENT**
**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**
**Proposed Resolution No. 238**

**AUTHORED BY: MP MOHAMMAD S. YACOB**

**“A RESOLUTION INSTRUCTING THE MINISTRY OF INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT (MILG) TO STRONGLY MANDATE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS WITHIN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION TO ESTABLISH AND ALLOCATE SPACE AS MUSALLA/SURAU, BOTH IN PUBLIC AND PRIVATE BUSINESSES/ ESTABLISHMENTS AND IN PUBLIC AND PRIVATE SCHOOLS ”**

**WHEREAS**, as provided under Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known as Bangsamoro Organic Law, Article IX, Basic Rights, Section 4, it states:

“*Customary Rights and Traditions. - The customs, beliefs, and traditions of the Bangsamoro people are hereby recognized, protected, and guaranteed.*

The Parliament shall adopt measures to ensure mutual respect and protection of the distinct beliefs, customs, and traditions of the Bangsamoro people and the other inhabitants in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.

No person in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region shall be subjected to any form of discrimination on account of creed religion, ethnic origin, parentage, or gender.

**WHEREAS** the Bangsamoro Government as the seat of power has the socio-cultural and moral obligation to provide for the needs and identity of its people, and correlatively to provide an environment for a community that fosters prayer which in turn provides the moral teachings for the community;

**WHEREAS**, for Government employees to fulfill work obligations without interruption, the Local Government has a strong interest in building a prayer area within its confines;

**WHEREAS**, thru the intervention and support of the government, the Ministry of Interior and Local Government should mandate Local Government Units and encourage private establishments to establish prayer rooms/spaces, for the Bangsamoro Government to fulfill its mandate to the people.

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, that the Bangsamoro Transition Authority-Parliament hereby instructs the Ministry of Interior and Local Government (MILG) to direct the following:

**1.** All Local Government Units within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region shall establish at least one room/area to be dedicated solely as a prayer room inside the building/area.
**2.** All private business entities including but not limited to malls, hotels, multi-story stores, and offices, are strongly encouraged to establish the same prayer room.
**3.** The LGU shall ensure that the establishment of the prayer room shall not in any manner interfere with the duties and functions of government employees.

**RESOLVED FURTHER** that the Ministry of Interior and Local Government (MILG) and all Local Government Units within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao be furnished with this resolution. Likewise, the Local Government Units shall ensure this resolution reaches the appropriate private business entity owners.
