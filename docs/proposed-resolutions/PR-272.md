**A RESOLUTION RECOGNISING AND CONGRATULATING BANGSAMORO STUDENTS WHO GRADUATED RECENTLY FROM THE RENOWNED AL-ADZHAR UNIVERSITY IN CAIRO, EGYPT, CANADIAN UNIVERSITY IN DUBAI AND AINU SHAM UNIVERSITY IN CAIRO, EGYPT**

**WHEREAS**, several Bangsamoro students have studied and graduated in various prestigious foreign learning institutions and universities over the years;

**WHEREAS**, Ad-Azhar University is Egypt's oldest degree-granting university and is renowned as the most prestigious university for Islamic learning, and was founded in the 1970s by the Fatimid Caliphate as a centre of Islamic learning, where its students studied the Qu’ran and Islamic law in detail, along with logic, grammar, rhetoric, and how to calculate the phases of the moon, and as of present, is the chief centre of Arabic literature and Islamic learning in the world;

**WHEREAS**, the Canadian University is a private university in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, founded in 2006, and offers education based on the Canadian curriculum, on the four faculties, namely, Architecture and Interior Design, Communication Arts and Sciences, Engineering, Applied Science and Technology, and Management;

**WHEREAS**, Ainu Sham University is a public university located in Cairo, Egypt. Founded in July 1950, it is the third-oldest non-sectarian native public Egyptian university under the name of Ibrahim Pasha’s University and provides education at the undergraduate, graduate and post-graduate levels;
