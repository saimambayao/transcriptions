**RESOLUTION**
**REQUESTING THE PROFESSIONAL REGULATION COMMISSION (PRC) - CENTRAL OFFICE TO INCLUDE COTABATO CITY AS ONE OF ITS TESTING CENTERS FOR LICENSURE EXAMINATIONS¹**

**WHEREAS,** Section 26, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution specifically provides that the State shall guarantee equal access to opportunities for public service;

**WHEREAS,** Section 7, Article IV of Republic Act (R.A.) No. 11054, or the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL), provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure every Filipino citizen in its territorial jurisdiction the provision of the basic necessities and equal opportunities in life;

**WHEREAS,** Section 3, Article V of the BOL, under the General Welfare Clause, states that the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the power expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective governance and those which are essential to the promotion of general welfare;

**WHEREAS,** the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) is the licensing and regulatory agency of the national government for the practice of regulated professions. It was established as a national government agency by Presidential Decree (P.D.) No. 223 dated June 22, 1973, signed by then President Ferdinand E. Marcos, mandated to enforce the laws regulating the various professions²;

**WHEREAS,** Section 2 of R.A. No. 8981, or the PRC Modernization Act of 2000, provides that the State recognizes the important role of professionals in nation-building and, towards this end, promotes the sustained development of a reservoir of professionals whose competence has been determined by honest and credible licensure examinations and whose standards of professional service and practice are internationally recognized and considered world-class brought about the regulatory measures, programs and activities that foster professional growth and advancement;

**WHEREAS,** among the powers, functions and responsibilities of the PRC, as laid out in Section 7 of R.A. No. 8981, are to administer and conduct the licensure examinations of the various regulatory boards in accordance with the rules and regulations promulgated by the Commission; to determine and fix the places and dates of examinations; and to use publicly or privately owned buildings and facilities for examinations purposes;

**WHEREAS,** the PRC administers, implements, and enforces the regulatory laws and policies of the country with respect to the regulation and licensing of the various professions and occupations under its jurisdiction, including the enhancement and maintenance of professional and occupational standards and ethics and the enforcement of the rules and regulations relative thereto;

**WHEREAS,** on September 11, 2006, PRC was attached to the Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE) pursuant to Executive Order No. 565. Being an attached agency of the DOLE, the PRC works closely to the mandate of its mother agency³;

**WHEREAS,** in Mindanao, there are five (5) regional offices of the PRC, namely: (1) Pagadian City; (2) Cagayan de Oro City; (3) Davao City; (4) Koronadal; and (5) Butuan City⁴;

**WHEREAS,** licensure examinations of various regulatory boards of professions are usually held and conducted in the abovementioned areas;

**WHEREAS,** applicant-examinees of various licensure examinations from the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) and its neighboring provinces, municipalities, and cities have to travel as far as the areas mentioned and incur higher amount of expenses, particularly in transportation, board and lodging expenses, in order to take the licensure examinations;

**WHEREAS,** although PRC Service Center in Cotabato City was opened in 2023, this only answered the burdens of those professionals, who already took and passed their boards, in obtaining and processing their applications and renewals of PRC licenses;

**WHEREAS,** Cotabato City has the potential to be one of the testing centers for licensure examinations for aspiring professionals from the BARMM and nearby provinces based on geographical location and facilities;

**WHEREAS,** in the BARMM, the counterpart agency of DOLE with which the PRC is attached, is the Ministry of Labor and Employment (MOLE);

**WHEREAS,** the MOLE shall facilitate the necessary steps in order to implement this Resolution;

**WHEREAS,** as the interim government of the BARMM, it is imperative for the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament to cater the needs of and look into the best interest of its aspiring professionals;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament to pass and approve this Resolution Requesting the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) – Central Office to Include Cotabato City as one of its Testing Centers for Licensure Examinations.

**BE IT RESOLVED FURTHER,** that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to Ministry of Labor and Employment (MOLE) and Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE) for their information and appropriate action.
