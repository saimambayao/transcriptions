**RESOLUTION FOR THE IMPLEMENTATION OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10916, TOGETHER WITH ITS IMPLEMENTING RULES AND REGULATIONS, REQUIRING THE MINISTRY OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATIONS (MOTC) IN COORDINATION WITH THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO), THE MINISTRY OF TRADE, INVESTMENTS AND TOURISM (MTIT), AND THE LAND TRANSPORTATION FRANCHISING & REGULATORY BOARD (LTFRB), TO IMPLEMENT THE LAW ON SPEED LIMIT IN THE AREA OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 10916 is known as an Act Requiring the Mandatory Installation of Speed Limiter in Public Utility and Certain Types of Vehicles. It is a consolidation of Senate Bill No. 2999 and House Bill No. 5911 which was passed into law on February 2, 2016, with its Implementing Rules and Regulations duly promulgated that became effective after 15 days from its signing;

WHEREAS, implementation of the law is vital in the BARMM to promote public safety on the roads and highways, and safeguard the economic welfare of the transport business;

WHEREAS, the Ministry of Transportation and Communications (MOTC) shall take its responsibility in the law, together with the Land Transportation Office (LTO), the Ministry of Trade, Investments and Tourism (MTIT), and the Land Transportation Franchising & Regulatory Board (LTFRB), which shall undertake their respective roles in the law, for its full enforcement in the BARMM;

WHEREAS, the said offices shall take steps to expedite the implementation and reinforcement of R.A. No. 10916. Either the MOTC or MTIT, as may be ordered by the Chief Minister, may spearhead the invitation of persons, establishments or institutions accredited by the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) as premised in Section 7 of the Act, to facilitate the provision and installation of such speed limiter equipment in strategic areas in the Bangsamoro region;

WHEREAS, appropriation of funds necessary for this purpose may be allocated by the proper Ministry for the smooth implementation and enforcement of the law.

**NOW THEREFORE**, we resolve as we herein resolved to implement Republic Act No. 10916 in the BARMM, requiring the Ministry of Transportation and Communications (MOTC) to take its responsibility in the law on speed limit together with the Land Transportation Office (LTO), the Ministry of Trade, Investment and Tourism (MTIT), and the Land Transportation Franchising & Regulatory Board (LTFRB), to undertake their respective roles in the law, for its full enforcement in the BARMM. Appropriation of funds necessary for the purpose may be allocated by the proper Ministry for the smooth implementation and enforcement of the law.
