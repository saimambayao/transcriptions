**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MOHAMMAD NUR GUMA-OS CASIB FOR HIS EXEMPLARY ACHIEVEMENT IN WINNING A BRONZE MEDAL IN THE 55TH INTERNATIONAL CHEMISTRY OLYMPIAD 2023 AND FOR GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, the 55th International Chemistry Olympiad (55th IChO) was held from 16 July 2023 to 25 July 2023 in Zurich, Switzerland, and was attended by participants from around the globe;

**WHEREAS**, the IChO is a prestigious annual competition that brings together the brightest young minds from around the world for the most talented chemistry students at the secondary school level;

**WHEREAS**, IChO aims to promote international contacts in chemistry, friendships between young scientific workers of different nationalities, cooperation among pupils, and exchange of pedagogical and scientific experience in chemistry;

**WHEREAS**, Mohammad Nur Casib, a Bangsamoro student, was one of the representatives who participated in the 55th International Chemistry Olympiad after winning the 2023 Philippine Chemistry Olympiad National Competition.

**WHEREAS**, Mohammad Nur Casib, 17 years of age, is a young Bangsamoro of Maranao descent and a proud son of Dr. Norodin S. Casib and Dra. Sittienor M. Guma-os - Casib who are natives of Municipalities of Bayang and Ganassi, Lanao del Sur, BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, his hard work and commitment have resulted in his exemplary achievement of winning a Bronze Medal in the 55th IChO;

**WHEREAS**, apart from his exceptional performance in 55th IChO, he has been an excellent quizzer, and his remarkable skills and achievements in the realm of quizzes brought him an impressive collection of at least 38 winning (gold, silver, and bronze) medals from international competitions and an additional 36 medals from national competitions in the fields of Mathematics, Science, Chemistry, Physics, Astronomy, and others;

**WHEREAS**, his success did not only showcase his competitive spirit and personal excellence, but also served as a source of inspiration and motivation for all Bangsamoro children to pursue academic excellence and excel in various field of knowledge;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority is cognizant of Mohammad Nur Casibâ€™s achievement in bringing proud and prestige to the Bangsamoro people on international platform, hence deems it fitting to commend his success as we stay true to our belief that the youth are not only our future, in as much as they are also our present;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to congratulate and commend Mohammad Nur Guma-os Casib for his exemplary achievement in winning a bronze medal in the 55th International Chemistry Olympiad 2023 and for giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people

**RESOLVE, FURTHER**, to present a copy of this resolution to Mohammad Nur Guma-os Casib in recognition and expression of appreciation of his exemplary dedication and hard work that brought pride to the Bangsamoro people.
