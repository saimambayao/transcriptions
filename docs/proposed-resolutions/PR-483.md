**13**      **A RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE GOVERNMENT SERVICE INSURANCE**
**14**      **SYSTEM (GSIS) TO ESTABLISH BRANCH OFFICES IN DIFFERENT AREAS**
**15**      **IN THE BARMM.**
**16**
**17**      **WHEREAS,** by virtue of Commonwealth Act No. 186 and Republic Act
**18**      No. 8291 or the GSIS Act of 1997, the GSIS is a social insurance institution
**19**      that provides a defined benefit scheme under the law. It insures its members
**20**      against the occurrence of certain contingencies in exchange for their monthly
**21**      premium contributions.
**22**
**23**      **WHEREAS,** the GSIS covers all government workers, except the
**24**      following: (1) Members of the Judiciary and Constitutional Commissions who
**25**      are covered by separate retirement laws; (2) Contractual employees who have
**26**      no employee-employer relationship with their agencies; (3) Uniformed
**27**      members of the Armed Forces of the Philippines and the Philippine National
**28**      Police, including the Bureau of Jail Management and Penology and the
**29**      Bureau of Fire protection; (4) Barangay and Sanggunian officials who are not
**30**      receiving fixed monthly compensation; and (5) Employees who do not have
**31**      monthly regular hours of work and are not receiving fixed monthly
**32**      compensation.
**33**
**34**      **WHEREAS,** GSIS offers a variety of social security benefits, such as life
**35**      insurance benefits, separation or retirement benefits, and disability benefits.
**36**
**37**      **WHEREAS,** aside from insurance benefits in the event of contingencies,
**38**      GSIS also offers products to assist members in their financial needs: (1) Multi-
**39**      purpose Loan; (2) Policy Loan; (3) Enhanced Emergency Loan; (4) Enhanced
**40**      Pension Loan; and (5) Pensioners Emergency Loan.
**41**
**42**      **WHEREAS,** as of January 18, 2022 there are a total of 42 Branch
**43**      Offices in the country and only one (1) office is established in the BARMM,
**44**      found in Cotabato City.
**45**
**46**      **WHEREAS,** this resolution seeks to assist our Bangsamoro government
**47**      workers in managing their affairs with the GSIS, and to encourage others to
**48**      enroll for membership.
**49**
**50**      **WHEREAS,** in view of the financial and economic developments in our
**51**      region, access to financial services should be convenient, in order to
**52**      encourage our Bangsamoro people to be financially literate, to be secure from
**53**      contingencies, and to safeguard their retirement.
**54**
**55**      **NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the
**56**      Bangsamoro Parliament – Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the
**57**      Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao **A RESOLUTION**
**58**      **REQUESTING THE GOVERNMENT SERVICE INSURANCE SYSTEM (GSIS)**
**59**      **TO ESTABLISH BRANCH OFFICES IN DIFFERENT AREAS IN THE**
**60**      **BARMM.**
