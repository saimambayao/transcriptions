**A RESOLUTION RECOMMENDING THE INCREASE OF CALAMITY ASSISTANCE FUND UNDER THE OFFICE OF THE CHIEF MINISTER AND RELEVANT BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT MINISTRIES OR OFFICES FOR THE RECONSTRUCTION AND REHABILITATION OF AFFECTED COMMUNITIES OF BARMM AND BUILDING HOMES FOR THE VICTIMS OF TROPICAL STORM "PAENG"**

WHEREAS, from 28 October 2022 and subsequent days thereafter, Tropical Strong Paeng brought unforeseen loss of lives, widespread damage, and destruction of properties in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) and many other parts of the country;

WHEREAS, a significant number of Bangsamoro and indigenous cultural communities, including the vulnerable such as children, senior citizens, and pregnant women in the provinces of BARMM, were tragically affected, with loss of lives, displacement, total destruction of homes and properties, as well as loss of livelihood, agri-fishery products, and other sources of income in several areas in the region;

WHEREAS, there is a provided Calamity Fund in the *Bangsamoro Appropriations Act 2022* intended for the relief, rehabilitation, reconstruction, and other works or services in connection with natural calamities, epidemics as declared by the Department of Health (DOH) / Ministry of Health – BARMM (MOH – BARMM), and other man-made crises which may occur during the budget year or those that occurred in prior years, including pre-disaster activities such as preparation of relocation facilities identified and training of personnel engaged in direct disaster management;

WHEREAS, based on data gathered from the Ministry of the Interior and Local Government BARMM-READi as of November 2, 2022, at 14:00 hours, the extent of damages in various local government units by severe Tropical Storm Paeng is as follows:

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Government has the moral and legal obligation to provide humanitarian assistance and to support its constituent-families who lost a member of the household, left their homes, and are now deprived of sources of income and who must start anew in the hopes of recovery;

WHEREAS, a massive rehabilitation and reconstruction of affected areas by Tropical Storm “Paeng” must be carefully planned and thoroughly undertaken.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, by the BTA Parliament, as it is hereby resolved, to recommend the **INCREASE OF CALAMITY ASSISTANCE FUND UNDER THE OFFICE OF THE CHIEF MINISTER AND RELEVANT BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT MINISTRIES AND OFFICES FOR THE RECONSTRUCTION AND REHABILITATION OF AFFECTED COMMUNITIES OF BARMM AND BUILDING HOMES FOR THE VICTIMS OF TROPICAL STORM "PAENG"**.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, as it is hereby resolved, to include such recommended increase in the computation of the Calamity Fund in the *Bangsamoro Appropriations Act 2023*.
