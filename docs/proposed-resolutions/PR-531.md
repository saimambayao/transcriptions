**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MS. ROSLAINE BATAO MAULANA FOR PLACING THIRD IN THE PHILIPPINE ACADEMY OF MICROBIOLOGY (PAM) CERTIFICATION EXAMINATION FOR REGISTERED MICROBIOLOGISTS HELD ON JANUARY 20 - 21, 2024 AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS**

**WHEREAS**, the Philippine Academy of Microbiology (PAM) announced the results of the Certification Examination for Registered Microbiologists on February 23, 2024;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Roslaine B. Maulana, an Iranun and Maguindanaon, obtained the third spot among the 1,203 passers with a general average of 84.45% in the said certification examination;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Maulana is a graduate of University of Southern Mindanao, Kabacan Municipality, Cotabato Province in June 2023, who is a recipient of Latin honor, Cum Laude, and consistent honor student and awardee from primary to college;

**WHEREAS**, she is the daughter of Mr. Khadafy S. Maulana, a government employee, and Mrs. Salamona B. Maulana, a public teacher in Secondary level, residing in Tapayan, Sultan Mastura, Maguindanao del Norte, BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) is cognizant of the feat of Ms. Roslaine B. Maulana and all those Bangsamoro passers of the 2024 PAM Certification Examination for Registered Microbiologists;

**NOW THEREFORE**, be it **RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, by the BTA, to congratulate and commend Ms. Roslaine Batao Maulana for placing third in the PAM Certification Examination for Registered Microbiologists held last January 20-21, 2024 at the SMX Convention Center Aura, Taguig, Metro Manila.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To forward the copy of this Resolution to Ms. Roslaine Batao Maulana, for information.
