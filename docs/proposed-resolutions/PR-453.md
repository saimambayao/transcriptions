**RESOLUTION**
**CONGRATULATING AND COMENDING BAI REHANA M. LAUBAN FOR PLACING NINTH IN THE OCTOBER 2023 FISHERIES TECHNOLOGY LICENSURE EXAMINATION (FTLE) AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS**

WHEREAS, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced that 716 out of 2,124 passed the Licensure Examination for Fisheries Professionals given by the Board of Fisheries in different cities of the country;

WHEREAS, Bai Rehana M. Lauban placed ninth out of the 716 passers with a general average of 84.50%;

WHEREAS, Ms. Lauban graduated Cum Laude at the Mindanao State University (MSU-Maguindanao) and received various accolades during her junior and senior high school and college education;

WHEREAS, Ms. Lauban parents are Hadji Bader T. Taug and Laguiabai L. Lauban who are both Maguindanaon and residents of Dalican, Maguindanao del Norte;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, to congratulate and commend Ms. Bai Rehana M. Lauban for placing 9th in the October 2023 Fisheries Technology Licensure Examination (FTLE) and all Bangsamoro passers.

**ADOPTED.**
