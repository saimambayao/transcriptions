**Proposed Resolution No. 149**

Introduced by BTA Member Khalid Ma-amor Hadji Abdullah

**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING COMMISSIONER ATTY. FAYDAH MANIRI DUMARPA FOR HER APPOINTMENT AS MEMBER OF THE SIXTH COMMISSION EN BANC OF THE COMMISSION ON HUMAN RIGHTS DATED 28 DECEMBER 2022**

**WHEREAS**, under the 1987 Philippine Constitution, the Commission on Human Rights is created as an independent National Human Rights Institution (NHRI), an independent office composed of a Chairman and four Members who must be natural-born citizens of the Philippines and a majority of whom shall be members of the Bar, mandated to conduct investigations on human rights violations against marginalized and vulnerable sectors of the society, involving civil and political rights;

**WHEREAS**, on the 28th day of December 2022, the Malacañan Palace, Manila, released an appointment paper, signed by President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr., addressed to Atty. Faydah Maniri Dumarpa, thru the Chairman of the Commission on Human Rights, hereby appointing the former as the Third Member of the sixth Commission *en banc* (CEB), Commission on Human Rights;

**WHEREAS**, after the appointment paper was formally transmitted to the Commission on Human Rights on 05 January 2023, Commissioner Dumarpa, along with the rest of the sixth CEB, will serve a full term of seven years from 2022 to 2029;

**WHEREAS**, Commissioner Dumarpa did not only achieve such an honorable public office, but she is also the first ever Bangsamoro woman who was appointed as member Commissioner of the Commission on Human Rights, and the second Bangsamoro appointee to the same position, next to Atty. Nasser A. Marohomsalic, who was the first Bangsamoro appointee, who served as a member of the second CEB of the Commission on Human Rights from 1994 to 2001;

**WHEREAS**, as a native of Lanao del Sur, and a proud member of the Bangsamoro Community, Commissioner Dumarpa not only offers her expansive experience in the legal, socio-political, and administrative and finance fields, her Southern and Muslim roots will also reflect plurality crucial to the Commission’s highest decision and policy-making body;

**WHEREAS**, for the pride and honor brought by Commissioner Dumarpa, and also for once again proving nationwide the great capability of a Bangsamoro woman, with the trust and confidence accorded her by President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr., and for inspiring not only the competent, strong, and dedicated women of the Bangsamoro, but also the Bangsamoro people in its entirety, warrants her commendation by the Bangsamoro Parliament;

**NOW THEREFORE**, be it resolved as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to congratulate and commend Commissioner Atty. Faydah Maniri Dumarpa for her appointment as Member of the sixth Commission *en banc* of the Commission on Human Rights dated 28 December 2022.

SUBMITTED, Jumada II/20, 1444 AH/January 13, 2023.

**KHALID M. HADJI ABDULLAH**
