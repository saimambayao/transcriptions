**RESOLUTION URGING THE BARMM MINDANAO RIVER BASIN TECHNICAL WORKING GROUP (BARMM MRB-TWG) TO CONVENE IMMEDIATELY, PROVIDE COMPREHENSIVE UPDATES TO THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA)**

**WHEREAS**, the Mindanao River Basin (MRB), spanning nine provinces across Mindanao, is a vital natural resource, home to a complex network of river systems, including the Mindanao River System, Ambal-Simuay River System, and the Allah River System, playing an indispensable role in the well-being and livelihoods of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) population;

**WHEREAS**, the MRB faces escalating challenges such as recurrent flooding, soil erosion, and property damage, primarily affecting the lives and livelihoods of those residing in Maguindanao, necessitating immediate attention;

**WHEREAS**, there is critical need to establish a well-informed and collaborative BARMM Mindanao River Basin Technical Working Group (BARMM MRB-TWG), composed of various ministries including the Ministry of Environment, Natural Resources and Energy (MENRE), Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries, and Agrarian Reform (MAFAR), Ministry of Health (MOH), Ministry of Trade, Investment and Tourism (MTIT), Ministry of Transportation and Communication (MITT), Ministry of Public Works (MPW), Ministry of Social Services and Development (MSSD), Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST), Ministry of Indigenous Peoples' Affairs (MIPA), Ministry of Local Government (MILG), BARMM Rapid Emergency Action on Disaster Incidence (BARMM-READY), Office of Civil Defense (OCD), Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority (BPDA), Maguindanao Flood Control Management (MFBM), alongside local government units of Maguindanao del Sur and Maguindanao del Norte, to collectively address pressing concerns related to continual flooding, soil erosion, and the general state of the MRB;

**WHEREAS**, swift convening and cooperative dialogues by the BARMM MRB TWG are essential to comprehensively address these challenges;

**WHEREAS**, it is imperative for the BARMM MRB-TWG to promptly provide the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) with a complete member list and a comprehensive report on the MRB's current state and the progress made in the Mindanao River Basis Master Plan;

**WHEREAS**, BARMM MRB-TWG should actively engage affected Local Government Units (LGUs) to raise public awareness and foster community participation in MRB-related matters;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority:

**1. The Bangsamoro Transition Authority underscores the crucial role of the BARMM MRB-TWG in addressing the multifaceted challenges of the Mindanao River Basin.**

**2. The Bangsamoro Transition Authority urgently calls upon the BARMM MRB-TWG to convene immediately to initiate collaborative discussions and coordinated action to address recurring flooding, soil erosion, and to comprehensively review the status of the MRB.**

**3. The BARMM MRB-TWG is further tasked with providing the Bangsamoro Transition Authority with a comprehensive roster of its members and promptly submitting to the Parliament a detailed report outlining the current state of the Mindanao River Basin and the notable progress achieved in the Mindanao River Basin Master Plan.**

**4. The BARMM MRB-TWG is directed to actively encourage all concerned and affected Local Government Units to intensify efforts to raise public awareness and promote community participation in matters concerning the Mindanao River Basin.**

**5. The BARMM MRB-TWG is hereby directed to furnish the Bangsamoro Parliament with precise, up-to-date information to facilitate the development of robust legislation addressing the challenges presented by recurring flooding, soil erosion, and property damage within the Mindanao River Basin.**

**6. The BARMM MRB-TWG is directed to ensure that the Bangsamoro Transition Authority receives accurate, current data to guide their formulation of effective laws and policies pertaining to the protection, preservation, and sustainable development of the Mindanao River Basin.**
