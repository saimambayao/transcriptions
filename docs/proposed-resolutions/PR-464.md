**REITERATING THE CALL FOR AN UNCONDITIONAL CEASEFIRE IN GAZA, CALLING FOR THE CESSATION OF VIOLENCE AND ABUSES COMMITTED IN THE WEST BANK, AND URGING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT TO SEND AID AND ASSISTANCE TO THE PEOPLE OF GAZA**

WHEREAS, more than forty (40) days have passed since the Government of Israel declared war against Hamas and started its military operations in Gaza;

WHEREAS, to date, more than 11,000 civilians have been killed of which more than 40% were children,¹ and thousands more injured, with the numbers increasing by the hour;

WHEREAS, more than 41,000 housing units in Gaza have been destroyed and 222,000 more damaged amounting to at least 45% of the housing units in the Gaza Strip;²

WHEREAS, according to the United Nations Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs (UN OCHA), 279 educational facilities have been damaged as of 15 November 2023, and that none of the 625,000 Gazan students have been able to access education;³

WHEREAS, until now, the people in Gaza are still deprived of access to sufficient or even tolerable levels of food, water, electricity, fuel, and essential medical supplies;

WHEREAS, acts of violence and abuses against the Palestinians have likewise been reported in the West Bank;

WHEREAS, the suffering of the Palestinians cannot and should not be ignored and left unabated;

WHEREAS, it is incumbent upon the rest of humanity to send any and all forms of assistance to the Palestinians – from being their voice in various platforms to providing aid to ease their suffering;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority – Parliament, to reiterate its urgent call for the unconditional ceasefire in Gaza and all the occupied territories of Palestine;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to urge the Bangsamoro Government to send aid and assistance to the people in Gaza, and to seek and adopt every appropriate means available toward this end;

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, to furnish the Office of the Chief Minister, the Department of Foreign Affairs, and the Embassy of Palestine a copy of this Resolution.
