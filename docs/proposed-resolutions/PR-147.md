**RESOLUTION APPROVING THE 2ND BANGSAMORO DEVELOPMENT PLAN (BDP) 2023-2028 OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

**WHEREAS**, Section 4, Article XIII of Republic Act (RA) No. 11054 or Act Providing for the Organic Law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao provides that the “[p]arliament shall establish the Bangsamoro Economic Development Council which shall serve as the planning, monitoring, and coordinating agency for all developments plans, programs, and projects of the Bangsamoro Government;”

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament enacted on 27 December 2019 Bangsamoro Autonomy Act (BAA) No. 6 establishing the Bangsamoro Economic Development Council (BEDC) and creating the Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority (BPDA) as its technical secretariat;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Development Plan (BDP) is the medium-term comprehensive development plan of the Bangsamoro Government that lays down the plans, programs, and projects for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region including the manner of utilization of the Special Development Plan (SDF) provided by the National Government for the rebuilding, rehabilitation, and development of conflict-affected communities in the region;

**WHEREAS**, the BEDC adopted the 1st Bangsamoro Development Plan (BDP) 2020-2022 on 05 June 2020 and the same was approved by the BTA Parliament on 03 July 2020;
**WHEREAS**, Chief Minister Ahod B. Ebrahim directed the formulation of the 2nd BDP 2023-2028 and its accompanying Results Matrix and the Bangsamoro Development Investment Program (BDIP) to serve as basis for multi-year planning, programming and budgeting processes in the region;

**WHEREAS**, the BEDC had convened for its 3rd regular meeting on 23 December 2022 to deliberate on the draft 2nd BDP 2023-2028;

**WHEREAS**, the BEDC has resolved to adopt the 2nd BDP 2023-2028 and endorse the same to the BTA Parliament for its approval;

**WHEREAS**, on 11 January 2023, the BPDA disseminated electronic and printed copies of the 2nd BDP 2023-2028 to the Members of the BTA Parliament, for advance reference and comments;

**WHEREAS**, the 2nd BDP 2023-2028 is the medium-term comprehensive development plan of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) that builds on the gains of the 1st BDP 2020-2022 and lays down the Bangsamoro Government’s 12-Point Priority Agenda. It is consistent with the 8-Point Socioeconomic Agenda of President Ferdinand “Bongbong” Marcos Jr. and the Philippine Development Plan 2023-2028;

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved,** to approve the 2nd Bangsamoro Development Plan 2023-2028 of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM).
