**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING FORMER GOVERNOR SUHARTO “TENG” T. MANGUDADATU FOR HIS APPOINTMENT AS DIRECTOR GENERAL OF THE TECHNICAL EDUCATION AND SKILLS DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY (TESDA)**

**WHEREAS,** former Governor Suharto “Teng” T. Mangudadatu has been appointed as the Director General of the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA);

**WHEREAS,** Governor Mangudadatu has shown remarkable leadership skills during his tenure as Governor of Sultan Kudarat, fostering a peaceful and inclusive community in the province;

**WHEREAS,** Governor Mangudadatu has also worked tirelessly for the development of education, particularly technical and vocational education, in his home province by initiating programs and projects that aim to equip students with practical skills necessary for the workforce;

**WHEREAS,** Governor Mangudadatu’s appointment as the Director General of TESDA is a testament to his commitment and dedication towards education, and his ability to lead and guide the agency towards achieving its goals;

**WHEREAS,** former congressman Suharto Teng T. Mangudadatu has proven his political capabilities by serving as a member of the House of Representatives of the Philippines from 2016 to 2019;

**WHEREAS,** Mangudadatu has demonstrated his leadership skills and dedication to public service by being an active participant in various committee hearings and discussions regarding significant issues in the country;

**WHEREAS,** Mangudadatu has continuously shown his compassion and empathy towards the plight of the underprivileged and marginalized sectors of society, as seen in his support for the Bangsamoro Organic Law and his creation of livelihood programs for farmers and indigenous peoples;

**WHEREAS,** Mangudadatu has also proved his administrative proficiency by leading the province of Sultan Kudarat as its Governor, where he implemented various social welfare programs, established infrastructure developments, and strengthened law enforcement measures;

**WHEREAS,** Mangudadatu’s commendable political experience as former congressman and current Governor inspires Filipinos to aspire for public service and to emulate his values of integrity, competency, and passion;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** That the members of this Bangsamoro Parliament extends its warmest congratulations to former Governor Suharto “Teng” T. Mangudadatu for his appointment as the Director General of TESDA;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED,** That we acknowledge and commend Governor Mangudadatu for his outstanding leadership, and his tireless efforts towards the development of technical and vocational education in the country.

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED,** That we express our support for Governor Mangudadatu and his endeavors to promote and improve technical education and skills development for the benefit of the Filipino youth, and the nation as a whole.

**BE IT FINALLY RESOLVED,** That a copy of this resolution be furnished to Governor Mangudadatu and the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority as a manifestation of our solidarity and support for their advocacies and programs.
