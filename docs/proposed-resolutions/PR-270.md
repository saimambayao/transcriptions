**A RESOLUTION URGING THE MINISTRY OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATION TO INSTALL AND DISPLAY ISLAMIC MOTIVATIONAL MESSAGES AND SIGNAGES ON BILLBOARDS AND OTHER ADVERTISING SIGNS IN ALL AIRPORTS AND SEAPORTS IN THE BARMM**

**WHEREAS**, the Five Pillars – Declaration of Faith (Shahada), Prayer (Salah), Alms-giving (Zakat), Fasting (Sawm) and Pilgrimage (Hajj) – constitute the basic norms of Islamic practice;

**WHEREAS**, there is a need to strengthen further Islamic faith in the Bangsamoro people of the Autonomous Region of Muslim Mindanao in order to adhere faithfully and conscientiously to the Five Pillars;

**WHEREAS**, reliable studies show that public messaging in public spaces have proven to be of influential persuasiveness and has an exponential effect in convincing and ultimately obtaining, compliance from the public, as concluded in a study conducted by the Melbourne School of Psychological Sciences, University of Melbourne, Victoria, Australia, (published 30 August 2017) that “signs, prompts and symbols are a common means to change behavior in society”;

**WHEREAS**, public signages in airports and seaports depicting Islamic motivational statements and inspirational messages prove to be of influential persuasiveness in further strengthening the Islamic faith and compelling obedience to the Five Pillars;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the BTA Parliament to pass and approve this Resolution **URGING THE MINISTRY OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATION TO INSTALL AND DISPLAY ISLAMIC MOTIVATIONAL MESSAGES AND SIGNAGES ON BILLBOARDS AND OTHER ADVERTISING SIGNS IN ALL AIRPORTS AND SEAPORTS IN THE Bangsamoro Autonomous Region of Musliprompt and symbols m Mindanao (BARMM)**.
