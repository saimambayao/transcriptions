**“A RESOLUTION COMMENDING ABDUL BATERBONIA LUMANGGAL, A BANGSAMORO GRADUATE OF UNIVERSITY OF SOUTHERN MINDANAO, FOR OBTAINING 10TH PLACE WITH AN IMPRESSIVE RATING OF 92.60% AMONGST THE 231,002 EXAMINEES IN THE OCTOBER 2022 LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR TEACHERS”**

WHEREAS, on the 16th day of October 2022, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced the results of the October 2022 Licensure Examination for Teachers;

WHEREAS, after taking the examination on October 2, 2022, Mr. Abdul Baterbonia Lumanggal, a graduate of the University of Southern Mindanao, earned a stellar score of 92.60%, placing him in the top 10 among 231,002 examinees, out of whom 120,863 passed;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Parliament pays tribute to all Bangsamoro examinees who took the said examination and made it;

WHEREAS, Mr. Lumanggal completed his degree of Bachelors of Science in Elementary Education in USM and honored as Magna Cum Laude of Class 2020;

WHEREAS, the outstanding performance of Mr. Lumanggal elevated the Bangsamoro's educational profile among our younger generations inspiring them as model of scholastic achievement;

WHEREAS, Mr. Lumanggal together with other LET Bangsamoro passers, deserve recognition as a source of pride for the Bangsamoro people;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to commend Abdul Baterbonia Lumanggal, a Bangsamoro graduate of University of Southern Mindanao for obtaining the 10th place with an impressive rating of 92.60% amongst the 231,002 examinees in the October 2022 Licensure Examination for Teachers.

**FURTHER RESOLVED,** to forward the foregoing Resolution to Abdul B. Lumanggal and University for Southern Mindanao, for information.
