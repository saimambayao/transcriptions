**A RESOLUTION**
**CALLING THE OFFICE OF THE CHIEF MINISTER (OCM) TO DESIGNATE A BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT CENTER (BGC) ADMINISTRATOR TO REGULATE DEVELOPMENT AND IMPROVEMENT OF STRUCTURES AND TO ENSURE COORDINATED MANAGEMENT OF GOVERNMENT ASSETS IN THE BGC**

**WHEREAS,** Section 10, Article XVI of the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) states that the powers, functions, assets, capital, records, funds, receivables, equipment, and facilities of the Autonomous Regional Government in Muslim Mindanao at the time of the ratification of this Organic Law shall be transferred to the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS,** Section 9, Book I of the Bangsamoro Administrative Code states that the seat of the Bangsamoro Government shall be in Cotabato City, unless otherwise provided by the Bangsamoro Parliament;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Government Center located in Cotabato City, has several government buildings, a museum, a library, training facilities, among others;

**WHEREAS,** the existing BGC houses iconic buildings such as the Office of the Chief Minister and the Shariff Kabungsuan Cultural Complex that need to be preserved and carefully maintained;

**WHEREAS,** the current organizational structure of the OCM has not provided for an Administrator for the BGC;

**WHEREAS,** several construction and repair work on government buildings are continuously being done by government agencies in the BGC, according to their respective plans;

**WHEREAS,** there is an observed congestion of government structures and facilities in the BGC that pose danger to the government assets and properties;

**WHEREAS,** there are no regulations in the construction and repair of government buildings and facilities in the BGC;

**WHEREAS,** several ministries and offices have utilized the roads surrounding the BGC as vehicle parking areas, which has contributed to the congestion of the roads in the government center;

**WHEREAS,** there is a need to assign an Administrator of the BGC in order to manage the development and maintenance of the government structures, including the proper utilization of the roads inside the BGC;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the BTA Parliament, to pass and approve this Resolution calling on the OCM to designate a Bangsamoro Government Center Administrator to regulate the development and improvement of structures and to ensure coordinated management of government assets in the BGC.

**BE IT RESOLVED FURTHER,** that a copy of this resolution be furnished to the Office of the Chief Minister.
