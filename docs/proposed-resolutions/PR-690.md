**RESOLUTION**
**CONDEMNING IN THE STRONGEST TERMS THE KILLING OF COMELEC MUNICIPAL ELECTION OFFICER OF DATU ODIN SINSUAT MUNICIPALITY BAI MACEDA LIDASAN-ABO AND HER HUSBAND JOJO OLEA ABO AND URGING THE CONCERNED LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES TO THOROUGHLY INVESTIGATE THE INCIDENT, IMMEDIATELY APPREHEND ALL PERPETRATORS, AND GIVE JUSTICE TO THE VICTIMS**

**WHEREAS,** Section 4, Article II of the 1987 Constitution of the Philippines states in part that “*The prime duty of the Government is to serve and protect the people*”;

**WHEREAS,** Section 1, Article III of the Bill of Rights states that “*No person shall be deprived of life, liberty, or property without due process of law, nor shall any person be denied the equal protection of laws*”;

**WHEREAS,** it is declared policy of the Bangsamoro Government that it values the dignity of every human person and guarantees full respect for human rights, and it shall give the highest policy to the enactment of measures to protect and enhance the rights of all people to human dignity at all stages of human life;

**WHEREAS,** on March 26, 2025, COMELEC Municipal Election Officer Bai Maceda Lidasan-Abo and her husband Jojo Olea Abo while on board to report for her official duty in Datu Odin Sinsuat (DOS), they were ambushed along Barangay Makir, DOS, Maguindanao del Norte;

**WHEREAS,** according to the statement of Provincial Director of Maguindanao del Norte PPO the victims were ambushed by riding in tandem, open-fired in broad daylight, shattering their vehicle’s window in public and leaving the victims vulnerable resulted to the death of the victims;

**WHEREAS,** Bai Maceda Lidasan-Abo has served the COMELEC with dedication and commitment as Municipal Election Officer for the period of twenty-eight (28) years and making a significant contributions to the orderly, fair and credible conduct of elections in the Bangsamoro Region;

**WHEREAS,** Surah Al-Israh 33 states: “*Do not take a ‘human’ life—made sacred by Allah—except with ‘legal’ right. If anyone is killed unjustly, We have given their heirs the authority, but do not let them exceed limits in retaliation, for they are already supported ‘by law’.*”

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority; to condemn in the strongest terms the killing of Comelec Municipal Election Officer of Datu Odin Sinsuat Municipality Bai Maceda Lidasan-Abo and husband Jojo Olea Abo;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to call upon the Philippine National Police, the National Bureau of Investigation, Local Government Unit of DOS, and other relevant authorities to conduct a thorough and impartial investigation into the incident, and take all necessary measures to ensure the safety of government workers and civilians within the Bangsamoro region;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** to furnish a copy of this resolution to appropriate authorities, including the Philippine National Police (PNP), the Commission on Elections (COMELEC), the Ministry of Public Order and Safety (MPOS), Local Chief Executive of DOS and other relevant government agencies for their information and necessary action.

**ADOPTED.**
