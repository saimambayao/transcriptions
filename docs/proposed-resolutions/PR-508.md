**A RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE MINISTRY OF INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT (MILG) TO REQUIRE ALL LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS (LGU) UNDER BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM) TO PASS AN ORDINANCE MANDATING THE INSTALLATION OF VIDEO SURVEILLANCE CAMERA OR CLOSED CIRCUIT TELEVISION (CCTV) SYSTEM IN ALL PUBLIC PLACES AND IN THE PREMISES OF BUSINESS ESTABLISHMENTS WITHIN THEIR TERRITORIAL JURISDICTION INCLUDING STREETS, ROADS, BUILDINGS, COMMERCIAL COMPLEXES, LEARNING INSTITUTIONS AND ALONG MAJOR THOROUGHFARES AND INTERSECTIONS TO INTENSIFY PEACE AND ORDER AND PUBLIC SAFETY**

WHEREAS, Article V, Section 3 of R.A. No. 11054 otherwise known as the “Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” (*OLBARMM for brevity*) states;

**Section 3. General Welfare.** *The Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the power expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective governance and those which are essential to the **promotion of general welfare.**^1 Within its territorial jurisdiction, the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure and support, among other things, the preservation and enrichment of culture, **promote health and safety,**^2 enhance the right of the people to a balanced ecology, encourage and support the development of appropriate and self-reliant scientific and technological capabilities, improve public morals, enhance economic prosperity and social justice, promote full employment among its residents, **maintain peace and order,**^3 and preserve the comfort and convenience of its inhabitants.*

WHEREAS, Title VIII, Chapter 1, Section 2 of the Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13 or otherwise known as the “Bangsamoro Administrative Code” provides;

**Sec. 2. Mandate** - *The Ministry of the Interior and Local Government shall exercise general supervision over the constituent local governments units of the Bangsamoro Government, and **ensure public safety**^4 and disaster preparedness, local autonomy, decentralization, and community empowerment.*

WHEREAS, in order to promote public safety and intensify peace and order, it is deemed proper to require the use of Video Surveillance Camera or CCTV system in all public places including premises of business establishments in BARMM.

WHEREAS, it is a proven and accepted fact that the installation and operation of Video Surveillance Camera or CCTV system, as a growing advanced surveillance and security technology worldwide, provide government offices, business establishments, learning institutions and homes, among others, in deterring disorder and violent crimes of potential offenders; combating crimes and providing a safer environment to business owners, employees, workers, students and the public. Thereby, eventually producing economic progress to our Bangsamoro people.

**NOW THEREFORE, be it;**

**RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to request the Ministry of Interior and Local Government (MILG) to require all local government units (LGU) under Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) to pass an ordinance mandating the installation of video surveillance camera or closed circuit television (CCTV) system in all public places and in the premises of business establishments within their territorial jurisdiction including streets, roads, buildings, commercial complexes, learning institutions and along major thoroughfares and intersections to intensify peace and order and public safety.
