Republic of the Philippines

**BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY**
Cotabato City

Second Regular Session

**PROPOSED RESOLUTION NO. 589**

Authored by:
MP Suharto S. Esmael, MSc

**A RESOLUTION ENCOURAGING BARMM MINISTRIES AND OFFICES TO PROVIDE ANNUAL MENTAL, MEDICAL-PHYSICAL EXAMINATIONS TO ALL GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION**

**WHEREAS**, Section 15, Article II of the Philippine Constitution provides that:

> ***Section 15.** The State shall protect and promote the right to health of the people and instill health consciousness among them.*

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro government is also mandated to protect and promote the right to health of the people and instill consciousness among them in the Bangsamoro Organic Law;

**WHEREAS**, in pursuance to Civil Service Commission Memorandum Circular No. 33 s. 1997 which recognizes the need to institutionalize viable programs to improve working conditions in the government;

**WHEREAS**, protection of the health of public servants and/or employees is fundamental to public service as the same would be effectively and efficiently done if the public servants and/or employees is at the peak of their health;

**WHEREAS**, ensuring the right to health of employees is not only a constitutional obligation but also a moral imperative, as it reflects a commitment to the dignity and welfare of the public servants and/or employees;

**WHEREAS**, with this initiative of protecting the health of our public servants and/or employees we could expect an optimal public service to our Bangsamoro people;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to encourage BARMM ministries and offices to provide annual general medical checkups to all government employees in the region.

Adopted,

**SUHARTO S. ESMAEL, MSc.**
Member of the Parliament
