**RESOLUTION**
**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING CARLOS EDRIEL POQUIZ YULO FOR WINNING TWO (2) GOLD MEDALS IN THE 2024 PARIS OLYMPICS**

**WHEREAS**, Section 19 (1), Article XIV of the 1987 Constitution states that the State shall promote physical education and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and amateur sports, including training for international competitions, to foster self-discipline, teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;

**WHEREAS**, Section 13, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054, or the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL), provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall recognize the vital role of the youth in nation-building, promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, and nationalism, encourage involvement in public and civic affairs, and promote mental and physical fitness through sports;

**WHEREAS**, Carlos Yulo, a 24-year old gymnast from Malate, Manila represented the Philippines on the Olympic Stage at the Bercy Arena in Paris, France for the 2024 Paris Summer Olympics;

**WHEREAS**, he concluded the 2024 Paris Olympics with one (1) gold medal for the men’s artistic gymnastic floor exercise and one (1) gold medal for men’s artistic gymnastics vault competition on August 3-4, 2024;

**WHEREAS**, on his first gold, he earned an impressive score of 15.000. He delivered a flawless routine that showcased his signature combination of high-flying tumbles and intricate spins. The routine’s difficulty level, combined with his exceptional performance, secured him the top spot on the podium¹;

**WHEREAS**, on his second gold, Yulo averaged 15.166 points as he collected 15.433 in his first vault and 14.800 in his second thereby claiming the top spot;

**WHEREAS**, Carlos Yulo made history as the Philippines’ first double Olympic gold medalist;

**WHEREAS**, he doubled not just his historic feat but the Filipinos’ pride and joy;

**WHEREAS**, aside from the Gold 2024 Paris Olympics (vault) and 2024 Paris Olympics (floor), some of his major achievements are the following²:

a) 2019 World Championships (floor); 2021 World Championships (vault); 2022 Asian Championships (floor, vault, parallel bars); 2023 Asian Championships (floor, still rings, all-around, vault, horizontal bars); 2024 Asian Championships (all-around, floor, vault, parallel bars)' 2019 SEA Games (all-around, floor)' 2021 SEA Games (floor, still rings, all-around, vault, horizontal bars); 2023 SEA Games (all-around, parallel bars); 2024 FIG World Cup - Doha leg (parallel bars); 2023 FIG World Cup - Baku leg (vault, parallel bars); 2023 FIG World Cup - Doha leg (floor);

b) Silver: 2021 World Championships (parallel bars); 2022 World Championships (vault); 2022 Asian Championships (all-around); 2023 Asian Championships (all-around); 2019 SEA Games (pommel horse, still rings, vault, parallel bars, horizontal bars); 2021 SEA Games (team, parallel bars); 2023 SEA Games (team, still rings); 2024 FIG World Cup - Doha leg (vault); 2023 FIG World Cup - Doha leg (parallel bars);

c) Bronze: 2018 World Championships (floor); 2022 World Championships (parallel bars); 2023 Asian Championships (horizontal bar); 2024 FIG World Cup - Doha leg (floor); 2023 FIG World Cup - Doha leg (vault); 2023 FIG World Cup - Cottbus leg (parallel bars);

**WHEREAS**, his outstanding achievements brought great pride to the whole Nation, including the Bangsamoro region, as he showed great perseverance, dedication and courage that is worthy of emulation by the entire Philippine populace;

**WHEREAS**, this hard-earned victory is a testament to the world-class talent of the Filipino people and serves as an inspiration to all the Filipino athletes, most especially the Bangsamoro youth to strive harder in order to excel in their chosen endeavor, and to aim for greater heights of excellence;

**WHEREAS**, in addition, his victory gives the youth the power to believe in themselves, recognize their capabilities, and to remind them that no dream is too big and no goal is impossible to achieve;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), as the interim government of the Bangsamoro Region, has often recognized outstanding Filipino athletes for their astounding accomplishments of bringing immense pride and glory to the Country by winning international sporting competitions; and

**WHEREAS**, his remarkable performance and exceptional contribution that brought pride, honor and glory to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region deserves the highest commendation from the BTA;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the BTA Parliament, to pass and approve this Resolution Expressing
