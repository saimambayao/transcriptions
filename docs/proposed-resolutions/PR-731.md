**A RESOLUTION URGING ALL MINISTRIES, OFFICES, AND AGENCIES UNDER THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT TO SET-UP GUIDELINES AND STANDARD DATE OF DOWNLOADING EMPLOYEES' MONTHLY SALARY AND OTHER EMOLUMENTS**

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article XII of the Republic Act No, 11054 provides:

> “**Section 1. *Fiscal Autonomy.*** - X x x. The Bangsamoro Government is entitled to all funds sources enumerated herein, and shall have the power to create its sources of revenues as provided in this Organic Law, which shall be spent in a programmatic, transparent, performance-based, and phased manner. X x x”

**WHEREAS**, the timely downloading of salaries and other monetary benefits is essential in ensuring the financial stability, productivity, and morale of public servants in the region;

**WHEREAS**, in accordance with the tenets of Islam and in line with the principles of moral governance and Shari'ah that guide the Bangsamoro Government, the Hadith stating, “*Give the worker his wages before his sweat dries*,” underscores the ethical obligation to uphold fairness and justice in labor, thereby affirming the necessity of promptly and regularly releasing employees’ salaries and other emoluments through the establishment of clear guidelines and a standardized disbursement schedule;

**WHEREAS**, there is currently no uniform schedule or clear guidelines adopted across all ministries, offices, and agencies under the Bangsamoro Government regarding the downloading of employees’ salaries and other emoluments, leading to discrepancies and delays in disbursement;

**WHEREAS**, the establishment of standardized guidelines and a unified schedule for salary and emolument downloading is consistent with the principles of good governance enshrined in the Bangsamoro Organic Law and will enhance coordination among government financial units;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, to urge all ministries, offices, and agencies under the Bangsamoro Government to formulate and adopt their respective guidelines and adhere to a standard schedule for the downloading and disbursement of monthly salaries and other emoluments to their employees;

**RESOLVED FINALLY**, that the Ministry of Finance, Budget and Management (MFBM) shall take the lead in crafting the standardized policies and timelines to be implemented region-wide.
