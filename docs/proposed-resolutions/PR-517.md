**RESOLUTION STRONGLY CONDEMNING THE SHOOTING INCIDENT TARGETING DR. SHARMAINE CEBALLOS BAROQUILLO, AND URGING ALL CONCERNED AGENCIES TO CONDUCT INVESTIGATION INTO THE INCIDENT AND ENSURE THE SAFETY AND SECURITY OF HEALTH WORKERS IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION**

**WHEREAS**, on the night of February 3, 2024, Dr. Sharmaine Ceballos Baroquillo was driving in her dark grey Suzuki Swift along the national highway in Barangay Digal, Buluan, Maguindanao del Sur when she was attacked by unidentified gunmen;

**WHEREAS**, according to the report of Buluan Municipal Police Station, the gunmen attempted first to stop Dr. Baroquillo by firing warning shots but she displayed remarkable courage and continued driving to evade the assailants. However, they eventually caught up to her and shot her three times;

**WHEREAS**, despite sustaining three gunshot wounds, Dr. Baroquillo managed to continue driving but collided with a tricycle loaded with three passengers before coming to a stop on the side of the highway;

**WHEREAS**, Dr. Baroquillo and the three tricycle passengers were rescued and brought by emergency responders of Buluan Municipal Police Station to a hospital for treatment;

**WHEREAS**, the 28-year old Dr. Baroquillo who works at the Sultan Kudarat Provincial Hospital has been transferred to Southern Philippines Medical Center for treatment while the three injured passengers are now in stable condition at Tomboc-Salayog Hospital in Tacurong City;

**WHEREAS**, the alarming incidents of violence against healthcare professionals have posed a significant threat to their safety and have the potential to undermine the quality of healthcare services in the region; and

**WHEREAS**, it is imperative to call upon all responsible agencies of the Bangsamoro Government to investigate said shooting incident, prosecute the perpetrators to the fullest extent of the law, and implement measures to prevent any and all future acts of violence against health workers.

**NOW THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to strongly condemn the shooting incident targeting Dr. Sharmaine Ceballos Baroquillo, and urging all concerned agencies to conduct investigation into the incident and ensure the safety and security of health workers in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, to furnish a copy of this resolution to the Ministry of Interior and Local Government (MILG), Police Regional Office - Bangsamoro Autonomous Region (PRO-BAR), National Bureau of Investigation - Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (NBI-BARMM), Ministry of Public Order and Safety (MPOS), and the Bangsamoro Human Rights Commission (BHRC) for their information and appropriate action.
