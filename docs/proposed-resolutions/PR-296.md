**RESOLUTION**

**EXPRESSING UTMOST GRATITUDE TO HIS EXCELLENCY PRESIDENT FERDINAND "BONGBONG" MARCOS, JR. FOR APPOINTING SECRETARY SUHARTO T. MANGUDADATU AS THE 14TH DIRECTOR GENERAL OF THE TECHNICAL EDUCATION AND SKILLS DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY (TESDA)**

**WHEREAS**, the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) plays a crucial role in advancing technical education and skills development in our country;

**WHEREAS**, to ensure the success and effectiveness of TESDA it requires the appointment of a competent and dedicated individual to lead its mandate;

**WHEREAS**, His Excellency President Ferdinand "Bongbong" Marcos, Jr. has demonstrated exemplary leadership and discernment in selecting qualified individuals to hold key positions in the government and on June 8, 2023, he appointed Secretary Suharto T. Mangudadatu, PhD, as the 14th Director General of TESDA;

**WHEREAS**, Secretary Suharto T. Mangudadatu has proven himself to be a highly accomplished and distinguished individual who had demonstrated his leadership through the years;

**WHEREAS**, he served and elected to positions in the government, particularly in Sultan Kudarat, such as its former Governor from 2019 to 2022, former Congressman for its lone district from 2004 to 2007, and former Mayor of the Municipality of Lutayan from 1998 to 2004;

**WHEREAS**, Secretary Mangudadatu's significant contributions in the government and visionary approach for progress and innovation will surely facilitate enhanced accessibility and quality technical education and skills development programs in the country by leading the TESDA;

**WHEREAS**, President Marcos, Jr.'s confidence in the dedication and commitment of Secretary Mangudadatu, a Bangsamoro leader, to the development of technical education and skills displays a significant impact on the Bangsamoro region and its pursuit of progress and prosperity;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to express our utmost gratitude to His Excellency President Ferdinand "Bongbong" Marcos, Jr. for appointing Secretary Suharto T. Mangudadatu as the 14th Director General of the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA);

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, to congratulate Secretary Suharto T. Mangudadatu for his outstanding leadership, remarkable achievements, and unwavering commitment to advancing technical education and skills development in our country; and

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, to present and transmit a copy of this resolution to His Excellency President Ferdinand "Bongbong" Marcos, Jr. and Secretary Suharto T. Mangudadatu as tokens of our appreciation and recognition for their contributions to the development and progress of our nation, and for their information.
