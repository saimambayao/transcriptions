**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING THE PROVINCE OF TAWI-TAWI AND ITS PEOPLE ON THE OCCASION OF ITS FIFTIETH (50th) KAMAHARDIKAAN (FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY) AND THE PROVINCE’S CONTRIBUTION TO THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM) AND THE NATION**

**WHEREAS**, on September 27, 1973, then President Ferdinand Edralin Marcos, issued Presidential Decree No. 302, creating the Province of Tawi-Tawi, now comprising the Municipalities of Bongao, Languyan, Mapun, Panglima Sugala, Sapa-Sapa, Sibutu, Simunul, Sitangkai, South Ubian, Tandubas, and Turtle Islands;

**WHEREAS**, the Province of Tawi-Tawi, since its creation, has been a significant contributor to the nation’s economy, as it has in fact consistently made BARMM as the region with the highest volume of fish production including seaweeds in the country per data from the Philippine Statistics Authority;

**WHEREAS**, on July 18, 2023, in due recognition of the importance of the Province of Tawi-Tawi in the Regional economy, the Bangsamoro Barter Trade had its soft launching in Bongao, Tawi-Tawi, following the formalization of the Bangsamoro Barter Trade Council Implementing Rules and Regulation;

**WHEREAS**, the Province of Tawi-Tawi has been recognized albeit unofficially as the seaweed capital of the Philippines as it produces the highest volume of seaweeds, which is one of the three major export commodities of the country along with Tuna and Shrimp/Prawn;

**WHEREAS**, the Province of Tawi-Tawi, under the leadership of Gov. Yshmael “Mang” I. Sali, will be holding a month-long celebration to commemorate this golden milestone with festivities that will highlight Agal-Agal (Seaweed) and Lepa Festivals, among others;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority – Bangsamoro Parliament celebrates with the Province of Tawi-Tawi in celebrating its 50th Founding Anniversary, and recognizes its immense role to the Country, in general, and this region, in particular;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority – Bangsamoro Parliament to Congratulate the Province of Tawi-Tawi, under the leadership of Governor Yshmael I. Sali, on the occasion of its 50th Kamahardikaan (Founding Anniversary) with a Theme: “Tawi-Tawi to the World: Bangsa, Hulah, Agama”, and commend the Province’s remarkable contributions over the years which have brought honor and pride not only to this region but to the entire nation.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That a copy of this Resolution be presented to the Office of the Provincial Governor of Tawi-Tawi, Yshmael “Mang” Sali, for information.

**ADOPTED**,
