**RESOLUTION CALLING FOR A THOROUGH AND IMPARTIAL INVESTIGATION BY THE NATIONAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION ON THE MV MARY JOY 3 MARITIME CONFLAGRATION DISASTER**

**WHEREAS**, on the evening of March 29, 2023, MV Lady Mary Joy 3, a vessel operated by Aleson Shipping Lines, Inc., caught fire while at sea resulting in the tragic deaths of at least 31 passengers;

**WHEREAS**, MV Mary Joy 3 was en route to Jolo Sulu from Zamboanga City when it caught fire off the shores of Baluk-Baluk island in Hadji Muhtamad, Basilan;

**WHEREAS**, the safety and security of passengers and crew members aboard vessels should be of utmost priority, and enforcement of such safety measures needs to be reviewed and investigated to avoid the recurrence of such loss of lives and damage to properties;

**WHEREAS**, it is the responsibility of the government to uphold and enforce the laws and regulations governing the maritime industry to ensure the safety and protection of the public and to hold those responsible to account and the victims appropriately compensated;

**THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, that the National Bureau of Investigation (NBI) be called upon to conduct a thorough and impartial investigation into the circumstances surrounding the MV Lady Mary Joy 3 maritime conflagration disaster, including the cause of the fire, the actions taken by the crew and authorities during the incident, and the compliance of the vessel with safety regulations;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the NBI be given the necessary support by the Bangsamoro Government to carry out the investigation, and that all concerned parties, including the vessel owner, operator, crew members, and other relevant individuals or organizations, be required to cooperate with the investigation;

**BE IT FINALLY RESOLVED**, that the findings and recommendations of the NBI be made public, furnished to the Bangsamoro Parliament and Ministry of Transportation and Communication, and be used to prevent similar incidents from occurring in the future, and that appropriate legal action be taken against any individual or organization found to be negligent or in violation of safety regulations.
