**Proposed Resolution No. 484**

Authored by Deputy Speaker Atty. Omar Yasser C. Sema

**RESOLUTION RESPECTFULLY REQUESTING THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE TO REALIGN THE ORIGINAL JURISDICTION OF THE NATIONAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (NBI-BARMM) AND ESTABLISH DISTRICT OFFICES IN EVERY PROVINCE OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO INCLUDING THE SPECIAL GEOGRAPHIC AREA**

**WHEREAS,** Republic Act 10867 otherwise known as National Bureau of Investigation Reorganization and Modernization Act, the policy of the State to promote and maintain an effective, modern, gender-responsive, competent, and highly trained investigative body, functionally integrated and national in scope. The National Bureau of Investigation shall establish a regional office in every region to be headed by a regional director, and district offices in every province to be headed by a head agent.

**WHEREAS,** pursuant to Paragraph 2, Section 2 of Republic Act 10867, the state shall formulate plans and programs to enhance and modernize the National Bureau of Investigation (NBI), expand its structure, capability, and manpower, responsive to the demands of the times;

**WHEREAS,** pursuant to the Department Circular of the Department of Justice No. 046 dated July 27, 2012 with subject “Assignment and Delineation of Region/District Offices of the National Bureau of Investigation (NBI)”, its office in the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (now BARMM) was reduced and only cover the areas of Cotabato City and the Province of Maguindanao (now Maguindanao Del Sur and Maguindanao Del Norte) excluding the Municipalities of Datu Paglas, Buluan, Pagalungan and Datu Montawal;

**WHEREAS,** the NBI-BARMM Regional Office has been entrusted with the responsibility of ensuring the effective function and responsibility of the Bureau that could effectively be accessible to all, particularly in the City of Cotabato and areas of Maguindanao del Norte and Maguindanao del Sur;

**WHEREAS,** exclusion of the other areas within the BARMM hinders the access to effective and most responsive services and support for the residents of the region and impedes the Bureau from meeting the growing needs of its intended clients;

**WHEREAS,** the realignment of the jurisdiction of NBI-BARMM to its original covered areas and establishment of District Offices to every province of BARMM, including Special Geographic Area, will help advance the full implementation of the R.A. No. 10867;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament recognize the crucial role of NBI in providing an effective and efficient investigative body for crime investigation and resolution;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** resolution respectfully requesting the Department of Justice to realign the original jurisdiction of the National Bureau of Investigation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (NBI-BARMM) and establish District Offices in every province of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao including the special geographic area.

**RESOLVED FINALLY,** that a copy of this resolution be submitted to the office of the Secretary of the Department of Justice (DOJ) and the National Director of the National Bureau of Investigation.
