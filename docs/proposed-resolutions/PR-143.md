**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING SECRETARY CARLITO G. GALVEZ JR. FOR HIS APPOINTMENT AS SECRETARY OF DEPARTMENT OF NATIONAL DEFENSE**

**WHEREAS,** Secretary Carlito E. Galvez Jr. was recently appointed by President Ferdinand R. Marcos Jr. as the Secretary of Department of National Defense (DND);

**WHEREAS,** Secretary Galvez served as the Presidential Adviser on Peace, Reconciliation, and Unity where he relentlessly supported the peace process in the Bangsamoro region;

**WHEREAS,** among his efforts to sustain the Bangsamoro peace process include facilitating the appointment of an inclusive Parliament and the historic reunification of Bangsamoro leaders;

**WHEREAS,** due to his genuine commitment to the peace process he was lauded and awarded by the United Nations Resident Coordinator in the Philippines himself, Gustavo Gonzalez, last year in 2022;

**WHEREAS,** the Parliament likewise recognizes and appreciates the unparalleled contribution of Secretary Galvez in the Parliament and to the Bangsamoro people in general;

**WHEREAS,** this parliament, on behalf of the Bangsamoro Government, expresses its heartfelt confidence and full support as he pursues another crucial role in government-building.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, BY THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** to extend our warmest congratulations and support to Secretary Carlito G. Galvez Jr as the newly appointed Secretary of Department of National Defense.

**BE IT RESOLVED FURTHER,** to express our gratitude and commendation to the President, His Excellency Ferdinand R. Marcos Jr., for appointing Secretary Galvez as the new Secretary of Department of National Defense. The Parliament puts trust and confidence in the capacity of the new Secretary of DND, being a General and a former Chief of Staff of Armed Forces of the Philippines and a Presidential Adviser on Peace, Reconciliation, and Unity.
