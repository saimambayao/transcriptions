**RESOLUTION URGING THE SECRETARY OF DEPARTMENT OF AGRARIAN REFORM (DAR) AND THE SECRETARY OF DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONEMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES (DENR) TO ADVANCE THE CONSERVATION OF LAKE BARANIBUD IN DADO, MUNICIPALITY OF ALAMADA, PROVINCE OF COTABATO**

**WHEREAS**, Section 16, Article II of the 1987 Constitution provides that "[the] State shall protect and advance the right of the people to a balanced and healthful ecology in accord with the rhythm and harmony of nature;"

**WHEREAS**, Section 12, Article VI of the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that "[the] National Government shall ensure the protection of the rights of the Bangsamoro People residing in communities outside the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and undertake programs for their rehabilitation and development;"

**WHEREAS**, Lake Baranibud, which is located at the borders between the Municipality of Alamada, Province of Cotabato and the Municipality of Buldon, Maguindanao del Norte, is an important ecological site that hosts a diverse flora and fauna;

**WHEREAS**, the Lake Baranibud feeds water into the Asik-Asik Falls in Alamada, the Simuay River, and other important natural waterbodies in the area;

**WHEREAS**, the lake is considered as the "last frontier in the first Congressional District of the Province of Cotabato" for its rich biodiversity and importance as a vital source of water in the area;"

**WHEREAS**, some of the areas adjacent to Lake Baranibud are home to a considerable population of Bangsamoro People, who have been awarded lands by former president Fidel Ramos for their settlement and development;

**WHEREAS**, the considerable population of Bangsamoro People who live near the areas adjacent to Lake Baranibud will be greatly affected in the event that the said lake is left not conserved;

**WHEREAS**, steps must be taken to promote and conserve Lake Baranibudâ€™s rich biodiversity and the wellbeing of the inhabitants in the area considering their importance;

**NOW, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to urge the secretary of Department of Agrarian Reform (DAR) and Secretary of Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) to advance the conservation of Lake Baranibud in Dado, Municipality of Alamada, Province of Cotabato to protect the rights and wellbeing of therein Bangsamoro inhabitants;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That a copy of this Resolution shall be furnished and presented to the secretary of DAR, the DENR, and the Office of the President.
