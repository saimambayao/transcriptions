**RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE COMMISSION ON ELECTIONS BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO REGIONAL OFFICE (COMELEC BARMM) AND PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE REGIONAL OFFICE (PRO BAR) TO PROVIDE THE PARLIAMENT DATA, INFORMATION, AND RECOMMENDATION ON THE INCIDENTS, VIOLENCE, AND ELECTION OFFENSES COMMITTED IN RELATION TO THE 2023 BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN ELECTION IN THE REGION**

**WHEREAS**, Republic Act No. 11935 postponed the Barangay and Sangguniang Kabataan Elections (BSKE) to October 30, 2023;

**WHEREAS**, pursuant to said Republic Act, the Comelec issued Resolution No. 10899 entitled “CALENDAR OF ACTIVITIES AND PERIODS OF CERTAIN PROHIBITED ACTS IN CONNECTION WITH THE OCTOBER 30, 2023 SYNCHRONIZED BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN (SK) ELECTIONS” providing for calendar of activities and prohibited acts;

**WHEREAS**, under said Comelec Resolution, the election period shall commence on July 3, 2023 until November 14, 2023;

**WHEREAS**, during the election period, Bearing, carrying or transporting firearms or other deadly weapons in public places, including any building, street, park, private vehicle or public conveyance, or even if licensed to possess or carry the same, unless authorized in writing by the Commission, Removing, destroying, obliterating, defacing, tampering or preventing distribution of lawful election propaganda, and vote-buying and vote selling are among the acts prohibited;

**WHEREAS**, under Article XXII, Section 261, of the Batas Pambansa 881 (BP 881), the following acts are also prohibited and constitutes an election offense: (e) Threats, intimidation, terrorism, use of fraudulent device or other forms of coercion. - Any person who, directly or indirectly, threatens, intimidates or actually causes, inflicts or produces any violence, injury, punishment, damage, loss or disadvantage upon any person or persons or that of the immediate members of his family, his honor or property, or uses any fraudulent device or scheme to compel or induce the registration or refraining from registration of any voter, or the participation in a campaign or refraining or desistance from any campaign, or the casting of any vote or omission to vote, or any promise of such registration, campaign, vote, or omission therefrom.

**WHEREAS**, on 30 October 2023, the Barangay and Sangguniang Kabataan Election was held nationwide;

**WHEREAS**, during the election period including the election day, the Philippine National Police Regional Office recorded sixteen (16) incidents related to election. In the said incidents, there are forty-two (42) identified victims with sixteen (16) casualties and twenty-seven (27) wounded;

**WHEREAS**, election offenses shall be filed in the Comelec and when probable cause is found, it shall be prosecuted through the latter;

**WHEREAS**, BARMM has a very low cases of election offense filed in courts;

**WHEREAS**, aside from high rate election offenses and low rate of cases filed in court, the region was also labeled as cheating capital in the past;

**WHEREAS**, the cycle of violence in the region during election seasons has been becoming a regular practice and ripening to become culture which must be stopped;

**WHEREAS**, in year 2025 or a year and a half from now, National and Local Election will be held. The probability of repeating those untoward incidents will most likely happen again if no concrete attention will be given to the problem;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament, to request the COMELEC and PNP PRO-BAR to provide this parliament data and information on incidents and election offenses or violations during the conduct of 2023 Barangay and Sangguniang Kabataan Election, and cases filed in relation to said offenses;

**Resolve further**, the PNP and COMELEC are requested to give their respective recommendations to address the problem on election offenses and violations during elections in the Region.
