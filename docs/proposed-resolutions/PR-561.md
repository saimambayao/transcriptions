**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MUZAHIR SHUEB BITO FOR THE HONOR AND PRIDE HE BROUGHT TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE AFTER REPRESENTING THE BANGSAMORO REGION AND WINNING THE 3RD PLACE DURING THE DUBAI QUR’AN COMPETITION 2024**

**WHEREAS,** the Dubai International Holy Quran Contest held its twenty-seventh (27th) session in Dubai, United Arab Emirates last 2-13 Ramadhan 1445 (March 12-23, 2024);

**WHEREAS,** different countries sent their respective delegates to qualify and participate in the prestigious competition;

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Muzahir Shueb Bito, 17 years old, and an Al-Hafidz from the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) was the sole representative of the Philippines in the said event;

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Muzahir Shueb Bito garnered the 3rd place in the mentioned Qur’an competition;

**WHEREAS,** his exemplary achievement of representing the Philippines, and coming from the Bangsamoro region, in the prestigious Holy Quran competition in Dubai, United Arab Emirates serves as an inspiration to the Bangsamoro youth and further brings pride and honor to the entire Bangsamoro community.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, to congratulate and commend Mr. Muzahir Shueb Bito for bagging the most coveted third-place spot during the twenty-seventh (27th) season of Dubai International Quran Contest, thereby giving pride and honor to the Philippines and most especially to the Bangsamoro people.
