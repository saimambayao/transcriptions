**RESOLUTION**

**CONDEMNING TO THE HIGHEST LEVEL THE SENSELESS KILLING OF SOUTH UPI VICE MAYOR ROLDAN M. BENITO, HIS COMPANION WENG MARCOS AND WOUNDING OTHERS, AND URGING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT THROUGH ITS CONCERNED AGENCIES TO CONDUCT AN IMMEDIATE, THOROUGH, AND IMPARTIAL INVESTIGATION.**

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Article II of the 1987 Constitution of the Philippines provides that “*The maintenance of peace and order, the protection of life, liberty and property, and promotion of general welfare are essential for the enjoyment by all the people of the blessings of democracy*”;

**WHEREAS**, Section 11, Article II of the same declares that, “*The State values the dignity of every human person and guarantees the full respect for human rights*”;

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article III of the Bill of Rights states that “*No person shall be deprived of life, liberty, or property without due process of law, nor shall any person be denied the equal protection of laws*”;

**WHEREAS**, Section 6 of Article IX of R.A. 11054, or also known as Organic law for the Bangsammoro Autonomos Region in Muslim Mindanao states that “*The Bangsamoro Government shall fully respect human rights*”;

**WHEREAS**, on August 2, 2024, an ambush incident transpired in Sitio Linao, Barangay Panda, South Upi, Maguindanao del Sur, which resulted in the deaths of the its incumbent Vice Mayor Roldan M. Benito and his companion Weng Marcos and wounded Barangay Chairperson of Barangay Pandan, and other companions;

**WHEREAS**, the senseless killing of Vice Mayor Benito and Mr. Marcos and wounding others are not the first of its kind. This killing is the latest incident to fall under the unexplained and senseless series of killings in the Bangsamoro Region;

**WHEREAS**, Vice Mayor Roldan Benito was a revered leader of the Tëduray community, a committed public servant, and a passionate proponent of peace and inclusive governance. He staunchly upheld the rights of Indigenous Peoples and safeguarded their ancestral domain, known as “fusaka inged”.

**WHEREAS**, given the gravity of the crime, it is crucial to urge the relevant government authorities to conduct a thorough and impartial investigation to shed light on the reasons behind this heinous act and ensure that justice for Vice Mayor Benito, Mr. Marcos, their families, and all affected parties;

**NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED AS HEREBY RESOLVED**, to strongly condemn to the highest level the senseless killing of South Upi Vice Mayor Roldan M. Benito, his companion Weng Marcos and urging the Bangsamoro Government through its relevant agencies to conduct an immediate, thorough and impartial investigation.

**ADOPTED**.
