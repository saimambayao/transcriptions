**RESOLUTION**
**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING AQEEL NIKOLAI ARUMPAC MAMUTUK FOR HIS EXEMPLARY ACHIEVEMENT IN WINNING A GOLD MEDAL IN THE 2024 THAILAND INTERNATIONAL MATHEMATICAL OLYMPIAD AND FOR BRINGING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS,** the 2024 Thailand International Mathematical Olympiad (TIMO), a prestigious math competition, took place on April 26 to 29, 2024 in Bangkok, Thailand. It was participated by various nations including representatives from Hong Kong, China, Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Ukraine, Philippines, Kyrgyzstan, Iran, Taiwan, Vietnam, Myanmar, and Cambodia;

**WHEREAS,** TIMO is an annual international Olympiad competition jointly organized by Olympiad Champion Education Centre (OCEC) from Hong Kong and Thailand Mathematics Society. It is a platform for young mathematicians from various countries to showcase their skills and compete in solving challenging mathematical problems;

**WHEREAS,** TIMO aims to promote interest in mathematics, nurture talent, and foster international collaboration among participants. The competition typically consists of multiple rounds, including individual and team-based challenges, designed to assess participants' problem-solving abilities, logical thinking, and mathematical creativity;

**WHEREAS,** among the participants, Aqeel Nikolai Arumpac Mamutuk, a 12-year-old prodigy from Cotabato City in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), Philippines, has demonstrated exceptional skill by earning the prestigious Gold Medal and achieving a Perfect Score in Logical Thinking;

**WHEREAS,** Aqeel Nikolai Arumpac Mamutuk is a Bangsamoro child of Maranao descent and a proud son of Judge Jamel Tabao Mamutuk and Atty. Aelan Biruar Arumpac who are natives of Marawi City and Cotabato City, BARMM, respectively;

**WHEREAS,** his journey to success has been marked by continuous dedication and perseverance, exemplified by his participation in international competitions since the tender age of 5;

**WHEREAS,** his academic excellence is further underscored by his distinction as a Legend Awardee of Math Olympiads Training League, Inc (MOTLI) upon attaining a Gold Award in the final rounds of three (3) esteemed international math competitions: the Hongkong International Mathematical Olympiad (HKIMO), TIMO, and the Guangdong-HongKong-Macau Greater Bay Area (Big Bay Bei);

**WHEREAS,** his success not only showcased his competitive spirit and personal excellence but also served as a source of inspiration and motivation for all Bangsamoro children to pursue academic excellence and excel in various fields of knowledge;

**WHEREAS,** nurturing young talents like Aqeel Nikolai Arumpac Mamutuk is crucial for the development of BARMM, as it contributes to building a knowledge-based society and enhances the region's competitiveness on the global stage;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Transition Authority recognizes the achievements of Aqeel Nikolai Arumpac Mamutuk in bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people on an international platform, hence deems it fitting to commend his success as it stays true to its belief that the youth are not only the nation’s future, but are also the present;

**NOW THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament to congratulate and commend Aqeel Nikolai Arumpac Mamutuk for his exemplary achievement in winning a Gold Medal in the 2024 Thailand International Mathematical Olympiad and for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people;

**RESOLVE, FURTHER,** to present a copy of this resolution to Aqeel Nikolai Arumpac Mamutuk as a token of recognition and appreciation for his invaluable achievement as well as for this resolution’s necessary effects.
