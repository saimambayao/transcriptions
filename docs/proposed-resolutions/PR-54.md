**A RESOLUTION**

**CALLING FOR THE SUSPENSION OF THE PLENARY SESSION ON THE THIRD WEEK OF NOVEMBER AND FOURTH WEEK OF DECEMBER 2022 AND FOR THE CHIEF MINISTER’S HOUR TO BE SCHEDULED ON NOVEMBER 23, 2022**

**WHEREAS,** the Committee on Rules, Committee on Accounts, Committee on Finance, Budget, and Management, and the Committee on Local Government have worked out a synchronized calendar to coordinate the conduct of committee activities, hereto appended as “Annex A,” to expedite the enactment of priority legislations, i.e. Budget of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority for F.Y. 2023, the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act for F.Y. 2023, the Bangsamoro Electoral Code, and the Bangsamoro Local Government Code.

**WHEREAS,** the proposed synchronized calendar necessitates the suspension of the plenary session in October except the October 17 session, on the third week of November and on the fourth week of December of this year;

**WHEREAS,** the proposed suspension of the plenary session in October 2022 would affect the usual schedule of the Chief Minister’s Hour, which is every first Wednesday of the quarter as per Rule X, Section 2 of the Rules, Procedures and Practices of the Parliament;

**WHEREAS,** the Chief Minister’s Hour is one of the Parliament’s effective oversight on the programs and activities of the executive;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to suspend the plenary session on the third week of November 2022 and on the fourth week of December 2022 to give way to committee activities for priority legislative measures.

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the Chief Minister’s Hour scheduled for the last quarter of this year be held on November 23, 2022.
