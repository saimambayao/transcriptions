**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING JAMAL RAHMAT PANDI OF LANAO DEL SUR FOR WINNING GOLD MEDAL IN BADMINTON SECONDARY CATEGORY AND COMMENDING THE ATHLETES AND COACHES WHO PARTICIPATED IN THE PALARONG PAMBANSA 2023, THEREBY GIVING HONOR AND PRIDE TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Parliament has, in numerous occasions acknowledged outstanding Bangsamoro athletes for their astounding accomplishments for bringing victory in different competitions held within and outside the region;

**WHEREAS**, the Palarong Pambansa 2023, officially known as the 63rd Palarong Pambansa was held in Marikina City last July 29 to August 05, 2023;

**WHEREAS**, student-athletes from seventeen (17) athletic associations representing all regions of the Philippines competed in different sporting events and disciplines;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region bagged two (2) gold, two (2) silver, two (2) bronze medals in the said Palarong Pambansa recently concluded;

**WHEREAS**, Jamal Rahmat Pandi from Schools Division of Lanao del Sur I won a gold medal in Badminton, Secondary category. His coach was Aisah Mae Mina Penduma;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Pandi is a native of Lanao del Sur who, based on records, had also brought silver and pride not only to the Bangsamoro Region but to the entire country in the Asia Junior Sports Exchange Games last 2022 for his competency in the field of Badminton;

**WHEREAS**, athletes from Schools Division of Maguindanao del Norte, Schools Division of Lamitan City assisted by their coaches, bagged one (1) gold medal in Volleyball Boys category, three (3) medals, one (silver) and two (2) bronze, in Para games, and one (1) bronze in Wrestling Girls category, respectively;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to **CONGRATULATE Jamal Rahmat Pandi for winning gold medal in Badminton, Secondary category and to COMMEND the athletes and their coaches for their remarkable effort in participating the Palarong Pambansa 2023;**

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to **Mr. JAMAL RAHMAT PANDI.**
