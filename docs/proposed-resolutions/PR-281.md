**RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING MS. RAYHANAH NUESTRO DECAMPONG FOR PLACING 2ND IN THE MAY 2023 CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANT LICENSURE EXAMINATION AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS**

**WHEREAS**, Out of 7,376, 2,239 examinees successfully passed the Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination and Ms. Decampong placed second with a general average of 89.33%

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Decampong, a Maranao Bangsamoro from the Municipality of Bayang, Lanao del Sur Province, finished her bachelor degree in De La Salle University - Manila.

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Decampong is the daughter of Mr. Norman Racman Decampong and Mrs. Sittie Sarah Nuestro.

**WHEREAS**, there are significant numbers of Bangsamoro who made it out of 7,376 examinees that deserve equal and due recognition.

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, to commend and congratulate Ms. Rayhanah Nuestro Decampong for placing 2nd in the May 2023 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination as well as all Bangsamoro CPA passers.

**ADOPTED.**
