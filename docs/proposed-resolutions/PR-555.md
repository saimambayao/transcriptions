**RESOLUTION**
**STRONGLY CONDEMNING THE SENSELESS KILLING OF MS. CHRISTHIA "THIA" ANGELA O. ARAGONCILLO, A LOGISTICS MANAGEMENT OFFICER (LMO) AT THE MINISTRY OF SOCIAL SERVICES AND DEVELOPMENT (MSSD) – MAGUINDANAO PROVINCIAL OFFICE**

**WHEREAS,** the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that the “prime duty of the Government is to serve and protect the people” (Art. II, Sec. 4) and that “no person shall be deprived of life, liberty, or property without due process of law” (Art. III, Sec. 1);

**WHEREAS,** the Constitution further states that it is the policy of the State to maintain “peace and order, the protection of life, liberty, and property, and promotion of the general welfare are essential for the enjoyment by all the people of the blessings of democracy” (Art. II, Sec. 5) and that the State “values the dignity of every human person and guarantees full respect for human rights” (Art. II, Sec. 11);

**WHEREAS,** Republic Act 11054, also known as the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, mandates that “Within its territorial jurisdiction, the Bangsamoro Government shall maintain peace and order and preserve the comfort and convenience of its inhabitants” (Art. V, Sec. 3);

**WHEREAS,** on 17 April 2024, the Ministry of Social Services and Development (MSSD) issued an Official Statement strongly condemning the fatal shooting of Ms. Christhia "Thia" Angela O. Aragoncillo, a dedicated Logistics Management Officer (LMO) at the MSSD Maguindanao Provincial Office, which occurred moments after she left her workplace;

**WHEREAS,** the statement further reported that another colleague was injured in the incident and is currently recovering in a medical facility;

**WHEREAS,** the MSSD added that the perpetrator of this cowardly act remains unidentified and that they are cooperating closely with the authorities in their investigation to serve justice;

**WHEREAS,** the fatal shooting of Ms. Aragoncillo, a public servant of the Bangsamoro Government, is a deplorable act of violence against an individual who is serving and improving the lives of the people in the Bangsamoro region;

**WHEREAS,** this act of violence against government employees are unacceptable and have no place in a society that values the rule of law, human rights, and the safety and well-being of its citizens;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Transition Authority extends its deepest condolences to the family of Ms. Christhia "Thia" Angela O. Aragoncillo and expresses sincere sympathy to her colleague who was injured in the incident;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to strongly condemn the senseless killing of Ms. Christhia "Thia" Angela O. Aragoncillo, a Logistics Management Officer (LMO) at the Ministry of Social Services and Development (MSSD) Maguindanao Provincial Office’;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** to express sincere sympathy and wish for the speedy recovery of Ms. Aragoncillo’s colleague who was injured in the incident;

**RESOLVED, FINALLY,** to forward a copy of this Resolution to the families Ms. Aragoncillo and her injured colleague, as well as to the Ministry of Social Services and Development (MSSD) for information and its necessary effects.
