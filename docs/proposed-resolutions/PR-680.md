**A RESOLUTION URGING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT, THROUGH THE MINISTRY OF BASIC, HIGHER, AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION (MBHTE) AND THE BANGSAMORO SPORTS COMMISSION (BSC), TO ENSURE THE AVAILABILITY OF STANDARD SPORTS EQUIPMENT AND FACILITIES FOR STUDENTS AND ATHLETES IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM), IN LIGHT OF RECENT INCIDENTS DURING REGIONAL ATHLETIC EVENTS**

**WHEREAS,** Section 9, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054, also known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law, mandates the Bangsamoro Government to develop a comprehensive sports development program that fosters self-discipline, teamwork, and excellence for the health, well-being, and holistic development of all Bangsamoro constituents¹;

**WHEREAS,** adequate and standard sports equipment and facilities are essential to ensure the safety, well-being, and optimal performance of student-athletes during training and competitions, in alignment with national and international safety standards²;

**WHEREAS,** there have been alarming reports of incidents during athletic events in BARMM that highlight the urgent need for improved sports facilities and equipment. For instance, during the **Palarong Panlalawigan 2025 in Upi, Maguindanao del Norte**, a 17-year-old high jump athlete **lost consciousness after landing on his head and shoulders**, underscoring potential deficiencies in safety measures and equipment standards³;

**WHEREAS,** investing in quality sports equipment and facilities not only enhances athletic performance but also promotes the physical health, mental well-being, and overall development of students, aligning with the Bangsamoro Government's commitment to youth empowerment and education⁴;

**WHEREAS,** collaboration between the **Ministry of Basic, Higher, and Technical Education (MBHTE), the Bangsamoro Sports Commission (BSC), local government units, educational institutions, and other stakeholders** is crucial to assess current needs, allocate resources effectively, and implement programs that ensure the availability and proper maintenance of sports equipment and facilities across the region;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved, to urge the Bangsamoro Government, through the Ministry of Basic, Higher, and Technical Education (MBHTE) and the Bangsamoro Sports Commission (BSC), to take immediate and concrete actions to provide and promote standard sports equipment and facilities for students and athletes in BARMM, ensuring their safety, well-being, and holistic development;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** that the concerned ministries conduct a comprehensive assessment of existing sports facilities and equipment in schools and communities within BARMM, identify gaps and areas for improvement, and develop a strategic plan to address these needs in a timely and sustainable manner;

**RESOLVED, FINALLY,** that copies of this resolution be furnished to the Office of the Chief Minister, the Ministry of Basic, Higher, and Technical Education (MBHTE), the Bangsamoro Sports Commission (BSC), local government units, and other relevant agencies for their information and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED,**
