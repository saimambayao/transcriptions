**RESOLUTION**

**ENCOURAGING ALL THE MINISTRIES, AGENCIES AND OFFICES (MAOs) INCLUDING LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS (LGUs) UNDER THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM) TO IMPLEMENT THE CIVIL SERVICE COMMISSION (CSC) MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 33 s. 1997 FOR THE BARMM EMPLOYEE**

**WHEREAS**, the 1987 Philippine Constitution, Article II, Section 15 provides that, The State shall protect and promote the right to health of the people and instill health consciousness among them;

**WHEREAS**, Article IX, Section 22, Paragraph II of the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that, The Bangsamoro Government shall protect and promote the right to health of the people and instill health consciousness among them. It shall also uphold the peoples' right to have access to essential goods health, and other social services that promote their well-being;

**WHEREAS**, Title V, Chapter I, Section I, Paragraph II of the Bangsamoro Administrative Code states that, The Bangsamoro Government shall protect and promote the right to health of the people and instill health consciousness among them. It shall also uphold the peoples' right to have access to essential goods, health, and other social services that promote their well-being;

**WHEREAS**, the Civil Service Commission issued a Memorandum Circular No. 33 s. 1997 which provides that, all government agencies and Government-Owned and/or-Controlled Corporations (GOCCs) shall provide, among others, a health program for their employees which includes free annual mental and medical-physical examinations;

**WHEREAS**, the Malacanang issued an Administrative Order No. 402 s. 1998 entitled "Establishment of a Medical Check-up Program for the Government Personnel" which support the CSC Memorandum Circular No. 33 s. 1997";

**WHEREAS**, the said Administrative Order state that, “keeping a healthy workforce is among the primary concerns of the government considering that the physical well-being of its employees has a significant impact on the efficiency and effectiveness of public service delivery”;

**WHEREAS**, Islam is the complete code of life and contemporary workplaces are no exception being well positive domain of health and a source of happiness. Healthy and happy employees symbolize health and happy organization. Workplace health promotion is attracting global attention to nurture employee wellbeing and happiness as a catalyst for organizational excellence;

**WHEREAS**, the MAOs are responsible in promoting the health of Bangsamoro employees to ensure effective and efficient delivery of quality services to the Bangsamoro people;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it;**

**RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to encourage all the Ministries, Agencies, and Offices (MAOs) including Local Government Units (LGUs) under the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) to implement the Civil Service Commission (CSC) Memorandum Circular no. 33 s. 1997 for the BARMM employee.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that a copy of this resolution be disseminated to all concerned Ministries, Agencies, and Offices (MAOs) including LGUs under the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) for information.
