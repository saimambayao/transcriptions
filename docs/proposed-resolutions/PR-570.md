**A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE CONSENSUS OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT ON THE SELECTION OF THE HONORABLE SHEIKH MUSLIM MASAGLANG GUIAMADEN AS THE WALI OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT**

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article VIII of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law, provides for the appointment of the Wali, the ceremonial head of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, Paragraph 2, Section 1, Title II, Book III of Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Administrative Code requires issuance of a resolution by the Parliament reflecting its consensus on the selection of the Wali from a list of eminent residents of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region submitted by the Council of Leaders;

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Article VIII of the Bangsamoro Organic Law sets forth the qualifications of the Wali at the time of his nomination;

**WHEREAS**, the Council of the Leaders convened on 9 May 2024 to deliberate over the selection of the next Wali of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, in its Council Resolution No. 01, Series of 2024, the Council of Leaders nominated and endorsed to the Parliament the names of Sheikh Muslim Masaglang Guiamaden and Dr. Muhammad Nadheer Ebil, who are both eminent residents of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS**, upon careful deliberation and discussion, the Bangsamoro Parliament has reached a consensus in selecting Sheikh Guiamaden as the next Wali of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, Sheikh Guiamaden served as the Chief Justice of Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) Shari’ah Court in Camp Abubakar;

**WHEREAS**, he was born in 1963 in Buluan, Maguindanao Del Sur, and has maintained his residence at his place of birth;

**WHEREAS**, he earned a bachelor’s degree in Shari’ah, major in Islamic Jurisprudence and its Principles from the Islamic University of Madinah, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia in 1989;

**WHEREAS**, he was previously associated with the Ministry of Endowments and Islamic Affairs of the State of Qatar for 27 years and is one of the founders of Philippine Muslims Federation, an organization that aims to promote the welfare and interests of Filipino Muslims living in Qatar;

**WHEREAS**, Sheikh Guiamaden’s morals and conduct are beyond reproach. He has dedicated more than 60 years of his life to making significant contributions to the development of the religious and cultural identity of the Bangsamoro;

**WHEREAS**, given his qualifications and eminence in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, Sheikh Muslim Masaglang Guiamaden is worthy of guiding the Bangsamoro Government as its ceremonial head;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it hereby resolved, to express the consensus of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament on the appointment of Hon. Muslim Masaglang Guiamaden as the Wali of the Bangsamoro Government pursuant to R.A. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law;
