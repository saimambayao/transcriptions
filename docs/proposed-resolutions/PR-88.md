**RESOLUTION**

**COMMENDING YASSER ABDULAZIZ NECOR BINSUAN FOR GARNERING 10TH PLACE WITH A REMARKABLE RATING OF 89.20% AMONGST THE 117,938 EXAMINEES OF THE 2022 CAREER SERVICE PROFESSIONAL EXAMINATION**

**WHEREAS,** on the 7th day of August 2022, the Civil Service Commission (CSC) disclosed the results of the August 2022 Career Service Professional Examination (CSPE);

**WHEREAS,** with a remarkable rating of 89.20%, Notre Dame University graduate BS Accountancy, and alumnus Mr. Yasser Abdulaziz Necor Binsuan garnered the rank of Top 10 amongst the one hundred seventeen thousand nine hundred thirty-eight (117,938) professional level examinees out of which twenty-two thousand two hundred forty-one (22,241) with the percentage of (18.86%) have passed the exam which was conducted nationwide on August 7, 2022 exam;

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Binsuan completed his undergraduate degree in Cotabato City, at the Notre Dame University - College of Business and Accountancy with the degree Bachelor of Science in Accountancy (BSA) on June 2022.

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Binsuan finished his Bachelor’s degree as Dean’s Lister and a consistent 3rd Honor Student from 1st year to 3rd year. He was also the College Governor of Notre Dame University - College of Business and Accountancy (CBA), the Vice-President of Academics of Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants (JPIA) – Notre Dame University and the Vice-President of Graduating Class Association 2022 during his college days in Notre Dame University.

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Binsuan is a proud Bangsamoro, residing in the heart of the Bangsamoro Region, the City of Cotabato. He is the second son of Atty. Yusoph Pato Binsuan, a public attorney, and Rosanie Bing Necor Binsuan, HR Head of BIR Cotabato.

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Binsuan’s vision was to become a public accountant. He was also an aspiring lawyer, seeking to follow in his father’s footsteps in the legal profession.

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Binsuan serves as a good example and inspiration for the young Bangsamoro people to emulate and excel in their chosen field of endeavors with the utmost goal of giving service to the public;

**WHEREAS,** the remarkable achievement of Mr. Binsuan is a source of inspiration for the young professionals and aspirants in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and the entire nation which merits this Parliament’s commendation and recognition.

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Binsuan brought honor to our region, even amidst the concerns brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic. His perseverance, dedication, and courage made evident by securing the top 10 in the Career Service Professional Examination. It will forever serve as an inspiration not only to the people of Maguindanao but also to the millions of Bangsamoro people:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED** to commend Yasser Abdulaziz Necor Binsuan for garnering 10th place with a remarkable rating of 89.20% amongst the one hundred seventeen thousand nine hundred thirty-eight (117,938) examinees in the 2022 Career Service Professional Examination.
