**RESOLUTION**
**URGING THE COMMITTEE ON LOCAL GOVERNMENT TO CONDUCT AN INQUIRY IN AID OF LEGISLATION ON THE MASS TERMINATION OF CONTRACTUAL EMPLOYEES OF COTABATO CITY GOVERNMENT**

WHEREAS, news and social media sources report that Cotabato City Mayor MOHAMMAD ALI DC MATABALAO has announced that about 3,000 contractual employees of the city government will be terminated effective 17 October 2024 in view of the *Sangguniang Panglungsod's* failure to provide for additional budget to cover the salaries of the contractual employees;

WHEREAS, news and social media sources also report that the City Vice Mayor JOHARIE "BUTCH" ABU claims that the *Sangguniang Panglungsod* has already provided for the salaries of the contractual salaries.

WHEREAS, their untimely mass termination of 3,000 city govenrmnet contractual employees at the very commencement of the fourth quarter of the fiscal year places puts the lives of around 3,000 families in dire uncertainty;

WHEREAS, there is an imperative to appreciate and understand the cause of such mass termination of city contractual employees and develop measures to manage human resources and financial situations placing so many hardworking but still lowly paid, untenured government employees in this predicament;

WHEREAS, the authority of the Bangsamoro Government to regulate the affairs of its constituent local government units is guaranteed in accordance with Republic Act No. 11054 and the Bangsamoro local Government code enacted by the Parliament;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to urge the Committee on Local Government to conduct an inquiry in aid of legislation on the mass termination of the contractual employees of Cotabato City Government.
