**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING THE YOUNG BANGSAMORO FIGHTERS IN THE IST KYOKUSHINRYU WORLD TOURNAMENT FOR AN OUTSTANDING ACHIEVEMENTS, THEREBY BRINGING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, Section 13, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that the State recognizes the vital role of the youth in nation-building and shall promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, intellectual, and social well-being. It shall inculcate in the youth patriotism and nationalism, and encourage their involvement in public and civic affairs;

**WHEREAS**, Section 17, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution Philippine also provides that the State shall give priority to education, science and technology, arts, culture and sports to foster patriotism and nationalism, accelerate social progress, and promote total human liberation and development;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government recognizes the vital role of women and youth, and the valuable contributions of differently-abled persons in nation-building and shall promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, intellectual, cultural, and social well-being. It shall inculcate in the minds of the youth patriotism and nationalism, and encourage their involvement in public and civic affairs;

**WHEREAS**, First Kyokushinryu World Tournament is a full contact karate competition organized by International Karate Alliance Kyokushinryu (IKAK), to be conducted every four (4) years starting year 2023 in the Philippines, provides an opportunity for all karatekas with strong interest in mastering martial arts skills and strict discipline to participate and contribute in the world of martials arts;

**WHEREAS**, Kyokushinryu World Tournament is a platform to stimulate and foster young people’s interest in learning martials arts, to strengthen their skills, widen their international perspective, and to promote camaraderie and social interaction among participants;

**WHEREAS**, Kyokushinryu World Tournament was conducted last November 11-12, 2023 at Caloocan Sports Complex, participated in by more than one hundred (100) martials arts practitioners coming from thirty-four (34) countries;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government congratulates and commends the following young Bangsamoro fighters who displayed brilliance and exceptional fighting skills by becoming a medalist in the said world tournament, *to wit*:

1. CHAMPION (55kgs Category –Blackbelt)
**AL-QUDDOS MOHAMAD, 23 years old**
International Karate Alliance Kyokushinryu
Cotabato City, BARMM

2. SECOND PLACER (65kgs Category – Blackbelt)
**AHMADINNIE UY TALAPAS, 28 years old**
International Karate Alliance Kyokushinryu
Cotabato City, BARMM

**WHEREAS**, the success of these young Bangsamoro fighters is a pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people, and serves as an inspiration to our Bangsamoro youth;

**WHEREAS**, it is appropriate to recognize their outstanding performance and contribution that brought pride, honor and glory to the Bangsamoro Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, to congratulate and commend the young Bangsamoro fighters in the 1st Kyokushinryu World Tournament for an outstanding achievement, thereby bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people;

**RESOLVED FURTHER** that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to the above-mentioned young Bangsamoro representative and to Sensei Engr. Usman Baraguir, the newly-elected IKAK Philippine Country Director, for information.

Adopted,

(Signed)
**SUHARTO S. ESMAEL, MSc.**
