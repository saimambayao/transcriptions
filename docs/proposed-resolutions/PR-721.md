**A RESOLUTION URGING THE ALLOCATION AND DESIGNATION OF A “CAR FREE SUNDAY” WITHIN THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT CENTER (BGC) FOR PURPOSES OF JOGGING AND RECREATIONAL ACTIVITIES EVERY SUNDAY, AND ENJOINING THE TEMPORARY PROHIBITION OF MOTORIZED VEHICULAR TRAFFIC THEREIN FROM 4:30 AM TO 9:30 AM TO PROMOTE PUBLIC HEALTH AND PHYSICAL WELL-BEING**

**WHEREAS**, Section 9, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution declares that “The State shall promote a just and dynamic social order that will ensure the prosperity and independence of the nation and free the people from poverty through policies that provide adequate social services, promote full employment, a rising standard of living, and an improved quality of life for all”;

**WHEREAS**, Section 15, Article II of the same Constitution further provides that “The State shall protect and promote the right to health of the people and instill health consciousness among them”;

**WHEREAS**, Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the *Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM)*, provides under Section 22, Article IX (Health) that:

> “The Bangsamoro Government shall protect and promote the right to health of the people and instill health consciousness among them. It shall also uphold the peoples' right to have access to essential goods health, and other social services that promote heir well-being..”

**WHEREAS**, regular physical activity, such as jogging and recreational exercise, is widely recognized as a key factor in promoting physical and mental health, preventing chronic diseases, and improving overall well-being;

**WHEREAS**, creating safe, accessible, and dedicated spaces for such physical activities contributes to building a health-conscious, active, and cohesive community, in line with the goals of the Bangsamoro Government to serve the welfare of every Bangsamoro citizen;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government Center (BGC) in Cotabato City is a central and symbolic location, accessible to employees, residents, and the public, making it an ideal venue for hosting community-based recreational and wellness activities;

**WHEREAS**, temporarily restricting motorized vehicular traffic within a designated area of the BGC during specific hours every Sunday would ensure safety, minimize pollution, and encourage participation in physical activities, without causing undue disruption to government operations or public order;

**WHEREAS**, To encourage other provincial and local government units to be open to the public for Bangsamoro Community Building.

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to encourage the **Office of the Chief Minister**, to allocate and designate a specific and safe area within the Bangsamoro Government Center (BGC) as a **Jogging and Recreational Zone every Sunday**;

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, the temporary prohibition of motorized vehicular traffic within the said designated area **every Sunday from 4:30 AM to 9:30 AM**, for the purpose of allowing public access to health-promoting physical and recreational activities;

**RESOLVED FINALLY**, that copies of this Resolution be furnished to the **Office of the Chief Minister**.
