**RESOLUTION**
**URGING THE PROMOTION AND USE OF RENEWABLE ENERGY IN BARMM**

**WHEREAS**, the Renewable Energy Act of 2008 aims to promote the use of renewable energy sources such as wind, solar, hydro, geothermal, and biomass to reduce the country's dependence on fossil fuels and promote sustainable development;

**WHEREAS**, the BARMM government, under the leadership of CM Ahod Balawag Ebrahim, has adopted an enhanced 2022-2028 12-point agenda, which includes promoting sustainable development and reducing the carbon footprint of the region;

**WHEREAS**, the Ministry of Environment, Natural Resources and Energy (MENRE) is tasked with implementing and enforcing the Renewable Energy Act of 2008 and promoting the use of renewable energy in the region;

**WHEREAS**, the government instrumentalities of BARMM and its constituents play a crucial role in promoting the use of renewable energy and reducing the region's carbon footprint;

**THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED** that the BARMM government, through the MENRE, be urged to promote and encourage all the government instrumentalities of BARMM and its constituents on the use of renewable energy in support of the Renewable Energy Act of 2008 and the enhanced 2022-2028 12-point agenda of CM Ahod Balawag Ebrahim.

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED** that the MENRE be tasked with developing and implementing programs and initiatives that will increase the awareness and adoption of renewable energy sources among the government instrumentalities of BARMM and its constituents.

**BE IT FINALLY RESOLVED** that copies of this resolution be furnished to the MENRE and all the government instrumentalities of BARMM for their information and appropriate action.
