**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING DR. ABDULLAH MANGU- LAMAS ABTAHI FOR ACHIEVING DOCTORAL DEGREE FROM THE FACULTY OF DA’WAH AND FUNDAMENTALS OF RELIGION, IS-LAMIC UNIVERSITY IN MADINA, KINGDOM OF SAUDI ARABIA.**

**WHEREAS**, Dr. Abdullah Mangulamas Abtahi, he being the son of the former Moro National Liberation Front [MNLF] ambassador to Libya, displayed his sincere and loyal support to the Bangsamoro cause, especially in the time of constraint and need, through the genuine leadership of the Moro National Liberation Front [MNLF];

**WHEREAS**, despite the great challenges and obstacles in life springing from the unconditional support to the Bangsamoro cause and the MNLF, Dr. Abtahi nonetheless managed to achieve his educational attainment all the way to the doctoral degree, the highest degree one may acquire, from the most prestigious and reputable Islamic university in the Kingdom; and

**WHEREAS**, thus, it is but highly moral and proper to immortalize our congratulation and commendation to him thru the passage of this Resolution to serve as a best example to the Bangsamoro people, most especially to the youth;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it resolved, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to congratulate Dr. Abdullah Mangulamas Abtahi for achieving doctoral degree from the Faculty of Da’wah and Fundamentals of Religion, Islamic University in Madina, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that let an official copy of this resolution be furnished to Dr. Abtahi.

**ADOPTED**,

(Signed)
**USTADZ ABDULKARIM TAN MISUARI**
