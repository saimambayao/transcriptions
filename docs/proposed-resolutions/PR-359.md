**RESOLUTION**
**REQUESTING THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT TO CONDUCT AN INVESTIGATION ON THE IRREGULARITIES DURING THE 2023 HAJJ PILGRIMAGE IN THE KINGDOM OF SAUDI ARABIA (KSA), IN AID OF LEGISLATION**

**WHEREAS**, the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos (NCMF) was created on 18 February 2010 under the Office of the President, through Republic Act No. 9997;

**WHEREAS**, it is the mandate of the NCMF to preserve and develop the culture, traditions, institutions, and well-being of Muslim Filipinos in conformity with the country’s laws and in consonance with national unity and development;

**WHEREAS**, furthermore, Section 9 (q), Article II of the Republic Act No. 9997 sanctions NCMF to “Administer all programs, projects and activities, formulate the necessary rules and regulations, and coordinate with pertinent offices to ensure the success of the annual Hajj (pilgrimage) to Mecca, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA)”;

**WHEREAS**, Section 11, Article II of the 1987 Constitution provides that the “State values dignity of every human person and guarantees full respect for human right.”

**WHEREAS**, hajj pilgrimage is the fifth pillar of Islam, and all physically and financially abled Muslims are obliged to perform it at least once in the holy city of Mecca, KSA;

**WHEREAS**, it was reported that two hundred (200) pilgrims had been stranded in Muzdalifa, near Mecca on 28 June 2023. In addition, some of the pilgrims posted on social media and called the attention of NCMF regarding the issues they have encountered, particularly in transportation, food distribution, accommodations, among others;

**WHEREAS**, there is a need to probe into the NCMF’s management of the Muslim Filipino pilgrims during the 2023 hajj pilgrimage in Saudi Arabia, KSA, to assess their overall performance in respect to their mandate, powers and functions. This includes the logistics, coordination efforts, tasking and monitoring assignments of the delegation of NCMF that should have ensured the success of the 2023 hajj pilgrimage in Saudia Arabia, KSA, as envisioned by the law.

**WHEREAS**, the probe shall provide constructive inputs to improve legislative acts in order to enhance the administration of annual pilgrimage of the Muslim Filipinos.

**NOW THEREFOR, be it**

**RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, as it is hereby resolved, to urge the Office of the Presidemt to conduct an investigation on the irregularities during the 2023 hajj pilgrimage in the kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA), in aid of legislation.
