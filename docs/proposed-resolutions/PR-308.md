**RESOLUTION**

**EXPRESSING UTMOST CONDOLENCES TO THE BEREAVED FAMILIES OF THE SEVEN (7) MEMBERS OF THE MORO ISLAMIC LIBERATION FRONT (MILF), WHO WERE KILLED IN A RAID LAST JUNE 18, 2023 AND JOINING THEIR CALL FOR A FAIR, IMPARTIAL, AND SPEEDY INVESTIGATION**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Parliament is committed to upholding the rights, welfare, and security of the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, it has come to the attention of the Bangsamoro Parliament that a raid conducted on June 18, 2023, in Barangay Damawato, Datu Paglas town in Maguindanao del Sur resulted in the tragic killing of seven members of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF);

**WHEREAS**, the victims who are Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) members, namely: NASSER YOUSEF M. HUSAIN (34y.o.), NORJIHAD M. HUSAIN (29y.o.), NASRULLAH MAMAY SINGKALA (38y.o.), IVAN PUMPUGAY (18y.o.), IZRAEL LAGUIAB (41y.o.), MORSID MADIDIS (50y.o.), AND MAMA KAKIM (53y.o.), all succumbed to fatal gunshot wounds;

**WHEREAS**, it is the duty of the Bangsamoro Parliament to ensure that the human rights of all Filipino citizens, including members of the MILF, are respected and protected;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Parliament recognizes the need for law enforcement agencies to apprehend individuals suspected of criminal activities, but this must be done in accordance with established legal procedures and in compliance with human rights standards.

**WHEREAS**, it has been reported that the victimswere hand-cuffed and killed in different areas, including a mosque, raising concerns about the use of excessive force and the violation of the victims’ human rights;

**WHEREAS**, the raid occurred in the midst of the implementation of the Comprehensive Agreement on the Bangsamoro, specifically the decommissioning and normalization track, where the MILF is a significant stakeholder committed to the pursuit of lasting peace;

**WHEREAS**, the victims have already filed their application for amnesty and were anticipating the grant thereof;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, that the Bangsamoro Parliament extends its deepest condolences to the families and loved ones of the victims and assures them that it will diligently pursue justice on their behalf;

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the Bangsamoro Parliament strongly condemns the raid that led to the killing of seven members of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) and expresses deep concern over the reported use of excessive force and the violation of the victims’ rights;

**FINALLY RESOLVED**, that copies of this resolution be transmitted to the Office of the Chief Minister, the Office of the Presidential Adviser on Peace, Reconciliation and Unity (OPAPRU), the Commission on Human Rights, and other relevant government agencies for their immediate attention and appropriate action.
