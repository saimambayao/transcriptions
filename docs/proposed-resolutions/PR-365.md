**A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING SINCERE GRATITUDE AND APPRECIATION TO PRESIDENT FERDINAND ROMUALDEZ MARCOS, JR. FOR APPOINTING GENERAL ROMEO SATURNINO BRAWNER, JR. AS THE CHIEF OF STAFF OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FURTHER CONGRATULATE THE LATTER ON HIS WELL-DESERVED APPOINTMENT**

**WHEREAS**, on July 19, 2023, President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr. appointed Lieutenant General Romeo Saturnino Brawner, Jr. as the 60th Chief of Staff of the Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP).

**WHEREAS**, having appointed to the highest-ranking military position next to the President reflects the excellence, commitment, and integrity of General Brawner to public service and acknowledges as well his devotion to upholding the AFP motto of “protecting the people and securing the state”.

**WHEREAS**, prior to his appointment as the Chief of Staff of the AFP, General Brawner served in different capacities in military service where he displayed exceptional performance throughout his career. He made significant contributions to peace and development in the country which include the following:

*   65th Commanding General of the Philippine Army
*   Commanding General of the 4th Infantry Division in Northern Mindanao
*   Company Commander of the 6th Special Forces Company
*   Battalion Commander of the 2nd Special Forces Battalion
*   Operations Officer of the Special Forces Regiment (Airborne)
*   Chief of Staff of the 6th Infantry “Kampilan” Division
*   Chief of Public Affairs of the Philippine Army
*   Spokesperson of the Armed Forces of the Philippines
*   Commander of the Civil-Military Operations Regiment
*   Brigade Commander of the 103rd Infantry Brigade during the aftermath of the Marawi Siege in 2017.

**WHEREAS**, General Brawner graduated as No. 2 of the “Makatao” Class 1989 of the Philippine Military Academy (PMA) where he later served as a commandant and was the main role player in the elimination of hazing and maltreatment among the PMA Cadets.

**WHEREAS**, in pursuit of knowledge to better serve our country and the people, General Brawner obtained further studies from different prestigious Universities both locally and abroad to wit:

*   Master’s Degree in Information Management from Ateneo de Manila University
*   Master’s Degree in Business Administration from the Asian Institute of Technology in Thailand
*   Master’s Degree in Business Administration from the European School of Management in Oxford, United Kingdom
*   Master’s Degree in Strategic Studies from the United States Army War College.

**WHEREAS**, during his long years in military service, General Brawner received several awards and recognitions which include; The Outstanding Philippines Soldier (TOPS) in the year 2013 given by the Metrobank Foundation and Rotary Club of Makati; the Lapu-Lapu Award by the former President Rodrigo Roa Duterte; inducted into the United States Army War College International Fellows Hall of Fame in recognition of his service at the highest command position in the Philippine Army; and the Legion of Merit – Degree of Commander in recognition of his exceptionally meritorious service as the Commanding General of the Philippine Army.

**WHEREAS**, some of the highlights of General Brawner’s career took place in Mindanao. Noteworthy are his contributions during the aftermath of the Marawi Siege which include; the rehabilitation of Marawi City; the pursuit of the remnants of the Maute terror group; the safeguarding of the conduct of an orderly plebiscite; and the ratification of the R.A. 11054 or Bangsamoro Organic Law.

**WHEREAS**, through this well-deserved appointment of General Brawner which vested him with additional powers and responsibilities, we hope and pray as it is highly conceivable that he will continue his resolve in championing the peace and development in Mindanao.

**WHEREAS**, with the aforementioned accomplishments and contributions of General Brawner, particularly in taking a substantial part in the triumph for the achievement of our right to self-determination as evidenced by the autonomy that we currently exercise, it is therefore proper and fitting that this Honorable Parliament extend our utmost congratulations to this remarkable leader.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament – Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING SINCERE GRATITUDE AND APPRECIATION TO PRESIDENT FERDINAND ROMUALDEZ MARCOS, JR. FOR APPOINTING GENERAL ROMEO SATURNINO BRAWNER, JR. AS THE CHIEF OF STAFF OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FURTHER CONGRATULATE THE LATTER ON HIS WELL-DESERVED APPOINTMENT.**
