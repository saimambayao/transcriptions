**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING GRATITUDE TO PRESIDENT FERDINAND “BONG BONG” R. MARCOS FOR APPOINTING FORMER GOVERNOR SUHARTO “TENG” T. MANGUDADATU AS DIRECTOR GENERAL OF THE TECHNICAL EDUCATION AND SKILLS DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY (TESDA) AND FORMER CONGRESSMAN MOHAMMED HUSSEIN P. PANGANDAMAN AS ADMINISTRATOR OF THE AUTHORITY OF THE FREEPORT AREA OF BATAAN (AFAB)**

**Whereas**, President Ferdinand Bongbong Marcos bestowed his trust and confidence in the appointments of former Gov. Suharto “Teng” T. Mangudadatu as Director General of Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) and former Congressman Hussein P. Pangandaman as Administrator of the Authority of the Freeport Area of Bataan (AFAB);

**Whereas**, the appointments of Director General Mangudadatu and Administrator Pangandaman ushered in positive developments in their respective agencies, contributing greatly to the growth and development of the technical education and skills sector and to the thriving economy of the Freeport Area of Bataan;

**Whereas**, Director General Mangudadatu’s expertise in governance and experience in public service as a Congressman and Governor of the Province of Sultan Kudarat will enable him to establish valuable programs and initiatives in TESDA that will foster skills and technical knowledge among Filipinos of all ages and backgrounds, redefining the face of the country’s labor force and contributing to the nation’s manpower capabilities;

**Whereas**, Administrator Pangandaman’s leadership and innovative approach to governance will pave the way for the unprecedented growth in the Freeport Area of Bataan, promoting investment and economic progress in the region;
**Whereas**, the appointments of Director General Mangudadatu and Administrator Pangandaman reflect President Marcos’ genuine concern for the welfare and benefits of the people he serves;

**Therefore, be it resolved**, that we the members of the Bangsamoro Parliament express our profound gratitude to President Ferdinand Bongbong Marcos for his wise and judicious appointments of Director General Suharto “Teng” T. Mangudadatu and Administrator Hussein P. Pangandaman, whose contributions to the nation’s progress and development are truly invaluable. We applaud President Marcos’ unwavering commitment to uplifting the lives of the Filipino people, and his dedication to promoting genuine change and progress in the country.
