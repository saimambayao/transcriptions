**RESOLUTION**
**COMMENDING MS. ROSLAINE B. MAULANA AND MS. FATIMA AIZA R. MAJAD FOR THEIR EXEMPLARY PERFORMANCE IN RANKING THIRD (3RD) AND SIXTH (6TH), RESPECTIVELY, AND CONGRATULATING ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS IN THE CERTIFICATION EXAMINATION FOR REGISTERED MICROBIOLOGISTS FOR BRINGING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS,** Section 13, Article II of the 1987 Constitution and Section 13, Article IX of the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) both recognize the vital role of the youth in nation-building and shall promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, intellectual, and social well-being, as well as their role to inculcate in the youth patriotism and nationalism, and encourage their involvement in public and civic affairs;

**WHEREAS,** the Philippine Academy of Microbiology (PAM) Certification Examination for Registered Microbiologists took place from January 20 to 21, 2024 at the SMX Convention Center Aura, Taguig, Metro Manila;

**WHEREAS,** on February 23, 2024, the Philippine Academy of Microbiology announced that two hundred thirteen (213) examinees successfully passed the PAM Certification Examination for Registered Microbiologists;

**WHEREAS,** among the accomplished candidates, two Bangsamoro topnotchers in the name of Ms. Roslaine B. Maulana and Ms. Fatima Aiza R. Majad dominated the PAM Certification Examination for Registered Microbiologists;

**WHEREAS,** Ms. Roslaine B. Maulana who graduated from University of Southern Mindanao and a resident of Sultan Mastura, Maguindanao del Norte, secured the Top 3 rank with an exceptional rating of eighty-four point forty-five percent (84.45%);

**WHEREAS,** Ms. Fatima Aiza R. Majad, who graduated from Mindanao State University -Iligan Institute of Technology and a native of Tipo-Tipo, Basilan, emerged as the Top 6 garnering an impressive rating of eighty-three point forty-eight percent (83.48%);

**WHEREAS,** acknowledging their remarkable achievement encourages and inspires all the Bangsamoro youth to pursue academic and professional excellence, drawing inspiration from their exemplary performance as role models;

**WHEREAS,** their success together with all the Bangsamoro passers serves as a guiding light for the Bangsamoro children, youth, and young professionals, resonating not only within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region but also across the entire nation;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to commend Ms. Roslaine B. Maulana and Ms. Fatima Aiza R. Majad for their exemplary performance in ranking third (3rd) and sixth (6th), respectively, and congratulating all Bangsamoro passers in the Certification Examination for Registered Microbiologists for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** to present a copy of this resolution to Ms. Roslaine B. Maulana and Ms. Fatima Aiza R. Majad as a token of our recognition and appreciation for their achievement as well as for this resolutionâ€™s necessary effects.
