**A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST CONDOLENCES AND SYMPATHIES TO THE FAMILY OF THE LATE ROSTOM SAABAN, THE 3RD BASE COMMANDER OF 106TH BASE COMMAND OF THE SOUTHERN MINDANAO FRONT**

**WHEREAS,** Rostom Saaban is a native resident of Barangay Malibpolok, Rajah Buayan, Province of Maguindanao Del Sur;

**WHEREAS,** he had immensely and significantly contributed to the present peace and development enjoyed by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region as he joined the struggle for the liberation of the Bangsamoro since 1970s;

**WHEREAS,** the late Rostom Saaban passed away peacefully before Fajr of March 24, 2024 (Ramadhan 14, 1445AH), Sunday;

**WHEREAS,** other than being a leader and a peace maker, he was also a teacher of Qur’an in Camp Abubakar and lived a very low-profile life that made the people truly admired him;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament expresses its profound condolences on the passing of Rostom Saaban, the 3rd Base Commander of the 106th Base Command of the Southern Mindanao Front under the leadership of Abduladzis ‘Kagui Jordan’ M. Esmael Al-Haj;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament also expresses its sincerest gratitude for his philanthropies to the Region;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED** to adopt this resolution expressing deepest condolences and sympathies to the family of the late Rostom Saaban, the 3rd Base Commander of 106th Base Command of the Southern Mindanao Front.

**RESOLVED FURTHER** that the bereaved family of Rostom Saaban be furnished a copy of this resolution.

Adopted,

**SUHARTO S. ESMAEL, MSc.**
