**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING ABDURAHIM MOH. SALI ABDULLA FOR RANKING 10TH IN THE NOVEMBER 2023 NURSING LICENSURE EXAMINATION (NLE) AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS**

WHEREAS, the Philippines Nurses Licensure Examination (NLE) was conducted last November 11 to 12, 2023 in nineteen (19) testing centers all over the Philippines;

WHEREAS, the Professional Regulation Commission announced on December 2, 2023 that out of thirty two thousand two hundred three (32,203) examinees in the NLE, twenty five thousand seven hundred sixty one (25,761) or eighty (80) percent passed the said board exam;

WHEREAS, Mr. Abdurahim Moh. Sali Abdulla is ranked 10th with an average score of 89.20%, wherein he was the only Bangsamoro among the coveted top 10 places in the said examination;

WHEREAS, Mr. Abdulla is a born Tausug, who graduated from Western Mindanao State University (WMSU) in Zamboanga City last May 2023 and received a latin honor of cum laude;

WHEREAS, his exemplary performance as topnotcher serves as a source of inspiration for the Bangsamoro children, youth, and young professionals not only in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region but also in the entire country which then merits this Parliament's recognition and commendation;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate and commend Abdurahim Moh. Sali Abdulla for ranking top 10 in the Nursing Licensure Examination (NLE) and including all Bangsamoro passers, thereby giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**ADOPTED.**
