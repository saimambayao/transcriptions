**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MR. ABDUL BATERBONIA LUMANGGAL FOR RANKING TOP 10 IN THE OCTOBER 2022 LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR TEACHERS IN ELEMENTARY LEVEL AND FOR GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, on 2 October 2022, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and the Professional Regulatory Board for Professional Teachers (BPT) conducted the regular Licensure Examination For Teachers (LET) in various testing centers nationwide;

**WHEREAS**, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced on 16 December 2022 that out of 91,468 examinees in the elementary level, 49,783 or 54.43% of them passed. Of the total number of examinees, 29,183 are first timers while 20,600 are repeaters;

**WHEREAS**, among the 49,783 elementary teacher passers, Mr. Abdul Baterbonia Lumanggal, a Bangsamoro of Maguindanaon descent, admirably ranked as Top 10 with an average score of 92.60%;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Abdul Baterbonia Lumanggal, who hails from Poblacion Carmen, Cotabato, is also a recipient of a Latin academic honor as Magna Cum Laude from the University of Southern Mindanao â€“ Kabacan. He was born on 3 November 1994 and is now 28 years of age. He is the son of proud parents, the late Mr. Abdul Bolingceg Lumanggal and Mrs. Melda Dasal Baterbonia;

**WHEREAS**, his exemplary performance to become one of the topnotchers is a source of inspiration for the Bangsamoro children, youth, and young professionals not only in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region but also in the entire country;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority is cognizant of Mr. Abdul Baterbonia Lumanggal for bringing honor and pride to the Bangsamoro people. He is a living proof that the youth are not only our future in as much as they are also our present.

**NOW THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to congratulate and commend Mr. Abdul Baterbonia Lumanggal for ranking Top 10 in the October 2022 Licensure Examination for Teachers in Elementary Level and for giving honor and pride to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVE, FURTHER**, to present a copy of this resolution to Mr. Abdul Baterbonia Lumanggal, for information.
