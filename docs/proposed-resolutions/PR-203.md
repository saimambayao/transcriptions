**A RESOLUTION APPEALING TO THE MINISTRY OF BASIC, HIGHER, AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION (MBHTE), MINISTRY OF SOCIAL SERVICES AND DEVELOPMENT (MSSD), MINISTRY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY (MOST), APPROPRIATE COMMISSIONS, AND OTHER PROPER AGENCIES WITH SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM TO ALLOCATE FUNDS FOR SCHOLARSHIP TO PERSONS WITH DISABILITIES (PWDs), SINGLE PARENTS, WIDOWS, WIDOWERS, AND THEIR CHILDREN**

**WHEREAS**, Section 2 (3), Article XIV, 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that the *State shall establish and maintain, a system of scholarship grants*, student loan programs, subsidies, and other incentives which shall be *available to deserving students* in both public and private schools, especially to the underprivileged.

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article XIV provides that, [T]he State shall protect and promote the *rights of all citizens to quality education* at all levels and shall take appropriate steps to make such education accessible to all.

**WHEREAS**, Section 183, Bangsamoro Autonomous Act — 18, Bangsamoro Education Code, provides that:

> **Section 183.** Grant of Scholarship Pursuant to Existing Laws. — *Schools and higher educational institutions shall be encouraged to grant scholarships to learners pursuant to the provisions of existing laws, and such scholarship measures as may hereafter be provided for by law.*

**WHEREAS**, Section 15 (i), Bangsamoro Autonomous Act — 13, Bangsamoro Administrative Code, mandates *Bangsamoro Youth Commission to extend and provide support or assistance to deserving youth and youth organizations including scholarship grants.*

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure *that persons with disabilities, single parents, widows, and widowers, and their children* are provided with adequate access to quality education and ample opportunities to develop their skills.

**WHEREAS**, *proper ministries, commissions, offices, and agencies* shall take appropriate steps to make such education accessible to them including the determination and establishment of the qualifications of the scholarship candidates.

**WHEREAS**, the amount necessary for the effective implementation of this Resolution shall be charged against the appropriations for the Ministry of Basic, Higher, and Technical Education (MBHTE), Ministry of Social Services and Development (MSSD), Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST), Bangsamoro Youth Commission (BYC) and other appropriate agencies having scholarship grants under the current General Appropriations Act.

**WHEREAS**, such amount as may be necessary for its continued implementation shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

**WHEREAS**, this resolution aims to ensure that the right to quality education in form of scholarship be accessible and afforded to all, particularly to the persons with disabilities, single parents, widows, and widowers, and their children.

**WHEREAS**, this Resolution purposely to enable them to be equipped with necessary educational competencies and to aid them in coping with the socio-economic challenges of life.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION APPEALING TO THE MINISTRY OF BASIC, HIGHER, AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION (MBHTE), MINISTRY OF SOCIAL SERVICES AND DEVELOPMENT (MSSD), MINISTRY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY (MOST), APPROPRIATE COMMISSIONS, AND OTHER PROPER AGENCIES WITH SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM TO ALLOCATE FUNDS FOR SCHOLARSHIP TO PERSONS WITH DISABILITIES (PWDs), SINGLE PARENTS, WIDOWS, WIDOWERS, AND THEIR CHILDREN**
