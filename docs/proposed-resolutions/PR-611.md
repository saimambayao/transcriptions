**A RESOLUTION COMMENDING THE MINISTRY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY (MOST) UNDER THE LEADERSHIP OF MINISTER MP ENGINEER AIDA M. SILONGAN, MPDS FOR ITS BANGSAMORO ASSISTANCE FOR SCIENCE EDUCATION (BASE) AND OTHER SIMILAR SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAMS, AND CONGRATULATING THE FIRST BATCH OF SCHOLARS (YEAR 2020) OF THE BASE FOR THE ACADEMIC YEAR 2023-2024 GRADUATES, SOME OF WHOM HAVE BROUGHT FURTHER HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO BY EARNING PRESTIGIOUS LATIN AWARDS, INCLUDING THE HIGHEST LATIN HONORS FROM ESTEEMED INSTITUTIONS WITHIN THE REGION, THEREBY SHOWCASING THE EXCELLENCE OF BANGSAMORO STUDENTS**

**WHEREAS**, the Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST), under the distinguished leadership of Minister MP Engineer Aida M. Silongan, MPDS, has successfully implemented the Bangsamoro Assistance for Science Education (BASE) along with other similar scholarship programs to support and advance the education of Bangsamoro youth;

**WHEREAS**, the first batch of BASE scholars (Year 2020) has successfully completed their studies and graduated in the Academic Year 2023-2024;

**WHEREAS**, several graduates from this cohort have earned prestigious Latin awards, including the highest Latin honors from esteemed institutions within the region, thereby bringing further honor and recognition to the Bangsamoro community;

**WHEREAS**, these achievements exemplify the dedication, perseverance, and academic excellence of Bangsamoro students, serving as a source of inspiration for future generations;

**WHEREAS**, a total of 714 students from the 1st batch to the 4th batch have been granted the BASE scholarship, 208 students with the BASE MERIT scholarship, 444 with the MASE scholarship, and 400 with the BSASE scholarship, reflecting the significant investment in the education of Bangsamoro children and youth as the future of the Bangsamoro;

**WHEREAS**, the Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST), as the leading agency in the implementation of programs, projects, and initiatives related to science, technology, and innovations, envisions technologically advanced and improved access to quality education through science and technology scholarship programs, capacity building for science and mathematics teachers, and upgrading of science laboratories;

**WHEREAS**, the Ministry has established two cash assistance programs under the Science Education, Scholarships, and Grants (SESG) of the Ministry, namely: Bangsamoro Assistance for Science Education (BASE), Mujahideen/Mujahidat Assistance for Science Education (MASE), Bangsamoro Assistance for Science Education – Merit (BASE-Merit) Program, and the Bangsamoro Special Assistance for Science Education (BSASE), helping students or youth in their educational attainment;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, that the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) formally commends the Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST) under the leadership of Minister MP Engineer Aida M. Silongan, MPDS for its commendable efforts in implementing the BASE and other similar scholarship programs;

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the BTA congratulates the first batch of BASE scholars (Year 2020) for their outstanding academic accomplishments and for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro community;
