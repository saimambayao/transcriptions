**Whereas**, these Bangsamoro students will graduate this year, 1445/2024, and will complete the following Islamic courses: Qur'an, Shariah, Dawa and Usuluddeen, Arabic Language, and Law and Judicial Studies;

**Whereas**, the Islamic University of Madinah was established by the government of Saudi Arabia in 1961 through a royal decree in the city of Medina. Currently, only Muslim males are eligible to attend this university. Students can study Sharia, the Qur'an, Usul al-din, and Hadith, with non-native speakers having the opportunity to study Arabic. The university currently offers bachelor of arts, master’s, and doctoral degrees.
The College of Sharia Islamic Law was the first to be established when the university opened. Since then, the university has also opened faculties of engineering, computer and information science, and science, with plans for a faculty of judiciary studies. There are also plans for online degrees, and the university currently offers an online course in Shariah and a program in Arabic language as well.

**Whereas**, one of the notable alumni of the Islamic University of Madinah is Ismail ibn Musa Menk, also known as "Mufti Menk," who is widely recognized as a leading global Islamic scholar and motivational speaker;

**Whereas**, one of the significant achievements of the Bangsamoro is the completion of studies, particularly in the field of Islamic studies;

**Whereas**, we, the Bangsamoro people, take great pride in the accomplishments of these Bangsamoro students who will graduate and complete various Islamic studies at the esteemed Islamic University of Madinah in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority:

**TO PASS AND APPROVE THIS RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND HONORING THE ESTEEMED THIRTY-SIX (36) GRADUATING BANGSAMORO STUDENTS FROM THE ISLAMIC UNIVERSITY OF MADINAH, KINGDOM OF SAUDI ARABIA IN THE DIFFERENT FIELDS OF ISLAMIC STUDIES.**
