**RESOLUTION**
**URGING THE BANGSAMORO ECONOMIC AND DEVELOPMENT COUNCIL (BEDC) THROUGH THE BANGSAMORO PLANNING AND DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY (BPDA) TO PREPARE AND SUBMIT A COMPREHENSIVE PERFORMANCE REPORT OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT TO THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT, COVERING THE PERIOD 2019 TO 2022.**

**WHEREAS**, Section 12, Article XVI of the Republic Act No. 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides for the dissolution of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority and the submission of its final report and recommendations on the status of government during the transition period to the Parliament, as well as to the House of Representatives, the Senate of the Philippines, and the Office of the President;

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article VI of the same law provides that the President exercises general supervision over the Bangsamoro Government to ensure that laws are faithfully executed;

**WHEREAS**, Section 13, Article XVI of the same law provides for the first regular election to be held and synchronized with the 2022 national elections. However, this was superseded with the passage of Republic Act No. 11593, resetting the first regular elections in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to be held and synchronized with the 2025 national elections;

**WHEREAS**, Section 3, Article XVI of the same law provides that the legislative and executive powers in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region shall be vested in the Bangsamoro Transition Authority and all powers and functions of the Bangsamoro Government as provided in the Organic Law is vested in the BTA during the transition period;

**WHEREAS**, the *first* Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) have served office from February 19, 2019 to August 10, 2022 following the appointment of the 80-member BTA by then President, His Excellency Rodrigo Roa Duterte;

**WHEREAS**, His Excellency President Ferdinand “Bongbong” Romualdez Marcos, Jr. appointed the second set of 80-member BTA last August 11, 2022;

**WHEREAS**, there is a need to prepare for the performance report of the *first* BTA in order to document and inform the national government and the general public of what has been accomplished so far in the *first* transition period;

**WHEREAS**, the governance of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao is guided by the principles of transparency, accountability and moral governance, as enunciated by the Chief Minister Al-Hadj Murad;

**WHEREAS**, Ministries, Agencies, and Offices were involved in the implementation of development programs, projects, and activities funded from the block grant and other sources from the National Government to the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, the implementation of programs and projects include major sectors such as economic, social, infrastructure, environment and climate change, and development administration and governance;

**WHEREAS**, significant gains and achievements of these major sectors shall be highlighted in the performance report, including laws and legislative measures passed and approved by the *first* BTA Parliament. Moreover, major implementation issues, concerns, and recommendations shall also be highlighted in the report;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Economic and Development Council, as the planning and policy-making body of the Bangsamoro Government, shall oversee the preparation and submission of the performance report; and the Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority, as the Technical Secretariat of the Council, shall coordinate the preparation of the performance report in cooperation with implementing ministries, offices, and agencies;

**WHEREAS**, the performance report of the Bangsamoro Government shall be submitted by the Office of the Chief Minister to the Office of the President, Senate President, and the Speaker of the House of Representatives.

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED** by the BTA Parliament to pass and approve this Resolution urging the Bangsamoro Economic and Development Council (BEDC) through the Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority (BPDA) to prepare and submit a comprehensive performance report of the Bangsamoro Government to the National Government, covering the period 2019 to 2022.

**RESOLVED FINALLY**, that copy of this Resolution be furnished to the Office of the Chief Minister for information and guidance of the ministries, agencies and offices of the Bangsamoro Government.
