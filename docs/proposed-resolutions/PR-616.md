**BTA RESOLUTION No. 616**

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**RESOLUTION URGING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE AND OTHER AUTHORITIES OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO TO IMMEDIATELY ACT AND INVESTIGATE THE BRUTAL KILLING OF VICE MAYOR ROLDAN BENITO OF MUNICIPALITY OF SOUTH UPI MAGUINDANAO DEL SUR**

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Introduced by:

**MP FROILYN T. MENDOZA**

Co-Author

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**WHEREAS**, the 1987 Philippine Constitution under the Bill of Rights provide that, No person shall be deprived of life, Liberty, or property without due process of law, nor shall any person be denied the equal protection of the Laws.

**WHEREAS**, On August 2, 2024 around 5:00 pm was abmushed in Brgy. Pandan, South Upi, Maguindanao Del Norte with her family resulting the death of Vice Mayor Benito with his employee Weng Marcos, injured by his wife and village captain of Pandan Hon. Analyn F. Benito Kian Percy Benito 11 years old son and certain Joseph Mutia.

**WHEREAS**, we strongest condemn the vicious killing of Hon. Roldan Benito who is a Non-Moro Indigenous Peoples Leader and Vice Mayor of South Upi, Maguindanao Del Sur.

**WHEREAS**, in the spirit of Justice, it is imperative to bring the perpetrators to the court of Laws for his criminal act.

**WHEREFORE, RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, that the Philippine National Police and other government authority to act immediately for impatial investigation of the brutal killing of Hon. Vice Mayor Roldan Benito, Weng Marcos and injured by families the Vice-Mayor and bring the assailant to the court of Justice for the criminal act have done.
