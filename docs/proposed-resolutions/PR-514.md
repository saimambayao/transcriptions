**BTA RESOLUTION NO. 514**

Co-Authors:

**A RESOLUTION CONDEMNING IN THE STRONGEST POSSIBLE TERMS THE AMBUSH OF DR. SHARMAINE CEBALLOS BAROQUILLO, CALLING ALL CONCERNED AGENCIES OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT TO EXPEDITE THE CONDUCT OF THEIR INVESTIGATION**

**WHEREAS**, Section 5, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that, “*The maintenance of peace and order, the protection of life, liberty, and property, and the promotion of the general welfare are essential for the enjoyment by all the people of the blessings of democracy*”;

**WHEREAS**, Section 11, Article II of the same declares, “*The State values the dignity of every human person and guarantees full respect for human rights*”;

**WHEREAS**, Section 6 of Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054, also known as “Organic Law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” states that “*The Bangsamoro Government shall fully respect human rights*”;

**WHEREAS**, on February 3, 2024, Dr. Sharmaine Ceballos Baroquillo, a 28-year-old physician, was ambushed while driving along a highway of Datu Paglas, Maguindanao del Sur, and thereafter collided with an incoming tricycle on her way to Buluan District Hospital;

**WHEREAS**, Dr. Baroquillo was on her way to work at Sultan Kudarat Provincial Hospital when two (2) men riding a motorcycle fired gunshots at Baroquillo. Wounded, the doctor managed to drive towards Buluan District hospital. But, Baroquillo lost control, rammed an incoming tricycle, and damaged a house;

**WHEREAS**, the incident left Dr. Baroquillo serious physical injuries, and injuring the four (4) passengers who boarded the tricycle;

**WHEREAS**, Dr. Baroquillo had to undergo multiple surgeries, and one of which is the injury near her spine that left her lower body unresponsive;

**WHEREAS**, the 28-year-old physician is a daughter of two (2) Overseas Filipino Workers (OFW), who worked tirelessly just to provide a bright future for their daughter. This incident is unbearable for every parent, ready to sacrifice everything in the hopes of a successful life for their children;

**WHEREAS**, the act done by the subject perpetrators should be condemned in the strongest possible terms, that any form of violence and harassment is a threat not only to the maintenance of peace and security in the region, but also to the safety and well-being of our healthcare professionals;

**WHEREAS**, the conduct of investigation should be expedited, in order to bring justice to the families of Dr. Baroquillo and the four (4) passengers, as well as to put an end to these ruthless activities;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament – Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to **CONDEMN IN THE STRONGEST POSSIBLE TERMS THE AMBUSH OF DR. SHARMAINE CEBALLOS BAROQUILLO**, and to **CALL ALL CONCERNED AGENCIES OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT TO EXPEDITE THE CONDUCT OF THEIR INVESTIGATION**

**ADOPTED**, February ____, 2024.
