**RESOLUTION**
**COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING MS. NISHRIEN AMATONDING BARUA FOR PLACING 5TH AND MR. AL-ZAHEER ADIL ABUBAKAR FOR PLACING 10TH IN ELEMENTARY LEVEL AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS IN THE MARCH 2023 LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR PROFESSIONAL TEACHERS IN BOTH ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY LEVEL**

**WHEREAS**, out of 60,896 examinees in elementary level, Nishrien Amatonding Barua ranked 5th with a general average of 91.40%, and Al-Zaheer A. Abubakar ranked 10th with a general average of 90.40%;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Barua is a product of Mindanao State University in Marawi City as Magna Cum Laude while Mr. Abubakar graduated as Cum Laude from Notre Dame University, Cotabato City;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Barua is a native of Malabang, Lanao Del Sur, daughter of Mr. Saipoden Ibra Barua born in Banday, Malabang, Lanao del Sur and Mrs Noraisa Bayabao Amatonding. On the other hand, Mr. Abubakar is a resident of Cotabato City, son of Musa K. Abubakar, a teacher/farmer, born in SPDA, D.O.S., now Maguindanao del Norte and mother Ermalyn U. Abubakar a housewife, born in Cotabato City;

**WHEREAS**, there are significant numbers of Bangsamoro who passed out of 163,168 examinees during the March 2023 Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers, both Elementary and Secondary Level, that deserve equal and due recognition;

**WHEREAS**, both topnotchers and all passers of the March 2023 Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers brought pride and honour to the Bangsamoro Government.

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, to commend Nishrien Amatonding Barua for placing 5th and Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar for placing 10th in the March 2023 Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers in the Elementary Level.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, to congratulate all the Bangsamoro passers in the March 2023 Licensure for Professional Teachers in both level.

**ADOPTED.**
