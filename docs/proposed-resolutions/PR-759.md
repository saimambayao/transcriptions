**RESOLUTION AMENDING RESOLUTION NO. 538 OR A RESOLUTION ADOPTING THE CALENDAR OF SESSION FOR THE FOURTH REGULAR SESSION OF THE SECOND BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT**

**WHEREAS**, in a decision promulgated on 30 September 2025, the Supreme Court of the Philippines held that the 13 October 2025 BARMM Parliamentary Elections provided in Republic Act No. 12123 cannot proceed due to the absence of a valid law apportioning the thirty-two (32) parliamentary districts in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM);

**WHEREAS**, in the same decision, the Supreme Court directed the Commission on Elections to promptly proceed with its preparations and conduct elections not later than 31 March 2026;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament in Resolution No. 538, entitled “Resolution Adopting the Calendar of Session for the Fourth Regular Session of the Second Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament,” has set 29 October 2025 as the date of its *sine die* adjournment;

**WHEREAS**, a number of key legislative measures, including priority legislations mandated under Republic Act No. 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law, are still pending before the different committees of the Parliament and would need to be deliberated in the plenary;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to amend the existing Calendar of Session as provided under Resolution No. 538 and adopt the following:

1. Resumption of Session - October 20, 2025
2. Adjournment of Session - October 23, 2025
3. Resumption of Session - October 27, 2025
4. Adjournment of Session - October 30, 2025
5. Resumption of Session - November 17, 2025
6. Adjournment of Session - November 20, 2025
7. Resumption of Session - November 24, 2025
8. Adjournment of Session - November 27, 2025
9. Resumption of Session - December 15, 2025
10. Adjournment of Session - December 18, 2025
11. Resumption of Session - December 22, 2024
12. Adjournment of Session - December 23, 2025
13. Resumption of Session - January 19, 2026
14. Adjournment of Session - January 22, 2026
15. Resumption of Session - January 26, 2026
16. **MANDATORY ADJOURNMENT** - January 29, 2026
