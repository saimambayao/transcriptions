**RESOLUTION**

**COMMENDING AND RECOGNIZING THE JAPAN INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION AGENCY (JICA) FOR ITS SUPPORT AND ASSISTANCE TO THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

**WHEREAS,** Section 23 of Article II of the 1987 Constitution provides that “*the State shall encourage non-governmental, community-based, or sectoral organizations that promote the welfare of the nation*”;

**WHEREAS,** Section 25 of Article XII of the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that “*The Bangsamoro Government may avail of official development assistance, upon review and approval of the National Government in accordance with Republic Act No. 8182, otherwise known as the " Official Development Assistance Act of 1996," as amended, to achieve inclusive growth and poverty reduction through the implementation of priority development projects.*”;

**WHEREAS,** the dedication of the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) in supporting the Bangsamoro Government and its people is evident in their efforts to improve lives and promote development. This support plays a vital role in building a foundation for peace, stability, and prosperity in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) and the entire Philippines;

**WHEREAS,** one of the means where the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) provides assistance is through sustained partnerships with the Ministries, Offices, and Agencies in the BARMM in advancing public finance and budget management in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Government declares that the principles of accountability, integrity, and moral governance shall be realized and maintained in the use of public funds and resources by ensuring transparency, fiscal responsibility, results-orientation, efficiency, and effectiveness in line with the aims of meaningful fiscal autonomy towards the attainment of economic self-sufficiency and genuine development in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS,** the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) has ceaselessly shown gestures of goodwill that has proven helpful as BARMM continues to work in achieving a transparent, inclusive, and responsible government anchored to the principles of moral governance;

**WHEREAS,** the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) is a truly remarkable institution. Generosity, unwavering commitment, and a sense of support and assistance are all important qualities that contribute to creating a positive and welcoming environment. These characteristics show that JICA cares deeply about helping others and making a difference;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro people, who are striving to improve their way of life and establish a more effective government, would like to extend their heartfelt recognition, commendation, and boundless appreciation to the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA).

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED** by the BTA Parliament, as it is hereby resolved, to commend and recognize the unwavering support and assistance by the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** that a copy of this resolution be presented to the JICA, for information.
