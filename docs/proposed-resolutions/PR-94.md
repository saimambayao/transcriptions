**RESOLUTION**

**URGING THE INTER-GOVERNMENTAL INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT BOARD, TO INCLUDE IN ITS PRIORITY AGENDA THE REHABILITATION AND UPGRADING OF THE NARCISO RAMOS HIGHWAY IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION**

**WHEREAS,** the 154-kilometer Narciso Ramos Highway that cuts across mainland Mindanao, links the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region to the other regions in Mindanao and beyond. Thus, its repair and maintenance is within the responsibility of the National Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH), as prescribed under Section 37, Article XIII of the Organic Law;

**WHEREAS,** the Narciso Ramos National Highway needs the urgent attention of the concerned departments for its immediate repair, rehabilitation, and upgrading considering its crucial role in accelerating the movement of the traveling public and the transport of goods and services from various production points to market outlets in Mindanao;

**WHEREAS,** the Inter-governmental Infrastructure Development Board is tasked by the Organic Law, Section 6, Article VI to coordinate and synchronize the National and Bangsamoro Infrastructure Development Plan.

**WHEREAS,** both the Secretary of National DPWH and Minister of MPW are members of the Board;

**WHEREAS,** with the joint action of the National DPWH and Bangsamoro MPW, there can be anticipated faster decision-making and actions for the urgent repair, rehabilitation, and upgrading of the Narciso Ramos Highway within the BARMM jurisdiction.

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED** by the BTA Parliament to pass and approve this Resolution urging the Inter-governmental Infrastructure Development Board, through its chairman, to include in its priority agenda the rehabilitation and upgrading of the Narciso Ramos Highway in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
