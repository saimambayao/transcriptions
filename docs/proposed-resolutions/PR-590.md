**PROPOSED RESOLUTION NO. 590**

**A RESOLUTION URGING THE BANGSAMORO INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATIONS TECHNOLOGY OFFICE (BICTO) TO PROVIDE MEASURES IN SAFEGUARDING OFFICIAL WEBSITES AND PAGES OF THE MEMBERS OF THE PARLIAMENT, MINISTRIES, AND OFFICES OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT FROM ANY FORM OF CYBER ATTACKS**

**WHEREAS**, Section 24, Article II of the Philippine Constitution provides that:

> *Section 24. "The State recognizes the vital role of communication and information in nation-building.*

**WHEREAS**, Republic Act No. 10175, otherwise known as the Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012, addresses crimes committed in cyberspace and use of information and communications technology (ICT) which includes, among others, illegal access (hacking);

**WHEREAS**, there has been several reports of illegal intrusion into the official websites and pages of certain offices in the Bangsamoro Government which is clearly alarming;

**WHEREAS**, it is imperative for the august body to immediately address the said pressing issue, otherwise, the integrity and security of sensitive government information may be compromised, leading to potential breaches of privacy, loss of public trust, and disruption of essential services.

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to urge the Bangsamoro Information and Communications Technology Office (BICTO) to provide measures and precaution to the official websites and pages of the Bangsamoro Government against this dilemma.

Adopted,
