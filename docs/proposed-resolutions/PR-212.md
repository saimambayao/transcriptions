**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING HON. ABDULRAOF A. MACACUA ON HIS APPOINTMENT BY HIS EXCELLENCY PRESIDENT FERDINAND MARCOS, JR. AS THE OFFICER-IN-CHARGE GOVERNOR OF MAGUINDANAO DEL NORTE**

**WHEREAS**, on April 5, 2023, His Excellency President Ferdinand Marcos, Jr. appointed Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua as Officer-in-Charge (OIC) Governor of the province of Maguindanao del Norte;

**WHEREAS**, Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua is a distinguished leader who has served the Bangsamoro people with distinction for many years, and his appointment as OIC Governor of Maguindanao del Norte is a testament and furtherance to this;

**WHEREAS**, Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua had served as Member of the Parliament, Bangsamoro Transition Authority and Senior Minister of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) and was also the Chief of Staff of the Bangsamoro Islamic Armed Forces of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front;

**WHEREAS**, the appointment of Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua as OIC Governor of Maguindanao del Norte is a positive development for the Bangsamoro people, as it represents a step forward in the ongoing of the peace process in the region;

**WHEREAS**, Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua's appointment as OIC Governor of Maguindanao del Norte is a recognition of his leadership, integrity, competence, dedication, and commitment to public service, and is a reflection of the trust and confidence that His Excellency President Ferdinand Marcos, Jr. has in his capabilities;

**WHEREAS**, Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua's appointment as OIC Governor of Maguindanao del Norte will undoubtedly be instrumental in promoting the culture of peace and in bringing about positive changes and reforms in the province, and will contribute to the overall development of the Bangsamoro region;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, that the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament congratulates and commends Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua on his appointment by His Excellency President Ferdinand Marcos, Jr. as OIC Governor of Maguindanao del Norte;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament expresses its full support for Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua as he takes on this new and important role, and pledges to work with him to advance the interests of the Bangsamoro people;

**BE IT FINALLY RESOLVED**, that a copy of this resolution shall be furnished to the Office of the OIC Governor of Maguindanao del Norte.
