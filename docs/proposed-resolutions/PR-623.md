**RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE MINISTRY OF BASIC HIGHER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION TO RENDER A REPORT ON THE UPDATES OF ENROLMENT FOR THE ACADEMIC YEAR 2024-2025 TO THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT**

**WHEREAS,** Section 1, Article XIV of the 1987 Constitution provides that:

> “The State shall protect and promote the right of all citizens to quality education at all levels, and shall take appropriate steps to make such education accessible to all.”

**WHEREAS,** Section 16, Article IX of the Bangsamoro Organic Law or the RA 11054 provides that:

> “xxx It shall be a top priority of the Bangsamoro Government to establish, maintain, and support a complete and integrated system of quality education, which shall be a subsystem of the national education system. The Bangsamoro Government shall develop an educational framework relevant and responsive to the needs, ideals, and aspirations of the Bangsamoro people. xxx”

**WHEREAS,** education is a fundamental right and a key factor in the development and progress of the Bangsamoro Region;

**WHEREAS,** it is essential for the government, to ensure that every child has access to quality education and that the enrolment process is efficient, transparent, and inclusive;

**WHEREAS,** the Academic Year 2024-2025 marks a significant period in our continuous efforts to improve the educational system, particularly in terms of access, retention, and quality;

**WHEREAS,** there is a need for the Ministry of Basic Higher and Technical Education to provide timely and accurate updates on the enrolment figures, challenges faced, and measures taken to address any issues during the enrolment process;

**WHEREAS,** the information provided by the Ministry will assist in making informed decisions and policy adjustments that will benefit students, educators, and the education system as a whole;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to **REQUEST THE MINISTRY OF BASIC HIGHER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION TO RENDER A REPORT ON THE UPDATES OF ENROLMENT FOR THE ACADEMIC YEAR 2024-2025.**
