**EXPRESSING THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY’S SYMPATHY AND SOLIDARITY WITH THE PEOPLE AND GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF TÜRKİYE FOR THE DEVASTATING DEATH AND INJURY OF THOUSANDS OF PEOPLE AND SEVERE DAMAGE TO THE COUNTRY CAUSED BY THE CATASTROPHIC EARTHQUAKE THAT OCCURRED ON FEBRUARY 6, 2023/RAJAB 15, 1444 AH**

**WHEREAS**, on February 6, 2023/Rajab 15, 1444 AH, a 7.8 magnitude earthquake hit the town of Gaziantep of the Republic of Türkiye which was closely followed by numerous aftershocks including a 7.5-magnitude¹ quake. The earthquake and its strong aftershock have caused the unfortunate death and injury of thousands of people and devastating widespread destruction throughout the country;

**WHEREAS**, in this trying time of the Republic of Türkiye, the Bangsamoro Government reiterates the strong brotherhood it has forged with the Turkish government by acknowledging the unwavering assistance the latter has extended to the Bangsamoro people in their struggle for self-determination, in the implementation of the Comprehensive Agreement on the Bangsamoro, and its continuous generosity to the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro people offer solace to their brothers and sisters in Türkiye in the midst of this great trial with the words of Allah (*s.w.t.*) that He does not burden with more than what can be handled beyond capacity², and the words of prophet Muhammad (*s.a.w.*) that never a believer is stricken with a discomfort, an illness, an anxiety, a grief or mental worry or even the pricking of a thorn but Allah (*s.w.t.*) will expiate his sins in account of his patience³;

**WHEREAS**, the people of the Republic of Türkiye and its government have long been helping the Bangsamoro people for years. In turn, the Bangsamoro Government is finding ways to extend help to Türkiye in this trying time as a gesture of solidarity;

**WHEREAS**, for the overwhelming distress it is suffering, the Bangsamoro Government sends its commiseration to the people of the Republic of Türkiye and its government with the confidence that with their strong faith and resilience, they will rise from this disaster;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to express its sympathy and solidarity with the people and government of the Republic of Türkiye for the devastating death and injury of thousands of people and severe damage to the country caused by the catastrophic earthquake that occurred on February 6, 2023/Rajab 15, 1444 AH;

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to the Embassy of the Republic of Türkiye in the Philippines.
