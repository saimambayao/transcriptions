**A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING SYMPATHY AND CONDOLENCES TO THE BEREAVED FAMILY OF THE LATE PASTOR TROY ERIC O. CORDERO AND RECOGNIZING HIS ACTIVE PARTICIPATION IN THE PEACE AND DEVELOPMENT OF BARMM AS WELL AS HIS CONTRIBUTIONS AS A REPRESENTATIVE OF THE SETTLER COMMUNITIES TO THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT COUNCIL OF LEADERS.**

**WHEREAS**, on February 20, 2023, upon satisfactorily meeting the criteria for selection, Pastor Troy Eric Obrigoso Cordero was appointed by Chief Minister Ahod B. Ebrahim, Al-Haj, as a representative of Settler Communities to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) Council of Leaders, pursuant to Article VI, Section 9 of RA 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law.

**WHEREAS**, as a Cotabateño since birth and an advocate of sustainable peace and development in BARMM, Pastor Troy Eric Obrigoso Cordero has passionately supported the passage of Bangsamoro Basic Law, now RA 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law, during the campaign activities for its approval in the Philippine Congress.

**WHEREAS**, Pastor Troy, as the Chairperson of the Metro Cotabato and Suburbs Ministerial Fellowship, Inc., earnestly campaigned for the inclusion of Cotabato City in the BARMM Region which resulted in a victory of YES votes during the plebiscite on January 21, 2019.

**WHEREAS**, Pastor Troy had been committed with his unrelenting support to attaining the lifelong dream of our right to self-determination as the Bangsamoro people.

**WHEREAS**, on June 4, 2023, Pastor Troy Eric Obrigoso Cordero departed as he was summoned and called by our Almighty Creator.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament – Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING SYMPATHY AND CONDOLENCES TO THE BEREAVED FAMILY OF THE LATE PASTOR TROY ERIC O. CORDERO AND RECOGNIZING HIS ACTIVE PARTICIPATION IN THE PEACE AND DEVELOPMENT OF BARMM AS WELL AS HIS CONTRIBUTIONS AS A REPRESENTATIVE OF THE SETTLER COMMUNITIES TO THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT COUNCIL OF LEADERS.**

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to his bereaved family.
