**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING THREE (3) REMARKABLE BANGSAMORO WOMEN NAMELY: DR. SITTI ZAYDA B. HALUN, BAI NORHATA M. ALONTO, AND FATIMA PEPE KANAKAN FOR THEIR MINDANAO WOMEN IN DEVELOPMENT AWARDS 2024**

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) recognizes the significance of empowering and honoring exemplary individuals who contribute to the development and advancement of communities in our region;

**WHEREAS,** the Mindanao Development Authority has bestowed the prestigious Mindanao Women in Development Awards 2024 to recognize outstanding women who have made remarkable contributions to community development in Mindanao;

**WHEREAS,** among the recipients of the inaugural Mindanao Women in Development Awards 2024 were three remarkable Bangsamoro women, namely: Dr. Sitti Zayda B. Halun, Bai Norhata M. Alonto, and Fatima Pepe Kanakan, who have demonstrated exceptional leadership and dedication in their respective fields;

**WHEREAS,** Dr. Sitti Zayda B. Halun of the Mindanao State University Tawi-Tawi College of Technology and Oceanography has been acknowledged for her innovative contributions in seaweed research and development, showcasing excellence in scientific endeavors and fostering sustainable solutions for the agriculture sector in the Bangsamoro region;

**WHEREAS,** Bai Norhata M. Alonto, Commissioner of the Social Security System has been recognized for her exemplary efforts in leading change for gender equality and women’s economic development, contributing significantly to the empowerment of women in our community;

**WHEREAS,** Fatima Pepe Kanakan has been honored for her tireless advocacy, resilience, dedication and unwavering commitment to uplift indigenous communities, particularly women, thereby promoting inclusivity and equality in the Bangsamoro region;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved, by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, to extend heartfelt congratulations and commendations to Dr. Sitti Zayda B. Halun, Commissioner Bai Norhata M. Alonto, and Fatima Pepe Kanakan for their outstanding achievements and for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people through their exemplary accomplishments;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED,** that copies of this Resolution be furnished to Dr. Sitti Zayda B. Halun, Commissioner Bai Norhata M. Alonto, Fatima Pepe Kanakan, Mindanao State University Tawi-Tawi College of Technology and Oceanography, Social Security System, Rajah Mamalo Descendants Organization of Southern Philippines, Inc., Bangsamoro Women Commission, and Mindanao Development Authority for their information and record.
