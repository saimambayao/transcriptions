**Proposed Resolution No. 439**

Introduced by BTA Member **Khalid Ma-amor Hadji Abdullah**

**RESOLUTION**
**COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE LATEST BANGSAMORO PRIDE, HEBBAN TALIB TAWANTAWAN, FOR RANKING 1ST IN THE RECENTLY CONCLUDED SEPTEMBER-OCTOBER 2023 CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANT LICENSURE EXAMINATION, AS WELL AS ALL OTHER NEW BANGSAMORO CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANTS**

**WHEREAS**, on 06 October 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) released the results of the September 30, October 01, and 02, 2023 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination (CPALE);

**WHEREAS**, a total of 2,740 out of 8,734 or 31.37% of examinees passed the CPALE;

**WHEREAS**, along with the release of the CPALE is also the announcement of the topnotchers (Top 10) to which fellow Bangsamoro, **HEBBAN TALIB TAWANTAWAN** from University of San Jose Recoletos, managed to secure the licensure exam’s **highest spot (TOP 1)**;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Tawantawan, graduated Magna Cum Laude in his beloved Alma Mater, a proud Meranao, a native of the Province of Lanao del Norte, and a good and responsible son to Abon Tawantawan and Saida Talib, who also deserve compliments for through their love, support, and cheer, triumph is achieved;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Tawantawan’s exceptional performance is definitely one for the books owing to the fact that for the very first time in the history of CPALE, a fellow Bangsamoro bested one of the hardest licensure examinations of the country;

**WHEREAS**, undeniably, Mr. Tawantawan is but among the greatest pride of the Bangsamoro community, and certainly a source of inspiration to all young Bangsamoro professional aspirants;

**WHEREAS**, the same recognition also goes to all new Bangsamoro Certified Public Accountants who successfully scaled what time and again has proven to be a Herculean battery of exams;

**WHEREAS**, it is of vital importance that resolute recognition be given to all Bangsamoro professionals in hurdling their respective licensure examinations to continuously ignite the youth of the Bangsamoros’ will and determination to achieve greatness and illustrate that Bangsamoros are, without a doubt, of exemplary quality;

**NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED, BY THE BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT**, to commend and congratulate the latest Bangsamoro pride, Hebban Talib Tawantawan, for ranking 1st in the recently concluded September-October 2023 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination, as well as all other new Bangsamoro Certified Public Accountants.

**RESOLVE, FURTHER**, to furnish a copy of this Resolution, either personally or through any modes of mailing, to the latest Bangsamoro pride himself, Hebban Talib Tawantawan, for information.

**ADOPTED**,

(Sgd.)
**KHALID M. HADJI ABDULLAH**
Member of Parliament
