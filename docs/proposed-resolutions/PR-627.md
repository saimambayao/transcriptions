**A RESOLUTION REQUESTING HIS EXCELLENCY PRESIDENT FERDINAND ROMUALDEZ MARCOS JR. TO HOLD IN ABEYANCE THE SERVING OF THE WARRANT OF ARRESTS INVOLVING THE LEADERS AND MEMBERS OF MORO ISLAMIC LIBERATION FRONT (MILF) AND MORO NATIONAL LIBERATION FRONT (MNLF) PENDING DECISIONS ON THEIR APPLICATION FOR AMNESTY WITH THE NATIONAL AMNESTY COMMISSION**

**WHEREAS,** part of the peace policy of the Philippine Government includes nurturing a climate conducive for peace and the implementation of the programs for reconciliation and reintegration of rebels into mainstream society;

**WHEREAS,** an amnesty program is an integral component of the national governmentâ€™s comprehensive peace efforts to bring long-term peace and stability of the country;

**WHEREAS,** after 17 years of negotiations, the Government of the Philippines reach landmark peace deal with MILF and signed a historic peace agreement with the Moro Islamic Liberation Front in Manila on 27 March 2014;

**WHEREAS,** pursuant to Paragraph 2, Section 19, Article VII of the Philippine Constitution: He (the President) shall also have the power to grant amnesty with the concurrence of a majority of all the members of the congress;

**WHEREAS,** Proclamation No. 405, and Proclamation No. 406, GRANTING AMNSETY TO MEMBERS OF THE MORO ISLAMIC LIBERATION FRONT (MILF) and MORO NATIONAL LIBERATION FRONT (MNLF) respectively, was signed by the President on November 22, 2023;

**WHEREAS,** Executive Order (EO) No. 125, Series of 2021, created the National Amnesty Commission with the mandate to receive and process applications for amnesty under Proclamation No. 1090 and 1091, all series of 2021;

**WHEREAS,** Executive Order (EO) No. 47, was signed by the President BBM on November 24, 2023, amending Executive Order No. 125, Series of 2021, entitled **CREATION OF THE NATIONAL AMNESTY COMMISSION**;

**WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 47, Section 2**, provides that the Commission created under Executive Order No. 125 shall continue to exist. It shall cease to exist upon completion of its mandate or as may be determined by the President;

**WHEREAS,** the proclamations provided for the creation of an Amnesty Commission which shall receive and process applications for amnesty and determine whether the applicants who shall apply therefore are entitled to amnesty; and

**WHEREAS,** leaders and members of the MILF and MNLF with a pending warrant of arrests, in compliance with the peace agreement, Proclamation No. 405, Proclamation No. 406 and EO No. 47, applied for amnesty program with the National Amnesty Commission;

**NOW, THEREFORE,** Be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, requesting His Excellency President Ferdinand Romualdez Marcos Jr. to hold in abeyance the serving warrant of arrests involving the leaders and members of Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) and Moro National Liberation Front (MNLF) pending decisions on their application for National Amnesty Commission.

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** to forward copy of this Resolution to His Excellency President FERDINAND MARCOS ROMUALDEZ JR. through the Office of the President at the Malacanang Palace, Metro Manila, Philippines for his information and appropriate action.

**RESOLVED FINALLY,** to furnish copy of this Resolution to the National Amnesty Commission for appropriate action.
