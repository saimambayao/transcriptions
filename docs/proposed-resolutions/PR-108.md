**RESOLUTION**

**URGING THE DEVELOPMENT ACADEMY OF THE BANGSAMORO (DAB) TO ESTABLISH AND MAINTAIN A DATABASE OF BANGSAMORO PROFESSIONALS IN AND OUTSIDE THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION**

**WHEREAS, Section 3, Article V** of Republic Act No. 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) states that the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the power expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective governance and those which are essential to the promotion of general welfare. Within its territorial jurisdiction, the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure and support, among other things, the preservation and enrichment of culture, promote health and safety, enhance the right of the people to a balanced ecology, encourage and support the development of appropriate and self-reliant scientific and technological capabilities, improve public morals, enhance economic prosperity and social justice, promote full employment among its residents, maintain peace and order, and preserve the comfort and convenience of its inhabitants;

**WHEREAS,** the Development Academy of the Bangsamoro (DAB) is a Civil Service Commission (CSC) recognized government learning and development institution which is mandated as the central human capital development training and research arm of the Bangsamoro Government for government officials and employees;

**WHEREAS,** as defined by techtarget, a database is information that is collectively set up for easy access, management and updating. It is used for storing, maintaining and accessing any sort of data. It is a collection of information on people, places or things. Such information is gathered in one place so that it can be observed and analyzed. Databases can be thought of as an organized collection of information;¹

**WHEREAS,** a database of Bangsamoro professionals shall be a systematic and organized collection of structured data involving all the professionals in and outside the Bangsamoro Region;

**WHEREAS,** no database yet exists that readily provides systematic and reliable information relating to Bangsamoro professionals in and outside the region;

**WHEREAS,** the database could provide better employment opportunities and enhanced conduct of proper trainings, thereby allowing further career advancement to the Bangsamoro professionals;

**WHEREAS,** establishing and maintaining a database system offers benefits to the region. It ensures the availability of data concerning human resources according to their profession, experiences, employment status, location, and so forth. These data can determine the strengths and weaknesses of the region’s human resources, which could further inform policy-making in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS,** the DAB as the human capital development training and research arm of the BARMM must therefore establish and maintain a database of Bangsamoro professionals;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the BTA Parliament, to pass and approve this Resolution urging the DAB to establish and maintain a database of Bangsamoro professionals in and outside the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.

**RESOLVED FINALLY,** that copy of this Resolution be furnished to the Development Academy of the Philippines for information and appropriate action.
