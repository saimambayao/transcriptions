**A RESOLUTION ENCOURAGING THE PRODUCTION AND CIRCULATION OF AN OFFICIAL VIDEO OF THE BANGSAMORO HYMN THROUGH THE BANGSAMORO INFORMATION OFFICE (BIO), IN COORDINATION WITH MBHTE AND THE OFFICE OF THE PARLIAMENT SPEAKER (OPS)**

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Article II of Republic Act No. 11054, or the Bangsamoro Organic Law, provides for the adoption of an Official Hymn of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM);

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Hymn was established by virtue of Bangsamoro Autonomy Act (BAA) No. 7 or “An Act Adopting the Official Hymn of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao,” passed and approved into law by the Bangsamoro Parliament on January 30, 2020;

**WHEREAS**, Section 4 of BAA No. 7 provides the official lyrics of the Bangsamoro Hymn is as follows:

*Simula noon hanggang ngayon*
*Iisa ang nagging layon*
*Magkaisa at magbuklod*
*Kagitingan ay marubdob*
*Tumayo tayo mula sa hamon ng nakaraan*
*Niyapos ang panganib na humahadlang*
*Pinangako sa puso at paniniwala*
*Ang ginhawang para sa kabattan*

*Bangsamoro’y tagumpay*
*Bunga ng pawis, dugo at buhay*
*Kapayapaan, katarungan*
*Ay atin nang nakamtan*
*Alhamdulillah, Alhamdulillah*

*Pagpalain Bangsamoro*
*Bangsamoro, Bangsamoro*
*Lagi kang mamahalin*
*Walang pipigil sa damdamin*
*Mga pangako’y tutuparin*
*Habang-buhay kami sayo’y magbabantay*

*Mananatili sa puso’t isipan*
*Ang kahapong humimlay na*
*Nagbuwis ng buhay*
*Nasa piling nang maykapal*

*Bangsamoro’y tagumpay*
*Bunga ng pawis, dugo at buhay*
*Kapayapaan, katarungan*
*Ay atin nang nakamtan*
*Alhamdulillah, Alhamdulillah*
*Pagpalain Bangsamoro*

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Hymn stands as a unifying symbol of the Bangsamoro identity, reflecting the collective history, aspirations, and values of the people of BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, Section 5 of BAA No. 7 provides that the rendition of the Bangsamoro Hymn, whether played or sung, shall always be in accordance with the official music arrangement and composition;

**WHEREAS**, the regular and proper rendition of the Bangsamoro Hymn in official, educational, and civic activities is essential in cultivating patriotism, regional pride, and shared purpose among the Bangsamoro people, especially the youth;

**WHEREAS**, the lack of an officially produced and widely accessible video of the Bangsamoro Hymn **often results in inconsistencies in its rendition, presentation, and usage during public events and school ceremonies**;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Information Office (BIO), as the lead communication arm of the regional government, is well-positioned to produce and disseminate an official video that is high-quality, culturally sensitive, and widely distributable across platforms and sectors;

**WHEREAS**, the Ministry of Basic, Higher and Technical Education (MBHTE) and the Office of the Parliament Speaker (OPS) can provide technical support, content guidance, and institutional endorsement to ensure that the official video adheres to historical, educational, and ceremonial standards;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, to encourage the production and circulation of an official video of the Bangsamoro Hymn through the Bangsamoro Information Office (BIO), in coordination with the Ministry of Basic, Higher and Technical Education (MBHTE) and the Office of the Parliament Speaker (OPS).

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that such a video shall be made available for use in government and private offices, educational institutions, and community activities, and be distributed through appropriate media and digital platforms for public access and awareness.

**RESOLVED FINALLY**, that copies of this Resolution be furnished to the BIO, MBHTE, OPS, and other concerned agencies for their guidance and appropriate action.
