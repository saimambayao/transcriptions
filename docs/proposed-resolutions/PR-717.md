**A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE FULL SUPPORT OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT FOR SECRETARY CARLITO G. GALVEZ, JR., PRESIDENTIAL ADVISER ON PEACE, RECONCILIATION, AND UNITY, AND RESPECTFULLY URGING HIS CONTINUED APPOINTMENT TO SUSTAIN PEACE EFFORTS IN THE BANGSAMORO REGION**

**WHEREAS**, President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr. has requested the courtesy resignations of Cabinet officials in a move to evaluate departmental performance and enhance the overall delivery of government services;

**WHEREAS**, Secretary Carlito G. Galvez, Jr., Presidential Adviser on Peace, Reconciliation, and Unity, has complied with the directive while continuing to uphold his mandate with dedication and sincerity;

**WHEREAS**, Secretary Galvez has been pivotal in moving forward the implementation of the 1996 Final Peace Agreement between the Government of the Philippines and the Moro National Liberation Front (MNLF), including the operationalization of the MNLF Peace Implementing Committee;

**WHEREAS**, under his leadership, the Office of the Presidential Adviser on Peace, Reconciliation and Unity (OPAPRU) has actively supported the delivery of transitional justice and development programs in partnership with the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, the MNLF Central Committee, through a statement issued by Vice Chairman concurrent Acting Chairman, Ustadz Abdulkarim Tan Misuari, on May 26, 2025, affirmed its support for Secretary Galvez and recognized his vital role in maintaining the momentum of peace implementation;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to express its highest commendation and continued support for Secretary Carlito G. Galvez, Jr., for his unwavering commitment to peace and reconciliation in the Bangsamoro Homeland;

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament respectfully urges President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr. to retain Secretary Galvez as Presidential Adviser on Peace, Reconciliation and Unity, in order to preserve institutional continuity and sustain the integrity of peacebuilding efforts in BARMM;

**RESOLVED FINALLY**, that copies of this Resolution be transmitted to the Office of the President, the Office of the Presidential Adviser on Peace, Reconciliation and Unity, the MNLF Central Committee, and relevant agencies for their information and appropriate action.

Adopted this _ day of ______ 2025.
