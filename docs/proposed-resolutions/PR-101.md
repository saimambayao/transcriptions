**A RESOLUTION URGING CONCERNED BARMM MINISTRIES AND LGUs TO PROVIDE HOUSING SETTLEMENT FOR VICTIMS OF TYPHOON PAENG IN BARMM AREAS WHO WERE RENDERED HOMELESS.**

**WHEREAS,** it is the policy of the State pursuant to Section 9, Article XIII of the 1987 Constitution to ensure that underprivileged and homeless citizens have access to an adequate, safe, secure, habitable, sustainable, resilient and affordable home;

**WHEREAS,** Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law mandates, thus:

**ARTICLE IX**
**BASIC RIGHTS**

**Section 8. Right of Access to Basic Services.** - The Bangsamoro Government shall provide, maintain, and ensure the delivery of basic and responsive health programs, quality education, appropriate services, livelihood opportunities, affordable and progressive housing projects, power and electricity, and water inhabitants of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. It shall maintain appropriate disaster-preparedness units for immediate and effective relief services to victims of natural and man-made calamities. It shall also ensure that rehabilitation of calamity-effected areas and victims of calamities.

**Section 9. Housing and Human Settlements.** - The Parliament shall pass a law creating a housing and human settlements agency to address the lack of shelters, settlements, and livelihood for the disadvantaged and homeless, especially those victims of conflicts and atrocities. The agency may directly solicit and receive assistance, donations, aids, and grants from donors for its housing, settlements, and livelihood programs and development.

The Bangsamoro Government, in cooperation with private sector shall develop its own housing and human settlement programs. (**Emphasis supplied**)

**WHEREAS,** the Ministry of Human Settlements and Development (MHSD) is the primary regional government entity responsible for the management of housing, human settlement and rural/urban development; the sole and main planning and policy-making, regulatory, program coordination, and performance monitoring entity for all housing, human settlement and rural/urban development concerns, primarily focusing on the access to and the affordability of basic human needs; and the ministry with the mandate develop and adopt a regional strategy to immediately address the provision of adequate and affordable housing to all inhabitants in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**WHEREAS,** various Bangsamoro government agencies and offices aside from the MHSD have their own settlement projects, some of which are already given their proper appropriation;

**WHEREAS,** Pambansang Pabahay Para sa Pilipino program is a housing project of the administration of President Marcos, Jr., xxx, that targets to sustain shelter for over six million families in six years.

**WHEREAS,** the Office of the Chief Minister initiated Kapayapaan sa Pamayanan (KAPYANAN) project a flagship which aimed to provide decent housing to poor communities in the autonomous region.

**WHEREAS,** on October 31, 2022 Severe Tropical Storm Paeng has left the region with most of the fatalities came from Mindanao, where they were victims of either landslides or flooding.¹

**WHEREAS,** report shows that in BARMM alone, a total number of populations affected by the tropical storm reached 568,969 or roughly half million of the population of the region or 217,719 families.²

**WHEREAS,** this pressing need for housing project aims to aid assistance to the victims of Typhoon Paeng and to recompense the internally displaced persons and families from great suffering of witnessing the death of loved ones, destruction of lives and property.

**WHEREAS,** immediate action is earnestly sought for housing settlement be awarded to homeless families primarily to barangays surged by the tropical storm. The provinces and the cities affected are as follows:³

1. Province of Basilan with five (5) municipalities affected.
2. Lanao del Sur, with six (6) municipalities affected.
3. Province of Maguindanao, combined Maguindanao del Sur and del Norte with thirty-one (31) municipalities.
4. Province of Sulu with one (1) municipality.
5. Province of Tawi-Tawi in particular the capital town of Bongao.
6. City of Cotabato; and
7. Special Geographical Area consisting of sixty-three (63) barangays in North Cotabato.

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Government recognizes the needs of housing settlement and desires that all its people be afforded the opportunity to attain a decent, safe, and social living environment.

**WHEREAS,** this Proposed Resolution encourages BARMM Government for an immediate implementation of housing settlement to the victims of Typhoon Paeng.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION URGING CONCERNED BARMM MINISTRIES AND LGUs TO PROVIDE HOUSING SETTLEMENT FOR VICTIMS OF TYPHOON PAENG IN BARMM AREAS WHO WERE RENDERED HOMELESS.**
