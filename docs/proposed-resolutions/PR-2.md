**A RESOLUTION**
**RE-AFFIRMING THE CONSENSUS OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) ON THE APPOINTMENT OF SHIEK KHALIFA USMAN NANDO AS THE WALI OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11054, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE BANGSAMORO ORGANIC LAW**

**WHEREAS,** Section 1, Article VIII of Republic Act No. 11054 provides for the appointment of the *Wali*, who shall serve as the ceremonial head of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS,** Section 2 of Republic Act No. 11593, signed into law on 28 October 2021, provides for the extension of the transition period. It provides that the BTA shall continue as the interim government in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), which shall serve until June 30, 2025, or until their successors shall have been elected and qualified;

**WHEREAS,** *Shiek Khalifa Usman Nando* possesses the qualifications of *Wali* as provided under Section 2, Article VIII of the BOL;

**WHEREAS,** *Shiek Khalifa Usman Nando* was appointed by the interim Chief Minister as the *Wali* of the Bangsamoro Government prior to the extension period. He served with utmost dedication and commitment to public service;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Transition Authority believes that *Shiek Khalifa Usman Nando* has the moral ascendancy to represent the Bangsamoro Government as its ceremonial head.

**NOW, THEREFOR, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the BTA Parliament to pass and approve this Resolution Re-Affirming the Consensus of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) on the Appointment of *Shiek Khalifa Usman Nando*, as the *Wali* of the Bangsamoro Government Pursuant to Republic Act No. 11054, Otherwise Known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law.

**BE IT RESOLVED FURTHER,** that a copy of this resolution be furnished to the Office of the Chief Minister and the Office of the *Wali*.

Adopted,

**MP ENGR. BAINTAN-ADIL AMPATUAN, MNSA**
