**RESOLUTION**

**COMMENDING THE COLLEGE OF LAW OF MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY IN MARAWI CITY AND NOTRE DAME UNIVERSITY IN COTABATO CITY IN PRODUCING FORTY-SIX (46) NEW BANGSAMORO LAWYERS IN THE RECENT 2022 BAR EXAMINATIONS**

**WHEREAS**, Section 16 of Article IX of Republic Act no. 11054 or otherwise known as the *Bangsamoro Organic Law* state that, it shall be a top priority of the Bangsamoro Government to establish, maintain, and support a complete and integrated system of quality education, which shall be a subsystem of the national education system.

**WHEREAS**, the Bar Examinations are the apex test for those aspiring to join the Philippine bar of which the Supreme court of the Philippines is the sole administrator of the examination;

**WHEREAS**, the 2022 Bar Examinations was administered under the chairmanship of Associate Justice Alfredo Benjamin S. Caguioa and the second to be conducted in a digitalized and regional format;

**WHEREAS**, the 2022 Bar Examinations were given last November 9, 13, 16, and 20, 2022 at fourteen (14) local testing locations throughout the Philippines.

**WHEREAS**, the results of 2022 Bar Examinations were released on April 14, 2023, with a 43.47% passing rate or 3,992 passers out of 9,183 takers;

**WHEREAS**, the 3,992 successful examinees took both Oath Taking and Roll Signing last May 02, 2023 at the Philippine International Convention Center (PICC) in Pasay City, Manila.

**WHEREAS**, College of Law of Mindanao State University in Marawi City and Notre Dame University in Cotabato City, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao territory's only existing law schools, produced forty-six (46) new Bangsamoro lawyers;

**WHEREAS**, the successful bar takers from Mindanao State University and Notre Dame University were nineteen (19) and twenty-seven (27), respectively.

**WHEREAS**, the increased availability of Bangsamoro lawyers in the region will enhance access to justice for those in need: Now therefore be it

**RESOLVED, BY THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY,** to commend the College of Law of Mindanao State University in Marawi City and Notre Dame University in Cotabato City in producing forty-six (46) new Bangsamoro lawyers in the recent 2022 bar examinations.

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that a copy of this resolution be furnished to the Mindanao State University in Marawi City, Notre Dame University in Cotabato City, Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP) Lanao Del Sur Chapter and IBP Cotabato Chapter.
