**RESOLUTION**

**URGING THE MINISTRY OF THE INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT (MILG) AND THE MINISTRY OF BASIC, HIGHER, AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION (MBHTE) TO ESTABLISH A “PARLIAMENTARY CORNER” IN EVERY LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNIT (LGU) AND ACADEMIC INSTITUTION IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM)**

**WHEREAS**, the right to information is a constitutionally protected right under Article III, Section 7 of the 1987 Philippine Constitution, which provides that “[T]he right of the people to information on matters of public concern shall be recognized.” This provision guarantees the public’s access to government activities and decisions, promoting transparency, accountability, and participation in governance;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) or R.A. No. 11054, upholds the principles of transparency and accountability in governance, consistent with national constitutional provisions and aligned with the promotion of the right to information;

**WHEREAS**, Article IV, Section 3 of the BOL promotes participatory governance, ensuring that all inhabitants of BARMM have the right to engage in the political processes within the region;

**WHEREAS**, Article IX, Sections 8 to 15 of the BOL emphasize the importance of social justice and the meaningful participation of all sectors, including youth, women, indigenous peoples, and other marginalized groups;

**WHEREAS**, Article IX, Section 16 of the BOL mandates the integration of Bangsamoro history, culture, and governance into the educational system;

**WHEREAS**, the Ministry of the Interior and Local Government (MILG) is tasked with general supervision over local government units (LGUs) in the Bangsamoro Government and is responsible for promoting public safety, disaster preparedness, local autonomy, decentralization, and community empowerment;

**WHEREAS**, the Ministry of Basic, Higher, and Technical Education (MBHTE) is primarily responsible for formal and non-formal education at all levels, supervising educational institutions, both public and private, and providing for the establishment, maintenance, and support of a comprehensive and integrated educational system;

**WHEREAS**, establishing a Parliamentary Corner in every LGU and academic institution within BARMM will create dedicated spaces where residents can access information related to the Bangsamoro Parliament’s legislative matters, thus promoting transparency, enhancing public participation, and empowering the people with knowledge about legislative processes;

**WHEREAS**, providing citizens with resources to understand and engage with the legislative process through a Parliamentary Corner enables them to exercise their democratic rights, fostering informed decision-making and ensuring accountability from their representatives in the Bangsamoro Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, the establishment of a Parliamentary Corner will empower grassroots communities to participate in governance by providing access to legislative information, thereby fostering inclusivity and social equity across BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, a Parliamentary Corner in academic institutions will provide students and educators with access to information on Bangsamoro legislative activities, thereby promoting political awareness, knowledge of governance, and an understanding of Bangsamoro identity and heritage; and

**WHEREAS**, it is imperative for the MILG and MBHTE to actively disseminate information and coordinate efforts to establish Parliamentary Corners across LGUs and academic institutions in the Bangsamoro region.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Parliament to urge the Ministry of the Interior and Local Government (MILG) and the Ministry of Basic, Higher, and Technical Education (MBHTE) to establish a Parliamentary Corner in every LGU and academic institution within the BARMM.

**BE IT RESOLVED FURTHER**, that a copy of this resolution be forwarded to the MILG and MBHTE for their appropriate action.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, that this Resolution shall take effect immediately upon its approval.

Adopted.
