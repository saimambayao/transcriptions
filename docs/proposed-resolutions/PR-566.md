**A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AL HAFIDZ MUZAHIR SUWEB BITO FOR WINNING 3RD PLACE IN THE 27TH SESSION OF DUBAI INTERNATIONAL HOLY QUR’AN COMPETITION HELD AT UNITED ARAB EMIRATES**

**WHEREAS,** The Bangsamoro Government recognizes the vital role of the youth in nation-building, promote and protect their moral, spiritual and social well-being, and inculcates upon them patriotism and nationalism;

**WHEREAS,** on March 12-23, 2024 / Ramadhan 2-13, 1445 AH, the 27th Session of Dubai International Holy Qur’an Competition was conducted at United Arab Emirates which caters youth aging not more than twenty-five (25) years of age;

**WHEREAS,** Al Hafidz Muzahir Suweb Bito, a 17-year old Philippine national and a resident of Datu Abdullah Sangki, Maguindanao Del Sur, is one of the seventy participants who was able to showcase his ability to compete internationally;

**WHEREAS,** Al Hafidz Muzahir Suweb Bito demonstrated outstanding performance in the event and secured the 3rd place;

**WHEREAS,** this success brings pride and prestige not only to the Philippines but most especially to the people of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro people is proud for this remarkable achievement;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament congratulates Al Hafidz Muzahir Suweb Bito for garnering 3rd place in the said event;

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To commend Al Hafidz Muzahir Suweb Bito for winning 3rd place in the 27th Session Of Dubai International Holy Qur’an Competition held at United Arab Emirates.

**Adopted,**

(Sgd.)
**SUHARTO S. ESMAEL, MSc.**
Member of the Parliament
