**PROPOSED RESOLUTION NO. 245**

Introduced By: **MP Suharto S. Esmael, MSc, MP Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba, MP Matarul M. Estino, MP Kadil M. Sinolinding, Jr., MP Mosber E. Alauddin, MP Tawakal B. Midtimbang, MP Ramon A. Piang, Sr., MP Hashemi N. Dilangalen**

**A RESOLUTION URGING THE OFFICE OF SECRETARY-GENERAL TO ESTABLISH THE PICTURE GALLERY OF THE FIRST BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT MEMBERS**

**WHEREAS**, it is enshrined in Section II of Article VII of Republic Act No. 11054 or otherwise known as the *Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao*, provides that the powers of government be vested in the Parliament which shall exercise those powers and functions expressly granted to it in this Organic Law, and those necessary for, or incidental to, the proper governance and development of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. It shall set policies, legislate on matters within its authority, and elect a Chief Minister who shall exercise executive authority on its behalf;

**WHEREAS**, Section 11 of Article VII of the same law provides that the term of office of members of the parliament shall be three (3) years;

**WHEREAS**, through professionalism, efficiency and immense hard work, the Parliament functioned to fully realize the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region’s change and development agenda that will make a positive impact on the lives of the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, BTA-Parliament worked faithfully, and thus, passed priority socio-economic measures enumerated under Republic Act No. 11054, the Bangsamoro Organic Law;

**WHEREAS**, the first BTA-Parliament enacted three (3) priority codes, which are now landmark laws, such as;

*   Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13, otherwise known as the ‘Bangsamoro Administrative Code. Entitled’.

*   Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 17, otherwise known as the, “Bangsamoro Civil Service Code”

*   Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 18, otherwise known as the “Bangsamoro Education Code”

**WHEREAS**, the Members of the Parliament, regardless of political affiliations, beliefs and direction, have successfully forged a harmonious working relationship with each other, with the Executive Department and other offices;

**WHEREAS**, the Members of the Parliament express their unwavering support and appreciation for the commitment of the Bangsamoro government to serve its constituents;

**WHEREAS**, to implement a directory in the chambers of the parliament is to mark an important history of the Bangsamoro government and to honor the Members of the Parliament for their invaluable contributions during the first transition period covering years 2019 to 2022; Now therefore, be it

**RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, urging the office of Secretary-General to establish the picture gallery of the first Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament Members;

**RESOLVED FURTHER** those copies of this resolution be furnished to all members of the first BTA parliament, ministers, deputy ministers, and Office of the Chief Minister;

**RESOLVED FINALLY**, that an official pictorial directory be displayed in the conspicuous place of the Parliamentary Building.
