**BTA RESOLUTION NO. 557**

**Introduced by:**
**MP DR. SUSANA SALVADOR - ANAYATIN**

**A RESOLUTION CONDEMNING THE TRAGIC KILLING OF CHRISTIA ANGELA O. ARAGONCILLO EXTENDING CONDOLENCES AND SYMPATHY TO THE BEREAVED FAMILIES AND CALLING ALL CONCERNED AUTHORITIES TO EXPEDITE THE INVESTIGATION SURROUNDING THE INCIDENT AND BRING THE PERPETRATORS TO JUSTICE**

**WHEREAS,** Section I, of the Bill of Rights states that "*No person shall be deprived of life, liberty, or property without due process of law, nor shall any person be denied the equal protection of the laws.*";

**WHEREAS,** the right to life is enshrined in Article 3 of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights and Article 6 of the International Covenant on Civil and Political Rights, which provides that "*Every human being has the inherent right to life. This right shall be protected by law. No one shall be arbitrarily deprived of his life.*";

**WHEREAS,** Section 6 of Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054, also known as "*Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao*" states that "*The Bangsamoro Government shall fully respect human rights.*";

**WHEREAS,** on April 17, 2024, the community in Barangay Semba, Datu Odin Sinsuat, Maguindanao del Norte were deeply disturbed of the shooting incident resulting in the death of Christia Angela O. Aragoncillo, the Logistics Management Officer of the Ministry of Social Services and Development and the injury of the two by standers;

**WHEREAS,** according to the information gathered by Brigada News TV-Cotabato, Aragoncillo was inside a car and on their way home when she was attacked by unidentified assailants. The men responsible for the attack, which resulted in Aragoncillo's untimely death, swiftly fled the scene;

**WHEREAS,** the Datu Odin Sinsuat Municipal Police Station is continuing its investigation and awaiting further reports into the motive and identity of suspects behind the shooting;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament condemns in the strongest terms the tragic killing of Christia Angela O. Aragoncillo, which represents a flagrant violation of her inherent human rights and a profound loss to her family, community, and society as a whole;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament expresses its heartfelt condolences to the family, friends, and loved ones of Chirstia Angela O. Aragoncillo, as well as those affected by this tragic loss, and acknowledges their profound grief and suffering;

**WHEREAS,** this Resolution calls upon all concerned authorities to fully investigate this heinous crime, bring the perpetrators to justice, and ensure that such acts of violence are met with the full force of the law, in accordance with their obligations and commitments;

**WHEREAS,** this Resolution also urges all members of Bangsamoro government to take effective measures to prevent and combat all forms of violence, discrimination, and intolerance, including those directed against women and vulnerable groups, and to promote a culture of peace, dialogue, and understanding within their societies;

**WHEREAS,** that this Resolution be widely disseminated throughout the Bangsamoro Region to enhance awareness and promote solidarity against violence and crime;

**WHEREAS,** a copy of this resolution be transmitted to the local authorities and representatives as a tangible demonstration of our unified stance against such heinous acts.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION CONDEMNING THE TRAGIC KILLING OF CHRISTIA ANGELA O. ARAGONCILLO EXTENDING CONDOLENCES AND SYMPATHY TO THE BEREAVED FAMILIES AND CALLING ALL CONCERNED AUTHORITIES TO EXPEDITE THE INVESTIGATION SURROUNDING THE INCIDENT AND BRING THE PERPETRATORS TO JUSTICE**

**Adopted,** April ____, 2024.
