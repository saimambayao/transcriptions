**A RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT OF THE DAY TO ENACT A LAW MAINSTREAMING PEACE EDUCATION IN ALL LEVELS OF EDUCATION PURSUANT TO EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 570 entitled “INSTITUTIONALIZING PEACE EDUCATION IN BASIC EDUCATION AND TEACHER EDUCATION” AND SENATE BILL NO. 672 entitled “INSTITUTIONALIZING PEACE EDUCATION IN THE SCHOOL CURRICULUM AT ALL LEVELS”.**

**WHEREAS**, the Philippines adheres to the policy of peace, equality, justice, and freedom as enunciated in the 1987 Philippine Constitution.

**WHEREAS**, **Section 20, Article X** of the 1987 Philippine Constitution reads:

> Within its territorial jurisdiction and subject to the provisions of this Constitution and national laws, the organic act of autonomous regions shall provide for legislative powers over:
> xxx
> (7) Educational policies.

**WHEREAS**, **Section 1, Article XIV** of the same law mentioned above, [T]he State shall: Establish maintain, and support a complete, adequate, and integrated system of education relevant to the needs of the people and society.

**WHEREAS**, **Section 16, paragraph 2, Article IX** of R.A. No. 11054 otherwise known as Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that, the Bangsamoro Government shall institutionalize peace education in all levels of education.

**WHEREAS**, **Chapter 2, Section 55** of BA Act. No 18 otherwise known as Bangsamoro Education Code states that: Peace Education shall be an integral part of the Basic Education Curriculum design to instill in the learners the culture of nonviolent culture, social justice and respect for human rights, freedom, and inclusivity.

**WHEREAS**, as enunciated in **Section 3 (d), Title II, Chapter I** of BAA13 Administrative Code, the Ministry of Basic Higher and Technical Education shall have the following powers and functions: xxx

> (d) Institutionalize peace education in all levels of education.

**WHEREAS**, in relation to the above-mentioned provisions, the then President Gloria Macapagal – Arroyo and Senator Paolo Binigno “Bam” A. Aquino introduced Executive Order No. 570 and Senate Bill No. 672 respectively:

> **EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 570** calling for **INSTITUTIONALIZING PEACE EDUCATION IN BASIC EDUCATION AND TEACHER EDUCATION** was introduced by the then President Gloria Macapagal – Arroyo in September 26, 2006.
> 
> Senate Bill No. 672 was introduced by Senator Paolo Binigno “Bam” A. Aquino, IV also calls for **INSTITUTIONALIZING PEACE EDUCATION IN THE SCHOOL CURRICULUM AT ALL LEVELS.**

**WHEREAS**, this Proposed Resolution aims to mainstream peace education in all levels of formal and non-formal education curriculum considering the religious pluralism, gender and sexuality, and cultural diversity in the Bangsamoro Region.

**WHEREAS**, this is in pursuit to Bangsamoro aspiration of reducing armed conflicts, crimes, and all forms of violence and to save succeeding generations from the scourge of dispute.

**WHEREAS**, in hope to solve historical conflict that hinders unity, advancement, and economic development in the region, Peace Education is one of the solutions, it calls to be integrated in all levels of education.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT OF THE DAY TO ENACT A LAW MAINSTREAMING PEACE EDUCATION IN ALL LEVELS OF EDUCATION PURSUANT TO EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 570 entitled “INSTITUTIONALIZING PEACE EDUCATION IN BASIC EDUCATION AND TEACHER EDUCATION” AND SENATE BILL NO. 672 entitled “INSTITUTIONALIZING PEACE EDUCATION IN THE SCHOOL CURRICULUM AT ALL LEVELS”.**
