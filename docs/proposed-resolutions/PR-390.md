**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING FADZRATI D. ASARUD, A BANGSAMORO YOUTH FROM TAWI-TAWI, FOR ATTAINING TOP 5 RANK WITH A RATING OF 88.20% OUT OF 6,133 PASSERS IN THE AUGUST 2023 PSYCHOMETRICIANS LICENSURE EXAMINATION ADMINISTERED BY THE PROFESSIONAL REGULATION COMMISSION (PRC) AND THE BOARD OF PSYCHOLOGY**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Parliament recognizes the importance of acknowledging exceptional accomplishments and celebrating the success of individuals who bring honor to the Bangsamoro community;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Fadzrati D. Asarud has achieved a remarkable feat by securing the 5th place with an impressive rating of 88.20% in the August 2023 Psychometricians Licensure Examination administered by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and the Board of Psychology;

**WHEREAS**, the result of the 2023 Psychometricians Licensure Examination was released on August 9, 2023, with a passing rate of 73.27% or 6,133 out of 8,370 examinees;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Asarud graduated Cum Laude in her undergraduate studies at Ateneo de Zamboanga University, exemplifying her consistent dedication to academic excellence; and an alumna of the Mindanao State University Tawi-Tawi College of Technology and Oceanography (MSU-TCTO) Science High School and Child Development Center-Laboratory Elementary School;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Asarud's outstanding performance in the licensure examination sets an admirable example and inspiration for the Bangsamoro youth;

**WHEREAS**, her remarkable achievement not only underscores her personal accomplishment but also brings pride and distinction to ADZU and MSU-TCTO Science High School and Child Development Center-Laboratory Elementary School, affirming their reputation as academically acclaimed nurturing institutions;

**NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament to congratulate FADZRATI D. ASARUD, a Bangsamoro youth from Tawi-Tawi, for attaining the top 5 rank with a rating of 88.20% out of 6,133 passers in the August 2023 Psychometricians Licensure Examination administered by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and the Board of Psychology.

**RESOLVED FINALLY**, a copy of this resolution be furnished Ms. Fadzrati D. Asarud.
