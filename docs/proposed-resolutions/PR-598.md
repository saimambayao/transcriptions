**RESOLUTION**
**DIRECTING THE MINISTRY OF PUBLIC WORKS (MPW) TO ESTABLISH, ORGANIZE, AND OPERATIONALIZE ITS QUALITY ASSURANCE UNIT (QAU) TO ENSURE QUALITY OF PUBLIC WORKS IMPLEMENTATION IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION**

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Article IV of Republic Act No. 11054, or the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL), provides that in the exercise of its right to self-governance, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region is free to pursue its political, economic, social and cultural development as provided for in the Organic Law;

**WHEREAS**, Section 3, Article V of the BOL provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the power expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective governance and those which are essential to the promotion of general welfare. Within its territorial jurisdiction, the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure and support, among other things, the preservation and enrichment of culture, promote health and safety, enhance the right of the people to a balanced ecology, encourage and support the development of appropriate and self-reliant scientific and technological capabilities, improve public morals, enhance economic prosperity and social justice, promote full employment among its residents, maintain peace and order, and preserve the comfort and convenience of its inhabitants;

**WHEREAS**, under Section 8, Article XVI of the BOL, 15 primary ministries are created to serve as the institutional mechanisms for the implementation of good governance policies in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. One of the primary ministries is the Ministry of Public Works (MPW) that takes the lead in the planning and implementation of infrastructure plans and programs;

**WHEREAS**, the MPW is responsible for the affairs of the Bangsamoro region relating to the construction and maintenance of government infrastructure in the region;

**WHEREAS**, the MPW performs infrastructure planning, such as roads and bridges, flood control, water resource projects and other public works, the maintenance of national roads and bridges, and major flood control system, and regulating the safety of all public and private structures in the region by adopting appropriate laws, policies and rules;

**WHEREAS**, the delivery of basic infrastructure services of the MPW through its programs, projects, and services are implemented by eight (8) District Engineering Offices and four (4) Area Equipment Services as support to the social and economic well-being of its constituents living in the six (6) provinces of BARMM – Maguindanao del Norte, Maguindanao del Sur, Lanao del Sur, Basilan, Sulu and Tawi-Tawi, as well as the newly included City of Cotabato and 63 barangays of the Cotabato province¹;

**WHEREAS**, public works are vital to society and the economy because they enhance the social infrastructure that supports the lives of every Bangsamoro, as well as present and future economic activity;

**WHEREAS**, efficient safety and quality assurance of all the programs and projects of the MPW are essential for reducing risks and improving project implementation outcomes;

**WHEREAS**, at the national level, the Quality Assurance Unit (QAU) was created through Department Order No. 26, series of 1990, and QAU-Regional Office (QAU-RO) through Department Order No. 169, series of 1991, who shall perform project assessment of the quality of construction and compliance with plans and specifications of both on-going and completed projects²;

**WHEREAS**, the quality of public works in the Bangsamoro region needs to be guaranteed by the MPW, thus the need for its QAU to be established, organized, and operationalized;

**WHEREAS**, it is imperative to increase the welfare and the steady expansion of the economy of the Bangsamoro region by resolving the basic challenges surrounding the quality of public works in the region; and

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, as the interim government of the Bangsamoro region, must ensure and advance quality assurance in public works both now and in the future.

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the BTA Parliament to pass and approve this Resolution Directing the Ministry of Public Works to establish, organize, and operationalize its Quality Assurance Unit (QAU) to ensure the quality of public works implementation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.

**RESOLVED FINALLY** that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to the Ministry of Public Works for information and appropriate action.
