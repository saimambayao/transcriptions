**A RESOLUTION**

**REQUESTING THE MINISTRY OF AGRICULTURE, FISHERIES, AND AGRARIAN REFORM TO IMPLEMENT EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 75 SERIES OF 2019 WITHIN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION BY INCLUDING THE NECESSARY OPERATING COST OF IMPLEMENTATION IN THE MINISTRY’S PROPOSED BUDGET FOR 2024**

**WHEREAS,** Executive Order No. 75 series of 2019 directed all departments, bureaus, offices and instrumentalities of the government to identify lands owned by the government devoted to or suitable for agriculture for distribution to qualified beneficiaries;

**WHEREAS,** Executive Order No. 75 series of 2019 applies to alienable and disposable lands of public domain which are within the jurisdiction of the Bangsamoro Government provided that the conditions prescribed in the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) and Bangsamoro Administrative Code (BAC) are strictly complied with;

**WHEREAS,** Section 1 of Executive Order No. 75 series of 2019 states that “Subject to the limitations and conditions provided under applicable laws, rules and issuances, the DAR shall acquire all lands devoted to or suitable for agriculture, which are owned by the departments, bureaus, offices and instrumentalities of the Government, and which are no longer actually, directly and exclusively used or necessary for the purpose for which they have been reserved or acquired, and thereafter submit list thereof to the DAR, indicating the location and area of the said lands, actual use and legal basis of ownership;

**WHEREAS,** Section 4 of Executive Order No. 75 series of 2019 states that “Subject to restrictions under applicable laws, rules, and issuances, the DAR shall acquire the identified lands of concerned agencies for eventual distribution thereof to qualified beneficiaries, for cultivation or agricultural use;

**WHEREAS,** Section 7 of Executive Order No. 75 series of 2019 provides that the amount necessary for the implementation of this Order shall be charged against sources to be identified by the Department of Budget and Management. Thereafter, the funding requirements for the implementation of this Order shall be included in the annual budget of the concerned agencies;

**WHEREAS,** the Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries, and Agrarian Reform and the Department of Agrarian Reform shall enter into Memorandum of Agreement relative to the implementation of EO 75 within Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS,** the Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries, and Agrarian Reform and the Department of Agrarian Reform signed a Memorandum of Agreement last May 25, 2021 to collaborate in the implementation of EO 75 within Lanao del Sur and Marawi City for the distribution of all unused Government-owned Lands to qualified beneficiaries;

**WHEREAS,** a DAR-MAFAR Joint Administrative Order No. 7 Series of 2021 was signed between DAR and MAFAR pursuant to the signed MOA for the rules and regulations that shall govern the identification, validation, segregation, transfer, and distribution of all Government-owned Lands in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region which are suitable or devoted to agriculture which are no longer actually, directly, and exclusively used or necessary for the purpose for which they have been reserved or acquired for eventual distribution to qualified beneficiaries in accordance with EO 75 and other pertinent laws;

**WHEREAS,** the DAR-MAFAR Joint Administrative Order No. 7 Series of 2021 provides that all transferred Government-owned Lands which were acquired by a department, bureau, office or instrumentality of the Government through purchase shall be subject to payment of compensation or reimbursement in accordance with the rules and guidelines to be formulated by the DBM, Department of Finance, and the Landbank o the Philippines;

**WHEREAS,** Section 2 Article IX of the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that the Parliament shall enact laws providing for adequate reparation to the Bangsamoro people affected by unjust dispossession of territorial and proprietary rights and customary land tenure, which may include just compensation to and relocation of such people. No land title issued by the National Government under the Torrens System shall be invalidated;

**WHEREAS,** given that deliberation on the budget 2024 is imminent, the Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Agrarian Reform is requested to incorporate the essential operational expenses for the implementation of Executive Order No. 75, Series of 2019 within its proposed budget for 2024;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to request the Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries, and Agrarian Reform to implement Executive Order No. 75 Series of 2019 within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region by including the necessary operating cost of implementation in the Ministry’s proposed budget for 2024.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** to require the Bangsamoro Government through the Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries, and Agrarian Reform to provide an update on the status of all Government Owned Lands already covered by the Executive Order No. 75 Series of 2019 as well as other Government-owned Land yet to be covered within the Bangsamoro Region.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY,** that a copy of this resolution be furnished to the Office of the Chief Minister and the Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries, and Agrarian Reform of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, for their information.
