**RESOLUTION COMMENDING SAKINUR-AIN MUGONG DELASAS, A NATIVE SAMA OF TAWI-TAWI, FOR BEING DECLARED MANLILIKHA NG BAYAN 2023**

**WHEREAS,** Section 15, Article XIV of the 1987 Constitution of the Philippines emphasizes the patronage of the State for arts and letters, as well as the conservation, promotion, and popularization of the nation's historical and cultural heritage and resources, including artistic creations;

**WHEREAS,** Republic Act (RA) No. 7355 recognizes "National Living Treasures" or "Manlilikha ng Bayan," referring to citizens or groups engaged in traditional Filipino arts, who have achieved a high level of technical and artistic excellence;

**WHEREAS,** Sec. 3, Article V of Republic Act 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that, "The Bangsamoro Government shall ensure and support, among other things, the preservation and enrichment of culture;"

**WHEREAS,** Sec. 24, Article IX of Republic Act 11054, further provides that, "The Bangsamoro Government shall preserve the history, culture, arts, traditions, and the rich cultural heritage of the Bangsamoro people;"

**WHEREAS,** Proclamation No. 427 issued by the President of the Philippines has declared nine individuals as Manlilikha ng Bayan for the year 2023, and among them is Sakinur-ain Mugong Delasas, a native Sama, and a Sama master of traditional dance from Simunul, Tawi-Tawi;

**WHEREAS,** Sakinur-ain Mugong Delasas is acknowledged for her unwavering commitment to safeguarding and promoting the Sama igal (dance) tradition, thereby contributing significantly to the preservation and enrichment of the cultural heritage of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** that the Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao extends its heartfelt congratulations and commendation to Sakinur-ain Mugong Delasas for being declared Manlilikha ng Bayan 2023;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED,** that this Parliament recognizes and appreciates the exceptional dedication and artistic excellence demonstrated by Sakinur-ain Mugong Delasas in preserving and promoting the Sama igal tradition, a valuable cultural heritage of the Sama community, of Tawi-Tawi, and of our region;

**BE IT FINALLY RESOLVED,** that a copy of this resolution be furnished to Sakinur-ain Mugong Delasas, Bangsamoro Commission for the Preservation of Cultural Heritage, and the National Commission for Culture and the Arts (NCCA).
