**A RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE GOVERNMENT OF THE DAY TO ENACT A BANGSAMORO CONSUMER ACT AND ESTABLISH COMPREHENSIVE MEASURES THAT PROMOTE FAIR AND TRANSPARENT BUSINESS PRACTICES, PROTECT CONSUMERS FROM DECEPTIVE PRACTICES, AND CONTRIBUTE TO THE OVERALL ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT AND SOCIAL WELL-BEING OF THE BANGSAMORO REGION.**

**WHEREAS**, the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides for the protection and promotion of consumers, under the following articles to wit:

> “The State shall protect Filipino enterprises against unfair foreign competition and trade practices” (Article XII, Section 1);
>
> “The State shall pursue a trade policy that serves the general welfare and utilizes all forms and arrangements of exchange on the basis of equality and reciprocity” (Article XII, Section 13);
>
> “The State shall regulate or prohibit monopolies when the public interest so requires. No combinations in restraint of trade or unfair competition shall be allowed” (Article XII, Section 19);
>
> “The State shall protect consumers from trade malpractices and from substandard or hazardous products” (Article XVI, Section 9);
>
> “The advertising industry is impressed with public interest, and shall be regulated by law for the protection of consumers and the promotion of the general welfare” [Article XVI, Section 11(2)].

**WHEREAS**, as declared under R.A. No. 7394 or The Consumer Act of the Philippines, it is the policy of the State to protect the interests of the consumers, promote their general welfare, and establish standards of conduct for business and industry. Towards this end, the State shall implement measures to achieve the following objectives:

> i. Protection against hazards to health safety;
> ii. Protection against deceptive, unfair, and unconscionable sales acts and practices;
> iii. provision of information and education to facilitate sound choice and the proper exercise of rights by the consumer;
> iv. provision of adequate rights and means of redress; and
> v. involvement of consumer representatives in the formulation of social and economic policies.

**WHEREAS**, under Article V, Section 2 of the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise its authority over the following matters without prejudice to the general supervision of the President of the Republic of the Philippines:
> xxx
> (zz) Trade and Industry
> xxx

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, as an integral part of the Philippines, recognizes the need for a comprehensive consumer protection framework tailored to the unique cultural, economic, and social conditions of the region providing particular emphasis on the halal industry;

**WHEREAS**, this Proposed Resolution aims to establish the Bangsamoro Consumer Act legal framework for the protection of consumers in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region; to ensure the right to fair business practices, accurate information, and redress for grievances. This also aims to foster a marketplace where consumers can make informed choices, confident in the knowledge that their rights are protected, and where consumers can actively contribute to the region’s economic growth.

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Consumer Act shall provide prohibitions on deceptive and unfair trade practices, including false advertising, misleading labeling, and the sale of substandard goods. It shall appoint and empower regulatory bodies within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region to enforce and investigate violations of fair-trade standards.

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Consumer Act shall establish programs to educate consumers about their rights, responsibilities, and avenues for redress. The programs shall include public awareness campaigns, educational materials, and community outreach initiatives.

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Consumer Act shall set forth stringent standards for product safety and quality to protect consumers from harm. Manufacturers and sellers must comply with these standards, and penalties for non-compliance will be enforced.

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Consumer Act shall create a transparent and accessible mechanism for the resolution of consumer disputes. This shall involve the establishment of consumer protection offices, mediation services, the accessible legal avenues for seeking redress.

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Consumer Act shall strengthen local consumer protection authorities within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, providing them with the necessary resources and authority to enforce consumer protection laws effectively.

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Consumer Act shall allocate resources for its effective implementation and enforcement. To empower regulatory bodies, law enforcement agencies, and other relevant entities shall take necessary actions against violators and ensure the provision of adequate support for consumer protection initiatives.

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Consumer Act shall establish a periodic review process to assess the effectiveness of the Bangsamoro Consumer Protection Act; to provide allowance for amendments necessary to adapt to the evolving market dynamics and emerging consumer protection challenges.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE GOVERNMENT OF THE DAY TO ENACT A BANGSAMORO CONSUMER ACT AND ESTABLISH COMPREHENSIVE MEASURES THAT PROMOTE FAIR AND TRANSPARENT BUSINESS PRACTICES, PROTECT CONSUMERS FROM DECEPTIVE PRACTICES, AND CONTRIBUTE TO THE OVERALL ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT AND SOCIAL WELL-BEING OF THE BANGSAMORO REGION.**
