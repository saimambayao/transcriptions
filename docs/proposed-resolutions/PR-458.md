**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING BAI REHANA MARAJAN LAUBAN FOR RANKING 9th IN THE OCTOBER 2023 FISHERIES TECHNOLOGIST LICENSURE EXAMINATION (FTLE) AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS FOR BRINGING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

WHEREAS, the Fisheries Technologist Licensure Examination (FTLE) was conducted from October 25 to 26, 2023 across sixteen (16) local testing centers throughout the Philippines administered by the Professional Regulation Commission;

WHEREAS, on November 6, 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced the results of the October 2023 Fisheries Technologist Licensure Examination, revealing that out of two thousand one hundred twenty-four (2,124) examinees, only seven hundred sixteen (716) or thirty-three point seventy one percent (33.71%) successfully passed the examination;

WHEREAS, Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban has displayed exceptional dedication and perseverance in passing the October 2023 Fisheries Technologist Licensure Examination and securing the remarkable position of 9th rank with an average score of eighty-four point fifty zero percent (84.50%) in the said rigorous examination administered by the Professional Regulation Commission;

WHEREAS, Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban is a young 23-year old Maguindanaon from Kabuntalan, Maguindanao Del Norte, an esteemed alumna of Mindanao State University – Maguindanao and a proud daughter of Mr. Hadji Badrudin T. Taug and Mrs. Laguiabai L. Lauban;

WHEREAS, the achievement of Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban and all the successful Bangsamoro passers in the October 2023 Fisheries Technologist Licensure Examination has not only showcased the competitive spirit and excellence of the Bangsamoro people at a national level but also become a source of immense pride and inspiration for all young Filipinos, especially the Bangsamoro youth and children;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, recognizing the remarkable accomplishments of Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban and all other Bangsamoro passers of the October 2023 Fisheries Technologist Licensure Examination, deems it fitting to commend their unwavering commitment to hard work and excellence;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to congratulate and commend Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban for ranking 9th in the October 2023 Fisheries Technologist Licensure Examination (FTLE) and all Bangsamoro passers for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, to present a copy of this resolution to Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban as a token of our recognition and appreciation for her achievement as well as for this resolution’s necessary effects.
