**A RESOLUTION OF THANKS AND GRATITUDE TO MALAYSIAN PRIME MINISTER DATO’ SERI ANWAR IBRAHIM FOR THE CONTINUING SUPPORT AND CONCERN OF THE BANGSAMORO PEACE PROCESS.**

**WHEREAS**, during the state visit in the Philippines of Malaysian Prime Minister Minister Dato’ Seri Anwar Ibrahim on March 1, 2023 at Malacañang, Manila, he expressed his commitment to continue their role in supporting the Bangsamoro Peace Process in pursuit of attaining lasting peace and development in Mindanao. He also stated that his government will provide assistance through various capacity-building programs to “former fighters so they can be involved in normal employment, which in the long run will contribute to long lasting peace”;

**WHEREAS**, the Malaysian Prime Minister subsequently met the Bangsamoro Chief Minister Ahod Ebrahim and had a telephone conversation with MNLF Founding Chairman Prof. Nur P. Misuari and discussed the assurances about cooperation so that reconciliation, especially among Muslims in the south of the Philippines, can be well established to ensure success of thousands of former fighters under the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) and the Moro National Liberation Front (MNLF);

**WHEREAS**, the Malaysian Prime Minister expressed that Malaysia has the “responsibility as a neighboring country, a Muslim country as well, to ensure that there is meaningful success and lasting peace in southern Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, the Malaysian Prime Minister expressed profound thanks and gratitude to His Excellency President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr. for taking great strides “On the peace process in Mindanao” and further expressed that “It is only our duty, as a good neighbor, to support and facilitate the process,”;

**WHEREAS**, the concern of the Malaysia Prime Minister for the Muslims in Southern Philippines exemplifies his adherence to the message of Prophet Muhammad, the Messenger of Allah ﷺ who said “The parable of the believers in their affection, mercy, and compassion for each other is that of a body. When any limb aches, the whole body reacts with sleeplessness and fever”.

**NOW THEFEFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, that the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, convey its gratitude and thanks to Malaysian Prime Minister Dato’ Seri Anwar Ibrahim for expressing the continuing concern and support of the Malaysian Government to the interest of peace process in Southern Philippines to ensure success of the plight of the Bangsamoro people for lasting peace and development in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao and in their homeland.
