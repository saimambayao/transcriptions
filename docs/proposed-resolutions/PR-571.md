**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MR. SAADANY PANUMPANG AMINODEN FOR BEING THE TOP 4 IN THE 2024 REGISTERED ELECTRICAL ENGINEERS LICENSURE EXAMINATION (REELE) AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS**

**WHEREAS**, on May 02, 2024, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced that 4,436 out of 7,005 passed the Registered Electrical Engineers Licensure Examination given by the Board of Electrical Engineering in NCR, Baguio, Butuan, Cagayan de Oro, Cebu, Davao, Iloilo, Legazpi, Lucena, Pampanga, Rosales, Tacloban, Tuguegarao, Zamboanga and Puerto Princesa, Palawan;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Saadany P. Aminoden, a Bangsamoro and native of Tugaya, Lanao del Sur, placed 4th in the aforementioned Licensure examination with a general average of 93.50%;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Aminoden graduated from Mindanao State University-Main Campus in Marawi City with a latin honor of Magna Cum Laude;

**WHEREAS**, both parents Aminoden Amito Edaros and Norhaya Papacol Panumpang are residents of Tugaya, Lanao del Sur and engaged into family owned business;

**WHEREAS**, placing 4th in any given licensure examination is a rare opportunity that gives utmost pride and honor not only to the family, school, tribe but the entire Bangsamoro people that inspires the young and aspiring students to follow the exemplary achievement;

**NOW THEREFORE**, be it **RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to congratulate and commend Mr. Saadany Panumpang Aminoden for placing 4th in the recent 2024 Registered Electrical Engineers Licensure Examination (REELE) and all Bangsamoro passers;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that the copy of this Resolution be forwarded to Mr. Saadany Panumpang Aminoden.
