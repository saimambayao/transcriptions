**RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING ALNAKRAN “RANRAN” ABDILLA FOR WINNING A BRONZE MEDAL IN THE MEN’S BEACH VOLLEYBALL COMPETITION AT THE 32ND SOUTHEAST ASIAN GAMES IN CAMBODIA**

**WHEREAS**, the Philippines secured a bronze medal in the men’s beach volleyball competition in the 32nd Southeast Asian (SEA) Games in Cambodia for the 3rd consecutive time;

**WHEREAS**, Alnakran “Ranran” Abdilla, a native of Tawi-Tawi, exhibited remarkable skill, dedication, and sportsmanship as a member of the Philippine men’s national beach volleyball team;

**WHEREAS**, Alnakran Abdilla’s achievement is a testament to his hard work, dedication, and perseverance in representing the Bangsamoro and the Philippines in an international sporting event;

**WHEREAS**, Alnakran Abdilla’s accomplishment has brought pride and honor to the region and the country and has inspired many Bangsamoro, especially the youth, to pursue their dreams and strives for excellence;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, that this Parliament commends and congratulates Alnakran “Ranran” Abdilla for his outstanding achievement in winning a bronze medal in the men’s beach volleyball competition at the 32nd Southeast Asian (SEA) Games in Cambodia;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that every member of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament shall give a cash incentive of two thousand five hundred pesos (₱2,500.00) to Alnakran “Ranran” Abdilla in recognition of such outstanding achievement; and the Bangsamoro Youth Commission shall provide for the round trip airfare tickets, accommodation and food while in Cotabato to receive the cash incentive and the resolution;

**BE IT FINALLY RESOLVED**, that a copy of this resolution be furnished to Alnakran “Ranran” Abdilla as a token of recognition and appreciation for his remarkable achievement.
