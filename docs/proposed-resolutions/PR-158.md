**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST GRATITUDE TO HIS EXCELLENCY, PRESIDENT FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., FOR THE CONSTITUTION OF THE MARAWI COMPENSATION BOARD, AND CONGRATULATING THE DULY APPOINTED CHAIRPERSON AND MEMBERS OF THE MARAWI COMPENSATION BOARD**

**WHEREAS**, on 13 April 2022, Republic Act No. 11696, titled "An Act Providing Compensation for the Loss or Destruction of Properties and Loss of Lives as a Result of the 2017 Marawi Siege, and Appropriating Funds Therefor", otherwise known as the *Marawi Siege Victims Compensation Act of 2022*, was signed into law to provide compensation or reparation to the internally-displaced persons (IDPs) of the 2017 Marawi Siege whose damaged or demolished properties and possessions cannot be recovered;

**WHEREAS**, the *Marawi Siege Victims Compensation Act of 2022* also provides for the creation and composition of the Marawi Compensation Board (hereinafter referred to as the "Board"), an independent and quasi-judicial body primarily mandated to receive, evaluate, process, investigate, and approve applications for claims, and promptly disburse compensation to qualified claimants, and which Board shall be composed of a Chairperson and eight (8) other members who shall be appointed by the President;

**WHEREAS**, on 13 January 2023, the Office of the President appointed the following officials of the Board:

**Chairperson: Maisara C. Dandamun-Latiph**

**Members: Sittie Aliyyah Lomondot Adiong**
**Dalomabi Lao Bula**
**Mustapha C. Dimaampao**
**Jamaica Lamping Dimaporo**
**Mabandes Sumndad Diron, Jr.**
**Moslemen T. Macarambon, Sr.**
**Romaisa Lomantong Mamutuk**
**Nasser Macapado Tabao**

**WHEREAS**, the role of the Board is crucial to the successful and prompt implementation of the provisions of the *Marawi Siege Victims Compensation Act of 2022*, which shall meaningfully contribute to the recovery and rehabilitation of Marawi City and to the social healing of the IDPs;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved that the Bangsamoro Transition Authority expresses its deepest gratitude to the His Excellency, President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr., for the constitution of the Marawi Compensation Board;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament extends its congratulations and well wishes to the duly appointed Chairperson and Members of the Marawi Compensation Board;

**RESOLVED, FINALLLY**, that a copy of this Resolution be forwarded to the Office of the President of the Philippines, to the Office of the Chief Minister, and to the Marawi Compensation Board.
