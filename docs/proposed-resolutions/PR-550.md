**RESOLUTION**
**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING AL HAFIDZ MUZAHER SUWEB BITO FOR HIS EXEMPLARY ACHIEVEMENT AS THIRD (3RD) PLACER IN THE 27TH DUBAI INTERNATIONAL HOLY QURAN COMPETITION 2024 AND FOR GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS,** Section 13, Article II of the 1987 Constitution and Section 13, Article IX of the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) both recognize the vital role of the youth in nation-building and shall promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, intellectual, and social well-being, as well as their role to inculcate in the youth patriotism and nationalism, and encourage their involvement in public and civic affairs;

**WHEREAS,** the 27th Dubai International Holy Quran Competition (DIHQC) 2024, a prestigious event held in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) from March 12 to 23, 2024, brought together seventy (70) contestants worldwide for Quran memorization competition;

**WHEREAS,** Al Hafidz Muzaqher Suweb Bito, a seventeen (17)-year-old Bangsamoro from Datu Abdulah Sangki, Maguindanao del Sur, BARMM, represented the Philippines in the said competition;

**WHEREAS,** in demonstrating outstanding skill and proficiency in Quranic memorization, Al Hafidz Muzaqher Suweb Bito emerged as 3rd Placer in the competition and was awarded with a cash prize;

**WHEREAS,** Al Hafidz Muzaqher Suweb Bito's achievement in the 27th Dubai International Holy Quran Competition 2024 has not only brought distinction to himself but also showcased the talent and excellence of the Bangsamoro people in their commitment to Islamic values and to the teachings of Noble Qur’an in the international arena, serving as an inspiration to the world, especially the Bangsamoro youth and children, to strive for excellence in their chosen endeavors;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament has previously passed Adopted Resolution No. 97, as amended by Adopted Resolution No. 306, which provides for the awarding of plaques of recognition and other tokens of appreciation to winners of international Qur’an recitation and memorization competitions, thereby establishing a precedent for honoring such achievements;

**WHEREAS,** on March 26, 2024, the Chief Minister Ahod B. Ebrahim warmly welcomed and presented Al Hafidz Muzaqher Suweb Bito with a plaque of recognition in commendation for representing the region and the country in the recently concluded 27th Dubai International Holy Quran Competition;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to congratulate and commend Al Hafidz Muzaher Suweb Bito for his exemplary achievement as Third (3rd) Placer in the 27th Dubai International Holy Quran Competition 2024 and for giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro People;

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** to forward a copy of this resolution to the Office of the Chief Minister, the BCPCH, and Al Hafidz Muzaher Suweb Bito for their information and consideration.
