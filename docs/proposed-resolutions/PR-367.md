**RESOLUTION**

**EXPRESSING GRATITUDE TO PRESIDENT FERDINAND "BONGBONG" ROMUALDEZ MARCOS, JR. FOR HIS COMMITMENT TO GRANT AMNESTY TO FORMER MILF/MNLF COMBATANTS AND FOR HIS RECOGNITION OF BARMM'S PERFORMANCE**

**WHEREAS**, on July 24, 2023, President Ferdinand "Bongbong" Romualdez Marcos, Jr. addressed the nation in his State of the Nation Address (SONA), wherein he made a significant commitment to include in his priority agenda the granting of amnesty to former combatants of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) and Moro National Liberation Front (MNLF);

**WHEREAS**, the promise of granting amnesty to former combatants is a crucial step towards building trust, confidence, and lasting peace in the Bangsamoro Region;

**WHEREAS**, President Marcos recognized the evolution of the armed struggle of some of our fellow Filipinos and highlighted the progress made towards peace and development in the region;

**WHEREAS**, the incorporation of capacity-building and social protection into reintegration programs will further ensure the full decommissioning of former combatants, making way for a peaceful and prosperous Bangsamoro;

**WHEREAS**, the acknowledgement of the efforts and performance of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) by President Marcos is a testament to the collaborative efforts of all key groups and stakeholders involved in the transition phase;

**WHEREAS**, the peaceful and smooth transition of the BARMM is a shining example of how former adversaries can become partners in peace, uniting towards a shared vision of progress and development for all Filipinos;

**WHEREAS**, the support of the international community in this transformative process has been invaluable in strengthening the nation's prospects for sustainable progress and lasting peace in Southern Philippines;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, that the Bangsamoro Parliament extends its heartfelt gratitude to President Ferdinand "Bongbong" Romualdez Marcos, Jr. for his steadfast commitment to granting amnesty to former MILF/MNLF combatants, as part of the Normalization Track of the Peace Process;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the Bangsamoro Parliament thanks President Marcos for his recognition of the BARMM'S performance, the collaborative efforts of all key groups, and the support of the international community in ensuring a peaceful and effective transition;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the Bangsamoro Parliament reaffirms its unwavering support to the President's efforts towards achieving true and lasting peace in the Bangsamoro Region, and pledges to work hand in hand with the national government to realize this shared vision;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that copies of this resolution be transmitted to the Office of the President, the Office of the Chief Minister of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, and other relevant government agencies, as a manifestation of the appreciation and unity of the Bangsamoro people in this journey towards peace and progress.
