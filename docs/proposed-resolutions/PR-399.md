**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MS. SABAHA PATO SALIK FOR HER OUTSTANDING PERFORMANCE IN WINNING THIRD PLACE FOR QUR’AN RECITATION CATEGORY IN THE 63RD MALAYSIAN INTERNATIONAL AL-QURAN RECITATION AND MEMORIZATION ASSEMBLY (MTHQA) 2023 AND FOR GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, the importance of preserving culture and identity, and enhancing understanding and appreciation of the Noble Qur’an, is vital for the global Muslim community;

**WHEREAS**, international competitions like the Malaysian International Al-Quran Recitation and Memorization Assembly (MTHQA) play a pivotal role in fostering a deeper connection and mutual respect among Muslim communities worldwide;

**WHEREAS**, Section 3 of Republic Act No. 11054, known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law, underscores the significance of establishing a government that genuinely reflects the aspirations and identity of the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, the 63rd MTHQA, a notable annual event held from 19 August 2023 to 24 August 2023 at Kuala Lumpur World Trade Centre, gathered esteemed representatives and officials from various nations;

**WHEREAS**, broadcast live through the social media channels of the Department of Islamic Development Malaysia (JAKIM), this assembly aims not only to recognize the talent in Qur’an recitation and memorization but also to provide a global platform for the further appreciation of the Qur’an;

**WHEREAS**, the vigorous competition saw 76 individuals from 52 countries, with distinct categories for men and women, vying for top honors in Qur’an recitation and memorization;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Sabaha Pato Salik, a 31-year-old Bangsamoro of Maguindanaon descent, born to Nasser A. Salik and Rohamina G. Pato of Brgy. Tulunan, Datu Anggal Midtimbang, Maguindanao Del Sur, BARMM, emerged as the Philippines’ representative in the Qur’an recitation for the female category;

**WHEREAS**, her exceptional skills and dedication led her to secure an impressive third place in the Qur’an recitation category with a commendable average score of ninety-one point fifty (91.50) percent. This achievement resonates deeply with her roots and reflects her strong upbringing;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Salik’s victory is a testament to her dedication, hard work, and talent. Her success does not only showcase her excellent performance in Qur’an recitation, but also serves as a source of inspiration and motivation for all Bangsamoro youth, especially women, to unleash their potentials as they pursue excellence in Qur’an and Islamic studies;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, recognizing Ms. Salik’s significant contribution in elevating the name of the Bangsamoro people and the Philippines on an international stage, deems it appropriate to laud her achievement;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to congratulate and commend Ms. Sabaha Pato Salik for her outstanding performance in winning third place for Qur’an recitation category in the 63rd Malaysian International Al-Quran Recitation And Memorization Assembly (MTHQA) 2023 and for giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people;

**RESOLVE, FURTHER**, to present a copy of this resolution to Ms. Sabaha Pato Salik in recognition and expression of appreciation of her exemplary performance and hard work that brought pride to the Bangsamoro people.
