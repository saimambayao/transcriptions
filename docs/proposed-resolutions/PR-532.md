**A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING BGEN. NASSER LIDASAN, PHD, FOR HIS APPOINTMENT AS THE FIRST BANGSAMORO MUSLIM CHAIRMAN OF GPH-MILF COORDINATING COMMITTEE ON THE CESSATION OF HOSTILITIES**

**Whereas,** it is with great pride and honor that we recognize the exemplary achievements of Brigadier General Nasser P. Lidasan, PhD, who has brought distinction and honor to the Bangsamoro people through his dedicated service and outstanding accomplishments;

**Whereas,** Brigadier General Nasser P. Lidasan, PhD, has made history as the first Bangsamoro Muslim to ascend to the esteemed position of Assistant Commanding General of the 6th Infantry Division (6ID) of the Philippine Army, demonstrating exceptional leadership, commitment, and professionalism in his duties;

**Whereas,** Brigadier General Nasser P. Lidasan, PhD, has also made and is making significant contributions to the peace process in the Bangsamoro region, having been appointed by the President of the Philippines, Ferdinand “Bongbong” R. Marcos, Jr., as Chairman of the GPH Coordinating Committee on the Cessation of Hostilities (GPH CCCH), a testament to his capacity for diplomacy, negotiation, and conflict resolution;

**Whereas,** Brigadier General Nasser Lidasan, PhD, has exemplified the values of integrity, dedication, and service to both the Bangsamoro people and the nation as a whole, serving as a beacon of hope and inspiration for future generations;

**Now, therefore, be it resolved,** that the Bangsamoro Transition Authority hereby commends and congratulates Brigadier General Nasser P. Lidasan, PhD, for his outstanding achievements and contributions to the Bangsamoro region and the Philippines as a whole;

**Be it further resolved,** that a copy of this resolution be transmitted to Brigadier General Nasser P. Lidasan, PhD, as a token of our deepest appreciation and admiration for his unwavering commitment to peace, progress, and prosperity in the Bangsamoro region;

**Be it finally resolved,** that this resolution be made part of the official records of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to serve as a testament to the exemplary leadership and service of Brigadier General Nasser P. Lidasan, PhD.

Principal Author:
