**A RESOLUTION COMMENDING CAROLYN O. ARGUILLAS OF MINDANEWS FOR BEING A RECIPIENT OF THE 2023 LUXEMBOURG PEACE PRIZE (LPP) AWARD FOR OUTSTANDING PEACE JOURNALISM.**

**WHEREAS,** on June 13, 2023, Carolyn O. Arguillas of the MindaNews has been named the 2023 Luxembourg Peace Prize (LPP) awardee for Outstanding Peace Journalism.

**WHEREAS,** the Luxembourg Peace Prize for Outstanding Peace Journalism is a recognition given by the Schengen Peace Foundation and the World Peace Forum which seeks to recognize journalists or media publications who have used their crafts to contribute to peace.

**WHEREAS,** she is the first Filipino to be given this award in the eleven (11) years that the Schengen Peace Foundation and the World Peace Forum have been giving out such awards to honor the outstanding in the field of peace.

**WHEREAS,** the LPP stated in its press release that over the past two decades, the MindaNews has earned recognition under the leadership of Ms. Arguillas, for its constructive coverage of peace efforts fostering an environment of dialogue and understanding.

**WHEREAS,** such constructive coverages of peace efforts made by Ms. Arguillas contributed a significant role in achieving milestones in the country’s peace processes which includes the signing of a peace agreement between the Philippine government and the Bangsamoro people in the southern part of the country and the passage into law of the RA11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law.

**WHEREAS,** Ms. Arguillas’ thirst for wisdom coupled with her desire to perfect her craft in order to better serve Mindanao, has led her to accomplish numerous educational milestones. She finished her Bachelor of Arts in Journalism at the University of the Philippines in Diliman and her Master of Arts in Journalism at the Asian Center for Journalism of the Ateneo de Manila University, she also finished the academic requirements for her Master of Arts in Peace and Development at Notre Dame University in Cotabato City and also took academic units in Master of Arts in Anthropology at the Ateneo de Davao University.

**WHEREAS,** Ms. Arguillas has won various national awards namely; the Catholic Mass Media Awards for Best News Reporting; the Jaime V. Ongpin Awards for Investigative Journalism; lifetime achievement awards from Titus Brandsma-Philippines and the Hildegarde Awards for Women of the St. Scholastica’s College; the Investigative Journalist of the Year award from the Rotary Club of Manila; Marshall McLuhan Prize from the Canadian Embassy; Gawad AKAp Peace Awards; and the Glory Award for Journalism conferred by the UP College of Mass Communication Alumni Association.

**WHEREAS,** Ms. Arguillas’ contributions to peace journalism have influenced the media platforms on the road to better broadcasting and news writing in Mindanao as emphasized by her firm dedication to responsible reporting.

**WHEREAS,** in view of the foregoing commitment and achievements of Ms. Arguillas which helped so much to the fight for continuing peace in Mindanao, it is therefore befitting that this Honorable Parliament express and extend the well-deserved commendation in recognition of this momentous achievement of this honorable guardian of peace.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament – Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao **A RESOLUTION COMMENDING CAROLYN O. ARGUILLAS OF MINDANEWS FOR BEING A RECIPIENT OF THE 2023 LUXEMBOURG PEACE PRIZE (LPP) AWARD FOR OUTSTANDING PEACE JOURNALISM.**
