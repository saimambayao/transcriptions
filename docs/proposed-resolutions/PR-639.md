**RESOLUTION RESPECTFULLY REQUESTING THE OFFICE OF THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE, AND BUDGET AND MANAGEMENT FOR THE CREATION OF A NEW PLANTILLA POSITION AND ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE OF THE BANGSAMORO BOARD OF INVESTMENTS OFFICE (BBOI) OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Board of Investments Office (BBOI) is an attached agency of the Bangsamoro Chief Minister's Office, tasked with promoting and facilitating investments in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Board of Investment is created in accordance with Section 25 of the Bangsamoro Autonomy Act (BAA) #13; the Corporate Recovery and Tax Incentives for Enterprises Act (RA 11534); and the Omnibus Investments Code of 1987 (Executive Order No. 226);

WHEREAS, the BBOI has been operating with limited human resources and organizational structure, which hinders its effective performance and implementation of its mandates;

WHEREAS, the BBOI has identified the need to create new plantilla positions and organizational structure to enhance its capacity and efficiency in serving the needs of investors and stakeholders in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

WHEREAS, the Ministry of Finance, and Budget and Management is hereby requested to create a new plantilla position and organizational structure for the Bangsamoro Board of Investments Office (BBOI) which shall be effective immediately;

WHEREAS, the new plantilla position and organizational structure shall be designed to enhance the capacity and efficiency of the BBOI in promoting and facilitating investments in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region including but not limited to:

Attached are copies of the requested plantilla position and the organization structure for reference.

WHEREAS, the Ministry of Finance and Budget and Management shall provide the necessary resources and support to implement the new plantilla position and organizational structure;

WHEREAS, the BBOI shall ensure that the new plantilla position and organizational structure are implemented in a manner consistent with the laws, regulations, and policies of the Bangsamoro Government.

WHEREAS, in order to help fully boost the investment in the region, it is imperative to hire officials who can discharge the duties and functions deemed required;

WHEREAS, in line with this, There is an urgent need to create a new Plantilla Position in the office of the Bangsamoro Board of Investment in addition to their currently occupied and to organize a new structure of staffing pattern thereof to achieve and fertile easefully its specific mandate under the existing Law;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in consultation with the Ministry of Finance and Budget and Management, to grant the request for the creation of the new plantilla position and organizational structure of the Bangsamoro Board of Investment office of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao,**

**RESOLVED FURTHER, to furnish copies of this resolution to the Office of the Chief Minister and all concerned Ministries, Offices, and Agencies.**
