**RESOLUTION**
**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MR. HEBBAN TALIB TAWANTAWAN FOR RANKING FIRST IN THE SEPTEMBER-OCTOBER 2023 CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANT LICENSURE EXAMINATIONS (CPALE) GIVEN BY THE PROFESSIONAL REGULATION COMMISSION (PRC)**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority has consistently given commendation to remarkable Bangsamoros for their outstanding achievements in various fields and exemplary body of work which bring great honor to the Region and contribute to the positive transformation of the Bangsa;

**WHEREAS**, the CPALE or the Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination is considered as one of the hardest licensure examinations which ranked five (5) out of ten as stated by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC)ยน;

**WHEREAS**, last September 30, October 1 and 2, 2023, the CPALE was conducted simultaneously in testing centers in National Capital Region (NCR), Baguio, Butuan, Cagayan de Oro, Calapan, Cebu, Davao, Iloilo, Koronadal, Legazpi, Lucena, Pagadian, Pampanga, Rosales, Tacloban, Tuguegarao, Zamboanga, and Palawan;

**WHEREAS**, on 06 October 2023, PRC released the result of the CPALE where only 2, 740 out of 8, 734 examinees or 31.37% successfully passed the said examination;

**WHEREAS**, one of the passers is a Bangsamoro and a Maranao in the person of Mr. Hebban Talib Tawantawan, a Magna Cum Laude graduate of the University of San Jose-Recoletos in Cebu City;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Tawantawan bested the examinees with a notable rating of 91.17%;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Tawantawan exhibited and showed proof that indeed the Bangsamoro people are competitive, intelligent and can excel in whatever profession;

**WHEREAS**, undoubtedly, Mr. Tawantawan brought tremendous honor and pride not just to his parents and family but to the whole Region and his achievement will certainly serve as an inspiration not only to our youth but to the entire Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, by recognizing the hard-earned achievement of Mr. Tawantawan, we promote a culture of excellence in the Region by giving credit and honor where these are due.

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the BTA Parliament, to congratulate and commend Mr. Hebban Talib Tawantawan for ranking first in the September-October 2023 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination (CPALE) given by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC).

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that a copy of this resolution be given and presented to Mr. Hebban Talib Tawantawan, to show and express to him our deepest gratitude for his excellence, perseverance, hard work and dedication.

Adopted, ________.
