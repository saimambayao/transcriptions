***

**A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING PROFOUND GRATITUDE AND SINCERE CONDOLENCES OF THE BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT ON THE DEATH OF ABDULWALID “Jimmy” M. RADIAMODA, 122nd BASE COMMANDER**

**WHEREAS,** Abdulwalid “Jimmy” M. Radiamoda, 122nd base Commander, passed away and return to the mercy of ALLAH on Safar 24, 1444 / 20 September 2022;

**WHEREAS,** Commander Abdulwalid “Jimmy” M. Radiamoda, was a dutiful and dedicated Mujahideen who exuded bravery and was not afraid to risk his own life in defending the cause of the MILF. He was among the mujahideens who defended camp nusa during the 1977 war, lead his men in defending MILF camps during the 2000, 2003 and 2008 wars;

**WHEREAS,** Commander Abdulwalid “Jimmy” M. Radiamoda was known for his programs centered on peace and crime-free community. He himself ensured that peace reigned within his area of jurisdiction. He assisted in the arrest and imprisonment of criminals especially drug pushers and users. He had also settled several local feuds. He is a pious leader who is loved by his men for his unselfish and dedicated service to the MILF and the community;

**WHEREAS,** the passing away of a dedicated and passionate Mujahideen as Commander Abdulwalid “Jimmy” M. Radiamoda, is a great loss to the Moro people;

**WHEREAS,** his services to the Bangsamoro people will be remembered with great honor and pride and will never be forgotten;

**NOW THEREFORE, be it;**

**RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** that the Bangsamoro Parliament to express, as it hereby expresses, its profound gratitude and sincerest condolences on the death of **Commander Abdulwalid “Jimmy” M. Radiamoda;**

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to the bereaved family of late Commander Abdulwalid “Jimmy” M. Radiamoda.
