**A RESOLUTION**

**URGING THE MINISTRY OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATION TO STRENGTHEN THE IMPLEMENTATION OF R.A 11314 OR THE STUDENT FARE DISCOUNT ACT IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO AND ENSURE STRICT COMPLIANCE THERETO.**

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article XIV, of the 1987 Constitution provides that "The State shall protect and promote the right of all citizens to quality education at all levels, and shall take appropriate steps to make such education accessible to all.”

**WHEREAS**, paragraph 3, Section 2, Article XIV, of the 1987 Constitution further provides that the State shall establish and maintain a system of scholarship grants, student loan programs, subsidies, and other incentives, which shall be available to deserving students in both public and private schools, especially to the underprivileged.

**WHEREAS**, Section 8, Article IX of RA 11054 states: “The Bangsamoro Government shall provide, maintain, and ensure the delivery of basic and responsive health programs, quality education, appropriate services, livelihood opportunities, affordable and progressive housing projects, power and electricity, and water inhabitants of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region…xxx” (*emphasis supplied*)

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Administrative Code provides that the Ministry of Transportation and Communications (MOTC) shall be the primary policy, planning, programming, coordinating, implementing, regulating, and administrative entity of the Bangsamoro Government in the promotion, development, and regulation of dependable and coordinated networks of transportation and communications systems, as well as fast, safe, efficient and reliable transportation and communications services.¹

**WHEREAS**, on 17 April 2019, Republic Act No. 11314 or “An Act Institutionalizing the Grant of Student Fare Discount Privileges on Public Transportation and For Other Purposes” was signed into law;

**WHEREAS**, Republic Act No. 11314 grants all students 20% discount on domestic regular fares whether they are travelling by land, sea or air;

**WHEREAS**, the law also provides that a student who is refused the fare discount privilege under this Act may file a complaint² and after due investigation and finding the complaint against any public transportation utility to be true and valid, penalties maybe imposed³;

**WHEREAS**, on 27 September 2019, Civil Aeronautics Board issued Resolution No. 05 (AR 05-2019) regarding the Guidelines on the Grant of 20% Discount on Air Fares for Students in accordance with the mandate of RA 11314;

**WHEREAS**, on 19 December 2019, Maritime Industry Authority (MARINA) issued Memorandum Circular No. GC-2019-01 relative to the Rules and Regulations Implementing Republic Act No. 11314 on the Grant of Student Fare Discount Privileges Onboard Domestic Passenger Ships;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Administrative Code provides that: “The Ministry of Transportation and Communications has the following powers and functions:

   a. Administer and enforce all transportation and communications laws, rules, and regulations within its jurisdiction and deputize appropriate law enforcement agencies for the purpose;…xxx”⁴

**WHEREAS**, in order to encourage the Bangsamoro students to pursue quality education by extending assistance to them, MOTC must ensure that laws beneficial to students be implemented within BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, by ensuring compliance and strict implementation of the law, the BARMM Government can help the students save the money supposedly for fare and instead allot the same for other school expenses;

**WHEREAS**, by ensuring compliance and strict implementation of the law, the BARMM Government can help parents in minimizing school expenses and saving for future school year;

**WHEREAS**, the purpose of this resolution is to alleviate the responsibilities of parents and assist them in planning for upcoming school years. Through this, the BARMM Government ensures that all Bangsamoro children have access to education and successfully complete their schooling.

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the BTA Parliament to pass and approve this **RESOLUTION URGING THE MINISTRY OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATION TO STRENGTHEN THE IMPLEMENTATION OF R.A 11314 OR THE STUDENT FARE DISCOUNT ACT IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO AND ENSURE STRICT COMPLIANCE THERETO.**
