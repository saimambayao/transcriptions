**RESOLUTION CALLING ON THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT THROUGH MINISTRY OF THE INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT, MINISTRY OF PUBLIC ORDER AND SAFETY AND BANGSAMORO COMMISSION ON HUMAN RIGHTS AND OTHER APPROPRIATE AGENCIES TO FORMALLY CONDUCT INVESTIGATION AND SUBMIT RECOMMENDATION PERTAINING TO THE ALLEGED SERIES OF KILLINGS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIKIT ESPECIALLY AREAS UNDER BARMM SPECIAL GEOGRAPHIC AREA BARANGAYS AND TERRITORY.**

**WHEREAS,** the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that:

*Section 5, Article II, Declaration of the Principles and State Policies – The maintenance of peace and order, the protection of life, liberty, and property, and the promotion of the general welfare are essential for the enjoyment by all the people of the blessings of democracy;*

*Section 1, Article III, Bill of Rights – No person shall be deprived of life, liberty, or property without due process of law, nor shall any person be denied the equal protection of the laws”; and*

*Section 16, Article III, all persons shall have the right to a speedy disposition of their cases before all judicial, quasi-judicial, or administrative bodies.*

**WHEREAS,** Article V, Section 3 of R.A. No. 11054 otherwise known as the “Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” states that:

“*The Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the power expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective government and those which are essential to the promotion of general welfare. Within its territorial jurisdiction, the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure and support, among other things, the preservation and enrichment of culture, promote health and safety, enhance the right of the people to a balanced ecology, encourage and support the development of appropriate and self-reliant scientific and technological capabilities, improve public morals, enhance economic prosperity and social justice , promote full employment among its residents, maintain peace and order, and preserve the comfort and convenience of its inhabitants.”*

**WHEREAS,** Title VIII, Chapter 1, Section 2 of the Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13 or otherwise known as the “Bangsamoro Administrative Code” provides that:

*“The Ministry of the Interior and Local Government shall exercise general supervision over the constituent local governments units of the Bangsamoro Government, and ensure public safety and disaster preparedness, local autonomy, decentralization, and community empowerment.”*

**WHEREAS,** the President of the Republic of the Philippines calls for lasting peace in the Southern Philippines and Mindanao, and support the peace process and progress of the region, his statement during his recent visit at 6ID Kampilan Division, Awang, Datu Odin Sinsuat, Maguindanao on September 15, 2022;

**WHEREAS,** the following series of heinous crimes and incidents are reported to media on that area, to wit:

1. July 19, 2022, 2:00 PM at Barangay Batulawan, Pikit, SGA. Victim Alex Salcedo (58 years old)
2. July 20, 2022, 7:00 AM at Barangay Nalapaan, Pikit, SGA. Victim Datu Guiamad Jalandoni
3. July 29, 2022, 12:55 PM at Baarangay Dunguan, Aleosan, SGA. Victim Burhan Pagayao Palwa (22 years old)
4. August 26, 2022, 12:15 Noon at Barangay Port, Pikit, SGA. Victims Kalid Selongan (28 years old), Ray Dodong Santander (49 years old), Welliams Cantonis (41 years old) and Darwin a.k.a Bugoy Cantonis (28 years old)
5. August 26, 2022, 5:40 PM at Barangay Gli, SGA. Victims Mujahid Guiabal (24 years old) and Anuadod Usman (26 years old).
6. September 17, 2022, at Barangay Batulawan, Pikit, SGA. Victims Romeo Malunes (23 years old) and John Rey Malunes (40 years old).
7. September 15, 2022, 3:40 PM at Barangay Poblacion, Pikit. Victim Patrol Brian Singco (41 years old).

**WHEREAS,** despite the series of heinous crimes, perpetuators are still unidentified and/or at large according to police reports;

**WHEREAS,** the Office of the Member of Parliament Dr. Kadil M. Sinolinding Jr., DPBO and Member of the Parliament Mohammad Kelie U. Antao calls for immediate investigation of the above mentioned incidents.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), calls for the Bangsamoro Government through Ministry of Interior and Local Government, Ministry of Public Order and Safety, Bangsamoro Commission on Human Rights and other appropriate agencies to formally conduct investigation and submit recommendation pertaining to the alleged series of killings in the municipality of Pikit especially areas under BARMM Special Geographic Area Barangays and Territory.

**FURTHER RESOLVED,** that the Statutory Committee being the Secretariat of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to furnish a copy of this resolution to Bill and Index Department and all members of the BTA a copy of this resolution.
