**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING HIS EXCELLENCY RECEP TAYYIP ERDOĞAN FOR WINNING THE 2023 TÜRKIYE PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION**

**WHEREAS**, the Philippines and Turkiye have a long-existing bilateral relationship, demonstrated by the Philippines having an embassy in Ankara and Türkiye having an embassy in Manila;

**WHEREAS**, in 2014 and during the administration of Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdoğan, the Turkey Government represented by the Prime Minister traveled to Manila for an official visit to partner with the National Government in establishing a just and lasting peace in Mindanao;

**WHEREAS**, Türkiye is involved in the peace talks between the Philippine Government and the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) as part of the International Contact Group composed of representatives from three (3) other countries and four (4) International Non-Governmental Organizations;

**WHEREAS**, Türkiye is part of the Independent Decommissioning Body (IDB) which oversees the decommissioning of MILF's combatants;

**WHEREAS**, Türkiye is also a member of the Third-Party Monitoring Team tasked to oversee the implementation of all agreements between the Government of the Philippines and the MILF;

**WHEREAS**, Incumbent President Recep Tayyip Erdoğan of the Justice and Development Party (AKP or AK Party) ran for re-election as the joint candidate of the People's Alliance, which includes the Nationalist Movement Party (MHP) and three (3) other smaller parties;

**WHEREAS**, during the first-round election results on May 14, 2023, Erdogan took 49.52 percent, with his opposition getting 44.88 percent of the total votes cast;

**WHEREAS**, during a run-off vote held on May 27, 2023, President Recep Tayyip Erdogan won as Türkiye’s president for a term of five (5) years, or from 2023 to 2028;

**WHEREAS**, with 99.43% of the votes counted, official results announced by Türkiye’s **Supreme Election Council (YSK)** on Sunday showed Erdogan winning with 52.14% of the votes and his opposition receiving 47.86%;

Now, therefore, be it

**RESOLVED BY THE MEMBERS OF THE BTAPARLIAMENT**, to congratulate His Excellency Recep Tayyip Erdoğan for winning the 2023 Türkiye presidential election;

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that a copy of this resolution be furnished to the Office of the President, Republic of Türkiye through its embassy in Manila, Philippines.
