**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING THE 1ST INTERNATIONAL NUCLEAR SCIENCE OLYMPIAD GOLD MEDALIST AND HIGHEST SCORER AWARDEE AND NAMED NUCLEAR AMBASSADOR MOHAMMAD NUR CASIB OF PSHS-CENTRAL MINDANAO CAMPUS THEREBY BRINGING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, Section 13, Article II of the 1987 Constitution provides that:

> “The State recognizes the vital role of the youth in nation-building and shall promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, intellectual, and social well-being. It shall inculcate in the youth patriotism and nationalism, and encourage their involvement in public and civic affairs”;

**WHEREAS**, Section 17, Article II of the 1987 Constitution provides that:
> “The State shall give priority to education, science and technology, arts, culture and sports to foster patriotism and nationalism, accelerate social progress, and promote total human liberation and development”;

**WHEREAS**, Section 13, Article IX of the Republic Act No. 11054 provides that:

> “The Bangsamoro Government recognizes the vital role of women and youth, and the valuable contributions of differently-abled persons in nation-building and shall promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, intellectual, cultural, and social well-being. It shall inculcate in the minds of the youth patriotism and nationalism, and encourage their involvement in public and civic affairs”;

**WHEREAS**, The Philippines greeted the world’s 1st International Nuclear Science Olympiad (INSO) with a blast as the Department of Science and Technology (DOST) welcomed the Olympiad participants in the opening ceremonies held at the SMX Convention Center in New Clark City, Pampanga marking a significant milestone in nuclear education;

**WHEREAS**, the historical international event, ran from August 1 to 6, 2024, featured fifty five (55) top contenders, twenty seven (27) team leaders, and fourteen (14) observers from fourteen (14) Asia-Pacific countries;

**WHEREAS**, The event aims to highlight the Philippines' commitment to advancing nuclear education and fostering international collaboration in the peaceful use of nuclear science;

**WHEREAS**, Marina Binti Mishar from Department of Technical Cooperation of the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA), describes the Olympiad as “*a global community united by a common goal: to explore, innovate, and push the boundaries of what is possible in the field of nuclear science*”;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Helena Zhivistkaya, an expert of the Nuclear Knowledge Management Section of the IAEA Department of Nuclear Energy, reminded the contenders that, “*The Olympiad is not just a competition but building long-lasting cooperation for future activities*”;

**WHEREAS**, Mohammad Nur G. Casib of Bangsamoro region, a gold medalist member of the Philippine team, displayed brilliance and excellence in the Olympiad;

**WHEREAS**, special awards were also conferred to Mr. Casib for garnering the highest points in the experimental and theoretical exams, respectively. The contenders competed as individuals and were ranked according to the points they obtained in the two exams, in which Mr. Casib emerged on top;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Casib secured the highest points in overall scores, earning him the title “Nuclear Ambassador”;

**WHEREAS**, The success of Mr. Casib, is a pride and honor to the Bangsamoro People, and serves as an inspiration to our Bangsamoro youth;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government congratulates and commends Mr Casib for his continued success, outstanding performance and contributions in the national and international competitions that brought pride, honor and glory to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**NOW THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, by the BTA Parliament to congratulate and commend the 1st International Nuclear Science Olympiad gold medalist and highest scorer awardee and named Nuclear Ambassador Mohammad Nur Casib of PSHS-Central Mindanao campus thereby bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to the family of Mohammad Nur Casib and to the PSHS-Central Mindanao for information.
