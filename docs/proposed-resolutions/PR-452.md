**A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING KAKAW MERANAW FOR WINNING THE AVPA PARIS 2023 – MEDAILLE GOURMET CHOCOLATE OF LOCAL ORIGIN**

WHEREAS, the State recognizes the indispensable role of the private sector, encourages private enterprise, and provides incentives to needed investments¹;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Parliament is vested with the duty to recognize and commend notable Bangsamoro people and achievements that bring pride and honor to our Bangsamoro Region;

WHEREAS, Kakaw Meranaw is a lone contestant from the Philippines that bagged the AVPA PARIS 2023 – MEDAILLE GOURMET AWARD at the concluded 3rd International Contest for Chocolate Processed at Origin Contest on the 25th day of April 2023²;

WHEREAS, Chocolates processed at Origin International Contest is organized by the Agency for Valorization of Agricultural Products (AVPA) in order to continue to recognize that cocoa-producing countries know how to develop chocolates of quality, equal and sometimes better than those made by major brands in consumer countries;

WHEREAS, this international contest reserved for chocolatiers or their groups (cooperatives, Denominations of Origin) for which AVPA proposes a classification that allows the expression of all the traditional tastes encountered among chocolatiers in cocoa-producing countries. In its two editions, nearly 200 exceptional chocolates spread over some 20 countries on 4 continents were tasted;

WHEREAS, Kakaw Meranaw is a proud pioneering chocolate bar producer from Barangay Tagoranao, Bayang, Lanao del Sur. It is a social enterprise that aims to provide livelihood and support to our dearest Maranao local farmers from the said barangay through the production of chocolate bars³;

WHEREAS, this remarkable achievement by Kakaw Meranaw highlights the importance of recognizing and encouraging our local entrepreneurs to participate on international contest to show that our local entrepreneurs possess the capability to excel and compete globally;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Parliament acknowledges its crucial role in encouraging the development and recognition of local entrepreneurs or enterprises that contributes to the economic development of our region;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to congratulate **Kakaw Meranaw For Winning the AVPA Paris 2023 – Medaille Gourmet Chocolate of Local Origin**;

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that this Resolution be presented to Kakaw Meranaw, for information.
