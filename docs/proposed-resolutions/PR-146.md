**A RESOLUTION SUPPORTING THE JOURNEY OF MS. FAUZIA TUMBAS - LIDASAN, A BANGSAMORO WOMAN AIMING TO REACH THE MT. EVEREST PEAK BY MAY 2025**

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Fauzia Tumbas - Lidasan is the first Bangsamoro mountain climber who conquered and raised the Bangsamoro flag at the base camp height of the highest mountain in the world, Mt. Everest;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Fauzia Tumbas - Lidasan and her team reached the base camp height of 5,364 meters of Mt. Everest on April 26, 2022;

**WHEREAS**, Mount Everest is the Earth's highest mountain above sea level at 8,849 meters (29,032 feet), located in the Mahalangur Himalaya, a sub-range of the Himalayas between Nepal and Tibet, an autonomous region of China;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Fauzia Tumbas - Lidasan, a mother of five, is 40 years old and a native of Gang, Sultan Kudarat Maguindanao del Norte, and the daughter of former Vice Mayor Muslimin Tumbas of Sultan Mastura, Maguindanao del Norte;

**WHEREAS**, according to Ms. Fauzia, the Everest base camp was her "toughest camp" since she started mountain climbing in 2016 when her husband passed away;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Fauzia brought and raised the Bangsamoro Emblem at the Base Camp of Mount Everest to honor her parents and mujahideen who fought for the liberation of the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Fauzia detailed her upcoming training schedule, stating that she intends to conquer the following mountains and raise the Bangsamoro flag in the coming years: Mount Kilimanjaro (5,895 masl), Africa's highest mountain, ranked fourth among the seven summits by June 2023; Mera Peak (6,476 masl), an advanced beginner and intermediate climb leading to a 7-day expedition, to be conquered by November 2023; Mount Cho Oyu (8,188 masl), the world's sixth-highest mountain, to be reached by April 2024; and Mount Everest (8,848 masl), which lies on the border between Nepal and Tibet, to be accomplished again by March-May 2025;

**WHEREAS**, with our utmost support, Ms. Fauzia Tumbas - Lidasan, with her faith and bravery, looks forward to achieving her objective of reaching the top of Mount Everest soon, In Shaa Allah.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority:

**TO PASS AND APPROVE THIS RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND SUPPORTING THE JOURNEY OF MS. FAUZIA TUMBAS - LIDASAN, A BANGSAMORO WOMAN AIMING TO REACH THE MT. EVEREST PEAK BY MAY 2025.**
