**RESOLUTION**
**EXPRESSING PROFOUND CONGRATULATIONS AND COMMENDATIONS TO MR. HEBBAN TALIB TAWANTAWAN FOR HIS EXCEPTIONAL ACHIEVEMENT IN RANKING FIRST IN THE SEPTEMBER-OCTOBER 2023 CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANT LICENSURE EXAMINATION (CPALE), AND TO ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS FOR THEIR EXEMPLARY CONTRIBUTION IN BRINGING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, the Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination (CPALE), a rigorous and highly competitive examination, took place from September 30 to October 2, 2023, at over twenty (20) testing centers throughout the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, on October 6, 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and the Board of Accountancy (BOA) announced the results of the September-October 2023 CPALE, revealing that two thousand seven hundred forty (2,740) of eight thousand seven hundred thirty-four (8,734) candidates, or 31.37%, successfully passed the examination;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Hebban Talib Tawantawan emerged as the first-ranked candidate in the September-October 2023 CPALE, achieving an impressive average score of ninety-one point seventeen percent (91.17%) alongside Ms. Allaine Beduya Collamar of the University of the Philippines (UP) Tacloban;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Tawantawan completed his Bachelor's degree with academic distinction, graduating Magna Cum Laude from the prestigious University of San Jose â€“ Recoletos (USJR) in Cebu City;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Tawantawan, a 23-year-old Bangsamoro professional of Maranao descent, is the proud son of Mr. Abon M. Tawantawan and Mrs. Saida T. Tawantawan of Poblacion, Municipality of Salvador, Lanao del Norte;

**WHEREAS**, his exceptional performance in the September-October 2023 CPALE is a testament to his hard work, perseverance, dedication, and unwavering commitment to academic excellence, which are commendable and worthy of emulation;

**WHEREAS**, his extraordinary achievement serves as a source of inspiration and immense pride for the Bangsamoro community as a whole, especially encouraging the younger generation to strive for academic excellence;

**WHEREAS**, the remarkable accomplishments of Mr. Tawantawan and all other Bangsamoro passers manifest their significant contributions in demonstrating the unwavering dedication and capabilities of the Bangsamoro people to uphold the values of integrity, professionalism, competitiveness, and excellence within the accounting profession at the national level;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority recognizes the outstanding accomplishments of Mr. Hebban Talib Tawantawan and all other Bangsamoro passers in the September-October 2023 CPALE, and deems it appropriate to commend their dedication in bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to express profound congratulations and commendations to Mr. Hebban Talib Tawantawan for his exceptional achievement in ranking first in the September-October 2023 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination, and to all Bangsamoro passers for their exemplary contributions in bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to present a copy of this resolution to Mr. Hebban Talib Tawantawan as a token of recognition and appreciation of his achievement, and for his information, as well as for the necessary effects of this resolution.
