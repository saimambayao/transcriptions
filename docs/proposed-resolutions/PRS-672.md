**PROPOSED RESOLUTION NO. 672**

Introduced by:
**Hon. Eddie M. Alih, Hon. Matarul M. Estino, Hon. Mohagher M. Iqbal, Hon. Aida M. Silongan, Hon. Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba, Hon. Mary Ann M. Arnado, Hon. Baileng S. Mantawil**

**A RESOLUTION URGING THE RELEVANT AUTHORITIES TO CONDUCT A JUST, SPEEDY, AND TRANSPARENT INVESTIGATION INTO THE BRUTAL KILLING OF DR. SONATRIA D. GASPAR, FORMER ASSISTANT SCHOOLS DIVISION SUPERINTENDENT OF THE MINISTRY OF BASIC, HIGHER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION OF SULU PROVINCE**

**WHEREAS**, Article II, Section 5 of the 1987 Constitution emphasizes the State’s obligation to maintain peace and order, protect life, liberty, and property, and promote the rule of law;

**WHEREAS**, Article IV of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL), affirms the Bangsamoro Government’s commitment to peace, justice, and the fundamental rights of the people;

**WHEREAS**, Article XIV, Section 3 of the 1987 Philippine Constitution recognizes the role of education in fostering patriotism, nationalism, and the total development of individuals and communities, emphasizing the importance of protecting educators who serve as pillars of knowledge, peace, and progress;

**WHEREAS**, Dr. Sonatria D. Gaspar, Assistant Schools Division Superintendent of the Province of Sulu, was a dedicated public servant committed to advancing education and improving the lives of the people of Sulu and the broader Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM);

**WHEREAS**, last February 7, 2025, Dr. Gaspar was shot multiple times in the head and killed inside her office at Barangay San Raymundo in Jolo, Sulu;

**WHEREAS**, such acts of violence against public officials and educators create an atmosphere of fear and intimidation, hindering the flow in essential public services and the pursuit of peace and development in the BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, the killing of Dr. Gaspar undermines the efforts of the BARMM to promote stability, development, and moral governance, particularly in the education sector;

**WHEREAS**, there is an urgent need to bring the perpetrators of this heinous crime to justice and to ensure that such acts are not repeated in the BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, the BOL establishes the Bangsamoro Government as a vehicle for lasting peace, social justice, and inclusive development, and explicitly mandates the protection of human rights, the promotion of moral governance, and the eradication of all forms of injustice and violence;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, that we, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, strongly condemn the killing of Dr. Gaspar and extend our deepest condolences to her family, colleagues and the entire educational community of Sulu and the BARMM;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that we call upon the relevant authorities, including the Bangsamoro Government, the Police Regional Office - Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, and the National Bureau of Investigation (NBI), to immediately conduct a thorough, impartial, and expeditious investigation into the killing of Dr. Gaspar, in accordance with the principles of justice and accountability enshrined in the 1987 Philippine Constitution and the BOL;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that we urge the Bangsamoro Government and the National Government to provide adequate protection for public officials, educators, and community leaders who are at risk of violence due to their work in promoting peace, development, and moral governance;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that we call upon all sectors of society, including civil society organizations, religious leaders, and local communities, to cooperate fully with the investigation and to support efforts to uphold justice and the rule of law, as envisioned in the 1987 Philippine Constitution and the BOL;

**BE IT FINALLY RESOLVED**, that copies of this resolution be transmitted to the Ministry of Basic, Higher and Technical Education, Ministry of the Interior and Local Government, the PNP, the Director of the NBI, and other relevant authorities for immediate action.
