**RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING ALL PARLIAMENTARY AND STATUTORY COMMITTEES OF THE BTA PARLIAMENT TO CONDUCT MEETINGS, HEARINGS, AND CONSULTATIONS DURING THE RECESS OF THE SECOND BTA PARLIAMENT**

**WHEREAS**, the First Regular Session of the Second BTA Parliament will adjourn sine die on 21 March 2023 | 29 Shaâ€™aban 1444 AH per Resolution No. 300 of the BTA Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, there are pending deliberations on priority codes such as the Bangsamoro Local Governance Code (BLGC) and other important bills and resolutions, and ongoing investigations in aid legislation before the different committees of the BTA Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, the expeditious consideration of these pending measures is in the best interest of the Bangsamoro;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the BTA Parliament, To authorize all Parliamentary and Statutory Committees of the Parliament to conduct meetings, hearings and consultations during the recess of the Parliament;

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, That this Resolution shall take effect on the date of its adoption and shall remain in force until amended or repealed.
