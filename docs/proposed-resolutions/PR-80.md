**A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING PROFOUND GRATITUDE AND APPRECIATION TO PRESIDENT FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR. FOR APPOINTING CARLITO G. GALVEZ, JR. AS SECRETARY OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENTIAL ADVISER ON PEACE, RECONCILIATION AND UNITY (OPAPRU) AND EXTENDING UTMOST CONGRATULATIONS TO SECRETARY GALVEZ, JR. FOR HIS WELL-DESERVED APPOINTMENT.**

**WHEREAS,** the OPAPRU is mandated to manage, direct, integrate, and supervise the implementation of the Comprehensive Peace Process, including initiatives that promote and reinforce national reconciliation and unity ¹.

**WHEREAS,** Section 6, Article IV of R.A. No 11054 otherwise known as Bangsamoro Organic Law, states that [T]he Bangsamoro Government shall promote unity, peace, justice, and goodwill among Bangsamoro Peoples.

**WHEREAS,** consistent with the above provision, Section 1, Article XIV of Bangsamoro Organic Law also provides that [T]he Bangsamoro Government, xxx, shall intensify development efforts for the rehabilitation, reconstruction, and development of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region as part of the normalization process. xxx

**WHEREAS,** in keeping with the foregoing and other pertinent laws, rules and regulations, the OPAPRU is tasked with a wide range of duties and responsibilities crucial in the delivery of appropriate services in response to the needs of their respective recipients among others:

1. To complete agreements with the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) and Moro National Liberation Front (MNLF) towards healing and reconciliation in the Bangsamoro.
2. To end local communist armed conflict.
3. To complete and sustain the gains of the signed peace agreements.
4. To enhance the reliance of peace of communities and vulnerable groups.
5. To enhance delivery of Conflict-Sensitive and Peace-Promoting (CSPP) complaint development interventions in addressing the key drivers of conflict through convergence and complementation in support of the peace processes.

**WHEREAS,** we, as members of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority wish to extend our utmost appreciation to the *President of this great Republic of the Philippines, Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr.* for the appointment of Carlito G. Galvez, Jr. as Secretary of OPAPRU. The need for a competent authority duly appointed by His Excellency to continue the peace process in Bangsamoro territory are invaluable and indispensable.

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Transition Authority also expresses utmost support and warm congratulations to Secretary of the Office of the Presidential Adviser on Peace, Reconciliation and Unity (OPAPRU), Secretary Carlito G. Galvez, Jr. We raise our hopes and prayers that Secretary Galvez, Jr. will continue advancing the gains of the Bangsamoro peace process and eventually attain a just and lasting peace for the nation and for all Filipinos.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING PROFOUND GRATITUDE AND APPRECIATION TO PRESIDENT FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR. FOR APPOINTING CARLITO G. GALVEZ, JR. AS SECRETARY OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENTIAL ADVISER ON PEACE, RECONCILIATION AND UNITY (OPAPRU) AND EXTENDING UTMOST CONGRATULATIONS TO SECRETARY GALVEZ, JR. FOR HIS WELL-DESERVED APPOINTMENT.**

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to His Excellency President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr. and Secretary Carlito G. Galvez, Jr.
