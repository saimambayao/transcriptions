**RESOLUTION**
**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING SAMSODIN ABDULRAHMAN, THE FIRST WEIGHTLIFTER FROM THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION, FOR WINNING A GOLD MEDAL IN THE 81-KILOGRAM DIVISION OF THE SINGAPORE WEIGHTLIFTING INTERNATIONAL 2023**

**WHEREAS,** Section 19 (1), Article XIV of the 1987 Constitution states that the State shall promote physical education and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and amateur sports, including training for international competitions, to foster self-discipline, teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;

**WHEREAS,** Section 13, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall recognize the vital role of the youth in nation-building, promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, and nationalism, encourage involvement in public and civic affairs, and promote mental and physical fitness through sports;

**WHEREAS,** the Singapore Weightlifting Federation hosted the 2023 Singapore Weightlifting International from June 2 to 4, 2023 at D’Marquee, Downtown East, Singapore¹;

**WHEREAS,** Samsodin Abdulrahman, a Maranao from the Bangsamoro town of Bubong, Saguiaran, Lanao del Sur², competed in the 81-kilogram bodyweight division at the Singapore Weightlifting International competition;

**WHEREAS,** he began his 18-year weightlifting journey in fitness training in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. He served as a head coach, personal trainer, and a rehabilitation specialist³;

**WHEREAS,** he is the first Maranao athlete to receive the distinction in the history of the weightlifting competition, therefore his success benefits not just himself but also the entire Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS,** Abdulrahman serves as an inspiration to all the Bangsamoro youth, young professionals, and aspirants to strive harder in order to excel in their chosen endeavor, to aim for greater heights of excellence and to relentlessly pursue in their respective sports. His perseverance, dedication and courage are worthy of emulation by the entire Bangsamoro populace;

**WHEREAS,** he is currently getting ready for the International Weightlifting Federation (IWF) tournament that will take place in Poland on August 18–26, 2023⁴;

**WHEREAS,** it is appropriate to recognize and commend him for his outstanding performance and contribution that brought pride, honor and glory to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** by the BTA Parliament, to pass and approve this Resolution Congratulating and Commending Samsodin Abdulrahman, the First Weightlifter from the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region for Winning a Gold Medal in the 81-Kilogram Division of the Singapore Weightlifting International 2023.

**RESOLVED FURTHER** that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to Mr. Samsodin Abdulrahman for information.

Adopted,

**ENGR. BAINTAN ADIL-AMPATUAN, MNSA**
