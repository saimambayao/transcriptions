**A RESOLUTION URGING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT TO RECONSTITUTE AND CONVENE THE BANGSAMORO COUNCIL OF LEADERS IN LIGHT OF THE 2025 NATIONAL AND LOCAL ELECTIONS AND THE PRIOR APPOINTMENT OF A NEW CHIEF MINISTER, PURSUANT TO SECTION 9, ARTICLE VI OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11054 AND SECTIONS 3 AND 4, BOOK II OF BANGSAMORO AUTONOMY ACT NO. 13**

**WHEREAS**, Section 9, Article VI of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL), provides for the creation of the Bangsamoro Council of Leaders as an advisory body to the Chief Minister, composed of elected and appointed officials as well as sectoral representatives;

**WHEREAS**, Sections 3 and 4, Book II of Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13, or the Bangsamoro Administrative Code, elaborate on the composition and mandate of the Bangsamoro Council of Leaders, emphasizing its role in promoting consultative, transparent, and inclusive governance;

**WHEREAS**, Honorable Abdulraof A. Macacua was appointed as Chief Minister of the Bangsamoro Government prior to the 2025 National and Local Elections, assuming leadership in a critical period of transition and political realignment;

**WHEREAS**, the results of the 2025 National and Local Elections have introduced changes to the roster of elected officials in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, thus affecting the composition of the Bangsamoro Council of Leaders as originally constituted;

**WHEREAS**, Chief Minister Abdulraof Macacua, in his statement following the 2025 elections, emphasized the importance of unity and moral governance, declaring:

*"Let us move forward united in purpose, grounded in the principles of moral governance, and committed to building a stronger Bangsamoro that reflects the dignity and dreams of every constituent."*

**WHEREAS**, in line with this call and in keeping with the spirit of representative and moral governance, there is a compelling need to reconstitute and convene the Bangsamoro Council of Leaders to ensure it accurately reflects the present political and sectoral landscape of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to respectfully urge the Bangsamoro Government, through the Office of the Chief Minister, to reconstitute and convene the Bangsamoro Council of Leaders in accordance with Section 9, Article VI of R.A. No. 11054 and Sections 3 and 4, Book II of Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13;

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that such reconstitution and subsequent convening be undertaken in full consultation with relevant stakeholders and sectors, upholding the principles of inclusivity, accountability, and moral governance enshrined in the Bangsamoro Organic Law.
