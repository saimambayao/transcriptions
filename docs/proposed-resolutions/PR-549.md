**RESOLUTION EXTENDING GRATITUDE TO PRESIDENT FERDINAND ROMUALDEZ MARCOS, JR. FOR THE APPOINTMENT OF SHEIKH SABUDDIN N. ABDURAHIM AS SECRETARY OF THE NATIONAL COMMISSION ON MUSLIM FILIPINOS**

**WHEREAS**, the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos (NCMF) plays a pivotal role in promoting and safeguarding the rights and welfare of the Muslim community in the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, the appointment of key officials within the NCMF is instrumental in ensuring the effective execution of its mandates and the representation of diverse Muslim communities across the nation;

**WHEREAS**, Sheikh Sabuddin N. Abdurahim, a distinguished individual hailing from Tawi-Tawi, has been appointed by His Excellency President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr. as the Secretary of the NCMF, marking a historic milestone as the first Tawi-Tawian to assume such a significant role;

**WHEREAS**, Sheikh Sabuddin N. Abdurahim brings with him a wealth of experience, profound knowledge, and unwavering dedication to advancing the interests of the Muslim populace, thereby enriching the leadership landscape within the NCMF;

**WHEREAS**, the appointment of Sheikh Sabuddin N. Abdurahim underscores President Marcos's commitment to inclusivity, diversity, and equitable representation within the government institutions, fostering unity and harmony among the various Muslim communities in the Philippines;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Members of the Bangsamoro Parliament to express its profound gratitude and appreciation to His Excellency President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr. for his discerning decision in appointing Sheikh Sabuddin N. Abdurahim as the Secretary of the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos; and that this Parliament commends and congratulates Sheikh Sabuddin N. Abdurahim on his historic appointment as the first Tawi-Tawian to hold the esteemed position of Secretary of the NCMF.

Resolved further that copies of this resolution be forwarded to His Excellency President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr., Sheikh Sabuddin N. Abdurahim, and the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos for their information and records.
