Authored by MPs **BASSIR D. UTTO MPA, TAWAKAL L. MIDTIMBANG, SUWAIB L. URANO, MUDJIB C. ABU, MOHAMMAD KELIE U. ANTAO, MICHAEL E. MIDTIMBANG, JAAFAR JAMBO L. MATALAM, ATTY. SITTIE FAHANIE U. UYOD, BAILENG UTTO SIMPAL MANTAWIL and KADIL MONERA SINOLINDING JR.**

Co-Authored: **GOVERNMENT OF THE DAY.**

**RESOLUTION ENCOURAGING EVERY BANGSAMORO TO PUT-ON THEIR RIGHT HAND ON THEIR LEFT BREAST EVERYTIME WHILE SINGING BANGSAMORO HYMN, AS A SIGNS OF RESPECT, UNIQUENESS, LOYALTY, UNITY AND SOLIDARITY UNDER ONE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT.**

WHEREAS, the Republic Act 11054 as the legal basis for the existence of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao is fruit of long-time armed struggle, poured with bloodsheds, spent with innocent lives and properties, where many Bangsamoro were became orphanage, single parent and homeless;

WHEREAS, with the aforementioned legal basis, the establishment Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) gave closure to the long-time armed struggle and open up new beginning of and for Bangsamoro Autonomous Government under one Philippines government;

WHEREAS, under the principle of said Republic Act 11054, otherwise known as Bangsamoro Basic Law, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Government, among all other rights granted, is entitled to make its own Bangsamoro Hymn to be sung in every official occasion within its territory as a sign of uniqueness in region, loyalty, unity and solidarity among all bangsamoros;

WHEREAS, putting-on of right hand in the left breast while singing Bangsamoro Hymn gave everyone a strong impression that we are a proud and unique Bangsamoro, that we respect and we are loyal to our homeland and religion, that we are united by principle and that we are in solidarity in standing against all hindrances and obstacles in fulfilling all our commitments to the0 peace agreement between the GRP and the MILF.

**NOW THEREFORE, be it resolved,**

**AS IT IS NOW HEREBY RESOLVED; that this BTA Resolution, Re; “RESOLUTION ENCOURAGING EVERY BANGSAMORO TO PUT-ON THEIR RIGHT HAND ON THEIR LEFT BREAST EVERYTIME WHILE SINGING BANGSAMORO HYMN, AS A SIGN OF RESPECT, UNIQUENESS, LOYALTY, UNITY AND SOLIDARITY UNDER ONE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT”, be passed in the service of Bangsamoro**

Adopted,
