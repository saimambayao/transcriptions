**RESOLUTION**

**URGING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT THROUGH THE MINISTRY OF TRADE, INVESTMENT AND TOURISM (MTIT) TO INTENSIFY MARKET MONITORING SYSTEM, IMPLEMENT PREVENTIVE MEASURES, AND CLOSELY MONITOR THE PRICES OF BASIC COMMODITIES IN STORES AND MARKETS TO PREVENT OVERPRICING PRACTICES DURING THE MONTH OF RAMADAN IN THE ENTIRE BANGSAMORO REGION**

**WHEREAS,** Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law, declared as one of its basic policies that the *Bangsamoro Government shall ensure every Filipino citizen in its territorial jurisdiction the provision of the basic necessities and equal opportunities in life. Social justice shall be promoted in all aspects of life and phases of development in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;*

**WHEREAS,** Section 24, Article XIII of the Bangsamoro Organic Law on Trade and Industry in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, states that *“the Bangsamoro Government recognizes the private sector as a mover of trade, commerce, and industry. To achieve equity, social justice, and economic development, it shall encourage and support the building up of entrepreneurial capability in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and shall recognize, promote, protect, and support the development of cooperatives and other medium, small, and micro enterprises”;*

**WHEREAS,** Ramadan is a sacred month in Islamic calendar year, observed by Muslims worldwide as a time for spiritual renewal, reflection, compassion, devotion, and charitable acts;

**WHEREAS,** every month of Ramadan prices of basic commodities have always been increased significantly. This price increase is a phenomenon that is recurring every year;

**WHEREAS,** majority of the Bangsamoro people, both the low and middle income class, are the one who are primarily suffering from the unfair and unreasonable rising of prices of commodities during the month of ramadan;

**WHEREAS,** the price gouging and overpricing practices will not only bring undue financial strains but also goes against the spirit of generosity and charity that defines the sacred month of ramadan;

**WHEREAS,** there is a need for the Bangsamoro Government to immediately act in anticipating and controlling the price increase of commodities and prevent businesses and traders from taking advantage of the situation during Ramadan where demand for food is increased;

**WHEREAS,** this Resolution intends to protect the consumers and make local markets, businesses and food related establishments to comply in maintaining fair and reasonable prices of the essential basic commodities during Ramadan;

**NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED AS HEREBY RESOLVED,** to urge the Bangsamoro Government through the Ministry of Trade, Investment, and Tourism (MTIT) to intensify the market monitoring system, implement preventive measures, and closely monitor the prices of basic commodities in stores and markets to prevent the overprising practices practices during the month of Ramadan in the entire Bangsamoro Region.

**ADOPTED.**
