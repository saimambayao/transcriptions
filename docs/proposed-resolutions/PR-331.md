**RESOLUTION**

**CALLING ON THE MINISTRY OF PUBLIC ORDER AND SAFETY (MPOS) TO FORMULATE AND SUBMIT TO THE OFFICE OF THE CHIEF MINISTER A REGIONAL INTEGRATION PROGRAM FOR REBEL RETURNEES, INCLUDING THE ARMED SUPPORTERS AND SYMPATHIZERS OF THE ABU SAYYAF AND OTHER TERRORIST GROUPS**

WHEREAS, Article II of the 1987 Constitution declares the State’s policy of renouncing war as an instrument of national policy;

WHEREAS, Article II also declares the following principles and policies of the State:

i. The maintenance of peace and order, the protection of life, liberty, and property, and the promotion of the general welfare are essential for the enjoyment by all the people of the blessings of democracy; and

ii. The State shall promote a just and dynamic social order that will ensure the prosperity and independence of the nation and free the people from poverty through policies that provide adequate social services, promote full employment, a rising standard of living, and an improved quality of life for all.

WHEREAS, pursuant to these principles and policies, the National Government entered into a Comprehensive Agreement with the Moro Islamic Liberation Front and the passage, ratification and implementation of the Bangsamoro Organic Law;

WHEREAS, among the powers granted to the BARMM are the (a) Administration of justice; (b) Customary laws; (c) Development programs and laws for women, labor, the youth, the elderly, the differently abled, and indigenous peoples; (d) Disaster risk reduction and management; (e) Education and skills training; (f) Housing and human settlements; (g) Humanitarian service and institutions; (h) Human rights; (i) Labor, employment, and occupation;

WHEREAS, the BOL also empowers the Bangsamoro Government to “*promote health and safety, xxxx, xxx, xxx, maintain peace and order, and preserve the comfort and convenience of its inhabitants.*”

WHEREAS, the BOL also declares it to be the policy of the Regional Government to “*promote social justice*”, “*unity, peace, justice, and goodwill among all peoples, as well as encourage a just and peaceful settlement of disputes*” and to “*ensure every Filipino citizen in its territorial jurisdiction the provision of the basic necessities and equal opportunities in life.*”

WHEREAS, pursuant thereto, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament created the Ministry of Public Order and Safety with the following functions, among others:

*e. Formulate and implement programs, projects, and activities relating to religious tolerance, preventing and countering violent extremism;*
*f. Conduct programs, projects and activities relating to the promotion of peace and sustainable development, reconciliation, unification, and peaceful co-existence; and*
*x - x - x - x*
*h. Implement laws, policies, programs and projects of the BARMM concerning peace, public order, and safety;*

WHEREAS, Former President Rodrigo Roa Duterte issued Proclamation No’s. 1090 and 1091, both concurred in by Congress, granting amnesty to MNLF and MILF combatants;

WHEREAS, the Office of the Presidential Adviser on the Peace Reconciliation and Unity (OPAPRU) is already pushing for a localized peace engagement (LPE), through a whole-of-nation approach, acknowledging that it is more efficient in resolving the long-standing armed revolution in the country. It also declared that:

“OPAPRU believes that LPE is the most effective way of talking peace. Through this approach, local government units and members of the community can directly address the roots of the insurgency, thus preventing the communities from being exploited by the insurgents.”

WHEREAS, the Quran declares the primacy of reconciliation in 8:61: “*If the enemy inclines towards peace, then you should also incline towards peace and trust in Allah, for He is the One that hears and knows all.*”;

WHEREAS, cognizant of all these principles, policies, and laws, the Bangsamoro Government, on its own, is also implementing various programs, like KAPIANAN and other programs being implemented by the MSSD, TESDA, MILG, and other agencies;

WHEREAS, these various programs are intended to help members of the MILF, and to some extent, the MNLF, who have voluntarily abandoned armed revolutions and joined the peace efforts of their organization;

WHEREAS, most members of the Abu Sayyaf Group and other terrorist groups in Sulu and even in other parts of the region, have already abandoned their terrorist ideologies and voluntarily surrendered to lawful authorities;

WHEREAS, unfortunately, there is no available program for ASG surrenderees and other former terrorists except one - time assistance that the local government can provide on the occasion of their formal surrender;

WHEREAS, there is a need to further reduce or remove the armed supporters and sympathizers of the ASG and other terrorist groups to completely defeat terrorism or reduce their capacity to perform terrorist acts;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved**, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to call on the Ministry of Public Order and Safety (MPOS) to formulate a Regional Reintegration Program for members of the Abu Sayyaf Group and other terrorist groups who voluntarily abandoned their terrorist ideologies and armed struggle in order become productive member of society; or, in the alternative, Adopt the National Reintegration Program for the Returnees under Administrative Order No. 10, Series of 2018.
