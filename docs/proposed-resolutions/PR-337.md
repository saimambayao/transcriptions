**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST CONDOLENCES AND SYMPATHIES TO THE BEREAVED FAMILY OF HADJI ABDULLAH “BOBBY BULDON” PACASEM AND EXTENDING PROFOUND APPRECIATION AND GRATITUDE FOR HIS CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE BANGSAMORO STRUGGLE AND TO THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION**

**WHEREAS,** Hadji Abdullah Pacasem, known as “Bobby Buldon” is a distinguished member of the Top 300 of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front;

**WHEREAS,** he fought together with the former Chairperson of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) Ustadz Hashim Salamat (Allah Yarhamu) for the cause of the Bangsamoro struggle in furtherance of a long-lasting peace in Mindanao;

**WHEREAS,** he was a member of the Central Committee of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) and served as the head of its Central Research Agency;

**WHEREAS,** on June 20, 2023, he peacefully passed away in his home in Parang, Maguindanao Del Norte;

**WHEREAS,** he is survived by his loving wife Hadja Samira Casad Pacasem and children namely; Norodin C. Pacasem, Nur-aisha C. Pacasem, Judge Nurhani C. Pacasem-Nur, RSW, PSSg Mubarak C. Pacasem, Atty Ubaida C. Pacasem, CPA, Farzaanah C. Pacasem, LPT, Atty. Amon Jadeedah C. Pacasem, CPA, Pat Abunawas C. Pacasem, Engr. Muammar C. Pacasem, Muhammad Abdu C. Pacasem, and Engr. Nur-Hashim C. Pacasem;

**WHEREAS,** his dedication and sacrifices for the welfare of the Bangsamoro people will be remembered with great honor and pride and will never be forgotten in our history as a Bangsamoro people;

**NOW THEREFORE,** be it;

**RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED,** that the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament expresses its deepest condolences and sympathies to the bereaved family of Hadji Abdullah “Bobby Buldon” Pacasem and extends profound appreciation and gratitude for his contributions to the Bangsamoro struggle and to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to the bereaved family of the late Hadji Abdullah “Bobby Buldon” Pacasem.

Adopted,

**SUHARTO S. ESMAEL**
Member of the Parliament
