**A RESOLUTION ISSUING DRESS CODE GUIDELINES FOR PARLIAMENTARY STAFF AND VISITORS DURING PARLIAMENT SESSIONS**

**WHEREAS,** under Section 4, Rule XII of Resolution No. 268 or the *Rules, Procedures, and Practices of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament*, the Committee on Ethics and Privileges shall have jurisdiction over all matters relating to the duties, conduct, rights, privileges, immunities, integrity, and reputation of the Parliament and its Members;

**WHEREAS,** in keeping with the dignity of the Bangsamoro Parliament, the Parliamentary Staff and Visitors present in the Parliament building during sessions are enjoined to observe the proper dress code;

**WHEREAS,** during sessions, Parliamentary Staff and Visitors shall wear formal, business, or smart casual attire;

**WHEREAS,** whenever practicable, the Secretariat, Legislative Pages, Security Personnel, and other similar parliamentary staff in the session premises shall wear their appropriate uniforms and readable Anti-Red Tape Authority (ARTA) Identification Cards;

**WHEREAS,** subject to exceptional cases,ยน staff of the respective Members of the Parliament and visitors, including caterers, shall be issued temporary visible ARTA Identification cards for identification purposes;

**WHEREAS,** the Office of the Secretary-General and the Protocol Division are hereby requested to implement the faithful compliance of this Code;

**WHEREAS,** the following are considered inappropriate for the abovementioned personnel and visitors to wear during parliament sessions:

1. Tattered or distressed jeans;
2. Shoes or heels exceeding 3 inches in height;
3. Noisy footwear;
4. Clothing in neon colors;
5. Miniskirts;
6. Outfits with plunging necklines;
7. Sleeveless garments; and
8. Leggings.

**WHEREAS,** non-compliance with any of these guidelines shall result in denial of entry to the session hall.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to implement these guidelines commencing with the Opening Session of the Second Regular Session of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority.
