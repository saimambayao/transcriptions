**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE SINCERE CONDOLENCES OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT ON THE DEATH OF PASTOR TROY ERIC O. CORDERO, REPRESENTATIVE OF SETTLER COMMUNITIES TO THE BARMM COUNCIL OF LEADERS, AND CONVEYING ITS PROFOUND GRATITUDE FOR HIS INVALUABLE CONTRIBUTION TO THE BANGSAMORO STRUGGLE FOR SELF- DETERMINATION**

**WHEREAS**, Pastor Troy Eric O. Cordero was one of the ardent supporters, in the areas of Cotabato City and North Cotabato province, of the Bangsamoro struggle for self-determination;

**WHEREAS**, Pastor Cordero was the Chairman of Metro Cotabato Ministerial Fellowship, Incorporated (MCMF), one of the most active religious organizations located in Cotabato City listed under Church / religious organizations in Cotabato City;

**WHEREAS**, Pastor Cordero was one of the thirty-two (32) MCMF pastors and key leaders who participated in an inter-faith dialogue between the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) and the Philippine Council of Evangelical Churches (PCEC) held at the Kadtuntaya Foundation Inc. in Cotabato City on March 09, 2011,;

**WHEREAS**, MCMF is known for leading churches in advancing peace and reconciliation ministries in their local contexts around Cotabato City and Maguindanao Provinces;

**WHEREAS**, Pastor Cordero was appointed by the Chief Minister Hon. Ahod B. Ebrahim as Settler Communitiesâ€™ representative to the BARMM Council of Leaders last February 20, 2023;

**WHEREAS**, on May 4, 2023, Pastor Cordero passed away due to health complications;

**WHEREAS**, the passing away of Pastor Cordero, a dedicated and passionate church leader, is a great loss not only to Christian communities but to the whole Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, his dedication and sacrifices in the furtherance of the welfare of the Bangsamoro people will be remembered with great honor and pride and will never be forgotten in our history as a Bangsamoro people;

**NOW THEREFORE**, be it;

**RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, that the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament expresses its sincere condolences on the death of Pastor Troy Eric O. Cordero, representative of Settler Communities to the BARMM Council of Leaders, and conveys its profound gratitude for his invaluable contribution to the Bangsamoro struggle for self-determination;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to the bereaved family of the late Pastor Troy Eric O. Cordero.

Adopted, _________

**SUHARTO S. ESMAEL, MSc**
