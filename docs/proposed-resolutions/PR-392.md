**A RESOLUTION URGING THE BANGSAMORO INTERGOVERNMENTAL RELATIONS BODY TO PROVIDE REGULAR UPDATES AND COMPREHENSIVE INFORMATION TO THE BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT REGARDING INTERGOVERNMENTAL RELATIONS BODY (IGRB) AGREEMENTS**

**WHEREAS**, Article VI of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law, establishes the National Government-Bangsamoro Government Intergovernmental Relations Body (IGRB) to coordinate and resolve issues on intergovernmental relations through regular consultation and continuing negotiation in a non-adversarial manner;

**WHEREAS**, the IGRB consists of seven mechanisms, including the Philippines Congress-Bangsamoro Parliament Forum, Intergovernmental Fiscal Policy Board, Joint Body for the Zones of Joint Cooperation, Intergovernmental Infrastructure Development Board, Intergovernmental Energy Board, Bangsamoro Sustainable Development Board, and the Council of Leaders;

**WHEREAS**, the IGRB serves as a crucial platform for facilitating discussions and reaching agreements between the National and Bangsamoro Governments on matters of mutual interest and governance;

**WHEREAS**, these agreements will provide valuable direction to the Bangsamoro Parliament in the creation of sensible and meaningful legislations, ensuring that what the National and Bangsamoro Governments agreed upon aligns well to enhance effective and efficient governance in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, that the Bangsamoro Parliament hereby calls upon the Intergovernmental Relations Body (IGRB) mechanisms of the Bangsamoro Government to provide regular and comprehensive updates to the parliament regarding the discussions and agreements made within the IGRB;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that these updates are intended to ensure that Members of Parliament (MPs) are well-informed about the outcomes and commitments resulting from the IGRB meetings and deliberations, emphasizing the identification of harmonies and potential disparities between proposed legislations and intergovernmental agreements;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the aforementioned updates shall be presented before the Bangsamoro Parliament sessions or relevant committees, facilitating MPs' opportunities to seek clarifications and make informed decisions during the legislative processes;

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the Bangsamoro Government, through its relevant agencies, shall provide all necessary support and cooperation to facilitate the process of conveying updates and ensuring their successful communication;

**BE IT FINALLY RESOLVED**, that this resolution shall take immediate effect upon its approval.
