**RESOLUTION**

**COMMENDING AL-BASHER “BASTY” JUMANGIT BUTO FOR COLLECTING SILVER MEDALS ON STANDARD EVENT, RAPID AND BLITZ CATEGORIES AND OBTAINING CANDIDATE MASTER (CM) WORLD TITLE IN THE 6TH EASTERN ASIA YOUTH CHESS CHAMPIONSHIP 2022 IN BANGKOK, THAILAND**

**WHEREAS,** on November 4-12, 2022, the Philippine delegation/team won the overall standing in just concluded 2022 Eastern Asia Youth Chess Championships at Asia Hotel, Bangkok, Thailand out of twelve (12) countries participated with almost two hundred (200) players competed. Participating countries are China, Hongkong, Indonesia, Korea, Malaysia, Mongolia, Myanmar, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, Taiwan, and Vietnam.

**WHEREAS,** the Philippine team garnered 32 Gold medals, 27 Silver medals and 22 Bronze medals and our Philippine Youth obtained several World Titles such Woman International Master (WIM), FIDE Master (FM), Woman FIDE Master (WFM) and Candidate Masters (CM) from this international event.

**WHEREAS,** Al-Basher collected silver medals on Boys-under 12 (Standard Event) Individual, Boys-under 12 (Rapid) Individual and Boys-under 12 (Blitz) Individual in the said international event. As a team, he also collected gold medal on Standard Event and silver medal on Boys-under 12 (Blitz) categories. On top of that, National Master Al-Basher obtained Candidate Master (CM) from this event.

**WHEREAS,** Al-Basher, a 12-year-old chess prodigy, has been competing in several chess tournaments and has been recognized for his excellence in the realm of chess battle. At the very young age, he amasses multiple winnings and medals in different age categories in local and international tournament representing the Philippines and was recognized as the youngest Bangsamoro to hold both National Master (NM) and Candidate Master (CM) titles.

**WHEREAS,** Al-Basher is a Maranao currently residing in Cainta, Rizal. The 8th son of Bong Buto and Jamellah. B oth parents are very supportive despite of financial constraints. Their overwhelming support, patience, resilience, and determination led their son to accomplished these remarkable achievements.

**WHEREAS,** Al-Basher is a grade school student and dreamed of studying at prestigious school in Metro Manila and becoming an airline pilot. He also wants to become a chess grandmaster and to create a chess club to share his skills and knowledge with the youngsters and build a Masjid.

**WHEREAS,** Al-Basher is a great inspiration to the community. He brings pride and hope to the Bangsamoro people. His feats will be forever lingering in the history of Philippines and Bangsamoro.

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** to commend Al-Basher “Basty” Jumangit Buto for collecting silver medals on Standard Event, Rapid and Blitz categories and obtaining Candidate Master (CM) world title in the 6th Eastern Asia Youth Chess Championship 2022 in Bangkok, Thailand.
