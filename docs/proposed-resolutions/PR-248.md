**RESOLUTION**
**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING DR. DATU MOHAMMAD SALIH A. NUR FOR RECEIVING AN OUTSTANDING DISTINCTION AWARD GIVEN DURING THE 2023 UNIVERSITY OF OKLAHOMA, UNITED STATES OF AMERICA COMMENCEMENT CEREMONY**

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Government recognizes the indispensable role of education in honing the good future of the youth vis-à-vis the outstanding young individuals for their contribution in building and shaping a better Bangsamoro Region;

WHEREAS, Dr. Mohammad Salih Abas Kida Nur is a native Maguindanaoan from Datu Odin Sinsuat of the Province of Maguindanao del Norte who graduated Summa Cum Laude in Doctor of Physical Therapy Studies from the University of Oklahoma in the United States of America;

WHEREAS, the University of Oklahoma’s Outstanding Distinction Award is the highest academic honor bestowed to Professional Degree Graduates of the University;

WHEREAS, Dr. Mohammad Salih A. Nur received the Outstanding Distinction Award by the University of Oklahoma during its 131st Commencement Ceremony held on May 10, 2023;

WHEREAS, Dr. Mohammad Salih A. Nur’s international academic achievement is another living proof that the Bangsamoro have worldclass qualities which could be a source of inspiration and motivation for many to strive for excellence;

WHEREAS, the Outstanding Distinction Award received by Dr. Mohammad Salih A. Nur has brought pride and honor to the Bangsamoro and has shown to the world the spirit of Bangsamoro competitiveness and excellence;

WHEREAS, as a token of appreciation, this outstanding performance by our fellow Bangsamoro deserves to be commended by passing a resolution expressing the Bangsamoro Transition Authority’s recognition and commendation to Dr. Mohammad Salih A. Nur for his exemplary academic achievement.

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to congratulate and commend Dr. Mohammad Salih A. Nur for receiving an Outstanding Distinction Award given during the 2023 University of Oklahoma, United States of America Commencement Ceremony.**

**RESOLVED, FURTHER, that a copy of this Resolution be presented to Dr. Mohammad Salih A. Nur, for information.**
