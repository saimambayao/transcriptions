**RESOLUTION**
**URGING THE CONCERNED MINISTRIES OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT TO MONITOR AND ENSURE THE ENFORCEMENT AND COMPLIANCE WITH BANGSAMORO AUTONOMY ACT NO. 1, SECTION 4 OR THE HOISTING AND DISPLAY OF THE BANGSAMORO FLAG**

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Article II of R.A. No. 11054, otherwise known as "*Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao*," states that:

xxx
*The Bangsamoro Parliament hereinafter referred to as the "Parliament," shall adopt the official flag, emblem, and hymn of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. The flag of the Republic of the Philippines shall always be displayed alongside the Bangsamoro flag.*"
xxx

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, of Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 1, otherwise known as "*An Act Adopting the Official Flag of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao*," (BAA No. 1) provides:

xxx
**Section 2.** Declaration of Policy. In the exercise of its right to self-governance, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao is free to adopt its Bangsamoro Flag that is reflective of its people's identity, history, heritage, struggles and aspiration.
xxx

**WHEREAS**, Section 4 of BAA Act No. 1 provides that:

xxx
"*All Bangsamoro offices, public and private schools, colleges and universities, **public offices**, government-owned or controlled corporations and other government instrumentalities **are required to use and display the Bangsamoro Flag**. Other offices of the Bangsamoro that are outside its area of autonomy are likewise required to use and display the Bangsamoro Flag.*"
xxx

**WHEREAS**, during the series of public consultations for the Bangsamoro Electoral Code and the Bangsamoro Local Governance Code, some members of this Parliament observed that there are public and private schools, colleges and universities, and public offices within the region that do not comply with provisions of Section 4, BAA No. 1;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Flag is reflective of our identity, history, struggles and aspiration as a people and its display upholds these ideals;

**WHEREAS**, the display of the Bangsamoro Flag serves as a reminder of the values and principles that our Bangsamoro Region stands for and the sacrifices made to uphold them;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED BY THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY**, to urge the concerned ministries of the Bangsamoro Government to monitor and ensure the enforcement and compliance with Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 1, Section 4 of the hoisting and display of the Bangsamoro Flag.

Adopted,
