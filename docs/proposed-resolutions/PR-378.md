**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING MS. FATIMA ALIAH QUIBRANZA DIMAPORO ON HER APPOINTMENT AS THE UNDERSECRETARY OF THE DEPARTMENT OF SOCIAL WELFARE AND DEVELOPMENT**

**WHEREAS**, pursuant to Republic Act No. 5416, the Department of Social Welfare and Development was created to develop and implement a comprehensive social welfare program consisting of (1) preventive and remedial programs and services for individuals, families and communities;(2) protective, remedial and developmental welfare services for children and youth; (3) vocational rehabilitation and related services for the physically handicapped, ex-convict and individuals with special needs; and (4) training and research and special projects¹;

**WHEREAS**, the Office of the Undersecretary for Operations shall be responsible for leading the implementation of the Department’s programs for social welfare and development, as mandated by laws, as well as directives of the President and of the Secretary. Particularly, the Undersecretary for Operations is concerned with the effective implementation of DSWD’s statutory programs, flagship social protection programs, disaster response projects, as well as other timely and relevant social welfare concerns as they emerge. Finally, the Undersecretary for Operations oversees three (3) Sub-Clusters: the Statutory Programs Sub-Cluster, the Specialized Programs Sub-Cluster, and the Disaster Response Management Sub-Cluster²;

**WHEREAS**, on the 28th day of July 2023, the Malacañang Palace, released an appointment paper signed by President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr., addressed to Fatima Aliah Quibranza Dimaporo, hereby appointing the latter as the Undersecretary of the Department of Social Welfare and Development;

**WHEREAS**, Dimaporo is a native of Lanao del Norte and del Sur, the daughter of the former governor of Lanao del Norte Abdullah Dimaporo and the incumbent governor of Lanao del Norte Imelda “Angging” Quibranza Dimaporo;

**WHEREAS**, Undersecretary Dimaporo was the former 2nd District Representative of Lanao del Norte during the 15th Congress and as well as Executive Director of the World Youth Alliance Foundation in New York, which promotes protection of human dignity³;

**WHEREAS**, the appointment of Undersecretary Dimaporo is a clear indication of trust and confidence accorded her by the President, and indicates her competence, dedication and commitment to the public service, signifying honor and pride to the Bangsamoro people and an inspiration for other Bangsamoro women;

**WHEREAS**, the national government’s recognition of the capability and competency of Bangsamoro Women through Undersecretary Dimaporo merits her commendation by the Bangsamoro Parliament;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to congratulate Ms. Fatima Aliah Quibranza Dimaporo on her appointment as the Undersecretary of the Department of Social Welfare and Development;**

**RESOLVED FURTHER, that this Resolution be presented to Undersecretary Fatima Aliah Quibranza Dimaporo, for information.**
