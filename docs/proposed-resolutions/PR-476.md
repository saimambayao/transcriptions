**A RESOLUTION CONDEMNING THE OUTRAGEOUS BOMBING INCIDENT TRANSPIRED ON DECEMBER 3, 2023 AT MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY (MSU) MARAWI GYMNASIUM.**

WHEREAS, Section 5, Article IX of R.A. No. 11054 provides that, [T]he Bangsamoro Government shall guarantee religious freedom and the free exercise thereof pursuant to the Constitution, national laws, and principles of international law. The Bangsamoro Government shall protect all persons from harassment or any undue pressure, coercion, and violence on account of religion.

WHEREAS, in accordance with legal principles and ethical considerations, this Parliament expresses a resolute condemnation of the outrageous bombing incident that transpired at the Mindanao State University (MSU) Marawi Gymnasium on the occasion of the First Sunday of Advent on December 3, 2023, during the commemoration of a Holy Mass by the Catholic faithfuls.

WHEREAS, the Parliament acknowledges the solemnity and significance of the First Sunday of Advent, a cherished occasion observed by the Catholic faithfuls worldwide, symbolizing hope, peace, and anticipation.

WHEREAS, the reprehensible bombing incident that killed at least four (4) and injured more than fifty (50) others on Sunday, December 3, 2023 at Mindanao State University on such a momentous day resulted in tragic loss of lives, causing injury to numerous innocent individuals and inflicting severe damage to the university’s premises.

WHEREAS, the bombing occurred during the celebration of religious worship is an act notoriously offensive to the feelings of the faithful.

WHEREAS, the Parliament vehemently condemns all forms of violence, terrorism, and acts that threaten the safety, peace, and harmony of any community, particularly during moments of reverence and communal gathering.

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Parliament extends heartfelt condolences to the families who lost their loved ones, expresses solidarity with the injured, and stands in unwavering support of the entire community during this challenging times.

WHEREAS, the Parliament urges law enforcement agencies to conduct a thorough investigation into this heinous act, swiftly bring the perpetrators to justice, and take proactive measures to prevent such incidents from occurring in the future.

WHEREAS, a copy of this resolution be transmitted to the appropriate authorities, the Mindanao State University administration, and made available to the public to express our resolute stance against such acts and our unwavering support for peace and unity.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION CONDEMNING THE OUTRAGEOUS BOMBING INCIDENT TRANSPIRED ON DECEMBER 3, 2023 AT MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY (MSU) MARAWI GYMNASIUM.**

Adopted, December ____, 2023.
