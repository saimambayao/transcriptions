**RESOLUTION**
**EXPRESSING WARM CONGRATULATIONS AND SALUTATIONS TO MP ATTY. SHA ELIJAH B. DUMAMA-ALBA**

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament extends its heartfelt congratulations to MP Atty. Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba for her historic appointment as the first female Minister of the Ministry of the Interior and Local Government (MILG);

**WHEREAS,** MP Atty. Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba, as the Bangsamoro Parliament Floor Leader, has demonstrated exceptional leadership, dedication, and competence, making her eminently qualified for the responsibilities entrusted to her in the crucial role of MILG Minister;

**WHEREAS,** MP Dumama-Alba, has graduated a degree of Bachelor of Arts in Public Administration at the University of the Philippines-Diliman, Quezon City; and has acquired a Degree of Law at the San Beda College, Mendiola;

**WHEREAS,** MP Dumama-Alba, is a graduate of Master’s in Public Management- Major in Regional and Local Governance;

**WHEREAS,** MP Atty. Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba, aside from being a lawyer, has a Career Executive Service Eligibility;

**WHEREAS,** MP Atty. Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba, has played a crucial role in the peace process between the Government and the Moro Islamic Liberation Front as of this present time;

**WHEREAS,** MP Dumama-Alba, held other different key government positions; the latest as an Attorney-General of the Bangsamoro Region;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament expresses its utmost confidence in MP Atty. Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba’s ability to lead the MILG and contribute significantly to the advancement and prosperity of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM);

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** that the Bangsamoro Parliament officially congratulates and warmly welcomes MP Atty. Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba as the new Minister of the Ministry of the Interior and Local Government (MILG);

**BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED,** that the Bangsamoro Parliament extends its heartfelt thanks and commendation to Atty. Naguib G. Sinarimbo for his exemplary works and accomplishments as the first Minister of the Ministry of the Interior and Local Government (MILG) in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM).
