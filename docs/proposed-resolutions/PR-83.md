**RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE GOVERNMENT OF THE DAY FOR THE IMMEDIATE ACTIVATION OF THE SHARI'AH PUBLIC ASSISTANCE OFFICE (SPAO), BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM)**

WHEREAS, pursuant to **Section 13, Title V** of the Administrative Code of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), which states:
**Section 13. Composition.** - There is hereby created a Shari'ah Public Assistance Office (SPAO), to be headed by a director assisted by two (2) counselors who are members of the Philippine Shari'ah Bar. They shall be assisted by an immediate staff;

WHEREAS, paragraph four (4) of **Section 13** of the same Title states: The Shari'ah Public Assistance Office is hereby established in each of the Shari'ah judicial districts to provide legal assistance to poor or indigent party litigants;

WHEREAS, paragraph five (5) of **Section 13** of the same Title states: That in the absence of an appropriate ministry or office to which the Shari'ah Public Assistance Office may be attached, it shall, in the *interim*, be attached to the office of the Chief Minister;

WHEREAS, it is believed to be highly constitutional and righteous that the immediate activation of the Shari'ah Public Assistance Office in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), will help our poor constituents who cannot afford to hire a Shari'ah Counselor to appear before the Shari'ah Courts;

WHEREAS, the activation of the said office has no any difference from other offices created by the BARMM which are now operational;

WHEREAS, with its timely relevance the Shari'ah Public Assistance Office (SPAO) is one of the Bangsamoro Justice Systems enunciated in the Administrative Code the implementation of Shari'ah in all aspects of lives for their survival in this mundane world, and the activation of the said office mentioned earlier signifies our concerns and empathy for our constituents who need the services of a Shari'ah Counselor;

WHEREAS, it is the prioritized concern of our office to address the issues and concerns affecting our constituents and with the activation of the Shari'ah Public Assistance Office, it will benefit our Shari'ah Counselors including the indigent pauper litigants-clienteles. In the eyes of the law, we remain equal no matter where we come from or how much money we have in our pockets;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it resolved, and it is hereby resolved, to request the Government of the Day for the immediate activation of the Shari'ah Public Assistance Office, Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM).

**IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED FUTHER** that this resolution will be submitted to the office of the Chief Minister, Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, Honorable Ahod B. Ebrahim for his kind consideration and approval.
