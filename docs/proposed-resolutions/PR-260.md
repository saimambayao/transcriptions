**RESOLUTION**

**CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING NISHRIEN AMATONDING BARUA, A GRADUATE OF MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY – MAIN CAMPUS FOR BEING TOP 5 IN THE RECENT LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR PROFESSIONAL TEACHERS GIVEN BY THE PROFESSIONAL REGULATION COMMISSION (PRC)**

**WHEREAS**, the March 19, 2023 Licensure Examination for professional teachers set the standard to effectively screen the next generation of selfless and compassionate teachers of the country;

**WHEREAS**, the result of said licensure examination was announced by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) on May 19, 2023, declaring officially that 24,819 out of 60,896 examinees passed the elementary level exam;

**WHEREAS**, Nishrien Amatonding Barua, a resident of Malabang, Lanao del Sur, and a graduate of Bachelor of Elementary Education in Mindanao State University, garnered the 5th highest place with a rating of 91.40 in the said examination;

**WHEREAS**, Nishrien Amatonding Barua, having proven her competence by spotting the Top 5 nationwide, brought fame and honor to the region and has become a role model to the young and aspiring Bangsamoro students;

**WHEREAS**, it is but fitting for the Bangsamoro Government through the Bangsamoro Parliament to give due recognition and commendation to Nishrien Amatonding Barua for her exemplary display of character in her chosen field of a career worthy of emulation;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Parliament, to **CONGRATULATE AND COMMEND NISHRIEN AMATONDING BARUA, A GRADUATE OF MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY – MAIN CAMPUS FOR BEING TOP 5 IN THE RECENT LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR PROFESSIONAL TEACHERS GIVEN BY THE PROFESSIONAL REGULATION COMMISSION (PRC);**

**BE IT RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that a copy of this resolution be furnished to Ms. Nishrien Amatonding Barua.
