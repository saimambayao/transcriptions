**Proposed Resolution No. 372**

Introduced by: **MP ROMEO K. SEMA**

**“A RESOLUTION COMMENDING HIS EXCELLENCY PRESIDENT FERDINAND R. MARCOS JR. FOR HIS 2nd STATE OF THE NATION ADDRESS & EXPRESSING GRATITUDE FOR HIS RECOGNITION AND SUPPORT TO THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO”**

**WHEREAS**, on July 24, 2023, the President of the Philippines, His Excellency Ferdinand R. Marcos Jr., delivered his 2nd State of the Nation Address in House of Representatives, Batasan Complex, Quezon City;

**WHEREAS**, several members and officials of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao attended and graced the same event as a show of support and solidarity of the Bangsamoro Parliament with the national government;

**WHEREAS**, His Excellency President Marcos expressed in his speech that he is proud of the progress that the BARMM has taken. He added that “it [BARMM] will be self-governing, progressive, and effective” and more astounding is his expressed declaration of continued support for the progress of BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, His Excellency President Marcos affirmed that it is through BARMM that the nation has strengthened its prospects for finally achieving sustainable progress that is anchored on a true and lasting peace in [Mindanao];

**WHEREAS**, His Excellency President Marcos also recognized the partnership of the national government and BARMM in regard to energy exploration, development, and utilization within BARMM which would expectedly renew investor interest;

**WHEREAS**, the Members of the Parliament are beyond delighted and grateful for the recognition of the good President for the efforts of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, the said recognition is wholeheartedly taken by the Bangsamoro government as an inspiration to keep steadfast in its efforts in sustaining the gains of the peace process and to continue the delivery of services and programs to the Bangsamoro people;

**NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament to commend His Excellency President Ferdinand R. Marcos Jr. for his 2nd State of the Nation Address and to express gratitude for his recognition and support to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.
