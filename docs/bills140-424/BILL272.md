**AN ACT CREATING THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAYANTOL IN THE PROVINCE LANAO DEL SUR, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

**BE IT ENACTED** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament assembled, as follows;

**SECTION 1. Declaration of Policy.** In the exercise of genuine autonomy and self-governance, the Bangsamoro Government is empowered to create, divide, merge, abolish, or substantially alter boundaries of municipalities or barangays in accordance with a law enacted by the Parliament. The municipalities or barangay created, divided or merged or whose boundaries are substantially altered shall be entitled to their appropriate share in the National Taxes Allocation (NTA), *Provided*, that it shall be approved by a majority of the votes cast in a plebiscite in the political units directly affected.

**SECTION 2. Creation of Municipality.** The Barangays of Runggayan, Kiyanodan, Cambong Bubong Maguing, Bubong Bayabao, Balintao, Maguing Proper, Lilod Maguing, Dilimbayan, Bato Bato, Balawag, Pilimoknan, Camalig, Panayangan and Ilalag are hereby separated from the Municipality of Maguing, Lanao del Sur and constituted into a distinct and independent municipality to be known as Municipality of Bayantol, which hereby created, in the province of Lanao del Sur.

**SECTION 3. Seat of Government.** The seat of government of the Municipality of Bayantol shall be established in Barangay Dilimbayan, which shall be known hereafter as the "Poblacion".

**SECTION 4. Territorial Boundary.** The territorial boundary of the new municipality shall follow the existing boundaries of the constituent barangays as above enumerated in Section 2 hereof.

**SECTION 5. Grants and Donations..** To accept local and foreign grants and donation.

**SECTION 6. Plebiscite Requirement.** The Municipality of Bayantol shall acquire corporate existence upon ratification of its creation by a majority of the votes cast by qualified voters in a plebiscite to be conducted in the barangays comprising the municipality pursuant to Section 2 hereof within sixty (60) days after the approval of the Act.

**SECTION 7. Conduct of Plebiscite.** The Commission on Elections (COMELEC), through the Bangsamoro Electoral Office, shall conduct and supervise the plebiscite for the purpose of creation of the Municipality of Bayantol. The expenses in holding the plebiscite shall be taken out of the available funds of the Commission on Election under the current fiscal year appropriation.

**SECTION 8. Appointment of Municipal Officials.** The municipal government is composed of a Municipal Mayor, Municipal Vice-Mayor and eight (8) members of its Sangguniang Bayan who shall be appointed either by the President of the Republic of the Philippines or Chief Minister of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) immediately after the ratification of this Act in a plebiscite. They shall continue to hold office until their successors shall have been duly elected in the next regular election: *Provided*, That the incumbent elective members of the Sangguniang Bayan of the Municipality of Maguing, Lanao del Sur who are actual residents of the new municipality shall serve the remaining terms of the elective offices in the mother municipality.

**SECTION 9. Financial Assistance Pending Entitlement to National Tax Allocation.** The Bangsamoro Government shall appropriate and provide financial assistance for the administration and operation of the Municipal Government of Bayantol in the amount of not less than THREE MILLION PESOS (PHP 3,000,000.00) every month until such time as the new municipality shall have been allocated its share in the revenue allotment.

**SECTION 10. Separability Clause.** If, for any cause, any provision of this Act is declared unconstitutional or contrary to the provisions of Republic Act No. 1054, the rest of the provisions thereof which are not affected shall in full force and effect.

**SECTION 11. Repealing Clause.** That any provision of this Act which is contrary to existing laws and declared unconstitutional shall be null and void.

**SECTION 12. Effectivity of the Law.** This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days from its publication in the Bangsamoro Gazette or in (1) newspaper of regional circulation.
