**ORGANIZATIONAL OUTCOMES**

**MANDATE**
The Bangsamoro Pilgrimage Authority (BPA) is primarily responsible for the administration of the annual Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia of pilgrims from within the Bangsamoro. It shall formulate and implement programs, projects, and activities for the efficient and effective administration and supervision of the conduct of pilgrimage activities of Bangsamoro pilgrims.

**OVERALL DEVELOPMENT GOAL/S**
Rich and Diverse Bangsamoro Cultures and Identity Preserved and Recognized.
