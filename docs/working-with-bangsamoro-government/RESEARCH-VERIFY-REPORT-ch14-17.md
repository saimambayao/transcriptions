## Errors Found and Fixed

### 1. OHRAORA — Fabricated Glossary Entry (FIXED)

**File**: `18-glossary.md`
**Error**: The glossary contained an entry "OHRAORA (Omnibus Hours Rendering and Attendance / Online Registration of Attendance)" described as "The CSC digital timekeeping system used in government offices." This acronym and expansion are fabricated — no such CSC system exists in any reference file.
**Fix**: Replaced with the correct entry **ORAOHRA (Omnibus Rules on Appointments and Other Human Resource Actions)** — the real CSC regulatory document referenced in footnotes throughout Chapters 14, 15, and 17.

### 2. BDP Pillar 4 — Misquoted Principle (FIXED)

**File**: `16-chapter-16.md`, Pillar 4 (Sense of Patriotism)
**Error**: Text said "love for the Bangsamoro" and "The reputation of the region must be maintained and protected."
**BDP Source**: Says "love for country" and "the reputation and integrity of the place must be maintained and protected."
**Fix**: Corrected to match BDP Ch 4 verbatim: "love for country" and "reputation and integrity of the place."

### 3. LSO Functional Competencies — Inaccurate Names (FIXED)

**File**: `15-chapter-15.md`, Section 15.3, Framework 1
**Error**: LSO competencies listed as "Investigation and research, policy analysis, legal/legislative competence, drafting quality, legislative oversight." Two names were wrong: "drafting quality" (should be "attention to details and quality") and "legislative oversight" (should be "oversight skills").
**Reference**: Parliament Staff Competencies reference file lists: Investigation and Research Skills, Policy Making Analysis and Evaluation, Legal and Legislative Competence, Attention to Details and Quality, Oversight Skills.
**Fix**: Corrected to match reference: "Investigation and research, policy making and analysis, legal/legislative competence, attention to details and quality, oversight skills."

### 4. PAO Functional Competencies — Inaccurate Names (FIXED)

**File**: `15-chapter-15.md`, Section 15.3, Framework 1
**Error**: PAO competencies listed as "Stakeholder engagement, representation, advocacy, monitoring and evaluation, constituent service." Missing "project and events management" (competency #4) and "constituent service" is part of competency #1, not a separate item.
**Reference**: Stakeholder Engagement Community Organizing and Constituency Building, Representation Skills, Social Marketing Advocacy and Lobbying, Project Program and Events Management, Monitoring Reporting and Evaluation.
**Fix**: Corrected to: "Stakeholder engagement and constituency building, representation, advocacy and lobbying, project and events management, monitoring and evaluation."
