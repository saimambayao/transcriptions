
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

SECOND REGULAR SESSION

**RESOLUTION NO. 330**

RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING HON. ABDULRAOF A. MACACUA ON HIS APPOINTMENT BY HIS EXCELLENCY PRESIDENT FERDINAND MARCOS, JR. AS THE OFFICER-IN-CHARGE GOVERNOR OF MAGUINDANAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on April 5, 2023, His Excellency President Ferdinand Marcos, Jr. appointed Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua as Officer-in-Charge (OIC) Governor of the Province of Maguindanao del Norte;

WHEREAS, Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua is a distinguished leader who had served the Bangsamoro people with distinction for many years, and his appointment as OIC Governor of Maguindanao del Norte is a testament and furtherance to this;

WHEREAS, Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua had served as Member of the Parliament of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), and Senior Minister of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) and was also the Chief of Staff of the Bangsamoro Islamic Armed Forces (BIAF) of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF);

WHEREAS, the appointment of Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua as OIC Governor of Maguindanao del Norte is a positive development for the Bangsamoro people, as it represents a step forward in the ongoing peace process in the region;

WHEREAS, Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua's appointment as OIC Governor of Maguindanao del Norte is a recognition of his leadership, integrity, competence, dedication, and commitment to public service, and is a reflection of the trust and confidence that His Excellency President Ferdinand Marcos, Jr. has in his capabilities;

WHEREAS, Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua's appointment as OIC Governor of Maguindanao del Norte will undoubtedly be instrumental in promoting the culture of peace and in bringing about positive changes and reforms in the province, and will contribute to the overall development of the Bangsamoro region:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the BTA Parliament, To congratulate and commend Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua on his appointment by His Excellency President Ferdinand Marcos, Jr. as OIC Governor of Maguindanao del Norte.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament expresses its full support for Hon. Abdulraof A. Macacua as he takes on this new and important role, and pledges to work with him to advance the interests of the Bangsamoro people.

RESOLVED, FINALLY, That a copy of this resolution be forwarded to the Office of the OIC Governor of Maguindanao del Norte, for information.

ADOPTED, Shawwal 25, 1444/May 16, 2023.
