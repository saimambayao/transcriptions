**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 19**

**A RESOLUTION CALLING FOR THE ACTIVE PARTICIPATION OF THE YOUTH IN THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT.**

**WHEREAS**, the future of the Bangsamoro Land belongs to the youth; and that according to Gat Jose Rizal, our Philippine national hero, the youth is the "Hope of the Motherland";

**WHEREAS**, RA 11054 also known as the "Bangsamoro Organic Law" Article IX Section 13 mandates the "Rights of the Youth";

**WHEREAS**, as mandated by RA 11054, the Bangsamoro Government needs to recognize the vital role of the youth in nation-building, promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, intellectual, and social well-being;

**WHEREAS**, the government is required to inculcate patriotism and nationalism into the youth;

**WHEREAS**, the youth has to be encouraged specially in their involvement in public and civil affairs, and to promote their mental and physical fitness through sports;

**WHEREAS**, the current generation of young people are the largest demographic group in the Philippine history with equal rights, risks and aspirations;

**WHEREAS**, emphasizing youth development in our government today shall greatly influence the adaptive capacity of our young people and our government in the future;

**WHEREAS**, to further enhance the impact of youth leadership, innovations must be introduced particularly the commitment of concerned government bodies to review the models to be used in the integration of modules and the allotment of resources which are essential elements in pursuing the implementation of the youth program;

**WHEREAS**, the P.D. 603 also known as the Child and Youth Welfare Code states that "The Child is one of the most important assets of the nation. Every effort should be exerted to promote his welfare and enhance his opportunities for a useful and happy life";

**WHEREAS**, young people experience various forms of psycho-social challenges including bullying, cyber bullying, all forms of discrimination depression, anxiety, violence, and suicide;

**WHEREAS**, Section 2b of the RA 8044 which states that the encouragement of youth involvement in character-building and development activities for civic-efficiency, stewardship of natural resources, agriculture and industrial productivity and an understanding of world economic commitments on tariffs and trade and participation in structure for policy-making and program implementation to reduce the incidence of poverty and accelerated socio-economic development;

**WHEREAS**, every youth has a right to protection against exploitation, improper influences, hazards, and other conditions or circumstances prejudicial to his physical, mental, emotional, social, and moral development;

**WHEREAS**,it is essential that the government shall recognize the need of active participation of the youth sector in the formulation of different committee for environment, sports, leadership, and community service;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, that through the efforts of recognizing the role of youth, the following action may be made:

1. The youth shall have positions in the government by joining the Bangsamoro Leaders in their work and offices to provide them an advanced training in the management of the Bangsamoro land for the future.
2. The youth shall be molded through the government’s investment on Programs on Youth Development which shall yield results on the full utilization of youth’s abilities, talents, and skills that will allow them to showcase their characters and capabilities, thereby making them integral part of citizenry building.
3. The youth shall be favored to develop an interest or realization of importance to combat issues such as corruption, active participation of civil society, demand for accountability, and transparency.
4. The government shall create appropriate programs for the youth that would care, protect, rehabilitate, and advocate psycho-social wellness to improve and mainstream the program with current government programs.
5. The government shall request all health care facilities to accommodate all women and young people regardless of economic status, ethnicity and race, age, religion, health status, sexual orientation and gender identities seeking health needs.
6. The youth of today thru the assistance of the government leaders shall create an advocacy for environmental preservation and conservation.
7. The youth shall be encouraged to recommend local level implementations through youth organizations the proper edification about a good quality family relationship - which can fabricate an attainment of proper attitudes, honing of a high level of self-confidence, behavior, and good social relationship among youth for self-fulfillment and productive living.

**Adopted, July 29, 2019.**
