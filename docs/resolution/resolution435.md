**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 435**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING ABDURAHIM MOH. SALI ABDULLA, WHO RANKED 10TH IN THE NOVEMBER 2023 NURSING LICENSURE EXAMINATION, AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS THEREBY GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS,** Section 13, Article II of the 1987 Constitution and Section 13, Article IX of the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao recognize the vital role of the youth in nation-building, shall promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, intellectual, and social well-being as well as their role to inculcate in the youth patriotism and nationalism, and encourage their involvement in public and civic affairs;

**WHEREAS,** the Philippines Nurses Licensure Examination (NLE) was conducted last November 11 to 12, 2023 in nineteen (19) testing centers all over the Philippines;

**WHEREAS,** the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced on December 2, 2023 that out of thirty-two thousand two hundred three (32,203) examinees in the NLE, twenty-five thousand seven hundred sixty-one (25,761) or eighty percent (80%) passed the said board exam;

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Abdurahim Moh. Sali Abdulla ranked 10th with an average score of 89.20% wherein he was the only Bangsamoro among the coveted top 10 places in the said examination;

**WHEREAS,** Mr. Abdulla is a Tausug who graduated cum laude from Western Mindanao State University (WMSU) in Zamboanga City in May 2023;

**WHEREAS,** acknowledging his remarkable achievement encourages and inspires the Bangsamoro youth to pursue academic and professional excellence, drawing inspiration from his exemplary performance as a role model;

**WHEREAS,** his exemplary performance as topnotcher is an inspiration for the Bangsamoro children, youth, and young professionals not only in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao but also in the entire country which then merits this Parliament’s recognition and commendation:

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate and commend Abdurahim Moh. Sali Abdulla, who ranked 10th in the November 2023 Nursing Licensure Examination, and all Bangsamoro passers thereby giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE,** To present this foregoing Resolution to Mr. Abdurahim Moh. Sali Abdulla as a token of our recognition and appreciation for his achievement, as well as for this resolution’s necessary effects.

**ADOPTED,** *Sha’ban 16, 1445/February 26, 2024.*
