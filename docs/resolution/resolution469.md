**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 469**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING THE BANGSAMORO TOPNOTCHERS IN THE MARCH 2024 LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR PROFESSIONAL TEACHERS, AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS**

**WHEREAS**, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and the Board for Professional Teachers on March 24, 2024 released the result of the Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers (LEPT), which was conducted on March 17, 2024, across thirty-six (36) testing centers nationwide;

**WHEREAS**, a total of twenty thousand eight hundred ninety (20,890) or 46.67% elementary teachers out of forty-four thousand seven hundred sixty-four (44,764) examinees and fifty thousand five hundred thirty-nine (50,539) or 58.78% secondary teachers out of eighty-five thousand nine hundred eighty (85,980) examinees successfully passed the LEPT;

**WHEREAS**, among the successful passers, seven (7) Bangsamoro professionals achieved top rankings in LEPT, with the following names, ranks, and average scores:

**ELEMENTARY LEVEL**

**NAME**  **RANK**  **SCORE**
(a) Fatmah Aliah T. Mandi  Top 8  91.00%
(b) Naharah D. Abdulazis  Top 9  90.80%
(c) Rahima A. Akmad  Top 9  90.80%
(d) Hanania P. Dimacaling  Top 10  90.60%

**SECONDARY LEVEL**

**NAME**  **RANK**  **SCORE**
(a) Kawther Sacor I. Abdulhalim  Top 9  91.00%
(b) Nidzraleen J. Abdurahim  Top 9  91.00%
(c) Almira O. Mimbala  Top 10  90.80%

**WHEREAS**, achieving a rank within the Top 10 in any national licensure examinations, such as the LEPT, is a rare and prestigious achievement, bringing profound pride and honor not only within the family, school, and tribe, but also resonating throughout the entirety of the Bangsamoro community;

**WHEREAS**, the exemplary performance of these topnotchers serves as an inspiration for the Bangsamoro children, youth, and all individuals striving for excellence, not only in the Bangsamoro region but also in the entire country;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To congratulate and commend the Bangsamoro topnotchers in the March 2024 Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers, and all Bangsamoro passers.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To extend heartfelt congratulations and commendations to Ms. Fatmah Aliah Talicop Mandi for achieving the remarkable feat of securing Top 8, Ms. Naharah Dimaocor Abdulazis and Ms. Rahima Adal Akmad as Top 9, and Ms. Hanania Pundato Dimacaling as Top 10 in the March 2024 LEPT for the Elementary Level along with Ms. Kawther Sacor Indanan Abdulhalim and Ms. Nidzraleen Julkarnain Abdurahim for securing Top 9, and Ms. Almira Omar Mimbala as Top 10 for the Secondary Level, and all Bangsamoro passers for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE**, To furnish a copy of this foregoing Resolution to the above-mentioned seven (7) topnotchers, for information.

**ADOPTED**, *Dhu Al-Hijjah* 21, 1445/June 27, 2024.
