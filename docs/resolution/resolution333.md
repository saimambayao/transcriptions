RESOLUTION NO. 333

RESOLUTION COMMENDING ENGR. MOHAIMEN HADJI OMAR PASAWILAN, A BANGSAMORO, FOR RANKING TOP TEN (10) IN THE APRIL 2023 PHILIPPINE CIVIL ENGINEERS LICENSURE EXAMINATION, THEREBY GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE

WHEREAS, Engr. Mohaimen Hadji Omar Pasawilan is a Maguindanaon from the Municipality of Matalam, North Cotabato;

WHEREAS, he is a 2022 Bachelor of Civil Engineering graduate of the University of Southern Mindanao-Kabacan, Cum Laude;

WHEREAS, on April 29, 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced that out of Sixteen Thousand Nine Hundred Thirty-Six (16,936) examinees, Five Thousand Eight Hundred Eighty-Seven (5,887) passed the Philippine Civil Engineers Licensure Examination given by the Board of Civil Engineering;

WHEREAS, Engr. Pasawilan, with an impressive rating of 89.30%, clinched the rank ten (10) of the coveted top ten (10) places of the said licensure examination;

WHEREAS, his exemplary performance brought honor not only to his family but also to the entire Bangsamoro People;

WHEREAS, his intelligence and resolve will reverberate across the halls of the Bangsamoro peopleâ€™s history and serve as an inspiration to the entire Bangsamoro populace that with ample hard work and determination, no dream is too big and no goal is too impossible to achieve:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To commend Engr. Mohaimen Hadji Omar Pasawilan for ranking top ten (10) in the April 2023 Philippine Civil Engineers Licensure Examination, thereby giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That a copy of this Resolution be presented to Engr. Mohaimen Hadji Omar Pasawilan, for information.

ADOPTED, Shawwal 27, 1444/May 18, 2023.
