**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 510**

**RESOLUTION RESPECTFULLY REQUESTING THE GOVERNMENT SERVICE INSURANCE SYSTEM TO ESTABLISH BRANCHES OR EXTENSION OFFICES IN DIFFERENT AREAS IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

**WHEREAS**, by virtue of Commonwealth Act No. 186 and Republic Act No. 829, otherwise known as *“The Government Service Insurance System (GSIS) Act of 1997”*, the GSIS is a social insurance institution that provides a defined benefit scheme under the law. It insures its members against the occurrence of certain contingencies in exchange for their monthly premium contributions;

**WHEREAS**, the GSIS covers all government workers, except the following:

a. Members of the Judiciary and Constitutional Commissions who are covered by separate retirement laws;

b. Contractual employees who have no employee-employer relationship with their agencies;

c. Uniformed members of the Armed Forces of the Philippines and the Philippine National Police, including the Bureau of Jail Management and Penology and the Bureau of Fire Protection;

d. Barangay and Sanggunian officials who are not receiving fixed monthly compensation; and

e. Employees who do not have monthly regular hours of work and are not receiving fixed monthly compensation;

**WHEREAS**, GSIS offers a variety of social security benefits, such as life insurance benefits, separation or retirement benefits, and disability benefits;

**WHEREAS**, aside from insurance benefits in the event of contingencies, GSIS also offers products to assist members in their financial needs, including:

a. Multi-purpose Loan;
b. Policy Loan;
c. Enhanced Emergency Loan;
d. Enhanced Pension Loan; and
e. Pensioners Emergency Loan;

**WHEREAS**, as of January 18, 2022, of the forty-two (42) branch offices in the country, only one (1) office is established in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), located in Cotabato City;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government has established provincial, district, and municipal offices in the six (6) provinces of the region, with hired employees who are members of the GSIS;

**WHEREAS**, the Local Government Units within the BARMM have also hired employees who are members of the GSIS;

**WHEREAS**, the collective number of employees of the Provinces of Lanao del Sur, Sulu, Tawi-Tawi, Basilan, and Maguindanao del Sur necessitates the services of the GSIS through the establishment of branch or extension offices;

**WHEREAS**, this Resolution seeks to assist our Bangsamoro government workers in managing their affairs with the GSIS, and to encourage others to enroll for membership;

**WHEREAS**, in view of the financial and economic developments in our region, access to financial services should be convenient in order to encourage our Bangsamoro people to be financially literate, to be secure from contingencies, and to safeguard their retirement:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To respectfully request the Government Service Insurance System to establish branches or extension offices in different areas in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That the Bangsamoro Government include this proposal in the Intergovernmental Relations Body agenda to ensure timely and appropriate action of the GSIS.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, To forward this Resolution to the Government Service Insurance System, for consideration and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED**, *Rabi Al-Akhir* 21, 1446/October 24, 2024.
