SECOND REGULAR SESSION

RESOLUTION NO. 346

RESOLUTION SUPPORTING THE CALL OF PRESIDENT FERDINAND "BONGBONG" MARCOS, JR. TO CREATE AND ORGANIZE A DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE OF THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS AND HIGHWAYS (DPWH) DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICES INCLUDING THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A DPWH REGIONAL OFFICE FOR THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM)

WHEREAS, Section 26, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution states, in part, that the State shall guarantee equal access to opportunities for public service;

WHEREAS, Section 7, Article IV of Republic Act No. 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure every Filipino citizen in its territorial jurisdiction the provision of the basic necessities and equal opportunities in life;

WHEREAS, Section 37, Article XIII of Republic Act No. 11054 provides that the National Government shall fund and implement the construction and maintenance of the national roads, bridges, water supply and services, and flood control and irrigation systems and the maintenance of existing airports, seaports, and wharves in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

WHEREAS, Section 8, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall maintain appropriate disaster-preparedness units for immediate and effective relief services to victims of natural and man-made calamities, and shall also ensure the rehabilitation of calamity-affected areas and victims of calamities;

WHEREAS, pursuant to Executive Order No. 710 series of 1981, the Ministries of Public Works and Public Highways were merged for a more effective and sustained implementation of infrastructure projects, and under the restructured set up, the agency was known as the Ministry of Public Works and Highways (MPWH);

WHEREAS, by virtue of Executive Order No. 124 series of 1987, the agency was renamed to Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH). As the primary engineering and construction arm of the government, the DPWH is responsible for the planning, designing, constructing, and maintaining of infrastructure such as roads and bridges, flood control systems, water resource development projects, and other public works in accordance with national objectives;

WHEREAS, on October 27, 2022, Severe Tropical Storm (STS) Paeng, with the international name Nalgae, battered some parts of Maguindanao del Norte, Maguindanao del Sur, Cotabato City, and other parts of Southwestern Mindanao. Heavy rains brought by the STS Paeng caused landslides and severe flooding in those mentioned areas;

WHEREAS, over ten (10) bridges have been damaged in both Maguindanaos due to STS Paeng. Specifically, these are the Ledepan Bridge, Kurintem Bridge, Datu Blah Sinsuat Bridge, Sarakan Street, Matengan Steel Bridge, Darapan Steel Bridge, Lower Magulat Kiga, Upper Magutay Rempes, Labu-labu Bridge, and Nituan Bridge;

WHEREAS, the relief efforts to Cotabato City and Maguindanao have been facing difficulties as medium to heavy vehicles cannot pass through due to the damage of the Labu-labu bridge;

WHEREAS, during the situation briefing to President Ferdinand “Bongbong” Marcos, Jr. on the effects of STS Paeng in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM),

Interior and Local Government Minister Naguib Sinarimbo enumerated the region’s most affected road network which is a critical lifeline to relief operations;

WHEREAS, other provinces of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region were also affected by the STS Paeng;

WHEREAS, President Ferdinand “Bongbong” Marcos, Jr. has recommended the establishment of a District Office of the DPWH in the BARMM to expedite repairs of bridges and national roads after the onslaught of natural calamities;

WHEREAS, noting many bridges were damaged during the storm, His Excellency President Marcos stressed that a District Office would make it easier for the region to get help expeditiously in times of crisis;

WHEREAS, the need for the establishment of a District Office of the DPWH applies also to the other provinces of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

WHEREAS, there is also a need to create a Regional Office of DPWH that will control and supervise the operations of the District Engineering Offices of the DPWH in the BARMM;

WHEREAS, having District Engineering Offices of the DPWH in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region will help ease-in the relief efforts of the government especially on the mobilization of goods and services to most affected areas during calamities;

WHEREAS, the creation and establishment of District Engineering Offices and a Regional Office of DPWH will help fast-track the recovery, rehabilitation, and development of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament, To support the call of President Ferdinand “Bongbong” Marcos, Jr. to create and organize a District Engineering Office of the Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH) District Engineering Offices including the establishment of a DPWH Regional Office for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) for consideration and approval of the BTA Parliament.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That a copy of this Resolution be forwarded to the Office of the President Ferdinand “Bongbong” Marcos Jr., for information.

ADOPTED, Dhul Hijjah 03, 1444/June 21, 2023.
