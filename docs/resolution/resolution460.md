**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 460**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING THE MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY KARATE CLUB MEMBER-ATHLETES FOR THEIR OUTSTANDING ACHIEVEMENTS AT THE 5TH KARATE PILIPINAS NATIONAL CHAMPIONSHIP IN TAGAYTAY CITY, AND FOR BRINGING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that the State shall promote physical education and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and amateur sports, including training for international competitions, to foster self-discipline, teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;

**WHEREAS**, Section 13, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as “*Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao*”, provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall promote, among others, the mental and physical fitness of the youth through sports, affirming its commitment to enhancing sports by fostering the development of programs, leagues, competitions, indigenous games, martial arts, and amateur sports activities;

**WHEREAS**, the 5th Karate Pilipinas National Championship, held at Tagaytay Combat Sports Center, Tagaytay City from March 1-3, 2024, saw the participation of sixty (60) teams with a combined roster of one thousand two hundred thirty-one (1,231) athletes, marking a significant event in the sports calendar of the nation;

**WHEREAS**, the Championship was organized by Karate Pilipinas Sports Federation, Inc., also known as “*Karate Philippines*”, the exclusive governing entity for karate within the Philippines, established in 2018. This entity stands as the only Karate Federation recognized by the World Karate Federation, Asian Karate Federation, and the Philippine Olympic Committee, with its competitions featuring the “*Kumite*” (sparring) and “*Kata*” (patterns of movements) categories;

**WHEREAS**, among the teams who participated, the Mindanao State University Karate Club (MSUKC), a non-profit organization led by *Hanshi* Prof. Punduma B. Sani, the renowned martial arts figure and former Dean of the College of Sports, Physical Education and Recreation at Mindanao State University-Main Campus, Marawi City, has shown exceptional performance;

**WHEREAS**, the MSUKC’s member-athletes, through their dedication and skill, have achieved significant success, namely:

(a) Mr. Abdulhakim A. Abdulrakim, securing a gold medal in Junior Male *Kumite* (-76kg);
(b) Mr. Ahmad G. Ali III, securing a silver medal in Junior Male *Kata*;
(c) Mr. Al-majeed C. Alonto, securing a bronze medal in U21 Men’s *Kumite* (+84kg);
(d) Ms. Pipay Minalang, securing a bronze medal in Senior Female *Kumite* (-56kg); and
(e) Mr. Duma G. Sani II, securing a bronze medal in 8-9 Years Old Boys, *Kumite* (+140cm);

**WHEREAS**, the achievements of the MSUKC’s member-athletes not only inspire young and emerging athletes within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region but also serve as a model of athletic excellence and competitiveness to the broader community;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority seeks to express its profound appreciation and immense pride in the MSUKC’s member-athletes’ distinguished accomplishments, thereby bestowing honor and recognition upon the Bangsamoro people:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To congratulate and commend the Mindanao State University Karate Club member-athletes for their outstanding achievements at the 5th Karate Pilipinas National Championship in Tagaytay City, and for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To furnish this Resolution to the honored MSUKC member-athletes, namely: Mr. Abdulhakim A. Abdulrakim, Mr. Ahmad G. Ali III, Mr. Al-majeed C. Alonto, Ms. Pipay Minalang, and Mr. Duma G. Sani II, as a token of our recognition and appreciation of their exemplary achievements, and for their information and its necessary effects.

**ADOPTED**, *Dhu Al-Hijjah* 12, 1445/June 18, 2024.
