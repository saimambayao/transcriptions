**RESOLUTION NO. 97**

**RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING THE TOP RANKED BANGSAMORO QUR'AN RECITERS WHO REPRESENTED THE COUNTRY IN VARIOUS INTERNATIONAL QUR'AN RECITING COMPETITIONS FOR AROUND HALF A CENTURY SINCE 1969**

**WHEREAS**, Section 4, Article IX of the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao provides that, "the customs, beliefs, and traditions of the Bangsamoro people are hereby recognized, protected, and guaranteed";

**WHEREAS**, Islam is the most dominant influence on the Moro cultures since the era of the Sultanate in the Bangsamoro region;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Qur’an reciters have been winning top placers in international Qur’an reading competitions for around half a century since 1969 garnering honor and strengthening the Islamic faith in our region;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to recognize the top ranked Bangsamoro Qur'an reciters who represented the country in various international Qur'an reciting competitions for around half a century since 1969. The Qur'an reciters brought to our nation and region great honor and pride, to wit:
