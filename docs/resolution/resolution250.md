
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
**BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT**
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

**FOURTH REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 250**

**A RESOLUTION GRACIOUSLY CONGRATULATING ALL SENATORS-ELECT NAMELY: SENATOR ROBIN PADILLA, SENATOR LOREN LEGARDA, SENATOR RAFFY TULFO, SENATOR SHERWIN GATCHALIAN, SENATOR FRANCIS ESCUDERO, SENATOR MARK VILLAR, SENATOR ALLAN PETER CAYETANO, SENATOR JUAN MIGUEL ZUBIRI, SENATOR JOEL VILLANUEVA, SENATOR J.V. EJERCITO, SENATOR RISA HONTIVEROS, AND SENATOR JINGGOY ESTRADA FOR THEIR VICTORY IN THE LAST MAY 9, 2022 SYNCHRONIZED NATIONAL AND LOCAL ELECTIONS**

**WHEREAS**, Senator Robin Padilla, Senator Mark Villar and Senator Raffy Tulfo, although they belong to different political parties, are all newly elected members of the Philippine Senate who, on the bases of their respective concrete plans and programs for their constituents, luckily acquired the fresh mandates and the full confidence of the Filipino voters last May 9, 2022;

**WHEREAS**, Senator Loren Legarda, Senator Sherwin Gatchalian, Senator Francis Escudero, Senator Allan Peter Cayetano, Senator Juan Miguel Zubiri, Senator Joel Villanueva, Senator Jose Victor Ejercito, Senator Risa Hontiveros, and Senator Jinggoy Estrada who also belong to multi-political parties, are all comeback Senators that still retained the trust and confidence of the Filipino people in the last May 9, 2022 synchronized local and national polls;

**WHEREAS**, considering that all of them, the new and the comeback senators, are all credible and possess a high degree of intelligence and mental capacity to legislate both national laws and the laws for local applications, the Bangsamoro people remain steadfast and are optimistic and that their aspirations for lasting peace, progress and development would still be protected, and thereby maintain the status of its integrity; respected in letter and in spirit as provided for under the inked peace agreement and the Bangsamoro Organic Law;

**WHEREAS**, in view of the guiding objectives of the UNITEAM, led by President-elect Ferdinand “Bongbong” Marcos, Jr., and Vice President-elect Sara Duterte-Carpio’s calling for a total change of good governance, unity, and solidarity of the Filipino people, the Bangsamoro populace in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region is confident that the new set of national legislators, the new and the comeback ones would be considerate enough in supporting the laudable programs and vision of the incoming administration which may be anchored on the economic recovery, prosperity and political maturity of the Filipinos as a whole;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate all Senators-elect namely: Senator Robin Padilla, Senator Loren Legarda, Senator Raffy Tulfo, Senator Sherwin Gatchalian, Senator Francis Escudero, Senator Mark Villar, Senator Allan Peter Cayetano, Senator Juan Miguel Zubiri, Senator Joel Villanueva, Senator Jose Victor Ejercito, Senator Risa Hontiveros, and Senator Jinggoy Estrada for their victory in the May 9, 2022 Synchronized National and Local Elections.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That a copy of this Resolution be furnished to the offices of the Senators-elect namely: Senator Robin Padilla, Senator Loren Legarda, Senator Raffy Tulfo, Senator Sherwin Gatchalian, Senator Francis Escudero, Senator Mark Villar, Senator Allan Peter Cayetano, Senator Juan Miguel Zubiri, Senator Joel Villanueva, Senator Jose Victor Ejercito, Senator Risa Hontiveros, and Senator Jinggoy Estrada.

**ADOPTED**, Dhu Al-Qa’dah 17, 1443/June 16, 2022.
