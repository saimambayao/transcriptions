**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 522**

**RESOLUTION SUPPORTING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT, THROUGH THE BANGSAMORO SPORTS COMMISSION, IN ENHANCING AND SUSTAINING THE CONDUCT OF THE INTER-GOVERNMENT AGENCY SPORTS FESTIVAL DURING THE ANNUAL CELEBRATION OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO’S FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY**

**WHEREAS**, Section 15, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that:

*“The State shall protect and promote the right to health of the people and instill health consciousness among them;”*

**WHEREAS**, Section 17, Article II of the same Constitution also expresses that the State shall give priority to sports to foster patriotism and nationalism, accelerate social progress, and promote total human liberation and development;

**WHEREAS**, Section 3, Article V of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the *“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao”*, states that:

*“The Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the power expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective governance and those which are essential to the promotion of general welfare. Within its territorial jurisdiction, the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure and support, among other things, the preservation and enrichment of culture, promote health and safety ... xxx;”*

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) commemorates its anniversary annually, celebrating the establishment of the Autonomous Region and its continuous progress toward peace, development, and prosperity;

**WHEREAS**, the celebration of BARMM’s founding anniversary is a significant event that symbolizes the unity, progress, and cultural heritage of the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, sports and physical activities play a vital role in promoting health, camaraderie, discipline, and teamwork among individuals and communities;

**WHEREAS**, holding a sports festival during the annual celebration of BARMM’s founding anniversary will provide an opportunity to unite employees from various Bangsamoro government ministries, offices, and agencies, fostering camaraderie, respect, and a sense of regional identity;

**WHEREAS**, an inter-government agency sports festival would serve as an excellent platform to strengthen relationships and collaboration among various government agencies within BARMM, enhancing inter-agency cooperation and coordination;

**WHEREAS**, furthermore, such an event will showcase the talents and skills of the Bangsamoro employees, inspiring them to be more participative in sports, and adopt healthy lifestyles;

**WHEREAS**, the sports festival can feature a variety of activities, such as basketball, volleyball, football, athletics, traditional games, and other sports, creating an inclusive event for participants of all ages and abilities;

**WHEREAS**, the conduct of such activity will give the employees something to look forward to, even amidst their hectic schedules and heavy workloads;

**WHEREAS**, organizing an event like this will showcase the Bangsamoro Government’s commitment to promoting health and wellness among its workforce:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To support the Bangsamoro Government, through the Bangsamoro Sports Commission, in enhancing and sustaining the conduct of the inter-government agency sports festival during the annual celebration of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao’s founding anniversary.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present this Resolution to the Bangsamoro Sports Commission, for information and appropriate consideration.
