**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 484**

**RESOLUTION CONDEMNING TO THE HIGHEST DEGREE THE SENSELESS KILLING OF SOUTH UPI VICE MAYOR ROLDAN M. BENITO, HIS COMPANION WENG MARCOS, AND WOUNDING OTHERS, AND URGING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT THROUGH ITS CONCERNED AGENCIES TO CONDUCT AN IMMEDIATE, THOROUGH, AND IMPARTIAL INVESTIGATION**

**WHEREAS,** Section 5, Article II of the 1987 Constitution of the Philippines provides that "*The maintenance of peace and order, the protection of life, liberty and property, and promotion of the general welfare are essential for the enjoyment by all the people of the blessings of democracy;*"

**WHEREAS,** Section 11, Article II of the same declares that "*The State values the dignity of every human person and guarantees the full respect for human rights;*"

**WHEREAS,** Section 1, Article III of the Bill of Rights states that "*No person shall be deprived of life, liberty, or property without due process of law, nor shall any person be denied the equal protection of laws;*"

**WHEREAS,** Section 6, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054, also known as "*Organic law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao*" states that "*The Bangsamoro Government shall fully respect human rights;*"

**WHEREAS,** on August 2, 2024, an ambush incident transpired in Sitio Linao, Barangay Pandan, South Upi, Maguindanao del Sur, which resulted in the deaths of its incumbent Vice Mayor Roldan M. Benito and his companion Weng Marcos and wounded barangay chairperson of Barangay Pandan, and other companions;

**WHEREAS,** we strongly condemn the vicious killing of Hon. Roldan Benito who is a Non-Moro Indigenous Peoples Leader and vice mayor of South Upi, Maguindanao del Sur;

**WHEREAS,** the senseless killing of Vice Mayor Benito and Mr. Marcos and wounding of others are not the first of its kind. This killing is the latest incident to fall under the unexplained and senseless series of killings in the Bangsamoro Region;

**WHEREAS,** Vice Mayor Benito was a revered leader of the TÃ«duray community, a committed public servant, and a passionate proponent of peace and inclusive governance. He staunchly upheld the rights of Indigenous Peoples and safeguarded their ancestral domain, known as "*fusaka inged;*"

**WHEREAS,** given the gravity of the crime, it is crucial that the relevant government authorities conduct a thorough and impartial investigation to shed light on the reasons behind this heinous act and ensure that justice is served for Vice Mayor Benito, Mr. Marcos, their families, and all affected parties;

**WHEREAS,** in the spirit of justice, it is imperative to bring the perpetrators to the court of Law for their criminal acts:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To condemn to the highest degree the senseless killing of South Upi Vice Mayor Roldan M. Benito, his companion Weng Marcos and wounding others, and urging the Bangsamoro Government through its concerned agencies to conduct an immediate, thorough and impartial investigation.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** That the Special Investigation Task Group organized by the Philippine National Police be directed to conduct an investigation on the death of Vice Mayor Roldan M. Benito and should submit a report to the Bangsamoro Parliament through the Committee on Public Order and Safety to guide the Bangsamoro Government in the investigation on the death of Vice Mayor Roldan Benito and his companions.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY,** To furnish a copy of this Resolution to the Special Investigation Task Group organized by the Philippine National Police, Police Regional Office Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, and South Upi Municipal Police Station, for information and compliance.

**ADOPTED,** Safar 15, 1446/August 19, 2024.
