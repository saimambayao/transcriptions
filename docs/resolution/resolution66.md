
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
**BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY**
BARMM Compound, Cotabato City

**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 66**

**RESOLUTION CALLING FOR THE DISPLAY OF THE BARMM FLAG IN ALL PUBLIC BUILDINGS, SUCH AS GOVERNMENT OFFICES, TOWN HALLS, PROVINCIAL, REGIONAL, MUNICIPAL, AND BARANGAY HALLS, PUBLIC PLAZAS, INSTITUTIONS OF LEARNING, AND SUCH OTHER PLACES AS MAY BE DEEMED APPROPRIATE BY THE PARLIAMENT**

**WHEREAS**, Section 2 Article 2 of RA 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that, the Bangsamoro Parliament, hereinafter referred to as the Parliament, shall adopt the official flag, emblem and hymn of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao. The flag of the Republic of the Philippines shall always be displayed alongside the Bangsamoro flag. Whenever the Bangsamoro hymn is sung it shall always be preceded by the national anthem;

**WHEREAS**, the Parliament on August 28, 2019 adopted the Bangsamoro Autonomous Act 1, entitled “An Act Adopting the Official Flag of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao”;

**WHEREAS**, the BARMM flag is the ultimate symbol of the identity, integrity, and self-determination of the Bangsamoro people. Reverence and respect, shall at all times be accorded to our flag, which embody our ideals and aspirations, and express our principle of sovereignty and solidarity;

**WHEREAS**, the BARMM flag shall be displayed in all public buildings, such as government offices, town halls, regional, provincial, municipal, and barangay halls, public plazas, institutions of learning, and other places as may be deemed appropriate by the parliament alongside with the Philippine flag in equal height;

**NOW THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, to cause the display of the BARMM flag in all public buildings, such as government offices, town halls, regional, provincial, municipal and barangay halls, public plazas, institutions of learning, and other places as may be deemed appropriate by the Parliament alongside with Philippine flag.

**Adopted**, February 21, 2020.
