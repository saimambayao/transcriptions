**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 454**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST CONDOLENCES AND SYMPATHIES TO THE FAMILY OF THE LATE ROSTOM SAABAN, THE THIRD BASE COMMANDER OF 106^TH BASE COMMAND OF THE SOUTHERN MINDANAO FRONT**

**WHEREAS,** Rostom Saaban is a native resident of Barangay Malibpolok, Rajah Buayan, Province of Maguindanao Del Sur;

**WHEREAS,** he had immensely and significantly contributed to the present peace and development enjoyed by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region as he joined the struggle for the liberation of the Bangsamoro since 1970s;

**WHEREAS,** the late Rostom Saaban passed away peacefully before Fajr of *Ramadhan* 14, 1445/March 24, 2024, Sunday;

**WHEREAS,** other than being a leader and a peacemaker, he was also a teacher of Qur’an in Camp Abubakar and lived a very low-profile life that made the people truly admire him;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament expresses its profound condolences on the passing of Rostom Saaban, the 3^rd Base Commander of the 106^th Base Command of the Southern Mindanao Front under the leadership of Abduladzis ‘*Kagui Jordan*’ M. Esmael Al-Haj;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament also expresses its sincerest gratitude for his philanthropies to the Region:

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To express deepest condolences and sympathies to the family of the late Rostom Saaban, the third Base Commander of 106^th Base Command of the Southern Mindanao Front.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** To present this foregoing Resolution to the bereaved family of Rostom Saaban for information.

**ADOPTED,** *Dhul Qa’dah* 14, 1445/May 22, 2024.
