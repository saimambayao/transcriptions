RESOLUTION NO. 354

RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST CONDOLENCES AND SYMPATHIES TO THE BEREAVED FAMILY OF HADJI ABDULLAH “BOBBY BULDON” PACASEM AND EXTENDING PROFOUND APPRECIATION AND GRATITUDE FOR HIS CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE BANGSAMORO STRUGGLE AND TO THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION

WHEREAS, Hadji Abdullah Pacasem, known as “Bobby Buldon”, is a distinguished of the Top three hundred (300) member of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) and the Moro National Liberation Front (MNLF);

WHEREAS, he fought together with the former Chairperson of the MILF Ustadz Hashim Salamat (Allah Yarhamuhu) for the cause of the Bangsamoro struggle in furtherance of a long-lasting peace in Mindanao;

WHEREAS, he was a member of the Central Committee of the MILF and served as the head of its Central Research Agency;

WHEREAS, on June 20, 2023, he peacefully passed away in his home in Parang, Maguindanao del Norte;

WHEREAS, he is survived by his loving wife Hadja Samira Casad Pacasem and children namely: Norodin C. Pacasem, Nur-aisha C. Pacasem, Judge Nurhani C. Pacasem-Nur, RSW, PSSg Mubarak C. Pacasem, Atty. Ubaida C. Pacasem, CPA, Farzaanah C. Pacasem, LPT, Atty. Amon Jadeedah C. Pacasem, CPA, Pat Abunawas C. Pacasem, Engr. Muammar C. Pacasem, Muhammad Abdu C. Pacasem, and Engr. Nur-Hashim C Pacasem;

WHEREAS, his dedication and sacrifices for the welfare of the Bangsamoro people will be remembered with great honor and pride and will never be forgotten in our history as a Bangsamoro people:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To express deepest condolences and sympathies to the bereaved family of Hadji Abdullah “Bobby Buldon” Pacasem and extends profound appreciation and gratitude for his contributions to the Bangsamoro struggle and to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That this Resolution be presented to the bereaved family of the late Hadji Abdullah “Bobby Buldon” Pacasem, for information.

ADOPTED, Dhul Hijjah 04, 1444/June 22, 2023.
