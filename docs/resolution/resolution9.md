**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 9**

**RESOLUTION UTILIZING THE UNEXPENDED 2019 LOCAL FUNDS ALLOCATED TO THE DEFUNCT REGIONAL LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY (RLA) FOR THE MONTH OF MARCH AND APRIL FOR THE INITIAL MAINTENANCE AND OTHER OPERATING EXPENSES (MOOE) OF THE OFFICES OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY, IN THE AMOUNT OF SIXTY THREE MILLION PESOS (P63,000,000.00).**

**WHEREAS**, during Session No. 3 of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) First Regular Session dated April 23, 2019 adopted and approved a motion creating an Ad Hoc Committee to study and plan for the utilization of the 2019 local funds allocated to the defunct Regional Legislative Assembly (RLA);

**WHEREAS**, the Ad Hoc Committee in its first meeting dated April 23, 2019, 6:45PM held at Bajao Hall, BARMM, Cotabato City has identified that cash in bank amounting to Sixty Three Million Pesos (P63M) local funds is available for utilization;

**WHEREAS**, members of the Ad Hoc Committee unanimously agreed that the sixty three million pesos (P63M) cash in bank of the 2019 defunct RLA local funds allocation shall be utilized for the initial Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) membersâ€™ maintenance and other operating expenses (MOOE) for the month of March and April 2019;

**WHEREAS**, the Ad Hoc Committee also agreed that the sixty three million pesos (P63M) cash in bank shall be allocated equally to ninety seven (97) members of the BTA in the amount of six hundred thousand pesos (P600,000.00) each for the month of March and April 2019.

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, to utilize the unexpended 2019 local funds allocated to the defunct Regional Legislative Assembly (RLA) for the month of March and April for the initial Maintenance and Other Operating Expenses (MOOE) of the offices of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, in the amount of Sixty Three Million Pesos (P63,000,000.00).

Adopted, April 24, 2019
