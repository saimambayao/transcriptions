**RESOLUTION NO. 428**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING WARM CONGRATULATIONS AND SALUTATIONS TO MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT, ATTORNEY SHA ELIJAH B. DUMAMA-ALBA**

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament extends its heartfelt congratulations to Member of the Parliament (MP) Atty. Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba for her historic appointment as the first female Minister of the Ministry of the Interior and Local Government (MILG);

**WHEREAS,** MP Atty. Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba, as the Bangsamoro Parliament Floor Leader, has demonstrated exceptional leadership, dedication, and competence, making her eminently qualified for the responsibilities entrusted to her in the crucial role of MILG Minister;

**WHEREAS,** MP Dumama-Alba graduated with a degree of Bachelor of Arts in Public Administration at the University of the Philippines-Diliman, Quezon City; and has acquired a Degree of Law at the San Beda College, Mendiola;

**WHEREAS,** she is a graduate of Master’s in Public Management-Major in Regional and Local Governance;

**WHEREAS,** aside from being a lawyer, MP Dumama-Alba has a Career Executive Service Eligibility;

**WHEREAS,** MP Dumama-Alba has played a crucial role in the peace process between the Philippine Government and the Moro Islamic Liberation Front as of this present time;

**WHEREAS,** she held other different key government positions; the latest as the Attorney-General of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM);

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Parliament expresses its utmost confidence in MP Atty. Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba’s ability to lead the MILG and contribute significantly to the advancement and prosperity of the BARMM:

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To express warm congratulations and salutations to Member of Parliament, Attorney Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** To congratulate and warmly welcome MP Dumama-Alba as the new Minister of the MILG.

**RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE,** To present a copy of this Resolution to the office of MP Dumama-Alba, for information.

**ADOPTED,** *Rajab 04, 1445/January 16, 2024.*
