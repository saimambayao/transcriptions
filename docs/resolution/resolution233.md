
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
**BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT**
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 233**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING SERIOUS CONCERN ON THE REPORTED CIVILIAN CASUALTIES, ESPECIALLY WOMEN AND CHILDREN, AND JOINING THE INTERNATIONAL CALL FOR THE IMMEDIATE END TO THE WAR IN UKRAINE**

**WHEREAS,** Section 2, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution states:

“The Philippines renounces war as an instrument of national policy, adopts the generally accepted principles of international law as part of the law of the land and adheres to the policy of peace, equality, justice, freedom, cooperation, and amity with all nations”;

**WHEREAS,** in support of maintaining and strengthening international peace and security, and in conformity with the principles of freedom, equality, justice, and respect for human rights, international disputes should be settled by peaceful means;

**WHEREAS,** it is reported that the humanitarian situation in Ukraine is deteriorating, the number of internally displaced persons and refugees needing humanitarian assistance keeps increasing, and its adverse impact on global food security is alarming;

**WHEREAS,** the life, liberty and property of civilians during war are internationally recognized and protected;

**WHEREAS,** the Preamble of the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL), in part, accepts the principles of human rights, liberty, justice, democracy, and the norms and standards of international laws;

**WHEREAS,** Section 8, Article IV of the BOL provides that the Bangsamoro Government respects and adheres to all international treaties and agreements binding upon the national government;

**WHEREAS,** the Holy Quran states “. . . because of that We declared upon the Children of Israel that whoever kills a soul for corruption (done) in the land – it is as if he had slain mankind entirely. And whoever saves one – it is as if he had saved mankind entirely”;

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to express serious concern on the reported civilian casualties, especially women and children, and joining the international call for the immediate end to the war in Ukraine.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** that the foregoing Resolution be forwarded to His Excellency President Rodrigo R. Duterte, copy-furnished the Office of the Secretary of Foreign Affairs of the Philippines, for information and appropriate consideration.

**ADOPTED,** Sha’baan 13, 1443/March 17, 2022.
