RESOLUTION NO. 332

RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST SYMPATHIES AND SINCEREST CONDOLENCES TO THE BEREAVED FAMILIES OF THE VICTIMS OF THE ILL-FATED MV LADY MARY JOY 3 PASSENGER SHIP, WHICH CAUGHT FIRE NEAR BALUK-BALUK ISLAND IN THE PROVINCE OF BASILAN

WHEREAS, MV Lady Mary Joy 3 is a 33-year-old passenger ship that was built in 1990 and is sailing under the flag of the Philippines;

WHEREAS, MV Lady Mary Joy 3, owned by Aleson Shipping Lines Inc., left Zamboanga Port at around 9 oâ€™clock in the evening of March 29, 2023, Wednesday, with two hundred five (205) passengers indicated in its manifest;

WHEREAS, while sailing to the province of Sulu, the vessel caught fire near the town of Hadji Muhtamad, Basilan and drifted to Baluk-Baluk Island where it ran aground;

WHEREAS, based on the passenger manifest, there were two hundred fifty-two (252) individuals onboard the vessel when it caught fire at 9.3 nautical miles off the littoral monitoring station (LTS) Pilas near Baluk-Baluk Island in Hadji Muhtamad, Basilan, around 10:40 pm of March 29, 2023;

WHEREAS, among the 252 individuals onboard were 205 passengers, thirty-five (35) crew, four (4) sea marshals from the PCG, and eight (8) security personnel from the Philippine Army (PA);

WHEREAS, according to the Philippine Coast Guard (PCG), the number of people who perished due to a fire in the passenger-cargo vessel MV Lady Mary Joy 3 reached thirty-three (33);

WHEREAS, search and rescue operations and coastal patrolling in the vicinity waters of Basilan continued for seven others who were still missing;

WHEREAS, of the thirty-three (33) fatalities, eleven (11) victims that drowned were already identified while the rest were burned to death; among those who died were six (6) children and minors;

WHEREAS, according to the PCG, there were two hundred sixteen (216) rescued individuals who were brought to Zamboanga City for profiling;

WHEREAS, according to the narrative of the survivors, the fire came from an air-conditioned room at the cabin which quickly spread to the adjacent kitchen room;

WHEREAS, many survivors jumped off the ship; some of them survived the freezing water, while others were drowned; several passengers could not immediately get out of the vessel because of the panic and the commotion. These passengers were then trapped, where their burnt bodies were found later;

WHEREAS, the sea tragedy had left a lot of families mourning for the loss of their loved ones;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) offers its profound sympathies and sincerest condolences to the bereaved families of the victims of the ill-fated MV Lady Mary Joy 3:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the BTA Parliament, To express profound sympathies and sincerest condolences to the bereaved families of the victims of the ill-fated MV Lady Mary Joy 3 passenger ship, which caught fire near Baluk-Baluk Island in the province of Basilan.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, To urge the Office of the Chief Minister to provide financial assistance to the victims of the said mishap or disaster.

RESOLVED, FINALLY, That a copy of this resolution be furnished to the bereaved families of the victims.

ADOPTED, Shawwal 27, 1444/May 18, 2023.
