**RESOLUTION NO. 373**

RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING CAROLYN O. ARGUILLAS OF MINDANEWS ON BEING A RECIPIENT OF THE 2023 LUXEMBOURG PEACE PRIZE (LPP) AWARD FOR OUTSTANDING PEACE JOURNALISM

WHEREAS, on June 13, 2023, Carolyn O. Arguillas of the MindaNews has been named the 2023 Luxembourg Peace Prize (LPP) awardee for Outstanding Peace Journalism;

WHEREAS, she is the first Filipino to be given this award in the eleven (11) years that the Schengen Peace Foundation (SPF) and the World Peace Forum (WPF) have been giving out such awards to honor outstanding individuals in the field of peace;

WHEREAS, the LPP for Outstanding Peace Journalism is a recognition given by the SPF and the WPF which seeks to recognize journalists or media publications who have used their crafts to contribute to peace;

WHEREAS, the LPP stated in its press release that over the past two decades, the MindaNews has earned recognition under the leadership of Ms. Arguillas for its constructive coverage of peace efforts fostering an environment of dialogue and understanding;

WHEREAS, Ms. Arguillas’ constructive coverage of peace efforts played a significant role in the reporting of the country’s peace process between the Philippine Government, the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF), and the Moro National Liberation Front (MNLF);

WHEREAS, her educational credentials include: Bachelor of Arts in Journalism at the University of the Philippines in Diliman, Master of Arts in Journalism at the Asian Center for Journalism of the Ateneo de Manila University, academic requirements for Master of Arts in Peace and Development at Notre Dame University in Cotabato City, and academic units in Master of Arts in Anthropology at the Ateneo de Davao University;

WHEREAS, she has won various national awards namely; the Catholic Mass Media Awards for Best News Reporting, the Jaime V. Ongpin Awards for Investigative Journalism, the Lifetime Achievement Awards from Titus Brandsma-Philippines, the Hildegarde Awards for Women of the St. Scholastica’s College, the Investigative Journalist of the Year award from the Rotary Club of Manila, the Marshall McLuhan Prize from the Canadian Embassy, the Gawad AKap Peace Awards, and the Glory Award for Journalism conferred by the UP College of Mass Communication Alumni Association;

WHEREAS, her contributions to peace journalism have influenced the media platforms on the road to better broadcasting and news writing in Mindanao as emphasized by her firm dedication to responsible reporting;

WHEREAS, in view of her steadfast commitment and outstanding achievements as a peace journalist, it is therefore befitting that this Honorable Parliament express and extend its warmest congratulations to Ms. Arguillas:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), To congratulate Carolyn O. Arguillas of MindaNews on being a recipient of the 2023 Luxembourg Peace Prize (LPP) award for outstanding peace journalism.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That this Resolution be presented to Ms. Carolyn O. Arguillas, for information.

ADOPTED, Muharram 02, 1445/July 20, 2023.
