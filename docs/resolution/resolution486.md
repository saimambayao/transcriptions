**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 486**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING CARLOS EDRIEL POQUIZ YULO FOR WINNING TWO GOLD MEDALS AND THE OTHER MEDALISTS IN THE 2024 PARIS OLYMPICS**

**WHEREAS**, Section 19 (1), Article XIV of the 1987 Constitution states that the State shall promote physical education and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and amateur sports, including training for international competitions, to foster self-discipline, teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;

**WHEREAS**, Section 13, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054, or the “*Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao*”, provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall recognize the vital role of the youth in nation-building, promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, and nationalism, encourage involvement in public and civic affairs, and promote mental and physical fitness through sports;

**WHEREAS**, Carlos Yulo, a 24-year-old gymnast from Malate, Manila, represented the Philippines on the Olympic Stage at the Bercy Arena in Paris, France for the 2024 Paris Summer Olympics;

**WHEREAS**, he concluded the 2024 Paris Olympics with one (1) gold medal for the men’s artistic gymnastic floor exercise and one (1) gold medal for men’s artistic gymnastics vault competition on August 3-4, 2024;

**WHEREAS**, on his first gold, he earned an impressive score of fifteen (15). He delivered a flawless routine that showcased his signature combination of high-flying tumbles and intricate spins. The routine’s difficulty level, combined with his exceptional performance, secured him the top spot on the podium;

**WHEREAS**, on his second gold, Yulo averaged fifteen and one hundred sixty-six thousandths (15.166) points as he collected fifteen and four hundred thirty-three thousandths (15.433) in his first vault and fourteen and eight hundred thousandths (14.800) in his second thereby claiming the top spot;

**WHEREAS**, Carlos Yulo made history as the Philippines’ first double Olympic gold medalist, doubling not only his historic feat but also the Filipinos’ pride and joy;

**WHEREAS**, aside from the gold medals in the vault and floor events at the 2024 Paris Olympics, some of his major achievements are the following:

a) 2019 World Championships (floor); 2021 World Championships (vault); 2022 Asian Championships (floor, vault, parallel bars); 2023 Asian Championships (floor, vault, parallel bars); 2024 Asian Championships (all-around, floor, vault, parallel bars); 2019 SEA Games (all-around, floor); 2021 SEA Games (floor, still rings, all-around, vault, horizontal bars); 2023 SEA Games (all-around, parallel bars); 2024 FIG World Cup - Doha leg (parallel bars); 2023 FIG World Cup - Baku leg (vault, parallel bars); 2023 FIG World Cup - Doha leg (floor);

b) Silver: 2021 World Championships (parallel bars); 2022 World Championships (vault); 2022 Asian Championships (all-around); 2023 Asian Championships (all-around); 2019 SEA Games (pommel horse, still rings, vault, parallel bars, horizontal bars); 2021 SEA Games (team, parallel bars); 2023 SEA Games (team, still rings); 2024 FIG World Cup - Doha leg (vault); 2023 FIG World Cup - Doha leg (parallel bars);

c) Bronze: 2018 World Championships (floor); 2022 World Championships (parallel bars); 2023 Asian Championships (horizontal bar); 2024 FIG World Cup – Doha leg (floor); 2023 FIG World Cup - Doha leg (vault); 2023 FIG World Cup - Cottbus (parallel bars);

**WHEREAS**, his outstanding achievements brought great pride to the whole nation, including the Bangsamoro region, as he showed great perseverance, dedication, and courage that is worthy of emulation by the entire Philippine populace;

**WHEREAS**, this hard-earned victory is a testament to the world-class talent of the Filipino people and serves as an inspiration to all Filipino athletes, especially the Bangsamoro youth to strive harder in order to excel in their chosen endeavors, and to aim for greater heights of excellence;

**WHEREAS**, in addition, his victory gives the youth the power to believe in themselves, recognize their capabilities, and reminds them that no dream is too big and no goal is impossible to achieve;

**WHEREAS**, other Filipino athletes who won Olympic medals are Nesthy Petecio and Aira Villegas;

**WHEREAS**, both Nesthy Petecio and Aira Villegas won bronze in the women’s boxing 57-kilogram (kg) and 50-kg categories, respectively;

**WHEREAS**, after finishing the 2024 Paris Olympics with four medals, the Philippines emerged as the best Southeast Asian team in the Olympics for the second time in a row;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), as the interim government of the Bangsamoro Region, has frequently recognized outstanding Filipino athletes for their astounding accomplishments in bringing immense pride and glory to the country through the victories in international sporting competitions;

**WHEREAS**, his remarkable performance and exceptional contribution, that brought pride, honor, and glory to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, deserve the highest commendation from the BTA:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To congratulate and commend Carlos Edriel Poquiz Yulo for winning two gold medals and the other medalists in the 2024 Paris Olympics.

**RESOLVED, FURHTER**, To present this Resolution to Carlos Edriel Poquiz Yulo and other medalists, for information.
