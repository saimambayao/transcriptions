SECOND REGULAR SESSION

RESOLUTION NO. 328

RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MS. FATIMA AYESHA JUWAK AMIRUL, A TAWI-TAWI NATIVE, FOR BAGGING GOLD MEDAL IN WOMEN’S SOFT TENNIS COMPETITION DURING THE RECENTLY CONCLUDED 32ND SOUTH EAST ASIAN GAMES HELD ON MAY 05 TO 17, 2023 IN PHNOM PENH, CAMBODIA

WHEREAS, the Southeast Asian Games, also known as the SEA Games, is a biennial multi-sport event involving participants from countries within Southeast Asia. The 32nd SEA Games was held from May 05 to 17, 2023 in Phnom Penh, Cambodia;

WHEREAS, Section 13, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 also known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall promote, among others, the mental and physical fitness of the youth through sports;

WHEREAS, pursuant to the above-mentioned mandate, the Bangsamoro Government is committed to promoting sports, by encouraging and supporting programs, leagues, competitions, indigenous games, martial arts and amateur sports;

WHEREAS, the Philippine delegation to the 2023 SEA Games is composed of eight hundred fourteen (814) athletes participating in all thirty-eight (38) sports;

WHEREAS, on May 09, 2023, the Philippines’ women’s team won a gold medal in soft tennis in the 32nd SEA Games in Cambodia. The women’s team is composed of Christy Sañosa, Fatima Ayesha Amirul, Noelle Conchita Mañalac, Noelle Nikki Zoleta, Princess Lorben Catindig and Virvienica Isearis Bejosano;

WHEREAS, one of the gold medalists is Ms. Fatima Ayesha Juwak Amirul, a Tawi-Tawi native, residing at Bongao Poblacion, Tawi-Tawi;

WHEREAS, her exemplary performance as a gold medalist serves as inspiration for Muslim Filipino children to excel in sports and other fields of endeavors and bring glory to Bangsamoro communities;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority is cognizant of Ms. Fatima Ayesha Juwak Amirul’s achievement of bringing pride and glory to the Bangsamoro communities:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate and commend Ms. Fatima Ayesha Juwak Amirul for bagging gold medal in women’s soft tennis competition during the recently concluded 32nd SEA Games held on May 05 to 17, 2023 in Phnom Penh, Cambodia thereby giving pride, glory and honor to the Bangsamoro communities.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That financial incentive in the amount of not less than Five Hundred Thousand Pesos (PHP 500,000.00) be given to Ms. Fatima Ayesha Juwak Amirul for winning the gold medal in the 32nd Sea Games and the amount be taken from the available fund of the Bangsamoro Government, subject to the determination of the Ministry of Finance, Budget and Management.

RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE, That the awardee, Ms. Fatima Ayesha Juwak Amirul, be invited by the Parliament to personally receive the commendation and monetary award.

RESOLVED, FINALLY, That a copy of this Resolution be presented to Ms. Fatima Ayesha Juwak Amirul, for information.

ADOPTED, Shawwal 25, 1444/May 16, 2023.
