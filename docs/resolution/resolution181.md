
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

THIRD REGULAR SESSION

RESOLUTION NO. 181

RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE HEARTFELT THANKS AND GRATITUDE OF THE PARLIAMENT TO PRESIDENT RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE FOR HIS APPROVAL OF REPUBLIC ACT 11054, OR THE BANGSAMORO ORGANIC LAW, AND SUBSEQUENTLY APPOINTING THE MEMBERS OF THE PARLIAMENT PARTICULARLY THE 39 FROM THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES (GRP) SIDE

WHEREAS, Republic Act 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law, was signed into law by President Rodrigo Roa Duterte on July 26, 2018 and became effective on August 10, 2018;

WHEREAS, the approval of the RA 11054, served as the manifestation of President Rodrigo Roa Duterte’s advocacy for lasting peace;

WHEREAS, by virtue of RA 11054, the interim government of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao was created;

WHEREAS, pursuant thereto, President Rodrigo Roa Duterte appointed the eighty (80) Members of the Parliament: forty-one (41) came from the Moro Islamic Liberation Front and thirty-nine (39) were from the government side:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to express the heartfelt thanks and gratitude of the Parliament to President Rodrigo Roa Duterte for his approval of Republic Act 11054, or the Bangsamoro Organic Law, and subsequently appointing the Members of the Parliament particularly the 39 from the Government of the Republic of the Philippines (GRP) side.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, to forward the foregoing Resolution to President Rodrigo Roa Duterte, Malacañan Palace, Manila.

ADOPTED, Rabi ul Awwal 14, 1443/October 21, 2021.
