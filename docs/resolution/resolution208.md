**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 208**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING THE NOTRE DAME HOSPITAL AND SIENA COLLEGE OF COTABATO, INC. (FORMERLY NDH SCHOOL OF MIDWIFERY) FOR PRODUCING FOUR (4) TOPNOTCHERS IN THE DECEMBER 2021 X-RAY TECHNOLOGIST LICENSURE EXAM AND RECOGNIZING THE INDIVIDUAL ACHIEVEMENTS OF THE 4 TOPNOTCHERS**

**WHEREAS**, Notre Dame Hospital and Siena College of Cotabato, Inc. (formerly NDH School of Midwifery), a private institute of higher learning in Cotabato City produces four (4) topnochers in the December 2021 X-Ray Technologist Licensure Examination administered by the Professional Regulation Commission, and held in various testing centers in the country;

**WHEREAS**, the names of the topnotchers coming from the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region who bested other hundred examinees are the following:

1. ISNAIRAH A. RANGAN - Top 2
2. FAIRODZ A. TAHIR - Top 3
3. JALILAH D. ALABAT - Top 5
4. ASMAIRA M. RANGAN - Top 10

**WHEREAS**, this marvelous feat is an inspiration both to the diverse communities in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, especially to the youth, and to our institutions whether public or private as this exemplifies excellence in all respects;

**WHEREAS**, it is no doubt that this achievement also brings honor and glory to our newly established Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. Hence, this resolution honors all those who are involved in this laudable achievement, because the triumph of the successful examinees is not only theirs, but of their parents, their teachers and the communities they belong to:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to congratulate and commend the Notre Dame Hospital and Siena College of Cotabato, Inc. (formerly NDH school of midwifery) for producing four (4) topnotchers in the December 2021 X-Ray Technologist Licensure Exam and recognizing the individual achievements of the 4 topnotchers and their parents, guardians, mentors and other individuals who are instrumental to this success.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to forward the foregoing Resolution to Isnaira A. Rangan, Fairodz A. Tahir, Jalilah D. Alabat, Asmaira M. Rangan and Notre Dame Hospital and Siena College of Cotabato, Inc., for information.

**ADOPTED**, Jumada Al-Akhirah 23, 1443/January 27, 2022.
