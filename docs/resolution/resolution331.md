SECOND REGULAR SESSION

RESOLUTION NO. 331

RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MR. AMINODDIN MASBUD DOMADO FOR RANKING TOP SIX (6) AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS IN THE RECENTLY CONCLUDED 2023 MEDICAL TECHNOLOGISTS LICENSURE EXAMINATION, FOR GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE

WHEREAS, on March 8 and 9, 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and the Board of Medical Technology conducted the regular Medical Technologists (MedTech) Licensure Examination in various testing centers nationwide;

WHEREAS, the PRC announced on March 14, 2023 that out of 6,165 examinees, only 4,714 or 76.46% successfully passed the Medical Technologists Licensure Examination;

WHEREAS, the PRC announced that Mr. Aminoddin M. Domado, who is a recipient of a Latin academic honor as Magna Cum Laude from Far Eastern University- Dr. Nicanor Reyes Medical Foundation, admirably ranked as top six (6) with an excellent score of 91.40%;

WHEREAS, Mr. Aminoddin M. Domado, 23 years of age, is a young Bangsamoro professional of Maranao descent and a proud son of the late Engr. Alimoden Domado and Bae Mokiin Masbud-Domado who are natives of the Municipalities of Poona Bayabao- Ramain and Poona Bayabao- Masiu, respectively;

WHEREAS, Mr. Domado has academic achievements in both tertiary and secondary education, including numerous awards in research, academic excellence, and poster and oral presentations;

WHEREAS, Mr. Domado is an active member of the Philippine Society of Microbiology (PSM), and his research experience spans various fields, including COVID-19, carbon credits, nutritional status, and the impact of computer gaming on adolescents;

WHEREAS, his exemplary performance as one of the topnotchers is a source of inspiration for the Bangsamoro youth and young professionals in the field of medical technologists and other allied health professions;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) is cognizant of the feat of Mr. Aminoddin M. Domado and those of all Bangsamoro passers of the 2023 Medical Technologists Licensure Examination for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people, being living proof that the youth are not only our future but also they are our present:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the BTA Parliament, To congratulate and commend Mr. Aminoddin Masbud Domado for ranking top six (6) and all Bangsamoro passers in the recently concluded 2023 Medical Technologists Licensure Examination, for giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, To present a copy of this Resolution to Mr. Aminoddin M. Domado, for information.

ADOPTED, Shawwal 27, 1444/May 18, 2023.
