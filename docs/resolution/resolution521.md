**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 521**

**RESOLUTION ENCOURAGING ALL CONSTITUENTS OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO TO OBSERVE AND COMMEMORATE THE YEARLY CELEBRATION OF THE BANGSAMORO FOUNDATION DAY**

**WHEREAS**, on July 27, 2018, President Rodrigo Roa Duterte signed into law the Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the *“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;”*

**WHEREAS**, as stated in the Bangsamoro Administrative Code, January 21 is proclaimed as the Bangsamoro Foundation Day and declared a non-working holiday in celebration of the establishment of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, which shall be celebrated in recognition of the dreams and aspirations of the people to chart their social, political, cultural and economic destiny in the exercise of their right to self-determination;

**WHEREAS**, Bangsamoro Foundation Day shall be embodied, adhered to, and inculcated in every heart and mind of men and women in the Bangsamoro Region to preserve the history of the struggles and sacrifices of our leaders and the *Mujahideen*;

**WHEREAS**, the founding anniversary of the Bangsamoro Government holds significant historical, political, and cultural importance, symbolizing the establishment of a new era of peace, unity, governance, and autonomy;

**WHEREAS**, it is essential to foster a sense of unity, pride, and collective memory among all people of the Bangsamoro Region and promote awareness and understanding of the Bangsamoro Government’s achievements and aspirations;

**WHEREAS**, all residents, whether public or private entities, play a crucial role in contributing to the commemoration of the founding anniversary and ensuring its meaningful observance;

**WHEREAS**, all residents, especially the private sector, within the jurisdiction of the Bangsamoro Government, are hereby encouraged to actively participate in commemorating and celebrating the yearly founding anniversary of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, private sectors and entities shall organize or support events, activities, and initiatives that promote the shared values, achievements, and aspirations of the Bangsamoro Government during the commemorative period and are encouraged to display commemorative symbols, messages, or decorations on their premises or public places showcasing their support and solidarity with the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, all ministries, agencies, and offices, including the local government units under the jurisdiction of the Bangsamoro Government as well as the private sectors, entities, and citizens, are encouraged to display the BARMM official flag and flaglets within their respective premises and in public areas as part of the commemoration of the Bangsamoro Foundation Day;

**WHEREAS**, all ministries, agencies, and offices, as well as all local government units under the jurisdiction of the Bangsamoro Government, shall conduct special activities and services with the participation of the private sector in supporting the celebration and commemoration of the Bangsamoro Foundation Day during its commemorative period:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To encourage all constituents of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to observe and commemorate the yearly celebration of the Bangsamoro Foundation Day.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To furnish this Resolution to all the ministries and offices, for information and appropriate consideration.

**ADOPTED**, *Rajab* 23, 1446/January 23, 2025.
