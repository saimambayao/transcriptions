
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

SECOND REGULAR SESSION

RESOLUTION NO. 356

RESOLUTION URGING THE CONCERNED BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM) MINISTRIES AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS (LGUs) TO PROVIDE HOUSING SETTLEMENT FOR THE VICTIMS OF TYPHOON PAENG IN BARMM AREAS WHO WERE CONSIDERED HOMELESS

WHEREAS, it is the policy of the State pursuant to Section 9, Article XIII of the 1987 Constitution to ensure that underprivileged and homeless citizens have access to an adequate, safe, secure, habitable, sustainable, resilient and affordable home;

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law mandates, thus:

ARTICLE IX

BASIC RIGHTS

Section 8. Right of Access to Basic Services. – The Bangsamoro Government shall provide, maintain, and ensure the delivery of basic and responsive health programs, quality education, appropriate services, livelihood opportunities, affordable and progressive housing projects, power and electricity, and water inhabitants of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. It shall maintain appropriate disaster-preparedness units for immediate and effective relief services to victims of natural and man-made calamities. It shall also ensure the rehabilitation of calamity-effected areas and victims of calamities.

Section 9. Housing and Human Settlements. – The Parliament shall pass a law creating a housing and human settlements agency to address the lack of shelters, settlements, and livelihood for the disadvantaged and homeless, especially those victims of conflicts and atrocities. The agency may directly solicit and receive assistance, donations, aids, and grants from donors for its housing, settlements, and livelihood programs and development.

The Bangsamoro Government, in cooperation with private sector shall develop its own housing and human settlement programs. (Emphasis supplied)

WHEREAS, the Ministry of Human Settlements and Development (MHSD) is the primary regional government entity responsible for the management of housing, human settlement and rural/urban development; the sole and main planning and policy-making, regulatory, program coordination, and performance monitoring entity for all housing, human settlement and rural/urban development concerns, primarily focusing on the access to and the affordability of basic human needs; and the ministry with the mandate to develop and adopt a regional strategy to immediately address the provision of adequate and affordable housing to all inhabitants in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

WHEREAS, various Bangsamoro government agencies and offices aside from the MHSD have their own settlement projects, some of which were already given their proper appropriation;

WHEREAS, Pambansang Pabahay Para sa Pilipino Program is a housing project of the administration of President Marcos, Jr. that targets to sustain shelter for over six million families in six years;

WHEREAS, the Office of the Chief Minister initiated Kapayapaan sa Pamayanan (KAPYANAN) a flagship program that aims to provide decent housing to poor communities in the autonomous region;

WHEREAS, on October 31, 2022, Severe Tropical Storm Paeng left a trail of destruction that displaced thousands of families and destroyed properties;

WHEREAS, the report shows that in BARMM alone, the total number of populations affected by the tropical storm reached 1,193,961 or over a million of the population in the region comprised of 244,050 families;

WHEREAS, this pressing need for housing project aims to provide assistance to the victims of Typhoon Paeng and to ease the great suffering of internally displaced persons and families who witnessed the death of loved ones, the destruction of lives and property;

WHEREAS, immediate action is earnestly sought for the awarding of housing settlement to homeless families primarily to barangays severely affected by the tropical storm. The provinces and the cities affected are as follows:

1. Province of Basilan with five (5) municipalities affected;
2. Lanao del Sur, with six (6) municipalities affected;
3. Province of Maguindanao, combined Maguindanao del Sur and del Norte with thirty-one (31) municipalities;
4. Province of Sulu with one (1) municipality;
5. Province of Tawi-Tawi in particular the capital town of Bongao;
6. City of Cotabato; and
7. Special Geographical Area consisting of sixty-three (63) barangays in North Cotabato.

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Government recognizes the need for housing settlement and desires and aspires to provide the people an opportunity to attain a decent and safe living environment;

WHEREAS, this Resolution encourages BARMM Government and LGUs for an immediate implementation of housing settlement for the victims of Typhoon Paeng:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To urge the concerned BARMM Ministries and LGUs to provide housing settlement for victims of typhoon Paeng in BARMM areas who were considered homeless.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That a copy of this Resolution be furnished to the concerned BARMM Ministries and LGUs, for information.

ADOPTED, Dhul Hijjah 04, 1444/June 22, 2023.
