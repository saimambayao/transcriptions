**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 78**

**RESOLUTION APPROVING THE 1ˢᵀ BANGSAMORO DEVELOPMENT PLAN (BDP) 2020-2022 OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

**WHEREAS**, Section 4, Article XIII of Republic Act (RA) No. 11054 provides that “the Parliament shall establish the Bangsamoro Economic and Development Council (BEDC) which shall serve as the planning, monitoring, and coordinating agency for all development plans, programs, and projects of the Bangsamoro Government. It shall evaluate and recommend short, medium, and long-term comprehensive development plans, programs, and projects for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region for Approval by the Parliament”;

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Article XIV of RA 11054 also provides that “the National Government shall provide a Special Development Fund (SDF) to the Bangsamoro Government for the rebuilding, rehabilitation, and development of its conflict-affected communities. The utilization of the funds shall be in accordance with the Bangsamoro Development Plan to be adopted by the Bangsamoro Government”;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament on December 27, 2019 enacted Bangsamoro Autonomy Act (BAA) No. 6 establishing the BEDC and created for the purpose the Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority (BPDA) as its technical secretariat;

**WHEREAS**, the BEDC on June 5, 2020 convened its first regular Council meeting and adopted the 1ˢᵀ BDP 2020-2022;

**WHEREAS**, the Honorable Chief Minister Ahod B. Ebrahim on June 18, 2020 endorsed to the BTA Parliament the copy of the 1ˢᵀ BDP 2020-2022 and its accompanying Results Matrices (RM) and Bangsamoro Development Investment Program (BDIP), along with the Resolution of the BEDC adopting the said Plan and endorsing the same to the Bangsamoro Parliament for approval;

**WHEREAS**, upon request by the Chief Minister, the BTA Parliament on July 2-3, 2020 held a Special session to deliberate on the BEDC endorsed 1ˢᵀ BDP 2020-2022;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, to approve the 1ˢᵀ BDP 2020-2022 of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM).

**Adopted, July 3, 2020.**
