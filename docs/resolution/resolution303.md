**RESOLUTION NO. 303**

**RESOLUTION APPROVING THE 2ND BANGSAMORO DEVELOPMENT PLAN (BDP) 2023-2028 OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

**WHEREAS**, Section 4, Article XIII of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Organic Law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, provides that the “Parliament shall establish the Bangsamoro Economic and Development Council which shall serve as the planning, monitoring, and coordinating agency for all developments plans, programs, and projects of the Bangsamoro Government;”

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament enacted on December 27, 2019 the Bangsamoro Autonomy Act (BAA) No. 6 establishing the Bangsamoro Economic and Development Council (BEDC) and creating the Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority (BPDA) as its technical secretariat;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Development Plan (BDP) is the medium-term comprehensive development plan of the Bangsamoro Government that lays down the plans, programs, and projects for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region including the manner of utilization of the Special Development Fund (SDF) provided by the National Government for the rebuilding, rehabilitation, and development of conflict-affected communities in the region;

**WHEREAS**, the 1st BDP 2020-2022 was adopted by the BEDC on June 05, 2020, and approved by the BTA Parliament on July 03, 2020;

**WHEREAS**, Chief Minister Ahod B. Ebrahim directed the formulation of the 2nd BDP 2023-2028 and its accompanying Result Matrices (RM) and the Bangsamoro Development Investment Program (BDIP) to serve as the basis for multi-year planning, programming, and budgeting processes in the region;

**WHEREAS**, during its 3rd regular meeting on December 23, 2022, the BEDC adopted the 2nd BDP 2023-2028 and endorse the same to the BTA Parliament for its approval;

**WHEREAS**, on January 11, 2023, the BPDA disseminated electronic and printed copies of the 2nd BDP 2023-2028 to the Members of the BTA Parliament, for advance reference and comments;

**WHEREAS**, the 2nd BDP 2023-2028 is the medium-term comprehensive development plan of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) that builds on the gains of the 1st BDP 2020-2022 and lays down the Bangsamoro Government’s 12-Point Priority Agenda. It is consistent with the 8-Point Socioeconomic Agenda of President Ferdinand “Bongbong” Marcos Jr. and the Philippine Development Plan 2023-2028;

**WHEREAS**, the attachments of the BDP which are the BDIP, Result Matrices, and Policy Research Agenda will be submitted to the Parliament for approval at the appropriate time:

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To approve the 2nd Bangsamoro Development Plan 2023-2028 of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM).

**RESOLVED, FINALLY,** That improvements in the Bangsamoro Development Plan can still be incorporated.

**ADOPTED,** *Jumada Al-Akhirah 25, 1444/January 18, 2023.*
