BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

SECOND REGULAR SESSION

RESOLUTION NO. 117

**RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE CHIEF MINISTER TO CONSIDER DESIGNATING A FOCAL PERSON OR COMMITTEE FOR EACH PROVINCE, INCLUDING THE 63 BARANGAYS, IN CREATING A DATABASE FOR BANGSAMORO INDIGENTS IN AID OF CONSOLIDATING A LIST OF IDENTIFIED AREAS WHERE PARLIAMENT MEMBERS CAN EXTEND SUPPORT AND TO AVOID DUPLICATION IN THE RELIEF ASSISTANCE TO ALL ELIGIBLE BENEFICIARIES IN THE BANGSAMORO REGION**

**WHEREAS**, on April 30, 2020, the Office of the Chief Minister through the Office of the Secretary General issued an advisory directing the provision of 16,000 sacks of rice to be equally distributed to all respective 80 Members of the Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, the Parliament shall create a database of Bangsamoro Indigents for members of the Parliament to have their assigned areas of responsibility, in such a way that their support and assistance shall be more systematic and have a wider reach even in the far-flung areas of the BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, this said strategic measure and system shall be automatically used whenever the need arises again for assistance and support to BARMM constituents;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to request the Chief Minister to consider designating a focal person or committee for each province including the 63 barangays, in creating a database for Bangsamoro indigents in aid of consolidating a list of identified areas where Parliament members can extend support and to avoid duplication in the relief assistance to all eligible beneficiaries in the Bangsamoro region.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to forward this Resolution to the office of the Chief Minister Hon. Ahod Balawag Ebrahim and the Ministry of Social Services and development, BARMM, for information and appropriate consideration.

**ADOPTED**, March 24, 2021.
