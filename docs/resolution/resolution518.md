**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 518**

**RESOLUTION REQUIRING ALL MINISTRIES AND OFFICES OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO THROUGH THE BANGSAMORO PLANNING AND DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY TO SUBMIT TO THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT AN ANNUAL ACCOMPLISHMENT REPORT OF 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, AND 2024**

**WHEREAS**, Section 3, Article V of Republic Act No. 11054, also known as *“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (OLBARMM)”* provides that *“the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the power expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective governance and those which are essential to the promotion of general welfare;”*

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Article VII of the OLBARMM states that *“the powers of government be vested in the Parliament which shall exercise those powers and functions expressly granted to it in this Organic Law, and those necessary for, or incidental to, the proper governance and development of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. It shall set policies, legislate on matters within its authority...;”*

**WHEREAS**, Section 38, Article VII of the same law and Section 11, Title IV of Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13, otherwise known as the *“Bangsamoro Administrative Code”* states that *“the Bangsamoro Government shall have the power to create offices and organize its own bureaucracy. It may establish other offices and institutions necessary for the exercise of its functions and delivery of social services in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;”*

**WHEREAS**, more than five (5) years have passed since the shift from the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM). Since then, several ministries and offices were created by the new autonomous government;

**WHEREAS**, several efforts, accomplishments, and progress have been achieved by such ministries and offices of the BARMM. However, it cannot be denied that there are also some gaps in the implementation of programs and projects of the ministries and offices. Thus, there must be thorough records of such efforts and accomplishments of such ministries and offices in order that their progress may be archived and reviewed. Such records will take the form of yearly accomplishment reports;

**WHEREAS**, these accomplishment reports will detail the accomplishments of each ministry and office for 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, and 2024. These reports must include comprehensive physical and financial accomplishments;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority, as the socio-economic planning and policy coordinating authority, shall devise a report format that contains the minimum physical and financial contents of the report;

**WHEREAS**, these accomplishment reports shall guide the current and future Bangsamoro Parliaments, in order that appropriate legislation may be crafted to fill in the gaps of such efforts or to further the ministries’ or offices’ progress and accomplishments:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To require all ministries and offices of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao through the Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority to submit to the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament an Annual Accomplishment Report of 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, and 2024.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To furnish this Resolution to all the ministries and offices, for consideration and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED**, *Rajab* 23, 1446/January 23, 2025.
