**FOURTH REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 255**

**RESOLUTION URGING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT TO COMMEND OUTGOING PRESIDENT RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE**

**WHEREAS**, Section 4, Article VII of the 1987 Philippine Constitution states that the term of the President shall start and end on the 30th day of June following the elections;

*“The President and the Vice-President shall be elected by direct vote of the people for a term of six years which shall begin at noon on the thirtieth day of June next following the day of the election and shall end at noon of the same date, six years thereafter.”*

**WHEREAS**, outgoing President Rodrigo Roa Duterte is the 16ᵗʰ President of the Republic of the Philippines, and is considered one of the greatest presidents who led the Republic;

**WHEREAS**, amongst his notable works and legacy include but not limited to the passage and ratification of the Bangsamoro Organic Law and the establishment of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) which spurred the growth in the Region;

**WHEREAS**, the COVID-19 response and programs of the outgoing President cannot be forgotten, the national vaccination, the establishment of Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) laboratories, and the implementation of the COVID-19 bio-surveillance program are his significant works;

**WHEREAS**, despite the pandemic, the Build Build Build program of the government continued which led to the establishment of numerous infrastructures on all sides of the country;

**WHEREAS**, outgoing President Rodrigo Roa Duterte is a great leader, a true nationalist with a strong political will and people will remember him for his achievements despite the hindrances he had faced;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Information Office may help to facilitate the use of social media in every Local Government Units in the Region to quickly and simultaneously show the appreciation gesture of the Bangsamoro government to outgoing President Rodrigo Roa Duterte on June 30, 2022;

**WHEREAS**, the legacy that has been made by President Rodrigo Roa Duterte will forever be engraved in the hearts of the Bangsamoro people and the Bangsamoro government will forever be grateful to have served his administration:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To urge the Bangsamoro government to commend outgoing President Rodrigo Roa Duterte.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To forward the foregoing Resolution to the Office of the Chief Minister of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

**ADOPTED**, Dhu Al-Qa’dah 24, 1443/June 23, 2022.
