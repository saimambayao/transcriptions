**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 529**

**RESOLUTION CALLING ON THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE AND OTHER RELEVANT LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES TO IMMEDIATELY CONDUCT A THOROUGH INVESTIGATION INTO THE SHOOTING OF ENGINEER DARLINE PACETE IN DATU ODIN SINSUAT, MAGUINDANAO DEL NORTE, AND TO ENSURE JUSTICE, ACCOUNTABILITY, AND ENHANCED SECURITY MEASURES IN THE AREA**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament reaffirms its commitment to promoting peace, justice, and security for all residents of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) in accordance with the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao and the Constitution of the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, the Parliament has learned with grave concern of the shooting of Engr. Darlene Pacete on November 14, 2024 in Datu Odin Sinsuat, Maguindanao del Norte, which is a grievous act of violence threatening the safety and security of individuals in the area;

**WHEREAS**, the shooting of Engr. Pacete, a respected professional and active contributor to the progress of the Bangsamoro community, is deserving of swift and decisive action to bring those responsible for this crime to justice;

**WHEREAS**, the BTA Parliament condemns in the strongest terms possible the attack on Engr. Pacete, deeming it a grave threat to peace and security and urging swift action to bring the perpetrators to justice;

**WHEREAS**, incidents of violence undermine public trust in governance and law enforcement, as well as the region’s commitment to lasting peace and development, necessitating comprehensive and proactive measures;

**WHEREAS**, the Police Regional Office - Bangsamoro Autonomous Region (PRO-BAR), as the primary law enforcement authority, is mandated to investigate crimes, ensure the safety of citizens, and uphold the rule of law, while other concerned agencies are likewise tasked with providing necessary support:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To call on the Philippine National Police and other relevant law enforcement agencies to immediately conduct a thorough investigation into the shooting of Engineer Darline Pacete in Datu Odin Sinsuat, Maguindanao del Norte, and to ensure justice, accountability, and enhanced security measures in the area.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To urge the PRO-BAR and other relevant law enforcement authorities to:

(1) Immediately initiate a comprehensive, impartial, and transparent investigation into the shooting of Engineer Darlene Pacete in Datu Odin Sinsuat, Maguindanao del Norte, to uncover the facts, establish accountability, and ensure that justice is served;

(2) Prioritize the apprehension and prosecution of those responsible for this heinous crime to deter similar incidents in the future and restore public confidence in the justice system;

(3) Strengthen law enforcement presence and operations in Datu Odin Sinsuat and neighboring municipalities by deploying additional security personnel and intensifying intelligence-gathering efforts;

(4) Coordinate with local government units (LGUs) and community stakeholders to enhance collaboration and establish mechanisms to prevent violence, promote safety, and uphold peace and order in the area;

(5) Provide appropriate assistance, protection, and psychological support to Engineer Pacete and her family to ensure their well-being and safety during and after the investigation;

(6) Conduct a public safety and risk assessment in the municipality to identify security gaps and implement targeted interventions aimed at crime prevention and community safety; and

(7) Submit a detailed progress report to the BTA Parliament on the actions taken and outcomes achieved in relation to this case, including recommendations for systemic improvements in law enforcement.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To furnish a copy of this resolution to the PRO-BAR, the Ministry of Public Order and Safety, the Bangsamoro Human Rights Commission, the Office of the Chief Minister, and other relevant agencies for their immediate and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED**, *Sha’ban* 12, 1446/February 11, 2025.
