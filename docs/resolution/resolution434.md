**RESOLUTION NO. 434**

**RESOLUTION CONDEMNING, IN THE STRONGEST POSSIBLE TERMS, THE AMBUSH OF DR. SHARMAINE CEBALLOS BARROQUILLO, CALLING FOR THE PURPOSE ON ALL CONCERNED AGENCIES OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT TO EXPEDITE THE CONDUCT OF THEIR INVESTIGATION**

**WHEREAS,** Section 5, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that, "*The maintenance of peace and order, the protection of life, liberty, and property, and the promotion of the general welfare are essential for the enjoyment by all the people of the blessings of democracy;*"

**WHEREAS,** Section 11, Article II of the same declares, "*The State values the dignity of every human person and guarantees full respect for human rights;*"

**WHEREAS,** Section 6, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 also known as the "*Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao*", states that "*The Bangsamoro Government shall fully respect human rights;*"

**WHEREAS,** on February 3, 2024, Dr. Sharmaine Ceballos Barroquillo, a 28-year-old physician, was ambushed while driving along a highway of Datu Paglas, Maguindanao del Sur, and thereafter collided with an incoming tricycle on her way to Buluan District Hospital;

**WHEREAS,** Dr. Barroquillo was on her way to work at Sultan Kudarat Provincial Hospital when two (2) men riding a motorcycle fired gunshots at her. Despite being injured, she managed to drive towards Buluan District Hospital. However, she lost control, rammed an incoming tricycle, and damaged a house;

**WHEREAS,** the incident left her with serious physical injuries, and injured the four (4) passengers who boarded the tricycle;

**WHEREAS,** Dr. Barroquillo had to undergo multiple surgeries, one of which was the injury near her spine that left her lower body unresponsive;

**WHEREAS,** medical professionals and health care workers play a vital role in providing essential healthcare services to the communities and saving lives. Their safety and security are crucial for the uninterrupted delivery of healthcare services;

**WHEREAS,** Dr. Barroquillo is the daughter of two (2) Overseas Filipino Workers, who worked tirelessly just to provide a bright future for their daughter. This incident is unbearable for every parent, ready to sacrifice everything in the hopes of a successful life for their children;

**WHEREAS,** the act done by the subject perpetrators should be condemned in the strongest possible terms, that any form of violence and harassment is a threat not only to the maintenance of peace and security in the region, but also to the safety and well-being of our health care professionals;

**WHEREAS,** the conduct of investigation should be expedited, in order to bring justice to the families of Dr. Barroquillo and the four (4) passengers, as well as to put an end to these ruthless activities:

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To condemn, in the strongest possible terms, the ambush of Dr. Sharmaine Ceballos Barroquillo, calling for the purpose on all concerned agencies of the Bangsamoro Government to expedite the conduct of their investigation.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** To furnish a copy of this Resolution to the Ministry of Interior and Local Government, Police Regional Office-Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, National Bureau of Investigation-Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, Ministry of Public Order and Safety, and the Bangsamoro Human Rights Commission, for their information and appropriate action.
