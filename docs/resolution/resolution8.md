**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 8**

**RESOLUTION ADOPTING A CALENDAR OF SESSION FOR THE FIRST REGULAR SESSION OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT.**

**WHEREAS**, Section 5, Rule VI of the Parliamentary Rules, Procedures and Practices of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao provides:

> “The BTA shall convene its regular session once every year on the fourth Friday of March and shall end not later than thirty (30) days before the opening of the next regular session;”

**WHEREAS**, adopting a Calendar of Session will guide the Members of the BTA Parliament in the performance of their legislative and constituency-servicing functions.

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, To adopt the following Calendar of Session for the First Regular Session of the BTA Parliament:
