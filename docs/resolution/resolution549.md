**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 549**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE FULL SUPPORT OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT FOR SECRETARY ANTONIO ERNESTO F. LAGDAMEO, JR., SPECIAL ASSISTANT TO THE PRESIDENT, AND RESPECTFULLY URGING HIS CONTINUED APPOINTMENT TO ENSURE SUSTAINED GOVERNMENT SUPPORT FOR PEACE AND DEVELOPMENT IN THE BANGSAMORO**

**WHEREAS**, President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr. has called for the courtesy resignation of Cabinet members to facilitate a review of government performance and align leadership with the evolving priorities of the administration;

**WHEREAS**, Secretary Antonio Ernesto F. Lagdameo, Jr., Special Assistant to the President, has long demonstrated his commitment to inclusive governance and the peace process in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), and has remained an influential bridge between the Office of the President and stakeholders in BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, Secretary Lagdameo has consistently advocated for the political and development agenda of the BARMM by enabling high-level engagements, aligning national priorities with regional aspirations, and supporting the efforts of both the Moro National Liberation Front (MNLF) and the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF);

**WHEREAS**, his advisory role has strengthened institutional cooperation between the national and regional governments, contributing to policy coherence and reinforcing the trust necessary for the successful implementation of peace agreements;

**WHEREAS**, Secretary Lagdameoâ€™s retention will ensure continuity in national government support for peace, autonomy, and sustainable development in BARMM:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To express its full support and appreciation for Secretary Antonio Ernesto F. Lagdameo, Jr., and to recognize his key role in supporting the peace and governance framework of Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That this Parliament respectfully urges President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr. to retain Secretary Lagdameo as Special Assistant to the President, in order to maintain institutional consistency and further advance the goals of unity and development in the Bangsamoro.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, That copies of this Resolution be transmitted to the Office of the President, the Office of the Special Assistant to the President, and other concerned agencies for their information and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED**, Dhul Hijjah 02, 1446/May 29, 2025.
