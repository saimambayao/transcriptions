**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 493**

**RESOLUTION ADHERING TO EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 64 SERIES OF 2024, UPDATING THE SALARY SCHEDULE FOR CIVILIAN GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL AND AUTHORIZING THE GRANT OF AN ADDITIONAL ALLOWANCE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

**WHEREAS**, one of the priorities and functions of the Bangsamoro Parliament is to act on other matters that may be necessary for the protection and promotion of the general welfare of the constituents of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. The General Welfare clause under the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (OLBARMM) provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the powers expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective governance and those which are essential to the promotion of general welfare. Within its territorial jurisdiction, the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure, support, enhance economic prosperity and social justice, and preserve the comfort and convenience of its inhabitants;

**WHEREAS**, in the allocation of the block grant, the national laws and the budgeting rules and regulations implemented by the Department of Budget and Management (DBM) and Department of the Interior and Local Government applicable to local government units shall apply;

**WHEREAS**, the Parliament shall, likewise, adopt the Revised Compensation and Position Classification System of the National Government as provided for in Republic Act No. 6758, as amended, and relevant executive issuances;

**WHEREAS**, President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr. issued on August 2, 2024, Executive Order No. 64, increasing the salaries of government workers and authorizing the grant of an additional medical allowance to state employees;

**WHEREAS**, Executive Order No. 64, s. 2024 applies to all civilian government personnel, now existing or hereafter created in the Executive, Legislative, and Judicial Branches, the Constitutional Commissions and other Constitutional Offices, SUCs, and Government-Owned and Controlled Corporations (GOCCs) not covered by Republic Act No. 10149 and Executive Order No. 150, s. 2021, regardless of appointment status, whether regular, casual, or contractual; appointive or elective; and on a full-time or part-time basis; and Local Government Units;

**WHEREAS**, Executive Order No. 64, s. 2024 shall not apply to Military and Uniformed Personnel; Government agencies that are exempt from Republic Act No. 6758, as amended; GOCCs under Republic Act No. 10149 and Executive Order No. 150; individuals engaged without employer-employee relationship and funded from non-Personnel Services appropriations/budget such as contract of service/job order workers and consultants;

**WHEREAS**, the DBM consequently issued National Budget Circular No. 594 which provides for the guidelines, rules, and regulations for the implementation of the first tranche of the updated Salary Schedule for Civilian Government Personnel under Executive Order No. 64, s. 2024;

**WHEREAS**, one of the general limitations on the use of funds requires that the total appropriations, whether annual or supplemental, for Personal Services of the Bangsamoro Government for one (1) fiscal year shall not exceed forty-five percent (45%) of the total revenue sources of the Bangsamoro Government as provided under Section 6, Article XII of the OLBARMM;

**WHEREAS**, Special Provisions under the Miscellaneous Personnel Benefits Fund of the 2024 General Appropriations Act of the Bangsamoro provides that the amounts therein appropriated shall be used for deficiencies in authorized salaries, allowances, associated premiums, and other similar personnel benefits of the Bangsamoro Government personnel. It shall cover for, among others, the payment of other Personnel Services as may be mandated by law or as provided in such appropriations act, subject to usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations;

**WHEREAS**, the Ministry of Finance, and Budget and Management shall promulgate guidelines in the implementation of the first tranche of the updated Salary Schedule for Civilian Government Personnel under Executive Order No. 64, s. 2024:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To adhere to Executive Order No. 64 Series of 2024, updating the Salary Schedule for Civilian Government Personnel and authorizing the grant of an additional allowance, and for other purposes.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To furnish this Resolution to all persons and entities concerned, for information.

**ADOPTED**, *Safar* 18, 1446/August 22, 2024.
