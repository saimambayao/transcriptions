**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 495**

**RESOLUTION URGING THE OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY-GENERAL TO ESTABLISH A PICTURE GALLERY OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT MEMBERS**

**WHEREAS**, it is enshrined in Section II, Article VII of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the “*Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao*” that “*the powers of government be vested in the Parliament which shall exercise those powers and functions expressly granted to it in this Organic Law, and those necessary for, or incidental to, the proper governance and development of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. It shall set policies, legislate on matters within its authority, and elect a Chief Minister who shall exercise executive authority on its behalf;*”

**WHEREAS**, Section 11, Article VII of the same law provides that the term of office of Members of the Parliament shall be three (3) years;

**WHEREAS**, through professionalism, efficiency, and immense hard work, the Parliament functioned to fully realize the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region’s change and development agenda that will make a positive impact on the lives of the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament worked faithfully, and thus, passed priority socio-economic measures enumerated under Republic Act No. 11054, or the Bangsamoro Organic Law;

**WHEREAS**, the BTA Parliament enacted five (5) priority codes, which are now landmark laws, such as:

(a) Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13, otherwise known as the “*Bangsamoro Administrative Code;*”
(b) Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 17, otherwise known as the “*Bangsamoro Civil Service Code;*”
(c) Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 18, otherwise known as the “*Bangsamoro Education Code;*”
(d) Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 35, otherwise known as the “*Bangsamoro Electoral Code*”; and
(e) Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 49, otherwise known as the “*Bangsamoro Local Governance Code;*”

**WHEREAS**, the Members of the Parliament, regardless of political affiliations, beliefs, and direction, have successfully forged a harmonious working relationship with each other, with the executive department, and other offices;

**WHEREAS**, the Members of the Parliament express their unwavering support and appreciation for the commitment of the Bangsamoro Government to serve its constituents;

**WHEREAS**, to implement a picture gallery in the chamber of the parliament is to mark an important history of the Bangsamoro Government and to honor the Members of the Parliament for their invaluable contributions during the transition period:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To urge the Office of the Secretary-General to establish a picture gallery of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament Members.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That the official picture gallery be displayed in a conspicuous place in the Parliamentary Building.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, To furnish a copy of this Resolution to all Members of the BTA Parliament, ministers, deputy ministers, and Office of the Chief Minister, for information.

**ADOPTED**, *Safar* 24, 1446/August 28, 2024.
