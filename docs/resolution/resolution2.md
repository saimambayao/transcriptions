**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 2**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE DEEPEST CONDOLENCES OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) ON THE DEMISE OF THE HONORABLE GHAZALI B. JAAFAR, MEMBER OF THE BTA PARLIAMENT.**

**WHEREAS**, the Honorable Ghazali B. Jaafar, First Vice-Chairman of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF), was appointed by President Rodrigo Roa Duterte and took his oath as Member of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority on February 22, 2019;

**WHEREAS**, the late Honorable Jaafar was the choice of the overwhelming majority of this august Body for the Speaker of the Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, the late Jaafar also served as Chairman of the 2ⁿᵈ Bangsamoro Transition Authority which crafted what is now Republic Act 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law;

**WHEREAS**, the late Jaafar was known for his integrity and dedication to the cause of the Bangsamoro struggle and the cause of the people;

**WHEREAS**, the late Jaafar will be missed by his colleagues and the young generation who looked up to him for inspiration in moral leadership and governance.

**NOW THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, To express the deepest condolences of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) on the demise of the Honorable Ghazali B. Jaafar, Member of the BTA Parliament.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to present the foregoing Resolution to the family of the late Jaafar, for information.

**Adopted**, March 29, 2019.
