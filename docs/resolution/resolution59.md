**RESOLUTION COMMENDING MP ZIAUR-RAHMAN ALONTO ADIONG FOR BEING AWARDED AS ONE OF THE TEN OUTSTANDING YOUNG MEN (TOYM) OF THE PHILIPPINES FOR 2019**

**WHEREAS**, MP Ziaur-Rahman Alonto Adiong is the then Marawi crisis spokesman, a former Majority Floor Leader and Assemblyman of the defunct Regional Legislative Assembly-Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM), a current Member of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, and now named as one of the Ten (10) Outstanding Young Men of the Philippines for year 2019;

**WHEREAS**, MP Adiong has been judged according to his outstanding leadership, excellence in his contribution and dedication in service to the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, his TOYM award was given last December 13, 2019 in Manila, Philippines and was initiated by the Junior Chamber International (JCI) Philippines;
