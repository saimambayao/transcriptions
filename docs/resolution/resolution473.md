**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 473**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING PHILIPPINE DRUG ENFORCEMENT AGENCY-BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO, UNDER THE LEADERSHIP OF REGIONAL DIRECTOR GIL CESARIO P. CASTRO, FOR THE SERIES OF SUCCESSFUL ANTI-ILLEGAL DRUG OPERATIONS IN DIFFERENT AREAS OF BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

**WHEREAS**, Section 82, Article IX of Republic Act No. 9165, otherwise known as “*Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act of 2002*”, by its virtue, the Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency (PDEA) was created for the efficient and effective law enforcement of all the provisions on dangerous drugs and/or precursors and essential chemicals as provided under said law;

**WHEREAS**, PDEA-BARMM, under the leadership of Regional Director Gil Cesario P. Castro, has conducted more than twenty (20) anti-illegal drug operations from January to March 2024;

**WHEREAS**, with the overall volume of anti-illegal drug operations, the PDEA-BARMM was able to confiscate huge quantities of suspected illegal drugs, valued at the millions, with the apprehension of suspects, and the dismantling of several drug dens;

**WHEREAS**, the PDEA-BARMM’s record shows that the operations resulted in the confiscation of a total of seven thousand eight hundred forty-one point four hundred seventy-six (7,841.476) grams of Methamphetamine Hydrochloride (Shabu) with an estimated value of Sixty-Two Million Seven Hundred Thirty-One Thousand Eight Hundred Eight Pesos (₱62, 731,808.00) more or less fifteen thousand (15,000) pieces of fully-grown marijuana; and more or less fifty (50) grams of dried marijuana with an estimated value of Twenty-Five Thousand Pesos (₱25,000.00);

**WHEREAS**, the same operations further resulted in the apprehension of thirty-two (32) suspected drug personalities and the dismantling of four (4) drug dens;

**WHEREAS**, the continuous efforts of PDEA-BARMM have led to successful anti-illegal drug operations wherein neither severe injuries nor death on either side of the government operating team and the group of persons subject to the operations. The confiscation of the illegal substances, as well as the arrest of the perpetrators, ultimately benefits the constituents of the Bangsamoro Region and saves Bangsamoro youths from the various negative effects of the same;

**WHEREAS**, the agency’s exceptional performance has advanced the government’s campaign against illegal drugs:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To commend Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency-Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, under the leadership of Regional Director Gil Cesario P. Castro, for the series of successful anti-illegal drug operations in different areas of Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present this Resolution to the Office of Regional Director Gil Cesario P. Castro, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Muharram 10, 1446/July 16, 2024.
