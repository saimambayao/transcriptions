**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 503**

**RESOLUTION URGING THE MINISTRY OF BASIC, HIGHER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION–MADARIS EDUCATION TO ESTABLISH A MADRASAH AND MARKADZ DATABASE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM**

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article XIV of the 1987 Constitution provides that the State shall protect and promote the right of all citizens to quality education at all levels, and shall take appropriate steps to make such education accessible to all;

**WHEREAS**, Paragraph 3, Section 18, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the *“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao”*, states that the Parliament shall enact legislation to develop and strengthen the Madaris educational system in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS**, the Madrasah and *Markadz* Database Management System (MMDBS) is designed to systematically monitor and assess the developmental progress and current status of all Madrasah within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. The MMDBS will provide comprehensive data on the enhancement of Madrasah educational quality;

**WHEREAS**, the MMDBS includes basic information including the list of Madrasah, their students, and the *asatidz*;

**WHEREAS**, the MMDBS encourages the non-registered Madrasah to officially register with the Ministry of Basic, Higher and Technical Education (MBHTE) –Madaris; Registration ensures their inclusion as recipients of priority government assistance, educational programs, and quality assurance initiatives under the MBHTE-Madaris Education system, promoting equal opportunities for all Madrasah;

**WHEREAS**, the MMDBS shall serve as a foundational tool for conducting research on the status of Madrasah, ensuring adherence to educational standards. This comprehensive monitoring system will help prevent the potential misuse of Madrasah as platforms for fostering violent extremism, promoting a secure and inclusive educational environment;

**WHEREAS**, MBHTE–Madaris, in partnership with the local government units and private organizations, may easily identify and create a program for the improvement and the quality education of the Madrasah:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To urge the Ministry of Basic, Higher and Technical Education–Madaris Education to establish a Madrasah and *Markadz* Database Management System.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To furnish the foregoing Resolution to the Ministry of Basic, Higher and Technical Education–Director General for Madaris Education, for consideration and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED**, *Rabi Al-Akhir* 12, 1446/October 15, 2024.
