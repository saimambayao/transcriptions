**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 389**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING MOHAMMAD NUR GUMA-OS CASIB, A NATIVE OF LANAO DEL SUR FOR WINNING THE BRONZE MEDAL IN THE INTERNATIONAL CHEMISTRY OLYMPIAD HELD IN ZURICH, SWITZERLAND, THEREBY GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, the International Chemistry Olympiad (IChO) is an annual competition for the world’s most talented chemistry students at the secondary school level;

**WHEREAS**, nations around the world send a team of four students who are tested on their chemistry knowledge and skills in a five-hour laboratory practical exam and a five-hour written theoretical examination that are held on separate days with the practical examination usually being before the theoretical examination;

**WHEREAS**, the 55$^{\text{th}}$ IChO held last July 16 to 25, 2023 in Zurich, Switzerland with one-hundred seven (107) participants from eighty-eight (88) countries, one of which is the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, IChO aims to promote international contacts in chemistry, friendships between young scientific workers of different nationalities, cooperation among pupils, and exchange of pedagogical and scientific experience in chemistry;

**WHEREAS**, Mohammad Nur Guma-os Casib, a Bangsamoro student, was one of the representatives who participated in the 55th IChO after winning the 2023 Philippine Chemistry Olympiad National Competition;

**WHEREAS**, Mohammad Nur G. Casib is a son of Dr. Norodin Casib and Dr. Sittienor Guma-os - Casib, both are natives of Marawi City and were also victims of the Siege;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Casib is a student of Philippine Science High School, Central Mindanao Campus, in Baloi, Lanao del Norte;

**WHEREAS**, being a Bangsamoro, Mr. Casib has been bringing honor and pride to the entire Bangsamoro Region by winning in competitions both international and national such as but not limited to International Mathematics and Science Olympiad for Primary School Students in Zhejiang, China and Metrobank-DepEd Math Challenge in the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, the exemplary achievement of Mr. Casib is a source of inspiration for the young professionals and aspirants in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and the entire nation which merits this Parliament’s recognition and commendation;

**WHEREAS**, it is without doubt that Mr. Casib is an honor and pride to this Region. His perseverance, dedication and courage were evident in attaining his achievements. It will forever serve as an inspiration not only to the people of Lanao del Sur but to the millions of Bangsamoro people across different countries of the world:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate Mohammad Nur Guma-os Casib, a native of Lanao del Sur for winning the bronze medal in the International Chemistry Olympiad held in Zurich, Switzerland, thereby giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present this Resolution to Mr. Mohammad Nur G. Casib, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Muharram 28, 1445/August 15, 2023.
