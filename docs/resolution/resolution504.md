**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 504**

**RESOLUTION DIRECTING THE MINISTRY OF PUBLIC WORKS, THE BANGSAMORO PLANNING AND DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY, AND ALL OTHER CONCERNED MINISTRIES AND AGENCIES IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO TO FACILITATE THE PREPARATION AND SUBMISSION OF THE PROVINCIAL AND MUNICIPAL PRIORITY DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS, FOR NATIONAL GOVERNMENT FUNDING**

**WHEREAS**, Section 8, Article XVI of Republic Act No. 11054, hereinafter referred to as *“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao”*, creates fifteen (15) primary ministries, including the Ministry of Public Works (MPW), which serves as the institutional mechanisms for implementing good governance policies in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and takes the lead in planning and implementing infrastructure plans and programs;

**WHEREAS**, Section 6, Article VI of Republic Act No. 11054 created an Intergovernmental Infrastructure Development Board, which shall be responsible for coordinating and synchronizing national and Bangsamoro infrastructure development plans, with members consisting of representatives from both the Bangsamoro Government and the National Government, respectively;

**WHEREAS**, Section 37, Article XIII of the aforementioned law provides that the National Government shall fund and implement the construction and maintenance of national roads, bridges, water supply system, flood control, and irrigation systems, as well as the maintenance of existing airports, seaports and wharves in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS**, Section 23, Article XII of the same law provides that immediately after its ratification, and for five (5) years thereafter, the National Government shall allocate for additional funds to subsidize development projects and infrastructure in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, including provincial and municipal roads in accordance with a development plan formulated by the Bangsamoro Government. However, despite the said period nearing its conclusion, there have been no notable or significant programs and projects implemented;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority (BPDA), created under Section 14 of Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 6, also known as *“Bangsamoro Socio-Economic Development Planning Act of 2019”*, is the socio-economic development planning and policy coordinating authority of the Bangsamoro Government responsible for development planning, investment programming, monitoring and evaluation and coordination of all development initiatives in the region;

**WHEREAS**, the MPW and the BPDA shall facilitate the preparation and submission of the proposed provincial and municipal development projects and infrastructure of the local government units (LGUs), particularly provincial and municipal roads, for funding by the national government agencies, as required by R.A. No. 11054, with the National Government and the Bangsamoro Government, through the Intergovernmental Relations Body, and shall agree on the amount and manner of release of said amount to the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, it is important to underscore that the funding assistance being extended by the National Government to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region for nationally funded infrastructure projects, including provincial and municipal road projects of the LGUs, is separate from the block grant provided by the National Government to the Bangsamoro Government:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To direct the Ministry of Public Works, the Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority, and all other concerned ministries and agencies in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to facilitate the preparation and submission of the provincial and municipal priority development projects, for national government funding.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To forward the foregoing Resolution to MPW, BPDA, other concerned ministries and agencies in BARMM for their information and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED**, *Rabi Al-Akhir* 14, 1446/October 17, 2024.
