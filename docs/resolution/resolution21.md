**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 21**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE DEEPEST SYMPATHY AND CONDOLENCES OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) PARLIAMENT ON THE DEMISE OF ITS MEMBER, THE HONORABLE ATTY. ABDUL M. DATAYA, SR, WITH RECOGNITION OF HIS ACHIEVEMENTS AND CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE CAUSE OF THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE.**

**WHEREAS**, Atty. Abdul M. Dataya, Sr, a member of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, peacefully died on July 9, 2019 due to heart ailment at Davao Doctor's Hospital in Davao City;

**WHEREAS**, the late Dataya was also chair of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front-Ad Hoc Joint Action Group (MILF-AHJAG) and the Provincial Executive Officer of the United Bangsamoro Justice Party (UBJP) in Maguindanao;

**WHEREAS**, he held various positions in the government which included, among others, as Regional Director of the Regional Anti-Smuggling Action Center (RASAC) Region XI and as Regional Director of the Economic Intelligence and Investigation Bureau (EIIB), Region XII;

**WHEREAS**, after his retirement from government service, he served the Office of the Late MILF Chairman Ustadz Hashim Salamat (Allah Yarhamu) in various capacities;

**WHEREAS**, the late MP Dataya actively and religiously lived the virtues of a true Bangsamoro in asserting the justness and legitimacy of the cause of the Bangsamoro People and bravely fought for meaningful self-governance and right to self-determination.

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Parliament hereby acknowledges the efforts, labors and hardwork of the late MP Dataya in the pursuit of a noble purpose which is the promotion of the general welfare and development of the entire Bangsamoro, which is worthy of emulation.

**NOW THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To express the deepest sympathy and condolences of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament on the demise of its member, the Honorable Atty. Abdul M. Dataya, Sr, with recognition of his achievements and contributions to the cause of the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to present the foregoing Resolution to the family of the late Atty. Dataya, Sr, for information.

**ADOPTED**, July 29, 2019.
