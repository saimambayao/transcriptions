RESOLUTION NO. 339

RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MS. NISHRIEN AMATONDING BARUA, A BANGSAMORO GRADUATE OF MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY – MARAWI CITY, FOR RANKING FIFTH (5TH) WITH AN IMPRESSIVE RATING OF 91.40% IN THE MARCH 2023 LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR PROFESSIONAL TEACHERS - ELEMENTARY LEVEL, FOR GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE

WHEREAS, Section 16 of Article IX or Republic Act No. 11054 or otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law states that education shall be a top priority of the Bangsamoro Government to establish, maintain, and support a complete and integrated system of quality education, which shall be a subsystem of the national education system;

WHEREAS, Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and the Professional Regulatory Board for Professional Teachers (BPT) administered the regular Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers (LET) on March 19, 2023 in various testing centers nationwide;

WHEREAS, the result of 2023 Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers – Elementary Level was released on May 19, 2023 with a 40.76% passing rate or 24,819 passers out of 60,896 takers;

WHEREAS, among the 24,819 elementary teacher passers, Ms. Nishrien Amatonding Barua, a Bangsamoro of Maranao descent, admirably ranked fifth (5th) with an average score of 91.40%;

WHEREAS, Ms. Barua, who hails from Malabang, Lanao del Sur, graduated Magna Cum Laude from Mindanao State University – Marawi City last 2022 and Class 2012 Valedictorian of Datu Timbul Ali Elementary School – Baloi, Lanao del Norte.

WHEREAS, her exemplary performance as one of the topnotchers in the professional licensure examinations have demonstrated the competitiveness and excellence of the Bangsamoro people at a national level, giving us greater hope and a source of inspiration and pride for the Bangsamoro children, youth, and professionals not only in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region but also in the entire country, and have elevated the Bangsamoro’s educational profile among our younger generations inspiring them as the model of scholastic achievement;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) is cognizant of Ms. Nishrien Amatonding Barua, together with other LET Bangsamoro passers, for bringing honor and pride to the Bangsamoro people. She is living proof that the youth are not only our future in as much as they are also our present:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, by the BTA Parliament, as it is hereby resolved, To congratulate and commend Ms. Nishrien Amatonding Barua, a Bangsamoro graduate of Mindanao State University, for ranking fifth (5th) with an impressive rating of 91.40% in the March 2023 Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers - Elementary Level, for giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That a copy of this resolution be presented to Ms. Nishrien Amatonding Barua, for information.

ADOPTED, Dhul Qa’dah 04, 1444/May 25, 2023.
