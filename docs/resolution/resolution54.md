**RESOLUTION NO. 54**

**RESOLUTION EXTENDING THE PHASE-OUT PLAN FOR ACCOUNTABLE ARMM OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES**

**WHEREAS**, Section 10, Article XVI of RA 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that the Bangsamoro Transition Authority shall schedule the gradual phasing out of offices of the Autonomous Regional Government in Muslim Mindanao, which are deemed abolished upon the ratification of the Bangsamoro Organic Law;

**WHEREAS**, Section 10, Article XVI of the Bangsamoro Organic Law also provides that in consideration of public interest and the delivery of services, officials holding appointive positions shall continue to perform their functions in accordance with the phase-out schedule;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority had already approved a transition plan and it was provided therein that the phasing out of employees shall end on December 31, 2019;

**WHEREAS**, the Ministries and Offices are mandated to settle all accounts, and submit annual financial accountability reports and physical accomplishment reports to the Parliament and other offices such as the Department of Budget and Management and the Commission on Audit;

**WHEREAS**, there is a need to extend the phase out period for accountable officials who are responsible for the preparation of the Annual Financial Reports, periodic physical inventory of property and equipment, and those officials who are in charge on the settlement of accounts and clearances of the various offices;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority as it is hereby resolved to extend the phase-out plan for accountable officials of ARMM employees, specifically the accountable officers including finance officers and select support personnel until the end of the first quarter of 2020 or until March 31, 2020, or upon settlement of all accounts, and submission of annual financial accountability reports and physical accomplishment whichever comes first.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that the Ministries and Offices are to submit the recommended list of officers and personnel for extension to the Office of the Chief Minister, and the Parliament to the Office of the Speaker on or before December 31, 2019.

**RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE** that the list of officers and personnel to be extended shall be subject to the review and approval of the Chief Minister for executive offices, and the Speaker for the legislative offices.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, that the payment for the salaries and wages of the extended personnel shall be charged to Miscellaneous Personnel Benefits Fund as provided in the FY 2020 Bangsamoro Appropriation Act.

**Adopted**, December 27, 2019.
