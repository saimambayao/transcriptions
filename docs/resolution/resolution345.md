SECOND REGULAR SESSION

RESOLUTION NO. 345

RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE PASSERS OF THE NOVEMBER 2022 PHILIPPINE BAR EXAMINATIONS CONDUCTED BY THE SUPREME COURT OF THE PHILIPPINES, MOST ESPECIALLY ALL OF THE NEW BANGSAMORO LAWYERS, UNDERSCORING THE EXAMINATIONS’ 14TH PLACER, FELLOW BANGSAMORO, AR-RASHID JAMMIH TARADJI

WHEREAS, on April 14, 2023, after almost five months from the last day of the November 2022 Bar Examination, the Supreme Court of the Philippines released the results of the said examinations;

WHEREAS, a total of 3,992 out of 9,183 or 43.47% of examinees passed the 2022 Bar Examinations;

WHEREAS, along with the release of the Bar Examinations result is the announcement of the Top 30 examinees to which a fellow Bangsamoro, Ar-Rashid Jammih Taradji from Ateneo de Zamboanga University, managed to secure the 14th spot;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Bar Examination is the professional licensure examination for lawyers in the country, that is not supervised by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and arguably the most difficult licensure examination in the Philippines exclusively administered by the Supreme Court of the Philippines;

WHEREAS, these Bar passers successfully scaled what time and again has proven to be a Herculean battery of examinations where families and supportive communities of these passers also deserve compliments for through their love and cheer, victory is achieved;

WHEREAS, this accomplishment by the new Bangsamoro lawyers will definitely serve as an inspiration, not only to the aspiring lawyers but also to all young professionals in the Bangsamoro region, as it demonstrates that with passion, hard work, determination and perseverance, one can excel in their chosen career;

WHEREAS, it is imperative that unwavering recognition be given to all Bangsamoro professionals in hurdling their respective licensure examinations to continuously ignite our young professionals’ will and determination to achieve greatness and illustrate that Bangsamoros are of exemplary quality:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To commend and congratulate the passers of the November 2022 Philippine Bar Examination conducted by the Supreme Court of the Philippines, most especially all of the new Bangsamoro lawyers, underscoring the examinations 14th placer, fellow Bangsamoro, Ar-Rashid Jammih Taradji.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That a copy of this Resolution be furnished to the new Bangsamoro lawyers, for their information.

RESOLVED, FINALLY, That a copy of this Resolution shall be posted on the official website of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority - Parliament for the information and reference of all concerned.

ADOPTED, Dhul Hijjah 03, 1444/June 21, 2023.
