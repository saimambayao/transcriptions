**RESOLUTION NO. 257**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING NUR-HAFEIZAH JANNAH I. ABINAL FOR OBTAINING 5ᵀᴴ PLACE WITH AN IMPRESSIVE RATING OF 87.33% AMONGST THE 4,442 EXAMINEES IN THE 2022 CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANT LICENSURE EXAMINATION**

**WHEREAS**, on the 10ᵗʰ day of June 2022, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced the results of the May 2022 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination (CPALE);

**WHEREAS**, with an impressive rating of 87.33%, Mindanao State University-Main Campus graduate, and alumna Ms. Nur-Hafeizah Jannah I. Abinal obtained the rank of Top 5 of the other four thousand four hundred forty-two (4,442) examinees out of which nine hundred ninety (990) with the percentage of (22.29%) have passed the exams after attending the May 22-24, 2022 examination;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Abinal completed her undergraduate degree in the Province of Lanao del Sur, at the Mindanao State University-Main Campus, Marawi with the degree Bachelor of Science in Accountancy (BSA) from year 2015 to 2020;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Abinal finished her Bachelor's degree as *Summa Cum laude* and a Class Valedictorian among the two thousand (2,000) graduates of the Mindanao State University-Main Campus-Batch *Fidem* 2022 with a Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) of 1.10;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Abinal herself is an exchange student to the United States of America (USA) in the school of Earl Warren Highschool, San Antonio Texas, USA, and was awarded as Outstanding Volunteer Service Award by the US Department of State, Student Community Service Award by Academic Year in the USA (AYUSA) International and lastly, Warren Award for Academic Excellence by Earl Warren High School;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Abinal is a proud Bangsamoro from the Province of Lanao del Sur specifically in the Municipality of Marantao, and is the eldest among the son of Japar A. Abinal and Jasmine A. Imam;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Abinal serves as a good example and inspiration for the young Bangsamoro people to emulate and excel in their chosen field of endeavors;

**WHEREAS**, the exemplary achievement of Ms. Abinal is a source of inspiration for the young professionals and aspirants in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and the entire nation which merits this parliament’s recognition and commendation;

**WHEREAS**, it is without a doubt that Ms. Abinal brought honor to our region, even amidst the concerns brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic. Her perseverance, dedication, and courage made evident by securing the top 5 in the Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination. It will forever serve as an inspiration not only to the people of Lanao del Sur but also to the millions of Bangsamoro people;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED** by the BTA Parliament, as it is hereby resolved, To commend Nur-Hafeizah Jannah I. Abinal for obtaining 5ᵗʰ place with an impressive rating of 87.33% amongst the four thousand four hundred forty-two (4,442) examinees in the 2022 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That a copy of this Resolution be furnished to Nur-Hafeizah Jannah I. Abinal, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Dhu Al-Hijjah 20, 1443/July 19, 2022.
