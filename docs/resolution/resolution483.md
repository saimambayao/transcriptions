**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 483**

**RESOLUTION URGING THE CONCERNED MINISTRIES, OFFICES, AGENCIES, THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE, AND THE PHILIPPINE ARMY IN THE BANGSAMORO REGION TO ENSURE THE SAFETY AND SECURITY OF RESIDENTS AND THEIR PROPERTIES IN BARANGAYS KUSIONG, LINEK, MOMPONG, AND BADAK, OF DATU ODIN SINSUAT, MAGUINDANAO DEL NORTE, AND THE SURROUNDING AREAS AMID ONGOING ARMED ATTACKS AND HARASSMENT FROM AN UNIDENTIFIED GROUP, AND TO PROTECT THE RIGHTS OF INTERNALLY DISPLACED PERSONS AND PROVIDE ASSISTANCE THEREOF**

**WHEREAS,** Section 4, Article II of the 1987 Constitution of the Philippines states that "*The primary duty of the Government is to serve and protect the people;*"

**WHEREAS,** Section 5, Article II of the same law provides that "*The maintenance of peace and order, the protection of life, liberty, and property, and the promotion of the general welfare are essential for the enjoyment by all people of the blessing of democracy;*"

**WHEREAS,** Section 1, Article III of the same law states that "*No person shall be deprived of life, liberty, or property without due process of law, nor shall any person be denied of the equal protection of the laws;*"

**WHEREAS,** on July 24, 2024, an armed conflict took place in the coastal areas of Datu Odin Sinsuat, Maguindanao del Norte, specifically in Brgy. Kusiong, Brgy. Linek, Brgy. Mompong, and Brgy. Badak. This event forced residents to leave their homes and relocate to safer areas;

**WHEREAS,** this Resolution seeks to ensure that the relevant ministries, offices, and agencies enhance security in the area to safeguard the lives and properties of innocent individuals. It also aims to provide suitable relocation options, deliver necessary assistance, and uphold the rights of the displaced persons:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To urge the concerned Ministries, Offices, Agencies, the Philippine National Police, and the Philippine Army in the Bangsamoro Region to ensure the safety and security of residents and their properties in Barangays Kusiong, Linek, Mompong, and Badak, of Datu Odin Sinsuat, Maguindanao del Norte, and the surrounding areas amid ongoing armed attacks and harassment from an unidentified group, and to protect the rights of internally displaced persons and provide assistance thereof.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** To forward this Resolution to the concerned Ministries, Offices, Agencies, the Philippine National Police, and the Philippine Army, for information and appropriate consideration.

**ADOPTED,** Muharram 19, 1446/July 25, 2024.
