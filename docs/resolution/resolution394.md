**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 394**

**RESOLUTION ENCOURAGING ALL OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES OF MINISTRIES, AGENCIES, AND OFFICES, INCLUDING THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS OF BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO, TO PARTICIPATE IN THE VALUES TRANSFORMATION TRAININGS**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government is committed to establishing a governance system rooted in moral principles and the teachings of Islam to moral governance;

**WHEREAS**, moral governance, as defined in Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13, also known as the *“Bangsamoro Administrative Code”*, encompasses a framework or set of rules, practices, and processes completely devoid of all the evils of graft and corruption, and explicitly driven by the moral principles of utmost dedication, devotion, honesty, justice, and integrity;

**WHEREAS**, the Civil Service Commission for Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao offers a Values Orientation Workshop for government officials and employees;

**WHEREAS**, the Development Academy of the Bangsamoro (DAB) serves as the central human capital development training arm of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, the essence of moral governance extends beyond Islamic principles, aiming to infuse ethical virtues derived from Islam into the functions of government, its officials, and employees within the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, recognizing the role of public servants as stewards, it is incumbent upon officials and employees of the Bangsamoro Government, including Local Government Units (LGUs), to internalize the moral, ethical, and spiritual dimensions of their roles;

**WHEREAS**, promoting moral governance requires comprehensive training for officials and employees to deeply comprehend and internalize the values underlying moral governance and the historical context of the region;

**WHEREAS**, the Values Transformation Training is designed to enhance participants’ faith, self-development, and commitment to serving the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, ministries, agencies, and offices, including the LGUs, are encouraged to designate a Values Reformation Officer from their respective Human Resource Unit to coordinate, plan, and facilitate the training in collaboration with DAB:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To wholeheartedly encourage all officials and employees of ministries, agencies, and offices, including the local government units of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, to participate in the Values Transformation Trainings.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To furnish a copy of this Resolution to all concerned agencies, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Rabi Al-Awwal 04, 1445/September 19, 2023.
