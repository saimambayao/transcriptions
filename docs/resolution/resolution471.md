**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 471**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT SPEAKER PANGALIAN M. BALINDONG FOR HIS CONFERMENT OF THE DEGREE OF DOCTOR OF LAWS HONORIS CAUSA BY THE MANUEL L. QUEZON UNIVERSITY ON JUNE 25, 2024**

**WHEREAS**, Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament Speaker Pangalian M. Balindong has been conferred the Degree of Doctor of Laws Honoris Causa by the Manuel L. Quezon University on June 25, 2024, recognizing his significant contributions to the field of law, governance, and peacebuilding;

**WHEREAS**, he obtained his Bachelor in Political Science degree in 1962 and his Bachelor of Laws degree in 1966 from the Manuel L. Quezon University, marking the beginning of a distinguished career dedicated to public service and the pursuit of justice;

**WHEREAS**, he has been acknowledged as one of the most influential figures in shaping the history of our country, particularly in the ongoing quest for peace in Mindanao, a testament to his steadfast commitment to resolving decades-long conflicts and political instability;

**WHEREAS**, his enduring legacy lies in his unblemished statesmanship, and unwavering dedication to the Filipino people’s aspiration for peace, significantly contributing to the facilitation of peaceful resolutions and harmonious coexistence among diverse communities;

**WHEREAS**, he has consistently advocated for peaceful pluralism in addressing the Moro problem in Southern Philippines, a philosophy he fervently upheld throughout his service in various capacities, including the Constitutional Convention of 1973, as Deputy Speaker in Congress, as Speaker in the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, as Member of the Consultative Committee on Review the 1987 Constitution, and currently as Speaker in the Bangsamoro Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, his advocacy for pluralism over mere plurality has profoundly impacted legislative and governance frameworks, emphasizing the importance of recognizing and respecting the ethnocultural distinctiveness of our Muslim communities, thereby fostering a culture of understanding and mutual respect;

**WHEREAS**, Speaker Balindong, a “Revolutionary Democrat”, has left an indelible mark on the Filipino nation by championing the peaceful resolution of ethnic conflicts in Mindanao, a legacy that calls for continuous recognition and application in our efforts toward nation-building:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To congratulate Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament Speaker Pangalian M. Balindong for his conferment of the Degree of Doctor of Laws Honoris Causa by the Manuel L. Quezon University on June 25, 2024.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To acknowledge his outstanding achievements and invaluable contributions to peace, justice, and governance in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and the entire country.

**RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE**, To furnish this Resolution to Speaker Pangalian M. Balindong and the Manuel L. Quezon University as a testament to the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament’s recognition of his exemplary public service, dedication, and statesmanship, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Muharram 09, 1446/July 15, 2024.
