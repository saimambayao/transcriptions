**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 34**

**RESOLUTION REALIGNING THE FUND ALLOTTED TO THE PERSONNEL SERVICES OF THE REGIONAL ASSEMBLYMEN AND THEIR CO-TERMINUS STAFF OF THE DEFUNCT REGIONAL LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY TO BE ALLOTTED TO THE FUNDING REQUIREMENTS FOR THE FIVE (5) BTA STAFF FOR THE MONTH OF JULY 2019**

**WHEREAS,** Paragraph 3, Section 2, Article XVI of the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) provides:
*"The Bangsamoro Transition Authority shall be composed of eighty (80) members, who shall be appointed by the President: Provided, That, in addition, the elected officials of the Autonomous Regional Government in Muslim Mindanao shall automatically become members of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority and shall serve until noon of the 30th of June 2019: Provided, further, That non-Moro indigenous communities, youth, women, settler communities, traditional leaders, and other sectors shall have representatives in the Bangsamoro Transition Authority";*

**WHEREAS,** the General Appropriations Act of 2019 allotted fund to the Personnel Services of the regional assemblymen and their co-terminus staff for calendar year 2019;

**WHEREAS,** the co-terminus staff of each RLA member includes a Legislative Officer IV, a Legislative Officer III, an Administrative Assistant V, an Administrative Assistant III, an Administrative Assistant IV and an Executive Assistant I;

**WHEREAS,** the BTA Parliament submitted its proposed staff for each BTA member to the Department of Budget and Management for its approval and funding requirements;

**WHEREAS,** the Department of Budget and Management approved the positions of one (1) Executive Assistant VI, one (1) Autonomous Region Legislative Officer VI, one (1) Autonomous Region Legislative Officer V, one (1) Executive Assistant III, and one (1) Executive Assistant II as the support staff of each BTA member;

**WHEREAS,** the funding requirements for the above-said positions commences on the first day of August 2019;

**WHEREAS,** BTA members already engaged their staff even prior to August 1, 2019 and utilized them already in the plenary sessions, committee meetings and other legislative works;

**NOW THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved, to realign the fund allotted to the Personnel Services of the Regional Assemblymen and their co-terminus staff to be allotted to the funding requirements for the five (5) BTA staff for the month of July 2019;

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that a copy of this resolution be furnished to the Civil Service Commission and the Commission on Audit;

**Adopted,** August 30, 2019.
