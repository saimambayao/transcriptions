**FOURTH REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 243**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY COLLEGE OF LAW IN MARAWI CITY AND NOTRE DAME UNIVERSITY COLLEGE OF LAW IN COTABATO CITY FOR PRODUCING NINETY-FOUR (94) NEW BANGSAMORO LAWYERS IN THE RECENT 2020/2021 BAR EXAMINATIONS**

**WHEREAS**, Section 16, Article 9 of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as “Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM)” provides that education shall be a top priority of the Bangsamoro Government to establish, maintain, and support a complete and integrated system of quality education, which shall be a subsystem of the national education system;

**WHEREAS**, the Bar Examinations are the apex test for those aspiring to join the Philippine Bar;

**WHEREAS**, the Supreme Court of the Philippines is the sole administrator of the Bar Examinations;

**WHEREAS**, the 2020/2021 Bar Examinations Chairperson was Supreme Court Associate Justice Marvic Mario Victor F. Leonen;

**WHEREAS**, the 2020/2021 Bar Examinations were the first to be conducted in the Philippines in a localized and digitalized format;

**WHEREAS**, the 2020/2021 Bar Examinations were administered on February 4 and 6, 2022, at twenty-nine (29) local testing locations throughout the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, the results of 2020/2021 Bar Examinations were released on April 12, 2022 with a 72.28% passing rate, living up to its tagline as the Best Bar Ever;

**WHEREAS**, Mindanao State University College of Law in Marawi City and Notre Dame University College of Law in Cotabato City, the BARMM territory’s only existing law schools, produced ninety-four (94) new Bangsamoro lawyers;

**WHEREAS**, the successful bar takers from Mindanao State University College of Law in Marawi City and Notre Dame University College of Law in Cotabato City were forty-six (46) and forty-eight (48), respectively;

**WHEREAS**, the increased availability of Bangsamoro lawyers in the region will enhance access to justice for those in need;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Parliament likewise commends all the Bangsamoro bar passers who came from the different law schools in the Philippines:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED BY the Bangsamoro Transition Authority**, as it is hereby resolved, To commend the Mindanao State University College of Law in Marawi City and Notre Dame University College of Law in Cotabato City for producing ninety-four (94) new Bangsamoro lawyers in the recent 2020/2021 Bar Examinations.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That a copy of this Resolution be furnished to the Mindanao State University in Marawi City, Notre Dame University in Cotabato City, Integrated Bar of the Philippines Lanao Del Sur Chapter, and Integrated Bar of the Philippines Cotabato Chapter, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Dhu Al-Qa’dah 16, 1443/June 15, 2022.
