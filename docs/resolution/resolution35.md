**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 35**

**RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH TO APPRISE THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) ON THE HEALTH SITUATION OF THE BANGSAMORO REGION PARTICULARLY IN LIGHT OF THE SPATE OF DENGUE OUTBREAKS IN THE COUNTRY**

**Whereas,** in the past few days, several provinces have declared Dengue outbreaks in their respective provinces following the surge of Dengue cases in their area;

**Whereas,** there is a need for the Bangsamoro public to be fully aware of the extent of the Dengue problem or perhaps other diseases in order for them prevent such occurrence and to prepare in the event that Dengue strikes in their area ;

**Whereas,** there is a need for the BTA to be aware of the health condition of the region not only in the case of Dengue but also in other cases where the people must be warned about;

**Whereas,** the BTA must be well aware of the health situation for it to be able to recommend to the Ministry of Health and to the Chief Minister certain actions for the benefit of the people including the allocation of more funds to address any contingency that the Ministry of Health may opt to implement;

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved, to request the Ministry of Health to apprise the BTA on the health situation of the region particularly in light of the spate of dengue outbreaks in the country.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** that the foregoing Resolution be forwarded to the Ministry of Health of the Bangsamoro Government.

**Adopted,** September 18, 2019.
