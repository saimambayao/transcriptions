**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 463**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MR. SAADANY PANUMPANG AMINODEN AND MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY-MAIN CAMPUS FOR ENGINEER AMINODEN’S PLACING FOURTH IN THE 2024 REGISTERED ELECTRICAL ENGINEERS LICENSURE EXAMINATION, AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS**

**WHEREAS**, on May 02, 2024, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced that four thousand four hundred thirty-six (4,436) out of seven thousand five (7,005) passed the Registered Electrical Engineers Licensure Examination given by the Board of Electrical Engineering in National Capital Region (NCR), Baguio, Butuan, Cagayan de Oro, Cebu, Davao, Iloilo, Legazpi, Lucena, Pampanga, Rosales, Tacloban, Tuguegarao, Zamboanga, and Puerto Princesa, Palawan;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Saadany P. Aminoden, a Bangsamoro and native of Tugaya, Lanao del Sur, placed fourth (4th) in the aforementioned licensure examination with a general average of ninety-three point five percent (93.50%);

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Aminoden graduated from Mindanao State University-Main Campus in Marawi City with a Latin honor of Magna Cum Laude;

**WHEREAS**, both parents, Aminoden Amito Edaros and Norhaya Papacol Panumpang, are residents of Tugaya, Lanao del Sur and engaged into family-owned business;

**WHEREAS**, placing 4th in any given licensure examination is a rare opportunity that gives utmost pride and honor not only to the family, school, tribe but also to the entire Bangsamoro people that inspires the young and aspiring students to follow the exemplary achievement:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To congratulate and commend Mr. Saadany Panumpang Aminoden and Mindanao State University-Main Campus for Engineer Aminoden’s placing fourth in the 2024 Registered Electrical Engineers Licensure Examination, and all Bangsamoro passers.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present this foregoing Resolution to Mr. Saadany Panumpang Aminoden and to Mindanao State University-Main Campus, for information.

**ADOPTED**, *Dhu Al-Hijjah* 13, 1445/June 19, 2024.
