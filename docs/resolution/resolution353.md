RESOLUTION NO. 353

RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING FORMER CONGRESSMAN MOHAMMED HUSSEIN P. PANGANDAMAN FOR HIS APPOINTMENT AS ADMINISTRATOR OF THE AUTHORITY OF THE FREEPORT AREA OF BATAAN (AFAB)

WHEREAS, Mohammed Hussein P. Pangandaman is a seasoned politician who served as the Representative of the 1st District of Lanao del Sur and displayed his exceptional leadership skills during his term as Representative, actively advocating for the welfare of his constituents and authoring several bills effectively addressing key concerns affecting his district;

WHEREAS, Mohammed Hussein P. Pangandaman was recently appointed by President Ferdinand “BongBong” R. Marcos Jr. as the Administrator of the Authority of the Freeport Area of Bataan (AFAB), which is tasked with promoting economic growth through the establishment of a world-class freeport area in Bataan;

WHEREAS, Mohammed Hussein P. Pangandaman’s extensive political experience, coupled with his outstanding track record of leadership, positions him as an excellent candidate for the said position, and his appointment is commendable:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To congratulate former Congressman Mohammed Hussein P. Pangandaman for his appointment as Administrator of the Authority of the Freeport Area of Bataan (AFAB).

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That a copy of this Resolution be presented to the office of Mohammed Hussein P. Pangandaman, for information.

ADOPTED, Dhul Hijjah 04, 1444/June 22, 2023.
