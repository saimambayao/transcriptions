**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 237**

**RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING THE OFFICE OF CHIEF MINISTER TO GRANT RAMADHAN ALLOWANCE TO ALL OFFICIALS, PERSONNEL, AND EMPLOYEES OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM), REGARDLESS OF EMPLOYMENT STATUS, SUBJECT TO AVAILABILITY OF FUNDS AND FURTHER SUBJECT TO STANDARD ACCOUNTING AND AUDITING RULES AND REGULATIONS**

**WHEREAS,** the noble month of Ramadan will begin on the first week of April, 2022;

**WHEREAS,** providing Ramadan allowance to all officials, personnel and employees of the BARMM is one way to motivate them to work harder and be more productive even in the difficult time of the month-long fasting and dusk-to-dawn prayers;

**WHEREAS,** moreover, the grant of Ramadan allowance will be of great help to all Bangsamoro government officials, personnel, employees and their families especially in coping up with the high cost of basic commodities, brought about by rising fuel prices;

**WHEREAS,** Parliament Bill No. 171 authorizing Ramadan Economic Assistance and Relief (REAR) is pending in the committee level and may not be approved in time for the purpose it is intended:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to authorize the Office of Chief Minister to grant Ramadhan allowance to all officials, personnel, and employees of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) regardless of employment status, subject to availability of funds and further subject to standard accounting and auditing rules and regulations.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** the Ministry of Finance, Budget and Management shall issue the necessary guidelines in the implementation of the purpose and objective of this resolution.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY,** to forward the foregoing Resolution to the Honorable Chief Minister Ahod B. Ebrahim, and the Ministry of Finance, Budget and Management, for information and appropriate consideration.
