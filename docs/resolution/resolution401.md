**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 401**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO MEN’S VOLLEYBALL TEAM FOR WINNING THE GOLD MEDAL IN THE RECENTLY CONCLUDED NATIONAL STATE COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES ATHLETIC ASSOCIATION GAMES 2023, THEREBY GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that the State shall promote physical education and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and amateur sports, including training for international competitions, to foster self-discipline, teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;

**WHEREAS**, Section 13, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known as the *“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao”*, provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall promote, among others, the mental and physical fitness of the youth through sports;

**WHEREAS**, pursuant to this mandate, the Bangsamoro Government is committed to promoting sports, by encouraging and supporting programs, leagues, competitions, indigenous games, martial arts, and amateur sports;

**WHEREAS**, the National State Colleges and Universities Athletic Association Games 2023 was held at New Clark City Athletics Stadium, Capas, Tarlac, Philippines on October 1 to 6, 2023, and was participated in by over sixty thousand (60,000) student-athletes nationwide from one hundred fourteen (114) state universities and colleges;

**WHEREAS**, on October 5, 2023, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) Men’s Volleyball Team representing Tawi-Tawi Regional Agricultural College clinched the gold medal, thereby making history for the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, the BARMM Men’s Volleyball Team consists of fifteen (15) Bangsamoro athletes, namely: Mr. Hamil I. Junni, Mr. Abdulrahul S. Janani, Mr. Elsad M. Akmad, Mr. Muktal T. Peria, Mr. Cesar Adamil, Mr. Alqhadie C. Jali, Mr. Alhadzkal J. Sasapan, Mr. Midzran Bangsa, Mr. Renard K. Casera, Mr. Alih Faizal Gampong, Mr. Alsahbil I. Hadji, Mr. Asnar L. Napie, Mr. Brayan K. Sahirin, Mr. Esmail M. Kasim, and Mr. Dickson Sapplani, coached by Mr. Abraham J. Tandi as the head coach and Mr. Edward R. Lee as the assistant coach, and with four (4) coaching staff, namely: Mr. Aljasmin Asaali, Mr. Herman Sappalani, Mr. Alfaidie Asaali, and Mr. Al-Siradz Asakil;

**WHEREAS**, the team exhibited commendable gallantry and sportsmanship by winning three (3) consecutive sets against Region VI (Western Visayas);

**WHEREAS**, the exemplary performance of the BARMM Men’s Volleyball Team serves as a source of inspiration not only to budding young athletes within the Bangsamoro but also to other aspiring athletes throughout the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, by winning this national tournament, the BARMM Men’s Volleyball Team has made the region proud and has shown the entire country the Bangsamoro spirit of competitiveness and excellence;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority expresses deep appreciation and immense pride in recognizing the BARMM Men’s Volleyball Team for their remarkable achievement, extending full recognition and highest commendation for the distinction they have brought to the Bangsamoro people:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate and commend the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao Men’s Volleyball Team for winning the gold medal in the recently concluded National State Colleges and Universities Athletic Association Games 2023, thereby giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present this Resolution to the BARMM Men’s Volleyball Team in recognition and appreciation of their exemplary achievement, and for their information and its necessary effects.

**ADOPTED**, Rabi Al-Akhir 01, 1445/October 16, 2023.
