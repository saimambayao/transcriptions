**RESOLUTION NO. 438**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING COTABATO STATE UNIVERSITY ON ITS CENTENNIAL ANNIVERSARY CELEBRATION ON FEBRUARY 8, 2024**

**WHEREAS,** Cotabato State University (CSU), formerly recognized as Cotabato High School (CHS), was established as a comprehensive secondary educational institution on February 8, 1924, sustaining operations for nearly forty-three (43) years;

**WHEREAS,** in 1977, CHS was converted into Cotabato City National High School (CCNHS), preserving this title for approximately six (6) years;

**WHEREAS,** in 1983, CCNHS underwent further development, assuming the designation of Cotabato City State Polytechnic College (CCSPC), retaining this appellation for over three decades;

**WHEREAS,** on May 24, 2013, Republic Act No. 10585 was signed into law by His Excellency President Benigno Simeon C. Aquino, III, directing the conversion of CCSPC into a state university to be formally known as Cotabato State University (CSU);

**WHEREAS,** CSU has steadfastly served as an institution of higher learning, dedicated to fostering academic excellence, thereby promoting community development for a century;

**WHEREAS,** CSU has consistently demonstrated an unwavering commitment to providing superior education, conducting impactful research, and extending valuable services to its student body and stakeholders, thereby significantly contributing to the advancement of knowledge and the socio-economic development of the Bangsamoro Region;

**WHEREAS,** the commemoration of CSU's centennial anniversary represents a momentous occasion, deserving of recognition and celebration from the academic community, governmental entities, alumni, partners, and the general public, and the accomplishments, contributions, and enduring legacy of CSU over the course of the past century merit recognition and acknowledgment, underscoring its impact on individuals, communities, and society as a whole:

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To commend and congratulate Cotabato State University on its centennial anniversary celebration on February 8, 2024.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** To present this Resolution to CSU, for information.
