**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 106**

**RESOLUTION APPROVING THE AMENDMENTS ON RULE VII, SECTION 3 (1) AND SECTION 4 (2) MADE BY THE COMMITTEE ON RULES ON THE PARLIAMENTARY RULES, PROCEDURES AND PRACTICES OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY - PARLIAMENT OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO.**

**WHEREAS**, The Parliamentary Rules, Procedures and Practices of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) provides for the jurisdiction of the different Statutory and Parliamentary Committees;

**WHEREAS**, last January 13, 2021 committee meeting, the Committee on Rules agreed to amend Rule VII, Section 3, Paragraph No. 1 of the Parliamentary Rules, Procedures and Practices of the BTA Parliament-BARMM to improve the styling and avoid confusion on the subject of scrutiny of the Committee on Finance, Budget and Management which shall be the internal budget and budget preparation, submission, approval, and execution of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, upon proposal of the Honorable Speaker of the BTA Parliament-BARMM, the Committee on Rules also agreed during the aforementioned meeting to amend Rule VII, Section 4, Paragraph No. 2 in the Parliamentary Rules, Procedures and Practices of the BTA Parliament-BARMM to avoid confusion in the jurisdiction of the Committee on Accounts, to delete jurisdiction related to audit and to improve the styling of the said provision;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to amend Rule VII, Section 3, Paragraph 1 of the Parliamentary Rules, Procedures and Practices of the BTA Parliament-BARMM, to read as follows:

**Committee on Finance, Budget and Management.** - The Committee on Finance, Budget and Management shall have thirty-one (31) Members. It shall have jurisdiction on all matters relating to the income, revenues, preparation of budget, and the appropriation of funds for the operations of the Bangsamoro Government including its expenditure, payment of public indebtedness, claims against the Bangsamoro Government, inter-governmental revenue sharing, and in general, all matters relating to public expenditures.

The work of the Committee on Finance, Budget and Management includes the scrutiny and review of the budget and expenditure of the Bangsamoro Government.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to amend Rule VII, Section 4, Paragraph No. 2 of the Parliamentary Rules, Procedures and Practices of the BTA Parliament-BARMM, to read as follows:

**Committee on Accounts.** - The Committee on Accounts shall have seventeen (17) Members. It shall have jurisdiction over all matters pertaining to the review and assessment of the budget of the Bangsamoro Parliament.

**ADOPTED**, January 28, 2021.
