**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 178**

**RESOLUTION URGING THE MINISTRIES, OFFICES AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS INCLUDING PRIVATE ESTABLISHMENTS TO ENSURE COMPLIANCE WITH NATIONAL OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH AND SAFETY STANDARDS IN WORKPLACES**

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Government’s general welfare policy is to ensure and support, among other things, the health and safety of its inhabitants;

WHEREAS, it is the policy of the Bangsamoro Government to guarantee the right of workers to humane work condition, promote health and safety and preserve the comfort and convenience of the workers in all places of employment;

WHEREAS, accidents and health emergencies including the life-threatening danger of COVID-19 pandemic in the workplace are likely to cause death, illness or physical harm to the workers are possible;

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 11058, otherwise known as “An Act Strengthening Compliance with Occupational Safety and Health Standards and Providing Penalties for Violations Thereof” was enacted last August 17, 2018;

WHEREAS, the office of the Ministry of Labor and Employment-Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (MOLE-BARMM) deems the importance of applying this Act not only to private establishments but also to all ministries, offices and LGU workplaces;

WHEREAS, for the reasons mentioned, it is highly requested that the ministries, offices and local government units provide/detail safety and health officers in their workplace, and compliance to the Occupational Safety and Health (OSH) standards be supported;

WHEREAS, a Joint Memorandum Circular No. 1, series of 2020 or Occupational Safety and Health (OSH) for the private sectors was issued last March 4, 2020:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to urge the different ministries, offices and Local Government Units including private establishments to ensure compliance with National Occupational Health and Safety Standards in work places.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, that copy of this Resolution be forwarded to the different ministries and offices of the Bangsamoro Government, for information and guidance.

ADOPTED, Rabi ul Awwal 7, 1443/October 14, 2021.
