**RESOLUTION NO. 252**

**RESOLUTION URGING ALL MINISTRIES, OFFICES, AND ATTACHED AGENCIES, THROUGH THE OFFICE OF THE INTERIM CHIEF MINISTER, TO DEVELOP AND INTEGRATE VALUES TRANSFORMATION PROGRAMS FOR THE PURPOSE OF INCULCATING THE PRINCIPLES OF MORAL GOVERNANCE IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM)**

**WHEREAS**, the Interim Chief Minister, Honorable Ahod “Alhaj Murad” Ebrahim, called for moral governance as the guiding principle of the new leadership in the administration of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**WHEREAS**, crafting of and implementing policies, programs and projects should be guided by the principles of moral governance to attain genuine progress and inclusive development for the Region;

**WHEREAS**, the key to instituting moral governance is to have a government that is responsive to the needs of its constituents, inclusive and democratic; all its actions are fair, legal and legitimate; its policy decisions are rational, deliberative and transparent; all its officials and employees are adhering to the principle of checks and balances, culturally and gender-sensitive, and are accountable to the people whom they swore to serve with all honesty and integrity;

**WHEREAS**, instituting moral governance in the bureaucracy remains to be a formidable challenge given its long history of corruption, manipulation, divisiveness, nepotism, and other social ills besetting the Region;

**WHEREAS**, inculcating ethical and moral values among the officials, employees, and partners directly or indirectly affiliated or working with the BARMM is very urgent and critical, especially during the transition period of the Bangsamoro Government:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To urge all ministries, offices, and attached agencies, through the office of the Interim Chief Minister, to develop and integrate values transformation programs for the purpose of inculcating the principles of moral governance in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, for the Office of the Interim Chief Minister to issue a memorandum outlining the guidelines to all ministries, offices, and all attached agencies for the immediate implementation of the same.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, That copy of this Resolution be furnished to the Honorable Chief Minister Ahod B. Ebrahim, for information and appropriate consideration.

**ADOPTED**, Dhu Al-Qa’dah 24, 1443/ June 23, 2022.
