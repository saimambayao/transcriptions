**RESOLUTION NO. 391**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST CONDOLENCES AND SYMPATHY TO THE BEREAVED FAMILY OF AMELITA A. PIANG, WIFE OF MEMBER OF THE PARLIAMENT RAMON A. PIANG, SR.**

**WHEREAS**, Amelita A. Piang is the wife of Member of the Parliament (MP) Ramon A. Piang, Sr., former mayor of Upi, Maguindanao, and the mother of Ms. Ma. Rona Cristina P. Flores, the incumbent mayor of the said municipality;

**WHEREAS**, the late Mrs. Piang was the President of the Upi Women’s Federation Inc., which during her term as president, the organization received a recognition from the Provincial Government of Maguindanao for its valuable effort against human trafficking through its program, *“Stop Trafficking and Exploitation of People Through Unlimited Potentials”* (STEP-UP);

**WHEREAS**, she was a member of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Red Cross Cotabato City - Maguindanao Chapter from February 2020 up to the present, and she was also the Chairperson of the Committee on Welfare and Services of the said agency;

**WHEREAS**, as an active church member, Mrs. Piang joined the Couples for Christ - Upi Chapter, a religious organization in their sect, and together with MP Piang, Sr., the couple became the Chapter Head of such organization;

**WHEREAS**, because of her generosity and commitment to helping people from different walks of life in their locality, most residents of Upi identified her as *“Ina ng bayan ng Upi;”*

**WHEREAS**, this resolution signifies the intent of the Bangsamoro Government to sympathize with the bereaved family of the deceased, especially with MP Piang, Sr., and with the whole community of Upi, Maguindanao;

**WHEREAS**, on August 4, 2023, Mrs. Piang drew her last breath and returned to our Almighty Creator:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To express deepest condolences and sympathy to the bereaved family of Amelita A. Piang, wife of Member of the Parliament Ramon A. Piang, Sr.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present this Resolution to the bereaved family of Mrs. Amelita A. Piang, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Rabi Al-Awwal 03, 1445/September 18, 2023.
