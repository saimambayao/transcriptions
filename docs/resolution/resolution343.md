RESOLUTION NO. 343

RESOLUTION EXPRESSING PROFOUND CONDOLENCES ON THE PASSING OF RAMEER AMILASAN TAWASIL AND COMMENDING HIS EXTRAORDINARY CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE BANGSAMORO CULTURE AND ARTS

WHEREAS, it is with deep sadness that the Bangsamoro Parliament learned of the passing of Rameer Amilasan Tawasil on June 6, 2023, a celebrated Tausug artist whose indelible mark on the world of art and culture has left an enduring legacy in the Bangsamoro region;

WHEREAS, Rameer Amilasan Tawasil, through his mastery of the "ukkil" brush strokes, has captivated audiences with his exquisite wood carvings, brass wares, and textiles, thus preserving and promoting the rich artistic traditions of the Tausug people;

WHEREAS, his artistic prowess transcended boundaries and elevated the vibrant colors and profound narratives of Sulu, showcasing the abundance and resilience of the Bangsamoro culture to the world;

WHEREAS, Rameer's dedication and commitment to his craft have earned him numerous accolades and recognition, including the prestigious Zamboanga Hermosa Recognition Award for Culture and the Arts in 2003 and the National Traveling Exhibition Grant from the National Commission for Culture and the Arts in the same year;

WHEREAS, he has brought pride and distinction to the Bangsamoro community through his participation in various solo and group exhibitions, both locally and internationally, including the Eidil Fitri International Art Festival in Manila City, the Julfekar in The Brush of Rameeta in Kuala Lumpur, and the Tanaw Mindanao in Brussels, Belgium;

WHEREAS, his masterpieces have graced esteemed galleries in London and Kuching, Malaysia, as well as private collections in Germany, Korea, the U.S., and the Philippines, affirming the universal appeal and appreciation of his art;

WHEREAS, his profound dedication to peace and unity is exemplified by his iconic rendition of the "peace dove" logo, which has become a symbol of peace locally and globally, earning him the Peace Citation Award from the Peace Advocates Zamboanga in 2006;

WHEREAS, his invaluable contributions to the Bangsamoro visual arts and culture have been recognized and supported by institutions such as the Cultural Center of the Philippines, Vargas Museum in the University of the Philippines, National Commission for Culture and the Arts Gallery, Metropolitan Museum of Manila, and various private galleries;

WHEREAS, Rameer Tawasil's passing leaves a tremendous void in the Bangsamoro artistic landscape, yet his enduring influence and inspirational works will continue to shape and inspire future generations;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Parliament desires to extend its deepest condolences and sympathies to the bereaved family, friends and the entire Bangsamoro community, mourning the loss of this exceptional artist and cultural icon:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To express profound condolences on the passing of Rameer Amilasan Tawasil and commending his extraordinary contributions to the Bangsamoro culture and arts.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That the Bangsamoro Commission for the Preservation of Cultural Heritage (BCPCH) explores avenues to preserve and promote the artistic legacy of Rameer Amilasan Tawasil, ensuring that his contributions continue to inspire and educate future generations.

RESOLVED, FINALLY, That this Resolution be presented to the family of Rameer Amilasan Tawasil and copies of the same be furnished to BCPCH and other concerned agencies for their information, guidance, and appropriate action.

ADOPTED, Dhul Hijjah 03, 1444/June 21, 2023.
