**RESOLUTION NO. 215**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING TWENTY-FOUR LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM) FOR QUALIFYING AS MOST COMPETITIVE LGUS OF THE REGION IN THE 2021 CITIES AND MUNICIPALITIES, COMPETITIVENESS INDEX OF THE NATIONAL COMPETITIVENESS COUNCIL**

**WHEREAS**, the Cities and Municipalities Competitiveness Index is an annual ranking developed by the National Competitiveness Council through the Regional Competitiveness Committees with the assistance of the United States Agency for International Development (USAID);

**WHEREAS**, the main objective of the same is to promote local competitiveness to enhance long-term national competitiveness;

**WHEREAS**, cities and municipalities are ranked on their competitiveness based on the overall competitiveness score which is the sum of the scores of the four main pillars which pool data from several sub-indicators. The four main pillars are economic dynamism, government efficiency, infrastructure, and resiliency;

**WHEREAS**, for the Fiscal Year 2021 data, the following BARMM LGUs qualified as most competitive LGUs in the said ranking:

1. Component Cities
   a. Marawi City, Lanao del Sur
   b. Lamitan City, Basilan

2. Class 1 to 2 Municipalities
   a. Talipapao, Sulu
   b. Jolo, Sulu
   c. Wao, Lanao del Sur
   d. Bubong, Lanao del Sur
   e. Marantao, Lanao del Sur
   f. Sultan Kudarat, Maguindanao
   g. Upi, Maguindanao

3. Class 3 to 4 Muncipalities
   a. Maluso, Basilan
   b. Balabagan, Lanao del Sur
   c. Bumbaran, Lanao del Sur
   d. Piagapo, Lanao del Sur
   e. Datu Paglas, Maguindanao
   f. General Salipada K. Pendatun, Maguindanao
   g. Matanog, Maguindanao
   h. Buluan, Maguindanao

4. Class 5 to 6 Municipalities
   a. Omar, Sulu
   b. Calanogas, Lanao del Sur
   c. Masiu, Lanao del Sur
   d. Datu Abdullah Sangki, Maguindanao
   e. Mamasapano, Maguindanao
   f. Kabuntalan, Maguindanao
   g. Paglat, Maguindanao

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority wishes to express its commendation and congratulations to the twenty-four LGUs who qualified as most competitive LGUs of the region, based on the Cities and Municipalities Competitive Index of the National Competitiveness Council:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To commend and congratulate the twenty-four LGUs of BARMM for qualifying as most competitive LGUs of the region in the Cities and Municipalities Competitiveness Index for Fiscal Year 2021 by the National Competitiveness Council.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To forward the foregoing Resolution to the above-mentioned qualifying LGUs, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Rajab 14, 1443/February 16, 2022.
