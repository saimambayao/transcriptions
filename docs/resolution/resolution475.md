**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 475**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING THE BANGSAMORO ATHLETES FROM COTABATO AND SULTAN MASTURA DOJO FOR BAGGING SEVERAL AWARDS DURING THE 19th NATIONAL FULL CONTACT KYOKUSHINRYU KARATE TOURNAMENT HELD IN DOLORES, EASTERN SAMAR LAST JUNE 22-23, 2024 AND URGING THE BANGSAMORO SPORTS COMMISSION TO CONTINUALLY PROVIDE SUPPORT FOR THE BANGSAMORO ATHLETES THROUGH ENCOURAGING ALLOCATION OF SUPPLEMENTARY BUDGET**

**WHEREAS**, Section 13, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known as “*Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao*”, provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall recognize the vital role of the youth in nation-building, promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, and nationalism, encourage involvement in public and civic affairs, and promote mental and physical fitness through sports;

**WHEREAS**, there are several institutions of sports within the Bangsamoro Region that serve as partners of the Bangsamoro Government in promoting the mental and physical fitness of the Bangsamoro youth;

**WHEREAS**, among these institutions are the Bangsamoro Cotabato Dojo and Sultan Mastura Dojo headed by Sensei Usman Baraguir and Coach Senpai Ahmadinnie Talapas which develops young Bangsamoro athletes in the field of KyokushinRyu Karate;

**WHEREAS**, last June 22-23, 2024, the Bangsamoro Cotabato Dojo and Sultan Mastura Dojo competed in the 19th National Full Contact KyokushinRyu Karate Tournament which garnered several awards, wherein the Bangsamoro Cotabato Dojo brought home ten (10) trophies with four (4) championships, two 2nd placements, and four 3rd placements, while the Sultan Mastura Dojo brought home four (4) trophies after garnering two (2) championships and two (2) 2nd placements. Thereby, a total of fourteen (14) trophies for the whole Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**WHEREAS**, the athletes not only brought an immense source of pride to the Bangsamoro Region but also served as a model to the rest of the Bangsamoro youth in putting importance on their holistic development and fitness:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To congratulate the Bangsamoro athletes from Cotabato and Sultan Mastura Dojo for bagging several awards during the 19th National Full Contact KyokushinRyu Karate Tournament held in Dolores, Eastern Samar last June 22-23, 2024 and urging the Bangsamoro Sports Commission to continually provide support for the Bangsamoro athletes through encouraging allocation of supplementary budget.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To urge the Bangsamoro Sports Commission (BSC) to continually provide support in the form of finances, logistics, opportunities, or resources to the Bangsamoro athletes who are competing in credible sports competitions.

**RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE**, To encourage the BSC to include in their annual budget proposal the allocation of supplementary budget for supporting the activities, facilities, and development of sports institutions inside the Bangsamoro Region.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, To furnish this Resolution to the athletes from Cotabato, Sultan Mastura Dojo, and the Office of the BSC, for information and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED**, Muharram 10, 1446/July 16, 2024.
