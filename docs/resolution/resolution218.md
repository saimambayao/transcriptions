
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
**BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT**
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 218**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING BENAMIN ELEGORES RINABOR FOR RANKING TOP 2 IN THE DECEMBER 2021 PHILIPPINE CRIMINOLOGY LICENSURE EXAMINATION, AND FOR GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Benamin Elegores Rinabor, a Bangsamoro and graduate of Lake Lanao College Incorporated in Marawi City with the degree Bachelor of Science in Criminology, ranked Top 2 in the recent Criminology Licensure Examination administered by the Professional Regulation Commission and the Board of Criminology on December 12,13, and 14, 2021;

**WHEREAS**, with an impressive rating of 89.55%, Mr. Rinabor obtained the second highest rank of the other 33,983 examinees out of which 11,610 or 34.16% have passed the examination;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Rinabor finished his Bachelor's degree as cum laude of the Batch Maharlika 2021 among the 110 graduates of Lake Lanao College Incorporated;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Rinabor is a proud Bangsamoro from the province of Lanao del Sur specifically in the Municipality of Tugaya, and is the son of Amin Munurun B. Rinabor and Agnes I. Antonio;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Rinabor's commendable performance brought honor not only to his family but to the entire Bangsamoro as well, setting him as a good example and inspiration especially to the young Bangsamoro people to emulate and to excel in their chosen field of endeavors;

**WHEREAS**, it is without doubt that Mr. Rinabor brought honor to the region, even amidst the concerns brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic. His perseverance, dedication and courage made evident by securing the top 2 rank in the Criminology Licensure Examination. It will forever serve as an inspiration not only to the people of Lanao del Sur but also to the millions of Bangsamoro people across the country;

**WHEREAS**, the exemplary achievement of Mr. Rinabor is a source of inspiration for the young professionals and aspirants in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and the entire nation which merits this parliament's recognition and commendation;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority is cognizant and proud of Mr. Rinabor's exemplary achievement, thus extending its warmest congratulations for obtaining the second highest rank in the December 2021 Criminology Licensure Examination:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To commend and congratulate Benamin Elegores Rinabor for ranking top 2 in the December 2021 Philippine Criminology Licensure Examination, and for giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present the foregoing Resolution to Benamin Elegores Rinabor, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Rajab 20, 1443/February 22, 2022.
