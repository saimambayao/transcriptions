**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 517**

**RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT THROUGH THE OFFICE OF THE HONORABLE CHIEF MINISTER AHOD BALAWAG EBRAHIM TO INSTITUTE A JURIS DOCTOR BRIDGING PROGRAM IN ORDER TO PREPARE AND QUALIFY OVERSEAS AND LOCAL *KULLIYAH* GRADUATE *ULAMA* FOR LEGAL PRACTICE AND TO A JUDGE POSITIONS IN THE BANGSAMORO *SHARI’AH* COURTS**

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article X of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the *“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (OLBARMM)”*, provides a justice system in accordance with the unique cultural and historical heritage of the Bangsamoro;

**WHEREAS**, Section 8, Article X of the OLBARMM specifies the qualifications of judges in the *Shari’ah* Circuit, District, and High Courts, which include passing the Philippine Bar;

**WHEREAS**, Section 13, Article X of said law specifies who can practice before the *Shari’ah* courts, which include *Shari’ah* Special Bar and the Philippine Bar members;

**WHEREAS**, Section 16, Article X of the same law calls on the Bangsamoro Parliament to create legal offices, such as *Shari’ah* Public Assistance Office, *Shari’ah* Special Prosecution Service, *Shari’ah* Academy, and Office of the Jurisconsult of Islamic Law;

**WHEREAS**, the *ulama* (religious leaders) and the *umara* (political leaders), in the Islamic context, are an integral part of Islamic leadership. Thus, a critical stakeholder and sector in the development of the Bangsamoro;

**WHEREAS**, in order to assume their roles and positive engagement in the regional *Shari’ah* justice system, a bridging program is needed to meet the legal qualification of the Philippine Bar and qualify for appointment to the *Shari’ah* courts within Bangsamoro:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To request the Bangsamoro Government through the Office of the Honorable Chief Minister Ahod Balawag Ebrahim to institute a Juris Doctor bridging program as a scholarship grant in order to prepare and qualify overseas and local *kulliyah* graduate *ulama* for legal practice and appointment to a judge positions in the Bangsamoro *Shari’ah* courts.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To forward this Resolution to the Office of the Honorable Chief Minister Ahod Balawag Ebrahim, for consideration and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED**, *Rajab* 23, 1446/January 23, 2025.
