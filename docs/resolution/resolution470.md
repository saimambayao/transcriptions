**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 470**

**RESOLUTION EXTENDING GRATITUDE AND APPRECIATION TO PRESIDENT FERDINAND ROMUALDEZ MARCOS JR. FOR APPOINTING AMILBAHAR P. AMILASAN JR. AS ACTING CHAIRMAN/CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER AND MEMBER OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS OF THE AL-AMANAH ISLAMIC INVESTMENT BANK OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FURTHER CONGRATULATING MR. AMILASAN FOR HIS APPOINTMENT**

**WHEREAS**, on June 19, 2024, President Ferdinand R. Marcos Jr. appointed Mr. Amilbahar P. Amilasan Jr. as Acting Chairman/Chief Executive Officer and Member of the Board of Directors of Al-Amanah Islamic Investment Bank of the Philippines (AAIIBP);

**WHEREAS**, pursuant to Section 3 of Republic Act No. 6848, otherwise known as “*The Charter of Al-Amanah Islamic Investment Bank of the Philippines*”, the primary purpose of the Islamic Bank is to promote and accelerate the socio-economic development of the Autonomous Region by performing banking, financing, and investment operations and to establish and participate in agricultural, commercial and industrial ventures based on the Islamic concept of banking;

**WHEREAS**, Section 32, Article XIII of Republic Act No. 11054, also known as the “*Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao*”, mentions that the Bangsamoro Government, the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas, the Department of Finance, and the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos shall jointly promote the development of an Islamic banking and finance system;

**WHEREAS**, the AAIIBP operates nine (9) banks in the cities of Cagayan de Oro, Cotabato, Davao, Iligan, General Santos, Marawi, Makati, Zamboanga, and Jolo in Sulu.

**WHEREAS**, currently, Mr. Amilasan Jr. serves as the Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Finance, Budget, and Management – Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (MFBM-BARMM), and also holds the position of Chairman of the Tabun Halal Multi-Purpose Cooperative;

**WHEREAS**, from 2020 to 2022, he served as a consultant for both the Office of the Chief Minister - BARMM and the Bangsamoro Board of Investment - BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Amilasan Jr., a graduate of Ateneo De Davao University in 1996, was hired by the Metropolitan Bank and Trust Company (Metrobank) Cotabato City branch in the same year, serving as Senior Manager from 2004 to 2020 and earning the distinction of Multi-Year Branch Performance Awardee;

**WHEREAS**, his extensive years in public service and commitment to excellence, particularly in the field of banking and finance, significantly contributed to his well-deserved appointment to the AAIIBP;

**WHEREAS**, this resolution aims to extend gratitude and appreciation to President Ferdinand R. Marcos Jr., for appointing Deputy Minister Amilasan Jr., and to congratulate the latter on his well-deserved appointment, foreseeing that his dedication to his craft, guided by moral governance, will significantly enhance the implementation of a dynamic Islamic banking system in the Philippines, effectively fulfilling the vision and mission of the AAIIBP:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To extend gratitude and appreciation to President Ferdinand Romualdez Marcos Jr. for appointing Amilbahar P. Amilasan Jr. as Acting Chairman/Chief Executive Officer and Member of the Board of Directors of the Al-Amanah Islamic Investment Bank of the Philippines and further congratulating Mr. Amilasan for his appointment.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To furnish a copy of this Resolution to the Office of the President Ferdinand R. Marcos Jr. and to Mr. Amilbahar P. Amilasan Jr., for their information.

**ADOPTED**, Muharram 09, 1446/July 15, 2024.
