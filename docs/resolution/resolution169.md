
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 169**

**RESOLUTION STRONGLY CONDEMNING THE KILLING OF OMAR ZINAL AND HAMZA RAUF AND URGING CONCERNED AGENCIES ESPECIALLY THE BANGSAMORO HUMAN RIGHTS COMMISSION (BHRC) TO ASSIST IN THE INVESTIGATION OF THE CASE**

WHEREAS, the Philippine Constitution and the RA 11054 known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law give high priority to right to life thus this killing incident is strongly condemned;

WHEREAS, two (2) students from Mindanao State University (MSU) Main Campus, namely Omar Maruhom Punag Zinal, 3rd year Bachelor of Science Civil Engineering and Hamza Ariong Rauf, 3rd Year Bachelor of Science in Physical Education, were shot dead on September 14, 2021 inside the said Campus;

WHEREAS, a school is considered to be the second home of the students, a place for learning and a place to be safe;

WHEREAS, this is not the first shooting incident that happened inside the campus and this must be immediately addressed and acted upon by concerned agencies;

WHEREAS, the Marawi City Local Government Unit needs to strengthen the safety and security measures in the City including the said campus;

WHEREAS, the progress of this case should be investigated by the Bangsamoro Human Rights Commission:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to strongly condemn the killing of Omar Zinal and Hamza Rauf and to concerned agencies especially the Bangsamoro Human Rights Commission to assist in the investigation of the case.

RESOLVED FURTHER, to forward this Resolution to the offices of the Bangsamoro Human Rights Commission, City Mayor of Marawi and MSU main campus Marawi, for information and appropriate action.

ADOPTED, Safar 14, 1443/September 22, 2021.
