**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 560**

**RESOLUTION AMENDING RESOLUTION NO. 538 OR A RESOLUTION ADOPTING THE CALENDAR OF SESSION FOR THE FOURTH REGULAR SESSION OF THE SECOND BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT**

**WHEREAS**, in a decision promulgated on September 30, 2025, the Supreme Court of the Philippines held that the October 13, 2025 Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) Parliamentary Elections provided under Republic Act No. 12123 cannot proceed due to the absence of a valid law apportioning the thirty-two (32) parliamentary districts in the BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, in the same decision, the Supreme Court directed the Commission on Elections to promptly proceed with its preparations and conduct elections not later than March 31, 2026;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament in Resolution No. 538, entitled *“Resolution Adopting the Calendar of Session for the Fourth Regular Session of the Second Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament”*, has set October 29, 2025, as the date of its *sine die* adjournment;

**WHEREAS**, a number of key legislative measures, including priority legislation mandated under Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the *“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao”*, are still pending before the different committees of the Parliament and need to be deliberated in the plenary:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To amend Resolution No. 538 or a Resolution Adopting the Calendar of Session for the Fourth Regular Session of the Second Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament:

5. Adjournment of Session - November 20, 2025
6. Resumption of Session - November 24, 2025
7. Adjournment of Session - November 27, 2025
8. Resumption of Session - December 1, 2025
