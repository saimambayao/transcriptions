**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 69**

**RESOLUTION ADOPTING A NEW CALENDAR OF SESSION FOR THE FIRST REGULAR SESSION OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE THE BTA RESOLUTION NO. 22**

**WHEREAS**, Section 5, Rule VI of the Parliamentary Rules, Procedures and Practices of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BTA-BARMM) provides that:

“*The BTA shall convene its regular session on the third Tuesday of the month following Eid’l Fitr and shall continue to be in session for such number of days as may be determined through a legislative calendar.*”

**WHEREAS**, Section 4, Rule XIII of the Parliamentary Rules, Procedures and Practices of the BTA-BARMM states that:

“*The BTA shall hold plenary sessions every Tuesday to Thursday for the third and fourth week of the month that the BTA is not in recess. The Speaker shall open, conduct, and close the sessions.*”

**WHEREAS**, adopting this new Calendar of Session will not only guide the Members of the BTA Parliament in the performance of their legislative and constituency-servicing functions and fast track their legislation, but will also help the Muslim BTA Members, staff and personnel in the performance of their *Jum’ah* or Friday prayer and fasting during the holy month of Ramadan;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to adopt a new Calendar of Session for the First Regular Session of the BTA Parliament, to wit:

1. Resumption of Session – March 17, 2020
2. Adjournment of Session – March 19, 2020
3. Resumption of Session – March 24, 2020
4. Adjournment of Session – March 26, 2020
5. Resumption of Session – April 14, 2020
6. Adjournment of Session – April 16, 2020
7. **MANDATORY ADJOURNMENT – April 21, 2020**

**Adopted, February 28, 2020.**
