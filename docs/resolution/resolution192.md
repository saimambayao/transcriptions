**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 192**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE PROFOUND CONDOLENCES OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT TO THE FAMILY OF THE LATE HONORABLE LINDA UNTAL AMPATUAN, 1971 CONSTITUTIONAL CONVENTION DELEGATE, FIRST REGIONAL DIRECTOR OF THE CIVIL SERVICE COMMISSION REGION XII, AND FORMER COUNCILOR OF COTABATO CITY**

**WHEREAS**, former Cotabato City Councilor Linda Untal Ampatuan, a longtime public servant, passed away on November 9, 2021;

**WHEREAS**, as one of the cityâ€™s most passionate and steadfast local leaders, the late Ampatuan was elected as a delegate to the Constitutional Convention of 1971 to represent the lone district of Cotabato, together with other influential public figures including former Sandiganbayan Justice Anacleto Badoy, Jr. and former Cotabato Governor Datu Duma Sinsuat;

**WHEREAS**, thereafter, on November 15, 1975, the strong-willed Ampatuan assumed the responsibility and was appointed as the first Regional Director of the Civil Service Commission Regional Office (CSC RO) XII, which was established in response to the clamor to regionalize public offices in the country;

**WHEREAS**, under her guidance and able leadership, the first civil service examination was administered in 1976, which paved the way to produce more eligible Bangsamoro civil servants from areas which are now part of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS**, in keeping her mandate as lawmaker, former City Councilor Ampatuan legislated measures which greatly benefited the constituents of Cotabato City;

**WHEREAS**, former Councilor Ampatuan, who was an undefeated councilor of Cotabato City, was a devoted public servant, passionate legislator, and kindhearted mentor who dedicated her life to serving the people;

**WHEREAS**, the untimely death of Former Councilor Ampatuan will be deeply grieved by his family, friends, and fellow Bangsamoro People:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to express its profound condolences to the family of the late Honorable Linda Untal Ampatuan, 1971 Constitutional Convention Delegate, first Regional Director of the Civil Service Commission Region XII, and former Councilor of Cotabato City.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that the foregoing Resolution be presented to the bereaved family of the late Honorable Ampatuan.

**ADOPTED**, Rabi Al-akhir 18, 1443/November 23, 2021.
