
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
**BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY**
BARMM Compound, Cotabato City

**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 55**

**RESOLUTION APPROVING THE ENHANCEMENT MADE BY THE COMMITTEE ON RULES ON THE APPROVED TRANSITION PLAN FOR THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO FOR THE YEAR 2019 TO 2022.**

**WHEREAS**, pursuant to Section 6, Article XVI of RA No. 11054, also known as the “Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (OLBARMM)”, within the first sixty (60) days of the transition period, the interim Chief Minister shall submit to the Bangsamoro Transition Authority a transitory plan that shall contain the proposed organizational plan, as well as the schedule for implementation therefor. The Bangsamoro Transition Authority shall, by a majority vote of all its members, approve or otherwise act on the proposed transition plan within ten (10) days upon submission by the interim Chief Minister;

**WHEREAS**, the BTA approved during the special session on June 17, 2019 the Proposed Resolution No. 20 as BTA Resolution No. 12, entitled: Resolution Approving the Transition Plan of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**WHEREAS**, the said approved BTA Transition Plan does not contain all of the organizational structure, mandates, functions, jurisdiction and similar information of the BTA, hence it requires further enhancement;

**WHEREAS**, the Committee on Rules submitted to the BTA in a plenary session on December 12, 2019 its Committee Report No. 9 recommending the approval of the enhancement in the approved BTA Transition Plan which contains the organizational structure, mandates, functions, jurisdiction and similar information of the BTA.

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, as it is hereby resolved, To approve the enhancement made by the Committee on Rules on the approved transition plan for the Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao for the year 2019 to 2022.

**Adopted**, December 27, 2019.
