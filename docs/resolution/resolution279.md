**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 279**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MR. KURAIZ ALAMADA ABUBAKAR FROM COTABATO CITY FOR RANKING TOP EIGHT (8) IN THE SEPTEMBER 2022 PHILIPPINE VETERINARIAN LICENSURE EXAMINATION, THEREBY GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, the Philippine Veterinary Medical Licensure Examination was conducted at testing centers located in Manila, Baguio, Cagayan de Oro, Cebu, Davao, Koronadal, Legazpi, and Tuguegarao last 27-29 September 2022;

**WHEREAS**, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC), on October 3, 2022, announced that out of the nine hundred twenty-eight (928) aspirants, three hundred thirty-five (335) passed the said licensure exam given by the Board of Veterinary Medicine;

**WHEREAS**, an examinee from Cotabato City, Mr. Kuraiz Alamada Abubakar, with an average score of 82.82%, clinched the rank 8 of the coveted top 10 places in the September 2022 Philippine Veterinary Medical Licensure Examination of the PRC;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Abubakar, who graduated from the University of Southern Mindanao – Kabacan at the age of 25, is a young Bangsamoro professional, and an *Iranun* hailing from Cotabato City of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM);

**WHEREAS**, his exemplary performance to become one of the topnotchers is a source of inspiration for the Bangsamoro children, youth, and young professionals not only in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region but also in the entire country which then merits this Parliament’s recognition and commendation;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) recognizes the feat of Mr. Kuraiz Alamada Abubakar for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro Community as we aim to stay true to our belief that the youth are not only our future, in as much as they are also our present:

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate and commend Mr. Kuraiz Alamada Abubakar from Cotabato City for ranking top eight (8) in the September 2022 Philippine Veterinarian Licensure Examination, thereby giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** That a copy of this Resolution be presented to Mr. Kuraiz Alamada Abubakar, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Rabi Al-Akhir 28, 1444/November 22, 2022.
