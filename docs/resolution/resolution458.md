**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 458**

**RESOLUTION RESPECTFULLY REQUESTING THE SUPREME COURT OF THE PHILIPPINES TO FACILITATE THE IMMEDIATE ORGANIZATION OF THE SHARI'AH HIGH COURT IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

**WHEREAS**, Section 6, Article VIII of the 1987 Constitution provides that “*the Supreme Court shall have administrative supervision over all courts and the personnel thereof*”, and it shall “*appoint all officials and employees of the Judiciary;*”

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Article V of Republic Act No. 11054, also known as the “*Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (OLBARMM)*”, granted the Bangsamoro Government authority, without prejudice to the general supervision of the President of the Republic of the Philippines, over the “*administration of justice;*”

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article X of the same Organic Law states that “*The Bangsamoro justice system shall be administered in accordance with the unique cultural and historical heritage of the Bangsamoro*” and that, further in Section 7, Article X thereof mandates that “*There is hereby created within the Bangsamoro territorial jurisdiction, as part of the Philippine judicial system, a Shari'ah High Court;*”

**WHEREAS**, under Section 2 thereof, the “*Shari'ah courts within the Bangsamoro territorial jurisdiction shall form part of the Philippine judicial system subject to the supervision of the Supreme Court;*”

**WHEREAS**, Section 2, Rule 4 of Internal Rules of the Supreme Court provides that the administrative functions of the Supreme Court are undertaken by the Supreme Court *En Banc*, “*assisted by the Office of the Clerk of Court in administrative matters and cases involving the Court and the collegial appellate courts, and by the Office of the Court in Administrative matters and cases involving the lower courts;*”

**WHEREAS**, Section 9, Article X of the same Organic Law provides for the composition of the Shari'ah High Court, to wit: “*the Shari'ah High Court shall be composed of five (5) Justices, including the Presiding Justice;*”

**WHEREAS**, under Section 7, Article X of the OLBARMM, the *Shari'ah High Court* in the Bangsamoro shall exercise appellate jurisdiction over *Shari'ah Courts* within and outside the region, and its decision “*shall be final and executory except on questions of law which may be raised before the Supreme Court xxx;*”

**WHEREAS**, Section 10, Article X of the OLBARMM, thus provides that the “*Justices of the Shari'ah High Court shall have the same rank, prerogatives, salaries, allowances, benefits, tenure, and privileges as the Justices of the Court of Appeals;*”

**WHEREAS**, Section 14, Article X of the OLBARMM provides explicitly that it is the “*Supreme Court shall appoint the Shari'ah Court personnel and shall have the power to discipline them;*”

**WHEREAS**, Section 7, Title V, Book III of the Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13, also known as “*Bangsamoro Administrative Code*”, provides that “*xxx the parliament may recommend to the Supreme Court, the complete organization of the Shari'ah High Court in Cotabato City, where the seat of the Bangsamoro Government is located;*”

**WHEREAS**, the creation or establishment of a *Shari'ah* appellate court has long been provided for by Republic Act No. 6734, or the first Organic Act of the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**WHEREAS**, Section 7, Article X of the OLBARMM states that “*Pending the complete organization of the Shari'ah High Court, the decisions of the Shari'ah Courts shall be appealable to the Court of Appeals;*”

**WHEREAS**, by organizing the *Shari'ah High Court*, the cases subject to review will be appropriately resolved by officer's experts in the application of the *Shari'ah* law and the Muslim Personal Laws of the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, there is an imperative need for the Supreme Court to fully organize the *Shari'ah High Court* in the BARMM to completely implement the provisions of the Bangsamoro Organic Law pertaining to the *Shari'ah* Justice System in the Bangsamoro Region;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To respectfully request the Supreme Court of the Philippines to facilitate the immediate organization of the *Shari'ah High Court* in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, That a copy of this Resolution be submitted to the Office of the Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of the Philippines, the Office of the Clerk of Court, and the Office of the Court Administrator.

**RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE**, That a copy of this Resolution be submitted to the Philippine Congress-Bangsamoro Parliament Forum for its inclusion as one of the agenda for discussion.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, To forward this Resolution to the Honorable Chief Minister Ahod Balawag Ebrahim, for favorable endorsement for the Supreme Court of the Philippines.

**ADOPTED**, Dhu Al-Qa'dah 14, 1445/May 22, 2024.
