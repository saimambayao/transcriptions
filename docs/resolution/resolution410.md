SECOND REGULAR SESSION

RESOLUTION NO. 410

RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING THE REPUBLIC OF TÜRKIYE ON ITS 100TH ANNIVERSARY

WHEREAS, on October 29, 2023, the Republic of Türkiye will celebrate its 100th anniversary as a nation, marking a significant milestone in its history;

WHEREAS, the Republic of Türkiye, under the dynamic leadership of President Recep Tayyip Erdoğan, has made remarkable strides in political, economic, and social development during its first century;

WHEREAS, the people of the Republic of Türkiye have demonstrated resilience, determination, and commitment to democratic principles, fostering peace and stability domestically and internationally;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, on behalf of the Bangsamoro people, recognizes the vital importance of international friendships and cooperation and is committed to maintaining a strong and enduring friendship with the people of the Republic of Türkiye;

WHEREAS, it is timely and fitting to acknowledge the indelible contribution of the Republic of Türkiye to the Bangsamoro peace process by joining the people of the Republic of Türkiye in the commemoration of the 100th anniversary of the Republic:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate the Republic of Türkiye on its 100th anniversary.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, To extend the warmest greetings of the Bangsamoro people to the Republic of Türkiye on the occasion of its 100th anniversary, celebrating the remarkable progress and accomplishments achieved by the Turkish Republic during this century.

RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE, That the BTA Parliament commends the leadership of President Recep Tayyip Erdoğan for his outstanding efforts for the advancement and prosperity of the Republic of Türkiye.

RESOLVED, FINALLY, To present this Resolution to the Embassy of the Republic of Türkiye as an expression of our solidarity and for information.

ADOPTED, Rabi Al-Akhir 08, 1445/October 23, 2023.
