SECOND REGULAR SESSION

**RESOLUTION NO. 348**

RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING THE COTABATO CITY SCHOOLS DIVISION OFFICE FOR BAGGING THE AWARD OF OVERALL CHAMPION IN THE 2023 BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO ATHLETIC ASSOCIATION (BARMMAA) MEET

WHEREAS, Paragraph 1, Section 19, Article XIV of the 1987 Constitution states that the State shall promote physical education and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and amateur sports, including training for international competitions, to foster self-discipline, teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;

WHEREAS, Section 13, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall recognize the vital role of the youth in nation-building, promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, and nationalism, encourage involvement in public and civic affairs, and promote mental and physical fitness through sports;

WHEREAS, Section 20 of the same article provides that the Bangsamoro educational system shall encourage and supports programs, league competitions, indigenous games, martial arts, and amateur sports including training for regional, national, and international competitions;

WHEREAS, the 2023 Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao Athletic Association (BARMMAA) Meet took place on May 25-29, 2023 at Cotabato City which brought together athletes from all across the Bangsamoro region;

WHEREAS, the BARMMAA Meet is an event that provides a platform for the Bangsamoro youth to showcase their inherent talents in various athletic disciplines and serves as a stage to exhibit their prowess in the field of sports;

WHEREAS, different sports were played in different parts of the city which included archery, arnis, athletics, badminton, baseball, boxing, chess, basketball, billiards, football, futsal, paragames, pencak silat, sepak takraw, softball, swimming, table tennis, taekwondo, volleyball, wrestling, and wushu;

WHEREAS, participating in the event were representatives from eleven (11) schools divisions, namely: Maguindanao del Sur, Maguindanao del Norte, Lanao del Sur I and II (including Marawi City Division), Basilan (including Lamitan City Division), Sulu, Tawi-Tawi, Cotabato City Division, and Special Geographic Area Division;

WHEREAS, the Cotabato City Schools Division Office (SDO) won overall champion during the Palarong BARMMAA 2023;

WHEREAS, the Cotabato City SDO hauled an impressive total of 67 gold, 54 silver, and 47 bronze medals, securing their victory, receiving a cash prize of Php150,000.00;

WHEREAS, the accomplishment of the Cotabato City SDO, as well as the participation of the other schools divisions, is a testament that Bangsamoro athletes, even at a young age, can compete and excel in their chosen endeavors. This can serve as an inspiration to future generations of Bangsamoro youth athletes to push themselves in developing their skills in order to reach their full potentials in their respective and chosen field of sports;

WHEREAS, this extraordinary feat by the Cotabato City SDO that highlights the skills and talents of young Bangsamoro Athletes is highly commendable:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate the Cotabato City Schools Division Office for bagging the award of overall champion in the 2023 Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao Athletic Association (BARMMAA) Meet.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That a copy of this Resolution be presented to the Cotabato City Schools Division Office, for information.

ADOPTED, Dhul Hijjah 04, 1444/June 22, 2023.
