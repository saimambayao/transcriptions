**RESOLUTION NO. 316**

**RESOLUTION CONVEYING THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITYâ€™S DEEPEST CONDOLENCES AND SYMPATHY TO THE BEREAVED FAMILIES OF THE VICTIMS, AND EXPRESSING ITS SOLIDARITY WITH THE PEOPLE AND GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKIYE ON THE DEVASTATING DEATH AND INJURY OF THOUSANDS OF PEOPLE AND SEVERE DAMAGE TO THE COUNTRY CAUSED BY THE CATASTROPHIC EARTHQUAKE THAT OCCURRED ON FEBRUARY 6, 2023/RAJAB 15, 1444 AH**

**WHEREAS**, on February 6, 2023/*Rajab* 15, 1444 AH, a 7.8 magnitude earthquake hit the town of Gaziantep of the Republic of Turkiye which was closely followed by numerous aftershocks including a 7.5-magnitude quake. The earthquake and its strong aftershock have caused the unfortunate death and injury of thousands of people and devastating widespread destruction throughout the country;

**WHEREAS**, according to CNBC News article published on Monday, February 13, 2023 at 7:46 AM, the combined death toll in the Republic of Turkiye and neighboring Syria exceeds 41,000 leaving other thousand injured;

**WHEREAS**, in this trying time of the Republic of Turkiye, the Bangsamoro Government reiterates the strong brotherhood it has forged with the Turkish government by acknowledging the unwavering assistance the latter has extended to the Bangsamoro people in their struggle for self-determination in the implementation of the Comprehensive Agreement on the Bangsamoro, and its continuous generosity to the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro people offer solace to their brothers and sisters in Turkiye in the midst of this great trial with the words of Allah (*S.W.T.*) that He does not burden with more than what can be handled beyond capacity, and the words of prophet Muhammad (*S.A.W.*) that never a believer is stricken with a discomfort, an illness, an anxiety, a grief or mental worry or even the pricking of a thorn but Allah (*S.W.T.*) will expiate his sins in account of his patience;

**WHEREAS**, the people of the Republic of Turkiye and its government have long been helping the Bangsamoro people for years. In turn, the Bangsamoro Government is finding ways to extend help to Turkiye in this trying time as a gesture of solidarity;

**WHEREAS**, for the overwhelming distress it is suffering, the Bangsamoro Government sends its commiseration to the people of the Republic of Turkiye and its government with the confidence that with their strong faith and resilience, they will rise from this disaster;

**WHEREAS**, the Government of the Day, together with tens of millions of people around the world, mourns the loss of so many lives, offers its deepest condolences to the bereaved loved ones of those that are departed and that may Allah (*S.W.T.*) protect the remaining people in the affected regions and ease the pain they are currently facing:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To convey its deepest condolences and sympathy to the bereaved families of the victims, and to express its solidarity with the people and government of the Republic of Turkiye on the devastating death and injury of thousands of people and severe damage to the country caused by the catastrophic earthquake that occurred on February 6, 2023/*Rajab* 15, 1444 AH.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That a copy of this Resolution be presented to the Embassy of the Republic of Turkiye in the Philippines, for information.

**ADOPTED**, *Rajab* 26, 1444/February 17, 2023.
