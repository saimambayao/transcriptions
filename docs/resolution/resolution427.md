**RESOLUTION NO. 427**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT ATTORNEY SHA ELIJAH B. DUMAMA-ALBA FOR BEING DESIGNATED AS THE NEW MINISTER OF THE MINISTRY OF THE INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT, AND THE OTHER MEMBERS OF PARLIAMENT WHO WERE DESIGNATED AND RE-DESIGNATED AS MEMBERS OF THE CABINET**

**WHEREAS,** on July 26, 2018, President Rodrigo Roa Duterte signed into law a landmark legislation, Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the “Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;”

**WHEREAS,** Section 2, Article IV of Republic Act No. 11054 provides that “*in the exercise of its right to self-governance, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region is free to pursue its political, economic, social, and cultural development as provided for in the Organic Law;*”

**WHEREAS,** Section 3, Article V of the above-mentioned Republic Act provides that “*the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise the power expressly granted, those necessarily implied therefrom, as well as powers necessary, appropriate, or incidental for its efficient and effective governance and those which are essential to the promotion of general welfare. Within its territorial jurisdiction, the Bangsamoro Government shall ensure and support, among other things, the preservation and enrichment of culture, promote health and safety, enhance the right of the people to a balanced ecology, encourage and support the development of appropriate and self-reliant scientific and technological capabilities, improve public morals, enhance economic prosperity and social justice, promote full employment among its residents, maintain peace and order, and preserve the comfort and convenience of its inhabitants;*”

**WHEREAS,** Section 4, Article VII of the same Republic Act states that “*the executive function and authority shall be exercised by the Cabinet which shall be headed by a Chief Minister, and the Chief Minister shall be elected by majority vote of all the Members of the Parliament;*”

**WHEREAS,** the same section further provides that “*the Chief Minister shall nominate two (2) Deputy Chief Ministers who shall be elected by the Parliament, as provided for in Section 35 of this Article, and appoint the members of the Cabinet, majority of whom shall come from the Parliament;*”

**WHEREAS,** the Chief Minister issued Memorandum Circular No. 189 s. 2023 directing all senior officials to tender their courtesy resignation in order for him to have a free hand to reorganize his cabinet to further improve the service delivery in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM);

**WHEREAS,** Chief Minister Ahod B. Ebrahim designated Member of Parliament (MP) Atty. Sha Elijah Dumama-Alba as the new Minister of the Ministry of the Interior and Local Government (MILG), replacing former Minister Naguib Sinarimbo;

**WHEREAS,** MP Atty. Dumama-Alba earned her Bachelor of Arts in Public Administration at the University of the Philippines in Diliman, finished her Law degree at San Beda Mendiola in 2007, and thereafter admitted to the Philippine Bar in 2008;

**WHEREAS,** she is an Asia Foundation Development Fellow alumna, placed 3rd in the Special Shariah Bar Examinations in 2018, was also part of the 2019 Obama Leaders Class in Asia, Tatler Asia’s Gen.T List 2020 Leaders of Tomorrow, alongside being an Asia Peace Innovators Fellow 2020, and a United States International Visitors Leadership Program grantee in 2021;

**WHEREAS,** she was a member of the Coordinating Team for Transition (CT4T) representing the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF), which drafted the Transition Plan from Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) to BARMM from 2018 to 2019;

**WHEREAS,** she served as Attorney VI of the Civil Service Commission – ARMM before being appointed as Bangsamoro Attorney General in 2019 and Member of Parliament in 2022;

**WHEREAS,** she is a member of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), the interim government in the BARMM during the transition period, serves as the Floor Leader of the Bangsamoro Parliament, and as the Floor Leader, she is an ex-officio member of all other Bangsamoro Parliament committees;

**WHEREAS,** she is the Chairperson of the Bangsamoro Parliament’s Committee on Rules and the Vice-Chairperson of the Statutory Committee on Bangsamoro Justice System;

**WHEREAS,** she was the Bangsamoro Attorney General of BARMM since its creation in March 2019 and had been designated as Focal for Justice Programs in BARMM. Her office has spearheaded initiatives in improving and strengthening the Shari’ah as a Justice System, as well as promoting the Tri-Justice Systems in BARMM;

**WHEREAS,** she is also the head of the Secretariat of the Intergovernmental Relations Body (IGRB), a mechanism for cooperation and coordination between the National Government and the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS,** MP Atty. Dumama-Alba assumed leadership on one of the biggest ministries of the Bangsamoro Government, the Ministry of the Interior and Local Government (MILG) on December 7, 2023;

**WHEREAS,** the MILG is responsible in strengthening the governance capabilities of Local Government Units (LGUs), deals with the LGU’s within the jurisdiction of the Bangsamoro Government, and shall retain supervisory functions on police matters within LGUs;

**WHEREAS,** the BTA, as the interim government of BARMM, extends its commendations and salutations to the newly appointed Minister of the MILG;

**WHEREAS,** MP Atty. Anna Tarhata S. Basman was designated as the new Attorney-General, replacing former Attorney-General Atty. Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba;

**WHEREAS,** MP Dr. Susana S. Anayatin was designated as the new Executive Director of the Office of the Settler Communities, replacing former Executive Director Prof. Edgar Ramirez;

**WHEREAS,** MP Said M. Sheik was designated as the new Assistant Cabinet Secretary and Program Manager of the Marawi Rehabilitation Program, replacing former Assistant Cabinet Secretary MP Atty. Anna Tarhata S. Basman;

**WHEREAS,** the Chief Minister also re-designated some MPs to include Minister Mohagher M. Iqbal of the Ministry of Basic, Higher and Technical Education (MBHTE), Minister Ar. Eduard U. Guerra of the Ministry of Public Works (MPW), Minister Atty. Paisalin P. Tago, CPA of the Ministry of Transportation and Communication (MOTC), Minister Engr. Aida T. Silongan of the Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST), and Minister Hussein P. Munoz of Ministry of Public Order and Safety (MPOS); and

**WHEREAS,** the BTA hopes for the new Minister and the re-designated Ministers to continue steering their respective ministry to greater heights:

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To commend and congratulate Member of Parliament Attorney Sha Elijah B. Dumama-Alba for being designated as the new Minister of the Ministry of Interior and Local Government, and the other Members of Parliament who were designated and re-designated as members of the cabinet.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** To present copies of this Resolution to the Minister of the MILG and other Honorable Members of Parliament who were re-designated, for their information.

**ADOPTED,** *Rajab 04, 1445/January 16, 2024.*
