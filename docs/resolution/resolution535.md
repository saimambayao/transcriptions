**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 535**

**RESOLUTION URGING THE GOVERNMENT AND THE MORO ISLAMIC LIBERATION FRONT PEACE IMPLEMENTING PANELS TO FAST-TRACK THE DEPLOYMENT OF JOINT PEACE AND SECURITY TEAM, ESPECIALLY IN AREAS WHERE THERE IS A DISTURBANCE OF PEACE AND ORDER**

**WHEREAS**, Section 5, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that *“The maintenance of peace and order, the protection of life, liberty and property, and promotion of the general welfare are essential for the enjoyment by all the people of the blessings of democracy;”*

**WHEREAS**, Section 17, Article XIV of the said Constitution states that *“The State shall recognize, respect, and protect the rights of indigenous cultural communities to preserve and develop their cultures, traditions, and institutions. It shall consider these rights in the formulation of national plans and policies;”*

**WHEREAS**, Section 9, Article IV of the Republic Act No. 11054, also known as the *“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao”*, states that *“The Bangsamoro Government shall recognize and promote the rights of non-Moro indigenous peoples within the framework of the Constitution and national laws;”*

**WHEREAS**, at least one hundred fourteen (114) non-Moro ethnic Teduray in a tribal enclave in Sitio Nunagen at the border of Barangays Blanting and Marader in Talayan, Maguindanao del Sur fled their homes on the night of July 26, 2023 when armed men stormed their village, burning houses and the tribal worship hall;

**WHEREAS**, the hapless and unarmed Tedurays were temporarily relocated to the hinterland Barangay Biarong, South Upi, Maguindanao del Sur, leaving their livestock and harvestable rice and corn crops behind;

**WHEREAS**, it is imperative that the high incidence of fires and other crimes involving destruction be prevented to protect and preserve the social, economic, and political stability of the Bangsamoro territory:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To urge the government and the Moro Islamic Liberation Front Peace Implementing Panels to fast-track the deployment of Joint Peace and Security Team, especially in areas where there is a disturbance of peace and order.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To forward this Resolution to the Government of the Philippines and the Moro Islamic Liberation Front Peace Implementing Panels, for information and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED**, *Sha’ban* 14, 1446/February 13, 2025.
