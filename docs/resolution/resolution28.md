**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 28**

**RESOLUTION AMENDING RESOLUTION NO. 9, PROVIDING FOR CONTINUOUS MAINTENANCE AND OTHER OPERATING EXPENSES (MOOE) ALLOCATION TO THE MEMBERS OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA)**

**WHEREAS,** the previous Regional Legislative Assembly (RLA) appropriated the 2019 local funds of the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**WHEREAS,** part of the appropriated budget is the allocation for the Maintenance and Other Operating Expenses for the members of the Regional Legislative Assembly;

**WHEREAS,** the 24 elected officials of the previous Regional Legislative Assembly have lapsed last June 30, 2019;

**WHEREAS,** Bangsamoro Transition Authority adopted Resolution No. 9, series of 2019 last April 24, 2019 particularly “Resolution Utilizing the Unexpended 2019 Local Funds Allocated to the Defunct Regional Legislative Assembly (RLA) for the Month of March and April for the Initial Maintenance and Other Operating Expenses (MOOE) of the Offices of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, in the Amount of Sixty Three Million Pesos (P63,000,000.00).”

**WHEREAS,** there is a need for continuing support to the Bangsamoro Transition Authority for its monthly operations, community activities, and other engagements to be charged against the local fund of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and other available national fund;

**WHEREAS,** there is a need to amend Resolution No. 9 to ensure continuing MOOE allocation for the Bangsamoro Transition Authority;

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved, To amend BTA Resolution No. 9 to ensure continuous MOOE allocation of the 80-member of the BTA Parliament;

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the Ministry of Finance, Budget and Management shall prepare the necessary computations and supporting documents;

**RESOLVED FINALLY,** that copy of this Resolution be furnished to the Office of the Chief Minister.

**Adopted,** August 30, 2019.
