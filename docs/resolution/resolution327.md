
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
**BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT**
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 327**

**RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING MP SAID Z. SALENDAB TO ACT AS WALI OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT FOR PURPOSES OF OPENING THE SECOND REGULAR SESSION OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT**

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article VIII of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law, provides for the appointment of the *Wali*, who shall serve as the ceremonial head of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, Paragraph 2, Section 1, Title II, Book III of Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Administrative Code, provides that, “*The Parliament shall issue a resolution reflecting its consensus on the selection of the Wali from a list of eminent residents of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region submitted by the Council of Leaders*”;

**WHEREAS**, His Eminence Sheikh Khalipha Usman Nando (Allahu Yarham) served as the first Wali of the Bangsamoro from March 29, 2019, until his passing on February 5, 2023;

**WHEREAS**, Section 5, Title II, Book III of the Bangsamoro Administrative Code provides that, “*In case of death, permanent disability, removal from office, or resignation of the Wali, the Parliament shall appoint a new Wali within thirty (30) days from vacancy*”;

**WHEREAS**, during an all Members of the Parliament caucus on May 11, 2023, a consensus has been reached to authorize the Honorable Chief Minister Ahod B. Ebrahim to designate a person of the latter’s choosing to act as *Wali* of the Bangsamoro Government for purposes of opening the Second Regular Session of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, the Honorable Chief Minister has chosen MP Said Z. Salendab to act as such for purposes of the opening of the Second Regular Session on May 15, 2023:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the BTA Parliament, To authorize MP Said Z. Salendab to act as *Wali* of the Bangsamoro Government for purposes of opening the Second Regular Session of the BTA Parliament.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That this authorization shall cease to exist upon the completion of the performance of the specific task for which it is granted.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, That a copy of this Resolution be forwarded to MP Said Z. Salendab, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Shawwal 24, 1444/May 15, 2023.
