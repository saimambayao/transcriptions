**RESOLUTION NO. 278**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING THE PARTICIPANTS FROM THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM) IN THE 2022 SMART/MVP SPORTS FOUNDATION NATIONAL INTERSCHOOL TAEKWONDO CHAMPIONSHIPS FOR BRINGING HOME TWENTY (20) NATIONAL MEDALS TO THE REGION**

**WHEREAS**, it is the Bangsamoro Government’s policy to promote physical education programs and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and amateur sports, including training for regional, national and international competitions, to foster discipline, teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;

**WHEREAS**, the 2022 Smart/MVP Sports Foundation National Interschool Taekwondo Championships was organized by the Philippine Taekwondo Association (PTA) on October 1-2, 2022 at the Ayala Malls, Manila Bay, Pasay City;

**WHEREAS**, the competition, supported by Philippine Sports Commission, Philippine Olympics Committee, and Milo, comprises two categories- the Kyurogi (sparring) and the Poomsae (form);

**WHEREAS**, Taekwondo as an art of self-defense that originated in Korea is recognized as one of the oldest forms of martial arts in the world;

**WHEREAS**, about two thousand (2,000) students from schools affiliated with the PTA all over the country participated in the biggest school-based tournament in the country;

**WHEREAS**, students from ten (10) different schools in BARMM trained by the Heart of the Champion (HOTC) Team won twenty (20) medals in the said national taekwondo competition;

**WHEREAS**, the team bagged six (6) gold medals, two (2) silver, and twelve (12) bronze;
