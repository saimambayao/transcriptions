## 1. Overview

### Title
*Strategic Planning Guidebook for the Bangsamoro Government*

### Subtitle
*A Practitioner's Guide to the 10-Point Strategic Planning Framework for Ministries, Offices, and Agencies*

### Purpose
A practitioner's guidebook that walks BARMM Ministries, Offices, and Agencies (MOAs) through the complete strategic planning cycle using the 10-Point Strategic Planning Framework. Not a training manual — a working reference that Planning Officers open on their desk and follow to produce an actual strategic plan, complete with fill-in templates, step-by-step instructions, worked examples, and quality checklists.

### Tier
Comprehensive (~80,000-100,000 words, ~250-350 pages including templates)

### Key Deliverable
Each chapter produces a modular working document (template). By Chapter 12, all 10 documents consolidate into a complete **MOA Strategic Plan** submission package.

## 2. Audience

| Level | Who | How They Use This Guidebook |
|-------|-----|-----------------------------|
| **Primary** | Planning Officers, Budget Officers, M&E Officers | Follow step-by-step to draft each planning component; fill in templates |
| **Secondary** | Ministers, Executive Directors, Deputy Ministers, Division Chiefs | Read "For Decision-Makers" callouts; understand what to approve at each stage |
| **Tertiary** | BPDA staff | Reference for reviewing and certifying MOA plans against the framework |

## 3. Scope

### What This Guidebook Covers
The complete 10-Point Strategic Planning Framework:
1. Legal Alignment
2. BDP Alignment
3. Priority Agenda Alignment
4. Stakeholder Analysis
5. Strategy Analysis (Vision, Mission, Values, Strategic Objectives)
6. Theory of Change
7. PPA (Program/Project/Activity) Planning
8. Resource Planning
9. Annual Planning (AWP, AFP, APP)
10. Results-Based Monitoring & Evaluation

Plus: introduction, consolidation/submission, and reference appendices.

### What This Guidebook Does NOT Cover
- Budget preparation forms and BBP process (planned BAA 84 Budget System guidebook)
- Legislative drafting (Bill Drafting Guidebook)
- Day-to-day program/project management and lifecycle (potential future guidebook)
- The e-Bangsamoro digital platform (no software UI references)

### Contextualization
Heavily BARMM-specific throughout. Every example uses real BARMM ministries, real BDP goals, real BAAs, and actual 12-Point Priority Agenda items. Templates pre-filled with BARMM legal hierarchy and governance structure.

## 4. Chapter Structure

### Standard Chapter Anatomy (Ch 2-11)

Each chapter follows this consistent internal structure:

1. **Purpose** — what this planning point accomplishes and why it matters
2. **Key Concepts** — definitions and framework elements with BARMM legal basis
3. **Step-by-Step Instructions** — numbered procedures the Planning Officer follows
4. **Worked Example** — a real BARMM ministry walking through the steps
5. **For Decision-Makers** — callout box: what leaders need to decide, review, or approve
6. **Common Pitfalls** — mistakes MOAs actually make, with how to avoid them
7. **Template** — the fill-in working document this chapter produces
8. **Quality Checklist** — self-review criteria before moving to the next point

### Chapter 1: Introduction

Covers:
- What strategic planning means in the BARMM context
- The 10-Point Framework overview (visual diagram)

- Who does what (role matrix: Planning Officer, Budget Officer, M&E Officer, Minister/ED)

## 7. Writing Approach

### Voice and Tone
- Direct, instructional, second-person ("You will now...", "Complete the following...")
- Same professional tone as the CSW and Supervision guidebooks
- Practitioner language — not academic theory, not software documentation

### Translation Method
The source material is software architecture. The guidebook translates this into human process:
- "Ambient AI suggests BDP alignment" → "Review the BDP Goal Reference Table (Appendix B) and identify which goals your mandate supports"
- "API validates stakeholder completeness" → "Use the Quality Checklist to verify you have identified stakeholders from all four categories"
- Database schemas → fill-in template tables
- Validation rules → checklist items

### Composing Skills
1. `/bangsamoro` — invoked first for domain context (officials, governance, BOL)
2. `/guidebook-writer` — primary composition skill
3. `/citation` — invoked after each chapter for Feliciano 10th Edition footnotes
4. `/fact-checker` — runs after completion (names, BAA numbers, BOL articles)

### Footnote Style
Feliciano 10th Edition format, consistent with all other BARMM guidebooks. Citations for BOL articles, BAAs, BDP chapters, executive issuances, and national laws.

## 9. What Makes This Guidebook Unique

1. **Multi-document output package** — the only guidebook that produces 10 modular templates rolling up into one consolidated plan
2. **Derived from original software architecture** — the 10-Point Framework was designed by the author for e-Bangsamoro; this guidebook is the methodology extracted and published independently
3. **Bridges mandate to output** — fills the gap between "what BARMM law requires" (legal mandates, BDP alignment) and "what MOAs actually submit" (strategic plans). No published guide currently exists for this in BARMM.
4. **Dual-audience design** — speaks to practitioners (Planning Officers) while surfacing decision points for leaders (Ministers, EDs) through structured callouts
