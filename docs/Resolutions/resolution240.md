**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 240**

**RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING ALL PARLIAMENTARY AND STATUTORY COMMITTEES OF THE BTA PARLIAMENT TO CONTINUE COMMITTEE WORK DURING THE MANDATORY BREAK FOR 2022**

**WHEREAS**, the remaining priority Codes mandated by the Bangsamoro Organic Law (RA 11054) and other important bills and resolutions are pending before the Bangsamoro Transition Authority and its Parliamentary and Statutory Committees;

**WHEREAS**, the Third Regular Session of the Parliament ends on March 24, 2022 as the Parliament goes on mandatory adjournment coinciding with the coming of Noble Month of Ramadan in April;

**WHEREAS**, the continuing deliberation of pending legislative measures is essential to the expediency of their finalization, approval, and implementation:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to authorize all Parliamentary and Statutory Committees to continue committee work during the mandatory break for 2022.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to forward the foregoing Resolution to the Office of the Chief Minister, the Office of the Parliament Speaker, and all Members of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, for information and guidance.
