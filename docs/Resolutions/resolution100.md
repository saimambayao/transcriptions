SECOND REGULAR SESSION

RESOLUTION NO. 100

RESOLUTION APPROVING THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) PARLIAMENT BUDGET FOR 2021

WHEREAS, under Section 4, Rule VII of BTA Resolution No. 6, the Committee on Accounts and Audit (CAA) exercises “jurisdiction over all matters relating to the income, revenues and internal auditing of the funds for all the expenditures and activities of the BTA, and the other ministries, agencies and offices of the Bangsamoro Government.” The same provision also confers CAA with “jurisdiction to examine and scrutinize the income, revenues, and the expenditure plans of all government ministries, bureaus and agencies of the Bangsamoro Government, including the audit thereof”;

WHEREAS, pursuant to its mandate, the CAA held a committee meeting on December 22, 2020 to deliberate on the Proposed 2021 Budget of the BTA Parliament;

WHEREAS, after due deliberation, the CAA submits its Committee Report No. 51 to the BTA, dated December 22, 2020, recommending the approval of the Proposed 2021 Budget of the BTA Parliament;

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to approve the 2021 Budget of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament.

ADOPTED, December 23, 2020.
