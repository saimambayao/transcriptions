**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 530**

**RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE OFFICE OF THE CHIEF MINISTER, HONORABLE AHOD BALAWAG EBRAHIM, TO GRANT INFLATION RELIEF ASSISTANCE OF AT LEAST TEN THOUSAND PESOS (PHP 10,000.00) TO ALL EMPLOYEES OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT, REGARDLESS OF EMPLOYMENT STATUS, INCLUDING PERSONS ENGAGED UNDER CONTRACTS OF SERVICE AND JOB ORDERS, SUBJECT TO THE AVAILABILITY OF FUNDS**

**WHEREAS**, the Holy Month of *Ramadhan* is expected to commence on or after *Sha’ban* 29, 1446/February 28, 2025;

**WHEREAS**, *Ramadhan* is a sacred and significant period for Muslims worldwide, observed with fasting, prayers, and communal reflection, bringing spiritual and communal blessings;

**WHEREAS**, the observance of *Ramadhan* entails additional financial obligations, including increased spending on food and other essential goods, which are further exacerbated by inflation and seasonal price surges;

**WHEREAS**, the inhabitants of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region have been plagued by a rise in the prices of basic commodities in the region;

**WHEREAS**, the Philippine Statistics Authority – Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao released the 2024 Annual Average Inflation Rates on January 7, 2025. The report highlighted a decline in the overall average inflation rate from 6.3% in 2023 to 3.9% in 2024. However, several essential commodity groups experienced inflationary pressures. The average inflation rate for health in the region increased to 2.1% in 2024 from only 0.8% in 2023. The inflation rate for transport services increased to 3.1% in 2024 from 0.6% in 2023. The inflation rate of housing, water, electricity, gas, and other fuels increased to 3.4% in 2024 from 3.0% in 2023. Finally, the inflation rate for education services increased to 6.8% in 2024 from 5.0% in 2023;

**WHEREAS**, granting additional financial assistance to employees of the Bangsamoro Government will help mitigate the financial burden faced by public sector workers, particularly those in lower income brackets, during this period;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government has previously provided similar financial assistance during the month of *Ramadhan*, demonstrating its commitment to the welfare of its employees and constituents:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To request the Office of the Chief Minister, Honorable Ahod Balawag Ebrahim, to grant inflation relief assistance of at least Ten Thousand Pesos (PHP 10,000.00) to all employees of the Bangsamoro Government, regardless of employment status, including persons engaged under contracts of service and job orders, subject to the availability of funds.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, as much as possible, the grant of inflation relief assistance shall be released before the end of March 2025.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, To forward this Resolution to the Honorable Chief Minister, Ahod Balawag Ebrahim, for his information and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED**, *Sha’ban* 12, 1446/February 11, 2025.
