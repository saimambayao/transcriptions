**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 513**

**RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE GOVERNMENT OF THE DAY FOR THE IMMEDIATE ACTIVATION OF THE SHARI’AH PUBLIC ASSISTANCE OFFICE, BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

**WHEREAS**, Section 13, Title V of the Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13, otherwise known as *“Bangsamoro Administrative Code”*, provides: *“There is hereby created a Shari’ah Public Assistance Office, to be headed by a director assisted by two (2) counselors who are members of the Philippine Shari’ah Bar. They shall be assisted by an immediate staff;”*

**WHEREAS**, paragraph four (4) of the same section states that *“The Shari’ah Public Assistance Office is hereby established in each of the Shari’ah judicial districts to provide legal assistance to poor or indigent party litigants;”*

**WHEREAS**, paragraph five (5) of the same section stipulates: *“That in the absence of an appropriate ministry or office to which the Shari’ah Public Assistance Office may be attached, it shall, in the interim, be attached to the office of the Chief Minister;”*

**WHEREAS**, it is believed that the immediate activation of the Shari’ah Public Assistance Office (SPAO) in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) will help our poor constituents who cannot afford to hire a Shari’ah counselor to appear before the Shari’ah courts;

**WHEREAS**, the activation of the said office is no difference from other offices created by the BARMM that are now operational;
