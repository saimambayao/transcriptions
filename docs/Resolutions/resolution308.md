**RESOLUTION NO. 308**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST GRATITUDE TO HIS EXCELLENCY, PRESIDENT FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., FOR THE CONSTITUTION OF THE MARAWI COMPENSATION BOARD, AND CONGRATULATING ITS DULY APPOINTED CHAIRPERSON AND MEMBERS**

**WHEREAS**, on April 13, 2022, Republic Act No. 11696, entitled: "An Act Providing Compensation for the Loss or Destruction of Properties and Loss of Lives as a result of the 2017 Marawi Siege, and Appropriating Funds Therefor", otherwise known as the *Marawi Siege Victims Compensation Act of 2022*, was signed into law to provide compensation or reparation to the internally-displaced persons (IDPs) of the 2017 Marawi Siege whose damaged or demolished properties and possessions cannot be recovered;

**WHEREAS**, the *Marawi Siege Victims Compensation Act of 2022* also provides for the creation and composition of the Marawi Compensation Board (hereinafter referred to as the "Board"), an independent and quasi-judicial body primarily mandated to receive, evaluate, process, investigate, and approve applications for claims, and promptly disburse compensation to qualified claimants, and which Board shall be composed of a Chairperson and eight (8) other members who shall be appointed by the President;

**WHEREAS**, on January 13, 2023, the Office of the President appointed the following officials to the Board:

**Chairperson:** Maisara C. Dandamun-Latiph

**Members:** Sittie Aliyyah Lomondot Adiong
Dalomabi Lao Bula
Mustapha C. Dimaampao
Jamaica Lamping Dimaporo
Mabandes Sumnad Diron, Jr.
Moslemen T. Macarambon, Sr.
Romaisa Lomantong Mamutuk
Nasser Macapado Tabao

**WHEREAS**, the role of the Board is crucial to the successful and prompt implementation of the provisions of the *Marawi Siege Victims Compensation Act of 2022*, which shall meaningfully contribute to the recovery and rehabilitation of Marawi City and the social healing of the IDPs:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To express its deepest gratitude to the His Excellency, President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr., for the constitution of the Marawi Compensation Board.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament extends its congratulations and well wishes to the duly appointed Chairperson and Members of the Marawi Compensation Board;

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, That the foregoing Resolution be forwarded to the Office of the President of the Philippines, to the Office of the Chief Minister, and to the Marawi Compensation Board, for information.

**ADOPTED**, *Rajab 02, 1444/January 24, 2023.*
