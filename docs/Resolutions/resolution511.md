**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 511**

**RESOLUTION URGING THE INTERGOVERNMENTAL INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT BOARD, TO INCLUDE IN ITS PRIORITY AGENDA THE REHABILITATION AND UPGRADING OF THE NARCISO RAMOS HIGHWAY IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION**

**WHEREAS**, the 154-kilometer Narciso Ramos Highway which cuts across mainland Mindanao, links the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region to the other regions in Mindanao and beyond. Thus, its repair and maintenance is within the responsibility of the National Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH), as prescribed under Section 37, Article XIII of the Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the *“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;”*

**WHEREAS**, the Narciso Ramos National Highway needs the urgent attention of the concerned departments for its immediate repair, rehabilitation, and upgrading considering its crucial role in accelerating the movement of the traveling public and the transport of goods and services from various production points to market outlets in Mindanao;

**WHEREAS**, Section 6, Article VI of the same Organic Law states that the Intergovernmental Infrastructure Development Board is tasked to coordinate and synchronize the National and Bangsamoro Infrastructure Development Plan;

**WHEREAS**, the DPWH Secretary and the Minister of Ministry of Public Works (MPW) are members of the Board;

**WHEREAS**, with the joint action of the National DPWH and the Bangsamoro MPW, they can anticipate faster decision-making and action for the urgent repair, rehabilitation, and upgrading of the Narciso Ramos Highway within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao jurisdiction:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To urge the Intergovernmental Infrastructure Development Board, to include in its priority agenda the rehabilitation and upgrading of the Narciso Ramos Highway in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To forward this Resolution to the Intergovernmental Infrastructure Development Board, for consideration and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED**, Rabi Al-Akhir 21, 1446/October 24, 2024.
