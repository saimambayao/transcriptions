**RESOLUTION NO. 205**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING HAMID SIMPAL SALIK FOR BRINGING HONOR AND PRESTIGE TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE BY PLACING NINTH (9TH) IN THE 2021 LICENSURE EXAMINATIONS FOR TEACHERS**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority expresses its salutations and commendations to Hamid Simpal Salik for being the ninth (9th) placer in the 2021 Licensure Examination for Teachers given last September 2021;

**WHEREAS**, only 57.76% or 10,318 out of 17, 863 examinees, passed the said examination and Salik garnered a rating of 91.40%;

**WHEREAS**, Salik is a Cum Laude graduate of Cotabato State University with a degree of Bachelor of Secondary Education-Major in Mathematics;

**WHEREAS**, the news of another Bangsamoro topping the licensure examination brings honor and prestige to the region and inspires our youth;

**WHEREAS**, it shows that despite the difficulties brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic, the Bangsamoro youth can excel in their chosen fields and careers:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to commend and congratulate Hamid Simpal Salik for bringing honor and prestige to the Bangsamoro people by placing ninth (9th) in the 2021 Licensure Examinations for Teachers.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to present the foregoing Resolution to Hamid Simpal Salik, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Jumada Al-Akhirah 23, 1443/January 27, 2022.
