**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 197**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING RAJIV NOOH DALAGAN AMIL FOR TOPPING THE 2021 PHILIPPINE CIVIL ENGINEERING LICENSURE EXAMINATION, AND FOR GIVING HONOR AND PRIDE TO THE BANGSAMORO REGION**

WHEREAS, on 25 November 2021, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced the results of the November 2021 Civil Engineering Licensure Examination;

WHEREAS, with an impressive rating of 93.25 percent, Ateneo de Davao University graduate Rajiv Nooh Dalagan Amil obtained the examination’s highest mark, besting 6,474 other examinees out of which 2,374 or 36.67%, passed the exams;

WHEREAS, Amil completed his secondary education at the Mindanao State University-Tawi-Tawi College of Technology and Oceanography;

WHEREAS, Amil hails from Tawi-tawi whose parents are both public servants. Amil’s mother is a Certified Public Accountant and his father is a Civil Engineer;

WHEREAS, Amil graduated from the Ateneo De Davao University in April 2019 and went to Cebu in June 2019 to review at the Megareview Tutorial Center, which took him as one of its scholars;

WHEREAS, Amil serves as a good example and inspiration for young people to emulate and to excel in their chosen fields of endeavors;

WHEREAS, the outstanding achievement of Engr. Amil is a source of inspiration for the young professionals and aspirants in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and the entire nation which merits this Parliament’s recognition and commendation:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to commend and congratulate Rajiv Nooh Dalagan Amil for topping the 2021 Philippine Civil Engineering Licensure Examination, and for giving honor and pride to the Bangsamoro Region.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, that Engr. Amil be granted One Hundred Thousand Pesos (P100,000.00) monetary gift for the same, to be charged against any available fund of the Bangsamoro Government.

RESOLVED, FINALLY, to forward the foregoing Resolution to Engr. Rajiv Nooh Dalagan Amil, for information.

ADOPTED, Jumada Al-Ula 17, 1443/December 22, 2021.
