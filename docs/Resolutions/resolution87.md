**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 87**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING PROFOUND SYMPATHY AND MOST SINCERE CONDOLENCES TO THE FAMILY OF THE LATE HONORABLE UNDERSECRETARY DIMASANGCAY ALANGADI PUNDATO OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENTIAL ADVISER ON THE PEACE PROCESS (OPAPP)**

WHEREAS, Dimasangcay Alangadi Pundato was a member of the first batch of foreign-trained fighters, otherwise known as the “Top 90” of the Moro National Liberation Front (MNLF) who fought against the abuses of state forces during the Martial Law regime;

WHEREAS, under the administrations of the late President Corazon C. Aquino and former President Fidel V. Ramos, Pundato served as the Executive Director of the now-defunct Office on Muslim Affairs (OMA) and was instrumental in promoting the development and welfare of Muslim Filipinos;

WHEREAS, during the administrations of former Presidents Gloria Macapagal Arroyo and Benigno C. Aquino III, Pundato served as the Undersecretary on Muslim Affairs for the Office of the Presidential Adviser on the Peace Process (OPPAP) where he actively engaged in conflict resolution and peacebuilding activities;

WHEREAS, Pundato played an active role in the peace front supporting the passage of the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) in the 17th Congress of the Philippines paving the way for the establishment of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM);

WHEREAS, Pundato dedicated his life to the service of the Bangsamoro, both as a revolutionary and an exemplary public servant. He was a staunch advocate for peace, justice, and meaningful autonomy in the Bangsamoro;

WHEREAS, on August 13, 2020, the Bangsamoro lost a brave revolutionary leader and dedicated public servant when former Undersecretary Pundato joined our Creator at his home in Marawi City.

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to express profound sympathy and most sincere condolences to the family of the late Honorable Undersecretary Dimasangcay Alangadi Pundato of the Office of the Presidential Adviser on the Peace Process (OPPAP).

RESOLVED, FURTHER, to forward the foregoing Resolution to the bereaved family of the late Undersecretary Dimasangcay Alangadi Pundato.

**Adopted, August 27, 2020.**
