FIRST REGULAR SESSION

RESOLUTION NO. 304

RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE SINCERE APPRECIATION AND GRATITUDE OF THE BANGSAMORO TO SENATORS IMEE ROMUALDEZ MARCOS AND ATTY. GILBERT TEODORO FOR PROVIDING DISASTER RELIEF AND ASSISTANCE TO THE PEOPLE OF THE BANGSAMORO WHO SUFFERED DAMAGE CAUSED BY SEVERE TROPICAL STORM PAENG

WHEREAS, on October 26, 2022, a tropical storm known as Paeng hit the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), resulting in massive destruction, loss of life, and damage to agriculture and critical infrastructure due to flooding, and landslides;

WHEREAS, as reported by the Bangsamoro Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council (BDRRMC), tropical storm Paeng affected approximately 570,285 individuals in the region, with 53 fatalities, 12 missing persons, and 38 injured persons;

WHEREAS, tropical storm Paeng damaged 371 houses and partially damaged 480 others in the BARMM. The storm also caused damage to infrastructure and agriculture, with losses estimated at almost Six-Hundred Million Pesos;

WHEREAS, on October 29, 2022, Bangsamoro Chief Minister Ahod Ebrahim declared a state of calamity in the Bangsamoro Region due to the heavy damage and destruction brought about by tropical storm Paeng;

WHEREAS, despite the efforts of different ministries and offices of the BARMM to address the difficulties of the people affected by tropical storm Paeng, the limitation of time and additional manpower required the intervention of national government offices and personalities;

WHEREAS, Senator Imee R. Marcos and Atty. Gilbert Teodoro immediately visited the typhoon-stricken areas of the Bangsamoro Region, including Cotabato Province where the BARMM Special Geographic Area consisting of 65 barangays is situated, to bring the relief goods and services the people desperately needed;

WHEREAS, to relieve the hardship of the Bangsamoro people, Senator Marcos and Atty. Teodoro expressed their support and solidarity with the affected families;

WHEREAS, Senator Marcos and Atty. Teodoro made sure that said basic relief goods and services were delivered to the Bangsamoro region as proof of their untiring support and commitment were essential in providing much-needed assistance to those in need:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, as representative of the BARMM, To extend its heartfelt gratitude and appreciation to Senator Imee R. Marcos and Atty. Gilbert Teodoro for their immediate response and assistance to the people of the Bangsamoro who suffered damage caused by Severe Tropical Storm Paeng.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That copy of this foregoing Resolution be given to the Office of Senator Imee R. Marcos and Atty. Gilbert Teodoro, for information.

ADOPTED, Jumada Al-Akhirah 26, 1444/January 19, 2023
