**RESOLUTION NO. 400**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING HEBBAN TALIB TAWANTAWAN FOR BEING THE TOPNOTCHER IN THE SEPTEMBER 2023 CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANT LICENSURE EXAMINATION**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority has consistently given commendation to remarkable Bangsamoros for their outstanding achievements in various fields and exemplary body of work which bring great honor to the Region and contribute to the positive transformation of the Bangsa;

**WHEREAS**, Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination (CPALE) is one of the most grueling and toughest board examinations in the Philippines administered by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC);

**WHEREAS**, on October 6, 2023, the PRC announced that a total of 2,740 out of 8,734 successfully passed the September 2023 CPALE constituting 31.25% overall passing rate;

**WHEREAS**, Hebban Talib Tawantawan gained an astounding score of 91. 17%, securing the top spot alongside Allaine Beduya Collamar of the University of the Philippines (UP) Tacloban;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Tawantawan is a proud Bangsamoro who graduated Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Science in Accountancy from the University of San Jose-Recoletos;

**WHEREAS**, he has consistently demonstrated diligence through his academic achievements and unwavering commitment to attaining excellence;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Tawantawan is a 23-year-old Bangsamoro professional of Maranao descent and is the proud son of Mr. Abon M. Tawantawan and Mrs. Saida T. Tawantawan of Poblacion, Municipality of Salvador, Lanao del Norte;

**WHEREAS**, one key to upholding progress and advancement within Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao is to cultivate a culture of excellence among the Bangsamoro youth who are the future holders of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, recognizing this achievement of a Bangsamoro youth will serve as an inspiration and motivation to the entire Bangsamoro community and the student population to always and continuously aim and pursue excellence and illustrate that Bangsamoros are, without a doubt, of exemplary quality:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate and commend Hebban Talib Tawantawan for being the topnotcher in the September 2023 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present this Resolution to Mr. Hebban Talib Tawantawan, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Rabi Al-Akhir 01, 1445/October 16, 2023.
