RESOLUTION NO. 421

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING THE BANGSAMORO ATHLETES IN THE 1ST KYOKUSHINRYU WORLD TOURNAMENT, MR. ABDULQUDDOS MOHAMAD FOR WINNING THE CHAMPIONSHIP IN THE 55-KG CATEGORY AND MR. AHMADINNIE YUSOP U. TALAPAS FOR SECURING 2ND PLACE IN THE 65-KG CATEGORY, FOR BRINGING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, Paragraph 1, Section 19, Article XIV of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that, *"the State shall promote physical education and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and amateur sports, including training for international competitions, to foster self-discipline, teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;"*

**WHEREAS**, Section 13, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 also known as the *"Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao"* provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall promote, among others, the mental and physical fitness of the youth through sports;

**WHEREAS**, pursuant to this mandate, the Bangsamoro Government is committed in promoting sports by encouraging and supporting programs, leagues, competitions, indigenous games, martial arts, and amateur sports;

**WHEREAS**, the 1st KyokushinRyu World Tournament was hosted by the International Karate Alliance, KyokushinRyu and Kyokushin Karate-do Philippines that was held in Caloocan Sports Complex, Bagumbong, Caloocan City from November 11-12, 2023;

**WHEREAS**, the International Karate Alliance KyokushinRyu Karate, emphasizing rigorous training, intense competitions, and moral principles like discipline, humility, and determination and providing guidance and opportunities for members to enhance their KyokushinRyu skills and participate in international karate events, was founded by Mr. Kancho Peter Chong;

**WHEREAS**, KyokushinRyu World Tournament is a platform to stimulate and foster young people's interest in learning martial arts to strengthen their skills, widen their international perspective, and promote camaraderie and social interaction among participants;

**WHEREAS**, the International Karate Alliance, KyokushinRyu (IKAK) Philippines, also known as Kyokushin Karate-do Philippines, is acknowledged as one of the country's strongest Karate organizations. Its success can be attributed to its well-organized training program, effective management, and extensive membership. As a non-profit organization, it aims to introduce the discipline of Kyokushin Karate to Filipino youth, with the goal of fostering a stronger society through martial arts;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro people made history for bagging awards after Mr. Abdulquddos D. Mohamad won the Championship in the 55-kg category and Mr. Ahmadinnie Yusop U. Talapas secured the 2nd Place in the 65-kg category of the 1st KyokushinRyu World Tournament;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Abdulquddos D. Mohamad is a Bangsamoro athlete of Iranun descent, hails from both the Municipality of Sultan Mastura and the City of Cotabato in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), and is the proud son of Mr. Abdulgani Salam Mohamad and Mrs. Salima Dimenda Mohamad;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Ahmadinnie Yusop U. Talapas is also a Bangsamoro athlete of Maguindanaon descent, hails from the City of Cotabato, BARMM, and is the proud son of Mr. Daud M. Talapas and Mrs. Audilene U. Talapas;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Mohamad and Mr. Talapas' exceptional demonstration of hard work, unwavering dedication, and relentless determination have served as a source of inspiration not only to the budding youth athletes hailing from Bangsamoro but also to many other aspiring athletes throughout the Philippines, and their outstanding performances on the global stage have showcased the Bangsamoro spirit of competitiveness and excellence;

**WHEREAS**, it is appropriate to recognize their outstanding performance and contribution that brought pride, honor, and glory to the BARMM;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority expresses deep appreciation and immense pride in recognizing Mr. Mohamad and Mr. Talapas for their remarkable achievements, extending full recognition and highest commendation for the distinction they have brought to the Bangsamoro people:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate and commend the Bangsamoro athletes in the 1st KyokushinRyu World Tournament, Mr. Abdulquddos D. Mohamad for winning the Championship in the 55-KG category and Mr. Ahmadinnie Yusop U. Talapas for securing 2nd Place in the 65-KG category, for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To furnish a copy of this Resolution to Mr. Abdulquddos D. Mohamad and Mr. Ahmadinnie Yusop U. Talapas as a token of our recognition and appreciation of their exemplary achievements and for their information and its necessary effects.

**RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE**, that a copy of this Resolution be presented to Sensei Engr. Usman Baraguir, the newly-elected IKAK Philippine Country Director, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Rajab 03, 1445/January 15, 2024.
