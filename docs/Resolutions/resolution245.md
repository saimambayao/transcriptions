**FOURTH REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 245**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING FERDINAND “BONGBONG” ROMUALDEZ MARCOS JR. AS PRESIDENT AND SARA ZIMMERMAN DUTERTE-CARPIO AS VICE-PRESIDENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES**

**WHEREAS**, on May 25, 2022, the Congress, sitting as the National Board of Canvassers (NBOC), at the House of Representatives Session Hall at Batasan Pambansa, Quezon City, proclaimed Ferdinand “Bongbong” Romualdez Marcos Jr. as the 17ᵗʰ President and Sara Zimmerman Duterte-Carpio as the Vice-President of the Republic of the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, Marcos won the presidency by a landslide of 31,629,783 votes or more than 16 million votes ahead of second placer presidential candidate. Meanwhile, Duterte-Carpio also overwhelmingly won the vice-presidential race with 32,208,417 votes or more than 22 million votes over her closest rival. To recall, Marcos and Duterte-Carpio had been topping pre-election surveys, including the latest from Pulse Asia;

**WHEREAS**, the recent election made a record-breaking 83.11 percent voter turnout with more than 55 million voters who casted their votes;

**WHEREAS**, Marcos previously served as a Senator from 2010 to 2016 while Duterte-Carpio served as Mayor of Davao City since 2016;

**WHEREAS**, Marcos and Duterte-Carpio, as running mates for elections, made an alliance known as the UNITEAM tandem. At the core of their platform lies the message of unity, which they highlighted in every town and province that they have visited during the campaign period;

**WHEREAS**, the UNITEAM’s platform includes recovering from the pandemic, enhancing the country’s digital infrastructure, ensuring accessible healthcare for all, strengthening agriculture and tourism, protecting the human capital, and continuing the Build, Build, Build program. The Marcos-Duterte-Carpio tandem also vows to continue, improve, and expand the achievements of President Rodrigo Duterte’s administration in order to rise and achieve progress in our country;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) expresses its full support to President Marcos and Vice-President Duterte-Carpio as it prepares to work closely with the new administration:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED** by the BTA Parliament, as it is hereby resolves, To congratulate and commend Ferdinand “Bongbong” Romualdez Marcos Jr. as President and Sara Zimmerman Duterte-Carpio as Vice-President of the Republic of the Philippines.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That copy of this foregoing Resolution be forwarded to the offices of President-elect Marcos and Vice President-elect Duterte, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Dhu Al-Qa’dah 17, 1443/June 16, 2022.
