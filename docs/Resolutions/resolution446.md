**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 446**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE GRATITUDE OF THE BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT TO HIS EXCELLENCY PRESIDENT FERDINAND ROMUALDEZ MARCOS, JR. FOR THE APPOINTMENT OF SHEIKH SABUDDIN N. ABDURAHIM AS SECRETARY OF THE NATIONAL COMMISSION ON MUSLIM FILIPINOS**

**WHEREAS,** the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos (NCMF) plays a pivotal role in promoting and safeguarding the rights and welfare of the Muslims in the Philippines;

**WHEREAS,** the appointment of key officials of the NCMF is instrumental in ensuring the effective execution of its mandates and the representation of diverse Muslim communities across the nation;

**WHEREAS,** Sheikh Sabuddin N. Abdurahim, a distinguished individual hailing from Tawi-Tawi, has been appointed by His Excellency President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr. as the Secretary of the NCMF, marking a historic milestone as the first Tawi-Tawian to assume such a significant role;

**WHEREAS,** Sheikh Abdurahim brings with him a wealth of experience, profound knowledge, and unwavering dedication to advancing the interests of the Muslim populace, thereby enriching the leadership landscape within the NCMF;

**WHEREAS,** his appointment underscores President Marcos's commitment to inclusivity, diversity, and equitable representation within the government institutions, fostering unity and harmony among the various Muslim communities in the Philippines:

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, Toexpress the gratitude of the Bangsamoro Parliament to His Excellency President Ferdinand Romualdez Marcos, Jr. for the appointment of Sheikh Sabuddin N. Abdurahim as Secretary of the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** To commend and congratulate Sheikh Sabuddin N. Abdurahim on his historic appointment as the first Tawi-Tawian to hold the esteemed position of Secretary of the NCMF.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY,** To forward the foregoing Resolution to His Excellency President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr., Sheikh Sabuddin N. Abdurahim, and NCMF, for their information and record.
