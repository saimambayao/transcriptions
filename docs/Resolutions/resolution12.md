**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 12**

**RESOLUTION APPROVING THE TRANSITION PLAN OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM).**

**WHEREAS**, Section 6, Article XVI of Republic Act 11054, otherwise known as the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) mandates the Chief Minister to submit to the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), within sixty (60) days of the transition period, “a transition plan that shall contain the proposed organizational plan, as well as, the schedule for the implementation therefor;”

**WHEREAS**, the Honorable Chief Minister Ahod Balawag Ebrahim on May 30, 2019 submitted to the BTA a transition plan pursuant to RA 11054;

**WHEREAS**, upon request of the Honorable Chief Minister Ebrahim, the BTA Parliament on June 17, 2019 held a special session to consider the Proposed BARMM Transiton Plan.

**WHEREAS**, the Transition Plan shall apply to the transition period of year 2019-2022, without prejudice to the passage of an Administrative Code and the creation of offices of the BARMM.

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, To approve the Transition Plan of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) for the transition period of year 2019-2022.

**Adopted, June 17, 2019.**
