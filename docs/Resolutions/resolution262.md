
Republic of the Philippines

**BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT**

**FOURTH REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 262**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST CONDOLENCES FOR THE MURDER OF ELECTION OFFICER ATTY. RUAYNA SAYYADI**

**WHEREAS,** the Bill of Rights of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that no person shall be deprived of life, liberty, or property without due process of law, nor shall any person be denied the equal protection of the laws;

**WHEREAS,** Atty. Ruayna Edris Sayyadi, Election Officer of the Municipality of Al-Barka, Basilan Province, Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) and a resident of Barangay Sumagdang, Isabela City, was shot dead by a still unidentified assailant on July 19, 2022, at around 3:15 pm while she was serving in the ongoing registration of voters at the Commission on Elections’ satellite office in Barangay Doña Ramona, Isabela City;

**WHEREAS,** Atty. Sayyadi was the 3rd election officer of Basilan since the May 2022 elections;

**WHEREAS,** the killing of the election officer, Atty. Ruayna Sayyadi, just a few months after the synchronized National and Local Elections is a cowardly and barbaric act committed to a defenseless public servant and a woman whose life is sacred and symbolizes the image of a mother;

**WHEREAS,** with the frequent occurrence of crimes in the same area, there is a need for the Philippine National Police of Isabela City and other concerned authorities to employ maximum and extra security visibility within the aforementioned area to ensure the primary safety of the innocent civilians who are working near that location and those just passing through that road;

**WHEREAS,** Islam has decreed those women, whether Muslims or non-Muslims, are forbidden to be beaten, harassed, and killed in any way, especially when there are no justifiable reasons for it;

**WHEREAS,** it is the aspiration of the Bangsamoro people and other inhabitants in the BARMM to establish an enduring peace on the basis of justice;

**WHEREAS,** it is morally upright for the Bangsamoro people, thru this Parliament, to express our deepest condolences and call in the proper authorities for the immediate investigation of the crime so as to bring the perpetrators to justice:

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To extend our deepest condolences on the murder of Atty. Ruayna Edris Sayyadi.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** To forward the foregoing resolution to the bereaved family of Atty. Ruayna Edris Sayyadi, for information.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY,** To furnish a copy of this resolution to the Philippine National Police of Isabela City, for appropriate action.

**ADOPTED,** *Dhu Al-Hijjah 29, 1443/July 28, 2022.*
