**FOURTH REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 254**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING SALEHA SACAR AS A RECIPIENT OF GLOBAL WOMEN'S ACHIEVERS AWARD GIVEN IN THE 9ᵀᴴ WORLD WOMEN LEADERSHIP CONGRESS LAST MARCH 2022, HELD IN MUMBAI, INDIA**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro people have been producing individuals that bring honor and pride to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), due to their exemplary performance and stellar achievements in their chosen fields and career lives;

**WHEREAS**, various international organizations hold conventions, conferences, and congresses where they give recognition to eligible organization, individual, man and woman alike, meeting the high standards prescribed to be entitled to their prestigious awards;

**WHEREAS**, the Philippines has been sending its representatives to various conventions, conferences, and congresses as part of our commitment and dedication to represent and advance great interest and the general welfare of the Filipino people;

**WHEREAS**, last March 2022, the 9ᵗʰ World Women Leadership Congress (WWLC) has been held in the Taj Land End, Mumbai, in Celebration of Women’s Month globally to honor the strategic and prominent role of various women leaders in the fields of economy, technology, advancement of the government and business organizations complying with global competitiveness standards;

**WHEREAS**, one of the Philippine delegates to the WWLC was Director Saleha P. Sacar of the Bureau of Muslim Economic Affairs, National Commission on Muslim Filipinos (NCMF), who, in effect, represented not only the Philippines but especially, the Muslims of the country and the BARMM, in particular;

**WHEREAS**, the international congress has recognized and accorded Director Sacar the prestigious Global Woman Achievers Award, which affords prominence and honor to the country and her people, the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, as the recipient of the said grand award, Director Sacar has become the first *Maranao* who has received international recognition from WWLC:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED** by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), as it is hereby resolved, To congratulate Saleha Sacar as a recipient of Global Women’s Achievers Award given in the 9ᵗʰ World Women Leadership Congress last March 2022, held in Mumbai, India.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That a copy of this Resolution be furnished to Director Saleha Sacar of the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos.

**ADOPTED**, Dhu Al-Qa’dah 24, 1443/June 23, 2022.
