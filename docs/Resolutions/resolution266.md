
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
**BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT**
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 266**

**RESOLUTION RE-AFFIRMING THE CONSENSUS OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) ON THE APPOINTMENT OF SHEIK KHALIPHA USMAN NANDO AS THE WALI OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11054, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ORGANIC LAW FOR THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

**WHEREAS,** Section 1, Article VIII of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, provides for the appointment of the *Wali*, who shall serve as the ceremonial head of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS,** Section 2 of Republic Act No. 11593, or the “Act Resetting the First Regular Elections in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, Amending for the Purpose, Section 13, Article XVI of Republic Act No. 11054, Otherwise Known as the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao”, provides for the extension of the transition period. Thus, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) shall continue as the interim government in the BARMM until June 30, 2025, or until their successors shall have been elected and qualified;

**WHEREAS,** *Sheik Khalipha Usman Nando* possesses the qualifications of *Wali* as provided under Section 2, Article VIII of the Bangsamoro Organic Law;

**WHEREAS,** *Sheik Nando* was appointed by the interim Chief Minister as the *Wali* of the Bangsamoro Government prior to the extension period. He served with utmost dedication and commitment to public service;

**WHEREAS,** the BTA believes that *Sheik Nando* has the moral ascendancy to represent the Bangsamoro Government as its ceremonial head:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To reaffirm the consensus of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority on the appointment of *Sheik Khalipha Usman Nando*, as the *Wali* of the Bangsamoro Government pursuant to Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** that a copy of this Resolution be presented to *Sheik Khalipha Usman Nando*, for information.

**ADOPTED,** *Safar* 25, 1444/September 21, 2022.
