**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 558**

**RESOLUTION URGING THE INTERGOVERNMENTAL INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT BOARD TO INCLUDE IN ITS PRIORITY AGENDA THE REHABILITATION AND UPGRADING OF THE AWANG–UPI–SOUTH UPI–LEBAK HIGHWAY IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

**WHEREAS**, the 67.9–kilometer Awang–Upi–South Upi–Lebak Highway is a major arterial road that connects key municipalities in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) and plays a crucial role in facilitating trade, commerce, and the transportation of goods and services within the region and beyond;

**WHEREAS**, the road’s current state has significantly deteriorated over time, posing safety risks to commuters, hindering economic activities, and limiting access to vital social services in the region;

**WHEREAS**, its repair and maintenance fall under the responsibility of the National Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH), as prescribed under Section 37, Article XIII of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the *“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;”*

**WHEREAS**, Section 6, Article VI of the same Organic Law provides that the Intergovernmental Infrastructure Development Board is tasked with coordinating and synchronizing the National and Bangsamoro Infrastructure Development Plan;

**WHEREAS**, the DPWH Secretary and the Minister of Public Works are both members of the Board;

**WHEREAS**, the Awang–Upi–South Upi–Lebak Highway requires the urgent attention of the concerned departments for its immediate repair, rehabilitation, and upgrading, which would lead to increased mobility, improved connectivity between municipalities, and enhanced economic integration within the BARMM, thereby promoting long-term regional development;

**WHEREAS**, this project aligns with the Bangsamoro Government’s vision to foster inclusive growth and development by addressing infrastructure gaps and ensuring sustainable infrastructure that supports the region’s socio-economic progress;

**WHEREAS**, with the joint action of the National DPWH and the Bangsamoro MPW, faster decision-making and action can be anticipated for the urgent repair, rehabilitation, and upgrading of the Awang–Upi–South Upi–Lebak Highway within the jurisdiction of the BARMM:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To urge the Intergovernmental Infrastructure Development Board to include in its priority agenda the immediate rehabilitation and upgrading of the Awang–Upi–South Upi–Lebak Highway in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To take into account the urgency of addressing its deteriorated condition and its importance to the economic and social development of the region.

**RESOLVED FINALLY**, To forward this Resolution to the Intergovernmental Infrastructure Development Board and the MPW, for immediate consideration and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED**, Safar 26, 1447/August 20, 2025.
