BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT

**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 388**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING JAMAL RAHMAT PANDI OF LANAO DEL SUR FOR WINNING THE GOLD MEDAL IN THE BADMINTON SECONDARY CATEGORY AND COMMENDING THE ATHLETES AND COACHES WHO PARTICIPATED IN THE PALARONG PAMBANSA 2023, THEREBY GIVING HONOR AND PRIDE TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall recognize the vital role of the youth in nation-building, promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, and nationalism, encourage involvement in public and civic affairs, and promote mental and physical fitness through sports;

**WHEREAS**, it is also stated that the Bangsamoro educational system shall encourage and support programs, league competitions, indigenous games, martial arts, and amateur sports, including training for regional, national, and international competitions;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Parliament has, on numerous occasions, acknowledged outstanding Bangsamoro athletes for their astounding accomplishments in bringing victory in different competitions held within and outside the region;

**WHEREAS**, the Palarong Pambansa 2023, officially known as the 63rd *Palarong Pambansa* was held in Marikina City last July 29 to August 05, 2023;

**WHEREAS**, student-athletes from seventeen (17) athletic associations representing all regions of the Philippines competed in different sporting events and disciplines;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region bagged two (2) gold, two (2) silver, and two (2) bronze medals in the said *Palarong Pambansa* recently concluded;

**WHEREAS**, Jamal Rahmat Pandi from the Schools Division of Lanao del Sur I won a gold medal in the Badminton Secondary category. His coach was Aisah Mae Mina Penduma;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Pandi is a native of Lanao del Sur who, based on records, had also brought silver and pride not only to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region but to the entire country in the Asia Junior Sports Exchange Games last 2022 for his competency in the field of badminton;

**WHEREAS**, hereby commended are the following team and student-athletes who garnered medals in the *Palaro* 2023:

(a) Regular Sports (6)
Volleyball Elementary Boys – gold
Maguindanao II Division Team
Badminton Secondary Boys – gold
Jamal Rahmat D. Pandi (Lanao del Sur I)
Taekwondo Elementary Boys – bronze
Dhan Joseph Eligio (Cotabato City)
Johari O. Bones (Cotabato City)
Taekwondo Elementary Girls – bronze
Dhivine Iriz Quine A. Lerado (Cotabato City)
Wrestling Girls – bronze
Angel T. Apolinario (Lamitan City)

(b) Demo Sports (2)
Pencak Silat Boys – bronze
Mahdum J. Maddatal (Basilan)
Mojib M. Omar (Cotabato City)

(c) Para Games (3)
Para Swimming Boys – 2 silver and 1 bronze
Jhon Rhey M. Bondan (Lamitan City)

**WHEREAS**, commending also the coaches, assistant coaches, trainers, chaperons, and other individuals who helped and contributed to the achievement of the student-athletes;

**WHEREAS**, the accomplishment of Mr. Jamal Rahmat Pandi and all the Bangsamoro athletes, with the support and direction of their respective coaches, serve as an example for aspiring young athletes in the region and deserve recognition and praise from the Parliament:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To congratulate Jamal Rahmat Pandi of Lanao del Sur for winning the gold medal in the Badminton Secondary category and to commend the athletes and coaches who participated in the *Palarong Pambansa* 2023, thereby giving honor and pride to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present this Resolution to Mr. Jamal Rahmat Pandi and furnish a copy of the same to the athletes and coaches who participated in the *Palarong Pambansa* 2023, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Muharram 28, 1445/August 15, 2023.
