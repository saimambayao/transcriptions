**RESOLUTION NO. 27**

**RESOLUTION AMENDING SECTION 1, RULE XVII OF THE PARLIAMENTARY RULES, PROCEDURES, AND PRACTICES OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA)**

**WHEREAS**, the Privilege Hour for the members of the BTA shall be held during Mondays of session days as provided in Section 1, Rule XVII of the Parliamentary Rules, Procedures and Practices of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) - Parliament approved the new Calendar of Session last July 29, 2019 through BTA Resolution No. 62 entitled, “Resolution Adopting a New Calendar of Session of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament amending for that purpose Resolution no. 22”;

**WHEREAS**, to synchronize to the new BTA Calendar of Session, it is deemed proper to change the schedule of the Privilege Hour in the Parliamentary Order of Business from Mondays to Thursdays of regular sessions of the BTA.

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, To amend Section 1, Rule XVII of the Parliamentary Rules, Procedures, and Practices of the BTA to read as follows:

“*Section 1. Day for Privilege Hour. – Privilege hour shall be held every Thursday of the regular sessions and shall not be interrupted by any question or motion except a point of order or a motion to adjourn. In exceptional circumstances, where the person or integrity of the BTA or of a Member is in clear danger of, or has been inflicted with such grave harm or injury, a question of personal or collective privilege may be allowed to interrupt the Privilege Hour.*”

**Adopted**, August 22, 2019.
