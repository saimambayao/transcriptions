**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 270**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE SINCERE CONDOLENCES OF THE BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT ON THE DEATH OF ABDULWALID “JIMMY” M. RADIAMODA, 122ND BASE COMMANDER OF THE BANGSAMORO ISLAMIC ARMED FORCES, AND CONVEYING ITS PROFOUND GRATITUDE ON HIS INVALUABLE CONTRIBUTION TO THE BANGSAMORO STRUGGLE FOR SELF-DETERMINATION**

**WHEREAS**, Abdulwalid “Jimmy” M. Radiamoda, 122nd Base Commander, passed away and returned to the mercy of Allah on *Safar* 24, 1444 / September 20, 2022;

**WHEREAS**, Commander Radiamoda, was a dutiful and dedicated *mujahideen* who exuded bravery and was not afraid to risk his own life in defending the cause of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF). He was among the *mujahideen* who defended Camp Nusa during the 1977 war and led his men in defending MILF camps during the 2000, 2003, and 2008 wars;

**WHEREAS**, Commander Radiamoda was known for his programs centered on peace and crime-free community. He ensured that peace reigned within his area of jurisdiction. He assisted in the arrest and imprisonment of criminals especially drug pushers and users. He had also settled several local feuds. He is a pious leader who is loved by his men for his unselfish and dedicated service to the MILF and the community;

**WHEREAS**, the passing away of a dedicated and passionate *mujahideen* as Commander Abdulwalid “Jimmy” M. Radiamoda, is a great loss to the Moro people;

**WHEREAS**, his services to the Bangsamoro people will be remembered with great honor and pride and will never be forgotten:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To express the sincere condolences of the Bangsamoro Parliament on the death of Abdulwalid “Jimmy” M. Radiamoda, 122nd Base Commander of the Bangsamoro Islamic Armed Forces, and conveying its profound gratitude on his invaluable contribution to the Bangsamoro struggle for self-determination.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that the foregoing Resolution be forwarded to the bereaved family of late Commander Abdulwalid “Jimmy” M. Radiamoda.

**ADOPTED**, *Safar* 26, 1444/September 22, 2022.
