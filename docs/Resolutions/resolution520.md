**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 520**

**RESOLUTION URGING THE BANGSAMORO PLANNING AND DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY TO ENHANCE, UPDATE, AND MAINTAIN ITS ONLINE DATABASE OF PROGRAMS AND PROJECTS**

**WHEREAS**, Section 4, Article XIII of Republic Act No. 11054, also known as the *“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao”* states that *“the Parliament shall establish the Bangsamoro Economic and Development Council (BEDC) which shall serve as the planning, monitoring, and coordinating agency for all development plans, programs, and projects of the Bangsamoro Government;”*

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority (BPDA) was created under Section 14 of the Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 06, otherwise known as the *“Bangsamoro Socio-Economic Development Planning Act of 2019”*. The BPDA is the socio-economic development planning and policy coordinating authority of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, Section 13 of the same law states that the technical and secretariat services of the BEDC shall be provided by the BPDA;

**WHEREAS**, BPDA needs to enhance, update, and maintain its online database, which will guide policy-making, planning, programming, budgeting, project implementation, and monitoring and evaluation;

**WHEREAS**, the online database system will inform the general public of the wide range of projects that are funded by national, regional, and local government units;

**WHEREAS**, the availability of information on the status of projects implemented at each level can help shape policy-making and future legislation;

**WHEREAS**, the existing database of the BPDA was developed in 2023 which needs enhancement and updating;

**WHEREAS**, there is a need to regularly update and maintain the data contained in the database:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To urge the Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority to enhance, update, and maintain its online database of programs and projects.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To forward this Resolution to the BPDA-Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, for information and appropriate consideration.

**ADOPTED**, *Rajab* 23, 1446/January 23, 2025.
