**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 198**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE RECOGNITION AND COMMENDATION OF THE BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT TO DR. FATIMA TASNEEM JIMLOK MOHAMMAD SALI FOR TOPPING THE 2021 PHILIPPINE BOARD OF EMERGENCY MEDICINE IN-SERVICE EXAMINATION**

WHEREAS, Fatima Tasneem “Tam” Jimlok Mohammad Sali, MD, a Resident of Ospital ng Makati’s Department of Emergency, topped the 2021 Philippine Board of Emergency Medicine (PBEM) In-Service Examination, a professional licensure examination for a specialized discipline in the medical field focused on giving timely and coordinated health and safety services to victims of sudden illness or injury;

WHEREAS, Dr. Mohammad Sali hails from Sapa, in the Municipality of Tandubas, Province of Tawi-Tawi, and is the daughter of Abraham and Nellie Mohammad Sali;

WHEREAS, consistent honor student, Dr. Sali received her primary education from the Sapa Elementary School as class salutatorian; her secondary education from the Mindanao State University-Tawi-Tawi College of Technology and Oceanography; and her bachelor’s degree from the Far Eastern University-Institute of Nursing as Cum Laude;

WHEREAS, she obtained her master’s degree in Nursing from the University of the Philippines Manila and went on to engage in further studies from two of the most prominent medical schools in the country: Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila (PLM) College of Medicine, where she placed 5th, and from the University of the Philippines-Philippine General Hospital (UP-PGH);

WHEREAS, by achieving this immense feat, the Bangsamoro Parliament is hereby recognizing the excellence and merit exemplified by Sali, especially in her competence and capacity in this specialized field of treating and managing patients who are victims of sudden or acute injuries or illnesses;

WHEREAS, it is without doubt that Dr. Mohammad Sali brought honor to our region, even amidst the concerns brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic. Her hard work, dedication, and mastery made evident by securing the top place in the 2021 PBEM In-Service Examination. It will forever serve as an inspiration not only to the thousands of Bangsamoro youth but also to the millions of Bangsamoros across the country:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to express the recognition and commendation of the Bangsamoro Parliament to Dr. Fatima Tasneem Jimlok Mohammad Sali for topping the 2021 Philippine Board of Emergency Medicine In-Service Examination.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, to forward the foregoing Resolution to Dr. Mohammad Sali, for information.

ADOPTED, Jumada Al-Ula 17, 1443/December 22, 2021.
