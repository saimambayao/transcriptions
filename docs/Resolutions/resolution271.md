**RESOLUTION NO. 271**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING THE INVALUABLE CONTRIBUTION OF THE MEMBERS OF THE FIRST BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) WHO ARE NOT REAPPOINTED BY HIS EXCELLENCY PRESIDENT FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR. TO THE SECOND BTA**

**WHEREAS**, the Members of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament perform a significant role in guaranteeing the BTA’s success in realizing the aspirations and ideals of the Bangsamoro People for lasting peace and societal development as embodied in the Bangsamoro Organic Law;

**WHEREAS**, underlying the BTA’s success was the Parliament’s enactment of essential legislations that respond to the crises and problems faced by the BARMM region, and pursue the general welfare of the Bangsamoro People by sustaining harmony in the diverse culture of Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS**, the Members of the first BTA Parliament not re-appointed deserve recognition and appreciation for their invaluable participation and contribution to the Bangsamoro Government’s quests and feats in the last three years;

**WHEREAS**, the BTA Parliament recognizes their utmost participation and contribution in rendering their legislative and executive powers lodged upon them during the transition cited in Section 3, Article XVI of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the “*Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao*,” to read:

**ARTICLE XVI**

**BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY**

**Section 3. Powers and Authorities.** – Legislative and executive powers in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region during transition shall be vested in the Bangsamoro Transition Authority.
Xxx
All powers and functions of the Bangsamoro Government as provided in this Organic Law is vested in the Bangsamoro Transition Authority during the transition period.

**WHEREAS**, as Members of the BTA Parliament they had shared collective efforts in carrying out their mandates in the accomplishment of various priorities set forth in Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, stated in Section 4 of the above Article:

**Section 3. Functions and Priorities.** – The Bangsamoro Transition shall ensure the accomplishment of the following priorities during the transition period:

(a) Enactment of priority legislations such as the Bangsamoro Administrative Code, Bangsamoro Revenue Code, Bangsamoro Electoral Code, Bangsamoro Local Government Code, and Bangsamoro Education Code consistent with powers and prerogatives vested in the Bangsamoro Government by this Organic Law. Xxx

The Bangsamoro Transition Authority may also enact a Bangsamoro Civil Service Code, as provided in this Organic Law, subject to the Constitutional mandate of the Civil Service Commission.

**WHEREAS**, for their indispensable contribution to and great participation in the crafting of Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known as the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, that the Bangsamoro Government is now implementing, and to them who tremendously exerted effort and strive to gain peace which we are now reaping, we honor your excellence and bravery;

**WHEREAS**, this Resolution aims to recognize their knowledge, efforts, competencies, and proficiencies in the execution of their skills in the advancement and development of the Bangsamoro Government:

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To commend the invaluable contribution of members of the first BTA who are not reappointed by His Excellency President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to forward the foregoing Resolution to the above-named members of the first BTA who are not reappointed by His Excellency President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr.
