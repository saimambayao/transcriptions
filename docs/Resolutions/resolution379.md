**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 379**

**RESOLUTION DIRECTING THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH (MOH) TO APPRISE AND UPDATE THE BTA PARLIAMENT ON THE IMPLEMENTATION OF COMMUNITY-INTENDED HEALTH PROJECTS**

**WHEREAS**, Presidential Proclamation No. 922, s. 2020, was issued, declaring a State of Public Health Emergency throughout the Philippines due to Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19);

**WHEREAS**, on September 11, 2020, the President signed into law Republic Act No. 11494, known as the *“Bayanihan to Recover as One Act”*;

**WHEREAS**, Section 3 of Republic Act No. 11494 provides that it is the policy of the State to promote a just and dynamic social order that will ensure the prosperity and independence of the nation and free the people from poverty, particularly in the aftermath of natural and man-made disasters, through policies that provide adequate social services, and promote full employment, a rising standard of living, and an improved quality of life for all;

**WHEREAS**, the continuous increase of confirmed cases of COVID-19 in the region poses a serious threat to the health, safety, security, and lives of the Bangsamoro people. The disruption to livelihood and all other productive activities were seen in the region’s economic reduction during the pandemic;

**WHEREAS**, a number of community-intended projects such as medical outreach programs, vaccination programs, maternal and child health care programs, among others, are programmed for implementation;

**WHEREAS**, the community projects aim to enhance the quality of life of the vulnerable sectors in the Bangsamoro region. It safeguards the needs of the community to be considered and met:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the BTA Parliament, To direct the Ministry of Health to apprise and update the BTA Parliament on the implementation of community-intended health projects to include facilities, equipment, human resource, extension/outreach and training by submitting a report through the Secretary-General within thirty (30) days following the adoption of this resolution.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To forward this Resolution to the MOH, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Muharram 09, 1445/July 27, 2023.
