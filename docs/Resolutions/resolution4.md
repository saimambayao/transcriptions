**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 4**

**RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING THE INTERIM CHIEF MINISTER TO REQUEST THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT THROUGH THE PRESIDENT RODRIGO R. DUTERTE TO TRANSFER THE CURRENT APPROPRIATION FUNDS OF THE DEFUNCT AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO TO BANGSAMORO TRANSITON AUTHORITY.**

**WHEREAS**, the transition period of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), has commenced with the ratification of the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) had been formally constituted on February 22, 2019, with the appointment of the members of the BTA;

**WHEREAS**, the BTA had been organized with the elections of the officers of the BTA on March 29, 2019;

**WHEREAS**, Section 14, Article XVI of Republic Act 11054, provides:

> “*In addition, the current year's appropriations for the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao shall also be transferred to the Bangsamoro Transition Authority for this purpose.*”;

**NOW, THEREFORE RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the majority vote of the members of the BTA present in session, to authorize the Interim Chief Minister to request the National Government through the President Rodrigo R. Duterte to transfer the current appropriation funds of the defunct Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to Bangsamoro Transition Authority.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, to forward the foregoing Resolution to His Excellency President Rodrigo Roa Duterte, and the Honorable Secretary of the Department of Budget and Management (DBM) for information and appropriate consideration.

**Adopted, March 29, 2019.**
