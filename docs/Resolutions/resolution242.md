**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 242**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING THE NEWLY LAUNCHED OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENTIAL ADVISER ON PEACE, RECONCILIATION, AND UNITY, FOR ITS SUCCESSFUL REORGANIZATION AND RENEWED COMMITMENT TO PEACE IN THE PHILIPPINES**

**WHEREAS**, Executive Order No. 125, s. 1993, adopted as government policy a comprehensive peace process and created the Office of the Presidential Adviser on the Peace Process to oversee, coordinate, and integrate its implementation. Executive Order No. 3, s. 2001, further strengthened the agency and reaffirmed the government's commitment to achieving just and lasting peace through a comprehensive peace process;

**WHEREAS**, the comprehensive peace process is guided by the "Three Principles and Six Paths to Peace" which provides the framework for the implementation, coordination, monitoring and integration of all government peace initiatives, and guides its partnership with civil society in the pursuit of a just and enduring peace;

**WHEREAS**, President Rodrigo Duterte issued Executive Order No. 158, s. 2021, strengthening the policy framework and administrative structure of the national government on peace, reconciliation and unity;

**WHEREAS**, pursuant to Executive Order No. 158, s. 2021, the Office of the Presidential Adviser on the Peace Process was renamed and reorganized into the Office of the Presidential Adviser on Peace, Reconciliation, and Unity, and was successfully launched on March 24, 2022;

**WHEREAS**, Presidential Peace Adviser Carlito G. Galvez, Jr., as head of the national peacebuilding agency, expressed the Office’s renewed commitment to sustain the gains of the peace process through successfully reintegrating former combatants and their families into society, and transforming former conflict-affected areas into peaceful and progressive communities;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to congratulate the newly launched Office of the Presidential Adviser on Peace, Reconciliation, and Unity, for its successful reorganization and renewed commitment to peace in the Philippines.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to forward the foregoing Resolution to His Excellency President Rodrigo Roa Duterte, copy-furnished the Office of the Presidential Adviser on Peace, Reconciliation, and Unity, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Sha’baan 20, 1443/March 24, 2022.
