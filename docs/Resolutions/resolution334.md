**RESOLUTION NO. 334**

RESOLUTION COMMENDING ENGR. CASMER DILANGALEN, A BANGSAMORO, FOR RANKING TOP TWO (2) IN THE APRIL 2023 PHILIPPINE CIVIL ENGINEERS LICENSURE EXAMINATION, THEREBY GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE

WHEREAS, Engr. Casmer Dilangalen is a Maguindanaon from Barangay Kayaga, Municipality of Kabacan, North Cotabato;

WHEREAS, he is a 2022 Bachelor of Civil Engineering graduate of the University of the Philippines-Diliman, Magna Cum Laude;

WHEREAS, on April 29, 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced that out of Sixteen Thousand Nine Hundred Thirty-Six (16,936) examinees, Five Thousand Eight Hundred Eighty-Seven (5,887) passed the Philippine Civil Engineers Licensure Examination given by the Board of Civil Engineering;

WHEREAS, Engr. Dilangalen, with an impressive rating of 90.45%, clinched the rank two (2) of the coveted top ten (10) places of the said licensure examination;

WHEREAS, his exemplary performance brought honor not only to his family but also to the entire Bangsamoro People;

WHEREAS, his intelligence and resolve will reverberate across the halls of the Bangsamoro peopleâ€™s history and serve as an inspiration to the entire Bangsamoro populace that with ample hard work and determination, no dream is too big and no goal is too impossible to achieve:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To commend Engr. Casmer Dilangalen for ranking top two (2) in the April 2023 Philippine Civil Engineers Licensure Examination, thereby giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That a copy of this Resolution be presented to Engr. Casmer Dilangalen, for information.

ADOPTED, Shawwal 27, 1444/May 18, 2023.
