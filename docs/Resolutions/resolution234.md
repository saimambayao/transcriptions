**RESOLUTION NO. 234**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING PIAMAE KEIL ROSLIN, A GRADUATE OF THE NOTRE DAME UNIVERSITY, FOR SUCCESSFULLY ATTAINING THE NINTH HIGHEST RANK IN THE FEBRUARY 2022 PSYCHOMETRICIAN LICENSURE EXAMINATION**

**WHEREAS,** the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced that 969 out of 2,241 passed the Psychometrician Licensure Examination given by the Board of Psychology in testing centers Manila, Baguio, Cagayan de Oro, Cebu, Davao, Iloilo, Koronadal, Legazpi, Lucena, Pampanga, Rosales, Tacloban, Tuguegarao and Zamboanga last February 2022;

**WHEREAS,** while passing a licensure examination is a great achievement in itself, more exceptional is obtaining a high rank therefrom;

**WHEREAS,** with a rating of 81.80%, a graduate of Notre Dame University, Cotabato City, Ms. Piamae Keil Roslin, obtained the ninth highest mark in this Psychometrician Licensure Examination;

**WHEREAS,** Ms. Roslin, who hails from the Municipality of Parang, Maguindanao, serves as an inspiration to our young Bangsamoro professionals, and a testament that hard work and perseverance lead to success in their chosen endeavor;

**WHEREAS,** Ms. Roslin together with the other Notre Dame University passers, deserves recognition, as a source of pride for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM):

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to commend and congratulate Piamae Keil Roslin, a graduate of Notre Dame University, for successfully attaining the ninth highest rank in the February 2022 Psychometrician Licensure Examination.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** to forward the foregoing Resolution to Piamae Keil Roslin and Notre Dame University, for information.
