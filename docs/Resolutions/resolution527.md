**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 527**

**RESOLUTION URGING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE AND OTHER AUTHORITIES OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO TO IMMEDIATELY ACT AND INVESTIGATE THE BRUTAL KILLING OF BAIWAN BANOG ANGAN IN SITIO KUKOR, BRGY. MANTAO, DATU HOFFER, MAGUINDANAO DEL SUR**

**WHEREAS**, as stated in Section 1, Article III of the 1987 Philippine Constitution, no person shall be deprived of life, liberty, or property without due process of law, nor shall any person be denied the equal protection of the laws;

**WHEREAS**, on December 7, 2027, at approximately 1:00 PM in Sitio Kukor, Brgy. Mantao, Datu Hoffer, Maguindanao del Sur, Baiwan Banog Angan and Cita Manguda Angan went to the river to fish, where they were approached by armed men who instructed Cita Manguda Angan to return home, and shortly thereafter, gunfire erupted from unidentified firearms, tragically resulting in the death of Baiwan Banog Angan;

**WHEREAS**, the perpetrators allegedly subjected the victim to abuse before his death, forcing him to dig his own grave before being fatally shot to death;

**WHEREAS**, we condemn in the strongest terms possible the vicious killing of Baiwan Banog Angan, a Non-Moro Indigenous Peoples Leader from the *Menubu Dulangan* tribe in Maguindanao Del Sur;

**WHEREAS**, in the spirit of justice, it is imperative to bring the perpetrators to the court of law for their criminal act:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To urge the Philippine National Police and other authorities of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to immediately act and investigate the brutal killing of Baiwan Banog Angan in Sitio Kukor, Brgy. Mantao, Datu Hoffer, Maguindanao del Sur.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To furnish a copy of this Resolution, to the Philippine National Police and other relevant government authorities for their information and appropriate consideration.
