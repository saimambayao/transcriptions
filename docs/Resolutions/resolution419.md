RESOLUTION NO. 419

**RESOLUTION REITERATING THE CALL FOR AN UNCONDITIONAL CEASEFIRE IN GAZA, CALLING FOR THE CESSATION OF VIOLENCE AND ABUSES COMMITTED IN THE WEST BANK, AND URGING THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT TO SEND AID AND ASSISTANCE TO THE PEOPLE OF GAZA**

**WHEREAS**, more than seventy (70) days have passed since the Government of Israel declared war against Hamas and started its military operations in Gaza;

**WHEREAS**, to date, more than eleven thousand (11,000) civilians have been killed of which more than forty percent (40%) were children, and thousands more injured, with the numbers increasing by the hour;

**WHEREAS**, a report by the Euro-Med Human Rights Monitor on December 18, 2023 highlights a severe rise in casualties in Gaza, with over twenty-five thousand (25,000) deaths, including five thousand three hundred ninety (5,390) women and ten thousand ninety-one (10,091) children, and more than fifty-two thousand (52,000) injured; the attacks on hospitals and clinics, the collapse of health systems and facilities, the deaths of health workers and journalists, and the displacement of over 1.85 million Palestinians intensify the humanitarian crisis;

**WHEREAS**, more than forty-one thousand (41,000) housing units in Gaza have been destroyed, and two hundred twenty-two thousand (222,000) more have been damaged amounting to at least forty-five percent (45%) of the housing units in the Gaza Strip;

**WHEREAS**, according to the United Nations Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs (UN OCHA), two hundred seventy-nine (279) educational facilities have been damaged as of November 15, 2023, and none of the six hundred twenty-five thousand (625,000) students of Gaza have been able to access education;

**WHEREAS**, until now, the people in Gaza are still deprived of access to sufficient or even tolerable levels of food, water, electricity, fuel, and essential medical supplies;

**WHEREAS**, acts of violence and abuses against the Palestinians have likewise been reported in the West Bank;

**WHEREAS**, the suffering of the Palestinians cannot and should not be ignored and left unabated;

**WHEREAS**, it is incumbent upon the rest of humanity to send any or all forms of assistance to the Palestinians â€“ from being their voice in various platforms to providing aid to ease their suffering:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To reiterate the call for an unconditional ceasefire in Gaza, to call for the cessation of violence and abuses committed in the West Bank, and to urge the Bangsamoro Government to send aid and assistance to the people of Gaza.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To reiterate its urgent call for the unconditional ceasefire in Gaza and all the occupied territories of Palestine.

**RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE**, To urge the Bangsamoro Government to send aid and assistance to the people in Gaza, and to seek and adopt every appropriate means available toward this end.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, To furnish a copy of this Resolution to the Office of the Chief Minister, the Department of Foreign Affairs, the Embassy of Palestine, the Embassy of Israel, and the Organization of Islamic Cooperation.

**ADOPTED**, Jumada Al-Akhirah 05, 1445/December 18, 2023.
