
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
**BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT**
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 109**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING THE ORGANIZATION OF ISLAMIC COOPERATION (OIC) FOR THEIR UNWAVERING SUPPORT AND COMMITMENT TO THE PEACE PROCESS IN THE BANGSAMORO BY THE PASSAGE OF RESOLUTION NO. 2/47-MM ENTITLED “ON QUESTION OF MUSLIMS IN SOUTHERN PHILIPPINES”**

**WHEREAS**, the Organization of Islamic Cooperation (OIC), the second-largest organization next to the United Nations is the collective voice of the Muslims which endeavors to safeguard and protect the interests of the Muslim world in the spirit of promoting international peace and harmony among various people of the world;

**WHEREAS**, the OIC has been actively involved in the mediation or facilitation of the peace negotiations between the Government of the Philippines (GPH) and both the Moro National Liberation Front (MNLF) and the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) to solve the Bangsamoro struggle;

**WHEREAS**, the Forty-Seventh Session of the Council of Foreign Ministers of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation (United Against Terrorism for Peace and Development) was held in Niamey, Republic of Niger, on November 27-28, 2020 (12-13 Rabi al Thani 1442H);

**WHEREAS**, in the said session of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation, the Council of Foreign Ministers approved Resolution No. 2/47-MM entitled “On Question of Muslims in Southern Philippines”;

**WHEREAS**, Resolution No. 2/47-MM, commended all the third-party facilitators in their role in reaching and signing all of the agreements between the Government of the Philippines (GPH) and the Moro National Liberation Front (MNLF), and the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF);

**WHEREAS**, the OIC reaffirms and renews its continuous support and urges the GPH for the faithful and full implementation of the provisions of the 1996 Final Agreement on implementation of the 1976 Tripoli Agreement concluded between the GPH and the MNLF, and the Framework Agreement on the Bangsamoro (FAB) and Comprehensive Agreement on the Bangsamoro (CAB) concluded between the GPH and the MILF;

**WHEREAS**, the OIC reiterates the call for the full implementation of all previous peace agreements in order to reach a comprehensive and lasting peace;

**WHEREAS**, the Council requests the GPH to facilitate a joint delegation of the General Secretariat, Member States, and representatives of the Islamic Development Bank to visit the area for developing viable mechanism to provide necessary development assistance and financing to the Bangsamoro Region;

**WHEREAS**, the OIC has been instrumental in bringing about peace and development in the Bangsamoro, thus, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority hereby commends the OIC for its unwavering support to the Bangsamoro People.

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby Resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority to commend the Organization of Islamic Cooperation (OIC) for their unwavering support and commitment in the peace process in the Bangsamoro by the passage of Resolution no. 2/47-MM entitled “On Question of Muslims in Southern Philippines.”

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, to forward the foregoing Resolution to the Office of the Chief Minister Ahod Balawag Ebrahim, for his information and appropriate action.

**ADOPTED**, February 24, 2021.
