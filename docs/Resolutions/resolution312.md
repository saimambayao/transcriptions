**RESOLUTION NO. 312**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE COMMENDATION AND SUPPORT TO THE JOURNEY OF MS. FAUZIA TUMBAS - LIDASAN, A BANGSAMORO WOMAN AIMING TO REACH THE MOUNT EVEREST SUMMIT BY MAY 2025 AFTER CONQUERING AND PLANTING THE BANGSAMORO FLAG AT THE MOUNTAIN BASE CAMP**

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Fauzia Tumbas - Lidasan became the first Bangsamoro mountain climber who conquered and raised the Bangsamoro flag at the base camp height of the highest mountain in the world, the Mount Everest;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Fauzia Tumbas - Lidasan and her team reached the base camp height of 5,364 meters of Mt. Everest on April 26, 2022;

**WHEREAS**, Mount Everest, towering at 8,849 meters (29,032 feet) above sea level, is the world’s highest mountain situated in the Mahalangur Himalaya, a sub-range of the Himalayas between Nepal and Tibet, an autonomous region of China;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Fauzia Tumbas - Lidasan, a mother of five (5), is 40-years-old and a native of Gang, Sultan Kudarat, Maguindanao del Norte, and the daughter of former Vice Mayor Muslimin S. Tumbas of Sultan Mastura, Maguindanao Del Norte;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Fauzia recalls that the Everest Base Camp was her "toughest camp" since she started mountain climbing in 2016 when her husband passed away;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Fauzia brought and raised the Bangsamoro Emblem at the Base Camp of Mount Everest to honor her parents and the *mujahideen* who fought for the liberation of the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Fauzia detailed her upcoming training schedule, stating that she intends to conquer the following mountains and raise the Bangsamoro flag in the coming years: Mount Kilimanjaro (5,895 masl), Africa’s highest mountain, ranked fourth among the seven summits by June 2023; Mera Peak (6,476 masl), an advanced beginner and intermediate climb leading to a 7-day expedition, to be conquered by November 2023; Mount Cho Oyu (8,188 masl), the world’s sixth-highest mountain, to be reached by April 2024; and Mount Everest (8,848 masl), which lies on the border between Nepal and Tibet, to be accomplished again by March-May 2025;

**WHEREAS**, with our utmost support, Ms. Fauzia Tumbas - Lidasan, with her faith and bravery, looks forward to achieving her objective of reaching the summit of Mount Everest soon, In Shaa Allah:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To express commendation and support the journey of Ms. Fauzia Tumbas - Lidasan, a Bangsamoro Woman aiming to reach the Mount Everest peak by May 2025.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That the Bangsamoro Women Commission (BWC) and the Bangsamoro Sports Commission (BSC) are hereby tasked to provide Ms. Fauzia Tumbas - Lidasan the needed assistance and support to prepare and aid her in her journey to reach the peak of Mount Everest.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, That a copy of this Resolution be presented to Ms. Fauzia Tumbas - Lidasan, for information.

**ADOPTED**, *Rajab 04, 1444/January 26, 2023.*
