**FOURTH REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 263**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING THE BANGSAMORO MARTIAL ARTS ATHLETES FOR THEIR EXCEPTIONAL PERFORMANCE IN THE RECENT PAN ASIAN INTERNATIONAL JIU JITSU COMPETITION IN AYALA MALLS CIRCUIT, CIRCUIT MAKATI, METRO MANILA**

**WHEREAS,** the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that the State shall promote physical education and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and amateur sports, including training for international competitions, to foster self-discipline, teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;

**WHEREAS,** the Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall recognize the vital role of the youth in nation-building, promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, and nationalism, encourage involvement in public and civic affairs, and promote mental and physical fitness through sports;

**WHEREAS,** Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) is a form of martial arts that focuses on grappling and ground fighting. The direct translation of Jiu Jitsu (柔術) means "gentle art" and the goal is to control the opponent without striking them and then to submit them with chokes, arm bars, and other joint locks;

**WHEREAS,** on June 25-26, 2022, in Ayala Malls Circuit, Circuit Makati, Metropolitan Manila, sixteen (16) Bangsamoro athletes competed in the Pan Asian International Jiu Jitsu Competition;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro athletes were trained by their coaches Abdulaziz Talusan and Mohannad S. Biruar in collaboration with Triple A Bros Academy Jiu-Jitsu and Wartime MMA Gym in Cotabato City;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro athletes, with their discipline and determination, bagged eleven (11) gold medals, six (6) silver medals, and eight (8) bronze medals, bringing a total of twenty-five (25) medals back to their Bangsamoro homeland;

**WHEREAS,** among the sixteen (16) Bangsamoro athletes, Ms. Sittie Farhana N. Ebrahim won two (2) gold medals in the said event;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Government encourages our youth to engage in sports and self-defense activities and mandates the Bangsamoro Sports Commission and the Bangsamoro Youth Commission to support our athletes and youth in their athletic pursuits:

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate the Bangsamoro Martial Arts Athletes for their exceptional performance in the recent Pan Asian International Jiu Jitsu Competition in Ayala Malls Circuit, Circuit Makati, Metro Manila.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** That a copy of this resolution be presented to the concerned athletes, the Bangsamoro Sports Commission, the Bangsamoro Youth Commission, and Wartime MMA Gym, for their information.

**ADOPTED,** *Dhu Al-Hijjah 29, 1443/July 28, 2022.*
