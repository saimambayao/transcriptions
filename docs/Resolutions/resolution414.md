**RESOLUTION NO. 414**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING KAKAW MERANAW FOR WINNING THE AGENCY FOR VALORIZATION OF AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS PARIS 2023 – MEDAILLE GOURMET CHOCOLATE OF LOCAL ORIGIN**

**WHEREAS**, Section 20, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that “*the State recognizes the indispensable role of the private sector, encourages private enterprise, and provides incentives to needed investments;*”

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Parliament is vested with the duty to recognize and to commend notable Bangsamoro people and achievements that bring pride and honor to our Bangsamoro Region;

**WHEREAS**, Kakaw Meranaw, a lone contestant from the Philippines, bagged the AVPA Paris 2023 – Medaille Gourmet Award at the concluded third ($3^{\text{rd}}$) International Contest for Chocolate Processed at Origin Contest last April 25, 2023;

**WHEREAS**, Chocolates Processed at Origin International Contest is organized by the Agency for Valorization of Agricultural Products (AVPA) in order to continue to recognize that cocoa-producing countries know how to develop chocolates of quality, equal, and sometimes better than those made by major brands in consumer countries;

**WHEREAS**, this international contest is reserved for chocolatiers or their groups (cooperatives, Denominations of Origin) for which AVPA proposes a classification that allows the expression of all the traditional tastes encountered among chocolatiers in cocoa-producing countries. In its two editions, nearly two hundred (200) exceptional chocolates spread over some twenty (20) countries on four (4) continents were tasted;

**WHEREAS**, Kakaw Meranaw is a proud pioneering chocolate bar producer from Barangay Tagoranao, Bayang, Lanao del Sur, and it is a social enterprise that aims to provide livelihood and support to our dearest Maranao local farmers from the said barangay through the production of chocolate bars;

**WHEREAS**, this remarkable achievement by Kakaw Meranaw highlights the importance of recognizing and encouraging our local entrepreneurs to participate in international contests to show that our local entrepreneurs possess the capability to excel and compete globally;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Parliament acknowledges its crucial role in encouraging the development and recognition of our local entrepreneurs or enterprises that contribute to the economic development of our region;

**WHEREAS**, as part of the Bangsamoro Government’s intervention, livelihood financial assistance from the Ministry of Trade, Investments and Tourism (MTIT), through the 2022 Transitional Development Impact Fund (TDIF) of the Office of Member of Parliament Diamila Disimban Ramos, and assistance from Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST), through the 2023 TDIF of Member of Parliament Atty. Rasul Mitmug, Jr., have been extended to Kakaw Maranao:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate Kakaw Meranaw for winning the AVPA Paris 2023 – Medaille Gourmet Chocolate of Local Origin;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present this Resolution to Kakaw Meranaw, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Jumada Al-Awwal 02, 1445/November 16, 2023.
