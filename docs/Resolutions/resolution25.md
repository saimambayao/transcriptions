**RESOLUTION NO. 25**

**RESOLUTION CALLING FOR THE CONSTITUTION AND ACTIVATION OF THE DIFFERENT STATUTORY AND PARLIAMENTARY COMMITTEES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.**

**WHEREAS**, pursuant to paragraph 2, section 1, Article X of our approved Code of Parliamentary Procedure and Practices, committees are tasked to consider, make recommendations and draw reports concerning disposition of bills, resolutions, and other preparatory works to be presented to the plenary sessions of the Parliament for its action;

**WHEREAS**, sections 3 and 4 of Article X provides for the creation of Statutory and Parliamentary committees, respectively;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it ,

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament to constitute and activate the following statutory and parliamentary committees as provided and mandated for in Republic Act No. 11054 and in the Code of Parliamentary Procedures and Practices;

2. Committee on Ways and Means
