
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
**BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY**
Cotabato City

**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 10**

**RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR AN EXPENDITURES PROGRAM FOR THE APPROPRIATION OF THE TRANSITION FUNDS OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) IN THE AMOUNT OF ONE BILLION FIVE HUNDRED FIVE MILLION THREE HUNDRED FIFTY EIGHT THOUSAND NINE HUNDRED SIX PESOS (P1,505,358,906.00).**

**WHEREAS,** Section 14, Article XVI of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) provides for the initial funding for transition which shall be charged available funds in the current General Appropriation Act (GAA);

**WHEREAS,** its letter to Interim Chief Minister of the Department of Budget and Management (DBM) to Hon. ICM Al-Haj Murad Ebrahim dated April 15, 2019 provides for the guidance on how the initial funding for the implementation of priority programs of BARMM in the amount of 1.2 Billion pesos;

**WHEREAS,** in its letter dated April 15, 2019 to BARMM Interim Chief Minister Al-Haj Murad Ebrahim, in reply to the earlier query to Executive Secretary Esmael O. Pasigan, the Department of Budget and Management provided for the guidance on how the initial transition fund amounting to 1.2 billion pesos may be secured;

**WHEREAS,** in compliance to the said guidance, the AdHoc Committee has come up with an expenditure program for the said initial transition fund.

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved, to provide for an Expenditures Program for the appropriation of the transition funds of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) in the amount of One Billion Five Hundred Five Million Three Hundred Fifty Eight Thousand Nine Hundred Six Pesos (P1,505,358,906.00).

Adopted, April 24, 2019

Author: Hon MP Eduard U. Guerra
