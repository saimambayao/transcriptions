**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 461**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY-SINING KAMBAYOKA ENSEMBLE FOR WINNING THE PHILIPPINE LIVE ENTERTAINMENT, ARTS, AND FESTIVAL OUTSTANDING CULTURAL THEATER GROUP AWARD**

**WHEREAS**, the State shall foster the preservation, enrichment, and dynamic evolution of a Filipino national culture based on the principle of unity in diversity in a climate of free artistic and intellectual expression;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government shall preserve the history, culture, arts, traditions, and the rich cultural heritage of the Bangsamoro people;

**WHEREAS**, the Mindanao State University-Sining Kambayoka Ensemble (MSU-SKE) received the Philippine Live Entertainment, Arts, and Festival (LEAF) Awards as the Outstanding Cultural Theater Group at the Metropolitan Theater on February 8, 2024;

**WHEREAS**, the Philippine LEAF Awards is a prestigious award-giving body dedicated to recognizing and celebrating outstanding achievements of Filipino Artists in the fields of live entertainment, arts, fashion, and festivals. Founded on the principles of integrity, creativity, and excellence, the Philippine LEAF Awards is committed to honoring Filipino individuals and organizations that push the boundaries of artistic expression and contribute to the enrichment of Filipino culture, arts, and society;

**WHEREAS**, the Philippine LEAF Awards offers a diverse range of award categories to encompass the broad spectrum of creative talent, and among these categories is Performing Arts: Honoring outstanding contributions in dance, music, theater, and other performing arts disciplines, in which the MSU-SKE's award belongs;

**WHEREAS**, the MSU-SKE is one of the duly supervised groups of the MSU-Cultural Affairs Office of the Mindanao State University Main Campus â€“ Marawi City. The MSU-SKE is in the thrust of mounting and remounting quality theater productions based on the Mindanao tri-peoples' culture and arts particularly the Meranaw cultural community for peace advocacy and educational purposes of the University. The group was founded in 1974 through the University Board of Resolution in 1974, the creation of resident folk theater group in the University. MSU-SKE is dubbed as the Premier Cultural Theater Group of the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, dubbed as the Premier Cultural Theater Group of the Philippines, the MSU-SKE is the only folk theater group in the Philippines that established and popularized the Kambayoka Theater Form, a theater form that is based on traditional performing arts of the Meranaw;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Parliament must acknowledge, give merit, and promote creativity, and excellence in the field of promoting Bangsamoro culture through commending, inspiring, and rewarding those Bangsamoro artistic individuals or groups who are continually raising the bar in these fields:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To congratulate Mindanao State University-Sining Kambayoka Ensemble for winning the Philippine Live Entertainment, Arts, and Festival Outstanding Cultural Theater Group Award.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present this foregoing Resolution to MSU-Sining Kambayoka Ensemble, for information.

**ADOPTED**, *Dhu Al-Hijjah* 13, 1445/June 19, 2024.
