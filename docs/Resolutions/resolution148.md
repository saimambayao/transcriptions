**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 148**

**RESOLUTION EXTENDING THE HIGHEST COMMENDATION BY THE BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT TO PHILIPPINE OLYMPIANS HIDILYN DIAZ, NESTHY PETECIO, CARLO PAALAM, AND EUMIR MARCIAL FOR BRINGING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE COUNTRY DURING THE RECENTLY CONCLUDED 2020 SUMMER OLYMPICS IN TOKYO, JAPAN**

WHEREAS, from July 23, 2021 to August 8, 2021, nineteen (19) athletes representing the Republic of the Philippines competed in the 2020 Summer Olympics held in Tokyo, Japan;

WHEREAS, the Philippine delegation surpassed the previous record in the 1932 Los Angeles Olympics, bringing home four (4) medals, consisting of one (1) gold medal, two (2) silver medals, and one (1) bronze medal;

WHEREAS, on July 26, 2021, Hidilyn Diaz of Zamboanga City brought home the first-ever gold medal for the Philippines after setting an Olympic record by lifting a combined weight of 224 kilograms, and besting her opponent from China, world record holder Liao Qiuyun, in the women’s 55 kilograms division of weightlifting;

WHEREAS, on August 3, 2021, Nesthy Petecio from Santa Cruz, Davao del Sur won the silver medal in the women’s featherweight division of boxing after an intense match with her rival Sena Irie of Japan;

WHEREAS, on August 5, 2021, Eumir Marcial from Zamboanga City bagged the bronze medal in the men’s middleweight division of boxing after finishing the semi-finals match against Ukraine’s Oleksandr Khyzhniak;

WHEREAS, on August 7, 2021, Carlo Paalam from the Province of Bukidnon brought home the silver medal in the men’s flyweight division of boxing after a very close match against Great Britain’s Galal Yafai;

WHEREAS, the entire nation is grateful for their commitment to bringing honor to the country, inspiring the youth to pursue sports, and instill the values of fair play, discipline, humility, and perseverance;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Parliament wishes to express its heartfelt gratitude to the four Olympians for their hard work and determination to represent the country and their desire to excel in their respective sport events;

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, as it is hereby resolved, to extend the highest commendation by the Bangsamoro Parliament to Philippine Olympians Hidilyn Diaz, Nesthy Petecio, Carlo Paalam, and Eumir Marcial for bringing pride and honor to the country during the recently concluded 2020 Summer Olympics held in Tokyo Japan.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, to forward this Resolution to the Philippine Sports Commission (PSC) and Philippine Olympic Committee for their information.

ADOPTED, Muharram 17, 1443/August 26, 2021.
