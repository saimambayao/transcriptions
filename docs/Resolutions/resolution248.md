**RESOLUTION NO. 248**

**RESOLUTION ADOPTING THE CALENDAR OF SESSION FOR THE FOURTH REGULAR SESSION OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY**

**WHEREAS**, Section 5, Rule VI of the Parliament Rules, Procedures and Practices of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BTA-BARMM) provides that:

*“The BTA shall convene its regular session on the third Tuesday of the month following Eid’l Fitr and continue to be in session for such number of days as may be determined through a legislative calendar.”*

**WHEREAS**, Section 4, Rule XIII of the Parliament Rules, Procedures and Practices of the BTA-BARMM states that:

*“The BTA shall hold a plenary session every Tuesday to Thursday for the third and fourth week of the month that BTA is not in recess. The speaker shall open, conduct, and close the session.”*

**WHEREAS**, adopting a Calendar of Session will guide the Members of the BTA Parliament in the performance of their legislative and constituency-serving functions:
