
Republic of the Philippines

BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT

**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 147**

**RESOLUTION CALLING ON THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT TO CONSIDER THE ESTABLISHMENT OF DRUG TREATMENT AND REHABILITATION CENTERS IN STRATEGIC AREAS WITHIN THE TERRITORIAL JURISDICTION OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

WHEREAS, the Philippine Government has been exerting extraordinary effort to reduce the prevalence of illegal drug use;

WHEREAS, the two primary ways of reducing the prevalence of illegal drug use are by reducing the drug supply or reducing drug demand;

WHEREAS, drug addicts have developed behavior and ways of thinking that both enable their addiction and discourage healthier habits;

WHEREAS, one of the main benefits of going to a rehabilitation facility is the structure that it provides such as treatment programs that emphasize creating daily routines full of productive activities and counselling session to help keep patients engaged and eliminate distractions;

WHEREAS, the structure of rehabilitation establishes a simple daily schedule that makes sobriety a priority while maintaining a balanced lifestyle that patients can stick to post-treatment;

WHEREAS, the healing environment of rehabilitation offers the support needed to make a successful recovery;

WHEREAS, rehabilitation programs include individualized treatment plans to help patients identify and overcome underlying issues that started their addiction;

WHEREAS, while the main priority of a rehabilitation facility is to help patients overcome addiction, there are other benefits of rehabilitation such as learning the necessary tools for building a productive, healthy, and happy life;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency (PDEA) has officially launched a community-based reformation program known as “Balay Silangan” which builds temporary shelters to drug offenders with the objective of reforming them into self-sufficient and law-abiding members of society;

WHEREAS, the program is intended only for drug personalities who are not users and are not eligible to undergo medical treatment and rehabilitation in facilities supervised by the Department of Health;

WHEREAS, drug rehabilitation centers will be intended for users who are eligible to undergo medical treatment and rehabilitation;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Supreme Court has described the effects of drugs and wrote in one of its decisions: *“Common knowledge that drug addicts become useless if not dangerous members of society and in some instances turn up to be among the living dead”*;

WHEREAS, the war on drugs has claimed many lives;

WHEREAS, rehabilitation is an option;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Government has no established rehabilitation center in any of the provinces and cities in the Bangsamoro Region including the new geographic areas;

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, as it is hereby resolved, to call on the Bangsamoro Government to consider the establishment of drug treatment and rehabilitation centers in strategic areas within the territorial jurisdiction of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, to forward this Resolution to the Office of the Bangsamoro Chief Minister, Hon. Ahod B. Ebrahim, and Ministry of Health-BARMM for their information and appropriate consideration.

ADOPTED, Muharram 17, 1443/August 26, 2021.
