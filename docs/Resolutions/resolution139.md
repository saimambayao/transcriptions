
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 139**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE PROFOUND SYMPATHY AND SINCERE CONDOLENCES OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) ON THE DEATH OF THE HONORABLE PARLIAMENT MEMBER ALEEM MOHAMMAD ZAINODEN P. BATO**

WHEREAS, the Honorable Member of the Parliament (MP) Mohammad Zainoden P. Bato was part of the group of young Bangsamoro students in Al-Azhar University who founded the *jihad* movement that ultimately led to the Bangsamoro struggle;

WHEREAS, the Honorable MP Aleem Bato was a Bangsamoro leader who was a M’ranao Central Committee Member of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF), concurrently leading the organizations’ Shura Council;

WHEREAS, the Honorable Mohammad Zainoden P. Bato passed away and returned to the Grace of the Almighty Allah on August 15, 2021;

WHEREAS, MP Aleem Bato had been a member of the Parliament since March 2019;

WHEREAS, this Parliament commemorates MP Aleem Bato for his invaluable service to the Bangsamoro people, to the Islamic faith, and for believing and fighting for the Bangsamoro cause and the right to self-determination.

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to express its profound sympathy and sincere condolences on the death of Honorable Parliament Member Aleem Mohammad Zainoden P. Bato.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, to forward this Resolution to the bereaved family of Honorable Parliament Member Aleem Mohammad Zainoden P. Bato.

ADOPTED, Muharram 8, 1443/August 17, 2021.
