**RESOLUTION NO. 29**

**RESOLUTION AMENDING RULE VIII OF RESOLUTION NO. 6, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PARLIAMENTARY RULES, PROCEDURES, AND PRACTICES OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO, REDEFINING THE QUORUM REQUIREMENT FOR COMMITTEE MEETINGS AND HEARINGS**

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), approved in plenary session as Parliament Resolution No. 6 providing for the parliamentary rules, procedures, and practices which guides its conduct of business;

**WHEREAS,** the BTA approved the activation of the Parliamentary and Statutory Committees with the expectation that measures falling under their jurisdiction shall be deliberated therein upon proper referral by the BTA;

**WHEREAS,** desirous of the efficient discharge of functions by these committees, there is a need to ensure that they are able to regularly conduct their affairs through a lower quorum requirement which finds basis in the practice of other legislative and collegial institutions;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved, To amend Section 3, Rule VIII of Resolution No. 6, which shall henceforth read as follows:
