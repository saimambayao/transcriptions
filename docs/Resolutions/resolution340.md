SECOND REGULAR SESSION

**RESOLUTION NO. 340**

RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING AL-ZAHEER ADIL ABUBAKAR, A GRADUATE OF NOTRE DAME UNIVERSITY, FOR BEING TOP 10 IN THE RECENT LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR PROFESSIONAL TEACHERS GIVEN BY THE PROFESSIONAL REGULATION COMMISSION (PRC)

WHEREAS, Section 16, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the “Bangsamoro Organic Law” states that education shall be a top priority of the Bangsamoro Government to establish, maintain, and support a complete and integrated system of quality education, which shall be a subsystem of the national education system;

WHEREAS, on the 19th day of May 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced the results of the March 2023 Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers;

WHEREAS, with an impressive rating of 90.40 percent, Notre Dame University graduate and alumnus Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar obtained the rank of top 10 of the other 60,896 examinees out of which 24,819 have passed the licensure examination for elementary level after attending the March 2023 examination;

WHEREAS, Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar, 23 years of age, is a graduate of Notre Dame University with an academic distinction of Cum Laude, and a proud Muslim Maguindanaon hailing from Cotabato City, BARMM;

WHEREAS, he serves as a good example and inspiration for the young Bangsamoro people to emulate and excel in their chosen field of endeavors;

WHEREAS, the exemplary achievement of Mr. Abubakar is a source of inspiration for the young professionals and aspirants in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and the entire nation which merits this Parliament’s recognition and commendation;

WHEREAS, it is without a doubt that Mr. Abubakar brought honor to our region. His perseverance, dedication, and courage were evident in securing the top 10 in the Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers. It will forever serve as an inspiration not only to the people of Cotabato City but also to the millions of Bangsamoro people across the country:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To congratulate and commend Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar, a graduate of Notre Dame University for being top 10 in the recent Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers given by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC).

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That a copy of this resolution be presented to Mr. Al-Zaheer Adil Abubakar, for information.

ADOPTED, Dhul Qa’dah 04, 1444/May 25, 2023.
