**RESOLUTION ADOPTING A NEW CALENDAR OF SESSION FOR THE FIRST REGULAR SESSION OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE THE BTA RESOLUTION NO. 8 .**

**WHEREAS**, Section 5, Rule VI of the Parliamentary Rules, Procedures and Practices of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao provides:

> “The BTA shall convene its regular session once every year on the fourth Friday of March and shall end not later than thirty (30) days before the opening of the next regular session;”

**WHEREAS**, adopting this new Calendar of Session will not only guide the Members of the BTA Parliament in the performance of their legislative and constituency-servicing functions but it will also fast track their legislation;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**
