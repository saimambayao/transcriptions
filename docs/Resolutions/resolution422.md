**RESOLUTION NO. 422**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING THE ESTEEMED BANGSAMORO SCHOLARS WHO RECENTLY GRADUATED IN THE DIFFERENT FIELDS OF ISLAMIC STUDIES AT THE AL-AZHAR UNIVERSITY IN CAIRO, EGYPT**

**WHEREAS**, in the pursuit of academic excellence in the field of Islamic studies, we should recognize and celebrate the accomplishments of our graduates from the Al-Azhar University in Cairo, Egypt;

**WHEREAS**, for Academic Year 2022-2023, thirteen (13) esteemed Bangsamoro scholars in the different fields of Islamic studies completed their courses at the said university, as follows;

| List of Graduates | Course |
| :--- | :--- |
| 1. Abdulmujid Jailani Usman | *Shari`ah wal Qanoon* Major *Shari`ah Islamia* |
| 2. Lukman Limanda Hudjatun | *Shari`ah wal Qanoon* Major *Shari`ah Islamia* |
| 3. Zambas Imamil Sangkula | *Usuluddin* Major in Hadith |
| 4. Abdulkadir Kappalahu Haddaman | *Usuluddin* Major in Hadith |
| 5. Ali Mamokhan Romanggar | *Usuluddin* Major in *Tafseer* |
| 6. Asma Pindilehen Lucman | *Shari`ah wal Qanoon* Major in *Qanoon* |
| 7. Nassar Salsal Aguam | *Kulliyyatu Da`wah* |
| 8. Jamal Palao Dimasipun | *Usuluddin* Major in Hadith |
| 9. Aslanie Domangcag Jalal | *Shari`ah Islamia* |
| 10. Normisbah Isah Abdulmohaimen | *Shari`ah Islamia* |
| 11. Najib Sarip Noor | *Shari`ah Islamia* |
| 12. Abdullah Polao Alang | *Shari`ah Islamia* |
| 13. Asfahanie Asim Abutazil | *Shari`ah wal Qanoon* Major in *Shari`ah Islamia* |

**WHEREAS**, Islamic scholars are the backbone of Bangsamoro liberation, and the remarkable achievements of these scholars have contributed great pride and honor to the Islamic community in general and to the Bangsamoro in particular;

**WHEREAS**, Islamic knowledge and propagation are greatly contributing in the uplifting of human welfare and well-being of Muslims in this world and in the hereafter, and the graduation of these Islamic scholars shall yield further strength and motivation to the Bangsamoro; and

**WHEREAS**, in recognition of their sacrifices, hard work, and perseverance, it is but proper to recognize the efforts of the foregoing esteemed Islamic scholars in their quest for eternal knowledge that is indispensable to the moral and spiritual liberation of the Bangsamoro:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To commend the esteemed Bangsamoro scholars who recently graduated in the different fields of Islamic studies at the Al-Azhar University in Cairo, Egypt.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To furnish a copy of this Resolution to the above-mentioned esteemed Bangsamoro scholars, for information.

**ADOPTED**, *Rajab* 03, 1445/January 15, 2024.
