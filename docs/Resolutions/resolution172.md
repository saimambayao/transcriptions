**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 172**

**RESOLUTION URGING THE DIFFERENT MINISTRIES, OFFICES, AND THE OFFICES OF THE MEMBERS OF PARLIAMENT OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT TO ADOPT GOVERNMENT INTERNSHIP PROGRAM (GIP) IN THEIR REGULAR PROGRAM AND ALLOCATE FUNDS FOR THE 25% GOVERNMENT COUNTERPART THERETO**

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) reiterates the recognition of the vital role of the youth in nation-building and mandate the Bangsamoro promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, intellectual, and social well-being;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Government through the Bureau of Employment Promotion and Welfare (BEPW) of the Ministry of Labor and Employment (MOLE) includes the GIP as one of its regular programs with the end goal of promoting the youthâ€™s exposure to public service;

WHEREAS, the interns of the said program will be assigned to different ministries or offices of the government and their stipend/allowance shall be paid based on the existing minimum wage for the service rendered of which 75% of the stipend/allowance shall be paid by the MOLE while the remaining 25% thereof shall be paid by the partner/employer;

WHEREAS, the duration of the internship under a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between MOLE and the GIP Partner /Employer shall be nine (9) months;

WHEREAS, most of the partner/employer-to-be in the Bangsamoro government cannot accommodate an intern due to lack of fund allocation in their annual budget appropriation:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, to urge the different ministries, offices, and the offices of the Members of Parliament of the Bangsamoro Government to adopt Government Internship Program in their regular program and allocate funds for the 25% government counterpart thereto.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, to forward the foregoing Resolution to the different ministries and offices of the Bangsamoro Government, for information and appropriate consideration.

ADOPTED, Rabi ul Awwal 5, 1443/October 12, 2021.
