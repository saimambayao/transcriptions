**RESOLUTION NO. 423**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING THE NEW LAWYERS IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION WHO SUCCESSFULLY PASSED THE 2023 BAR EXAMINATION**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro community has continually produced individuals of exceptional talent, dedication, and commitment to the legal profession;

**WHEREAS**, the recent successful passage of aspiring lawyers from the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in the 2023 Bar Examination stands as a testament to their unwavering determination, hard work, and scholarly dedication;

**WHEREAS**, a total number of three thousand eight hundred twelve (3,812) out of ten thousand three hundred eighty-seven (10,387) or 36.77% of examinees passed the 2023 Bar Examinations on December 5, 2023;

**WHEREAS**, Mindanao State University-Marawi and Notre Dame University in Cotabato City, the only existing law schools within the territory of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, produced twenty-three (23) and thirty-two (32) successful Bar passers, respectively, in the 2023 Bar Examinations;

**WHEREAS**, these new lawyers from the Bangsamoro Region have demonstrated outstanding academic achievement, ethical standards, and legal expertise, showcasing their capability to contribute meaningfully to the legal landscape of our nation;

**WHEREAS**, their success not only signifies personal accomplishment but also reflects the collective support and encouragement from their families, mentors, educators, and the entire Bangsamoro community;

**WHEREAS**, we, in the Parliament express our pride in their accomplishments and offer our unwavering support as they embark on this noble profession, aiming to make meaningful contributions to the legal community and the advancement of justice in our society:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate the new lawyers in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region who successfully passed the 2023 Bar Examination.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present this Resolution to the new lawyers in Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, for information.

**ADOPTED**, *Rajab* 03, 1445/January 15, 2024.
