**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 468**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING THE BANGSAMORO TOPNOTCHERS, NAMELY: MR. ABDEL-NASHER SARAIL TANGGOR, MS. NORJANNAH KHALID TANOGA, MR. RUSTOM DIPALING LAMOSAO, AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS IN THE MAY 2024 LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANTS**

**WHEREAS**, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) on June 1, 2024 released the official result of the May 2024 Licensure Examination for Certified Public Accountants, conducted across the various testing centers nationwide from May 26 to 28, 2024;

**WHEREAS**, out of ten thousand four hundred twenty-one (10,421) examinees, three thousand one hundred fifty-five (3,155) or thirty point two seven five percent (30.275%) passed the said licensure examination given by the Board of Accountancy in National Capital Region, Baguio, Butuan, Cagayan de Oro, Cebu, Davao, Iloilo, Koronadal, Legazpi, Lucena, Pagadian, Pampanga, Rosales, Tacloban, Tuguegarao, Zamboanga, and Puerto Princesa, Palawan;

**WHEREAS**, out of the successful passers, three (3) young Bangsamoro garnered the highest scores and ranked as topnotchers, namely:

1. Mr. Abdel-Nasher Sarail Tanggor (Top 7), with a general average of 89.67%,
2. Ms. Norjannah Khalid Tanoga (Top 7), with a general average of 89.67%, and
3. Mr. Rustom Dipaling Lamosao (Top 10), with a general average of 89.17%;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Tanggor is a graduate of Mindanao State University - Iligan Institute of Technology (MSU-IIT) and resident of Bayang, Lanao del Sur; Ms. Tanoga is a graduate from Mindanao State University, (MSU) Marawi City and resident of Tanghal Subdivision, Bagua 2, Cotabato City; and Mr. Lamosao is a graduate of Notre Dame University (NDU) and resident of Rosary Heights 5, Cotabato City;

**WHEREAS**, being included in the top 10 in any given licensure examination is a rare and prestigious achievement that gives utmost pride and honor not only to the family, school, and tribe, but also to the entire Bangsamoro community;

**WHEREAS**, their exemplary performance as topnotchers serves as an inspiration for the Bangsamoro children, youth, and all individuals who are striving for excellence, not only in the Bangsamoro region but also in the entire country:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To congratulate and commend the Bangsamoro topnotchers, namely: Mr. Abdel-Nasher Sarail Tanggor, Ms. Norjannah Khalid Tanoga, Mr. Rustom Dipaling Lamosao, and all Bangsamoro passers in the May 2024 Licensure Examination for Certified Public Accountants.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present a copy of this Resolution to Mr. Abdel-Nasher Sarail Tanggor, Ms. Norjannah Khalid Tanoga, and Mr. Rustom Dipaling Lamosao, for information.

**ADOPTED**, *Dhu Al-Hijjah* 21, 1445/June 27, 2024.
