**RESOLUTION NO. 382**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING LIEUTENANT GENERAL ROY M. GALIDO FOR BEING APPOINTED AS THE 66$^{\text{TH}}$ COMMANDING GENERAL OF THE PHILIPPINE ARMY**

**WHEREAS**, President Ferdinand “Bongbong” Marcos Jr. has appointed Lieutenant General Roy M. Galido as the 66$^{\text{th}}$ commanding general of the Philippine Army;

**WHEREAS**, Lt. Gen. Galido replaced Gen. Romeo Brawner Jr., who was recently named as the chief of the Armed Forces of the Philippines;

**WHEREAS**, Lt. Gen. Galido was a member of the Philippine Military Academy’s *“Bigkis Lahi”* Class of 1990, a characteristic he also shares with fellow *Mistah* (Batchmate) Gen. Brawner Jr.;

**WHEREAS**, he was assigned in Mindanao as a young lieutenant at the 6$^{\text{th}}$ Infantry Division (ID), also known as the *“Kampilan”* Division;

**WHEREAS**, he then became the commander of the 40th Infantry Battalion and headed the 601$^{\text{st}}$ Infantry Brigade;

**WHEREAS**, he returned to the 6$^{\text{th}}$ ID succeeding fellow *Bigkis Lahi Mistah* Major General Roberto S. Capulong as the Commander from August 2022 to January 2023;

**WHEREAS**, he then led the Western Mindanao Command (WESTMINCOM), which oversees military operations in the Zamboanga Peninsula (Region 9), Northern Mindanao (Region 10), and the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

**WHEREAS**, as the Command Chief, the WESTMINCOM has experienced several successful campaigns against terrorism and lawlessness under his supervision;

**WHEREAS**, in light of the achievements mentioned above, he selflessly credited to the progress and momentum through the “hard work and dedication of our soldiers, the indefatigable support of our partners, stakeholders, and the peace-loving people of Mindanao”;

**WHEREAS**, the WESTMINCOM also achieved a relatively high Annual General Inspection (AGI) score of 93%, an achievement he attributes to the “hardworking officers, enlisted personnel, and civilian human resources of the command”;

**WHEREAS**, the Department of National Defense (DND) spokesperson Arsenio Andolong said that the extensive experience and well-honed skills of Lt. Gen. Galido resulted in the significant victories of his command against lawless elements, especially in Mindanao;

**WHEREAS**, he added that, “With Lt. Gen. Galido at the helm, the Philippine Army will undoubtedly and excellently pursue the strategic path set by the Commander-in-Chief in pursuit of the country’s stability amid the changing security landscape.”; and

**WHEREAS**, amidst his appointment, Lt. Gen. Roy Galido recently vowed to sustain peace and development efforts, especially in Mindanao;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), as the interim government of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, extends it commendations to the newly appointed Commanding General of the Philippine Army:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To congratulate and commend Lieutenant General Roy M. Galido for being appointed as the 66$^{\text{th}}$ Commanding General of the Philippine Army.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present this Resolution to Lieutenant General Roy M. Galido, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Muharram 27, 1445/August 14, 2023.
