**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 203**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING SULTAN ABANTAS ASAALI FOR HIS HISTORIC, OUTSTANDING, AND EXEMPLARY PERFORMANCE, HAVING PLACED THIRD IN THE DECEMBER 2021 RADIOLOGIC TECHNOLOGIST LICENSURE EXAMINATION**

**WHEREAS**, on January 4, 2022, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) released the results of the December 2021 Radiologic Technologist (RadTech) Licensure Examination, given by the Board of Radiologic Technology in Saint Joseph College, Quezon City on December 20 and 21, 2021;

**WHEREAS**, with an impressive rating of 90.20%, Universidad de Zamboanga – School of Allied Medicine graduate Sultan Abantas Asaali obtained the examination’s third-highest mark, besting 2,177 other examinees. As announced, only 786 out of 2,177, or 36.10%, passed the exams;

**WHEREAS**, Sultan Abantas Asaali, a native of Simunul in the Province of Tawi-Tawi, graduated from the Mindanao State University-Tawi-Tawi College of Technology and Oceanography (MSU-TCTO) Preparatory High School under Class 2013;

**WHEREAS**, the December 2021 RadTech Licensure Examination was administered in testing centers in Metro Manila, Baguio, Cagayan de Oro, Cebu, Davao, Iloilo, Legaspi, Lucena, Rosales, Tacloban, Tuguegarao, and Zamboanga;

**WHEREAS**, his success brings immense pride and honor to the region, as he demonstrated the ingrained values of excellence, determination, and hard work of the Bangsamoro people. His achievement deserves to be commended by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority;

**WHEREAS**, the triumph of Sultan Abantas Asaali shines as an inspiration not only to the youth but to the general Bangsamoro population to pursue their dreams with courage and purpose. His impressive performance, despite the difficulties in preparation amid a pandemic, proves that anyone can conquer seemingly insurmountable odds and achieve greatness with perseverance and discipline:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to commend and congratulate Sultan Abantas Asaali for his historic, outstanding, and exemplary performance, having placed third in the December 2021 Radiologic Technologist Licensure Examination.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER** to forward the foregoing Resolution to Sultan Abantas Asaali, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Jumada Al-Akhirah 21, 1443/January 25, 2022.
