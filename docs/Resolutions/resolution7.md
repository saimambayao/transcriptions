**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 7**

**RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING THE HONORABLE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) MINISTER OF FINANCE EDUARD U. GUERRA TO TRANSACT WITH THE DEPARTMENT OF BUDGET AND MANAGEMENT (DBM) AND OTHER PERTINENT AGENCIES OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT FOR THE IMMEDIATE RELEASE OF THE TRANSITION FUNDS ALLOCATED FOR THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA).**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) had been formally constituted on February 22, 2019 with the appointment of the members of the BTA and has since performed its functions;

**WHEREAS**, in order to ensure the smooth flow of the operation of the Parliament, the ministries and other offices of the BTA, there is an urgent need for the release of the transition funds appropriated by the national government for the current year;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, to authorize the Honorable Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Minister of Finance Eduard U. Guerra to transact with the Department of Budget and Management (DBM) and other pertinent agencies of the national government for the immediate release of the transition funds allocated for the BTA.

**Adopted, May 22, 2019**
