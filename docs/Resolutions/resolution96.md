**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 96**

**RESOLUTION APPROVING THE ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE OF BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY (BTA) PARLIAMENT**

**WHEREAS**, under Section 4, Rule VII of BTA Resolution No. 6, the Committee on Accounts and Audit (CAA) exercises "jurisdiction over all matters relating to the income, revenues and internal auditing of the funds for all the expenditures and activities of the BTA, and the other ministries, agencies and offices of the Bangsamoro Government." The same provision also confers the Committee with "jurisdiction to examine and scrutinize the income, revenues, and the expenditure plans of all government ministries, bureaus and agencies of the Bangsamoro Government, including the audit thereof;

**WHEREAS**, pursuant to its mandate, the CAA held a committee meeting on 27 October 2020 to deliberate on the Proposed Organizational Structure of the BTA Parliament, as submitted by the Office of the Speaker;

**WHEREAS**, after due deliberation, the CAA submits its Committee Report No. 42 to the BTA, dated 27 October 2020, recommending the approval of the Proposed Organizational Structure of the BTA Parliament, as amended;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to approve the Organizational Structure of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament.

**ADOPTED**, December 16, 2020.
