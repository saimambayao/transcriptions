**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 403**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING THE YOUNG BANGSAMORO REPRESENTATIVES FOR AN OUTSTANDING ACHIEVEMENT IN THAILAND INTERNATIONAL MATHEMATICAL OLYMPIAD FINAL ROUND 2022-2023 AND HONG KONG INTERNATIONAL MATHEMATICAL OLYMPIAD FINAL ROUND 2023, THEREBY BRINGING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, Section 13, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that the State recognizes the vital role of the youth in nation-building and shall promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, intellectual, and social well-being. It shall inculcate in the youth patriotism and nationalism, and encourage their involvement in public and civic affairs;

**WHEREAS**, Section 17, Article II of the 1987 Philippine Constitution Philippine also provides that the State shall give priority to education, science and technology, arts, culture, and sports to foster patriotism and nationalism, accelerate social progress, and promote total human liberation and development;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government recognizes the vital role of women and youth, and the valuable contributions of differently abled persons in nation-building and shall promote and protect their physical, moral, spiritual, intellectual, cultural, and social well-being. It shall inculcate in the minds of the youth patriotism and nationalism, and encourage their involvement in public and civic affairs;

**WHEREAS**, Thailand International Mathematical Olympiad (TIMO) is an annual Mathematical Olympiad Competition organized by the Olympiad Champion Education Center and the Thailand Mathematics Society which provides an opportunity for all students with a strong interest in Mathematics to participate in Mathematical Olympiad Competition. Its platform is to stimulate and foster young people’s interest in learning mathematics, strengthen their creative thinking, widen their international perspective, and promote the development of primary and secondary education and exchange of educational cultures throughout countries/regions;

**WHEREAS**, Hong Kong International Mathematical Olympiad (HKIMO) is an annual international Olympiad competition organized by Olympiad Education from Hong Kong and is developed by a team of former International Mathematical Olympiad winners from Hong Kong which aims to create a platform for all math enthusiasts around the world to come together for an educational and cultural exchange. The objective of HKIMO is to foster students’ interest in the Mathematical Olympiad and to promote Mathematical Olympiad all over the world;

**WHEREAS**, the TIMO Final Round 2022-2023 was concluded last April 1-2, 2023 at Pattaya, Thailand, while the HKIMO Final Round 2023 was concluded last August 25-28, 2023 at Hong Kong. Both TIMO and HKIMO consist of five (5) topics such as Logical Thinking, Arithmetic/Algebra, Number Theory, Geometry, and Combinatorics;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government congratulates and commends the following young Bangsamoro representatives who displayed brilliance and excellence in the field of mathematics by bringing home awards in the said Mathematical Olympiad, to wit;

   Silver Awardee Category Primary 4 HKIMO Final Round 2023

**WHEREAS**, these young Bangsamoro representatives are qualified to join the 2024 World International Mathematical Olympiad as a result of their triumph in the TIMO Final Round 2022-2023 and HKIMO 2023;

**WHEREAS**, the success of these young Bangsamoro representatives is a pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people, and serves as an inspiration to our Bangsamoro youth;

**WHEREAS**, it is appropriate to recognize their outstanding performance and contribution that brought pride, honor, and glory to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate and commend the young Bangsamoro representatives for an outstanding achievement in Thailand International Mathematical Olympiad Final Round 2022-2023 and Hong Kong International Mathematical Olympiad Final Round 2023, thereby bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people;

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To furnish a copy of this Resolution to the above-mentioned young Bangsamoro representatives, for information.
