**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 539**

**RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING ALL PARLIAMENTARY AND STATUTORY COMMITTEES OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT TO CONDUCT MEETINGS, HEARINGS, AND CONSULTATIONS DURING THE RECESS OF THE SECOND BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT**

**WHEREAS**, the Third Regular Session of the Second Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament will adjourn *sine die* on *Sha’ban* 21, 1446/February 20, 2025, per Memorandum Order No. 0225-544, Series of 2025;

**WHEREAS**, there are pending deliberations on priority codes such as the Bangsamoro Revenue Code, the Bangsamoro Gender and Development Code, and other important bills and resolutions before the different committees of the BTA Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, the expeditious consideration of these pending measures is in the best interest of the Bangsamoro:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To authorize all Parliamentary and Statutory Committees of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament to conduct meetings, hearings, and consultations during the recess of the Second Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That this Resolution shall take effect on the date of its adoption and shall remain in force until amended or repealed.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, To furnish a copy of this Resolution to the Parliamentary and Statutory Committees of the BTA Parliament, for their information and ready reference.

**ADOPTED**, *Sha’ban* 19, 1446/February 18, 2025.
