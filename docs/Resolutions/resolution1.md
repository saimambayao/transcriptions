**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 1**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE CONSENSUS OF THE BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT ON THE APPOINTMENT OF THE HONORABLE KHALIPHA USMAN NANDO AS THE WALI OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT 11054, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE BANGSAMORO ORGANIC LAW.**

**WHEREAS**, Section 1, Article VIII of Republic Act No. 11054 provides for the appointment by the Parliament of the Wali who shall serve as the ceremonial head of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority has selected the Honorable Khalipha Usman Nando as the Wali of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS**, the Honorable Nando is a graduate of Islamic Theology from the Azhar University in Cairo, Egypt;

**WHEREAS**, he possesses the following qualifications: chairman of the Bangsamoro Consultative Assembly for two years; chairman of the committee on education of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front Central Committee for 16 years; Chief Justice of the MILF Shariah Supreme Court for 14 years now; senior member of the MILF Central Committee and Jihad Executive Council; and member of the Ceasefire Committee Eastern Cotabato Region;

**WHEREAS**, the Honorable Nando meets the qualifications for the Wali as provided under RA 11054;

**WHEREAS**, this august Parliament believes that the Honorable Nando has the moral ascendancy to represent the Bangsamoro Government as its ceremonial head.

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, To express the consensus of the Bangsamoro Parliament on the appointment of the Honorable Khalipha Usman Nando as the Wali of the Bangsamoro government pursuant to Republic Act 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law.

**Adopted, March 29, 2019.**
