**AN ACT**
**CREATING AND ESTABLISHING AN INFIRMARY HOSPITAL IN TIPO-TIPO MUNICIPALITY, PROVINCE OF BASILAN, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS TIPO TIPO COMMUNITY HOSPITAL, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

Be enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region Muslim in Mindanao (BARMM).

**SECTION 1. Title.** – This act shall be known as the “**Tipo - Tipo Community Hospital (TCH) Act of 2024**”.

**SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy.** – It is the policy of the Bangsamoro Government to protect and promote the right to health of the people and instill health consciousness among them by making health services available, accessible, and affordable for all. Further, the Bangsamoro Government is mandated to uphold the people’s right to have access to essential good health, and other social services that promote their well-being.

**SECTION 3. Creation.** – There is hereby established an Infirmary hospital in Tipo Tipo Municipality, Province of Basilan, otherwise known as “**Tipo-tipo Community Hospital**”

**SECTION 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program.** – The establishment of the Tipo Tipo Community Hospital shall integrate into its system of operations all pertinent and relevant provisions of Republic Act No. 11233, otherwise known as the Universal Health Care Act.

**SECTION 5. Administration.** - The TCH shall be under the administrative supervision of the Ministry of Health (MOH) who is hereby authorized to determine and approve the organizational structure for the effective and efficient management and operation of the hospital.

**SECTION 6. Appropriation.** – The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act for the Bangsamoro. Thereafter, funds necessary for the maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be provided under the provision of the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act and other pertinent laws.

**SECTION 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations.** - Within sixty (60) days from the effectivity of this Act, the Ministry of Health and Ministry of Finance, Budget, and Management shall promulgate the corresponding Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) for the effective implementation of this Act.

**SECTION 8. Separability Clause.** - Should any provision herein be declared unconstitutional or the application of such provision to any person or circumstances is held invalid, the other provisions or parts thereof which are inconsistent thereof thereby shall remain to be in full force and effect.

**SECTION 9. Repealing Clause.** - All other regional acts, decrees, instructions, orders, rules, and regulations or parts thereof which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

**SECTION 10. Effectivity.** - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in at least one (1) national newspaper and one (1) local newspaper of general circulation.
