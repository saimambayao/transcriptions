**THE GOVERNMENT OF THE DAY**

**AN ACT TO DECLARE THE BURIAL SITE OF THE LATE SHEIKH SALAMAT HASHIM, MILF FOUNDING CHAIRMAN, AND THE LATE ALIM ABDUL AZIZ MIMBANTAS, MILF VICE CHAIRMAN & VICE CHAIRMAN FOR MILITARY AFFAIRS, AS “HASHIM AND MIMBANTAS MEMORIAL SITE”, TO PROVIDE FOR THE PRESERVATION OF THE SAME, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in Parliament assembled:

**SECTION 1.** The burial site of Sheikh Salamat Hashim and Alim Abdul Aziz Mimbantas in Camp Bushra Somiorang in the Municipality of Butig, Lanao del Sur, BARMM, Philippines is hereby declared a Bangsamoro Historical Shrine and shall be known as the “Hashim and Mimbantas Memorial Site”.

**SECTION 2.** The memorial site shall be preserved and/or reconstructed whenever necessary as much as possible in their original form.

**SECTION 3.** The Bangsamoro Commission on the Preservation of Cultural Heritage shall exercise supervision and control over the reconstruction and/or preservation of the aforesaid memorial site and shall issue rules and regulations to effectuate the preceding sections of this Act.

**SECTION 4.** Necessary funds for the purposes of this Act shall be provided for in the annual appropriations for public works and disbursements shall be made by the Bangsamoro Commission on the Preservation of Cultural Heritage.

**SECTION 5.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
