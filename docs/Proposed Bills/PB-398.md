**BTA PB No. 398**

**AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE GRANT OF RAMADHAN BONUS AS AN ADDITIONAL YEARLY BENEFIT TO ALL EMPLOYEES OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR**

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament assembled:

**ARTICLE I.**
**GENERAL PROVISIONS**

**Section 1. Short Title.** – This Act shall be known as the **“Ramadhan Bonus Act of 2025.”**

**Section 2. Declaration of Policy** – It is hereby declared the policy of the Bangsamoro Government to promote the general welfare of its officials and employees by exercising the powers expressly granted to it, those necessarily implied therefrom, and those essential to the attainment of efficient and effective governance. In pursuance of this policy, the grant of a Ramadhan Bonus as an annual benefit is hereby institutionalized as a recognition of their dedicated service and as a means of supporting their well-being during the observance of the Holy Month of Ramadhan.

**ARTICLE II.**
**GRANT OF RAMADHAN BONUS**

**Section 3. Grant of Ramadhan Bonus.** – A Ramadhan Bonus, as an additional yearly benefit, shall be granted to all qualified officials and employees of the Bangsamoro Government in an amount not less than Ten Thousand Pesos (₱10,000.00) for each qualified employee.

**Section 4. Coverage.** – This Act shall cover all officials and employees of the ministries, offices, and agencies of the Bangsamoro Government, including those engaged through Contract of Service (COS) or Job Order (JO), subject to the conditions under the Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR).

**Section 5. Exclusions.** – The following personnel shall not be entitled to the Ramadhan Bonus:

a. Those who are absent without official leave (AWOL);

b. Those engaged as consultants, experts, apprentices, student laborers, or through institutional COS arrangements not considered part of the organic manpower of the Bangsamoro Government.

**ARTICLE III.**
**FUNDING SOURCE AND IMPLEMENTATION**

**Section 6. Funding Source and Release.** – The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be charged against the appropriate funds under the General Appropriations Act of the Bangsamoro Government.

**Section 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations.** – Within thirty (30) days from the effectivity of this Act, the Ministry of Finance, and Budget and Management (MFBM), in consultation with concerned ministries, offices, and agencies, shall promulgate the IRR for the effective implementation of this Act.

The MFBM may issue supplemental guidelines on an annual basis as may be necessary to ensure the orderly release of the Ramadhan Bonus.

**ARTICLE IV.**
**FINAL PROVISIONS**

**Section 9. Separability Clause.** – If any provision of this Act is declared unconstitutional or invalid, the provisions not affected thereby shall remain in full force and effect.

**Section 10. Repealing Clause.** – All laws, orders, rules and regulations which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed, modified, superseded, or amended accordingly.

**Section 11. Effectivity.** – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days following its complete publication in a newspaper of general circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.
