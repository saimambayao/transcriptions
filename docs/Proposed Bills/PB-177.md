**AN ACT CREATING A MUJAHIDEEN MEMORIAL WALL IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR**

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament in session assembled:

**Article 1. Short title.** This Act shall be known as the “Memorial Wall of Mujahideen Act”

**Article 2. Declaration of Policy.** Pursuant to Section 24, Article IX of the Bangsamoro Organic Law, “The Bangsamoro Government shall preserve the history, culture, arts, traditions, and rich cultural heritage of the Bangsamoro people xxx”.

**Article 3. Mujahideen Memorial Wall.** The Bangsamoro Commission for the Preservation of Cultural Heritage shall establish the Mujahideen Memorial Wall in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. The Wall shall include a list of names of certified Moro Islamic Liberation Front and Moro National Liberation Front members who died in the Bangsamoro struggle to fight for the Right to Self-determination of the Moros.

**Article 4. Committee.** –there shall be created a Committee that will establish eligibility criteria for standard verification in the identification of the Mujahideen. The Committee shall be composed of five (5) members appointed by the Chief Minister. They shall provide the Bangsamoro Commission for the Preservation of Cultural Heritage (BCPCH) with the final list of names for inclusion on the Mujahideen Memorial Wall.

**Article 5. Appropriations.** — The funds necessary to implement this Act shall be charged against the general appropriations of the Bangsamoro (GAAB).

**Article 6. Repealing Clause.** — All regional ordinances, resolutions, orders, memoranda, and other issuances not consistent with this Act are hereby altered, amended, or repealed accordingly.

**Article 7. Separability Clause.** – If for certain reasons some provisions of this Act are found invalid or unconstitutional, all other provisions hereof not affected thereby shall remain in full force and effect.

**Article 8. Effectivity.** — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
