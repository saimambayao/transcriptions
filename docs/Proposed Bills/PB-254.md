**AN ACT**
**STRENGTHENING AND INSTITUTIONALIZING THE “BRIGADA ESKWELA PROGRAM” IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

*Be it enacted by the Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao assembled:*

**Section 1. Title.** – This Act shall be known as the “*Brigada Eskwela Program Act of 2023*”.

**Section 2. Declaration of Policy.** – It shall be the declared policy of the Bangsamoro Government to establish, maintain, and support a complete and integrated system of quality basic education relevant and responsive to the needs, ideals, and aspirations of the Bangsamoro people. Towards this end, the Bangsamoro Government shall mandate all the schools operating within the Bangsamoro Region to participate in the Brigada Eskwela Program in order to ensure that schools are adequately prepared for the upcoming school year.

**Section 3. Brigada Eskwela Program.** – There is hereby created and institutionalized in all public schools, a Brigada Eskwela Program, herein referred to as the Program. It shall be the streamlined and central approach in engaging all the education stakeholders to contribute their time, efforts, and resources in ensuring that all public schools in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region are all set and ready for the class opening.

The program to be mandated at all public schools operating within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region shall include the following:

a) Conduct of orientation activities for school heads;
b) Assessment of the needs of the school;
c) Implementation of the maintenance and repair activities;
d) Monitor accomplishments of the identified needs of the school through submission of accomplishment report; and
e) Conduct of trainings and other relevant seminars to fully sustain the Program.

The program shall be enforced yearly and scheduled two (2) to three (3) weeks prior to the opening of classes without awaiting the release of annual Memorandum or Order from the Department of Education (DepEd) and the Ministry of Basic, Higher, and Technical Education (MBHTE).

**Section 4. Brigada Eskwela Committee.** – There is hereby created a Committee, which shall plan, provide guidance, and monitor all the public schools operating within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region to comply with the *Brigada Eskwela Program*.

The Committee shall be headed by the Minister of Basic, Higher, and Technical Education. The following shall be its members:

a) Minister, Ministry of the Interior and Local Government;
b) Minister, Ministry of Social Services and Development;
c) Chairperson of the Bangsamoro Youth Commission; and
d) Chairperson of the Bangsamoro Women Commission.

The Committee shall create a framework that will serve as the blueprint in the implementation of the Brigada Eskwela Program.

**Section 5. Functions of the Committee.** – The Committee shall have the following functions:

a) Formulate the *Brigada Eskwela Program*;
b) Create a framework that will serve as a blueprint in the implementation of the *Brigada Eskwela Program*;
c) Increase public awareness, education, and information on the benefits of the Brigada Eskwela Program; and
d) Prepare an accomplishment report of the Program to be submitted yearly to the Parliament.

**Section 6. Implementation and Coordination.** – The MBHTE shall have the primary responsibility in the administration and implementation of the *Brigada Eskwela Program*. To ensure the successful implementation of the Program, the following shall also be responsible of the following tasks:

a) The Ministry of Interior and Local Government (MILG) shall encourage the support of the local government units (LGU) as partners of MBHTE in the implementation of the Program;

b) The Ministry of Social Services and Development (MSSD) shall support the MBHTE in the implementation of the Program. It shall support community activities through its food for work or cash for work program;

c) The Bangsamoro Youth Commission (BYC) shall encourage, advocate, and mobilize the youth to take part in the implementation of the Program; and

d) The Bangsamoro Women Commission (BWC) shall encourage women or parents in the implementation of the Program.

**Section 7. Annual Report to the Parliament.** – The Committee shall submit a Report to the Parliament within one year from the implementation of this Act, and every year thereafter, on the progress of the program.

**Section 8. Coverage of the Program.** – This Act shall cover all the public schools operating within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.

Public schools shall refer to primary and secondary educational institutions established and administered by the government as defined in Section 26 of Batas Pambansa (BP) Bilang 232. All other schools are to be considered private schools.

**Section 9. Implementing Rules and Regulation.** – Within sixty (60) days from the promulgation of this Act, the necessary rules and regulations for the proper implementation of its provision shall be formulated by the Ministry of Basic, Higher, and Technical Education (MBHTE).

**Section 10. Appropriations.** – The funds necessary for the strengthening and institutionalizing of the Brigada Eskwela Program in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region shall be included in the annual appropriations of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and submitted to the Parliament for its approval.

**Section 11. Separability Clause.** – If for any cause, any part of this Act is declared unconstitutional or contrary to the provisions of Bangsamoro Organic Law, the rest of the provisions shall remain in force and effect.

**Section 12. Repealing Clause.** – All laws, orders, rules, and regulations which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act, are hereby repealed, modified, superseded, or amended accordingly.

**Section 13. Effectivity.** – This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following its complete publication in a newspaper of general circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.
