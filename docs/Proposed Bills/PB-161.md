**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF LUUK DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN TUBIG PUTIH, MUNICIPALITY OF LUUK, PROVINCE OF SULU, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in Parliament assembled:

**SEC. 1. Short Title.** – This Act shall be known as the **“UPGRADING OF LUUK HOSPITAL ACT OF 2023.”**

**SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy.** – It is the policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to provide for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services.

**SEC. 3. Increase of Bed Capacity of the Luuk District Hospital.** – The authorized bed capacity of the Luuk District Hospital is hereby increased from Twenty-Five (25) to Fifty (50) beds. Any future increase in bed capacity shall be consistent with its hospital development plan.

**SEC. 4. Health Services, Facilities, and Workforce.** - The existing professional health care services and facilities of the Luuk District Hospital are also hereby upgraded to conform with and be commensurate to the increase in bed capacity.

The existing workforce of the Luuk Hospital, including doctors, nurses and support personnel shall be correspondingly increased to conform to the upgrade.

The existing medical personnel shall also be correspondingly increased. The Ministry of Health, in coordination with the Ministry of Finance, Budget, and Management and the Civil Service Commission, shall determine the additional plantilla positions to be created and filled up in coordination with the management of the Luuk District Hospital.

The complementary infrastructure, facilities and medical equipment shall also be correspondingly constructed and procured in conformity with the increased bed capacity.

**SEC. 5. Appropriations.** – The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act. Thereafter, funds necessary for the maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be provided in accordance with the provision of the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act and other pertinent laws.

**SEC. 6. Repealing Clause.** - All regional laws, decrees, executive orders, rules and regulations which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed, amended or modified accordingly.

**SEC. 7. Separability Clause.** - If any provision of this Act is declared unconstitutional or invalid by a court of competent jurisdiction, all other parts or provisions not affected thereby shall continue to remain in full force and effect.

**SEC. 8. Effectivity.** - This act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its complete publication in at least two (2) regional newspapers of general circulation.
