**AN ACT**
**ESTABLISHING IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DATU SAUDI AMPATUAN, PROVINCE OF MAGUINDANAO DEL SUR, A LEVEL I 25 BED CAPACITY GENERAL HOSPITAL KNOWN AS DATU SAUDI AMPATUAN MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Interim Parliament

**Section 1. Title.** This Act shall be known as the “DATU SAUDI AMPATUAN Municipal Hospital Act of 2025.”

**Section 2. Declaration of Policy.** It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to provide for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services. Towards this end, and in order to adequately respond to emergencies, disasters and emerging diseases, the Bangsamoro Government shall protect the interest and promote the well-being and the medical needs of the Bangsamoro People.

**Section 3. DATU SAUDI AMPATUAN Municipal Hospital.** - There shall be established a twenty (25) bed capacity municipal hospital in the Municipality of DATU SAUDI AMPATUAN to be known as the DSAMH.

**Section 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program.** - The establishment of the DSAMH shall integrate into its system of operations all pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic Act No. 11223 otherwise known as the Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

**Section 5. Administration, Management, and Operation.** - The DSAMH shall remain under the administrative supervision and control of the Ministry of Health which shall approve the appropriate organizational structure and personnel, hospital plans, programs, and activities for its effective management and operation of the herein-mentioned upgraded hospital.

**Section 6. Appropriations.** - The Ministry of shall immediately include in the Ministry’s program the implementation of this Act, the necessary funding of which shall be included in the Annual Bangsamoro Appropriations Act.

**Section 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations** - The BARMM-MOH shall promulgate the Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of this Act within sixty (60) days after the enactment of this Act.

**Section 8. Separability Clause.** - In the event any provision of this Act or the application of such provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this Act shall not be affected by such declaration.

**Section 9. Repealing Clause.** - All laws and regulations under the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) government including those passed under the defunct Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) government regarding the construction, establishment and operations of the Gambar Municipal Hospital not consistent with this Act are hereby repealed.

**Section 10. Effectivity** - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
