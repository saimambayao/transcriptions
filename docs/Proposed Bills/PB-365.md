**AN ACT UPGRADING THE DATU HALUN SAKILAN MEMORIAL HOSPITAL TAWI-TAWI IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGAO, PROVINCE OF TAWI-TAWI FROM A LEVEL 1, 50 BED CAPACITY TO A LEVEL 2, 200 BED CAPACITY HOSPITAL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREOF;**

**Be it enacted by the Parliament in session assembled:**

**SECTION 1. Short Title.** This Act shall be known as the “*Datu Halun Sakilan Memorial Hospital Upgrading Act of 2025*”.

**SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy.** It is the policy of the Bangsamoro Government to improve healthcare access and quality, especially in remote and underserved areas, by strengthening medical facilities and upgrading their capacity to deliver essential health services.

**SECTION 3. Upgrading of Hospital.** The Datu Halun Sakilan Memorial Hospital in the Municipality of Bongao, Province of Tawi-Tawi is hereby upgraded from a Level 1, 50 bed capacity to a Level 2, 200 bed capacity hospital and shall remain under the supervision of the Ministry of Health.

**SECTION 4. Enhancement of Services and Facilities.** As a Level 2 hospital, Datu Halun Sakilan Memorial Hospital shall provide more advanced medical services, including intensive care units (ICUs), departmentalized clinical services such as internal medicine, pediatrics, surgery, obstetrics and gynecology, and enhanced diagnostic, laboratory, and imaging facilities. It shall also be staffed with the required number of specialists, nurses, and support personnel as per national health standards.

**SECTION 5. Appropriations.** The initial funding for the upgrading of the hospital shall be charged against the current year’s appropriations of the Ministry of Health. Thereafter, the necessary funds for its continued operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual budget of the Ministry of Health.

**SECTION 6. Implementing Rules and Regulations.** Within sixty (60) days from the effectivity of this Act, the Ministry of Health, in coordination with the Department of Health and other relevant agencies, shall issue the rules and regulations necessary for the effective implementation of this Act.

**SECTION 7. Separability Clause.** If any provision of this Act is declared unconstitutional or invalid, the other provisions not affected thereby shall remain in full force and effect.

**SECTION 8. Repealing Clause.** All laws, orders, rules and regulations, or parts thereof inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

**SECTION 9. Effectivity.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.
