**AN ACT**
**TO UPGRADE THE EXISTING WAO DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF WAO, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR, FROM A LEVEL 1 TO A LEVEL 2 HOSPITAL, INCREASING ITS AUTHORIZED BED CAPACITY FROM FIFTY (50) TO ONE HUNDRED (100), AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament in session assembled:

**SECTION 1. Title.** – This Act shall be known as the “Wao District Hospital Upgrading Act of 2025.”

**SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy.** – It is the policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), pursuant to Section 22, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the "Organic Law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao," to establish by law a general hospital system and ensure a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery that addresses the health needs of its people and upholds the basic right to life through the provision of timely, quality, and affordable medical services.

**SEC. 3. Upgrade of Wao District Hospital.** – The Wao District Hospital, located in the Municipality of Wao, Province of Lanao del Sur, is hereby upgraded from a Level 1 to a Level 2 hospital in accordance with the standards and requirements prescribed by the Ministry of Health (MOH-BARMM) and the Department of Health (DOH), as applicable.

**SEC. 4. Enhancement of Services and Facilities.** –As a Level 2 hospital, Wao District Hospital shall offer more advanced medical care, including intensive care units (ICUs), specialized clinical departments such as internal medicine, pediatrics, surgery, and obstetrics and gynecology, as well as upgraded diagnostic, laboratory, and imaging services, and shall be manned by the appropriate number of specialists, nurses, and support staff in accordance with national health standards.

**SEC. 5. Appropriations.** – The initial funding for the upgrading of the hospital shall be charged against the current year's appropriations of the Ministry of Health. Thereafter, the necessary funds for its continued operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual budget of the Ministry of Health.

**SEC. 6. Implementing Rules and Regulations.** – Within sixty (60) days from the effectivity of this Act, the Ministry of Health, in coordination with the Department of Health and other relevant agencies, shall issue the rules and regulations necessary for the effective implementation of this Act.

**SEC.. 7. Separability Clause.** – If any provision of this Act is declared unconstitutional or invalid, the other provisions not affected thereby shall remain in full force and effect.

**SEC. 8. Repealing Clause.** – All laws, orders, rules, and regulations, or parts thereof, inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

**SEC. 9. Effectivity.** – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.
