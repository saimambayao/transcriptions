Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
**BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY**

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in Parliament assembled:

**“AN ACT UPGRADING THE DATU ALAWADDIN T. BANDON, SR. MEMORIAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIBUTU, PROVINCE OF TAWI-TAWI FROM ITS PRESENT LEVEL I OF TEN (10) BED CAPACITY TO LEVEL II GENERAL HOSPITAL OF FIFTY (50) BED CAPACITY AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES”**

**SECTION 1. Creation.** – The Datu Alawaddin T. Bandon Sr. Municipal Hospital was created by Republic Act No. 6853 establishing a ten (10) bed municipal hospital in the Municipality of Sitangkai, Province of Tawi-Tawi on February 24, 1990.

**SECTION 2. Change of Location.** – Republic Act No. 6853 provides that Datu Alawaddin T. Bandon, Sr. Municipal Hospital is located in the Municipality of Sitangkai. However, Muslim Mindanao Act 197 created the Municipality of Sibutu out of the Municipality of Sitangkai. Hence, the location of the hospital shall be in the Municipality of Sibutu.

**SECTION 3. Upgrading of the Hospital.** – The upgrading of the Datu Alawaddin T. Bandon, Sr. Municipal Hospital from its existing ten (10) bed capacity municipal hospital to a Level II general hospital with fifty (50) bed capacity shall be consistent with the prescribed standards set by the Ministry of Health (MOH) of the Bangsamoro Government. It shall integrate into its system of operations and services the relevant provisions of Republic Act No. 11223, otherwise known as the Universal Health Care Act.

**SECTION 4. Services.** The existing health service facilities and professional manpower of the Datu Alawaddin T. Bandon, Sr. Municipal Hospital are hereby upgraded and increased to conform with the standards of Level II general hospital and to ensure that the operating systems of the hospital keep up with the advancement in science and technology.

The information system of the hospital shall be streamlined to create a complete patients’ information in a simplified and easily comprehensible format. It can sync and share the inputs of doctors and authorized personnel among departments and offices seamlessly, generating clear and instantaneous communication. This will help the doctors and other medical personnel to confidently do their job and serve the patients better.

**SECTION 5. Organization; Administrative Supervision.** – The MOH, in coordination with the Ministry of Finance, Budget and Management (MFBM) shall determine and approve its appropriate organizational structure and plantilla positions to meet the prescribed standards of the upgraded level of the hospital. It shall supervise and monitor the administration and management of the hospital.

**SECTION 6. Appropriations.** – The Ministry of Finance, Budget and Management shall provide annual funding for the plantilla positions and the maintenance and other operating expenses of the hospital in the annual General Appropriations Act of the Bangsamoro.

**SECTION 7. Separability Clause.** – If any provision or part of this act is declared invalid, the remaining provisions or parts not affected shall remain in full force and effect.

**SECTION 8. Repealing Clause.** – All regional laws, executive orders, issuances, rules, and regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed, amended, or modified accordingly.

**SECTION 9. Effectivity.** – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or a newspaper of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.
