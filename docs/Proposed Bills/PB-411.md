**AN ACT**
**PROVIDING FOR THE CREATION OF PARLIAMENTARY DISTRICTS IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (BARMM).**

*Be it enacted by the Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao assembled:*

**SECTION 1. Short Title.** – This Act shall be known as the "**Bangsamoro Parliamentary Redistricting Act of 2025**"

**SECTION 2. Guiding Principles in the Creation of Parliamentary Districts.** – The creation and apportionment of parliamentary districts in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) shall be guided by the following principles:

a) ***Adherence to Constitutional and Legal Mandates.*** – The districting process shall strictly comply with the Bangsamoro Organic Law, the 1987 Philippine Constitution, relevant jurisprudence, and other applicable national and regional laws, rules, and regulations;

b) ***Equitable Representation.*** – Parliamentary districts shall be apportioned to ensure fair and proportional representation based on population and geographic distribution, guaranteeing meaningful participation for all communities, including indigenous peoples, linguistic minorities, and residents of geographically isolated and disadvantaged areas;

c) ***Contiguity, Compactness, and Adjacency.*** – In accordance with Article VII, Section 10 of the Bangsamoro Organic Law, districts shall, as far as practicable, consist of contiguous, compact, and adjacent territory, with due regard to natural features, cultural and historical ties, transportation networks, and established political and administrative boundaries;

d) ***Transparency and Participatory Deliberation.*** – The formulation of districts shall be undertaken through an open, transparent, and accountable process, with due consultations and meaningful participation from local government units, civil society, traditional and religious leaders, sectoral representatives, and affected communities; and

e) ***Inclusivity and Social Justice.*** – The drawing of districts shall promote the equitable representation of marginalized and underrepresented groups, ensure respect for cultural diversity, and facilitate fair access to political participation, basic services, and development opportunities.

**SECTION 3. Criteria in the Apportionment of Parliamentary Districts.** – Each parliamentary district shall, as far as practicable, be apportioned among the provinces, cities, municipalities, and geographic areas of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region based on the following criteria: (1) it shall consist of contiguous, compact, and adjacent territorial jurisdiction; and (2) it shall have a population of at least one hundred thousand (100,000).

**SECTION 4. Creation of Parliamentary Districts.** - For purposes of the election of district representatives in the Bangsamoro Parliament, the provinces, cities, and municipalities are hereby apportioned to create thirty-two (32) single-member parliamentary districts as follows:

**SECTION 5. Separability Clause.** - If any provision of this Act declared unconstitutional or inconsistent with Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the "Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao", the provisions not affected thereby shall remain valid and effective.

**SECTION 6. Repealing Clause.** – All regional laws, orders, rules, and regulations or parts thereof which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed, modified, superseded, or amended accordingly.

**SECTION 7. Effectivity.** - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its complete publication in a newspaper of general circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM).
