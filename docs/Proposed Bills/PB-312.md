**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FIFTY BED CAPACITY BANGSAMORO PEOPLE GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE SPECIAL GEOGRAPHIC AREAS (SGA) AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

*Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament*

**Section 1. Short Title.** - This Act shall be known as the "The Bangsamoro People General Hospital Act".

**Section 2. Declaration of Policy.** - It is the policy of the State to adopt an integrated and comprehensive approach to health development which shall endeavor to make essential goods, health and other social services available to all people at affordable cost.

Towards this end, and in order to adequately respond to emergencies, disasters and emerging diseases, the State shall protect the interest and promote the well-being and the medical needs of its citizens.

It is the policy of the BARMM to establish a general hospital system to serve the health needs of its people and ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable and complete medical services.

**Section 3. The Bangsamoro People General Hospital.** - To carry out the above policy, a fifty bed capacity hospital is hereby established to be known as the Bangsamoro People General Hospital.

**Section 4. Objectives.** - To ensure the availability, accessibility and affordability of quality health care, the BP General Hospital shall have the following objectives:

a) To provide a comprehensive and total health care services to the Bangsamoro people;
b) To complement the existing package of services under the health care program so as to include preventive, promotive, diagnostic, curative and rehabilitative programs;
c) To conduct medical examination of all staff to ensure their physical and mental capability; and
d) To monitor the condition of patients and to generate relevant information and data in aid of policy information.

**Section 5. Administration.** - Upon the effectivity of this Act, the hospital shall be under the administrative supervision of the MOH Minister. The appropriate organizational structure for the management and operations of the hospital shall be determined and approved by the Minister of Health.

**Section 7. Appropriations.** - the amount required to implement the provisions of this Act for the construction and operationalization of the hospital shall be included in the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act. Thereafter, maintenance and operational funds shall be allocated in accordance with the provisions of the BAA and other pertinent laws.
