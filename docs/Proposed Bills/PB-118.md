**AN ACT**
**PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF DIGITAL PUBLIC LIBRARY HUBS IN ALL EXISTING PUBLIC LIBRARIES AND READING CENTERS ACROSS THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

*Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Parliament in session:*

**Section 1. Title.** This Act shall be known as the “BARMM Digital Public Library Hubs Act of 2022.”

**Section 2. Declaration of Policy.** It is hereby declared to be the policy of the Bangsamoro Government to establish, maintain, and support a complete and integrated system of quality education, which shall be a subsystem of the national education system. It further recognizes science and technology as essential to national and regional progress.

Towards this end, the Bangsamoro Government shall promote and develop infrastructures that would ensure the availability of reliable and secure information for the Bangsamoro people. It recognizes that an effective public library and research system is crucial to the development of an educational framework suitable to the needs of the region, and will ultimately result in an information system that guarantees efficiency, security, convenience, and accessibility.

**Section 3. Definition of Terms.** As used in this Act, the following terms shall mean:

a. Digital Library Hub – refers to a library in which collections are stored in digital formats (as opposed to print, microform, or other media), and accessible free of charge to the public;
b. Digital Database – refers to a collection of recorded information in a form capable of being stored in, processed, and operated on by a computer;
c. Library – refers to a collection of books, audiobooks, journals, conference papers, theses, dissertations, preprints, abstracts, technical reports, articles, video or film recordings, and other scholarly literature systematically organized and administered for reading, consultation, and study;
d. Commercial Academic Database Services – refers to specialized sources of aggregate traditional legal materials, including court opinions, historical, governmental, and political documents, parliament debates, parliament and cabinet reports, and other documents of international organizations.

**Section 4. Establishment of Digital Public Library Hubs.** The Ministry of Basic, Higher, and Technical Education (MBHTE), in close coordination with the Bangsamoro Library and Archives and the Bangsamoro Information and Communications Technology Office (BICTO), shall undertake the establishment of at least one (1) digital public library hub in every existing public library and reading center within BARMM, which shall be free of charge to the public.

All digital public library hubs established pursuant to this Act shall be allocated a sufficient and conducive space inside all BARMM public libraries and reading centers, and shall be managed by the administrative staff of the public library and reading center concerned, with periodic consultation and coordination with the BICTO.

This Act shall not, in any manner, proscribe the establishment of other digital public library hubs in other potential areas of BARMM component municipalities, or barangays within their respective areas of jurisdiction.

**Section 5. Purpose of the Digital Public Library Hub.** The Digital Public Library Hub shall have the following functions:

a. To provide greater access to reading, audio-visual, and audio materials;
b. To expand the collection of existing public libraries and reading centers with quality and appropriate books and similar materials, whether from domestic or international sources;
c. To provide the centralized management of a digital information database that contains all BARMM public library and reading center collections; and
d. To aid in the preservation and conservation of physical collections.

All digital public library hubs shall incorporate efficient data and document management practices, effective information retrieval mechanisms, library science principles, innovative and up-to-date information systems, and image processing resources.

**Section 6. BARMM Centralized Library Digital Database.** The BICTO, in close consultation and coordination with the Bangsamoro Library and Archives, shall create a centralized library digital database containing all collections found in any public library and reading center across BARMM.

Within two (2) years from the effectivity of this Act, the Bangsamoro Library and Archives, with the assistance of the BICTO, shall undertake to make copies of, scan, or convert all traditional and physical collections of public libraries and reading centers into digital formats, especially historical materials prone to dilapidation, and that are representative of the Bangsamoro culture and literature.

Thereafter, these digitized copies shall be uploaded and maintained in the digital database which shall be accessible in any digital public library hub established pursuant to this Act.

**Section 7. Provision for Access to Digitized Materials.** – The MBHTE shall ensure access to digitized copies of collections by providing sufficient, fast, and secure computers, laptops, or appropriate wherewithal, as well as fast and reliable internet services to all digital public library hubs established pursuant to this Act.

The BICTO, together with all BARMM public libraries and reading centers, shall continuously manage, update, and maintain the software installed in all Bangsamoro Government-owned ICT infrastructures supplied to digital public library hubs across the BARMM, including continuous subscriptions to relevant commercial academic database services.

In case of subscriptions to commercial academic database services, all digital public library hubs shall formulate mechanisms for the end-users to securely access it remotely at any time day or night.

**Section 8. Provision for Continuous Staff Training.** To address the potential gaps in the management of digital public library hubs, the BICTO shall regularly conduct consultative skills training, orientation, advisory services, and technical assistance to all BARMM public libraries and reading centers, with the objective of assisting the end-users to effectively utilize all adopted ICT mechanisms in all digital public library hubs.

The BICTO shall likewise monitor and ensure the technical management of all digital public library hubs, and together with the public library and reading center concerned, undertake its periodic review, updating, and maintenance.

**Section 9. Additional Online Educational Materials.** The MBHTE shall coordinate with the Bangsamoro Library and Archives to complement the repository of all digital public library hubs of textbooks and additional materials that is representative of the Bangsamoro culture and literature. The MBHTE shall also be allowed to add other digital materials, whether online or otherwise, that it deems necessary for expanding the learning opportunities of its patrons.

**Section 10. Appropriations.** The Minister of MBHTE shall include in the Ministry’s programs the establishment and maintenance of such digital public library hubs, the funding of which shall be included in the annual Bangsamoro Appropriations Act.

**Section 11. Implementing Rules and Regulations.** The MBHTE, in consultation with the Bangsamoro Library and Archives, and the BICTO, shall issue rules and regulations necessary to effectively implement the provisions of this Act.

**Section 12. Separability Clause.** If any section or part of this Act is held unconstitutional or invalid, all other sections or provisions not otherwise affected shall remain in full force and effect.

**Section 13. Repealing Clause.** All ordinances, executive orders, administrative orders, or other rules and regulations contrary to or inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed or amended accordingly.

**Section 14. Effectivity.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of regional circulation.
