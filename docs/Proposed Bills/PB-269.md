**AN ACT**
**CREATING AN INFIRMARY HOSPITAL IN BONGO ISLAND, PARANG, MAGUINDANAO DEL NORTE, DEFINING ITS SERVICE CAPACITY, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

Be enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region Muslim in Mindanao.

**SECTION 1. Title.** — This Act shall be known as the **“Bongo Community Hospital of 2024”**

**SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy.** — It is the policy of the Bangsamoro Government to protect and promote the right to health of the people and instill health consciousness among them by making health services available, accessible and affordable for all. Further, the Bangsamoro Government is mandated to uphold the people’s right to have access to essential good health, and other social services that promote their well-being.

**SECTION 3. Creation.** — There is hereby established an Infirmary Hospital with ten (10) bed capacity in Bongo Island, Municipality of Parang, Maguindanao del Norte, otherwise known as the Bongo Community Hospital (BCH). All systems of operation of BCH shall be in accordance with pertinent and relevant provisions of the Universal Health Care (UCH) Program under Republic Act 11223.

**SECTION 4. Site of the Infirmary Hospital.** — The site of the Infirmary Hospital shall be determined by the barangay local government units in coordination and with approval of the Ministry of Health (MOH), and Ministry of Environment and Natural Resources and Energy (MENRE).

**SECTION 5. Administration.** – The BCH shall be under the administrative supervision of the MOH who is hereby authorized to determine and approve the organizational structure for the effective and efficient management and operation of the hospital.

**SECTION 6. Appropriation.** - The amount necessary for the initial implementation of this Act shall be charged against the present General Appropriation Act for the Bangsamoro (GAAB). Thereafter, the funds necessary for the continued maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be included in the GAAB.

**SECTION 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations.** - Within sixty (60) days from the effectivity of this Act, the MOH shall promulgate the corresponding Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) for the effective implementation of this Act

**SECTION 8. Separability Clause.** — Should any provision herein be declared unconstitutional or the application of such provision to any person or circumstances is held invalid, the other provisions of this Act or any provision not affected thereby shall remain to be in full force and effect.

**SECTION 9. Repealing Clause.** All other regional acts, decrees, instructions, orders, rules and regulations or parts thereof which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

**SECTION 10. Effectivity.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general or regional circulation.
