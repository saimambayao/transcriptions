**AN ACT**
**UPGRADING THE MAGUINDANAO PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL (MPH) TO A LEVEL III HOSPITAL AND THEREBY INCREASING ITS BED CAPACITY TO THREE HUNDRED FIFTY (350), APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

*Be it enacted by the Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao assembled:*

**Section 1. *Title.*** – This Act shall be known as the **“Maguindanao Provincial Hospital Upgrading Act of 2023”**.

**Section 2. *Declaration of Policy.*** – It is the declared policy of the Bangsamoro Government to protect and promote the right of the health of the people and instill health consciousness among them. Furthermore, it shall adopt an integrated and comprehensive approach to health development which shall endeavor to make essential goods, health, and other social services to all the people at an affordable cost.

**Section 3. *Objectives.*** – This Act generally aims to upgrade the Maguindanao Provincial Hospital to a Level III hospital with 350 bed capacity. Specifically, it has the following objectives:

a) Integrate in its system of operations all pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic Act No. 11223, otherwise known as the Universal Health Care (UHC) Act;

b) Improve and upgrade the government health services and facilities of the Maguindanao Provincial Hospital for the benefit of the general public, especially residents of Maguindanao del Sur and nearby areas;

c) Provide all essential and quality health services to the people, especially the sick, the poor, and marginalized individuals from Maguindanao del Sur; and

d) Improve and uplift the morale, social, and economic well-being of health workers of the Maguindanao Provincial Hospital.

**Section 4. *Definition of Terms.*** – For the purposes of this Act, the following terms are hereby defined as follows:

1. *Classification Level* – refers to the classification of hospitals based on their functional capacity as prescribed by the Department of Health under Administrative Order No. 2012-0012 effective July 18, 2012 and subsequent amendment thereto as Administrative Order No. 2012-0012a dated September 03, 2015.

2. *Level I* – The hospital shall have as minimum the services stipulated under Rule V. B. 1. b. 1. of the Department of Health Administrative Order No. 2012-0012", including, but not limited to, the following:

2.1. A staff of qualified medical, allied medical and administrative personnel headed by a physician duly licensed by PRC;

2.2. Bed space for its authorized bed capacity in accordance with DOH guidelines in the planning and design of hospitals;

2.3. An operating room with standard equipment and provision of sterilization of equipment and supplies in accordance with:
a. DOH Reference Plan in the Planning and Design of an Operating Room/Theater; and
b. DOH Guidelines on Cleaning, Disinfection and Sterilization of Reusable Medical Devices in Hospital Facilities in the Philippines.

2.4. A post-operative recovery room;

2.5. Maternity facilities, consisting of ward(s), room(s), a delivery room, exclusively for maternity patients and newborns;

2.6. Isolation facilities with proper procedures for the care and control of infectious and communicable diseases as well as for the prevention of cross infections;

2.7. A separate dental section/clinic;

2.8. Provision for blood station;

2.9. A DOH licensed secondary clinical laboratory with the services of a consulting pathologist;

2.10. A DOH licensed level 1 imaging facility with the services of a consulting radiologist; and

2.11. A DOH licensed pharmacy.

3. *Level II* – The hospital shall have as minimum, all of Level 1 capacity, including, but not limited to the following:

3.1. An organized staff of qualified and competent personnel with chief of hospital/medical director and appropriate board-certified clinical department heads;

3.2. Departmentalized and equipped with the services capabilities needed to support board certified/eligible medical specialists and other licensed physicians rendering services in the specialties of Medicine, Pediatrics, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Surgery; their subspecialties and ancillary services;

3.3. Provision for general ICU for critical ill patients;

3.4. Provision for NICU (Neonatal Intensive Care Unit);

3.5. Provision for HRPU (High Risk Pregnancy Unit);

3.6. Provision for respiratory therapy services;

3.7. A DOH licensed tertiary clinical laboratory; and

3.8. A DOH licensed level 2 imaging facility with mobile x-ray inside the institution and with capability for contrast examinations.

4. *Level III* – The hospital shall have as minimum, all of Level 2 capacity, including, but not limited to, the following:

4.1 Teaching and/or training hospital with accredited residency training program for physicians in the four (4) major specialties, namely: Medicine, Pediatrics, Obstetrics and Gynecology, and Surgery;

4.2 Provision for physical medicine and rehabilitation unit;

4.3 Provision for ambulatory surgical clinic;

4.4 Provision for dialysis facility;

4.5 Provision for blood bank;

4.6 A DOH licensed tertiary clinical laboratory with standard equipment/reagents/supplies necessary for the performance of histopathology examinations; and

4.7 A DOH licensed level III imaging facility with interventional radiology.

5. *Upgrading* – refers to the reclassification of a hospital into a level higher than its present state by providing and complying with the requirements prescribed for its accreditation to the higher level aspired for in accordance with the Department of Health Administrative Order No. 2012-0012 effective July 18, 2012 and subsequent amendment thereto as Administrative Order No. 2012-0012a dated September 03, 2015.

**Section 5. *Upgrading of the Maguindanao Provincial Hospital.*** – The Maguindanao Provincial Hospital located in Datu Hoffer Ampatuan, Maguindanao del Sur is hereby upgraded from its present Level II classification into a Level III hospital with a three hundred fifty (350) bed capacity in accordance with the national classification level standards set by the Department of Health – Bureau of Health Facilities and Services as defined under Section 4 of this Act to gain legitimate accreditation.

**Section 6. *Existing Physical Structures and Services.*** - The existing physical structures, professional health care services, facilities and equipment are likewise upgraded to conform with and be commensurate to the increase in bed capacity.

**Section 7. *Existing Workforce.*** - The existing workforce of the hospital, which includes doctors, nurses, and other health care practitioners and support personnel, shall be increased correspondingly. The Ministry of Health, in coordination with the Ministry of Finance, Budget and Management, shall determine the additional plantilla positions to be created and filled up in coordination with the management of the Maguindanao Provincial Hospital.

**Section 8. *Administration, Management and Operation.*** – The upgraded Maguindanao Provincial Hospital shall remain under the administrative supervision of the Ministry of Health who is hereby authorized to establish and/or approve the appropriate organizational structure and personnel for the management and operations of the herein-mentioned upgraded facility.

**Section 9. *Implementation.*** – Within sixty (60) days from the promulgation of this Act, the necessary rules and regulations for the proper implementation of its provision shall be formulated by the Ministry of Health (MOH).

**Section 10. *Appropriations.*** – The Ministry of Health shall immediately include in the Ministry's programs the implementation of this Act, the funding of which shall be included in the annual Bangsamoro Appropriations Act.

**Section 11. *Separability Clause.*** – If for any cause, any part of this Act is declared unconstitutional or contrary to the provisions of Bangsamoro Organic Law, the rest of the provisions shall remain in force and effect.

**Section 12. *Repealing Clause.*** – All laws, orders, rules, and regulations which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act, are hereby repealed, modified, superseded, or amended accordingly.

**Section 13. *Effectivity.*** – This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following its complete publication in a newspaper of general circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.
