## Quick Reference

### Three Financial Statements

| Statement | Shows | Time Frame |
|-----------|-------|------------|
| **Income Statement** | Profitability (Revenue - Expenses = Net Income) | Period |
| **Balance Sheet** | Financial position (Assets = Liabilities + Equity) | Point in time |
| **Cash Flow Statement** | Cash movements (Operating, Investing, Financing) | Period |

### Key Financial Ratios

| Category | Key Ratios |
|----------|------------|
| **Liquidity** | Current Ratio, Quick Ratio |
| **Profitability** | Gross Margin, Net Margin, ROA, ROE |
| **Efficiency** | Asset Turnover, Inventory Turnover |
| **Solvency** | Debt-to-Equity, Interest Coverage |

### Budget Types

| Type | Focus | Time Frame |
|------|-------|------------|
| **Operating** | Day-to-day expenses | 1 year |
| **Capital** | Long-term investments | Multi-year |
| **Cash** | Cash flow planning | Monthly |

### Internal Control Principles

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Segregation of Duties** | No single person controls entire transaction |
| **Authorization** | Proper approvals for transactions |
| **Documentation** | Written records of all transactions |
| **Reconciliation** | Regular verification of records |

## Workflow 2: Financial Consulting Mode

For organizational financial assessment and advisory.

### Financial Health Assessment

**Step 1: Gather Financial Data**
```
□ Financial statements (3 years minimum)
□ Budgets and actuals
□ Cash flow records
□ Accounts receivable/payable aging
□ Debt schedules
□ Member equity records (cooperatives)
□ Impact metrics (social enterprises)
```

**Step 2: Ratio Analysis**

| Category | Calculate | Benchmark |
|----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Liquidity** | Current ratio, quick ratio | Industry/peer comparison |
| **Profitability** | Margins, ROA, ROE | Historical trend |
| **Efficiency** | Turnover ratios | Best practices |
| **Solvency** | Debt ratios, coverage | Lending requirements |

**Step 3: Identify Issues**
```
□ Liquidity concerns?
□ Profitability declining?
□ Cash flow problems?
□ High debt levels?
□ Internal control weaknesses?
□ Budget variances significant?
```

**Step 4: Recommendations**
- Prioritize by urgency and impact
- Provide actionable steps
- Consider organizational context
- Balance short-term and long-term

### Context-Specific Considerations

**For Cooperatives** (use with `/cooperative`):
- Patronage refund adequacy

## Key Formulas Reference

### Liquidity
```
Current Ratio = Current Assets / Current Liabilities
Quick Ratio = (Current Assets - Inventory) / Current Liabilities
```

### Profitability
```
Gross Margin = (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue
Net Margin = Net Income / Revenue
ROA = Net Income / Average Total Assets
ROE = Net Income / Average Shareholders' Equity
```

### Efficiency
```
Asset Turnover = Revenue / Average Total Assets
Inventory Turnover = COGS / Average Inventory
Days Sales Outstanding = 365 / Receivables Turnover
```

### Solvency
```
Debt-to-Equity = Total Debt / Total Equity
Interest Coverage = EBIT / Interest Expense
```

### Break-Even
```
Break-Even Units = Fixed Costs / (Price - Variable Cost per Unit)
Break-Even Sales = Fixed Costs / Contribution Margin Ratio
```

## Philippine Financial Reporting Framework for Cooperatives (PFRF)

**Primary Reference**: [MC 2022-25 - PFRF for Cooperatives](../cooperative/references/memorandum-circulars/mc-2022-25-pfrf-cooperatives.md)

The PFRF for Cooperatives is the authoritative financial reporting framework for all Philippine cooperatives registered with CDA or CSEA-BARMM (except Cooperative Banks, Insurance Cooperatives, and Electric Cooperatives which follow full PFRS).

### PFRF Quick Reference

| Chapter | Topic | Key Content |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| I-III | General & Concepts | Scope, qualitative characteristics, recognition |
| IV | Statement of Financial Condition | Assets, liabilities, equity classification |
| V | Statement of Operations | Revenue, expenses, net surplus allocation |
| VI | Changes in Equity | Equity movements, statutory funds |
| VII | Cash Flows | Operating, investing, financing activities |
| VIII | Notes | Disclosures, accounting policies |
| IX | Policies & Errors | Changes, corrections |
| X | Financial Instruments | Recognition, measurement |
| XI-XV | Assets | Inventories, investments, property, intangibles |
| XVI-XVII | Surplus & Funds | Net surplus allocation, statutory funds |
| XVIII-XXI | Liabilities | Leases, provisions, equity, grants |
| XXII-XXIV | Costs & Benefits | Borrowing costs, impairment, employee benefits |
| XXV-XXVII | Events & Parties | Subsequent events, related parties, specialized |
| XXVIII | Final Provisions | Transition, sanctions, effectivity |

### PFRF Statutory Funds Allocation (RA 9520 Art. 86)

| Fund | Allocation | Usage |
|------|------------|-------|
| **Reserve Fund** | At least 10% (50% in first 5 years) | Stability, operating losses |
| **Education & Training Fund** | Not more than 10% | Training, development |
| **Community Development Fund** | Not less than 3% | Community benefit projects |
| **Optional Fund** | Not more than 7% | Assets, member benefits |
| **Remaining** | Balance | Interest on share capital, patronage refund |

### PFRF Exclusions

| Cooperative Type | Required Framework |
|------------------|-------------------|
| Cooperative Banks | Full PFRS |
| Insurance Cooperatives | Full PFRS |
| Electric Cooperatives | Full PFRS |

> **Always invoke PFRF** when dealing with cooperative financial statements, audits, or compliance reporting.

### CDA/CSEA Financial Reporting Requirements

| Requirement | Legal Basis |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Financial Reporting Framework** | [MC 2022-25 PFRF](../cooperative/references/memorandum-circulars/mc-2022-25-pfrf-cooperatives.md) |
| Reserve Fund | At least 10% of Net Surplus (Article 87, RA 9520; PFRF Chapter XVI-XVII) |
| Education & Training Fund | Not more than 10% of Net Surplus (PFRF Chapter XVII) |
| Patronage Refund | At least 30% of remainder after statutory reserves |
| Interest on Share Capital | Non-cumulative, limited rate (Article 76) |

> **Note**: CSEA-BARMM adopts the same PFRF standards for cooperatives registered in the Bangsamoro region.
