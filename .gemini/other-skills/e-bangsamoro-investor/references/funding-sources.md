## Phased Funding Approach

```text
FUNDING PHASES:
│
├── PHASE 1: SEED/PROTOTYPE (₱5-15M)
│   ├── Source: Government budget, small grants
│   ├── Purpose: MVP development, proof of concept
│   └── Milestone: Working prototype, pilot users
│
├── PHASE 2: PILOT (₱20-50M)
│   ├── Source: Development partner grant
│   ├── Purpose: Full platform, BTA deployment
│   └── Milestone: BTA adoption, metrics baseline
│
├── PHASE 3: SCALE (₱50-150M)
│   ├── Source: Multi-donor, PPP, impact investment
│   ├── Purpose: MOA rollout, full features
│   └── Milestone: Regional coverage, sustainability
│
└── PHASE 4: EXPANSION (₱100-500M)
    ├── Source: Commercial, PPP, replication license
    ├── Purpose: Philippine LGU expansion
    └── Milestone: National platform, revenue
```
