## Executive Summary

MoroAcademy is positioned to transform capacity building in the Bangsamoro cooperative and social enterprise sector through scalable, accessible online learning. This Enterprise Development Plan (EDP) translates the validated learning model (Lean Canvas) and impact framework (Theory of Change) into an actionable execution roadmap.

**Strategic Position:**
- **Growth Stage**: Greiner Phase 1 (Creativity) with strong foundations
- **Adizes Stage**: Infancy → Go-Go
- **Primary Focus**: Platform adoption, content creation, learner engagement
- **Key Metric**: Course Completion Rate

**Year 1 Targets:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Course Completion Rate | 60%+ |
| Monthly Active Learners | 2,000+ |
| Certificates Issued | 500+ |
| Courses Published | 30+ |
| TSPs Onboarded | 10+ |

**Crisis to Anticipate: Leadership Crisis**
- Content creation bottleneck as demand grows
- Need for quality control and instructional design standards

2. Create instructor development program
3. Build no-code course builder for scalable content creation

## 12. Risk Management

### Critical Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|-------|
| Low completion rates | High | High | Microlearning, cohorts, gamification | Product |
| Connectivity barriers | High | High | Offline-first, low-bandwidth mode | Tech |
| Content quality issues | Medium | High | Standards, review process, templates | Content |
| Trainer adoption | Medium | Medium | Easy tools, training, incentives | Content |

| Assumption | Monitoring Method | Trigger for Review |
