# MoroNegosyo Enterprise Development Plan

## Platform: Negosyo Bangsamoro Digital Platform (E-Commerce)
## URL: https://negosyo.bangsamoro.site
## Date: December 2024
## Version: 1.0
## Phase: 4 - DELIVER & DRIVE (4D SE Development Journey)

---

## Executive Summary

MoroNegosyo is positioned to transition from platform development to market activation. This Enterprise Development Plan (EDP) outlines the strategic execution roadmap for Year 1, translating the validated business model (Lean Canvas) and impact framework (Theory of Change) into actionable operations.

**Strategic Position:**
- **Growth Stage**: Greiner Phase 1 (Creativity) → Transitioning to Phase 2 (Direction)
- **Adizes Stage**: Infancy → Go-Go
- **Primary Focus**: Seller activation, buyer acquisition, transaction facilitation
- **Key Metric**: Gross Merchandise Value (GMV)

**Year 1 Targets:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| GMV | PHP 5,000,000 |
| Active Sellers | 100+ coops/SEs |
| Registered Buyers | 1,000+ |
| Conversion Rate | 5%+ |
| FRAMES Compliance | 80%+ |

### Greiner Growth Model Assessment

**Current Phase: Phase 1 (Creativity)**

| Phase | Description | Status |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| Phase 1: Creativity | Entrepreneurial, informal, product-focused | **CURRENT** |
| Phase 2: Direction | Professional management, formal structure | Preparing |
| Phase 3: Delegation | Decentralized, autonomous units | Future |

**Crisis to Anticipate: Leadership Crisis**
- As MoroNegosyo scales, informal decision-making will become bottleneck
- Need to establish clear roles, processes, and professional management
- CSEA must balance regulatory role with platform operations

**Transition Actions:**
1. Define clear organizational structure for platform operations
2. Establish Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
3. Create performance metrics and accountability framework
4. Build management layer between CSEA leadership and operations

**Go-Go Stage Priorities:**
1. Capitalize on early momentum
2. Establish sales and marketing rhythm
3. Build operational infrastructure before growth overwhelms
4. Maintain focus (avoid chasing every opportunity)

## 3. Scaling Strategy

### Scaling Up: Four Decisions Framework

| Priority | Action | Timeline |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Define Roles | Platform Manager, Seller Support, Marketing Lead | Q1 |
| Right People | Train CSEA staff or hire dedicated resources | Q1-Q2 |
| Right Seats | Clear accountability matrix | Q1 |
| Develop Talent | Training on e-commerce, customer service | Ongoing |

**2. STRATEGY**

| Element | Definition |
|---------|------------|
| Core Customer | CSEA-registered cooperatives and social enterprises (supply), Conscious consumers including diaspora (demand) |
| Brand Promise | "Trusted marketplace for authentic Bangsamoro products" |
| BHAG | 500 active sellers, PHP 50M annual GMV by Year 3 |
| Profit per X | Revenue per transaction, value per seller onboarded |

**3. EXECUTION**

| Rhythm | Cadence | Focus |
|--------|---------|-------|

| Weekly Meeting | 60 min | Key metrics, problem-solving |
| Monthly Review | 2 hours | Performance vs. targets, adjustments |
| Quarterly Planning | 1 day | OKR setting, strategic alignment |

**4. CASH**

| Metric | Target | Monitoring |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Cash Reserves | 6 months operating | Monthly |
| Payment Cycle | 3-5 day settlement | Weekly |
| Cost per Transaction | < 5% of GMV | Monthly |
| Customer Acquisition Cost | < PHP 200/buyer | Monthly |
