## Y Combinator Framework for Social Enterprises

YC's startup methodology applies to social enterprises with dual-track adaptation. See shared references for comprehensive YC content.

### Adapting YC Principles for Impact Ventures

| YC Principle | SE Adaptation |
|--------------|---------------|
| **"Make Something People Want"** | Make something beneficiaries need AND customers will pay for |
| **Launch in 2 weeks** | Minimal Viable Impact - test core intervention quickly |
| **5-7% weekly growth** | Track both user growth AND impact growth |
| **Talk to users** | Talk to beneficiaries AND paying customers |
| **Do things that don't scale** | Manual impact delivery before systematizing |

### YC PMF for Social Enterprises

**Dual PMF Requirements:**
1. **Impact-Market Fit**: Beneficiaries experiencing real change
2. **Business-Market Fit**: Customers willing to pay for the solution

**YC-Style SE Validation:**
```
□ 40%+ beneficiaries would be "very disappointed" if SE disappeared
□ Organic growth without marketing spend
□ Clear path to sustainable unit economics
□ Impact evidence (not just outputs)
```

### YC Growth Metrics Adapted

| Metric | Business Track | Impact Track |
|--------|----------------|--------------|
| **Weekly Growth** | Revenue/Users | Beneficiaries reached |
| **Retention** | Customer return rate | Sustained behavior change |
| **Unit Economics** | CAC/LTV positive | Cost per impact reasonable |
