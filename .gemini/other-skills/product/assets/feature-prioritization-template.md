**RICE Formula (SE-Adapted):**
```
Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence × Mission) / Effort
```

**Scale Reference:**
- **Reach**: 1 (few) to 10 (all users/beneficiaries)
- **Impact**: 0.25 (minimal), 0.5 (low), 1 (medium), 2 (high), 3 (massive)
- **Confidence**: 0.5 (low), 0.8 (medium), 1.0 (high)
- **Mission**: 0.5 (low alignment), 1 (medium), 1.5 (high alignment)
- **Effort**: 1-10 person-months
