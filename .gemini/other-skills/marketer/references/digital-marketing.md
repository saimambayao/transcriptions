## Digital Marketing Overview

### What is Digital Marketing?

Marketing through digital channels to reach and engage customers online.

### Digital Marketing Channels

| Channel | Description | Best For |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Social Media** | Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok | Brand awareness, engagement |
| **Content Marketing** | Blogs, videos, infographics | Education, SEO, authority |
| **SEO** | Search engine visibility | Organic traffic, discovery |
| **Email** | Direct communication | Nurturing, retention, sales |
| **Paid Ads** | PPC, display, social ads | Quick reach, targeting |
| **Influencer** | Partnerships with influencers | Trust, reach, authenticity |

### Digital Marketing Funnel

```
ATTRACT → CONVERT → CLOSE → DELIGHT
   ↓         ↓         ↓        ↓
 Traffic   Leads     Sales   Advocates
```

## Social Media Marketing

### Platform Selection Guide

| Platform | Audience | Best Content | Best For |
|----------|----------|--------------|----------|
| **Facebook** | Broad, 25-55 | Mixed, community | Local business, community building |
| **Instagram** | 18-40, visual | Photos, Reels, Stories | Products, lifestyle, visual brands |
| **TikTok** | 16-30, entertainment | Short video | Viral reach, youth engagement |
| **LinkedIn** | Professionals, B2B | Articles, industry content | B2B, thought leadership |
| **YouTube** | All ages | Long-form video | Education, tutorials, reviews |
| **Twitter/X** | News, tech, professionals | Short updates, threads | Real-time, customer service |

### Content Mix (50/30/20 Rule)

| Type | Percentage | Purpose |
|------|------------|---------|
| **Engagement** | 50% | Entertain, inspire, connect |
| **Educational** | 30% | Inform, teach, add value |
| **Promotional** | 20% | Sell, promote, convert |

### Social Media Strategy Framework

1. **Set Goals**: What do you want to achieve?
2. **Know Audience**: Who are you reaching?
3. **Choose Platforms**: Where is your audience?
4. **Create Content Plan**: What will you post?
5. **Schedule Consistently**: When will you post?
6. **Engage Actively**: How will you interact?
7. **Measure Results**: What's working?

### Social Media Metrics

| Metric | What It Measures | Why It Matters |
|--------|------------------|----------------|
| **Reach** | Unique viewers | Awareness |
| **Engagement Rate** | Interactions / Reach | Content resonance |
| **Follower Growth** | New followers over time | Audience building |
| **Click-Through Rate** | Clicks / Impressions | Interest level |
| **Conversion Rate** | Actions / Clicks | Effectiveness |

## Content Marketing

### Content Marketing Definition

Creating and distributing valuable, relevant content to attract and retain a defined audience.

### Content Types

| Type | Format | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| **Blog Posts** | Written articles | SEO, education, thought leadership |
| **Videos** | YouTube, Reels, TikTok | Engagement, tutorials, stories |
| **Infographics** | Visual data | Complex information, shares |
| **Podcasts** | Audio content | Thought leadership, convenience |
| **E-books/Guides** | Long-form PDFs | Lead generation, authority |
| **Case Studies** | Success stories | Proof, credibility |
| **User-Generated** | Customer content | Trust, community |

### Content Strategy Framework

1. **Define Goals**: What business objective does content serve?
2. **Know Audience**: What are their needs, questions, pain points?
3. **Content Audit**: What do you already have?
4. **Content Plan**: What topics, formats, frequency?
5. **Create Calendar**: When will content be published?
6. **Distribute**: Where will content be shared?
7. **Measure**: What metrics indicate success?

## Search Engine Optimization

### What is SEO?

Optimizing content to rank higher in search engine results for relevant queries.

### SEO Components

| Component | Description | Key Actions |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| **On-Page** | Content optimization | Keywords, titles, meta descriptions, headings |
| **Technical** | Site structure | Speed, mobile-friendly, crawlability, security |
| **Off-Page** | External signals | Backlinks, social signals, brand mentions |
| **Local** | Geographic visibility | Google Business, local keywords, reviews |

### On-Page SEO Checklist

| Element | Best Practice |
|---------|---------------|
| **Title Tag** | Include keyword, 50-60 characters |
| **Meta Description** | Compelling summary, 150-160 characters |
| **H1 Heading** | One per page, includes keyword |
| **URL Structure** | Short, descriptive, includes keyword |
| **Image Alt Text** | Descriptive, includes keyword naturally |
| **Internal Links** | Link to related content |
| **Content Quality** | Comprehensive, valuable, original |

### Keyword Research Process

1. **Brainstorm**: List topics relevant to your audience
2. **Research**: Use tools to find search volume, difficulty
3. **Analyze Intent**: What does the searcher want?
4. **Prioritize**: Balance volume, difficulty, relevance
5. **Map**: Assign keywords to content pieces

### SEO for Small Business/Cooperatives

| Strategy | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Local SEO** | Claim Google Business Profile, local keywords |
| **Content** | Answer common customer questions |
| **Reviews** | Encourage and respond to reviews |
| **Mobile** | Ensure mobile-friendly website |
| **Speed** | Optimize page load time |

## Email Marketing

### Email Marketing Benefits

- Direct communication channel
- High ROI (average $42 per $1 spent)
- Owned audience (not dependent on algorithms)
- Personalization capabilities
- Measurable results

### Email Types

| Type | Purpose | Frequency |
|------|---------|-----------|
| **Newsletter** | Regular updates, value | Weekly/Monthly |
| **Promotional** | Sales, offers, launches | As needed |
| **Transactional** | Order confirmations, receipts | Triggered |
| **Welcome Series** | Onboard new subscribers | Automated |
| **Re-engagement** | Win back inactive subscribers | Periodic |

### Email Best Practices

| Element | Best Practice |
|---------|---------------|
| **Subject Line** | Clear, compelling, 40-50 characters |
| **Preview Text** | Extends subject, adds context |
| **Personalization** | Use name, relevant content |
| **Mobile Design** | Responsive, single column |
| **Clear CTA** | One primary action per email |
| **Unsubscribe** | Easy to find, required by law |

### Email Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Open Rate** | Opens / Delivered | 15-25% |
| **Click Rate** | Clicks / Delivered | 2-5% |
| **Conversion Rate** | Conversions / Clicks | Varies |
| **Unsubscribe Rate** | Unsubscribes / Delivered | <0.5% |
| **Bounce Rate** | Bounces / Sent | <2% |

## Analytics and Measurement

### Key Digital Marketing Metrics

| Category | Metrics |
|----------|---------|
| **Awareness** | Reach, impressions, brand mentions |
| **Engagement** | Likes, comments, shares, time on page |
| **Conversion** | Leads, sales, sign-ups, downloads |
| **Retention** | Return visitors, repeat purchases, churn |
| **Revenue** | Sales, average order value, CLV |

### Google Analytics Key Reports

| Report | What It Shows |
|--------|---------------|
| **Acquisition** | Where traffic comes from |
| **Behavior** | What users do on site |
| **Conversions** | Goal completions |
| **Audience** | Who your visitors are |

### Marketing Dashboard KPIs

| KPI | Target Example |
|-----|----------------|
| Website Traffic | 10,000 monthly visitors |
| Conversion Rate | 3% |
| Cost Per Lead | ₱100 |
| Social Engagement Rate | 5% |
| Email Open Rate | 25% |
| Customer Acquisition Cost | ₱500 |

### Attribution Models

| Model | Description | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Last Click** | Credit to final touchpoint | Simple analysis |
| **First Click** | Credit to initial touchpoint | Awareness focus |
| **Linear** | Equal credit to all touchpoints | Balanced view |
| **Time Decay** | More credit to recent touchpoints | Short sales cycles |

## Digital Marketing for BARMM Context

### Considerations

| Factor | Adaptation |
|--------|------------|
| **Connectivity** | Mobile-first, low-bandwidth friendly |
| **Language** | Multilingual content (Filipino, local languages) |
| **Platforms** | Facebook dominance, emerging TikTok |
| **Trust** | Community endorsements, local influencers |
| **Access** | Consider offline-to-online bridges |

### Recommended Channels for Coops/SEs

1. **Facebook** - Community building, local reach
2. **Instagram** - Product showcase, visual storytelling
3. **WhatsApp/Messenger** - Direct customer communication
4. **Google Business** - Local discovery, reviews
5. **Simple Website** - Credibility, information hub
