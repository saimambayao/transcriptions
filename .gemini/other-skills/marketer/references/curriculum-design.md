## Learning Levels

### Level 1: Foundational (Awareness)

**Target Audience**: First-time learners, cooperative members, SE staff

**Duration**: 4-8 hours (1 day workshop or online course)

**Outcomes**:
- Understand marketing fundamentals (4Ps, STP)
- Know basic digital marketing channels
- Create simple marketing messages
- Identify target customers

### Level 2: Practitioner (Application)

**Target Audience**: Marketing staff, coop officers, SE managers

**Duration**: 16-24 hours (2-3 day workshop or multi-week course)

**Outcomes**:
- Develop marketing plans
- Execute social media campaigns
- Measure marketing effectiveness
- Apply sector-specific strategies

### Level 3: Advanced (Mastery)

**Target Audience**: Marketing leads, consultants, trainers

**Duration**: 40+ hours (week-long intensive or certification)

**Outcomes**:
- Design comprehensive marketing strategies
- Lead marketing teams
- Train others on marketing
- Adapt strategies for different sectors

## Standard Course Modules

### Module 1: Introduction to Marketing

**Learning Objectives**:
- Define marketing and its purpose
- Explain the marketing concept
- Identify marketing's role in organizations

**Content**:
- What is marketing? (AMA definition)
- Marketing vs. selling
- Marketing orientations (production, product, selling, marketing, societal)
- Marketing in cooperatives, SEs, and government

**Activities**:
- Marketing examples discussion
- Identifying marketing in daily life
- Sector-specific case analysis

### Module 2: Understanding Your Market

**Learning Objectives**:
- Apply STP framework
- Conduct basic market research
- Create customer personas

**Content**:
- Market segmentation (geographic, demographic, psychographic, behavioral)
- Target market selection
- Positioning strategy
- Customer persona development

**Activities**:
- Segmentation exercise with local market
- Persona creation workshop
- Positioning statement writing

### Module 3: The Marketing Mix

**Learning Objectives**:
- Apply 4Ps/7Ps to product/service offerings
- Develop pricing strategies
- Plan distribution channels
- Design promotional approaches

**Content**:
- Product: Features, benefits, branding
- Price: Strategies, value perception
- Place: Distribution, accessibility
- Promotion: Communication channels
- Extended Ps for services (People, Process, Physical Evidence)

**Activities**:
- Marketing mix audit of own organization
- Pricing strategy simulation
- Channel selection exercise

### Module 4: Digital Marketing Fundamentals

**Learning Objectives**:
- Identify appropriate digital channels
- Create social media content
- Understand basic SEO principles
- Plan email campaigns

**Content**:
- Social media platforms and selection
- Content creation and planning
- Search engine basics
- Email marketing fundamentals
- Analytics introduction

**Activities**:
- Social media content creation
- Platform selection exercise
- Simple content calendar

### Module 5: Branding and Positioning

**Learning Objectives**:
- Develop brand identity elements
- Create positioning statements
- Ensure brand consistency

**Content**:
- Brand elements (name, logo, colors, voice)
- Brand positioning
- Brand equity
- Consistency across touchpoints

**Activities**:
- Brand audit exercise
- Positioning statement development
- Brand guidelines creation

### Module 6: Marketing Communication

**Learning Objectives**:
- Create compelling marketing messages
- Apply AIDA framework
- Write for different channels

**Content**:
- Message development
- AIDA model application
- Copywriting basics
- Visual communication
- Storytelling for impact

**Activities**:
- Message creation workshop
- Before/after copywriting exercise
- Story development practice

### Module 7: Marketing Planning

**Learning Objectives**:
- Develop a marketing plan
- Set SMART marketing goals
- Allocate marketing budget
- Create action timelines

**Content**:
- Marketing plan structure
- Situation analysis (SWOT)
- Goal setting (SMART)
- Budget allocation
- Implementation timeline

**Activities**:
- Marketing plan development
- SWOT analysis
- Budget exercise

### Module 8: Marketing Measurement

**Learning Objectives**:
- Identify key marketing metrics
- Track campaign performance
- Use data for improvement

**Content**:
- Marketing KPIs
- Digital analytics basics
- ROI calculation
- Reporting and optimization

**Activities**:
- Metrics selection exercise
- Mock dashboard creation
- Campaign analysis

## Workshop Formats

### Format 1: Half-Day Introduction (4 hours)

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 0:00-0:30 | Welcome, icebreaker |
| 0:30-1:15 | Marketing fundamentals |
| 1:15-1:30 | Break |
| 1:30-2:15 | Understanding your market |
| 2:15-3:00 | Marketing mix basics |
| 3:00-3:15 | Break |
| 3:15-3:45 | Digital marketing overview |
| 3:45-4:00 | Action planning, close |

### Format 2: One-Day Intensive (8 hours)

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 9:00-9:30 | Welcome, overview |
| 9:30-10:30 | Marketing fundamentals + STP |
| 10:30-10:45 | Break |
| 10:45-12:00 | Marketing mix workshop |
| 12:00-13:00 | Lunch |
| 13:00-14:30 | Digital marketing + social media |
| 14:30-14:45 | Break |
| 14:45-16:00 | Branding and messaging |
| 16:00-16:45 | Marketing plan development |
| 16:45-17:00 | Presentations, close |

### Format 3: Marketing Bootcamp (3 days)

| Day | Focus | Outcomes |
|-----|-------|----------|
| Day 1 | Foundations | STP, personas, marketing mix |
| Day 2 | Digital & Branding | Social media, content, brand identity |
| Day 3 | Planning & Execution | Marketing plan, measurement, action |

### Format 4: Sector-Specific Workshop

| Track | Duration | Focus |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Cooperative Marketing** | 1 day | Member engagement, shared marketing |
| **SE Marketing** | 1 day | Impact storytelling, mission communication |
| **Digital for Small Business** | Half day | Social media, content, basics |

## Learning Objectives by Level

### Foundational Level (Bloom's: Remember, Understand)

| Module | Objectives |
|--------|------------|
| Intro | Define marketing; identify marketing examples |
| Market | Describe segmentation; explain targeting |
| Mix | List the 4Ps/7Ps; describe each element |
| Digital | Identify social media platforms; explain SEO basics |
| Branding | Describe brand elements; explain positioning |
| Communication | Explain AIDA; describe effective messaging |
| Planning | List marketing plan components; describe SWOT |
| Measurement | Identify key metrics; explain ROI concept |

### Practitioner Level (Bloom's: Apply, Analyze)

| Module | Objectives |
|--------|------------|
| Intro | Analyze marketing approaches; compare orientations |
| Market | Conduct segmentation; create personas |
| Mix | Develop marketing mix; analyze competitors |
| Digital | Create social media content; implement campaigns |
| Branding | Develop brand identity; create positioning statement |
| Communication | Write marketing copy; apply AIDA framework |
| Planning | Create marketing plan; set SMART goals |
| Measurement | Track metrics; analyze campaign performance |

### Advanced Level (Bloom's: Evaluate, Create)

| Module | Objectives |
|--------|------------|
| Intro | Evaluate marketing strategies; design marketing approach |
| Market | Design research methodology; evaluate segments |
| Mix | Create integrated strategies; optimize mix |
| Digital | Design multi-channel campaigns; evaluate performance |
| Branding | Create brand strategy; evaluate brand equity |
| Communication | Design communication strategy; evaluate messaging |
| Planning | Create comprehensive plans; design frameworks |
| Measurement | Design measurement systems; create dashboards |

## Facilitation Guidelines

### Before the Session

1. **Prepare materials**: Handouts, templates, examples
2. **Know audience**: Industry, experience level, goals
3. **Set up space**: Tables for group work, projector, supplies
4. **Prepare examples**: Sector-relevant cases (coop, SE, government)

### During the Session

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Use local examples | Rely on foreign case studies only |
| Encourage participation | Lecture for extended periods |
| Apply to their context | Teach theory without application |
| Provide templates | Assume they'll create from scratch |
| Practice skills | Just explain concepts |

### Common Challenges

| Challenge | Solution |
|-----------|----------|
| Limited experience | Use simple examples, basic terminology |
| Budget constraints | Focus on low-cost strategies |
| Digital literacy gaps | Start with basics, provide hands-on practice |
| Time constraints | Prioritize actionable takeaways |
| Mixed experience levels | Pair advanced with beginners |

### Post-Session

1. **Provide resources**: Templates, checklists, further reading
2. **Assign homework**: Apply learning to own organization
3. **Follow up**: Check-in on implementation
4. **Gather feedback**: Improve future sessions
