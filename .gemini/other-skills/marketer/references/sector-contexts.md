# Marketing in Sector Contexts

Adapting marketing for cooperatives, social enterprises, and government in BARMM, aligned with the 2nd Bangsamoro Development Plan 2023-2028.

## BDP 2023-2028 Vision and Marketing Alignment

### Regional Vision and Goals

> "A Bangsamoro that is united, enlightened, self-governing, peaceful, just, morally upright, and progressive."

**Overall Goal:** Empowered, Cohesive, and Progressive Bangsamoro

| Development Goal | Marketing Alignment |
|------------------|---------------------|
| Stable, accountable government | Trust-building, transparency messaging |
| Equitable, robust economy | Economic opportunity, growth stories |
| Peaceful, resilient communities | Safety messaging, community resilience |
| Quality services | Service accessibility, citizen-centered |
| Rich Bangsamoro culture | Cultural pride, heritage marketing |
| Climate-resilient infrastructure | Sustainability, development progress |

### Macroeconomic Targets (2028)

| Target | Goal | Marketing Angle |
|--------|------|-----------------|
| GRDP Growth | 8-9% annually | Economic momentum |
| Industry/Services | 10-12% growth | Business opportunity |
| Poverty Incidence | 20-25% (from 29.8%) | Impact stories |
| Unemployment | 3-5% | Job creation success |

## BIMP-EAGA and Export Marketing

### Strategic Position

**BIMP-EAGA Markets:**
- Brunei: Halal products
- Indonesia: Agricultural trade
- Malaysia (Sabah): Historical trade links

### Barter Trade

**BDP Barter Trading Program:** Accreditation, zero-tariff benefits under ASEAN FTA, barter ports

**Export Products:** Seaweed, coconut, fish to Sabah and BIMP-EAGA

### Polloc Freeport (PFEZ-BARMM)

Only existing ecozone in BARMM - gateway for international trade, investment hub

## Cultural Marketing Considerations

### Shari'ah Compliance

| Aspect | Approach |
|--------|----------|
| Products | Clear halal certification |
| Imagery | Modest, culturally appropriate |
| Timing | Consider prayer times, Ramadan |
| Finance | Shari'ah-compliant messaging |

### Language

| Language | Usage |
|----------|-------|
| Filipino | Official communications |
| Maguindanaon | Maguindanao engagement |
| Maranao | Lanao del Sur engagement |
| Tausug | Sulu engagement |
| Sama/Bajau | Tawi-Tawi engagement |

### Cultural Guidelines

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Respect Islamic values | Inappropriate imagery |
| Community endorsements | Ignore local customs |
| Highlight collective benefit | Focus only on individual gain |
| Include local languages | One-size-fits-all messaging |

### Islamic Calendar

| Period | Opportunity |
|--------|-------------|
| Ramadan | Iftar promotions, community giving |
| Eid al-Fitr | Celebration products |
| Eid al-Adha | Halal meat, sacrifice-related |

## Cooperative Marketing

### Characteristics

| Aspect | Difference |
|--------|-----------|
| Ownership | Member-owned, democratic |
| Purpose | Member benefit, not profit max |
| Values | ICA principles |

### BDP Objectives

- Profitability increased
- Compliance rate to 50%
- LGU participation strengthened

### Strategies

**Member Engagement:** Newsletters, assemblies, recognition, education

**Shared Resources:** Joint advertising, shared brand, collective events

**Compliance Marketing (11.66% rate):** Clear benefits, success stories, LGU partnerships

## Social Enterprise Marketing

### BDP Framework

- No national SE legislation yet
- BARMM pursuing institutionalization
- Goal: Established social enterprises increased

### Impact Storytelling

1. THE PROBLEM - Social/environmental issue
2. THE SOLUTION - How SE addresses it
3. THE PROOF - Measurable impact
4. THE INVITATION - How customers participate

### Strategies

**Mission-Led:** Lead with why, impact badges, transparent pricing

**Community Building:** Behind-the-scenes, impact updates, ambassador programs
