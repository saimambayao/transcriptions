# COOP Curriculum Series - Training for Cooperatives

Comprehensive training curriculum based on **CDA MC 2025-09** (July 2025), adopted by CSEA for BARMM cooperatives.

## Series Overview

| Series | Target Audience | Duration | Prerequisite | Compliance |
|--------|-----------------|----------|--------------|------------|
| **COOP-100** | All Members | 16 hours | None | Within 1 year of membership |
| **COOP-200** | Officers of Micro Cooperatives (≤₱3M assets) | 24 hours | COOP-100 | Within first half of term |
| **COOP-300** | Officers of Small/Medium/Large Cooperatives (>₱3M assets) | 32 hours | COOP-100 | Within first half of term |
| **COOP-400** | Officers of S&C Cooperatives (≥₱5M deposit liabilities) | 16 hours | COOP-300 | Additional requirement |

---

## COOP-100 Series: Basic Cooperative Course for Members (16 hours)

**Target**: All cooperative members (new and existing)
**Compliance**: MC 2025-09 Section VI.1 - Complete within 1 year of membership

| Course | Title | Duration | Topics |
|--------|-------|----------|--------|
| **COOP-101** | Fundamentals of Cooperatives | 6 hours | Origin, philosophy, identity, principles, cooperative difference |
| **COOP-102** | Cooperative Laws and Relevant Issuances | 6 hours | RA 9520, RA 11364, RA 10744, member rights |
| **COOP-103** | Leadership and Values Formation | 2 hours | Cooperative leadership, values, active participation |
| **COOP-104** | Financial Literacy | 2 hours | Personal finance, cooperative finances, digital literacy |

**Certificate**: Basic Cooperative Course Certificate (16 hours)

---

## COOP-200 Series: Advanced Course for Micro Cooperative Officers (24 hours)

**Target**: Officers of Micro Cooperatives (≤₱3M assets)
**Prerequisite**: COOP-100 Series (16 hours)
**Compliance**: MC 2025-09 Section VI.2.1 - Within first half of term

| Course | Title | Duration | Topics |
|--------|-------|----------|--------|
| **COOP-201** | Cooperative Management and Governance | 8 hours | BOD roles, GA, committees, policy formulation, relevant laws |
| **COOP-202** | Financial Management Fundamentals | 8 hours | Financial statement interpretation, ratios, analysis |
| **COOP-203** | Line of Business Training | 8 hours | Specific to cooperative type (credit, marketing, agri, etc.) |

**Certificate**: Advanced Cooperative Course Certificate - Micro (24 hours)

---

## COOP-300 Series: Advanced Course for S/M/L Cooperative Officers (32 hours)

**Target**: Officers of Small, Medium, and Large Cooperatives (>₱3M assets)
**Prerequisite**: COOP-100 Series (16 hours)
**Compliance**: MC 2025-09 Section VI.2.1 - Within first half of term

| Course | Title | Duration | Topics |
|--------|-------|----------|--------|
| **COOP-301** | Expanded Cooperative Management and Governance | 16 hours | Advanced governance, relevant laws, parliamentary procedure, policy formulation |
| **COOP-302** | Financial Management for Officers | 8 hours | Basic accounting for non-accountants, internal control |
| **COOP-303** | Line of Business Training | 4 hours | Specific to cooperative type |
| **COOP-304** | Role/Function Specific Training | 4 hours | Training specific to officer position (BOD, Treasurer, Secretary, Committees) |

**Certificate**: Advanced Cooperative Course Certificate - S/M/L (32 hours)

---

## COOP-400 Series: Specialized Training for S&C Cooperative Officers (16 hours)

**Target**: Officers of cooperatives engaged in savings and credit with ≥₱5,000,000 deposit liabilities
**Prerequisite**: COOP-300 Series (32 hours)
**Compliance**: MC 2025-09 Section VI.2.1 - Additional requirement

| Course | Title | Duration | Topics |
|--------|-------|----------|--------|
| **COOP-401** | Advanced Financial Management | 4 hours | Financial statements, accounting principles, internal control systems |
| **COOP-402** | Risk Management | 6 hours | Credit risk, operational risk, liquidity risk, compliance risk |
| **COOP-403** | Credit Management | 6 hours | Credit evaluation, loan management, collection strategies, delinquency control |

**Certificate**: Specialized S&C Management Certificate (16 hours)

---

## Complete Officer Training Summary

| Cooperative Category | Asset Threshold | Required Training | Total Hours |
|---------------------|-----------------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Micro** | ≤₱3,000,000 | COOP-100 + COOP-200 | 16h + 24h = **40 hours** |
| **Small/Medium/Large** | >₱3,000,000 | COOP-100 + COOP-300 | 16h + 32h = **48 hours** |
| **S&C with deposits** | ≥₱5,000,000 deposits | COOP-100 + COOP-300 + COOP-400 | 16h + 32h + 16h = **64 hours** |

## Compliance Timeline

| Requirement | When to Complete |
|-------------|------------------|
| **Members** (COOP-100) | Within **1 year** from date of membership acceptance |
| **Officers** (COOP-200/300/400) | Within **first half of officer's term** |

### Sanctions for Non-Compliance

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| Cooperative fails to ensure officer training | Non-issuance of Certificate of Compliance |
| Officer fails to complete training | Disqualification from elective/appointive position |

---

## Micro Coop Upgrade Path

When a Micro Cooperative graduates to Small category (assets exceed ₱3M):

```
Current: COOP-100 (16h) + COOP-200 (24h) = 40 hours
Upgrade: + Additional 8h on Expanded Governance (Macro-level concepts, ICA Blueprint)
New Total: 48 hours (equivalent to COOP-300 pathway)
```

## Regulatory Basis

- **CDA MC 2025-09** (July 29, 2025) - Required and Recommended Trainings for Members, Officers, and Staff of Cooperatives
- **RA 9520** - Philippine Cooperative Code of 2008
- **RA 11364** - CDA Charter of 2019
- **Section 5, Rule 7** - Revised IRR of RA 9520 (Training Requirements)

---

## BARMM Context

- **CSEA** is the sole regulatory agency for cooperatives in BARMM
- All CDA memorandum circulars are adopted by CSEA for BARMM cooperatives
- Training may be conducted through traditional, hybrid, or virtual/online means
- Courses include BARMM contextualization (Shari'ah-compliant practices, Moral Governance alignment)
