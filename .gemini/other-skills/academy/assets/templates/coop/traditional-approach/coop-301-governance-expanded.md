## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

1. **Apply** advanced governance principles in cooperative management
2. **Implement** parliamentary procedures in meetings effectively
3. **Formulate** comprehensive policies aligned with RA 9520 and CDA regulations
4. **Analyze** relevant laws affecting cooperative operations
5. **Lead** strategic planning processes for the cooperative
6. **Demonstrate** ethical leadership and conflict management
7. **Ensure** regulatory compliance with CDA/CSEA requirements
8. **Align** cooperative strategies with international cooperative development (ICA Blueprint)

## Script Requirements

### Script Pacing (~90 WPM)

All scripts use **~90 wpm** to accommodate BARMM's ESL beginner learners.

**Technical Term Pattern**: `"[English term], o [Filipino explanation]"`

- Example: "Ang **fiduciary duty**, o tungkuling mapagkakatiwalaan..."
