## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

1. **Explain** the roles and responsibilities of BOD members, officers, and committees
2. **Apply** parliamentary procedure basics in cooperative meetings
3. **Formulate** cooperative policies aligned with RA 9520 and CDA regulations
4. **Conduct** effective General Assembly meetings
5. **Demonstrate** ethical decision-making in cooperative governance
6. **Comply** with CDA/CSEA reporting and regulatory requirements

### Unit 1: Board of Directors Roles and Functions (2 hours)

> **MC 2025-09 Topic**: Governance and Management

**Lesson 1.1: BOD Composition and Qualifications** (VIDEO - 12 min)

- BOD composition (5-15 members per RA 9520 Art. 39)
- Qualifications for directors
- Term limits and election process
- Disqualifications and grounds for removal
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 1.2: BOD Duties and Responsibilities** (VIDEO - 15 min)

- Fiduciary duties: Duty of care, loyalty, obedience
- Strategic planning and policy formulation
- Oversight of management and operations
- Financial oversight and budget approval
- Accountability to members
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 1.3: Officer Positions and Functions** (VIDEO - 12 min)

- **Chairperson**: Presiding over meetings, representing the cooperative
- **Vice-Chairperson**: Assisting chair, succession
- **Secretary**: Records, correspondence, membership
- **Treasurer**: Financial custody, disbursements
- **General Manager**: Day-to-day operations (if applicable)
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 1.4: Board Meetings and Decision-Making** (TEXT - 10 min)

- Regular vs special meetings
- Quorum requirements
- Voting procedures
- Board resolutions
- Minutes documentation
- **Reading**: English + Filipino versions

**Quiz 1: BOD Roles and Functions** (5 questions)

## Script Requirements

### Script Pacing (~90 WPM)

All scripts use **~90 wpm** to accommodate BARMM's ESL beginner learners.

**Technical Term Pattern**: `"[English term], o [Filipino explanation]"`

- Example: "Ang **Board of Directors**, o Lupon ng mga Direktor..."
- Example: "Ang **fiduciary duty**, o tungkuling mapagkakatiwalaan..."
