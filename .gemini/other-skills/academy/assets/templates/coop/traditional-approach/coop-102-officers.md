# COOP-102: Advanced Cooperative Course

Required training for all cooperative officers per CDA MC 2025-09.

---

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Code** | COOP-102 |
| **Type** | Online Course (Self-Paced) |
| **Duration** | 24 hours (Micro) / 32 hours (Larger Coops) |
| **Target Audience** | BOD, Secretary, Treasurer, Committee Officers, CEO/GM |
| **Prerequisites** | COOP-101: Fundamentals of Cooperatives |
| **Compliance** | CDA MC 2025-09 Mandatory - Complete within first half of term |
| **Certificate** | Yes (80% completion + 70% assessment) |
| **Languages** | English + Filipino (bilingual scripts) |

## Training Structure by Cooperative Category

### Micro Cooperatives (≤ ₱3M Assets) - 24 Hours

| Module | Duration | Topics |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Module 1: Cooperative Management & Governance | 8 hours | Governance, relevant laws, policy formulation |
| Module 2: Financial Management | 8 hours | Financial statements, ratios, analysis |
| Module 3: Line of Business Training | 8 hours | Industry-specific operations |
| **Total** | **24 hours** | |

### Small, Medium, and Large Cooperatives (> ₱3M Assets) - 32 Hours

| Module | Duration | Topics |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Module 1: Expanded Governance & Management | 16 hours | Governance, laws, parliamentary procedure, policy |
| Module 2: Financial Management | 8 hours | Basic accounting, internal control |
| Module 3: Line of Business Training | 4 hours | Industry-specific operations |
| Module 4: Role-Specific Training | 4 hours | Committee/position-specific duties |
| **Total** | **32 hours** | |

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, officers will be able to:

### Governance Competencies

1. **Apply** principles of good governance in cooperative decision-making
2. **Distinguish** roles and responsibilities of BOD, management, and committees
3. **Implement** parliamentary procedures in board and committee meetings
4. **Develop** policies aligned with cooperative laws and regulations

### Financial Competencies

5. **Read** and **interpret** cooperative financial statements
6. **Calculate** key financial ratios (PEARLS framework)
7. **Design** internal control systems appropriate to cooperative size
8. **Prepare** financial reports for regulatory compliance

### Operational Competencies

9. **Apply** industry-specific best practices to cooperative operations
10. **Execute** role-specific functions effectively
11. **Monitor** performance using appropriate indicators

## Script Requirements

### Video Scripts

Each video lesson requires:

1. **English Script** - Primary language for formal terms
2. **Filipino Script** - Localized version for accessibility
3. **Timing Markers** - Scene/slide change indicators
4. **Visual Cues** - On-screen text and graphics notes

### Script Pacing (~90 WPM)

All scripts use **~90 wpm** to accommodate BARMM's ESL beginner learners. Additional pacing considerations:

- Longer words with affixes (mag-, nag-, -in, -an) in Filipino
- Technical term explanation pairs (English term + Filipino meaning)
- Natural code-switching (Taglish) pauses
- Clearer enunciation for educational content

**Technical Term Pattern**: `"[English term], o [Filipino explanation]"`

- Example: "Ang **fiduciary duty**, o responsibilidad bilang pinagkakatiwalaan, ay..."
- Example: "I-compute natin ang **PEARLS ratio**, ang sukat ng kalusugan pinansyal..."

**Timing Buffers**:

- Add +15 seconds per technical definition
- Add +20 seconds for calculation walkthroughs
- Total buffer: 15-20% of estimated duration

### Governance Best Practices

**Fiduciary Duties of Cooperative Board Members:**

- **Duty of Care**: Act with reasonable care, make informed decisions, attend meetings, ask questions
- **Duty of Loyalty**: Put the cooperative's interests first; avoid conflicts of interest
- **Duty of Obedience**: Follow laws, bylaws, and cooperative policies
- **Business Judgment Rule**: Protects directors who act in good faith within their authority

**Key Principle**: A director has a fiduciary obligation to the cooperative and its members as a whole, not to any constituency or group.
