# COOP-203: Line of Business Training (Micro)

Specialized training based on cooperative type and primary line of business.

> **Series**: COOP-200 (Advanced Course for Micro Cooperative Officers)
> **Regulatory Basis**: CDA MC 2025-09 Annex A (adopted by CSEA for BARMM)

## Course Structure

This course is **modular by cooperative type**. Officers select the track matching their cooperative's primary line of business:

| Track | Cooperative Types | Duration |
|-------|-------------------|----------|
| **Track A** | Credit Cooperative | 8 hours |
| **Track B** | Marketing Cooperative | 8 hours |
| **Track C** | Producer Cooperative | 8 hours |
| **Track D** | Consumer Cooperative | 8 hours |
| **Track E** | Service Cooperative | 8 hours |
| **Track F** | Multi-Purpose Cooperative | 8 hours |
| **Track G** | Agriculture/Fisheries Cooperative | 8 hours |
| **Track H** | Transport Cooperative | 8 hours |

**Note:** Officers complete ONE track based on their cooperative type. Multi-purpose cooperatives may need multiple tracks.

---

## Track A: Credit Cooperative (8 hours)

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this track, learners will be able to:

1. **Explain** the basic credit operations of a cooperative
2. **Apply** loan evaluation and approval processes
3. **Implement** collection strategies and delinquency management
4. **Calculate** simple interest and loan amortization
5. **Manage** savings mobilization programs

### Track A Curriculum

| Unit | Duration | Topics |
|------|----------|--------|
| **Unit A1** | 2 hours | Savings Mobilization |
| **Unit A2** | 2 hours | Loan Products and Evaluation |
| **Unit A3** | 2 hours | Collection and Delinquency Management |
| **Unit A4** | 2 hours | Credit Risk Basics |

**Unit A1: Savings Mobilization (2 hours)**

- Types of savings products
- Savings policy development
- Member savings promotion
- Interest rate setting
- Deposit insurance considerations

**Unit A2: Loan Products and Evaluation (2 hours)**

- Types of loan products
- Loan application process
- Credit investigation basics
- Character, capacity, capital, collateral, condition
- Loan approval procedures

**Unit A3: Collection and Delinquency Management (2 hours)**

- Collection procedures
- Aging of receivables
- Delinquency ratios
- Remedial management
- Write-off policies

**Unit A4: Credit Risk Basics (2 hours)**

- Credit risk identification
- Portfolio monitoring
- Provisioning requirements
- Risk mitigation strategies
- Reporting to BOD

## Appendix: Track Selection Guide

| If Your Cooperative Primarily... | Select Track |
|----------------------------------|--------------|
| Provides loans and savings services | Track A: Credit |
| Markets members' products collectively | Track B: Marketing |
| Produces goods through member labor | Track C: Producer |
| Operates retail stores for members | Track D: Consumer |
| Provides services (water, electricity, health) | Track E: Service |
| Has multiple business activities | Track F: Multi-Purpose |
| Supports farming/fishing activities | Track G: Agriculture/Fisheries |
| Operates public transportation | Track H: Transport |

**Multi-Purpose Cooperatives**: Officers may complete Track F plus one additional track based on the cooperative's major business activity.
