## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

1. **Define** cooperative leadership as service to members and community
2. **Identify** core values essential for cooperative members
3. **Demonstrate** commitment to ethical behavior in cooperative activities
4. **Explain** the importance of active participation in cooperative governance
5. **Apply** cooperative values in personal and community decision-making
6. **Align** leadership principles with BARMM's Moral Governance framework

### Unit 1: Cooperative Leadership (45 minutes)

> **MC 2025-09 Topic**: Leadership and Values Formation

**Lesson 1.1: What is Cooperative Leadership?** (VIDEO - 12 min)
- Leadership as service, not power
- Every member as a potential leader
- The democratic nature of cooperative leadership
- Servant leadership principles
- Examples of cooperative leaders in the Philippines
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 1.2: Leadership Opportunities in Cooperatives** (VIDEO - 10 min)
- Pathways to leadership: member → committee → board
- Roles where members can contribute:
  - Committee membership
  - Representative to federation
  - Community liaison
  - Training champion
- Building leadership skills through participation
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Quiz 1: Cooperative Leadership** (5 questions)

---

### Unit 2: Core Values for Cooperative Members (45 minutes)

> **MC 2025-09 Topic**: Leadership and Values Formation

**Lesson 2.1: Essential Values for Cooperative Members** (VIDEO - 12 min)
- **Integrity and Honesty**: Truthfulness in all dealings
- **Commitment**: Dedication to cooperative success
- **Respect**: Valuing all members equally
- **Responsibility**: Taking ownership of duties
- **Continuous Learning**: Growing skills and knowledge
- How values connect to ICA principles
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 2.2: Values in BARMM Context - Moral Governance** (VIDEO - 12 min)
- **Moral Governance 12 Pillars** alignment:

| Pillar | Cooperative Application |
|--------|------------------------|
| *Amanah* (Trust) | Honest stewardship of cooperative resources |
| *Adalah* (Justice) | Fair treatment of all members |
| *Khidmah* (Service) | Servant leadership |
| *Ihsan* (Excellence) | Quality in all cooperative work |

- Islamic values reinforcing cooperative ethics
- Building ethical cooperative culture in BARMM
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Quiz 2: Core Values** (5 questions)

---

### Unit 3: Active Participation (30 minutes)

> **MC 2025-09 Topic**: Leadership and Values Formation

**Lesson 3.1: Participating in Cooperative Governance** (VIDEO - 10 min)
- **General Assembly participation**:
  - Attending meetings
  - Speaking up in discussions
  - Voting on resolutions
  - Running for positions
- Being an informed member
- Supporting cooperative decisions even when you disagree
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 3.2: Beyond the GA - Daily Participation** (TEXT - 10 min)
- Patronizing cooperative services
- Recruiting new members
- Promoting the cooperative in the community
- Volunteering for activities
- Paying obligations on time
- Providing constructive feedback
- **Reading**: English + Filipino versions

**Quiz 3: Active Participation** (5 questions)

## Script Requirements

### Script Pacing (~90 WPM)

All scripts use **~90 wpm** to accommodate BARMM's ESL beginner learners.

**Technical Term Pattern**: `"[English term], o [Filipino explanation]"`
- Example: "Ang **servant leadership**, o pamumunong naglilingkod..."
- Example: "Ang **integrity**, o katapatan sa lahat ng pagkakataon..."
