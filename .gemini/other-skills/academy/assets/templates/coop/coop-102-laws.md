## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

1. **Explain** the purpose and scope of RA 9520 (Philippine Cooperative Code)
2. **Identify** member rights under RA 9520 and how to exercise them
3. **Describe** member responsibilities and duties to the cooperative
4. **Outline** the basic governance structure of cooperatives (GA, BOD, Committees)
5. **Explain** the role of CDA/CSEA as regulatory authorities
6. **Summarize** RA 11364 (CDA Charter) provisions relevant to members
7. **Describe** RA 10744 (Credit Surety Fund) and its benefits to cooperatives

### Unit 1: RA 9520 - Philippine Cooperative Code of 2008 (3 hours)

> **MC 2025-09 Topic**: Salient Provisions of RA 9520 and its IRR

**Lesson 1.1: Overview of RA 9520** (VIDEO - 10 min)
- **Full Title**: Philippine Cooperative Code of 2008
- **Effectivity**: February 17, 2009
- **Structure**: 19 Chapters, 146 Articles
- **Purpose**: Foster creation and growth of cooperatives
- **Scope**: All cooperatives registered in the Philippines
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 1.2: Member Rights Under RA 9520** (VIDEO - 12 min)
- Right to vote and be voted upon (Art. 27)
- Right to avail of cooperative services
- Right to receive patronage refund (Art. 86)
- Right to inspect cooperative books (Art. 56)
- Right to attend and participate in General Assembly (Art. 38)
- Right to due process in disciplinary actions (Art. 30)
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 1.3: Member Responsibilities Under RA 9520** (VIDEO - 10 min)
- Pay share capital subscription (Art. 54)
- Patronize cooperative services
- Attend General Assembly meetings
- Comply with bylaws and policies
- Participate in cooperative activities
- Protect cooperative interests
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 1.4: Cooperative Governance Structure** (VIDEO - 12 min)
- **General Assembly**: Supreme authority, composed of all members (Art. 38)
- **Board of Directors**: Policy-making body, 5-15 members (Art. 39)
- **Mandatory Committees**: Audit, Election, Ethics, Mediation, Education (Art. 46-51)
- **Management**: CEO/GM and staff
- How decisions flow from GA to daily operations
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 1.5: Share Capital and Member Equity** (VIDEO - 10 min)
- What is share capital? (Art. 53)
- Minimum capital requirements
- Share capital vs. savings deposits
- Withdrawal of share capital (Art. 55)
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 1.6: Net Surplus Allocation (Article 86)** (VIDEO - 12 min)
- Understanding net surplus
- **Statutory Fund Allocations**:

| Fund | Rate | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| Reserve Fund | 10% | Cover losses, stability |
| Education & Training Fund (ETF) | Up to 10% | Training, development |
| Community Development Fund (CDF) | 3% minimum | Community projects |
| Optional Fund | Up to 7% | Land, building, other |

- **After allocations**: Patronage refund (based on your transactions)
- Interest on share capital
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 1.7: Tax Privileges Under RA 9520** (TEXT - 12 min)
- Tax exemptions for cooperatives (Art. 61)
- Conditions for maintaining exemptions
- Non-tax privileges (court fees, procurement preference)
- Member benefits from tax privileges
- **Reading**: English + Filipino versions

**Quiz 1: RA 9520** (15 questions)

### Unit 2: RA 11364 - CDA Charter of 2019 (1.5 hours)

> **MC 2025-09 Topic**: Salient Provisions of RA 11364

**Lesson 2.1: Overview of RA 11364** (VIDEO - 10 min)
- **Full Title**: Cooperative Development Authority Charter of 2019
- **Purpose**: Strengthen CDA as the sole agency for cooperative development
- **Key provisions**: Registration, supervision, development programs
- **BARMM Context**: CSEA as the regional equivalent
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 2.2: CDA/CSEA Functions and Powers** (VIDEO - 10 min)
- Registration of cooperatives
- Technical assistance and training
- Supervision and examination
- Policy formulation
- How CDA/CSEA supports your cooperative
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 2.3: Training Requirements Under RA 11364** (VIDEO - 12 min)
- Standard training requirements for members and officers
- MC 2025-09 training curriculum
- Why this training is mandatory (Section 17, RA 11364)
- Training providers: CDA, CDOs, CTPros
- Consequences of non-compliance
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 2.4: Compliance and Reporting** (TEXT - 10 min)
- Annual reports required
- Certificate of Compliance (COC)
- Inspection and examination
- Sanctions for non-compliance
- **Reading**: English + Filipino versions

**Quiz 2: RA 11364** (10 questions)

---

### Unit 3: RA 10744 - Credit Surety Fund Act of 2015 (1.5 hours)

> **MC 2025-09 Topic**: Salient Provisions of RA 10744

**Lesson 3.1: What is the Credit Surety Fund?** (VIDEO - 10 min)
- **Purpose**: Enable cooperatives and MSMEs to access credit without traditional collateral
- **How it works**: Pooled guarantee fund
- **Who benefits**: Cooperatives lacking collateral
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 3.2: How CSF Benefits Cooperative Members** (VIDEO - 10 min)
- Access to loans without traditional collateral
- Lower interest rates
- Building credit history
- Requirements to access CSF
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 3.3: CSF Cooperatives and Participation** (TEXT - 10 min)
- How cooperatives participate in CSF
- Member contributions to CSF
- Rights and obligations
- CSF in BARMM context
- **Reading**: English + Filipino versions

**Quiz 3: RA 10744** (5 questions)
