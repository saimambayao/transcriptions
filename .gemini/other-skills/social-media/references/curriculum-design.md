## Learning Levels

### Level Definitions

| Level | Audience | Focus | Duration |
|-------|----------|-------|----------|
| **Foundational** | Beginners, new to social media | Basics, getting started | 1-2 days |
| **Practitioner** | Active users, marketing staff | Strategy, content, analytics | 3-5 days |
| **Advanced** | Managers, consultants | Strategy, paid ads, crisis mgmt | 5+ days |

### Level Prerequisites

| Level | Prerequisites |
|-------|--------------|
| **Foundational** | Basic smartphone/computer skills |
| **Practitioner** | Foundational + active social accounts |
| **Advanced** | Practitioner + managing organizational accounts |

## Standard Course Modules

### Foundational Level Modules

| Module | Topics | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **1. Introduction to Social Media** | What is social media, platforms overview, benefits | 2 hrs |
| **2. Getting Started** | Account setup, profile optimization, security | 2 hrs |
| **3. Understanding Your Audience** | Target market, personas, where they are | 2 hrs |
| **4. Content Basics** | Content types, what to post, basic creation | 2 hrs |
| **5. Posting and Engagement** | When to post, how to engage, responding | 2 hrs |
| **6. Basic Measurement** | Insights overview, simple metrics, what to track | 2 hrs |
| **7. Social Media Safety** | Privacy, security, handling negativity | 2 hrs |
| **8. Action Planning** | Creating a simple plan, next steps | 2 hrs |

### Practitioner Level Modules

| Module | Topics | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **1. Social Media Strategy** | SMART goals, platform selection, content pillars | 4 hrs |
| **2. Content Strategy** | Content calendar, formats, scheduling, tools | 4 hrs |
| **3. Visual Content Creation** | Photography, graphics, Canva, video basics | 4 hrs |
| **4. Video Content** | Reels, short-form video, live streaming | 4 hrs |
| **5. Community Management** | Engagement, moderation, building community | 4 hrs |
| **6. Analytics and Reporting** | KPIs, platform analytics, reporting | 4 hrs |
| **7. Paid Social Basics** | Boosting posts, Facebook Ads basics | 4 hrs |
| **8. Campaign Planning** | Planning, executing, measuring campaigns | 4 hrs |

### Advanced Level Modules

| Module | Topics | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **1. Strategic Social Media Management** | Long-term strategy, brand alignment, governance | 4 hrs |
| **2. Advanced Content Strategy** | UGC, influencer collaboration, viral content | 4 hrs |
| **3. Paid Social Advertising** | Campaign objectives, targeting, optimization | 4 hrs |
| **4. Social Media for Sales** | Social selling, lead generation, conversion | 4 hrs |
| **5. Crisis Management** | Prevention, response, recovery | 4 hrs |
| **6. Team and Process Management** | Workflows, tools, team coordination | 4 hrs |
| **7. Advanced Analytics** | Attribution, ROI, competitive analysis | 4 hrs |
| **8. Emerging Trends** | New platforms, AI tools, future of social | 4 hrs |

## Workshop Formats

### Half-Day Workshop (4 hours)

**Title**: "Social Media Basics for [Coops/SEs/Government]"

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 0:00-0:30 | Introduction and expectations |
| 0:30-1:30 | Platforms overview and getting started |
| 1:30-1:45 | Break |
| 1:45-2:45 | Content basics and hands-on creation |
| 2:45-3:30 | Posting, engagement, and measurement |
| 3:30-4:00 | Action planning and wrap-up |

### Full-Day Workshop (8 hours)

**Title**: "Building Your Social Media Presence"

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 9:00-9:30 | Introduction and expectations |
| 9:30-10:30 | Strategy fundamentals (goals, audience, platforms) |
| 10:30-10:45 | Break |
| 10:45-12:00 | Content strategy and creation |
| 12:00-13:00 | Lunch |
| 13:00-14:30 | Hands-on content creation workshop |
| 14:30-14:45 | Break |
| 14:45-16:00 | Community management and engagement |
| 16:00-17:00 | Analytics, measurement, and action planning |

### Multi-Day Intensive (3 days)

**Title**: "Complete Social Media Management"

| Day | Focus | Modules |
|-----|-------|---------|
| **Day 1** | Strategy | Goals, audience, platforms, content pillars |
| **Day 2** | Execution | Content creation, scheduling, engagement |
| **Day 3** | Optimization | Analytics, paid social, campaigns, action plan |

## Learning Outcomes

### Foundational Level Outcomes

By end of course, participants can:

```
□ Explain what social media is and its benefits
□ Set up and optimize social media accounts
□ Identify their target audience
□ Create basic content (text, images)
□ Post and engage appropriately
□ Read basic analytics
□ Maintain account security
```

### Practitioner Level Outcomes

By end of course, participants can:

```
□ Develop a social media strategy with SMART goals
□ Create and manage a content calendar
□ Create engaging visual and video content
□ Build and manage online communities
□ Analyze performance and create reports
□ Run basic paid advertising campaigns
□ Plan and execute social media campaigns
```

### Advanced Level Outcomes

By end of course, participants can:

```
□ Develop long-term social media strategies
□ Lead content and influencer programs
□ Manage advanced paid advertising
□ Handle social media crises
□ Manage teams and workflows
□ Calculate social media ROI
□ Stay current with trends and innovations
```

## Assessment Methods

### Assessment Types by Level

| Level | Assessment Methods |
|-------|-------------------|
| **Foundational** | Quiz, account setup, first post |
| **Practitioner** | Content calendar, campaign plan, analytics report |
| **Advanced** | Strategy document, crisis simulation, portfolio |

### Practical Exercises

| Exercise | Level | Duration |
|----------|-------|----------|
| **Account Optimization** | Foundational | 30 mins |
| **Content Creation** | Foundational | 45 mins |
| **Content Calendar** | Practitioner | 60 mins |
| **Campaign Planning** | Practitioner | 90 mins |
| **Strategy Document** | Advanced | 2-3 hrs |
| **Crisis Simulation** | Advanced | 60 mins |

### Portfolio Assessment Components

| Component | Weight | Criteria |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| **Strategy Document** | 25% | Clarity, alignment, feasibility |
| **Content Calendar** | 20% | Organization, variety, consistency |
| **Sample Content** | 25% | Quality, brand alignment, engagement potential |
| **Analytics Report** | 20% | Accuracy, insights, recommendations |
| **Presentation** | 10% | Clarity, professionalism |

### Rubric Template

| Criterion | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Satisfactory (2) | Needs Work (1) |
|-----------|---------------|----------|------------------|----------------|
| **Strategy Alignment** | Clear goals, platform fit | Good alignment | Basic alignment | Unclear |
| **Content Quality** | Engaging, on-brand | Good quality | Acceptable | Poor |
| **Technical Execution** | Expert use of tools | Competent | Basic | Struggling |
| **Analytics Understanding** | Deep insights | Good analysis | Basic reading | Confused |

## Integration with /academy

### Invoking Social Media Curriculum

When `/academy` needs social media content:

2. Select appropriate modules from this guide
3. Adapt for sector context (coop, SE, government)
