# Policy Brief Template

**Reference Number Format:** `[AGENCY]-PB-YYYY-XXX`
Example: `MOGESMED-PB-2026-001`, `OOBC-PB-2026-003`

**Length:** 2–8 pages | **Use when:** Decision-maker briefing, time-sensitive, front-loaded action

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

*1 paragraph (5–7 sentences): State the problem, the recommended action, the expected outcome, and the specific ask from the reader. Write for a non-specialist. This paragraph alone should enable a decision.*

---

## 1. PROBLEM STATEMENT AND EVIDENCE

*2–3 focused paragraphs. Cover: What is the problem? Who is affected and at what scale? What does available data show? What is the root cause? Keep it factual and direct — no background story, only what is needed to justify action.*

## 3. RECOMMENDED ACTION

*2–3 paragraphs. State clearly which option is recommended and why it outperforms the others. Explain the core rationale: what changes, who benefits, and why now. Include a Theory of Change in one sentence.*

**Theory of Change:** If [inputs/actions] → then [outputs] → then [outcomes] → leading to [impact], assuming [key assumption].

*Close with what specifically the reader is being asked to approve, authorize, or direct.*
