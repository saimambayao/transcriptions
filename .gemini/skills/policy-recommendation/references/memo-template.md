# Policy Memo Template

**Reference Number Format:** `[AGENCY]-PM-YYYY-XXX`
Example: `MOGESMED-PM-2026-001`, `OOBC-PM-2026-002`

**Length:** 1–2 pages | **Use when:** Internal communication, routine decision request, quick guidance needed

---

## DOCUMENT HEADER

```
To:      [Name, Title]
From:    [Name, Title, Office]
Date:    [DD Month YYYY]
Re:      [Subject — one line, specific]
Ref No.: [AGENCY]-PM-[YYYY]-[XXX]
Classification: Internal
```

---

## ISSUE

*1–2 sentences. State the specific decision, action, or guidance needed and the deadline for it.*

---

## BACKGROUND

*1 paragraph. Provide only the context needed to understand the issue — relevant history, legal basis, or triggering event. Do not restate the issue.*

---

## ANALYSIS

*2–3 paragraphs. Present key findings. If there are options, briefly describe them and their tradeoffs. Lead with the most important finding. Stay concise — this is not a policy paper.*

---

## RECOMMENDATION

*1 paragraph. State clearly what action is recommended, why it is the right choice, and what authority or resource it requires.*
