# Example Resolution: Commending Resolution

## User Prompt
"Draft a resolution commending a military general for his support in the Bangsamoro peace process."

## Drafting Notes
- **Type:** Commending (operative verb: "to commend")
- **Deductive ordering applied:** The WHEREAS clauses follow the required order:
  1. Global/constitutional context (AFP mandate under Constitution)
  2. National context (peace and order provision)
  3. Specific biographical facts (service record, decorations)
  4. BARMM-specific contribution (peace process, regional command)
  5. Immediate situation (new assignment)
- **No Islamic citations needed** — this is a secular commendation
- **Operative clause** matches the title exactly (per skill rules)

## Resolution Output

Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY
Cotabato City

FIRST REGULAR SESSION

RESOLUTION NO. 18

RESOLUTION COMMENDING M/GEN CIRILITO E. SOBEJANA FOR HIS UNWAVERING SUPPORT IN THE PEACE PROCESS AND COMMITMENT IN BRINGING PEACE IN THE BANGSAMORO HOMELAND.

WHEREAS, Section 3 of Article II of the 1987 Constitution provides that "the Armed Forces of the Philippines is the protector of the people and the State. Its goal is to secure the sovereignty of the State and the integrity of the national territory";

WHEREAS, Section 5 of Article II of the 1987 Constitution provides that "the maintenance of peace and order, the protection of life, liberty, and property, and promotion of the general welfare are essential for the enjoyment by all the people of the blessings of democracy";

WHEREAS, M/Gen Cirilito E. Sobejana has served in various positions and capacities in the Armed Forces of the Philippines since 1987 and is recipient of several prestigious awards in recognition of his dedication to serve the Filipino people and his sincere devotion in achieving lasting peace in Mindanao;

WHEREAS, M/Gen Cirilito E. Sobejana was decorated with the Medal of Valor — the highest combat award given by the military — for conspicuous gallantry during an encounter with Abu Sayyaf members in Basilan on 13 January 1995;

WHEREAS, M/Gen Cirilito E. Sobejana has been instrumental in the massive efforts of both the government and the Bangsamoro people in bringing about peace and order in Mindanao;

WHEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby Resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to commend M/Gen Cirilito E. Sobejana for his unwavering support in the peace process and commitment in bringing peace in the Bangsamoro homeland;

RESOLVED FINALLY, that copy of this Resolution will be forwarded to the Offices of the Senate President of the Philippines, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and to the Interim Chief Minister.

## Annotations

**WHEREAS clause analysis:**
- Clauses 1-2: Constitutional foundation (national context layer)
- Clause 3: Biographical overview (specific situation — person's credentials)
- Clause 4: Specific achievement (Medal of Valor — the strongest evidence)
- Clause 5: BARMM connection (peace process contribution — the resolution's core)

**Structural notes:**
- Title = operative clause (identity rule satisfied)
- "RESOLVED FINALLY" handles distribution — standard for commending resolutions
- No "RESOLVED FURTHER" needed — single action resolution
- Simplified from the actual AR-18 which had more biographical clauses — for a draft, 5 WHEREAS clauses is sufficient for a commending resolution

**Good vs Weak WHEREAS comparison:**

WEAK: "WHEREAS, M/Gen Sobejana is a good soldier who has done many things for peace;"
- Vague ("good soldier", "many things")
- No citation, no specific achievement
- No constitutional or legal grounding

STRONG: "WHEREAS, M/Gen Cirilito E. Sobejana was decorated with the Medal of Valor — the highest combat award given by the military — for conspicuous gallantry during an encounter with Abu Sayyaf members in Basilan on 13 January 1995;"
- Specific award named
- Context given (highest combat award)
- Specific date, location, and circumstance
