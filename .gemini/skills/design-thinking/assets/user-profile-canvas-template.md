## Persona Twins Warning

Remember: Brief descriptions can be deceiving.

Example: Prince Charles and Ozzy Osbourne share identical brief descriptions (born 1948, grew up in England, married twice, has children, successful, rich, takes vacations in the Alps, likes dogs) yet are completely different users.

**Always dig one level deeper to understand needs in greater detail.**
