## Quadrant Guide

### SAYS (Top Left)
What does the user say out loud? Capture direct quotes.

**Prompts**:
- What words do they use?
- What do they tell others?
- What questions do they ask?
- What phrases do they repeat?

### THINKS (Top Right)
What might be going on in their mind? What do they think but not say?

**Prompts**:
- What occupies their thoughts?
- What matters to them?
- What worries them?
- What do they dream about?

### DOES (Bottom Left)
What actions and behaviors do you observe?

**Prompts**:
- What do they do currently?
- What workarounds do they use?
- What behaviors have you observed?
- What do they spend time on?

### FEELS (Bottom Right)
What emotions are they experiencing?

**Prompts**:
- What frustrates them?
- What excites them?
- What fears do they have?
- What gives them joy?

## How to Use This Template

### Individual Use
1. Gather your research (interview notes, observations)
2. Fill in each quadrant with relevant data
3. Look for patterns and contradictions
4. Complete synthesis section

### Team Workshop Use
1. Each person writes on sticky notes (one idea per note)
2. Place sticky notes in appropriate quadrants
3. Discuss and cluster similar ideas
4. Vote on key insights
5. Complete synthesis together

### Tips
- Use specific examples, not generalizations
- Include contradictions (they reveal tensions)
- Keep the user at the center
- Base on real data, not assumptions
- Update as you learn more

## Example (Cooperative Member)

**User**: Maria, Weaving Cooperative Member, 45

| SAYS | THINKS |
|------|--------|
| "The registration process is confusing" | "Why do I need so many documents?" |
| "I don't know if my products are good enough" | "Maybe CSEA can help us sell more" |
| "Our treasurer doesn't share reports" | "I hope we don't get in trouble" |

| DOES | FEELS |
|------|-------|
| Asks neighbors for help with forms | Frustrated by paperwork |
| Checks with other coops for advice | Proud of her weaving skills |
| Attends trainings when available | Anxious about compliance |

**Key Insight**: Maria wants to focus on her craft but feels burdened by administrative requirements she doesn't fully understand.
