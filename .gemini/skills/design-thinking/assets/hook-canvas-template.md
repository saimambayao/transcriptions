## Validation Questions

After completing the canvas, verify:

1. [ ] Is the external trigger contextually relevant?
2. [ ] Does the internal trigger create genuine need?
3. [ ] Is the action truly simple enough?
4. [ ] Is the reward sufficiently variable?
5. [ ] Does the investment create stored value?
6. [ ] Does the loop naturally repeat?
