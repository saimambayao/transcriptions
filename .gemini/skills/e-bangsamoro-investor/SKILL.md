# e-Bangsamoro Investor Skill

## What is e-Bangsamoro?

> "Positioning BARMM as the Philippines' first digitally-governed region through unified e-governance."

e-Bangsamoro is a unified e-governance platform that integrates:
- **Parliamentary Portal** - Legislative work, oversight, representation
- **Ministerial Portal** - Strategic planning, performance monitoring
- **Budget Portal** - BBSA-compliant fiscal management

### Investment Value Proposition

| Dimension | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Market** | First mover in BARMM e-governance (5.4M population) |
| **Impact** | Peace dividend enabler, governance transformation |
| **Legal Mandate** | RA 12254 (E-Governance Act 2025), RA 11054 (BOL) |
| **Scalability** | Replicable model for Philippine LGUs |
| **Technology** | Modern stack (React, Django, AI-powered) |

### Mode 3: PPP Structuring

For public-private partnership models.

#### PPP Models for e-Bangsamoro

| Model | Description | Revenue | Risk Allocation |
|-------|-------------|---------|-----------------|
| **Build-Operate-Transfer (BOT)** | Private builds, operates, transfers to govt | User fees, govt payments | High private risk |
| **Build-Own-Operate (BOO)** | Private builds, owns, operates permanently | SaaS fees | Moderate risk |
| **Joint Venture** | Govt + private co-invest | Shared revenue | Shared risk |
| **Service Contract** | Private provides platform as service | Annual fee | Low private risk |
| **Management Contract** | Private manages govt-owned platform | Management fee | Minimal risk |

#### PPP Value Proposition

Three-way benefit: Government gains tech expertise and risk transfer; Private partner gains long-term revenue and reference implementation; Citizens gain better service delivery and accountability. See [references/investor-pitch.md](references/investor-pitch.md) for detailed PPP benefits.
