## Workflow 1: Curriculum Design Mode

When invoked with `/academy` for financial education.

### STAGE 0: Deep Research (Mandatory)

**Before curriculum design, invoke `/deep-research` to gather evidence-based content.**

```
STAGE 0: DEEP RESEARCH (MANDATORY) → Output: Validated Research Brief
│
├── LAYER 1: Invoke /deep-research skill
│   ├── Workflow 1: Initial Deep Research (10+ searches, 5+ fetches)
│   ├── Workflow 2: Research Validation (re-fetch top sources)
│   ├── Workflow 3: Factual Accuracy Verification
│   ├── Workflow 4: Refinement (corrections, citations)
│   └── OUTPUT: Research Brief with Works Cited
│
└── LAYER 2: Finance Curriculum Validation
    ├── Review for curriculum applicability
    ├── Cross-reference with authoritative finance sources
    ├── Validate against organizational context (coop, SE)
    ├── Ensure alignment with GAAP and best practices
    ├── Verify pedagogical alignment
    └── OUTPUT: Validated Research Brief for finance curriculum design
```

**Do not proceed to curriculum design without completing deep research.**

### Step 1: Determine Course Level

| Level | Audience | Duration | Focus |
|-------|----------|----------|-------|
| **Foundation** | Board, new managers, staff | 8-16 hours | Financial literacy, reading statements |
| **Intermediate** | Managers, treasurers | 24-40 hours | Analysis, budgeting, controls |
| **Advanced** | Finance managers, executives | 40+ hours | Strategy, forecasting |
| **Executive** | C-suite, board chairs | 16-24 hours | Oversight, strategic finance |

### Step 2: Select Curriculum Modules

**Foundation Course:**
1. Financial Basics
2. Income Statement
3. Balance Sheet
4. Cash Flow Statement
5. Budgeting Basics
6. Internal Controls

**Intermediate Course:**
1. Financial Analysis
2. Cash Flow Management
3. Budgeting & Planning
4. Cost Management
5. Internal Controls
6. Financial Reporting

**Advanced Course:**
1. Strategic Finance
2. Capital Management
3. Financial Planning
4. Performance Management
5. Risk Management
6. Context-Specific Finance

### Step 3: Apply to /academy

Provide `/academy` with:
- Course level and modules
- Target audience profile
- Learning objectives per module
- Assessment methods
- Context (cooperative, SE, or both)

See [references/curriculum-design.md](references/curriculum-design.md) for detailed curriculum.
