## Learning Levels

### Level Definitions

| Level | Audience | Focus | Duration |
|-------|----------|-------|----------|
| **Foundational** | New entrepreneurs, coop officers | Brand basics, identity awareness | 1-2 days |
| **Practitioner** | Marketing staff, brand managers | Applied branding, execution | 3-5 days |
| **Advanced** | Senior managers, consultants | Strategy, portfolio, measurement | 5+ days |

### Level Prerequisites

| Level | Prerequisites |
|-------|--------------|
| **Foundational** | Basic business knowledge |
| **Practitioner** | Foundational + marketing exposure |
| **Advanced** | Practitioner + brand management experience |

## Standard Course Modules

### Foundational Level Modules

| Module | Topics | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **1. Introduction to Branding** | What is a brand, brand vs logo, brand equity basics | 2 hrs |
| **2. Brand Foundation** | Mission, vision, values, brand story | 2 hrs |
| **3. Target Audience** | Segmentation, personas, customer understanding | 2 hrs |
| **4. Brand Positioning** | Positioning statement, differentiation | 2 hrs |
| **5. Visual Identity Basics** | Logo, colors, typography fundamentals | 2 hrs |
| **6. Brand Communication** | Messaging, tone of voice, storytelling | 2 hrs |
| **7. Brand Touchpoints** | Customer journey, consistency | 2 hrs |
| **8. Brand Action Plan** | Implementation roadmap, next steps | 2 hrs |

### Practitioner Level Modules

| Module | Topics | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **1. Brand Strategy Frameworks** | Aaker, Keller CBBE, positioning strategy | 4 hrs |
| **2. Brand Identity Systems** | Kapferer prism, identity development | 4 hrs |
| **3. Visual Identity Development** | Logo design, color systems, typography | 4 hrs |
| **4. Verbal Identity** | Voice framework, messaging hierarchy, naming | 4 hrs |
| **5. Brand Guidelines** | Creating comprehensive brand guidelines | 4 hrs |
| **6. Digital Branding** | Website, social media, content branding | 4 hrs |
| **7. Brand Architecture** | Portfolio management, sub-brands, extensions | 4 hrs |
| **8. Brand Measurement** | Metrics, audits, tracking effectiveness | 4 hrs |

### Advanced Level Modules

| Module | Topics | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **1. Strategic Brand Management** | Long-term brand strategy, lifecycle management | 4 hrs |
| **2. Brand Valuation** | Brand equity measurement, financial valuation | 4 hrs |
| **3. Brand Portfolio Strategy** | Multi-brand management, architecture decisions | 4 hrs |
| **4. Rebranding** | When and how to rebrand, change management | 4 hrs |
| **5. Crisis Brand Management** | Reputation management, crisis response | 4 hrs |
| **6. Global/Regional Branding** | Cultural adaptation, localization | 4 hrs |
| **7. Employer Branding** | Internal branding, culture alignment | 4 hrs |
| **8. Brand Innovation** | Brand extensions, new product branding | 4 hrs |

## Workshop Formats

### Half-Day Workshop (4 hours)

**Title**: "Brand Basics for [Coops/SEs/New Businesses]"

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 0:00-0:30 | Introduction and ice-breaker |
| 0:30-1:30 | What is a brand? Brand equity basics |
| 1:30-1:45 | Break |
| 1:45-2:45 | Brand positioning exercise |
| 2:45-3:30 | Visual identity fundamentals |
| 3:30-4:00 | Action planning and wrap-up |

### Full-Day Workshop (8 hours)

**Title**: "Building Your Brand Identity"

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 9:00-9:30 | Introduction and expectations |
| 9:30-10:30 | Brand foundation (mission, vision, values) |
| 10:30-10:45 | Break |
| 10:45-12:00 | Target audience and positioning |
| 12:00-13:00 | Lunch |
| 13:00-14:30 | Visual identity workshop |
| 14:30-14:45 | Break |
| 14:45-16:00 | Verbal identity and messaging |
| 16:00-17:00 | Brand guidelines and action plan |

### Multi-Day Intensive (3 days)

**Title**: "Comprehensive Brand Development"

| Day | Focus | Modules |
|-----|-------|---------|
| **Day 1** | Strategy | Brand foundation, audience, positioning |
| **Day 2** | Identity | Visual identity, verbal identity, guidelines |
| **Day 3** | Execution | Touchpoints, measurement, action plan |

## Learning Outcomes

### Foundational Level Outcomes

By end of course, participants can:

```
□ Define what a brand is and why it matters
□ Articulate their organization's brand foundation
□ Identify target audience and positioning
□ Recognize basic visual identity elements
□ Create a simple brand action plan
```

### Practitioner Level Outcomes

By end of course, participants can:

```
□ Apply brand strategy frameworks (Aaker, Keller, Kapferer)
□ Develop comprehensive brand identity systems
□ Create brand guidelines for consistent execution
□ Manage digital brand presence
□ Measure and evaluate brand performance
```

### Advanced Level Outcomes

By end of course, participants can:

```
□ Develop long-term brand strategy
□ Manage multi-brand portfolios
□ Lead rebranding initiatives
□ Handle brand crises effectively
□ Value and protect brand equity
```

## Assessment Methods

### Assessment Types by Level

| Level | Assessment Methods |
|-------|-------------------|
| **Foundational** | Brand canvas, positioning statement, reflection |
| **Practitioner** | Brand audit, identity brief, guidelines draft |
| **Advanced** | Strategic plan, case analysis, portfolio review |

### Portfolio Assessment Components

| Component | Weight | Criteria |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| **Brand Audit** | 20% | Thoroughness, insight quality |
| **Strategy Document** | 25% | Framework application, feasibility |
| **Identity System** | 25% | Coherence, execution quality |
| **Implementation Plan** | 20% | Actionability, timeline |
| **Presentation** | 10% | Clarity, professionalism |

### Rubric Template

| Criterion | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Satisfactory (2) | Needs Work (1) |
|-----------|---------------|----------|------------------|----------------|
| **Framework Application** | Correctly applies multiple frameworks | Applies frameworks correctly | Basic application | Incorrect or missing |
| **Strategic Thinking** | Clear, differentiated positioning | Good positioning | Basic positioning | Unclear |
| **Visual Execution** | Professional, cohesive system | Good execution | Basic execution | Inconsistent |
| **Practical Feasibility** | Highly actionable | Mostly actionable | Somewhat actionable | Not realistic |

## Integration with /academy

### Invoking Branding Curriculum

When `/academy` needs branding content:

1. Identify learning level and audience
2. Select appropriate modules from this guide
3. Adapt for sector context (coop, SE, government)
4. Design assessments aligned with outcomes
5. Include relevant templates from `/branding` assets

### Cross-Reference with /marketer

For marketing-branding synergy in curriculum:

| Topic | Branding Focus | Marketing Focus |
|-------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Positioning** | Brand positioning | Market positioning |
| **Communication** | Brand voice, messaging | Promotion, campaigns |
| **Identity** | Visual/verbal identity | Marketing materials |
| **Measurement** | Brand equity | Marketing ROI |
