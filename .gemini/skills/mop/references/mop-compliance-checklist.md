## BOL (RA 11054) Provisions Relevant to MOPs

### Governance Structure (Articles III-IV)

- **Art. III, Sec. 1-4**: Bangsamoro government powers and structure -- verify MOP's org chart aligns with BOL-mandated structure
- **Art. IV, Sec. 1-8**: Parliamentary system -- for BTA/Parliament offices, ensure procedures align with parliamentary framework

### Fiscal Autonomy (Article XII)

- **Sec. 1-3**: Annual block grant and special development fund -- budget procedures must reference these
- **Sec. 4-7**: Revenue powers and taxing authority -- financial management chapter must comply
- **Sec. 8**: Audit by COA -- internal audit procedures must facilitate COA compliance

### Civil Service (Article IX)

- **Sec. 1-4**: Bangsamoro civil service -- HR chapter must align with BARMM civil service rules
- Merit and fitness principle in appointments
- Performance-based advancement

### Shari'ah (Article X)

- **Sec. 1-7**: Shari'ah justice system -- for justice-sector MOPs, include Shari'ah procedures
- Coordination between regular courts and Shari'ah courts where applicable

### Basic Rights (Article V)

- Service delivery procedures must respect right to due process
- Citizen feedback mechanisms must be accessible
- Anti-discrimination provisions in HR procedures

## Compliance Verification Checklist

Use this checklist during Phase 5 (REVIEW) to verify the MOP meets all requirements.

### Structure and Format
- [ ] MOP has a clear purpose statement linking to entity mandate
- [ ] Scope explicitly defines coverage and exclusions
- [ ] Legal basis section cites all applicable laws (BOL, BAAs, national laws)
- [ ] Table of contents with page numbers
- [ ] Consistent numbering system (Chapter.Section.Subsection)
- [ ] Glossary of terms and acronyms
- [ ] Amendment procedures and version log included

### Organizational
- [ ] Org chart is current and matches enabling law
- [ ] All divisions/sections are described with functions
- [ ] Job descriptions provided for all positions
- [ ] Reporting lines are clear and consistent with org chart
- [ ] Decision-making authority levels defined

### Human Resources
- [ ] Recruitment procedure documented and merit-based
- [ ] Performance evaluation system with clear criteria
- [ ] Leave management procedure included
- [ ] Training and development plan referenced
- [ ] Discipline and grievance mechanisms documented

### Financial
- [ ] Budget preparation process documented with timeline
- [ ] Disbursement procedure with required supporting documents
- [ ] Cash advance and liquidation procedures included
- [ ] Internal controls identified at each financial process step
- [ ] Audit readiness measures documented

### Service Delivery (for frontline offices)
- [ ] Citizen's Charter with service standards
- [ ] Processing times and requirements specified
- [ ] Feedback and complaint mechanism documented
- [ ] Anti-Red Tape Act compliance (service guarantees, no fixing)

### Procurement
- [ ] Annual Procurement Plan preparation process
- [ ] Procurement modes identified with thresholds
- [ ] BAC composition and functions defined
- [ ] Property management and inventory procedures

### Reporting and Accountability
- [ ] All mandatory reports listed with deadlines
- [ ] Reporting chain documented
- [ ] Records management system described
- [ ] Transparency requirements addressed

### Annexes
- [ ] All referenced forms are included in annexes
- [ ] Process flowcharts provided for key procedures
- [ ] Contact directory included
- [ ] Legal reference compilation is complete
