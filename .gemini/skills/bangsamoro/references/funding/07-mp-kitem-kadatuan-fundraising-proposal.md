## 2. The Challenge

### Your Critical Mandate

The Committee on Finance, Budget and Management reviews the General Appropriations Bill (GAB), conducts budget hearings across all ministries, monitors budget execution throughout the fiscal year, and ensures accountability through oversight inquiries.

As BBCC member, you coordinate budget preparation priorities, executive-legislative budget alignment, and fiscal policy recommendations.

### Current Oversight Gaps

**Limited Real-Time Visibility.** Budget execution data arrives through periodic reports. Variance detection happens after the fact. No live dashboard supports committee deliberations.

**Fragmented Budget Information.** MOA budget data comes in different formats. Historical comparisons require manual compilation. No integrated view connects preparation to execution.

**BBCC Coordination Challenges.** Executive-legislative budget discussions depend on static reports. No shared platform exists for budget coordination. Policy recommendations rely on dated information.

## 3. Your Platform Access

### Budget Portal Access

Comprehensive fiscal visibility through BBSA lifecycle:

| Phase | Capability | Oversight Value |
|-------|------------|-----------------|
| **Preparation** | Budget call, MOA submissions | Review budget proposals real-time |
| **Authorization** | GAB tracking, amendments | Monitor deliberation progress |
| **Execution** | Allotment, obligation, disbursement | Track spending against appropriations |
| **Accountability** | BFAR, COA findings | Assess compliance, audit readiness |

### BBCC Coordination Dashboard

Tools for cross-branch budget coordination include executive budget preparation status, legislative authorization progress, fiscal policy impact analysis, and cross-branch communication logs.

### Parliament Portal Access

Legislative workspace for committee leadership:

**Legislative Work Module** covers budget legislation tracking, committee hearing management, and budget amendment registry.

**Oversight Work Module** provides ministry budget performance, PPA implementation status, and service delivery monitoring.
