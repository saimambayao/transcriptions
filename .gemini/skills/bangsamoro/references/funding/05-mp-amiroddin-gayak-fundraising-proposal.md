## 2. The Challenge

### Why Champions Matter

Digital transformation succeeds or fails based on leadership adoption. Estonia's transformation began with committed champions who demonstrated value through use. BARMM needs similar pioneers—MPs who prove digital governance works.

### Current Parliamentary Gaps

Legislative tracking scatters across fragmented tools with no unified view. Oversight remains limited to periodic reporting that arrives after the fact. Constituent services operate ad hoc without systematic tracking. No integrated dashboard shows government operations across branches.

### The Champion Opportunity

Funding this initiative creates a working demonstration of digital parliamentary operations. Your experience generates evidence for broader platform advocacy. Practitioner insights guide development priorities. Champion success builds momentum for government-wide adoption.

---

## 3. Champion Platform Access

### Full Platform Visibility

Unlike standard MP access, champions receive comprehensive visibility:

| Portal | Standard MP | Champion Access |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Parliament** | Full access | Full access + analytics |
| **Ministry** | None | Read visibility to all ministry plans |
| **Budget** | Limited | Comprehensive fiscal dashboards |

### Champion Dashboard

Consolidated view enabling advocacy provides government-wide performance metrics, digital adoption tracking across Parliament, platform utilization analytics, transformation progress indicators, and evidence base for e-governance promotion.

### Parliament Portal Features

Your primary workspace for legislative operations:

**Legislative Work Module** covers bill/resolution registry with end-to-end tracking, AI-assisted drafting with legal databases, committee coordination tools, and legislative records repository.

**Oversight Work Module** provides real-time ministry performance visibility, budget execution monitoring, law enforcement tracking, and service delivery oversight.

**Representation Work Module** includes constituent request tracking, geographic service mapping, public information management, and office PPA tracking.
