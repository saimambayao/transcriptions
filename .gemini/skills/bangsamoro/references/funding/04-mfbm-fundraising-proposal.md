## 2. The Challenge

### MFBM's Critical Mandate

MFBM manages fiscal operations for the Bangsamoro Government. This includes coordinating the PHP 114+ billion annual budget, monitoring allotment and obligation and disbursement flows, ensuring compliance with BBSA and Budget Memoranda and COA requirements, producing BFAR and other accountability reports, and managing cash to ensure liquidity for government operations.

### Current Fiscal Management Gaps

**Manual Budget Consolidation.** MOA submissions require manual compilation. Consolidation takes weeks each cycle. Format inconsistencies across ministries complicate analysis and comparison.

**Delayed Execution Visibility.** Budget execution reports arrive monthly or quarterly. Variances appear after the fact. Limited drill-down capability prevents detailed analysis when problems surface.

**Compliance Burden.** BBSA requirements depend on manual tracking. Budget Memorandum compliance consumes significant staff time. COA audit preparation diverts resources from core fiscal management.

**Limited Analytical Capability.** Data availability constrains trend analysis. Current tools cannot support scenario modeling. Cross-ministry comparisons require manual compilation from multiple sources.

### Why This Matters

MFBM's effectiveness directly affects service delivery, determining whether funds reach programs on time. Strong accountability builds government credibility with oversight bodies. Reduced administrative costs free resources for program delivery. Transparent fiscal management earns public trust.

## 3. Our Solution: Budget Portal

### Theory of Change

The platform's logic works as follows: When MFBM has digital tools automating the complete BBSA lifecycle with real-time visibility into budget execution, fiscal management can shift from periodic reporting to continuous monitoring. This produces faster fund release, earlier variance detection, automated compliance, and enhanced accountability. The ultimate outcome is more effective resource allocation and improved service delivery to Bangsamoro communities.

### Budget Portal Features

#### Phase 1: Budget Preparation

| Feature | Function |
|---------|----------|
| **Budget Call Issuance** | Digital ceiling notifications to all MOAs |
| **Two-Tier Budgeting** | Separate baseline (Tier 1) from new spending (Tier 2) |
| **BBP Forms 100-900** | Digital submission with UACS compliance |
| **Endorsement Workflow** | Coordinated certification process |
| **Consolidation Engine** | Automated government-wide budget compilation |
| **Calendar Tracking** | Deadline management from call to submission |

#### Phase 2: Budget Authorization

| Feature | Function |
|---------|----------|
| **GAB Tracking** | Progress through parliamentary deliberations |
| **Hearing Coordination** | Document management, scheduling |
| **Amendment Tracking** | Changes from proposal to enactment |
| **GAAB Viewer** | Final authorized amounts by MOA |
| **Historical Analysis** | Multi-year budget comparisons |

#### Phase 3: Budget Execution

| Feature | Function |
|---------|----------|
| **Allotment Management** | Track SARO/GAABAO releases |
| **Obligation Tracking** | ORS/OBRS against appropriations |
| **Disbursement Monitoring** | NCA/NCAA to payment |
| **Utilization Dashboards** | Drill-down from ministry to transaction |
| **Variance Alerts** | Immediate notification of deviations |
| **Cash Programming** | Liquidity planning and monitoring |

#### Phase 4: Budget Accountability

| Feature | Function |
|---------|----------|
| **BFAR Automation** | Auto-generated accountability reports |
| **COA Audit Management** | Finding tracking, response documentation |
| **Compliance Monitoring** | BBSA, Budget Memo requirements |
| **Year-End Workflows** | Complete fiscal closing documentation |

#### MFBM Fiscal Command Center

The government-wide fiscal dashboard shows all-MOA budget status with execution rates, cash position and disbursement projections, UACS-compliant classification views, COA compliance tracking, and alert management interface.
