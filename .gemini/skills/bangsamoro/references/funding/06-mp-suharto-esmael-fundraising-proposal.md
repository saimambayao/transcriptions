## 2. The Challenge

### Why Champions Matter

Digital transformation succeeds based on leadership adoption. Estonia's transformation began with committed champions who demonstrated value through use. BARMM needs similar pioneers—MPs who prove digital governance works.

### Current Parliamentary Gaps

Legislative tracking scatters across fragmented tools. Oversight depends on periodic reporting. Constituent services operate ad hoc without systematic follow-up. No unified view of government operations exists across branches.

### The Champion Opportunity

Funding this initiative creates a working demonstration of digital parliamentary operations. Your experience generates evidence for broader platform advocacy. Practitioner insights guide development priorities. Success builds momentum for government-wide adoption.

---

## 3. Champion Platform Access

### Full Platform Visibility

| Portal | Standard MP | Champion Access |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Parliament** | Full access | Full access + analytics |
| **Ministry** | None | Read visibility to all ministry plans |
| **Budget** | Limited | Comprehensive fiscal dashboards |

### Champion Dashboard

Consolidated view enabling advocacy provides government-wide performance metrics, digital adoption tracking, platform utilization analytics, and transformation progress indicators.

### Parliament Portal Features

**Legislative Work Module** covers bill/resolution registry with end-to-end tracking, AI-assisted drafting with legal databases, committee coordination tools, and legislative records repository.

**Oversight Work Module** provides real-time ministry performance visibility, budget execution monitoring, and law enforcement tracking.

**Representation Work Module** includes constituent request tracking, geographic service mapping, and public information management.
