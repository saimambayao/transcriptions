## 2. The Challenge

### Your Advocacy Mandate

Effective citizen representation requires responsive services (every constituent request tracked and addressed), equitable access (fair service distribution across communities), transparent governance (citizens informed about government actions), and accountability (oversight that holds government responsible).

### Current Representation Gaps

**Ad Hoc Constituent Services.** Requests arrive but get logged inconsistently. No systematic follow-up mechanisms exist. Service history remains unavailable, making it impossible to track patterns or ensure continuity.

**No Equity Measurement.** Geographic service distribution remains unknown. Underserved communities go unidentified. Without data to inform advocacy, inequity persists unaddressed.

**Limited Transparency Tools.** Public information scatters across sources. No systematic citizen communication channel exists. Governance visibility remains constrained by outdated information sharing methods.
