## 2. The Challenge

### Your Bridging Mandate

Effective Bangsamoro governance requires coordination between Parliament (legislative priorities, oversight activities), Executive (policy implementation, ministry operations), and Budget (resource allocation, execution status).

### Current Coordination Gaps

**Fragmented Branch Visibility.** Parliamentary status arrives through separate channels. Executive operations run in disconnected systems. No unified cross-branch view exists for coordinators who need complete situational awareness.

**Manual Coordination Processes.** Policy alignment tracking happens through meetings and memos. Implementation status requires manual compilation. Communication gaps persist between branches because no shared platform connects them.

**Delayed Information Flow.** Legislative developments reach the executive late. Executive responses to oversight face delays. Without real-time coordination capability, the bridging function operates on outdated information.

## 3. Your Platform Access

### Full Platform Visibility

Your bridging role requires complete cross-branch access:

| Portal | Access | Coordination Value |
|--------|--------|-------------------|
| **Parliament** | Full | Track legislative priorities, oversight |
| **Ministry** | Full read | Monitor executive implementation |
| **Budget** | Full read | See resource allocation, execution |

### Coordination Dashboard

Tools for cross-branch coordination include legislative agenda vs. executive priorities alignment, bill status with implementation readiness, oversight activities with executive response tracking, and cross-branch communication logs.

### Parliament Portal Features

**Legislative Work Module** covers bill/resolution status tracking, committee activity monitoring, and legislative calendar visibility.

**Oversight Work Module** provides ministry performance data for briefings, budget execution for Chief Minister updates, and service delivery status.
