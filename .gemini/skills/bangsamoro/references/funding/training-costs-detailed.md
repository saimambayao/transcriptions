# e-Bangsamoro Training Costs - Detailed Breakdown

## Overview

Simplified training allocation model for the Bangsamoro Government based on organizational size and platform usage intensity.

**Training Location:** Cotabato City (no accommodation costs)

**Source:** GAAB 2026, BAA No. 13 (Bangsamoro Administrative Code)

---

## 1. Training Cost Model

### Per-Batch Costs (40-50 participants)

| Cost Component | Amount (PHP) | Calculation |

## 4. Two-Phase Training Structure

| Phase | Timing | Allocation | Amount (PHP) |
|-------|--------|------------|--------------|
| **Phase 1: Initial Onboarding** | Months 6-12 (Year 1) | 50% | 10,000,000 |
| **Phase 2: Follow-Up & Cascading** | Year 2 (2027) | 50% | 10,000,000 |
| **Total** | | 100% | **20,000,000** |

### Phase 1: Initial Onboarding (50%)

| MOA | Slots | Cost (PHP) |
|-----|-------|------------|
| Parliament | 1,800 | 6,300,000 |
| OCM | 100 | 350,000 |
| MFBM | 100 | 350,000 |
| BPDA | 100 | 350,000 |
| MBHTE, MOH, MAFAR | 300 | 1,050,000 |
| Key agencies (BICTO, BIO, BAGO, DAB) | 80 | 280,000 |
| Additional costs (prorated) | - | 1,320,000 |
| **Phase 1 Total** | **2,480** | **10,000,000** |

### Phase 2: Follow-Up, Feedback & Cascading Training (50%)

Phase 2 enables trained personnel to mentor colleagues and sustain platform adoption:

- **Follow-up training** for Phase 1 participants (refresher, advanced features)
- **Feedback sessions** to refine training materials based on real usage
- **Cascading training** where trained staff mentor their colleagues
- Remaining ministries and offices onboarding
- Advanced module training (AI features, analytics)
- Certification programs
- Train-the-trainer reinforcement

**Phase 2 Total: PHP 10,000,000**

## 5. Slot Allocation Rationale

| Allocation | Slots | Basis |
|------------|-------|-------|
| **100 slots** | Large MOAs, key portals | High user volume, complex workflows |
| **50 slots** | Medium ministries | Standard ministry operations |
| **20 slots** | Offices/agencies | Focused functions, smaller teams |
| **MP Offices (20 each)** | Parliamentary operations | Chief of Staff, Legislative/Political Affairs, Admin/Finance, Media |
| **Organic Parliament (200)** | Secretariat support | Committees, records, research, protocol |
