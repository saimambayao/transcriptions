## Economic Overview

### Structural Transformation (2000-2021)

The BARMM economy has undergone significant structural change:

| Sector | 2000 Share | 2021 Share | Change |
|--------|------------|------------|--------|
| Agriculture, Forestry, Fishing | 55.75% | 38.94% | -16.81% |
| Industry | 8.13% | 24.65% | +16.52% |
| Services | 36.12% | 36.41% | +0.29% |

**Key Insight:** The economy is moving toward a more balanced structure with industry registering dramatic growth.

### Economic Performance (2021)

- **GRDP Growth Rate:** 7.5% (second highest in the country)
- **Contribution to National Economy:** 1.4% (lowest in country)
- **Ranking in Mindanao:** Highest growth rate among Mindanao regions

### Historic Poverty Reduction

| Year | Poverty Incidence | Subsistence Incidence |
|------|-------------------|----------------------|
| 2015 | - | 20.0% |
| 2018 | 54.20% | 23.3% |
| 2021 | 29.80% | 8.8% |

**24.4 percentage point drop** - historic reduction in poverty incidence. Subsistence incidence dropped from 23.3% (highest nationally in 2018) to 8.8% (second highest in 2021).

## Fiscal Autonomy

### Revenue Sources

| Source | % of Total | Description |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| Annual Block Grant | ~90% | 5% of net national internal revenue |
| Special Development Fund | ~7% | PHP 5B/year for 10 years |
| Local Collections | <1% | Regional taxes, fees, charges |

### Annual Block Grant

**Calculation:** 5% of net national internal revenue tax collection of BIR and BOC from the third fiscal year preceding the current year.

### Special Development Fund

**Total:** PHP 50 billion over 10 years (PHP 5 billion/year)

**Purpose:** Rebuilding, rehabilitation, and development of conflict-affected communities.

### Share of National Taxes

**75% of taxes collected by national government** within BARMM goes to the Bangsamoro government (inclusive of LGU shares).
