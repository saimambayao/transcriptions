## Peace and Security Overview

### Gains from the Peace Process

Since the ratification of the BOL in 2019:
- Significant decline in frequency and intensity of violence
- Crime-related incidents, deaths, and displacement at record lows
- Relative peace and security maintained during transition

### Remaining Challenges

1. Violent extremism remains endemic in selected localities
2. Land conflicts intensified in some areas (2020)
3. Rido-related incidents persist
4. Shadow economies (illicit firearms, illegal drugs) continue

## Normalization Process

### Overview

Under the Comprehensive Agreement on the Bangsamoro (CAB), the normalization track includes:
- Decommissioning of MILF-Bangsamoro Islamic Armed Forces (BIAF)
- Socioeconomic development programs
- Transitional justice and reconciliation

### Joint Peace and Security Team (JPST)

**Tri-forces composition:**
- Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP)
- Philippine National Police (PNP)
- MILF-Bangsamoro Islamic Armed Forces (MILF-BIAF)

**Functions:**
- Maintenance of peace and public order
- Stability in mutually agreed areas
- Coverage of conflict-affected areas in Mindanao

### AFP and Security Presence

**Infrastructure:**
| Type | Number | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| AFP Base Camps | Multiple | Strategic locations |
| MILF Camps | As designated | CAB provisions |
| PCG Stations | Key locations | Maritime security |

## Rido (Clan Feuds)

### Overview

**Rido** - Traditional clan feuding that represents one of the most persistent peace and order challenges in BARMM.

### Statistics

**Total Profiled Cases (as of 2022):** 552 cases

| Province/City | Cases |
|---------------|-------|
| Lanao del Sur | 332 |
| Maguindanao | 95 |
| Basilan | 41 |
| Sulu | 31 |
| Special Geographic Area | 26 |
| Tawi-Tawi | 24 |
| Cotabato City | 3 |

**Settled Cases (as of October 2022):** 272 cases

| Province | Settled |
|----------|---------|
| Lanao del Sur | 116 |
| Maguindanao | 98 |
| Basilan | 23 |
| Special Geographic Area | 18 |
| Sulu | 7 |
| Lanao del Norte | 6 |
| Tawi-Tawi | 4 |

### 2021 Breakthrough

The Ministry of Public Order and Safety (MPOS) successfully resolved a **three-decade long rido** between two prominent families in Lanao del Sur.

### Settlement Mechanisms

1. **MPOS Intervention** - Government-led mediation
2. **Traditional Leaders** - Sultan, datu, and community elders
3. **Religious Leaders** - Islamic scholars and clerics
4. **Lupong Tagapamayapa** - Barangay justice system

### Counter-Terrorism Efforts

Despite threats, the peace and order situation is **manageable** through:
1. Proactive security measures
2. Whole-of-nation approach (AFP + LEAs)
3. Community cooperation
4. Intelligence-driven operations

### Private Armed Groups (PAGs)

**Status:**
- 18 PAGs recorded operating in BARMM
- Reduced to 1 as of 2021 through:
  - Delisting due to inactivity
  - Voluntary surrender
  - Law enforcement operations

## Disaster Risk Reduction

### Vulnerability

BARMM is increasingly vulnerable to:
- Flooding (low-lying areas)
- Landslides
- Typhoons (e.g., Typhoon Paeng, October 2022)

**Typhoon Paeng Impact:**
- 61 deaths in BARMM (highest in country)
- Widespread flooding
- Homes covered in mud

### DRRM Framework

- 5% DRRM Fund implementation
- Community-based disaster risk reduction
- Climate change adaptation
