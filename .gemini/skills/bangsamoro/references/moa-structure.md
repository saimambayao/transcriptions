# BARMM Government Organizational Structure

Source: Bangsamoro Administrative Code (BAA No. 13)

## Office of the Chief Minister (OCM)

The OCM is the executive staff and administrative office of the Chief Minister, separate from the line ministries.

### Executive Offices
- Office of the Senior Minister
- Office of the Chief of Staff
- Cabinet Secretariat
- Bangsamoro Attorney General's Office (BAGO)

### Bangsamoro Management Support System (BMSS)
- Technical Management Service (TMS)
- Administrative Management Service (AMS)
- Finance Management Service (FMS)
- Intelligence and Security Service (ISS)
- Procurement Service (PS)
- Clinic
- Bangsamoro Satellite Coordinating Offices
- Bangsamoro Liaison Office in Metro Manila
- Bangsamoro Library and Archives

### Councils under OCM
1. Bangsamoro Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council (DRRMC)
2. Bangsamoro Economic and Development Council (BEDC)
3. Bangsamoro Regional Peace and Order Council (BRPOC)

### Attached Agencies and Offices
1. Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority (BPDA)
2. Bangsamoro Board of Investments (BBOI)
3. Bangsamoro Information Office (BIO)
4. Bangsamoro Information and Communications Technology Office (BICTO)
5. Office for Settler Communities (OSC)
6. Office for Other Bangsamoro Communities (OOBC)
7. Bangsamoro Pilgrimage Authority (BPA)
8. Development Academy of the Bangsamoro (DAB)
9. Bangsamoro Darul-Ifta' (BDI)
10. Cooperatives and Social Enterprise Authority (CSEA)

## Office of the Wali

The Wali is the ceremonial head of the Bangsamoro government, representing the moral and cultural authority of the Bangsamoro people. The Wali is appointed by the Chief Minister.

---

## BTA Parliament

The Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament is the legislative body. Key structural elements:
- **Speaker** — presiding officer
- **Deputy Speaker(s)**
- **80 Members of Parliament** (appointed during transition; elected post-transition)
- **Statutory Committees** — standing committees by subject area
- **Parliamentary Committees** — procedural and administrative committees
