# Executive Briefer / Talking Points Template

6-section document for preparing BTA officials before meetings, events, forums, and engagements.

## Header
- **Document Title:** [Concise title reflecting the purpose]
- **Date:** [Date of event/meeting]
- **Location:** [Venue or platform]

## 1. OBJECTIVES
- **Main purpose and goals:** Primary objectives, desired outcomes, anticipated impact
- **Expected outcomes:** Specific results and benefits expected

## 2. OPENING STATEMENT/MESSAGE (Speech format)
- **Salutation:** Appropriate cultural/formal/Islamic greetings
- **Introduction:** Concise overview of event and its significance
- **Key messages:** Main talking points — clear, concise, impactful
- **Call to action:** Strong closing statement inspiring action or collaboration

## 3. BACKGROUND INFORMATION
- **Event background:** Historical context, key facts, pertinent information
- **Participants:** Key individuals/groups, their roles, relevance
- **Broader significance:** How event contributes to larger BTA/BARMM goals

## 4. KEY DISCUSSION POINTS AND QUESTIONS
- **Topics/themes:** Main areas of discussion aligned with objectives
- **Guiding questions:** Thoughtful prompts for each topic
- **Anticipated responses:** Prepared answers/talking points — well-informed, persuasive

## 5. AREAS OF COLLABORATION (if applicable)
- **Partnership opportunities:** Specific collaboration, resource-sharing, joint initiatives
- **Action steps:** Concrete next steps to initiate or strengthen collaboration

## 6. CLOSING REMARKS AND WAY FORWARD (if applicable, Speech format)
- **Gratitude:** Thank organizers, participants, key contributors
- **Key takeaways:** Recap main points and commitments
- **Next steps:** Follow-up actions
- **Continued engagement:** Encourage ongoing communication and support
