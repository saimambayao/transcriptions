## Source Trust Tiers

```
Tier 1 — CITE WITH CONFIDENCE
├── Official government documents (.gov, .gov.ph)
├── Peer-reviewed academic papers
├── Primary legislation and court decisions
└── International organization official reports

Tier 2 — CITE WITH CONTEXT
├── Established news outlets (Reuters, Philippine Star, Rappler, Inquirer)
├── Industry reports (McKinsey, Gartner, Deloitte)
├── Expert publications from recognized practitioners
└── Official organizational websites (.org with known authority)

Tier 3 — USE FOR LEADS, VERIFY ELSEWHERE
├── Wikipedia (overview only — follow its citations to primary sources)
├── Blog posts from unknown authors
├── Forum discussions (Reddit, Stack Overflow)
└── Content marketing or sponsored research
```

## Red Flags — Do Not Cite

- Domains with no identifiable organization behind them
- Content farms and SEO-optimized aggregator sites
- Sources that cite themselves circularly
- Undated pages on rapidly changing topics
- "Research" published by companies selling the solution they recommend
