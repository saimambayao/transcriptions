# COOP-304: Role/Function Specific Training

Training specific to officer positions and committee assignments.

> **Series**: COOP-300 (Advanced Course for S/M/L Cooperative Officers)
> **Regulatory Basis**: CDA MC 2025-09 Annex A (adopted by CSEA for BARMM)

---

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Code** | COOP-304 |
| **Series** | COOP-300 (S/M/L Officers) |
| **Type** | Online Course (Self-Paced) |
| **Duration** | 4 hours |
| **Target Audience** | Officers of Small/Medium/Large Cooperatives (>₱3M assets) |
| **Prerequisites** | COOP-100 Series, COOP-301, 302, 303 |
| **Compliance** | Part of 32h officer training - complete within first half of term |
| **Certificate** | COOP-304 Course Certificate + COOP-300 Series Certificate |
| **Languages** | English + Filipino (bilingual scripts) |

## Course Structure

This course is **modular by officer role**. Officers select the track matching their position or committee assignment:

| Track | Position/Role | Duration |
|-------|---------------|----------|
| **Track A** | Board of Directors (BOD Members) | 4 hours |
| **Track B** | Chairperson | 4 hours |
| **Track C** | Treasurer | 4 hours |
| **Track D** | Secretary | 4 hours |
| **Track E** | Audit Committee | 4 hours |
| **Track F** | Election Committee | 4 hours |
| **Track G** | Credit Committee (S&C Coops) | 4 hours |
| **Track H** | Education & Training Committee | 4 hours |

**Note:** Officers complete ONE track based on their primary role. Those with multiple roles may complete additional tracks.

---

## Track A: Board of Directors (4 hours)

### Learning Objectives

1. **Execute** BOD duties effectively
2. **Participate** in strategic decision-making
3. **Monitor** cooperative performance
4. **Fulfill** fiduciary responsibilities

### Track A Curriculum

| Unit | Duration | Topics |
|------|----------|--------|
| **Unit A1** | 1 hour | Director Duties and Responsibilities |
| **Unit A2** | 1 hour | Strategic Oversight |
| **Unit A3** | 1 hour | Financial Monitoring |
| **Unit A4** | 1 hour | Risk Oversight |

**Unit A1: Director Duties and Responsibilities (1 hour)**

- Legal duties under RA 9520
- Fiduciary obligations
- Duty of care, loyalty, obedience
- Director liability and protection
- Conflict of interest management

**Unit A2: Strategic Oversight (1 hour)**

- Strategic planning participation
- Policy development role
- Performance monitoring
- Management oversight
- Major decision-making

**Unit A3: Financial Monitoring (1 hour)**

- Reading financial reports
- Key financial indicators
- Budget approval and monitoring
- Capital management oversight
- External audit review

**Unit A4: Risk Oversight (1 hour)**

- Risk identification
- Risk management framework
- Compliance oversight
- Internal control monitoring
- Crisis management

## Assessment

Each track has its own assessment:

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Duration | 15 minutes |
| Questions | 15 questions |
| Coverage | All 4 units of selected track |
| Passing Score | 70% |

---

## Certificate Requirements

| Requirement | Threshold |
|-------------|-----------|
| Track Completion | 100% (all units in selected track) |
| Quiz Scores | 70% each |
| Final Assessment | 70% |

**Upon Completion:**

After completing COOP-304 (any track), you will receive:

1. **COOP-304 Course Certificate** (4 hours - specify role track)
2. **COOP-300 Series Certificate - Advanced Cooperative Course for S/M/L Officers** (32 hours)

This completes your mandatory officer training under MC 2025-09 for S/M/L cooperative officers!
