## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

1. **Apply** basic budgeting principles to personal finances
2. **Explain** how cooperative financial services work (savings, loans, patronage)
3. **Calculate** simple interest and understand loan repayment
4. **Demonstrate** safe use of digital financial tools
5. **Identify** common financial scams and how to avoid them
6. **Manage** personal finances responsibly as a cooperative member

### Unit 1: Basic Financial Concepts (45 minutes)

> **MC 2025-09 Topic**: Financial Literacy

**Lesson 1.1: Personal Budgeting Basics** (VIDEO - 12 min)
- **Income vs. Expenses**: Understanding your money flow
- **Needs vs. Wants**: Prioritizing spending
- **The 50/30/20 Rule**: A simple budgeting framework
  - 50% Needs (food, housing, utilities)
  - 30% Wants (entertainment, extras)
  - 20% Savings
- Creating a simple household budget
- Tracking your spending
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 1.2: Saving and Emergency Funds** (VIDEO - 10 min)
- Why saving matters
- Building an emergency fund (3-6 months expenses)
- Types of savings:
  - Regular savings
  - Time deposits
  - Cooperative share capital (a form of forced savings)
- Saving strategies for irregular income
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Quiz 1: Basic Financial Concepts** (5 questions)

---

### Unit 2: Cooperative Financial Services (45 minutes)

> **MC 2025-09 Topic**: Financial Literacy

**Lesson 2.1: Understanding Cooperative Financial Products** (VIDEO - 12 min)
- **Share Capital**: Your investment in the cooperative
- **Savings Deposits**: Safe place for your money
- **Loans**: Accessing credit through your cooperative
- **Patronage Refund**: Getting back your share of surplus
- How cooperative rates compare to banks and lenders
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 2.2: Responsible Borrowing** (VIDEO - 12 min)
- When to borrow and when not to
- Understanding loan terms:
  - Principal amount
  - Interest rate
  - Loan term
  - Monthly payment
- Simple interest calculation example
- Managing loan repayments
- Avoiding over-indebtedness
- Consequences of loan default
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Quiz 2: Cooperative Financial Services** (5 questions)

---

### Unit 3: Digital Literacy (30 minutes)

> **MC 2025-09 Topic**: Financial Literacy including Digital Literacy

**Lesson 3.1: Using Digital Financial Tools** (VIDEO - 10 min)
- Mobile banking basics
- E-wallets (GCash, Maya, etc.)
- Online cooperative services
- QR code payments
- Digital transaction records
- **Script**: English + Filipino

**Lesson 3.2: Digital Safety and Avoiding Scams** (TEXT - 10 min)
- Common digital scams:
  - Phishing (fake emails/messages)
  - Fake investment schemes
  - Money mule schemes
  - SIM swap fraud
- Red flags to watch for
- How to protect yourself:
  - Never share OTPs
  - Verify before clicking links
  - Use strong passwords
  - Report suspicious activity
- What to do if you're scammed
- **Reading**: English + Filipino versions

**Quiz 3: Digital Literacy** (5 questions)

## Script Requirements

### Script Pacing (~90 WPM)

All scripts use **~90 wpm** to accommodate BARMM's ESL beginner learners.

**Technical Term Pattern**: `"[English term], o [Filipino explanation]"`
- Example: "Ang **budget**, o talaan ng kita at gastos..."
- Example: "Ang **phishing**, o pandaraya gamit ang pekeng email o mensahe..."

## Appendix: Financial Terms Glossary

| Term | Filipino | Definition |
|------|----------|------------|
| Budget | Badyet | Plan for income and expenses |
| Interest | Interes | Cost of borrowing or earnings on savings |
| Principal | Prinsipal | Original amount borrowed or deposited |
| Savings | Ipon | Money set aside for future use |
| Share Capital | Puhunan | Member's ownership stake in the cooperative |
| Patronage Refund | Balik-patronage | Member's share of surplus based on transactions |
| Loan | Utang | Money borrowed to be repaid with interest |
| E-wallet | E-wallet | Digital money storage app |
| Phishing | Phishing | Scam using fake messages to steal information |
