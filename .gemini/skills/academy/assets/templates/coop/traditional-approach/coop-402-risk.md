## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

1. **Identify** major risk categories affecting S&C cooperatives
2. **Implement** credit risk management frameworks
3. **Manage** operational risk effectively
4. **Monitor** liquidity risk and maintain adequate buffers
5. **Ensure** compliance risk is properly managed
6. **Design** a comprehensive risk management framework

## Appendix: Risk Categories Summary

| Risk Type | Definition | Key Mitigants |
|-----------|------------|---------------|
| **Credit Risk** | Risk of borrower default | Credit analysis, collateral, diversification |
| **Liquidity Risk** | Unable to meet obligations | Liquidity buffers, funding diversity, planning |
| **Operational Risk** | Process/people/system failures | Internal controls, BCP, insurance |
| **Compliance Risk** | Regulatory violations | Compliance program, training, monitoring |
| **Interest Rate Risk** | Rate changes affecting margins | Gap management, repricing |
| **Reputation Risk** | Loss of confidence | Good governance, communication |

## Appendix: Key Risk Indicators (KRIs)

| Risk Area | Key Risk Indicator | Threshold |
|-----------|-------------------|-----------|
| Credit | Portfolio at Risk (PAR 30) | < 5% |
| Credit | Non-Performing Loans | < 3% |
| Credit | Large Exposures | < 25% of capital |
| Liquidity | Liquid Assets / Deposits | > 15% |
| Liquidity | Deposit Concentration | < 10% per depositor |
| Operational | Fraud Losses | < 0.1% of assets |
| Compliance | Regulatory Findings | Zero material findings |
