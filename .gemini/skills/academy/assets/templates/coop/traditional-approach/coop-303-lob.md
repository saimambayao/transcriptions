## Course Structure

This course is **modular by cooperative type**. Officers select the track matching their cooperative's primary line of business. This is a condensed version focused on strategic considerations for larger cooperatives.

| Track | Cooperative Types | Duration |
|-------|-------------------|----------|
| **Track A** | Credit/Savings & Credit Cooperative | 4 hours |
| **Track B** | Marketing Cooperative | 4 hours |
| **Track C** | Producer Cooperative | 4 hours |
| **Track D** | Consumer Cooperative | 4 hours |
| **Track E** | Service Cooperative | 4 hours |
| **Track F** | Multi-Purpose Cooperative | 4 hours |
| **Track G** | Agriculture/Fisheries Cooperative | 4 hours |
| **Track H** | Transport Cooperative | 4 hours |

**Note:** Officers complete ONE track based on their cooperative type.

---

## Track A: Credit/Savings & Credit Cooperative (4 hours)

### Learning Objectives

1. **Develop** strategic credit operations for growth
2. **Implement** advanced portfolio management techniques
3. **Apply** risk-based pricing strategies
4. **Ensure** regulatory compliance for S&C cooperatives

### Track A Curriculum

| Unit | Duration | Topics |
|------|----------|--------|
| **Unit A1** | 1 hour | Strategic Credit Management |
| **Unit A2** | 1 hour | Portfolio Quality Management |
| **Unit A3** | 1 hour | Product Development |
| **Unit A4** | 1 hour | Regulatory Compliance for S&C |

**Unit A1: Strategic Credit Management (1 hour)**

- Credit strategy alignment with cooperative goals
- Market analysis and member needs assessment
- Competitive positioning
- Growth planning

**Unit A2: Portfolio Quality Management (1 hour)**

- Portfolio at Risk (PAR) management
- Loan loss provisioning policies
- Write-off and recovery strategies
- Portfolio diversification

**Unit A3: Product Development (1 hour)**

- New loan product design
- Pricing strategies
- Member-centric product innovation
- Digital lending considerations

**Unit A4: Regulatory Compliance for S&C (1 hour)**

- CDA S&C cooperative requirements
- Capital adequacy considerations
- BSP regulations (if applicable)
- Deposit insurance (PDIC considerations)

## Appendix: Track Selection Guide

| If Your Cooperative Primarily... | Select Track |
|----------------------------------|--------------|
| Provides loans and savings services | Track A: Credit |
| Markets members' products collectively | Track B: Marketing |
| Produces goods through member labor | Track C: Producer |
| Operates retail stores for members | Track D: Consumer |
| Provides services (water, electricity, health) | Track E: Service |
| Has multiple business activities | Track F: Multi-Purpose |
| Supports farming/fishing activities | Track G: Agriculture/Fisheries |
| Operates public transportation | Track H: Transport |
