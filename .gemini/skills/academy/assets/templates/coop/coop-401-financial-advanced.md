## Why This Training?

Cooperatives with **≥₱5,000,000 in deposit liabilities** handle significant member funds and face greater financial risks. This specialized training ensures officers can:

- Protect member deposits effectively
- Manage financial risks appropriately
- Maintain adequate capital and liquidity
- Comply with heightened regulatory requirements

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

1. **Analyze** financial statements using advanced techniques
2. **Apply** cooperative accounting principles correctly
3. **Design** effective internal control systems for S&C operations
4. **Monitor** capital adequacy and liquidity requirements
5. **Prepare** financial reports meeting CDA standards
6. **Assess** financial health using PEARLS and other frameworks

## Appendix: PEARLS Framework Reference

| Component | Description | Key Ratios |
|-----------|-------------|------------|
| **P**rotection | Allowance for loan losses | Allowance / Delinquent Loans |
| **E**ffective Financial Structure | Asset, liability, capital mix | Net Loans / Total Assets |
| **A**sset Quality | Loan portfolio quality | Delinquency rate, Non-performing loans |
| **R**ates of Return and Cost | Profitability and efficiency | Net interest margin, Operating expense ratio |
| **L**iquidity | Ability to meet obligations | Liquid assets / Deposits + Borrowings |
| **S**igns of Growth | Sustainable growth indicators | Asset growth, Membership growth |
