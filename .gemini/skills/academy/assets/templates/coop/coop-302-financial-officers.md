## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

1. **Apply** basic accounting principles to cooperative operations
2. **Interpret** cooperative financial statements for decision-making
3. **Calculate** and analyze key financial ratios
4. **Design** effective internal control systems
5. **Prepare** and monitor cooperative budgets
6. **Ensure** compliance with CDA financial reporting requirements

### Unit 1: Basic Accounting for Non-Accountants (2 hours)

**Lesson 1.4: Understanding Cooperative Accounts** (TEXT - 15 min)

## Appendix: Financial Ratios Reference

| Category | Ratio | Formula | Target |
|----------|-------|---------|--------|
| **Liquidity** | Current Ratio | Current Assets / Current Liabilities | 1.5 - 2.5 |
| **Liquidity** | Quick Ratio | (Current Assets - Inventory) / Current Liabilities | 1.0 - 1.5 |
| **Solvency** | Debt-to-Equity | Total Debt / Member Equity | < 2.0 |
| **Solvency** | Debt-to-Assets | Total Debt / Total Assets | < 0.6 |
| **Profitability** | Net Surplus Margin | Net Surplus / Revenue | > 5% |
| **Profitability** | ROA | Net Surplus / Total Assets | > 3% |
| **Profitability** | ROE | Net Surplus / Member Equity | > 8% |
| **Efficiency** | Asset Turnover | Revenue / Total Assets | Varies |
