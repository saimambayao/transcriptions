## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

1. **Conduct** comprehensive credit evaluation and analysis
2. **Manage** loan portfolios for optimal performance
3. **Implement** effective collection strategies
4. **Control** delinquency and non-performing loans
5. **Develop** sound credit policies
6. **Monitor** credit operations effectively

## Appendix: Credit Management Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Portfolio at Risk (PAR 30)** | Loans 30+ days overdue / Total loan portfolio | < 5% |
| **Non-Performing Loan Ratio** | NPLs / Total loan portfolio | < 3% |
| **Collection Rate** | Collections / Amount due | > 95% |
| **Loan Loss Reserve Ratio** | Loan loss reserve / PAR 30 | > 100% |
| **Write-off Ratio** | Write-offs / Average portfolio | < 2% |
| **Recovery Rate** | Recoveries / Write-offs | > 30% |

## Appendix: 5 Cs of Credit Reference

| C | Factor | Key Questions |
|---|--------|---------------|
| **Character** | Integrity & willingness to pay | Credit history? References? Track record? |
| **Capacity** | Ability to repay | Income level? Debt burden? Cash flow? |
| **Capital** | Financial cushion | Net worth? Savings? Investment in loan purpose? |
| **Collateral** | Security for loan | Asset value? Ease of liquidation? |
| **Condition** | Loan purpose & environment | Use of funds? Economic conditions? |

## Appendix: Delinquency Management Timeline

| Days Past Due | Status | Action Required |
|---------------|--------|-----------------|
| 1-7 days | Early warning | SMS/call reminder |
| 8-15 days | Initial contact | Phone follow-up, payment arrangement |
| 16-30 days | First notice | Formal demand letter |
| 31-60 days | Intensified | Home/work visit, restructuring offer |
| 61-90 days | Remedial | Final demand, legal preparation |
| 91+ days | Legal/Recovery | Legal action, collateral execution |
