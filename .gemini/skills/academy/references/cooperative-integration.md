# Cooperative Integration for Academy

## Pre-Registration Seminar (PRS)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Requirement** | Mandatory for all cooperative organizers before registration |
| **Duration** | 6-8 hours |
| **Target** | Prospective cooperative organizers (minimum 15 persons) |
| **Facilitator** | CDA-accredited Training Service Provider (TSP) or CDA Extension Office |
| **Certificate** | PRS Certificate of Completion (required for registration) |

**Minimum Content**: Cooperative Identity (1-1.5h), Cooperative Types (1h), Legal Framework (1.5h), Registration Process (1h), Governance Basics (1h), Financial Foundation (1h), Q&A (30min).

## Cooperative Officer Training Pathway

PRS -> COOP-101 (Fundamentals) -> COOP-201 (Governance) -> COOP-301 (Financial Management) -> Specialized Training

## CDA MC 2015-09 Compliance

### Training Requirements by Size

| Category | Asset Threshold | Required Training | Total Hours |
|---------------------|-----------------|-------------------|-------------|
| Micro | <= PHP 3M | COOP-101 (8h) + COOP-201 (8h) | 16 |
| Larger | > PHP 3M | COOP-101 (16h) + COOP-201 (16h) | 32 |
| S&C (>PHP 5M deposits) | > PHP 5M | + COOP-301 + Risk + Credit + Internal Control | 32+ |

**Timeline**: Complete within first half of officer's term.
**Non-compliance**: Disqualification from position, affects Certificate of Compliance.

## ETF Utilization (Art. 59, RA 9520)

**Source**: Maximum 10% of net surplus after statutory reserves.

**Eligible**: Officer training, member education, staff development, training materials, TSP fees, venue/logistics.
**Non-Eligible**: General office supplies, entertainment, non-training travel, capital expenditures.

**Documentation**: Training plan (BOD approved), attendance records, receipts, certificates, ETF ledger.
