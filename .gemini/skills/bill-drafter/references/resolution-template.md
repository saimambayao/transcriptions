# BTA Parliament Resolution Template

## Standard Heading

```
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

[Session — e.g., Third Regular Session]

Proposed Resolution No.____

Principal Author(s): [Use plural when multiple, singular otherwise]

Co-Author(s): [Use plural when multiple, singular otherwise]
```

## Title
The title must be identical to the main operative clause. Keep it concise and clear.

## Preambulatory (WHEREAS) Clauses

Present a logical, deductive argument in this order:

1. **Global context** — established facts, data, research (verified and accurate)
2. **Islamic principles** — expressed in general legislative terms, with footnoted Qur'an/Hadith sources
3. **National context** — Constitutional provisions, national laws, agency policies and issuances
4. **BARMM context** — BOL provisions, Bangsamoro Autonomy Acts, local policies and issuances

Each clause begins with "WHEREAS," and builds logically toward the necessity of the resolution.

### Example WHEREAS Clause with Islamic Citation

```
WHEREAS, Islamic tradition emphasizes the preservation of life and well-being
as one of the five essential objectives (maqasid al-shari'ah) of Islamic law;¹

---
¹ The preservation of life (hifz al-nafs) is one of the five objectives of
Shari'ah as articulated by Imam al-Ghazali in Al-Mustasfa. The Qur'an states:
"...whoever saves a life, it is as if he has saved all of humanity"
(Surah Al-Ma'idah, 5:32).
```

## Operative Clauses

**Main clause:**
```
NOW, THEREFORE, be it

  RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority
  Parliament, [main operative clause — identical to the title]
```

**Additional clauses** (only when genuinely necessary):
```
  RESOLVED FURTHER, [additional operative clause]
```

**Final clause:**
```
  RESOLVED FINALLY, [final operative clause]
```

Keep operative clauses minimal. Add only when necessary to avoid making the resolution complex.

## Adopted Resolution References

These adopted resolutions serve as format precedents:
- AR-200 (GOTD/Peoples Agenda) — multiple operative clauses
- AR-204 (Strengthening the Internet) — longer resolution
- AR-421, AR-423 — short resolutions
- AR-425 — standard format
- AR-441 (Inflation Relief Assistance) — economic topic
