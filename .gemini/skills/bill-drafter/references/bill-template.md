# BTA Parliament Bill Template

## Standard Heading (used for both Explanatory Note and Main Body)

```
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

[Session — e.g., Third Regular Session]

Proposed Bill No.____

Introduced by:
MP [Name of the Member of Parliament]

Co-Author(s): [Use plural when multiple co-authors, singular otherwise]
```

## Part A: Explanatory Note

After the heading and long title ("AN ACT..."), include:
a. The problem or issue the bill aims to address
b. How the proposed legislation will solve the problem
c. The expected benefits and impact
d. Relevant background information or context

## Part B: Main Body

After the heading, long title, and enacting clause:

```
BE IT ENACTED by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in Parliament assembled:
```

### Section Format

```
SECTION [Number]. [Title]. — [Content as continuous text.]
```

### Standard Section Sequence

1. **Short Title** — "This Act shall be known as the '[Name] Act'."
2. **Declaration of Policy** — Principles, objectives, alignment with Constitution, BOL, Islamic principles
3. **Definition of Terms** — Alphabetical definitions of key terms
4. **Substantive Provisions** — As many sections as needed
5. **Implementing Agency/Agencies** — Responsible BARMM agency
6. **Penal Provisions** (if applicable)
7. **Implementing Rules and Regulations** — Within 60-90 days of approval
8. **Appropriations** — Initial funding and annual budget inclusion
9. **Transitory Provisions** — Transition period details
10. **Separability Clause**
11. **Repealing Clause**
12. **Effectivity** — 15 days after publication in Bangsamoro Gazette or newspaper of general circulation

### Standard Closing Sections (verbatim)

**Separability:**
> If any provision of this Act or the application thereof to any person or circumstance is held invalid, the remainder of the Act, and the application of such provision to other persons or circumstances shall not be affected thereby.

**Repealing:**
> All laws, orders, decrees, rules, and regulations, or parts thereof inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

**Effectivity:**
> This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Bangsamoro Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.
