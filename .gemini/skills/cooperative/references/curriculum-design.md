## BARMM Training Context

*Source: Bangsamoro Development Plan (BDP) 2023-2028, Chapter 7*

### Cooperative Sector Challenge

The BDP 2023-2028 identifies a key problem: **low functionality and unsustainable profitability of cooperatives**. This is addressed through:

- **Low compliance rate**: Only 11.66% of 12,214 registered cooperatives are compliant (target: 50%)
- **Lack of enacted Code**: Pending Bangsamoro Cooperative and Social Enterprise Code currently slows down full operationalization and institutionalization
- **Inadequate LGU participation**: Most LGUs in the Bangsamoro Region have no appointed Municipal Cooperative Officers (MCOs)
- **Combatant transition challenges**: Newly registered cooperatives, mainly composed of former combatants, encounter difficulties in complying with requirements due to policies aligned with the previous CDA entity

*Source: BDP 2023-2028, Chapter 7*

### Training as a Strategic Response

**BDP Strategy 21** mandates: "Ensure profitability and functionality of cooperatives through:
- Sufficient technical and financial assistance
- Institutionalization of monitoring and evaluation system and mechanism
- Community promotion
- **Capacitation of human resources**"

*Source: BDP 2023-2028, Chapter 7*

### Priority Training Programs (BDP 2023-2028)

| Program | Description | Projects |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Development of Cooperatives and SE Program** | Strengthen cooperative capacities to ensure profitability and functionality | PESO Project, LAKAS SE Program |
| **Combatant Transitional Program** | Support former combatants to organize into cooperatives and legally conduct businesses | STOCK Project, Cooperative Financial and Material Support Project |

*Source: BDP 2023-2028, Chapter 7*

### Key Training Needs

Based on BDP 2023-2028 assessment:

1. **Compliance training** - Help cooperatives meet legal requirements (target: raise compliance from 11.66% to 50%)
2. **Profitability training** - Business management, financial literacy, value chain development
3. **Governance training** - Leadership, accountability, transparency, M&E institutionalization
4. **Moral Governance integration** - 12 pillars of BARMM governance
5. **Shari'ah compliance** - Islamic finance principles for cooperatives
6. **Combatant transition** - Special programs for former combatants organizing cooperatives (STOCK Project)
7. **Social Enterprise development** - LAKAS SE Program for SE institutionalization
8. **Agri-cooperative strengthening** - Strategy 8 alignment for agricultural development

### Alignment with BARMM Vision

All training should support:
> "A Bangsamoro that is united, enlightened, self-governing, peaceful, just, morally upright, and progressive."

### Alignment with BDP 2023-2028 Sectoral Goals

| Goal | Training Application |
|------|---------------------|
| **Goal 2** | Equitable, competitive, robust economy - ensure cooperative profitability and functionality |
| **Objective 2.5** | Functional cooperatives and social enterprises increased |
| **Objective 2.5.1** | Profitability of cooperatives increased |
| **Objective 2.5.2** | Established social enterprises increased |

## Training Levels

### Four-Tier Framework

| Level | Target Audience | Duration | Focus |
|-------|-----------------|----------|-------|
| **PRS** | Prospective members | 4-8 hours | Join decision, basics |
| **Basic** | New members, officers | 8-16 hours | Principles, rights, duties |
| **Intermediate** | Officers, managers | 24-40 hours | Governance, management |
| **Advanced** | Leaders, trainers | 40+ hours | Leadership, policy, scaling |

### Level Objectives

**PRS (Pre-Registration Seminar)**:
- Understand cooperative concept
- Know rights and responsibilities
- Make informed decision to join

**Basic Cooperative Course**:
- Deepen understanding of principles
- Understand governance structure
- Know legal framework

**Intermediate/Officer Training**:
- Master governance functions
- Develop management skills
- Ensure compliance knowledge

**Advanced/Leadership**:
- Strategic leadership skills
- Policy development capability
- Movement contribution

## PRS Curriculum

### Required by RA 9520

Pre-Registration Seminar is mandatory before joining or organizing a cooperative.

### Standard Duration: 4-8 hours

### Module Outline

| Module | Topics | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **1. What is a Cooperative?** | Definition, characteristics, difference from other organizations | 30-45 min |
| **2. Cooperative Principles & Values** | ICA 7 Principles, core and ethical values | 45-60 min |
| **3. Types of Cooperatives** | Classification, services, choosing the right type | 30-45 min |
| **4. Member Rights & Responsibilities** | Articles 10-11 of RA 9520, membership types | 30-45 min |
| **5. Organizational Structure** | GA, BOD, Committees, Officers, Management | 30-45 min |
| **6. Capital & Statutory Funds** | Share capital, reserve fund, ETF, CDF, patronage refund | 30-45 min |
| **7. Benefits of Membership** | Services, dividends, networking, community | 30 min |
| **8. Registration Requirements** | Documents, process, compliance | 30 min |

### Learning Objectives

By end of PRS, participants can:
1. Define a cooperative and its key characteristics
2. Explain the 7 Cooperative Principles
3. Identify different types of cooperatives
4. Describe member rights and responsibilities
5. Understand basic governance structure
6. Explain how statutory funds work
7. Make informed decision about joining

### Delivery Methods

- Lecture/presentation
- Video materials
- Group discussions
- Q&A sessions
- Knowledge check quiz

## Basic Cooperative Course

### Target: New members and first-time officers

### Duration: 8-16 hours (1-2 days)

### Module Outline

| Module | Topics | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **1. Cooperative History & Movement** | Rochdale Pioneers, Philippine coop history, BARMM context | 1-2 hours |
| **2. ICA Statement on Cooperative Identity** | Deep dive into definition, values, principles | 2 hours |
| **3. Philippine Cooperative Code (RA 9520)** | Key provisions, rights, obligations | 2-3 hours |
| **4. Governance Structure** | GA powers, BOD roles, Committee functions | 2-3 hours |
| **5. Financial Management Basics** | Bookkeeping, budgeting, reports | 2 hours |
| **6. Member Participation** | Meetings, voting, patronage, engagement | 1-2 hours |

### BARMM-Specific Module (Add-on)

*Required for BARMM cooperatives per BDP 2023-2028 alignment*

| Module | Topics | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **7. BARMM Cooperative Context** | BDP 2023-2028, CSEA mandate, BTA Bill 210, Goal 2/Objective 2.5 | 1 hour |
| **8. Moral Governance for Cooperatives** | 12 pillars, ethical leadership, accountability | 1 hour |
| **9. Shari'ah Compliance Basics** | Halal transactions, no riba, risk sharing, Islamic finance alternatives | 1-2 hours |
| **10. Compliance Requirements** | COC, CAPR, CAFS, annual reporting, 50% compliance target | 1 hour |

### Learning Objectives

By end of course, participants can:
1. Trace cooperative movement history
2. Apply cooperative principles to decisions
3. Navigate RA 9520 provisions
4. Understand governance roles
5. Read basic financial statements
6. Participate effectively in cooperative affairs

### BARMM-Specific Learning Objectives

7. Explain how cooperatives support BDP 2023-2028 Goal 2 and Objective 2.5
8. Apply Moral Governance principles to cooperative operations
9. Identify Shari'ah-compliant business practices
10. Meet CSEA compliance requirements to contribute to 50% compliance target

## Officer Training

### Mandated by RA 9520 and CDA Guidelines

All elected and appointed officers must complete required training within first half of term.

### Board of Directors Training

**Duration**: 24-40 hours (3-5 days)

| Module | Topics | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **1. Board Governance** | Roles, responsibilities, accountability | 4 hours |
| **2. Strategic Planning** | Vision, mission, goals, action plans | 4 hours |
| **3. Policy Development** | Policy creation, implementation, review | 3 hours |
| **4. Financial Oversight** | Reading reports, monitoring, controls | 4 hours |
| **5. Legal Compliance** | RA 9520, bylaws, CDA regulations, CSEA requirements | 3 hours |
| **6. Risk Management** | Identification, assessment, mitigation | 3 hours |
| **7. Ethics & Conduct** | Code of ethics, conflicts of interest, Moral Governance | 2 hours |
| **8. Board Operations** | Meetings, decision-making, minutes | 2 hours |

### BDP 2023-2028 Alignment Module for Officers

*Additional module for BARMM cooperative officers*

| Module | Topics | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **9. BDP 2023-2028 Implementation** | Strategy 21 & 22, M&E institutionalization, PESO Project alignment | 2 hours |
| **10. Profitability Enhancement** | Ensuring cooperative functionality and sustainable profitability | 2 hours |
| **11. LGU Coordination** | Working with MCOs, provincial extension offices, community promotion | 1 hour |

### Committee-Specific Training

**Audit Committee** (8-16 hours):
- Internal control systems
- Audit procedures
- Financial analysis
- Report writing
- GA accountability

**Election Committee** (4-8 hours):
- Election rules and procedures
- Candidate validation
- Conduct of elections
- Results certification
- Protest resolution

**Mediation & Conciliation Committee** (8-16 hours):
- Conflict resolution
- Mediation techniques
- Hearing procedures
- Decision writing
- Confidentiality

**Ethics Committee** (8-16 hours):
- Code of ethics development
- Ethics case analysis
- Investigation procedures
- Sanction recommendations
- Ethical leadership
- Moral Governance integration

**Education & Training Committee** (8-16 hours):
- Training needs assessment
- Program planning
- ETF management
- Provider coordination
- Impact evaluation
- BDP 2023-2028 training alignment

| CAIS Reporting | Online submission, CAPR, CAFS |

### Credit Management Course

**Target**: Credit Committee, Loan Officers

**Duration**: 16-24 hours

| Module | Topics |
|--------|--------|
| Credit Policies | Loan products, terms, conditions |
| Loan Evaluation | Credit investigation, analysis |
| Collection | Follow-up, restructuring, recovery |
| Delinquency Management | Prevention, remediation |
| Regulatory Compliance | CDA guidelines, BSP requirements |

**Target**: Chairpersons, Senior Officers, Managers

**Duration**: 40+ hours

| Module | Topics |
|--------|--------|
| Leadership Styles | Transformational, servant, ethical, Moral Governance |
| Strategic Management | Planning, execution, evaluation |
| Change Management | Leading transformation |
| Stakeholder Relations | Members, community, government, LGUs |
| Advocacy & Policy | Influencing cooperative policy, BTA Bill 210 |
| Movement Building | Federation, networking, solidarity |
| BDP 2023-2028 Leadership | Implementing Strategy 21 & 22, community promotion |

### Combatant Transitional Training (STOCK Project)

*Special training for former combatant cooperatives per BDP 2023-2028*

**Target**: Former combatants organizing cooperatives

**Duration**: 24-40 hours

| Module | Topics |
|--------|--------|
| Cooperative Fundamentals | Basic concepts adapted for transitional context |
| Business Legalization | How to legally conduct business through cooperatives |
| Compliance Orientation | Understanding CDA/CSEA requirements |
| Financial Literacy | Basic bookkeeping, budgeting, fund management |
| Market Access | Connecting to markets and value chains |

*Source: BDP 2023-2028, Chapter 7 - Combatant Transitional Program*

### Social Enterprise Development (LAKAS SE Program)

*Training for social enterprise institutionalization per BDP 2023-2028*

**Target**: Social enterprise organizers, cooperative leaders exploring SE model

**Duration**: 16-24 hours

| Module | Topics |
|--------|--------|
| Social Enterprise Fundamentals | Definition, characteristics, difference from cooperatives |

| Social Mission Development | Identifying social, cultural, or environmental challenges |

*Source: BDP 2023-2028, Chapter 7 - Development of Cooperatives and SE Program*

### Agri-Cooperative Strengthening (Strategy 8 Alignment)

*Training for agricultural cooperatives per BDP 2023-2028 Strategy 8*

**Target**: Farmer's organizations, agri-cooperatives, ARBOs

**Duration**: 24-40 hours

| Module | Topics |

## Integration with /academy

### When to Use with /academy

Invoke `/cooperative` with `/academy` when:
1. Creating new cooperative course/workshop
2. Developing PRS curriculum for CSEA
3. Building officer training programs
4. Designing specialized cooperative training
5. Developing combatant transitional training (STOCK Project)
6. Creating social enterprise programs (LAKAS SE)
7. Building agri-cooperative strengthening courses (Strategy 8)

### Workflow

```
1. IDENTIFY training need
          |
2. SELECT course level (PRS/Basic/Officer/Specialized)
          |
3. CHECK BDP 2023-2028 alignment (Goal 2, Objective 2.5, Strategies 8, 21, 22)
          |
4. USE /cooperative for content expertise
          |
5. USE /academy for curriculum design
          |
6. DEVELOP learning materials
          |
7. IMPLEMENT training
          |
8. EVALUATE and improve
```

### Information to Provide /academy

When invoking with `/academy`:

1. **Course Level**: PRS, Basic, Intermediate, Advanced
2. **Target Audience**: Members, officers, committees, managers, former combatants, SE organizers
3. **Duration**: Hours/days available
4. **Delivery Mode**: In-person, online, hybrid
5. **Specific Topics**: If not full curriculum
6. **Compliance Requirements**: CDA mandates, CSEA requirements
7. **Context**: BARMM/Shari'ah considerations if applicable
8. **BDP Alignment**: Relevant BDP 2023-2028 strategies and objectives (Goal 2, Objective 2.5, Strategy 8, 21, 22)
9. **Program Type**: PESO Project, LAKAS SE, STOCK Project, or regular training

### Sample Request Format

```
Create a [course level] course for [target audience]:
- Duration: [X hours/days]
- Delivery: [mode]
- Topics: [specific or full curriculum]
- Must include: [compliance requirements]
- Context: [BARMM/Shari'ah if applicable]
- BDP Alignment: [Goal 2, Objective 2.5, Strategy 21, etc.]
- Program Type: [PESO/LAKAS SE/STOCK/Regular]
```

## CDA Training Standards

### Accredited Training Providers

Only CDA-accredited providers may conduct mandatory training.

**Provider Requirements**:
- Juridical personality
- At least 2 years existence
- 5+ qualified trainers
- Standard curriculum adoption
- Proper facilities

### Trainer Qualifications

- Legal age, Filipino citizen
- Bachelor's degree minimum
- Training of Trainers (TOT) completion
- 2+ years experience as resource person
- Good moral character

### Training Documentation

Cooperatives must maintain:
- Attendance sheets
- Certificates of completion
- Training materials used
- Evaluation results
- Provider accreditation proof
