# CDA Memorandum Circular No. 2020-20

## Guidelines Governing the Registration of Cooperatives Through the Electronic Cooperative Registration Information System (E-CoopRIS)

**Series of 2020**

**Approved**: September 23, 2020 (BOD Resolution No. 264, S-2020)

---

## Overview

This circular prescribes the procedure for online registration of cooperatives through the **Electronic Cooperative Registration Information System (E-CoopRIS)**, enabling clients to apply for cooperative registration via the internet and minimizing face-to-face transactions.

## Section 1. Purpose

This Guidelines is issued to prescribe the procedure on the registration of cooperatives and other related processes through the Electronic Cooperative Registration Information System (E-CoopRIS) that would allow clients/cooperatives to apply for the registration of cooperatives and other related processes via the internet and thereby minimize the face to face transactions.

---

## Section 2. Legal Bases

The legal bases of this Guidelines are:

### RA 11364, Section 4

> **(c)** Register all cooperatives including amendments to the Articles of Cooperation and Bylaws (ACBL), division, merger and consolidation.
>
> **(j)** Prescribe and collect reasonable fees, fines or charges in the performance of its registration and regulatory functions.

### RA 9520, Article 136 - Register of Cooperatives

> The Authority shall establish a register which shall contain a chronological entry of the name of every cooperative registered and dissolved under this Code together with the basic information required for registration and dissolution and any other information considered useful.

## Section 3. Definition of Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|

## Section 5. Procedure for Online Registration of New Cooperative

### Step-by-Step Process Flow

```
Step 1: Account Creation
         ↓
Step 2: Select Application Type ("New Registration")
         ↓
Step 3: Cooperative Name Reservation (4-day validity)
         ↓
Step 4: Fill Cooperative Information (within 30 days)
         ↓
Step 5: CDA Processing, Review & Approval
         ↓
Step 6: Submit Hard Copies (within 30 days of approval)
         ↓
Step 7: Payment (within 10 days of notice)
         ↓
Step 8: Certificate of Registration Issued
```

---

### Step 1: Account Creation

**Action**: Click "Create an Account" button

**Required Information**:
- Basic Information
- Address
- Contact Details
- Client Information
- Valid email address
- Password

> **Important**: The email address and password shall be used to log in. All communications will be sent to the email address provided.

---

### Step 2: Application Type

**Action**: Once logged in, click "New Registration" button from the dashboard.

---

### Step 3: Cooperative Name Reservation

**Action**: Fill out the Cooperative Reservation form.

| Process | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Name Check | System will inform if proposed name is available |
| If Available | Fill out additional required data and click "Submit" |
| **Validity** | **4 days** - must proceed to Step 4 |
| If Not Proceeded | Reserved name expires and becomes available to others |

---

### Step 4: Cooperative's Information

**Action**: Fill out the online cooperative information form.

**System-Generated Documents**:
1. Articles of Cooperation
2. By-Laws
3. Economic Survey
4. Treasurer's Affidavit

**Upload Requirements**:
- Scanned copies of other required documents
- **Except**: Endorsements or certificates of authority from other regulatory agencies

**Timeline**: Must be completed within **30 days**

> **Tip**: Regularly check application status by logging in to E-CoopRIS.

---

### Step 5: Processing, Review and Approval

**CDA Action**: Process and review applications based on submitted information and uploaded documents.

**Possible Outcomes**:

| Result | Client Action |
|--------|---------------|
| **Approval** | Proceed to Step 6 |
| **Deferment** | Login and comply with findings within **15 days** from email receipt |
| **Denial** | May appeal to CDA Board; adverse decision may be appealed to Office of the President |

---

### Step 6: Submission of Hard Copies

**Timeline**: Within **30 days** from date of approval email notification

**Documents to Submit**:
- Printed hard copies of all required documents
- Endorsements or certificates of authority from other regulatory agencies

**Verification Process**:
- CDA personnel will verify hard copies match soft copies filed online
- Signatures of cooperators will be validated
- **If differences found**: Certificate of Registration will NOT be issued

> **Warning**: Failure to submit within 30 days shall be considered **abandonment** of application and records shall be purged from E-CoopRIS.

---

### Step 7: Payment of Assessed Fees

**Timeline**: Within **10 days** from payment notice

**Process**:
1. Receive payment advice via email
2. Login to E-CoopRIS account
3. Click payment button
4. Order of Payment generated
5. Choose payment method

#### Option A: Online Payment Through LANDBANK

| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Transaction fee | ₱10.00 |
| BancNet Internet Payment Gateway fee | ₱10.00 |
| **Total Additional Fees** | **₱20.00** |

**Output**: Payment Confirmation Slip generated

#### Option B: Payment Through CDA Treasury

1. Login to E-CoopRIS to view/print Order of Payment
2. Proceed to CDA Office Cashier
3. Pay assessed fees
4. Cashier issues Official Receipt

> **Warning**: Failure to pay within 10 days results in **automatic removal** of application from the system.

---

### Step 8: Issuance of Certificate of Registration

**Trigger for Issuance**:

| Payment Method | Issuance Trigger |
|----------------|------------------|
| Online Payment | Upon payment confirmation |
| CDA Treasury | Upon issuance of Official Receipt |

## BARMM/CSEA Context

### Important: CSEA Has Its Own Registration System

**E-CoopRIS is a CDA system and does NOT apply to BARMM cooperatives.**

CSEA (Cooperative and Social Enterprise Authority) has its own separate registration system for cooperatives within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM).

| Aspect | CDA (Rest of Philippines) | CSEA (BARMM) |
|--------|---------------------------|--------------|
| **Authority** | Cooperative Development Authority | Cooperative and Social Enterprise Authority |
| **Online System** | E-CoopRIS | CSEA Registration Portal |
| **Jurisdiction** | Outside BARMM | Within BARMM |
| **Website** | www.cda.gov.ph | negosyo.bangsamoro.site |

### Why This Reference is Included

This MC 2020-20 reference is maintained for:
- Understanding CDA's online registration procedures
- Reference for cooperatives operating outside BARMM
- Benchmark for CSEA's own registration system development
- Cooperatives transitioning between CDA and CSEA jurisdictions
