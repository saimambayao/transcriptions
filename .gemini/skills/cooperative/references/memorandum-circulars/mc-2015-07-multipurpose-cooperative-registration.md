# CDA Memorandum Circular No. 2015-07

## Guidelines Governing the Registration of Multipurpose Cooperatives

**Series of 2015**

**Approved**: September 29, 2015 (BOA Resolution No. 234, S-2015)

**Date**: October 7, 2015

**Signed by**: Orlando R. Ravanera, Chairman

---

## Overview

This circular provides guidelines for registering cooperatives as multipurpose cooperatives (combining 2+ business/economic activities). It establishes requirements for converting existing single-purpose cooperatives to multipurpose status, including minimum capital, operational history, and profitability requirements.

---

## Quick Reference

### Key Requirements for Multipurpose Registration

| Item | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| **Minimum Paid-up Capital** | ₱100,000.00 (or as required in feasibility study, whichever is higher) |
| **Operational History** | At least 2 years in operation |
| **Financial Requirement** | Profitable operations for past 2 years |
| **Feasibility Study** | Required for each proposed business activity |
| **Books of Account** | Separate recording for each business activity |

### Exceptions to ₱100,000 Minimum

| Cooperative Type | Capital Requirement |
|------------------|---------------------|
| Agriculture Cooperatives | ₱15,000.00 (per MC 2015-05) |
| Agrarian Reform Cooperatives | ₱15,000.00 |

### Document Requirements

| # | Document | Copies |
|---|----------|--------|
| 1 | Cooperative Name Reservation Notice (CNRN) | 1 (if changing name) |
| 2 | Amended Articles of Cooperation and By-Laws | 3 |
| 3 | BOD/GA Resolution (2/3 vote approval) | 3 |
| 4 | Surety Bond of Accountable Officers | 3 |
| 5 | Audited Financial Statement (past 2 years) | 3 |
| 6 | Undertaking to Change Name | 3 |
| 7 | Favorable Endorsement from Other Government Agencies | 3 (if applicable) |
| 8 | Detailed Feasibility Study (each business activity) | 3 |
| 9 | Certificate of Compliance with Auditing/Accounting Standards | 3 |

## Article I. Title

These Guidelines shall be known as the **"Guidelines Governing the Registration of Multipurpose Cooperatives"**.

---

## Article II. Policy

It is the declared policy of the State to foster the creation and growth of cooperatives as practical vehicles for promoting self-reliance and harnessing people power towards the attainment of economic development and social justice.

---

## Article III. Purpose

These Guidelines are issued for the orderly registration of multipurpose cooperatives in compliance with the requirements as provided under:
- RA 9520 (Philippine Cooperative Code of 2008)
- Revised Implementing Rules and Regulations
- Relevant administrative issuances by the Authority

---

## Article IV. Coverage

This shall govern:
- Registration of multipurpose cooperatives
- Operation of existing cooperatives registered with the Authority

> **IMPORTANT**: No cooperative shall be registered as a multipurpose cooperative unless it has been in operation for at least **two (2) years**.

---

## Article V. Definition of Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Agriculture Cooperative** | A primary cooperative which or whose members are involved/engaged in raising/culture of plants, animals, fungi, and other living organisms for productive and economic purpose and in related activities that lead to the reduction of cost and/or value addition of outputs |
| **Agrarian Reform Cooperative (ARCo)** | One organized by marginal farmers, majority of which are Agrarian Reform Beneficiaries, duly registered with the Authority, for developing appropriate system of land tenure, land development, land consolidation or management in areas covered by agrarian reform. Also refers to ARB Coop as defined in DAR Administrative Order No. 05, Series of 2009 |
| **Amendment** | Changes for legitimate purposes in any provision stated in the Articles of Cooperation and By-Laws in accordance with prescribed requirements under RA 9520 and other issuances |
| **Authority** | Cooperative Development Authority |
| **Certificate of Registration of Amendment** | Official document issued by the Authority under its official seal serving as conclusive evidence of registered amendment |
| **Cooperative** | Autonomous and duly registered association of persons, with common bond of interest, who have voluntarily joined together to achieve their social, economic, and cultural needs and aspirations by making equitable contributions to capital, patronizing products and services, and accepting fair share of risks and benefits |
| **Multipurpose Cooperative** | A cooperative which combines two (2) or more business/economic activities |
| **Primary Cooperative** | A cooperative whose members are natural persons (except electric cooperative, water service cooperative, and others allowed by IRR or Authority) |
| **Paid-up Share Capital** | The portion of member's share capital that has been paid |
| **Validation** | An activity or series of activities ensuring the proposed cooperative is organized and registered for the primary purpose and benefit of the intended group |

---

## Article VI. Minimum Capitalization Requirements

### General Rule

Only cooperatives with a **minimum paid-up capital of ₱100,000.00** (or as required in feasibility study, whichever is higher) may be allowed to transform into a multipurpose cooperative.

### Exceptions

| Cooperative Type | Exception |
|------------------|-----------|
| Agriculture Cooperatives | Not subject to ₱100,000 minimum |
| Agrarian Reform Cooperatives | Not subject to ₱100,000 minimum |

### Capital Requirement Comparison

| Cooperative Type | Minimum Paid-up Capital |
|------------------|------------------------|
| Single-Purpose (Primary) | ₱15,000.00 |
| Agriculture Cooperative | ₱15,000.00 |
| Agrarian Reform Cooperative | ₱15,000.00 |
| **Multipurpose Cooperative** | **₱100,000.00** |
| Multipurpose (per Feasibility) | Higher of ₱100,000 or FS requirement |

---

## Article VII. Requirements for Registration of Amendment

### Section 1. Documentary Requirements

The following documents shall be submitted to the Authority in **three (3) copies** except for item (a):

| # | Document | Copies | Notes |
|---|----------|--------|-------|
| a | Cooperative Name Reservation Notice (CNRN) | 1 | Only if changing name |
| b | Amended Articles of Cooperation and By-Laws | 3 | With amendments properly indicated |
| c | BOD/GA Resolution | 3 | Certified by Secretary and majority of BOD, approved by 2/3 vote of all voting members |
| d | Surety Bond of Accountable Officers | 3 | |
| e | Audited Financial Statement | 3 | Showing profitable operations for past 2 years |
| f | Undertaking to Change Name | 3 | In case another coop has prior right to proposed name |
| g | Favorable Endorsement from Other Government Agencies | 3 | If applicable |
| h | Detailed Feasibility Study | 3 | For each proposed business activity |
| i | Certificate of Compliance with Auditing/Accounting Standards | 3 | |

> **IMPORTANT**: No cooperative shall be registered as a multipurpose cooperative unless it has been in operation for at least **two (2) years**.

### Section 2. Document Specifications

| Specification | Requirement |
|---------------|-------------|
| **Paper Size** | 8.5" x 13" or 8.5" x 14" |
| **Language** | English, Filipino, or local dialect with English translation |
| **Font** | Bookman Old Style 12 (as far as practicable) |
| **Interpretation** | English translation prevails in case of confusion |

### Amendment Indication

The amended articles and bylaws shall contain amendments properly indicated by:
- **Underscoring** new provisions
- **Enclosing in parenthesis** or **bracketing** provisions proposed to be changed

### Section 3. Book of Accounts

Cooperatives covered by this Rule shall be required to maintain **separate recording in the books of account for each business activity**.

### Section 4. Amendment Fee

| Aspect | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| **Amount** | Per CDA Schedule of Fees (MC 2004-07, MC 2014-03) |
| **When Paid** | After approval of amendments |
| **Collection** | Before issuance of Certificate of Registration of Amendment |

---

## Article VIII. Jurisdiction

### General Rule

All applications for registration of amendment shall be filed and registered with the **CDA Extension Office (EO)** having jurisdiction over the principal office of the proposed multipurpose cooperative.

### Exceptions (CDA Central Office)

| Type | Jurisdiction |
|------|--------------|
| Selected types per MC 2012-08 | CDA Central Office |
| Tertiary cooperatives with national coverage | CDA Central Office |

---

## Article IX. Registration Procedure

The procedures for registration of multipurpose cooperative shall follow the same procedures outlined for the registration of amendments, as approved by the Board of Administrators.

---

## Article X. Evaluation of Amendments

The Authority, upon receipt of the complete required documents, shall evaluate the same with due regard to:
- Provisions of the Code (RA 9520)
- Other relevant laws

---

## Article XI. Validation Requirement

### Mandatory Validation

All single-purpose cooperatives intending to register as multipurpose cooperative shall be **subjected to validation before registration**.

### Validation Conduct

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Conducted by** | CDA Extension Office having jurisdiction over principal office |
| **Type** | On-site validation |
| **Reference** | Office Order No. 2015-04 |

---

## Article XII. Amendments for Existing Cooperatives

### Mandatory Amendment Scenarios

The following cooperatives shall be **required to amend** their Articles of Cooperation and By-Laws:

#### Scenario 1: Multipurpose Operating as Single-Purpose

| Situation | Required Action |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Multipurpose cooperative operating single-business activity | Amend to single-purpose cooperative |

#### Scenario 2: Inactive Multipurpose Activities

| Situation | Required Action |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Multipurpose cooperative with purpose in AOC not undertaken after 2 years from registration | Amend AOC to delete said purpose |

#### Scenario 3: Single-Purpose Operating Multiple Activities

| Situation | Paid-up Capital | Required Action |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Single-purpose operating multiple activities | ≥ ₱100,000.00 | Amend to multipurpose |
| Single-purpose operating multiple activities | < ₱100,000.00 | Cease other business activity |

### Decision Flowchart

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│               COOPERATIVE STATUS REVIEW                          │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                                  │
│  MULTIPURPOSE COOPERATIVE                                        │
│  ├── Operating single activity only?                             │
│  │   └── YES → Amend to single-purpose                           │
│  │                                                               │
│  └── Purpose in AOC not undertaken after 2 years?                │
│      └── YES → Amend AOC to delete that purpose                  │
│                                                                  │
│  SINGLE-PURPOSE COOPERATIVE                                      │
│  └── Operating multiple activities?                              │
│      ├── Paid-up ≥ ₱100,000 → Amend to multipurpose              │
│      └── Paid-up < ₱100,000 → Cease other activity               │
│                                                                  │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```
