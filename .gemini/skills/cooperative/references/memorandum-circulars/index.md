# CDA Memorandum Circulars Index

*Reference collection of Cooperative Development Authority Memorandum Circulars relevant to cooperative registration, compliance, and operations.*

> **CSEA-BARMM Adoption**: These CDA memorandum circulars serve as the regulatory framework for all Philippine cooperatives. The Cooperative and Social Enterprise Authority (CSEA) in BARMM adopts and implements these guidelines for cooperatives registered within the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region. Where specific CSEA guidelines differ (such as registration systems), this is noted in the relevant sections.

## Financial Reporting Guidelines

| Circular | Title | Status |
|----------|-------|--------|
| [MC 2022-25](mc-2022-25-pfrf-cooperatives.md) | Philippine Financial Reporting Framework for Cooperatives (PFRF) | Active (repeals MC 2015-06, MC 2016-06) |

### MC 2022-25 Key Provisions

| Topic | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Scope** | All cooperatives except Cooperative Banks, Insurance Coops, Electric Coops |
| **Total Chapters** | 28 chapters covering all aspects of financial reporting |
| **Exclusions** | Cooperative Banks, Insurance Coops, Electric Coops follow full PFRS |
| **Reserve Fund** | At least 10% (50% in first 5 years) |
| **CETF** | Not more than 10% |
| **CDF** | Not less than 3% |
| **Optional Fund** | Not more than 7% |

> **Note for BARMM**: CSEA shall ensure cooperatives in BARMM comply with PFRF for Cooperatives for audit and financial reporting purposes.

## Training Requirements

| Circular | Title | Status |
|----------|-------|--------|
| [MC 2025-09](mc-2025-09-training-requirements.md) | Required and Recommended Trainings for Members, Officers, and Staff | Active |

### MC 2025-09 Key Provisions

| Category | Required Training Hours |
|----------|------------------------|
| **Members (All)** | 16 hours (Fundamentals, Laws, Leadership, Financial Literacy) |
| **Officers - Micro** | 24 hours (Management, Financial, Line of Business) |
| **Officers - Small/Medium/Large** | 32 hours (Expanded Management, Financial, Line of Business, Role-specific) |
| **Officers - S&C Operations (PHP 5M+)** | +16 hours (Financial, Risk, Credit Management) |

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Member Training Deadline** | Within 1 year of membership acceptance |
| **Training Providers** | CDA (free), CDOs (free), Accredited CTPros, Unions, Federations |
| **Delivery Methods** | Traditional, hybrid, virtual/online |
| **Non-Compliance Penalty** | Non-issuance of COC; disqualification from holding positions |

> **Note for MoroAcademy**: This MC provides the framework for cooperative training programs. CSEA can serve as training provider for BARMM cooperatives, and MoroAcademy courses should align with required hour durations and content.

## Quick Reference

### MC 2015-01 Key Provisions

| Article | Topic |
|---------|-------|
| Article V | Definition of Terms |
| Article VI | Types of Cooperatives |
| Article VII | Jurisdiction |
| Article VIII | Primary Cooperatives (Requirements) |
| Article IX | Secondary/Tertiary Cooperatives |
| Article X | Validation Requirement |

### Primary Cooperative Requirements (MC 2015-01 as amended by MC 2016-02)

| Requirement | Specification |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Minimum Members** | 15 Filipino citizens |
| **Common Bond** | Residential, Institutional, Associational, or Occupational |
| **Subscribed Capital** | Min. 25% of Authorized |
| **Paid-up Capital** | Min. 25% of Subscribed |
| **Min. Paid-up (Primary)** | P15,000.00 |
| **Min. Paid-up (Multipurpose)** | P100,000.00 |
| **Document Copies** | 4 original copies |
| **Paper Size** | 8.5" x 13" or 8.5" x 14" |
| **Font** | Bookman Old Style 12 |
| **Processing Time** | 60 days |

### Documentary Requirements

1. Economic Survey (4 copies)
2. Articles of Cooperation (4 copies, all original, signed on each page)
3. By-laws (4 copies, signed by majority on adoption page)
4. Treasurer's Affidavit (4 copies)
5. Surety Bonds of accountable officers (4 copies)
6. PMES Certificate (signed by CDA PMES Team)
7. Registration Fee
