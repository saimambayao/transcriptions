# CDA Memorandum Circular No. 2020-11

## Guidelines Supplementing MC 2015-01, MC 2015-11, and MC 2016-05 on the Conduct of Validation

**Series of 2020**

**Approved**: May 22, 2020 (BOD Resolution No. 130, S-2020)

**Signed by**: Orlando R. Ravanera, CSEE, CEO VI, Chairman

---

## Overview

This circular supplements existing memorandum circulars by prescribing procedures for conducting **onsite or off-site validation** of applications for cooperative registration, amendments to Articles of Cooperation and Bylaws, and establishment of branches and satellite offices.

---

## Section 1. Title

This Memorandum Circular shall be known as **"Guidelines Supplementing CDA Memorandum Circular (MC) No. 2015-01, MC No. 2015-11, and MC No. 2016-05 on the Conduct of Validation of Application for Registration of Cooperatives, Amendments to the Articles of Cooperation and Bylaws, and Establishment of Branches and Satellite Offices of Cooperatives"**.

---

## Section 2. Objectives

This Guidelines is issued with the following objectives:

### 2.1. Supplement Existing Circulars
To supplement the afore-cited CDA Memorandum Circulars by prescribing the procedure on the conduct of onsite or off-site validation to ensure that the application for registration of cooperatives, amendments to the Articles of Cooperation and Bylaws or establishment of branches and satellite offices of cooperatives has met the minimum requirements of the law and adheres to the cooperative principles, values and concept.

### 2.2. Provide Alternative Validation Mode
To include off-site validation as an alternative mode of validation/verification considering the declaration of a State of Public Health Emergency throughout the Philippines due to the outbreak of COVID-19 by President Rodrigo R. Duterte through Proclamation No. 922 on March 08, 2020.

---

## Section 3. Scope

This Guidelines shall cover all applications for:

| Application Type | Description |
|------------------|-------------|
| **New Registration** | All types and categories of cooperatives |
| **Amendments** | Amendments to Articles of Cooperation and Bylaws |
| **Branch/Satellite Offices** | Establishment of cooperative branches and satellite offices |

## Section 4. Definition of Terms

### 4.1. Validation

An activity or series of activities aimed at ensuring that the requirements for applications for registration, amendments to the Articles of Cooperation and Bylaws or establishment of branches or satellite offices are:
- Complete
- Correct
- In accordance with applicable laws

Validation may be conducted **onsite or off-site**.

### 4.1.1. Onsite Validation

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Definition** | Physical conduct of validation or actual visit to the applicant's or cooperative's proposed office |
| **Purpose** | Gather relevant information through direct observation |
| **Method** | In-person visit by assigned validator |

### 4.1.2. Off-site Validation

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Definition** | Virtual conduct of validation |
| **Methods** | Phone calls, emails, other forms of social media |
| **Additional Requirements** | Applicant may be asked to send pictures and copies of documents |

### 4.2. Validation Tool/Report

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Definition** | Prescribed report prepared by the authorized validator |
| **Contents** | Findings on the validation/verification conducted |
| **Includes** | Recommendation on the application |

---

## Section 5. Conduct of Validation

### 5.1. General Rule

Applications for the following shall be subject to validation/verification:
- New registration
- Amendments to the Articles of Cooperation and Bylaws
- Establishment of branches or satellite offices

### Timeline

| Activity | Timeframe |
|----------|-----------|
| Validation conduct | Within **2 days** from assignment |
| Purpose | Ensure necessary data for complete report with recommendations are gathered |

### Validation Mode Priority

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│           VALIDATION MODE SELECTION             │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                 │
│   1. ONSITE VALIDATION (Primary)                │
│      └── Physical visit to proposed office      │
│                                                 │
│   ↓ If not possible due to causes beyond        │
│     control (e.g., health emergency)            │
│                                                 │
│   2. OFF-SITE VALIDATION (Alternative)          │
│      └── Virtual via calls/emails/social media  │
│      └── Request photos and document copies     │
│                                                 │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### When Off-site Validation May Be Used

Off-site validation may be conducted when onsite validation is not possible due to causes beyond the control of the assigned CDS II, such as:
- Public health emergencies (e.g., Proclamation No. 922 - COVID-19)
- Natural disasters
- Security concerns
- Other force majeure situations

---

## Section 6. Validation Tool/Report

### Purpose
The submitted validation tool/report shall **primarily be taken into consideration** in evaluating the application.

### Confidentiality

| Aspect | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| **Classification** | Confidential |
| **Disclosure** | Shall NOT be disclosed even to the client |
| **Use** | Internal evaluation purposes only |

## Validation Process Summary

### For New Registration

```
Application Received
        ↓
Assignment to Validator (CDS II)
        ↓
Validation (within 2 days)
    ├── Onsite: Physical visit
    └── Off-site: Virtual verification
        ↓
Validation Tool/Report Prepared
        ↓
Report Submitted (Confidential)
        ↓
Application Evaluation
        ↓
Approval/Deferment/Denial
```

### For Amendments to ACBL

```
Amendment Application Received
        ↓
Assignment to Validator
        ↓
Validation (within 2 days)
    ├── Verify GA approval
    ├── Check BOD resolution
    └── Validate compliance
        ↓
Validation Report Submitted
        ↓
Processing per MC 2015-11
```

### For Branch/Satellite Establishment

```
Branch Application Received
        ↓
Assignment to Validator
        ↓
Validation (within 2 days)
    ├── Verify proposed location
    ├── Check cooperative standing
    └── Validate requirements
        ↓
Validation Report Submitted
        ↓
Processing per MC 2016-05
```

## Validation Checklist Reference

### For New Registration Validation

| Item | Verify |
|------|--------|
| **Proposed Office** | Physical existence, accessibility |
| **Organizers** | Identity, residency, common bond |
| **Capital** | Paid-up capital availability |
| **PRS Completion** | Certificates of all organizers |
| **Economic Survey** | Accuracy of data, viability |
| **Cooperative Principles** | Adherence to ICA principles |

### For Amendment Validation

| Item | Verify |
|------|--------|
| **GA Resolution** | Proper conduct, quorum, 2/3 vote |
| **BOD Recommendation** | Compliance with bylaws |
| **Amendment Content** | Legal compliance |
| **Member Notification** | Proper notice given |

### For Branch/Satellite Validation

| Item | Verify |
|------|--------|
| **Location** | Proposed branch address exists |
| **Parent Cooperative** | Good standing, compliance |
| **Justification** | Business case for expansion |
| **Resources** | Capability to operate branch |

## Off-site Validation Methods

When conducting off-site validation, the following methods may be used:

| Method | Use For |
|--------|---------|
| **Phone Calls** | Interview organizers, verify information |
| **Email** | Request documents, formal correspondence |
| **Video Conference** | Virtual meetings with applicants |
| **Social Media** | Verify organization existence, community presence |
| **Photo Requests** | Verify proposed office location, facilities |
| **Document Scans** | Obtain copies of supporting documents |
