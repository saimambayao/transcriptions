# Articles of Cooperation - Cooperative Bank

*Official template prescribed by the Cooperative Development Authority (CDA) and Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) pursuant to Republic Act No. 9520 (Philippine Cooperative Code of 2008)*

*Note: Cooperative Banks are secondary or tertiary cooperatives formed by member-cooperatives to provide banking services. They are subject to additional regulation by the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas.*

## ARTICLE II
### Purpose and Scope of Business

That the purpose and scope of business for which the Cooperative Bank is formed are:

1. To provide a wide range of financial services primarily to cooperative organizations and their members, and the general public; and

2. To perform any or all transactions and banking services offered by other types of banks subject to applicable laws, rules, and regulations.
