## Reporting Templates

### Cooperative Annual Progress Report (CAPR)
**File:** [report_capr.md](report_capr.md)

Annual progress report required for all registered cooperatives, covering:
- General Information (registration, address, type, category)
- Business Activities (sales, products, services, import/export)
- Membership (demographics, composition, age groups)
- Officers and Committee Members
- Financial Information (deposits, taxes, investments)
- Type-specific sections (Credit, Transport, Agriculture, etc.)
- Secondary/Tertiary cooperative sections (CETF, training programs)

### Performance Audit Report - Governance and Management (PAR Annex A)
**File:** [report_par-governance-management.md](report_par-governance-management.md)

Governance and management performance audit scoring template (Annex A), covering:
- A. Governance Report (General Assembly, BOD, Committees, Officers) - 72 points
- B. Management Reports (Organizational Structure, Systems, HR, Performance, Compensation, Compliance) - 20 points
- C. Partnership - 4 points
- D. Membership - 4 points
- Total: 100 Standard Points

### Performance Audit Report - Financial Performance Standards (PAR Annex B)
**File:** [report_par-financial-performance-standards-primary.md](report_par-financial-performance-standards-primary.md)

Financial performance standards for primary and other special type cooperatives (Annex B), covering:
- 1. Stability (Liquidity, Quick Asset, Solvency, Net Institutional Capital, Capital Adequacy) - 19 points
- 2. Turn-over Ratio (Asset, Receivable) - 6 points
- 3. Efficiency (Administrative, Borrowing Costs, Volume of Business, Delinquency, Provisioning) - 38 points
- 4. Profitability (Return on Assets, Return on Member's Share, Net Surplus Rate) - 15 points
- 5. Structure of Assets (Non-Earning, Investments, Deposit Financing, External Borrowings, Members' Capital) - 22 points
- Total: 100 Standard Points

### Performance Audit Report - Summary Report (PAR Annex C)
**File:** [report_par-summary-primary.md](report_par-summary-primary.md)

Summary report for primary and other special type cooperatives (Annex C), covering:
- Cooperative Information (registration, address, business)
- A. Governance and Management (Strong Areas, Areas for Improvement)
- B. Result of Financial Performance (STEPS summary)
- C. Total Points (40% Governance + 60% STEPS)
- Adjectival Rating interpretation (Outstanding to Needs Improvement)
- Audit Committee signatures

### Social Audit Report
**File:** [report_social-audit.md](report_social-audit.md)

Social audit working paper and report for cooperative social performance (7th Cooperative Principle - Concern for Community), covering:

**Part I - Working Paper:**
- A. Basic Information of the Cooperative
- B. Statement of Vision, Mission, Core Values, Social Goals
- C. Existing Policies and Guidelines on Social Development
- D. Social Development Program (D.1 Cooperative: Members, Officers, Staff; D.2 Community)
- E. Community Development Fund (CETF, Optional Fund, Outright Expense)

**Part II - Social Audit Report:**
- Key Areas Comments and Recommendations
- Areas for Improvement with Recommended Actions
- Management Commitment and Conforme signatures

### List of Officers and Trainings Undertaken/Completed
**File:** [report_list-officers-trainings.md](report_list-officers-trainings.md)

Record of cooperative officers and their mandatory training compliance, covering:
- Cooperative Information (name, registration number, address, assets, category)
- Officers Record Table:
  - Name of Officers (Last Name, First Name, Initial)
  - Position/Designation in the Cooperative
  - Sex (M/F)
  - Term of Office (Inclusive year)
  - Mandatory Training Undertaken/Completed
  - Date/Schedule of the Training
  - Training Provider
  - Accreditation Number
  - Remarks
- Signatories: Education Committee Chairperson, Manager, Chairperson - BOD

### Reports on Mediation/Conciliation Conducted
**File:** [report_mediation-conciliation.md](report_mediation-conciliation.md)

Semestral report on mediation and conciliation cases handled by the cooperative, covering:
- Cooperative Information (name, address, registration number)
- Cases Table:
  - Ref./s No.
  - Nature of Controversies/Issues Mediated/Conciliated
  - Parties Involved (Complainant/Requesting Party, Respondent/Responding Party)
  - Name of Mediator/Conciliator
  - Results/Agreement
  - Remarks
- Submitted twice yearly:
  - First Semester: For period ending June 30
  - Second Semester: For period ending December 31
- Signatories: Member and Chairman, Mediation & Conciliation Committee

## Registration Requirements Checklist

### Minimum Document Requirements

- [ ] Articles of Cooperation (4 original copies, notarized)
- [ ] By-Laws (4 original copies)
- [ ] Treasurer's Affidavit (notarized)
- [ ] Economic Survey
- [ ] Name Reservation Slip/Notice
- [ ] Pre-Membership Education Seminar (PMES) Certificates
- [ ] Bonds of Accountable Officers (if applicable)

### Capital Requirements

| Requirement | Minimum |
|-------------|---------|
| Subscribed Capital | 25% of Authorized Capital |
| Paid-up Capital | 25% of Subscribed Capital |

### Membership Requirements

| Cooperative Level | Minimum Members |
|-------------------|-----------------|
| Primary | 15 natural persons |
| Secondary | 2 primary cooperatives |
| Tertiary | 2 secondary cooperatives |

### Board of Directors

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Minimum | 5 members |
| Maximum | 15 members |
| Number | Must be odd |
