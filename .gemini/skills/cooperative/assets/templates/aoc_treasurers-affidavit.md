## Instructions for Completing the Treasurer's Affidavit

### Required Information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Affiant Name** | Complete name of the duly elected Treasurer |
| **Cooperative Name** | Full registered name of the cooperative as indicated in the Name Reservation Slip |
| **Authorized Share Capital** | Total authorized capital in words and figures (must match Articles of Cooperation) |
| **Subscribed Share Capital** | Total subscribed capital in words and figures (minimum 25% of authorized capital) |
| **Paid-up Share Capital** | Total paid-up capital in words and figures (minimum 25% of subscribed capital) |
| **Total Membership Fees** | Total membership fees collected in words and figures |
| **Date of Execution** | Day, month, and year the affidavit is signed |
| **Place of Execution** | City/Municipality where the affidavit is executed |

### Capital Requirements Summary

| Requirement | Minimum Percentage |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Subscribed Capital | At least 25% of Authorized Capital |
| Paid-up Capital | At least 25% of Subscribed Capital |

**Example Calculation:**
- Authorized Capital: Php 100,000.00
- Minimum Subscribed Capital: Php 25,000.00 (25% of Php 100,000.00)
- Minimum Paid-up Capital: Php 6,250.00 (25% of Php 25,000.00)

### Important Reminders

1. **Consistency** - All figures must tally with those provided in the Articles of Cooperation.

2. **Original Signature** - The Treasurer must affix his/her original signature on all copies.

3. **Notarization** - The affidavit must be notarized by a commissioned Notary Public.

4. **Proof of Identity** - The Treasurer must present a valid government-issued ID with photo and signature.

5. **Four Copies** - Submit four (4) original copies of the notarized affidavit.

### Proof of Identity Requirements

Acceptable proof of identity includes:
- Passport
- Driver's License
- Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) ID
- National ID (PhilSys ID)
- Voter's ID
- Senior Citizen ID
- UMID
- Other government-issued IDs with photo and signature
