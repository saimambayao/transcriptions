
## Usage Notes

- The transmittal memo is the COVER DOCUMENT — it wraps the CSW for formal submission
- The Brief Description should stand alone — the decision-maker may read only this before deciding whether to read the full CSW
- The Recommended Action must be specific and actionable — never say "for your information" without a clear ask
- Include approval/return checkboxes to facilitate the decision-maker's response
- The Reviewed/Endorsed block is for the supervisor's clearance (ADDRESS IT Step 5.10) BEFORE submission to the decision-maker
