# Project Proposal CSW Template (PP)

For CSW documents proposing new projects, programs, or initiatives to decision-makers.

---

### PP01 — PROJECT TITLE AND PROPONENT
- Complete project title
- Proponent office/division and responsible officer
- Date of submission

### PP02 — EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Write LAST. One page: problem, proposed project, expected outcomes, total cost, recommended action.

### PP03 — BACKGROUND AND CONTEXT (ADDRESS IT Step 1)
- The problem or opportunity the project addresses
- Stakeholders and beneficiaries
- Relevant laws, policies, BDP goals
- Current situation and baseline data

### PP04 — PROBLEM / NEED STATEMENT (ADDRESS IT Step 2)
- Clear problem statement with root cause analysis
- Consequences of inaction
- SMART objectives

### PP05 — RESEARCH AND EVIDENCE BASE (ADDRESS IT Step 3)
- Data supporting the need
- Best practices and benchmarks
- Feasibility assessment

### PP06 — PROJECT DESCRIPTION (ADDRESS IT Step 4)
- Scope and coverage (geographic, demographic)
- Components and activities
- Target beneficiaries (number, profile)
- Implementation strategy and timeline
- Alternative approaches considered and why this was chosen

### PP07 — BUDGET AND RESOURCE REQUIREMENTS
- Detailed budget by component (PS, MOOE, CO)
- Funding source (block grant, SDF, GAA, external)
- Cost-benefit analysis
- Budget sustainability beyond initial funding

### PP08 — IMPLEMENTATION PLAN (ADDRESS IT Steps 5-6)
- Phased timeline with milestones
- Organizational structure and staffing
- Roles and responsibilities
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Coordination with other agencies

### PP09 — MONITORING AND EVALUATION (ADDRESS IT Step 7)
- Performance indicators and targets
- M&E methodology
- Reporting schedule
- Success criteria and review points

### PP10 — LEGAL AND POLICY FRAMEWORK
- BOL provisions enabling the project
- BAAs and national laws applicable
- Shari'ah considerations (where applicable)
- Required approvals and compliance

### PP11 — RECOMMENDATION AND CALL TO ACTION
- Clear recommendation for the decision-maker
- Budget approval requested
- Timeline for decision

---

## Output Checklist
- [ ] All PP sections filled
- [ ] Budget figures are realistic and sourced
- [ ] Legal basis verified (BOL, BAAs)
- [ ] Beneficiary data is specific (not vague "communities")
- [ ] Risk mitigation is actionable
- [ ] Executive summary written last
- [ ] Transmittal memorandum prepared
