# Legal Opinion CSW Template (LO)

For CSW documents providing legal analysis, opinions, or reviews to decision-makers.

---

### LO01 — QUERY / LEGAL QUESTION
- The specific legal question(s) posed by the assigning authority
- Context in which the question arose
- Parties or entities involved

### LO02 — EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Write LAST. Concise statement of the legal question and the opinion rendered.

### LO03 — FACTUAL BACKGROUND (ADDRESS IT Step 1)
- Relevant facts of the case or situation
- Timeline of events
- Parties, roles, and relationships
- Documents reviewed

### LO04 — LEGAL ISSUE(S) DEFINED (ADDRESS IT Step 2)
- Precise articulation of the legal issue(s)
- Sub-issues if the main question branches
- Scope limitations

### LO05 — APPLICABLE LEGAL FRAMEWORK (ADDRESS IT Steps 1 + 3)
- Constitutional provisions
- BOL (RA 11054) provisions — cite Article, Section verbatim
- Relevant BAAs — cite number and title
- National laws and IRRs applicable
- Relevant jurisprudence (Supreme Court, if applicable)
- Shari'ah law provisions (where applicable — BOL Art. IX)
- Internal policies, administrative orders, resolutions

### LO06 — LEGAL ANALYSIS (ADDRESS IT Steps 3 + 4)
- Analysis of each legal issue against the applicable framework
- Application of law to the facts
- Consideration of competing interpretations
- Discussion of risks and implications of each interpretation
- Relevant precedents or analogous situations

### LO07 — LEGAL OPINION / RECOMMENDATION (ADDRESS IT Step 4-5)
- Clear statement of the legal opinion for each issue
- Recommended course of action
- Conditions or caveats attached to the opinion
- Risk assessment of the recommended action

### LO08 — PROPOSED NEXT STEPS (ADDRESS IT Steps 5-6)
- Actions the decision-maker should take
- Documents or approvals needed
- Timeline considerations
- Stakeholders to consult or notify

---

## Output Checklist
- [ ] All legal citations verified verbatim (invoke `/legal-reviewer`)
- [ ] BOL articles/sections accurately quoted
- [ ] BAA numbers and titles correct
- [ ] Opinion is clearly stated and actionable
- [ ] Risks and caveats explicitly stated
- [ ] Disclaimer: "This opinion is for internal guidance; formal legal proceedings require licensed counsel review"
- [ ] Executive summary written last
- [ ] Transmittal memorandum prepared
