This document provides examples of resolutions across different **categories** (purpose-based classifications). Note that all examples shown are **Simple Resolutions** (Type 1 per AR 268), as this is the most common resolution type used in the Bangsamoro Parliament.

## Internal/Procedural

**Resolution Type:** Simple Resolution (Type 1)
**Resolution Category:** Internal/Procedural

### Example: Adopting Parliamentary Rules

```
Republic of the Philippines
BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT
Cotabato City

**PROPOSED RESOLUTION NO. ___**

**A RESOLUTION ADOPTING THE REVISED RULES OF PROCEDURE OF THE BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT**

**Authored by:** MP [Name], MP [Name]
**Co-authored by:** MP [Name], MP [Name], MP [Name]

WHEREAS, Section 5, Article VII of the Bangsamoro Organic Law mandates the Bangsamoro Parliament to adopt its own rules of procedure for the conduct of its business;

WHEREAS, the current Rules of Procedure have been in effect since the first session of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament;

WHEREAS, there is a need to update and revise these rules to reflect the evolving needs of the Parliament and to ensure more efficient legislative processes;

WHEREAS, the Committee on Rules has conducted extensive consultations and deliberations on the proposed revised rules;

WHEREAS, the proposed revised rules incorporate best practices in parliamentary procedure while respecting the unique cultural and traditional context of the Bangsamoro;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament, that the Revised Rules of Procedure, as attached hereto and made an integral part of this Resolution, be adopted as the official rules governing the conduct of parliamentary business;

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that these revised rules shall take effect fifteen (15) days after adoption to allow Members to familiarize themselves with the changes;

**RESOLVED FINALLY,** that copies of this Resolution and the Revised Rules of Procedure be furnished to all Members of Parliament, the Office of the Chief Minister, and the Secretary-General of the Bangsamoro Parliament.
```

## Commendatory

**Resolution Type:** Simple Resolution (Type 1)
**Resolution Category:** Commendatory/Recognition

### Example: Honoring Frontline Workers

```
Republic of the Philippines
BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT
Cotabato City

**PROPOSED RESOLUTION NO. ___**

**A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND EXPRESSING GRATITUDE TO THE FRONTLINE HEALTH WORKERS OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION FOR THEIR HEROIC SERVICE DURING THE COVID-19 PANDEMIC**

**Authored by:** MP [Name]
**Co-authored by:** MP [Name], MP [Name]

WHEREAS, the COVID-19 pandemic has posed unprecedented challenges to the health system of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao;

WHEREAS, the frontline health workers, including doctors, nurses, medical technologists, midwives, and hospital staff, have demonstrated extraordinary courage and dedication in the face of this health crisis;

WHEREAS, these health workers have worked long hours under extremely difficult conditions, risking their own health and safety to care for COVID-19 patients and to prevent the spread of the virus;

WHEREAS, many health workers have contracted the virus themselves while performing their duties, and some have made the ultimate sacrifice, losing their lives in service to the Bangsamoro people;

WHEREAS, the selfless service of these frontline health workers exemplifies the highest ideals of public service and deserves the recognition and gratitude of the Bangsamoro Parliament and the entire region;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the Bangsamoro Parliament formally recognize and commend these modern-day heroes for their invaluable contributions to public health and safety;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament, that the Parliament hereby commends and expresses its deepest gratitude to all frontline health workers of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region for their heroic and selfless service during the COVID-19 pandemic;

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the Parliament calls upon the Bangsamoro Government to provide adequate support, benefits, and recognition programs for all frontline health workers;

**RESOLVED FINALLY,** that copies of this Resolution be furnished to the Ministry of Health, the Bangsamoro Frontline Workers Association, and all health facilities in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
```

## Policy/Statement

**Resolution Type:** Simple Resolution (Type 1)
**Resolution Category:** Policy/Statement

### Example: Directing an Investigation

```
Republic of the Philippines
BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT
Cotabato City

**PROPOSED RESOLUTION NO. ___**

**A RESOLUTION DIRECTING THE COMMITTEE ON PUBLIC WORKS AND HIGHWAYS TO CONDUCT AN INQUIRY, IN AID OF LEGISLATION, INTO THE REPORTED DELAYS AND SUBSTANDARD CONSTRUCTION OF ROAD PROJECTS IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION**

**Authored by:** MP [Name], MP [Name]
**Co-authored by:** MP [Name]

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Government has allocated substantial funds for road infrastructure projects aimed at improving connectivity and promoting economic development in the region;

WHEREAS, numerous reports have surfaced regarding significant delays in the completion of several major road projects, particularly in the provinces of Maguindanao and Lanao del Sur;

WHEREAS, there have been complaints from local communities about the alleged substandard quality of some completed road segments, with reports of rapid deterioration and premature damage;

WHEREAS, these delays and quality issues not only waste public resources but also deprive the Bangsamoro people of essential infrastructure that could improve their quality of life and economic opportunities;

WHEREAS, Section 10, Article VII of the Bangsamoro Organic Law empowers the Bangsamoro Parliament to conduct inquiries in aid of legislation;

WHEREAS, there is a need to investigate these matters to determine the causes of the delays and quality issues, to hold accountable those responsible, and to formulate legislative measures to prevent similar problems in the future;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament, that the Committee on Public Works and Highways is hereby directed to conduct an inquiry, in aid of legislation, into the reported delays and substandard construction of road projects in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the Committee shall submit its findings and recommendations to the Parliament within ninety (90) days from the adoption of this Resolution;

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the Committee may invite or subpoena officials from the Ministry of Public Works, contractors, engineers, and other relevant parties to appear and testify during the inquiry;

**RESOLVED FINALLY,** that copies of this Resolution be furnished to the Office of the Chief Minister, the Ministry of Public Works, the Commission on Audit, and the Office of the Ombudsman.
```

## Budget/Financial

**Resolution Type:** Simple Resolution (Type 1)
**Resolution Category:** Budget/Financial

### Example: Supplemental Appropriations

```
Republic of the Philippines
BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT
Cotabato City

**PROPOSED RESOLUTION NO. ___**

**A RESOLUTION APPROVING A SUPPLEMENTAL BUDGET FOR THE MINISTRY OF SOCIAL SERVICES AND DEVELOPMENT TO FUND EMERGENCY RELIEF OPERATIONS FOR COMMUNITIES AFFECTED BY TYPHOON [NAME]**

**Authored by:** MP [Name]
**Co-authored by:** MP [Name], MP [Name], MP [Name]

WHEREAS, Typhoon [Name] struck several municipalities in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region on [Date], causing widespread damage to homes, infrastructure, and agricultural lands;

WHEREAS, thousands of families have been displaced and are in urgent need of food, shelter, medical assistance, and other forms of emergency relief;

WHEREAS, the Ministry of Social Services and Development (MSSD) has initiated relief operations but requires additional funds to adequately address the scale of the disaster;

WHEREAS, the MSSD has submitted a request for supplemental appropriations amounting to [Amount] Pesos to fund emergency relief operations, including the provision of food packs, temporary shelter materials, medical supplies, and cash assistance to affected families;

WHEREAS, Section 12, Article IX of the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that the Bangsamoro Parliament may authorize supplemental appropriations for urgent and unforeseen expenditures;

WHEREAS, the immediate provision of relief assistance is necessary to alleviate the suffering of our people and to support their recovery from this natural disaster;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament, that a supplemental budget in the amount of [Amount] Pesos is hereby approved and appropriated for the Ministry of Social Services and Development to fund emergency relief operations for communities affected by Typhoon [Name];

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the Ministry of Social Services and Development shall submit a detailed report on the utilization of these funds within sixty (60) days from the conclusion of relief operations;

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the Commission on Audit shall conduct a special audit of the expenditures to ensure proper and transparent use of public funds;

**RESOLVED FINALLY,** that copies of this Resolution be furnished to the Office of the Chief Minister, the Ministry of Finance and Budget Management, the Ministry of Social Services and Development, and the Commission on Audit.
```

## Key Observations from Examples

**Note on Types vs Categories:** All four examples above are **Simple Resolutions** (Type 1 per AR 268), which is the most commonly used resolution type. They differ in their **purpose-based categories** (Internal/Procedural, Commendatory, Policy/Statement, Budget/Financial). Most resolutions you draft will be Simple Resolutions falling into one of these categories.

1. **WHEREAS Clauses:**
   - Build logical progression from context → problem → authority → solution
   - Cite specific legal provisions when relevant
   - Include facts, statistics, or specific details when available

2. **RESOLVED Clauses:**
   - Primary resolution states the main directive clearly
   - "RESOLVED FURTHER" adds implementation details or additional directives
   - "RESOLVED FINALLY" typically handles distribution of copies

3. **Tone:**
   - Formal and dignified
   - Respectful of institutions and individuals
   - Clear and unambiguous in directives

4. **Specificity:**
   - Names specific entities, dates, and amounts where applicable
   - Provides clear timelines for action
   - Identifies responsible parties
