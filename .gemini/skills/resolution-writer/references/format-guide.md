# Bangsamoro Parliament Resolution Format Guide

## Resolution Structure

Every Bangsamoro Parliament resolution follows this standard structure:

### 1. Header Section

```markdown
Republic of the Philippines
BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT
Cotabato City

**PROPOSED RESOLUTION NO. ___**

**[TITLE OF THE RESOLUTION IN ALL CAPS]**
```

**Guidelines:**
- Resolution number should be in bold
- Title should be in ALL CAPS and bold
- Title may be broken into multiple lines for readability

### 2. Authorship Section

```markdown
**Authored by:** [Principal Author Name(s)]
**Co-authored by:** [Co-author Name(s)]
```

**Guidelines:**
- "Authored by:" and "Co-authored by:" labels should be in bold
- List principal author(s) first
- Include "MP" title before names (e.g., "MP Juan Dela Cruz")
- Separate multiple authors with commas
- Co-authors are optional

### 3. Preamble - WHEREAS Clauses

The preamble consists of multiple "WHEREAS" clauses that provide:
- Background and context
- Legal basis and authorities
- Statement of facts
- Justification for the resolution

**Format:**
```
WHEREAS, [First contextual statement];

WHEREAS, [Second contextual statement];

WHEREAS, [Additional contextual statements as needed];
```

**Guidelines:**
- Each WHEREAS clause starts with "WHEREAS," (with comma)
- Clauses end with semicolons (;)
- Use formal, legislative language
- Present facts chronologically or logically
- Cite relevant laws, policies, or authorities when applicable
- Build a logical argument leading to the resolution

**Common WHEREAS Clause Patterns:**

1. **Legal Authority:**
   - "WHEREAS, Article X, Section 15 of the Bangsamoro Organic Law provides that..."
   - "WHEREAS, under Republic Act No. ____, the Parliament is mandated to..."

2. **Statement of Facts:**
   - "WHEREAS, on [date], [event or situation occurred]..."
   - "WHEREAS, it has come to the attention of this Parliament that..."

3. **Policy Justification:**
   - "WHEREAS, it is in the interest of the Bangsamoro people to..."
   - "WHEREAS, there is a need to [address/recognize/investigate]..."

### 4. Resolving Clause

The operative section that states the specific action or decision:

```
**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament, that [specific action or directive];

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that [additional directives if needed];

**RESOLVED FINALLY,** that copies of this Resolution be furnished to [relevant agencies/officials].
```

**Guidelines:**
- Always use "**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,**" (in bold)
- Full phrase: "as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament"
- State the directive clearly and specifically
- Use "**RESOLVED FURTHER,**" (in bold) for additional directives
- Use "**RESOLVED FINALLY,**" (in bold) for administrative matters (copies, effectivity)

## Numbering System

- **Proposed Resolutions:** "PROPOSED RESOLUTION NO. ___" (filled in upon filing)
- **Adopted Resolutions:** "RESOLUTION NO. ___" (assigned upon adoption)
- Sequential numbering per parliamentary session

## Language Style

1. **Formal tone:** Use formal, legislative language
2. **Active voice:** Prefer active voice in RESOLVED clauses
3. **Precision:** Be specific and clear in directives
4. **Consistency:** Maintain parallel structure in similar clauses

## Standard Citation Format for Legal Bases

**Format Structure:** Start with the provision identifier (Section X, Article Y), followed by "of the" and then the law.

### First Mention of a Law

**Constitutional Provisions:**
```
Section [Y], Article [X], of the 1987 Philippine Constitution
```
Example: `Section 15, Article X, of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that...`

**Republic Acts:**
```
Section [Y], Article [X], of Republic Act No. [XXXX], otherwise known as the [Official Name] ([ABBREVIATION])
```
Example: `Section 3, Article VII, of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL), vests...`

**Bangsamoro Autonomy Acts:**
```
Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. [XX], otherwise known as the [Official Name] ([ABBREVIATION])
```
Example: `Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Administrative Code (BAC),...`

### Subsequent Mentions

Use the established abbreviation without repeating the full law name:

```
Section [Y], Article [X], of the [ABBREVIATION]
```

Examples:
- `Section 5(a), Article VII, of the BOL further provides that...`
- `Section 1, Article X, of the BOL establishes that...`
- `Section 5 of the BAC provides that...`

### Why This Format?

1. **Clarity:** Provision identifier comes first for immediate reference
2. **Consistency:** Same structure across all legal citations
3. **Readability:** Reduces repetition and improves document flow
4. **Professional:** Follows parliamentary drafting standards

## Common Formatting Elements

- **Font:** Times New Roman or similar serif font
- **Spacing:** Single or 1.5 line spacing
- **Margins:** Standard 1-inch margins
- **Capitalization:**
  - "WHEREAS" always capitalized
  - "RESOLVED" clauses fully capitalized
  - Resolution title in all caps
  - Proper nouns capitalized per standard rules

## Amendment Notes

During parliamentary deliberation, WHEREAS clauses may be amended. Track amendments by:
- Striking through deleted text
- Underlining new text
- Or maintaining clean version with amendment notes
