### Cooperative Governance Extension

| Governance Element | Description |
|-------------------|-------------|
| **Membership** | Open to all licensed drivers in BARMM |

## Canvas Comparison Summary

| Element | Coop Example | SE Example | Platform Coop |
|---------|--------------|------------|---------------|
| **Primary Goal** | Member income | Cultural preservation | Driver ownership |
| **Key Metric** | Income increase % | Living wage weavers | Driver earnings |
| **Unfair Advantage** | Member network | Master weavers | Democratic control |
| **Revenue Model** | Product sales | Product + workshops | Platform fees |
| **Social Impact** | Farmer welfare | Heritage + livelihoods | Worker dignity |

---

## Key Takeaways for BARMM Context

1. **Member-Owner Focus**: Cooperatives prioritize member benefits over profit extraction
2. **Social Mission First**: SEs measure impact alongside revenue
3. **Community Trust**: Local relationships are genuine unfair advantages
4. **Halal/Shari'ah Alignment**: Consider Islamic principles in business model
5. **Platform Potential**: Digital cooperatives can compete with extractive platforms
