## What is Enterprise Development?

Enterprise Development is the discipline of systematically growing organizations from startup through maturity. It encompasses:

1. **Enterprise Growth Management** - Understanding lifecycle stages, navigating transitions
2. **Scaling Strategy** - Methods for sustainable growth while maintaining quality
3. **Operational Excellence** - Process improvement, efficiency, continuous improvement
4. **Organizational Development** - Capacity building, leadership, culture
5. **Strategic Execution** - Planning rhythms, accountability, measurement

### The Enterprise Development Triangle

```text
                    GROWTH
                 (Where are we going?)
                       ▲
                      / \
                     /   \
                    /  ◆  \
                   / ENTER-\
                  /  PRISE  \
                 /___________\
    OPERATIONS ◄─────────────► ORGANIZATION
    (How do we    (Do we have
     execute?)     the capacity?)
```

| Framework | Author | Core Concept |

## Workflow Modes

### Mode 1: Standalone Enterprise Development Consulting

Use this mode for direct consulting on enterprise development without SE journey context.

#### Step 1: Assess Current State

**Growth Stage Assessment:**

```text
GROWTH STAGE DIAGNOSIS:
│
├── Which Greiner phase are you in?
│   ├── Phase 1: Creativity → Leadership crisis coming?
│   ├── Phase 2: Direction → Autonomy crisis coming?
│   └── Phase 3+: Delegation/Coordination/Collaboration
│
├── Which Adizes stage are you in?
│   ├── Infancy → Go-Go → Adolescence → Prime
│   └── Are you showing aging signs?
│
└── What's your primary challenge?
    ├── Growth: Need scaling strategy
    ├── Operations: Need efficiency improvement
    └── Organization: Need capacity building
```

**Operational Assessment:**

```text
OPERATIONAL HEALTH CHECK:
□ Do you have documented core processes?
□ Are processes measured and monitored?
□ Is there a continuous improvement culture?
□ Are strategic goals clear and tracked?
□ Is there an execution rhythm (daily/weekly/quarterly)?
```

#### Step 2: Apply Appropriate Framework

| Challenge | Framework | Reference |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| Stuck in growth | Greiner crisis resolution | enterprise-theories.md |
| Ready to scale | Scaling Up / Good to Great | scaling-frameworks.md |
| New market entry | Crossing the Chasm | scaling-frameworks.md |
| Process inefficiency | Lean / BPM | operational-excellence.md |
| No improvement culture | Kaizen | operational-excellence.md |
| Misaligned goals | OKRs / Balanced Scorecard | operational-excellence.md |
| Weak organization | Capacity building | enterprise-theories.md |

#### Step 3: Provide Recommendations

**Output Format:**

```text
ENTERPRISE DEVELOPMENT RECOMMENDATIONS

CURRENT STATE:
- Growth Stage: [Greiner Phase X / Adizes Stage Y]
- Primary Challenge: [Description]
- Readiness Level: [Low/Medium/High]

RECOMMENDED FRAMEWORK: [Name]

ACTION PLAN:
1. [Immediate action - Week 1-2]
2. [Short-term action - Month 1]
3. [Medium-term action - Quarter 1]

KEY METRICS TO TRACK:
- [Metric 1]
- [Metric 2]

RESOURCES NEEDED:
- [Resource 1]
- [Resource 2]
```
