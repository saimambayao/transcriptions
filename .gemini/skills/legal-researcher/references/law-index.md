## Republic Acts

### RA 9520 — Philippine Cooperative Code of 2008
- **Subject**: Comprehensive law governing cooperatives in the Philippines. Defines types, registration, governance, rights, privileges, and dissolution of cooperatives.
- **Local PDF**: `/Users/saidamenmambayao/Library/Mobile Documents/com~apple~CloudDocs/Business Development/Cooperatives/RA 9520 - EDITABLE.pdf`
- **Key topics**: Registration (Art. 6-14), Types of cooperatives (Art. 23-28), Governance (Art. 37-53), Privileges (Art. 60-62), Dissolution (Art. 63-68)
- **Citation format**: `RA 9520, Book [I-IV], Title [I-V], Chapter [1-n], Article [n], Section [n]`

### RA 11054 — Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL)
- **Subject**: Foundational law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM). Defines territory, government structure, powers, fiscal autonomy, and transitory provisions.
- **Local file**: `~/Vault/bangsamoro/bangsamoro-laws/bol-ra-11054/` (5 chapter files)
- **Fallback PDF**: `/Users/saidamenmambayao/apps/transcriptions/PDFs/RA 11054.pdf`
- **Key topics**: Territory (Art. III), Government structure (Art. VII), Fiscal autonomy (Art. XII), Block grant (Art. XII, Sec. 10), CSEA mandate (Art. VI, Sec. 12), Transitory provisions (Art. XVI)
- **Citation format**: `RA 11054 (BOL), Article [Roman], Section [n]`

### RA 11364 — CDA Charter
- **Subject**: Charter of the Cooperative Development Authority. Defines CDA's mandate, powers, functions, and organizational structure.
- **Local PDF**: `/Users/saidamenmambayao/Library/Mobile Documents/com~apple~CloudDocs/Business Development/Cooperatives/RA-11364-CDA-Law.pdf`
- **Key topics**: Powers and functions (Sec. 3), Organization (Sec. 5-7), Regulatory authority (Sec. 3(a)-(r))
- **Citation format**: `RA 11364, Section [n]`

## Key Institutional Relationships

```
National Level                     Bangsamoro Level
--------------                     ----------------
CDA (RA 11364)        <--->        CSEA (BOL Art. VI, Sec. 12)
  Cooperative Code                   BAA No. 13 (Admin Code)
  (RA 9520)                          Bangsamoro Autonomy Acts
  National MCs                       BARMM regulatory issuances
```

- **CDA** regulates cooperatives nationally under RA 9520 and RA 11364.
- **CSEA** exercises cooperative regulation in BARMM under the BOL mandate.
- Devolution of cooperative regulatory functions from CDA to CSEA is governed by BOL Article VI, Section 12.
- Sulu: Per SC ruling, Sulu is not part of BARMM. CSEA's Sulu mandate derives from BOL Art. VI, Sec. 12 (include data with notation when applicable).
