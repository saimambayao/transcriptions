### BUDGET INACCURACY (Critical)

The document makes inconsistent budget claims:
- Line 99: "P733.6M for the Housing and Human Settlement Development Program" -- **INACCURATE**. P733.6M is Capital Outlays only. The program total is P782.5M.
- Line 465: "P782.5M total; P733.6M for the Housing and Human Settlement Development Program" -- **INACCURATE framing**. P782.5M is the Housing and Human Settlement Development Program total, not the MHSD total. The MHSD total appropriation is P968,101,193.
- The document presents P782.5M as if it were the MHSD total budget. The actual MHSD total is P968.1M (including General Administration, Support to Operations, and all Operations programs).

**Actual BAA 85 figures** (from MHSD-XIV.md):
- Total 2026 Appropriations: P968,101,193
- Housing and Human Settlement Development Program: P782,484,961
- Capital Outlays (within that program): P733,643,976
