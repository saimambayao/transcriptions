# (cc) Housing and Human Settlements

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(cc)** — "Housing and human settlements;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(cc) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | No — BOL Art. IX, Sec. 9 grants authority to create housing agency and develop programs without requiring national law consistency |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Human Settlements and Development (MHSD) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Ministry of Social Services and Development (MSSD); Special Development Fund (BAA 85, Ch. XXXII) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 9 — Social Services (Education, Health) |
| **Priority Code** | — |

| BDP Chapter | Relevant Goals/Strategies | Connection to Power |
|-------------|--------------------------|---------------------|
| Chapter 9 — Social Services | Address housing backlog; provide shelter for IDPs and conflict-affected communities; develop human settlements in peace corridors | Housing and human settlements power enables BDP targets for adequate shelter provision |
| Chapter 3 — Spatial Development Strategy | Urban-rural planning integration; settlement pattern rationalization | Housing programs should align with spatial development framework |

## Legislative Gaps

Note: most powers are exercised by MOAs through their BAA 13 mandate even without standalone legislation.

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A** — No standalone legislation | No Bangsamoro Housing and Human Settlements Act has been enacted despite the explicit BOL mandate in Art. IX, Sec. 9 to "pass a law creating a housing and human settlements agency." MHSD exercises this mandate through BAA 13 (Administrative Code) only | Enact the Bangsamoro Housing and Human Settlements Act creating the agency mandated by Art. IX, Sec. 9, defining housing programs, socialized housing standards, and donor solicitation procedures |
| **Type A** — No standalone legislation | No legislation specifically addresses Marawi post-siege resettlement and permanent housing for IDPs, despite Art. XIV, Sec. 1 mandate for IDP rehabilitation | Enact a Marawi Reconstruction and Permanent Resettlement Act or broader IDP housing legislation |
| **Type C** — National law interaction | DHSUD programs operate in BARMM but coordination mechanism with MHSD is not legislatively defined | Formalize MHSD-DHSUD coordination through intergovernmental agreement or enabling legislation |
