# (g) Business Name Registration

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(g)** — "Business name registration;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(g) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. XIII, Sec. 25 requires listing in the Philippine Business Registry |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Trade, Investments and Tourism (MTIT) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) — national counterpart for Philippine Business Registry |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 7 — Economic Development |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## BDP 2023-2028 Alignment

| BDP Chapter | Relevant Goals/Strategies | Connection to Power |
|-------------|--------------------------|---------------------|
| Chapter 7 — Economic Development | Promote private sector-led growth; ease of doing business in BARMM; support MSMEs | Business name registration is a foundational service for formalizing the Bangsamoro private sector and enabling MSME development |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A** — No dedicated BAA | No BAA establishes a Bangsamoro business name registration system as required by Art. XIII, Sec. 25 | Enact a Bangsamoro Business Name Registration Act establishing the registration system, fees, procedures, and integration with the Philippine Business Registry |
| **Type A** — No Bangsamoro Revenue Office interface | Business name registration fees are a potential revenue source (Art. XII, Sec. 6(b)) but no legislation links registration to revenue generation | Include registration fee schedule and revenue allocation in the proposed Business Name Registration Act |
| **Type C** — No DTI-MTIT coordination framework | Art. XIII, Sec. 25 requires listing in the Philippine Business Registry but no MOA or operational framework exists for DTI-MTIT data sharing | Execute intergovernmental coordination agreement between MTIT and DTI for Philippine Business Registry integration |
