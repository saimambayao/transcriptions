# (f) Budgeting

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(f)** — "Budgeting;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(f) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. VII, Sec. 28 ("consistent with existing laws, rules and regulations of the National Government"); Art. XII, Sec. 2 (COA as auditor); Art. XII, Sec. 19 ("the national laws and the budgeting rules and regulations implemented by the Department of Budget and Management and Department of the Interior and Local Government applicable to local government units shall apply") |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Finance, and Budget and Management (MFBM) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority (BPDA); Bangsamoro Treasury Office; Contingent Fund (BAA 85, Ch. XXXI) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 5 — Governance and Institutional Development; Chapter 13 — Plan Financing and Fiscal Strategy |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## BDP 2023-2028 Alignment

| BDP Chapter | Relevant Goals/Strategies | Connection to Power |
|-------------|--------------------------|---------------------|
| Chapter 5 — Governance and Institutional Development | Strengthen fiscal management, transparency, and accountability in budget execution | BAA 84 (Budget System Act) provides the comprehensive framework |
| Chapter 13 — Plan Financing and Fiscal Strategy | Diversify revenue sources, optimize block grant utilization, develop medium-term expenditure framework | Budgeting power is the central mechanism for implementing the BDP's fiscal strategy |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | BAA 84 (Budget System Act) enacted but IRR and operational guidelines for performance-based budgeting still under development | Complete IRR for BAA 84 with performance indicators aligned to BDP targets |
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | No Bangsamoro Revenue Office established yet (Art. XII, Sec. 11) — revenue collection still handled by BIR | Coordinate with IFPB on Bangsamoro Revenue Office establishment timeline |
| **Type C** — No medium-term framework | No BAA establishes a medium-term expenditure framework (MTEF) linking the BDP to multi-year budget commitments | Enact MTEF legislation or integrate into BAA 84 IRR |
