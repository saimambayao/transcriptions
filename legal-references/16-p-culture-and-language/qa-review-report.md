### IMPROVE-2: Art. IX, Sec. 24 verbatim slightly condensed in Q&A Theme A
**Location**: Theme A, second Q&A answer, item (1) (line ~404)
**Problem**: The Q&A quotes the provision as: "the history, culture, arts, traditions, and the rich cultural heritage of the Bangsamoro people and their Sultanates." This is accurate as a partial quote. However, the BOL continues with the specific named Sultanates and Royal Houses. Since the table already has the full text, this is acceptable -- but the Q&A could note "quoted in full in the table above" for clarity.
