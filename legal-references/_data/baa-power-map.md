# BAA-to-Power Map

**Key:** Powers are referenced by their letter designation in Art. V, Sec. 2 of RA 11054 (a through ccc). "General/Administrative" means the BAA does not implement a specific Sec. 2 power.

## Powers with NO BAA Implementation (as of BAA-89)

The following Art. V, Sec. 2 powers have not yet been implemented through a dedicated BAA:

(e) Barter trade/countertrade, (g) Business name registration, (h) Cadastral land survey, (j) Classification of public lands, (k) Cooperatives/social entrepreneurship, (m) Creation of GOCCs, (n) Creation of revenue sources, (o) Cultural exchange/economic/technical cooperation, (q) Customary laws, (s) Disaster risk reduction, (t) Ecological solid waste/pollution, (u) Economic zones/free ports, (w) Eminent domain, (z) Grants/donations, (aa) Hajj/Umrah, (cc) Housing/human settlements, (gg) Inland waters, (hh) Inland waterways, (ii) Islamic banking/finance, (ll) Loans/credits, (nn) People's organizations, (oo) Power sector, (pp) Public utilities, (qq) Public works/infrastructure, (rr) Quarantine, (ss) Registration of births/marriages/deaths, (tt) Regulation of food/drugs/tobacco, (yy) Tourism, (zz) Trade/industry, (bbb) Urban land reform/land use, (ccc) Water supply/flood control/irrigation

*Note: Some of these powers may be partially addressed within omnibus codes (BAA 13 Administrative Code, BAA 49 Local Governance Code) without dedicated legislation.*
