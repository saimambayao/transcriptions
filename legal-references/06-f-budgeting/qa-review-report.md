### Missing Questions

1. **Art. XII, Sec. 24 (Loans and Borrowing):** No Q&A addresses the Bangsamoro Government's borrowing powers -- bonds, debentures, debt servicing limits (20% of regular revenue, 20% of block grant for loan payments). This is a significant fiscal power directly affecting budgeting.

2. **Art. XII, Sec. 10 (Sharing of Taxes Collected by National Government):** The 75%/25% sharing scheme and the 10-year accrual provision are not separately analyzed despite being a major revenue source feeding the budget.

3. **Art. XII, Sec. 40 (Full Disclosure Policy):** The BOL mandates full disclosure of budget, finances, bids, and public offerings. This budget transparency requirement deserves its own Q&A.
