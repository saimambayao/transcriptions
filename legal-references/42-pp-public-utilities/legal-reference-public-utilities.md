# (pp) Public Utilities' Operations

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(pp)** — "Public utilities' operations"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(pp) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. XIII, Sec. 38: "the National Government shall regulate the airside operations of all existing airports"; Art. XIII, Sec. 39: "nothing herein shall be construed as limiting in any way the power of the Congress of the Philippines to grant national franchises in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region" |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Transportation and Communications (MTC) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Ministry of Environment, Natural Resources and Energy (MENRE) for power utilities, Department of Transportation (national), National Telecommunications Commission (national) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 8 (Technology and Innovation), Chapter 11 (Infrastructure Development) |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A — No BAA** | No dedicated transportation code or franchise framework despite Art. XIII, Sec. 38 granting comprehensive regional transport authority | Enact a Bangsamoro Transportation Code covering franchising, registration, and quasi-judicial proceedings |
| **Type A — No BAA** | No dedicated telecommunications code despite Art. XIII, Sec. 39 granting CPCN issuance and franchise authority | Enact a Bangsamoro Telecommunications Code or include telecom provisions in a public utilities omnibus act |
| **Type B — Partial** | MTC exists institutionally but lacks an enabling organic act defining franchise-granting procedures, quasi-judicial rules, and coordination with national agencies | Enact MTC charter legislation defining franchise procedures and quasi-judicial authority |
| **Type C — Potential Conflict** | Congress retains power to grant national franchises in BARMM (Art. XIII, Sec. 39 proviso) — no mechanism to coordinate national and regional franchise grants for the same service area | Establish coordination protocol between MTC and NTC/DOTr through intergovernmental relations mechanism |
| **Type C — Potential Conflict** | National Government regulates airside operations (Art. XIII, Sec. 38 proviso) while BARMM has authority over air transportation franchises — boundary between "airside operations" and "air transportation franchises" undefined | Clarify scope through intergovernmental agreement or implementing legislation |
