- **Source**: `legislation/baas/BAA-13.md`:2041-2082 (MENRE is under Title III of Book VI)

Similarly, MOTC is cited as "BAA 13, Book VI, Sec. 1-20" without the Title number, and MPW as "BAA 13, Book VI, Sec. 1-3" without the Title number. All three should include Title numbers.

**FAIL #2**: Line 67 (Art. XIII, Sec. 37 explanation) says "creating a overlapping jurisdiction" -- grammatical error ("a" should be "an"). While not a legal accuracy issue, this is a quality issue for a publication-grade reference.
