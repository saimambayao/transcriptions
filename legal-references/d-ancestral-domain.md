# (d) Ancestral Domain and Natural Resources

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(d)** — "Ancestral domain and natural resources;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(d) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. XIII, Sec. 8 ("Subject to the provisions of the Constitution"); Art. XIII, Sec. 10 (fossil fuels and uranium require joint exercise with National Government); Art. XIII, Sec. 13 ("Subject to the provisions of the Constitution and national laws" for mines and minerals) |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Environment, Natural Resources and Energy (MENRE) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Ministry of Indigenous Peoples' Affairs (MIAP); Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Agrarian Reform (MAFAR); Bangsamoro Sustainable Development Board (Art. VI, Sec. 8) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 12 — Environment and Natural Resources |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## Legislative Gaps

Note: most powers are exercised by MOAs through their BAA 13 mandate even without standalone legislation.

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A** — No standalone legislation | No Bangsamoro Mining Code enacted; mining regulation exercised through MENRE under BAA 13 | Enact a Bangsamoro Mining Code operationalizing Art. XIII, Sec. 13-16 |
| **Type A** — No standalone legislation | No comprehensive Ancestral Domain Code; ancestral domain powers exercised through MIAP under BAA 13 and BAA 64 | Enact an Ancestral Domain Code addressing native title recognition, FPIC mechanisms, and domain delineation |
| **Type C** — National law interaction | Fossil fuels and uranium require joint exercise with National Government per Art. XIII, Sec. 10; no implementing MOA or joint protocol established | Establish joint protocol with DOE for fossil fuel/uranium exploration under Art. XIII, Sec. 10 |
| **Type C** — National law interaction | FTAAs require Presidential endorsement per Art. XIII, Sec. 14 | Coordinate with national agencies on FTAA process |
