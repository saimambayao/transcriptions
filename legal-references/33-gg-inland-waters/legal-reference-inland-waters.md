# (gg) Inland Waters

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(gg)** — "Inland waters;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(gg) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | No specific national law consistency required for inland waters management. However, Art. XIII, Sec. 22 (para. 2) provides co-management applies if any inland water is a source of energy for areas outside BARMM. Art. III, Sec. 3 establishes inland waters as part of Bangsamoro territorial jurisdiction. |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Environment, Natural Resources and Energy (MENRE) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Agrarian Reform (MAFAR); Bangsamoro Sustainable Development Board; constituent LGUs |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 12 — Environment and Natural Resources |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## Legislative Gaps

Note: most powers are exercised by MOAs through their BAA 13 mandate even without standalone legislation.

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A** — No standalone legislation | No Bangsamoro Inland Waters Act enacted despite express BOL mandate in Art. XIII, Sec. 22 for Parliament to "enact laws on the regulation, conservation, management, and protection" of inland waters; no Bangsamoro Authority for specific bodies of water created; MENRE exercises functions through BAA 13 | Enact a Bangsamoro Inland Waters Code per the express BOL mandate, including creation of authorities for Lake Lanao and Liguasan Marsh |
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | PB No. 310 (Liguasan Marsh conservation framework) and PB No. 352 (Lake Lanao sustainable management) have been filed but not yet enacted | Prioritize passage of pending inland waters bills |
| **Type C** — National law interaction | Lake Lanao provides energy for areas outside BARMM (Agus Hydropower Complex); co-management principle applies per Art. XIII, Sec. 22 (para. 2) and Sec. 36(b); coordination through Intergovernmental Energy Board required | Establish co-management protocol with NPC/PSALM and the Intergovernmental Energy Board for Lake Lanao |
