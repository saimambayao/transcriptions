### Critical Issues

1. **MISQUOTED: Art. VI, Sec. 8 (Sustainable Development Board)** -- The document presents a fabricated paraphrase as verbatim BOL text. The actual BOL says "The Parliament shall create" and mentions "integration and harmonization of economic, social, and environmental considerations." The document's version is entirely different.

2. **MISQUOTED: Art. V, Sec. 2 chapeau constitutional reference** -- The BOL references "Section 20, Article X" (legislative powers), NOT "Section 15" (autonomous regions framework). The footnote partially addresses this but the main text at line 450 still says "Section 20, Article X" which it then "corrects" to Sec. 15 -- this correction is wrong. The BOL says Sec. 20 and means Sec. 20.

3. **MISQUOTED: Art. X, Sec. 4 (Shari'ah)** -- The document says Parliament can enact "Shari'ah laws governing persons in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region" on property matters. The actual text says "laws on personal, family, and property law jurisdiction" with no "governing persons in" language.

4. **MISQUOTED: MTC in "Book IX"** -- BAA 13 has only 7 Books. MTC is in Book VI, Title XV, not "Book IX."

5. **UNSOURCED geographic claims not flagged** -- "Philippines' second-largest lake" and "Philippines' largest freshwater marsh" are presented as fact without [UNVERIFIED] tags, unlike other geographic claims that were correctly flagged.

6. **Many national law EOs cannot be verified** -- EO 123, EO 860, EO 510, EO 375, EO 753 are all UNSOURCED. The years and titles should be verified.
