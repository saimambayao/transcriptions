# (l) Creation of Municipalities

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(l)** — "Creation, division, merger, abolition or alteration of boundaries of municipalities and barangays;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(l) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. VI, Sec. 10 ("the criteria laid down in Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, and other national laws shall be satisfied"); Art. VI, Sec. 10 ("Nothing in this Organic Law shall be construed to allow the Bangsamoro Government to create legislative districts") |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of the Interior and Local Government (MILG) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Bangsamoro Parliament (BTA); Sustainable Assistance Mechanism for Local Moral Governance (SAMLMG, BAA 85 Ch. XXXIII); Commission on Elections — Bangsamoro Electoral Office |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 1 — BARMM Extended Transition; Chapter 5 — Governance and Institutional Development |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## BDP 2023-2028 Alignment

| BDP Chapter | Relevant Goals/Strategies | Connection to Power |
|-------------|--------------------------|---------------------|
| Chapter 1 — Extended Transition | Complete territorial and institutional setup; integrate SGA communities | Municipality creation in SGA (BAA 41-48) directly implements extended transition goals |
| Chapter 5 — Governance and Institutional Development | Strengthen LGU governance; decentralize service delivery | New municipalities expand governance reach and service delivery to underserved communities |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | BAA 49 (Local Governance Code) enacted but IRR for municipality creation procedures (compliance verification with RA 7160 criteria) still being developed | Complete IRR for BAA 49 LGU creation provisions |
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | Newly created municipalities (BAA 41-48, 53-55) need capacity building and organizational support during transition | Enact enabling legislation for transition support packages for newly created municipalities |
| **Type C** — No barangay creation framework | No BAA or bill specifically addresses barangay creation or boundary alteration procedures within BARMM despite Art. V, Sec. 2(l) covering barangays | Develop barangay creation guidelines under BAA 49 or separate legislation |
