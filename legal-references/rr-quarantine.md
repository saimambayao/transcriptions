# (rr) Quarantine Regulations

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(rr)** — "Quarantine regulations;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(rr) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. IX, Sec. 22, para. 3 ("shall assist and cooperate with the National Government in the provision of quarantine services"); Art. XIII, Sec. 28 (cooperation on customs, immigration, and quarantine services for economic zones) |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Health (MOH) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Office of the Chief Minister (DRRM Council for epidemic response); Bangsamoro Economic Zone Authority (quarantine at economic zones) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 9 — Social Services (Education, Health) |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## Legislative Gaps

Note: most powers are exercised by MOAs through their BAA 13 mandate even without standalone legislation.

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A** — No standalone legislation | No Bangsamoro Quarantine Regulations Act has been enacted despite the enumerated power. MOH exercises quarantine functions through its BAA 13 (Administrative Code) mandate. Bill 229 (Bureau of Health Quarantine under MOH) and Bill 421 (Bangsamoro Bureau of Quarantine) have been filed but not yet enacted | Enact the Bangsamoro Quarantine Act establishing quarantine protocols, a Bureau of Health Quarantine, and coordination mechanisms with the DOH Bureau of Quarantine |
| **Type C** — National law interaction | Art. IX, Sec. 22 requires "cooperation" with National Government on quarantine but does not define the coordination mechanism — cooperation was improvised during COVID-19 pandemic | Formalize MOH-DOH quarantine cooperation through an intergovernmental agreement defining roles, reporting, and joint response protocols |
| **Type C** — National law interaction | Art. XIII, Sec. 28 requires cooperation on quarantine for economic zones, but no Bangsamoro Economic Zone Authority has been created yet | Economic zone quarantine protocols should be included in the enabling legislation for the Bangsamoro Economic Zone Authority when enacted |
