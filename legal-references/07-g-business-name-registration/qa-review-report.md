XIII, Sec. 24) is not discussed as an elaborating provision in the Q&A, though it provides the economic context (cooperatives, entrepreneurial capability, domestic trade preference) within which business name registration operates. ### Missing Questions

1. **Interaction with RA 11534 (CREATE Act, 2021):** The Corporate Recovery and Tax Incentives for Enterprises Act reformed the investment incentive system and the Fiscal Incentives Review Board. This interacts with business registration for enterprises seeking tax incentives in BARMM. A Q&A addressing how CREATE Act incentives interact with BARMM business registration could strengthen the national law analysis.

2. **Transition mechanism from DTI to MTIT:** While Q25 (status of devolution) addresses this briefly and Q32 (overlapping jurisdictions) discusses it, a dedicated power-specific question on "What would a DTI-to-MTIT transition plan look like?" with specific legal steps would be valuable.

3. **Intellectual property / trademark interaction:** Business name registration intersects with intellectual property protection. The Intellectual Property Code (RA 8293) and the IP Office of the Philippines have relevance -- a business name registration system must check against registered trademarks. This intersection is not addressed.
