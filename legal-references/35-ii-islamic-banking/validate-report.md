### Critical Issues

1. **CITATION ERROR — Art. XII, Sec. 6(e)**: The document cites "Art. XII, Sec. 6(e)" for the IFPB function on AAIIBP participation. The correct citation is **Art. XII, Sec. 37(e)**. Art. VI, Sec. 4 creates the IFPB; Art. XII, Sec. 37 lists its functions. This error appears in both the BOL Provisions table and the Legal Briefer Q&A. The footnote [^6] correctly cites "sec. 33, art. XII" for the AAIIBP mandate, but the separate "Sec. 6(e)" reference in the Power Overview and BOL table is wrong.

2. **UNSOURCED RA 11439 specifics**: Multiple verbatim quotes and section references attributed to RA 11439 (Sec. 2(a)(4), Sec. 5, Sec. 6(c), Sec. 6(d)) are not verified against local text. The existence of RA 11439 is confirmed, but specific section contents remain unverified.

4. **UNSOURCED Parliamentary Resolutions**: Res. No. 24, 130, 200, and 470 — titles and relevance descriptions are not verified against resolution texts.

5. **UNSOURCED Parliament Bills**: PB No. 383, 337, 164, 268 — titles and descriptions not verified.
