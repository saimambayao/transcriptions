# (yy) Tourism Development

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(yy)** — "Tourism development"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(yy) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. XIII, Sec. 35: "in accordance with Republic Act No. 9593" (Tourism Act of 2009) |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Trade, Investments and Tourism (MTIT) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Tourism Infrastructure and Enterprise Zone Authority (TIEZA, national), Department of Tourism (national), Bangsamoro Commission for Preservation of Cultural Heritage (BCPCH) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 7 (Economic Development) |
| **Priority Code** | — |

| BDP Chapter | Topic | Alignment |
|-------------|-------|-----------|
| Chapter 7 | Economic Development | Tourism as key economic diversification strategy; targets ecotourism development in island provinces, cultural tourism in Marawi, and halal tourism promotion |
| Chapter 6 | Cultural Diversity and Heritage | Heritage sites as tourism assets; cultural preservation supporting tourism product development |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A — No BAA** | No comprehensive tourism development legislation despite Art. XIII, Sec. 35 mandate and multiple filed bills (261, 280) | Prioritize passage of a Bangsamoro Tourism Code establishing promotion framework, TEZ recommendation procedures, and tourism standards |
| **Type A — No BAA** | No legislation creating a Tourism Promotion Board or equivalent institutional mechanism | Include institutional framework in tourism code or establish by executive order pending legislation |
| **Type B — Partial** | MTIT handles tourism as part of broader trade/investment mandate but no legislation specifies tourism-specific regulatory authority | Define tourism-specific functions within MTIT charter or standalone tourism legislation |
| **Type B — Partial** | Multiple ecotourism site designation bills filed (217, 218, 324) but no framework law for ecotourism zone designation criteria and management | Enact ecotourism framework legislation before designating individual sites |
