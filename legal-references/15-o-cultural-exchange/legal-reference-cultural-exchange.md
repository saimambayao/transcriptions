# (o) Cultural Exchange, and Economic and Technical Cooperation

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(o)** — "Cultural exchange, and economic and technical cooperation"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(o) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. XII, Sec. 28: "upon review and approval of the National Government" |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Office of the Chief Minister (OCM) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Ministry of Trade, Investments and Tourism (MTIT), Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority (BPDA), Department of Foreign Affairs (national) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 7 (Economic Development), Chapter 13 (Plan Financing and Fiscal Strategy) |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A — No BAA** | No legislation establishes the framework, protocols, or institutional mechanism for cultural exchange and economic/technical cooperation with foreign countries | Enact legislation creating an international cooperation framework including approval protocols, eligible activities, and institutional lead |
| **Type A — No BAA** | No legislation implements Art. XII, Sec. 28's provision for recommending Bangsamoro personnel to Philippine embassies and consulates | Enact legislation or executive policy establishing the selection and recommendation process for embassy/consulate assignments |
| **Type B — Partial** | OCM handles international linkages operationally but no BAA defines its authority, scope, or coordination mechanism for this function | Codify OCM's international cooperation mandate through legislation or amend BAA 13 (Administrative Code) |
