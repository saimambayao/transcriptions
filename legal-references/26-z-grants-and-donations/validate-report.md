### Critical Issues

1. **MFBM organizational details** (BAA 13 Sec. 15 — International and Other Finance Division) — not verified from BAA 13 text.
2. **Analytical inference** on foreign private entity exemption from Presidential clearance — this is a legally significant interpretation. The reading is textually defensible (par. 2 specifically names "governments of foreign countries, their agencies and instrumentalities or multilateral institutions") but no court or authoritative interpretation has confirmed this distinction. Should be flagged as INFERENTIAL, not presented as settled law.
3. **RA 10168** (Terrorism Financing Prevention and Suppression Act) — relevance to grant acceptance claimed but not verified.

### Assessment

**BOL provisions are extensively verified** — the file cites Art. XII Secs. 6, 24, 25, 26, 28, and 38, all matching source files. The **three-tier grant regime** analysis (domestic / foreign government-multilateral / ODA) is a well-constructed analytical framework grounded in verified BOL text. The key analytical risk is the **inference about private foreign entities** not requiring Presidential clearance — a legally important distinction that should be clearly marked as inferential.
