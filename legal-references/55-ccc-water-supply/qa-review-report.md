**Score: PASS**

### 2. Evidence

**Issues found:**

- **FAIL-E1**: Art. VII, Sec. 8 misattribution. The Power Overview table (line 10) states: "Art. V, Sec. 2 chapeau subjects all 55 powers to Art. X, Sec. 15 of the Constitution." The BOL chapeau actually says: ***"Subject to Section 20, Article X of the Constitution and this Organic Law."*** The chapeau references **Sec. 20**, NOT Sec. 15. Art. X, Sec. 15 is the general "within the framework" provision for creating autonomous regions — it is a correct constitutional reference but is NOT what the BOL chapeau cites.
  - Source: `bol-ra-11054/01-articles-I-to-V.md:134`
  - Constitution: `1987-The-Philippines-Contitution.md:634` (Sec. 15) and `:646` (Sec. 20)

- **FAIL-E2**: "Art. VII, Sec. 8" should be "Art. IX, Sec. 8". The file cites "Art. VII, Sec. 8" for the "Right of Access to Basic Services" provision (line 51, and in Section A at line 411). The actual BOL has this provision at **Art. IX, Sec. 8** ("Right of Access to Basic Services" under the "Social Justice" heading of Article IX — Basic Rights).
  - Source: `bol-ra-11054/02-articles-VI-to-IX.md:288` (Art. IX, Sec. 8)
  - Art. VII, Sec. 8 in the BOL is actually about "Election for Reserved Seats for Non-Moro Indigenous Peoples" (`01b-articles-V-to-VII.md:134`)
  - Footnote [^9] reads "Rep. Act No. 11054, sec. 8, art. VII" — should read "sec. 8, art. IX"

- **FAIL-E3**: "Art. III, Sec. 4" should be "Art. III, Sec. 3". Line 61-63 cites "Art. III, Sec. 4" for the inland waters territorial provision ("All inland waters such as lakes, rivers, river systems, and streams within its territorial jurisdiction..."). The actual BOL has this at **Art. III, Sec. 3** ("Inland Waters"). Art. III, Sec. 4 is about "Bangsamoro Municipal and Regional Waters."
  - Source: `bol-ra-11054/01-articles-I-to-V.md:90` (SEC. 3. Inland Waters)
  - Source: `bol-ra-11054/01-articles-I-to-V.md:92` (SEC. 4. Municipal and Regional Waters)

**Score: FAIL (3 issues — all are wrong article/section citations)**
