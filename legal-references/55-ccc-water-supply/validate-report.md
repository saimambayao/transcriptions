### Critical Issues

1. **Art. IX, Sec. 8 misattributed as Art. VII, Sec. 8 (P2 — Wrong Identifier)**: The "Right of Access to Basic Services" provision is in Article IX (Basic Rights), not Article VII (Bangsamoro Government). Art. VII, Sec. 8 is actually about reserved seats for indigenous peoples. This error appears in the BOL Provisions table, Section A Q&A, and footnote [^9].
   - Source: `bol-ra-11054/02-articles-VI-to-IX.md:288` (Art. IX, Sec. 8)
   - Fix: Replace all "Art. VII, Sec. 8" with "Art. IX, Sec. 8"; fix footnote [^9]

2. **Art. III, Sec. 3 misidentified as Art. III, Sec. 4 (P2 — Wrong Identifier)**: The "Inland Waters" territorial provision is Sec. 3, not Sec. 4. Sec. 4 is about municipal and regional waters.
   - Source: `bol-ra-11054/01-articles-I-to-V.md:90` (Sec. 3) vs `:92` (Sec. 4)
   - Fix: Change "Art. III, Sec. 4" to "Art. III, Sec. 3"

3. **BOL Chapeau cites Art. X, Sec. 20, not Sec. 15 (P2 — Wrong Identifier)**: The Power Overview table says the chapeau subjects powers to Sec. 15. The chapeau actually says "Subject to Section 20, Article X."
   - Source: `bol-ra-11054/01-articles-I-to-V.md:134`
   - Fix: Correct the chapeau reference to cite Sec. 20; can note Sec. 15 as the general framework provision separately
