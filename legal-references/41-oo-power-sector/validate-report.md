21. **"Renewable energy revenues pertain fully to BARMM; fossil fuel revenues are shared equally (50-50)"** -- INFERENTIAL. Art. XII, Sec. 34 says revenues from "all natural resources" pertain fully to BARMM except fossil fuels which are "shared equally." The inference that renewable energy revenues = 100% BARMM is logical but the term "renewable energy" does not appear in Art. XII, Sec. 34. The provision distinguishes "uranium and fossil fuels" (shared) from "all natural resources" (full BARMM). Renewable energy would fall under "all natural resources" by implication.
