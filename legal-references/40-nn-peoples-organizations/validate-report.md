## CRITICAL ERRORS (P0-P1)

### 1. FABRICATED BOL CITATIONS — Art. IV, Sec. 2(i), (j), (k) DO NOT EXIST

**Severity**: P0 — Fabricated source citation
**Locations**: Lines 46-58 (BOL Provisions table), Lines 341-370 (Theme A Q&A), footnotes [^2], [^3]

**Claim**: The document repeatedly cites "Art. IV, Sec. 2(i)", "Art. IV, Sec. 2(j)", and "Art. IV, Sec. 2(k)" as BOL provisions containing policy declarations about NGO participation, moral governance, and full disclosure.

**Finding**: Art. IV of RA 11054 contains Sections 1-10, each a standalone provision with NO subsections. Art. IV, Sec. 2 is titled "Self-Governance" and reads: *"In the exercise of its right to self-governance, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region is free to pursue its political, economic, social, and cultural development as provided for in this Organic Law."* It has no subsections (i), (j), or (k).

**Verification**: Confirmed against RA 11054 PDF pages 6-7. Art. IV sections are:
- Sec. 1: Territorial Integrity and Allegiance
- Sec. 2: Self-Governance
- Sec. 3: Democratic Political System
- Sec. 4: Electoral System
- Sec. 5: Civilian Government
- Sec. 6: Promotion of Unity
- Sec. 7: Social Justice
- Sec. 8: International Treaties and Agreements
- Sec. 9: Rights of Non-Moro Indigenous Peoples
- Sec. 10: Freedom of Choice

**Impact**: Three verbatim-quoted provisions are attributed to non-existent BOL citations. The quoted text may exist elsewhere in the BOL or may be paraphrases of constitutional provisions (Art. II, Sec. 23 mirrors the NGO participation language), but the citations are wrong. This affects:
- BOL Provisions table rows for Art. IV, Sec. 2(i), (j), (k)
- Theme A answers citing these provisions
- Footnotes [^2] and [^3]
- The Power Overview table ("Named Legislative Mandate" row)
- Theme F divergence analysis referencing Art. IV, Sec. 2(i)

**Classification**: UNSOURCED — text attributed to fabricated citations

### 2. WRONG ARTICLE NUMBER — "Art. XIII, Sec. 40" is actually Art. XII, Sec. 40

**Severity**: P1 — Wrong article citation for key provision
**Locations**: Lines 70-73 (BOL Provisions table), Line 10 (Power Overview), Lines 347, 492, 531-532, 658, 681, 704, and multiple other references

**Claim**: The document consistently cites "Art. XIII, Sec. 40" for the Full Disclosure Policy requiring CSO participation in the budget process.

**Finding**: Section 40 (Full Disclosure Policy) falls under **Article XII** (Fiscal Autonomy), NOT Article XIII (Regional Economy and Patrimony). The BOL PDF page 48 and the local transcription at `04-articles-XIII-to-XV.md` line 48 confirm: Sec. 40 is the final section of Art. XII before Art. XIII begins at Sec. 1 (Social Justice and Economic System).

**Verification**: BOL PDF; local file `~/Vault/bangsamoro/bangsamoro-laws/bol-ra-11054/04-articles-XIII-to-XV.md` lines 48-49.

**Impact**: Every reference to "Art. XIII, Sec. 40" throughout the document is wrong. This is a systematic error appearing in ~15 locations including the Power Overview table, BOL Provisions table, Theme A, Theme D, Theme F, Theme G, Theme H, Theme I, and the Legislative Gaps table. Footnote [^4] cites "sec. 40, art. XIII" — should be art. XII.

**Classification**: UNSOURCED — correct provision, wrong article number

### 3. VERBATIM INACCURACY — BOL Art. V, Sec. 2 chapeau

**Severity**: P2 — Misquotation of statutory text
**Location**: Line 41 (BOL Provisions table)

**Claim**: Quotes the chapeau as: *"Subject to Section 20, Article X of the Constitution, and the provisions of this Organic Law, the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise its authority over the following areas:"*

**Actual text** (from RA 11054 PDF page 7 and local BOL transcription): *"Subject to Section 20, Article X of the Constitution and this Organic Law, the Bangsamoro Government shall exercise its authority over the following matters without prejudice to the general supervision of the President of the Republic of the Philippines:"*

**Discrepancies**:

### 5. CONFLATION — Constitutional Art. X, Sec. 15 vs. Sec. 20

**Severity**: P2 — Conflation of two distinct constitutional provisions
**Location**: Line 42 (BOL Provisions table, Explanation column)

**Claim**: In explaining the chapeau, states: "(Art. X, Sec. 15 of the Constitution — autonomous regions operate 'within the framework of this Constitution'). This is the universal constitutional ceiling."

**Finding**: The BOL chapeau cites **Sec. 20**, not Sec. 15. These are distinct provisions:
- Art. X, Sec. 15: Creates autonomous regions "within the framework of this Constitution"
- Art. X, Sec. 20: Enumerates the legislative powers the organic act shall provide

The document treats them as interchangeable. While both are relevant, attributing the chapeau's reference to Sec. 15 when the BOL actually cites Sec. 20 is a citation error.

**Classification**: INFERENTIAL — correct general concept but wrong specific section

### 14. BAA 13 — Council of Leaders Composition

**Severity**: P4 — Minor characterization issue
**Location**: Line 361

**Claim**: "the Council of Leaders (Art. VII) includes sectoral representatives and CSO nominees"

**Finding**: BAA 13, Sec. 3-4 defines the Council of Leaders. It includes representatives of "other sectors" (Sec. 3(e)) and five specific sectors: professionals, business, private educational institutions, labor, farmers (Sec. 4). These are nominated by registered organizations (Sec. 5(c)). The document's characterization as "CSO nominees" is a simplification — they are sectoral representatives nominated by organizations, not CSO representatives per se.

**Classification**: INFERENTIAL — reasonable but imprecise characterization
