# (a) Administration of Justice

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(a)** — "Administration of justice;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(a) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. X, Sec. 1 ("in consonance with the Constitution"); Art. X, Sec. 2 ("shall form part of the Philippine judicial system subject to the supervision of the Supreme Court"); Art. X, Sec. 19 ("Regular courts ... shall continue to exercise their judicial functions, as provided by national law") |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Bangsamoro Attorney-General's Office (BAGO) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Bangsamoro Support Fund to Judiciary (BAA 85, Ch. XXXIV); Office for Tribal Justice System; Shari'ah courts (under Supreme Court supervision) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 5 — Governance and Institutional Development |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## BDP 2023-2028 Alignment

| BDP Chapter | Relevant Goals/Strategies | Connection to Power |
|-------------|--------------------------|---------------------|
| Chapter 5 — Governance and Institutional Development | Strengthen rule of law, access to justice, and Bangsamoro justice institutions | Operationalization of Shari'ah courts, traditional justice systems, and BTJRC |
| Chapter 10 — Peace and Security | Transitional justice and reconciliation; human rights promotion | BAA 89 (BTJRC) directly implements transitional justice commitments |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A** — No dedicated BAA | No comprehensive Bangsamoro Justice System Code has been enacted; BAA 89 covers only transitional justice, and BAA 5 covers legal services | Enact a Bangsamoro Justice System Code operationalizing Art. X (Shari'ah courts, traditional justice, ADR) |
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | Shari'ah High Court created by BOL (Art. X, Sec. 7) but not yet organized by the Supreme Court | Parliament resolution urging Supreme Court organization (Res. 458 filed; follow-up needed) |
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | Art. X, Sec. 16 authorizes creation of Shari'ah public assistance office, prosecution service, and academy — academy enacted (Bill 363) but others pending | Enact enabling legislation for Shari'ah public assistance office and Shari'ah special prosecution service |
| **Type C** — No revenue code interface | No BAA establishes the relationship between justice system funding and the Bangsamoro budget system | Integrate justice system funding framework into BAA 84 (Budget System Act) implementing rules |
