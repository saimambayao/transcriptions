**FAIL #1**: The footnote [^7] in the quarantine file references "CONST. (1987), art. II, sec. 15; art. III, secs. 1, 6; art. X, sec. 20." The text at line 317-321 discusses Art. X, Sec. 15 (not Sec. 20) as the universal constraint. The footnote bundles Art. X, Sec. 20 for the broader constitutional framework discussion -- but the text specifically quotes Art. X, Sec. 15 language ("within the framework of this Constitution and the national sovereignty..."). The footnote should reference Sec. 15, not Sec. 20, for this specific quote. Art. X, Sec. 20 is relevant elsewhere (it is what the BOL chapeau references) but the footnote mismatches the text it supports.
- **Source**: `reference/constitution/1987-The-Philippines-Contitution.md`:634 (Sec. 15) vs. :646 (Sec. 20). The quote "within the framework of this Constitution and the national sovereignty as well as territorial integrity of the Republic of the Philippines" is from Sec. 15, not Sec. 20.

**Rating: FAIL (1 issue -- footnote cites Sec. 20 but text quotes Sec. 15)**
