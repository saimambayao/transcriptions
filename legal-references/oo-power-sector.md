# (oo) Power Sector Investments

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(oo)** — "Power sector investments"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A — No BAA** | BOL Art. XIII, Sec. 36(a) mandates creation of a "Ministry of Energy" — none has been created; MENRE handles energy as part of broader mandate | Establish a dedicated Ministry of Energy or formalize MENRE's energy regulatory authority through legislation |
| **Type A — No BAA** | No legislation establishes the framework for power sector investment promotion, grid interconnection protocols, or divestment timeline | Enact a Bangsamoro Power Sector Investment Code |
| **Type A — No BAA** | Intergovernmental Energy Board (Art. VI, Sec. 7) has no implementing legislation defining its procedures, composition specifics, or dispute resolution mechanisms | Enact IEB enabling legislation or include in a comprehensive intergovernmental relations act |
| **Type B — Partial** | Electric cooperative assistance authorized under Art. XIII, Sec. 36(a) but no mechanism, fund, or criteria established | Enact legislation creating an electric cooperative assistance framework |
| **Type C — Potential Conflict** | BARMM power generation must comply with EPIRA but no mechanism coordinates BARMM Ministry of Energy (once created) with ERC on tariff-setting and franchise granting | Establish coordination protocol between BARMM energy authority and ERC through IEB or bilateral agreement |
