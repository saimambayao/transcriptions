# (h) Cadastral Land Survey

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(h)** — "Cadastral land survey;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(h) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. XIII, Sec. 17 ("upon authority of the President"); results must be furnished to national agencies for inclusion in National Cadastral Survey |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Environment, Natural Resources and Energy (MENRE) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | National Mapping and Resource Information Authority (NAMRIA); Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR); Land Management Bureau |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 12 — Environment and Natural Resources |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## Legislative Gaps

Note: most powers are exercised by MOAs through their BAA 13 mandate even without standalone legislation.

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A** — No standalone legislation | No Bangsamoro Cadastral Survey Act enacted; cadastral survey functions exercised through MENRE under BAA 13 | Enact a Bangsamoro Land Survey Act defining procedures, institutional arrangements, and coordination protocols with NAMRIA/LMB |
| **Type C** — National law interaction | BOL Art. XIII, Sec. 17 requires Presidential authority before conducting cadastral surveys; no standing delegation or protocol has been established | Negotiate standing delegation of authority from the President or establish a protocol for expedited survey authorization |
| **Type C** — National law interaction | Survey results must be furnished to national agencies for integration into the National Cadastral Survey | Formalize data-sharing agreement with NAMRIA and DENR-Land Management Bureau |
