# (i) Civil Service

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(i)** — "Civil service;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(i) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. VII, Sec. 39 ("shall be in accordance with existing national civil service laws. In case of conflict with the national laws, the Constitution and existing national civil service laws, rules, and regulations shall prevail"); Art. VII, Sec. 13 (RA 6758 Compensation and Position Classification System); Art. VII, Sec. 16(b) (RA 6713 prohibitions); Art. VII, Sec. 18 (SALN per national law) |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Civil Service Commission — Bangsamoro Regional Field Office; Development Academy of the Bangsamoro (DAB) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Pension and Gratuity Fund (BAA 85, Ch. XXIX); Miscellaneous Personnel Benefits Fund (BAA 85, Ch. XXX) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 5 — Governance and Institutional Development |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## BDP 2023-2028 Alignment

| BDP Chapter | Relevant Goals/Strategies | Connection to Power |
|-------------|--------------------------|---------------------|
| Chapter 5 — Governance and Institutional Development | Merit-based recruitment, capacity building, professional civil service, performance management | BAA 17 (Civil Service Code) is the primary implementing legislation; DAB provides capacity development |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | BAA 17 (Civil Service Code) enacted but IRR and implementing mechanisms for performance management still under development | Complete IRR for BAA 17 with SPMS framework and capacity building pathway |
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | No comprehensive Bangsamoro Government Rationalization Plan linking civil service positions to organizational structure under BAA 13 | Develop rationalization plan per DBM MC guidelines, aligned with BAA 13 and BAA 17 |
| **Type C** — No discipline integration | No unified disciplinary mechanism integrating BAA 17 (civil service discipline) with Ombudsman jurisdiction and BAA 13 (administrative code) procedures | Enact implementing guidelines harmonizing Bangsamoro disciplinary procedures with RA 6770 and RA 3019 |
