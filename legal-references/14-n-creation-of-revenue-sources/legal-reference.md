# (n) Creation of Revenue Sources

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(n)** — "Creation of sources of revenues;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(n) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. XII, Sec. 6 ("subject to the provisions of this Organic Law and consistent with the principles of equalization, equity, accountability, administrative simplicity, harmonization and economic efficiency, and fiscal autonomy"); Art. XII, Sec. 7 ("subject to the provisions of the Constitution"); Art. XII, Sec. 9 (12 categories of taxes the Bangsamoro Government cannot impose) |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Finance, and Budget and Management (MFBM); Bangsamoro Parliament (BTA) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Intergovernmental Fiscal Policy Board (IFPB); Bureau of Internal Revenue (until Bangsamoro Revenue Office is established) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 5 — Governance and Institutional Development; Chapter 13 — Plan Financing and Fiscal Strategy |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## BDP 2023-2028 Alignment

| BDP Chapter | Relevant Goals/Strategies | Connection to Power |
|-------------|--------------------------|---------------------|
| Chapter 5 — Governance and Institutional Development | Strengthen fiscal governance and revenue administration capacity | Revenue creation power enables fiscal self-sufficiency goals |
| Chapter 13 — Plan Financing and Fiscal Strategy | Diversify revenue sources beyond block grant; establish Bangsamoro Revenue Office; optimize tax administration | Central to BDP fiscal strategy — moving from block grant dependency to own-source revenues |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A** — No Bangsamoro Revenue Office | Art. XII, Sec. 11 mandates creation of the Bangsamoro Revenue Office but it has not been established; BIR continues to collect taxes in BARMM | Enact legislation creating the Bangsamoro Revenue Office and coordinate transition with BIR through IFPB |
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | Bill 286 (Revenue Code) enacted but implementing rules for the 4 BARMM-imposable taxes (CGT, DST, Donor's, Estate) require IFPB rules per Art. XII, Sec. 9 | Coordinate with IFPB to promulgate rules on taxable element determination for the 4 specified taxes |
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | Revenue sharing formula (Art. XII, Sec. 10) operational but Parliament has not yet enacted the law detailing LGU shares per Art. XII, Sec. 13 | Enact legislation detailing constituent LGU shares in the 75% Bangsamoro share |
| **Type C** — No tax incentive framework | Art. XII, Sec. 8 grants Parliament tax exemption/incentive power but no comprehensive Bangsamoro Investment Incentive Code exists | Enact a Bangsamoro Investment Incentive Code aligned with the Revenue Code and RA 11534 (CREATE) |
