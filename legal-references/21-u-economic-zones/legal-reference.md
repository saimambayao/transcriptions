# (u) Economic Zones, Industrial Centers, and Free Ports

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(u)** — "Economic zones, industrial centers, and free ports"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(u) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. XIII, Sec. 28: "the Bangsamoro Government and the National Government shall cooperate on customs, immigration, and quarantine services including the attendant international commitments thereto"; goods consumed outside zones subject to "all relevant national taxes" |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Trade, Investments and Tourism (MTIT) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Bangsamoro Economic Zone Authority (BEZA, to be created), Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA, national — transitional), Bureau of Customs (national), Bureau of Immigration (national) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 7 (Economic Development) |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A — No BAA** | Bangsamoro Economic Zone Authority (BEZA) has not been created despite explicit BOL mandate in Art. XIII, Sec. 28; PEZA continues to operate in BARMM by default | Enact BEZA charter legislation as highest priority — triggers PEZA jurisdictional transfer per Art. XIII, Sec. 28, para. 2 |
| **Type A — No BAA** | No economic zones, industrial estates, or free ports have been established through Bangsamoro legislation | Designate priority economic zones concurrently with or immediately after BEZA creation |
| **Type A — No BAA** | No legislation establishes customs/immigration/quarantine cooperation protocols required by Art. XIII, Sec. 28, para. 3 | Include intergovernmental cooperation protocols in BEZA charter or enact standalone agreement |
| **Type A — No BAA** | No fiscal incentives framework for BARMM economic zone investors despite Art. XIII, Sec. 28 mandate | Enact Bangsamoro Investment Incentives Code or include in BEZA charter |
| **Type A — No BAA** | No legislation addresses transfer of existing ARMM free ports (Art. XIII, Sec. 28, para. 4) | Address in BEZA charter legislation with transitional provisions for existing free ports |
| **Type C — Potential Conflict** | National CREATE Act and PEZA incentives coexist with future BARMM incentives — potential overlap or inconsistency in incentive packages for zone enterprises | Harmonize BARMM and national incentives through IFPB (Art. XII, Sec. 37) and in BEZA enabling legislation |
