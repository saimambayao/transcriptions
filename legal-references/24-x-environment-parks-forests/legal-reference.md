# (x) Environment, Parks, Forest Management, Wildlife, and Nature Reserves Conservation

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(x)** — "Environment, parks, forest management, wildlife, and nature reserves conservation;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(x) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | No specific BOL provision requires national law consistency for this power beyond the general constitutional limitation in Art. XIII, Sec. 8 ("Subject to the provisions of the Constitution"). Art. XIII, Sec. 9 provides for transfer of management from national agencies. |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Environment, Natural Resources and Energy (MENRE) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Bangsamoro Sustainable Development Board (Art. VI, Sec. 8); Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority (BPDA); constituent LGUs |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 12 — Environment and Natural Resources |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## Legislative Gaps

Note: most powers are exercised by MOAs through their BAA 13 mandate even without standalone legislation.

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A** — No standalone legislation | No Bangsamoro Protected Areas Act enacted despite BOL Art. XIII, Sec. 8 mandate for Parliament to "pass a law to establish protected areas, the procedure for the declaration and the management thereof"; MENRE exercises protected area functions through BAA 13 | Enact a Bangsamoro Protected Areas Act per the express BOL mandate |
| **Type A** — No standalone legislation | No Bangsamoro Forestry Code enacted; forest management exercised through MENRE under BAA 13 | Enact a Bangsamoro Forestry Code covering forest classification, timber regulation, reforestation mandates, and community-based forest management |
| **Type A** — No standalone legislation | No Bangsamoro Wildlife Protection Act enacted | Enact regional wildlife legislation addressing species protection, habitat conservation, and wildlife trade regulation |
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | BAA 31 (Sustainable Development Board) provides institutional oversight but does not substantively regulate environment, parks, or forests | Complement BAA 31 with substantive environmental legislation |
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | PB No. 238 (rainwater harvesting), PB No. 248/389 (single-use plastics), PB No. 278 (river basin management), PB No. 362 (greening program), PB No. 385 (water sustainability) filed but not yet enacted | Prioritize passage of pending environmental bills |
