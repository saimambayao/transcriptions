### Critical Findings (FAILs)

1. **Art. XIII, Sec. 1 quote is incomplete** --- the document quotes only the first sentence and omits the second sentence about Parliament enacting laws. The quoted text is accurate but the explanation in the BOL Provisions table claims this provision is about Congress creating an agency for cooperatives (conflating it with the Constitution's Art. XII, Sec. 15).
2. **Art. X, Sec. 4 paraphrase presented as verbatim** --- Q on Shari'ah uses "enact laws on... commercial..." as if it were a verbatim quote, but the actual BOL text is "enact laws governing commercial and other civil actions not provided for under Presidential Decree No. 1083." The compression misrepresents the scope.
3. **EO 316 claim about "10 CDA functions devolved"** appears multiple times but EO 316 Sec. 1 lists exactly 10 items. Verified PASS but the framing should acknowledge Sec. 2 (registration deferred to MOA) and Sec. 7 (shared powers) as additional --- the "10 functions" framing is accurate but potentially misleading since 3 additional categories exist.
4. **"No national law consistency required" claim** --- while Art. XIII, Sec. 24 and 27 lack consistency qualifiers, the document does not sufficiently acknowledge that Art. XIII, Sec. 1 itself requires the economic system to be ***"based on the principles and state policies declared in the Constitution"*** --- this IS a form of consistency requirement, not with national law but with the Constitution. The document addresses this in Theme C and F but the Power Overview table flatly says "No."
