# (zz) Trade and Industry

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(zz)** — "Trade and industry"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(zz) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | No explicit national law consistency clause for general trade and industry — Art. XIII, Sec. 24 grants broad authority; however, Art. XIII, Sec. 30 (Halal) and Art. XIII, Sec. 28 (economic zones) have specific national coordination requirements |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Trade, Investments and Tourism (MTIT) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Bangsamoro Board of Investments, Bangsamoro Enterprise Support Corporation (proposed), Cooperatives and Social Enterprise Authority (CSEA), Department of Trade and Industry (national) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 7 (Economic Development) |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A — No BAA** | No comprehensive trade and industry code despite Art. XIII, Sec. 24 providing detailed mandate for entrepreneurial capability building, MSME support, and domestic trade preference | Enact a Bangsamoro Trade and Industry Code |
| **Type A — No BAA** | No halal development act despite Art. XIII, Sec. 30 mandating halal-certifying body accreditation and a halal campaign program | Enact a Bangsamoro Halal Development Act |
| **Type A — No BAA** | No PPP framework legislation despite multiple filed bills (243, 329) | Prioritize passage of PPP framework legislation to attract private investment |
| **Type A — No BAA** | No investment incentives code to complement national CREATE Act and attract investment to BARMM | Enact Bangsamoro Investment Incentives Code providing BARMM-specific fiscal incentives |
| **Type B — Partial** | Bangsamoro Board of Investments exists operationally but has no enabling organic act defining its investment promotion authority, incentive-granting powers, and registration procedures | Enact BOI charter legislation or include in trade and industry code |
| **Type B — Partial** | No consumer protection framework despite filed Bill 401 | Prioritize consumer protection legislation to regulate trade practices |
