XII, Sec. 24(a) = "Loans, Credits, and Other Forms of Indebtedness." The verbatim text is correct but attributed to the wrong section. Affects 9+ instances including the BOL Provisions table, footnote [^4], and multiple Q&A answers. **Must fix before publication.**

2. **BDP statistics unverified**: The 3%, 59%, and 11,544 km figures are attributed to BDP Chapter 11 but not verified against local BDP files. These are specific quantitative claims that would be embarrassing if wrong.

3. **Resolutions and PBs entirely unverified**: All 10 resolution numbers and 10 PB numbers with their titles are unverified. If any number-title pairing is wrong, it undermines the document's reliability.

4. **Jurisprudence unverified**: Both SC cases cited are unverified from local archive. The *Ali v. BTA Parliament* quote is particularly important as it's used to establish the constitutional constraint.

5. **RA 12009 unverified**: Claimed as the revision of RA 9184 but not confirmed from local files.
