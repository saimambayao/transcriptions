### Critical Issues

1. **FAIL: BSC Commissioner term length** -- File claims 7-year term for Chairperson (line 570: "7-year term, with staggered initial terms -- Chairperson (7 years), 2 commissioners (5 years), 2 commissioners (3 years)"). BAA 13 Sec. 38 (`BAA-13.md:4438`) states 5/4/3 years with 3-year fixed term thereafter. Either BAA 12 has different terms than BAA 13's codification (in which case the discrepancy should be noted), or the claim is wrong. Source: `BAA-13.md:4438`.

2. **MARGINAL: BAA 13 Title designation** -- "Bk. VI, Title I, Ch. 3" is likely incorrect for BSC. Title I of Book VI is Agriculture. The section numbers are correct.

3. **MARGINAL: Hadith attribution** -- authentication status not specified.
