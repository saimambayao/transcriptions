# (m) Creation of GOCCs

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(m)** — "Creation of government-owned or controlled corporations and pioneer firms;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(m) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. XII, Sec. 30 ("Upon coordination with the National Government ... in compliance with the provisions of Republic Act No. 10149") |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Bangsamoro Parliament (BTA) — legislative charter authority; Ministry of Finance, and Budget and Management (MFBM) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Governance Commission for GOCCs (GCG) — national oversight; Intergovernmental Fiscal Policy Board (IFPB) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 7 — Economic Development |
| **Priority Code** | — |

| Chapter 7 — Economic Development | Promote economic development through government enterprise; pioneer firms for economic zones and key industries | GOCC/pioneer firm creation enables state-driven economic development in underserved sectors |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A** — No dedicated BAA | No BAA establishes a comprehensive framework for Bangsamoro GOCC creation, governance, and oversight despite Art. XII, Sec. 30-33 | Enact a Bangsamoro GOCC Act establishing creation procedures, governance standards, and IFPB coordination protocols |
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | Bill 376 (BESC) enacted but IFPB has not yet determined Bangsamoro Government participation in AAIIBP and SPDA as required by Art. XII, Sec. 33 | Coordinate with IFPB to complete the Art. XII, Sec. 33 determination for AAIIBP and SPDA |
| **Type C** — No GOCC dividend framework | Art. XII, Sec. 6(f) identifies GOCC dividends as a revenue source but no legislation establishes dividend remittance policies for Bangsamoro GOCCs | Include GOCC dividend policy in the proposed Bangsamoro GOCC Act or the Revenue Code |
