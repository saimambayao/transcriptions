### Critical Issues

1. **RA 9997 provisions** — the Amirul Hajj appointment mechanism and NCMF Hajj functions are critical to understanding the BARMM-national government jurisdictional boundary for this power, but the specific RA 9997 sections are not verified from local files.
2. **BAA 85 budget figures** — the P278.8M total and P200M Hajj Financing allocation are specific financial claims that need verification.
3. **Saudi Hajj quota** and **10,000+ pilgrims annually** — external factual claims without local source verification.
4. **Hajj travel agency regulation** — inferred from sheikh accreditation authority but not explicitly stated in BAA 13.

### Assessment

**BOL provisions are verified** — Art. V, Sec. 2(aa) and Art. VII, Sec. 42 match source files. **BAA 13 BPA provisions** (Sec. 43-46) are comprehensively verified against BAA 13 text — this is one of the strongest BAA 13 verification results in this batch. The document correctly identifies the unique character of this power as distinctly Islamic and the BPA's geographic limitation to "pilgrims from within the Bangsamoro." The main gap is the **RA 9997 details** governing the national side of Hajj administration.
