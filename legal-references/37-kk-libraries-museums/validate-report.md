## Errors Requiring Correction

### ERROR 1: "Sec. h(d)" Citation (Line 191)
**Location**: National Laws table, RA 10087 row
**Claim**: `"maintain cooperation with the National Library for book allocations and reading materials" (Sec. h(d))`
**Problem**: "Sec. h(d)" is a meaningless reference. The correct citation is BAA 13, Bangsamoro Library and Archives, function (d). The (d) refers to item (d) in the Library and Archives powers/functions list, not "Section h(d)."
**Severity**: Minor (citation formatting error, not a substantive error)

### ERROR 2: Museum Management Budget Figure (Line 527)
**Location**: Section D.3, BARMM Implementation
**Claim**: "dedicated Museum Management and Development budget line (P3.1M in BAA 85)"
**Problem**: 3,109,077 (P3.1M) is the MOOE allocation only, not the total budget for Museum Management and Development. The total includes Personnel Services and Capital Outlays. Presenting MOOE-only as the program budget understates the actual allocation.
**Severity**: Moderate (misleading budget figure)
