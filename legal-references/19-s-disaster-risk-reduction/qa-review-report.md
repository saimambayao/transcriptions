### 2. EVIDENCE

**FAIL 1 -- Wrong Book reference for DRRM Council (line ~92 and throughout)**:
The BAAs table (line 92) states: "**Book III, Title III, Ch. 2, Secs. 9-11**: establishes the Bangsamoro DRRM Council". The correct reference is **Book IV, Title III, Ch. 2, Secs. 9-11**. Book III is "The Bangsamoro Autonomous Government" (starting at `BAA-13.md:274`); Book IV is "The Office of the Chief Minister" (starting at `BAA-13.md:736`). The DRRM Council sections (Secs. 9-11) are under Book IV, Title III ("Functions"), Chapter 2 ("Councils in the Office of the Chief Minister").

- **Source**: `BAA-13.md:274` (Book III), `BAA-13.md:736` (Book IV), `BAA-13.md:1026` (Title III under Book IV), `BAA-13.md:1086-1099` (DRRM Council sections)
- **Fix**: Change "Book III, Title III, Ch. 2" to "Book IV, Title III, Ch. 2" everywhere it appears

**FAIL 2 -- Wrong Title reference for READi (line ~92 and throughout)**:
The BAAs table (line 92) states: "**Title VII, Ch. 8, Secs. 26-29**: establishes READi". The correct reference is **Book VI, Title VIII, Ch. 8, Secs. 26-29**. Title VII in Book VI is "Indigenous Peoples' Affairs" (MIAP). Title VIII is "Interior and Local Government" (MILG), and READi is Chapter 8 under MILG.

- **Source**: `BAA-13.md:2595` (Title VII = Indigenous Peoples' Affairs), `BAA-13.md:2702` (Title VIII = Interior and Local Government), `BAA-13.md:2875-2878` (READi under Title VIII, Ch. 8)
- **Fix**: Change "Title VII, Ch. 8" to "Book VI, Title VIII, Ch. 8" everywhere it appears

Note: The footnote [^9] (line 605) says "BAA No. 13, secs. 26-29, tit. VII, ch. 8 (READi)" -- this should also be corrected to "tit. VIII".
