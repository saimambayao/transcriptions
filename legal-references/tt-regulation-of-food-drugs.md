# (tt) Regulation of the Manufacture and Distribution of Food, Drinks, Drugs, and Tobacco

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(tt)** — "Regulation of the manufacture and distribution of food, drinks, drugs, and tobacco;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(tt) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. IX, Sec. 22 ("including the regulation of the manufacture and distribution of food, drinks, drugs, and tobacco in accordance with the policies, rules and regulations of the concerned national government agencies") |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Health (MOH) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Bangsamoro Nutrition Commission (BAA 83); Ministry of Trade, Investments and Tourism (MTIT) for trade aspects |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 9 — Social Services (Education, Health) |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## Legislative Gaps

Note: most powers are exercised by MOAs through their BAA 13 mandate even without standalone legislation.

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A** — No standalone legislation | No Bangsamoro Food, Drugs, and Tobacco Regulation Act has been enacted despite the enumerated power and the specific legislative mandate in Art. IX, Sec. 22. MOH exercises regulatory functions through BAA 13 (Administrative Code) in coordination with FDA | Enact a Bangsamoro Food, Drugs, and Tobacco Regulation Act defining regional regulatory standards, inspection protocols, and enforcement mechanisms consistent with FDA policies |
| **Type C** — National law interaction | Art. IX, Sec. 22 requires regulation "in accordance with the policies, rules and regulations of the concerned national government agencies" but no formal coordination mechanism exists between MOH and FDA; Res. 409 requested establishment of an FDA regional office in BARMM | Establish a formal MOH-FDA coordination agreement and support creation of an FDA regional office in BARMM |
| **Type A** — No halal food regulation | No standalone Bangsamoro halal food regulation act exists despite Art. XIII, Sec. 30 granting halal certification authority | Enact a Bangsamoro Halal Standards and Certification Act covering food production, processing, and distribution standards |
