## Errors Found

### ERROR 1 — BAA 13 Section Range (P2: Wrong Number)
- **Location**: Lines 11, 100-102, 594, 599, 602
- **Claim**: "BAA 13, Book VI, Title XIII, Sec. 1-20" (repeated 5 times)
- **Actual**: BAA 13, Book VI, Title XIII runs **Sec. 1 through Sec. 22**. Sections 21 (Child Welfare Agency) and 22 (Foster Homes) are part of Title XIII but omitted from the range.
- **Source**: BAA-13.md, lines 3373-3548. Sec. 21 begins at line 3532, Sec. 22 at line 3547.
- **Severity**: MEDIUM — omits two substantive sections on child welfare agency licensing and foster home regulation, which are directly relevant to the (vv) social services power.
- **Fix**: Change all instances of "Sec. 1-20" to "Sec. 1-22" for Title XIII.

### ERROR 2 — Art. X, Sec. 4 Paraphrased as Verbatim (P3: Verbatim Fabrication)
- **Location**: Line 703
- **Claim**: The document quotes Art. X, Sec. 4 as authorizing Parliament to enact ***"laws in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region on matters concerning the Shari'ah which shall apply to all Muslims in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region"*** on personal, family, and property matters.
- **Actual verbatim text**: *"The Parliament shall have the power to enact laws on personal, family, and property law jurisdiction."* (BOL file 02-articles-VI-to-IX.md, line 414)
- **Severity**: MEDIUM — the paraphrased text is placed in bold-italic quotation format (used throughout the document for verbatim text), creating the impression of a direct quote. The actual BOL text is shorter and does not contain the phrases "in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region," "matters concerning the Shari'ah," or "all Muslims."
- **Fix**: Either (a) use the actual verbatim text, or (b) paraphrase without bold-italic formatting.

## Art. X, Sec. 15 vs. Sec. 20 Note

The document uses Art. X, Sec. 15 (line 544) as the "universal constraint" for autonomous regions. Sec. 15 creates autonomous regions "within the framework of this Constitution." However, the constraint on **legislative powers** is Art. X, Sec. 20: "Within its territorial jurisdiction and **subject to the provisions of this Constitution and national laws**, the organic act of autonomous regions shall provide for legislative powers over..." Both sections establish constitutional constraints, but Sec. 20 is the more precise citation for the constraint on legislative authority. This is not an error per se (the document correctly states the constraint), but for maximum precision the Sec. 20 reference should be primary, with Sec. 15 as supporting.
