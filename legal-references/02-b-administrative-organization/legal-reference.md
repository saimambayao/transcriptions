# (b) Administrative Organization

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(b)** — "Administrative organization;"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(b) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. VII, Sec. 13 (Parliament shall adopt RA 6758 Compensation and Position Classification System); Art. XVI, Sec. 10 ("consistent with existing budgeting, organization, staffing, position classification and compensation policies, guidelines, and standards of the National Government") |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Office of the Chief Minister (OCM); Bangsamoro Parliament (BTA) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Ministry of the Interior and Local Government (MILG); Bangsamoro Planning and Development Authority (BPDA); Sustainable Assistance Mechanism for Local Moral Governance (SAMLMG, BAA 85 Ch. XXXIII) |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 1 — BARMM Extended Transition; Chapter 5 — Governance and Institutional Development |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## BDP 2023-2028 Alignment

| BDP Chapter | Relevant Goals/Strategies | Connection to Power |
|-------------|--------------------------|---------------------|
| Chapter 1 — Extended Transition | Completing institutional setup before elected government takes over | BAA 13, BAA 49, and transition plan resolutions directly implement institutional framework |
| Chapter 5 — Governance and Institutional Development | Strengthening governance structures, bureaucratic reform, merit-based public administration | Core power for building the Bangsamoro Government's organizational architecture |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | BAA 13 (Administrative Code) enacted but implementing rules and regulations (IRR) for some chapters still pending | Complete IRR development for all BAA 13 chapters |
| **Type B** — Partial implementation | Several ministries created by Art. XVI, Sec. 8 lack individual organic charters defining powers, functions, and organizational structure in detail | Enact individual ministry organic acts for remaining ministries without dedicated legislation |
| **Type C** — No rationalization plan | No BAA provides a comprehensive Bangsamoro Government Rationalization Plan per DBM MC standards | Enact a rationalization plan BAA aligning organizational structures with RA 6758 and DBM guidelines |
