# (e) Barter Trade and Countertrade

> **BOL Art. V, Sec. 2(e)** — "Barter trade and countertrade"

## Power Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **BOL Basis** | Art. V, Sec. 2(e) — enumerated power |
| **National Law Consistency Required** | Yes — Art. XIII, Sec. 26: goods traded "shall not be sold elsewhere in the country without the payment of appropriate customs or import duties in accordance with existing national laws"; countertrade "in accordance with the spirit, intent, goals, processes, modalities, and arrangements in the ASEAN economic integration agreements" |
| **Lead Ministry/Office** | Ministry of Trade, Investments and Tourism (MTIT) |
| **Supporting Agencies** | Bureau of Customs, BIMP-EAGA National Secretariat, Department of Trade and Industry |
| **BDP Chapter(s)** | Chapter 7 (Economic Development) |
| **Priority Code** | — |

## BDP 2023-2028 Alignment

| BDP Chapter | Topic | Alignment |
|-------------|-------|-----------|
| Chapter 7 | Economic Development | Identifies barter trade as a traditional economic activity to be regularized and expanded within the BIMP-EAGA framework; promotes cross-border trade facilitation |
| Chapter 13 | Plan Financing and Fiscal Strategy | International economic cooperation including trade linkages as revenue source |

## Legislative Gaps

| Gap Type | Description | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Type A — No BAA** | No dedicated BAA regulates barter trade and countertrade despite BOL Art. XIII, Sec. 26 providing detailed mandate | Enact a Bangsamoro Barter Trade and Countertrade Code establishing registration, permitted goods, trading posts, and enforcement mechanisms |
| **Type A — No BAA** | No Bangsamoro legislation establishes barter trade posts or customs cooperation protocols per Art. XIII, Sec. 28 | Enact legislation operationalizing customs/immigration/quarantine cooperation for barter trade zones |
| **Type B — Partial** | BAA 13 (Administrative Code) establishes MTIT but does not detail barter trade regulatory functions | Amend MTIT mandate or enact standalone legislation to specify barter trade regulatory authority |
