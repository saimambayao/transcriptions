**Verdict: PASS**

BAA 84 source confirms Sec. 30(b): *"Debt service for interest payments on authorized foreign and domestic loans: Provided, That appropriations for debt servicing shall not exceed twenty percent (20%) of the regular sources of revenue...and shall not exceed twenty percent (20%) of the block grant."*

### BAA 84, Sec. 63(1) — Contingent liabilities and borrowing programs [UNVERIFIED]
**Verdict: FAIL — Wrong section number**

The document cites "Sec. 63(1)" for "management of contingent liabilities and evaluation of borrowing programs." The actual text is in **Sec. 64(f)(1)**: *"Fiscal planning and programming, such as the management of contingent liabilities and the evaluation and approval of borrowing programs, investment and financing plans, and capital outlays."* Sec. 63 is "Performance Evaluation" and contains no reference to contingent liabilities or borrowing programs. The [UNVERIFIED] tag was correctly applied, but the section is wrong.

**Fix required**: Change "Sec. 63(1) [UNVERIFIED]" to "Sec. 64(f)(1)" and remove the [UNVERIFIED] tag.
