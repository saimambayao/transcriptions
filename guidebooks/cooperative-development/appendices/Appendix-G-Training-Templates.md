# Appendix G — Training Templates

This appendix provides templates for planning, conducting, and reporting on training activities within your cooperative. All training should be aligned with RA 9520 requirements, CDA guidelines, and the BDP 2023-2028 capacity-building goals. The Education and Training Fund (ETF) finances these activities.

---

### Important Reminders on ETF Usage

1. **Allowed uses of ETF:** Member education, officer training, employee development, public information campaigns about cooperative nature and benefits.
2. **Prohibited uses:** Personal expenses of officers, entertainment, purchase of equipment not directly related to training.
3. **Documentation required:** Official receipts, attendance sheets, training reports, and certificates for every ETF expenditure.
4. **Reporting:** Include ETF utilization in the CAPR and Treasurer's report. The Audit Committee should verify ETF usage.
5. **Federation contribution:** The 50% ETF allocation for the federation/union must be remitted within the period specified in your federation agreement.
6. **CDA-accredited providers only** for mandatory officer training. Other training may use non-accredited providers but document the provider's qualifications.
