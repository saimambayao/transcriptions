# Appendix C -- Registration Documentary Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure your registration application is complete before filing with CSEA. Every missing document, incorrect format, or inconsistency will cause delays. Go through each item line by line. Do not file until every box is checked.

**Filing location for BARMM cooperatives:** CSEA Provincial Office where your cooperative's principal office is located.

**Filing location for cooperatives outside BARMM:** CDA Extension Office where your cooperative's principal office is located.

---

## Part 1: Pre-Formation Requirements

- [ ] **Minimum 15 cooperators identified** -- All must be natural persons, Filipino citizens, of legal age, with capacity to contract
  - *Common rejection reason: Including minors or non-Filipino citizens in the list of cooperators*

- [ ] **Common bond established** -- One type only: Residential, Institutional, Associational, or Occupational
  - *Common rejection reason: Claiming multiple common bonds or stating a bond that is too broad (e.g., "all residents of BARMM")*

- [ ] **Organizing meetings conducted** -- Minutes of at least one organizing meeting documented

---

## Part 2: Name Reservation

- [ ] **Cooperative Name Reservation Report Form (CNRRF)** submitted to CSEA/CDA
  - Format: Official CSEA/CDA form
  - Fee paid: PHP 100 (30 days) / PHP 200 (60 days) / PHP 300 (90 days)

- [ ] **Name Reservation Slip/Notice received** -- 1 original copy
  - *Common rejection reason: Filing after the reservation period has expired. Check the expiry date and file well before it lapses.*
  - *Common rejection reason: Cooperative name on the reservation slip does not exactly match the name on the AOC and Bylaws*

- [ ] **Name includes "Cooperative"** -- The word "Cooperative" or its abbreviation must appear in the name
  - *Common rejection reason: Omitting the word "Cooperative" from the name*

- [ ] **Name is not confusingly similar** to any existing registered cooperative
  - *Common rejection reason: Using a name nearly identical to an already-registered cooperative in the same area*

---

## Part 3: Pre-Registration Seminar (PRS)

- [ ] **PRS completed by all cooperators** -- Conducted by a CSEA/CDA-accredited training provider
  - Duration: 4-8 hours minimum
  - *Common rejection reason: PRS certificates from a non-accredited provider*

- [ ] **PRS Certificates collected** -- 1 original certificate per cooperator
  - Certificates must bear the names exactly as they appear on the cooperators' valid IDs
  - *Common rejection reason: Name on PRS certificate does not match name on AOC*

---

## Part 4: Articles of Cooperation (AOC)

- [ ] **4 original copies prepared** -- No photocopies accepted
  - Paper size: 8.5" x 13" (legal) or 8.5" x 14" (long)
  - Font: Bookman Old Style, size 12
  - *Common rejection reason: Submitting photocopies instead of 4 sets with original signatures*

- [ ] **Cooperative name consistent** across all pages and with the Name Reservation Slip

- [ ] **Cooperative type specified** in the preamble (Multi-Purpose, Credit, Agriculture, Producer, Consumer, Worker, etc.)

- [ ] **Article II -- Purposes** are specific to the cooperative type
  - *Common rejection reason: Using generic or vague purposes that do not describe actual business activities*

- [ ] **Article V -- Term of existence** stated (maximum 50 years)

- [ ] **Article VI -- Common bond** matches the field of membership
  - Only ONE common bond type is stated
  - Field of membership is specific and delimited
  - *Common rejection reason: Common bond says "Residential" but field of membership describes an occupational group*

- [ ] **Article VII -- Area of operation** is consistent with common bond
  - Principal office address is complete (number, street, barangay, city/municipality, province, zip code)
  - Principal office is WITHIN the area of operation
  - *Common rejection reason: Area of operation says "Marawi City" but principal office address is in Iligan City*

- [ ] **Article VIII -- List of cooperators** contains at least 15 names with complete postal addresses
  - *Common rejection reason: Incomplete addresses (missing barangay or zip code)*

- [ ] **Article IX -- Board of Directors** is an odd number between 5 and 15
  - All directors are listed by name
  - All directors appear in the cooperators list (Article VIII)

- [ ] **Article X -- Capitalization** figures are internally consistent
  - Par value per share does not exceed PHP 1,000.00
  - Par value is divisible by PHP 1.00
  - *Common rejection reason: Par value of PHP 1,500 (exceeds maximum)*

- [ ] **Article XI -- Subscribed and paid-up capital** figures are correct
  - Subscribed capital is at least 25% of authorized capital
  - Paid-up capital is at least 25% of subscribed capital
  - Paid-up capital meets minimum floor: PHP 15,000 (primary) or PHP 100,000 (multi-purpose)
  - All cooperators appear in the subscriber table
  - Sub-totals and grand total are arithmetically correct
  - *Common rejection reason: Math errors in the capital subscription table*
  - *Common rejection reason: Paid-up capital below the minimum floor*

- [ ] **Article XII -- Arbitral clause** is included (standard text, do not modify)

- [ ] **Treasurer designation clause** is included with the elected Treasurer's full name

- [ ] **All cooperators signed on every page** (margin signatures on each page)
  - *Common rejection reason: Missing margin signatures on one or more pages*

- [ ] **Two witnesses signed** who are NOT cooperators/members of the cooperative

- [ ] **AOC is notarized** with complete acknowledgment section
  - All cooperators' IDs are listed in the acknowledgment table
  - IDs are current and valid (not expired)
  - *Common rejection reason: Expired government ID listed in the acknowledgment*

- [ ] **No metallic fasteners used** (no staples, paper clips, or metal binders)
  - *Common rejection reason: Documents fastened with staple wires*

- [ ] **Each of the 4 copies is in a separate folder**

---

## Part 5: Bylaws

- [ ] **4 original copies prepared** -- Same paper and font specifications as AOC

- [ ] **Cooperative name is consistent** with the AOC and Name Reservation Slip

- [ ] **Membership qualifications** are consistent with the common bond in AOC Article VI

- [ ] **Number of Board of Directors** is consistent with AOC Article IX

- [ ] **Capitalization details** are consistent with AOC Articles X and XI

- [ ] **Statutory fund allocation percentages** meet minimum requirements
  - Reserve Fund: at least 10% (50% in first 5 years)
  - Education and Training Fund: up to 10%
  - Community Development Fund: at least 3%
  - *Common rejection reason: CDF allocation below 3%*

- [ ] **Quorum requirement** is at least 25% for General Assembly meetings

- [ ] **All required committees** are provided for: Audit, Election, Education and Training, Mediation and Conciliation, Ethics

- [ ] **All fill-in blanks are completed** (no empty fields marked with underscores)
  - *Common rejection reason: Submitting bylaws with unfilled blanks*

- [ ] **At least a majority of members signed** the adoption page
  - For 15 cooperators, at least 8 must sign
  - *Common rejection reason: Fewer than majority signatures on the adoption page*

- [ ] **Board of Directors Certification** is signed by the majority of the Board

- [ ] **Shari'ah provisions included** *(if applicable for Bangsamoro cooperatives)*
  - Shari'ah Advisory Committee establishment clause
  - Riba prohibition clause
  - Halal business activities requirement
  - Moral Governance Code

---

## Part 6: Economic Survey

- [ ] **4 original copies prepared**

- [ ] **General Information section** completed (proposed name, type, principal address, area of operation)

- [ ] **Membership Profile** completed (number of prospective members, common bond, economic profile)

- [ ] **Market Analysis** completed (products/services, target market, competition, pricing)

- [ ] **Financial Projections** completed (initial capital requirements, sources of funds, projected income/expenses, break-even analysis)

- [ ] **Organizational Plan** completed (management structure, staffing, facilities, equipment)

- [ ] **Signed by the organizing committee**

---

## Part 7: Treasurer's Affidavit

- [ ] **1 original copy prepared** -- Notarized

- [ ] **Treasurer's full name** is consistent with the AOC Treasurer designation clause

- [ ] **Capitalization figures** are consistent with AOC Article XI
  - Total authorized capital matches
  - Total subscribed capital matches
  - Total paid-up capital matches
  - Total membership fees received are stated
  - *Common rejection reason: Capital figures in the Treasurer's Affidavit do not match the AOC*

- [ ] **Notarized** with the Treasurer's valid government ID

---

## Part 8: Surety Bond

- [ ] **Surety bond secured** for the Treasurer

- [ ] **Surety bond secured** for all other officers handling funds or property

- [ ] **Bond amounts are adequate** based on the amount of funds/property to be handled

- [ ] **Bonds are current** and will not expire before the cooperative begins operations

---

## Part 9: Registration Fee

- [ ] **Registration fee prepared** per current CSEA/CDA fee schedule
  - Confirm the exact amount with the CSEA Provincial Office before filing

---

## Part 10: Final Assembly Check

Before submitting, verify the following cross-document consistency:

- [ ] **Cooperative name** is exactly the same on: Name Reservation Slip, AOC, Bylaws, Treasurer's Affidavit, Economic Survey

- [ ] **Cooperators list** is exactly the same on: AOC Article VIII, AOC Article XI (subscriber table), AOC signature page, AOC acknowledgment, PRS certificates

- [ ] **Capital figures** are exactly the same on: AOC Article X, AOC Article XI, Treasurer's Affidavit, Economic Survey

- [ ] **Board of Directors count** is exactly the same on: AOC Article IX, Bylaws Article IV Section 1

- [ ] **Common bond and field of membership** are consistent on: AOC Article VI, Bylaws Article II Section 2

- [ ] **Area of operation and principal office** are consistent on: AOC Article VII, Economic Survey

- [ ] **All 4 copies of each document** are in separate folders with non-metallic fasteners

- [ ] **Filing receipt obtained** with the date of filing noted (for tracking the 60-day processing period)

---

## Quick Reference: Common Reasons for Registration Denial

| Reason | How to Avoid |
|--------|-------------|
| Incomplete documentary requirements | Use this checklist -- do not file until every box is checked |
| Inconsistent cooperative name across documents | Copy the name exactly from the Name Reservation Slip |
| Capital figures do not match across AOC and Treasurer's Affidavit | Have one person compute all figures, then have another person verify |
| Common bond not aligned with field of membership | Choose ONE common bond type and ensure the field of membership matches |
| Principal office outside area of operation | Verify the address is within the stated area of operation |
| Missing margin signatures on AOC pages | Have all cooperators sign every page before notarization |
| Photocopies instead of originals | Prepare 4 sets with original signatures from the start |
| PRS certificates from non-accredited provider | Verify the training provider's CDA/CSEA accreditation before attending |
| Expired name reservation | File well before the reservation period expires; renew if needed |
| Use of metallic fasteners | Use plastic or paper fasteners only |
| Unfilled blanks in the Bylaws | Review every page for [___] fields and complete all of them |
| Math errors in capital subscription table | Double-check all arithmetic; have a second person verify totals |
