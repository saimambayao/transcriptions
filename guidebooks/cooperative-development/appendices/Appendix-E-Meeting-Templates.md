### Common Resolution Subjects — Reference List

The following are common subjects for cooperative resolutions. Use the template above for each.

**General Assembly Resolutions:**

| Subject | Voting Requirement |
|---------|--------------------|
| Approval of Audited Financial Statements | Majority of members present |
| Approval of Net Surplus Distribution | Majority of members present |
| Approval of Annual Budget | Majority of members present |
| Approval of Development Plan | Majority of members present |
| Amendment to Articles of Cooperation | Two-thirds (2/3) of members present |
| Amendment to Bylaws | Two-thirds (2/3) of members present |
| Approval of Merger or Consolidation | Two-thirds (2/3) of all members |
| Voluntary Dissolution | Three-fourths (3/4) of all members |
| Removal of Director or Officer for Cause | Two-thirds (2/3) of members present |
| Delegation of Powers to the Board | Three-fourths (3/4) of all members |

**Board of Directors Resolutions:**

| Subject | Voting Requirement |
|---------|--------------------|
| Appointment of General Manager | Majority of quorum |
| Appointment of Secretary / Treasurer | Majority of quorum |
| Approval of Loan Applications (major) | Majority of quorum |
| Opening / Closing of Bank Accounts | Majority of quorum |
| Authorization to Enter Contracts | Majority of quorum |
| Approval of Policies | Majority of quorum |
| Creation of Special Committees | Majority of quorum |
| Membership Approval / Termination | Majority of quorum |

*These templates are based on RA 9520 (Philippine Cooperative Code of 2008), CDA guidelines, and standard cooperative governance practice. Adapt provisions as needed to align with your cooperative's bylaws and any applicable CSEA-BARMM requirements.*
