# Appendix F — Financial Management Templates

This appendix provides financial management templates designed for small to medium cooperatives in BARMM. These templates follow Philippine Financial Reporting Standards for Cooperatives and CDA requirements. Adapt account names and line items to match your cooperative's specific business activities.

---

## F.1 Simple Chart of Accounts for a Small Cooperative

The chart of accounts is the backbone of your bookkeeping system. Every transaction is recorded under one of these accounts. The numbering system below follows standard cooperative accounting practice.

---

**Note on Shari'ah Compliance:** For cooperatives operating under Shari'ah-compliant models, replace "Interest Income — Loans" (4030) with "Profit Income — Murabaha" or "Profit Share — Mudarabah/Musharakah" as applicable. Replace "Interest Expense" (6120) with "Profit Distribution — Deposits" or the appropriate Islamic finance equivalent. The account structure remains the same; only the labels and underlying transaction nature change.
