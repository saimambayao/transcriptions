# Appendices

The following appendices provide ready-to-use templates, checklists, and reference cards. Print them, photocopy them, and distribute them to your cooperative's officers and members. Every template has been adapted for Bangsamoro cooperatives -- they include Shari'ah provisions where relevant and reference CSEA rather than CDA where appropriate.

---

**Appendix A -- Articles of Cooperation Templates by Type**
Complete multi-purpose AOC template with fill-in fields, plus guidance for adapting to credit, producer, consumer, agriculture, and worker cooperatives. Use alongside Chapter 4, Section 4.2.

**Appendix B -- Bylaws Template with Shari'ah Provisions**
Full bylaws template based on the CDA model, enhanced with Shari'ah committee establishment, riba prohibition, halal business requirements, and moral governance code provisions. Use alongside Chapter 4, Section 4.3.

**Appendix C -- Registration Documentary Checklist**
Complete checklist of every document required for CSEA registration, with format specifications, copy requirements, and common rejection reasons flagged inline. Use alongside Chapter 4, Section 4.4.

**Appendix D -- Compliance Calendar and Reporting Templates**
Month-by-month calendar of every compliance deadline from January through December, covering CAPR, CAFS, COC, General Assembly, board elections, and statutory fund allocations. Includes reporting template overviews. Use alongside Chapter 9.

**Appendix E -- Sample General Assembly and Board Meeting Templates**
Agenda templates, minutes formats, resolution forms, and attendance sheets for both General Assembly meetings and regular board meetings. Use alongside Chapter 5.

**Appendix F -- Financial Management Templates**
Budget worksheet, cash flow projection template, net surplus allocation worksheet, and statutory fund tracking sheet. Use alongside Chapter 7.

**Appendix G -- Training Needs Assessment and Plan Template**
Officer training tracker, annual education plan template, and training needs assessment form for the Education and Training Committee. Use alongside Chapter 10.

**Appendix H -- Quick Reference Cards**
Single-page reference cards for cooperative principles, officer duties, statutory fund allocation rules, member rights and responsibilities, and key RA 9520 provisions. Handy references for posting in your cooperative office.
