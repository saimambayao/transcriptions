Appendix F — Legislative Research Briefer Template

This template follows the Completed Staff Work (CSW) framework described in Chapter 8. It is designed to be filled in for each legislative measure that requires analysis. Print the template, fill it in by hand or digitally, and attach supporting documents.

The template covers every section of the legislative briefer. For detailed guidance on how to complete each section, refer to the corresponding sections in Chapter 8.

Section 8 — Interpellation Questions

(Refer to Chapter 8, Section 8.4)

Purpose: Prepare the MP for interpellation with structured question sequences that expose weaknesses and build the case for amendments.

What to include: 3-7 interpellation sequences using the six-part structure (context, question, anticipated answer, manifestation if satisfactory, manifestation if evasive, proposed amendment with justification); questions ranked by importance — most critical first.

Sources: The bill as amended by committee; Committee Report; BOL provisions on legislative authority; BEP data for budget questions; agency consultations for implementation questions.

Prepare three to seven interpellation sequences. Each follows the six-part structure. Arrange in order of importance — most critical questions first.
