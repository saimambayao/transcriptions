# AI Integration Pattern for All Guidebooks

Three components, consistent across every guidebook:

---

## 1. AI Declaration (About This Guidebook section)

Placed in the About section of every guidebook, after "How to Use This Guidebook" and before "About the Author." 3-4 paragraphs. Honest, transparent, no jargon.

---

## 2. AI in Practice Callouts (inside existing chapters)

Short boxed callouts within chapters, placed where AI is most relevant to the methodology being taught. Format:

```markdown
> **AI in Practice**
>
> [1-3 sentences showing how AI applies to THIS specific step/concept]
>
> **What AI does:** [concrete action — e.g., "Searches 11,866 RAs for matching keywords"]
> **What you do:** [the human judgment — e.g., "Apply the National Law Relationship Test to determine which tier controls"]
> **Watch for:** [the risk — e.g., "AI may cite wrong BOL article numbers — verify every citation against the source"]
```

Rules:
- Maximum 5-7 callouts per guidebook — only where AI meaningfully changes the workflow
- Each callout must be self-contained — a reader skipping them loses nothing
- Never position AI as the primary method — the chapter teaches the methodology; the callout shows how AI accelerates it
- Always include "Watch for" — every callout acknowledges a specific AI limitation

---

## 3. AI Appendix (end of guidebook)

A 2-3 page appendix titled "Appendix [X]: AI-Augmented [Domain Name]" that collects:

- Summary of how AI was used in producing this guidebook (2 paragraphs)
- The AI Disclosure Template (from AI Governance Standards, Section 6)
- Reference to the full AI Governance Standards document
- List of which chapters have AI in Practice callouts and what they cover

---

## Where Each Component Lives

| Component | Location in Guidebook | Length |
|-----------|----------------------|--------|
| AI Declaration | About This Guidebook (before About the Author) | 3-4 paragraphs |
| AI in Practice callouts | Inside relevant chapters | 4-6 lines each, 5-7 per guidebook |
| AI Appendix | After last appendix | 2-3 pages |
