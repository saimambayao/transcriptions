## C. Jurisprudence

*Ali et al. v. Bangsamoro Transition Authority*, G.R. No. E-02219 (Supreme Court of the Philippines, September 30, 2025). Declaring BAA 58 (Parliamentary Districts Creation) unconstitutional.

*Macapaar et al. v. Commission on Elections*, G.R. No. E-02235 (Supreme Court of the Philippines, September 30, 2025). Declaring BAA 77 (Reconstituted Parliamentary Districts) unconstitutional.

*Province of Sulu v. Medialdea*, G.R. Nos. 242255, 243246, 243693 (Supreme Court of the Philippines, September 9, 2024; finalized November 26, 2024). Upholding the constitutionality of the Bangsamoro Organic Law but ruling that Sulu is not part of BARMM.
