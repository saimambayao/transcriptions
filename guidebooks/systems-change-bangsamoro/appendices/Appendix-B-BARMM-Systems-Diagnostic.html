BARMM Systems Diagnostic

A self-assessment checklist for determining where your sector sits in the adaptive cycle
How to Use This Diagnostic: Work through Section 1 by checking all indicators that apply to your sector. The phase with the most checked items is likely your current phase. Then complete Section 2 for a deeper sector-specific assessment, and Section 3 to analyze cross-sector alignment. This diagnostic works best when completed by a team of 3-5 people with diverse roles in the sector.

BARMM Context

The Bangsamoro transition from ARMM represents a system-wide Release-to-Reorganization shift, but different sectors are moving through the adaptive cycle at different speeds. Governance structures may still be in Reorganization while the economy remains in a pattern inherited from the Conservation phase of ARMM. Understanding these differences is critical for sequencing interventions effectively.
