Interpretation

120-150
Strong Systems Leader — You are ready to lead systems change initiatives. Share your capabilities by mentoring others and championing cross-sector collaboration.
96-119
Developing — You have a strong foundation. Focus on your 2-3 weakest areas through deliberate practice, reading, and peer learning.
72-95
Emerging — Significant growth is needed. Seek a mentor who scores in the "Strong" range. Prioritize Sections 1 and 3 for development.
Below 72
Beginning — Start with Chapter 5 (Futuring) of the guidebook and build your understanding of systems thinking before taking on change leadership roles.
Important Note

Your score reflects your current capacity, not your potential. All four score bands represent valid starting points. The Bangsamoro transition needs change agents at every level — the most important thing is knowing where you are and committing to grow.

Action Planning

Use your assessment results to create a focused development plan. Concentrate on a few areas rather than trying to improve everything at once.
