## Critical Finding: BOL Article Structure Error

The most pervasive error across chapters was the **incorrect BOL article numbering**. The chapters (and the bol-key-provisions.md reference file) treated the BOL as having 13 articles. The actual BOL has **18 articles** (I-XVIII):

| Correct Article | Subject | What chapters said |
|---|---|---|
| Art. VIII | **Wali** | Was lumped with Art. VII |
| Art. XIII | **Development Planning** | Was labeled "Transitory Provisions" |
| Art. XIV | **Rehabilitation and Development (SDF)** | Not listed |
| Art. XV | **Plebiscite** | Not listed |
| Art. XVI | **BTA / Transition** | Was labeled Art. VIII |
| Art. XVII | **Amendments** | Not listed |
| Art. XVIII | **Transitory Provisions** | Was Art. XIII |

**Root cause**: The bol-key-provisions.md reference file has an incomplete article structure table. The fact-check-error-log.md also incorrectly states "The BOL has 13 articles (I-XIII)." Both reference files need updating.

**Section-level errors caused by this**:
- Annual Block Grant cited as Art. XII, Sec. 9 (actual: Art. XII, Sec. 15-16)
- SDF cited as Art. XII, Sec. 10 (actual: Art. XIV, Sec. 2)
- Right to education cited as Art. IX, Sec. 14 (actual: Art. IX, Sec. 16)
- Transition cited as Art. VIII (actual: Art. XVI)

All section-level errors have been fixed in the chapter files.
