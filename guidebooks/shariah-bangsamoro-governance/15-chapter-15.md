# Chapter 15 — Sector Directory: All 55 Powers Mapped

This chapter is a reference tool. It maps every one of the 55 BOL powers to its Shari'ah dimension so you can look up any power and immediately know: how strong is the Shari'ah connection, which principles apply, where in this guidebook it is covered, and whether the power needs standalone Shari'ah legislation or values-based mainstreaming.

Use this directory when you encounter a power not covered in depth in Chapters 11-14, or when you need a quick overview before starting research on any legislative subject.

---

## 15.1 The Shari'ah Integration Spectrum

Every BOL power falls somewhere on this spectrum:

| Level | Meaning | Model | Example |
|-------|---------|-------|---------|
| **Mandatory** | Shari'ah compliance is definitional — the power *is* an Islamic function | Full Shari'ah review | Hajj, Islamic banking, Shari'ah courts |
| **Strong** | Enacted legislation already includes Shari'ah provisions | Follow enacted law | Labor (BAA 82), IDP protection (BAA 62) |
| **Significant** | BOL expressly mandates Shari'ah-informed legislation | Dual-track or direct mandate | Education (Madaris), halal, revenue (*zakat*) |
| **Moderate** | Shari'ah principles meaningfully inform policy design | Principled integration | Health, environment, cooperatives, housing |
| **Minimal** | Shari'ah values apply but no specific compliance needed | Values in preamble | Civil service, DRR, public works, business registration |
