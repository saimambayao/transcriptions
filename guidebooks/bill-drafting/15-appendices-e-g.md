# Appendix E — Quality Review Checklist (Detachable)

This checklist is designed to be printed and used after completing a bill draft, before submitting it for review. Work through every item. If you check "No" or cannot answer confidently, stop and fix the problem before proceeding.

The checklist is the companion to Appendix D (Pre-Drafting Checklist). Appendix D helps you prepare before writing. This appendix helps you verify after writing.

Print this. Keep a copy on your desk. Use it every time.
