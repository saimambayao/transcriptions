## Biosketch

**Biosketch of MP Akmad I. Abas**

MP Akmad I. Abas is the commander of the Eastern Mindanao Front of the Bangsamoro Islamic Armed Forces, the Military wing of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front, he is also member of the Board of Directors of the Independent Decommissioning Body (IDB) as MILF representative. The mechanism mandated by the Government of the Philippines (GPH) and the Moro Islamic Liberation Front to oversee the process of decommissioning of the MILF forces and weapons in accordance to the Comprehensive Agreement on the Bangsamoro (CAB). He took his college level of education in Central Mindanao College in Kidapawan City. He is a member of the first Bangsamoro Transition Authority appointed by former President Rodrigo Roa Duterte and reappointed member of this current Bangsamoro Transition Authority by President Ferdinand Romualdez Marcos Jr.
