## Biosketch

**Biosketch of MP Lanang T. Ali, Jr.**

Born and raised in Sultan Kudarat, Maguindanao, Ali followed the footstep of his father Atty. Lanang S. Ali, Sr. who was the Legal Counsel of Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF).

He obtained his Bachelor of Arts Major in Political Science as well as his College of Law (LLB) degree at Lyceum of the Philippines University.  
He was the former Chief Legal Officer of the Bangsamoro Transition Commission (BTC) that drafted the then Bangsamoro Basic Law (BBL) now Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL). In line with the GPH- MILF Peace Process, he is also the legal counsel of the MILF Peace Implementing Panel.
