## Biosketch

**Biosketch of MP Mosber E. Alauddin**

Before becoming a Member of the Parliament, Mr. Mosber E. Alauddin has served as Chairman of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) Basilan Provincial Political Committee from January of 2019 up to Present. He was then the Vice-Chairman of the same organization from 2006 to 2019 but was the Acting Chairman at the same time since he was the one representing the Provincial Political Committee in meetings conducted by the Central Committee.

MP Alauddin has helped the Province of Basilan from prioritizing infrastructure developments to standardizing the educational institutions. He has also assisted several individuals pursuing the secular education in the different Islamic Countries worldwide.

He has also experience in the government service, serving as Legislative Officer II under the Office of the Sangguniang Bayan (SB) in the Municipality of Tipo-Tipo from 1993-1994. Although he was in the government from 1988-1994, he was already supporting the MILF Organization in different ways and means.
