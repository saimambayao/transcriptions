## Biosketch

**Biosketch of MP Ahod Balawag Ebrahim**

– Member of Top 300, 2nd Batch

-Zone Commander for the then unified MNLF

-Military Chairman of the MNLF for the entire Kutawato Region

-Chairman, Ad Hoc Coordinating Committee

-Vice Chairman for Military Affairs, MILF

-Chief of Staff, Bangsamoro Islamic Armed Forces

-Envoy of the late Salamat Hashim (allaho yarhamu) to top level engagements with Muslim World League and Organization of the Islamic Conference

-Chairman, MILF Peace Panel negotiating team (2001-2003)

-Chairman, Moro Islamic Liberation Front
