> - In Dr. Alday v. Judge Cruz, Jr.,9 the Court dismissed the respondent
> Judge Escolastico U. Cruz, Jr. from the service for his deliberate refusal to
> obey our Order dated March 14, 2001, suspending him from the service. In so
> ruling, the Court gave no credence to his explanation that he was impelled "by
