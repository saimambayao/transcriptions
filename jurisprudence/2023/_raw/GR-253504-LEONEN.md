> In my view, there was no reasonable suspicion or genuine reason for
> the police officers to conduct a stop and frisk search leading to the seizure of
> the firearm and ammunition from Roel Pablo y Pascual (Pablo). Because of
> the illegal warrantless search, the articles allegedly confiscated from Pablo
> are inadmissible as evidence against him. His acquittal should follow.
