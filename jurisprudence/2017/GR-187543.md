> **JARDELEZA, *J.:***
>
> These are consolidated petitions1 seeking to nullify the Court of Appeals' (CA) February 9, 2009 Decision2 and April 16, 2009 Resolution3 in CA-G.R. SP No. 105013. The CA modified the August 11, 2008 Decision4 of the Construction Industry Arbitration Commission (CIAC) in CIAC Case No. 09-2008, *viz.:*
>
> **> WHEREFORE,**premises considered, the instant petition for review is **PARTLY GRANTED.** The assailed Decision dated August 11, 2008 of the Construction Industry Arbitration Commission in CIAC Case No. 09-2008 is hereby **MODIFIED** as follows:
>
> 1) Respondent Werr Corporation International shall pay petitioner Highlands Prime, Inc. liquidated damages in the amount of ₱8,969,330.70;
>
> 2) Petitioner Highlands Prime, Inc. shall return to respondent Werr Corporation International the balance of its retention money in the amount of ₱10,955,899.80 with the right to offset the award for liquidated damages in the aforesaid amount of ₱8,969,330.70; and
>
> 3) The cost of arbitration shall be shared equally by the parties.
>
> The rest of the decision stands.
>
> **SO ORDERED.**5
