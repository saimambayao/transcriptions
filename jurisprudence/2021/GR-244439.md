> Tt is a well-settled principle that issues of fact and arguments not adequately brought to the attention of the lower courts will not he considered by the reviewing courts as they cannot be raised for the [irst time on appeal. Pomnis of law, theories, issues, and arguments not brought to the attention of the trial court are barred by cstoppcl and cannoi be considered by a reviewing court, as these cannot be ratsed for the frst ttme on appeal.
