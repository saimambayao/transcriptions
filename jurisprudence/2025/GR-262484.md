> Penalty
> The Court upholds the penalty imposed.
> This finds basis in Section (I)(A) of SEC MC No.08-09, which provides
> that for material misstatements, the fine imposed shall be an amount based on
> the scale or 1/10 of 1% of the amount of misstatement, whichever is higher,
> in relation to Section 54(a)(ii) of the SRC, which in turn imposes a cap on
> imposable fines. Therefore, for the misstatements in the 2008 and 2009 AFS,
> the SEC En Banc was correct in ruling that the total imposable fine shall be
> PHP 2 million:
> [A ]fter re-evalution, the OGA confirmed that the penalty should be based

> Commission's scale of fines, the penalty should be 1/10 of 1% of the

> Conclusion
