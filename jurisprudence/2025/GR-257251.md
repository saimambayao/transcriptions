> At the outset, the respondents' failure to file their comment, despite the

> A cursory reading of the Petition reveals that the issues raised are
> essentially questions of fact. The Rules of Court requires that only questions
> of law should be raised in Rule 45 petitions, since factual questions are not
> the proper subject of an appeal by certiorari. It is not this Court's function to
