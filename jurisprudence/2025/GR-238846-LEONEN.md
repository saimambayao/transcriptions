> The phrase "including all taxes paid by or on behalf of the Contractor'' means "additionally" or "in addition to," such that both the annual net revenue or government's share in petroleum production and all taxes paid would be taken into account in meeting the minimum threshold of 60%. It comes from an understanding that the "taxes paid" are part oft he larger or broader group ofr evenues to the government, and not necessarily of the "annual net revenue or share of the government in petroleum production." Otherwise, had the latter been the intent, a clearer phrasing should have been used such as "which includes," "inclusive of," or any language clearly and unequivocally indicative of such intent.
