> In general, the qualifications for y person to vie for President of the Republic of the Philippines is limited tot ose enumerated in Section 2, Article VII of the Constitution. It will, in the future, allow a socialist, a uni n leader, an activist that had already been convicted of illegal possession of fi earms during martial law, or even a former government employee who may have been wrongly convicted by a final judgment of failure to file an incom tax return-even when taxes were withheld from his or her monthly compe sation- to run for President.

It is submitted to the Commission on Elections and is not required to be publis ed.
