> ***PER CURIAM:***
>
> Before this Court is an administrative matter concerning officers of the Office of the Clerk of Court (OCC) of the Regional Trial Court (RTC), Davao City, Davao Del Sur, namely: Edipolo P. Sarabia, Jr. (Clerk of Court VI), Haydee B. Salazar (Cash Clerk III), Marifi A. Oquindo (Clerk III), Aimee May D. Agbayani (Clerk III), and Orlando A. Marquez (Clerk III).
>
> In a Memorandum dated April 30, 2010 from Deputy Court Administrator Thelma C. Bahia, an immediate financial audit on the books of accounts of the OCC, RTC, Davao City was ordered due to the continued failure of Clerk of Court VI Edipolo P. Sarabia, Jr. (Sarabia) to submit his monthly financial report of collections, deposits and withdrawals despite notice from the OCA's Financial Management Office's (FMO) Accounting Division.
>
> Pursuant to Travel Order1 No. 83-2015 dated August 7, 2015 issued by the Office of the Court Administrator (OCA), a financial audit was conducted by a team led by Mr. Romulo E. Tamanu, Jr. in the Office of the Clerk of Court OCC, RTC, Davao City.
>
