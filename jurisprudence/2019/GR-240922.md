> **D E C I S I O N**
>
> INTING, *J.:*
>
> Before Us is an appeal filed by Patricio Honasan *y* Grafil (appellant) assailing the Decision 1 dated February 22, 2018 of the Court of Appeals (CA) in CA-G.R. CR-HC No. 09014 which affirmed with modification the Decision2 dated December 9, 2016 of the Regional Trial Court (RTC) of Sorsogon City, Branch 65, in Criminal Case Nos. 10- 1193 and 10-1195 convicting him of violation of Sections 5 and 11 of Article II of Republic Act (R.A.) No. 9165, otherwise known as the Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act of 2002, as amended.
>
> Appellant was charged in two separate Informations with violation of Sections 5 and 11 of R.A. No. 9 I 65 on August 23, 2010, to wit:
>
> In Criminal Case No. 10-1193:
>
> "That on or about 12:30 o'clock in the early morning of July 28, 2010 at Barangay Zone 8, Municipality of Bulan, Province or Sorsogon, Philippines, and within the jurisdiction of this Honorable Court, the above-named accused, without authority of law, did then and there, willfully, unlawfully and feloniously have in his possession and control one (1) heat-sealed transparent plastic sachet containing rnetharnphetamine hydrochloride, with a total weight of 0.019 gram, a prohibited drug, to the damage and prejudice of the state and the general public.
>
> CONTRARY TO LAW."3
