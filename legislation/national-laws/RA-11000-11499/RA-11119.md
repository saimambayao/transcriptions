# Republic Act No. 11119

**AN ACT DECLARING APRIL 3 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ALBAY, INCLUDING THE CITIES THEREIN, IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY, TO BE KNOWN AS "ALBAY DAY**

> Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session

> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.

> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11119**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING APRIL 3 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ALBAY, INCLUDING THE CITIES THEREIN, IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY, TO BE KNOWN AS "ALBAY DAY
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** April 3 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Albay, including the cities therein, in commemoration of its founding anniversary, to be known as "Albay Day".
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.1âwphi1
