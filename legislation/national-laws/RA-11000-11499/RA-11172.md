# Republic Act No. 11172

**An Act Changing the Name of Inoyonan High School in Barangay Inoyonan, Municipality of Bula, Province of Camarines Sur to Felipe P. Panton High School**

- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2019/ra_11172_2019.html)

> **Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session**
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11172**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Inoyonan High School in Barangay Inoyonan, Municipality of Bula, Province of Camarines Sur to Felipe P. Panton High School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** - The name of Inoyonan High School in Barangay Inoyonan, Municipality of Bula, Province of Camarines Sur is hereby changed to Felipe P. Panton High School.
>
> **Section 2.** - The main campus of the Felipe P. Panton High School shall continue to remain in its location in Barangay Inoyonan, Municipality of Bula, Province of Camarines Sur.
>
> **Section 3.** - This act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in any newspaper of general circulation.1âwphi1
