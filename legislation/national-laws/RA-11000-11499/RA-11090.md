AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11090**
>
> AN ACT SEPARATING THE IGBARAS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL - ALAMEDA EXTENSION IN BARANGAY ALAMEDA, MUNICIPALITY OF IGBARAS, PROVINCE OF ILOILO FROM THE IGBARAS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, CONVERTING IT INTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS ALAMEDA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL. AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Igbaras National High School Alameda Extension in Barangay Alameda, Municipality of Igbaras, Province of Iloilo is hereby separated from the Igbaras National High School and converted into an independent national high school to be known as Alameda National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records of the Igbaras National High School - Alameda Extension are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Alameda National High School.
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the Alameda National High School, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current year's appropriation of the Igbaras National High School - Alameda Extension. Thereafter, the amount necessary for the continued operation of the school shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 4.** The Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act within one hundred twenty (120) days after its effectivity.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.
