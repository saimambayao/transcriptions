> Eighteenth Congress
> First Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, two thousand nineteen.
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11489, August 14, 2020 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CREATING A DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE IN THE SEVENTH LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT OF THE PROVINCE OF CEBU, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** A district engineering office is hereby created in the Seventh Legislative District of the Province of Cebu, to be located in the Municipality of Dumanjug and referred to as the Seventh District Engineering Office.
>
> **Section 2.** The Seventh District Engineering Office shall serve the municipalities of Dumanjug, Ronda, Alcantara, Moalboal, Badian, Alegria, Malabuyoc and Ginatilan, all located in the Province of Cebu.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of Public Works and Highways shall include in the Departrment's program the operationalization of the Seventh District Engineering Office in the Province of Cebu, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
