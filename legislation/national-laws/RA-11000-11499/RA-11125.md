# Republic Act No. 11125

**AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 17 OF EVERYEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 11125
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2018/ra_11125_2018.html)

> Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11125**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 17 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** June 17 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Agusan del Sur in commemoration of its founding anniversary.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
