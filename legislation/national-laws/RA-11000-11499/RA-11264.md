> **Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session**
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.

> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11264**
>
> An Act Establishing a National High School in Barangay Francisco Homes-Mulawin, City of San Jose del Monte, Province of Bulacan to Be Known as Mulawin National High School and Appropriating Funds Therefor
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established, under the supervision of the Department of Education, a national high school in Barangay Francisco Homes-Mulawin, City of San Jose del Monte, Province of Bulacan, to be known as Mulawin National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Departments program the operationalization of the Mulawin National High School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations implementing the provisions of this Act.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
