**An Act Declaring January 17 of Every Year As James Leonard Tagle Gordon Day, a Special Nonworking Holiday in the City of Olongapo and in the Subic Bay Freeport Zone, in Recognition of the Achievements, Contributions and Heroism of James Leonard Tagle Gordon**

> **Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session**
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11217**
>
> An Act Declaring January 17 of Every Year As James Leonard Tagle Gordon Day, a Special Nonworking Holiday in the City of Olongapo and in the Subic Bay Freeport Zone, in Recognition of the Achievements, Contributions and Heroism of James Leonard Tagle Gordon
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** January 17 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the City of Olongapo, Province of Zambales, and in the Subic Bay Freeport Zone to be known as the ***"James Leonard Tagle Gordon Day",*** in recognition of the achievements, contributions and heroism of James Leonard Tagle Gordon.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.1âwphi1
