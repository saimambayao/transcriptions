> **Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session**
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11437**
>
> **An Act Establishing a Regular District Office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Ronda in the 7th District of the Province of Cebu and Appropriating Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established a regular district office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) to be located in the Municipality of Ronda in the 7th District of the Province of Cebu.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately include in the Departments program the operationalization of the LTO Regular District Office in the Municipality of Ronda in the 7th District of the Province of Cebu, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.1âшphi1
