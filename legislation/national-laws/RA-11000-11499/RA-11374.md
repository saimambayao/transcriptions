> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11374**
>
> **An Act Declaring April 5 of Every Year a Special Working Holiday in the Province of Pangasinan, in Commemoration of its Founding Anniversary, to be Known as The "Pangasinan Day"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** April 5 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the Province of Pangasinan, in commemoration of its founding anniversary, to be known as the "Pangasinan Day". Upon proclamation of the President, April 5 of every year shall be a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Pangasinan.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.
