# Republic Act No. 11209

**An Act Changing the Name of Lupon School of Fisheries in Lupon, Davao Oriental, Into the Davao Oriental Polytechnic Institute**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 11209
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2019/ra_11209_2019.html)

---

> **Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session**
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11209**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Lupon School of Fisheries in Lupon, Davao Oriental, Into the Davao Oriental Polytechnic Institute
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section 1 of Batas Pambansa Blg. 298 is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Sec. 1. There shall be established a school of fisheries in the Municipality of Lupon, Province of Davao Oriental, to be known as the Davao Oriental Polytechnic Institute."
>
> **Section 2.** All laws, decrees, executive orders, rules, regulations or parts thereof inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
> Approved,
