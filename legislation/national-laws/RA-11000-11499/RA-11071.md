> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11071**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE MAMAGUM ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MAMAGUM, MUNICIPALITY OF SULTAN NAGA DIMAPORO. PRO\TNCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS MAMAGUM INTEGRATED SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Mamagum Elementary School in Barangay Mamagum, Municipality of Sultan Naga Dimaporo, Province of Lanao del Norte is hereby converted into an integrated school to be known as Mamagum Integrated School.
>
> **Section 2.** All personnel, assets, liabihties, and records of the Mamagum Elementary School are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Mamagum Integrated School.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Departments program the operationalization of the Mamagum Integrated School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 4.** The Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations implementing the provisions of this Act within one hundred twenty (120) days after its effectivity.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.
