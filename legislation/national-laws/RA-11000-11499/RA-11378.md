> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11378**
>
> **An Act Declaring June 16 of Every Year a Special Working Holiday in the City of Baybay, Province of Leyte, to be Known as "Baybay City Charter Day"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** June 16 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the City of Baybay, Province of Leyte, to be known as "Baybay City Charter Day".
>
> **Section 2.** On June 16 of every year, the City of Baybay, Province of Leyte, shall engage in festivities and conduct ceremonies and programs in cognizance of its history and culture, to be led by its officials and employees in coordination with civic, sports, religious and nongovernmental organizations and private businesses.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.
