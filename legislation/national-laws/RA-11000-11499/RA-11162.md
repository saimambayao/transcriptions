# Republic Act No. 11162

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BROOKES POINT, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11162**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BROOKES POINT, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the supervision of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) Palawan District Office, an Extension Office of the LTO in the Municipality of Brookes Point, Province of Palawan.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately include in the Departments programs the operationalization of the LTO Extension Office in the Municipality of Brookes Point, Province of Palawan, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The Chairperson of the LTO shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.
