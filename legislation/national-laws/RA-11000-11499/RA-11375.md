> Third Regular Session**

> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11375**
>
> **An Act Declaring February 3 of Every Year a Special Working Holiday in the City of Cabanatuan, Province of Nueva Ecija in Commemoration of its Founding Anniversary**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** February 3 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the City of Cabanatuan, Province of Nueva Ecija, in commemoration of its founding anniversary. Upon proclamation of the President, February 3 of every year shall be a special non working holiday in the City of Cabanatuan, Province of Nueva Ecija.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.
