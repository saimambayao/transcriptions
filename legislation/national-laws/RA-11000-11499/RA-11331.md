> **Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session**
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.

> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11331**
>
> **An Act Increasing the Bed Capacity of Tondo Medical Center in the City of Manila from Two Hundred (200) to Three Hundred (300) Beds, Upgrading its Professional Health Care Services and Facilities, Authorizing the Increase of its Medical Personnel, and Appropriating Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The authorized bed capacity of the Tondo Medical Center in the City of Manila is hereby increased from two hundred (200) to three hundred (300) beds.1âшphi1 The increase in the bed capacity shall be implemented consistent with the development plan of the hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The existing professional health care services and facilities of the Tondo Medical Center are hereby upgraded to conform with and be commensurate to the increase in its authorized bed capacity as provided for in Section 1 hereof.
>
> **Section 3.** The existing workforce of the Tondo Medical Center shall also be correspondingly increased. The Department of Health (DOH) shall, in coordination with the Department of Budget and Management (DBM) and the Civil Service Commission (CSC), and after consultation with the Tondo Medical Center, determine the additional plantilla positions to be created.
>
> **Section 4.** The Secretary of Health shall immediately include in the Departments programs the implementation of this Act, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 5.** The Secretary of Health, in consultation and coordination with local government officials of the City of Manila, shall promulgate the necessary rules and regulations for the effective implementation of this Act.
>
> **Section 6.** If any provision or part of this Act is declared unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected shall remain in full force and effect.
>
> **Section 7.** All other laws, decrees, orders, issuances, rules and regulations, or parts thereof which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed, amended or modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 8.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in any newspaper of general circulation.
>
