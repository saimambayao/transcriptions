# Republic Act No. 11434

**An Act Converting the Quezon Licensing Center of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in Lucena City, Province of Quezon into a Regular Licensing Center to be Known as The "Lucena City Licensing Center" and Appropriating Funds Therefor**

> **Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session**
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11434**
>
> **An Act Converting the Quezon Licensing Center of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in Lucena City, Province of Quezon into a Regular Licensing Center to be Known as The "Lucena City Licensing Center" and Appropriating Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Land Transportation Office (LTO) Quezon Licensing Center in the City of Lucena, Province of Quezon is hereby converted into a regular LTO licensing center.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately include in the Departments program the operationalization of the Lucena City Licensing Center, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current years appropriation of the Quezon Licensing Center. Thereafter, such sum as may be necessary for the continued implementation of this Act shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** Within sixty (60) days from the passage of this Act, the Secretary of Transportation shall issue such rules and regulations necessary for its effective implementation.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days following the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
