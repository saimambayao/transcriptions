# Republic Act No. 11143

**An Act Declaring June 15 of Every Year a Special Nonworking Holiday in the Municipality of Opol, Province of Misamis Oriental, in Commemoration of its Founding Anniversary, To be Known as Araw ng Opol**

> Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11143**
>
> An Act Declaring June 15 of Every Year a Special Nonworking Holiday in the Municipality of Opol, Province of Misamis Oriental, in Commemoration of its Founding Anniversary, To be Known as "Araw ng Opol".
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** June 15 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Opol, Province of Misamis Oriental, in commemoration of its founding anniversary, to be known as "Araw ng Opol".
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.1âwphi1
