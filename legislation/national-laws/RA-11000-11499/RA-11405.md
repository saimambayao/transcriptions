> **Section 3.** *Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite.* The plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission on Elections (COMELEC) pursuant to Provincial Ordinance No. 4-S91 of the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of the Province of Cavite, which ratified the creation of Barangay Sampaloc V (New Era) as proclaimed by the Plebiscite Board of Canvassers on August 1,1993 shall serve as substantial compliance with the requirement under Section 10 of Republic Act No. 7160, otherwise known as the "Local Government Code of 1991", as amended.
>
> **Section 4.** *Incumbent Barangay Officials.* The incumbent elected barangay officials of Barangay Sampaloc V (New Era) shall continue to hold office until their successors shall have been elected and qualified.
>
> **Section 5.** *Public Infrastructure and Facilities.* All public infrastructure and facilities existing therein at the time of the approval of this Act are hereby transferred without cost or compensation to and shall be administered by the new Barangay Sampaloc V (New Era).
>
> **Section 6.** *Internal Revenue Allotment Shares.* Barangay Sampaloc V (New Era) shall be entitled to internal revenue allotment (IRA) share pursuant to Section 285 of the Local Government Code of 1991, as amended.
>
