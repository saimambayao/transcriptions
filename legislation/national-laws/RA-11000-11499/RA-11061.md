> Seventeenth Congress
> Second Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand seventeen.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11061**
>
> An Act Renaming the Gurel-Bokod-Kabayan-Abatan Road in the Province of Benguet to Cong. Andres Acop Cosalan Road
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In recognition of his public service rendered to the people of the Province of Benguet from the sixth to Seventh Congress and the Interim Batasang Pambansa, The Gurel-Bokod-Kabayan-Abatan Road is renamed as the Cong. Andres Acop Cosalan Road.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Public Works and Highways shall place appropriate markers along the Cong. Andres Acop Cosalan Road and issue the necessary orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon approval.
