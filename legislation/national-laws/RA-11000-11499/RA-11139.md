# Republic Act No. 11139

**AN ACT DECLARING OCTOBER 26 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES TO BE KNOWN AS THE "FOUNDATION DAY OF CATANDUANES"**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 11139
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2018/ra_11139_2018.html)

> Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11139**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING OCTOBER 26 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES TO BE KNOWN AS THE "FOUNDATION DAY OF CATANDUANES"
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** October 26 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Catanduanes to be known as the "Foundation Day of Catanduanes".
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
