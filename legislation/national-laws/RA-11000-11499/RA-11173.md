# Republic Act No. 11173

**An Act Changing the Name of Tawantawan Elementary School in Barangay Tawantawan, Baguio District, Davao City to Rosalia Aglay Ayag Elementary School**

> **Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session**
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11173**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Tawantawan Elementary School in Barangay Tawantawan, Baguio District, Davao City to Rosalia Aglay Ayag Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** - The name of Tawantawan Elementary School in Barangay Tawantawan, Baguio District, Davao City is hereby changed to Rosalia "Aglay" Ayag Elementary School.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 2.** - The main campus of the Rosalia "Aglay" Ayag Elementary School shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Tawantawan, Baguio District, Davao City.
>
> **Section 3.** - The act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in any newspaper of general circulation.
>
