**An Act Declaring September 3 of Every Year a Special Working Public Holiday Throughout the Country, in Commemoration of the Surrender of the Japanese Military Forces Led by General Tomoyuki Yamashita in Baguio City**

> **Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session**
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11216**
>
> An Act Declaring September 3 of Every Year a Special Working Public Holiday Throughout the Country, in Commemoration of the Surrender of the Japanese Military Forces Led by General Tomoyuki Yamashita in Baguio City
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** September 3 of every year is hereby declared a special working public holiday throughout the country, in commemoration of the surrender of the Japanese military forces led by General Tomoyuki Yamashita at the American High Commissioners Residence in Camp John Hay, Baguio City.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
