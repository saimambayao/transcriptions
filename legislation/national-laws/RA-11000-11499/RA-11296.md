# Republic Act No. 11296

**An Act Renaming the Hilltop Rizal Philippine National Police (PNP) Command in the Municipality of Taytay, Province of Rizal as Camp General Licerio Geronimo**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 11296
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2019/ra_11296_2019.html)

> **Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session**
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11296**
>
> **An Act Renaming the Hilltop Rizal Philippine National Police (PNP) Command in the Municipality of Taytay, Province of Rizal as Camp General Licerio Geronimo**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Hilltop Rizal Philippine National Police (PNP) Command in the Municipality of Taytay, Province of Rizal is hereby renamed as Camp General Licerio Geronimo.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
>
> (Sgd) **VICENTE C. SOTTO, III**
> President of the Senate
>
> (Sgd) **GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO**
> Speaker of the House of Representatives
>
> This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate of the Philippines on May 2, 2017 and February 4, 2019, respectively.
