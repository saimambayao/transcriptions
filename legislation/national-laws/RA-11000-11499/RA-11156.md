# Republic Act No. 11156

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) DISTRICT OFFICE IN THE CITY OF SIPALAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11156**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) DISTRICT OFFICE IN THE CITY OF SIPALAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a district office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the City of Sipalay, Province of Negros Occidental.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Transportation (DOTr) shall immediately include in the Departments programs the operationalization of the LTO District Office in the City of Sipalay, Province of Negros Occidental, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of the DOTr shall issue rules and regulations necessary for the effective implementation of this Act within sixty (60) days from the effectivity of this Act.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.1âwphi1
