# Republic Act No. 11049

**An Act Renaming the Molino Boulevard Traversing Barangay Talaba 4 and Barangay San Nicolas 3 in the City of Bacoor, Province of Cavite as Bacoor Boulevard**

> Seventeenth Congress
> Second Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand seventeen.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11049**
>
> "An Act Renaming the Molino Boulevard Traversing Barangay Talaba 4 and Barangay San Nicolas 3 in the City of Bacoor, Province of Cavite as Bacoor Boulevard"
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Molino Boulevard which traverses barangay Talaba 4, Niog 3, Ligas 2, Ligas 3, Bayanan, Mambog 4, Molino 2, Molino 3, San Nicolas 1 and San Nicolas 3 in the City of Bacoor, Province of Cavite is hereby renamed as Bacoor Boulevard.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Public Works and Highways shall place appropriate markers along Bacoor Boulevard and issue the necessary orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in at least two (2) newspapers of general circulation.1âwphi1
