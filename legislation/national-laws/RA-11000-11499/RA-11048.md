> Seventeenth Congress
> Second Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand seventeen.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11048**
>
> An Act Renaming the Molino-Salawag-Paliparan Road, Traversing Barangay Talaba 4 in the City of Bacoor, and Barangay Paliparan I in the City of Dasmarinas, All in the Province of Cavite, as "Bacoor-Dasmariñas National Road"
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Molino-Salawag-Paliparan Road, traversing barangay Talaba 4, Niog 3, Ligas 2, Ligas 3, Bayanan, Mambog 4, Molino 2, Molino 3 and Molino 4 in the City of Bacoor, Cavite to barangays Salawag, Palipara 3, Paliparan 2 and Paliparan 1 in the City of Dasmariñas National Road.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Public Works and Highways shall place appropriate markers along the aforementioned national road and issue the necessary orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in at least two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
>
