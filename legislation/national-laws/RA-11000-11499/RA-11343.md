> **An Act Creating a Barangay to be Known as Barangay Ipil in the City of Tabuk, Province of Kalinga**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Creation of Barangay Ipil.* The Sitio of Ipil is hereby separated from Barangay Bulanao in the City of Tabuk, Province of Kalinga to constitute a distinct and independent barangay to be known as Barangay Ipil.
>

> **Section 3.** *Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite.* The plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission on Elections (COMELEC) on December 21, 2002 in Barangay Bulanao pursuant to Provincial Ordinance No. 2002-02 of the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of the Province of Kalinga, which ratified the creation of Barangay Ipil, shall serve as substantial compliance with the plebiscite requirement under Section 10 of Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as the "Local Government Code of 1991".
>
> **Section 4.** *Incumbent Barangay Officials.* The incumbent barangay officials of Barangay Ipil shall continue to hold office until such time that their successors have been duly elected and qualified.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 5.** *Public Infrastructure and Facilities.* All public infrastructure and facilities for public use existing therein at the time of the approval of this Act are hereby transferred without cost or compensation to and shall be administered by the new barangay.
>
> **Section 6.** *Internal Revenue Allotment Shares.* Barangay Ipil shall be entitled to Internal Revenue Allotment (IRA) shares pursuant to Section 285 of Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as the "Local Government Code of 1991".
>
> **Section 7.** *Effectivity.* This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.
