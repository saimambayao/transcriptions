**An Act Declaring September 9 of Every Year a Special Working Holiday in the Municipality of Mina, Province of Iloilo, in Commemoration of its Founding Day**

> **Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session**
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11380**
>
> **An Act Declaring September 9 of Every Year a Special Working Holiday in the Municipality of Mina, Province of Iloilo, in Commemoration of its Founding Day**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** September 9 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the Municipality of Mina, Province of Iloilo, in commemoration of its founding day. Upon proclamation of the President, September 9 of every year shall be a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Mina, Province of Iloilo.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
