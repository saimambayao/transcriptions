> Seventeenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand eighteen.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 11159**
>
> AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO MAYLEEN TING
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Ms. Mayleen Ting is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives, as well as the duties and obligations appurtenant thereto.
>
> **Section 2.** Ms. Mayleen Ting shall take the Oath of Allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines before an officer duly authorized to administer the same.1a\^/phi1 The Oath of Allegiance shall be registered with the Bureau of Immigration.
>
> **Section 3.** Upon registration of the Oath of Allegiance, the Bureau of Immigration shall issue a Certificate of Naturalization to Ms. Mayleen Ting who shall thereupon enter into the full enjoyment of Philippine citizenship.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
