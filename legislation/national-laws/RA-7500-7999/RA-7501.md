> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7501, May 18, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE MEDINA-BANGAY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGRAS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO MEDINA-PARADO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Medina-Bangay Elementary School located in the Municipality of Dingras, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby renamed as the Medina-Parado Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** All allocations provided for the operation and maintenance of the Medina-Bangay Elementary School shall be implemented to the Medina-Parado Elementary School.⌊aшΡhi⌊
>
> **Section 3.** The complete organizational staff of the Medina-Bangay Elementary School is deemed adopted by the Medina-Parado Elementary School.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
