# Republic Act No. 7803

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN THE BARANGAY OF TACLOBO, CITY OF DUMAGUETE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE TACLOBO HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7803, September 01, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN THE BARANGAY OF TACLOBO, CITY OF DUMAGUETE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE TACLOBO HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a high school in the Barangay of Taclobo, City of Dumaguete, Province of Negros Oriental, to be known as the Taclobo High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.**  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Lapsed into law on September 1, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
