> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7612, June 13, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LUZON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY LUZON, MUNICIPALITY OF CABATUAN, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO SEVERO MACUGAY-LUZON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Luzon Elementary School situated in Barangay Luzon, Municipality of Cabatuan, Province of Isabela, is hereby changed to Severo Macugay-Luzon Elementary School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
