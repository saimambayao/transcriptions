# Republic Act No. 7502

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE FELIPE ABRIGO MEMORIAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES IN GUIUAN, EASTERN SAMAR, TO FELIPE ABRIGO NATIONAL MEMORIAL COLLEGE OF ARTS AND TRADES**

- **RA Number**: 7502
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1992/ra_7502_1992.html)

> Fifth Regular Sessio
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7502, May 18, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE FELIPE ABRIGO MEMORIAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES IN GUIUAN, EASTERN SAMAR, TO FELIPE ABRIGO NATIONAL MEMORIAL COLLEGE OF ARTS AND TRADES
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Felipe Abrigo Memorial School of Arts and Trades in Guiuan, Eastern Samar, is hereby changed to Felipe Abrigo Memorial College of Arts and Trades.⌊aшΡhi⌊
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
