# Republic Act No. 7789

**AN ACT DECLARING DECEMBER 18 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALLESTEROS, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO BE KNOWN AS "ALDAW TI BALLESTEROS"**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7789, August 08, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING DECEMBER 18 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALLESTEROS, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO BE KNOWN AS "ALDAW TI BALLESTEROS"
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Municipality of Ballesteros, Province of Cagayan, was founded on December 18, 1911 by virtue of Executive Order No. 79.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** December 18 of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Ballesteros, Province of Cagayan, to be known as "Aldaw ti Ballesteros."
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on August 8, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
