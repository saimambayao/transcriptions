# Republic Act No. 7986

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BELEN, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ILDEFONSO, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE BELEN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7986, April 22, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BELEN, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ILDEFONSO, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE BELEN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a national high school in Barangay Belen, Municipality of San Ildefonso, Province of Ilocos Sur, to be known as the Belen National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue the rules and regulations necessary to implement this Act.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the current year of its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on April 22, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
