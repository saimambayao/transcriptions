# Republic Act No. 7856

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TEN-BED MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN VICENTE, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN VICENTE MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7856, December 31, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TEN-BED MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN VICENTE, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN VICENTE MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a ten-bed municipal hospital in the Municipality of San Vicente, Province of Palawan, to be known as the San Vicente Municipal Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Health in consultation and coordination with local government officials concerned, shall promulgate the necessary rules and regulations for the effective implementation of the Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary for the establishment of the hospital shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law; thereafter, funds necessary for its maintenance and operation shall be provided in accordance with the provisions of the Local Government Code.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
