# Republic Act No. 7724

**AN ACT DECLARING OCTOBER NINETEEN OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF BACOLOD TO BE KNOWN AS "BACOLOD CITY CHARTER DAY"**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7724, May 19, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING OCTOBER NINETEEN OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF BACOLOD TO BE KNOWN AS "BACOLOD CITY CHARTER DAY"
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** October Nineteen is the Charter Day Anniversary of the City of Bacolod and its celebration is highlighted, with the staging of the now famous Maskara Festival.
>
> **Section 2.** October Nineteen of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking public holiday in the City of Bacolod.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, May 19, 1994.
