# Republic Act No. 7976

**AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CREATION OF AN ENGINEERING DISTRICT FOR THE FOURTH CONGRESSIONAL DISTRICT, QUEZON PROVINCE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR THE OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE THEREOF**

## Metadata

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7976, April 14, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CREATION OF AN ENGINEERING DISTRICT FOR THE FOURTH CONGRESSIONAL DISTRICT, QUEZON PROVINCE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR THE OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE THEREOF
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Any provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, an engineering district of the Department of Public Works and Highways is hereby created in the fourth congressional district of Quezon Province.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on April 14, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
