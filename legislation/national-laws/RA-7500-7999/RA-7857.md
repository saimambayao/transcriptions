# Republic Act No. 7857

**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE ROXAS DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, FROM TWENTY-FIVE TO FIFTY BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7857, January 21, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE ROXAS DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, FROM TWENTY-FIVE TO FIFTY BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** the bed capacity of the Roxas District Hospital the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is hereby increased from twenty-five to fifty beds.
>
> **Section 2.** the amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.
>
