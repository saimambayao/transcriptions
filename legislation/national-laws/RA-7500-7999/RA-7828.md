# Republic Act No. 7828

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A VOCATIONAL-TECHNICAL SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARAMOAN, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CARAMOAN VOCATIONAL-TECHNICAL SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7828, December 02, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A VOCATIONAL-TECHNICAL SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARAMOAN, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CARAMOAN VOCATIONAL-TECHNICAL SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the supervision of the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA), a vocational-technical school in the Municipality of Caramoan, Province of Camarines Sur, to be known as the Caramoan Vocational-Technical School, hereinafter referred to as the School.
>
> **Section 2.** The School shall offer vocational and technical courses as may be authorized by the TESDA.
>
> **Section 3.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports, and the TESDA shall take the necessary steps for the establishment and operation of the School.
>
> **Section 4.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 5.**  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, December 2, 1994.
