# Republic Act No. 7994

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE BALAGUNAN BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STO. TOMAS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE BALAGUNAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7994, April 22, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE BALAGUNAN BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STO. TOMAS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE BALAGUNAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Balagunan Barangay High School in the Municipality of Sto. Tomas, Province of Davao, is converted into a national high school to be known as the Balagunan National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on April 22, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
