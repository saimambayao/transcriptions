**AN ACT CONVERTING THE BURAUEN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN STO. NIÑO, MUNICIPALITY OF BURAUEN, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, INTO A COMPREHENSIVE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE BURAUEN COMPREHENSIVE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7997, April 22, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE BURAUEN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN STO. NIÑO, MUNICIPALITY OF BURAUEN, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, INTO A COMPREHENSIVE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE BURAUEN COMPREHENSIVE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Burauen National High School in Sto. Ni-o, Municipality of Burauen, Province of Leyte, is hereby converted into a national comprehensive high school to be known as the Burauen Comprehensive National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records of the Burauen National High School are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Burauen Comprehensive National High School.
>
> **Section 4.** The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be charged against the current year's appropriations of the Burauen National High School in Sto. Ni-o, Burauen, Leyte. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on April 22, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
