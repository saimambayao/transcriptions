**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE VIGAN PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIGAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, FROM ONE HUNDRED (100) TO ONE HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE (125) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7525, May 21, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE VIGAN PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIGAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, FROM ONE HUNDRED (100) TO ONE HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE (125) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Vigan Provincial Hospital in the Municipality of Vigan, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby increased from one hundred (100) to one hundred twenty-five (125) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Health shall take the necessary steps for the immediate implementation of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
