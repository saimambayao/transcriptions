**AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE MALAY MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN BARANGAY BALUSBUS, MUNICIPALITY OF MALAY, PROVINCE OF AKLAN, FROM A TEN-BED CAPACITY MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL TO A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE MALAY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7548, May 22, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE MALAY MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN BARANGAY BALUSBUS, MUNICIPALITY OF MALAY, PROVINCE OF AKLAN, FROM A TEN-BED CAPACITY MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL TO A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE MALAY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Malay Municipal Hospital in Barangay Balusbus, Municipality of Malay, Province of Aklan, is hereby expanded and upgraded from a ten-bed capacity municipal hospital to a twenty-five-bed capacity district hospital, to be known as the Malay District Hospital.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
