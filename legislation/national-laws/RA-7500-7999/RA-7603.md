# Republic Act No. 7603

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MALAGUIT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MALAGUIT, MUNICIPALITY OF PARACALE, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO PEDRO V. MORENO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7603, June 03, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MALAGUIT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MALAGUIT, MUNICIPALITY OF PARACALE, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO PEDRO V. MORENO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Malaguit Elementary School in Barangay Malaguit, Municipality of Paracale, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Pedro V. Moreno Elementary School.
>
