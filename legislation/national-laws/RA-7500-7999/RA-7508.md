# Republic Act No. 7508

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TALISAY PROVINCIAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO RAFAEL B. LACSON MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> Fifth Regular Session
>
> Begun and held In Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, nineteen hundred a\*nd ninety-one.
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7508, May 18, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TALISAY PROVINCIAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO RAFAEL B. LACSON MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Talisay Provincial High School in the Municipality of Talisay, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Rafael B. Lacson Memorial High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1âшphi1
