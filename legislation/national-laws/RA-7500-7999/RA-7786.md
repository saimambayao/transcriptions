# Republic Act No. 7786

**AN ACT NAMING THE NATIONAL HIGHWAY BEGINNING FROM BARANGAY ANAYAN, MUNICIPALITY OF PILI UP TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAGONOY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, AS THE GOVERNOR JOSE T. FUENTEBELLA NATIONAL HIGHWAY**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7786, August 08, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT NAMING THE NATIONAL HIGHWAY BEGINNING FROM BARANGAY ANAYAN, MUNICIPALITY OF PILI UP TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAGONOY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, AS THE GOVERNOR JOSE T. FUENTEBELLA NATIONAL HIGHWAY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The national highway beginning from Barangay Anayan in the Municipality of Pili up to the Municipality of Lagonoy, Province of Camarines Sur, shall be named as the Governor Jose T. Fuentebella National Highway.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on August 8, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.1a⍵⍴h!1
