# Republic Act No. 7615

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PANTOK-PALANGOY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINANGONAN, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO SUSANA MADRIGAL MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7615, June 19, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PANTOK-PALANGOY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINANGONAN, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO SUSANA MADRIGAL MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Pantok-Palangoy Elementary School in the Municipality of Binangonan, Province of Rizal, is hereby changed to Susana Madrigal Memorial Elementary School.
>
