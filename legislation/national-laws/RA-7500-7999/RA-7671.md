# Republic Act No. 7671

**AN ACT DECLARING JUNE NINETEEN OF EVERY YEAR AS 'ARAW NG SURIGAO DEL SUR' AND SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE ENTIRE PROVINCE**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7671, January 10, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING JUNE NINETEEN OF EVERY YEAR AS 'ARAW NG SURIGAO DEL SUR' AND SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE ENTIRE PROVINCE
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Province of Surigao del Sur was created by virtue of Republic Act No. 2786 which was finally approved on June 19, 1960.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** June Nineteen of every year is hereby declared as "Araw ng Surigao del Sur" and consequently a special nonworking holiday in the entire province.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on January 10, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
