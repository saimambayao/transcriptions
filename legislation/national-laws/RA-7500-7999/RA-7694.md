# Republic Act No. 7694

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MANAGOPAYA PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANATE, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO FRANCISCO S. BACTUNG MEMORIAL PRIMARY SCHOOL**

## Metadata

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7695, April 09, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MANAGOPAYA PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANATE, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO FRANCISCO S. BACTUNG MEMORIAL PRIMARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Managopaya Primary School in the Municipality of Banate, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Francisco S. Bactung Memorial Primary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on April 9, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27 (1), Article VI of the Constitution.1a⍵⍴h!1
