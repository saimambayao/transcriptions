> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7685, February 23, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 16 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF DAVAO, TO BE KNOWN AS "ARAW NG DABAW"
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The City of Davao was created by virtue of Commonwealth Act No. 51 which took effect on March 16, 1936.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** March 16 of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking public holiday in the City of Davao, to be known as the "Araw ng Dabaw."
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, February 23, 1994.
