> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7604, June 03, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CANAUILI BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CANAUILI, MUNICIPALITY OF JANIUAY, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO MANUEL A. AARON MEMORIAL BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Canauili Barangay High School in Barangay Canauili, Municipality of Janiuay, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Manuel A. Aaron Memorial Barangay High School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
