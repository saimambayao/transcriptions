# Republic Act No. 7771

**AN ACT DECLARING OCTOBER THIRTY-ONE OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUMANGAS, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO COMMEMORATE THE BIRTH ANNIVERSARY OF COLONEL QUINTIN D. SALAS**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7771, June 19, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING OCTOBER THIRTY-ONE OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUMANGAS, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO COMMEMORATE THE BIRTH ANNIVERSARY OF COLONEL QUINTIN D. SALAS
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** October thirty-one of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking public holiday in the Municipality of Dumangas, Province of Iloilo, to commemorate the birth anniversary of Colonel Quintin D. Salas, a Visayan revolutionary hero of the Philippine-American War who serves as an inspiration to the Filipinos especially the Visayan and youth of the land.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on June 19, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
