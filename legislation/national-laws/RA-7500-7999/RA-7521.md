> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7521, May 21, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE TINAMBAC MEDICARE HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TINAMBAC, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SURE, FROM A FIFTEEN-BED CAPACITY MEDICARE HOSPITAL INTO A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE TINAMBAC DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled
>
> **Section 1.** The Tinambac Medicare Hospital in the Municipality of Tinambac, Province of Camarines Sur, is hereby expanded and upgraded from a fifteen-bed capacity medicare hospital into a twenty-five bed capacity district hospital, to be known as the Tinambac District Hospital.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, May 21, 1992.
