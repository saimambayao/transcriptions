> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7631, July 17, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MAGUINDA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MAGUINDA, BUTUAN CITY, TO SANTIAGO B. ECLEO, SR., MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Maguinda Elementary School in Barangay Maguinda, Butuan City, is hereby changed to Santiago B. Ecleo, Sr., Memorial Elementary School.
>
