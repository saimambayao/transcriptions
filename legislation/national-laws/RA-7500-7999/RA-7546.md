**AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE MEDICARE COMMUNITY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MIGUEL, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL SUR, FROM A TEN-BED CAPACITY MEDICARE HOSPITAL INTO A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN MIGUEL DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7546, May 22, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE MEDICARE COMMUNITY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MIGUEL, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL SUR, FROM A TEN-BED CAPACITY MEDICARE HOSPITAL INTO A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN MIGUEL DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The medicare community hospital in the Municipality of San Miguel, Province of Surigao del Sur, is hereby expanded and upgraded from a ten-bed capacity medicare hospital into a twenty-five-bed capacity district hospital, to be known as the San Miguel District Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
