# Republic Act No. 7791

**AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER TWENTY-NINE OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MIGUEL, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO BE KNOWN AS THE "SAN MIGUEL DAY"**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7791, August 08, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER TWENTY-NINE OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MIGUEL, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO BE KNOWN AS THE "SAN MIGUEL DAY"
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The twenty-ninth day of September of every year, being the annual town fiesta of San Miguel, Leyte, honoring its most holy patron saint, St. Michael the Archangel, is hereby declared a special nonworking public holiday in the Municipality of San Miguel, Province of Leyte, to be known as the "San Miguel Day."
>
> **Section 2.** On September twenty-nine of every year, the municipal officials of San Miguel, Leyte, shall lead appropriate and meaningful celebrations, activities, and programs participated in by regional heads of departments and other government offices and their employees holding office in San Miguel, Leyte, civic, sports, religious, and nongovernmental organizations and private companies doing business in San Miguel, Leyte.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Lapsed into law on August 8, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
