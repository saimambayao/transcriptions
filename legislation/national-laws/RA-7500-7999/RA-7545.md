# Republic Act No. 7545

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGAT, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE ANGAT HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7545, May 22, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGAT, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE ANGAT HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established in the Municipality of Angat, Province of Bulacan, a five-bed capacity health infirmary, to be known as the Angat Health Infirmary.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
