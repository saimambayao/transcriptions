# Republic Act No. 7817

**AN ACT NAMING THE GRANADA-MURCIA ROAD AND EXTENSION (CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD) WHICH IS A NATIONAL ROAD IN BACOLOD CITY, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, AS THE CARLOS HILADO HIGHWAY**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7817, November 10, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT NAMING THE GRANADA-MURCIA ROAD AND EXTENSION (CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD) WHICH IS A NATIONAL ROAD IN BACOLOD CITY, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, AS THE CARLOS HILADO HIGHWAY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Granada-Murcia Road and Extension (Circumferential Road), a national road in Bacolod City, Negros Occidental, is hereby named as the Carlos Hilado Highway.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, November 10, 1994.
