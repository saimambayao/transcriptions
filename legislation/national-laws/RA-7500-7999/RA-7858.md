# Republic Act No. 7858

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF RIZAL COLLEGE OF AGRICULTURE AND TECHNOLOGY IN SAMPALOC, MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO RIZAL STATE COLLEGE**

## Metadata

> Nineth Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7858, January 21, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF RIZAL COLLEGE OF AGRICULTURE AND TECHNOLOGY IN SAMPALOC, MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO RIZAL STATE COLLEGE
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** the name of the Rizal College of Agriculture and Technology located in Sampaloc, Municipality of Tanay, Province of Rizal, is hereby changed to Rizal State College.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
