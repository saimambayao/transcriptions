> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7514, May 21, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DACUTON BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUMARAO, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, TO MAYOR RAMON A. BENJAMIN, SR., MEMORIAL HLGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Dacuton Barangay High School in the Municipality of Dumarao, Province of Capiz, is hereby changed to Mayor Ramon A. Benjamin, Sr., Memorial High School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
