# Republic Act No. 7955

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TEN-BED DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN BARANGAY MARILOG, CITY OF DAVAO, TO BE KNOWN AS THE MARILOG DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7955, March 29, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TEN-BED DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN BARANGAY MARILOG, CITY OF DAVAO, TO BE KNOWN AS THE MARILOG DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a ten-bed district hospital in Barangay Marilog, City of Davao, to be known as the Marilog District Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Health, in consultation and coordination with the local government officials concerned, shall promulgate the rules and regulations necessary for the effective implementation of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary for the establishment of the hospital shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law; thereafter, funds necessary for its maintenance and operation shall be provided in accordance with the provisions of the Local Government Code or the applicable law at the time.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
