# Republic Act No. 7602

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BAYAMBANG NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, CARUNGAY ANNEX, IN BARANGAY CARUNGAY, MUNICIPALITY OF BAYAMBANG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, TO MOISES B. REBAMONTAN MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> Fifth Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, *two thousand twenty two*.
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7602, June 03, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BAYAMBANG NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, CARUNGAY ANNEX, IN BARANGAY CARUNGAY, MUNICIPALITY OF BAYAMBANG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, TO MOISES B. REBAMONTAN MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Bayambang National High School, Carungay Annex, in Barangay Carungay, Municipality of Bayambang Province of Pangasinan, is hereby changed to Moises B. Rebamontan Memorial High School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval
