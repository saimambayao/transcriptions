# Republic Act No. 7812

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE ROSARIO BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN ROSARIO, ALOGUINSAN, CEBU, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE ROSARIO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7812, October 26, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE ROSARIO BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN ROSARIO, ALOGUINSAN, CEBU, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE ROSARIO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The barangay high school of Barangay Rosario in the Municipality of Aloguinsan, Cebu, is hereby converted into a national high school to be known as Rosario National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** Upon organization of Rosario National High School, all assets, liabilities, personnel, equipment and records of Rosario Barangay High School shall be transferred to the Rosario National High School.
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports is hereby empowered to promulgate such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 4.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
