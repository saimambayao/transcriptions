> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7520, May 21, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE BUTUAN CITY GENERAL HOSPITAL IN AGUSAN DEL NORTE FROM ONE HUNDRED (100) TO ONE HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE (125) BEDS, UPGRADING IT INTO A PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS THE AGUSAN DEL NORTE PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Butuan City General Hospital in Agusan del Norte is hereby increased from one hundred (100) to one hundred twenty-five (125) beds and upgrading it into a provincial hospital to be known as the Agusan del Norte Provincial Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.
>
