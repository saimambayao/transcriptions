> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7535, May 22, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE DINALUPIHAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINALUPIHAN, PROVINCE OF BATAAN, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Dinalupihan District Hospital in the Municipality of Dinalupihan, Province of Bataan, is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
