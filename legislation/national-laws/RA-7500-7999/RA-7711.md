# Republic Act No. 7711

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE KILING HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY KILING, MUNICIPALITY OF TANAUAN, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO KILING NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7711, May 05, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE KILING HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY KILING, MUNICIPALITY OF TANAUAN, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO KILING NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Kiling High School in Tanauan, Leyte, is hereby changed to Kiling National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved,
>
> Lapsed into law on May 5, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
