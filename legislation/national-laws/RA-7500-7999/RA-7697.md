# Republic Act No. 7697

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LICANAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ISIDRO, BUNAWAN DISTRICT, DAVAO CITY, TO PABLO PIATOS, SR. ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7697, April 17, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LICANAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ISIDRO, BUNAWAN DISTRICT, DAVAO CITY, TO PABLO PIATOS, SR. ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Licanan Elementary School in Barangay San Isidro, Bunawan District, Davao City, is hereby changed to Pablo Piatos, Sr. Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on April 17, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.1a⍵⍴h!1
