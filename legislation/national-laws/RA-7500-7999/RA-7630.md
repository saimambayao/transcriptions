> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7630, July 17, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DINAGAT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINAGAT, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE, TO DON RUBEN E. ECLEO, SR. MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.**  The name of Dinagat National High School in the Municipality of Dinagat, Province of Surigao del Norte, is hereby changed to Don Ruben E. Ecleo, Sr. Memorial National High School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.**  The Department of Education, Culture and Sports is hereby directed to take cognizance and implement the provision of Section 1 hereof.
>
> **Section 3.**  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 17, 1992.
