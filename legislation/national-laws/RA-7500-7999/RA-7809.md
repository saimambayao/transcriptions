# Republic Act No. 7809

**AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 14 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANCHEZ MIRA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO BE KNOWN AS "ALDAW TI SANCHEZ MIRA"**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7809, September 16, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 14 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANCHEZ MIRA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO BE KNOWN AS "ALDAW TI SANCHEZ MIRA"
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Municipality of Sanchez Mira, Province of Cagayan, was founded on September 14, 1892 by the Spanish Governor General and the members of the Catholic hierarchy assigned thereat.
>
> **Section 2.** September 14 of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Sanchez Mira, Province of Cagayan, to be known as "Aldaw ti Sanchez Mira."
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on September 16, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.1a⍵⍴h!1
