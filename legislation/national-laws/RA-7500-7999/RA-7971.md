# Republic Act No. 7971

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TEN-BED MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGUINALDO, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO, TO BE KNOWN AS THE AGUINALDO MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7971, March 30, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TEN-BED MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGUINALDO, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO, TO BE KNOWN AS THE AGUINALDO MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a ten-bed capacity municipal hospital in the Municipality of Aguinaldo, Province of Ifugao, to be known as the Aguinaldo Municipal Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Health, in consultation and coordination with the local government officials concerned, shall promulgate such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary for the establishment of the hospital shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law; thereafter, funds necessary for the maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be provided in accordance with the provisions of the Local Government Code or the applicable law at the time.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 4.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
