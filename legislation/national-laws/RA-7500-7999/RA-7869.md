# Republic Act No. 7869

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING a HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN AGAPITO, ISLA VERDE, BATANGAS CITY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN AGAPITO HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7869, January 21, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING a HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN AGAPITO, ISLA VERDE, BATANGAS CITY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN AGAPITO HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a high school in Barangay San Agapito, Isla Verde, Batangas City, to be known as the San Agapito High School.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** the Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** the amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
