> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7841, December 16, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING THE PARAÑAQUE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PARAÑAQUE, METRO MANILA, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be a Parañaque National High School in the Municipality of Parañaque, Metro Manila, under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, December 16, 1994.
