# Republic Act No. 7672

**AN ACT DECLARING JUNE EIGHTEEN OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET**

- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1994/ra_7672_1994.html)

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7672, January 10, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING JUNE EIGHTEEN OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Province of Benguet was created by virtue of Republic Act No. 4695 which was enacted into law on June 18, 1966.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** It is hereby declared that June Eighteen of every year be a nonworking public holiday in the Province of Benguet.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on January 10, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
