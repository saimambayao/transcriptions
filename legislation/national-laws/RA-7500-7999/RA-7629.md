> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7629, July 17, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE NORTHERN LA UNION MATERNITY AND CHILDREN'S HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALAOAN, PROVINCE OF LA UNION, TO BALAOAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Northern La Union Maternity and Children's Hospital situated in the Municipality of Balaoan, Province of La Union, is hereby changed to Balaoan District Hospital.
>
