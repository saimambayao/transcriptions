> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7842, December 16, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A ONE HUNDRED-BED DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGIG, METRO MANILA, TO BE KNOWN AS THE TAGIG-PATEROS DISTRICT HOSPITAL, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established under the supervision of the Department of Health a one hundred-bed district hospital in the legislative district of Tagig-Pateros, Metro Manila, to be known as the Tagig-Pateros District Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** Any provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding and for purposes of this Act, certain parcels of land owned by the Republic of the Philippines, situated in the Barangay of Western Bicutan, Municipality of Tagig, Metro Manila and described herein below are hereby established as the hospital site and the administration thereof is hereby transferred to the Department of Health, to wit:

>
