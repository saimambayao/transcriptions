# Republic Act No. 7979

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF DINAS MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINAS, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, TO DON SANTIAGO A. NAHIAL, SR. MEMORIAL HOSPITAL**

## Metadata

- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1995/ra_7979_1995.html)

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7979, April 22, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF DINAS MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINAS, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, TO DON SANTIAGO A. NAHIAL, SR. MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name Dinas Municipal Hospital in the Municipality of Dinas, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, is hereby changed to Don Santiago A. Nahial, Sr. Memorial Hospital.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on April 22,1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
