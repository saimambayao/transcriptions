# Republic Act No. 7980

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PUG-OS (SINAIT) ELEMENTARY SCHOOL LOCATED AT BARANGAY PUG-OS, MUNICIPALITY OF SINAIT, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO DEAN LEOPOLDO YABES MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7980, April 22, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PUG-OS (SINAIT) ELEMENTARY SCHOOL LOCATED AT BARANGAY PUG-OS, MUNICIPALITY OF SINAIT, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO DEAN LEOPOLDO YABES MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Pug-os (Sinait) Elementary School located at Barangay Pug-os, Municipality of Sinait, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby changed to Dean Leopoldo Yabes Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1aшphi1
>
> Lapsed into law on April 22,1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
