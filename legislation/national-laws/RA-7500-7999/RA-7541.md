**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE BALAMBAN MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALAMBAN, PROVINCE OF CEBU, FROM TEN (10) TO TWENTY-FIVE (25) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7541, May 22, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE BALAMBAN MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALAMBAN, PROVINCE OF CEBU, FROM TEN (10) TO TWENTY-FIVE (25) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Balamban Municipal Hospital in the Municipality of Balamban, Province of Cebu, is hereby increased from ten (10) to twenty-five (25) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
