# Republic Act No. 7946

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE SAN ROQUE BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF CEBU, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN ROQUE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7946, March 03, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE SAN ROQUE BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF CEBU, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN ROQUE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The San Roque Barangay High School in the Municipality of Talisay, Province of Cebu, is bereby converted into a national high school to be known as the San Roque National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue the necessary rules and regulations for the effective implementation of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
