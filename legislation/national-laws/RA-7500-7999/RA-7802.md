**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MINUHANG BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BARUGO, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO CELESTINO DE GUZMAN MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7802, September 01, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MINUHANG BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BARUGO, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO CELESTINO DE GUZMAN MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Minuhang Barangay High School in the Municipality of Barugo, Province of Leyte, is hereby changed to Celestino de Guzman Memorial National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, Lapsed into law on September 1, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.1a⍵⍴h!1
