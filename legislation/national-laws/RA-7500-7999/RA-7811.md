# Republic Act No. 7811

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A PUBLIC SECONDARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MILAOR, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN ANTONIO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7811, October 26, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A PUBLIC SECONDARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MILAOR, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN ANTONIO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a public secondary school in Barangay San Antonio in the Municipality of Milaor, Province of Camarines Sur, to be known as the San Antonio National High School under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.
>
> Approved, October 26, 1994.
