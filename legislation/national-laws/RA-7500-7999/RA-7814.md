# Republic Act No. 7814

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE CARURAY BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CARURAY, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN VICENTE, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, INTO THE CARURAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7814, October 26, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE CARURAY BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CARURAY, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN VICENTE, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, INTO THE CARURAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The existing barangay high school of Barangay Caruray, Municipality of San Vicente, Province of Palawan, is hereby converted into a national high school under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall promulgate the necessary rules and regulations for the effective implementation of this Act.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, October 26, 1994.
