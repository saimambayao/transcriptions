> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7553, May 22, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING JUNE NINETEEN OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE, TO BE KNOWN AS "ADLAW NAN SURIGAO DEL NORTE"
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** June Nineteen of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Surigao del Norte, to be known as "Adlaw Nan Surigao Del Norte."1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days following its publication in two (2) national newspapers of general circulation in the country.
