# Republic Act No. 7807

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. TERESITA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, TO BE KNOWN AS THE STA. TERESITA HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7807, August 30, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. TERESITA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, TO BE KNOWN AS THE STA. TERESITA HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a high school in the Poblacion, Municipality of Sta. Teresita, Province of Batangas, to be known as the Sta. Teresita High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue and publish the necessary rules and regulations to implement the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in Filipino and English in at least two (2) newspapers of general circulation.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, August 30, 1994.
