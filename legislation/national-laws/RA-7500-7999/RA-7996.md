# Republic Act No. 7996

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE DR. GERARDO SABAL MEMORIAL MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF CLAVERIA, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE DR. GERARDO SABAL MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7996, April 22, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE DR. GERARDO SABAL MEMORIAL MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF CLAVERIA, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE DR. GERARDO SABAL MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Dr. Gerardo Sabal Memorial Municipal High School in the Poblacion, Municipality of Claveria, Province of Misamis Oriental, is hereby converted into a national high school to be known as the Dr. Gerardo Sabal Memorial National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations and guidelines as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Act.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on April 22, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
