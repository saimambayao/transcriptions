# Republic Act No. 7543

**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE LIBMANAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIBMANAN, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, FROM FIFTY (50) TO ONE HUNDRED (100) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7543, May 22, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE LIBMANAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIBMANAN, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, FROM FIFTY (50) TO ONE HUNDRED (100) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Libmanan District Hospital in the Municipality of Libmanan, Province of Camarines Sur, is hereby increased from fifty (50) to one hundred (100) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.
>
