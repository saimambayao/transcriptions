# Republic Act No. 7608

**AN ACT RENAMING THE MAGALLANES SOUTH CENTRAL SCHOOL LOCATED IN MAGALLANES, SORSOGON, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, TO EDUARDO LEE CHAN MEMORIAL SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1992/ra_7608_1992.html)

> Fifth Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7608, June 11, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE MAGALLANES SOUTH CENTRAL SCHOOL LOCATED IN MAGALLANES, SORSOGON, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, TO EDUARDO LEE CHAN MEMORIAL SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Magallanes South Central School is hereby renamed as the Eduardo Lee Chan Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1âшphi1
