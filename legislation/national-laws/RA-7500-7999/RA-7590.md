> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7590, June 01, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCRESING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE SIPOCOT DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIPOCOT, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative s of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.**  The bed capacity of the Sipocot District Hospital in the Municipality of Sipocot, Province of Camarines Sur, is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.**  The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriation of the year following its enactment into law.
>
> **Section 3.**  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 1, 1992
