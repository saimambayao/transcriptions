> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7984, April 22, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY LA UNION, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ISIDRO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, a high school in Barangay La Union, Municipality of San Isidro, Province of Davao Oriental.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall implement this Act so as to make the school operational during the first school term after the effectivity of this Act.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to implement the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on April 22, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
