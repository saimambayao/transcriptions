# Republic Act No. 7943

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE AMAI PAKPAK GENERAL HOSPITAL IN MARAWI CITY, LANAO DEL SUR, INTO A MEDICAL CENTER, TO BE KNOWN AS THE AMAI PAKPAK MEDICAL CENTER, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7943, March 03, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE AMAI PAKPAK GENERAL HOSPITAL IN MARAWI CITY, LANAO DEL SUR, INTO A MEDICAL CENTER, TO BE KNOWN AS THE AMAI PAKPAK MEDICAL CENTER, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Amai Pakpak General Hospital located in Marawi City, Lanao del Sur, is hereby converted into a medical center with all the necessary personnel, equipment and facilities, to be known as the Amai Pakpak Medical Center.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Health shall formulate the necessary guidelines for the operation of the said medical center.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
