> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7682, February 23, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING MARCH NINE OF EVERY YEAR A NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ABRA, TO BE KNOWN AS "ARYA ABRA DAY"
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Philippine Legislature enacted on March 9,1917 Act No. 2683, which segregated Abra from Ilocos Sur and gave it full provincial status.
>
> **Section 2.** March nine of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking public holiday in the Province of Abra, to be known as"AryaAbraDay."
>
> **Section 3.**  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, February 23, 1994.
