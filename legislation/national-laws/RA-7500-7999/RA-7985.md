# Republic Act No. 7985

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ISIDRO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUERO, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN ISIDRO HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 7985, April 22, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ISIDRO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUERO, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN ISIDRO HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, a high school in Barangay San Isidro, Municipality of Duero, Province of Bohol, to be known as the San Isidro High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on April 22, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
