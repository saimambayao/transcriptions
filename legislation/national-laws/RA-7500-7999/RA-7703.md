# Republic Act No. 7703

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BENIGNO S. AQUINO MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL IN CONCEPCION, TARLAC, TO BENIGNO S. AQUINO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 7703
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1994/ra_7703_1994.html)

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7703, May 05, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BENIGNO S. AQUINO MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL IN CONCEPCION, TARLAC, TO BENIGNO S. AQUINO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Benigno S.Aquino Memorial High School in Concepcion, Tarlac, is hereby changed to Benigno S. Aquino National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved,
>
> Lapsed into law on May 5, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
