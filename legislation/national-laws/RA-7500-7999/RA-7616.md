> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7616, June 19, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LUBOG PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAPIAN, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, TO TELESFORO APEROCHO MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Lubog Primary School in the Municipality of Sapian, Province of Capiz, is hereby changed to Telesforo Aperocho Memorial Elementary School.
>
