**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE DRA. OLIVIA SALAMANCA HOSPITAL IN CAVITE CITY, FROM FIFTY (50) TO SEVENTY-FIVE (75) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7522, May 21, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE DRA. OLIVIA SALAMANCA HOSPITAL IN CAVITE CITY, FROM FIFTY (50) TO SEVENTY-FIVE (75) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Dra. Olivia Salamanca Hospital in Cavite City, is hereby increased from fifty (50) to seventy-five (75) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
