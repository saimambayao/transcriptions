# Republic Act No. 7762

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PALOMPON, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO BE KNOWN AS THE ALFREDO PARILLA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Republic of the Philippines
> Congress of the Philippines
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7762, June 19, 1994 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PALOMPON, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO BE KNOWN AS THE ALFREDO PARILLA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a comprehensive public high school in Barangay San Miguel, Municipality of Palompon, Province of Leyte, to be known as the Alfredo Parilla National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be funded out of the appropriations authorized by Republic Act No. 7663 for the Department of Education, Culture and Sports intended for the operational expenses of newly legislated high schools. Thereafter, the amount needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on June 19, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
