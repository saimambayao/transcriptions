>
> **Section 1.** *Division of Barangay Tangos.* - Barangay Tangos in the City of Navotas is hereby divided into two (2) distinct and independent barangays to be known as Barangay Tangos North and Barangay Tangos South.

> **Section 3.** *Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite.* - The Commission on Elections (COMELEC) shall conduct and supervise the plebiscite to be held in Barangay Tangos in the City of Navotas not later than one hundred twenty (120) days from the effectivity of this Act.
>
> The expenses for such plebiscite shall be borne by the City of Navotas.
>
> **Section 4.** *Special Election of Barangay Officials.* - The first set of officials of the barangays created under Section 1 hereof shall be elected not later than ninety (90) days from approval by majority of the votes cast in a plebiscite held for the purpose.1awp++i1
>
> The COMELEC shall conduct and supervise the special elections to be held in barangays Tangos North and Tangos South in the City of Navotas within sixty (60) days after the ratification of this Act in a plebiscite.
>
> The expenses for such special elections shall be borne by the City of Navotas.
>
> The incumbent barangay officials of Barangay Tangos shall continue to hold office and administer barangays Tangos North and Tangos South until such time that the officials of the newly created barangays have been duly elected and qualified.
>
> **Section 5.** *Public Infrastructure and Facilities.* - All public infrastructure and facilities for public use existing therein at the time of the approval of this Act are hereby transferred without cost or compensation to and shall be administered by the new barangays.
>
> **Section 6.** *Internal Revenue Allotment Shares.* - All barangays herein created shall be entitled to Internal Revenue Allotment (IRA) shares pursuant to Section 285 of Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as the "*Local Government Code of 1991.*"
>
> **Section 7.** *Effectivity.* - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its complete publication in at least two (2) newspapers of general circulation in the Philippines.
