> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10685**
>
> AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO PETER LESLIE WALLACE
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** Mr. Peter Leslie Wallace is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives, as well as the duties and obligations appurtenant thereto under the Constitution and the laws of the Republic of the Philippines.
>
> **SECTION 2.** After the taking by Mr. Peter Leslie Wallace of his Oath of Allegiance before an officer duly authorized for this purpose, and the registration of the same with the Bureau of Immigration, Mr. Peter Leslie Wallace shall enter upon the full enjoyment of his Philippine citizenship.
>
> **SECTION 3.** Upon registration of the Oath of Allegiance, the Bureau of Immigration shall forthwith issue Mr. Peter Leslie Wallaces Certificate of Naturalization.
>
> **SECTION 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in at least two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
>
> Approved
