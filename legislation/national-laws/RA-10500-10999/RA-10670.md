# Republic Act No. 10670

**AN ACT NAMING THE DIVERSION ROAD IN TACLOBAN CITY, LEYTE AS GOV. BENJAMIN "KOKOY" T. ROMUALDEZ DIVERSION ROAD**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 10670
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2015/ra_10670_2015.html)

---

> **Sixteenth Congress
> Second Regular Session**
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, two thousand fourteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10670**
>
> AN ACT NAMING THE DIVERSION ROAD IN TACLOBAN CITY, LEYTE AS GOV. BENJAMIN "KOKOY" T. ROMUALDEZ DIVERSION ROAD
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The diversion road in Tacloban City, Leyte shall hereinafter be known as ***Gov. Benjamin "Kokoy" T. Romualdez Diversion Road.***
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette.*
>
> Approved.
