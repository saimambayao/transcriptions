# Republic Act No. 10830

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE BAKHAW ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BAKHAW, DISTRICT OF MANDURRIAO, ILOILO CITY INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS BAKHAW INTEGRATED SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10830**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE BAKHAW ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BAKHAW, DISTRICT OF MANDURRIAO, ILOILO CITY INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS BAKHAW INTEGRATED SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** The Bakhaw Elementary School in Barangay Bakhaw, District of Mandurriao, Iloilo City is hereby converted into an integrated school to be known as Bakhaw Integrated School.
>
> **SECTION 2.** All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Bakhaw Elementary School are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Bakhaw Integrated School.
>
> **SECTION 3.** The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Departments program the operationalization of the Bakhaw Integrated School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **SECTION 4.** The Secretary of Education shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.1âwphi1
>
> **SECTION 5.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
