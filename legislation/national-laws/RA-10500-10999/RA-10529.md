# Republic Act No. 10529

**AN ACT NAMING THE POLICE REGIONAL OFFICE 11 AT SITIO CATITIPAN, BARANGAY COMMUNAL, BUHANGIN DISTRICT, DAVAO CITY AS CAMP QUINTIN M. MERECIDO**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 10529
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2013/ra_10529_2013.html)

> Fifteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand twelve.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10529**
>
> AN ACT NAMING THE POLICE REGIONAL OFFICE 11 AT SITIO CATITIPAN, BARANGAY COMMUNAL, BUHANGIN DISTRICT, DAVAO CITY AS CAMP QUINTIN M. MERECIDO
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Police Regional Office 11 at Sitio Catitipan, Barangay Communal, Buhangin District, Davao City is hereby named as Camp Quintin M. Merecido.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in any newspaper of general circulation.
>
