# Republic Act No. 10746

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN MANUEL NORTE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGOO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION TO DR. MANUEL T. CASES SR. NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10746**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN MANUEL NORTE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGOO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION TO DR. MANUEL T. CASES SR. NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** *Renaming of a National High School.*  The name of San Manuel Norte National High School in the Municipality of Agoo, Province of La Union is hereby changed to Dr. Manuel T. Cases Sr. National High School.
>
> **SECTION 2.** *Location of the Main Campus.*  The main campus of the Dr. Manuel T. Cases Sr. National High School shall continue to remain in its present location in the Municipality of Agoo, Province of La Union.
>
> **SECTION 3.** *Effectivity.*  This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
>
