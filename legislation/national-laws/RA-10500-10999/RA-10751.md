# Republic Act No. 10751

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAVERONA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAVERONA, MUNICIPALITY OF TALUGTUG, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA TO ALBERTO G. BAUTISTA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2016/ra_10751_2016.html)

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10751**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAVERONA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAVERONA, MUNICIPALITY OF TALUGTUG, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA TO ALBERTO G. BAUTISTA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** *Renaming of an Elementary School.*  The name of Saverona Elementary School in Barangay Saverona, Municipality of Talugtug, Province of Nueva Ecija is hereby changed to Alberto G. Bautista Elementary School.
>
> **SECTION 2.** *Location of the Main Campus.*  The main campus of the Alberto G. Bautista Elementary School shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Saverona, Municipality of Talugtug, Province of Nueva Ecija.
>
> **SECTION 3.** *Effectivity.*  This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
>
