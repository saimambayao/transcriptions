# Republic Act No. 10840

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE SALIOK NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SALIOK, MUNICIPALITY OF NATONIN, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE INTO A NATIONAL TECHNICAL-VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS SALIOK NATIONAL TECHNICAL-VOCATIONAL AND SKILLS TRAINING HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10840**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE SALIOK NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SALIOK, MUNICIPALITY OF NATONIN, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE INTO A NATIONAL TECHNICAL-VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS SALIOK NATIONAL TECHNICAL-VOCATIONAL AND SKILLS TRAINING HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** *The Saliok National High School in Barangay Saliok,* Municipality of Natonin, Mountain Province is hereby converted into a national technical-vocational high school to be known as Saliok National Technical-Vocational and Skills Training High School.
>
> **SECTION 2.** All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Saliok National High School are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Saliok National Technical-Vocational and Skills Training High School.
>
> **SECTION 3.** The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Departments program the operationalization of the Saliok National Technical-Vocational and Skills Training High School, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current years appropriation of the Saliok National High School. Thereafter, the amount necessary for the continued operation of the school shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **SECTION 4.** The Secretary of Education shall issue rules and regulations necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.1âwphi1
>
> **SECTION 5.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
