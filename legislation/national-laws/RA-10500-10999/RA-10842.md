>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10842**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE HULO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY HULO, CITY OF MANDALUYONG INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS HULO INTEGRATED SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** The Hulo Elementary School in Barangay Hulo, City of Mandaluyong is hereby converted into an integrated school to be known as Hulo Integrated School.
>
> **SECTION 2.** All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Hulo Elementary School are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Hulo Integrated School.1âwphi1
>
> **SECTION 3.** The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Departments program the operationalization of the Hulo Integrated School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **SECTION 4.** The Secretary of Education shall issue rules and regulations necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **SECTION 5.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
