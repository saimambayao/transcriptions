# Republic Act No. 10833

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE BENTUCO HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BENTUCO, MUNICIPALITY OF GUBAT, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS BENTUCO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10833**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE BENTUCO HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BENTUCO, MUNICIPALITY OF GUBAT, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS BENTUCO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** *The Bentuco High School in Barangay Bentuco,* Municipality of Gubat, Province of Sorsogon is hereby converted into a national high school to be known as Bentuco National High School.
>
> **SECTION 2.** All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Bentuco High School are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Bentuco National High School.
>
> **SECTION 3.** The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Departments program the operationalization of the Bentuco National High School, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current years appropriation of the Bentuco High School. Thereafter, the amount necessary for the continued operation of the school shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **SECTION 4.** The Secretary of Education shall issue rules and regulations necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **SECTION 5.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.1âwphi1
