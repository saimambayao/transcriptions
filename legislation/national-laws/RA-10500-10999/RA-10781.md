# Republic Act No. 10781

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE CITY OF IMUS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.**

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10781, May 03, 2016 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE CITY OF IMUS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the supervision of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) Main Office in Quezon City, a regular district office of the LTO in the City of Imus, Province of Cavite.ᇈWᑭHIL
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Transportation and Communications shall immediately include int eh Department's programs the operationalization of the LTO Regular District Office in the City of Imus, Province of Cavite, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
>
