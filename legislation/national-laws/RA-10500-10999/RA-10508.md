# Republic Act No. 10508

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MALALIM ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MALALIM, CITY OF BATANGAS, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS TO EULALIA TALBAN EBORA MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> Fifteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand twelve.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10508**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MALALIM ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MALALIM, CITY OF BATANGAS, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS TO EULALIA TALBAN EBORA MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Renaming of an Elementary School.*  The name of Malalim Elementary School in Barangay Malalim, City of Batangas, Province of Batangas is hereby changed to Eulalia Talban Ebora Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** *Location of the Main Campus.*  The main campus of the Eulalia Talban Ebora Memorial Elementary School shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Malalim, City of Batangas, Province of Batangas.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 3.** *Effectivity.*  This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette.*
>
