# Republic Act No. 10782

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10782, May 03, 2016 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ORANI, PROVINCE OF BATAAN AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congree assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** Theres shall be established, under the Department of Transportation and Communications, a regular district office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Orani, Province of Bataan.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Transportation and Communications shall immediately include in the Department's programs the operationalization of the LTO Regular District's Office in the Municipality of Orani, Province of Bataan, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in two (2) newspapers of general circulation.ℒαwρhi৷
