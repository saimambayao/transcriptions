# Republic Act No. 10812

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10812, May 11, 2016 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN RAQUE, ZAMBOANGA CITY TO BE KNOWN AS SAN ROQUE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established, under the suprevision of the Department of Education, a national high school in Barangay San Roque, Zamboanga City to be known as San Roque National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the San Roque National High School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of Education shall issue rules and regulations necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
