> Seventeenth Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10980**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MANLABANG NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MANLABANG, MUNICIPALITY OF CAIBIRAN, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN, TO CAIBIRAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the* *Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Manlabang National High School in Barangay Manlabang, Municipality of Caibiran, Province of Biliran, is hereby changed to Caibiran National High School.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The main campus of the Caibiran National High School shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Manlabang, Municipality of Caibiran, Province of Biliran.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.
