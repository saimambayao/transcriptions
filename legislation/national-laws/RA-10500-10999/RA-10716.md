# Republic Act No. 10716

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BATASAN HILLS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BATASAN HILLS, CITY OF QUEZON, METRO MANILA TO CORAZON C. AQUINO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2015/ra_10716_2015.html)

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10716**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BATASAN HILLS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BATASAN HILLS, CITY OF QUEZON, METRO MANILA TO CORAZON C. AQUINO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **SECTION 1.** *Renaming of a National High School.*  The name of Batasan Hills National High School in Barangay Batasan Hills, City of Quezon, Metro Manila is hereby changed to Corazon C. Aquino National High School.
>
> **SECTION 2.** *Location of the Main Campus.*  The main campus of the Corazon C. Aquino National High School shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Batasan Hills, City of Quezon, Metro Manila.
>
> **SECTION 3.** *Effectivity.*  This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its official publication in the Official Gazette.
>
