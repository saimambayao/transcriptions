> **Sixteenth Congress
> Second Regular Session**
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, two thousand fourteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10671**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF GUBAT DISTRICT HOSPITAL TO SALVADOR R. ENCINAS DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN GUBAT, SORSOGON
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Change of Hospital Name. * The name of Gubat District Hospital in the Municipality of Gubat, Province of Sorsogon is hereby changed to Salvador R. Encinas District Hospital.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 2.** *Effectivity.*  This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in any newspaper of general circulation.
>
