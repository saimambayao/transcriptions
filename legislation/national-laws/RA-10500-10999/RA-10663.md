# Republic Act No. 10663

**AN ACT NAMING AS THE 'PRESIDENT CORAZON C. AQUINO AVENUE' THE CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD WHICH COMMENCES AT THE JUNCTION OF THE ILO-ILO-DUMANGAS COASTAL ROAD IN BALABAGO JARO DISTRICT, ILOILO CITY, THEN FOLLOWS A GENERAL WESTWARD DIRECTION ALONG THE FLOODWAYS SOUTH BANK TOWARDS BUHANG, JARO; TACAS, JARO AND UNGKA II, PAVIA, THEN TRAVERSES SOUTHWARDS TO THE MANDURRIAO DISTRICT, ILOILO CITY-PAVIA-SAN MIGUEL TRI-BORDER, AND ENDS AT THE ILO-ILO ANTIQUE ROAD, NEAR THE CENTER OF AREVALO DISTRICT, ILOILO CITY**

> **Sixteenth Congress
> Second Regular Session**
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, two thousand fourteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10663**
>
> AN ACT NAMING AS THE 'PRESIDENT CORAZON C. AQUINO AVENUE' THE CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD WHICH COMMENCES AT THE JUNCTION OF THE ILO-ILO-DUMANGAS COASTAL ROAD IN BALABAGO JARO DISTRICT, ILOILO CITY, THEN FOLLOWS A GENERAL WESTWARD DIRECTION ALONG THE FLOODWAYS SOUTH BANK TOWARDS BUHANG, JARO; TACAS, JARO AND UNGKA II, PAVIA, THEN TRAVERSES SOUTHWARDS TO THE MANDURRIAO DISTRICT, ILOILO CITY-PAVIA-SAN MIGUEL TRI-BORDER, AND ENDS AT THE ILO-ILO ANTIQUE ROAD, NEAR THE CENTER OF AREVALO DISTRICT, ILOILO CITY
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** A four (4)-lane circumferential road that spans 14.3217 kilometers and commences at the junction of the Ilo-ilo Dumangas Coastal Road in Balabago, Jaro District, Iloilo City, then follows a general westward direction along the floodway's south bank towards Buhang, Jaro; Tacas, Jaro and Ungka II, Pavia, then traverses southwards to the Mandurriao District Iloilo City-Pavia-San Miguel tri-border, and ends at the Iloilo-Antique Road, near the center of Arevalo District, Iloilo City, shall be named as the ***"President Corazon C. Aquino Avenue".***
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provisions of this Act.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
