> An Act Creating a Barangay to be Known as Barangay San Isidro in the Municipality of Titay, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Creation of Barangay San Isidro.* - There is hereby created a barangay to be known as Barangay San Isidro in the Municipality of Titay, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.
>

> **Section 3.** *Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite.* - The plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission on Elections (COMELEC) pursuant to Provincial Ordinance No: 2002-122 dated August 7, 2002 of the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of the Province of Zamboanga Sibugay, which ratified the creation of Barangay San Isidro as proclaimed by the Plebiscite Board of Canvassers on April 16, 2005, shall serve as substantial compliance with the plebiscite requirement under Section 10 of Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as the "Local Government Code of 1991".
>
> **Section 4.** *Incumbent Barangay Officials.* - The incumbent barangay officials of Barangay San Isidro shall continue to hold office until such time that their successors have been duly elected and qualified.
>
> **Section 5.** *Public Infrastructure and Facilities.* - All public infrastructure and facilities for public use existing therein at the time of the approval of this Act are hereby transferred without cost or compensation to and shall be administered by the new barangay.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 6.** *Internal Revenue Allotment Shares.* - Barangay San Isidro shall be entitled to Internal Revenue Allotment (IRA) shares pursuant to Section 285 of Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as the "*Local Government Code of 1991*".
>
> **Section 7.** *Effectivity.* - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
