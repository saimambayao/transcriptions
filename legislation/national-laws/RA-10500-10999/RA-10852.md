# Republic Act No. 10852

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY STO. NIÑO, PARAÑAQUE CITY TO BE KNOWN AS STO. NINO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

## Metadata

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10852**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY STO. NIÑO, PARAÑAQUE CITY TO BE KNOWN AS STO. NINO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** There is hereby established, under the supervision of the Department of Education, a national high school in Barangay Sto. Niño, Parañaque City to be known as Sto. Niño National High School.
>
> **SECTION 2.** The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Departments program the operationalization of the Sto. Niño National High School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **SECTION 3.** The Secretary of Education shall issue rules and regulations necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **SECTION 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
