# Republic Act No. 10849

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ALCANTARA NATIONAL TRADE SCHOOL TO ROMBLON NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 10849
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2016/ra_10849_2016.html)

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10849**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ALCANTARA NATIONAL TRADE SCHOOL TO ROMBLON NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** *Renaming of a National Trade School.* -The name of Alcantara National Trade School in the Municipality of Alcantara, Province of Romblon is hereby changed to Romblon National Institute of Technology.
>
> **SECTION 2.** *Location of the Main Campus.*  The main campus of the Romblon National Institute of Technology shall continue to remain in its present location in the Municipality of Alcantara, Province of Romblon.
>
> **SECTION 3.** *Effectivity.*  This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.1âwphi1
