> **AN ACT SEPARATING THE PANAGAAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL  PARAISO ANNEX IN BARANGAY PANAGAAMN, MUNICIPALITY OF MAHAYAG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR FROM THE PANAGAAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, CONVERTING IT INTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS PARAISO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The Panangaan National High School  Paraiso Annex in Barangay Panagaan, Municipality of Mahayag, Province of Zamboanga del Sur is hereby separated from the Panagaan National High School and converted into an independent national high school to be known as Paraiso National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Panagaan National High School  Paraiso Annex are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Paraiso National High School.
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of shall be charged against the current year's appropriation of the Panagaan Nationa High School  Paraiso Annex. Thereafter, the amount necessary for the continued operation of the school shall included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 4.**The Secretary of Education shall issue rules and regulations necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
