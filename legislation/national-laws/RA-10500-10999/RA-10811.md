# Republic Act No. 10811

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF JUAN LUMOSAD MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL - MONTE ALEGRE ANNEX IN BARANGAY MONTHE ALEGRE, MUNICIPALITY OF AURORA, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR TO MONTE ALEGRE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10811, May 11, 2016 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF JUAN LUMOSAD MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL - MONTE ALEGRE ANNEX IN BARANGAY MONTHE ALEGRE, MUNICIPALITY OF AURORA, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR TO MONTE ALEGRE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Juan Lumosad Memorial National High School - Monte Alegre Annex in Barangay Monte Alegre, Municipality of Aurora, Province of Zamboanga del Sure is hereby changed to Monte Alegre National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The main campus of the Monte Alegre National High School shall continue to remain in tis present location in Barangay Monte Alegre, Municipality of Aurora, Province of Zamboanga del Sur.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
