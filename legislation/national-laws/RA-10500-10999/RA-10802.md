> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10802, May 11, 2016 ]**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING JULY 4 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY AND NO CLASS DAY IN SCHOOLS IN THE PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDATION DAY**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** July 4 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday and no class day in schools in the Province of Lanao del Sure in commemoration ot its foundation day.
>
> **Section 2.** For work performed on a special working holiday, an employee is entitled to his/her regular wage.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
