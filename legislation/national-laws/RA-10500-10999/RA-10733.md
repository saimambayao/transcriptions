# Republic Act No. 10733

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE ANDRES BONIFACIO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY JALANDONI-WILSON, CITY PROPER DISTRICT, ILOILO CITY INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS ANDRES BONIFACIO INTEGRATED SCHOOL**

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10733**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE ANDRES BONIFACIO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY JALANDONI-WILSON, CITY PROPER DISTRICT, ILOILO CITY INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS ANDRES BONIFACIO INTEGRATED SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Confess assembled:
>
> **SECTION 1.** *Conversion into an Integrated School.*  The Andres Bonifacio Elementary School in Barangay Jalandoni-Wilson, City Proper District, Iloilo City is hereby converted into an integrated school to be known as Andres Bonifacio Integrated School.
>
> **SECTION 2.** *Transfer of Assets and Liabilities.*  All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Andres Bonifacio Elementary School are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Andres Bonifacio Integrated School.
>
> **SECTION 3.** *Appropriations.*  The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Departments program the operationalization of the Andres Bonifacio Integrated School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **SECTION 4.** *Implementation.*  The Secretary of Education shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **SECTION 5.** *Effectivity.*  This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
>
