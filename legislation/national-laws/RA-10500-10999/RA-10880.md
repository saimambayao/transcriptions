> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10880**
>
> **An Act Creating an Additional Branch of the Regional Trial Court with Seat at Calapan City, Oriental Mindoro in the Fourth Judicial Region, Further Amending for the Purpose Batas Pambansa Blg. 129, Otherwise Known as "The Judiciary Reorganization Act of 1980", as Amended, and Appropriating Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** An additional branch of the Regional Trial Court with seat at Calapan City, Oriental Mindoro in the Fourth Judicial Region is hereby created.
>
> **Section 2.** The Supreme Court shall assign the branch number of the newly created Regional Trial Court branch under this Act.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The Chief Justice of the Supreme Court, in coordination with the Secretary of the Department of Justice, shall immediately include in the Courts program the operationalization of the newly created Regional Trial Court branch under this Act, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act: *Provided,* That the funds necessary for its operation shall be appropriated and released only upon its actual organization and the appointment of its personnel.
>
> **Section 4.** Section 14(e) of Batas Pambansa Big. 129, otherwise known as "The Judiciary Reorganization Act of 1980", as amended, and all other laws, executive orders, and rules and regulations which are inconsistent with this Act are hereby amended, repealed, or modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 5.** *Effectivity.* - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
>
