> Seventeenth Congress
> First Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, two thousand sixteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10936**
>
> An Act Creating a Barangay to be Known as Barangay Comawas in the City of Bislig, Province of Surigao del Sur
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Creation of Barangay Comawas.* - There is hereby created a barangay to be known as Barangay Comawas in the City of Bislig, Province of Surigao del Sur.
>

> **Section 2.** *Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite.* - The plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission on Elections (COMELEC) pursuant to Provincial Ordinance No. 103-94 of the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of the Province of Surigao del Sur, which ratified the creation of Barangay Comawas as proclaimed by the Plebiscite Board of Canvassers on September 9, 1996, shall serve as a substantial compliance with the plebiscite requirement under Section 10 of Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as the "Local Government Code of l991".
>
> **Section 3.** *Incumbent Barangay Officials.* - The incumbent barangay officials of Barangay Comawas, City of Bislig, Province of Surigao del Sur shall continue to hold office until such time that their successors have been duly elected and qualified.
>
> **Section 4.** *Public Infrastructure and Facilities.* - All public infrastructure and facilities for public use existing therein at the time of the approval of this Act are hereby transferred without cost or compensation to and shall be administered by the new barangay.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 5.** *Internal Revenue Allotment Shares.* - Barangay Coma was shall be entitled to Internal Revenue Allotment shares pursuant to Section 285 of Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as the "Local Government Code of 1991".
>
> **Section 6.** *Effectivity.* - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in any newspaper of general circulation.
