# Republic Act No. 10828

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE PROVINCE OF SARANGANI AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Sixteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10828**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE PROVINCE OF SARANGANI AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** There shall be established in the Municipality of Alabel, Province of Sarangani a Regular District Office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO).1âwphi1
>
> **SECTION 2.** The Secretary of Transportation and Communications shall include in the Departments program the operationalization of the Regular District Office of the LTO in the Municipality of Alabel, Province of Sarangani, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **SECTION 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
>
