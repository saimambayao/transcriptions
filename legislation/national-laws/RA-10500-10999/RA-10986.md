> Seventeenth Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 10986**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BANAHAO, CITY OF BAYBAY, PROVINCE OF LEYTE TO BE KNOWN AS BANAHAO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the* *Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established, under the supervision of the Department of Education, a national high school in Barangay Banahao, City of Baybay, Province of Leyte to be known as Banahao National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the Banahao National High School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.1âwphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations implementing the provisions of this Act within one hundred twenty (120) days after its effectivity.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette* or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
