> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2128**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of New Piñan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios are constituted into barrios in the Municipality of New Piñan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte:
>
> 1. The sitios of Desin, Upper Disoy, San Fernando, San Vicente, Santa Fe, Sikitan, Dewacat, Dilawa and Lambog to be known as the barrio of Villarico; and
>
> 2. The sitios of Upper Boboringan, Lower Boboringan, Penacio, Bagong Silang and Situbo to be known as the barrio of Teresita.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 7, 1959.
