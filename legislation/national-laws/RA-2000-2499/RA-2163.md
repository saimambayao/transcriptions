> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2163**
>
> **An Act Transferring the Seat of Government of the Municipality of Hagonoy, Province of Davao, to the Site of the Barrio of Pawa**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The seat of government of the Municipality of Hagonoy, Province of Davao, is hereby transferred to the site of the barrio of Pawa.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
