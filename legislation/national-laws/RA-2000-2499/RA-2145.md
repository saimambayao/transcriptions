> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2145**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Tigbao, Nato, Pangalalan and Maras in the Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Tigbao, Nato, Pangalalan and Maras, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
