> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2238**
>
> **An Act to Authorize the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office to Hold a Lottery for the Benefit of the "Asociacion De Damas De Filipinas"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office is authorized to hold a lottery, the whole proceeds of which shall, after deducting the payment of the prizes and expenses as provided in Republic Act Numbered Eleven hundred sixty-nine, as amended, be turned over to the "Asociacion de Damas de Filipinas" for the maintenance of its settlement house.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1959.
