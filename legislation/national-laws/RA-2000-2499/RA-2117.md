> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2117**
>
> **An Act Transferring the Seat of Government of the Municipality of San Pablo, Province of Isabela, to the Site in the Old Poblacion Commonly Known as Centro**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The seat of government of the Municipality of San Pablo, Province of Isabela, is transferred to the site in the old poblacion commonly known as Centro in the same municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** Republic Act Numbered Eleven hundred seventy-seven is hereby repealed.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 5, 1959.
