> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2455**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (g) Group II, Page 29, Subparagraph (10), for the Province of Bulacan, Paragraph "f", Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (g), group II, page 29, subparagraph (10), for the Province of Bulacan, paragraph "f," section two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(g) Sta. Maria Public Market Acquisition of site and construction       ₱15,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
