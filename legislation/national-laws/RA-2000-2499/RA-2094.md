> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2094**
>
> **An Act Defining the Boundaries of the Municipality of Hagonoy, Province of Davao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The boundaries of the Municipality of Hagonoy, Province of Davao, shall be, on the north, and imaginary line cutting the Digos-Malalag-Makar National Road at Km. 311, running due west from the shoreline of the Davao Gulf up to an imaginary north-south line with longitude 125º 14� E., thence due south along this line to its intersection with an imaginary line running due west from the shoreline of the Davao Gulf and cutting the Digos-Malalag-Makar National Road at Km. 318.1, so that the territory of said municipality shall include the barrios of Kibuaya, Upper Sacub, Lower Sacub, Maliit-Digos, La Union, Malabang, Tulogan, Malinao, Guihing, Pawa, Hagonoy, Balutakay, Leling, and Sinayawan, and the sitios of Quezon and Polopolo.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: March 3, 1959.
