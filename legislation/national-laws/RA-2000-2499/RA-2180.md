> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2180**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Briones in the Municipality of Kalibo, Province of Aklan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Eu-ok in the barrio of Mabilo, Municipality of Kalibo, Province of Aklan, is separated from said barrio, and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Briones.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 2, 1959.
