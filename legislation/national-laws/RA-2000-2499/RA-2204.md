> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2204**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Polanco, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Polanco, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted as barrios thereof, to wit:
>
> 1. The sitios of Lantoy, Dalemaso, Banban, Apil, Salvar and Macasawad to be known as the barrio of Lantoy;
>
> 2. The sitios of Magangon, Sianib B and Sialo to be known as the barrio of Magangon; and
>
> 3. The sitios of Lapayanbaba, Bolagoc, Tena-an, Gosa wan, Miasi, Lobayan, Dewacat, Galematok, Sekot and Lenabo to be known as the barrio of Lapayanbaba.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 7, 1959.
