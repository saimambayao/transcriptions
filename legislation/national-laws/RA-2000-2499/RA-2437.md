> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2437**
>
> **An Act Converting the Gonzaga High School in the Province of Cagayan into a National Rural High School of the Alcala Rural High School Type to be Known as the Gonzaga National Rural High School and Authorizing the Appropriation of the Sum of Seventy-Five Thousand Pesos for the Purpose**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Gonzaga High School in the Municipality of Gonzaga, Province of Cagayan, is hereby converted into a national rural high school of the Alcala Rural High School type to be known as the Gonzaga National Rural High School. The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to reorganize the said school accordingly.
>
> Upon the organization of the Gonzaga National Rural High School, the assets, liabilities, equipment and record of the Gonzaga High School shall be transferred to the Gonzaga National Rural High School .
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of seventy-five thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the Gonzaga National Rural High School for the first year nineteen hundred fifty-nine to nineteen hundred sixty. Thereafter, the necessary sum for the operation of the said school shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon the date the funds authorized herein to be appropriated shall have been provided for.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
