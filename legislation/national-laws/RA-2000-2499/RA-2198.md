> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2198**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of the Bago Bantay Elementary School in Quezon City to Esteban Abada Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Bago Bantay Elementary School in Quezon City is hereby changed to Esteban Abada Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 7, 1959.
