> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2196**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Kiug-Ang, Municipality of Jasaan, Province of Misamis Oriental, to Luz**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Kiug-ang in the Municipality of Jasaan, Province of Misamis Oriental is changed to Luz, to wit: Barrio Luz, Municipality of Jasaan, Province to Misamis Oriental.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 7, 1959.
