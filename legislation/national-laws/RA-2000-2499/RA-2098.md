> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2098**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Bagong Silang, Municipality of San Miguel, Province of Bulacan, into a Barrio to be Known as the Barrio of Bagong Silang**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Bagong Silang, Municipality of San Miguel, Province of Bulacan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Bagong Silang.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: March 28, 1959.
