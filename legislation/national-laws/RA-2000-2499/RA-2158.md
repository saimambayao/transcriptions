> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2158**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Jones, Province of Romblon, to Municipality of Banton**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Jones, Province of Romblon, is changed to Municipality of Banton.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
