> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2164**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Patnongon, Province of Antique**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Patnongon, Province of Antique, are constituted as distinct and independent barrios, to wit:
>
> 1. The sitios of Magranting, Tigangcal, Agsaman, Epgas, Epayo and Eglapang to be known as the barrio of Alvañiz;
>
> 2. The sitios of Igtuba Maradiona and Igtuba Aureliana to be known as the barrio of Magsaysay;
>
> 3. The sitios of Amarong, Tagbacan, Da-o, Yapo and Embalodan to be known as the barrio of Elio; and
>
> 4. The sitios of Tigabobo, Tiglomabong, Maybangcal, Calagtiñgan and Bongboñgan to be known as the barrio of Vista Alegre.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
