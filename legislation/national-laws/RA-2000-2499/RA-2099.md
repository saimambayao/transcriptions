> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2099**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Mangaratungat in the Municipality of Jones, Province of Isabela, to San Vicente**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio Mangaratungat in the Municipality of Jones, Province of Isabela, is hereby changed to San Vicente.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: March 28, 1959.
