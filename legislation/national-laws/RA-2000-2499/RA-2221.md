> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2221**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Manyaya in the Municipality of Murcia, Province of Negros Occidental**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Manyaya in the barrio of Sta. Rosa, Municipality of Murcia, Province of Negros Occidental, is separated from said barrio and converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Manyaya.1aшphi1
>
> The boundary of the proposed barrio of Manyaya and the barrio of Sta. Rosa, Municipality of Murcia, Province of Negros Occidental, shall be the Puncian River.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 16, 1959.
