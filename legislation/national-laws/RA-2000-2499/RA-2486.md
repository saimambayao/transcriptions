> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2486**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Icogan in the Municipality of Looc, Province of Romblon, to San Isidro**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Icogan in the Municipality of Looc, Province of Romblon, is changed to San Isidro.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
