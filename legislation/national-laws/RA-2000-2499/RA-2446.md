> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2446**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items in Section One, Republic Act Numbered Two Thousand Ninety-Three**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Items (m) and (n), for the Province of Leyte, sub-paragraph twenty-three, paragraph a, Title D, section one of Republic Act Numbered Two thousand ninety-three, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(m) San Roque Waterworks, Macrohon       ₱5,000.00
> >
> > "(n) San Isidro Waterworks, Malitbog       20,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
