> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2477**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of San Isidro in the Municipality of Calolbon, Province of Catanduanes**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of San Isidro in the Municipality of Calolbon, Province of Catanduanes, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Isidro.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
