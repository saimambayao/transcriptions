> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2096**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Uldogan in the Municipality of San Agustin, Province of Isabela, to Laoag**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Uldogan in the Municipality of San Agustin, Province of Isabela, is changed to Laoag.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: March 22, 1959.
