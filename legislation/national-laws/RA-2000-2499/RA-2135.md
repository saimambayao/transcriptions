> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2135**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Santo Tomas in the Municipality of Tingloy, Province of Batangas**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Pook and Ricodo are separated from the poblacion of the Municipality of Tingloy, Province of Batangas, and the sitio of Pulang-Buli is separated from the barrio of Talahib of the same municipality, and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Santo Tomas.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 7, 1959.
