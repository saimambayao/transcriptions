> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2103**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Cagayancillo, Province of Palawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Cagayancillo, Province of Palawan, are constituted into barrios:
>
> 1. The sitios of Banted, Nipay, Dool, Cavili, Cabayog and Langtad to be known as the barrio of Talaga:
>
> 2. The sitios of Ibiok, Panderse, Lato and Gatung-an to be known as the barrio of Mampio; and
>
> 3. The sitio of Nusa to be known as the barrio of Nusa.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 3, 1959.
