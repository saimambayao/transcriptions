> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2014**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (a), Group IV, Subparagraph 28, for the City of Manila, Paragraph "f," Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven, and to Add Thereto a New Item**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (a), page eighty-six, group IV, subparagraph twenty-eight, for the City of Manila, paragraph "f," section two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(a) Public Baths and Toilets, 4th District Construction | ₱15,000.00." |
>
> **Section 2.** Between items (a) and (b), page eighty-six, group IV, subparagraph twenty-eight, for the City of Manila, paragraph "f," section two of the same Act, the following new item is inserted:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(a-1) Araullo High School, Isaac Peral Branch Construction of fence | ₱20,000.00" |
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
