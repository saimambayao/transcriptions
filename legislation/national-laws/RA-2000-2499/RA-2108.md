> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2108**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Del Gallego, Province of Camarines Sur**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios are converted into barrios of the Municipality of Del Gallego, Province of Camarines Sur:
>
> 1. The sitio of Sinuknipan to be known as the barrio of Sinuknipan;
>
> 2. The sitio of Sabang to be known as the barrio of Sabang;
>
> 3. The sitio of Pasay to be known as the barrio of Pasay; and
>
> 4. The sitio of Bajo to be known as the barrio of San Juan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 3, 1959.
