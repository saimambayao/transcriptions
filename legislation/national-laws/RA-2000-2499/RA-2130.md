> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2130**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Butubut in the Municipality of Magsingal, Province of Ilocos Sur, to Barrio Miramar**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Butubut in the Municipality of Magsingal, Province of Ilocos Sur, is changed to barrio Miramar.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 7, 1959.
