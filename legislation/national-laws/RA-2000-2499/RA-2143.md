> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2143**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Bulan, Province of Sorsogon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Cabogaan, San Juan Daan, Cadandanan, Tanga, Togbogñon and Bagñog in the Municipality of Bulan, Province of Sorsogon, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Cabogaan, San Juan Daan, Cadandanan, Tanga, San Rafael and Magsaysay, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
