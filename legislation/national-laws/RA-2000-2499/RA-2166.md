> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2166**
>
> **An Act to Constitute the Sitios of Bugho, Seco, and Cuatro, All in the Municipality of Basey, Province of Samar, into a Barrio to be Known as Barrio Tingib of the Same Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Bugho, Seco and Cuatro, all in the Municipality of Basey, Province of Samar, are hereby constituted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Tingib of the same municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
