> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2222**
>
> **An Act Changing to Marilag the Name of Barrio Pinagkaanuran in the Municipality of Rosario, Province of Batangas**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Pinagkaanuran in the Municipality of Rosario, Province of Batangas, is hereby changed to Marilag.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 16, 1959.
