> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2184**
>
> **An Act to Amend Section One Hundred Four, Schedule XV, Chapter 83, Heading Numbered 83.07 and 83.15 of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred and Thirty-Seven, Otherwise Known as the Tariff and Customs Code of the Philippines**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section one hundred four, Schedule XV, Chapter 83, Heading 83.07 of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred and thirty-seven is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |  |  |
> | --- | --- | --- | --- |
> | "83.07 Lamps and Lighting fitting and parts thereof, of base metal (excluding switches, electric lamp holders, electric lamps for vehicles, electric battery or magneto lamps, and other articles falling within Chapter 85): | | | |
> |  | A. Kerosene air pressure lamps and parts thereof: | | |
> |  |  | 1. Assembled | ad val. 65% |
> |  |  | 2. Unassembled | ad val. 20% |
> |  | B. Miners lamps and parts thereof | | ad val. 35% |
> |  | C. Others | | ad val. 100%" |
>
> **Section 2.** Section one hundred four, Schedule XV, Chapter 83, Heading 83.15 of the same Act is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |  |
> | --- | --- | --- |
> | "83.15 Welding electrodes, rods or tubes, solder in wire or roads, coated or cored with flux materials; prepared metal brazing plate of a kind used in metallurgy: | | |
> |  | A. Mild steel welding electrodes, coated with flux | ad val. 100% |
> |  | B. Other | ad val. 10%" |
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 6, 1959.
