> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2401**
>
> **An Act Repealing Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred Seventy-Five**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred seventy-five, entitle "An Act to grant to Amador Dayola and R. Mulet (partners) a franchise for an electric light, heat and power system in the Municipality of Panabo, Province of Davao," is hereby repealed.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
