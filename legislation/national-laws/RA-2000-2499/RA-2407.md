> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2407**
>
> **An Act Making the Sitios of Mabini in the Municipality of Matag-ob, Province of Leyte, a Part of the Municipality of Palompon, Same Province**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Mabini in the Municipality of Matag-ob, Province of Leyte, is separated from said municipality and made a part of the barrio of Santiago, Municipality of Palompon, same province.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
