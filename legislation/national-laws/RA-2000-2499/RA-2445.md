> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2445**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (17), Page 4, Section 1-A-a, Title (A) of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (17), page 4, section 1-A-a, Title (A) of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(17) Industrial Arts Annex, Iloilo Vocational High School-Lambunao       ₱20,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
