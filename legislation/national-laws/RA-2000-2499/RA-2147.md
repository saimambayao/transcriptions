> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2147**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Cogon in the Municipality of Dipolog, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Cogon in the Municipality of Dipolog, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Cogon.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
