> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2350**
>
> **An Act Appropriating the Sum of Eight Million Two Hundred Thirty-Eight Thousand Three Hundred Four Pesos for the Current Operation and Maintenance of the Three Thousand Five Hundred Twenty Additional Extension Classes Organized in August and November, Nineteen Hundred and Fifty-Eight**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of eight million two hundred thirty-eight thousand three hundred four pesos, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is thereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated for the operation and maintenance of the three thousand five hundred twenty additional elementary classes organized in August and November, nineteen hundred and fifty-eight, up to the end of the current fiscal year, in the following manner and form:
>

> | Life insurance premiums, government share, at three per centum of salary | 185,568 |
> | Retirement insurance premiums, government share, at six per centum of salary | 371,136 |
> | For the purchase of textbooks, at ₱225 per class | 792,000 |
