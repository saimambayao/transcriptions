> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2213**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Ragan Norte Elementary School in the Municipality of Magsaysay, Province of Isabela, to San Ramon Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Ragan Norte Elementary School in the Municipality of Magsaysay, Province of Isabela, is hereby changed to San Ramon Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 16, 1959.
