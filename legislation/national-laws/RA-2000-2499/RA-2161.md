> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2161**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitios of Turod, Paco, Calamagui and Kim-Malabasa in the Municipality of Magsaysay, Province of Isabela, into Barrios**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Turod, Paco, Calamagui and Kim-malabasa in the Municipality of Magsaysay, Province of Isabela, are hereby converted into barrios.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
