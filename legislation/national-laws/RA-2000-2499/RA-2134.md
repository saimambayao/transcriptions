> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2134**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Manggahan in the Barrio of San Martin, Municipality of sa Luis, Province of Batangas, into a Barrio**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Manggahan in the barrio of San Martin, Municipality of San Luis, Province of Batangas, is hereby separated from the said barrio and converted into a new barrio to be known as the barrio of Manggahan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 7, 1959.
