> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2416**
>
> **An Act Converting the Bulacan National Agricultural High School into a National Agricultural School to be Known as the Bulacan National Agricultural School, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Bulacan National Agricultural High School in the Municipality of San Ildefonso, Province of Bulacan, is hereby converted into a national agricultural school of the Pampanga National Agricultural College type, to be known as the Bulacan National Agricultural School. The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to reorganize the said school accordingly.
>
> Upon the organization of the Bulacan National Agricultural School, the assets, liabilities, personnel, equipment and records of the Bulacan National Agricultural High School shall stand transferred to the Bulacan National Agricultural School.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the said school of the fiscal year nineteen hundred fifty-nine to nineteen hundred sixty. Thereafter, the necessary sum for the operation of the said school shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon the date the funds authorized herein to be appropriated are provided for.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
