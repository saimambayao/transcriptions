> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2495**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Malitlit in the City of Lipa**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Malitlit in the City of Lipa is converted into a barrio of said city to be known as the barrio of Malitlit.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
