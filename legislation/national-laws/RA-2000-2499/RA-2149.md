> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2149**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Lagnas in the Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, to San Pascual**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Lagnas in the Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, is changed to San Pascual.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
