> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2132**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Liberty in the Municipality of Palompon, Province of Leyte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Liberty, Calunasan, Cabulihan, Casagawan, Otap and Cabaliwan in the Municipality of Palompon, Province of Leyte, are constituted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Liberty.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 7, 1959.
