> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2190**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Balogo in the Municipality of San Agustin, Province of Romblon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Balogo in the barrio of Calatrava, Municipality of San Agustin, Province of Romblon, is separated from said barrio and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Balogo.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 7, 1959.
