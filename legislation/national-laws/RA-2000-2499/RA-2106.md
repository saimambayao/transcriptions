> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2106**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Kialeg in the Municipality of Bansalan, Province of Davao, to Barrio Magsaysay**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Kialeg in the Municipality of Bansalan, Province of Davao, is changed to Barrio Magsaysay.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 3, 1959.
