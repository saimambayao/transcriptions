> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2133**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Cubay Sur in the Municipality of Bugasong, Province of Antique**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Cubay Sur in the Municipality of Bugasong, Province of Antique, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Cubay Sur.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 7, 1959.
