> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2122**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Payo in the Province of Catanduanes to Panganiban**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Payo in the Province of Catanduanes is changed to Panganiban.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 5, 1959.
