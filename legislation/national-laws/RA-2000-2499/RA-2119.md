> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2119**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Dallao in the Municipality of Cordon, Province of Isabela, into a Barrio to be Known as Barrio Dallao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Dallao in the Municipality of Cordon, Province of Isabela, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as barrio Dallao.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 5, 1959.
