> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2177**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Saluyong in the Municipality of Manukan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Saluyong, Sipakong, Upper Dipani, Boob, Lupasang and Messes in the Municipality of Manukan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Saluyong.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 26, 1959.
