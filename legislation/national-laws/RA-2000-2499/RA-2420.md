> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2420**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (r), Page 77, Group II, Subparagraph (25), for the Province of Laguna, Paragraph f, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (r), page 77, group II, subparagraph (25), for the Province of Laguna, paragraph f, section two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(r) Majayjay Slaughterhouse Repair and improvement       ₱3,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
