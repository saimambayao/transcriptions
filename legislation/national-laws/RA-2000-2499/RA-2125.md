> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2125**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrios of Libertad and San Isidro in the Municipality of Dolores, Province of Samar**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following barrios are hereby created in the Municipality of Dolores, Province of Samar:
>
> (1) Barrio Libertad consisting of the sitios of Tanawan, Gape and Borac; and
>
> (2) Barrio San Isidro consisting of the sitios of Tangcop, Ojot, Japay, Deit and Maynipa.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 5, 1959.
