> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2481**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Antique School of Fisheries in the Municipality of Tibiao, Province of Antique, to Tario Lim Memorial Antique School of Fisheries**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Antique School of Fisheries in the Municipality of Tibiao, Province of Antique, is hereby changed to Tario Lim Memorial Antique School of Fisheries.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
