> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2403**
>
> **An Act Converting the Siocon High School in the Municipality of Siocon, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, into a National Vocational High School to be Known as the Siocon National Vocational High School, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Siocon High School in the Municipality of Siocon, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is hereby converted into a national vocational high school to be known as the Siocon National Vocational High School. The said school shall be under the supervision of the Bureau of Public Schools and subject to the provisions of the school law. Courses of the secondary level shall be offered in said school.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty. Such sums as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon the date the funds authorized herein to be appropriated shall have been provided for.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
