> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2126**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Tallag Barrio School in the Municipality of Cabagan, Province of Isabela, to Biuag Community School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Tallag Barrio School in the Municipality of Cabagan, Province of Isabela, is hereby changed to Biuag Community School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 7, 1959.
