> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2118**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Del Pilar in the Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Malaking Pook Ibaba is separated from the barrio of Malaking Pook, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, and is constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Del Pilar.1aшphi1

> Approved: April 5, 1959.
