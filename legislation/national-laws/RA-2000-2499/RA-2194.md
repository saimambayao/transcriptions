> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2194**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Tagabaca in the City of Butuan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Tagabaca in the City of Butuan is converted into a barrio of said city to be known as the barrio of Tagabaca.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 7, 1959.
