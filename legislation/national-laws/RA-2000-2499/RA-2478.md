> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2478**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of Campaclan Primary School, Sibulan, Negros Oriental, to Magsaysay Memorial School, Sibulan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Campaclan Primary School at Sibulan, Negros Oriental, is changed to Magsaysay Memorial School, Sibulan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
