> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2151**
>
> **An Act to Provide that the Seat of Government of the Municipality of Aglipay, Nueva Vizcaya, be Located at Guinalbin**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The seat of government of the Municipality of Aglipay, Nueva Vizcaya, shall be located at Guinalbin.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
