> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2131**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Bunog in the Municipality of Quezon, Province of Palawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Isugod, Canikitan, Bunog and Tagbuaya in the Municipality of Quezon, Province of Palawan, are constituted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Bunog.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 7, 1959.
