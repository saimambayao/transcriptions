> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2121**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Bonsoran in the Barrio of Ginag-Oman, Municipality of Odiongan, Province of Romblon, into a Barrio of Said Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Bonsoran in the barrio of Ginag-oman, Municipality of Odiongan, Province of Romblon, is converted into a barrio of said municipality with the following as boundaries: on the north by the Agnocnoc River, on the east by the mountain range starting from Macatol Point to Laposlapos Point, on the west by the sea, and on the south by the Looc Bay.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 5, 1959.
