> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2160**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Bolobolo in the Municipality of Jabonga, Province of Agusan, to San Vicente**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Bolobolo in the Municipality of Jabonga, Province of Agusan, is changed to San Vicente.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
