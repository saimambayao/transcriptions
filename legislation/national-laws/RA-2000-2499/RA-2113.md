> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2113**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Tible in the Municipality of Sipocot, Province of Camarines Sur**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Tigman, Belnas, Cacao, and Binatagan in the Municipality of Sipocot, Province of Camarines Sur, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Tible.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 5, 1959.
