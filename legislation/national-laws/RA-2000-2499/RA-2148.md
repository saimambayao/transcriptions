> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2148**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Bayakbakin in the Municipality of Torrijos, Province of Marinduque**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Bayakbakin in the Municipality of Torrijos, Province of Marinduque, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Bayakbakin.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
