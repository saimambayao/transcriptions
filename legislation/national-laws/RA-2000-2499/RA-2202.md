> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2202**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of San Nicolas in the Municipality of Dapitan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of San Nicolas, Domonob, Barita, Malobog, Pangi and Lo-ok in the Municipality of Dapitan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Nicolas.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 7, 1959.
