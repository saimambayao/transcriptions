> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2444**
>
> **An Act Amending Item 81, for the Province of Laguna, Page 162, Paragraph g, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item 81, for the Province of Laguna, page 162, paragraph g, section two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "81. Santa Cruz Town Hall, Sta. Cruz       ₱10,000.00."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
