> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2018**
>
> **An Act Converting the Jones High School in the Province of Isabela into a Rural High School, to be Known as the Jones Rural High School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Jones High School in the Province of Isabela is hereby converted into a rural high school, to be known as the Jones Rural High School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of said school for the school year nineteen hundred fifty-seven to nineteen hundred fifty-eight. Thereafter, the necessary sum for said purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
