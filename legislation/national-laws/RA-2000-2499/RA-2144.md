> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2144**
>
> **An Act to Create Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Santa Fe, Province of Romblon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following barrios in the Municipality of Santa Fe, Province of Romblon, are created:
>
> (a) Barrio Magsaysay to be composed of the present sitios of Catolog, Palate, Layog, Simbiray and Mambong:
>
> (b) Barrio Mat-i to be composed of the present sitios of Mat-i, Guinpasilan, Bolagsong, Aglagtang and Isabela;
>
> (c) Barrio Agmanic to be composed of the present sitios of Agmanic, Capdang, Tipolo-Bahol, Bolocawe, Binaloca and Cabali-an;
>
> (d) Barrio Tabugon to be composed of the present sitios of Tabugon, Cabungahan, Pitugo, Guinpoyuñgan, Grande, Cahawagan and Hinaclopan;
>
> (e) Barrio Canyayo to be composed of the present sitios of Canyayo, Lonoc, Campong, Payong-payong and Capid;
>
> (f) Barrio Pandan to be composed of the present sitios of Pandan, Sayao, Guinda, Camonsel, Capinayan, Baybay, Candoyong, Lojo and Catopaz;
>
> (g) Barrio Danao Norte to be composed of the present sitios of Danao Norte, Hacienda, Sabang, Timba-on, Danao Sur and Bunga;
>
> (h) Barrio Pinamihagan to be composed of the present sitios of Pinamihagan, Pacol, Buyahon, Sugod, Pinamandayan, Pajo and Angas; and
>
> (i) Barrio Busay to be composed of the present sitios of Busay, Hinologan, Buenavista, Nausa, Tipocpoc, Agborot, Boyongboyoñgan and Mihason.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
