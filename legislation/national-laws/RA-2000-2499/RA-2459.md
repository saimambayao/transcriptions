> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2459**
>
> **An Act Amending Certain Items of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred, for the Province of Cebu**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (u), for the Province of Cebu, page 259, paragraph f, section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(u1) Perrelos Elementary School, Carcar Construction of one-room standard shop building, Noel type-size: 6.90 × 8.55 M 5,000.00
> >
> > "(u2) Tuyan Elementary School, Naga Construction of one-room standard shop building, Noel type-size: 6.90 × 8.55 M 5,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** Item (au), for the Province of Cebu, page 260, paragraph "f" of section two, Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(au1) Tabunoc Elementary School, Talisay Construction of one-room standard shop building, Noel type-size: 6.90 × 8.55 M 5,000.00
> >
> > "(au2) Lipata Elementary School, Minglanilla Construction of one-room standard shop building, Noel type-size: 6.90 × 8.55 M 5,000.00"
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
