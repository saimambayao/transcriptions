> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2114**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Bajo Barrio School in the Municipality of Del Gallego, Province of Camarines Sur, to San Juan Barrio School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Bajo Barrio School in the Municipality of Del Gallego, Province of Camarines Sur, is changed to San Juan Barrio School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 5, 1959.
