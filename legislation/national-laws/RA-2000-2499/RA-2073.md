> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2073**
>
> AN ACT TO AMEND PARAGRAPHS 73.11 AND 73.21 OF CHAPTER 73, SCHEDULE XV, OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED THIRTY-SEVEN, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS TARIFF AND CUSTOMS CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
>
> **Section 1.** Paragraph 73.11 of Chapter 73, Schedule XV, of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred thirty-seven is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |  |  |
> | --- | --- | --- | --- |
> | "73.11 | Angles, shapes, sections and sheet piling, of iron or steel, hot-rolled, forged or cold-finished, whether or not drilled or punched, but not assembled: | | |
> | A. | Angles of which the maximum cross-sectional dimension does not exceed eighty-eight millimeters and channels of which the maximum cross-section dimension does not exceed seventy-five millimeters | ad val. 25% |
> | B. | Riveted, welded, punched, or drilled | ad val. 80% |
> | C. | Other | ad val. 15%." |
>
> **Section 2.** Paragraph 73.21 of Chapter 73, Schedule XV, of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred thirty-seven, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |  |
> | --- | --- | --- |
> | "73.21 | Assembled sheet pilling of cast iron, iron or steel; girders, beams, joists and pillars made from assembled elements; structures and finished parts for assembly into structures (e.g., bridge sections, roofing frameworks, shutters, pylons, balconies, balustrades, verandas, window and frames) of cast iron, iron or steel | ad val. 80%." ||
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 13, 1958
