> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2220**
>
> **An Act Transferring the Seat of Government of the Municipality of Liloy, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, from Barrio Timan to Upper Liloy Beach**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The seat of government of the Municipality of Liloy, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is hereby transferred from Barrio Timan to upper Liloy Beach.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 16, 1959.
