> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2179**
>
> **An Act to Recreate the Municipal District of Imugan, Province of Nueva Vizcaya, Which Name is to be Changed to Santa Fe**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Municipal District of Imugan in the Province of Nueva Vizcaya is hereby recreated.
>
> **Section 2.** The boundaries existing between said Municipal District of Imugan and the towns of Aritao and Kayapa before the effectivity of Executive Order Numbered Three hundred sixty-eight, series of nineteen hundred fifty, are hereby restored.
>
> **Section 3.** The name of Imugan is hereby changed to Santa Fe.
>
> **Section 4.** The seat of the municipal government shall be located in Santa Fe.
>
> **Section 5.** The officials of the recreated municipal district shall be appointed by the President of the Philippines.
>
> **Section 6.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 2, 1959.
