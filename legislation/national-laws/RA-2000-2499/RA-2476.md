> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2476**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Manlungay of the Barrio of Canaan, Municipality of Rizal, Province of Nueva Ecija, into a Barrio of the Said Municipality to be Known as Barrio Villa Paraiso**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Manlungay of the barrio of Canaan, Municipality of Rizal, Province of Nueva Ecija, is separated from said barrio of Canaan and constituted into a barrio of the said municipality to be known as Barrio Villa Paraiso.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
