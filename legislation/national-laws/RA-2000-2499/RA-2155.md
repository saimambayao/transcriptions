> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2155**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF PANTOK, MUNICIPALITY OF MEYCAUAYAN, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF PANTOK.
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Pantok, Municipality of Meycauayan, Province of Bulacan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Pantok.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
