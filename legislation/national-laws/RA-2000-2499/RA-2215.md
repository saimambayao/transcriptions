> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2215**
>
> **An Act Converting the Pototan Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Pototan, Province of Iloilo, into a General Hospital to be Known as the Pototan General Hospital**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Pototan Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Pototan, Province of Iloilo, is converted into a general hospital to be known as the Pototan General Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The expenses for the operation and maintenance shall be governed by the provisions of Republic Act Numbered One thousand nine hundred thirty-nine, otherwise known as the Hospital Financing Act: *Provided,* That the present appropriation for its operation and maintenance shall continue to be borne by the National Government and shall be increased as conditions warrant.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 16, 1959.
