> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2137**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Bagacay in the Municipality of Lapinig, Province of Samar**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Kajaguekjekan and Lobok in the Municipality of Lapinig, Province of Samar, are hereby constituted into a barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Bagacay.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 7, 1959.
