> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2100**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Putol in the Province of Nagcarlan, Province of Laguna, to Malaya**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Putol in the Municipality of Nagcarlan, Province of Laguna, is changed to Malaya.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: March 28, 1959.
