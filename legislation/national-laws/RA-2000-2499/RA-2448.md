> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2448**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (g), Page 953, Subparagraph 41, for the Province of Sorsogon, Paragraph a, Title G, Section Three of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1** Item (g), page 953, subparagraph 41, for the Province of Sorsogon, paragraph a, Title G, section three of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(g) Tuba-Malapoc Communal Irrigation Project, Donsol       ₱25,000.00"
>
> **Section 2** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
