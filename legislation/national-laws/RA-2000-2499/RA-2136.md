> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2136**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Malibayo, Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, into a Barrio to be Known as the Barrio of Malibayo**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Malibayo, barrio of Simlong, Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Malibayo.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 7, 1959.
