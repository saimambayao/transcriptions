> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2007**
>
> **An Act Amending Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered Twelve Hundred, Entitled "An Act Appropriating Funds for Public Works and Amending Republic Act Numbered Nine Hundred and Twenty"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Items (153) and (156), under paragraph "aa. Leyte," section two of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred are amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(153) Construction of Home Economics Building, Central School, Capoocan | ₱10,000.00" |
> | "(156) Construction of upper Caghalo Barrio School, Carigara | 10,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** Under paragraph "aa. Leyte," section two of the same Act, the following item, to be known as item (162), is inserted:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(162) Construction of San Vicente Barrio School Building, Alang-alang | ₱20,000.00" |
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
