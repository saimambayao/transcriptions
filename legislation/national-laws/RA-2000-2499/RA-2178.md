> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2178**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Faire, Province of Cagayan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Calassitan, Nagbayugan and Cabalansaan in the Municipality of Faire, province of Cagayan, are converted into barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Calassitan, Nagbayugan and Cabalansaan, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 2, 1959.
