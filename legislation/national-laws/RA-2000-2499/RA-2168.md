> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2168**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitios of Abra, Ambalatungan, Balintocatoc, Baluarte, Bugallon, Calaocan, Divisoria, Luna, Mabini, Naggasican, Pagrang-Ayan Norte, Pagrang-Ayan Sur, Plaridel, Raniag, Rosario, Sagana, Salvador, San Isidro, San Andres, San Jose, Santa Rosa, San Miguel, San Sebastian, Bannawag, Sinili, Victory, Sinaayon and Villasis, All in the Municipality of Santiago, Province of Isabela, into Barrios**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Abra, Ambalatungan, Balintocatoc, Baluarte, Bugallon, Calaocan, Divisoria, Luna, Mabini, Naggasican, Pagrang-ayan Norte, Pagrang-ayan Sur, Plaridel, Raniag, Rosario, Sagana, Salvador, San Isidro, San Andres, San Jose, Santa Rosa, San Miguel, San Sebastian, Bannawag, Sinili, Victory, Sinaayon and Villasis, all in the Municipality of Santiago, Province of Isabela, are hereby converted into barrios.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 25, 1959.
