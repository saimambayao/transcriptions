> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2110**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Gonayen in the Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Gonayen, Gowayan, Domogok, Dinoyak, Mangilay, Pange, Balak, Laclac, Siriac, Macasing and Diongan in the Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Gonayen.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 3, 1959.
