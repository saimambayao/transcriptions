> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2310**
>
> **An Act to Amend the Armed Forces Retirement Act so that the Benefits Therein Granted Shall not be Subject to Attachment, Levy, Execution, or Any Tax Whatsoever**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Republic Act Numbered Three hundred forty, otherwise known as the Armed Forces Retirement Act, is amended by inserting between sections ten and eleven thereof the following new section:
>
> > **Sec. 10-A** The benefits herein granted shall not be subject to attachment, levy, execution or any tax whatsoever."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1959.
