> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2167**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Villa Galed in the Municipality of Patnongon, Province of Antique, to Villa Crespo**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Villa Galed in the Municipality of Patnongon, Province of Antique, is hereby changed to Villa Crespo.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
