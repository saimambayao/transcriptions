> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2186**
>
> **An Act Providing that the Santa Cruz Irrigation System in the Province of Laguna be Hereafter Known as the Magsaysay Irrigation System**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Santa Cruz Irrigation System in the Province of Laguna shall hereafter be known as the Magsaysay Irrigation System.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 7, 1959.
