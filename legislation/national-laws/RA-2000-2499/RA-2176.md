> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2176**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Sitios of Bañgon, Lero, Canmanila and Canaguini-An, All in the Municipality of Basey, Province of Samar, to a Barrio to be Known as Barrio Bacubac of the Same Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Bañgon, Lero, Canmanila and Canaguini-an, all in the Municipality of Basey, Province of Samar, are hereby converted to a barrio to be known as barrio Bacubac of the same municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 26, 1959.
