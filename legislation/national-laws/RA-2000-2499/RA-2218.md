> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2218**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Quinapundan, Province of Samar**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Palactad, Pana-ogan, Loro nga Diyo and Anislag in the Municipality of Quinapundan, Province of Samar, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Mons. Guimbaolibot, Victor Terencio, Mons. Joel and Ramon Magsaysay, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 16, 1959.
