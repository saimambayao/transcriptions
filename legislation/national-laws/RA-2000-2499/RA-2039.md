> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2039**
>
> **An Act Transferring the Seat of Government of the Municipality of Kayan, Mountain Province, from its Present Site to the Site of the Barrio of Tadian in the Same Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The seat of government of the Municipality of Kayan, Mountain Province, is transferred to the site of the barrio of Tadian in the same municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 23, 1957.
