> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2127**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Dapitan Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios are constituted into barrios in the Municipality of Dapitan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte:
>
> 1. The sitios of Sicayab, Larayan, Pagatpatan, Boton, and Aligway Island, bounded on the north by Polawan River, on the east by Polawan Lo-ok, on the south by Dapitan and Dipolog, and on the west by Dapitan Sea, to be known as the barrio of Sicayab;
>
> 2. The sitios of Tag-olo, Banbanan, Tobod, and Silonog Island to be known as the barrio of Tag-olo; and
>
> 3. The sitios of Loang, Masidlakon, Socon and Simay to be known as the barrio of Aseniero.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 7, 1959.
