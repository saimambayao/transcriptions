> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2173**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of the Manambong Community School in the Municipality of Bayambang, Province of Pangasinan, to Asuncion P. Guevara Community School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Manambong Community School in the Municipality of Bayambang, Province of Pangasinan, is hereby changed to Asuncion P. Guevara Community School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 26, 1959.
