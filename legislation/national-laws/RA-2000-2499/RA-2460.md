> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2460**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (g), Page 205, Paragraph f, Title M, Section One of Republic Act Numbered Twenty Hundred Ninety-Three**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (g), for the Province of Cebu, page 205, paragraph f, Title M, section one of Republic Act Numbered Twenty hundred ninety-three is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(g1) Kalangyawon Barrio School, Carcar Construction of 1-room school building ₱4,000.00
> >
> > "(g2) Bolinawan Primary School, Carcar Completion, repair and painting 3,500.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
