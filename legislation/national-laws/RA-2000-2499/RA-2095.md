> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2095**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Balud in the Municipality of Salcedo, Province of Samar, into a Barrio, to be Known as the Barrio of Balud**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Balud in the Municipality of Salcedo, Province of Samar, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Balud.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: March 22, 1959.
