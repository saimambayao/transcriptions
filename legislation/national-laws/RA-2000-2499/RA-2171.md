> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2171**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrios of Roxas, Sto. Tomas, Talolora and Salvacion in the Municipality of Mondragon, Province of Samar**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following barrios are hereby created in the Municipality of Mondragon, Province of Samar:
>
> (1) Barrio Roxas, consisting of the sitios of Manlupig, Camaybayan and Caglitid;
>
> (2) Barrio Sto. Tomas, consisting of the sitios of Palanas, Malbog and Catoto-ogan;
>
> (3) Barrio Talolora, consisting of the sitios of Canamocan, Pamogha-an and Cancaturas; and
>
> (4) Barrio Salvacion, consisting of the sitios of Cagpanitan, Caghanadgong, Caragingay and Cagmanaba.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 26, 1959.
