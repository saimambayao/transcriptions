> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2224**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Bagong Pook in the Municipality of San Jose, Province of Batangas**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Pulo in the barrio of Tugtog, Municipality of San Jose Province of Batangas, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Bagong Pook.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 17, 1959.
