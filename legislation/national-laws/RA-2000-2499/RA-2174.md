> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2174**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Malitbog, Province of Leyte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Malitbog, Province of Leyte, are constituted into barrios, to wit:
>
> 1. The sitios of Mag-ata, Canlupao and Canomay to be known as the barrio of Ponong;
>
> 2. The sitios of Canlumen, Sampungen, Santander and Tagpaayaw to be known as the barrio of Hinapo; and
>
> 3. The sitios of Cambalhin, Cadaruhan Dos, Catagaman and Ponog to be known as the barrio of Cadaruhan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 26, 1959.
