> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2427**
>
> **An Act Converting the Tarlac Trade School in the Municipality of Tarlac, Province of Tarlac, into a Regional National School of Arts and Trades to be Known as the Tarlac School of Arts and Trades, and Authorizing the Appropriation of the Necessary Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Tarlac Trade School in the Municipality of Tarlac, Province of Tarlac, is hereby converted into a regional national school of arts and trades of the Philippine School of Arts and Trades type to be known as the Tarlac School of Arts and Trades. The said school shall be under the supervision of the Bureau of Public Schools and subject to the provisions of the school law. Courses of collegiate and secondary levels shall be offered in the said school.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty. Such sum as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon the date the funds authorized herein to be appropriated shall have been provided for.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
