**An Act Converting the Sitios of Siguinon, Lusod, Cantomoha, Hagnaya and Karapdapan in the Municipality of Salcedo, Province of Samar, to Barrios to be Known as the Barrios of Siguinon, Lusod, Cantomoha, Hagnaya and Karapdapan, Respectively**

> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2165**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitios of Siguinon, Lusod, Cantomoha, Hagnaya and Karapdapan in the Municipality of Salcedo, Province of Samar, to Barrios to be Known as the Barrios of Siguinon, Lusod, Cantomoha, Hagnaya and Karapdapan, Respectively**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Siguinon, Lusod, Cantomoha Hagnaya and Karapdapan in the Municipality of Salcedo Province of Samar, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Siguinon, Lusod, Cantomoha, Hagnaya and Karapdapan, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
