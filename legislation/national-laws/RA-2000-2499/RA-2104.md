> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2104**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Pitawe in the Municipality of Labason, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Bacong, Gabong, Pitawe and Banga-an in the Municipality of Labason, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Pitawe.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 3, 1959.
