> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2454**
>
> **An Act to Amend Priority Number Five, Page Twenty-Eight-B, Group III, for the Province of Cebu, Appendix B of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Priority number five, page twenty-eight-B, Group III, for the Province of Cebu, Appendix B of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen, is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "5a. Carcar, TalutCompletion and painting       ₱1,000.00
> >
> > "5b. Carcar, PoblacionRepair, improvement and painting of Central School Home Economics Building      2,500.00
> >
> > "5c. Carcar, ValenciaCompletion and painting of Home Economic Building       1,500.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
