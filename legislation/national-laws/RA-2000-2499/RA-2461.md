> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2461**
>
> **An Act Amending Item (g), Page 258, Paragraph f, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (g), for the Province of Cebu, page 258, paragraph f, section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(g) Guadalupe Elementary School, Carcar, Construction of one room standard shop building, Noel type-size: 6.90 X 8.55 M ₱5,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
