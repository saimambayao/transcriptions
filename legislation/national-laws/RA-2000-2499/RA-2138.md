> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2138**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Sitios of Malaguining and Cancorvenus, Both in the Municipality of Talalora, Province of Samar, to a Barrio to be Known as Barrio San Francisco of the Same Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Malaguining and Cancorvenus, both in the Municipality of Talalora, Province of Samar, are hereby converted to a barrio to be known as Barrio San Francisco of the same municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 7, 1959.
