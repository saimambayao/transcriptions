> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2112**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Baquilao in the Municipality of Alcala, Province of Cagayan, to San Esteban**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Baquilao in the Municipality of Alcala, Province of Cagayan, is changed to San Esteban.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 5, 1959.
