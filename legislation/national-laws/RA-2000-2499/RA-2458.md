> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2458**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item 88, for the Province of Laguna, Page 163, Paragraph "g," Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item 88, for the Province of Laguna, page 163, paragraph "g," section two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "88. Memorial Public Library building, Lumban ₱5,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
