> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2154**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Asia, Province of Occidental Negros, to Municipality of Hinoba-An**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Asia, Province of Occidental Negros, is changed to Municipality of Hinoba-an, and its seat of government shall be, as it is now, the site of the present barrio of Hinoba-an.
>
> **Section 2.** Executive Order Numbered One hundred eighty-six, series of 1948, is amended accordingly.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 24, 1959.
