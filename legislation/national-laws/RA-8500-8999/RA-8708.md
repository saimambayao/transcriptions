# Republic Act No. 8708

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DIMIAO, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE DIMIAO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8708, July 16, 1998 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DIMIAO, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE DIMIAO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a national high school in the Municipality of Dimiao, Province of Bohol, to be known as the Dimiao National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary for the implementation of this Act.Ꮮαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on July 16, 1998 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
