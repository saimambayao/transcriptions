# Republic Act No. 8937

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE PANIQUE HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PANIQUE, MUNICIPALITY OF AROROY, PROVINCE OF MASBATE TO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE PANIQUE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8937, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE PANIQUE HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PANIQUE, MUNICIPALITY OF AROROY, PROVINCE OF MASBATE TO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE PANIQUE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The Panique High School in Barangay Panique, Municipality of Aroroy, Province of Masbate shall be converted into a national high school to be known as the Panique National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Panique National High School shall be under the direct supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports (DECS).
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
