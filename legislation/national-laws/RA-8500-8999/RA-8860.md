# Republic Act No. 8860

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. ANA, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA TO BE KNOWN AS THE STA. ANA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8860, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. ANA, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA TO BE KNOWN AS THE STA. ANA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** *Establishment of the National High School.* - There shall be established a national high school in the Municipality of Sta. Ana, Province of Pampanga to be known as the Sta. Ana National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** *Implementing Rules and Regulations.* - The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue and publish rules and regulations necessary for the effective implementation of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** *Appropriation.* - The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** *Effectivity.* - This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, August 22, 2000.
