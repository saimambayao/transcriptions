> Tenth Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8629, April 30, 1998 ]**
>
> **AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO BROTHER STEPHEN KIN-SANG LO**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** Brother Stephen Kin-Sang Lo is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives appurtenant thereto.Ꮮαwρhi৷
>
> Upon the taking and registration of the oath of allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines, Brother Stephen Kin-Sang Lo shall enter the full enjoyment of Philippine citizenship.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
