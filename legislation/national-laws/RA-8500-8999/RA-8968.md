# Republic Act No. 8968

**AN ACT RENAMING THE CARAMOAN PENINSULA ROAD IN THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR TO GOVERNOR FELIX A. FUENTEBELLA NATIONAL HIGHWAY**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 8968
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2000/ra_8968_2000.html)

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8968             October 4, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT RENAMING THE CARAMOAN PENINSULA ROAD IN THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR TO GOVERNOR FELIX A. FUENTEBELLA NATIONAL HIGHWAY**
>
> **SECTION 1.** The Caramoan Peninsula road, a national road connecting and comprising the municipalities of Lagonoy, Presentacion, Garchitorena and Caramoan, all in the Province of Camarines Sur and bounded by the following road sections, to wit: (a) the 31.73 km. Lagonoy - Presentacion road; (b) the 18.65 km. Presentacion - Maligaya road; (c) the 31.748 km. Maligaya Junction - Caramoan - Guijalo road; and (d) the 12.075 km. Maligaya - Garchitorena road, is hereby renamed as the Governor Felix A. Fuentebella National Highway.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **October 4, 2000**
