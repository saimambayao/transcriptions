# Republic Act No. 8663

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY KAUSWAGAN, MUNICIPALITY OF TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO BE KNOWN AS KAUSWAGAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8663, June 25, 1998 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY KAUSWAGAN, MUNICIPALITY OF TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO BE KNOWN AS KAUSWAGAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the direct supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, a national high school in Barangay Kauswagan, Municipality of Trinidad, and Province of Bohol, to be known as the Kauswagan National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
