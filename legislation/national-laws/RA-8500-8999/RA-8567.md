# Republic Act No. 8567

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE BINAKAYAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAWIT, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Tenth Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8567, February 27, 1998 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE BINAKAYAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAWIT, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a national high school in Barrio Binakayan, Municipality of Kawit, Province of Cavite, under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, to be known as the Binakayan National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.Ꮮαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
