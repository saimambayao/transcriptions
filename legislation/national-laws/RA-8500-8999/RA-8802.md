> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8802, July 19, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING THE 2ND OF AUGUST OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF BUTUAN**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** August 2 of every year is hereby declared as a special working public holiday in the City of Butuan, in recognition and in commemoration of the city's charter foundation day.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.
