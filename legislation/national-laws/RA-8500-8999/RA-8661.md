# Republic Act No. 8661

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY JAYBANGA, LOBO, BATANGAS, TO BE KNOWN AS THE JAYBANGA HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 8661
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1998/ra_8661_1998.html)

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8661, June 25, 1998 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY JAYBANGA, LOBO, BATANGAS, TO BE KNOWN AS THE JAYBANGA HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and the House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a high school in Barangay Jaybanga, Lobo, Batangas, to be known as Jaybanga High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
