> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8939, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AT BARANGAY UBAO, MUNICIPALITY OF AGUINALDO, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established in Barangay Ubao, Municipality of Aguinaldo, Province of Ifugao a national high school to be known as Ubao National High School under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its approval and thereafter.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
