# Republic Act No. 8814

**AN ACT DECLARING 9 FEBRUARY OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MANDALUYONG**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 8814
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2000/ra_8814_2000.html)

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8814, August 21, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING 9 FEBRUARY OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MANDALUYONG**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** February 9 of every year is hereby designated as a special working holiday in the City of Mandaluyong, in recognition and commemoration of the city's liberation from foreign occupying forces.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.
>
