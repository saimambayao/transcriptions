# Republic Act No. 8945

**AN ACT DECLARING MARCH TWENTY OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8945             August 24, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING MARCH TWENTY OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION**
>
> **SECTION 1.** March twenty of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the City of San Fernando, Province of La Union to commemorate the creation of said city.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **August 24, 2000**
