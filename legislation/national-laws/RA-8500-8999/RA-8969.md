# Republic Act No. 8969

**AN ACT DECLARING THE THIRD (3RD) FRIDAY OF AUGUST OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY FOR THE CITY OF DAVAO**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8969             October 27, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING THE THIRD (3RD) FRIDAY OF AUGUST OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY FOR THE CITY OF DAVAO**
>
> **SECTION 1.** The third (3rd) Friday of the month of August of every year is hereby declared as a special working public holiday for the City of Davao.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **October 27, 2000**
