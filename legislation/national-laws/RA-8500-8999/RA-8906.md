# Republic Act No. 8906

**AN ACT RE-ESTABLISHING THE COTABATO CITY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2000/ra_8906_2000.html)

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8906, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT RE-ESTABLISHING THE COTABATO CITY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby re-established the Cotabato City National High School in Cotabato City.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This law shall take effect immediately upon approval.
>
