# Republic Act No. 8697

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SANTA FE EXTENSION HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MARCELINO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES, TO SAN GUILLERMO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 8697
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1998/ra_8697_1998.html)

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8697, July 09, 1998 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SANTA FE EXTENSION HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MARCELINO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES, TO SAN GUILLERMO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Santa Fe Extension High School in the Municipality of San Marcelino, Province of Zambales, is hereby changed to San Guillermo National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
