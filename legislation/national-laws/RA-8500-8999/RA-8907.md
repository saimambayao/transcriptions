# Republic Act No. 8907

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL TRADE HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN FELIPE, MUNICIPALITY OF TANTANGAN, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO TO BE KNOWN AS TANTANGAN NATIONAL TRADE HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8907, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL TRADE HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN FELIPE, MUNICIPALITY OF TANTANGAN, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO TO BE KNOWN AS TANTANGAN NATIONAL TRADE HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a national trade high school in Barangay San Felipe, Municipality of Tantangan, Province of South Cotabato to be known as Tantangan National Trade High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue rules and regulations necessary for the effective implementation of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon approval.
>
