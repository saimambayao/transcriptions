# Republic Act No. 8780

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE CALINOG NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALINOG, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CALINOG NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8780             April 27, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE CALINOG NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALINOG, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CALINOG NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **SECTION 1.** There is hereby established the Calinog National Comprehensive High School to be located in the Municipality of Calinog, Iloilo.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports is hereby directed to take such measures as are necessary to establish the Calinog National Comprehensive High School.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **April 27, 2000**
