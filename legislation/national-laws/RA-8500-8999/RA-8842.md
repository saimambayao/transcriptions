# Republic Act No. 8842

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AT BARANGAY UGONG, VALENZUELA, METRO MANILA TO BE KNOWN AS THE SITERO FRANCISCO MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8842, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AT BARANGAY UGONG, VALENZUELA, METRO MANILA TO BE KNOWN AS THE SITERO FRANCISCO MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a national high school at Barangay Ugong, Valenzuela, Metro Manila to be known as the Sitero Francisco Memorial National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
