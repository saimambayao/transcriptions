# Republic Act No. 8813

**AN ACT DECLARING MARCH TWENTY-TWO OF EVERY YEAR AS MALAYBALAY CITY CHARTER DAY AND A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MALAYBALAY IN ORDER TO COMMEMORATE THE ANNIVERSARY OF THE SAID CITY**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8813, August 21, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING MARCH TWENTY-TWO OF EVERY YEAR AS MALAYBALAY CITY CHARTER DAY AND A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MALAYBALAY IN ORDER TO COMMEMORATE THE ANNIVERSARY OF THE SAID CITY**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** In order to commemorate the charter anniversary of the City of Malaybalay, March twenty-two of every year is hereby declared as Malaybalay City Charter Day and a special working holiday in the said city.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
