> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8944             August 22, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE NAGA CITY SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL INTO A NATIONAL SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL AS APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **SECTION 1.** The Naga City Science High School in Barangay Concepcion Pequeña, Naga City is hereby nationalized to be known in its original name as the Naga City Science High School.
>
> **Section 2.** All personnel, records, equipment, buildings, facilities and other properties of the Naga City Science High School are hereby transferred to the nationalized Naga City Science High School.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **August 22, 2000**
