# Republic Act No. 8898

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL AT BARANGAY POBLACION IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PROSPERIDAD, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR INTO A NATIONAL GOVERNMENT-FUNDED SECONDARY SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS PROSPERIDAD NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8898, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL AT BARANGAY POBLACION IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PROSPERIDAD, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR INTO A NATIONAL GOVERNMENT-FUNDED SECONDARY SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS PROSPERIDAD NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The Poblacion Barangay High School in the Municipality of Prosperidad, Province of Agusan del Sur is hereby absorbed by the national government and converted into a regular secondary school under the full control and supervision of the national government through the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 2.** This school shall be known officially as the Prosperidad National High School.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, August 22, 2000.
