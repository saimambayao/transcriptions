# Republic Act No. 8778

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CERDENA, MUNICIPALITY OF MALABUYOC, CEBU TO BE KNOWN AS THE CERDENA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8778             April 7, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CERDENA, MUNICIPALITY OF MALABUYOC, CEBU TO BE KNOWN AS THE CERDENA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, a national high school in Barangay Cerdena, Municipality of Malabuyoc, Cebu to be known as the Cerdena National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall take the necessary steps for the establishment of the school so as to make it operational during the first school term after the effectivity of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **April 7, 2000**
