# Republic Act No. 8788

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTOL, PROVINCE OF LA UNION, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SANTOL VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8788             May 3, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTOL, PROVINCE OF LA UNION, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SANTOL VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **SECTION 1.** There shall be established, under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, a vocational high school in the Municipality of Santol, Province of La Union, to be known as the Santol Vocational High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **May 3, 2000**
