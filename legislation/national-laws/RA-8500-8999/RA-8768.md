# Republic Act No. 8768

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE PAMBUHAN HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GARCHITORENA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE PAMBUHAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8768             March 28, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE PAMBUHAN HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GARCHITORENA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE PAMBUHAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **SECTION 1.** The Pambuhan High School in the Municipality of Garchitorena, Province of Camarines Sur, is hereby converted into a national high school to be known as the Pambuhan National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports (DECS) is hereby directed to implement the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **March 28, 2000**
