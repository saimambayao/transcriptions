# Republic Act No. 8816

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MAGSAYSAY, MUNICIPALITY OF PANAON, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL TO BE KNOWN AS THE PANAON NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8816, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MAGSAYSAY, MUNICIPALITY OF PANAON, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL TO BE KNOWN AS THE PANAON NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a national high school in Barangay Magsaysay, Municipality of Panaon, Province of Misamis Occidental to be known as the Panaon National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports, hereinafter referred to as the Department, shall implement the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, August 22, 2000.
