# Republic Act No. 8964

**AN ACT SEPARATING THE JUAN R. LIWAG HIGH SCHOOL ANNEX I IN BARANGAY STA. CRUZ, GAPAN, NUEVA ECIJA FROM THE JUAN R. LIWAG HIGH SCHOOL AND CONVERTING THE SAME INTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE STA. CRUZ NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8964             September 16, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT SEPARATING THE JUAN R. LIWAG HIGH SCHOOL ANNEX I IN BARANGAY STA. CRUZ, GAPAN, NUEVA ECIJA FROM THE JUAN R. LIWAG HIGH SCHOOL AND CONVERTING THE SAME INTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE STA. CRUZ NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **SECTION 1.** The Juan R. Liwag High School Annex I in Barangay Sta. Cruz, Gapan, Nueva Ecija is hereby separated from the Juan R. Liwag High School and converted into an independent national high school to be known as the Sta. Cruz National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** All personnel, records, assets and liabilities of the Juan R. Liwag High School Annex I are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Sta. Cruz National High School.
>
> **Section 4.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **September 16, 2000**
