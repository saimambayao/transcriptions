# Republic Act No. 8786

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAUSWAGAN, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE TO BE KNOWN AS THE MARCELA T. MABANTA MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8786             May 3, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAUSWAGAN, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE TO BE KNOWN AS THE MARCELA T. MABANTA MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **SECTION 1.** There shall be established a national high school in the Municipality of Kauswagan, Province of Lanao del Norte to be known as the Marcela T. Mabanta Memorial National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act is hereby authorized to be appropriated in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **May 3, 2000**
