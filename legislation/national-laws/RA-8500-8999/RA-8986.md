# Republic Act No. 8986

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8986             December 9, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING MARCH TWENTY-ONE, A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF CALAPAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO**
>
> **Section 1.** March twenty-one of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the City of Calapan, Province of Oriental Mindoro to commemorate the creation/birth of the said City.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **December 9, 2000**
