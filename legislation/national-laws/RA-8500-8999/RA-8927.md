# Republic Act No. 8927

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PANDAN, MUNICIPALITY OF CAOAYAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CAOAYAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8927, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PANDAN, MUNICIPALITY OF CAOAYAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CAOAYAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established a national high school in Barangay Pandan, Municipality of Caoayan, Province of Ilocos Sur to be known as the Caoayan National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon approval thereof.
>
