# Republic Act No. 8765

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY KALIPAY, GINGOOG CITY TO BE KNOWN AS THE KALIPAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8765             March 28, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY KALIPAY, GINGOOG CITY TO BE KNOWN AS THE KALIPAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **SECTION 1.** There shall be established a high school in Barangay Kalipay, Gingoog City, Province of Misamis Oriental, to be known as the Kalipay National high School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports (DECS) shall take the necessary steps for the establishment of the school so as to make it fully operational during the school term immediately following the effectivity of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **March 28, 2000**
