# Republic Act No. 8772

**AN ACT SEPARATING THE CALAMBA ANNEX HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CALAMBA, CABADBARAN, AGUSAN DEL NORTE FROM THE AGAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY AGAY, RTR, AGUSAN DEL NORTE, AND THE SAME IS HEREBY CONVERTED INTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE CALAMBA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8772             March 28, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT SEPARATING THE CALAMBA ANNEX HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CALAMBA, CABADBARAN, AGUSAN DEL NORTE FROM THE AGAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY AGAY, RTR, AGUSAN DEL NORTE, AND THE SAME IS HEREBY CONVERTED INTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE CALAMBA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Section 1.** The existing Calamba Annex High School in Barangay Calamba, Cabadbaran, Agusan del Norte is hereby separated from the Agay National High School in Barangay Agay, RTR, Agusan del Norte, and the same is hereby converted into a national high school to be known as the Calamba National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** All personnel, records, assets and properties, funds and obligations of the Calamba Annex High School are hereby transferred to the Calamba National High School.
>
> **Section 4.** The amount necessary for the initial implementation of this Act shall be charged against the current year's appropriations of the Agay National High School - Calamba Annex. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the continuous operation and maintenance of the Calamba National High School shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **March 28, 2000**
