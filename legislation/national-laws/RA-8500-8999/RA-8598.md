# Republic Act No. 8598

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONALIZED HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALUNGAO, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE BALUNGAO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Tenth Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8598, March 20, 1998 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONALIZED HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALUNGAO, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE BALUNGAO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** A nationalized high school is hereby established in the Municipality of Balungao, Province of Pangasinan, to be known as Balungao National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.Ꮮαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
