# Republic Act No. 8850

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE LA SALVACION HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SALVACION, MUNICIPALITY OF TIGAON, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE LA SALVACION NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8850, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE LA SALVACION HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SALVACION, MUNICIPALITY OF TIGAON, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE LA SALVACION NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The La Salvacion High School in Barangay Salvacion, Municipality of Tigaon, Province of Camarines Sur is hereby converted into a national high school to be known as the La Salvacion National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports (DECS) is hereby directed to implement the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
