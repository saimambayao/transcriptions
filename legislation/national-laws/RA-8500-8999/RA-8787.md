# Republic Act No. 8787

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAMPLONA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN TO BE KNOWN AS THE DAVID M. PUZON MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 8787             May 3, 2000**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAMPLONA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN TO BE KNOWN AS THE DAVID M. PUZON MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **SECTION 1.** There is hereby established a national high school in the Municipality of Pamplona, Province of Cagayan to be known as the David M. Puzon Memorial National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, **May 3, 2000**
