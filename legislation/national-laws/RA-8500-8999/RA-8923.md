# Republic Act No. 8923

**AN ACT CONVERTING ANITAPAN HIGH SCHOOL ANNEX OF BARANGAY ANITAPAN, MUNICIPALITY OF MABINI, COMPOSTELA VALLEY PROVINCE, INTO ANITAPAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND PROVIDING APPROPRIATE FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8923, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING ANITAPAN HIGH SCHOOL ANNEX OF BARANGAY ANITAPAN, MUNICIPALITY OF MABINI, COMPOSTELA VALLEY PROVINCE, INTO ANITAPAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND PROVIDING APPROPRIATE FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The Anitapan High School Annex of Barangay Anitapan, Municipality of Mabini, Compostela Valley Province shall be converted into and known as Anitapan National High School under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports (DECS).
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
