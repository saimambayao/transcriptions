# Republic Act No. 8812

**AN ACT DECLARING JULY 1 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY KNOWN AS ADLAW SA DIPOLOG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8812, August 21, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING JULY 1 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY KNOWN AS ADLAW SA DIPOLOG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** July 1 is the Adlaw sa Dipolog in the City of Dipolog, Province of Zamboanga del Norte. This day is celebrated to generate participation in recognition and commemoration of the Foundation Anniversary of Dipolog.
>
> **Section 2.** July 1 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the City of Dipolog, Province of Zamboanga del Norte.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
