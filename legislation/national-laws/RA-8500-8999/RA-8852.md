# Republic Act No. 8852

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN AGRO-INDUSTRIAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SIBAGUAN, MUNICIPALITY OF SAGNAY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SIBAGUAN AGRO-INDUSTRIAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8852, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN AGRO-INDUSTRIAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SIBAGUAN, MUNICIPALITY OF SAGNAY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SIBAGUAN AGRO-INDUSTRIAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established an agro-industrial high school in Barangay Sibaguan, Municipality of Sagnay, Province of Camarines Sur, to be known as the Sibaguan Agro-Industrial High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Act.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
