# Republic Act No. 8831

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PANITIAN, MUNICIPALITY OF QUEZON, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE QUEZON-PANITIAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8831, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PANITIAN, MUNICIPALITY OF QUEZON, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE QUEZON-PANITIAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established a national high school in Barangay Panitian, Municipality of Quezon, Province of Palawan, to be known as the Quezon-Panitian National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue the necessary rules, circulars and regulations to implement this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, August 22, 2000.
