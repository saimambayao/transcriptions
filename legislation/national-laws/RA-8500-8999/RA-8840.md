# Republic Act No. 8840

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TUBA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AT TWIN PEAKS, MUNICIPALITY OF TUBA, PROVINCE OF BENGUET TO THE TWIN PEAKS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 8840
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2000/ra_8840_2000.html)

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8840, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TUBA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AT TWIN PEAKS, MUNICIPALITY OF TUBA, PROVINCE OF BENGUET TO THE TWIN PEAKS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Tuba National High School at Twin Peaks in the Municipality of Tuba, Province of Benguet, is hereby changed to Twin Peaks National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
