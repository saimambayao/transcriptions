# Republic Act No. 8644

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BAGONG SILANG, KALOOKAN CITY, TO BE KNOWN AS KALAYAAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8644, May 30, 1998 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BAGONG SILANG, KALOOKAN CITY, TO BE KNOWN AS KALAYAAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a national high school at Phase 10 in Bagong Silang, First District of Kalookan City, to be known as Kalayaan National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.Ꮮαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
