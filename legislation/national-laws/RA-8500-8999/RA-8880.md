# Republic Act No. 8880

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY TUBURAN, MUNICIPALITY OF POTOTAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO BE NAMED AS THE JOSE FACULTAD MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8880, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY TUBURAN, MUNICIPALITY OF POTOTAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO BE NAMED AS THE JOSE FACULTAD MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established a national high school in Barangay Tuburan, Pototan, Iloilo, to be named as the Jose Facultad Memorial National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports is hereby directed to take such measures as are necessary to establish the Jose Facultad Memorial National High School.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
