# Republic Act No. 8914

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE SAN MARTIN BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN MARTIN, CITY OF MALAYBALAY, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON INTO A NATIONAL AGRO-INDUSTRIAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN MARTIN NATIONAL AGROINDUSTRIAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8914, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE SAN MARTIN BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN MARTIN, CITY OF MALAYBALAY, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON INTO A NATIONAL AGRO-INDUSTRIAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN MARTIN NATIONAL AGROINDUSTRIAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The San Martin Barangay High School in Barangay San Martin, City of Malaybalay, Province of Bukidnon is hereby converted into a national agro-industrial high school to be known as the San Martin Agro-Industrial National High School. The said school shall be under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
