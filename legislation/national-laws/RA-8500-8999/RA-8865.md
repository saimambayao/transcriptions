# Republic Act No. 8865

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE BILAR HIGH SCHOOL, MUNICIPALITY OF BILAR, PROVINCE OF BOHOL INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE BILAR NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8865, August 22, 2000 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE BILAR HIGH SCHOOL, MUNICIPALITY OF BILAR, PROVINCE OF BOHOL INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE BILAR NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The Bilar High School of the Municipality of Bilar, Province of Bohol is hereby converted into a national high school to be known as the Bilar National High School under the direct supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
