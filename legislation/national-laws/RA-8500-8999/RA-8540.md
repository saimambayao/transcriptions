# Republic Act No. 8540

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN JOSE, MUNICIPALITY OF OSMENA, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

## Metadata

> Tenth Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8540, February 23, 1998 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN JOSE, MUNICIPALITY OF OSMENA, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established in Barangay San Jose, Municipality of Osmeña, Province of Zamboanga Del Norte, a national high school to be known as the San Jose National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The San Jose National High School shall be under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
