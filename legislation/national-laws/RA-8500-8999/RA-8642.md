# Republic Act No. 8642

**AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NASUGBU, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 8642
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1998/ra_8642_1998.html)

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8642, May 27, 1998 ]**
>
> **AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NASUGBU, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** A municipal high school is hereby established in the Municipality of Nasugbu, Province of Batangas.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
