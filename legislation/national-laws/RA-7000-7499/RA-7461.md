> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7461, April 28, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE STA. ROSA NORTE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY STA. ROSA NORTE, MUNICIPALITY OF JOSE PANGANIBAN, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO SEGUNDO AGUIRRE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Sta. Rosa Norte Elementary School in Barangay Sta. Rosa Norte, Municipality of Jose Panganiban, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Segundo Aguirre Elementary School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
