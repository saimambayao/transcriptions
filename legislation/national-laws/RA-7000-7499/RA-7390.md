**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MALABAG MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MALABAG, MUNICIPALITY OF GIPORLOS, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR, TO MALABAG HIGH SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7390, April 10, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MALABAG MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MALABAG, MUNICIPALITY OF GIPORLOS, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR, TO MALABAG HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Malabag Municipal High School in Barangay Malabag, Municipality of Giporlos, Province of Eastern Samar, is hereby changed to Malabag High School.
>
