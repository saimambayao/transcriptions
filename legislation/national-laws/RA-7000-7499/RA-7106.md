> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 7106             August 14, 1991**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF TANAUAN SCHOOL OF CRAFTSMANSHIP AND HOME INDUSTRIES IN TANAUAN, LEYTE, TO LEYTE STATE SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Tanauan School of Craftsmanship and Home Industries in Tanauan, Leyte, is hereby changed to Leyte State School of Arts and Trades.
>
> **Section 2.** The College shall offer secondary course in arts and trades, short-term technical or vocational courses, and higher professional instruction and training in arts and trades as may be authorized by the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **August 14, 1991**.
