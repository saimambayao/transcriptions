> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7433, April 23, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SURIGAO DEL SUR PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANDAG, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL SUR, TO ADELA SERRA TY MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Surigao del Sur Provincial Hospital in the Municipality of Tandag, Province of Surigao del Sur, is hereby changed to Adela Serra Ty Memorial Hospital.
>
