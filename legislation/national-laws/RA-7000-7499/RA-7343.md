> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7343, April 01, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LAWAAN SCHOOL OF CRAFTSMANSHIP AND HOME INDUSTRIES IN LAWAAN, EASTERN SAMAR, TO LAWAAN NATIONAL SCHOOL OF CRAFTSMANSHIP AND HOME INDUSTRIES
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Lawaan School of Craftsmanship and Home Industries in Lawaan, Eastern Samar, is hereby changed to Lawaan National School of Craftsmanship and Home Industries.1âшphi1
