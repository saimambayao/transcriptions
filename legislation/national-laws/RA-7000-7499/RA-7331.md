**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARIA, BULACAN, TO FORTUNATO F. HALILI NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7331, March 31, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARIA, BULACAN, TO FORTUNATO F. HALILI NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:1âшphi1
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Philippine National Agricultural School in Ouyong, Sta. Maria, Bulacan, is hereby changed to Fortunato F. Halili National Agricultural School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
