# Republic Act No. 7113

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A SPECIAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE ISLAND OF CULION, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CULION SANITARIUM SPECIAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 7113             August 22, 1991**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A SPECIAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE ISLAND OF CULION, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CULION SANITARIUM SPECIAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a special high school in the Island of Culion, Province of Palawan, to be known as the Culion Sanitarium Special High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act is hereby authorized to be appropriated in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **August 22, 1991**.
