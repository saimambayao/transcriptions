> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7449, April 27, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING AUGUST THIRTY A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** August Thirty of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Bulacan to commemorate the birth anniversary of Marcelo H. del Pilar.
>
