> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7203, March 02, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MALAMIG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY UMIRAY, MUNICIPALITY OF DINGALAN, PROVINCE OF AURORA, TO UMIRAY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:1âшphi1
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Malamig Elementary School in Barangay Umiray, Municipality of Dingalan, Province of Aurora, is hereby changed to Umiray Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
