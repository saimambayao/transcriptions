# Republic Act No. 7275

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MORONG GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MORONG, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO RIZAL PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 7275
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1992/ra_7275_1992.html)

> Fifth Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manilam on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-one
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7275, March 24, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MORONG GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MORONG, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO RIZAL PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Morong General Hospital located in the Municipality of Morong, Province of Rizal, is hereby changed to Rizal Provincial Hospital.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
