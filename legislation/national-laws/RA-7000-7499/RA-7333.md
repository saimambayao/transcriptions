> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7333, March 31, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE ABUYOG GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ABUYOG, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO OCTAVIO TRAYA, SR., MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Abuyog General Hospital in the Municipality of Abuyog, Province of Leyte, is hereby changed to Octavio Traya, Sr., Memorial Hospital.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
