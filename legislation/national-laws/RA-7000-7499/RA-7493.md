**AN ACT RENAMING THE FILIPINAS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN NICOLAS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, AS THE SAN NICOLAS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7493, May 13, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE FILIPINAS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN NICOLAS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, AS THE SAN NICOLAS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Filipinas National High School located in the Municipality of San Nicolas, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby renamed as the San Nicolas National High School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** All allocations provided for the operation and maintenance of the Filipinas National High School shall be carried over to the San Nicolas National High School.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
