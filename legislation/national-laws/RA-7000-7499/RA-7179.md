**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE DAVAO REGIONAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, FROM ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) TO TWO HUNDRED (200) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7179, January 14, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE DAVAO REGIONAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, FROM ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) TO TWO HUNDRED (200) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Davao Regional Hospital in the Municipality of Tagum, Province of Davao, is hereby increased from one hundred fifty (150) to two hundred (200) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
