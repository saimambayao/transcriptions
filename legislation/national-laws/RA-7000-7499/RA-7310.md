> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7310, March 30, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE ILIGAN CITY HOSPITAL IN PALA-O, ILIGAN CITY, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE, TO GREGORIO T. LLUCH, SR., MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Iligan City Hospital in Pala-o, Iligan City, Province of Lanao del Norte, is hereby changed to Gregorio T. Lluch, Sr., Memorial Hospital.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
