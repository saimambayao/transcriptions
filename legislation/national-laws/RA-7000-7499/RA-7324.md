> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7324, March 30, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE MEMBERS OF THE BOARD OF THE PHILIPPINE RACING COMMISSION, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED FOUR HUNDRED TWENTY, AS AMENDED
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Section four of Presidential Decree Numbered Four hundred twenty is amended to read as follows:
>
> "**Section 4.** *Membership of the Commission.* - The Commission shall be composed of a chairman and six (6) members:  *Provided,* That of the (6) members, there shall be one (1) representative of the Racehorse Owners Association, one (1) representative of the Jockeys Association and one (1) representative of the Trainers Association.  The Commissioners shall be appointed by the President of the Philippines."1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** Section six of the same decree is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> "**Section 6.** *Meetings and Quorum.* - The Commissioners shall meet regularly once a week and as often as the exigencies of the service may demand, upon the call of the Chairman or upon the request of three (3) members of the Commission.  The vote of at least four (4) members shall be necessary for the adoption of any rule, resolution or decision of the Commission."
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
