> AN ACT RENAMING THE SAN ISIDRO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ISIDRO, SORSOGON, TO PIO JEBULAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
