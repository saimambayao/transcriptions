> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7254, March 20, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY SIX OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF TOLEDO, PROVINCE OF CEBU
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** January Six of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in the City of Toledo, Province of Cebu, to commemorate its charter day anniversary.
>
