> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7415, April 14, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE CADIZ DISTRICT HOSPITAL FROM A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY TO A ONE HUNDRED-BED CAPACITY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Cadiz District Hospital is hereby expanded and improved by increasing the bed capacity from a twenty-five-bed to a one hundred-bed capacity district hospital under the supervision of the Department of Health.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
