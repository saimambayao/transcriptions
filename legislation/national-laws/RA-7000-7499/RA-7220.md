**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE CAMARINES NORTE PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN DAET, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, FROM ONE HUNDRED (100) TO ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7220, March 06, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE CAMARINES NORTE PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN DAET, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, FROM ONE HUNDRED (100) TO ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Camarines Norte Provincial Hospital in Daet, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby increased from one hundred (100) to one hundred fifty (150) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.
>
