> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7410, April 14, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE MEDICARE COMMUNITY (MUNICIPAL) HOSPITAL IN LEBAK, SULTAN KUDARAT, FROM A FIFTEEN-BED CAPACITY COMMUNITY HOSPITAL INTO A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE LEBAK DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.**  The Medicare Community (Municipal) Hospital in Lebak, Sultan Kudarat, is hereby expanded and upgrade from a fifteen-five-bed capacity district hospital, to be known as the Lebak District Hospital.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.**  The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.**  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, April 14, 1992.
