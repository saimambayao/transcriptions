# Republic Act No. 7422

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUARTERO, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CUARTERO HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7422, April 14, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUARTERO, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CUARTERO HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:1âшphi1
>
> **Section 1.** The rural health unit in the Municipality of Cuartero, Province of Capiz, is hereby converted into a five-bed capacity health infirmary, to be known as the Cuartero Health Infirmary.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
