> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7358, April 08, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LA PAZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, LAOAG CITY, TO EULALIO F. SIAZON MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the La Paz Elementary School situated in Barangay La Paz, Laoag City, is hereby changed to Eulalio F. Siazon Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports is hereby directed to promulgate and issue rules for the implementation of this Act.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
