**AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT IN THE CITY OF IRIGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE IRIGA HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7385, April 10, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT IN THE CITY OF IRIGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE IRIGA HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The rural health unit in the City of Iriga, Province of Camarines Sur, is hereby converted into a five-bed capacity health infirmary, to be known as the Iriga Health Infirmary.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** The existing and available facilities, equipment, and hospital staff may be increased subject to the approval of the Department of Health, to meet required services.
>
