# Republic Act No. 7462

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DALAKIT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CATARMAN, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR, TO GENEROSO FRIGILLANA MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7462, April 28, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DALAKIT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CATARMAN, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR, TO GENEROSO FRIGILLANA MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Dalakit Elementary School in Barangay Dalakit, Municipality of Catarman, Province of Northern Samar, is hereby changed to Generoso Frigillana Memorial Elementary School.
