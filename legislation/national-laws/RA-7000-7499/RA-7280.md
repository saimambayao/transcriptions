>
> AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 26 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF CEBU
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
