>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAN ROQUE BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN TANAUAN, LEYTE, TO TANAUAN HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
