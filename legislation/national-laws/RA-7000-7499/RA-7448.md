> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7448, April 27, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER FOUR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BOHOL, DESIGNATING NOVEMBER TWO TO SIX AS CARLOS P. GARCIA WEEK IN SAID PROVINCE
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** To commemorate the birth anniversary of the late President Carlos P. Garcia, November Four of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Bohol, and November Two to Six designated as Carlos P. Garcia Week in said province.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its publication in at least two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
