> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7345, April 01, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE HERNANI PROVINCIAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF HERNANI, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR, TO HERNANI HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Hernani Provincial High School in the Municipality of Hernani, Province of Eastern Samar, is hereby changed to Hernani High School.
>
