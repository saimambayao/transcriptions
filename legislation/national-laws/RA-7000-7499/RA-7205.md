# Republic Act No. 7205

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MANAT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NABUNTURAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, TO MANAT CENTRAL SCHOOL**

## Metadata

> Fifth Regular session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7205, March 02, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MANAT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NABUNTURAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, TO MANAT CENTRAL SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Manat Elementary School in the Municipality of Nabunturan, Province of Davao del Norte, is hereby changed to Manat Central School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
