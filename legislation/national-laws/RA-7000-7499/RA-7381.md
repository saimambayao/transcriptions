> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7381, April 10, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE CAGAYAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF APARRI, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, FROM FIFTY (50) TO SEVENTY-FIVE (75) BEDS, AND UPGRADING IT INTO A PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS THE APARRI PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Cagayan District Hospital in the Municipality of Aparri, Province of Cagayan, is hereby expanded and upgraded from fifty (50) to seventy-five (75) beds and upgrading it into a provincial hospital to be known as the Aparri Provincial Hospital.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
