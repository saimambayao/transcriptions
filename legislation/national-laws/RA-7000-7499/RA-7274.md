**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE NEGROS ORIENTAL PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN DUMAGUETE CITY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL, FROM TWO HUNDRED FIFTY (250) TO THREE HUNDRED (300) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7274, March 24, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE NEGROS ORIENTAL PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN DUMAGUETE CITY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL, FROM TWO HUNDRED FIFTY (250) TO THREE HUNDRED (300) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Negros Oriental Provincial Hospital in Dumaguete City, Province of Negros Oriental, is hereby increased from two hundred fifty (250) to three hundred (300) beds.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
