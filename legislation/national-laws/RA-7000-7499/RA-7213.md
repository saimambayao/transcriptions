> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7213, March 06, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE CAUAYAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN CAUAYAN, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, FROM FIFTY (50) TO ONE HUNDRED (100) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Cauayan District Hospital in Cauayan, Province of Isabela, is hereby increased from fifty (50) to one hundred (100) beds.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
