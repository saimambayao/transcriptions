**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE STA. MARIA GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARIA, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO ROGACIANO M. MERCADO MEMORIAL GENERAL HOSPITAL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7402, April 14, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE STA. MARIA GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARIA, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO ROGACIANO M. MERCADO MEMORIAL GENERAL HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:1âшphi1
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Sta. Maria General Hospital in the Municipality of Sta. Maria, Province of Bulacan, is hereby changed to Rogaciano M. Mercado Memorial General Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
