> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7357, April 08, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING CALLAO CAVE AND ITS PREMISES LOCATED AT CALLAO, MUNICIPALITY OF PENABLANCA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, A TOURIST ZONE
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Callao Cave and its premises located at Callao, Municipality of Penablanca, Province of Cagayan, is hereby declared a tourist zone.  As such, it shall be accorded priority development by the Department of Tourism and shall be subject to the rules and regulations governing the development of tourist zones.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Tourism, in coordination with the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and other government agencies concerned, shall, within one (1) year from the approval of this Act, prepare a tourism development plan for Callao Cave and its premises, which shall provide for the construction, improvement and/or maintenance of such appropriate facilities and infrastructure as shall encourage tourism in the area.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The Department of Tourism, through the Philippine Tourism Authority, shall take immediate steps to implement said development plan and shall incorporate the same in the department's overall tourism development programs for the ensuing calendar year.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
