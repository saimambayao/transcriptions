# Republic Act No. 7271

**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE BARILI DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE PROVINCE OF CEBU, FROM TEN (10) TO TWENTY-FIVE (25) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

>
> BBegun and held in Metro Manila on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-one

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7271, March 20, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE BARILI DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE PROVINCE OF CEBU, FROM TEN (10) TO TWENTY-FIVE (25) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:1âшphi1
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Barili District Hospital in the Municipality of Barili, Province of Cebu, is hereby increased from ten (10) to twenty five (25) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
