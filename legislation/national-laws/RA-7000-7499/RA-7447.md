**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE DISTRICT HOSPITAL OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABON, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7447, April 27, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE DISTRICT HOSPITAL OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABON, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The existing bed capacity of the district hospital of the Municipality of Bongabon, Province of Nueva Ecija, is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be charged against the lump-sum appropriations authorized for the purpose under the current General Appropriations Act. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance of the hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
