# Republic Act No. 7044

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL AT THE POBLACION IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ESTEBAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN ESTEBAN HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 7044             June 19, 1991**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL AT THE POBLACION IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ESTEBAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN ESTEBAN HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a high school at the Poblacion in the Municipality of San Esteban, Province of Ilocos Sur, to be known as the San Esteban High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act is hereby authorized to be appropriated in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **June 19, 1991**.
