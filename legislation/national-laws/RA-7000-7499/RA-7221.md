> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7221, March 11, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE WAWA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN SAN AGUSTIN VILLAGE (FORMERLY SITIO WAWA-BUKID) IN BARANGAY MOONWALK, MUNICIPALITY OF PARAÑAQUE, METRO MANILA, TO SAN AGUSTIN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Wawa Elementary School in San Agustin Village (formerly Sitio Wawa-Bukid) in Barangay Moonwalk, Municipality of Parañaque, Metro Manila, is hereby changed to San Agustin Elementary School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
