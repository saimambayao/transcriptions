> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7097, August 02, 1991 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ISIDRO, MUNICIPALITY OF MARIHATAG, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO, TO BE KNOWN AS THE MARIHATAG AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established an agricultural high school in Barangay San Isidro, Municipality of Marihatag, Province of Surigao, to be known as the Marihatag Agricultural High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act is hereby authorized to be appropriated in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
