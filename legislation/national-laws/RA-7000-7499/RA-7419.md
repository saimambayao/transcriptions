> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7419, April 14, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PONTEVEDRA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE PONTEVEDRA HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.**  The rural health unit in the municipality of Pontevedra, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby converted into a five-bed capacity health infirmary, to be known as the Pontevedra Health Infirmary.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.**  The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.**  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, April 14, 1992.
