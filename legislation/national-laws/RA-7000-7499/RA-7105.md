> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 7105             August 14, 1991**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DANIEL Z. ROMUALDEZ MEMORIAL SCHOOL OF FISHERIES IN TOLOSA, LEYTE, TO LEYTE STATE SCHOOL OF FISHERIES**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Daniel Z. Romualdez Memorial School of Fisheries in Tolosa, Leyte, is hereby changed to Leyte State School of Fisheries.
>
> **Section 2.** The College shall offer secondary course in fisheries, short-term technical or vocational courses, and higher technological and professional instruction and training in fisheries as may be authorized by the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **August 14, 1991**.
