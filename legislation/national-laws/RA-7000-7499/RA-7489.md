> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7489, May 13, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BALIWAG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGALLANES, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO PACIFICO VILLACARLOS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.**  The name of the Baliwag elementary School in the Municipality of Magallanes, Provinc of Cavite, is hereby changed to Pacifico Villacarlos Elementary School.LaW㏗iL
>
> **Section 2.**  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, May 13, 1992.
