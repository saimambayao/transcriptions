# Republic Act No. 7288

**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE CANDELARIA MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CANDELARIA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, FROM TEN (10) TO TWENTY-FIVE (25) BEDS**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7288, March 24, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE CANDELARIA MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CANDELARIA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, FROM TEN (10) TO TWENTY-FIVE (25) BEDS
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Candelaria Municipal Hospital in the Municipality of Candelaria, Province of Quezon, is hereby increased from ten (10) to twenty-five (25) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
