**AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT 11 IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF CEBU, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE TALISAY HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7453, April 27, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT 11 IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF CEBU, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE TALISAY HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.**The rural health unit II in the Municipality of Talisay, Province of Cebu, is hereby converted into a five-bed capacity health infirmary, to be known as the Talisay Health Infirmary.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1âшphi1
