**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE ISABELA DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISABELA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO DON GIL MONTILLA MEMORIAL HOSPITAL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7334, March 31, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE ISABELA DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISABELA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO DON GIL MONTILLA MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Isabela District Hospital in the Municipality of Isabela, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Don Gil Montilla Memorial Hospital.
>
