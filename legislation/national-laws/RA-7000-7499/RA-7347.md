> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7347, April 01, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE JIPAPAD BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JIPAPAD, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR, TO JIPAPAD HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Jipapad Barangay High School in the Municipality of Jipapad, Province of Eastern Samar, is hereby changed to Jipapad High School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
