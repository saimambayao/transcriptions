> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7268, March 20, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE SAN MIGUEL DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN SAN MIGUEL, BULACAN, FROM FIFTY (50) TO SEVENTY-FIVE (75) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assemble:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the San Miguel District Hospital in San Miguel, Bulacan, is hereby increased from fifty (50) to seventy-five (75) beds.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
