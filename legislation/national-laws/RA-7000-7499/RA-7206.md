# Republic Act No. 7206

**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE STA. LUCIA DISTRLCT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. LUCIA, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Fifth Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7206, March 06, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE STA. LUCIA DISTRLCT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. LUCIA, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Sta. Lucia District Hospital in the Municipality of Sta. Lucia, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1âшphi1
