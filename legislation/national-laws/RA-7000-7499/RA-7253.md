> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7253, March 20, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING THE 24TH DAY OF NOVEMBER OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL HOLIDAY FOR CITY OF DUMAGUETE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:1âшphi1
>
> **Section 1.** The 24th day of November of every year is hereby declared as a special holiday for the City of Dumaguete, Province of Negros Oriental, in observance of its charter day.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, March 20, 1992.
