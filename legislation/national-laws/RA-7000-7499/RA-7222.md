# Republic Act No. 7222

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE ARANJUEZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY ARANJUEZ, MUNICIPALITY OF BALASAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO PEDRO BEDEZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7222, March 11, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE ARANJUEZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY ARANJUEZ, MUNICIPALITY OF BALASAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO PEDRO BEDEZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Aranjuez Elementary School in Barangay Aranjuez, Municipality of Balasan, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Pedro Bedez Elementary School.
>
