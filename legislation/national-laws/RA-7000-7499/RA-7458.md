# Republic Act No. 7458

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT IN DON MARIANO MARCOS AVENUE, CONGRESSIONAL HILLS, QUEZON CITY, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CONGRESSIONAL HILLS HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7458, April 27, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT IN DON MARIANO MARCOS AVENUE, CONGRESSIONAL HILLS, QUEZON CITY, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CONGRESSIONAL HILLS HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled;
>
> **Section 1.** The rural health unit in Don Mariano Marcos Avenue, Congressional Hills, Quezon City, is hereby converted into a five-bed capacity health infirmary, to be known as the Congressional Hills Health Infirmary.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
