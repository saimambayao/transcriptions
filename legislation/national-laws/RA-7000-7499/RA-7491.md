> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7491, May 13, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE AMPID ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MATEO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO VICENTE SANTIAGO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Ampid Elementary School located in the Municipality of San Mateo, Province of Rizal, is hereby changed to Vicente Santiago Elementary School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
