**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DINALUPIHAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINALUPIHAN, PROVINCE OF BATAAN, TO JOSE C.PAYUMO, JR., MEMORIAL HOSPITAL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7404, April 14, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DINALUPIHAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINALUPIHAN, PROVINCE OF BATAAN, TO JOSE C.PAYUMO, JR., MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:1âшphi1
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Dinalupihan District Hospital in the Municipality of Dinalupihan, Province of Bataan, is hereby changed to Jose C. Payumo, Jr., Memorial Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
