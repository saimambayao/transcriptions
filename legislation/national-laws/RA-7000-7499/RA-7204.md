>

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7204, March 02, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE ECHAGUE EAST CENTRAL SCHOOL IN ECHAGUE, ISABELA, TO TUGUEGARAO-STO. DOMINGO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
