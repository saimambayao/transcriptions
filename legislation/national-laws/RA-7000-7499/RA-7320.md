# Republic Act No. 7320

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARLES, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CARLES HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7320, March 30, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARLES, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CARLES HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The rural health unit in the Municipality of Carles, Province of Iloilo, is hereby converted into a five-bed capacity health infirmary, to be known as the Carles Health Infirmary.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
