# Republic Act No. 7153

**AN ACT DECLARING APRIL FIFTEEN OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, IN HONOR OF THE LATE GENERAL ANECITO LACSON**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 7153             August 30, 1991**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING APRIL FIFTEEN OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, IN HONOR OF THE LATE GENERAL ANECITO LACSON**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** April Fifteen of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the Municipality of Talisay, Province of Negros Occidental, in honor of the late General Anecito Lacson.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **August 30, 1991**.
