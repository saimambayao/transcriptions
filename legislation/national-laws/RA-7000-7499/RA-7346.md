> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7346, April 01, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MARIGAEG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN SALAMAN, LEBAK, SULTAN KUDARAT, TO AMPAD-GUIABAR MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:1âшphi1
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Marigaeg Elementary School in Salaman, Lebak, Sultan Kudarat, is hereby changed to Ampad-Guiabar Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take upon its approval.
