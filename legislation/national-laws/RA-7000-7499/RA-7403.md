# Republic Act No. 7403

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PHILIPPINE CHILDREN'S MEDICAL CENTER IN QUEZON BOULEVARD, QUEZON CITY, TO PHILIPPINE PEDIATRIC HOSPITAL AND DEVELOPMENT CENTER**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7403, April 14, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PHILIPPINE CHILDREN'S MEDICAL CENTER IN QUEZON BOULEVARD, QUEZON CITY, TO PHILIPPINE PEDIATRIC HOSPITAL AND DEVELOPMENT CENTER
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Philippine Children's Medical Center in Quezon Boulevard, Quezon City, is hereby changed to Philippine Pediatric Hospital and Development Center.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1âшphi1
