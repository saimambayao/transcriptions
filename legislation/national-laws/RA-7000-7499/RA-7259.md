**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE ALBAY PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN LEGASPI CITY, PROVINCE OF ALBAY, FROM ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) TO TWO HUNDRED (200) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7259, March 20, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE ALBAY PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN LEGASPI CITY, PROVINCE OF ALBAY, FROM ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) TO TWO HUNDRED (200) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Albay Provincial Hospital in Legaspi City, Province of Albay, is hereby increased from one hundred fifty (150) to two hundred (200) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Health is hereby empowered to reorganize said hospital accordingly.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
