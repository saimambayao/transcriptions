# Republic Act No. 7200

**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE M'LANG DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF M'LANG, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Fifth Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7200, February 25, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE M'LANG DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF M'LANG, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the M'lang District Hospital in the Municipality of M'lang, Province of Cotabato, is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
