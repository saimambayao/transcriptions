# Republic Act No. 7407

**AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE GINGOOG DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE CITY OF GINGOOG, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL, FROM A FIFTY-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL INTO A SEVENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE GINGOOG PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7407, April 14, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE GINGOOG DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE CITY OF GINGOOG, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL, FROM A FIFTY-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL INTO A SEVENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE GINGOOG PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assemble:
>
> **Section 1.** The Gingoog District Hospital in the City of Gingoog, Province of Misamis Oriental, is hereby expanded and upgraded from a fifty-bed capacity district hospital into a seventy-five-bed capacity provincial hospital, to be known as the Gingoog Provincial Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
