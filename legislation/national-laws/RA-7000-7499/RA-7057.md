# Republic Act No. 7057

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONIFACIO, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE BONIFACIO HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 7057             June 21, 1991**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONIFACIO, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE BONIFACIO HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a high school in the Municipality of Bonifacio, Province of Misamis Occidental, to be known as the Bonifacio High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act is hereby authorized to be appropriated in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **June 21, 1991**.
