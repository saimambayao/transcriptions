# Republic Act No. 7263

**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE PASSI DISTRICT HOSPITAL, IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PASSI, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Fifth Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-one
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7263, March 20, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE PASSI DISTRICT HOSPITAL, IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PASSI, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Passi District Hospital in the Municipality of Passi, Province of Iloilo, is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be charged against the lump-sum appropriations authorized for the purpose under the current General Appropriations Act. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance of the hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
