**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE JAGNA DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JAGNA, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7416, April 14, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE JAGNA DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JAGNA, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Jagna District Hospital in the Municipality of Jagna, Province of Bohol is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be charged against the lump-sum appropriations authorized for the purpose under the current General Appropriations Act. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance of the hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
