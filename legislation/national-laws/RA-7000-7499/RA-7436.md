**AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE AURORA MEMORIAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALER, PROVINCE OF AURORA, FROM A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL INTO A ONE HUNDRED-BED CAPACITY PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE AURORA PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7436, April 23, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE AURORA MEMORIAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALER, PROVINCE OF AURORA, FROM A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL INTO A ONE HUNDRED-BED CAPACITY PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE AURORA PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Aurora Memorial Hospital in the Municipality of Baler, Province of Aurora, is hereby expanded and upgraded from a twenty-five bed capacity district hospital into a one hundred-bed capacity provincial hospital, to be known as the Aurora Provincial Hospital.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
