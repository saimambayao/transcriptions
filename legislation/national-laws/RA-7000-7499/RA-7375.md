> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BOLUSAO BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN BOLUSAO, LAWAAN, EASTERN SAMAR, TO BOLUSAO HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
