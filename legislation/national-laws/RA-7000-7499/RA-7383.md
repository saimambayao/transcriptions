> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7383, April 10, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN GUILLERMO, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN GUILLERMO HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The rural health unit in the Municipality of San Guillermo, Province of Isabela, is hereby converted into a five-bed capacity health infirmary, to be known as the San Guillermo Health Infirmary.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The purpose of this Act is to provide adequate health services directly and promptly to the people of the town of San Guillermo, Province of Isabela.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
