> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7495, May 13, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE NAMUAC ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ANDRES, MUNICIPALITY OF SANCHEZ MIRA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO NAMUAC-SAN ANDRES ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Namuac Elementary School in Barangay San Andres, Municipality of Sanchez, Mira, Province of Cagayan, is hereby changed to Namuac-San Andres Elementary School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
