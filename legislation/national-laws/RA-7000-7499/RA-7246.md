**AN ACT EXPANDING THE CARIGARA DISTRICT HOSPITAL, WITH A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY, IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARIGARA, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, INTO A FIFTY-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7246, March 19, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT EXPANDING THE CARIGARA DISTRICT HOSPITAL, WITH A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY, IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARIGARA, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, INTO A FIFTY-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Carigara District Hospital, with a twenty-five-bed capacity, in the Municipality of Carigara, Province of Leyte, is hereby expanded into a fifty-bed capacity district hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be charged against the lump-sum appropriations authorized for the purpose under the current General Appropriations Act.  Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance of the hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
