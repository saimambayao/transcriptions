> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7326, March 31, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT REPEALING REPUBLIC ACTS NUMBERED SIXTY-ONE HUNDRED FORTY-FIVE AND FIFTY-FOUR HUNDRED SEVENTY-FOUR (RE: PROHIBITION TO CATCH FISH CALLED "IPON" DURING CERTAIN MONTHS OF THE YEAR, ETC.)
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Republic Acts Numbered Sixty-one hundred forty-five and Fifty-four hundred seventy-four are hereby repealed by this Act.
>
> **Section 2.** All provisions of existing laws, orders, decrees, rules and regulations inconsistent herewith are likewise repealed.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
