> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7311, March 30, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE WESTERN LEYTE HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAYBAY, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO DR. JOSE SILAO MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Western Leyte Hospital in the Municipality of Baybay, Province of Leyte, is hereby changed to Dr. Jose Silao Memorial Hospital.
>
