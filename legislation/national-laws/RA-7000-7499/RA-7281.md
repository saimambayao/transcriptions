> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7281, March 24, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING MARCH TEN OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ABRA TO BE KNOWN AS ABRA DAY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** March Ten of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Abra to commemorate the creation of the Province, to be known as Abra Day.
>
