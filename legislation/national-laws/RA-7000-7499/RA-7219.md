> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7219, March 06, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE EASTERN SAMAR PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BORONGAN, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR, FROM ONE HUNDRED (100) TO ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Eastern Samar Provincial Hospital in the Municipality of Borongan, Province of Eastern Samar, is hereby increased from one hundred (100) to one hundred fifty (150) beds.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
