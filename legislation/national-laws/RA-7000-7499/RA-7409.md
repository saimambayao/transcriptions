# Republic Act No. 7409

**AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE SALCEDO MEDICARE HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SALCEDO, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, FROM A FIFTEEN-BED CAPACITY MEDICARE HOSPITAL INTO A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SALCEDO DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7409, April 14, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE SALCEDO MEDICARE HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SALCEDO, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, FROM A FIFTEEN-BED CAPACITY MEDICARE HOSPITAL INTO A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SALCEDO DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Salcedo Medicare Hospital in the Municipality of Salcedo, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby expanded and upgraded into a twenty-five-bed capacity district hospital, to be known as the Salcedo District Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
