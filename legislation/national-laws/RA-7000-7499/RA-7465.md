**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TALOBATIB ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY TALOBATIB, MUNICIPALITY OF LABO, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO MABABANG PAARALAN NG CLAUDIO VILLAGEN**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7465, April 28, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TALOBATIB ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY TALOBATIB, MUNICIPALITY OF LABO, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO MABABANG PAARALAN NG CLAUDIO VILLAGEN
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Talobatib Elementary School in Barangay Talobatib, Municipality of Labo, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Mababang Paaralan ng Claudio Villagen.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
