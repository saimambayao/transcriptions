# Republic Act No. 7260

**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE SOCORRO DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOCORRO, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE, FROM TEN (10) TO TWENTY-FIVE (25) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7260, March 20, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE SOCORRO DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOCORRO, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE, FROM TEN (10) TO TWENTY-FIVE (25) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Socorro District Hospital in the Municipality of Socorro, Province of Surigao del Norte, is hereby increased from ten (10) to twenty-five (25) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
