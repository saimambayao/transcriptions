> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7441, April 27, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE LEYTE STATE SCHOOL OF AGRICULTURE IN ALANGALANG, LEYTE, TO OFFER THE BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN AGRICULTURE AND BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN AGRI-BUSINESS AND OTHER AGRICULTURE-RELATED PROGRAMS
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Leyte State School of Agriculture in Alangalang, Leyte, is hereby authorized to offer the Bachelor of Science in Agriculture and Bachelor of Science in Agri-business and other agriculture-related programs.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
