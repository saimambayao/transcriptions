> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7340, April 01, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE JOSE ABAD SANTOS HIGH SCHOOL TO PAMPANGA HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Jose Abad Santos High School in San Fernando, Pampanga, shall hereafter be known and referred to as the Pampanga High School.
>
