**AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARMEN, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CARMEN AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7124, August 22, 1991 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARMEN, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CARMEN AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established an agricultural high school on a 60-hectare lot donated by civic-minded citizens in the Municipality of Carmen, Province of Surigao del Sur, to be known as the Carmen Agricultural High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act is hereby authorized to be appropriated in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
