> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7374, April 10, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE COBOL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL TO PETRA Q. PIMENTEL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Cobol Elementary School situated in Barangay Petra Q. Pimentel, Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Petra Q. Pimentel Elementary School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
