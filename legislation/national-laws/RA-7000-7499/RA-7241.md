> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7241, March 19, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE DON JOSE C. LOCSIN MEMORIAL HOSPITAL IN THE CITY OF SILAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, FROM SEVENTY-FIVE (75) TO ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Don Jose C. Locsin Memorial Hospital in the City of Silay, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby increased from seventy-five (75) to one hundred fifty (150) beds.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
