# Republic Act No. 7312

**AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE MAGALLANES MEDICARE HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGALLANES, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, FROM A FIFTEEN-BED CAPACITY MEDICARE HOSPITAL INTO A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE MAGALLANES DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Fifth Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7312, March 30, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE MAGALLANES MEDICARE HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGALLANES, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, FROM A FIFTEEN-BED CAPACITY MEDICARE HOSPITAL INTO A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE MAGALLANES DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Magallanes Medicare Hospital in the Municipality of Magallanes, Province of Sorsogon, is hereby expanded and upgraded from a fifteen-bed capacity medicare hospital into a twenty-five-bed capacity district hospital, to be known as the Magallanes District Hospital.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
