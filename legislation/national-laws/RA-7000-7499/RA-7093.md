> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7093, August 02, 1991 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE ALANGALANG AGRO-INDUSTRIAL SCHOOL IN ALANGALANG, LEYTE, TO LEYTE STATE SCHOOL OF AGRICULTURE
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Alangalang Agro-Industrial School in Alangalang, Leyte, is hereby changed to Leyte State School of Agriculture.
>
> **Section 2.** The College shall offer secondary course in agriculture, short-term technical or vocational courses, and higher technological and professional instruction and training in agricultural and industrial fields as may be authorized by the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
