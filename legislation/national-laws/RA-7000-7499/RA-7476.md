> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7476, May 05, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALER, PROVINCE OF AURORA, TO BE KNOWN AS THE BALER HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a high school in the Municipality of Baler, Province of Aurora, to be known as the Baler High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
