# Republic Act No. 7194

**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE NAVAL GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, SUBPROVINCE OF BILIRAN, LEYTE, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7194, February 25, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE NAVAL GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, SUBPROVINCE OF BILIRAN, LEYTE, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Naval General Hospital in the Municipality of Naval, Subprovince of Biliran, Leyte, is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.
>
