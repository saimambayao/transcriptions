# Republic Act No. 7250

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MAGUILLING ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIAT, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO STA. BARBARA-MAGUILLING ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7250, March 19, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MAGUILLING ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIAT, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO STA. BARBARA-MAGUILLING ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Maguilling Elementary School in the Municipality of Piat, Province of Cagayan, is hereby changed to Sta. Barbara-Maguilling Elementary School.
>
