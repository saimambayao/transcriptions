> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7386, April 10, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT EXPANDING AND UPGRADING THE MONTEVISTA EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONTEVISTA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, FROM A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY EMERGENCY HOSPITAL INTO A SEVENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE DAVAO DEL NORTE PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The. Montevista Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Montevista, Province of Davao del Norte, is hereby expanded and upgraded from a twenty-five-bed capacity emergency hospital into a seventy-five-bed capacity provincial hospital, to be known as the Davao del Norte Provincial Hospital.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
