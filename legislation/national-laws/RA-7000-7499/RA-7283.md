> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7283, March 24, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING MARCH TWO OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOILO
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** In order to honor the memory of the late Tomas Confesor as a model Filipino citizen and inspiration of our people, March Two of every year is hereby declared as a special working holiday in the whole province of Iloilo.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
