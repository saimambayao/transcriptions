# Republic Act No. 7276

**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE GUIHULNGAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN GUIHULNGAN, NEGROS ORIENTAL, FROM FIFTY (50) TO SEVENTY-FIVE (75) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7276, March 24, 1992 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE GUIHULNGAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN GUIHULNGAN, NEGROS ORIENTAL, FROM FIFTY (50) TO SEVENTY-FIVE (75) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Guihulngan District Hospital in Guihulngan, Negros Oriental, is hereby increased from fifty (50) to seventy-five (75) beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
