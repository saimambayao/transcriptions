> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3635**
>
> **An Act Constituting the Province of Ilocos Sur into Two Engineering Districts**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Any provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, the Province of Ilocos Sur is hereby constituted into two engineering districts.1awp++i1 The municipalities comprising the first and second congressional districts shall likewise comprise the first and second engineering districts thereof, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** Each of the two engineering districts shall have one highway district engineer and such other personnel who shall be appointed in accordance with existing laws and regulations.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
