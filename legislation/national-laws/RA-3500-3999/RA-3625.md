> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3625**
>
> **An Act Converting the Leon Regional High School in the Municipality of Leon, Province of Iloilo, into a National Agricultural High School and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Leon Regional High School in the Municipality of Leon, Province of Iloilo, is hereby converted into a national agricultural high school and this school shall be located at Barrio Bucari, Municipality of Leon, Province of Iloilo.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-four. Thereafter, such sum as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
