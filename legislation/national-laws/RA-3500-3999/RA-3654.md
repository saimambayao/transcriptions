> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3654**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Teresa in the Municipality of Aborlan, Province of Palawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Catubangan, Bato-Bato and Pinagdogoan in the Municipality of Aborlan, Province of Palawan, are constituted into a district and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the Barrio of Teresa.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
