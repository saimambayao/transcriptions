> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3552**
>
> **An Act Transferring the Possession, Control and Ownership of Lots Nos. 1, 2-A, 2-B, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8 of the Cadastral Survey of the Province of Capiz Situated in Barrio Culasi, City of Roxas, from the National Government to the City of Roxas**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The possession, control and ownership of Lots Nos. 1, 2-A, 2-B, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8 of the Cadastral Survey of the Province of Capiz situated in Barrio Culasi, City of Roxas, which lots were surveyed in the name of the Commonwealth of the Philippines and now owned and in the possession of the Republic of the Philippines, are hereby transferred to the City of Roxas: *Provided,* However, That whatever rights might have been granted by the National Government to private individuals over the aforesaid parcels of land shall not be disturbed and shall continue to subsist until their termination.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1963.
