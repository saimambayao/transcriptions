> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3744**
>
> **An Act to Declare Barrio Cabaroan of the Municipal District of San Emilio, Province of Ilocos Sur, as the Seat of the Municipal District Government of the Said Municipal District**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Barrio Cabaroan of the Municipal District of San Emilio, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby declared as the seat of the municipal district government of the said municipal district.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
