> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3569**
>
> **An Act Constituting the First Congressional District of the Province of Cebu into a Separate Engineering District**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Any provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, the first congressional district of the Province of Cebu is hereby constituted into a separate engineering district to be known as the Second Engineering District of the Province of Cebu, with its office at the City of Danao.1âшphi1
>
> The district engineer for said engineering district shall be appointed in accordance with existing laws.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1963.
