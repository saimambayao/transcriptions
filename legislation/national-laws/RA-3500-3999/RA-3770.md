> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3770**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Buluan Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Buluan, Province of Cotabato, to Datu Luminog Mangelen Central Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Buluan Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Buluan, Province of Cotabato, is hereby changed to Datu Luminog Mangelen Central Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
