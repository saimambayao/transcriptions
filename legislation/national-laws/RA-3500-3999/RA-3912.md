> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3912**
>
> **An Act Extending the Term of the Electric Franchise Originally Granted to Natalio Digal but Subsequently Sold to Ocmis Electric Service Co., Inc.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The term of the franchise for an electric light, heat and power system granted to Natalio Digal under Act Numbered Thirty-six hundred forty-five, and subsequently sold and transferred to Ocmis Electric Service Co., Inc., by authority of Act Numbered Thirty-nine hundred fifty-one, is hereby extended with the same terms and conditions for a period of twenty-five years from December seven, nineteen hundred sixty-four.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1964.
