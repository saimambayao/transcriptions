> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3687**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Sta. Catalina Elementary School in the Municipality of Lubao, Province of Pampanga, to Graciano Paule Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Sta. Catalina Elementary School in the Municipality of Lubao, Province of Pampanga, is changed to Graciano Paule Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
