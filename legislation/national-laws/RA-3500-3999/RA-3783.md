> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3783**
>
> AN ACT REORGANIZING THE PROSECUTION DIVISION AND THE DEPORTATION BOARD OF THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE AND FIXING THE COMPENSATION OF CERTAIN PERSONNEL THEREIN.
>
> **Section 1.** The Prosecution Division of the Department of Justice shall be composed of the following personnel with annual compensation hereinafter fixed:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | Chief prosecuting attorney | P12,000.00 |
> | Two trial attorneys IV, each at | 11,400.00 |
> | Eight trial attorneys III, each at | 10,800.00 |
> | Two trial attorneys III, each at | 10,800.00 |
> | Five trial attorneys III, each at | 9,600.00 |
> | One trial attorney III | 9,000.00 |
> | Seven trial attorneys II, each at | 8,400.00 |
> | Six trial attorneys II, each at | 7,200.00 |
> | One research attorney I | 5,400.00 |
> | One supervising clerk I | 2,952.00 |
> | Three stenographic reporters, each at | 2,676.00 |
> | Six stenographic reporters, each at | 2,676.00 |
> | One research attorney I | 4,800.00 |
> | One stenographer | 2,676.00 |
> | One statistical aide I | 2,196.00 |
> | One clerk I | 1,980.00 |
> | One janitor | 1,680.00 |
>
> **Section 2.** The Deportation Board of the Department of Justice shall be composed of the following personnel with annual compensation hereinafter fixed:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | One Chairman (by detail). |  |
> | Two members (by detail). |  |
> | One trial attorney IV | P11,400.00 |
> | One trial attorney II | 7,200.00 |
> | Two trial attorneys II, each at | 7,200.00 |
> | One legal officer I | 5,400.00 |
> | One senior clerk | 2,952.00 |
> | One sheriff | 2,400.00 |
> | One janitor | 1,680.00 |
>
> **Section 3.** The officials and attorneys mentioned in Sections one and two of this Act shall receive the salary provided for herein, notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary.
>
> **Section 4.** There is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, such sums as any be necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 22, 1963.
