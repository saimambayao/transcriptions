> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3736**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Lacaron Elementary School in Barrio Lacaron, Municipality of Valladolid, Negros Occidental, to Maria Palacios Presbitero Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Lacaron Elementary School in barrio Lacaron, Municipality of Valladolid, Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Maria Palacios Presbitero Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
