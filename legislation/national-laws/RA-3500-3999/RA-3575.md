> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3575**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Albay High School in the Municipality of Guinobatan, Province of Albay, to Pio Duran Memorial School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Albay High School in the Municipality of Guinobatan, Province of Albay, is hereby changed to Pio Duran Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1963.
