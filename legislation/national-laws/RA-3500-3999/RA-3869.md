> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3869**
>
> **An Act Increasing the Bed Capacity of the Doña Gregoria Memorial Hospital in the Municipality of Agoo, Province of La Union, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Doña Gregoria Memorial Hospital in the Municipality of Agoo, Province of La Union, is hereby increased to fifty.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds of the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to carry out the purposes of this Act. Every year thereafter, the said sum must be included in the annual General Appropriations Acts.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1964.
