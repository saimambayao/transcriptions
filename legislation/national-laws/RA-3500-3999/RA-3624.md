> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3624**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipal District of Tatarikan in the Province of Lanao del Sur, to Pagayawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipal District of Tatarikan in the Province of Lanao del Sur, is hereby changed to Pagayawan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
