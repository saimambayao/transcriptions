> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3646**
>
> **An Act to Provide for the Establishment of One Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Initao, Province of Misamis Oriental, and to Authorize the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the direct supervision of the Director of Medical Services, one emergency hospital in the Municipality of Initao, Province of Misamis Oriental, to be known as the Initao Emergency Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of four hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-four. Thereafter, such sum as may be needed for its operation and maintenance of said hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
