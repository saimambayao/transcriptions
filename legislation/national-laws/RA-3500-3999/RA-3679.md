> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3679**
>
> **An Act Changing Certain Names of Schools in the Municipality of Tuy, Province of Batangas**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The names of the following schools in the Municipality of Tuy, Province of Batangas, are changed as follows:
>
> (a) Tuy Elementary School to Don Gregorio Paradero Elementary School;
>
> (b) Putol-Guinhawa Elementary School to Don Jose Zabarte, Sr. Elementary School;
>
> (c) Malibu Elementary School to Don Santiago de Guzman Elementary School; and
>
> (d) Mataywanak Barrio School to Doña Josefa A. de Guzman Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
