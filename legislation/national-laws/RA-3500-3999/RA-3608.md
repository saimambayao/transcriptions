> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3608**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Maguininango in the Municipality of Allacapan, Province of Cagayan, to San Juan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Maguininango in the Municipality of Allacapan, Province of Cagayan, is hereby changed to San Juan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
