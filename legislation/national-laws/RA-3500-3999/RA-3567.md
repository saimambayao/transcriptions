> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3567**
>
> **An Act Creating a Branch Office of the Register of Deeds of the Province of Ilocos Sur, with Permanent Station at the Municipality of Narvacan, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby created a branch office of the Register of Deeds of the Province of Ilocos Sur, with permanent station at the Municipality of Narvacan. which shall be under the immediate control and supervision of a deputy register of deeds to be appointed by the Secretary of Justice.1âшphi1 The said deputy register of deeds shall be class III under WAPCO classification and shall receive such compensation in the range now provided by laws and regulations. He shall be assisted by such additional personnel, likewise appointed by the Secretary of Justice, as may be necessary to carry out the duties appurtenant to the said branch office.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of fifty thousand pesos, or as much thereof as may be necessary is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to carry out the purposes of this Act. Thereafter, such sum necessary for the same purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1963.
