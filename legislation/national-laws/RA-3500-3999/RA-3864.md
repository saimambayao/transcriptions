> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3864**
>
> **An Act Increasing the Bed Capacity of Allen Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Allen, Province of Samar, and Authorizing the Additional Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of Allen Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Allen, Province of Samar, is hereby increased from twenty-five to fifty.
>
> **Section 2.** Additional sum necessary in accordance with the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred thirty-nine is hereby authorized to be appropriated to carry out the purposes of this Act for the fiscal year nineteen hundred and sixty-five, in addition to its existing appropriations.1âшphi1 Thereafter, said sums shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1964.
