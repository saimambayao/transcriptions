> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3603**
>
> **An Act Providing for the Operation and Maintenance of an Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Bongabon, Province of Nueva Ecija, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the direct supervision of the Department of Health, an emergency hospital in the Municipality of Bongabon, Province of Nueva Ecija.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of seventy-five thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of said hospital during the current fiscal year. Thereafter, said hospital shall be financed in accordance with the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred thirty-nine.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
