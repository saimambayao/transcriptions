> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3816**
>
> **An Act to Further Amend Certain Sections of the Charter of the City of Baguio so as to Provide a New Schedule of Salaries of its Officials**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item 114, paragraph "a," Title A, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-one hundred and one, entitled "An Act appropriating funds for public works, synchronizing the same with previous public works appropriations," is hereby deleted.
>
> **Section 2.** Under paragraph "b," Title A, Section one of the same Act, a new item is hereby inserted to read as follows:
>
> > "191. Bogo National Hospital, Bogo, Cebu ₱50,000.00"
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
