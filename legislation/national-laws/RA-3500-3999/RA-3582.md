> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3582**
>
> **An Act to Increase the Bed Capacity of the Cagayan Provincial Hospital in the Province of Cagayan from Fifty to Seventy-Five Beds and to Authorize the Appropriation of Funds for the Purpose**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Cagayan Provincial Hospital in the Province of Cagayan is hereby increased from fifty to seventy-five beds.
>
> **Section 2.** In addition to the appropriations for the said hospital under existing law, the sum of two hundred ninety-six thousand seven hundred twenty-one pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance thereof.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1963.
