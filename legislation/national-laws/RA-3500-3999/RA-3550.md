> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3550**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Talibon, Province of Bohol**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Busulian, Suba and Pinamgo in the Municipality of Talibon, Province of Bohol, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Busulian, Suba and Pinamgo, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1963.
