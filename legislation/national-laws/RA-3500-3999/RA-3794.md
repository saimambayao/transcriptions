> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3794**
>
> **An Act Authorizing the Appropriation of Three Hundred Thousand Pesos as Additional Sum for the Implementation of Republic Act Numbered Fifteen Hundred Ninety-Seven. as Amended**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of three hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, as additional sum for the implementation of Republic Act Numbered Fifteen hundred ninety-seven, as amended.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
