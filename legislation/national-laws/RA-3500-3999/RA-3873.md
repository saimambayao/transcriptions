> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3873**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Bureau of Public Libraries to the National Library**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Bureau of Public Libraries is hereby changed to that of The National Library.1awp++i1
>
> **Section 2.** All references to the Bureau of Public Libraries in laws, executive orders, rules and regulations shall henceforth be understood to refer to The National Library.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1964.
