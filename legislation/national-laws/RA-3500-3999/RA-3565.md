> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3565**
>
> **An Act Amending Item (a), Page 45, Subparagraph (17) III, for the Province of Cebu, Paragraph f, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (a), page 45, subparagraph (17) III, paragraph f, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(a) Talisay Municipal Hall Construction ₱75,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1963.
