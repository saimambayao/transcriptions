> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3772**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Libacao Community School in the Municipality of Himamaylan, Province of Negros Occidental, to Don Remigio Limsiaco Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In recognition of the invaluable services rendered to the people of Barrio Libacao, Municipality of Himamaylan, Province of Negros Occidental, by the late Don Remigio Limsiaco, a philanthropist and civic leader of the said barrio, the name of Libacao Community School is changed to Don Remigio Limsiaco Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
