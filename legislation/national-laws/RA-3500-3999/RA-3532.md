> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3532**
>
> **An Act Appropriating One Hundred Fifty Thousand Pesos for the Construction of a Monument at Fort Santiago, to Honor the Memory of the Unknown Soldiers**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the construction of a monument at Fort Santiago, to honor the memory of the unknown soldiers.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1963.
