> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3549**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Abulug, Province of Cagayan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Sirit and Canayum in the Municipality of Abulug, Province of Cagayan, are concerted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Sirit and Canayun, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1963.
