> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3972**
>
> **An Act Approving Any Assignment, Sale, and Transfer of the Franchise Granted to Rafael Consing Under Republic Act Numbered Six Hundred Ninety-Seven in Favor of Marcela S. Aquino of Butuan City**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Act Numbered Thirty-six hundred thirty-six, and to the provisions of the Constitution, approval is hereby given to any assignment, sale and transfer of the franchise granted to Rafael Consing to install, operate and maintain a telephone system in the City of Butuan, under Republic Act Numbered Six hundred ninety-seven, in favor of Marcela S. Aquino of Butuan City, together with all the property and equipment acquired thereunder.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

> Approved: June 18, 1964.
