> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3827**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Dewey Boulevard, Extending from the City of Manila, Through the City of Pasay, to the Municipality of Parañaque, Province of Rizal, and Any Future Extension Thereof Up to Cavite City, to President Roxas Boulevard**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Dewey Boulevard, extending from the City of Manila, through the City of Pasay, to the Municipality of Parañaque, Province of Rizal, and any future extension thereof up to Cavite City, is changed to President Roxas Boulevard.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
