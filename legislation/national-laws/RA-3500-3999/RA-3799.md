> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3799**
>
> **An Act Granting Pedro R. Banzon a Franchise to Install, Maintain and Operate an Electric Light, Heat and Power System in the Barrio of Liminangcong, Municipality of Taytay, Province of Palawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Subject to the terms and conditions established in Act numbered Thirty-six hundred and thirty-six as amended by Commonwealth Act numbered One hundred and thirty-two, and to the provisions of the Constitution. There is granted to Pedro R. Banzon, the barrio of Liminangcong, Municipality of Taytay, Province of Palawan, for a period of twenty-five years from the approval of this Act, the right, privilege and authority to install, maintain and operate an electric light, heat and power system for the purpose of generating and distributing electric light, heat and/or power for sale within the barrio of Liminangcong, Municipality of Taytay, Province of Palawan.
>
> **Section 2.** In the event that the grantee shall purchase and secure from the National Power Corporation electric heat and power, the National Power Corporation is hereby authorized to negotiate and transact for the benefit and in behalf of the public consumers with reference to rates.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
