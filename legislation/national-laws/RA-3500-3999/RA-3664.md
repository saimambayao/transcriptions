> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3664**
>
> **An Act to Amend Republic Act Numbered Thirty-Four Hundred Twenty, Entitled "An Act Creating the Municipality of Surallah in the Province of Cotabato"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section one of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-four hundred twenty is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Sec. 1. The barrios of Centrallah, Sinolon, New Dumangas, Veterans, Lake Sebu, Lambongtong, Solution, Lamian, Lamla-at, Lahit, Barrio Ten and Barrio Eleven (Libertad), including the sitios of Colongolo, Tubi-Ala, Bayabas, Head Sapali, Tabodtod, Nawan, Lacag, Kolon Bong, Laconon, Salacape, Bogad, North Seguil, Tabanlid, Lambod, Bacnob, Lamcade, San Jose, Dalag, Talihik Bac-alo, Datu Ligal, Datu Ba-oy, Lamlosok, Bacdolong, Tabawao, Tanon, Camoning, Head Ala, Lambilan, Maculan, Lamsogod, Buenavista, Canajay, Lamlako, San Francisco, Little Baguio, Lambosong, Tamsalon. Tacamsad, Lamhinik, Tucubo, Sla-Abon, Datal Kling, Head Sipaca, Lawa, South Kiantay, Siop, Moto Bantong, Moloy, Crossing Cabayo, Ila-elis, Datu Glam, Datu Godwino, Datu Ouel and Sala Banog, of Bañga, Cotabato, are separated from said municipality, and are constituted into a distinct and independent municipality to be known as the Municipality of Surallah, Province of Cotabato, with the seat of government at the present site of Barrio Ten and Barrio Eleven (Libertad) and the boundary of this new municipality shall be more particularly defined as follows:
> >

> >
> > "Thence, following the farm road in a southeasterly direction to Point 2 (corner 3 of Lot No. 500, PLS-285-D, Barrio Cinco (5), Map of Bañga, Province of Cotabato);
> >

> >
> > "Thence, following the farm road in a southeasterly direction to Point 4 (corner 3 of Lot No. 548, PLS-285-D, Barrio Cinco (5), Map of Bañga, Province of Cotabato);
> >

> >
> > "Thence, in a southeasterly direction to Point 6 (northeastern corner of Lot No. 555, PLS-214-D-9, Map of Bañga, Province of Cotabato);
> >
> > "Thence, following the boundary of Bañga Settlement District (PLS-214-D-9, Project I-MSA-45) in a southwesterly direction to Point 7 (intersection of PLS-214-D-9 and SWO-26589 on Lot No. 278, SWO-26589);

> >
> > "Thence, following the farm road in a Southeasterly direction to Point 17 (southeastern corner of Lot No. 232, PLS-214-D-8, Barrio 8, Map of Bañga, Province of Cotabato);
> >
> > "Thence, following the farm road in a northeasterly direction to Point 18 (northwestern corner of Lot No. 110, PLS-214-D-8, Map of Bañga, Province of Cotabato);

> > "Thence, following the farm road in a southeasterly direction to Point 1, which is the point of beginning."
>
> **Section 2.** Section two of the same Act is hereby repealed.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
