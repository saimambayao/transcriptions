> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3754**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Municipal District of Real in the Province of Quezon into a Regular Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Municipal District of Real, Province of Quezon, is hereby converted into a regular municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** The present mayor, vice-mayor and councilors of the Municipal District of Real shall be the mayor, vice-mayor and councilors, respectively, of the new municipality until their successors shall have been elected in the next regular elections for provincial and municipal officials and shall have qualified.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
