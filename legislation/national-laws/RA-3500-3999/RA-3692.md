> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3692**
>
> AN ACT TO AMEND CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWENTY-SEVEN HUNDRED AND ONE.
>
> **Section 1.** Item (a) Group III, page 179, subparagraph 4, for the Province of Albay, paragraph (b), Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred and one is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |  |
> | --- | --- | --- |
> | "(a) | For the construction of School buildings with pre-fabricated steel framings in the Third District of Albay | P50,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** Item (a), Group III, page 255, subparagraph 4, for the Province of Albay, paragraph c, Section two of the same Act, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |  |
> | --- | --- | --- |
> | "(a) | For the construction of school buildings with pre-fabricated steel framings in the Third District of Albay | P250,000.00" |
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963
