> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3890**
>
> **An Act Providing for the Establishment of an Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Hinatuan, Province of Surigao del Sur, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established under the direct supervision of the Department of Health, an emergency hospital in the Municipality of Hinatuan, Province of Surigao del Sur, to be known as Hinatuan Emergency Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-five.1âшphi1 Thereafter, such sums as may be needed for the operation and maintenance of said hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1964.
