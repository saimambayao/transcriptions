> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3724**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Ifugao Pilot Opportunity School of Agriculture in the Municipal District of Lamut, Subprovince of Ifugao, Mountain Province, to Payon Bugan Pilot Opportunity School of Agriculture**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Ifugao Pilot Opportunity School of Agriculture in the Municipal District of Lamut, Subprovince of Ifugao, Mountain Province, is hereby changed to Payon Bugan Pilot Opportunity School of Agriculture.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
