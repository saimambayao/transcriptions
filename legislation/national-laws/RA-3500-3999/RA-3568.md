> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3568**
>
> **An Act Converting the Initao Forest Reserve Located in the Municipality of Initao, Province of Misamis Oriental, into a National Park, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Initao Forest Reserve located in the Municipality of Initao, Province of Misamis Oriental, is hereby converted into a national park to be known as the Initao National Park.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to defray the expenses for development, beautification, and construction of rest houses and other facilities within the vicinity of the Initao National Park.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1963.
