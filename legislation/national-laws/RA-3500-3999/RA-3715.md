> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3715**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Sapa-Sapa Barrio School in the Municipality of Tandu Bas, Province of Sulu, to Tuan Ligaddung Lipae Barrio School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Sapa-Sapa Barrio School in the Municipality of Tandu Bas, Province of Sulu, is hereby changed to Tuan Ligaddung Lipae Barrio School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
