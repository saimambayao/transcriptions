> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3695**
>
> **An Act Appropriating the Sum of Fifty Thousand Pesos for the Rehabilitation and/or Reconstruction of the House Where the Late Claro M. Recto was Born in the Municipality of Tiaong, Province of Quezon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of fifty thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the rehabilitation and/or reconstruction of the house where the late Claro M. Recto was born in the Municipality of Tiaong, Province of Quezon.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
