> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3767**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Tiaman Community School in the Barrio of Bolinsong, Municipality of Bonifacio, Province of Misamis Occidental, to Epifanio De Los Santos Community School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Tiaman Community School in the Barrio of Bolinsong, Municipality of Bonifacio, Province of Misamis Occidental, is hereby changed to Epifanio de Los Santos Community School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
