> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3556**
>
> **An Act to Increase the Bed Capacity of the Mindoro Oriental Provincial Hospital**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Mindoro Oriental Provincial Hospital is hereby increased from seventy-five beds to one hundred beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The financing of this hospital shall be in accordance with the Hospital Financing Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1963.
