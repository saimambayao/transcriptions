> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3547**
>
> **An Act to Authorize the Establishment of a Fresh-Water Investigation Unit (Limnological) and Fish Nursery Station of Lake Paoay in the Municipality of Paoay, Province of Ilocos Norte, and to Authorize the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established and maintained, under the supervision of the Commissioner of Fisheries, a fresh-water investigation unit (limnological) and a fresh-water fish nursery station at Paoay Lake in the Municipality of Paoay, Province of Ilocos Norte.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to carry into effect the purposes of this Act during the fiscal year ending June thirtieth, nineteen hundred and sixty-four. This amount shall be apportioned as follows:
>
> a. Fifty thousand pesos for the procurement of site, essential equipment and installation of buildings and laboratories.1âшphi1
>
> b. Twenty-five thousand pesos for the maintenance of personnel for the fresh-water investigation unit.
>
> c. Twenty-five thousand pesos for the maintenance of personnel for the fish nursery station project.
>
> Thereafter, the amount of not less than fifty thousand pesos needed for maintenance and operation shall be included in the General Appropriation Act each year.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1963.
