> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3899, June 18, 1964 ]**
>
> **AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED TWENTY-EIGHT BY FIX­ING THE COMPENSATION OF THE OFFICIALS OF THE CITY OF MANILA.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section one of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred and twentyeight is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Section 1. The provisions of the Revised Charter of the City of Manila, as amended, to the contrary notwithstanding, officials of the city shall receive the following annual compensation:
> >
> > |  |  |
> > | --- | --- |
> > | Mayor | ₱20,000.00 |
> > | Vice-Mayor | 18,000.00 |
> > | Secretary to the Mayor | 16,000.00 |
> > | Secretary to the Board | 14,000.00 |
> > | Two assistant Secretaries to the Mayor, each at ₱14,000.00 | 28,000.00 |
> > | Twenty Councilors, each at ₱14,000.00 | 280,000.00 |
> > | City Engineer | 16,000.00 |
> > | Assistant City Engineer | 14,000.00 |
> > | Chief of Police | 16,000.00 |
> > | Deputy Chief of Police | 14,000.00 |
> > | Chief, Detective Bureau | 14,000.00 |
> > | Chief, Fire Department | 16,000.00 |
> > | Deputy Chief, Fire Department | 14,000.00 |
> > | City Treasurer | 16,000.00 |
> > | Assistant City Treasurer | 14,000.00 |
> > | City Assessor | 16,000.00 |
> > | Assistant City Assessor | 14,000.00 |
> > | Assistant City Health Officer | 14,000.00 |
> > | City Health Officer | 16,000.00 |
> > | Superintendent of City Schools including the National share of ₱7,200 *per annum* | 16,000.00 |
> > | Two Assistant Superintendent of City Schools, each at ₱14,000.00 | 28,000.00 |
> > | City Auditor | 16,000.00 |
> > | Assistant City Auditor | 14,000.00 |
> > | City Public Service Officer | 16,000.00 |
> > | Assistant City Public Service Officer | 14,000.00 |
>
> **Section 2.** The incumbent officials holding the positions affected by this Act shall receive the salary increases upon the effectivity thereof without the necessity of a new appointment.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June  18, 1964.
