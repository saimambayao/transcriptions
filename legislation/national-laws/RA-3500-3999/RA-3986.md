> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3986**
>
> **An Act to Amend Section Three of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Seven Hundred and Thirty-Two, Entitled "An Act Providing for the Maintenance and Operation of a Hospital in the Municipality of Tagudin, Province of Ilocos Sur, to be Known as the Southern Ilocos Sur Hospital, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section three of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred and thirty-two is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Sec. 3. The sum of three hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the maintenance and operation of the said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred and sixty-one: *Provided,* That the sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos shall be used for the construction of buildings.(awÞhi( Such sums as may be necessary for its maintenance and operation in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1964.
