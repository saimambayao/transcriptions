> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3613**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Moalboal Provincial High School in the Municipality of Moalboal, Province of Cebu, into a National School of Fisheries, to be Known as the Moalboal Southern Cebu School of Fisheries and to Authorize the Appropriations of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Moalboal Provincial High School located in the Municipality of Moalboal, Province of Cebu, is hereby converted into a national school of fisheries, to be known as the Moalboal Southern Cebu National School of Fisheries and to be headed by a fishery school principal. The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to reorganize said school accordingly.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, as additional appropriation to carry out the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
