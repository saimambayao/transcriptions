> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3577**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Gutad Elementary School in the Municipality of Floridablanca, Province of Pampanga, to Atanacio Pangan Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Gutad Elementary School in the Municipality of Floridablanca, Province of Pampanga, is hereby changed to Atanacio Pangan Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1963.
