> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3557**
>
> **An Act Appropriating the Sum of Two Hundred Thousand Pesos for the Purchase of Hospital Furnishings and Equipment of the New National Orthopedic Hospital**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the purchase of hospital furnishings and equipment of the new National Orthopedic Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1963.
