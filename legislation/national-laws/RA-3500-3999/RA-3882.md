> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3882**
>
> **An Act Converting San Carlos Rural High School in the Municipality of San Carlos, Province of Pangasinan, into a National Agricultural School to be Known as Speaker Eugenio Perez National Agricultural School, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The San Carlos Rural High School in the Municipality of San Carlos, Province of Pangasinan, is hereby converted into a national agricultural school to be known as Speaker Eugenio Perez National Agricultural School.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-five.1âшphi1 Thereafter, such sums as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1964.
