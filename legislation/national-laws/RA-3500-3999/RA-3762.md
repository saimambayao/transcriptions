> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3762**
>
> **An Act Declaring the Barrio of Alañgan in the Municipality of Limay, Province of Bataan, a Duly Constituted Barrio Thereof**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrio of Alañgan in the Municipality of Limay, Province of Bataan, is hereby declared a duly constituted barrio of said municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
