> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3639**
>
> **An Act Providing for the Establishment of Emergency Hospitals in the Municipality of Angeles and the Municipality of Macabebe, Province of Pampanga**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the direct supervision of the Department of Health, one emergency hospital in each of the municipalities of Angeles and Macabebe in the Province of Pampanga.
>
> **Section 2.** The emergency hospitals established under its Act shall be financed in accordance with the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred and thirty-nine.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1963.
