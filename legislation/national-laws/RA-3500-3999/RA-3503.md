> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3503**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Tuburan National Vocational School in the Municipality of Tuburan, Province of Cebu, to Tuburan National School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Tuburan National Vocational School in the Municipality of Tuburan, Province of Cebu, is hereby changed to Tuburan National School. General courses as well as vocational courses shall be offered in said school.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1962.
