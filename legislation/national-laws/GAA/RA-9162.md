## Title

AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR THE OPERATION OF THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES FROM JANUARY ONE TO DECEMBER THIRTY-ONE, TWO THOUSAND AND TWO, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.

## Section 1. Short Title

This Act shall be known as the "General Appropriations Act of FY 2002."
