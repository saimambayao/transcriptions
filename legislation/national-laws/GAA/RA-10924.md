## Title

AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR THE OPERATION OF THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES FROM JANUARY ONE TO DECEMBER THIRTY-ONE, TWO THOUSAND AND SEVENTEEN, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.

## Structure

The GAA FY 2017 is organized into the following volumes:

### Volume I-A - Appropriations (Part 1)
- Legislative Branch
- Executive Branch (Offices under the Office of the President through Department of Health)

### Volume I-B - Appropriations (Part 2)
- Executive Branch (Department of the Interior and Local Government through Department of Transportation)
- Judicial Branch
- Constitutional Commissions
- Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM)

### Volume II - General and Special Provisions

### Major Budget Allocations

The P3.35 trillion national budget for FY 2017 prioritized:
- Infrastructure development
- Education
- Social services
- Peace and order
- Bangsamoro transition (ARMM operations during transition period)
