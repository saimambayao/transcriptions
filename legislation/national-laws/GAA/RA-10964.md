## Title

AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR THE OPERATION OF THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES FROM JANUARY ONE TO DECEMBER THIRTY-ONE, TWO THOUSAND AND EIGHTEEN, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.

## Context

The GAA FY 2018 was significant as one of the last national budgets before the creation of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM). The Bangsamoro Organic Law (Republic Act No. 11054) was signed on July 26, 2018, during this fiscal year.
