## Title

AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR THE OPERATION OF THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES FROM JANUARY ONE TO DECEMBER THIRTY-ONE, TWO THOUSAND AND TWENTY, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.

## Context

The GAA FY 2020 was the first full-year national budget that included appropriations for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), which was established in 2019 following the ratification of the Bangsamoro Organic Law (R.A. No. 11054). The BARMM replaced the former Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM).

This was also the budget year affected by the COVID-19 pandemic, which began in early 2020, leading to the enactment of the Bayanihan to Heal as One Act (R.A. No. 11469) to allow the President to realign budget items.
