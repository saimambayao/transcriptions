## Note on Full Text Availability

The full text of RA 10352 (GAA FY 2013) is a multi-volume budget document spanning approximately 1,700+ pages with detailed appropriations across every government agency. The complete document is available through the Internet Archive and DBM.

## Title

AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR THE OPERATION OF THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES FROM JANUARY ONE TO DECEMBER THIRTY-ONE, TWO THOUSAND AND THIRTEEN, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.

## Structure

The GAA FY 2013 is organized into the following volumes:

### Volume I - Appropriations

Appropriations organized by department/agency with detailed breakdowns by:
- Program/Activity/Project (P/A/P)
- Personal Services (PS)
- Maintenance and Other Operating Expenses (MOOE)
- Capital Outlays (CO)

### Volume II - Special Provisions and General Provisions

Contains:
- Special Provisions applicable to specific agencies
- General Provisions applicable to all agencies
- Effectivity clause
