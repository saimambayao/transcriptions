> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 967, June 20, 1953 ]**
>
> AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS FOR THE CONSTRUCTION, EQUIPMENT AND MAINTENANCE OF A LEPROSY RESEARCH LABORATORY AND TRAINING CENTER.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated the sum of three hundred thousand pesos for the construction, equipment and maintenance of an adequate leprosy research laboratory and training center building.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 20, 1953.
