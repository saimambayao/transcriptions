# Republic Act No. 882

**AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE OLIMPIA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAKATI, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO FRANCISCO BENITEZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 882, June 19, 1953 ]**
>
> AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE OLIMPIA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAKATI, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO FRANCISCO BENITEZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Olimpia Elementary School in the municipality of Makati, Province of Rizal, is hereby changed to Francisco Benitez Elementary School.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 19, 1953.
