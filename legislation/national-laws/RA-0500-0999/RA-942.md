> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 942, June 20, 1953 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF PAGKILATAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATANGAS, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Pagkilatan is separated from the barrio of Matoco, municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, and constituted into a barrio in the said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Pagkilatan.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 20, 1953
