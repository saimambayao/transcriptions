>

>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The barrio of Zarragoza, in the municipality of Batad, Province of Iloilo, is transferred from said municipality to that of the municipality of Balasan, Province of Iloilo.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
