# Republic Act No. 883

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE JESUS DE LA PESA BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARIKINA, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO LEODEGARIO VICTORINO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 883, June 19, 1953 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE JESUS DE LA PESA BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARIKINA, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO LEODEGARIO VICTORINO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Jesus de la Pena Barrio School in the municipality of Marikina, Province of Rizal is hereby changed to Leodegario Victorino Elementary School.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 19, 1953.
