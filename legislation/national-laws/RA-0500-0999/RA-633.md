> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 633, June 08, 1951 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANTATET IN THE PROVINCE OF ISABELA TO LUNA.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the municipality of Antatet, Province of Isabela, is hereby changed to Luna.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 8, 1951.
