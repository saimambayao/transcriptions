# Republic Act No. 880

**AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF MARIVELES ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARIVELES, PROVINCE OF BATAAN, TO "ANTONIO G. LLAMAS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL."**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 880, June 19, 1953 ]**
>
> AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF MARIVELES ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARIVELES, PROVINCE OF BATAAN, TO "ANTONIO G. LLAMAS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL."
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Mariveles Elementary School in the municipality of Mariveles, Province of Bataan, is hereby changed to "Antonio G. Llamas Elementary School."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 19, 1953.
