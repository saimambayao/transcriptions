> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 534, June 16, 1950 ]**
>
> AN ACT TO NATIONALIZE THE LA UNION TRADE SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The La Union Trade School in the Province of La Union shall hereafter be maintained by the National Government, and the necessary amount therefor is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
