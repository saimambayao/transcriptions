> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 940, June 20, 1953 ]**
>
> AN ACT TO EMPOWER MEMBERS OF THE CONGRESS TO ADMINISTER OATH OF OFFICE TO ELECTIVE PUBLIC OFFICIALS.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Members of the Congress of the Republic of the Philippines are hereby empowered to administer oath of office to elective public officials.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 20, 1953.
