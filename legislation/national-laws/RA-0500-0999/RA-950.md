# Republic Act No. 950

**AN ACT APPROPRIATING A SUM OF MONEY FOR SALARY OF ONE COMMERCIAL AGENT IN THE PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO.**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 950
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1953/ra_950_1953.html)

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 950, June 20, 1953 ]**
>
> AN ACT APPROPRIATING A SUM OF MONEY FOR SALARY OF ONE COMMERCIAL AGENT IN THE PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of two thousand on hundred and sixty pesos is appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for salary of one commercial agent in the Province of Occidental Mindoro, in accordance with Republic Act Numbered Three hundred and eighty-eight.  Thereafter, the necessary sum for the purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.
>
> **Section 2.**  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 20, 1953
