# Republic Act No. 960

**AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BAGUIDBIRIN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE.**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 960
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1953/ra_960_1953.html)

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 960, June 20, 1953 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BAGUIDBIRIN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Baguidbirin, in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of Marinduque, is converted into a barrio to be called Baguidbirin.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 20, 1953.
