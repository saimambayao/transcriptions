> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 838, March 26, 1953 ]**
>
> AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF KAWIT HIGH SCHOOL TO EMILIANO T. TIRONA MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** In recognition of the distinguished services rendered by Emiliano T. Tirona, Filipino statesman and leader and an illustrious son of the Province of Cavite, the name of Kawit High School is changed to Emiliano T. Tirona Memorial High School.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, March 26, 1953.
