> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 890, June 19, 1953 ]**
>
> AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF CATMON, MUNICIPALITY OF SIBALOM, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, TO PAJARITO.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Catmon in the municipality of Sibalom, Province of Antique, is hereby changed to Pajarito.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 19, 1953.
