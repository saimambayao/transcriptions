> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 933, June 20, 1953 ]**
>
> AN ACT TO NAME "HALSEMA MOUNTAIN ROAD" THE NATIONAL HIGHWAY FROM BAGUIO TO BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The national highway from Baguio to Bontoc, Mountain Province, is named "Halsema Mountain Road".1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 20, 1953.
