# Republic Act No. 11905

**AN ACT SEPARATING THE SITIO OF BUTACAN FROM BARANGAY DEL MONTE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALACOGON, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR, AND CONSTITUTING IT INTO A DISTINCT AND INDEPENDENT BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY BATUCAN**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11905, July 29, 2022 ]**
>
> **AN ACT SEPARATING THE SITIO OF BUTACAN FROM BARANGAY DEL MONTE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALACOGON, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR, AND CONSTITUTING IT INTO A DISTINCT AND INDEPENDENT BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY BATUCAN**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** *Creation of Barangay Batucan.* - The sitio of Batucan is hereby separated from Barangay del Monte in the Municipality of Talacogon, Province of Agusan del Sur and constituted as a distinct and independent barangay to be known as Barangay Batucan.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** *Territorial Boundaries.* - The territorial boundaries of Barangay Batucan are hereby described as follows:
