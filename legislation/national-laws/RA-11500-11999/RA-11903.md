> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11903, July 28, 2022 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CREATING A BARANGAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN LUIS, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY DOÑA MAXIMA**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** *Creation of Barangay Doña Maxima.* - There is hereby created a barangay in the Municipality of San Luis, Province of Agusan del Sur, to be known as Barangay Doña Maxima.
>

> **Section 3.** *Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite.* - The plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission on Elections (COMELEC) pursuant to Provincial Ordinance No. 07, Series of 1991 oof the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of the Province of Agusan del Sur, which ratified the creation of Barangay Doña Maxima as proclaimed by the Plebiscite Board of Canvassers on December 19, 1998, shall serve as substantial compliance with the plebiscite requirement under Section 10 of the Local Government Code of 1991, as amended.
>
> **Section 4.** *Incumbent Barangay Officials.* - The incumbent elected barangay officials of Barangay Doña Maxima shall continue to hold office until such time their successors have been duly elected and qualified.
>
> **Section 5.** *Public Infrastructure and Facilities.* - All public infrastructure and facilities existing therein at the time of the approval of this Act are hereby transferred without cost or compensation to and shall be administered by  Barangay Doña Maxima.
>
> **Section 6.** *National Tax Allotment (NTA) Shares.* - Barangay Crossing shall be entitled to internal revenue allotment (IRA) shares pursuant to Section 285 of the Local Government Code of 1991, as amended.
>
> **Section 7.** *Repealing Clause.* - All laws, decrees, executive orders, rules and regulations contrary to or inconsistent with any provision of this Act are hereby repealed or amended accordingly.
>
> **Section 8.** *Effectivity.* - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
