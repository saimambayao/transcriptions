# Republic Act No. 11812

**AN ACT RENAMING THE MUNICIPALITY OF RODRIGUEZ IN THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL AS THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONTALBAN, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 275**

> Eighteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, two thousand twenty one.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11812, June 02, 2022 ]**
>
> **AN ACT RENAMING THE MUNICIPALITY OF RODRIGUEZ IN THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL AS THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONTALBAN, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 275**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The Municipality of Rodriguez in the Province of Rizal is hereby renamed as the Municipality of Montalban.
>
> **Section 2.** The renaming of the Municipality of Rodriguez as Municipality of Montalban shall be effective upon ratification of the majority of votes cast by qualified voters in a plebiscite to be conducted and supervised by the Commission on Elections (COMELEC) in the present Municipality of Rodriguez.
>
> The expenses for such plebiscite shall be borne by the Municipality of Rodriguez, Province of Rizal.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** This Act repeals Batas Pambansa Blg. 275, entitled "An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Montalban, Province of Rizal, to the Municipality of Rodriguez".
>
> **Section 4.**This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
