# Republic Act No. 11620

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN MANUEL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN MANUEL, CITY OF SAN JOSE DEL MONTE, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO SAN MANUEL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> Eighteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, two thousand twenty one.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11620, December 10, 2021 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN MANUEL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN MANUEL, CITY OF SAN JOSE DEL MONTE, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO SAN MANUEL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The name of San Manuel High School in Barangay San Manuel, City of San Jose Del Monte, Province of Bulacan, is hereby changed to San Manuel National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The main campus of the San Manuel National High School shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay San Manuel, City of San Jose Del Monte, Province of Bulacan,
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.ℒαwρhi৷
