# Republic Act No. 11819

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE SATELLITE OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) LOCATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF INITAO, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL INTO A REGULAR LTO DISTRICT OFFICE, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eighteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, two thousand twenty one.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11819, June 02, 2022 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE SATELLITE OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) LOCATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF INITAO, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL INTO A REGULAR LTO DISTRICT OFFICE, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The Satellite Office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Initao, Province of Misamis Oriental is hereby converted into a regular LTO district office to be known as the LTO District Office of Initao, Misamis Oriental.7!ᕼdMᗄ7
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of a regular LTO district office in the Municipality of Initao, Province of Misamis Oriental, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current year's appropriations of the Initao LTO Satellite Office located in the Municipality of Initao, Province of Misamis Oriental. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the continued operation of the regular LTO district office shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** Within ninety (90) days from the effectivity of this Act, the Secretary of Transportation shall promulgate the rules and regulations necessary for its effective implementation.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
