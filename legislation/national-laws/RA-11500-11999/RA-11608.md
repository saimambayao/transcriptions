# Republic Act No. 11608

**AN ACT RENAMING ROOSEVELT AVENUE LOCATED IN LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT I OF QUEZON CITY AS FERNANDO POE JR. AVENUE**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 11608
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2021/ra_11608_2021.html)

---

> Eighteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, two thousand twenty one.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11608, December 10, 2021 ]**
>
> **AN ACT RENAMING ROOSEVELT AVENUE LOCATED IN LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT I OF QUEZON CITY AS FERNANDO POE JR. AVENUE**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** Roosevelt Avenue located in Legislative District I of Quezon City is hereby renamed as Fernando Poe Jr. Avenue.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders, and circulars to implement the provisions of this Act within sixty (60) days from its effectivity.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
