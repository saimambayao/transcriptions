> Eighteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, two thousand twenty one.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11849, June 18, 2022 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE SATELLITE OFFICE OF THE MARITIME INDUSTRY AUTHORITY LOCATED IN CALABAYOG CITY, PROVINCE OF SAMAR INTO AN EXTENSION OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The existing Maritime Industry Authority (MARINA) Satellite Office in Calbayog City, Province of Samar is hereby converted into a regular MARINA Extension Office. The extension office shall provide services for the residents of the provinces of Northern Samar, Samar, and Eastern Samar.
>
> **Section 2.** The Administrator of MARINA shall immediately include in the Agencys programs the operationalization of the MARINA Extension Office in Calbayog City, Province of Samar, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
