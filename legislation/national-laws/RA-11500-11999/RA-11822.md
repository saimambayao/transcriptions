> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11822, June 02, 2022 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) EXTENSION OFFICE IN THE CITY OF PASSI, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, INTO A REGULAR LTO DISTRICT OFFICE, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The existing Passi Land Transportation Office (LTO) Extension Office in the City of Passi, Province of Iloilo, is hereby converted into a regular LTO district office.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of a regular LTO district office in the City of Passi, Province of Iloilo, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current year's appropriations of the Passi LTO Extension Office. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the continued operation of the Passi LTO District Fffice shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
