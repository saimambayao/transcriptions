# Republic Act No. 11818

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) LICENSING EXTENSION OFFICE IN BACOLOD CITY INTO A REGULAR LICENSING CENTER TO BE KNOWN AS THE NEGROS OCCIDENTAL LICENSING CENTER, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11818, June 02, 2022 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) LICENSING EXTENSION OFFICE IN BACOLOD CITY INTO A REGULAR LICENSING CENTER TO BE KNOWN AS THE NEGROS OCCIDENTAL LICENSING CENTER, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** Conversion into a Regular Licensing Center. - The Land Transportation Office (LTO) Licensing Extension Office in Bacolod City is hereby converted into a regular licensing center to be known as the Negros Occidental Licensing Center.
>
> **Section 2.** Appropriations. - The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the Negros Occidental Licensing Center in the City of Bacolod, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.ℒαwρhi৷
