# Republic Act No. 11693

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINAMALAYAN, ORIENTAL MINDORO INTO A DISTRICT OFFICE TO BE KNOWN AS THE LTO DISTRICT OFFICE-PINAMALAYAN AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eighteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, two thousand twenty one.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11693, April 11, 2022 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINAMALAYAN, ORIENTAL MINDORO INTO A DISTRICT OFFICE TO BE KNOWN AS THE LTO DISTRICT OFFICE-PINAMALAYAN AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The extension office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Pinamalayan, Province of Oriental Mindoro is hereby converted into a Class B District Office to be known as the Land Transportation Office (LTO) District Office  Pinamalayan.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately include in the Department's programs the operationalization of the LTO District Office in the Municipality of Pinamalayan, Oriental Mindoro, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.ᇈWᑭHIL
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days following the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
