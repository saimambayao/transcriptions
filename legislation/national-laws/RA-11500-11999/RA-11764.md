# Republic Act No. 11764

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SALIN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SADSADAN, MUNICIPALITY OF BAUKO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE TO TALBAN P. BITAYAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> Eighteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, two thousand twenty one.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11764, April 29, 2022 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SALIN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SADSADAN, MUNICIPALITY OF BAUKO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE TO TALBAN P. BITAYAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Salin Elementary School located in Barangay Sadsadan, Municipality of Bauko, Mountain Province is hereby changed to Talban P. Bitayan Elementary School.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall become effective fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
