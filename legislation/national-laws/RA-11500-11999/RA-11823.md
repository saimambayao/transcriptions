**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUIPOS, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11823, June 02, 2022 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUIPOS, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall hereby established a regular district office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Guipos, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, to be known as the LTO District Office of Guipos.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Transportation shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the LTO district office in the Municipality of Guipos, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of the DOTr shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
