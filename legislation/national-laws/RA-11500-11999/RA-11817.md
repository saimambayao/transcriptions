# Republic Act No. 11817

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JAVIER, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, INTO A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11817, June 02, 2022 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JAVIER, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, INTO A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The extension office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Javier, Province of Leyte, is hereby converted into a regular district office to be known as the LTO Javier District Office.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Transportation shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the LTO district office in the Municipality of Javier, Province of Leyte, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current year's appropriation of the LTO Javier extension office. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the continued operation of the regular LTO district office shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.1aшphi1
