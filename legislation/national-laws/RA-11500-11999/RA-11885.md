> Eighteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, two thousand twenty one.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11885, June 29, 2022 ]**
>
> **AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE UNAYAN MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINIDAYAN, PROVINC OF LANAO DEL SUR, FROM TEN (10) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The authorized bed capacity of the Unayan Municipal Hospital in the Municipality of Binidayan, Province of Lanao del Sur is hereby increased from ten (10) to fifty (50) beds. Any future increase in bed capacity shall be consistent with the hospital development plan that shall be prepared by Chief of Hospital of the Unayan Municipal Hospital, and submitted to the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao  Ministry of Health.
>
> **Section 2.** The existing professional health care services and facilities of Unayan Municipal Hospital shall also be upgraded to conform with and be commensurate to the bed capacity increase as provided for Section 1 hereof.
>
> **Section 3.** The existing workforce of the Unayan Municipal Hospital, which includes doctors, nurses and support personnel, shall also be increased correspondingly.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 4.** The Bangsamoro Autonomous Government shall provide the necessary funds to cover the maintenance and other operating expenses of the Unayan Municipal Hospital. It shall also appropriate funds for the additional human resource complement to support the increase in bed capacity and the corresponding upgrading of its service facilities and professional health care services.
>
> **Section 5.** The Secretary of the DOH shall include in the Departments subsidy program, support for the capital outlay requirements of the Unayan Municipal Hospital.
>
> **Section 6.** All laws, executive orders, issuances, rules and regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 7.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
