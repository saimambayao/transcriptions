> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6225, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIOS OF PECIO, PAWAAN, LINOGEN, TIWALES, GIGANG, GUAGAN, PINGOLES AND BATANG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAPUYAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Sitios Pecio, Pawaan, Linogen, Tiwales, Gigang, Guagan, Pingolea and Batang in the Municipality of Lapuyan, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, are hereby converted into distinct and independent barrios of said municipality.
>
