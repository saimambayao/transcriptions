> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6288, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAMBOAN, PROVINCE OF CEBU, TO BE KNOWN AS SAMBOAN GENERAL HOSPITAL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established under the direct supervision of the Director of Medical Services, a general hospital in the Municipality of Samboan, Province of Cebu, to be known as Samboan General Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of five hundred thousand pesos is hereby-authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred seventy-one.  Thereafter, such sum as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1971.
