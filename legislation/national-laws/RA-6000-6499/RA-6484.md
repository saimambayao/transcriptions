> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6484, June 17, 1972 ]**
>
> AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A SOIL CONSERVATION DEMONSTRATION STATION IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SINILOAN PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO BE KNOWN AS LAGUNA SOIL CONSERVATION DEMONSTRATION STATION, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a soil conservation demonstration station in the Municipality of Siniloan, Province of Laguna.  The said station shall be under the supervision of the Director of Soils and shall be subject to the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Six hundred twenty-two.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of the soil conservation demonstration station for the fiscal year nine-teen hundred and seventy-three.  Thereafter, such sum as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
