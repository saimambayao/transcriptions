> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6199, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING BARRIO GORDON HEIGHTS IN THE CITY OF OLONGAPO.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled;
>
> **Section 1.** The whole area of Purok 7 in the Barrio of Santa Rita, City of Olongapo, is hereby separated from said barrio and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said city, to be known as Barrio Gordon Heights. Barrio Gordon Heights shall be bounded on the North by Barrio Barretto, on the east by watershed area, on the south by Mabayuan River and on the west by Mabayuan and Santa Rita boundaries.
>
