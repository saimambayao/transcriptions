> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6205, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING SITIO VILLA NOGALES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAJAYJAY, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA INTO A BARRIO.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio known as Villa Nogales, formerly a part of Barrio Malinao, Municipality of Majayjay, Province of Laguna, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Villa Nogales of the same municipality.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 19, 1971.
