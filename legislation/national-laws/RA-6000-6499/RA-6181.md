> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6181, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BUTONG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABUYAO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Butong in the Municipality of Cabuyao, Province of Laguna, is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the Barrio of Butong.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 19, 1971.
