> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6393, September 10, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALABEL IN THE PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The barrios of Dumolok, Alegria, Cawas, Alabel, Bagakay, Pag-asa, Tocawal and Maribulan, in the City of General Santos, are separated from said city, and constituted into a distinct and independent municipality, to be known as the Municipality of Alabel, South Cotabato.  The seat of government of the new municipality shall be in the present site of Barrio Alabel.1âшphi1
>
> The boundary between the Municipality of Alabel and the City of General Santos shall be the Buayan River.
>
> **Section 2.** The first mayor, vice-mayor and councilors of the new municipality shall be elected in the next general elections for local officials in November, nineteen hundred seventy-one.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, September 10, 1971.
