> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6206, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN JOAQUIN HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOAQUIN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO DON FELIX SERRA MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the San Joaquin High School in the Municipality of San Joaquin, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Don Felix Serra Memorial High School.
>
