> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6074, August 04, 1969 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF TOMINOBO COMMUNITY SCHOOL IN ILIGAN CITY TO TOMAS CABILI MEMORIAL COMMUNITY SCHOOL.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Hereafter, Tominobo Community School in Iligan City shall be known as Tomas Cabili Memorial Community School.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, August 4, 1969.
