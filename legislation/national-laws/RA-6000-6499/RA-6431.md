> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6431, June 17, 1972 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO CALANUTAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSA-LES, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, TO DON FELIX COLOMA.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Calanutan int he Municipality of Rosales, Province of Pangasinan is hereby changed to Don Felix Coloma.
