> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6194, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SAN SEBASTIAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MATAG-OB, PROVINCE OF LEYTE.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Cap-on in the Barrio of Candelaria, Municipality of Matag-ob, Province of Leyte, is hereby separated from said barrio, and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the Barrio of San Sebastian.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 19, 1971.
