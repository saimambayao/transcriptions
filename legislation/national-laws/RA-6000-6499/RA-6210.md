>
> AN ACT CREATING BARRIO KAPATAGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Kapatagan in the Municipality of Digos, Province of Davao del Sur, is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Kapatagan.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
