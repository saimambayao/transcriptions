> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6196, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF ILOCOS NORTE PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of llocos Norte Provincial Hospital is hereby increased from fifty to one hundred fifty beds.
>
> **Section 2.** Such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance and other facilities shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 19, 1971.
