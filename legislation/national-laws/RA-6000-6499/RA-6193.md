> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6193, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VILLABA, PROVINCE OF LEYTE.
>
> Be it enacted, by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Villaba, Province of Leyte, are hereby converted into barrios, of said municipality, to wit:
>
> a. Sitio Tabunoc, to be known as Barrio Tabunoc;
>
> b. Sitio Hibulangan, to be known as Barrio Hibulangan;
>
> c. Sitio Camporog, to be known as Barrio Camporog;
>
> d. Sitio Payao, to be known as Barrio Payao;
>
> e. Sitio Cabungahan, to be known as Barrio Cabungahan;
>
> f. Sitio Tinghob, to be known as Barrio Tinghob;
>
> g. Sitio Catagbacan, to be known as Barrio Gatagbacan;
>
> h. Sitio Halas, to be known as Barrio Halas;
>
> i. Sitio Fatima, to be known as Barrio Fatima;
>
> j. Sitio Iligay, to be known as Barrio Iligay;
>
> k. Sitio Baga-Buga to be known as Barrio Baga-Buga;
>
> l. Sitio Capinyahan, to be known as Barrio Capinyahan;
>
> m. Sitio Poblacion Norte, to be known as Barrio Poblacion Norte; and
>
> n. Sitio Poblaeion Sur, to be known as Barrio Poblacion Sur.
>
