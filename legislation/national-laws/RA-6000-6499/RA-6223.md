> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6223, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING BARRIO CRISPINA MARCOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Palongpong, Calogoan, Sampalocan and Aracua in the Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the Barrio of Crispina Marcos.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
