> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 6158**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Pook Elementary School Situated at Barrio Pook, Municipality of Sta. Rosa, Province of Laguna, to Jose Zavalla Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Pook Elementary School situated at Barrio Pook, Municipality of Sta. Rosa, Province of Laguna, is hereby named and is to be known henceforth as Jose Zavalla Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: November 9, 1970.
