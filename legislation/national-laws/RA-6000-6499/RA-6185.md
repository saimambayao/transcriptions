> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6185, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE SEAT OF GOVERNMENT OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIAYAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, FROM BARRIO PARANGLUMBA TO BARRIO SIAYAN PROPER.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The present seat of government of the Municipality of Siayan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is hereby transferred from Barrio Paranglumba to Barrio Siayan Proper.
>
> **Section 2.** The provisions of Republic Act Numbered Forty-nine hundred eighty-nine which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 19, 1971.
