> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6182, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO TALLAG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABAGAN, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO BARRIO BIUAG.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Tallag in the Municipality of Cabagan, Province of Isabela, is hereby changed to Barrio Biuag.
>
