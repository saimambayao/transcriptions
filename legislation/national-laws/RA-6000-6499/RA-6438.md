> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6438, June 17, 1972 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF OCCIDENTAL NEGROS PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN THE CITY OF BACOLOD TO CORAZON LOCSIN-MONTELIBANO MEMORIAL HOSPITAL.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Occidental Negros Provincial Hospital, situated in the City of Bacolod Philippines, is hereby changed and, henceforth, shall be named Corazon Locsin-Montelibano Memorial Hospital.
>
