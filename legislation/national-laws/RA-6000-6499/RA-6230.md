> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6230, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING BARRIO KAHUPI-AN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOGOD, PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Bood Taas, Tabunan, Hap-on, Kabugua-an, Tigbawan, Lubong Sapa, Kahupian and Pangalkagan in the Municipality of Sogod, Province of Southern Leyte, are hereby constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Kahupi-an.
>
> The proposed barrio of Kahupi-an shall be bounded as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | North-- | Lubong Sapa Creek |
> | East-- | Bulak River |
> | South-- | Pan-Phil. Highway, Km. 13 |
> | WesWLayug River |
>
