> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6324, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF OCCIDENTAL NEGROS SOUTH GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF HIMAMAYLAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of Occidental Negros South General Hospital is hereby increased from twenty-five beds to fifty.
>
> **Section 2.** The financing of this hospital shall be in accordance with the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred thirty-nine, otherwise known as the Hospital Financing Act.  The necessary sums for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
