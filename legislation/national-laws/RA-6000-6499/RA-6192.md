> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6192, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIOS OF BINALOAN, CANBAGAHAY, DACOL, UPPER DANAO, BONGDO II, PANGABOTAN AND GAYAM, ALL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAFT, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR, INTO BARRIOS TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIOS BINALOAN, SAN PABLO, DACOL, DEL REMEDIOS, BETO, PANGABOTAN AND GAYAM, RESPECTIVELY.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Binaloan, Canbagahay, Dacol, Upper Danao, Bongdo II, Pangabotan and Gayaro, all in the Municipality of Taft, Province of Eastern Samar, are hereby converted into barrios to be known as Barrios Binaloan, San Pablo, Dacol, Del Remedios, Beto, Pangabotan and Gayam, respectively.
>
