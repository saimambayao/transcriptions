> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6316, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT TO ESTABLISH AN EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILAR, IN THE PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, TO BE KNOWN AS PILAR EMERGENCY HOSPITAL, AND TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS THEREFOR.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established an emergency hospital in the Municipality of Pilar in the Province of Sorsogon, to be known as Pilar Emergency Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred seventy-one.  Thereafter, such sum as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 19, 1971.
