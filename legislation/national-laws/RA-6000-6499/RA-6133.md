> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 6133**
>
> **An Act Amending Republic Act Numbered Five Thousand Two Hundred Five, Entitled "An Act Fixing the Salaries of Certain Positions in the Veterans Memorial Hospital"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section one of Republic Act Numbered Five thousand two hundred five is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Sec. 1. The WAPCO classification and pay plan shall be adjusted to the following salaries of certain positions in the Veterans Memorial Hospital, which are hereby fixed as follows:
> >
> > "(a) Director, fifteen thousand pesos, per annum;
> >
> > "(b) Assistant Director, fourteen thousand five hundred pesos per annum;
> >
> > "(c) Chief of Professional Staff, fourteen thousand pesos per annum;
> >
> > "(d) Medical Specialist III, thirteen thousand five hundred pesos per annum;
> >
> > "(e) Medical Specialist II, twelve thousand pesos, per annum;
> >
> > "(f) Hospital Department Head, Chief of Service and Chief of Division (non-medical), twelve thousand pesos, per annum;
> >
> > "(g) Medical Specialist I, eleven thousand pesos, per annum;
> >
> > "(h) Senior Resident Physician and Clinic Supervisor, nine thousand pesos, per annum;
> >
> > "(i) Resident Physician, Clinic Physician and Junior Physician, seven thousand pesos, per annum;
> >
> > "Those serving on part-time basis shall receive one-half of the corresponding salaries mentioned herein."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: August 31, 1970.
