> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6218, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANAO-AON, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE, TO SAN FRANCISCO.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Anao-aon in the Province of Surigao del Norte, is hereby changed to San Francisco,
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
