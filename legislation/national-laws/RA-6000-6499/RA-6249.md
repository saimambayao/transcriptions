> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6249, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALIAGA, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of liagia, Province of Nueva Ecija, are hereby converted into barrios of said municipality, to wit:
>
> a. The sitio of Macabucod in the barrio of Sto. Tomas is converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Macabucod; and
>
> b. The sitio of San Felipe Bata in the barrio of San Felipe Matanda is converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of San Felipe Bata.
>
