> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 6164**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Plaza Leon XIII (Trece) in Front of Tondo Church, Manila, to Ka Amado Hernandez**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Plaza Leon XIII (Trece) situated in front of Tondo Church, Manila, is hereby changed to Ka Amado Hernandez.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: November 9, 1970.
