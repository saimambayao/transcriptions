> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 6154**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Tagbilaran Elementary School in Barrio Doña Regina, Municipality of Santa Maria, Province of Davao del Sur, to Doña Regina Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Tagbilaran Elementary School in Barrio Doña Regina, Municipality of Santa Maria, Province of Davao del Sur, is hereby changed to Doña Regina Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: November 9, 1970.
