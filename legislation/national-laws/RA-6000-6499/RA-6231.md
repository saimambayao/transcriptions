> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6231, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING BARRIO SAGAWSAWAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAPUL, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Sagawsawan in the Municipality of Capul, Province of Northern Samar, is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Sagawsawan.
>
