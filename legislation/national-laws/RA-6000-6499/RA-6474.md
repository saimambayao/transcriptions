> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6474, June 17, 1972 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A LIVESTOCK BREEDING CENTER IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CORON, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established, under the supervision of the Bureau of Animal Industry, a livestock breeding center in the Municipality of Coron, Province of Palawan, at a site to be selected by the Director of Animal Industry through donations, to be known as Coron Livestock Breeding Center.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of seventy-five thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury  not otherwise appropriated, for the construction of an office-laboratory building and animals, and for such other expenses as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Act.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall be take effect upon its approval.
>
> Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1972.
