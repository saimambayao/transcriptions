> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6311, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT DEFINING THE TERRITORIAL JURISDICTION OF THE FIRST HIGHWAY ENGINEERING DISTRICT IN THE PROVINCE OF BOHOL.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The First Highway Engineering District in the Province of Bohol shall comprise the Municipalities of Calape, Loon, Maribojoc, Antequera, Cortes, Corella, Baclayon, Dauis and Panglao with principal station in the Municipality of Loon.
>
