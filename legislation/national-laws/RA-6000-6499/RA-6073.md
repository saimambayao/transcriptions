> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6073, August 04, 1969 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO BANTAY-BATAC IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BARRIO NALUPTA.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Bantay-Batac in the Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Nalupta.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved. August 4, 1969.
