> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6177, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING BARRIO BAGUMBAYAN NORTE IN THE CITY OF NAGA
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Sitio Bagumbayan Norte, City of Naga, is hereby constituted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Bagumbayan Norte.
>
> **Section 2.** The Barrio of Bagumbayan Norte shall be bounded on the North by the boundary line between the Municipality of Canaman and the City of Naga; on the east, by Barrio Calawag; on the south, by the culvert beside De Jesus' residence; on the west, by the boundary line between the Municipality of Canaman and the City of Naga.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The new barrio officials shall be appointed by the City Mayor, and they shall hold office until their successors are elected in the next regular barrio elections.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 19, 1971.
