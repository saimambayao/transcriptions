> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6108, August 04, 1969 ]**
>
> AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THREE THOUSAND NINETY-NINE, ENTITLED "AN ACT CREATING THE BUREAU OF FIBER INSPECTION SERVICE."
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Section nine of Republic Act Numbered Three thousand ninety-nine is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Section 9. *Disposition of Funds.*All the amounts collected as fees for grading permits, inspection fees, sale of specimens of the official standards and other income shall be deposited in the National Treasury of the Philippines: *Provided,* That the appropriations provided in the General Appropriations Act for the fiscal year nineteen hundred and seventy to cover expenses of the Bureau of Fiber Inspection Service shall be funded fully from the General Fund."
>
> **Section 2.** Section ten of the same Act is hereby repealed.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, August 4, 1969.
