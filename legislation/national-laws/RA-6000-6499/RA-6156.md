> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 6156**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Davao Central Elementary School in Davao City to Capitan Tomas Monteverde, Sr. Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Davao Central Elementary School in Davao City is hereby changed to Capitan Tomas Monteverde, St. Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: November 9, 1970.
