> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6075, August 04, 1969 ]**
>
> AN ACT APPROPRIATING ONE HUNDRED THOUSAND PESOS TO DEFRAY THE EXPENSES FOR THE HOLDING OF THE FIRST WORLD CONVENTION OF THE WORLD ASSOCIATION OF MILITARY SURGEONS IN QUEZON CITY, IN OR ABOUT MAY, NINETEEN HUNDRED AND SEVENTY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to defray the expenses for the holding of the First Congress of the World Association of Military Surgeons in Quezon City, in or about May, nineteen hundred and seventy, including the acquisition and installation of modern conference equipment and facilities, communication and transportation services and essential conference supplies.
>
> All disbursements to be made shall be previously approved by a committee composed of the Auditor General, as chairman, the Secretary of Finance and the Budget Commissioner, as members, or their respective representatives, and all serviceable equipment, supplies, materials and other property acquired hereunder shall be placed thereafter under the custody of the Department of General Services for further future use.
>
> The sum herein appropriated shall be made available immediately upon the approval of this Act and be at the disposal of the Philippine Association of Military Surgeons as host organization.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, August 4, 1969.
