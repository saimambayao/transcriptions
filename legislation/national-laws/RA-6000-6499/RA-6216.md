> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6216, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO SANTO TOMAS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINILI, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BARRIO ARCANGEL.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Santo Tomas in the Municipality of Pinili, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Arcangel.
>
