> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6298, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POTIA, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO, TO BE KNOWN AS POTIA EMERGENCY HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established under the Bureau of Medical Services, a twenty-five bed emergency hospital in the Municipality of Potia, Province of Ifugao, to be known as Potia Emergency Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of the said hospital for the fiscal year nineteen hundred seventy-one. *Provided,* That no portion of the appropriation shall be transferred.  Thereafter, such sum as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1971.
