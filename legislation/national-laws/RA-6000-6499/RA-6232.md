> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6232, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING BARRIO PEÑAFRANCIA IN THE CITY OF NAGA
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Sitio Peñafrancia, City of Naga, is hereby constituted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Peñafrancia.
>
> **Section 2.** The Barrio of Peñafrancia shall be bounded on the North by the Catholic Cemetery; on the East, by Naga River; on the South, by Santoja Street; and on the West, by Jacob Street.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The new barrio officials shall be appointed by the City Mayor, and they shall hold office until their successors are elected in the next regular barrio elections.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 19, 1971.
