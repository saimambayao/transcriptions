> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6437, June 17, 1972 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO COGON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINTUYAN, PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE TO BARRIO JUAN AVILA.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Cogon in the Municipality of Pintuyan, Province of Southern Leyte, is changed to Barrio Juan Avila.
>
