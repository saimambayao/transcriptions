> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6203, June 19, 1971 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING BARRIO ANTIPOLO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ANTONIO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Antipolo in the Municipality of San Antonio, Province of Zambales, is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Antipolo.
>
