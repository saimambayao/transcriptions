> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6090, August 04, 1969 ]**
>
> AN ACT FIXING THE MAXIMUM COMPENSATION OF MUNICIPAL VICE-MAYORS AND COUNCILORS.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** In lieu of *per diem*s, the municipal council may grant to each Vice-Mayor and councilor an annual salary not to exceed the following:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | Class of Municipality | Salary *per annum* |
> | First Class1 | ₱4,800.00 |
> | First Class2 | 4,500.00 |
> | First Class3 | 4,200.00 |
> | First Class4 | 3,900.00 |
> | First Class5 | 3,600.00 |
> | First Class6 | 3,300.00 |
> | Second Class | 3,000.00 |
> | Third Class | 2,400.00 |
>
> **Section 2.** Municipalities of the fourth, fifth, sixth and seventh classes shall not be covered by the provisions of this Act: *Provided, however,* That the Municipal Councils of the municipalities belonging to these classes may grant to each Vice-Mayor and Councilor a *per diem* not exceeding fifteen pesos for every day of regular or special session of the Council actually attended by them: *Provided,* finally, That no *per diem* may be granted for more than two special sessions a month.
>
> **Section 3.** All Acts, executive orders and administrative orders, or parts thereof, which are inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, August 4, 1969.
