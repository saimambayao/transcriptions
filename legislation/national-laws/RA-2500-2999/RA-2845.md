> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2845**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Simbahan in the Municipality of Casiguran, Province of Quezon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Mabudo, Simbahan, Talagas and Abulig are hereby separated from the barrio of Dinalungan in the Municipality of Casiguran, Province of Quezon, and are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Simbahan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
