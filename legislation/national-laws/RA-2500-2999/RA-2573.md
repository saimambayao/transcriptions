> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2573**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Cabambanan Barrio School in the Municipality of Pagsanjan, Province of Laguna, to Salvador Unson Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Cabambanan Barrio School in the barrio of Cabambanan, Municipality of Pagsanjan, Province of Laguna, is changed to Salvador Unson Elementary School.
>
