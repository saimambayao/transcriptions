> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2811**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Minalin, Province of Pampanga**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Dawe and Maniañgo in the Municipality of Minalin, Province of Pampanga, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Dawe and Maniañgo, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
