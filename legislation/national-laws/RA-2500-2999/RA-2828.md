> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2828**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Naujan, Province of Oriental Mindoro**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Naujan, Province of Oriental Mindoro, are separated from their respective barrios and constituted into distinct and independent barrios therein to wit:
>
> 1. The sitios of San Agustin Ilaya, Kabilang Ilog, Mangaya and San Andres of the barrio of San Agustin No. 1, to be known as the barrio of San Agustin;
>
> 2. The sitios of Butik Proper, Labak, Ibulo and Bukayao Grande of the barrio of Tagumpay, to be known as the barrio of San Andres;
>
> 3. The sitios of Arangin Proper, Bagtu ng Arangin, Bansana, Barumbong and Kaowayan of the barrio of Malvar, to be known as the barrio of Arangin; and
>
> 4. The sitios of Paitan, Bandol, Bugayan and Timbolaten of the barrio of Evangelista, to be known as the barrio of Metolza (Paitan).
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
