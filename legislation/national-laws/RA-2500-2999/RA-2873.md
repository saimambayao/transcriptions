> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2873**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of San Jose in the Municipality of Aborlan, Province of Palawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Outer Apoaporawan in the Municipality of Aborlan, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of San Jose.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
