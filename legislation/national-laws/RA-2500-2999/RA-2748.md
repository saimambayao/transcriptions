> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2748**
>
> **An Act Amending Item (5), Under "d. Bataan," Paragraph 2, Title G, Section One of Republic Act Numbered Twelve Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (5) under "d. Bataan," paragraph 2, Title G, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(5) Abucay Communal Irrigation System, (Labangan River)Construction of diversion works ₱15,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
