> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2751**
>
> **An Act Amending Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (ac), page 313, Group III, subparagraph (28), for the Province of Leyte, paragraph f , Section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(ac) San Isidro Social Hall, MalitbogConstruction ₱3,000.00."
> >
> > Section 2. Item (140), page 709, subparagraph (ab), for the Province of Leyte, paragraph b, Title A, Section three of the same Act is amended to read as follows:
> >
> > "(140) Construction, Hogpa Barrio School, Malitbog ₱4,000.00."
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
