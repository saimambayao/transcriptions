> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2881**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of San Isidro in the Municipality of Labangan, Province of Zamboanga del Sur**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Matico, Panabagon, West Luya and Nabalan in the Municipality of Labangan, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Isidro.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
