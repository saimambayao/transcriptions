> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2882**
>
> AN ACT AMENDING ITEM (13) ON PAGE 151, SUBPARAGRAPH 38 FOR THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL, UNDER PARAGRAPH C, TITLE A OF SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWENTY-THREE HUNDRED AND ONE.
>
> **Section 1.** Item (13) on page 151, subparagraph 38 for the Province of Negros Oriental, under paragraph c, Title A of Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred and one is amended to read as follows:
>
> "(13) Pacuan Elementary School Building,
>
> La Libertad P10,000.00."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
