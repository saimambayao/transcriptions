> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2595**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Pagsulhogon in the Municipality of Babatngon, Province of Leyte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Pagsulhogon in the Municipality of Babatngon, Province of Leyte, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Pagsulhogon.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
