> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2833**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Alegria Street in the Municipality of Bilar, Province of Bohol, to Juan Luna Street**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Alegria Street in the Municipality of Bilar, Province of Bohol, is hereby changed to Juan Luna Street.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
