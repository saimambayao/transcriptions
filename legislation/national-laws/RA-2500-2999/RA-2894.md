> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2894**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrios of Balaniog and Pulacan in the Municipality of Labangan, Province of Zamboanga del Sur**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Bulanit, East Tawagan Norte and Dalapang in the Municipality of Labangan, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Balaniog.
>
> **Section 2.** The sitios of Lower Pulacan, Limpang Proper, West Limpang, Waterfalls and Lantawan in the Municipality of Labangan, same province, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Pulacan.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
