> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2860**
>
> **An Act Transferring the Appropriation of Four Thousand Pesos for the Cambijang Barrio School, Cajidiocan, Romblon, to Lumbang Community School of the Same Municipality, by Amending Republic Act Numbered Twenty Hundred Ninety-Three**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (n) under (46) Romblon, paragraph f, Title M, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twenty hundred ninety-three, is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(n) Lumbang Community School, CajidiocanConstruction of one room ₱4,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
