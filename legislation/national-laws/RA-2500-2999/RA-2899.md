> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2899**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Three Hundred One**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (t) of subparagraph 46, for the Province of Romblon, under paragraph d, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred one is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(t) Maternity and Childrens Clinic RomblonConstruction and improvement ₱25,000.00."
>
> **Section 2.** Item (c) of subparagraph 1, for Jones, Romblon, under paragraph e, Section two of the same Act, is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(c) Libtong Barrio School building ₱5,000.00."
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
