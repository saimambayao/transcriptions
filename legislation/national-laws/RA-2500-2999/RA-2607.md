> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2607**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Padildil in the Municipality of San Quintin, Province of Abra, to Villa Mercedes**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Padildil in the Municipality of San Quintin, Province of Abra, is changed to Villa Mercedes.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
