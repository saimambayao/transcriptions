> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2879**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Ecuan Primary School in the Barrio of Golictop, Municipality of Dumingag, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, to Golictop Primary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Ecuan Primary School in the barrio of Golictop, Municipality of Dumingag, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, is hereby changed to Golitop Primay School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
