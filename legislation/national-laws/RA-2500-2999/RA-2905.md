> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2905**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Three Hundred One**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (q), page 377 of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred one, is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(q) Lipata Elementary School shop building, Minglanilla Construction ₱5,000.00."
> >
> > Section 2. Item 15(a), page 528 of the same Act, is amended to read as follows:
> >
> > "15. Carcar: (a) Valencia public market ₱15,000.00."
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
