> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2780**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (14), Page 157, Paragraph Four, Section One-A of Republic Act Numbered Twelve Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (14), page 157, paragraph four, Section One-a of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(14) New municipal building, Tanauan, BatangasConstruction and/or improvement ₱20,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
