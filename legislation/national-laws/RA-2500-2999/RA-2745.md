> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2745**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Naasag in the Municipality of Mambajao, Subprovince of Camiguin, Province of Misamis Oriental**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Naasag, Baring, Volcan, Tangub, Basiao and Looc in the Municipality of Mambajao, Sub-province of Camiguin, Province of Misamis Oriental, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Naasag.
>
