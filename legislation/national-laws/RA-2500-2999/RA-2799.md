> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2799**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Almeria, Province of Leyte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following places in the Municipality of Almeria, Province of Leyte, are declared as barrios of said municipality, to wit:
>
> 1. Tamarindo as Barrio Tamarindo;
>
> 2. Sampao as Barrio Sampao;
>
> 3. Eyusan as Barrio Eyusan;
>
> 4. Salangi as Barrio Salangi; and
>
> 5. Look as Barrio Look.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
