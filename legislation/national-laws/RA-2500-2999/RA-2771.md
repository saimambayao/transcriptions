> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2771**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrios of Bug-O and Molobolo in the Municipalities of Asturias and Tuburan, Respectively, Province of Cebu**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Bug-o in the Municipality of Asturias, Province of Cebu, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Bug-o.
>
> **Section 2.** The sitio of Molobolo in the Municipality of Tuburan, same province, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Molobolo.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
