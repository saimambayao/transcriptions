> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2510**
>
> **An Act Creating the Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrios of Salug, Mucas, Libertad, Tambalang, Balacan, Danao, Caracol, Canawan, Bacong, Lipakan, and Palandok in the Municipality of Liloy, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are separated from said municipality, and are constituted into a distinct and independent municipality to be known as the Municipality of Salug, same province, with the seat of government located at the site of the present barrio of Mucas.
>

>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
