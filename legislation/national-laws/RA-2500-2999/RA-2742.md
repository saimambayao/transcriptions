> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2742**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Cabugao Elementary School in the City of Roxas to Anatolia A. Arcenas Memorial School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Cabugao Elementary School located at Barrio Cabugao, Loctugan, City of Roxas, is changed to Anatolia A. Arcenas Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
