> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2503**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Aborlan, Province of Palawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Tinagong-dagat, Apoaporawan and Malatgao in the Municipality of Aborlan, Province of Palawan, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Tinagong-dagat, Sandoval and Malatgao, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
