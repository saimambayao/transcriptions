> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2838**
>
> **An Act Changing the Names of Maningaw and Washington Streets in the Municipality of Bilar, Province of Bohol, to Marcelo H. Del Pilar Street and Magsaysay Street, Respectively**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The names of Maningaw Street and Washington Street in the Municipality of Bilar, Province of Bohol, are hereby changed to Marcelo H. del Pilar Street and Magsaysay Street, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
