> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2849**
>
> **An Act Amending Certain Items of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Three Hundred and One, Entitled "An Act Appropriating Funds for Public Works"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (a) on page 441, under Group II for the Province of Negros Oriental, paragraph d, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred and one is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(a) Lazi Municipal BuildingConstruction ₱30,000.00"
> >
> > Section 2. Item No. 4 on page 587, under "Public Buildings," paragraph e, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred and one is hereby amended to read as follows:
> >
> > "4. Lazi (a) Municipal Building ₱4,000.00"
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
