> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2525**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Cuasi Elementary School in the Barrio of Tontonan, Municipality of Loon, Province of Bohol, to Tontonan Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Cuasi Elementary School in the barrio of Tontonan, Municipality of Loon, Province of Bohol, is changed to Tontonan Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
