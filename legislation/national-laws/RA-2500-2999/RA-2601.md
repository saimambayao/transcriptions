> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2601**
>
> **An Act Creating Barrios in Certain Municipalities of the Province of Rizal**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Caniogan, Calero and Lunang in the Municipality of Morong, Province of Rizal, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Caniogan.
>
> **Section 2.** The sitios of Barangka, Parang and Nangka in the Municipality of Marikina, same province, are converted into barrios of the said municipality to be known as the barrios of Barangka, Parang and Nangka, respectively.
>
> **Section 3.** The sitio of Manggahan in the Municipality of Pasig, same province, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Manggahan.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
