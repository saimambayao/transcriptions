> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2812**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Talinga in the Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Talinga in the Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Talinga.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
