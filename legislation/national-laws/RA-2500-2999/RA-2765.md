> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2765**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (a), Group I, Subparagraph (47), for the Province of Sorsogon, Page 118, Paragraph F, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (a), Group I, subparagraph (47), for the Province of Sorsogon, page 118, paragraph f, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(a) Gubat Public MarketPurchase and improvement of additional site, including construction of market tiendas ₱50,000,00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
