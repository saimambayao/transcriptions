> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2840**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Bagong Bayan, Barrio of Lual, Municipality of Mauban, Province of Quezon, into a Distinct and Independent Barrio to be Known as the District of Bagong Bayan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Bagong Bayan is hereby separated from the barrio of Lual in the Municipality of Mauban, Province of Quezon, and is converted into a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the District of Bagong Bayan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
