> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2808**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item One Hundred Eight, for the Province of Iloilo, Page One Hundred Ninety-Five, Paragraph g, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item one hundred eight, for the Province of Iloilo, page one hundred ninety-five, paragraph g, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "108. Sta. Barbara Barrio School Building Igbaras ₱6,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
