> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2901**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Binanwaan in the Municipality of Igbaras, Province of Iloilo**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Binanwa-an in the Municipality of Igbaras, Province of Iloilo, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Binanwa-an.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
