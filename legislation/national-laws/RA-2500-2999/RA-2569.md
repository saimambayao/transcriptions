> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2569**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Agpangi Elementary School in the Municipality of Calatrava, Province of Negros Occidental, to Ramon Magsaysay Memorial Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Agpangi Elementary School in the Municipality of Calatrava, Province of Negros Occidental, is changed to Ramon Magsaysay Memorial Elementary School.
