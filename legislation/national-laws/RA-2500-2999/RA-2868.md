> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2868**
>
> **An Act Creating Barrios in Certain Municipalities of the Province of Davao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The place known as Ceboza is separated from the poblacion of the Municipality of Matanao, Province of Davao, and is constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Ceboza.
>
> **Section 2.** The sitio of Sta. Cruz in the Municipality of Asuncion, same province, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Sta. Cruz.
>
> **Section 3.** The sitio of Cacao in the Municipality of Panabo, same province, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Cacao.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
