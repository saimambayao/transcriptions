> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2859**
>
> **An Act Transferring Certain Funds Appropriated for the Cajidiocan Health Clinic and the Rural Health Center Building in the Municipality of Cajidiocan, Province of Romblon, to the Sibuyan, Hospital, by Amending Certain Items of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (k) under (46) Romblon, paragraph f, Title I, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(k) Sibuyan Hospital Nurses Dormitory ₱11,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** The amount appropriated in item 7 under Romblon, paragraph g, Title I, Section two of the same Act is transferred to item (k) as amended by Section one hereof and the totals for paragraphs f and g are adjusted accordingly.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
