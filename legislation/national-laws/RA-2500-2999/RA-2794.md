> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2794**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Wakas Barrio School in the Barrio of Wakas, Municipality of Kawit, Province of Cavite, to General Tomas Mascardo Memorial School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Wakas Barrio School in the barrio of Wakas, Municipality of Kawit, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to General Tomas Mascardo Memorial School.
>
