> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2776**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Project Numbered B-221.25, for the Province of Iloilo, page 464-A, Title B-Highways (Feeder and Secondary Roads), Appendix A of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "B-221.25 Guimbal-Igbaras Road. Construction of bridges GR 8.00 8.00 8.00 4"
> >
> > Section 2. Priority Numbered 19, Group I, for the province of Iloilo, page 42-B, Appendix B of the same Act is amended to read as follows:
> >
> > "19 MiagaoPungtod-Cavite Construction 5,000.00."
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
