> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2822**
>
> **An Act Changing the Names of Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Panabo, Province of Davao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The names of the following barrios in the Municipality of Panabo, Province of Davao, are changed as follows:
>
> 1. Barrio Upper Catungan to Barrio Sta. Cruz; and
>
> 2. Barrio Southern Manay to Barrio Casilac.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
