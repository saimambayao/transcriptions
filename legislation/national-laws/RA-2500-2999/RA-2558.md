> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2558**
>
> **An Act Creating Barrios in the Province of Isabela**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of San Agustin, Province of Isabela, are constituted into a barrio of the said municipality, as follows:
>
> Sitios Salay and Narien to Barrio Salay.
>
> **Section 2.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Tumauini, Province of Isabela, are constituted into a barrio of the said municipality, as follows:
>
> Sitios Sical and Cabal to Barrio Namnama.
>
> **Section 3.** The sitio of Puroc No. 6, Salinungan, in the Municipality of San Mateo, Province of Isabela, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Salinungan East.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
