> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2567**
>
> **An Act Providing that the Public Plaza in the Poblacion of the Municipality of Calatrava, Province of Negros Occidental, Shall Hereafter be Known as Andres Menchaca Park**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The public plaza in the poblacion of the Municipality of Calatrava Province of Negros Occidental, shall hereafter be known as Andres Menchaca Park.
>
