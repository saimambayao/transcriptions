> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2909**
>
> **An Act Reappropriating Certain Sums Under Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Three Hundred One**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item 14 (b), page 530 of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred and one, is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "14. (b) Bario school building, Sitio Saay, Barrio Can-asujan, CarcarConstruction of one room ₱4,000.00."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
