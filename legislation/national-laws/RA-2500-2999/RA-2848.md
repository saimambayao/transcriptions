> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2848**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (o), Subparagraph 5, for the Province of Antique, Page 234, Paragraph a, Title B, Section One of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Three Hundred and One**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (o), subparagraph 5, for the Province of Antique, page 234, paragraph a, Title B, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred and one is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > (o) For the asphalting of Felicidad and Rizal streets, Dao ₱10,000.00."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
