> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2807**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Argao Intermediate School in the Municipality of Argao, Province of Cebu, to Antonio Miñoza Intermediate School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Argao Intermediate School in the Municipality of Argao, Province of Cebu, is hereby changed to Antonio Miñoza Intermediate School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
