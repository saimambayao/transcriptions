> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2823**
>
> **An Act Converting Sitio Caligangan, Municipality of Liloan, Province of Leyte, into a Barrio**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Sitio Caligangan, Municipality of Liloan, Province of Leyte, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Caligangan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
