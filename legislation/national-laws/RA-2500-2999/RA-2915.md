> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2915**
>
> **An Act to Amend a Certain Item of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (31) on page 148 of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(31) Cantabaco Elementary School, Naga, Plan No. 4, modifiedConstruction of one additional room ₱6,000.00."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
