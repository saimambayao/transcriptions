> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2561**
>
> **An Act Creating Barrios in the Province of Batangas**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Nasugbu, Province of Batangas, are hereby converted into barrios of the said municipality, as follows:
>
> Sitio Lumbangan to Barrio Lumbangan; Sitio Balaytigui to Barrio Balaytigui; Sitio Malapad na Bato to Barrio Malapad na Bato; Sitio Aga to Barrio Aga; Sitio Kayrilao to Barrio Kayrilao; and Sitio Mataas na Pulo to Barrio Mataas na Pulo.
>
> **Section 2.** The sitios of Sambal in the Municipality of Lemery, Province of Batangas, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of Sambal.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
