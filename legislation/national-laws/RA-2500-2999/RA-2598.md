> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2598**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Kaga-Olas in the Municipality of Matanao, Province of Davao, to Swerte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Kaga-olas in the Municipality of Matanao, Province of Davao, is changed to Swerte.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
