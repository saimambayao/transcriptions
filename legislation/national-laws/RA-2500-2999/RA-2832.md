> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2832**
>
> **An Act Amending Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (ad), page 76, group I, subparagraph (25), for the Province of Laguna, paragraph f, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(ad) Municipal Hall, AlaminosConstruction ₱1,000.00"
> >
> > Section 2. Item 3, for the Province of Laguna, page 202, paragraph g, Section two of the same Act is amended to read as follows:
> >
> > "3. Municipal Hall, AlaminosConstruction ₱2,000.00"
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
