> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2555**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Pulantubig Elementary School at Dumaguete City to Ramon Magsaysay Memorial School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Pulantubig Elementary School at Dumaguete City is changed to Ramon Magsaysay Memorial School.1awp++i1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
