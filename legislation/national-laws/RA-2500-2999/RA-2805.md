> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2805**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Binuañgan in the Municipality of Alicia, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, to Bella**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Binuangan in the Municipality of Alicia, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, is changed to Bella.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
