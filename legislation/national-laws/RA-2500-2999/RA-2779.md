> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2779**
>
> **An Act Fixing the Boundaries of the Barrios of Binagbag and Salvacion in the Municipality of Agdangan, Province of Quezon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrio of Binagbag in the Municipality of Agdangan, Province of Quezon, shall be bounded on the North by the Manila Railroad Line; on the East by Lots Numbers 387, 386, 385, 382, and Bolo River; on the West by Lots Numbers 426, 425, 424, 423, 421, 3175, 420, and 418, and on the South by Tayabas Bay.
>
> The barrio of Salvacion in the Municipality of Agdangan, Province of Quezon, shall be bounded on the North by the Manila Railroad Line, on the East by Lots Numbers 422, 323, 413, 414, 415, and 417; on the West by Lots Numbers 816, 1125, 818, 456, 452, and 453, and on the South by Tayabas Bay.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
