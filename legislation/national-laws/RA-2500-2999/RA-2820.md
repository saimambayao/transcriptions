> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2820**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Bato, Province of Catanduanes**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Bato, Province of Catanduanes, are converted into barrios therein, to wit:
>
> 1. Sitio Mintay to Barrio Mintay;
>
> 2. Sitio Carorian to Barrio Carorian; and
>
> 3. Sitio Buenavista to Barrio Buenavista.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
