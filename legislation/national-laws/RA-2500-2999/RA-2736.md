> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2736**
>
> **An Act Providing for the Establishment, Operation and Maintenance of a School of Fisheries in the Municipality of Palanan, Province of Isabela**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the supervision and administration of the Director of the Bureau of Public Schools, a school of fisheries in the Municipality of Palanan, Province of Isabela, to be known as the Isabela School of Fisheries. The Director of Public Schools shall promulgate, subject to the approval of the Secretary of Education, such rules and regulations to carry out the purpose of this Act. Said Director shall also acquire the necessary site for the school.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of the said school for the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-one; and for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years, the yearly General Appropriations Act shall provide for its appropriation.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
