> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2532**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Magsaysay in the Municipality of Magallanes, Province of Sorsogon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Tamboleled in the Municipality of Magallanes, Province of Sorsogon, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Magsaysay.
>
