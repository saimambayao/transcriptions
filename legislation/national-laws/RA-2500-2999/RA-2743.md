> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2743**
>
> **An Act to Amend a Certain Item in Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred for the Province of Leyte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item 67, page 470 of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "67. Municipal building, Hinundayan ₱10,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
