> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2835**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Midsayap, Province of Cotabato**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Midsayap, Province of Cotabato, are converted into barrios therein to wit:
>
> 1. Sitio Dado to Barrio Dado;
>
> 2. Sitio Central Bulanan (Km. 52) to Barrio Central Bulanan (Km. 52);
>
> 3. Sitio Malamonte to Barrio Malamonte;
>
> 4. Sitio Samlang to Barrio Samlang;
>
> 5. Sitio Kiloyno to Barrio Kiloyno;
>
> 6. Sitio Biacnabato to Barrio Biacnabato;
>
> 7. Sitio Kamanal to Barrio Kamanal; and
>
> 8. Sitio Pacao to Barrio Pacao.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
