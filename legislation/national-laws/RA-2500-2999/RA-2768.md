> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2768**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (ai), Group I, Subparagraph 43, for the Province of Pangasinan, Page 371, Paragraph (f), Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (ai), Group I, subparagraph 43, for the Province of Pangasinan, page 371, paragraph (f), Section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(ai) Quinaoayanan-Tipor barrio road, Bani Construction ₱10,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
