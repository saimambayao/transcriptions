> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2669**
>
> **An Act Creating Additional Positions of Assistant Provincial Fiscals in the Provinces of Cebu and Pangasinan, Amending for This Purpose Section Sixteen Hundred Seventy-Four of the Revised Administrative Code, as Amended**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The provision of Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended, relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals in the provinces of Cebu and Pangasinan, is further amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Cebu, thirteen assistant provincial fiscals;
> >
> > "Pangasinan, eighteen assistant provincial fiscals;"
>
> **Section 2.** This act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1960.
