> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2747**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Bungog in the Municipality of Echague, Province of Isabela, to Mabuhay**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Bungog in the Municipality of Echague, Province of Isabela, is changed to Mabuhay.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
