> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2855**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Sitio of Leguet in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Palawan, into a Barrio of Said Municipality to be Known as the Barrio of Santa Teresa**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Leguet in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Palawan, is hereby converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Santa Teresa.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
