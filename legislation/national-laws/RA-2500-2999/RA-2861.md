> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2861**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (97), Page 657, Title (A) of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (97), page 657, Title (A) of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred, is amended to read as follows:
>
> > (97) Construction of Cabilauan Barrio School Home Economics Building, Barotac Nuevo ₱4,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
