> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2817**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Masalasa in the Municipality of Victoria, Province of Tarlac**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Masalasa and Balingog in the Municipality of Victoria, Province of Tarlac, are constituted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Masalasa.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
