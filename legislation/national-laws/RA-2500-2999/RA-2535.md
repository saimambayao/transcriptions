> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2535**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Buayan Elementary School in the Municipality of General P. Santos, Province of Cotabato, to Lagao Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Buayan Elementary School in the Municipality of General P. Santos, Province of Cotabato, is changed to Lagao Elementary School.1awp++i1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
