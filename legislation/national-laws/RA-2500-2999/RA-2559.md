> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2559**
>
> **An Act Creating Barrios in the Province of Pangasinan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Labrador, Province of Pangasinan, are constituted into a barrio of the said municipality, as follows:
>
> Sitios of Tokoc, Polowen and the adjoining part of Barrio Bolo bordering Silag to Barrio Magsaysay.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Mabini, Province of Pangasinan, are constituted into a barrio of the said municipality, as follows:
>
> Sitios Loboc, Lalasoran, Libsong and Biga to Barrio Kaabiangan.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
