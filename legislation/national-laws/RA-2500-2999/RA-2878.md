> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2878**
>
> **An Act Amending a Certain Item Under Appendix B of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Priority No. 39, page 77-B, under "Pangasinan III," Appendix B of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "39. Sta. Barbara Sonquil Repair and Improvement ₱2,500.00."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
