> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2844**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Pulongcawayan in the Municipality of La Paz, Province of Tarlac, to La Purisima**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Pulongcawayan in the Municipality of La Paz, Province of Tarlac, is changed to La Purisima.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
