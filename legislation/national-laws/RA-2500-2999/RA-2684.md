> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2684**
>
> **An Act Appropriating the Sum of One Hundred Fifty Thousand Pesos to Defray the Expenses for the Training, Preparation, and Participation of a Filipino Athletic Delegation in the Nineteen Hundred and Sixty Olympics to be Held in Rome, Italy**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is hereby appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated as aid to the Philippine Amateur Athletic Federation to defray the expenses for the selection, assembling, equipping, preparation and sending of a competent athletic delegation to represent the Philippines at the World Olympic Games to be held in Rome, Italy, in the year nineteen hundred and sixty.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1960.
