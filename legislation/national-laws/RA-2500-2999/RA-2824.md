> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2824**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Palompon, Province of Leyte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Cantandoy, Tambis and Oguinsan in the Municipality of Palompon, Province of Leyte, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Cantandoy, Tambis and Oguinsan, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
