> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2810**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Sogod, Province of Leyte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Sogod, Province of Leyte, are hereby converted or constituted into barrios:
>
> a. Sitios Kantabuan, Baycasili, Mamingaw, Tagabaca and Kampuwa, into a barrio to be named Barrio Kauswagan;
>
> b. Sitio Ilo, into a barrio to be named Barrio Malinaw; and
>
> c. Sitios Hubasan, Agta, Manduduknay, Kabas-an and Cabadbaran, into a barrio to be named Barrio San Juan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
