> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2911**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Bagong Pook in the Municipality of Pila, Province of Laguna**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Bagong Pook in the Municipality of Pila, Province of Laguna, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Bagong Pook.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
