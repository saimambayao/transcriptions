> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2599**
>
> **An Act Changing the Names of Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Goa, Province of Camarines Sur**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The names of the barrios of Balaynan and Aroro in the Municipality of Goa, Province of Camarines Sur, are changed to San Rafael and San Pedro, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
