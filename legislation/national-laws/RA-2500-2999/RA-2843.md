> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2843**
>
> **An Act Declaring Certain Places in the Municipality of La Paz, Province of Tarlac, as Duly Constituted Barrios**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following places in the Municipality of La Paz, Province of Tarlac, are declared as duly constituted barrios therein, to wit:
>
> 1. Balanoy as barrio Balanoy;
>
> 2. Bantog-Caricutan as barrio Bantog-Caricutan,
>
> 3. Caramutan as barrio Caramutan;
>
> 4. Caut as barrio Caut;
>
> 5. Gomillas as barrio Gomillas, with the sitio of Riolova as part thereof;
>
> 6. Dumarais as barrio Dumarais;
>
> 7. Guevara as barrio Guevara, with the sitio of Particion as part thereof;
>
> 8. Kapanikian as barrio Kapanikian;
>
> 9. Lara as barrio Lara;
>
> 10. Laungcupang as barrio Laungcupang;
>
> 11. Lomboy as barrio Lomboy, with the sitio of Mapalad as part thereof;
>
> 12. Macalong as barrio Macalong;
>
> 13. Matayumtayum as barrio Matayumtayum;
>
> 14. Mayang as barrio Mayang;
>
> 15. Motrico as barrio Motrico;
>
> 16. Paludpud as barrio Paludpud;
>
> 17. La Purisima (Pulongcawayan) as barrio La Purisima;
>
> 18. San Isidro as barrio San Isidro;
>
> 19. San Roque as barrio San Roque, with the sitio of Libtong as part thereof; and
>
> 20. Sierra as barrio Sierra.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
