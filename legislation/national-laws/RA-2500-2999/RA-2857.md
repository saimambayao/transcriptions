> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2857**
>
> **An Act Amending a Certain Item of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Three Hundred and One, Entitled "An Act Appropriating Funds for Public Works"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** On page 588 of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred and one, item No. 5, under
>
> > "Miscellaneous," of Section two, entitled "Projects under maintenance and short-term program of capital improvements," is hereby amended to read as follows:
> >
> > "5. Zamboanguita: (a) Mayabon River Control ₱15,000.00."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
