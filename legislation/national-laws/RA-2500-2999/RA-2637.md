> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2637**
>
> **An Act to Repeal Republic Act Numbered One Thousand Seven Hundred Ninety-Eight**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Republic Act Numbered One thousand seven hundred ninety-eight is repealed.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1960.
