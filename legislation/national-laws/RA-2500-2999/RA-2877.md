> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2877**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (3), Subparagraph (ab), for the Province of Leyte, Page 700, Paragraph b, Title A, Section Three of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (3), subparagraph (ab), for the Province of Leyte, page 700, paragraph b, Title A, Section three of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(3) Construction of 2-room building including painting, Campokpok Barrio School, Tabango ₱12,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
