> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2617**
>
> **An Act Amending Item (e), Page 212 Subparagraph (6), for the Province of Bataan, Paragraph f, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (e), page 212, subparagraph (6), for the Province of Bataan, paragraph f, section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(e) Pantalan Luma-Wawa Streets, Orani Improvement ₱2,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: March 15, 1960.
