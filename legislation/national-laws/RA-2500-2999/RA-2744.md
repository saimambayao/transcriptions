> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2744**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Monkayo, Province of Davao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Palina, Quidapang, Banglasan, Kikonay, Casoon, Nagasi and Tagbunawan in the Municipality of Monkayo, Province of Davao, are constituted into a district and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of San Isidro.
>
> The Sitios of Banlag, Upper Totoy, Tina, Tuba-an, Tubu-an, Wagusan and Bucana in the Municipality of Monkayo, Province of Davao, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Banlag.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
