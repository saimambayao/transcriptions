> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2502**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Province of Zamboanga del Sur**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Bolarong, Talanusa, Guinimanan, Bius, Linoguayan, Kapatagan, Waling Waling, Limason, Lonib, Cogon, Lumbog, Macaulay, Limbatong, Tulapok, Calian and Igat Islan in the Municipality of Margosatubig, province of Zamboanga del Sur, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Bolarong, Talanusa, Guinimanan, Bius, Linoguayan, Kapatagan, Waling Waling, Limason, Lonib, Cogon, Lumbog, Macaulay, Limbatong, Tulapok, Calian, and Igat Island, respectively.
>

>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
