> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2886**
>
> **An Act Reappropriating the Sum of Two Thousand Five Hundred Pesos Appropriated Under Item (ak), Page 232, Group II, Subparagraph 28, for the Province of Leyte, Paragraph f, Title M, Section One of Republic Act Numbered Two Thousand Ninety-Three**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of two thousand five hundred pesos appropriated under item (ak), page 232, group II, subparagraph 28, for the Province of Leyte, paragraph f, Title M, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Two thousand ninety-three, to wit:
>
> > "(ak) Victory Barrio School, Matalom Construction and/or improvement ₱2,500.00"
>
> is hereby reappropriated for the construction of a school building in the barrio of Taylan, Municipality of Matalom, Province of Leyte.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
