> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2767**
>
> **An Act Changing the Names of Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Liargao, Province of Zamboanga del Sur**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Sominot in the Municipality of Liargao, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, is changed to Oliva.
>
> **Section 2.** The name of the barrio of Lower Malating in the same municipality is changed to Magsaysay.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
