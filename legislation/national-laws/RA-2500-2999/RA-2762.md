> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2762**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of Davao (Sasa) Airport in Davao City to Francisco Bangoy Airport in Honor of the Late Don Francisco Bangoy**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In recognition of the philanthropic acts of the late Don Francisco Bangoy, the name of Davao (Sasa) Airport in Davao City is hereby changed to Francisco Bangoy Airport.
>
