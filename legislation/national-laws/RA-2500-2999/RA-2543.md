> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2543**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Maruclap in the Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Maruclap and Cotocoto in the barrio of Haliging Kanluran, Maarangan in the barrio of Haliging Silangan, Cacawan in the barrio of Sto. Niño, and Payapa in the barrio of Talahib Payapa, all in the Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, are separated from the said barrios, and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Maruclap.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
