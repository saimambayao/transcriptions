> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2775**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Liloan in the Municipality of Catarman, Subprovince of Camiguin, Province of Misamis Oriental**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Manduaw, Pasanan, Bae, Kiab, Banao, Dumilag, Napo, Cogon, Tubos and Tag-ibo in the Municipality of Catarman, Sub-Province of Camiguin, Province of Misamis Oriental, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Liloan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
