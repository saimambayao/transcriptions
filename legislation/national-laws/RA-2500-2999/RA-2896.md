> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2896**
>
> **An Act Transferring the Barrio of Braulan in the Municipality of Passi, Province of Iloilo, to the Municipality of San Enrique**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrio of Braulan in the Municipality of Passi, Province of Iloilo, is separated from said municipality, and made a part of the Municipality of San Enrique.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
