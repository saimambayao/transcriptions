> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2769**
>
> **An Act Declaring Regular and Official the Barrios of Dabubu Pequeño, Rang-Ay, Sto. Niño, Mapalad, San Antonio, Quimalabasa Norte, Quimalabasa Sur, Panang, Santos, Calaocan and Masaya Centro in the Municipality of San Agustin, Province of Isabela**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrios of Dabubu Pequeño, Rang-ay, Sto. Niño, Mapalad, San Antonio, Quimalabasa Norte, Quimalabasa Sur, Panang, Santos, Calaocan and Masaya Centro in the Municipality of San Agustin, Province of Isabela, are hereby declared regular and official barrios thereof.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
