> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2553**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Siayan in the Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Siayan, Bentol, Litalet, Dibalan, Colaton, Moyo Gamay, Pasi and Dalongdong in the Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Siayan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
