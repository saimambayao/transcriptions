> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2550**

> **An Act Dividing the Barrio of Manaring in the Municipality of Ilagan, Province of Isabela, into Two Barrios to be Known as Barrio Manaring Primero and Barrio Manaring Segundo**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrio of Manaring in the Municipality of Ilagan, Province of Isabela, is hereby divided into two barrios to be known as Barrio Manaring Primero and Barrio Manaring Segundo.1âшphi1
