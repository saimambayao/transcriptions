> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2774**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Bongato West in the Municipality of Bayambang, Province of Pangasinan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Bongato West is separated from the Barrio of Bongato, Municipality of Bayambang, Province of Pangasinan, and same is hereby constituted as a distinct and separate barrio of the said municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** The remaining portion shall be known as Barrio Bongato East.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
