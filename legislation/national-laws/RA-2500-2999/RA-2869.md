> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2869**
>
> **An Act Amending Certain Items of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Three Hundred and One, Entitled "An Act Appropriating Funds for Public Works and for Other Purposes"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** On page 154 of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred and one, item (23) under Group II (Projects for the Second District, Negros Oriental), embraced by subsection c (Re implementation of Republic Act Numbered Eight hundred thirty-six, as amended), is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(23) Central Primary School Building, SiquijorImprovement ₱5,000.00"
> >
> > Section 2. On page 441 of the same Act, item (k) under Group II (Projects for the Second District, Negros Oriental), under subsection d of Section two thereof, is hereby amended to read as follows:
> >
> > "(k) Central Primary School, SiquijorAid to PTA for improvement ₱5,000.00"
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
