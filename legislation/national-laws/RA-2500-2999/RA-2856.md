> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2856**
>
> **An Act Amending a Certain Item of Republic Act Numbered Twenty Hundred Ninety-Three, Entitled "An Act Appropriating Funds for Public Works and for Other Purposes"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** On page 250 of Republic Act Numbered Twenty hundred ninety-three, item (g), under Group II for the Province of Negros Oriental, and under Title MShort Term Projects, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(g) Cangmalalag-Cabulihan feeder road, LarenaRepair and/or construction ₱10,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
