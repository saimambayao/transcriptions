> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2676**
>
> **An Act Making the Suribao River the Boundary Between the Municipalities of Borongan and Maydolong, Province of Samar**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Suribao River is hereby made the boundary between the municipalities of Borongan and Maydolong, Province of Samar.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1960.
