> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2541**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Liargao, Province of Zamboanga del Sur**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Lumangoy, Lumpanid, Montay, Midsapin, Patang, Dipalusan, Guminambon, Dinuyog, San Miguel, Bibilog and Dipalutao in the Municipality of Liargao, Province of Zamboanga Sur, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Lumangoy, Lumpanid, Montay, Midsapin, Patang, Dipalusan, Guminambon, Dinuyog, San Miguel, Bibilog and Dipaluto, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
