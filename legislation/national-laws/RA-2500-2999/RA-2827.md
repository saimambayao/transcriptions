> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2827**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Mongolbongol in the Municipality of Capul, Province of Samar, to San Vicente**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio Mongolbongol in the Municipality of Capul, Province of Samar, is changed to San Vicente.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
