> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2722**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (73), Page 737, Subparagraph aj, for Mountain Province, Paragraph b, Title A, Section Three of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (73), page 737, subparagraph aj, for Mountain Province, paragraph b, Title A, Section three of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(73) Construction and completion of 3 rooms, Mompolia Barrio School, Burnay ₱11,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
