> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2594**
>
> **An Act Changing the Names of Barrios in the Municipality of Lemery, Province of Iloilo**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The names of the following barrios in the Municipality of Lemery, Province of Iloilo, are changed as follows:
>
> 1. Barrio Tuguis to Barrio Jose Almenana;
>
> 2. Barrio Nabaloto to Barrio Pedro Velasco;
>
> 3. Barrio Tuburan to Barrio Inocencio Sepanton;
>
> 4. Barrio Alaguingay to Barrio Patricio Alcantara; and
>
> 5. Barrio Bajo to Barrio San Diego.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
