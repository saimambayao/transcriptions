> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2787**
>
> **An Act to Amend Project Numbered D-143.2, for the Province of Aklan, Page 928-A, Title D, Appendix A of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Project numbered D-143.2, for the Province of Aklan, page 928-A, Title D, Appendix A of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "D-143.2 Aklan Waterworks, for the towns GOB 500 (500) (300) (200) 4 New project of Makato, Kalibo, Banga, New Washington, Lezo, Malinao, Numancia and Tangalan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
