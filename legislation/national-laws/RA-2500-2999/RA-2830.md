> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2830**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Lico in the Municipality of Naval, Province of Leyte, to Barrio Bilbao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Lico in the Municipality of Naval, Province of Leyte, is hereby changed to Barrio Bilbao.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
