> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2892**
>
> **An Act to Amend Subparagraph (23) for the Province of Samar, Page 465, Paragraph C, Title J, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty Hundred and Ninety-Three**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum appropriated under item (j), subparagraph (23) for the Province of Samar, page 465, paragraph C, Title J, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty hundred and ninety-three is transferred to item (i) of the same subparagraph, which is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(i) Oras Breakwater, Improvement ₱25,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
