> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2604**
>
> **An Act Changing the Names of a Barrio and of Sitios in the Municipality of Polanco, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Loboc in the Municipality of Polanco, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is changed to San Miguel, and the names of the sitios of Lantoy and Biga-an in the same municipality are changed to Sto. Niño and Mabuhay, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
