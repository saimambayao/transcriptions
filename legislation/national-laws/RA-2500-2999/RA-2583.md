> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2583**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Province of Isabela**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Rang-ayan in the Municipality of Mallig, Province of Isabela, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Rang-ayan.
>
> **Section 2.** The sitio of Sta. Rita in the Municipality of Aurora, same province, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Sta. Rita.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The sitios of Balmarin, Belma and Salipan in the Municipality of San Agustin, same province, are hereby constituted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Damabal.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
