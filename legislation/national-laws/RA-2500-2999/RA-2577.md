> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2577**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Samalague in the Municipality of Dao, Province of Antique, to Barrio Santa Maria**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Samalague in the Municipality of Dao, Province of Antique, is changed to Barrio Santa Maria.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1awp++i1
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
