> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2814**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Casiguran, Province of Sorsogon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following places and sitios in the Municipality of Camarines, Province of Sorsogon, are declared as barrios thereof, to wit:
>
> 1. The sitios of Cabogñahan, Cagdagat, Fabrica, Lubas and Marines, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Boton;
>
> 2. The sitio of Mambot and Namuut, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Burgos;
>
> 3. The barrio of Casay as barrio Casay;
>
> 4. The barrio of Cogon as barrio Cogon;
>
> 5. The barrio of Inlagadean as barrio Inlagadean;
>
> 6. The barrio of Lugñid as barrio Lugñid;
>
> 7. The barrio of Mabini as barrio Mabini;
>
> 8. The sitio of Pamakiran as a barrio to be known as the barrio of Ponong;
>
> 9. The sitios of Colambis, Lugi, Malagomgom, Rawag and Tiris, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Rizal;
>
> 10. The barrio of San Antonio as barrio San Antonio;
>
> 11. The sitios of Tigbao and Nabugñaren, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of San Isidro;
>
> 12. The sitio of Escuela, as a barrio to be known as the barrio of San Juan;
>
> 13. The sitios of Cadiang and Purog, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of San Pascual;
>
> 14. The sitios of Buraburan, Calayogan, Sog-ong and Talahib, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Santa Cruz;
>
> 15. The sitios of Mauhang and Storeroom, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Trece Martires; and
>
> 16. The sitios of Adovis, Busing, Cawit, Central, Somalot, Timbayog, Tinampo and Tulay, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Poblacion.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
