> **REPUBLIC ACTNo. 2574**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Tanayan in the Municipality of Katipunan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Pucay, Lapan, Upper Irasan, Montay and Upper Malupis in the Municipality of Katipunan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Tanayan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
