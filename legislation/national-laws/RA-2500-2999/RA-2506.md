> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2506**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrios of Santa Cruz in the Municipality of Puerto Princesa, Province of Palawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Kalatubog and Kilometer 32 of Puerto Princesa North Road in the Municipality of Puerto Princesa, Province of Palawan, are constituted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Santa Cruz.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
