> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2534**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Tagpupungan in the Municipality of Babak, Province of Davao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Tagpupungan in the Municipality of Babak, Province of Davao, is converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Tagpupungan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
