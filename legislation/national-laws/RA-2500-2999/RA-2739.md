> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2739**
>
> **An Act to Constitute the Sitios of Salokegue, Can-Abay, Soksokon, Capatagan, Danglay and Lologayan, All in the Municipality of Basey, Province of Samar, into a Barrio to be Known as Barrio May-et of the Same Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Salokegue, Can-abay, Soksokon, Capatagan, Danglay and Lologayan, all in the Municipality of Basey, Province of Samar, are hereby constituted into a barrio to be known as Barrio May-et of the same municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
