> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2764**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (p), Page 281, Paragraph F, Group IV for the Province of Iloilo, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (p), page 281, paragraph f, Group IV for the Province of Iloilo, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(p) Licoan Elementary School, construction of additional rooms ₱5,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
