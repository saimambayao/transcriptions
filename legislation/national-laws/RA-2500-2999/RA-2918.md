> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2918**
>
> **An Act Transferring the Seat of Government of the Municipality of Burgos, Province of Ilocos Sur, to the Barrio of Luna**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The seat of government of the Municipality of Burgos, Province of Ilocos Sur, is transferred from the present site to the barrio of Luna.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
