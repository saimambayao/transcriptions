> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2821**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Panabo, Province of Davao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Panabo, Province of Davao, are converted or constituted into barrios therein, to wit:
>
> 1. Sitio Magopsing, to be known as the barrio of Magopsing; and
>
> 2. Sitios of Carriedo and New Camiling, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Alejal.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
