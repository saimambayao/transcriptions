> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2900**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Kambuhawi in the Municipality of Balamban, Province of Cebu**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Kambuhawi in the Municipality of Balamban, Province of Cebu, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Kambuhawi.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
