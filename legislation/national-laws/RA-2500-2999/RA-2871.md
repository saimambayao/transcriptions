> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2871**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Anilao, Province of Iloilo**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Anilao, Province of Iloilo, are constituted and declared as barrios therein, to wit:
>
> The sitios of Janicaman Ilaya, Dinum-at Bugas, Cantoy, Bobog, H. Lourdes and Buena Lastima, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Poblacion;
>
> The sitios of Binun-an Sulod and Janicaman Ilawod, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Pantalan;
>
> The sitios of Pangpang Norte, Pangpang Sur, Ilawod Sur, Moros, Baluarte and Balañga-an, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Sta. Rita;
>
> The sitios of Bato, Bat-us, Himogbugan and Mumwick, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Dangula-an;
>
> The sitios of Laligan, Lu-awan, Bogtong Bukid, Payud, Dalipe Sur, Guibuañgan Sur and Bariñgan Sur, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of San Carlos;
>
> The sitios of Orioegan, Alibayug Ilawod, Agloy-a Ilawod, Aglankawas, Hulot and Suyakan, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as barrio of Manganese;
>
> The sitios of Marka Bobog, Agtambo, Olo Anilao, Anilao Cayo, Orol and Mangiyakiya, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Balunos;
>
> The sitios of Buenavista, Guintapukan, Inadlawan, Kabangkalan, Tangcong, Dalipe, Lampuyang, Sibucawan and Guiraguid, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Cag-an;
>
> The sitios of Pangurayan, Kapaclan, Lininti-an, Badiaoegan and Agpandan Daku, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Mostro;
>
> The sitios of Guintingfi Sta. Ana Sulod, Amador, Kaliburan, Boyongan Talus and Linao Kalayo, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Palaypay;
>
> The sitios of Bacolod, Bonsod Norte, Hda. San Marcelino, Bairan Sur and Patio, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Sabang Culob;
>
> The sitios of Bonsod Sur, Balagon, Igang and Botitihon, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Badiang;
>
> The sitios of Biga-a and Plantation No. 1, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Serallo;
>
> The sitios of Odok, Kadugang and Genis Bukid, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Medina;
>
> The sitios of Agtalus, Ilawad, Trencera, and Genis Punta Andagao, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of San Juan Crisostomo;
>
> The sitios of Bairan, Baguio, Agtalus, Ilaya and Genis Olo Agbatuan, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Agbatuan;
>
> The sitios of Pitogo, Manise Ilaya, Bagongbong and Olo Pitogo, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Vista Alegre;
>
> The sitios of Manise Ilawod, Bognay, Kangong, Talangban and Dumingding, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Camiros;
>
> The sitios of Namatay, Lanawon, Ilaho, Kagnayahan, Bacolod Kalubihan and Guinbalayan Ato, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Sto. Rosario;
>
> The sitios of Aglay-ao, Malublub and Agbalatong, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Balabag; and
>
> The sitios of Agcarope, Apao, Aladedhan, Agloy-a Ilaya, Alibuyug and Agbatasan, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Guipes.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
