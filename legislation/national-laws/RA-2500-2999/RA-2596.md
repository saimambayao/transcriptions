> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2596**
>
> **An Act Dividing the Barrio of Casibarag in the Municipality of Cabagan, Province of Isabela, into Two Barrios to be Known as Casibarag Norte and Casibarag Sur**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrio of Casibarag in the Municipality of Cabagan, Province of Isabela, is hereby divided into two barrios to be known as Casibarag Norte and Casibarag Sur.1âшphi1

> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
