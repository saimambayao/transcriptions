> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2893**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Balangkayan, Province of Samar**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Guinpoliran, Calubian, Bangon, Caisawan and Julag in the Municipality of Balangkayan, Province of Samar, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Guinpoliran, Calubian, Bangon, Caisawan and Julag, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
