> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2903**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of San Ramon in the Municipality of Pambujan, Province of Samar**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Magalotgamot, Catan-agan and Paray in the Municipality of Pambujan, Province of Samar, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Ramon. The proposed barrio is bounded on the north by the Banase Brook, on the east by the dapdap tree, point one; on the south by the coconut tree, point two; and on the west by the coconut tree, point three.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
