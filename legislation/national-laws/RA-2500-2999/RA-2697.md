> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 2697, June 18, 1960 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CREATING A DEPARTMENT AND ADDITIONAL POSITIONS IN THE GENERAL AUDITING OFFICE AND PROVIDING FUNDS FOR THE OPERATION THEREOF.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>

> **Section 2.** There is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the amount of one hundred eighty-eight thousand,  six hundred fifty pesos and forty centavos for the fiscal year nineteen hundred and sixty-one.  The corresponding appropriation for the succeeding fiscal years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
