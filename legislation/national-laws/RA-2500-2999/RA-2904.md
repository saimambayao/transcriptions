> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2904**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (aa), Under Group 7, Batanes, Section 2-d, Page 350 of Republic Act Numbered Twenty Three Hundred One**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (aa), under Group 7, Batanes, Section 2-d, page 350 of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred one is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(aa) For drilling and construction of artesian wells in the Municipality of Basco ₱10,000.00."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
