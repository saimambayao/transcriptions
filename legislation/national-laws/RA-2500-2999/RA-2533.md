> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2533**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of the Mabini Primary School in the Municipality of Valladolid, Province of Negros Occidental, to Emilio Infante Primary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Mabini Primary School in the Municipality of Valladoid, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Emilio Infante Primary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1959.
