> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 2746**
>
> **An Act Creating Barrios in the Third District of the Province of Samar**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Libertad in the Municipality of Dolores, Province of Samar, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Libertad.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The sitios of Camada, Guindalitan and Tagaslian in the Municipality of Maydolong, Province of Samar, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Camada, Guindalitan and Tagaslian, respectively.
>
> **Section 3.** The sitio of Cambilla in the Municipality of Quinapundan, Province of Samar, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Cambilla.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
