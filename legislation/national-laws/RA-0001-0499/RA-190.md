> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 190**
>
> An Act Creating the Barrio of Bucal in the Municipality of Rosario, Province of Batangas
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The territory comprising the western half of barrio Maugat, in the municipality of Rosario, Province of Batangas, is hereby segregated from said barrio and constituted into an independent barrio to be known as Bucal.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1947.
