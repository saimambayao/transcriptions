> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 322**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Plaza Guipit in the City of Manila to Cayetano Arellano
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In order to perpetuate the memory of the greatest jurist this country has ever producedthat pure and erudite Titan of Philippine jurisprudencethe late first Chief Justice Cayetano Arellano, who, more than any other man in our history, made of the Supreme Court of the Philippines the torchlight and fountain-head of dignity, purity, and integrity in our judicial structure, the Plaza now called Guipit in the City of Manila shall henceforth to be known as Plaza Cayetano Arellano.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1948.
