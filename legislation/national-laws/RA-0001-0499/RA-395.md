> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 395**
>
> An Act Making the Positions of Mayor and Vice-Mayor in the City of Bacolod Elective
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The positions of mayor and vice-mayor of the City of Bacolod shall be elective, notwithstanding provisions to the contrary in the Act creating said city and amendments thereto. They shall hold office for a term of four years, and shall be elected by the qualified voters of the city.
>
