> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 302**
>
> An Act to Appropriate the Sum of Three Million Pesos as Additional Capital for the National Tobacco Corporation
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury, the sum of three million pesos, as additional capital for the National Tobacco Corporation: *Provided,* That not more than twenty per centum shall be spent for salaries and wages.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1948.
