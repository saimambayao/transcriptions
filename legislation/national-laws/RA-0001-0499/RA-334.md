> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 334**
>
> An Act Granting Monetary Reward to Any Person Who Shall Locate or Find the Remains of the Late Governor Hilario Abellana of Cebu
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Any person who shall locate or find the remains of the late Governor Hilario Abellana of Cebu shall be granted a reward of five thousand pesos.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of five thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to carry out the purposes of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: July 20, 1948.
