> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 195**
>
> An Act Granting the "Municipal Government of Janiuay, Province of Iloilo" a Franchise for an Electric Light, Heat and Power System in Said Municipality
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Subject to the terms and conditions established in Act Numbered Thirty-six hundred and thirty-six as amended by Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and thirty-two, and to the provisions of the Constitution, there is granted to the "Municipal Government of Janiuay, Province of Iloilo." For a period of twenty-five years from the approval of this Act, the right, privilege, and authority to construct, maintain, and operate an electric light, heat and/or power system for sale within the limits of the municipality of Janiuay, Province of Iloilo.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The franchise granted to Janiuay Electric Light Co., Inc., by the municipality of Janiuay to operate an electric light, heat and/or power system in said municipality, is hereby repealed.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1947.
