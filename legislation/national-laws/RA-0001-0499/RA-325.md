> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 325**
>
> An Act Amending Item D-VI-9, Special Provisions for the Department of Foreign Affairs, Page Ninety-Three of Republic Act Numbered Three Hundred Twenty
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item D-VI-9, Special Provisions for the Department of Foreign Affairs, page 93 of Republic Act No. 320, is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "9. No lump sum appropriation authorized for the Department of Foreign Affairs may be used for increases of salaries nor the creation of new positions except abroad."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect July 1, 1948.1awp++i1
>
> Approved: July 13, 1948.
