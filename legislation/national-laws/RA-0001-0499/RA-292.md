> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 292**
>
> An Act Creating the Municipality of Placer in the Province of Masbate
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrio of Placer in the municipality of Cataignan, Province of Masbate, is separated from the municipality and constituted into a new municipality to be known as the municipality of Placer.
>
> **Section 2.** The elective officials of the new municipality shall be appointed by the President of the Philippines and shall hold office until their successors shall have been elected and qualified.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1948.
