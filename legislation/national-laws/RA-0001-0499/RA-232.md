> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 232**
>
> An Act to Separate the Barrios of Ungale, Tucdao, and Inasuyan from the Municipality of Caibiran, Province of Leyte, and to Merge These Barrios with the Municipality of Kawayan, of the Same Province
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrios of Ungale, Tucdao, and Inasuyan of the municipality of Caibiran, Province of Leyte, are hereby separated from said municipality and merged with the municipality of Kawayan, of the same province.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 9, 1948.
