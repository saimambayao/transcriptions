> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 297**
>
> An Act Creating the Municipality of Madalag in the Province of Capiz
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The arrabal of Madalag, municipality of Libacao, Province of Capiz, is hereby separated from Libacao and created and constituted into a new and separate municipality to be known as the municipality of Madalag.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The said new municipality of Madalag shall include the following barrios: Logohon, Singay, Balactasan, Cabangahan, Cabilawan, Pangitan, San Jose, Talimagao, Talangban, Alaminos, Catabana, Bakyang, Calicia, Mercedes, Maria Cristina, Dit-ana, Guinato-an, Tigbauan, Alas-as, Mamba, Medina, Panikyason, and Paningayan.
>
> **Section 3.** The elective officials of the new municipality shall be appointed by the President of the Philippines to hold office until their successors are elected and qualified.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1948.
