> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 197**
>
> An Act Creating the Municipality of Lemery in the Province of Iloilo
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrios of Lemery, Tabunan, Tuburan, Nagsulang, Daga, Tuguis, Singcua, Agpipili, Pacuan, Milan, Alagiñgay, Tuga, Bajo, San Antonio, Capeñahan, Bankal, Geroñgan, Omio, Nasapahan, Abuac-Dalipe, San Jose, Cabangtohan, Dapdapan, Butuan, Anabo, and Buenavista are separated from the municipality of Sara and constituted into a new municipality to be known as the municipality of Lemery in the Province of Iloilo, with the present barrio of Lemery as the seat of the government.
>
> **Section 2.** The new municipality shall be organized on January one, nineteen hundred and forty-eight.1aшphi1 The elective officials of said municipality shall be appointed by the President of the Philippines and shall hold office until their successors shall have been elected and qualified.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1947.
