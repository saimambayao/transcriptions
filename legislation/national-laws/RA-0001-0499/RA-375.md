> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 375, June 14, 1949 ]**
>
> AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE SARA HIGH SCHOOL TO VICTORINO SALCEDO HIGH SCHOOL.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** In recognition of the meritorious services tendered by Victorino Salcedo to the municipality of Sara, Province of Iloilo, the name of Sara High School hereby changed to Victorino Salcedo High School.⌊aшΡhi⌊
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 14, 1949.
