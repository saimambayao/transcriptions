> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 153**
>
> **An Act to Amend Section Two Hundred and Sixty-Five of Commonwealth Act Numbered Four Hundred and Sixty-Six, Known as the National Internal Revenue Code**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section two hundred and sixty-five of Commonwealth Act Numbered Four hundred sixty-six, known as the National Internal Revenue Code, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Sec. 265. *Charges on firewood cut in the public forest.*  On firewood cut in the forests and forest reserves, the following charges shall be collected:
> >
> > On bacauan, langaray, pototan, and tangal, per cubic meter, forty centavos.1â#x0448;phi1
> >
> > On other woods, per cubic meter, twenty centavos.
> >
> > Only third or fourth-group wood can be taken for firewood. However, at the discretion of the Director of Forestry, first and second-group woods may be removed for firewood purposes from land which is more valuable for agricultural than for forest purposes."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 14, 1947.
