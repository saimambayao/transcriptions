> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 58**
>
> **An Act Creating the Municipality of Tubod, Province of Lanao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The existing barrios of Tubod, Baroy, Lala, Daromawang, Bolod, Taguiquiron, Sagadan and Princesa in the municipality of Kolambugan, Province of Lanao, are hereby separated from the said municipality and are herein constituted into a new district and separate municipality to be known as the municipality of "Tubod" in the said Province of Lanao.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1aшphi1
>
> Approved: October 17, 1946
