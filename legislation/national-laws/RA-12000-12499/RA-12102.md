# Republic Act No. 12102

**AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 3 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KALIBO, PROVINCE OF AKLAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE "KALIBO FOUNDATION DAY"**

> Nineteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12102, December 13, 2024 ]
>
> AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 3 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KALIBO, PROVINCE OF AKLAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE "KALIBO FOUNDATION DAY"
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** November 3 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the Municipality of Kalibo, Province of Aklan, to be known as the "Kalibo Foundation Day". Upon proclamation of the President, November 3 of every year shall be a special non-working holiday in the Municipality of Kalibo, Province of Aklan.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
