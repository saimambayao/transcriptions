# Republic Act No. 12105

**AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY 8 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAVIA, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO BE KNOWN AS "PAVIA DAY", TO COMMEMORATE ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY**

> Nineteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12105, December 13, 2024 ]
>
> AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY 8 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAVIA, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO BE KNOWN AS "PAVIA DAY", TO COMMEMORATE ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** January 8 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the Municipality of Pavia, Province of Iloilo, to be known as "Pavia Day", to commemorate its founding anniversary. Upon proclamation of the President January 8 of every year shall be a special non-working holiday in the Municipality of Pavia, Province of Iloilo.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
