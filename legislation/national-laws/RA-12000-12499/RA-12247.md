> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12247, August 29, 2025 ]
>
> AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 18 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANSALAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** September 18 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the Municipality of Bansalan, Province of Davao del Sur, in commemoration of its foundation anniversary. However, upon proclamation of the President, September 18 of every year shall be a special non-working holiday in the Municipality of Bansalan, Province of Davao del Norte.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.ℒαwρhi৷
