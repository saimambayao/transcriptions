> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12172, April 11, 2025 ]
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAIC, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, INTO A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The extension office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) located in the Municipality of Naic, Province of Cavite, is hereby converted into a regular district office.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Transportation shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the LTO District Office in the Municipality of Naic, Province of Cavite, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current year's appropriation of the LTO Extension Office in the Municipality of Naic.ℒαwρhi৷ Thereafter, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
