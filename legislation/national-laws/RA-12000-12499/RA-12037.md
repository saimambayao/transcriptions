> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12037, October 26, 2024 ]
>
> AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 2 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF LA UNION TO BE KNOWN AS "LA UNION DAY", IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** March 2 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the Province of La Union to be known as "La Union Day", in commemoration of its founding anniversary.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
