> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12062, November 06, 2024 ]
>
> AN ACT DECLARING JULY 2 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN PASIG CITY TO BE KNOWN AS THE "ARAW NG PASIG"
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** July 2 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holidayt in Pasig City to be known as the ***Araw ng Pasig.***
>
> Section 2. This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following its publication in the Official Gazette or in a national newspaper of general circulation.ℒαwρhi৷
