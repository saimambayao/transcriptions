> REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12275, September 7, 2025
>
> AN ACT DECLARING THE KUYAMIS FESTIVAL AS THE OFFICIAL FESTIVAL OF THE PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Kuyamis Festival is hereby declared the official festival of the Province of Misamis Oriental.
>
> **Section 2.** The second Friday of January of every year is hereby declared a special non-working holiday in the province in celebration of the Kuyamis Festival.
>
> **Section 3.** To carry out the purpose of this Act, there is hereby created a Special Festival Fund to be charged against any available fund of the province, including its share from the National Tax Allotment.ℒαwρhi৷ The Province of Misamis Oriental is also authorized to receive donations and contributions from private individuals, corporations, and non-government organizations.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.ℒαwρhi৷
