# Republic Act No. 12085

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN SEBASTIAN FISHERIES HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN SEBASTIAN, MUNICIPALITY OF LAGONOY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR TO SAN SEBASTIAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> Nineteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12085, December 13, 2024 ]
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN SEBASTIAN FISHERIES HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN SEBASTIAN, MUNICIPALITY OF LAGONOY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR TO SAN SEBASTIAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of San Sebastian Fisheries High School in Barangay San Sebastian, Municipality of Lagonoy, Province of Camarines Sur is hereby changed to San Sebastian National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following its publication in the Official Gazette or in a national newspaper of general circulation.
>
