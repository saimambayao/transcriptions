> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12200, May 08, 2025 ]
>
> AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 12 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF VALENZUELA, IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** November 12 of very year is hereby declared a special non-working holiday in the City of Valenzuela, in commemoration of its foundation anniversary.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
