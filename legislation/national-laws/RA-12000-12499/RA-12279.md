> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12279, September 07, 2025 ]
>
> AN ACT CREATING A BARANGAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NORZAGARAY, PROVINCE OF BULACAN TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY FRIENDSHIP VILLAGE RESOURCES OR BARANGAY FVR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** *Creation of Barangay Friendship Village Resources.* - There is hereby created a barangay in the Municipality of Norzagaray, Province of Bulacan, to be known as Barangay Friendship Village Resources or "Barangay FVR".

> **Section 3.** *Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite.* - The plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission on Elections pursuant to Sangguniang Panlalawigan Ordinance No. 003, which was duly ratified and proclaimed by the Plebiscite Board of Canvassers on October 12, 2002 shall serve as substantial compliance with the plebiscite requirement under Section 10 of Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as the Local Government Code of 1991.
>
> **Section 4.** *Incumbent Barangay Officials.* - The incumbent elected barangay officials of Barangay FVR shall continue to hold office until such time that their successors have been duly elected and qualified.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 5.** *Public Infrastructure and Facilities.* - All public infrastructure and facilities existing in Barangay FVR at the time of the approval of this Act are hereby transferred without cost or compensation to and shall be administered by the new barangay.
>
> **Section 6.** *National Tax Allotment Shares.* - Barangay FVR shall be entitled to National Tax Allotment shares pursuant to Section 285 of the Local Government Code of 1991, as amended.
>
> **Section 7.** *Repealing Clause.* - All laws, decrees, executive orders, rules and regulations which are inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 8.** *Effectivity.* - This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a national newspaper of general circulation.
