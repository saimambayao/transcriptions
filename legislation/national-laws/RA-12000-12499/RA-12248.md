> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12248, August 29, 2025 ]
>
> AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 15 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS CITY IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS CHARTER ANNIVERSARY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** June 15 of every year is hereby declared a special non-working holiday in the City of General Santos in commemoration of its charter anniversary.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.ℒαwρhi৷
