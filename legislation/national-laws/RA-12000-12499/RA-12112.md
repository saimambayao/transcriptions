# Republic Act No. 12112

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RODRIGUEZ (MONTALBAN), PROVINCE OF RIZAL, A LEVEL II HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS THE NORTHERN TAGALOG REGIONAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12112, December 13, 2024 ]
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RODRIGUEZ (MONTALBAN), PROVINCE OF RIZAL, A LEVEL II HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS THE NORTHERN TAGALOG REGIONAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, in the Municipality of Rodriguez (formerly Montalban), Province of Rizal, a Level II hospital with a two hundred (200)-bed capacity to be known as the Northern Tagalog Regional Hospital, which shall be under the direct control and supervision of the Department of Health (DOH).
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Health shall immediately include in the Department's program the implementation of this Act, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** If any provision or part of this Act is declared unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provision not affected shall remain in full force and effect.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 4.** All laws, executive orders, issuances, and rules and regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
