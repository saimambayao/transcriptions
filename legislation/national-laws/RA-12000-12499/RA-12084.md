# Republic Act No. 12084

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF NEW PARTIDO HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CATAGBACAN, MUNICIPALITY OF GOA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR TO NEW PARTIDO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> Nineteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12084, December 13, 2024 ]
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF NEW PARTIDO HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CATAGBACAN, MUNICIPALITY OF GOA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR TO NEW PARTIDO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of New Partido High School in Barangay Catagbacan, Municipality of Goa, Province of Camarines Sur is hereby changed to New Partido National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following its publication in the Official Gazette or in a national newspaper of general circulation.ℒαwρhi৷
