> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12249, August 29, 2025 ]
>
> AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 24 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALAYAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, IN CELEBRATION OF THE "PARADA NG LECHON"
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** June 24 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the Municipality of Balayan, Province of Batangas, in celebration of the "Parada ng Lechon." Upon proclamation of the President, June 24 of every year shall be a special non-working holiday in the said municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.ℒαwρhi৷
