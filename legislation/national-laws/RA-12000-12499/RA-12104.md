# Republic Act No. 12104

**AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY 22 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUIGUINTO, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, IN CELEBRATION OF HALAMANAN FESTIVAL**

> Nineteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12104, December 13, 2024 ]
>
> AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY 22 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUIGUINTO, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, IN CELEBRATION OF HALAMANAN FESTIVAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** January 22 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the Municipality of Guiguinto, Province of Bulacan, in celebration of the Halamanan Festival. Upon proclamation of the President, January of every year shall be a special non-working holiday in the Municipality of Guiguinto, Province of Bulacan.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
