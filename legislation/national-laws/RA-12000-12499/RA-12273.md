> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12273, September 07, 2025 ]
>
> AN ACT DECLARING APRIL 24 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SULOP, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS "ARAW NG SULOP", IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** April 24 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the Municipality of Sulop, Province of Davao del Sur, to be known as "Araw ng Sulop ", in commemoration of its foundation anniversary. However, upon proclamation of the President, April 24 of every year shall be a special non-working holiday in the Municipality of Sulop, Province of Davao del Sur.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.ℒαwρhi৷
