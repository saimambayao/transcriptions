# Republic Act No. 12007

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL IN PROGRESSIVE VILLAGE 3, BARANGAY BAYANAN, CITY OF BACOOR, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO BE KNOWN AS THE PROGRESSIVE SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineteenth Congress
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12007, June 13, 2024 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL IN PROGRESSIVE VILLAGE 3, BARANGAY BAYANAN, CITY OF BACOOR, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO BE KNOWN AS THE PROGRESSIVE SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established, under the supervision of the Department of Education, a senior high school in Progressive Village 3, Barangay Bayanan, City of Bacoor, Province of Cavite, to be known as the Progressive Senior High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the Progressive Senior High School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this Act, the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations implementing the provisions of this Act. Such implementing rules and regulations shall provide for the school's organization, administration, operations and management, in accordance with Republic Act No. 9155, or the "Governance of Basic Education Act of 2001", Republic Act No. 10533, or the "Enhanced Basic Education Act of 2013", and other basic education-related laws.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.1aшphi1
