> Nineteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12097, December 13, 2024 ]
>
> AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 20 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUINAYANGAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** June 20 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the Municipality of Guinayangan, Province of Quezon, in commemoration of its founding anniversary. Upon proclamation of the President, June 20 of every year shall be a special non-working holiday in the Municipality of Guinayangan, Province of Quezon.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.ℒαwρhi৷
