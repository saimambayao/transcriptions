> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12185, May 02, 2025 ]
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF UBAY, IN THE SECOND DISTRICT OF BOHOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a district office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Ubay, Province of Bohol.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the LTO District Office in the Municipality of Ubay, Province of Bohol, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
