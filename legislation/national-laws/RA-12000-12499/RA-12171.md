> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12171, April 11, 2025 ]
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE SATELLITE OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JACINTO, TICAO ISLAND, PROVINCE OF MASBATE, INTO A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The satellite office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) located in the Municipality of San Jacinto, Ticao Island, Province of Masbate, is hereby converted into a regular district office.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Transportation shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the regular LTO District Office in the Municipality of San Jacinto, Province of Masbate, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current year's appropriation of the LTO Satellite Office in the Municipality of San Jacinto, Ticao Island, Province of Masbate. Thereafter, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
