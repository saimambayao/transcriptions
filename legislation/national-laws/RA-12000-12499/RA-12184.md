> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12184, May 02, 2025 ]
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN TAGUIG CITY INTO A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Land Transportation Office (LTO) Extension Office in Taguig City is hereby converted into a regular district office.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the LTO District Office in Taguig City, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.ℒαwρhi৷
