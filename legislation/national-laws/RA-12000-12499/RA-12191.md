> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12191, May 02, 2025 ]
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAYAMBANG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, INTO A CLASS "C" DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The extension office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Bayambang, Province of Pangasinan, is hereby converted into a Class "D" district office.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the Department's program the operationalization of Class "C" LTO District Office in the Municipality of Bayambang, Province of Pangasinan, the initial funding of which shall be charged against current year's appropriation of the LTO Bayambang Extension Office. Thereafter, the funding shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
