> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12142, March 27, 2025 ]
>
> AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 28 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SARANGANI IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDATION DAY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** November 28 of every year is hereby declared a special non-working holiday in the Province of Sarangani in commemoration of its foundation anniversary.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.ℒαwρhi৷
