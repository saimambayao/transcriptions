> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1714**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Look in the City of Lipa**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Waniwani, Sayatin and Look, barrio of Balete, City of Lipa, are separated from said barrio and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said city, to be known as the barrio of Look.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
