> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1750**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Emilod, Municipality of Cuyo, Province of Palawan, into a Barrio of Said Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Emilod, Municipality of Cuyo, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Emilod.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
