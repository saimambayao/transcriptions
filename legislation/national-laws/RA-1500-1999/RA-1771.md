> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1771**
>
> **An Act Amending a Certain Item in Republic Act Numbered Twelve Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (36) under q. Cebu, title A, section two of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(36) Construction of a permanent 2-room Academic Building, Plan No. 1-4, San Fernando | ₱12,800.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
