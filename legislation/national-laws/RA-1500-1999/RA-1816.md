> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1816**
>
> **An Act Amending a Certain Item in Republic Act Numbered Twelve Hundred Entitled "An Act Appropriating Funds for Public Works and Amending Republic Act Numbered Nine Hundred and Twenty"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (16), paragraph g., Batangas, subsection three, title A, section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(16) Construction of Industrial Arts Building, Sabang, Lipa City | ₱4,000.00 |
> | (16-a) Construction of academic building, Sabang, Lipa City | 26,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon it approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
