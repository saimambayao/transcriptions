> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1584**
>
> **An Act Creating the Municipal District of Wao in the Province of Lanao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrios of Wao, Katubungan, Panang and Capigis in the Municipal District of Maguing, Province of Lanao, are separated from the said district, and constituted into a separate district in the same province to be known as the Municipal District of Wao, with its seat of government at the poblacion of Wao.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
