> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1530**
>
> **An Act to Amend Section Three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven, Otherwise Known as "The Public Works Appropriation Act of 1956"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, particularly item "as Samar," Roman Numeral II, is hereby amended so as to include the following appropriations:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "49. Minarog Barrio School Building, MontiongConstruction and Improvement | 4,000.00 |
> | "50. Mualbual Barrio School Building, Zumarraga Repair, improvement and reconstruction | 4,000.00 |
> | "51. Malakining Barrio School Building, TalaloraRepair, improvement and reconstruction | 4,000.00 |
> | "52. Loquilocon Barrio School Building, WrightConstruction and improvement | 4,000.00 |
> | "53. Paco Barrio School Building, WrightConstruction and improvement | 4,000.00 |
> | "54. Lamingao Barrio School Building, San SebastianConstruction and improvement | 3,400.00 |
> | "55. Dolores Barrio School Building, San SebastianConstruction and improvement | 3,400.00 |
> | "56. Maylobe Barrio School Building, MotiongConstruction and improvement | 4,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon it approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1956.
