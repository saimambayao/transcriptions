> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1757**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Sinonogan in the Municipality of Pambujan, Province of Samar**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Canagman, Pamantangan, Cabalyanan, Nagbong, Nabaye, Ele, Amotayong, and Cagkilikili in the Municipality of Pambujan, Province of Samar, are hereby constituted into a barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Sinonogan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
