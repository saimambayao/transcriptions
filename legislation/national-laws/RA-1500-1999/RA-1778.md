> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1778**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Ilayang Iyam in the Municipality of Lucena, Province of Quezon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The northern portion of the barrio of Iyam in the Municipality of Lucena, Province of Quezon, which is bounded on the north by the barrios of Gulang-gulang and Bocohan; on the east, by Iyam River; on the west, by the barrio of Isabang; and on the south, by the provincial road, is hereby separated from the said barrio and is created into a new distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Ilayang Iyam in the same municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
