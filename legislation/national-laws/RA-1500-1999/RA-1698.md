> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1698**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Inawangan, Municipality of Dulag, Province of Leyte, into a Barrio of Said Municipality to be Known as the Barrio of Kamitok**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Inawangan, Municipality of Dulag, Province of Leyte, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Kamitok.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
