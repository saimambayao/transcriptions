> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1687**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Pulang-Bato in the Municipality of San Nicolas, Province of Batangas**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Pulang-Bato is separated from the barrio of Alas-as, Municipality of San Nicolas, Province of Batangas, and constituted into a barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Pulang-Bato.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
