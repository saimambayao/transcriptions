> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1811**
>
> **An Act Amending Item (21), Under "al. Nueva Vizcaya," Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Twelve Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (21), under "al. Nueva Vizcaya," section two of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(21) Construction and improvement of: |  |
> | (a) La Torre Elementary School Home Economics Building, Bayombong | ₱10,000.00 |
> | (b) Malasin Elementary School Home Economics Building, Dupax | 10,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2**. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
