> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1707**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Macalang in the Municipality of Dasol, Province of Pangasinan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Macalang, Pantol, Tapac and Pagdagaan, Municipality of Dasol, Province of Pangasinan, are constituted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Macalang.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
