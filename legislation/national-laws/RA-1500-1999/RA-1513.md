> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1513**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Ponsod, in the Municipality of Maribojoc, Province of Bohol, to a Barrio to be Known as Barrio Ponsod**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Ponsod, in the Municipality of Maribojoc, Province of Bohol, is hereby converted to a barrio to be known as Barrio Ponsod.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1956.
