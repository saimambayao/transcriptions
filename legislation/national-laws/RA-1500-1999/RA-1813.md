> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1813**
>
> **An Act Amending Item (af), Under "(27) Leyte," Paragraph f, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (af), under "(27) Leyte," paragraph f, section two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(af) For the construction of the Bahay, Liloan, Leyte, barrio school building | ₱5,000.00 |
> | "(ag) For the completion of Nueva Estrella school building, Pintuyan, Leyte | ₱5,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
