> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1959**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Oton, Municipality of El Nido, Province of Palawan, to Mabini**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio Oton, Municipality of El Nido, Province of Palawan, is changed to Mabini.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
