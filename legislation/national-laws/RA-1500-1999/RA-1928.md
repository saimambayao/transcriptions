> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1928**
>
> **An Act Fixing the Compensation of the Officials of the City of Manila**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The provision of the Revised Charter of the City of Manila, as amended, to the contrary notwithstanding, officials of the said city shall receive the following annual compensation:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | Mayor | ₱15,000.00 |
> | Vice-Mayor | 13,000.00 |
> | Councilors | 10,000.00 |
> | City Engineer | 12,000.00 |
> | City Treasurer | 12,000.00 |
> | Chief of Police | 12,000.00 |
> | Chief, Fire Department | 12,000.00 |
> | City Assessor | 12,000.00 |
> | City Health Officer | 12,000.00 |
> | City Public Service Officer | 12,000.00 |
> | City Auditor | 12,000.00 |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
