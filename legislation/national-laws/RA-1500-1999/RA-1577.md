> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1577**
>
> **An Act Appropriating the Sum of Sixty-Three Thousand Five Hundred Seventy-Two Pesos to Enable the Government of the Philippines to Meet its Commitments with the South East Asia Treaty Organization**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sums are appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated for the period from July first, nineteen hundred fifty-six to June thirtieth, nineteen hundred fifty-seven, for the purpose specified hereunder:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | 1. For Philippine contribution to the SEATO Secretariat | ₱42,512.00 |
> | 2. For the SEATO Division, Home Office, Department of Foreign Affairs: | |
> | (a) One Chief of Division, Foreign Affairs Officer Class II | 7,200.00 |
> | (b) One Economist | 6,000.00 |
> | (c) One Political and Cultural Assistant | 5,100.00 |
> | (d) One Stenographer-clerk | 2,760.00 |
> | TOTAL | ₱63,572.00 |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1956.
