> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1666**
>
> **An Act Changing to General Roxas the Name of the Barrio of Dacudao in the City of Davao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Dacudao in the City of Davao is hereby changed to General Roxas.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
