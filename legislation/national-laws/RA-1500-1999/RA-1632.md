> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1632**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Zimigui-Ziuanan, Municipality of Pamplona, Province of Cagayan, to Masi**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Zimigui-Ziuanan of the Municipality of Pamplona, Province of Cagayan, is hereby changed to Masi.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
