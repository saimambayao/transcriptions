> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1717**
>
> AN ACT CREATING A CERTAIN BARRIO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GINGOOG, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL.
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Malibod, Municipality of Gingoog, Province of Misamis Oriental, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Malibod.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957
