> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1675**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Saug, Province of Davao, to Asuncion**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Saug, Province of Davao, is changed to Asuncion.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
