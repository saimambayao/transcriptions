> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1706**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Colonia, Municipality of Abulug, Province of Cagayan, to Libertad**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio Colonia, in the Municipality of Abulug, Province of Cagayan, is changed to Libertad.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
