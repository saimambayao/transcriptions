> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1544**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item 21(s), Ilocos Sur, Paragraph (e), Subsection B, Section One of Republic Act Numbered Nine Hundred Twenty**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item 21 (s), Ilocos Sur, paragraph (e), subsection B, section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred twenty, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(s) Pudoc Barrio School | ₱1,000.00 |
> | "(s-1) Becques Home Economics | 1,000.00 |
> | "(s-2) Libtong Barrio School | 1,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1956.
