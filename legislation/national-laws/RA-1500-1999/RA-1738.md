> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1738**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of Barrio Hinubuan, Municipality of Gasan, Province of Marinduque, to Antipolo**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio Hinubuan, Municipality of Gasan, Province of Marinduque, is hereby changed to Antipolo.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
