> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1711**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Cabili, Municipality of Panabo, Province of Davao, into a Barrio of Said Municipality to be Known as the Barrio of Quezon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Cabili, Municipality of Panabo, Province of Davao, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Quezon.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
