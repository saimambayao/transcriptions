> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1774**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Nasuli-"B" in the Municipality of Dao, Province of Antique, to Santo Tomas**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Nasuli-"B" in the Municipality of Dao, Province of Antique, is hereby changed to Santo Tomas.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
