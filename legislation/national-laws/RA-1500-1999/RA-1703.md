> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1703**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Calape, Province of Bohol**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Binogawan, Masonoy, Bintig, Cahayag, and Lawis, Municipality of Calape, Province of Bohol, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Binogawan, Masonoy, Bintig, Cahayag, and Lawis, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
