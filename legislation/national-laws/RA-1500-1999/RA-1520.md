> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1520**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of San Rafael in the Municipality of Guagua, Province of Pampanga**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Duck Island in the Municipality of Guagua, Province of Pampanga, is hereby constituted into a barrio of the said municipality, which shall be known as the barrio of San Rafael.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1956.
