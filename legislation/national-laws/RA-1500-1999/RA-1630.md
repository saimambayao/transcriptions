> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1630**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Magallon in the Province of Negros Occidental to Municipality of Moises Padilla**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Municipality of Magallon in the Province of Negros Occidental is hereby renamed Municipality of Moises Padilla.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
