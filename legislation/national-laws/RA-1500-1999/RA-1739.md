> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1739**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of Barrio Banto-anin, Municipality of Gasan, Province of Marinduque, to Barrio Bukal**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio Banto-anin, in the Municipality of Gasan, Province of Marinduque, is hereby changed to Bukal.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
