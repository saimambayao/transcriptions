> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1737**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Sawmill, Municipality of Aroroy, Province of Masbate, to Concepcion**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Sawmill, Municipality of Aroroy, Province of Masbate, is changed to Concepcion.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
