> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1667**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Lipras, Municipality of Manukan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, to Villa Ramos**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio Lipras, Municipality of Manukan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is changed to Villa Ramos.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
