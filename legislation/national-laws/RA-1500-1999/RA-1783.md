> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1783**
>
> **An Act Appropriating the Sum of Fourteen Thousand Four Hundred Pesos to be Paid as Compensation to the Widow of the Late Representative Pedro Lopez**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the sum of fourteen thousand four hundred pesos is appropriated to be paid as compensation to the widow of the late Representative Pedro Lopez. This amount shall be exempt from all kinds of taxes.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
