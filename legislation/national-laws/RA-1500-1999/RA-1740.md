> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1740**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Malirong, Municipality of Palo, Province of Leyte, to Libertad**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Malirong, Municipality of Palo, Province of Leyte, is changed to Libertad.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
