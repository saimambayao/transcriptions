> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1744**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Talisay, Municipality of Giñgoog, Province of Misamis Oriental**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Binuangan, Municipality of Giñgoog, Province of Misamis Oriental, is converted into a barrio thereof, to be hereafter known as the barrio of Talisay.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
