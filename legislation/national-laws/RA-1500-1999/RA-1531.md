> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1531**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered One Thousand Four Hundred Eleven, Entitled, "An Act Appropriating Funds for Public Works"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Republic Act Numbered One thousand four hundred eleven is hereby amended on the following particulars:
>
> (a) On page 103, Subdivision II:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(a) Asphalt paving of the following streets, Municipality of Bayawan, Negros Oriental: Buenavista Street from Zamora Street to Mabini Street; Mabini Street from Progreso Street to Burgos Street; Burgos Street from Mabini Street to General Luna Street; General Luna Street from Burgos Street to Nueva Street; Independence Street from Provincial Road to Nueva Street; La Libertad Street from Provincial Road to Rizal Street; Martirez Street from Provincial Road to Progreso Street; Progreso Street from General Luna Street to Martirez Street; Rizal Street from General Luna Street to La Libertad Street; Nueva Street from General Luna Street to Independence Street | ₱15,000.00" |
>
> (b) On page 541:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "13. Barrio Minalolan, Maria, Elementary School Building Construction of additional rooms | ₱10,000.00" |
>
> (c) On page 168-A, Appendix A, Item B-362.14, the words under Column, "name and location of project," be changed to read as follows:
>
> "B-362.14 Arellano Street, leading to cemetery, Zamboanguita repair and improvement."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1956.
