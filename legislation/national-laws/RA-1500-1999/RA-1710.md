> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1710**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Puting Buhangin, Barrio Magalanggalang, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, into a Barrio of Said Municipality to be Known as the Barrio of Orense**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Puting Buhangin, barrio Magalanggalang, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Orense.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
