> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1724**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Cogon-Bingkay in the Municipality of Dulag, Province of Leyte, to Salvacion**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio Cogon-Bingkay in the Municipality of Dulag, Province of Leyte, is hereby changed to Salvacion.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
