> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1775**
>
> **An Act Amending Items (9) and (29), Under "af. Mindoro Oriental." Title A, Section One of Republic Act Numbered Twelve Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Items (9) and (29) under "af. Mindoro Oriental," Title A, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred are amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(9) Construction of Pinamalayan Central School | ₱4,000.00" |
> | "(29) (a) Aid to PTA for the construction of Bulalacao Central School | 1,000.00" |
> | "(b) Aid to PTA for the completion of Ranso Barrio School, Pinamalayan | 1,000.00" |
> | "(c) Aid to PTA for the completion of San Agustin School Shop, Naujan | 500.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
