> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1672**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Bugang in the Municipality of Pio V. Corpus, Province of Masbate, into a Barrio to be Known as Barrio Bugang**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Bugang in the Municipality of Pio V. Corpus, Province of Masbate, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as barrio Bugang.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
