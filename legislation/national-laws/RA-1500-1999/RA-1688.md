> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1688**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Taug Norte, Barrio of Taug, Municipality of Lila, Province of Bohol, into a Barrio of Said Municipality to be Known as the Barrio of La Fortuna**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Taug Norte, barrio of Taug, Municipality of Lila, Province of Bohol, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of La Fortuna.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
