> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1743**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Ilaures in the Municipality of Bugasong, Province of Antique**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Ilaures is separated from the barrio of Camangahan, Municipality of Bugasong, Province of Antique, and constituted into a barrio in the said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Ilaures.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
