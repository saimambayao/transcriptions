> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1696**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Curba, Municipality of Santa Fe, Province of Leyte, to Barrio San Roque**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Curba, Municipality of Santa Fe, Province of Leyte, is changed to barrio San Roque.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
