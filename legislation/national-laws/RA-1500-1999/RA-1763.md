> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1763**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Puangi in the Municipality of Dipaculao, Province of Quezon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Puangi in the Municipality of Dipaculao, Province of Quezon, is hereby constituted into a new barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Puangi.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
