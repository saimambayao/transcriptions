> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1730**
>
> **An Act to Amend a Certain Item in Section Three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item four, Group I, under aw. Tarlac, page six hundred forty-three, section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "4. Malacampa Elementary School Building, Camiling Repair and completion | ₱10,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
