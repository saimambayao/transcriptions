> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1911**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Sitio of Binalayan in the Municipality of Tarangnan, Province of Samar, into a Regular Barrio**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Binalayan in the Municipality of Tarangnan, Province of Samar, is hereby converted into a regular barrio to be called henceforth as the barrio of Binalayan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
