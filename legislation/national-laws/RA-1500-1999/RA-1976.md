> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1976**
>
> **An Act Amending Item (e), Under (17) Cebu, Subsection f, and Item Ten, Under Cebu, Subsection g, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred and Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (e), under (17) Cebu, group III, subsection f, section two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred and thirteen, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(e) Tabunoc Primary School building TalisayConstruction of one additional room | ₱6,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** Item ten, under Cebu, subsection g, section two of the same Act, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "10. Central-upland Elementary School Building, CarcarRepair, improvement and painting | 2,500.00 |
> | "10-a. Home Economics building, CarcarRepair, improvement and painting | 2,500.00" |
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
