> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1670**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Tudela, Municipality of Araceli, Province of Palawan, to Calandagan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio Tudela, Municipality of Araceli, Province of Palawan, is changed to barrio Calandagan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
