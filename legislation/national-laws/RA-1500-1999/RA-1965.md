> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1965**
>
> **An Act to Establish an Agricultural High School in the Municipality of Balanga, Province of Bataan, to be Known as the Bataan Agricultural High School, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the direct supervision of the Director of Public Schools, an agricultural high school in the Municipality of Balanga, Province of Bataan, at a suitable site to be selected by said Director, to be known as the Bataan Agricultural High School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** Upon the recommendation of the Director of Public Schools, the President shall set aside and reserve such portion of the public lands located within the Municipality of Balanga of said province, as may be necessary and convenient for the establishment of the said school and of its farm site.
>
> **Section 3.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of the said school during the fiscal year ending June thirty, nineteen hundred fifty-seven. Such sums as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
