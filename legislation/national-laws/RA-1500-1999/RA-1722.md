> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1722**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Campitic in the Municipality of Palo, Province of Leyte, into a Barrio to be Known as the Barrio of Campitic**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Campitic in the Municipality of Palo, Province of Leyte, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of Campitic.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
