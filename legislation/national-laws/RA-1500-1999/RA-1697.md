> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1697**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitios of Pinanhadsan and Km. 17, Municipality of Santa Fe, Province of Leyte, into Barrios of Said Municipality to be Known as the Barrios of Katipunan and Milagrosa, Respectively**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Pinanhadsan and Km. 17, Municipality of Santa Fe, Province of Leyte, are converted into barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Katipunan and Milagrosa, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
