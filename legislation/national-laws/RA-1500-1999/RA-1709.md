> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1709**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (d), Page One Hundred Twenty-Five, Section Two-43 (Quezon) of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (d), page one hundred twenty-five, section two-43 (Quezon) of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(d) Masalocot Barrio Road, CandelariaConstruction | ₱3,000.00 |
> | "(d-1) San Isidro Barrio School Building, CandelariaConstruction | 2,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
