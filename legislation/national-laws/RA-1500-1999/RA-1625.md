> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1625, June 20, 1957 ]**
>
> **AN ACT TO AMEND CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN REGARDING PUBLIC WORKS PROJECTS FOR THE PROVINCES OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL AND LANAO.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Project Item No. "D-351.1, Silay Water works", on pages four hundred four-A and four hundred five-A of Appendix A of  Republic Act Numbered One thousand four hundred eleven, is amended as follows:
>
> Under the column "Total estimated cost", change the figure "one hundred seventy" to "twenty five";
>
> Under the column "Total for five years", change the figure "one hundred fifty" to "five"; Under the column "nineteen hundred fifty-six to fifty-seven", change the figure "one hundred fifty" to "five";
>
> Under the column "Remarks", delete all and in lieu thereof put "Purchase and installation of engine for pumps".
>
> **Section 2.** Under paragraph g, section two of the same Act, Negros Occidental", add the following items  after "item Numbered  fifty-five (a):
>
> |  |  |  |  |  |
> | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
> | "55 | (b) | Municipal Building, Silay | ......................... | ₱35,000" |
> | "55 | (c) | Medical Center and Maternity and Children's  Hospital Building, Silay | ......................... | ₱90,000" |
> | "55 | (d) | Rizal Civic and Cultural Center including purchase  of equipment, Silay | ......................... | ₱10,000" |
> | "55 | (e) | Public Market Building, Sitio Hawaiian, Silay | ......................... | ₱10,000" |
>
> **Section 3.** As a consequence of the above amendments, the total for paragraph "a", *Title* D of section one, and the total for paragraph "g" of section two of Republic Act Numbered One thousand four hundred eleven are correspondingly adjusted.
>
> **Section 4.** Item twelve, page four hundred seventy-three under sub-paragraph "z. Lanao" of section three of Republic Act Numbered One thousand four hundred eleven is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |  |
> | --- | --- | --- |
> | "12. | Bacolod Grande Primary School Building, BacolodConstruction | ₱5,000,00" |
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 20, 1957.
