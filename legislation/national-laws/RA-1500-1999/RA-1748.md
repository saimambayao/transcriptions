> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1748**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Dibangan, Municipality of Taytay, Province of Palawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Island of Dibangan is constituted into a barrio in the Municipality of Taytay, Province of Palawan, to be known as the barrio of Dibangan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
