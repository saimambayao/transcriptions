> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1973**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Llavac in the Municipality of Infanta, Province of Quezon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Biga, Pandaruan, Bubuyog, Maragundong, Riao, Mahabang Salongan, Llavac, Calauang, Tipuan, Malangam and Tulay-bato in the Municipality of Infanta, Province of Quezon, are hereby constituted into a new barrio in the said municipality to be known as barrio Llavac.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
