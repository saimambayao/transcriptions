> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1728**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Bual in the Municipality of Pilar, Province of Sorsogon, to Del Rosario**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio Bual in the Municipality of Pilar, Province of Sorsogon, is hereby changed to Del Rosario.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
