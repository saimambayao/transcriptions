> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1676**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Kalangkang, Barrio of Kasily, Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of Marinduque, into a Barrio to be Known as the Barrio of Kalangkang of Said Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Kalangkang, barrio of Kasily, Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of Marinduque, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Kalangkang.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
