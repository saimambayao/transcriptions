> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1702**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrios of Abucayan Norte and Abucayan Sur, Municipality of Calape, Province of Bohol**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following barrios in the Municipality of Calape, Province of Bohol, are created:
>
> 1. Barrio Abucayan Norte, which shall constitute the sitios of Cabulihan and Cabinong; and
>
> 2. Barrio Abucayan Sur, which shall constitute the sitios of Rama and Bino.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
