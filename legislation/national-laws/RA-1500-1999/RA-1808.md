> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1808**
>
> **An Act to Grant a Life Pension to General Emilio Aguinaldo and for Other Purposes**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **WHEREAS,** by Act Numbered Two thousand nine hundred and twenty-two, approved on March 24, 1920, the Philippine Legislature granted a life pension of twelve thousand pesos a year, payable in monthly installments at the end of each month, to General Emilio Aguinaldo is recognition of his services to the cause of the Philippine Revolution;
>
> **WHEREAS,** for some reason or another, the said life pension granted him was stopped effective January first, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, by the express repeal of Act Numbered Two thousand nine hundred and twenty-two by Commonwealth Act Numbered Two hundred and eighty-eight;
>
> **WHEREAS,** the Filipino people owe a lasting debt of gratitude to General Aguinaldo for his services to the Philippines as the country's Revolutionary leader and as President of the First Philippine Republic; and
>
> **WHEREAS,** his need for a life pension has become urgent due to his old age and his consequential physical inability to engage in any gainful trade or occupation; Now, therefore:
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby granted to General Emilio Aguinaldo a life pension of twelve thousand pesos a year payable in equal monthly installments.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum necessary to pay said pension from the approval of this Act until and including the fiscal year nineteen hundred fifty-seven to nineteen hundred fifty-eight is hereby appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated. Therefore, the sum necessary therefor shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
