> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1704**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Mansalay, Province of Oriental Mindoro**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Sta. Brigida, Sta. Maria, Roma, Budburan, and Mahabangsapa, Municipality of Mansalay, Province of Oriental Mindoro, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Sta. Brigida, Sta. Maria, Roma, Budburan, and Mahabangsapa, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
