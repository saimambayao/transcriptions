> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1734**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Agtabo in the Barrio of Salngan, Municipality of Passi, Province of Iloilo, into a Barrio of Said Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Agtabo in the barrio of Salngan, Municipality of Passi, Province of Iloilo, is converted into a barrio of said municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
