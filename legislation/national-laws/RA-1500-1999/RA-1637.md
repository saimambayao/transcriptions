> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1637**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Fort Corcuera in the Municipality of Malabang, Province of Lanao, to Fort Jose Abad Santos**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Fort Corcuera in the Municipality of Malabang, Province of Lanao, is changed to Fort Jose Abad Santos.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
