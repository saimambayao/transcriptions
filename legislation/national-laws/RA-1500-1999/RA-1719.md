> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1719**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Tabango, Province of Leyte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Gibacungan, Catmon and Manlawa-an, Municipality of Tabango, Province of Leyte, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Gibacungan, Catmon and Manlawa-an, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
