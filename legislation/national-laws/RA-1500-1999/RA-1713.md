**An Act to Convert the Sitio of Bagbagin, Municipality of Caloocan, Province of Rizal, into a Barrio to be Known as Barrio Bagbagin of the Same Municipality and Separating it from the Barrio of Caybiga of the Same Municipality**

> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1713**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Sitio of Bagbagin, Municipality of Caloocan, Province of Rizal, into a Barrio to be Known as Barrio Bagbagin of the Same Municipality and Separating it from the Barrio of Caybiga of the Same Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Bagbagin in the Municipality of Caloocan, Province of Rizal, is hereby separated from the barrio of Caybiga and converted into a distinct barrio to be known as barrio Bagbagin of the same municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
