> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1943**
>
> **An Act to Amend Section Two Thousand One Hundred and Eighty-Seven of the Revised Administrative Code**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Paragraph one of section two thousand one hundred eighty-seven of the Revised Administrative Code is hereby amended so as to read as follows:
>
> > "Sec. 2187. *Compensation of vice-mayor and councilorsFull pay for sick mayor.* -The municipal council may grant to the vice-mayor and each councilor a per diem not to exceed ten pesos in the municipalities of the first class; eight pesos in municipalities of the second class; six pesos in municipalities of the third class; and four pesos in the fourth and fifth class municipalities, for each day of regular or special session of the council actually attended by them: *Provided, However*, That no per diems may be granted for more than four special sessions a month. When, by reason of absence, suspension, or other disability, the mayor ceases to discharge the duties of his office, the vice-mayor or councilor acting as mayor shall receive compensation equivalent to the salary of the mayor during the period of such service."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
