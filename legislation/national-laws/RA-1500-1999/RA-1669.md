> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1669**
>
> **An Act Dividing the Barrio of Talumpoc, Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, into Two Barrios to be Known as the Barrios of Talumpoc Silangan and Talumpoc Kanluran of Said Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrio of Talumpoc, Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, is divided into two barrios to be known as follows:
>
> > Barrio of Talumpoc Silangan, which shall be constituted by the sitios of Romano, Poyesan, Bondeo and Latag; and
> >
> > Barrio of Talumpoc Kanluran, which shall be constituted by the sitios of Ginto, Duhatan, Kulingkang, Piit and Cuaba.
> >
> > The boundaries of the two barrios shall be Bilogo Bridge at the north end, the barrio school building and the Bondoc ñg Sapangan at the center, and Munting Bondoc at the south end.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
