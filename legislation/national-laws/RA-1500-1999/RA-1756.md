> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1756**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Naawan River in the Municipality of Manticao, Province of Misamis Oriental, to Manticao River**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Naawan River in the Municipality of Manticao, Province of Misamis Oriental, is changed to Manticao River.
>
> **Section 2.** This act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
