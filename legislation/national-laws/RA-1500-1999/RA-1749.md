> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1749**
>
> **An Act to Amend a Certain Item in Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item J-045.4 under Romblon, paragraph d, title J, section five, Appendix A of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "J-045.3 Ambulong (Sibuyan Island) -Construction of causeway and landing: 130: 50: 50: 50 . . . 1 . . ."
>
> **Section 2.** This act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
