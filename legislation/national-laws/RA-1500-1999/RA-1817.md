> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1817**
>
> **An Act Appropriating the Sum of One Hundred Thousand Pesos as a Grant to Mrs. Luz B. Magsaysay**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, as a grant to Mrs. Luz B. Magsaysay, widow of the late President Ramon Magsaysay. This amount shall be tax exempt.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
