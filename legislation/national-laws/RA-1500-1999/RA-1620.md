> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1620**
>
> **An Act Creating the Municipality of Naawan, Province of Misamis Oriental**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrio of Naawan is separated from the Municipality of Initao, Province of Misamis Oriental, and constituted into an independent municipality, to be known as the Municipality of Naawan, Province of Misamis Oriental.
>

> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
