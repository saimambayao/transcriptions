> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1733**
>
> **An Act Changing the Names of Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Sara, Province of Iloilo**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The names of the following barrios in the Municipality of Sara, Province of Iloilo, are changed as follows:
>
> Barrio Ilongbukid to barrio Bagaygay;
>
> Barrio Maremhon to barrio Villahermosa;
>
> Barrio Lawa-an to barrio Gildore;
>
> Barrio Bai-ran to barrio Castor;
>
> Barrio Batuan to barrio Aldeguer;
>
> Barrio Agsinapot to barrio Del Castillo;
>
> Barrio Maligayligay to barrio Malapaya;
>
> Barrio Samponongbolo to barrio Juaneza;
>
> Barrio Alabidhan to barrio Tady;
>
> Barrio Ambolong to barrio Lanciola;
>
> Barrio Busay to barrio Preciosa;
>
> Barrio Caburra to barrio Zerrudo;
>
> Barrio Asue Pabriaga to barrio Pabriaga;
>
> Barrio Balabago to barrio Apologista;
>
> Barrio Alawehao to barrio Pesadas;
>
> Barrio Quinasop-an to barrio Muyco;
>
> Barrio Bolod to barrio Padios;
>
> Barrio Aglahog to barrio Bacabac;
>
> Barrio Madarag to barrio Crespo;
>
> Barrio Serruco to barrio Ferraris;
>
> Barrio Salvacion to barrio Aranto;
>
> Barrio Ajuz to barrio Salcedo;
>
> Barrio Talisay to barrio Apolo;
>
> Barrio Masonson to barrio Devera;
>
> Barrio Quipot to barrio Aguirre, and
>
> Barrio Damasco to barrio Aspera.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
