> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1661**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Igdacoton in the Barrio of Magdalena, Municipality of Anini-y, Province of Antique, into a Barrio to be Known as the Barrio of San Ramon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Igdacoton in the barrio of Magdalena, Municipality of Anini-y, Province of Antique, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of San Ramon.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
