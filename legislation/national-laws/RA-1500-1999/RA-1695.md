> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1695**
>
> **An Act Changing the Names of Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The names of the following barrios in the Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, are changed as follows:
>
> Barrio Patay to barrio Balagtas;
>
> Barrio Sambat Ilaya to barrio Kumintang Ilaya; and
>
> Barrio Sambat Ibaba to barrio Kumintang Ibaba.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
