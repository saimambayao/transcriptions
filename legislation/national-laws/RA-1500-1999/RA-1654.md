> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1654**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Jose Panganiban, Province of Catanduanes, to Payo**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Jose Panganiban, Province of Catanduanes, is changed to Payo.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
