> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1678**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Milad, Municipality of Polanco, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Dilawa, Desin, Lambog, Gumatob, Boboringan, Tubongon, Debolok, Tiaman, Guintom, Lower Disoy, and New Tipan, Municipality of Polanco, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of Milad of said municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
