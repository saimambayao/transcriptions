> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1758**

> **An Act to Amend Certain Items of Republic Acts Numbered Twelve Hundred, Fourteen Hundred Eleven and Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**

>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(39) Cainta public plaza, including purchase of site | ₱5,000.00" |
>

>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(c) Drilling artesian well in Ganutan Cardona | ₱1,000" |
>
> **Section 3.** Item (d), page one hundred twenty-nine of Republic of Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(d) Cainta public plaza, including purchase of site | ₱5,000.00" |
>
> **Section 4.** Item 52, page one hundred ninety-one of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "52. Acquisition and repair of Juan Sumulong Memorial High School building for use as annex of the Central Elementary School Taytay | ₱10,000" |
>
> **Section 5.** Item (3), page two hundred eighty-eight of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(3)-a Construction or improvement of San Joaquin-Calawan road, Pasig, Rizal | ₱10,000" |
> | "(3)-b Asphalt paving of A. Mabini street, Marikina, Rizal | 10,000" |
> | "(3)-c Improvement of Villalon street, Marikina, Rizal | 5,000" |
>

>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
