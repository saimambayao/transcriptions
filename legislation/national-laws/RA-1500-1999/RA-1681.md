> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1681**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered One Thousand Two Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (37), on page 18, paragraph 2a, title A, section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(37) Negros Occidental Provincial Hospital, Bacolod A-000.2a (76) |  |
> | (a) Construction of Kitchen for Contagious Diseases Pavilion | ₱15,000.00 |
> | (b) Construction of Laboratory Building | 15,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** The following item is hereby inserted on page 22, after item (62) (d), paragraph 2a, title A, section one of same Act:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(63) Negros Occidental Emergency Hospital Himamaylan A-000.2a (80) Construction | ₱25,000.00" |
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
