> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1747**
>
> **An Act to Amend a Certain Item in Republic Act Numbered One Thousand Four Hundred Eleven, Entitled "An Act Appropriating Funds for Public Works"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Republic Act Numbered One thousand four hundred eleven is hereby amended on the following particular:
>
> On page one hundred seventy-A, appendix A, item B-362.16, the words under Column "name and location of project" be changed to read as follows:
>
> > "Widening of the National Highway at Lacoblacob Point, Santa Catalina."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
