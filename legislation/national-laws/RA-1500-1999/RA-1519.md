> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1519**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Mesalipit in the Municipality of Bacolor, Province of Pampanga**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Mesalipit in the Municipality of Bacolor, Province of Pampanga, is hereby constituted into a barrio in the said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Mesalipit.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1956.
