> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1742**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Macrohon, Province of Leyte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following barrios are created in the Municipality of Macrohon, Province of Leyte, to be constituted as follows:
>
> Barrio of Ilihan shall be composed of the sitios of Ilihan Proper, Camalig, Sindangan, Gawisan, Guinabonan, Lunang, Mabanga and Danao;
>
> Barrio Villa Jacinta shall be composed of the sitios of Upper Villa Jacinta Proper, Peng-on, Mahayahay, Canodto, Laray, Luan, Santa Rosa and Camangahan; and
>
> Barrio Lower Villa Jacinta shall be composed of the sitios of Lower Villa Jacinta Proper, Suba, Central, Babayon, Muldes, Boayahan and Pancil.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
