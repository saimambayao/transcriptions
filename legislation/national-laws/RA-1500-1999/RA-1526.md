> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1526**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Calatan, Municipality of Taytay, Province of Palawan, into a Barrio to be Known as Barrio Sandoval**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Calatan in the Municipality of Taytay, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as Barrio Sandoval.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1956.
