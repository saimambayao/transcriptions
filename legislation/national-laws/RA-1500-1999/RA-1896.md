> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1896**
>
> **An Act to Establish, Organize and Maintain One National Agricultural School in the Province of Zamboanga del Norte and to Appropriate the Sum of Three Hundred Thousand Pesos for the Purpose**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Secretary of Education is hereby authorized to establish, organize and maintain one national agricultural school in the Province of Zamboanga del Norte of the Central Luzon Agricultural School type.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to locate and acquire or cause to be located and acquired a suitable site for the purpose of this Act, which may be public or private lands situated within the Province of Zamboanga del Norte.
>
> **Section 3.** The sum of three hundred thousand pesos, for its establishment and operation and maintenance during the fiscal year nineteen hundred and fifty-eight, is appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated. Thereafter, the necessary appropriation for its maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Act.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
