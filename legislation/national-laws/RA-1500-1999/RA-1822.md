> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1822**
>
> **An Act Reappropriating for Certain Purposes the Appropriation in Item (19), Page One Hundred Eighty-Three, Sub-Paragraph "ab," for the City of Manila, Paragraph 4, Title A, Section One of Republic Act Numbered Twelve Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of ten thousand pesos appropriated in item (19), page one hundred eighty-three, subparagraph "ab," for the City of Manila, paragraph 4, title A, section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is hereby reappropriated for the following purposes:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | (1) Sta. Clara Street Asphalting from Lamayan to Havana | ₱6,000.00 |
> | (2) Trece de Agosto Street Asphalting from Paz to Estero de Paco | 4,000.00 |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
