> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1663**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven, Regarding Public Works Projects for the Province of Masbate**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Items (f), (g), (h) and (i), paragraph thirty, page eighty-eight of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, for the Province of Masbate, are amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(f) Placer Municipal Building Construction | ₱5,000.00 |
> | "(g) Uson Municipal Building Construction | ₱5,000.00 |
> | "(h) Uson Municipal Building Construction | ₱5,000.00 |
> | "(i) Uson Municipal Building Construction | ₱5,000.00." |
>
> **Section 2.** Item seven, page two hundred ten of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, for the Province of Masbate, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | ""7. Municipal Building, Placer | ₱5,000.00" |
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
