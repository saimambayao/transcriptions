> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1671**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitios of Kanhaayon, Kapudlosan, Himokilan, Anolon, Mahilom, Baldoza and Tagbibi, Municipality of Hindang, Province of Leyte, into Barrios of Said Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Kanhaayon, Kapudlosan, Himokilan, Anolon, Mahilom, Baldoza, and Tagbibi in the Municipality of Hindang, Province of Leyte, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Kanhaayon, Kapudlosan, Himokilan, Anolon, Mahilom, Baldoza and Tagbibi, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
