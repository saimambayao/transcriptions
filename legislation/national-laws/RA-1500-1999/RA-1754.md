> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1754**
>
> **An Act Amending Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered One Thousand Six Hundred Thirteen, Entitled "An Act Appropriating Funds for Public Works"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item sixteen, Appendix B, School Buildings, page seventy-B, for the Province of Negros Oriental, second congressional district, of Republic Act Numbered One thousand six hundred thirteen is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "16. Siquijor - Cang-alwangAid to PTA for reconstruction and construction | ₱5,000.00 |
> | "16(a) Siquijor - PoblacionRepairs and fencing of the Siquijor Central Primary School | 2,000.00 |
> | "16(b) Siquijor - CantabonAid to PTA school building | 1,000.00 |
> | "16(c) Siquijor - PanlautanAid to PTA school building | 1,000.00 |
> | "16(d) Siquijor - PulangyutaFor fencing and ground improvement, Siquijor Elementary school | 1,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957.
