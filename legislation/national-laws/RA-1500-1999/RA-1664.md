> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1664**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Paghaluban, Municipality of Barcelona, Province of Sorsogon, to Peña Francia**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Paghaluban, Municipality of Barcelona, Province of Sorsogon, is changed to Peña Francia.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
