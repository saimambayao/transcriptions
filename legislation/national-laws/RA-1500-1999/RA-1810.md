> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1810**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is hereby amended in the following particulars:
>
> 1. Item (b), paragraph twenty-five, Laguna I, subsection f, section two, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(b) Linga Barrio School, Calamba Construction | ₱2,500.00" |
>
> 2. Item 12, subsection y, Laguna I, section three, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "12. Sala Barrio School Building, Cabuyao Construction | 3,500.00" |
>
> 3. On page 170-A, Appendix A, Item B-362.16, the words under column "Name and Location of Project," shall read as follows:
>
> "B-362.16Dumaguete South Road-widening at Lacob-lacob Point, Santa Catalina"
>
> **Section 2.** If any item of appropriation amended by this Act has already been partially expended, the amendment to said item shall refer only to the unexpended balance thereof.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
