> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1655**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of San Roque in the Municipality of Marabut, Province of Samar**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Cabugao in the Municipality of Marabut, Province of Samar, is hereby constituted into a barrio of the said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Roque.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1957.
