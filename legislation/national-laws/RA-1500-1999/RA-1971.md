> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1971**
>
> **An Act Converting the Bambang High School in the Municipality of Bambang, Province of Nueva Vizcaya, into a National School of Arts and Trades, to be Known as the Nueva Vizcaya School of Arts and Trades, and Appropriating the Necessary Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Bambang High School in the Municipality of Bambang, Province of Nueva Vizcaya, is hereby converted into a national school of arts and trades, to be known as the Nueva Vizcaya School of Arts and Trades.
>
> The said school shall be under the supervision of the Bureau of Public Schools and subject to the provisions of the School Law. Courses of collegiate and secondary levels shall be offered in the said school.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred fifty-eight. Thereafter, the necessary funds for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 22, 1957.
