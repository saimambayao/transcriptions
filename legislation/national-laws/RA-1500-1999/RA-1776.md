> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1776**
>
> **An Act Amending Item Numbered Eighty-Six, Under "Camarines Sur II," Appendix B of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item numbered eighty-six, under "Camarines Sur II," appendix B of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "86. Tandoc, Siruma Construction of one room | ₱4,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

> Approved: June 21, 1957.
