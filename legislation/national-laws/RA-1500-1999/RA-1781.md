> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1781**
>
> AN ACT AMENDING ITEM (d) GROUP II, PARAGRAPH (43), SUBSECTION (f) OF SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN, ENTITLED "AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR PUBLIC WORKS."
>
> **Section 1.** Item (d), group II, paragraph (43) Quezon Province, subsection (f) of section two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(d) Lavides Bridge, Atimonan Completion and construction of protection works for the approaches of the bridge and the adjacent landing P20,000.00."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 21, 1957
