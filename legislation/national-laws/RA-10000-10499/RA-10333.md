# Republic Act No. 10333

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ROMBLON NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL-ODIONGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ODIONGAN, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON TO ODIONGAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> Fifteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-three day of July, two thousand twelve.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10333, November 15, 2012 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ROMBLON NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL-ODIONGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ODIONGAN, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON TO ODIONGAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** *Renaming of a National High School.*  The name of Romblon National High School - Odiongan in the Municipality of Odiongan, Province of Romblon is hereby changed to Odiongan National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** *Location of the Main Campus.* - The main campus of the Odiongan National High School shall continue to remain in its present location in the Municipality of Odiongan, Province of Romblon.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** *Effectivity.* - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
