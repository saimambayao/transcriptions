# Republic Act No. 10079

**AN ACT CONVERTING GIL FERNANDO AVENUE IN BARANGAYS STO. NIñO AND SAN ROQUE, CITY OF MARIKINA INTO A NATIONAL ROAD**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 10079
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2010/ra_10079_2010.html)

---

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10079**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING GIL FERNANDO AVENUE IN BARANGAYS STO. NIñO AND SAN ROQUE, CITY OF MARIKINA INTO A NATIONAL ROAD
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Gil Fernando Avenue in barangays Sto. Niño and San Roque, City of Marikina is hereby converted into a national road.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
