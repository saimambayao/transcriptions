# Republic Act No. 10162

**AN ACT CREATING TWO (2) ADDITIONAL REGIONAL TRIAL COURT BRANCHES IN THE PROVINCE OF LAGUNA TO BE STATIONED AT STA. ROSA CITY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 14, PARAGRAPH (E) OF BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 129, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE JUDICIARY REORGANIZATION ACT OF 1980, AS AMENDED, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Fifteenth Congress
>
> Second Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, two thousand eleven.*

> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10162       April 17, 2012**
>
> AN ACT CREATING TWO (2) ADDITIONAL REGIONAL TRIAL COURT BRANCHES IN THE PROVINCE OF LAGUNA TO BE STATIONED AT STA. ROSA CITY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 14, PARAGRAPH (E) OF BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 129, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE JUDICIARY REORGANIZATION ACT OF 1980", AS AMENDED, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Two (2) additional branches of the Regional Trial Court to be stationed at Sta. Rosa City, Province of Laguna are hereby created in the Fourth Judicial Region.
>
> The Supreme Court shall assign the branch number of the newly-created Regional Trial Court branches in the Fourt Judicial Region.
>
> **Section 2.** The Chief Justice of the Supreme Court, in coordination with the Secretary of the Department of Justice, shall immediately include in the Court's program the operationalization of the two (2) additional Regional Trial Court branches to be stationed at Sta. Rosa City, Province of Laguna, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** Section 14(e) of Batas Pambansa Blg. 129, otherwise known as "The Judiciary Reorganization Act of 1980", as amended, and all other laws, rules and regulations which are inconsistent with this Act are hereby amended, repealed or modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its complete publication in the *Official Gazetter* or in two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
>
