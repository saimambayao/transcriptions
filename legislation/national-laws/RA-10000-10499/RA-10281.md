# Republic Act No. 10281

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BIGNAY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BIGNAY, CITY OF VALENZUELA TO ROBERTA DE JESUS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 10281
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2012/ra_10281_2012.html)

---

> Fifteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-three day of July, two thousand twelve.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10281**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BIGNAY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BIGNAY, CITY OF VALENZUELA TO ROBERTA DE JESUS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Renaming of an Elementary School. * The name of Bignay Elementary School in Barangay Bignay, City of Valenzuela is hereby changed to Roberta de Jesus Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** *Location of the Main Campus. * The main campus of Roberta de Jesus Elementary School shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Bignay, City of Valenzuela.
>
> **Section 3.** *Effectivity. * This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette.*
>
