# Republic Act No. 10076

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE ROAD WHICH STARTS FROM THE ILIGAN - MARAWI NATIONAL HIGHWAY AT BALOI AND TRAVERSES MATUNGGAO TO THE ILIGAN - AURORA HIGHWAY AT LINAMON, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE INTO A NATIONAL ROAD**

## Metadata

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10076**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE ROAD WHICH STARTS FROM THE ILIGAN - MARAWI NATIONAL HIGHWAY AT BALOI AND TRAVERSES MATUNGGAO TO THE ILIGAN - AURORA HIGHWAY AT LINAMON, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE INTO A NATIONAL ROAD
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The road which starts from the Iligan - Marawi National Highway at Baloi and traverses Matunggao to the Iligan - Aurora Highway at Linamon, Province of Lanao del Norte is hereby converted into a national road.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
