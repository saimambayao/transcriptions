# Republic Act No. 10148

**AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO MARCUS EUGENE DOUTHIT**

> Fifteenth Congress
>
> First Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, two thousand ten.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10148**
>
> AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO MARCUS EUGENE DOUTHIT
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Marcus Eugene Douthit is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives appurtenant thereto.
>
> **Section 2.** Upon taking and registration of the oath of allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines, Marcus Eugene Douthit shall enter upon the full enjoyment of Philippine citizenship.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
