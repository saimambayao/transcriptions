# Republic Act No. 10074

**AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CONVERSION OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) EXTENSION OFFICE IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA INTO A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE TO BE KNOWN AS THE LTO DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10074**
>
> AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CONVERSION OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) EXTENSION OFFICE IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA INTO A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE TO BE KNOWN AS THE LTO DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Land Transportation Office (LTO) Extension Office in the City of Calamba, Province of Laguna is hereby converted into a regular district office to be known as the LTO District Office.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Transportation and Communications shall issue the necessary rules and regulations to implement the said conversion.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount needed for the operationalization of a regular LTO District Office in the City of Calamba, Province of Laguna shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
