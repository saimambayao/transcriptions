# Republic Act No. 10280

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ORION NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BALAGTAS, MUNICIPALITY OF ORION, PROVINCE OF BATAAN TO JUSTICE EMILIO ANGELES GANCAYCO MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 10280
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2012/ra_10280_2012.html)

> Fifteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-three day of July, two thousand twelve.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10280**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ORION NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BALAGTAS, MUNICIPALITY OF ORION, PROVINCE OF BATAAN TO JUSTICE EMILIO ANGELES GANCAYCO MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Renaming of a National High School.*  The name of Orion National High School in Barangay Balagtas, Municipality of Orion, Province of Bataan is hereby changed to Justice Emilio Angeles Gancayco Memorial High School.
>
> **Section 2.** *Location of the Main Campus.*  The main campus of the Justice Emilio Angeles Gancayco Memorial High School shall remain in its present location in Barangay Balagtas, Municipality of Orion, Province of Bataan.
>
> **Section 3.** *Effectivity.*  This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette.*
>
