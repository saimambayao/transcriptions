# Republic Act No. 10090

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TWENTY-FIVE (25)-BED CAPACITY MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGUINALDO, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO TO BE KNOWN AS THE AGUINALDO MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10090**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TWENTY-FIVE (25)-BED CAPACITY MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGUINALDO, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO TO BE KNOWN AS THE AGUINALDO MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a twenty-five (25)-bed capacity municipal hospital in the Municipality of Aguinaldo, Province of Ifugao to be known as the Aguinaldo Municipal Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Health, in coordination with the local government officials concerned, shall promulgate and issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to implement the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The Department of Health shall provide subsidy to the Municipality of Aguinaldo for the initial establishment of the municipal hospital. Thereafter, such funds necessary for the maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be provided in accordance with the provisions of the Local Government Code and placed under the jurisdiction of the Municipality of Aguinaldo, Province of Ifugao.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
