# Republic Act No. 10052

**AN ACT RENAMING REAL STREET IN DUMAGUETE CITY AS MAYOR RAMON T. PASTOR SR. STREET**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 10052
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2010/ra_10052_2010.html)

---

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10052**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING REAL STREET IN DUMAGUETE CITY AS MAYOR RAMON T. PASTOR SR. STREET
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Real Street in Dumaguete City shall hereinafter be known as Mayor Ramon T. Pastor Sr. Street.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
