# Republic Act No. 10053

**AN ACT RENAMING THE BINAKAYAN DIVERSION ROAD IN KAWIT, PROVINCE OF CAVITE AS THE LABANAN SA BINAKAYAN ROAD**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 10053
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2010/ra_10053_2010.html)

---

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10053**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE BINAKAYAN DIVERSION ROAD IN KAWIT, PROVINCE OF CAVITE AS THE LABANAN SA BINAKAYAN ROAD
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Binakayan Diversion Road in Kawit, Province of Cavite shall hereinafter be known as The Labanan sa Binakayan Road.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
