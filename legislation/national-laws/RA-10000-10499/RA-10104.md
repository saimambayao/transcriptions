# Republic Act No. 10104

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE INTERIOR CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD OF LANAO DEL NORTE, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE INTO A NATIONAL ROAD**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 10104
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2010/ra_10104_2010.html)

---

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10104**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE INTERIOR CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD OF LANAO DEL NORTE, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE INTO A NATIONAL ROAD
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The interior circumferential road which starts from the Iligan - Aurora National Highway at Iligan and traverses Tagoloan, Baloi, Pantao - Ragat, Munai, Tangkal, Magsaysay, Tubod, Salvador, Sapad, Nunungan and ends at Malabang - Tukuran National Highway at Sultan Naga Dimaporo, Province of Lanao del Norte is hereby converted into a national road.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
