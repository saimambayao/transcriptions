# Republic Act No. 10283

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAGAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAGAY, PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN TO EULALIO U. PABILLORE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

> Fifteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-three day of July, two thousand twelve.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10283**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAGAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAGAY, PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN TO EULALIO U. PABILLORE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Renaming of a National High School.*  The name of Sagay National High School in the Municipality of Sagay, Province of Camiguin is hereby changed to Eulalio U. Pabillore National High School: *Provided,* That all official communications and signages shall still carry the previous name of the school placed after its new name for better identification of the school.
>
> **Section 2.** *Location of the Main Campus.*  The main campus of the Eulalio U. Pabillore National High School shall continue to remain in its present location in the Municipality of Sagay, Province of Camiguin.
>
> **Section 3.** *Effectivity.*  This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette.*
>
