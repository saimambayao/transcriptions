# Republic Act No. 10227

**AN ACT RENAMING THE APARRI SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADE (ASAT) IN APARRI, CAGAYAN INTO THE APARRI POLYTECHNIC INSTITUTE (API)**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 10227
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2012/ra_10227_2012.html)

---

> Fifteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-three day of July, two thousand twelve.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10227**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE APARRI SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADE (ASAT) IN APARRI, CAGAYAN INTO THE APARRI POLYTECHNIC INSTITUTE (API)
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Renaming of School.*  The name of the Aparri School of Arts and Trade (ASAT) in the Municipality of Aparri, Province of Cagayan is hereby changed to the Aparri Polytechnic Institute (API).
>
> **Section 2.** *Effectivity.*  This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette.*
>
