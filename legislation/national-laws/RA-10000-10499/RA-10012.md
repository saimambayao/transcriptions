# Republic Act No. 10012

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE EASTERN BOBONGAN - SOMINOT - MIDSALIP - DUMINGAG PROVINCIAL ROAD TRAVERSING THE MUNICIPALITIES OF RAMON MAGSAYSAY, SOMINOT, MIDSALIP AND DUMINGAG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR INTO A NATIONAL ROAD**

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10012**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE EASTERN BOBONGAN - SOMINOT - MIDSALIP - DUMINGAG PROVINCIAL ROAD TRAVERSING THE MUNICIPALITIES OF RAMON MAGSAYSAY, SOMINOT, MIDSALIP AND DUMINGAG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR INTO A NATIONAL ROAD
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Eastern Bobongan - Sominot - Midsalip - Dumingag Provincial Road traversing the municipalities of Ramon Magsaysay, Sominot, Midsalip and Dumingag, Province of Zamboanga del Sur is hereby converted into a national road.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
