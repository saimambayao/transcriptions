# Republic Act No. 9345

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE PANGLAO ISLAND CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF PANGLAO AND DAUIS, INCLUDING THE AMBASSADOR PABLO SUAREZ BRIDGE IN THE SITIO UBOS-TOTULAN, TAGBILARAN CITY, PROVINCE OF BOHOL INTO A NATIONAL ROAD**

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Second Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, two thousand five.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9345             June 5, 2006**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE PANGLAO ISLAND CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF PANGLAO AND DAUIS, INCLUDING THE AMBASSADOR PABLO SUAREZ BRIDGE IN THE SITIO UBOS-TOTULAN, TAGBILARAN CITY, PROVINCE OF BOHOL INTO A NATIONAL ROAD**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** The Panglao Island Circumferential Road in the Municipalities of Panglao and Dauis, including the Ambassador Pablo Suarez Bridge in the Sitio Ubos-Totulan, Tagbilaran City, Province of Bohol, consisting of 37 kilometers, more or less, is hereby converted into a national road.
>
> **SEC. 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.
>
> **SEC. 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
