# Republic Act No. 9291

**AN ACT UPGRADING THE ZAMBALES SUBDISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE INTO A REGULAR ENGINEERING OFICE TO BE KNOWN AS THE ZAMBALEZ SECOND DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eight day of July, two thousand and three.*

> **Republic Act No. 9291             April 16, 2004 (lapsed)**
>
> **AN ACT UPGRADING THE ZAMBALES SUBDISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE INTO A REGULAR ENGINEERING OFICE TO BE KNOWN AS THE ZAMBALEZ SECOND DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** The Zambales Subdistrict Engineering Office is hereby upgraded into a regular engineering district office. It shall be known as the Zambales Second Engineering District Subsequently, the existing engineering district office located at Iba, Zambales shall be known as the Zambales First Engineering District.
>
> **SEC. 2.** The Zambales Second Engineering District shall assume jurisdiction over infrastructure and public works in all barangays of the First Congressional District of Zambales.
>
> **SEC. 3.** The Secretary of the Department of Public Works and Highways shall include in its program the funding requirements for operationalization of the Zambales Second Engineering District in the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **SEC. 4.** This act shall take effect upon its approval.
