# Republic Act No. 9193

**AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 7 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9193
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2003/ra_9193_2003.html)

> **Twelfth Congress
> Second Regular Session**
>
>
>
> Begun held in Metro Manila on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, two thousand two
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9193 March 06, 2003**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 7 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** It is hereby declared that March 7 of every year be a special working public holiday in the City of Tagum to enable its residents to fittingly commemorate and celebrate its foundation day.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
