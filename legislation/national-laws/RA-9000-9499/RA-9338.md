# Republic Act No. 9338

**AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO MAHMOUD A. M. ASFOUR**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9338
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2005/ra_9338_2005.html)

---

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> First Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, two thousand and four.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9338**
>
> **AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO MAHMOUD A. M. ASFOUR**
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, two thousand four.*:
>
> **SECTION 1.** Mahmoud A. M. Asfour is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives appurtenant thereto.
>
> After the taking and registration of the oath of allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines, Mahmoud A. M. Asfour shall enter into the full enjoyment of Philippine citizenship.
>
> **SECTION 2.** this Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following its publication in the Official Gazette or in at least two national newspaper circulation.
>
> Approved,
