# Republic Act No. 9126

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PANGAO-AN, MUNICIPALITY OF MAGPET, PROVINCE OF COTABATO TO BE KNOWN AS PANGAO-AN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9126
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2001/ra_9126_2001.html)

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9126       April 24, 2001**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PANGAO-AN, MUNICIPALITY OF MAGPET, PROVINCE OF COTABATO TO BE KNOWN AS PANGAO-AN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby created a new national high school in Barangay Pangao-an, Municipality of Magpet, Province of Cotabato to be known as Pangao-an National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
