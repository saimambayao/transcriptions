# Republic Act No. 9453

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF POTIA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PINTO, MUNICIPALITY OF ALFONSO LISTA, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO TO PINTO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9453
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2007/ra_9453_2007.html)

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Special Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9453             May 15, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF POTIA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PINTO, MUNICIPALITY OF ALFONSO LISTA, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO TO PINTO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1. *Separation and Conversion into a National High School.*** - The name of Potia National High School in Barangay Pinto, Municipality of Alfonso Lista, Province of Ifugao is hereby changed to Pinto National High School.
>
> **SEC. 2. *Effectivity.*** - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette.*
>
