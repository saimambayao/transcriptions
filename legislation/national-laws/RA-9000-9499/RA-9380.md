# Republic Act No. 9380

**AN ACT DECLARING MAY 6 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILAR, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON TO COMMEMORATE THE DEATH ANNIVERSARY OF MAYOR MANUEL T. SIA**

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand six.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9380             March 8, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING MAY 6 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILAR, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON TO COMMEMORATE THE DEATH ANNIVERSARY OF MAYOR MANUEL T. SIA**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** To commemorate the death anniversary of Mayor Manuel T. Sia, Municipal Mayor of the Municipality of Pilar, Province of Sorsogon, May 6 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the said municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
