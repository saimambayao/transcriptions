# Republic Act No. 9332

**AN ACT NAMING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE (PNP) PROVINCIAL OFFICE IN THE CITY OF MAASIN, PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE AS CAMP GOVERNOR ALFREDO KANGLEON BANTUG**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9332
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2004/ra_9332_2004.html)

> Twelfth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eight day of July, two thousand and three.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9332**
>
> **AN ACT NAMING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE (PNP) PROVINCIAL OFFICE IN THE CITY OF MAASIN, PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE AS CAMP GOVERNOR ALFREDO KANGLEON BANTUG**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> Section 1. The Philippine National Police (PNP) Provincial Office in the City of Maasin, Province of Southern Leyte is hereby named Camp Governor Alfredo Kangleon Bantug.
>
> Section 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
>
