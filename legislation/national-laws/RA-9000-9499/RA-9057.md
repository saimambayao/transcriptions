# Republic Act No. 9057

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY DURIAN, MUNICIPALITY OF LAS NIEVES, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL NORTE TO BE KNOWN AS DURIAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

## Metadata

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9057       April 04, 2001**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY DURIAN, MUNICIPALITY OF LAS NIEVES, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL NORTE TO BE KNOWN AS DURIAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a national high school in Barangay Durian, Municipality of Las Nieves, Province of Agusan del Norte to be known as the Durian National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of the Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
