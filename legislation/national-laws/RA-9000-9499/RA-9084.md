# Republic Act No. 9084

**AN ACT TO ESTABLISH THE UMIRAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAYUMIRAYIN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGALAN IN THE PROVINCE OF AURORA AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9084       April 08, 2001**
>
> AN ACT TO ESTABLISH THE UMIRAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAYUMIRAYIN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGALAN IN THE PROVINCE OF AURORA AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established a national high school in Barangay Umiray in the Municipality of Dingalan, Province of Aurora to be known as the Umiray National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon approval thereof.
>
