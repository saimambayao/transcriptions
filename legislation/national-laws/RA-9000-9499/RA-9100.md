# Republic Act No. 9100

**AN ACT NAMING A NATIONAL ROAD FROM THE MARIKINA SPORTS CENTER IN THE CITY OF MARIKINA UP TO OLIVEROS STREET/CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD IN THE CITY OF ANTIPOLO AS THE DON JUAN SUMULONG AVENUE**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9100       April 08, 2001**
>
> AN ACT NAMING A NATIONAL ROAD FROM THE MARIKINA SPORTS CENTER IN THE CITY OF MARIKINA UP TO OLIVEROS STREET/CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD IN THE CITY OF ANTIPOLO AS THE DON JUAN SUMULONG AVENUE
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The national road stretching from the Marikina Sports Center in the City of Marikina up to Oliveros Street, Circumferential Road in the City of Antipolo shall be known as the Don Juan Sumulong Avenue.
>
> **Section 2.** Any law, ordinance, administrative order, designation or identification which is inconsistent with this Act shall be deemed repealed or modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 8, 2001.
