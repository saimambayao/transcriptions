# Republic Act No. 9210

**AN ACT ENTITLED AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 18 OF EACH YEAR AS NAGA CITY CHARTER DAY AND SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF NAGA IN ORDER TO COMMEMORATE THE CHARTER ANNIVERSARY OF THE SAID CITY**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9210
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2003/ra_9210_2003.html)

> **Twelfth Congress
> Second Regular Session**
>
>
>
> Begun held in Metro Manila on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, two thousand two
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9210 June 11, 2003**
>
> AN ACT ENTITLED "AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 18 OF EACH YEAR AS NAGA CITY CHARTER DAY AND SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF NAGA IN ORDER TO COMMEMORATE THE CHARTER ANNIVERSARY OF THE SAID CITY". THEREBY REPEALING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9089, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In order to communicate the charter anniversary of the City of Naga, June 18 each year is hereby declared as Naga City Charter Day and a special nonworking holiday in the said city.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall repeal Republic Act No. 9089.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
