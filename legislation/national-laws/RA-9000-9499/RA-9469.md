# Republic Act No. 9469

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BANGUI SCHOOL OF FISHERIES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANGUI, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE TO BANGUI INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9469
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2007/ra_9469_2007.html)

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Special Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9469             May 15, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BANGUI SCHOOL OF FISHERIES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANGUI, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE TO BANGUI INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1. *Renaming of School.*** - The name of Bangui School of Fisheries in the Municipality of Bangui, Province of Ilocos Norte is hereby changed to Bangui Institute of Technology.
>
> **Sec. 2. *Repealing Clause.*** - All laws, presidential decrees, executive orders, rules and regulations contrary to or inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.
>
> **Sec. 3. *Effectivity.*** - This Act shall take effect upon its approval and after its publication in at least two newspapers of general circulation.
>
