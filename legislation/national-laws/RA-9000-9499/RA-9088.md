# Republic Act No. 9088

**AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 17 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALAMBAN, PROVINCE OF CEBU TO COMMEMORATE THE DEATH ANNIVERSARY OF PRESIDENT RAMON B. MAGSAYSAY, SR.**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9088       April 08, 2001**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 17 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALAMBAN, PROVINCE OF CEBU TO COMMEMORATE THE DEATH ANNIVERSARY OF PRESIDENT RAMON B. MAGSAYSAY, SR.
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In commemoration of the death anniversary of President Ramon B. Magsaysay Sr. in Mount Manunggal, Balamban, Cebu, March 17 of every year is hereby declared as a special working holiday in the Municipality of Balamban, Province of Cebu.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
