# Republic Act No. 9426

**AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 8 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE ENTIRE PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY TO COMMEMORATE ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9426
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2007/ra_9426_2007.html)

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand six.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9426             April 10, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 8 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE ENTIRE PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY TO COMMEMORATE ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** To commemorate its founding anniversary as a province, March 8 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the entire Province of Compostela Valley.
>
> **SEC. 2.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.
>
