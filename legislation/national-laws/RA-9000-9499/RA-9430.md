**AN ACT DECLARING JULY 23 OF EACH YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF TANAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS TO BE KNOWN AS**

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand six.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9430             April 10, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING JULY 23 OF EACH YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF TANAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS TO BE KNOWN AS "APOLINARIO MABINI DAY"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** To commemorate the birth anniversary of Apolinario Mabini, national hero and illustrious son of the City of Tanauan, Province of Batangas, July 23 of each year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the said city to be known as "Apolinario Mabini Day".
>
> **SEC. 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
