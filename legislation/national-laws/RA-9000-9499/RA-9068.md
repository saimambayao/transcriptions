# Republic Act No. 9068

**AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 5 OF EVERY YEAR AS REMEMBRANCE DAY IN ORMOC CITY, A REGULAR WORKING HOLIDAY IN ORMOC CITY, TO COMMEMORATE THE NOVEMBER 5,1991 TRAGEDY**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9068       April 08, 2001**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 5 OF EVERY YEAR AS "REMEMBRANCE DAY IN ORMOC CITY," A REGULAR WORKING HOLIDAY IN ORMOC CITY, TO COMMEMORATE THE NOVEMBER 5,1991 TRAGEDY
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** November 5 of every year is hereby declared as "Remembrance Day in Ormoc City," a regular working holiday for the City of Ormoc, Province of Leyte.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in at least two (2) national newspapers of general circulation.
>
