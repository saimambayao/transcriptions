# Republic Act No. 9249

**AN ACT RENAMING THE BULACAN NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL STATE COLLEGE (BNASC) IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ILDEFENSO, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, AS THE BULACAN AGRICULTURAL STATE COLLEGE (BASC)**

> Twelfth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eight day of July, two thousand and three.*
>
> **Republic Act No. 9249             February 19 2004**
>
> **AN ACT RENAMING THE BULACAN NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL STATE COLLEGE (BNASC) IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ILDEFENSO, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, AS THE BULACAN AGRICULTURAL STATE COLLEGE (BASC)**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** The Bulacan National Agricultural State College (BNASC) in the Municipality of San Ildenfonso, Province of Bulacan, is hereby renamed as the Bulacan Agricultural State College (BASC)
>
> **SEC. 2.** All laws or decrees inconsistent with the provision of this Act are hereby deemed amended accordingly.
>
> **SEC. 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
