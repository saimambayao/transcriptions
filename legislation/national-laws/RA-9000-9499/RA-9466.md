# Republic Act No. 9466

**AN ACT RENAMING CALIFORNIA AVENUE IN THE CITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION AS MAYOR LORENZO L. DACANAY AVENUE**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9466
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2007/ra_9466_2007.html)

---

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Special Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9466             May 15, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT RENAMING CALIFORNIA AVENUE IN THE CITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION AS MAYOR LORENZO L. DACANAY AVENUE**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** California Avenue, a national secondary road in the City of San Fernando, Province of La Union is hereby renamed as Mayor Lorenzo L. Dacanay Avenue.
>
> **Sec. 2.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
