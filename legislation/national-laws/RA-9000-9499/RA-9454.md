# Republic Act No. 9454

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SALVACION NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY TALOJONGON, MUNICIPALITY OF TIGAON, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR TO THE DR. RODOLFO V. PAMOR, JR. MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9454
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2007/ra_9454_2007.html)

---

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Special Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9454             May 15, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SALVACION NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY TALOJONGON, MUNICIPALITY OF TIGAON, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR TO THE DR. RODOLFO V. PAMOR, JR. MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1. *Renaming of National High School.*** - The Salvacion National High School in Barangay Talojongon, Municipality of Tigaon, Province of Camarines Sur is hereby changed to Dr. Rodolfo V. Pamor, Jr. Memorial National High School.
>
> **SEC. 2. *Effectivity.*** - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette.*
>
