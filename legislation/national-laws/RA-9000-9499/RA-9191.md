# Republic Act No. 9191

**AN ACT DECLARING THE DAY OF MARCH OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MUNTINLUPA TO BE KNOWN AS THE MUNTINLUPA CITY CHARTER DAY**

> **Twelfth Congress
> Second Regular Session**
>
>
>
> Begun held in Metro Manila on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, two thousand two
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9191 February 21, 2003**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING THE DAY OF MARCH OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MUNTINLUPA TO BE KNOWN AS THE MUNTINLUPA CITY CHARTER DAY
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** March 1 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the City of Muntinlupa to be known as "Muntinlupa City of Charter Day" in order to commemorate the Charter Anniversary of the said city.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.
