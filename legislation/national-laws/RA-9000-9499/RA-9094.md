# Republic Act No. 9094

**AN ACT DECLARING THE SEVENTH (7TH) DAY OF JANUARY OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY FOR THE PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN, TO BE KNOWN AS ARAW NG CAMIGUIN**

## Metadata

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9094       April 08, 2001**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING THE SEVENTH (7TH) DAY OF JANUARY OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY FOR THE PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN, TO BE KNOWN AS "ARAW NG CAMIGUIN"
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** January seven of every year is hereby declared as a special working public holiday in the Province of Camiguin to commemorate its charter day anniversary.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
