# Republic Act No. 9464

**AN ACT RENAMING THE BACOLOD - MURCIA - SAN CARLOS ROAD (BACOLOD SECTION) IN THE CITY OF BACOLOD TO ROMEO G. GUANZON AVENUE**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9464

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Special Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9464             May 15, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT RENAMING THE BACOLOD - MURCIA - SAN CARLOS ROAD (BACOLOD SECTION) IN THE CITY OF BACOLOD TO ROMEO G. GUANZON AVENUE**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The 9.62 kilometer stretch comprising the Bacolod Section of the Bacolod - Murcia - San Carlos Road from the junction of Araneta Street to the boundary of Bacolod City and the Municipality of Murcia in Negros Occidental, is hereby renamed as the Romeo G. Guanzon Avenue.
>
> **Sec. 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
