# Republic Act No. 9489

**AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO MR. JOSE R. RODRIGUEZ**

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Special Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9489             June 27, 2007**
>
> AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO MR. JOSE R. RODRIGUEZ
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Jose R. Rodriguez is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives appurtenant thereto.
>
> Upon the taking and registration of the oath of allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines, Jose R. Rodriguez shall enters upon the full enjoyment of Philippine citizenship.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
