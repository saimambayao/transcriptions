# Republic Act No. 9425

**AN ACT DECLARING DECEMBER 13 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN GENERAL TRIAS IN THE PROVINCE OF CAVITE**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9425
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2007/ra_9425_2007.html)

---

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand six.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9425             April 10, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING DECEMBER 13 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN GENERAL TRIAS IN THE PROVINCE OF CAVITE**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** December 13 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the Municipality of General Trias in the Province of Cavite to commemorate its founding day.
>
> **SEC. 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
