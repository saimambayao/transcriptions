# Republic Act No. 9033

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE SUB-DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE LOCATED IN NAGUILIAN, LA UNION INTO A REGULAR ENGINEERING DISTRICT TO BE KNOWN AS THE LA UNION SECOND ENGINEERING DISTRICT AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9033       March 12, 2001**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE SUB-DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE LOCATED IN NAGUILIAN, LA UNION INTO A REGULAR ENGINEERING DISTRICT TO BE KNOWN AS THE LA UNION SECOND ENGINEERING DISTRICT AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Sub-District Engineering Office located in Naguilian, La Union, under the supervision of the Department of Public Works and Highways, shall be converted into a regular engineering district to be known as the La Union Second Engineering District.
>
> **Section 2.** The La Union Second Engineering District shall comprise the municipalities of Agoo, Aringay, Bagulin, Bauang, Burgos, Caba, Naguilian, Pugo, Rosario, Sto. Tomas and Tubao.
>
> **Section 3.** The Department of Public Works and Highways shall promulgate rules and regulations necessary for the effective implementation of this Act.
>
> **Section 4.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
