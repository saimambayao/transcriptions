# Republic Act No. 9447

**AN ACT RENAMING FLORIDA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL SITUATED IN BARANGAY FLORIDA, BUTUAN CITY, INTO MARIANA L. PINEDA MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9447
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2007/ra_9447_2007.html)

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Special Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9447             May 15, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT RENAMING FLORIDA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL SITUATED IN BARANGAY FLORIDA, BUTUAN CITY, INTO MARIANA L. PINEDA MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1. *Renaming of School.*** - Florida Elementary School, a public elementary school situated in Barangay Florida, Butuan City, is hereby renamed and henceforth shall be known as Mariana L. Pineda Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Sec. 2. *Repealing Clause.*** - All laws, decrees, executive orders and other administrative issuances and parts thereof which are inconsistent with the provision of this Act are hereby modified or superseded accordingly.
>
> **Sec. 3. *Separability Clause.*** - If any clause, provision, paragraph or part hereof shall he declared unconstitutional or invalid, it shall not affect the remaining provisions of this Act.
>
> **Sec. 4. *Effectivity.*** - This Act shall take effect upon its approva1.
>
