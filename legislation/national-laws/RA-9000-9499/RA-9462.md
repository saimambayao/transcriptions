# Republic Act No. 9462

**AN ACT NAMING THE SOUTHERN TAGALOG ARTERIAL ROAD (STAR) TO APOLINARIO MABINI SUPERHIGHWAY (AMS)**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9462
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2007/ra_9462_2007.html)

---

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Special Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9462             May 15, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT NAMING THE SOUTHERN TAGALOG ARTERIAL ROAD (STAR) TO APOLINARIO MABINI SUPERHIGHWAY (AMS)**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** This Act shall be officially known as the **"*Apolinario Mabini Superhighway Act*"**.
>
> **Sec. 2.** This Act officially names the Southern Tagalog Arterial Road (STAR) to Apolinario Mabini Superhighway (AMS) .
>
> **Sec. 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
