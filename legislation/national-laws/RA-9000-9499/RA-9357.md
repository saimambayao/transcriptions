# Republic Act No. 9357

**AN ACT REAPPORTIONING THE PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT INTO TWO LEGlSLATIVE DISTRICT**

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand six.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9357             October 10, 2006**
>
> **AN ACT REAPPORTIONING THE PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT INTO TWO LEGlSLATIVE DISTRICT**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** The Province of Sultan Kudarat is hereby reapportioned into two legislative districts to commence in the next national elections following the effectivity of this Act. Henceforth, the municipalities of Columbio, Isulan, Lambayong, Lutayan, President Quirino and Tacurong shall comprise the First District while the municipalities of Bagumbayan, Esperanza, Kalamansig, Lebak, Palimbang and Senator Ninoy Aquino shall comprise the Second District.
>
> **SEC. 2.** All laws, executive orders, rules and regulations inconsistent with the provision of this Act are hereby repealed, amended or modified accordingly.
>
> **SEC. 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in at least two newspapers of general circulation.
>
