# Republic Act No. 9041

**AN ACT NAMING THE NATIONAL ROAD FROM CROSSING CORONACION TO BARANGAY TALUBANGI IN THE CITY OF KABANKALAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL AS JUSTICE JESUS Y. PEREZ MEMORIAL HIGHWAY**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9041       March 22, 2001**
>
> AN ACT NAMING THE NATIONAL ROAD FROM CROSSING CORONACION TO BARANGAY TALUBANGI IN THE CITY OF KABANKALAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL AS JUSTICE JESUS Y. PEREZ MEMORIAL HIGHWAY
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Justice Jesus Y. Perez was the first and only Kabankalanon to serve as Justice to the Philippine Court of Appeals.
>
> **Section 2.** To perpetuate his memory, the national road from Crossing Coronacion to Barangay Talubangi in the City of Kabankalan, Province of Negros Occidental is hereby named Justice Jesus Y. Perez Memorial Highway.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
