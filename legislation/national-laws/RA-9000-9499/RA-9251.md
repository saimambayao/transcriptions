# Republic Act No. 9251

**AN ACT RENAMING THE PADRE BURGOS - AGDANGAN - UNISAN - PITOGO - MACALELON - GENERAL LUNA - CATANAUAN - MULANAY - SAN FRANCISCO - SAN ANDRES - SAN NARCISO - BUENAVISTA NATIONAL ROAD IN BONDOC PENINSULA IN THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON AS THE MIGUEL SUAREZ HIGHWAY**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9251
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2004/ra_9251_2004.html)

> Twelfth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eight day of July, two thousand and three.*
>
> **Republic Act No. 9251             February 19 2004**
>
> **AN ACT RENAMING THE PADRE BURGOS - AGDANGAN - UNISAN - PITOGO - MACALELON - GENERAL LUNA - CATANAUAN - MULANAY - SAN FRANCISCO - SAN ANDRES - SAN NARCISO - BUENAVISTA NATIONAL ROAD IN BONDOC PENINSULA IN THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON AS THE MIGUEL SUAREZ HIGHWAY**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** The Padre Burgos - Agdangan - Unisan - Pitogo - Macalelon - General Luna - Catanauan - Mulanay - San Franciso - San Andres - San Narciso - Buenavista National Road in Bondoc Peninsula in the Province of Quezon shall hereafter be known as the Miguel Suarez Highway.
>
> **SEC. 2.** The Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
