# Republic Act No. 9467

**AN ACT RENAMING THE OZAMIZ CITY BY-PASS ROAD LOCATED IN OZAMIZ CITY AS CONGRESSMAN HILARION J. RAMIRO BY-PASS ROAD**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9467
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2007/ra_9467_2007.html)

---

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Special Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9467             May 15, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT RENAMING THE OZAMIZ CITY BY-PASS ROAD LOCATED IN OZAMIZ CITY AS CONGRESSMAN HILARION J. RAMIRO BY-PASS ROAD**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Ozamiz City By-pass Road in Ozamiz City is hereby renamed as Congressman Hilarion J. Ramiro By-Pass Road.
>
> **Sec. 2.** The Department of Public Works and Highways is hereby directed to implement the provision of this Act.
>
> **Sec. 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
