# Republic Act No. 9203

**AN ACT DECLARING THE FIFTEENTH (15TH) DAY OF AUGUST OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN MUNICIPALITY OF CAMIGUIN, TO BE KNOWN AS CATARMAN DAY**

> **Twelfth Congress
> Second Regular Session**
>
>
>
> Begun held in Metro Manila on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, two thousand two
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9203 April 20, 2003**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING THE FIFTEENTH (15TH) DAY OF AUGUST OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN MUNICIPALITY OF CAMIGUIN, TO BE KNOWN AS "CATARMAN DAY"
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** August Fifteenth (15th) every year is hereby declared as special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Catarman, Province of Camiguin, to commemorate its foundation day anniversary.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
