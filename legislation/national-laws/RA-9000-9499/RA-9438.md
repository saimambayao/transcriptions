# Republic Act No. 9438

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BABATNGON NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BABATNGON, PROVINCE OF LEYTE TO JUAN S. TISMO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9438
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2007/ra_9438_2007.html)

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Special Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9438             April 27, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BABATNGON NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BABATNGON, PROVINCE OF LEYTE TO JUAN S. TISMO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1. *Renaming a National High School.***  The name of the Babatngon National High School in the Municipality of Babatngon, Province of Leyte is hereby changed to Juan S. Tismo National High School.
>
> **Sec. 2. *Effectivity.***  This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
>
