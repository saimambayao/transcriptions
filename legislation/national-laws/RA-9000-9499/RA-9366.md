> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand six.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9366             January 09, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO REV. FR. ULRICH H. SCHLECHT. SVD**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** Rev. Fr. Ulrich H. Schlecht is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives appurtenant thereto.
>
> **SEC. 2.** Upon the taking and registration of the of allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines before the Bureau of Immigration and the issuance to him of the Certificate of Naturalization, Rev. Fr. Ulrich H. Schlecht shall enter upon the full enjoyment of Philippine Citizenship.
>
> **SEC. 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
