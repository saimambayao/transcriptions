# Republic Act No. 9073

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE BARTOLOME AND MANUELA PAÑARES MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF BARILI, PROVINCE OF CEBU AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REFUBLIC ACT NO. 9073       April 08, 2001**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE BARTOLOME AND MANUELA PAÑARES MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF BARILI, PROVINCE OF CEBU AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Bartolome and Manuela Pañares Memorial National High School in Barangay Poblacion, Municipality of Barili, Province of Cebu is hereby established.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations that would carry out the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and therefor.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
