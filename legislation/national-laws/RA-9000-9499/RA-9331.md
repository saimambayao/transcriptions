# Republic Act No. 9331

**AN ACT NAMING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE PROVINCIAL OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAGAWE, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO AS CAMP COLONEL JOAQUIN P. DUNUAN**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9331
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2004/ra_9331_2004.html)

---

> Twelfth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eight day of July, two thousand and three.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9331**
>
> **AN ACT NAMING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE PROVINCIAL OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAGAWE, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO AS CAMP COLONEL JOAQUIN P. DUNUAN**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Philippine National Police (PNP) Provincial Office in the Municipality of Lagawe, Province of Ifugao is hereby named Camp Col Joaquin P. Dunuan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
