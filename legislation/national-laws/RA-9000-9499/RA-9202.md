# Republic Act No. 9202

**AN ACT DECLARING AUGUST TENTH OF EACH YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MAASIN, PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9202
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2003/ra_9202_2003.html)

> **Twelfth Congress
> Second Regular Session**
>
>
>
> Begun held in Metro Manila on Monday, the twenty-second day of July, two thousand two
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9202 April 04, 2003**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING AUGUST TENTH OF EACH YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MAASIN, PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** August ten (August 10) of each year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the City of Maasin Province of Southern Leyte to commemorate its Charter Day Anniversary.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
