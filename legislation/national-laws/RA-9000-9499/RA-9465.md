# Republic Act No. 9465

**AN ACT RENAMING PENNSYLVANIA AVENUE IN THE CITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION AS GOVERNOR JOAQUIN L. ORTEGA AVENUE**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9465
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2007/ra_9465_2007.html)

---

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Special Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9465             May 15, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT RENAMING PENNSYLVANIA AVENUE IN THE CITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION AS GOVERNOR JOAQUIN L. ORTEGA AVENUE**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Pennsylvania Avenue, a secondary national road situated in the City of San Fernando, Province of La Union is hereby renamed as Governor Joaquin L. Ortega Avenue.
>
> **Sec. 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
