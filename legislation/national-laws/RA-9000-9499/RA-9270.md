# Republic Act No. 9270

**AN ACT NAMING THE NATIONAL ROAD FROM INTERSECTION OF GOVERNOR CAMINS ROAD AND VETERANS AVENUE UP TO LICOMO IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA TO MARIA CLARA L. LOBREGAT HIGHWAY**

> Twelfth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eight day of July, two thousand and three.*
>
> **Republic Act No. 9270             March 19, 2004**
>
> **AN ACT NAMING THE NATIONAL ROAD FROM INTERSECTION OF GOVERNOR CAMINS ROAD AND VETERANS AVENUE UP TO LICOMO IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA TO MARIA CLARA L. LOBREGAT HIGHWAY**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The portion of the national road starting from the intersection of Governor Camins Road and Veterans Avenue up to Licomo in the City of Zamboanga is hereby named Maria Clara L. Lobregat Highway.
>
> **Sec. 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
