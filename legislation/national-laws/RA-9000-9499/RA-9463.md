# Republic Act No. 9463

**AN ACT NAMING THE SAN NICOLAS-SOLSONA PORTION OF THE ILOCOS NORTE-APAYAO ROAD AS THE JOSEFA LLANES ESCODA NATIONAL HIGHWAY**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9463
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2007/ra_9463_2007.html)

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Special Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9463             May 15, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT NAMING THE SAN NICOLAS-SOLSONA PORTION OF THE ILOCOS NORTE-APAYAO ROAD AS THE JOSEFA LLANES ESCODA NATIONAL HIGHWAY**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** This Act shall be known as the **"*Josefa Llanes Escoda National Highway Act*"**.
>
> **Sec. 2.** The San Nicolas - Solsona portion of the Ilocos Norte-Apayao Road starting from Km. Post 484+000 to Km. Post 524+000 in the Province of Ilocos Norte is hereby named as the Josefa Llanes Escoda National Highway.
>
> **Sec. 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
