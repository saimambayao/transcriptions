# Republic Act No. 9330

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE SAN ANTONIO AIRPORT IN THE ISLAND-MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ANTONIO, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Twelfth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eight day of July, two thousand and three.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9330**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE SAN ANTONIO AIRPORT IN THE ISLAND-MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ANTONIO, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** To enhance tourism in the Island-Municipality of San Antonio, Province of Northern Samar, which is endowed with exotic white sand beaches, there is hereby established in said municipality an airport to be known as the San Antonio Airport, the funding which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Transportation and Communications shall include in its program the establishment and operationalization of the San Antonio Airpot.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
