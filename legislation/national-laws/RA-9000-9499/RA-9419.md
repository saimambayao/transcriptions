# Republic Act No. 9419

**AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE AMANG RODRIGUEZ MEMORIAL MEDICAL CENTER FROM ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) TO THREE HUNDRED (300) BEDS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Special Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9421             April 10, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE AMANG RODRIGUEZ MEMORIAL MEDICAL CENTER FROM ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) TO THREE HUNDRED (300) BEDS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** The authorized bed capacity of Amang Rodriguez Memorial Medical Center (ARMMC) , a national medical center created through Republic Act No. 8019, is hereby increased from one hundred fifty (150) to three hundred beds with concomitant manpower complement.
>
> **SEC. 2.** The increase in the bed capacity of the ARMMC from one hundred fifty (150) to three hundred beds shall be implemented for a period of not more than four years from the approval of this Act.
>
> **SEC. 3.** The Secretary of Health shall include in the Department's program the implementation of this increase in bed capacity of the ARMMC, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Act.
>
> **SEC. 4.** all laws, decrees, rules and regulations inconsistent with this Act shall be deemed amended or repealed accordingly.
>
> **SEC. 5.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
