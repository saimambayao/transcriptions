# Republic Act No. 9091

**AN ACT DECLARING EVERY 28th DAY OF MAY AS ARAW NG LABANAN SA ALAPAN, A SPECIAL WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, CAVITE**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9091
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2001/ra_9091_2001.html)

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9091       April 08, 2001**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING EVERY 28th DAY OF MAY AS ARAW NG LABANAN SA ALAPAN, A SPECIAL WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, CAVITE
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** May 28 of every year is hereby declared as Araw ng Labanan sa Alapan a special working public holiday in the Municipality of Imus, Cavite, in commemoration of the historic "Labanan sa Alapan."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect immediately after its publication in two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
>
