# Republic Act No. 9322

**AN ACT RECLASSIFYING A PORTION OF LAND COMPRISING FIVE HUNDRED TWELVE AND EIGHTY (512.80) HECTARES OF PUBLIC LAND UNDER TIMBERLAND PROJECT NUMBER 13-G, BLOCK A, AS PER LAND CLASSIFICATION MAP NUMBER 3400 IN BARANGAY RANSANG, MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN AS AGRICULTURAL LAND OPEN FOR DISPOSITION**

> Twelfth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eight day of July, two thousand and three.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9322             June 24, 2006**
>
> **AN ACT RECLASSIFYING A PORTION OF LAND COMPRISING FIVE HUNDRED TWELVE AND EIGHTY (512.80) HECTARES OF PUBLIC LAND UNDER TIMBERLAND PROJECT NUMBER 13-G, BLOCK A, AS PER LAND CLASSIFICATION MAP NUMBER 3400 IN BARANGAY RANSANG, MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN AS AGRICULTURAL LAND OPEN FOR DISPOSITION**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The portion of the territory of Barangay Ransang in the Municuipality of Rizal, Province of Palawan, consisting of five hundred twelve and eighty (512.80) hectares of land covered by Project Number 13-G, Block A, of Land Classification Map Number 3400 is hereby reclassified as agricultural land open for disposition.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources shall issue all the necessary implementing orders, rules, regulations and circulars to implement the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
