# Republic Act No. 9414

**AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 19 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR TO COMMEMORATE ITS FOUNDATION DAY**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9414
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2007/ra_9414_2007.html)

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand six.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9414             March 24, 2007**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 19 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR TO COMMEMORATE ITS FOUNDATION DAY**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** June 19 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Northern Samar to commemorate its foundation day.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act sha1l take effect immediately upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
