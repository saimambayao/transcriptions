# Republic Act No. 9030

**AN ACT RENAMING THE ASINGAN-STA. MARIA BRIDGE IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF ASINGAN AND STA. MARIA IN THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN AS THE NARCISO RAMOS BRIDGE**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9030
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2001/ra_9030_2001.html)

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9030       March 05, 2001**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE ASINGAN-STA. MARIA BRIDGE IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF ASINGAN AND STA. MARIA IN THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN AS THE NARCISO RAMOS BRIDGE
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Asingan-Sta. Maria Bridge connecting the municipalities of Asingan and Sta. Maria in the Province of Pangasinan is hereby renamed Narciso Ramos Bridge in honor of the late Narciso Ramos as a fitting tribute to a life devoted to public service, for his invaluable contributions to nation-building, and for his inspiring leadership and exemplary statesmanship worthy of admiration and emulation by the present and future generations of Filipinos.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
