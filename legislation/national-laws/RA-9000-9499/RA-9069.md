# Republic Act No. 9069

**AN ACT DECLARING MAY 14 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY FOR OBANDO, BULACAN**

> Eleventh Congress
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9069       April 08, 2001**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING MAY 14 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY FOR OBANDO, BULACAN
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** It is hereby declared that May 14 of every year, being the Foundation Day of Obando, Bulacan, shall be a special working holiday for the same.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of national circulation and at least in two (2) local newspapers and/or community newsletters.
>
