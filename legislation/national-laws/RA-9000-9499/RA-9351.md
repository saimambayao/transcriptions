# Republic Act No. 9351

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE VICTORIA-LA PAZ ROAD IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF VICTORIA AND LA PAZ, PROVINCE OF TARLAC INTO A NATIONAL ROAD**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9351
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2006/ra_9351_2006.html)

> Thirteenth Congress
>
> Second Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, two thousand five.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9351**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE VICTORIA-LA PAZ ROAD IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF VICTORIA AND LA PAZ, PROVINCE OF TARLAC INTO A NATIONAL ROAD**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **SECTION 1.** The Victoria - La Paz Road in the municipalities of Victoria and La Paz, Province of Tarlac, consisting of 15.1 kilometers, more or less, is hereby converted into a national road.
>
> **SEC. 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.
>
> **SEC. 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
