> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1041**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Sitio of Pook Ni Banal in the Barrio of Malaking Pook, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, into a Barrio**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Pook ni Banal in the Barrio of Malaking Pook, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, is converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of Pook ni Banal.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
