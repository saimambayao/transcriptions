> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1239**
>
> **An Act Requiring the Registration of Agents, Contractors and Dealers in Logs, Lumber and Commercial Piles**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Any provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, any agent, contractor, or dealer in logs, lumber and commercial piles shall register with the Bureau of Forestry.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** All persons found to be directly or indirectly responsible for the violation of this Act or of the rules and regulations issued thereunder shall be punished by a fine of not more than one thousand pesos or imprisonment of not more than one year.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
