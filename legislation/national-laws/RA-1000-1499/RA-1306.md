> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1306**
>
> **An Act Amending Sub-Item (w), Item 2, Subsection (a), Title H, Section One of Republic Act Numbered Nine Hundred Twenty**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Sub-item (w), item 2, subsection (a), title H, section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred twenty, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(w) Masalasa River Irrigation System, Tarlac | ₱30,000.00 |
> | "(w-1) Bamban River Irrigation System, Tarlac | 10,000.00 |
> | "(w-2) Cutcut River Irrigation System, Tarlac | 15,000.00 |
> | "(w-3) Caut-Bantog-Balanoy-San Roque communal Irrigation System | 20,044.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
