> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1044**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Communal Barrio School in the Municipality of Calapan, Province of Oriental Mindoro, to Filemon Samaco Memorial School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Communal Barrio School in the Municipality of Calapan, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is changed to Filemon Samaco Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
