> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1433**
>
> **An Act to Amend Items Nineteen and Twenty, aa. La Union, Paragraph I, Title B, of Section One of Republic Act Numbered One Thousand Two Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Items nineteen and twenty, aa. La Union, paragraph I, title B, of section one Republic Act Numbered One thousand two hundred, are amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(19) Leones-Macalva Tubao-Agoo-Completion | ₱8,000.00 |
> | "(20) Sta. Teresa Road, Tubao-Completion | 5,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 14, 1956.
