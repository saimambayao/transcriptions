> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1238**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of New Agutaya, Municipality of Taytay, Province of Palawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Nasalogan in the Municipality of Taytay, Province of Palawan, is constituted into a barrio of the said municipality, to be known as the barrio of New Agutaya.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 7, 1955.
