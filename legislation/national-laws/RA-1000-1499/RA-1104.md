> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1104**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Pinagsabangan Elementary School in the Municipality of Naujan, Province of Oriental Mindoro, to Juan L. Luna Memorial School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Pinagsabangan Elementary School in the municipality of Naujan, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is changed to Juan L. Luna Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1954.
