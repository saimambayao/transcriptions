> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1223**
>
> **An Act Amending and Repealing Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered Nine Hundred and Twenty, Regarding Public Works Projects for the Province of Cebu**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item 17 (f), for the Province of Cebu, paragraph (a), Title E, section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred twenty, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(f) Carmen Waterworks-Construction | ₱65,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** The following new items are inserted after item 17 (h), for the Province of Cebu, paragraph (a), Title E, section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred twenty:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(h-1) Barrio San Juan Waterworks, Municipality of Pilar-Construction | ₱2,000.00 |
> | "(h-2) Barrio Villa Hermosa Waterworks, Municipality of Pilar-Construction | 2,000.00" |
> | "(h-3) Barrio Kawit Waterworks, Municipality of Pilar-Construction | 6,000.00" |
>
> **Section 3.** Item 17 (c), paragraph (a), Title C, and item 3 (x), paragraph (a), Title H, of section one of said Republic Act, are repealed.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 11, 1955.
