> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1462**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item Ten, ab. Marinduque, Section Three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item ten, ab. Marinduque, section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "10. Cabahuan Barrio School BuildingBoac, Construction | ₱5,000.00" |
> | "10-a. Binunga Barrio School BuildingBoac, Construction | 5,000.00 |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 14, 1956.
