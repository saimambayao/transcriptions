> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1466**
>
> **An Act Amending Items Eighty-One, Eighty-Two, Eighty-Three, and Eighty-Four, Subsection d, for the Province of Antique, Section Three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Items eighty-one, eighty-two, eighty-three, and eighty-four, subsection d, for the Province of Antique, section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, are amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "82. Bitas Primary School BuildingConstruction of two Type B room | ₱4,000.00" |
> | "83. Villa-Solomon Primary School Building, PatnongonConstruction of two Type B room | ₱4,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 14, 1956.
