> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1046**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Concepcion in the Municipality of Saug, Province of Davao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Concepcion in the municipality of Saug, Province of Davao, is hereby constituted into a barrio of the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Concepcion.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
