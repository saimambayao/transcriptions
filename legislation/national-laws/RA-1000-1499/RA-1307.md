> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1307**
>
> **An Act Converting Certain Sitios in the Municipality of Babak, Province of Davao, into Barrios**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Mambago, San Isidro, Sto. Niño, San Antonio, San Agustin, Dangcaan, Balet, Tambo, Camudmud, and Cogo, in the Municipality of Babak, Province of Davao, are converted into barrios.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
