> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1011**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Nasumi in the Municipality of Dingle, Province of Iloilo**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Nasumi in the municipality of Dingle, Province of Iloilo, is hereby converted into a new barrio in the same municipality to be known as the barrio of Nasumi.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
