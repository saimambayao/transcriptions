> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1111**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Dumaran, Province of Palawan, to Araceli**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Dumaran, Province of Palawan, is changed to Araceli.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1954.
