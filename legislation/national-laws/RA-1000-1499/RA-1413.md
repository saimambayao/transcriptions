> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1413**
>
> **An Act Appropriating the Sum of Two Hundred Thousand Pesos for the Employment of Temporary Personnel in the Bureau of Posts During the Period from February to June, Nineteen Hundred Fifty-Six**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury of the Philippines not otherwise appropriated, for the employment of temporary personnel of the Bureau of Posts in Manila and in the different post offices in the Philippines during the period from February to June, nineteen hundred fifty-six.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect as of February 1, 1956.
>
> Approved: April 21, 1956.
