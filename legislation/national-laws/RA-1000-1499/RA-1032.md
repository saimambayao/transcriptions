> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1032**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of San Jacinto, Province of Bohol, to Catigbi-An**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the municipality of San Jacinto, Province of Bohol, is changed to Catigbi-an.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
