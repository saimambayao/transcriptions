> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1452**
>
> **An Act Amending Sub-Item Ten of Item al, Subsection One, Title B of Section One, Republic Act Numbered Twelve Hundred, Entitled "An Act Appropriating Funds for Public Works and Amending Republic Act Numbered Nine Hundred and Twenty"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Sub-item ten of item al, subsection one, Title B of section one, Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(10) Asphalt paving of the following streets, Municipality of Balawan, Negros Oriental: Buenavista Street from Zamora Street to Mabini Street; Mabini Street from Progreso Street to Burgos Street; Burgos Street from Mabini Street to General Luna Street; General Luna Street from Burgos Street to Nueva Street; Independence Street from provincial road to Nueva Street; La Libertad Street from provincial road to Rizal Street; Martires Street from provincial road to Progreso Street; Progreso Street from General Luna Street to Martires Street; Rizal Street from General Luna Street to La Libertad Street; Nueva Street from General Luna Street to Independence Street | ₱15,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 14, 1956.
