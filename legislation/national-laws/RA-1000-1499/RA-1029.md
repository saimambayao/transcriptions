> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1029**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of Barrio Balayang, Mabini, Pangasinan, to Barrio Luna**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio Balayang, Mabini, Pangasinan, is hereby changed to barrio Luna.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
