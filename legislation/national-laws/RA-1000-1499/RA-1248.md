> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1248**
>
> **An Act to Create the Municipality of Upi in the Province of Cotabato**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrio of Upi in the Municipality of Dinaig, Province of Cotabato, is separated from the said municipality and constituted into a municipality in the said province, to be known as the Municipality of Upi.
>
> **Section 2.** The first municipal mayor, vice-mayor and councilors of the newly created municipality shall be appointed by the President of the Philippines to hold office until their successors are duly elected and have qualified.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 10, 1955.
