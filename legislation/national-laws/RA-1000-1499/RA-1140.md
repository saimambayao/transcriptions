> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1140**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Bacuit in the Province of Palawan to El Nido**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Bacuit, Province of Palawan is hereby changed to El Nido.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1954.
