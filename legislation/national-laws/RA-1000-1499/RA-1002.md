> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1002**
>
> **An Act Providing that the Street in the City of Zamboanga at Present Known as Bailen Street, from its Point of Intersection with Ignacio Magno Street to that with Governor Alvarez Avenue, Shall Hereafter be Known as La Purisima Street**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The street in the City of Zamboanga at present known as Bailen Street, from its point of intersection with Ignacio Magno Street to that with Governor Alvarez Avenue, shall hereafter be known as La Purisima Street.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
