> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1332**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Sitio of Diptan in the Municipality of Basco, Province of Batanes, into a Barrio to be Known as the Barrio of San Antonio De Padua of Said Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Diptan, in the Municipality of Basco, Province of Batanes, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of San Antonio de Padua of said municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
