> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1254**
>
> **An Act to Amend Sub-Items One to Sixteen of Item (d) for the Province of Samar, Sub-Section (e), Title B, Section One, Republic Act Numbered Nine Hundred and Twenty, Otherwise Known as Public Works Act of Nineteen Hundred and Fifty-Three**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Sub-items one to sixteen of Item (d) for the Province of Samar, sub-section (e), Title B, section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred and twenty, otherwise known as the Public Works Act of nineteen hundred and fifty-three, are hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(d) For the construction of the municipal building of San Antonio | ₱25,000.00 |
> | "(d-1) For the completion of the municipal building of Catubig | 8,000.00 |
> | "(d-2) For the completion of the municipal building of Palapag | 8,000.00 |
> | "(d-3) For the improvement of the 6-room academic building of the Central Elementary School of Pambujan | 5,000.00 |
> | "(d-4) For the completion of the municipal building of Las Navas | 4,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 10, 1955.
