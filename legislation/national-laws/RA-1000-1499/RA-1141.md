> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1141**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Sitio of Malitam, in the Barrio of Libjo, Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, into a Barrio**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Malitam, in the barrio of Libjo, municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, is converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of Malitam.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1aшphi1
>
> Approved: June 17, 1954.
