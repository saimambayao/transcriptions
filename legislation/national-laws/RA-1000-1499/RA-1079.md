> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1079**
>
> **An Act Providing that Civil Service Eligibility Shall be Permanent**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Civil service eligibility shall be permanent and shall have no time limit.
>
> **Section 2.** Any provision of law, rule or regulation which is inconsistent with the provisions of this Act is repealed.£a⩊phi£
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall be retroactive so as to include persons whose civil service eligibility had expired prior to its approval.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1954.
