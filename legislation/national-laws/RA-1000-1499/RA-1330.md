> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1330**
>
> **An Act to Create the Barrio of Bagsac in the Municipality of Madrin, Province of Surigao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Bagsac in the Municipality of Madrid, Province of Surigao, is hereby constituted into a barrio in the same municipality, to be known as Bagsac.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
