> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1076**
>
> **An Act to Amend Paragraph Two, Section One of Republic Act Numbered Nine Hundred and Forty-Eight**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Paragraph two, section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred and forty-eight is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "2. For the construction of the North General Hospital Building, including the purchase of the site, if necessary, and of hospital equipment; one million five hundred thousand pesos: *Provided,* That not more than two hundred thousand pesos may be used for the purchase, reclamation or development of the site."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 14, 1954.
