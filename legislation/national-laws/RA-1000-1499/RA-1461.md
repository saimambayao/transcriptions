> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1461**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven, Regarding Public Works Projects for the Province of Negros Oriental**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (h), Group I, paragraph thirty-seven, subsection of section two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(h) La Libertad Waterworks Improvement | ₱10,000.00." |
>
> **Section 2.** Item eight, under caption "Other Buildings," for the Province of Negros Oriental, under subsection g, section two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "8. Public Market, La Libertad | ₱4,000.00" |
>
> **Section 3.** Items twenty-five, forty, forty-one and forty-two, Group I, paragraph aj, section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, are amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "25. Majuyod Central School BuildingConstruction of five room | ₱25,000.00." |
> | "40. San Jose School Building, San Jose Construction of one room | 5,000.00." |
> | "41. Tubod Barrio School Building, ManjuyodConstruction of one room | 5,000.00." |
> | "42. Cambaloctot Barrio School Building San JoseConstruction of one room | 5,000.00." |
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 14, 1956.
