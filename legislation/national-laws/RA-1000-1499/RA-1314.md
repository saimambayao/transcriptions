> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1314**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Iraya Norte in the Municipality of San Vicente, Province of Camarines Norte, to San Jose of the Said Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio Iraya Norte in the Municipality of San Vicente, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to barrio San Jose of the said municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
