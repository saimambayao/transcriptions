> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1230**
>
> **An Act Appropriating the Sum of Seventeen Million Pesos to Carry Out the Provisions of Republic Act Numbered Eight Hundred Forty-Two with Respect to Public School Officials, Teachers and Other School Personnel of the Bureau of Public Schools and Welfareville Teachers, for the Fiscal Year 1955-1956**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of seventeen million pesos, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to carry out the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Eight hundred forty-two with respect to public school officials, teachers and other school personnel of the Bureau of Public Schools and Welfareville teachers, for the fiscal year 1955-1956, subject to the availability of funds as certified by the Commissioner of the Budget, the Secretary of Finance and the Auditor General: *Provided,* That this appropriation shall be given top priority. The necessary sum to carry out the provisions of said Act in succeeding years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 1, 1955.
