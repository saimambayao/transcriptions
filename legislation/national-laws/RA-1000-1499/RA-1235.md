> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1235**
>
> **An Act Providing that the Street in the Poblacion of the Municipality of Dao, Province of Antique, at Present Known as Libertador Street, Shall Hereafter be Known as Ysulat Street**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The street in the poblacion of the Municipality of Dao, Province of Antique, at present known as Libertador Street, shall hereafter be known as Ysulat Street.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 7, 1955.
