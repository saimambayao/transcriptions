> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1326**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Taniongbobog, Municipality of Brookes Point, Province of Palawan, to Saraza**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio of Taniongbobog, Municipality of Brookes Point, Province of Palawan, is changed to Saraza.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
