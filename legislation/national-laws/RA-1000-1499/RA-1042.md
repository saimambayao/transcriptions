> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1042**
>
> **An Act to Create the Barrio of Badong in the Municipality of Tago, Province of Surigao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio in Badong in the municipality of Tago, Province of Surigao, is hereby constituted into a barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Badong.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
