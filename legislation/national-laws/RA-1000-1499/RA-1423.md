> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1423**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Sabang Elementary School in the Barrio of Sabang, City of Lipa, to G.B. Lontok Memorial Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Sabang Elementary School in the barrio of Sabang, City of Lipa, is changed to G.B. Lontok Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 10, 1956.
