> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1021**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Sinisian Elementary School in the Municipality of Calaca, Province of Batangas, to Pedro A. Paterno Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Sinisian Elementary School in the municipality of Calaca, Province of Batangas, is hereby changed to Pedro A. Paterno Elementary School, in honor of the patriot, Don Pedro A. Paterno.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
