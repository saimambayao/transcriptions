> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1208**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item Twenty-One of the Public Works Projects for the Province of Quezon, Sub-Section (a), Title A, Section One of Republic Act Numbered Six Hundred Seventy**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item twenty-one of the public works projects for the Province of Quezon, subsection (a), title A, section one of Republic Act Numbered Six hundred seventy, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "21. For the construction of the Sto. Cristo School Building, Sariaya | ₱5,000.00." |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 20, 1955.
