> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1025**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Casa Real in Municipality of Pakil, Province of Laguna, to a Barrio to be Known as Barrio Casa Real**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Casa Real in Municipality of Pakil, Province of Laguna, is hereby converted to a barrio to be known as barrio Casa Real.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
