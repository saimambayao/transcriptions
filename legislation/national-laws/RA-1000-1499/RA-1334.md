> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1334**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Sitios of Coral, Hago, Tuva, Diojango, Canalaan, Honaan and Dimnalamay, into a Barrio to be Known as the Barrio of San Joaquin, in the Municipality of Basco, Province of Batanes**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Coral, Hago, Tuva, Diojango, Canalaan, Honaan and Dimnalamay are hereby converted into a barrio, to be known as barrio San Joaquin, in the Municipality of Basco, Province of Batanes.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
