> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1458**
>
> **An Act Amending and Repealing Certain Items of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Items (c) and (i), paragraph thirty-two, for the Province of Mindoro Oriental, subsection "f," section two of Republic Act numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(c) Extension of Pinamalayan Waterworks to Lumangbayan | ₱8,000.00 |
> | "(i) Pinamalayan Municipal Building, Construction | 9,000.00 |
>
> **Section 2.** Item (v), paragraph thirty-two, for the Province of Mindoro Oriental, subsection "f," section two of the same Act is repealed.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 14, 1956.
