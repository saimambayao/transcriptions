> [ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1126, June 16, 1954 ]
>
> **AN ACT APPROPRIATING TWO MILLION FOUR HUNDRED AND FORTY-FIVE THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE PURCHASE AND INSTALLATION OF AUTOMATIC TELEPHONE TO REPLACE THE PRESENT GOVERNMENT SECOND-HAND AND OBSOLETE MANUAL TELEPHONE SYSTEM, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.**

>
> **Section 2.** Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Public Works and Communications, and with the approval president, excess in any item mentioned in section one hereof may be transferred to other items to cover shortages in the amounts appropriated therefor.
>
> **Section 3.** All balances of the appropriations made in this remaining unexpended on June thirty, nineteen hundred fifty-seven, shall be available fox expenditures for this project until it is completed.
>
> **Section 4.** The funds herein appropriated shall not be released unless and until the Secretary of Finance and the Auditor General shall have certified to the President that there are existing and available funds in the National Treasury for the purpose.
