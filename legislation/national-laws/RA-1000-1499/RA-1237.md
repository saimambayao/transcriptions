> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1237**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Atelan, in the Municipality of Echague, Province of Isabela, to San Miguel**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of barrio Atelan, in the Municipality of Echague, Province of Isabela, is hereby changed to barrio San Miguel.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 7, 1955.
