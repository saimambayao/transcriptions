> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1116**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Limbuhan, Province of Masbate to Pio V. Corpus**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In recognition of the eminent and patriotic services rendered by the late Pio V. Corpus, the name of the municipality of Limbuhan, Province of Masbate, is changed to Pio V. Corpus.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1954.
