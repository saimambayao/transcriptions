> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1103**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the San Agustin Elementary School in the Municipality of Naujan, Province of Oriental Mindoro, to Santiago Garong Memorial School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the San Agustin Elementary School in the municipality of Naujan, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is changed to Santiago Garong Memorial School.
>
