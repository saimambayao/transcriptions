> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1255**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of the Municipality of Lucena, Province of Iloilo, to that of "New Lucena"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Lucena, Province of Iloilo, is changed to that of "New Lucena".
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 10, 1955.
