> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1207**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of the Barrio of Pajarito, Municipality of Sibalom, Province of Antique, to Catmon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio of Pajarito in the Municipality of Sibalom, Province of Antique, is hereby changed to Catmon.
>
> **Section 2.** Republic Act Numbered Eight hundred and ninety is hereby repealed.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 20, 1955.
