> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1455**
>
> **An Act Amending the Project Number and Location of the Project in Appendix A, Title G, of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The project number and location of the project for Project Numbered G-05.10 in Appendix A, Title G, of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "G-32.1: Camalig-Catmon Irrigation Project, Albay."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 14, 1956.
