> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1486**
>
> **An Act Amending Item (b), Group I, Paragraph Seventeen, for the Province of Cebu, Subsection "f," Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (b), Group I, paragraph seventeen, for the Province of Cebu, subsection "f," section two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(b) Barrio Schools to be designated by the Municipal Mayor, Danao Construction and/or repair | ₱18,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1956.
