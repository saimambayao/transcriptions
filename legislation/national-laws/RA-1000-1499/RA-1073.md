> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1073**
>
> **An Act Authorizing the Appropriation of the Sum of Two Hundred Thousand Pesos for the Creation of the Cagayan Valley Agricultural High School in the Province of Cagayan as a Regional National Agricultural School of the Central Luzon Agricultural College Type to be Known as the Cagayan Valley National Agricultural School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, which shall be expended for the fiscal year for which the appropriation has been approved by the Director of Public Schools with the approval of the Secretary of Education for the establishment, operation and maintenance of the Cagayan Valley National Agricultural School patterned after the former Central Luzon Agricultural School. Thereafter, the necessary funds for the operation and maintenance of said school annually shall be included in the General Appropriation Act.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
