> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1005**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Sitio of Mayamot, Barrio of Banga, Municipality of Plaridel, Province of Bulacan, to Bagong Silang**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Sitio of Mayamot, in the Barrio of Banga, Municipality of Plaridel, Province of Bulacan, is changed to Bagong Silang.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
