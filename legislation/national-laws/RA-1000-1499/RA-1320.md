> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1320**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of San Juan in Maricaban Island, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Pirasan in the barrio of Payapa, Maricaban Island, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, is separated from the said barrio of Payapa and constituted into a separate and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of San Juan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
