> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1045**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Pinamalayan Elementary School, in the Municipality of Pinamalayan, Province of Oriental Mindoro, to Juan Morente, Sr. Memorial School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Pinamalayan Elementary School, in the Municipality of Pinamalayan, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is changed to Juan Morente, Sr. Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
