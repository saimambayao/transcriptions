> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1043**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Sitio of Malalim in the Barrio of Mahabang Dahilig, Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, into a Barrio**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The southern portion of the barrio of Sirang Lupa, the northern portion of the barrio Mahabang Dahilig, and the eastern portion of San Isidro, all in the municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, are separated from their respective barrios and together with the sitio of Malalim of the same municipality are converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of Malalim.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
