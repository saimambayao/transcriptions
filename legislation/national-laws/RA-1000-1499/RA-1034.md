> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1034**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio of Banawa, in the Municipality of Kabagan, Province of Cotabato, to Banawag**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio of Banawa, in the Municipality of Kabagan, Province of Cotabato, is hereby changed to Banawag.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
