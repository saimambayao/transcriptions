> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1311**
>
> **An Act Making the Barrio of New Sabonga in the Municipality of Saug, Province of Davao, as a Part of the Municipality of Compostela of the Same Province**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrio of New Sabonga in the Municipality of Saug, Province of Davao, is hereby declared as a part of the Municipality of Compostela of the same province.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
