> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1316**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Ilayang Basiad in the Municipality of Capalonga, Province of Camarines Norte, into Barrio San Pedro of Said Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Ilayang Basiad in the Municipality of Capalonga, Province of Camarines Norte, is converted into barrio San Pedro of said municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
