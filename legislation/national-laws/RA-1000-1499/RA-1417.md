> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1417**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Longos, Province of Laguna, to Municipality of Kalayaan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Longos, Province of Laguna, is changed to Municipality of Kalayaan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 17, 1956.
