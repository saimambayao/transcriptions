> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1024**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Pampanga Elementary School in the City of Davao to Vicente Hizon Memorial School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Pampanga Elementary School in the City of Davao shall hereafter be known as the Vicente Hizon Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
