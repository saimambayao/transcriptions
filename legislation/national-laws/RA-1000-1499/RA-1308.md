> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1308**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Sitios of Ilaya Labac, Matalisay, Pajo and Cacawan in Isla Verde, in the Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, to a Barrio to be Known as Barrio San Antonio of the Same Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Ilaya, Labac, Matalisay, Pajo and Cacawan in the barrio of San Agapito in Isla Verde, Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, are hereby converted to a barrio to be known as Barrio San Antonio of the same municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
