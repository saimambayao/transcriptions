> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1213**
>
> **An Act Repealing Item Thirty-Four (e), Subsection (a), Title C of Section One of Republic Act Numbered Nine Hundred and Twenty, and Appropriating the Amount of Forty-Five Thousand Pesos Therein Provided for the Construction of the Calanan-Tuga-Pinukpuk Road in Mountain Province**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item thirty-four (e), subsection (a), Title C of section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred and twenty, on page one hundred and fourteen thereof, is hereby repealed, and the sum of forty-five thousand pesos therein provided is hereby appropriated for the construction of the Calanan-Tuga-Pinukpuk Road in Mountain Province.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 10, 1955.
