> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1422**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of the Kabankalan Elementary School, Situated at Kabankalan, Negros Occidental, to Esteban R. Abada Memorial School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Kabankalan Elementary School situated at Kabankalan, Negros Occidental, is hereby named and to be known henceforth as the Esteban R. Abada Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Director of Public Schools shall effect the change immediately upon the approval in this Act.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 10, 1956.
