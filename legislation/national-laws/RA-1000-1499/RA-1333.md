> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1333**
>
> **An Act Changing the Names of Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Naval, Province of Leyte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The names of the following barrios in the Municipality of Naval, Province of Leyte, are changed as follows:
>
> Barrio Catmon to barrio Santo Rosario; barrio Agpangi to barrio La Purisima Concepcion; barrio Lico to barrio San Vicente; barrio Talostusan to barrio Santa Fe; barrio Locso-on to barrio San Isidro; and barrio Villa Caneja to barrio Sulphur Valley.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
