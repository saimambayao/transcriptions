> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1033**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio of Ondol in the Municipality of Mabini, Province of Bohol, to San Jose**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Barrio of Ondol in the municipality of Mabini, Province of Bohol, is hereby changed to San Jose.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
