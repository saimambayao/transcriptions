> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1282**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitios of Tuno, Jubay, and Napantao, Municipality of San Francisco, Province of Leyte, into Barrios of Said Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Tuno, Jubay, and Napantao in the Municipality of San Francisco, Province of Leyte, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Tuno, Jubay, and Napantao, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 14, 1955.
