> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1367**
>
> **An Act Appropriating the Sum of Three Hundred Thousand Pesos for the Construction of a Leprosy Research Laboratory and Training Center Building and for the Expenses of its Equipment and Maintenance**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the sum of three hundred thousand pesos for the construction of an adequate leprosy research laboratory and training center building and for the expenses of its equipment and maintenance.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1955.
