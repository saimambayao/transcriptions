> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1494**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item B-031.13, Appendix A of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item B-031.13, Appendix A of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "B-031.13: GR: 40: 20: 10: 10: -: -: -: b 20: -:1: -: -Construction of a Pinamalayan-Lumangbayan Road Junction-Macanlig Puntod (3 Kms.), Construction road connecting barrios Macanlig and Puntod to Pinamalayan-Lumangbayan Road."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1956.
