> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1324**
>
> **An Act to Create the Barrio of Bungahan in the City of Lipa**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Duhatan, Tagbakin, Initan, Malabong and Halang, all in the barrio of Pinagtungolan, City of Lipa, are separated from the said barrio, and constituted into a separate barrio in the said city to be known as the barrio of Bungahan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
