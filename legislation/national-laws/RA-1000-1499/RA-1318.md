> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1318**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Sabani, Province of Nueva Ecija, to Gabaldon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Sabani, Province of Nueva Ecija, is hereby changed to Gabaldon.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
