> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1100**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Sitio of Hinangayon, Mogpog, Marinduque, into a Barrio to be Known as the Barrio of Hinangayon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Hinangayon, barrio of Argao, municipality of Mogpog, Province of Marinduque, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of Hinangayon.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1954.
