> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1454**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Provisions of Republic Act Numbered Twelve Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (one), paragraph "bq" for the City of Manila, section two of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(1) Construction of sixteen-room concrete administration building with office and (one) library, Torres High School (1st district) | ₱200,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** Item (two), paragraph "bq" for the City of Manila of section two of the same Act is repealed.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 14, 1956.
