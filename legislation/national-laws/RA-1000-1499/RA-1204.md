> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1204**
>
> **An Act to Amend the First Paragraph of Section Twenty-Four of Act Numbered Thirty-Nine Hundred and Ninety-Two, as Amended**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The first paragraph of section twenty-four of Act Numbered Thirty-nine hundred and ninety-two, as amended, is further amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Sec. 24. *Drivers license, fees, examination.* -Every person who desires personally to operate any motor vehicle should make application to the Chief, Motor Vehicles Office, or his deputies for a license to drive motor vehicles: *Provided,* *However*, That no person shall be issued a professional drivers license unless he has first obtained a police clearance, which shall be issued without charge or fee, from the police department of the city or municipality in which he resides: And *Provided, further,* That no person should be issued a professional drivers license who is suffering from highly contagious diseases, such as, advanced tuberculosis, gonorrhea, syphilis, and the like".
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 20, 1955.
