> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1487**
>
> **An Act Amending Certain Items of Republic Acts Numbered Twelve Hundred and Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item one hundred two, paragraph m, section two of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(102) Construction of Home Economics Building, San Vicente (Sur) Barrio School | ₱1,000.00" |
>
> **Section 2.** Items fifty-eight and sixty of paragraph m, group II, section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, are hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(58) San Vicente (Sur) Barrio School, Home Economics Building, Iriga Construction | ₱3,000.00 |
> | "(60) San Nicolas School Building, Completion | 1,800.00" |
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1956.
