> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1366**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitio of Son-oc, in the Municipality of Cortes, Province of Bohol, to a Barrio to be Known as Barrio Fatima**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Son-oc, in the Municipality of Cortes, Province of Bohol, is hereby converted to a barrio to be known as barrio Fatima.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1955.
