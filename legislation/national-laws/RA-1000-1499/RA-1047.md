> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1047**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Tarug Barrio School in the Barrio of Tarug, Municipality of Alimodian, Province of Iloilo, to Amaquin Memorial School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Tarug Barrio School in the Barrio of Tarug, Municipality of Alimodian, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Amaquin Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
