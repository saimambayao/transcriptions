> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1102**
>
> **An Act Fixing the Boundary Line Between the Municipality of Tagum and the Municipalities of Doña Alicia and Compostela, Province of Davao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The boundary line between the municipality of Tagum and Doña Alicia shall be the Dumlan River from the Gulf of Davao to the source of said river and a straight line from said source running northwestward to the concrete monument in the sitio of Libaybay. The boundary line between the municipalities of Tagum and Compostela shall be the Hijo River.
>
> **Section 2.** Executive Order No. 596, series of 1953, is amended accordingly.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1954.
