> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1329**
>
> **An Act to Create the Barrios of Uba, Mabahin, Tag-Anongan and Manlico, in the Municipality of Cortes, Province of Surigao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Uba, Mabahin, Tag-anongan and Manlico, in the Municipality of Cortes, Province of Surigao, are hereby constituted into barrios in the same municipality, to be known as Uba, Mabahin, Tag-anongan and Manlico, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
