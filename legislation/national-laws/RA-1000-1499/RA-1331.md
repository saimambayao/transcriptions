> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1331**
>
> **An Act to Convert the Sitio of San Vicente in the Municipality of Ivana, Province of Batanes, into a Barrio to be Known as the Barrio of San Vicente of Said Municipality**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of San Vicente in the Municipality of Ivana, Province of Batanes, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio San Vicente of said municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
