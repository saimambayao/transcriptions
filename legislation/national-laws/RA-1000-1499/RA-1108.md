> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1108**
>
> **An Act to Create the Barrios of Lingasad, Silawe, Isis, and Loboc in the Municipality of Polanco, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Lingasad, Silawe, Isis, and Loboc in the municipality of Polancio, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are hereby constituted into barrios in the said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Lingasad, Silawe, Isis, and Loboc, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1954.
