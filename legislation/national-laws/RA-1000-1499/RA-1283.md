> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1283**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of San Roque in the Municipality of Unisan, Province of Quezon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of San Roque in the barrio of Tagumpay, Municipality of Unisan, Province of Quezon, is hereby separated from the barrio of Tagumpay and constituted into a separate and independent barrio of the same municipality, to be known as barrio San Roque.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 14, 1955.
