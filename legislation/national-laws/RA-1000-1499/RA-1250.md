> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1250**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Moron, Province of Bataan, to Morong**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The seat of the Municipality of Sto. Tomas, Province of Pampanga, is hereby transferred from Barrio San Matias to Barrio Sto. Tomas.1awp++i1 The municipal government shall occupy the site and building it formerly occupied before the said municipality was annexed to the Municipality of San Fernando in the same province.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 10, 1955.
