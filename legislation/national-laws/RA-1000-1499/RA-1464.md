> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1464**
>
> **An Act Amending Item One, Subparagraph I, Under Provincial Capitols, Paragraph Four, Section One of Republic Act Numbered Twelve Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item one, subparagraph i, paragraph four, section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is amended to read as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "1. Nueva Vizcaya Provincial Capitol(1) Construction of Annex to Office of Provincial Governor | ₱5,000.00." |
>
> **Section 2.** Insert the following item as Item seven, subparagraph am, paragraph four, section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred to compensate for the deletion in the preceding section:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | "(7) Diffun Municipal Town HallConstruction | ₱10,000.00." |
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 14, 1956.
