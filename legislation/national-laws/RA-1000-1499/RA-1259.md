> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1259**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of Barrio Bugsayon, Municipality of Masbate, Province of Masbate, to Barrio B. Titong**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In recognition of the eminent and patriotic services of the late Benigno Titong, the name of Barrio Bugsayon, Municipality of Masbate, Province of Masbate, is changed to barrio B. Titong.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 10, 1955.
