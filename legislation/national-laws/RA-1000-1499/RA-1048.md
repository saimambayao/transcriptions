> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1048**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of Tago Central Elementary School to Falcon Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Tago, Province of Surigao**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In recognition of the philanthropic services of Bernardo Falcon to the municipality of Tago, Province Surigao, the name of Tago Central Elementary School is hereby changed to Falcon Central Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
