> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1036**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of the Naipit Community School in the Barrio of Bulauan, Municipality of Prieto Diaz, Province of Sorsogon, to Bulauan Community School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Naipit Community School in the Barrio of Bulauan, Municipality of Prieto Diaz, Province of Sorsogon, is hereby changed to Bulauan Community School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 12, 1954.
