> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 1317**
>
> **An Act Converting the Sitios of Lidong, Taba-Taba, Manmontoy, Borabod, Taisan, Hinampacan, Manlimonsito, Polantuna, and Oliva in the Municipality of Basud, Province of Camarines Norte, into Barrios of Said Municipality with the Same Names**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Lidong, Taba-taba, Manmontoy, Borabod, Taisan, Hinampacan, Manlimonsito, Polantuna, and Oliva in the Municipality of Basud, Province of Camarines Norte, are converted into barrios of said municipality with the same names.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1955.
