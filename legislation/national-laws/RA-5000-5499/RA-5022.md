> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5022**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Bagoñgon Elementary School in the Municipality of Concepcion, Province of Iloilo, to Narciso Centeno Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Bagoñgon Elementary School in the Municipality of Concepcion, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Narciso Centeno Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
