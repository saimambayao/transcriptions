> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5089**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Kalamansig Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Kalamansig, Province of Cotabato, to Datu Guiabar Central Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Kalamansig Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Kalamansig, Province of Cotabato, is hereby changed to Datu Guiabar Central Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
