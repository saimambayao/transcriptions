> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5026**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Salangan Elementary School in the Municipality of San Miguel, Province of Bulacan, into Mababang Paaralang Pang-Alaala Kay Dr. Juan Pascual
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Salangan Elementary School in Barrio Salangan, Municipality of San Miguel, Province of Bulacan, is hereby changed into Mababang Paaralang Pang-alaala kay Dr. Juan Pascual.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
