> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5155**
>
> An Act Constituting the Fifth Representative District of the Province of Pangasinan into a Separate Highway Engineering District
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Any provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, the fifth representative district of the Province of Pangasinan is hereby constituted into a separate highway engineering district which shall hereafter be known as the Second Highway Engineering District of the Province of Pangasinan. The office of the second highway engineering district shall be established in the Municipality of Tayug of said province.
>
> **Section 2.** The second highway engineering district shall have a highway district engineer who shall have the qualification of a Supervising Civil Engineer I, and such other personnel who shall be appointed in accordance with existing laws and regulations.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
