> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5042**
>
> An Act Amending Item (cb), Page 465, Group IV, Subparagraph 29, for the Province of Leyte, Paragraph "c", Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Seven Hundred One
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (cb), page 465, group IV, subparagraph 29, for the Province of Leyte, paragraph "c", Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred one is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(cb) Tigbao Bridge Tacloban City Construction ₱100,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
