> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5073**
>
> An Act Authorizing the Appropriation of an Additional One Hundred Fifty Thousand Pesos for the Operation and Maintenance of the Cagayan Valley School of Arts and Trades in the Municipality of Tuguegarao, Province of Cagayan
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** An additional sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the Cagayan Valley School of Arts and Trades in the Municipality of Tuguegarao, Province of Cagayan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
