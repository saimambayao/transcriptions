> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5400**
>
> An Act Creating Barrio Tapalan in the Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Tapalan in Barrio Palandoc, Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is separated from said barrio and converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Tapalan. The said barrio shall be bounded as follows:
>
> 1. North-Starting from the Northeastern corner of Lot No. 2711, going counterclockwise, proceed to Lot No. 2716, 2725, 2726, 2728 and 2729;
>
> 2. East-Take all areas traversed by Palandoc River;
>
> 3. South-By Pls-742;
>
> 4. West-Take all areas traversed by Lipakan River.
>
> All areas within bounds of delineation comprise the proposed Barrio Tapalan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
