> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5377**
>
> An Act Naming the Longest Street in Barrio San Isidro, Municipality of San Francisco, Province of Cebu, the Quirino Maningo Road
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The longest street in Barrio San Isidro, Municipality of San Francisco, Province of Cebu, is hereby named Quirino Maningo Road.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
