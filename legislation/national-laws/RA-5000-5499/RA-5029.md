> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5029**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Calabasa Elementary School in the Municipality of Labo, Province of Camarines Norte, to Paaralang Bayan ng Eugenio Dasco, Sr.
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Calabasa Elementary School in the Municipality of Labo, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Paaralang Bayan ng Eugenio Dasco Sr.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
