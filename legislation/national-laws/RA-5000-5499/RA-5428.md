> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5428**
>
> An Act Amending Section Three of Republic Act Numbered Forty-Nine Hundred and Sixty-Seven, and for Other Purposes
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section three of Republic Act Numbered Forty-nine hundred and sixty-seven is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Sec. 3. The life of the Abaca and Other Fibers Development Board shall be deemed extended up to June thirty, nineteen hundred and seventy-two."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
