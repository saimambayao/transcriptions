> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5437**
>
> An Act Granting Philippine Citizenship to Patricia B. Rosal
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Patricia V. Rosal is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives appurtenant thereto.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: September 9, 1968.
