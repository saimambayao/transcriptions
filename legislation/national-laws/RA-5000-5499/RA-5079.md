> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5079**
>
> An Act Amending Item (27), Page 135, Subparagraph 38, for Mountain Province, Paragraph "a", Title B, Section One of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-One Hundred One
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (27), Page 135, subparagraph 38, for Mountain Province, paragraph "a", Title B, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-one hundred one is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(27) Taloctoc Mananga Trail ₱8,000"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
