> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5020**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Nasugbu Central School in the Municipality of Nasugbu, Province of Batangas, to Nasugbu West Central School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Nasugbu Central School in the Municipality of Nasugbu, Province of Batangas, is hereby changed to Nasugbu West Central School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
