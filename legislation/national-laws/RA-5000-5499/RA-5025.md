> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5025**
>
> An Act Amending Item (52), Page 20, Subparagraph 5, for the Province of Antique, Paragraph (c), Title A, Section Two, of Republic Act Numbered Twenty Three Hundred and One
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (52), Page 20, Subparagraph 5, for the Province of Antique, Paragraph (c), Title A, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred and one is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(52) San Francisco Elementary School Home Economics Building, Aniniy ₱5,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
