> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5287**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Nalasin in the Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, to General Artemio Ricarte
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Nalasin in the Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to General Artemio Ricarte.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
