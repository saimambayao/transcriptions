> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5024**
>
> An Act Authorizing the Appropriation of Fifty Thousand Pesos for the Improvement of Argao Breeding Station in the Municipality of Argao, Province of Cebu
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the improvement of Argao Breeding Station in the Municipality of Argao, Province of Cebu.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
