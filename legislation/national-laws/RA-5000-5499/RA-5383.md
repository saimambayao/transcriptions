> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5383**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Jepaca Barrio School in Barrio Jelicuon Lusaya, Municipality of Cabatuan, Province of Iloilo, to Pablo Muyuela Barrio School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Jepaca Barrio School in Barrio Jelicuon Lusaya, Municipality of Cabatuan, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Pablo Muyuela Barrio School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
