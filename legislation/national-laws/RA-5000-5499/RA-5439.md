> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5439**
>
> An Act Constituting the Second Representative District of the Province of Albay into a Separate Highway Engineering District
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Any provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, the Second Representative District of the Province of Albay, comprising the municipalities of Rapu-Rapu, Manito, Daraga and Camalig, is hereby constituted into a separate highway engineering district. The office of the district highway engineer shall be in Legaspi City.
>
> The Legaspi City Engineer shall concurrently be the ex officio highway district engineer for said engineering district.
>
> The Second Highway Engineering District herein created shall have jurisdiction of the funds for public works projects within the municipalities comprised in the said engineering district only.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: September 9, 1968.
