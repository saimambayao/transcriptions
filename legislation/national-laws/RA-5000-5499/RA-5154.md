> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5154**
>
> An Act Creating the Barrio of Inga of Maracuru in the Municipality of Enrile, Province of Cagayan
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Inga of Maracuru in the Municipality of Enrile, Province of Cagayan, is hereby converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the Barrio of Inga of Maracuru.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
