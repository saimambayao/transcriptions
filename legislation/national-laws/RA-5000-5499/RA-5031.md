> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5031**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Tanay Elementary School Building No. 1 in the Municipality of Tanay, Province of Rizal, to Patricio Jarin Memorial Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Tanay Elementary School Building No. 1 in the Municipality of Tanay, Province of Rizal, is hereby changed to Patricio Jarin Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
