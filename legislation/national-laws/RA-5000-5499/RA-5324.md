> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5324**
>
> An Act Creating the Barrio of Toketek in the Municipality of Tayug, Province of Pangasinan
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Toketek in the Municipality of Tayug, Province of Pangasinan, is hereby converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as Barrio Toketek.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
