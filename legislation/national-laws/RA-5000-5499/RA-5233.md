> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5233**
>
> An Act Including Nueva Era, Ilocos Norte, Within the Sphere and/or Jurisdiction of the Commission on National Integration
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The town of Nueva Era, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby included within the sphere and/or jurisdiction of the Commission on National Integration.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
