> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5286**
>
> An Act Creating Four Additional Positions of Assistant Provincial Fiscal for the Province of Occidental Misamis, Amending for the Purpose Section Sixteen Hundred Seventy-Four of the Administrative Code
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The provisions of Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Administrative Code, as amended, relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals in the Province of Occidental Misamis, is further amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Occidental Misamis, six assistant provincial fiscals;"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
