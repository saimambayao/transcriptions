> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5340**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Tolosa Central Community School in the Municipality of Tolosa, Province of Leyte, to Daniel Z. Romualdez Memorial School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Tolosa Central Community School in the municipality of Tolosa, Province of Leyte, is hereby changed to Daniel Z. Romualdez Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
