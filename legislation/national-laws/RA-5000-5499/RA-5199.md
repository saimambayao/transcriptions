> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5199**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Kabankalan Maternity Hospital in the Municipality of Kabankalan, Province of Negros Occidental, to Dr. Gumersindo Garcia, Sr. Memorial Hospital
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Kabankalan Maternity Hospital, in the Municipality of Kabankalan, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Dr. Gumersindo Garcia, Sr. Memorial Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 27, 1968.
