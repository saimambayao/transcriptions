> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5395**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Masbate North Elementary School in the Municipality of Masbate, Province of Masbate, to Amancio Aguilar Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Masbate North Elementary School in the Municipality of Masbate, Province of Masbate, is hereby changed to Amancio Aguilar Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
