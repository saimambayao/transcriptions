> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5276**
>
> An Act Transferring the Seat of Government of the Municipality of Ramon, Province of Isabela, from Barrio Burgos to Barrio Bugallon of Said Municipality, Amending for the Purpose Republic Act Numbered Thirty-Three Hundred and Twenty
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section one of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-three hundred and twenty is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Sec. 1. Barrios Bugallon, Burgos, San Miguel, San Sebastian, Raniag and Pagrang-ayan are separated from the Municipality of Santiago, and Barrios Oscariz, Nagbacalan and Bantug Petinez are separated from the Municipality of San Mateo, all in the Province of Isabela, and constituted into a new and separate municipality of said province to be known as the Municipality of Ramon. The seat of government of the municipality shall be located at the present site of Barrio Bugallon.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
