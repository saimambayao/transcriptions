> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5205**
>
> An Act Fixing the Salaries of Certain Positions in the Veterans Memorial Hospital
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The WAPCO classification and pay plan shall be adjusted to the following salaries of certain positions in the Veterans Memorial Hospital, which are hereby fixed as follows:
>
> (a) Director, fifteen thousand pesos, per annum;
>
> (b) Assistant Director, fourteen thousand five hundred pesos, per annum;
>
> (c) Chief of Professional Staff, fourteen thousand pesos, per annum;
>
> (d) Medical Specialist III, thirteen thousand pesos, per annum;
>
> (e) Medical Specialist II, twelve thousand pesos, per annum;
>
> (f) Hospital Department Head, twelve thousand pesos per annum;
>
> (g) Medical Specialist I, eleven thousand pesos, per annum;
>
> (h) Senior Resident Physician and Clinic Supervisor, nine thousand pesos, per annum;
>
> (i) Resident Physician, Clinic Physician and Junior Physician, seven thousand pesos, per annum;
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
