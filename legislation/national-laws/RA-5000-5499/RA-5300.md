> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5300**
>
> An Act Making the Municipality of Solana, Province of Cagayan, as the Seat of the Highway Engineering District of the Second Congressional District of the Said Province
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Municipality of Solana, Province of Cagayan, is hereby made the seat of the highway engineering district of the second congressional district of the same province.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
