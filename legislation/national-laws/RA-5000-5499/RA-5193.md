> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5193**
>
> An Act Creating the Municipality of Victoria in the Province of Samar
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby created in the Province of Samar a municipality to be known as the Municipality of Victoria, which shall consist of the portion of the Municipality of Allen of the same province, comprising barrios Mawo, Colab-of, San Lazaro, Buenos Aires, Maxvilla, Buena Suerte and San Miguel; and that portion of the Municipality of San Isidro, also of the same province, comprising barrios Erenas, Acedillo and San Ramon.
>
> **Section 2.** The Municipality of Victoria shall have its seat of government in Barrio Mawo.
>
> **Section 3.** The Municipalities of Allen and San Isidro shall retain their present territories minus the portion included in the Municipality of Victoria.
>
> **Section 4.** The first mayor, vice-mayor, and councilors of the Municipality of Victoria, shall be elected in the next general elections for local officials.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 27, 1968.
