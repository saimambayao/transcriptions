> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5311**
>
> An Act Changing the Names of Certain Schools in the Municipality of Dingle, Province of Iloilo
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Licu-an Elementary School in the Municipality of Dingle, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to San Matias Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** The name of Agtatacay Elementary School also in the Municipality of Dingle, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Agtatacay-Gutao Elementary School.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
