> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5308**
>
> An Act Naming that Portion of the North Diversion Road Passing Through the Province of Bulacan "Marcelo H. Del Pilar Highway"
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** That portion of the North Diversion Road passing through the territorial jurisdiction of the Province of Bulacan is hereby named "Marcelo H. del Pilar Highway."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
