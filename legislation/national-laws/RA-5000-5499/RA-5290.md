> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5290**
>
> An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Mawab, Province of Davao
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Mawab, Province of Davao, are constituted as barrios of said municipality, as follows:
>
> (a) The sitios of San Isidro and Mahayag are constituted into a district and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Katipunan;
>
> (b) The sitio of Lunoluno is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Lunoluno;
>
> (c) The sitio of Annaman is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Tan-awan;
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
