> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5438**
>
> An Act Amending Subparagraph Twenty of Article Fourteen of the Revised Penal Code
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Subparagraph twenty of Article fourteen of the Revised Penal Code is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "20. That the crime be committed with the aid of persons under fifteen years of age or by means of motor vehicles, motorized watercraft, airships, or other similar means."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: September 9, 1968.
