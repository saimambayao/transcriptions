> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5408**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Barrio San Magro in the Municipality of Marcos, Province of Ilocos Norte, to Barrio Santiago
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio San Magro in the Municipality of Marcos, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Santiago.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
