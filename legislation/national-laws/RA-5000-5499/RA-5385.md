> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5385**
>
> An Act Constituting the Second Representative District of the Province of Bulacan into a Separate Highway Engineering District
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Any provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, the Second Representative District of the Province of Bulacan is hereby constituted into a separate highway engineering district. The office of the highway district engineer shall be in the Municipality of Santa Maria.
>
> The highway district engineer for said highway engineering district shall be appointed in accordance with existing laws.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
