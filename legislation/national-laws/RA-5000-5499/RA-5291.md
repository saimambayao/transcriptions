> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5291**
>
> An Act Creating Additional Positions of Assistant Provincial Fiscal in the Province of Ilocos Norte, Amending for the Purpose Section Sixteen Hundred and Seventy-Four of the Administrative Code, as Amended
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section sixteen hundred and seventy-four of the Administrative Code, as amended, relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals in the Province of Ilocos Norte, is further amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Ilocos Norte, twelve assistant provincial fiscals, at least two of whom shall be assigned to each branch of the Court of First Instance of Ilocos Norte.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
