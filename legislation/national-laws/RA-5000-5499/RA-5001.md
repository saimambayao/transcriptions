> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5001**
>
> An Act Creating the Barrio of Siko in Lipa City
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Siko in the barrio of Tambo, Lipa City, is hereby separated from said barrio, and converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said city, to be known as the barrio of Siko.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
