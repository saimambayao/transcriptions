> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5390**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Barrio San Jose Sur in the Municipality of Talibon, Province of Bohol, to Barrio Santo Niño
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio San Jose Sur in the Municipality of Talibon, Province of Bohol, is hereby changed to Barrio Santo Niño.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
