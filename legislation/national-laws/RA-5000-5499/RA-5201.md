> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5201**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Binacag Elementary School in the Municipality of Espiritu, Province of Ilocos Norte, to Quiaoit Memorial Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Binacag Elementary School in the Municipality of Espiritu, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Quiaoit Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 27, 1968.
