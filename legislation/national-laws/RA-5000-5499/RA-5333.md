> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5333**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Dao Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Dao, Province of Antique, to Tobias Fornier Memorial Hospital
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Dao Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Dao, Province of Antique, is hereby changed and hereafter to be known as the Tobias Fornier Memorial Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
