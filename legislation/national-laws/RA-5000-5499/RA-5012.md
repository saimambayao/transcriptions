> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5012**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Borongan Pilot Elementary School in the Municipality of Borongan, Province of Samar, to Eugenio S. Daza Pilot Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Borongan Pilot Elementary School in the Municipality of Borongan, Province of Samar, is hereby changed to Eugenio S. Daza Pilot Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
