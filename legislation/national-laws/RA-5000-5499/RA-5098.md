> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5098**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Sipalay Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Sipalay, Province of Negros Occidental, to Genaro P. Alvarez Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Sipalay Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Sipalay, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Genaro P. Alvarez Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
