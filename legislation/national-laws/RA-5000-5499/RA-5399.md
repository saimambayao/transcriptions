> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5399**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Dinokot in the Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, to Barrio Ricardo G. Macias
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Dinokot in the Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Ricardo G. Macias.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
