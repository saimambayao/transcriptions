> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5141**
>
> An Act Increasing the Bed Capacity of the Nueva Ecija Provincial Hospital at Cabanatuan City to Four Hundred Beds and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Nueva Ecija Provincial hospital at Cabanatuan City is hereby increased to four hundred beds.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two million pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation, improvement and/or construction of new buildings.1avvphi1 Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
