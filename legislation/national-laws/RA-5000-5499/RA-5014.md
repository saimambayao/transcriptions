> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5014**
>
> An Act Establishing an Animal Breeding Station in the Municipality of Padada, Province of Davao, to be Known as Padada Animal Breeding Station, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established, under the supervision of the Director of Animal Industry, an animal breeding station in the Municipality of Padada, Province of Davao, to be known as Padada Animal Breeding Station.
>
> **Section 2.** The Director of Animal Industry shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of the said animal breeding station. Said sum shall be expended by the Director of the Animal Industry, subject to the approval of the Secretary of Agriculture and National Resources, for the purpose of breeding animals, supplies and materials; for the construction of buildings, sheds, corals and other structures for salaries and wages of personnel; and for such other expenses as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
