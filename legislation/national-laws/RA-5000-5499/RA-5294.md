> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5294**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Maternity and Childrens Hospital to Dr. Jose Fabella Memorial Hospital
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Maternity and Children's Hospital in the City of Manila under the Department of Health is hereby changed to Dr. Jose Fabella Memorial Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
