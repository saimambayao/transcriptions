> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5027**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Northern Padada Elementary School in the Barrio of Guihing, Municipality of Hagonoy, Province of Davao, to Guihing Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Northern Padada Elementary School in the barrio of Guihing. Municipality of Hagonoy, Province of Davao, is hereby changed to Guihing Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
