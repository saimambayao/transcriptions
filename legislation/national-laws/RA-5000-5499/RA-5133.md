> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5133**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Siran Primary School in Barrio Abad Santos, Municipality of Guagua, Province of Pampanga, to Abad Santos Primary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Siran Primary School in Barrio Abad Santos, Municipality of Guagua, Province of Pampanga, is hereby changed to, and shall hereafter be known as, Abad Santos Primary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
