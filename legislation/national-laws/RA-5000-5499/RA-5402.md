> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5402**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Palandoc in the Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, to Barrio Ramon Magsaysay
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Palandoc in the Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Ramon Magsaysay.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
