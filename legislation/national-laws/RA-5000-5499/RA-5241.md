> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5241**
>
> An Act Abolishing the Requirement of Non-Subversive Affidavits by Officers of Labor Organizations, Repealing for the Purpose Subparagraph (2), Subsection, (b), Section Twenty-Three of Republic Act Numbered Eight Hundred Seventy-Five
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Subparagraph (2), subsection (b) of Section twenty-three of Republic Act Numbered Eight hundred seventy-five, entitled "an Act to promote industrial peace and for other purposes," requiring non-subversive affidavits by all the officers of every labor organization, association or union is hereby repealed. All other provisions in said Republic Act referring to such non-subversive affidavits are likewise hereby repealed.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** All Acts, parts of Acts, and regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
