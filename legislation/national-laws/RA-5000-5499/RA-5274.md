> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5274**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Paoay Elementary School in the Municipality of Atok, Province of Benguet, to Celo Haight Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Paoay Elementary School located in the Municipality of Atok, Province of Benguet, is hereby changed to Celo Haight Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
