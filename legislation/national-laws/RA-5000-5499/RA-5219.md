> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5219**
>
> An Act Repealing Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Nine Hundred and Fifty-Nine, Entitled "An Act Granting Pedro Bangug a Franchise for an Electric Light, Heat and Power System in the Municipality of Cabagan, Province of Isabela"
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Republic Act Numbered Twenty-nine hundred and fifty-nine, entitled "An Act granting Pedro Bangug a franchise for an electric light, heat and power system in the Municipality of Cabagan, Province of Isabela," is hereby repealed.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
