> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5018**
>
> An Act Amending Republic Act Numbered Six Hundred Eighty-Five by Transferring the Branch of the Philippine Institute of Fisheries Technology from Butuan City to Barrio Matabao, Municipality of Buenavista, Province of Agusan
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (b) of sub-paragraph ten, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Six hundred eighty-five is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > (b) Matabao, Buenavista, Agusan ₱50,000"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
