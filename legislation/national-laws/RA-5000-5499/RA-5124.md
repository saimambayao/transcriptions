> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5124**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Culilang Barrio Elementary School in Barrio Culilang, Municipality of President Roxas, Province of Capiz, to Vedasto T. Javelosa Memorial Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Culilang Elementary School in Barrio Culilang, Municipality of President Roxas, Province of Capiz, is hereby changed to Vedasto T. Javelosa Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
