> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5111**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Tungusung Elementary School in the Municipality of Simunul, Province of Sulu, to Panglima Hussin Arupin Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In recognition of the many benevolent acts rendered by the late Panglima Hussin Arupin for the cause of education of his people in his native island of Simunul, Province of Sulu, the name Tungusung Elementary School in the Municipality of Simunul, Province of Sulu, is hereby changed to Panglima Hussin Arupin Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
