> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5055**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Pio Duran Memorial School in the Municipality of Guinobatan, Province of Albay, to Marcial O. Rañola Memorial School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Pio Duran Memorial School in the Municipality of Guinobatan, Province of Albay, is hereby changed to Marcial O. Rañola Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
