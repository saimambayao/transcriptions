> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5160**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Subaan Barrio Elementary School in the Municipality of Socorro, Province of Oriental Mindoro, to Ginhawa Barrio Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Subaan Barrio Elementary School in the Municipality of Socorro, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is hereby changed to Ginhawa Barrio Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
