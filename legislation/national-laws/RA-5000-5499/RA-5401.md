> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5401**
>
> An Act Creating a Barrio Rambon in the Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Rambon in the Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Rambon. Said barrio shall be bounded as follows:
>
> 1. North-Salug River;
>
> 2. East-Matingon Creek;
>
> 3. South-Starting from corner of Lot 3391, 3392, 3393, 3398 and 3447, thence draw a straight line due East until it reaches Matingon Creek;
>
> 4. West-Guisapong Creek.
>
> All areas within bounds of delineation comprise the proposed barrio of Rambon.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
