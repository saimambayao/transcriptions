> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5237**
>
> An Act Creating Two Addition Branches of the City Court of Ozamis, Amending for the Purpose Section Seventy-Five of the Charter of Said City
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The first paragraph of Section seventy-five of Republic Act Numbered Three hundred thirty-one, otherwise known as the Charter of the City of Ozamis, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Sec. 75. *Regular, auxiliary and acting judges of city courts.* There shall be a city for the City of Ozamis for which there shall be appointed three judges and auxiliary city judge."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
