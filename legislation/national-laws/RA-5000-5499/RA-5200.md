> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5200**
>
> An Act Granting the Name of Lipay Elementary School in the Municipality of Solsona, Province of Ilocos Norte, to Nalipasan Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Lipay Elementary School in the Municipality of Solsona, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Nalipasan Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 27, 1968.
