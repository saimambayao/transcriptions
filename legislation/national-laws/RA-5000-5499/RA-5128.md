> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5128**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Cebu Normal School in Cebu City to Cebu Normal College
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Cebu Normal School in Cebu City is hereby changed to Cebu Normal College.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
