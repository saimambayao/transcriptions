> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 5403**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Barrio No. 1 Caoayan, Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, to Barrio Hilario Valdez
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name Barrio No. 1, Caoayan, Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Hilario Valdez.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 15, 1968.
