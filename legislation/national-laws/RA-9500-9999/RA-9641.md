# Republic Act No. 9641

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF KAPITAN RAMON NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY KAPITAN RAMON CITY OF SILAY PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL TO DON FELIX T. LACSON MEMORIAL, NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Second Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, two thousand eight.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9641**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF KAPITAN RAMON NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY KAPITAN RAMON CITY OF SILAY PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL TO DON FELIX T. LACSON MEMORIAL, NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Renaming of a National High School.* - The name of Kapitan Ramon National High School in Barangay Kapitan Ramon, City of Silay, Province of Negros Occidental is hereby changed to ***Don Felix T. Lacson Memorial National High School.***
>
> **Section 2.** *Effectivity.* - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the official Gazette.
>
