# Republic Act No. 9752

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE DEMOLOC-LITTLE BAGUIO-ALABEL ROAD LINKING THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALITA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR AND THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALABEL, PROVINCE OF SARANGANI INTO A NATIONAL ROAD**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9752

> Fourteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9752**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE DEMOLOC-LITTLE BAGUIO-ALABEL ROAD LINKING THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALITA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR AND THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALABEL, PROVINCE OF SARANGANI INTO A NATIONAL ROAD
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Demoloc-Little Baguio-Alabel Road linking the Municipality of Malita, Province of Davao del Sur and the Municipality of Alabel, Province of Sarangani is hereby converted into a national road.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
