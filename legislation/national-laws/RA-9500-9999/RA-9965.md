# Republic Act No. 9965

**AN ACT RENAMING THE BACACA ROAD WHICH CONNECTS J.P. LAUREL STREET AND CARLOS P. GARCIA HIGHWAY THAT LEADS TO DAVAO CITY INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT AS FRANCISCO S. DIZON ROAD**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9965
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2010/ra_9965_2010.html)

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty - seventh day of July, two thousand nine.
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9965**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE BACACA ROAD WHICH CONNECTS J.P. LAUREL STREET AND CARLOS P. GARCIA HIGHWAY THAT LEADS TO DAVAO CITY INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT AS FRANCISCO S. DIZON ROAD
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Bacaca Road which connects J.P. Laurel Street and Carlos P. Garcia Highway that leads to Davao City International Airport shall hereinafter be known as ***Francisco S. Dizon Road.***
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
