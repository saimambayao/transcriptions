# Republic Act No. 9706

**AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 29 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALILIHAN, PROVINCE OF BOHOL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9706
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2009/ra_9706_2009.html)

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Second Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, two thousand eight.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9706               August 07, 2009**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 29 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALILIHAN, PROVINCE OF BOHOL
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** September 29 of every year as a special nonworking public holiday in the Municipality of Balilihan, province of Bohol to commemorate its *Sumad* Day or foundation anniversary.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days from its publication in the Official Gazette or in at least two (2) newspaper of general circulation.
>
