# Republic Act No. 9528

**AN ACT SEPARATING THE NUEVO DE FEBRERO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL-PLEASANT HILLS ANNEX IN BARANGAY PLEASANT HILLS, CITY OF MANDALUYONG FROM THE NUEVO DE FEBRERO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, CONVERTING IT INTO AN INDEPENDENT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL TO BE KNOW AS THE PLEASANT HILLS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Second Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, two thousand eight.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9528             March 24, 2009**
>
> **AN ACT SEPARATING THE NUEVO DE FEBRERO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL-PLEASANT HILLS ANNEX IN BARANGAY PLEASANT HILLS, CITY OF MANDALUYONG FROM THE NUEVO DE FEBRERO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, CONVERTING IT INTO AN INDEPENDENT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL TO BE KNOW AS THE PLEASANT HILLS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*:
>
> **Section 1. *Separation and Convertion into an Independent Elementary School.*** - The Nuevo de Febrero Elementary School-Pleasant Hills Annex in Barangay Pleasant Hills, City of Mandaluyong is hereby separated from Nuevo de Febrero Elementary School and converted into an independent elementary school to be known as the Pleasant Hills Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2. *Transfer of Assets and Liabilities.*** - All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Nuevo de Febrero Elementary School-Pleasant Hills Annex are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Pleasant Hills Elementary School.
>
> **Section 3. *Appropriations.*** - The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the Pleasant Hills Elementary School, the initial funding if which shall be charged against the current year's appropriations of the Nuevo de Febrero Elementary School-Pleasant Hills Annex. Thereafter, the amount necessary for the continued operation of the school shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 4. *Implementation.*** - The Secretary of Education shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 5. *Effectivity.*** - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette.*
>
