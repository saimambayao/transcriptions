# Republic Act No. 9949

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY EFEGENIO LIZARES, CITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL TO BE KNOWN AS GUINTOLOAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9949             January 15, 2010**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY EFEGENIO LIZARES, CITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL TO BE KNOWN AS GUINTOLOAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Establishment of a National High School.* &mdash There shall be established, under the supervision of the Department of Education, a national high school in Barangay Efegenio Lizares, City of Talisay, Province of Negros Occidental to be known as Efegenio Lizares National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** *Appropriations.* &mdash The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of Efegenio Lizares National High School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** *Implementation.* &mdash The Secretary of Education shall issue rules and regulations that may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 4.** *Effectivity.* &mdash This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette.*
>
