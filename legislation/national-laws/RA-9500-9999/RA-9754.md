# Republic Act No. 9754

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN NARCISO VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN SAN NARCISO, PROVINCE OF QUEZON TO GODOFREDO M. TAN MEMORIAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9754
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2009/ra_9754_2009.html)

---

> Fourteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9754**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN NARCISO VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN SAN NARCISO, PROVINCE OF QUEZON TO GODOFREDO M. TAN MEMORIAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Renaming of High School.* - The name of San Narciso Vocational High School in the Municipality of San Narciso, Province of Quezon is hereby changed to ***Godofredo M. Tan Memorial School of Arts and Trades.***
>
> **Section 2.** *Effectivity.* - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
>
