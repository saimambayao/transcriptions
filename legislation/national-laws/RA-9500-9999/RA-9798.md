# Republic Act No. 9798

**AN ACT RENAMING THE PANGASINAN COLLEGE OF FISHERIES (PCF) AS THE PANGASINAN TECHNOLOGICAL INSTITUTE (PTI)**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9798
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2009/ra_9798_2009.html)

---

> Fourteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9798**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE PANGASINAN COLLEGE OF FISHERIES (PCF) AS THE PANGASINAN TECHNOLOGICAL INSTITUTE (PTI)
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Pangasinan College of Fisheries (PCF) is hereby renamed as the ***Pangasinan Technological Institute (PTI).***
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
> Approved,
