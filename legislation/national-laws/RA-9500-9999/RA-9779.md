# Republic Act No. 9779

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LAMO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY LAMO, MUNICIPALITY OF DUPAX DEL NORTE, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA TO DUPAX DEL NORTE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

> Fourteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9779**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LAMO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY LAMO, MUNICIPALITY OF DUPAXDELNORTE, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA TO DUPAX DEL NORTE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Renaming of a National High School.* - The name of Lama National High School in Barangay Lama, Municipality of Dupax del Norte, Province of Nueva Vizcaya is hereby changed to ***Dupax del Norte National High School.***
>
> **Section 2.** *Location of the Main Campus.* - The main campus of the Dupax del Norte National High School shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Lama, Municipality of Dupax del Norte, Province of Nueva Vizcaya.
>
> **Section 3.** *Effectivity.* - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.lawphil
