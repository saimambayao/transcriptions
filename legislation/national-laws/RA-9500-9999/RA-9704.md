# Republic Act No. 9704

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LIBOTON STREET IN THE CITY OF NAGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR TO M.T. VILLANUEVA AVENUE**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9704
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2009/ra_9704_2009.html)

---

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Second Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, two thousand eight.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9704               August 07, 2009**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LIBOTON STREET IN THE CITY OF NAGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR TO M.T. VILLANUEVA AVENUE
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Department of Libton Street in the CIty of Naga, Province of Camarines Sur shall hereinafter be known as M.T. Villanueva Avenue.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
