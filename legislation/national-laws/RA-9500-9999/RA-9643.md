# Republic Act No. 9643

**AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 21 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN VICTORIAS CITY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9643
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2009/ra_9643_2009.html)

---

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Second Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, two thousand eight.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9643, June 11, 2009 ]**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 21 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN VICTORIAS CITY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** March 21 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in Victorias City, Province of Negros Occidental, to commemorate the day it was converted into a component city.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
