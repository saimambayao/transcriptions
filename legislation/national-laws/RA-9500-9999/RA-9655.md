# Republic Act No. 9655

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CONCEPCION HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MALANDAY, CITY OF MARIKINA, TO MALANDAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9655
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2009/ra_9655_2009.html)

---

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Second Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, two thousand eight.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9655               July 17, 2009**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CONCEPCION HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MALANDAY, CITY OF MARIKINA, TO MALANDAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Renaming of a National High School.* - The name of Concepcion High School in Barangay Malanday, City of Marikina is hereby changed to Malanday National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** *Effectivity.* - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette.*
>
