# Republic Act No. 9613

**AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 21 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN VICTORIAS CITY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9613
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2009/ra_9613_2009.html)

---

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Second Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, two thousand eight.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9613**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 21 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN VICTORIAS CITY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** March 21 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in Victorias City, Province of Negros Occidental, to commemorate the day it was converted into a component city.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
