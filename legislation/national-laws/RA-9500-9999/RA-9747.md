# Republic Act No. 9747

**AN ACT RENAMING THE OCCIDENTAL MINDORO NATIONAL COLLEGE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO AS THE OCCIDENTAL MINDORO STATE COLLEGE**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9747
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2009/ra_9747_2009.html)

---

> Fourteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9747**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE OCCIDENTAL MINDORO NATIONAL COLLEGE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO AS THE OCCIDENTAL MINDORO STATE COLLEGE
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Occidental Mindoro National College in the Municipality of San Jose, Province of Occidental Mindoro is hereby renamed as the ***"Occidental Mindoro State College".***
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in at least two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
>
