> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9927**
>
> AN ACT SEPARATING THE ROBERTO R. TIROL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL - TAMBALIZA ANNEX IN BARANGAY TAMBALIZA, MUNICIPALITY OF CONCEPCION, PROVINCE OF ILOILO FROM THE ROBERTO H. TIROL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, GONVERTING IT INTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS TAMBALIZA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** *Separation and Conversion into a National High School.* - The Roberto H. Tirol National High School - Tambaliza Annex in Barangay Tambaliza, Municipality of Concepcion, Province of Iloilo is hereby separated from the Roberto H. Tirol National High School and converted into an independent national high school to be known as ***Tambaliza National High School.***
>
> **Section 2.** *Transfer of Assets and Liabilities.* - All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Roberto H. Tirol National High School - Tambaliza Annex are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Tambaliza National High School.
>
> **Section 3.** *Appropriations.* - The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the Tambaliza National High School, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current year's appropriations of the Roberto H. Tirol National High School - Tambaliza Annex. Thereafter, the amount necessary for the continued operation of the school shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 4.** *Implementation.* - The Secretary of Education shall issue rules and regulations that may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 5.** *Effectivity.* - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
>
