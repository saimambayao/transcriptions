# Republic Act No. 9748

**AN ACT DECLARING THE TWENTY-THIRD OF JUNE OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE ENTIRE PROVINCE OF PALAWAN AND THE CITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9748
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2009/ra_9748_2009.html)

---

> Fourteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9748**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING THE TWENTY-THIRD OF JUNE OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE ENTIRE PROVINCE OF PALAWAN AND THE CITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In commemoration of the "Foundation of the First Civil Government of the Province of Palawan after the American-Philippine War", June 23 of every year is hereby declared as a *"Special Nonworking Public Holiday in the Province of Palawan and the City of Puerto Princesa".*
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
