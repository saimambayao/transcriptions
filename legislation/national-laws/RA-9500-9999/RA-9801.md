# Republic Act No. 9801

**AN ACT CONVERTING MAUBAN-TIGNOAN PROVINCIAL ROAD, PROVINCE OF QUEZON INTO A NATIONAL ROAD**

- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2009/ra_9801_2009.html)

> Fourteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9801**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING MAUBAN-TIGNOAN PROVINCIAL ROAD, PROVINCE OF QUEZON INTO A NATIONAL ROAD
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Mauban - Tignoan Provincial Road in the municipalities of Mauban and Real, Province of Quezon is hereby converted into a national road.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
