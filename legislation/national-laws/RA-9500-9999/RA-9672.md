# Republic Act No. 9672

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF CALOOCAN TO BE KNOWN AS CALOOCAN NATIONAL SCIENCE AND SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Fourteenth Congress
> Second Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, two thousand eight.*
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9672, July 17, 2009 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF CALOOCAN TO BE KNOWN AS CALOOCAN NATIONAL SCIENCE AND SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** *Establishment of a National Science and Technology High School.*  There is hereby established, under the supervision of the Department of Education, a national science and technology high school in City of Caloocan to be known as Caloocan National Science and Technology High School.
>
> **Section 2.** *Appropriations.*  The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Department's program the operationalization of the Caloocan National Science and Technology High School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** *Implementation.*  The Secretary of Education shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 4.** *Effectivity.*  This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
>
