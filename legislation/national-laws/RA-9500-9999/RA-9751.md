> Fourteenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9751**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE MALINTA-LAGTA ROAD, PROVINCE OF MASBATE INTO A NATIONAL ROAD
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Malinta-Lagta Road which traverses Barangay Malinta in Masbate City and Barangay Lagta in the Municipality of Bale no, Province of Masbate is hereby converted into a national road.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Public Works and High ways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
