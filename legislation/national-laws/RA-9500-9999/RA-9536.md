# Republic Act No. 9536

**AN ACT AMENDING SECTION ONE (1) OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED EIGHT THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED SIXTY-NINE, ENTITLED**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 9536
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra2009/ra_9536_2009.html)

> Fourteenth Congress
>
> Second Regular Session
>
> *Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, two thousand eight.*
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 9536             March 24, 2009**
>
> **AN ACT AMENDING SECTION ONE (1) OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED EIGHT THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED SIXTY-NINE, ENTITLED "AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CATAGBACAN, LOON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE GOV. JACINTO C. BORJA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*:
>
> **Section 1.** Section 1 of Republic Act No. 8569 is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> "SECTION 1. There shall be established a national high school in Barangay Cantaongon, Municipality of Loon, Province of Bohol, to be known as the Gov. Jacinto C. Borja National High School."
>
> **Section 2.** The Title of Republic Act No. 8569 is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> "AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CANTAONGON, MUNICIPALITY OF LOON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE GOV. JACINTO C. BORJA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR".
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the *Official Gazette.*
>
> Approved,
