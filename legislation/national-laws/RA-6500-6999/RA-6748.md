# Republic Act No. 6748

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE NORTHERN BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN TACLOBAN CITY TO TACLOBAN CITY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1989/ra_6748_1989.html)

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6748, August 11, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE NORTHERN BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN TACLOBAN CITY TO TACLOBAN CITY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Northern Barangay High School in Tacloban City is hereby changed to Tacloban City National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
