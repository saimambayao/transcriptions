# Republic Act No. 6839

**An Act Renaming the Ilijan Elementary School to Pedro S. Tolentino Memorial Elementary School**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
> REPUBLIC ACT No. 6839
>
> An Act Renaming the Ilijan Elementary School to Pedro S. Tolentino Memorial Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Ilijan Elementary School located in Barangay Ilijan, City of Batangas, is hereby renamed as the Pedro S. Tolentino Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: January 5, 1990.
