> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 6961             August 17, 1990**
>
> **AN ACT REPEALING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THREE THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED EIGHTY-FOUR**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Republic Act No. 3884 entitled: "An Act establishing a normal college in Dipolog, Zamboanga del Norte, and authorizing the appropriation of funds therefor" is hereby repealed.
>
> **Section 2.** This repealing Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **August 17, 1990**
