# Republic Act No. 6674

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6674, September 05, 1988 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARANGAY COBOL, MUNICIPALITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BARANGAY PETRA PIMENTEL AND FOR SOME OTHER PURPOSE
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and the House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barangay Cobol in the Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barangay Petra Pimentel.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1âшphi1
