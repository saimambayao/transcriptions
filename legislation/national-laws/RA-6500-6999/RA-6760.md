>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6760             September 25, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SOUTH SUPERHIGHWAY TO PRESIDENT SERGIO OSMEÑA SR. HIGHWAY**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The South Superhighway stretching from Metro Manila to the Province of Laguna, and any further extension of the same, is hereby renamed President Sergio Osmeña Sr. Highway.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in two (2) national newspapers of general circulation.
>
> Approved: **September 25, 1989**
