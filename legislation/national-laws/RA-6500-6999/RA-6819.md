# Republic Act No. 6819

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BAGACAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BAGACAY, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN DIONISIO, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO CARLOS LOPEZ NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6819             December 18, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BAGACAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BAGACAY, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN DIONISIO, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO CARLOS LOPEZ NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Bagacay National High School in Barangay Bagacay, Municipality of San Dionisio, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Carlos Lopez National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **December 18, 1989**
