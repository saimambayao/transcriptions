> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 6962             August 17, 1990**
>
> **AN ACT TRANSFERRING TITLE TO AND OWNERSHIP OF THE REAL PROPERTY OF THE DIPOLOG NORMAL COLLEGE TO THE ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The site intended for the establishment of the Dipolog Normal College is hereby reassigned and transferred to the Zamboanga del Norte National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The title to and ownership of the real property covered by Transfer Certificate of Title No. (T-16876)-5256 with an area of 38,723 square meters registered in the name of the Dipolog Normal College are hereby transferred to the Zamboanga del Norte High School.
>
> **Section 3.** The proper register of deeds shall register the same and issue the corresponding certificate of title to the Zamboanga del Norte National High School.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **August 17, 1990**
