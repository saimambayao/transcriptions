> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6749, August 11, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAGKAHAN BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN TACLOBAN CITY TO SAGKAHAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Sagkahan Barangay High School in Tacloban City is hereby changed to Sagkahan National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
