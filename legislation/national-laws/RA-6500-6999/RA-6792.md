> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6792, November 29, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE NUEVA ECIJA PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN SAN JOSE CITY, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, TO SAN JOSE CITY GENERAL HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Nueva Ecija Provincial Hospital in San Jose City, Province of Nueva Ecija, is hereby changed to San Jose City General Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
