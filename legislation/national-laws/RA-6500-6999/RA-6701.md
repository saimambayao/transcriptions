**AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER ONE OF EVERY YEAR, THE DEATH ANNIVERSARY OF GREGORIO AGLIPAY Y LABAYAN, AS GREGORIO L. AGLIPAY DAY AND A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6701, February 10, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER ONE OF EVERY YEAR, THE DEATH ANNIVERSARY OF GREGORIO AGLIPAY Y LABAYAN, AS GREGORIO L. AGLIPAY DAY AND A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The first day of the month of September of every year, being the death anniversary of Gregorio L. Aglipay y Labayan, is hereby declared as Gregorio L. Aglipay Day and a special non-working holiday in the Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte.
>
> **Section 2.** Any provision of law, decree, order, rules or regulations inconsistent with this Act is hereby repealed and/or modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
