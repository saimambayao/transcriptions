# Republic Act No. 6697

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MEDINA I ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CALUANGAN, MUNICIPALITY OF MAGALLANES, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO CALUANGAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6697, February 10, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MEDINA I ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CALUANGAN, MUNICIPALITY OF MAGALLANES, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO CALUANGAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Medina I Elementary School in Barangay Caluangan, Municipality of Magallanes, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to Caluangan Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
