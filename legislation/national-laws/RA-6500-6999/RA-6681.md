# Republic Act No. 6681

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6681             November 4, 1988**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF WRIGHT, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, TO MUNICIPALITY OF PARANAS**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Wright, Province of Samar, is hereby changed to Municipality of Paranas.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **November 4, 1988.**
