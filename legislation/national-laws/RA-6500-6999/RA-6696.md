**AN ACT NAMING A NATIONAL HIGHWAY FROM THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAVIA UP TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF JANIUAY, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AS THE TOMAS CONFESOR HIGHWAY**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6696, February 10, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT NAMING A NATIONAL HIGHWAY FROM THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAVIA UP TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF JANIUAY, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AS THE TOMAS CONFESOR HIGHWAY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The national highway starting from Km. 10 + 000 in the Municipality of Pavia to Km. 33 + 000 in the Municipality of Janiuay shall be known as the Tomas Confesor Highway.
>
> **Section 2.** Any law, ordinance, administrative order, designation or identification shall be considered repealed or modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
