# Republic Act No. 6976

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SEXMOAN IN THE PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA TO MUNICIPALITY OF SASMUAN**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 6976
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1991/ra_6976_1991.html)

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 6976             January 15, 1991**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SEXMOAN IN THE PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA TO MUNICIPALITY OF SASMUAN**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Sexmoan in the Province of Pampanga changed to Municipality of Sasmuan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **January 15, 1991**.
