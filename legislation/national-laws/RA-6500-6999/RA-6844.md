# Republic Act No. 6844

**An Act Changing the Name of the Marcos Bridge in Barangay Lauis, Municipality of Candelaria, Province of Zambales, to Lauis Bridge**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 6844
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1990/ra_6844_1990.html)

---

> **Eighth Congress**
>
> REPUBLIC ACT No. 6844
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Marcos Bridge in Barangay Lauis, Municipality of Candelaria, Province of Zambales, to Lauis Bridge
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Marcos Bridge in Barangay Lauis, Municipality of Candelaria, Province of Zambales, is hereby changed to Lauis Bridge.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: January 18, 1990.
