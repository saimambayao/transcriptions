# Republic Act No. 6663

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PANDI CENTRAL SCHOOL SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANDI, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO MAMERTO C. BERNARDO MEMORIAL CENTRAL SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6663, June 22, 1988 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PANDI CENTRAL SCHOOL SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANDI, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO MAMERTO C. BERNARDO MEMORIAL CENTRAL SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and the House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Pandi Central School situated in the Municipality of Pandi, Province of Bulacan, hereby changed to Mamerto C. Bernardo Memorial Central School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
