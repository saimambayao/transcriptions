# Republic Act No. 6803

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MAYO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MAYO, MUNICIPALITY OF MATI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL, TO VICENTE ALMARIO, SR. MEMORIAL SCHOOL**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6803             December 12, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MAYO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MAYO, MUNICIPALITY OF MATI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL, TO VICENTE ALMARIO, SR. MEMORIAL SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Mayo Elementary School in Purok Malipayon, Barangay Mayo, Municipality of Mati, Province of Davao Oriental, is hereby changed to Vicente Almario, Sr. Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **December 12, 1989**
