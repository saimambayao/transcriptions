# Republic Act No. 6840

**An Act Changing the Name of the Paaralang Elementarya ng Bading in Butuan City to Paaralang Elementarya ng Obrero**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 6840
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1990/ra_6840_1990.html)

---

> **Eighth Congress**
>
> REPUBLIC ACT No. 6840
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Paaralang Elementarya ng Bading in Butuan City to Paaralang Elementarya ng Obrero
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Paaralang Elementarya ng Bading in Butuan City is hereby changed to Paaralang Elementarya ng Obrero.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: January 5, 1990.
