> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6789, November 29, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DR. EFRAIN C. MONTEMAYOR MEDICAL CENTER IN BAGUIO CITY TO BAGUIO GENERAL HOSPITAL AND MEDICAL CENTER
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Dr. Efrain C. Montemayor Medical Center in Baguio City is hereby changed to Baguio General Hospital and Medical Center.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
