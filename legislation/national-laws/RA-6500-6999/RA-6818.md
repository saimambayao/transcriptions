# Republic Act No. 6818

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LOOC PRIMARY SCHOOL IN SITIO LOOC, BARANGAY TALOTO-AN, MUNICIPALITY OF CONCEPCION, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO DONATO VALDERAMA PRIMARY SCHOOL**

## Metadata

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6818             December 18, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LOOC PRIMARY SCHOOL IN SITIO LOOC, BARANGAY TALOTO-AN, MUNICIPALITY OF CONCEPCION, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO DONATO VALDERAMA PRIMARY SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Looc Primary School in Sitio Looc, Barangay Taloto-an, Municipality of Concepcion, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Donato Valderama Primary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **December 18, 1989**
