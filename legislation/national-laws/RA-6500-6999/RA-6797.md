> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6797, December 12, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE KENRAM ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN KENRAM, ISULAN, SULTAN KUDARAT, TO J. P. GARCIA MEMORIAL SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Kenram Elementary School located in Kenram, Isulan, Sultan Kudarat, is hereby changed to J. P. Garcia Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
