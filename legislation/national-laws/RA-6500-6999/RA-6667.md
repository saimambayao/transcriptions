> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6667, June 22, 1988 ]**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE JANIUAY NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL, IN MUNICIPALITY OF JANIUAY, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AS THE DON TIBURSIO A. LUTERO NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Janiuay National Comprehensive High School in Municipality of Janiuay, Province of Iloilo is hereby changed to Don Tibursio A. Lutero National Comprehensive High School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
