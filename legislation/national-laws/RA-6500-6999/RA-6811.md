# Republic Act No. 6811

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MINUYAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MINUYAN, MUNICIPALITY OF NORZAGARAY, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO TIMOTEO POLICARPIO MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6811             December 18, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MINUYAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MINUYAN, MUNICIPALITY OF NORZAGARAY, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO TIMOTEO POLICARPIO MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Minuyan Elementary School located in Barangay Minuyan, Municipality of Norzagaray, Province of Bulacan, is hereby changed to Timoteo Policarpio Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **December 18, 1989**
