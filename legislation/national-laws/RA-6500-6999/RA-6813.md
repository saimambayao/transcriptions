# Republic Act No. 6813

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BINAGBAG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BINAGBAG, MUNICIPALITY OF ANGAT, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO FRANCISCO F. ILLESCAS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6813             December 18, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BINAGBAG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BINAGBAG, MUNICIPALITY OF ANGAT, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO FRANCISCO F. ILLESCAS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Binagbag Elementary School located in Binagbag, Municipality of Angat, Province of Bulacan, is hereby renamed as Francisco F. Illescas Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **December 18, 1989**
