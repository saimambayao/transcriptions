# Republic Act No. 6945

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE AMAI-PAKPAK GENERAL HOSPITAL IN MARAWI CITY TO AMAI-PAKPAK PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL TO ENSURE A MORE EFFICIENT AND EFFECTIVE DELIVERY OF SERVICES TO THE PEOPLE**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 6945             April 5, 1990**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE AMAI-PAKPAK GENERAL HOSPITAL IN MARAWI CITY TO AMAI-PAKPAK PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL TO ENSURE A MORE EFFICIENT AND EFFECTIVE DELIVERY OF SERVICES TO THE PEOPLE**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Amai-Pakpak General Hospital in Marawi City is hereby changed to Amai-Pakpak Provincial Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Health is hereby directed to implement the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **April 5, 1990**
