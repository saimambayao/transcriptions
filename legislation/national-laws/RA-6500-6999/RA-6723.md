# Republic Act No. 6723

**AN ACT DECLARING FEBRUARY ONE OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE TO COMMEMORATE THE BIRTH ANNIVERSARY OF ITS HERO, DR. JOSE MARIA PANGANIBAN**

> **Eighth Congress**

>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6723             March 20, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING FEBRUARY ONE OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE TO COMMEMORATE THE BIRTH ANNIVERSARY OF ITS HERO, DR. JOSE MARIA PANGANIBAN**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** It is hereby declared that February One of every year is a special working holiday in the Province of Camarines Norte to commemorate the birth anniversary of its hero, Dr. Jose Maria Panganiban.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **March 20, 1989.**
