# Republic Act No. 6699

**AN ACT RENAMING THE TUY-AN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY TUY-AN, MUNICIPALITY OF CABATUAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AS THE COLOMER ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6699, February 10, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE TUY-AN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY TUY-AN, MUNICIPALITY OF CABATUAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AS THE COLOMER ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Tuy-an Elementary School in Barangay Tuy-an, Municipality of Cabatuan, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Colomer Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
