**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE UPI EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF UPI, PROVINCE OF MAGUINDANAO, TO DATU BLAH T. SINSUAT DISTRICT HOSPITAL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6783, November 29, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE UPI EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF UPI, PROVINCE OF MAGUINDANAO, TO DATU BLAH T. SINSUAT DISTRICT HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Upi Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Upi, Province of Maguindanao, is hereby changed to Datu Blah T. Sinsuat District Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
