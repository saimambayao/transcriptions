# Republic Act No. 6773

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CANDUAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CANDUAL, MUNICIPALITY OF PANAY, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, TO JUSTICE JOSE HONTIVEROS MEMORIAL SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6773, November 29, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CANDUAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CANDUAL, MUNICIPALITY OF PANAY, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, TO JUSTICE JOSE HONTIVEROS MEMORIAL SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Candual Elementary School in Barangay Candual, Municipality of Panay, Province of Capiz, shall hereafter be known as the Justice Jose Hontiveros Memorial School.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
