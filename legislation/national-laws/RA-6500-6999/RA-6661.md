> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6661, June 22, 1988 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF URDANETA I ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BENDITA, MUNICIPALITY OF MAGALLANES, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO BENDITA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and the House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Urdaneta I Elementary School in Barangay Bendita, Municipality of Magallanes, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to Bendita Elementary School.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
