# Republic Act No. 6841

**An Act Changing the Name of the Manggahan Elementary School Unit I in the Municipality of Pasig, Metro Manila, to San Lorenzo Ruiz Elementary School**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 6841
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1990/ra_6841_1990.html)

---

> **Eighth Congress**
>
> REPUBLIC ACT No. 6841
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Manggahan Elementary School Unit I in the Municipality of Pasig, Metro Manila, to San Lorenzo Ruiz Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Manggahan Elementary School Unit I in the Municipality of Pasig, Metro Manila, is hereby changed to San Lorenzo Ruiz Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: January 5, 1990.
