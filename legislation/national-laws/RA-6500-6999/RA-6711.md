# Republic Act No. 6711

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MEDICION ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, IN MEDICION 2ND, MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO GOV. DOMINADOR CAMERINO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6711             February 10, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MEDICION ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, IN MEDICION 2ND, MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO GOV. DOMINADOR CAMERINO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Medicion Elementary School in the Municipality of Imus, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to Gov. Dominador Camerino Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> ***Enacted without executive approval, February 10, 1989.***
