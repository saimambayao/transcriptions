# Republic Act No. 6774

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MATAAS NA PAARALAN NG BARANGAY BINTOG, MUNICIPALITY OF PLARIDEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO JOSE J. MARIANO MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6774, November 29, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MATAAS NA PAARALAN NG BARANGAY BINTOG, MUNICIPALITY OF PLARIDEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO JOSE J. MARIANO MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Mataas na Paaralan ng Barangay Bintog, Municipality of Plaridel, Province of Bulacan, is hereby changed to Jose J. Mariano Memorial High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
