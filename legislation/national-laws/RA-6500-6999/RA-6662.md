# Republic Act No. 6662

**AN ACT RENAMING THE BAY-ANG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BAY-ANG, MUNICIPALITY OF AJUY, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AS BEATRIZ D. TUPAS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1988/ra_6662_1988.html)

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6662, June 22, 1988 ]**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE BAY-ANG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BAY-ANG, MUNICIPALITY OF AJUY, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AS BEATRIZ D. TUPAS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and the House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Bay-ang Elementary School in Barangay Bay-ang, Municipality of Ajuy, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Beatriz D. Tupas Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
