# Republic Act No. 6946

**AN ACT MAKING THE DON JOSE SUSTIGUER MONFORT MEMORIAL NATIONAL SCHOOL A BRANCH OF THE WESTERN VISAYAS COLLEGE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY TO BE KNOWN AS THE DON JOSE SUSTIGUER MONFORT MEMORIAL NATIONAL COLLEGE (WESTERN VISAYAS COLLEGE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY BRANCH)**

> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 6946             April 5, 1990**
>
> **AN ACT MAKING THE DON JOSE SUSTIGUER MONFORT MEMORIAL NATIONAL SCHOOL A BRANCH OF THE WESTERN VISAYAS COLLEGE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY TO BE KNOWN AS THE DON JOSE SUSTIGUER MONFORT MEMORIAL NATIONAL COLLEGE (WESTERN VISAYAS COLLEGE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY BRANCH)**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Don Jose Sustiguer Monfort Memorial National School is hereby made a branch of the Western Visayas College of Science and Technology to be known as the Don Jose Sustiguer Monfort Memorial National College (Western Visayas College of Science and Technology Branch).
>
> **Section 2.** The said college shall offer such courses as are programmed under the charter of the Western Visayas College of Science and Technology, including automotive, electrical communications and computer courses.
>
> **Section 3.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall promulgate such rules and regulations to implement the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
> Approved: **April 5, 1990**
