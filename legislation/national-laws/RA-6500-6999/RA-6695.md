> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6695, February 10, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE TUMAGA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN ZAMBOANGA CITY AS THE CATALINA VDA. DE JALON MEMORIAL SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Tumaga Elementary School located in Barangay Tumaga, Zamboanga City, is hereby renamed as the Catalina Vda. de Jalon Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
