**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MABABANG PAARALAN NG OBISPO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUY, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, TO MABABANG PAARALANG GREGORIO AGONCILLO**

> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MABABANG PAARALAN NG OBISPO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUY, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, TO MABABANG PAARALANG GREGORIO AGONCILLO
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Mababang Paaralan ng Obispo in the Municipality of Tuy, Province of Batangas, is hereby changed to Mababang Paaralang Gregorio Agoncillo.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
