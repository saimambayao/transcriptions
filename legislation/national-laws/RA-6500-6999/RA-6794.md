> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6794, December 12, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TOLOSA BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN TOLOSA, LEYTE, TO TOLOSA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Tolosa Barangay High School in Tolosa, Leyte, is hereby changed to Tolosa National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
