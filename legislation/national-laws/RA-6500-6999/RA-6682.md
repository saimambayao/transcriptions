>
> **AN ACT AMENDING THE EFFECTIVITY CLAUSE OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 292 DATED JULY 25, 1987**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The effectivity clause provided in Section 29, Book VII, of Executive Order No. 292 dated July 25, 1987, known as the Administrative Code of 1987, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> "Sec. 29. Effectivity.  This Code shall take effect two years after its publication in the Official Gazette."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect on November 24, 1988.lawphil.net
>
