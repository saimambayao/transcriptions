**AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER THIRTEEN OF EACH YEAR A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6721             March 20, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER THIRTEEN OF EACH YEAR A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In order to commemorate the birth anniversary of the late Speaker Eugenio Perez, November Thirteen of each year is hereby declared a special non-working holiday in the Province of Pangasinan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **March 20, 1989.**
