# Republic Act No. 6651

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMELDA IN THE PROVINCE OF ROMBLON TO MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA MARIA, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE BATAS PAMBANSA BILANG DALAWANG DAAN AT TATLUMPU'T APAT**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6651, April 17, 1988 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMELDA IN THE PROVINCE OF ROMBLON TO MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA MARIA, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE BATAS PAMBANSA BILANG DALAWANG DAAN AT TATLUMPU'T APAT
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Imelda in the Province of Romblon is hereby changed to Municipality of Santa Maria.
>
