> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6786, November 29, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE NATIONAL ORTHOPEDIC HOSPITAL AND REHABILITATION MEDICAL CENTER IN QUEZON CITY TO PHILIPPINE ORTHOPEDIC CENTER
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the National Orthopedic Hospital and Rehabilitation Medical Center in Quezon City is hereby changed to Philippine Orthopedic Center.1a⍵⍴h!1
