# Republic Act No. 6771

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DENNIS MOLINTAS MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BOKOD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET, TO DENNIS MOLINTAS MEMORIAL HOSPITAL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6771, November 29, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DENNIS MOLINTAS MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BOKOD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET, TO DENNIS MOLINTAS MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Dennis Molintas Municipal Hospital in the Municipality of Bokod, Province of Benguet, is hereby changed to Dennis Molintas Memorial Hospital.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** Any amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be charged to the appropriations of the Department of Health.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
