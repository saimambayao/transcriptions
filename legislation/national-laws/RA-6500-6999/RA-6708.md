> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6708, February 10, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMELDA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO SAN LORENZO RUIZ
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name Municipality of Imelda, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Municipality of San Lorenzo Ruiz.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
