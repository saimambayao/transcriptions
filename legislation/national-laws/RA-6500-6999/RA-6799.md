# Republic Act No. 6799

>
>

> **Republic Act No. 6799             December 12, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT RENAMING THE LOAY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOAY, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO SENATE PRESIDENT PRO TEMPORE JOSE A. CLARIN MEMORIAL SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Loay Elementary School located in the Municipality of Loay, Province of Bohol, is hereby renamed as the Senate President Pro Tempore Jose A. Clarin Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **December 12, 1989**
