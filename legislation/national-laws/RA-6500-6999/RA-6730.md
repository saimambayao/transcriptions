>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6730             June 14, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT NAMING THE MANILA-CAVITE COASTAL ROAD AS THE AGUINALDO BOULEVARD**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Manila-Cavite coastal road starting from the corner of Roxas Boulevard and MIA road to Cavite shall hereafter be known as the Aguinaldo Boulevard.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its publication in at least two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
>
> Approved: **June 14, 1989**
