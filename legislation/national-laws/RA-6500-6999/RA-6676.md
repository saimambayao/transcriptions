> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6676             October 12, 1988**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARIANO MARCOS IN THE PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT TO LAMBAYONG**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Municipality of Mariano Marcos in the Province of Sultan Kudarat shall hereafter be known as the Municipality of Lambayong.
>
> **Section 2.** All laws or decrees inconsistent with any provision of this Act are hereby deemed amended accordingly.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **October 12, 1988.**
