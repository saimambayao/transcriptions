# Republic Act No. 6815

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LAFU ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ANTONIO, MUNICIPALITY OF LALLO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO SAN ANTONIO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6815             December 18, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LAFU ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ANTONIO, MUNICIPALITY OF LALLO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO SAN ANTONIO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Lafu Elementary School in Barangay San Antonio, Municipality of Lallo, Province of Cagayan, is hereby changed to San Antonio Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **December 18, 1989**
