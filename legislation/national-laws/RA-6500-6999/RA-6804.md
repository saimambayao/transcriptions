# Republic Act No. 6804

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAN FERNANDO HIGH SCHOOL IN SAN FERNANDO, MASBATE, TO ANDRES CLEMENTE, JR. HIGH SCHOOL**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6804             December 12, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAN FERNANDO HIGH SCHOOL IN SAN FERNANDO, MASBATE, TO ANDRES CLEMENTE, JR. HIGH SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the San Fernando High School in San Fernando, Masbate, is hereby changed to Andres Clemente, Jr. High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **December 12, 1989**
