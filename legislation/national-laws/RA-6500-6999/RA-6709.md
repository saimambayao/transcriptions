> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6709, February 10, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER FIVE OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** November five of every year is hereby declared a special non-working holiday in the Province of Negros Occidental.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
