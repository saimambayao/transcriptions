# Republic Act No. 6717

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MAYTUBIG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISABELA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO FRANCISCO ESTEBAN SR. MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6717, March 17, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MAYTUBIG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISABELA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO FRANCISCO ESTEBAN SR. MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Maytubig Elementary School in the Municipality of Isabela, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Francisco Esteban Sr. Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
