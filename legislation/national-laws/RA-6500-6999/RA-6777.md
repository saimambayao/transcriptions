# Republic Act No. 6777

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE GUISICAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY GUISICAN, MUNICIPALITY OF LABO, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO FLORENCE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6777, November 29, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE GUISICAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY GUISICAN, MUNICIPALITY OF LABO, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO FLORENCE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Guisican Elementary School in Barangay Guisican, Municipality of Labo, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Florence Elementary School.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
