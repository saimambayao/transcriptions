# Republic Act No. 6693

**AN ACT RENAMING THE BARANGAY BULWAGAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF HINOBA-AN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, AS THE PAZ VDA DE ESCANILLA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6693, February 10, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE BARANGAY BULWAGAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF HINOBA-AN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, AS THE PAZ VDA DE ESCANILLA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Barangay Bulwagan Elementary School locates in Barangay Bulwagan, Municipality of Hinoba-an, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby renamed as the Paz Vda de Escanilla Elementary School.1a⍵⍴h!1
