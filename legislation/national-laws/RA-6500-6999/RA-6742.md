> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6742, August 04, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BATAAN NATIONAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES IN BALANGA, BATAAN, TO MEDINA LACSON DE LEON NATIONAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Bataan National School of Arts and Trades in Balanga, Bataan, is hereby changed to Medina Lacson de Leon National School of Arts and Trades.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
