# Republic Act No. 6677

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARANGAY LOWER Q-M IN BAGUIO CITY TO BARANGAY GENERAL EMILIO F. AGUINALDO**

## Metadata

- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1988/ra_6677_1988.html)

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6677             October 12, 1988**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARANGAY LOWER Q-M IN BAGUIO CITY TO BARANGAY GENERAL EMILIO F. AGUINALDO**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barangay Lower Q-M in Baguio City is hereby changed to Barangay General Emilio F. Aguinaldo.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **October 12, 1988.**
