>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6761             September 25, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT NAMING THE CAMP AT TAYUG, PANGASINAN, NOW HOUSING THE 151ST PC COMPANY/1ST POLICE DISTRICT, AS CAMP NARCISO R. RAMOS**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The camp at Tayug, Pangasinan, now housing the 151st PC Company/1st Police District, is hereby named as Camp Narciso R. Ramos.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **September 25, 1989**
