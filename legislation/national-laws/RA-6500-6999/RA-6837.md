# Republic Act No. 6837

**An Act Changing the Name of the Lulub National High School in Barangay Purificacion D. Manfort, Municipality of Dumangas, Province of Iloilo, to Purificacion D. Monfort National School**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 6837
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1990/ra_6837_1990.html)

> **Eighth Congress**
>
> REPUBLIC ACT No. 6837
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Lulub National High School in Barangay Purificacion D. Manfort, Municipality of Dumangas, Province of Iloilo, to Purificacion D. Monfort National School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1**. The name of the Lublub National High School in Barangay Purificacion D. Manfort, Municipality of Dumangas, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Purificacion D. Monfort National School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: January 5, 1990.
