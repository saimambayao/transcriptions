# Republic Act No. 6806

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE GATBUCA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALUMPIT, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO ARSENIO SANTOS MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6806             December 12, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE GATBUCA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALUMPIT, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO ARSENIO SANTOS MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Gatbuca Elementary School in the Municipality of Calumpit, Province of Bulacan, is hereby changed to Arsenio Santos Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **December 12, 1989**
