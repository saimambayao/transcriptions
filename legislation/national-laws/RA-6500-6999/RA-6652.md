> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6652, April 17, 1988 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARCOS, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, TO MUNICIPALITY OF DR. JOSE P. RIZAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Marcos in the Province of Palawan is hereby changed to Municipality of Dr. Jose P. Rizal.
>
