# Republic Act No. 6707

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CRUZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CRUZ, MUNICIPALITY OF BUENAVISTA, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO POLICARPO TIROL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6707, February 10, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CRUZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CRUZ, MUNICIPALITY OF BUENAVISTA, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO POLICARPO TIROL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Cruz Elementary School in Barangay Cruz, Municipality of Buenavista, Province of Bohol, shall hereafter be known as the Policarpo Tirol Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
