>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6654             May 20, 1988**
>
> **AN ACT REPEALING SECTION FORTY OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED EIGHT HUNDRED SEVEN, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CIVIL SERVICE DECREE**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section Forty of Presidential Decree Numbered Eight Hundred Seven, otherwise known as the Civil Service Decree, is hereby repealed.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **May 20, 1988.**
