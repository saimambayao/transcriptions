# Republic Act No. 6703

**AN ACT RENAMING THE SAN RAFAEL-BOLILAO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL SITUATED IN BARANGAY SAN RAFAEL, MUNICIPALITY OF MANDURRIAO, CITY OF ILOILO, TO DONATO PISON MEMORIAL SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6703, February 10, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT RENAMING THE SAN RAFAEL-BOLILAO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL SITUATED IN BARANGAY SAN RAFAEL, MUNICIPALITY OF MANDURRIAO, CITY OF ILOILO, TO DONATO PISON MEMORIAL SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The San Rafael-Bolilao Elementary School in Barangay san Rafael, Mandurriao, Iloilo City, shall hereafter be known as the Donato Pison Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
