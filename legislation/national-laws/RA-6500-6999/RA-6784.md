> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6784, November 29, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE GUAGUA DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUAGUA, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA, TO PAMPANGA PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Guagua District Hospital in the Municipality of Guagua, Province of Pampanga, is hereby changed to Pampanga Provincial Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
