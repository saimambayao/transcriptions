# Republic Act No. 6824

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BUAYAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL TO DATU ACAD DALID CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

## Metadata

- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1989/ra_6824_1989.html)

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6824             December 18, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BUAYAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL TO DATU ACAD DALID CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Buayan Elementary School in General Santos City, Province of South Cotabato, shall hereafter be known and referred to as the Datu Acad Dalid Central Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **December 18, 1989**
