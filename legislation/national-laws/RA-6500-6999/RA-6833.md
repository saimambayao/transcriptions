# Republic Act No. 6833

**An Act Changing the Name of the Marinduque Institute of Science and Technology in Barangay Tanza, Municipality of Boac, Province of Marinduque, to Marinduque State College**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 6833
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1990/ra_6833_1990.html)

---

> **Eighth Congress**
>
> REPUBLIC ACT No. 6833
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Marinduque Institute of Science and Technology in Barangay Tanza, Municipality of Boac, Province of Marinduque, to Marinduque State College
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1**. The name of the Marinduque Institute of Science and Technology in Barangay Tanza, Municipality of Boac, Province of Marinduque, is hereby changed to Marinduque State College.
>
> **Section 2**. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: January 5, 1990.
