> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6795, December 12, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE STA. FE BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN STA. FE, LEYTE, TO STA. FE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Sta. Fe Barangay High School in Sta. Fe, Leyte, is hereby changed to Sta. Fe National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
