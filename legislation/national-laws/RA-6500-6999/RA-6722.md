> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6722             March 20, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING APRIL TWENTY-FIVE A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF AKLAN**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** April Twenty-five of every year is hereby declared a special non-working holiday in the Province of Aklan to commemorate the creation of said province.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **March 20, 1989.**
