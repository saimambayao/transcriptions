> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6743, August 09, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING JULY SIXTEEN OF EVERY YEAR AS OZAMIZ CITY CHARTER DAY AND A SPECIAL PUBLIC NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF OZAMIZ
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** July Sixteen of every year is hereby declared as Ozamiz City Charter Day and a special public non-working holiday in the City of Ozamiz.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
