> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 6974             December 8, 1990**
>
> **AN ACT RESTORING THE NAME OF "NATIONAL RESEARCH COUNCIL OF THE PHILIPPINES," AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PARAGRAPH (a), SECTION 33 OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 128**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Paragraph (a), Section 33 of Executive Order No. 128 dated January 30, 1987 is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> "(a) National Research Council of the Philippines as created under Act 4120 of the Ninth Philippines Legislature."
>
> **Section 2.** All references to the name "Philippine National Science Society" shall be understood as referring to the National Research Council of the Philippines pursuant to the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **December 8, 1990**
