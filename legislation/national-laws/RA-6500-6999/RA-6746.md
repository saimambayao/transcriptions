> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6746, August 11, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PIEDAD BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL TO DOÑA CARMEN DENIA CITY HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Piedad Barangay High School in Davao City is hereby changed to Doña Carmen Denia City High School.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
