# Republic Act No. 6660

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LAKESIDE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO CORAZON M. AZCARATE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6660             June 22, 1988**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LAKESIDE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO CORAZON M. AZCARATE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Lakeside Elementary School in the City of San Pablo, Province of Laguna, is hereby changed to Corazon M. Azcarte Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **June 22, 1988.**
