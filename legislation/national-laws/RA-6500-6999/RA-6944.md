# Republic Act No. 6944

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAN ENRIQUE AGRICULTURAL COLLEGE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ENRIQUE, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO SAN ENRIQUE POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE**

## Metadata

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 6944             April 5, 1990**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAN ENRIQUE AGRICULTURAL COLLEGE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ENRIQUE, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO SAN ENRIQUE POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In keeping with its sound operations, the name of the San Enrique Agricultural College is hereby changed into a polytechnic college to be known as the San Enrique Polytechnic College.
>
> **Section 2.** The funds required for the implementation of this Act shall be charged to the budget for the SY 1988-1989 of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports allocated for San Enrique Agricultural College.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
>
> Approved: **April 5, 1990**
