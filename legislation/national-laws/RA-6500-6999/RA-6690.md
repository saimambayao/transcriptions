# Republic Act No. 6690

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TANAY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO SIMEON R. BENDAÑA, SR. MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6690             February 10, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TANAY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO SIMEON R. BENDAÑA, SR. MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Tanay Elementary School located in the Municipality of Tanay, Province of Rizal, is hereby charged to Simeon R. Bendaña, Sr. Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> ***Enacted without executive approval, February 10, 1989.***
