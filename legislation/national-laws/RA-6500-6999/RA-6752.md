> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6752, August 11, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAN MIGUEL, MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL IN SAN MIGUEL, LEYTE, TO SAN MIGUEL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the San Miguel Municipal High School in San Miguel, Leyte, is hereby changed to San Miguel National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved: August 11, 1989.
