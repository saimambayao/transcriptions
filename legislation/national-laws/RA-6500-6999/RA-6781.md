> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6781, November 29, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LOON DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO CONGRESSMAN NATALIO P. CASTILLO, SR. MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Loon District Hospital in the Municipality of Loon, Province of Bohol, is hereby changed to Congressman Natalio P. Castillo, Sr. Memorial Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** If, for any reason, any section or provision of this Act is declared unconstitutional, the other provisions hereof shall not be affected and shall remain in full force and effect.
>
> **Section 3.** All laws, executive orders, decrees and proclamations or any part or parts thereof inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are deemed repealed or amended accordingly.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
