# Republic Act No. 6796

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MABITON BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CLAVERIA, PROVINCE OF MASBATE, TO FELIXBERTO DEL ROSARIO, SR. MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6796, December 12, 1989 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MABITON BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CLAVERIA, PROVINCE OF MASBATE, TO FELIXBERTO DEL ROSARIO, SR. MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Mabiton Barangay High School in Barangay Mabiton, Municipality of Claveria, Province of Masbate, is hereby changed to Felixberto del Rosario, Sr. Memorial High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
