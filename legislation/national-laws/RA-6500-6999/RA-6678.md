# Republic Act No. 6678

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MARIANO IN THE FIRST DISTRICT OF THE PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE TO MUNICIPALITY OF MARAGUSAN**

## Metadata

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6678             October 12, 1988**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MARIANO IN THE FIRST DISTRICT OF THE PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE TO MUNICIPALITY OF MARAGUSAN**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Municipality of San Mariano in the first district of Davao del Norte shall hereafter be known as the Municipality of Maragusan.
>
> **Section 2.** All laws or decrees inconsistent with any provision of this Act are hereby deemed amended accordingly.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **October 12, 1988.**
