# Republic Act No. 6802

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAGANA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABONG, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, TO TOMAS S. VILLANUEVA MEMORIAL SCHOOL**

> **Eighth Congress**
>
>
>
> **Republic Act No. 6802             December 12, 1989**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAGANA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABONG, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, TO TOMAS S. VILLANUEVA MEMORIAL SCHOOL**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Sagana Elementary School in the Municipality of Bongabong, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is hereby changed to Tomas S. Villanueva Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **December 12, 1989**
