> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4799**
>
> An Act Creating a Branch Office of the Register of Deeds of the Province of Pangasinan, with Permanent Station at the Municipality of Alaminos, Same Province, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby created a branch office of the Register of Deeds of the Province of Pangasinan, with permanent station at the Municipality of Alaminos, same province, which shall perform all the duties and function of the register of deeds for the municipalities of Alaminos, Infanta, Burgos, Dasol, Agno, Bani, Balinao, Anda and Sual, all in the Province of Pangasinan.
>
> **Section 2.** To carry out the provisions of this, Act, there is hereby authorized to be appropriated for the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-eight, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the sum of twenty thousand seven hundred eighty-four pesos to be spent for the following:
>
> (a) Deputy Register of Deeds ₱5,112.00
>
> (b) One supervising clerk 3,300.00
>
> (c) One senior cash clerk 2,424.00
>
> (d) One clerk 2,196.00
>
> (e) One messenger 1,800.00
>
> (f) One janitor 1,476.00
>
> (g) One laborer 1,476.00
>
> (h) Sundry expenses 3,000.00
>
> Such sums as may be necessary for the same purpose in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
