> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4753**
>
> An Act Providing for the Establishment of a Community Hospital in the Municipality of Lubang, Province of Occidental Mindoro, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the supervision of the Director of Medical Services, a hospital in the Municipality of Lubang, Province of Occidental Mindoro, to be known as Lubang Community Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Health shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment and operation of the said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-seven. Such sums as may be needed for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
