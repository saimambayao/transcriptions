> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4508**
>
> An Act Amending a Certain Item in Republic Act Numbered Thirty-One Hundred and One Regarding Capital Improvements to be Financed from Bond Issues or Loans in the Municipality of Badian, Province of Cebu
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item No. 3-G, 20, Irrigation-Hydroelectric Water Supply Project, Badian, Cebu, on page two hundred seventy-one of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-one hundred and one, is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "30. Hydroelectric Project, Badian, Cebu ₱200,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
