> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4738**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Paracales, Mountain Province, to Paracelis
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Paracales, Mountain Province, is hereby changed to Paracelis.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
