> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4786**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Numancia, Province of Surigao del Norte, to Del Carmen
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Numancia in the Province of Surigao del Norte, is hereby changed to Del Carmen.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
