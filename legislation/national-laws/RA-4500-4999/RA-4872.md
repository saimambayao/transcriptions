> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4872**
>
> An Act Creating the Municipality of Bayog in the Province of Zamboanga del Sur
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrios and sitios of Bayog, Buboan, Datagan, Pulang Bato, Dipili, Salawagan, Lamari, Damit, Depasi, Pangi, Liba, Datagan 1, Datagan 2, Mataga, Dimalinao, Baking, Dipuri, Kwai, Maton-og, and Kanipa-an in the Municipality of Buug, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, are separated from said municipality, and constituted into a distinct and independent municipality to be known as the Municipality of Bayog.1avvphi1 The seat of government of the new municipality shall be in Barrio Bayog.
>
> **Section 2.** The first mayor, vice-mayor and councilors of the new municipality shall be elected in the next general elections for local officials and shall have been qualified.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 8, 1967.
