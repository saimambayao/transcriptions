> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4985**
>
> An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Siocon, Province of Zamboanga del Norte
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Siocon, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted as follows:
>
> 1. The sitios of Gumbos, Baliangan, Bulacan, Pisawak, Kilalaban, Bacungan, Guli, Upper Diangas, Lower Diangas, Guimotan, Bolinawan, Linay, Batang-Lungon, Lotogan, Kulambongan, Liman, Tabingan and Tanuman in the Municipality of Siocon, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as barrio San Jose;
>
> 2. The sitios of Guisakan, Leopan, Galibogan, Katian, Domayon, Somioc, Tabayo, Bitonganan, Makot, Bolobuan, Golangan, Malaboc and Malongon in the Municipality of Siocon, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Dionisio Riconalla.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
