> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4877**
>
> An Act Creating the Municipality of Burgos in the Province of Isabela
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrios of Caliguian, Catabban, Gullalabo, Masigun East, Malasin, San Bonifacio, San Roque, San Miguel and Raniag in the Municipality of Gamu; and the barrios of Bacnor East, Bacnor West and Dalig in the Municipality of Aurora, Province of Isabela, are separated from said municipalities, and constituted into a distinct and independent municipality, to be known as the Municipality of Burgos, same province.
>
> The seat of government of the new municipality shall be in any place between barrios Caliguian and Gullalabo.
>
> **Section 2.** The first mayor, vice-mayor and councilors of the said municipality shall be elected in the next general elections for local officials.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 18, 1967.
