> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4971**
>
> An Act Amending Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Four Thousand Six Hundred and Five
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Four thousand six hundred and five is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> **Section 2.** The grantee is hereby authorized to manufacture and supply one hundred tons of ice a day.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
