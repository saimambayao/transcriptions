> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4783**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Barrio School in Maligaya, Municipality of Tarlac, Province of Tarlac, from Buenavista-Tete-Ungot Primary School to Maligaya Primary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the barrio school in Maligaya, Municipality of Tarlac, Province of Tarlac, is hereby changed from Buenavista-Tete-Ungot Primary School to Maligaya Primary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
