> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4757**
>
> An Act Creating the Municipal District of Dinalongan in the Province of Quezon
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Barrios Simbahan and Dinalongan and Sitios Abuleg, Bungo, Balante and Nipoo are hereby separated from the Municipality of Casiguran, Province of Quezon, and converted into a distinct and independent municipal district to be known as the Municipal District of Dinalongan, same province.1avvphi1 The seat of the said municipal district shall be Barrio Dinalongan.
>
> The Municipal District of Dinalongan shall be bounded on the North by the Dinalongan River; on the South, by the Dinadiawan Creek; on the West, by the provincial boundary between the Province of Quezon and the Province of Nueva Vizcaya; and on the East, by the Casiguran Sound.
>
> **Section 2.** The mayor, vice-mayor and councilors of the Municipal District of Dinalongan shall be elected in the next regular elections for provincial and municipal officials.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
