> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4876**
>
> An Act Creating the Municipality of Mabuhay in the Province of Zamboanga del Sur
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Barrios Mabuhay, Malinao, Sagay, Katipan, Mualbual, Tuburan, Kawilan, Abunda, Punawan, Kaliran, Taguisian, Bag-ong Silang, Siotan, Bagurod, Paminsa-an, Pinalim, Kasigpitan, Sawa and Ligaya, all in the Municipality of Olutanga, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, are separated from said municipality, and constituted into a distinct and independent municipality to be known as the Municipality of Mabuhay.
>
> The seat of the government of the new municipality shall be at the present site of Barrio Mabuhay.
>
> **Section 2.** The first mayor, vice-mayor and councilors of the new municipality shall be duly elected in the next general elections for local officials.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 18, 1967.
