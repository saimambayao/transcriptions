> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4837**
>
> An Act Creating Six Additional Positions of Assistant Provincial Fiscal in the Province of Iloilo, Amending for the Purpose Section Sixteen Hundred Seventy-Four of the Revised Administrative Code, as Amended
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The provisions of Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended, relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals in the Province of Iloilo, is further amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Iloilo, fifteen assistant provincial fiscals;"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
