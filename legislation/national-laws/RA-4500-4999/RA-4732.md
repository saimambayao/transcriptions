> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4732**
>
> An Act Creating the Municipality of Calintaan in the Province of Mindoro Occidental
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Barrios Concepcion, Iriron, Poypoy, Malpalon, Dagupan, Tanyag and Calintaan in the Municipality of Sablayan, Province of Mindoro Occidental, are separated from said municipality and are constituted into a distinct and independent municipality, to be known as the Municipality of Calintaan, same province.1avvphi1 The seat of government shall be located at the present site of the barrio of Calintaan.
>
> **Section 2.** The first mayor, vice-mayor and councilors of the new municipality shall be elected in the next general election for local officials and shall have qualified.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
