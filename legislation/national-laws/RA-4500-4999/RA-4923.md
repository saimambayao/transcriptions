> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4923**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of a Certain Barrio in the Municipality of Aroroy, Province of Masbate, to Barrio San Isidro
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Tete in the Municipality of Aroroy, Province of Masbate, is charged to Barrio San Isidro.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
