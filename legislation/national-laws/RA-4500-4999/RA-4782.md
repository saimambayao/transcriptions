> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4782**
>
> An Act Increasing the Number of Assistant Provincial Fiscals for the Province of Oriental Negros, Amending Sixteen Hundred Seventy-Four of the Administrative Code
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The provision of Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Administrative Code, as amended, relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals, in the Province of Oriental Negros, is further amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Oriental Negros, seven assistant provincial fiscals;"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
