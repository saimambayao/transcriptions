> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4896**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Saravia in the Province of Negros Occidental to Enrique B. Magalona in Honor of the Late Senator Enrique B. Magalona
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Saravia in the Province of Negros Occidental is changed to Enrique B. Magalona in honor of the late Senator Enrique B. Magalona.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
