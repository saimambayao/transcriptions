> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4795**
>
> An Act Providing for Two Additional Assistant Provincial Fiscals for the Province of Southern Leyte, by Further Amending Section Sixteen Hundred Seventy-Four of the Administrative Code
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Administrative Code, as amended, is further amended by inserting after the subparagraph relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals in the Province of Leyte, the following new subparagraph to read as follows:
>
> > "Southern Leyte, four assistant provincial fiscals;"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
