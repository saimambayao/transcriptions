> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4763**
>
> An Act Transferring Barrio Kiling in the Municipality of Paracales, Mountain Province, to the Municipality of Potia, Same Province
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Barrio Kiling in the Municipality of Paracales, Mountain Province, is hereby transferred to the Municipality of Potia, same province.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
