> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4981**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Albor, Province of Surigao del Norte, to Libjo
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Albor in the Province of Surigao del Norte is hereby changed to Libjo.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
