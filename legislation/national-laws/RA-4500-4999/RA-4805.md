> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4805**
>
> An Act Increasing the Bed Capacity of the Abra Provincial Hospital in the Municipality of Bangued, Province of Abra, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Abra Provincial Hospital in the Municipality of Bangued, Province of Abra, is hereby increased to fifty beds.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-seven. Thereafter, such sums as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
