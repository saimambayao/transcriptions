> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4562**
>
> An Act Amending Certain Items in Republic Acts Numbered Thirty-One Hundred and One and Twenty-Seven Hundred and One
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Items 15 and 20 on page 785 of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-one hundred and one are hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "15. Iloilo:
> >
> > (a) President Carlos P. Garcia Hall ₱200,000.00
> >
> > (b) Provincial Capitol Building, Iloilo 500,000.00
> >
> > "20. Oton:
> >
> > (a) New Provincial Capitol Building 400,000.00
> >
> > (b) Iloilo Police Academy Building 30,000.00
> >
> > Section 2. Items a, b and c, under the Municipality of Oton on page 412 of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred and one are hereby amended to read as follows:
> >
> > "Oton
> >
> > (a) Municipal Building 2,000.00"
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
