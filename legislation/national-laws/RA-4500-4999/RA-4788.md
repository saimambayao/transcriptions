> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4788**
>
> An Act Creating the Municipality of Kalilangan in the Province of Bukidnon
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Barrios Lampanosan, Macatol, Macaopao, Sabang, Kalilangan, Boribod, Pamutohan, Kabaning, Malinoa, Imbariz, Kibaritan and Mayohay in the Municipality of Pangantucan, Province of Bukidnon, are hereby separated from said municipality and constituted into a distinct and independent municipality, with the seat of government in the present site of Barrio Kalilangan. The new municipality shall be known as the Municipality of Kalilangan, same province.
>
> **Section 2.** The first mayor, vice-mayor and councilors shall be elected in the nineteen hundred sixty-seven general elections for local officials.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
