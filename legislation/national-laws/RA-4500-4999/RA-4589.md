> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4589**
>
> An Act Authorizing the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office to Hold a Lottery for the Benefit of the National League of Puericulture Centers of the Philippines, Inc.
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office is hereby authorized to hold a lottery, the whole proceeds of which shall, after deducting the prizes and expenses as provided for in Republic Act Numbered Eleven hundred sixty-nine, as amended, be turned over to the National League of Puericulture Centers of the Philippines, Inc. for its support and maintenance, and expenses incurred thereon shall be subject to accounting and auditing regulations.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
