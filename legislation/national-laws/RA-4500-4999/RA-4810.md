> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4810**
>
> An Act Amending Republic Act Numbered Four Thousand Twenty-Five, Entitled "An Act Granting the Dagupan Electric Corporation a Permit to Construct, Establish, and Operate Private Fixed Land-Based to Land-Mobile Radio Stations for the Reception and Transmission of Radio Communications in Dagupan City"
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section one of Republic Act Numbered Four thousand twenty-five is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Sec. 1. There is hereby granted to Dagupan Electric Corporation a permit to construct, maintain, and operate, subject to the approval of the Secretary of Public Works and Communications, a private fixed point-to-point land-based and land-mobile radio stations in the Philippines for the reception and transmission of wireless messages on radiotelephony, each to be provided with radio transmitting apparatus and radio receiving apparatus."
>
> **Section 2.** The title of the same Act is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "An Act granting the Dagupan Electric Corporation a permit to construct, establish and operate private fixed point-to-point land-based and land-mobile radio stations for the reception and transmission of radio communications in the Philippines."
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
