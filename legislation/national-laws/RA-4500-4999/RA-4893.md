> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4893**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Itawes Agricultural College in the Municipality of Piat, Province of Cagayan, to the Northern Luzon State College of Agriculture
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Itawes Agricultural College in the Municipality of Piat, Province of Cagayan, is hereby changed to the Northern Luzon State College of Agriculture.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
