> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4818**
>
> An Act Enlarging the Territory of the Municipality of Mabinay, Province of Negros Oriental
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrios of Pandanon, Dahili, Samac, and Namangka in the Municipality of Manjuyod, and the barrios of Tara, Campanon-an, Pantao, Cansaling, Dagbasan, Bagtic, Mayaposi, and Mambaja in the Municipality of Bais, all in the Province of Negros Oriental, are separated from said municipalities and are transferred and made part of the Municipality of Mabinay.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1aшphi1
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
