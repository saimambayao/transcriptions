> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4952**
>
> An Act Authorizing the Appropriation of Three Hundred Thousand Pesos to Implement the Provisions of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-Six Hundred Five
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of three hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to implement the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-six hundred five during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-seven. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the same purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
