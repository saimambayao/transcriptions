> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4987**
>
> An Act Creating Barrio San Isidro in the Municipality of Maribojoc, Province of Bohol
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Sitios Taytay, Datag, Saon and Kabulihan, in Barrio Tinibgan, Municipality of Maribojoc, Province of Bohol, are separated from said barrio, and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio San Isidro.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
