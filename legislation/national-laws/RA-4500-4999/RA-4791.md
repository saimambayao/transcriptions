> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4791**
>
> An Act Creating a Branch Office of the Register of Deeds of Pangasinan with Permanent Station in the Municipality of Tayug, Province of Pangasinan
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby created a branch office of the Register of Deeds of Pangasinan, with permanent station in the Municipality of Tayug, which shall be under the supervision of a deputy register of deeds who shall perform the duties and functions of the Register of Deeds for the following municipalities, to wit: Asingan, Balungao, San Manuel, Santa Maria, Natividad, San Nicolas, San Quintin, Rosales, Tayug and Umingan.
>
> **Section 2.** The said branch office shall consist of one deputy register of deeds with a salary of four thousand four hundred four pesos per annum; one senior clerk with a salary of two thousand nine hundred fifty-two pesos per annum; one clerk II with a salary of two thousand six hundred seventy-six pesos per annum; one clerk I with a salary of two thousand four hundred twenty-four pesos per annum; one janitor-messenger with a salary of two thousand one hundred sixty pesos per annum and sundry expenses in the amount of ten thousand pesos.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 3.** To carry out the purpose of this Act, twenty-four thousand six hundred sixteen pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-seven. Thereafter, such sums as may be needed for the same purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
