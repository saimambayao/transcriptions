> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4749**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Sampaloc Avenue in Quezon City to Don Tomas Morato Avenue
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Sampaloc Avenue in Quezon City is hereby changed to Don Tomas Morato Avenue, the provision of any law, ordinance, resolution or executive order to the contrary notwithstanding.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
