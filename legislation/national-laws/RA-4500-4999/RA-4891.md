> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4891**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Ebus Elementary School in Barrio San Vicente, Municipality of Guagua, Province of Pampanga, to San Vicente Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Ebus Elementary School in Barrio San Vicente, Municipality of Guagua, Province of Pampanga, is hereby changed to and shall hereafter be known as San Vicente Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
