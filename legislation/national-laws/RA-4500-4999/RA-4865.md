**An Act Naming the Portion of the Pan-Philippine Highway from Barrio Guintiguian, Municipality of Babatngon, Province of Leyte, to Barrio Pinamono-an, Municipality of Mahaplag of the Same Province as the Daniel Z. Romualdez Highway**

> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4865**
>
> An Act Naming the Portion of the Pan-Philippine Highway from Barrio Guintiguian, Municipality of Babatngon, Province of Leyte, to Barrio Pinamono-an, Municipality of Mahaplag of the Same Province as the Daniel Z. Romualdez Highway
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The portion of the Pan-Philippine Highway from Barrio Guintiguian, Municipality of Babatngon, Province of Leyte, to Barrio Pinamono-an, Municipality of Mahaplag of the same Province shall hereafter be known and referred to as the Daniel Z. Romualdez Highway.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 26, 1967.
