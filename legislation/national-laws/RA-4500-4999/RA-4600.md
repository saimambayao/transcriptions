> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4600**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Callang Municipality, Province of Isabela, to San Manuel Municipality
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Callang Municipality in the Province of Isabela, is hereby changed to San Manuel Municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
