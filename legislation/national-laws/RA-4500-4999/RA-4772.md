> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4772**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Tacloban Airport in the Province of Leyte to Speaker Daniel Z. Romualdez Airport
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Tacloban Airport in the Province of Leyte is hereby changed to Speaker Daniel Z. Romualdez Airport.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
