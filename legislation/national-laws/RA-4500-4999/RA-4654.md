> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4654**
>
> An Act Providing for the Establishment of a Community Hospital in the Municipality of Dingalan, Province of Quezon, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Two Hundred Fifty Thousand Pesos for the Purpose
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established in the Municipality of Dingalan, Province of Quezon, a community hospital to be known as Maria Aurora Community Hospital. Said hospital shall be maintained and operated under the supervision of the Director of Medical Services.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of said hospital for the fiscal year ending June thirty, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven. Such sums as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1966.
