> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4999**
>
> An Act Creating a Certain Barrio in the Municipality of Valencia, Province of Bohol
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Kisaw and Can-apo are hereby constituted into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as Barrio Mahayag, in the Municipality of Valencia, Province of Bohol.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
