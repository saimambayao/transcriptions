> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4909**
>
> AN ACT EXEMPTING FROM INCOME TAX THE TEN THOUSAND DOLLARS PRIZE OF MISS GEMMA CRUZ AS MISS INTERNATIONAL
>
> **Section 1.** The ten thousand dollars prize of Miss Gemma Cruz as Miss International, donated by her to Boys Town, shall not be included in gross income and shall be exempt from income tax, any provision of the National International Revenue Code to the contrary notwithstanding.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967
