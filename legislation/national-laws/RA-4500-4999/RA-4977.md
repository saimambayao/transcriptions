> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4977**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Aurora, Province of Quezon, to San Francisco
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Aurora, Province of Quezon, is hereby changed to San Francisco.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1967.
