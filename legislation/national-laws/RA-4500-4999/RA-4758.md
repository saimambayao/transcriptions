> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4758**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Ebus in the Municipality of Guagua, Province of Pampanga, to Barrio San Vicente
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Ebus in the Municipality of Guagua Province of Pampanga, is changed to Barrio San Vicente.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1966.
