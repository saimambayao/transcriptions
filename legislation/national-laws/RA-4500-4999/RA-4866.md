> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4866**
>
> An Act Declaring Basista, Province of Pangasinan, Created by Executive Order Numbered Four Hundred Forty-Six Dated September Five, Nineteen Hundred Sixty-One, as a Duly Constituted Municipality
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The town of Basista, Province of Pangasinan, created by Executive Order Numbered Four hundred forty-six dated September five, nineteen hundred sixty-one, is hereby declared a duly constituted municipality as of the date it was created by said executive order.
>
> All elective officials of said town shall continue to hold office until their successors are elected and shall have qualified.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: May 8, 1967.
