> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3300**
>
> **An Act Further Amending Republic Act Numbered Twenty Hundred Ninety-Three, Entitled "An Act Appropriating Funds for the Public Works and for Other Purposes" as Amended**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (g) , page 250, Group II for the Province of Negros Oriental, and under Title M-Short term Projects of Republic Act Numbered Twenty hundred ninety-three, as amended by Republic Act Numbered Twenty-eight hundred fifty-six, is further amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(g) Repair and/or improvement, Larena-Basac-Maria Road ₱10,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
