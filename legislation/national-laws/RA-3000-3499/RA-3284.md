> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3284**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (28), Page 434, Title (A), Group IV for the Province of Iloilo, Section Three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item 28, page 434, title (A), Group IV for the Province of Iloilo, Section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(20) So-ol barrio school building, Barotac Nuevo Construction, repair and improvement ₱3,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
