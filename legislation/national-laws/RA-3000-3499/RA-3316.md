> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3316**
>
> **An Act Further Amending Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred, Entitled "An Act Appropriating Funds for Public Works," as Amended**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (f -1), page 352, Group II, for the Province of Negros Oriental, Section two, entitled "Short Term Projects," of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred, as amended by Republic Act Numbered Twenty-eight hundred fifty-eight, is hereby further amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(f-1) Larena-Basac-Maria Road Repair and/or improvement ₱10,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
