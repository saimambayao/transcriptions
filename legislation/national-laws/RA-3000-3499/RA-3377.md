> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3377**
>
> **An Act Declaring Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Lupi, Province of Camarines Sur, as Duly Constituted Barrios Thereof**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrios of San Vicente, Buenasuerte, Napolidan, San Jose, Sooc, La Purisima, Haluban, Caibigan, Barrera Jr., Tapi, Badas, Bagong-Sikat, Colacling, Tanawan, Lourdes, Belcruz, Alleomar, Salvacion, Tible, Casay, Del Carmen and San Pedro in the Municipality of Lupi, Province of Camarines Sur, are hereby declared as duly constituted barrios of said municipality.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
