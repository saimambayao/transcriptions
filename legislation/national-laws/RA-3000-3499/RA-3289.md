> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3289**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (62), Page 162, Paragraph (G) for the Province of Laguna, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred and Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (62), Page 162, Paragraph (G) for the Province of Laguna, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred and thirteen, is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "62. Municipal Building, Alaminos Aid to the municipality for construction and/or improvement ₱3,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** The above amendment shall pertain only to the unexpended balance of the appropriation.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
