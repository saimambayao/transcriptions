> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3431**
>
> **An Act to Establish the Western Cagayan Vocational High School in the Municipality of Lasam, Province of Cagayan, and to Authorize the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the supervision of the Director of Public Schools, a vocational high school in the Municipality of Lasam, Province of Cagayan, to be known as the Western Cagayan Vocational High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred and sixty-two. Such sums as may be needed for the same purpose in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
