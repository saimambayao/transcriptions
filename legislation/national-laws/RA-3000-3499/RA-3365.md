> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3365**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Balangigan Barrio School in the Municipality of Duenas, Province of Iloilo, to Biba Barrio School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Balangigan Barrio School in the Municipality of Duenas, Province of Iloilo, is changed to Biba Barrio School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
