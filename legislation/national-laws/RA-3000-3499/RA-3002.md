> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3002**
>
> **An Act to Establish, Organize and Maintain a Regional National Secondary School of Arts and Trades in the Municipality of Dipolog, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, to be Known as the Zamboanga del Norte School of Arts and Trades, and to Authorize the Appropriation of Funds for the Purpose**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Secretary of Education is hereby authorized to establish, organize and maintain a regional national secondary school of arts and trades in the Municipality of Dipolog, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, to be known as the Zamboanga del Norte Schools of Arts and Trades.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of the said school.
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to locate and acquired or cause to be located and acquired a suitable site for the said school, which may be public or private land situated within the Municipality of Dipolog, Province of Zamboanga del Norte.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
