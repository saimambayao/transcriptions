> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3053**
>
> **An Act Providing Minimum Compensation for Certain Members of the City Police Force of the City of Cebu, Amending for the Purpose Section Thirty-Five of Commonwealth Act Numbered Fifty-Eight**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section thirty-five of Commonwealth Act Numbered Fifty-eight is amended by adding at the end thereof the following new paragraph:
>
> > "The following members of the City Police Force shall receive minimum compensation at the following rates:
> >
> > "Patrolmen and Patrolmen
> >
> > Detectives ₱2,160.00 per annum
> >
> > "Corporals and Corporal Detectives 2,280.00 per annum
> >
> > "Sergeants and Sergeant Detectives 2,400.00 per annum
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1961.
