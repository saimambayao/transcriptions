> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3334**
>
> **An Act Transferring the Seat of Government of the Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, from the Barrio of Mucas to the Barrio of Salug**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The seat of government of the Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is transferred from the barrio of Mucas to the barrio of Salug.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
