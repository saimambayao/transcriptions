> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3418**
>
> **An Act Creating the Municipality of Dumaran in the Province of Palawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrios of Dumaran, San Juan, Bacao, Calasag and Bohol in the Municipality of Araceli; the barrios of Ilian, Capayas, and Leguit in the Municipality of Roxas; and the barrios of Danleg and Pangolasian in the Municipality of Taytay, all in the province of Palawan, are separated from the said municipalities, and are constituted into a distinct and independent municipality, to be known as the Municipality of Dumaran, with the seat of government at the site of the barrio of Dumaran.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon the election of the new officials in the general elections for local officials in November of nineteen hundred and sixty-three.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
