> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3030**
>
> **An Act to Amend Project Number (B-224.79), Page 464-A, Appendix (A), Title (B) of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred and Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Project Number (B-224.79), page 464-A, Appendix (A), Title (B) of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred and thirteen is amended to read as follows:
>
> B-224.79 Tiniclan Creek GOB 50.00
>
> To Bridge Minabitasan Road (50.00)
>
> To replace temporary Talembong-Na bridge with permanent one (50.00)
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 8, 1961.
