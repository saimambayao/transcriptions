> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3454**
>
> **An Act Granting Philippine Citizenship to Miguel Agorilla**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Miguel Agorilla is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives appurtenant thereto: *Provided,* That this grant shall become effective upon Miguel Agorilla taking an oath of allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines and executing a renunciation of his former citizenship, copies of which shall be recorded in the civil registrars office in the locality of his residence: *Provided, further,* That the citizenship herein granted carries with it all the duties and responsibilities incident to such citizenship.
>
