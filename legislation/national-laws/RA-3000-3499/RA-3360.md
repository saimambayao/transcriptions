> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3360**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Camogtong, Municipality of Despujols, Province of Romblon, to Mabini**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Barrio Camogtong in the Municipality of Despujols, Province of Romblon, shall hereafter be known as Barrio Mabini.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
