> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3078**
>
> **An Act Providing for Two Assistant Provincial Fiscals for the Province of Southern Leyte, by Further Amending Section Sixteen Hundred Seventy-Four of the Administrative Code**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Administrative Code, as amended, relative to assistant provincial fiscals, is further amended so that the Province of Southern Leyte shall have two assistant provincial fiscals.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1961.
