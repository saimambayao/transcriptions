> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3033**
>
> **An Act Converting the Puericulture Center in the Municipality of Calinog, Province of Iloilo, into a Maternity and Childrens Hospital**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Calinog Puericulture Center in the Municipality of Calinog, Province of Iloilo, is hereby converted into a maternity and childrens hospital to be known as the Calinog Maternity and Childrens Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of fifty thousand pesos is hereby appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the construction of the necessary buildings and purchase of equipment.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The maternity and childrens hospital established under this Act shall be financed in accordance with the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred and thirty-nine.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 8, 1961.
