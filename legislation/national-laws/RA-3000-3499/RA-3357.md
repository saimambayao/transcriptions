> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3357**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Nuling, Province of Cotabato**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Nuling, Province of Cotabato, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to wit:
>
> 1. Sitio Tambo to Barrio Tambo;
>
> 2. Sitio Olas to Barrio Olas;
>
> 3. Sitio Bungabong to Barrio Bungabong;
>
> 4. Sitio Tuka to Barrio Tuka;
>
> 5. Sitio Panambacan to Barrio Panambacan;
>
> 6. Sitio Tiduran to Barrio Tiduran;
>
> 7. Sitio Nara to Barrio Nara;
>
> 8. Sitio Tula-Tula to Barrio Tula-Tula;
>
> 9. Sitio Banatin to Barrio Banatin; and
>
> 10. Sitio Kurmatan to Barrio Kurmatan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
