> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3281**
>
> **An Act to Amend an Item in Republic Act Numbered Fourteen Hundred Eleven**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item 24, Group I, subsection aj, for the Province of Negros Oriental, Section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "24. Owacan Barrio School Building, Jimalalud Construction one room ₱5,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
