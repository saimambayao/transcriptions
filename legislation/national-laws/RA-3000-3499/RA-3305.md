> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3305**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (u), Page Two Hundred Eighty-One, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (u), page two hundred eighty-one, paragraph f, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(u) Lambunao Municipal building, construction, improvement and/or repair ₱25,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
