> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3427**
>
> **An Act Creating the Municipal District of Quezon in the Province of Nueva Vizcaya**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** All the barrios and sitios of the Magat River including Daresbes, Boliwao, Caliat, Dariba, Mayabbuang, Madiangat and Nalub-unan in the Municipality of Solano, Province of Nueva Vizcaya, are separated from said municipality, and constituted into a distinct and independent municipal district to be known as the Municipal District of Quezon. The Magat River is the boundary between the Municipality of Solano and this municipal district. Unless otherwise provided by two-thirds vote of all the members of the Municipal Council of said municipal district within one year from its creation, the seat of government of the new Municipal District of Quezon shall be in the present site of the barrio of Caliat.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon the election of the new municipal officials in the general elections of the municipal officials in November, nineteen hundred and sixty-three.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
