> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3166**
>
> **An Act Converting the Calubian Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Calubian, Province of Leyte, into a General Hospital to be Known as the Northwestern Leyte General Hospital, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Calubian Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Calubian, Province of Leyte, is hereby converted into a general hospital to be known as the Northwestern Leyte General Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, operation and maintenance of said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-two. Thereafter, such sum as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1961.
