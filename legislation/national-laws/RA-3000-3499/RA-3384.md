> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3384**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Cantagda in the Municipality of Cajidiocan, Province of Romblon**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Cantagda in the barrio of Danao, Municipality of Cajidiocan, Province of Romblon, is separated from said barrio and converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Cantagda.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
