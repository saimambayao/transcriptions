> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3330**
>
> **An Act Authorizing the Appropriation of Seventy Thousand Pesos for the Acquisition of a School Site for the Tanauan School of Fisheries and for the Improvement Thereof**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of seventy thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the acquisition of a school site for the Tanauan School of Fisheries in Ambulong, Tanauan, Province of Batangas, established under Republic Act Numbered Fifteen hundred fifty-nine, and for the improvement thereof.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
