> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3339**
>
> **An Act Creating an Additional Position of Assistant Provincial Fiscal in the Province of Palawan, Amending for This Purpose Section Sixteen Hundred Seventy-Four of the Revised Administrative Code, as Amended**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The provision of Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended, relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals in the Province of Palawan, is further amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Palawan, two assistant provincial fiscals."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
