> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3381**
>
> **An Act Declaring Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Oras, Province of Samar, as Duly Constituted Barrios Thereof**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrios of Pangodtan, Sabang, Gamot, Cabagakyan and Himamayo in the Municipality of Oras, Province of Samar are hereby declared as duly constituted barrios of said municipality.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
