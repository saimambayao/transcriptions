> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3382**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Province of Palawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Taritien in the Municipality of Aborlan, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Taritien.
>
> **Section 2.** The sitio of Sibaring in Apo-Bugsuk Island in the Municipality of Balabac, same province, is separated from the barrio of Apo-Bugsuk Island and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Sibaring.1avvphi1 T he barrio of Sibaring shall be from and including the sitio of Balug-Bulugin to and including the sitio of Baring-Baringin.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
