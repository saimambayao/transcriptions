> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3175**
>
> **An Act to Further Amend Commonwealth Act Numbered One Hundred Eighty-Six, as Amended, to Provide for the Payment by the National Government of the Additional Premiums for Services Prior to June Sixteen, Nineteen Hundred Fifty-One**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Subsection (a) of Section seven of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred eighty-six, as amended, is hereby further amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Sec. 7. *Additional amounts or premiums.* -(a) For the amount of annuity corresponding to the services rendered by an employee prior to June sixteen, nineteen hundred and fifty-one, the National Government shall pay the necessary additional amounts or premiums as may be determined by the System, which shall be paid in equal annual amortizations for a period not to exceed fifty years under such terms and conditions as the Board may prescribe.1âшphi1 The sums necessary for this purpose are deemed appropriated in accordance with Section twenty-seven of this Act.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval: *Provided,* That the additional amounts or premiums for services rendered prior to June sixteen, nineteen hundred fifty-one, payable by an employer which are unpaid as of the approval of this amendatory Act shall be paid by the National Government.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1961.
