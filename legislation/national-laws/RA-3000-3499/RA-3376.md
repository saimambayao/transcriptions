> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3376**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrios of Luy-a and Dayhagon in the Municipality of Medellin, Province of Cebu**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Luy-a and Dayhagon in the Municipality of Medellin, Province of Cebu, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Luy-a and Dayhagon, respectively.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
