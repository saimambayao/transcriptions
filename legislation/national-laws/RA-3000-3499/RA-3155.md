> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3155**
>
> **An Act Converting the Dr. Gregorio B. Tan Memorial Hospital in the Municipality of Laoang, Province of Samar, into a General Hospital to be Known as the Dr. Gregorio B. Tan General Hospital**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Dr. Gregorio B. Tan Memorial Hospital in the Municipality of Laoang, Province of Samar, is converted into a general hospital to be known as the Dr. Gregorio B. Tan General Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The operation and maintenance of this hospital shall be governed by the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, otherwise known as the Hospital Financing Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1961.
