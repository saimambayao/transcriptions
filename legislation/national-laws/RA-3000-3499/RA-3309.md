> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3309**
>
> **An Act Amending Certain Item of Republic Act Numbered Two Thousand Seven Hundred and One, Entitled "An Act to Appropriate Funds for Public Works, Synchronizing the Same with Previous Public Works Appropriations"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (a), under Group II (re projects for the Province of Negros Oriental), page 534, Section two, entitled "Short-term Projects," of Republic Act Numbered Two thousand seven hundred and one, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(a) Santa Catalina Municipal Building ₱4,000.00
> >
> > "(a-1) Lazi Municipal Building Construction 17,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
