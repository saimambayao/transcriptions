> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3312**
>
> **An Act to Amend a Certain Item in Republic Act Numbered Twenty Hundred and Ninety-Three**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (b), under 11-Cebu, page 162, paragraph (b), Title G, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twenty hundred and ninety-three, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(b) Talaytay Communal Irrigation Project, Argao ₱20,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
