> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3280**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Seven Hundred and One**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Items (a) and (d) under Group IV, for the Province of Cebu page 341, paragraph c, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred and one is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(a) For the construction, repair and/or improvement of provincial, municipal and/or barrio roads and other public works projects situated in the Municipality of Sibonga, Cebu ₱40,000.00
> >
> > "(d) For the construction, repair and/or improvement of provincial, municipal and/or barrio roads and other public works projects situated in the Municipality of Alcoy, Cebu 30,000.00."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
