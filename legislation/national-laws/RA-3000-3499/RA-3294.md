> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3294**
>
> **An Act Reappropriating Certain Sums Appropriated in Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sums appropriated in items (g) and (af ), pages 119 and 121, respectively, subparagraph 70 for the City of Manila, paragraph, "a", Title B, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred, to wit:
>
> > "(g) Marques de Comillas (NaC) Street, from Oregon to Isaac Peral and around Paco Cemetery Improvement and paving ₱25,000.00
> >
> > "(af) Esguerra Street, Aua-Marina Section, San Andres Subdivision 15,000.00
> >
> > and the sums appropriated in items (e) and (g) page 323 and item ( j), page 324, all under Group IV, subparagraph (29) for the City of Manila, paragraph" f Section two of the same Act, to wit:
> >
> > "(e) Sases Puericulture Center General improvement ₱10,000.00
> >
> > "(g) Waiting Shed in Herran and Calderon Streets, Sta. Ana Construction 10,000.00
> >
> > "(j) Jose Rizal Monument, De la Peña Paco Construction 10,000.00"
> >
> > totalling seventy thousand pesos, are hereby reappropriated, for the following purposes:
> >
> > (1) Social Welfare Administration Building, Kansas St., Malate Construction ₱53,000.00
> >
> > (2) Arellano Streetfrom Diamante to Esguerra Ext., and Diamante Street from Pasig Line to Arellano Improvement 17,000.00
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
