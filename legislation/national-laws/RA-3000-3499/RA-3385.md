> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3385**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** (a) The sitio of Balanga in the barrio of San Andres, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, is separated from said barrio and converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Sto. Niño Domingo; and
>
> (b) The sitio of San Antonio-Aplaya in the barrio of San Antonio, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, is separated from said barrio and converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Bagong Danglayan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
