> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3346**
>
> **An Act Granting Gratuity to the Heirs of the Late Representative Atanacio Mapping and Appropriating Funds for the Purpose**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The legal heirs of the late Representative Atanacio MAPPING, who was among those killed in the sinking of S.S. Corregidor, are hereby given a gratuity of fifteen thousand pesos.
>
> **Section 2.** There is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the sum of fifteen thousand pesos for the purpose indicated in the preceding section.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
