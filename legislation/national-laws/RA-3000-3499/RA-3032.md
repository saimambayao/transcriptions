> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3032**
>
> **An Act to Amend Item (21), Page 208, Title (A) of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred and Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (21), page 208, Title (A) of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred and thirteen is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(21) Dumangas Municipal Building, Iloilo ₱20,000
> >
> > "(21a) Dumangas Public Market, Iloilo 40,000
> >
> > "(21b) Dumangas Public Plaza or Park, Iloilo 20,000."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 8, 1961.
