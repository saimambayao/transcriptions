> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3097, June 17, 1961 ]**
>
> **AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE SUM OF ONE MILLION ONE HUNDRED SIXTY-FIVE THOUSAND PESOS FOR BREEDING STATIONS IN THE PHILIPPINES.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
