> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3103**
>
> **An Act Extending to Ten More Years the Term of the Contractual Grant Under Commonwealth Act Numbered Four Hundred and Eighty-Five**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The term of the contractual grant under Commonwealth Act Numbered Four hundred eighty-five is hereby extended to ten more years from the date the original term of twenty-five years shall have expired, subject to the same terms and conditions.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1961.
