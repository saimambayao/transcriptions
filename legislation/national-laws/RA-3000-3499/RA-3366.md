> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3366**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Laparan in the Municipality of Kauswagan, Province of Lanao del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Larapan in the Municipality of Kauswagan, Province of Lanao del Norte, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Larapan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
