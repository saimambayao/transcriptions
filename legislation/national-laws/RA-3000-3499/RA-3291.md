> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3291**
>
> **An Act to Amend Section One of Republic Act Numbered Eighteen Hundred Twenty-Two**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Section one of Republic Act Numbered Eighteen hundred twenty-two is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Sec. 1. The sum of ten thousand pesos appropriated in item (19), page one hundred eighty-three, subparagraph "ab", for the City of Manila, paragraph 4, title A, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is hereby reappropriated for the following purposes:
> >
> > "(1) Sta. Clara Street Asphalting from Lamayan to Havana ₱6,000.00
> >
> > "(2) Eden Streetfrom Herran to Rennaisance Improvement 2,000.00
> >
> > "(3) La Torre Streetfrom Calderon to M. Roxas Improvement 2,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
