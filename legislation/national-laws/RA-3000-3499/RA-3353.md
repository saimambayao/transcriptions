> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3353**
>
> **An Act to Change the Name of Barrio No. 2 Elementary School of Laoag, Ilocos Norte, to Agripino P. Santos Elementary School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name "Barrio No. 2. Elementary School" in the Municipality of Laoag, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Agripino P. Santos Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
