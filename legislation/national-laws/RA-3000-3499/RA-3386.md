> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3386**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Lapog, Province of Ilocos Sur, to San Juan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Lapog in the Province of Ilocos Sur is changed to San Juan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
