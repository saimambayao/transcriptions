> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3285**
>
> **An Act Amending Item Fifty-One, Page Seven Hundred Eighty-Six, Section Three of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item fifty-one, page seven hundred eighty-six, Section three of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "51. Construction and/or repair of Home Economics building, Lalig Barrio School, Tiaong ₱5,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
