> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3027**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Abellana National Vocational School in the City of Cebu to Abellana National School**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Abellana National Vocational School in the City of Cebu is hereby changed to Abellana National School. General courses as well as vocational courses shall be offered in said school.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 7, 1961.
