> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3361**
>
> **An Act Making the Barrio of Matipunso of the Municipality of Tiaong, Province of Quezon, a Part of the Municipality of San Antonio, Same Province**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrio of Matipunso is hereby separated from the Municipality of Tiaong, Province of Quezon, and is consolidated with and made a part of the Municipality of San Antonio, same province.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
