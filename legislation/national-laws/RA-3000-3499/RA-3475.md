> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3475**
>
> **An Act Converting the Bayambang High School in the Municipality Bayambang, Province of Pangasinan, into a National High School to be Known as the Bayambang National High School, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Bayambang High School in the Municipality of Bayambang, Province of Pangasinan, is hereby converted into a national High School to be known as the Bayambang National High School. A comprehensive curriculum which includes both academic and vocational courses shall be offered.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to reorganize said school accordingly.
>
> **Section 3.** The sum of two hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, operation and maintenance of the said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-three.1âшphi1 Thereafter, the necessary sum may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 16, 1962.
