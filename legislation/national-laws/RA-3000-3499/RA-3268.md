> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3268**
>
> **An Act Authorizing the Zamboanga City Regional School of Arts and Trades to Offer the Teacher Curriculum in Industrial Education, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Zamboanga City Regional School of Arts and Trades in Zamboanga City is hereby authorized to offer the teacher curriculum in industrial education beginning with the school year ending nineteen hundred sixty-two.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to carry out the purpose of this Act. Thereafter, the sum necessary for the same purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1961.
