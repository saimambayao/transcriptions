> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3012**
>
> **An Act to Amend Heading 73.14, Chapter 73, Schedule XV, Section One Hundred Four of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred Thirty-Seven, Otherwise Known as the Tariff and Customs Code of the Philippines**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Heading 73.14, Chapter 73, Schedule XV, Section one hundred four of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred thirty-seven is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "73.14. Iron or Steel Wire, single strand, with
> >
> > a. cross-sectional dimension of 5.5mm to 6.5m ad val. 10%
> >
> > b. cross-sectional dimension of 1.65mm to 5.49m ad val. 75%
> >
> > or G.W., 100 kg ₱16.00
> >
> > c. other ad val. 15%
> >
> > d. coated but not insulate ad val. 15%"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1960.
