> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3295**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Provisions of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen Regarding Public Works Projects for the Province of Iloilo**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Priority No. 23, page 43-B and Priorities Nos. 30 and 33, page 44, group III, for the Province of Iloilo, Appendix B, Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen, are hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "23. Janiuay (Elementary) Tolarucan Completion ₱3,000.00
> >
> > "30. Janiuay Poblacion Repair 4,000.00
> >
> > "3. Leon (High School) Poblacion Repair 6,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
