> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3451, April 25, 1962 ]**
>
> **AN ACT APPROPRIATING ADDITIONAL FUNDS FOR THE OPERATION OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES FOR THE FISCAL YEAR ENDING JUNE THIRTY, NINETEEN HUNDRED SIXTY-TWO.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of three million pesos, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated as additional funds for the operation of the House of Representatives for the fiscal year ending June thirty, nineteen  hundred sixty-two, itemized as follows:
>
> |  |  |
> | --- | --- |
> | 1. For settlement of unpaid obligations with the Bureau of Printing | ₱500,000.00 |
> | 2. For repairs and improvement of rooms and buildings | 250,000.00 |
> | 3. For payment of overtime services of employees during session | 157,000.00 |
> | 4. For expenses in connection with committee meetings | 250,000.00 |
> | 5. For terminal leave of officials and employees | 400,000.00 |
> | 6. For the payment of retirement and insurance premiums | 443,000.00 |
> | 7. For employment of supplementary personnel for a period of three months | 450,000.00 |
> | 8. For supplies and equipment | 550,000.00 |
> | TOTAL | ₱3,000,000.00 |
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, April 25, 1962.
