> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3390**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Bugsuk in the Municipality of Balabac, Province of Palawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Bugsuk in the Municipality of Balabac, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Bugsuk.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
