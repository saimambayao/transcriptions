> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3391**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Province of Marinduque**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Kay Duque, Mabuhay, Dampulan and Talawan in the Municipality of Torrijos, Province of Marinduque, are converted into barrios to be known as the barrios of Kay Duque, Mabuhay, Dampulan and Talawan, respectively.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The sitio of Bambangalon in the Municipality of Boac, same province, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of Bambangalon.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
