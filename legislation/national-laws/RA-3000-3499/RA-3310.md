> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3310**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Seven Hundred One**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item 20, page 119, subparagraph 54, for the Province of Tarlac, paragraph c, Title B, Section one Republic Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred one, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "20. San Felipe-Mangandingay Road, San Manuel, Tarlac 5,000.00"
> >
> > Section 2. Item (db), page 663, Group I, subparagraph 53, for the Province of Tarlac, paragraph c, Section two of the same Act, is hereby amended to read as follows:
> >
> > "(db) Camposanto 2nd Barrio School, Moncada, Tarlac Construction, repair and/or improvement 5,000.00"
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
