> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3288**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Three Hundred One**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (cu) on page 255, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred one is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(cu) Pook-Biasong Road, Talisay, Cebu ₱5,000.00."
> >
> > Section 2. Item (cv) on page 255, Section one of the same Act is amended to read as follows:
> >
> > "(cv) Lawaan-Maghaway Road, Talisay, Cebu ₱5,000.00"
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
