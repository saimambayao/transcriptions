> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3374**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Manlabang in the Municipality of Caibiran, Subprovince of Biliran, Province of Leyte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Manlabang, Jubasan, Balilid and Toril in the Municipality of Caibiran, Sub-province of Biliran, Province of Leyte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Manlabang.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
