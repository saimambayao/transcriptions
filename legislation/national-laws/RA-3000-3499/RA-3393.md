> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3393**
>
> **An Act Creating a Certain Barrio in the Municipality of Diffun, Province of Nueva Vizcaya**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Amañga, Campamiento, Veniado, Peña, Dumanting Decal-la, and Makati, in the Municipality of Diffun, Province of Nueva Vizcaya, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Magsaysay.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
