> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3378**
>
> **An Act Declaring Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Bago, Province of Negros Occidental, as Duly Constituted Barrios Thereof**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrios of Tabunan, Busay, Sampinit, Malingin, Bagroy, Alianza, Ma-ao Central, Binobohan, Ilijan, Mailum and Bacong-Montilla in the Municipality of Bago, Province of Negros Occidental, are hereby declared as duly constituted barrios of said municipality.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
