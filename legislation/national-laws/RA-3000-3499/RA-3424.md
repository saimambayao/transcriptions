> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3424**
>
> **An Act Changing the Name of the Atimonan Emergency Hospital to Doña Marta Memorial Hospital**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** In recognition of the invaluable services rendered to the people of the municipality of Atimonan, Province of Quezon, by the late Doña Marta Montes Guinto, a former civic and spiritual leader of the said municipality, the name of the Atimonan Emergency Hospital is changed to Doña Marta Memorial Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
