> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3203**
>
> **An Act to Amend Republic Act Numbered Eighteen Hundred Forty-Five, Entitled "An Act Granting the Tagum Agricultural Development Company, Inc. a Temporary Permit to Construct, Maintain and Operate Private Fixed Point-to-Point and Land-Based Radio Stations for the Reception and Transmission of Radio Communications Within the Philippines"**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section six of Republic Act Numbered Eighteen hundred forty-five is hereby repealed.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1961.
