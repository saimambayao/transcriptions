> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3345**
>
> AN ACT TO AMEND SECTIONS ONE AND TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINE HUNDRED TWENTY-FOUR, STANDARDIZING THE SALARIES OF MUNICIPAL JUDGES IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE CLASSIFICATION OF CHARTERED CITIES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.
>
> **Section 1.** Section 1 of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred twenty-four is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> "Sec. 1. The annual salary of each judge of the municipal courts of chartered cities shall be:
>
> "(a) Of the City of Manila, eleven thousand six hundred pesos;
>
> "(b) Of Baguio, Quezon City and Pasay City, ten thousand six hundred pesos;
>
> "(c) Of first class cities, nine thousand six hundred pesos;
>
> "(d) Of second class cities, eight thousand seven hundred sixty pesos;
>
> "(e) Of third class cities, seven thousand eight hundred pesos;
>
> "(f) Of fourth class cities, seven thousand two hundred pesos; and
>
> "(g) Of fifth class cities, six thousand six hundred pesos."
>
> **Section 2.** Section two of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred twenty-four is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> "Sec. 2. The municipal judges shall be entitled to thirty days vacation leave with pay for each year of service.
>
> "Any provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, the designation of the positions of municipal judge in chartered cities in the Philippines is hereby changed to that of city judge."
>
> **Section 3.** All Acts or parts of Acts inconsistent herewith are hereby repealed.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Enacted, without Executive signature on June 18, 1961.
