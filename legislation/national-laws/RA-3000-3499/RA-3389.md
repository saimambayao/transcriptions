> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3389**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Alaminos, Province of Pangasinan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** (a) The sitio of Quibuar East and West in the barrio of Alos, Municipality of Alaminos, Province of Pangasinan, is separated from said barrio and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Quibuar; and
>
> (b) The sitio of Dimayat in the barrio of Alos, Municipality of Alaminos, Province of Pangasinan, is separated from said barrio and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Dimayat.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
