> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3355**
>
> **An Act Creating Barrios in the Province of Leyte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Pagsulhugon and Victory in the Municipality of Babatngon, Province of Leyte, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Pagsulhugon and Victory, respectively.
>
