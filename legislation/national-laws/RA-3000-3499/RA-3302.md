> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3302**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Seven Hundred and One**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Items (e) and (f) under Group III, for the Province of Cebu, page 193, paragraph (b), Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred and one are hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(e) Tungkop Elementary School Building, Minglanilla Repair and remodelling ₱8,000.00
> >
> > "(f) Carcar Municipal Hall, Carcar Construction 10,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
