> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3368**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of San Jose in the Municipality of Polanco, Province of Zamboanga del Norte**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Sangaynapo, Caloyan, Napuyan, Bariocan and Maliwanag in the Municipality of Polanco, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Jose.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
