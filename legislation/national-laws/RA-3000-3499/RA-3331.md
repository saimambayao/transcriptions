> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3331**
>
> **An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Palawan**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of San Miguel, Arasan, New Cuyo, Salvacion and Narra in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Palawan, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of San Miguel, Arasan, New Cuyo, Salvacion and Narra, respectively.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
