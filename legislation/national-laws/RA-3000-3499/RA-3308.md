> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3308**
>
> **An Act to Amend Certain Items in Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (b), paragraph II, page 218, Section 2-f, of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(b) Orense Park, Bauan Construction and/or improvement ₱10,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
