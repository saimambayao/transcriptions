> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3301**
>
> **An Act Reappropriating Certain Sums Appropriated in Republic Act Numbered Sixteen Hundred Thirteen**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sums appropriated in Priorities Numbered 50, 52, 60 and 61, page 58-B, group II, for the Province of Leyte, appendix B of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen, to wit:
>
> > "50 Bato Katipunan Construction ₱2,000.00
> >
> > "52 Bato Tabunoc Construction 2,000.00
> >
> > "60 Bato Mabini Construction 2,000.00
> >
> > "61 Bato Naga Construction 2,000.00
>
> totalling eight thousand pesos, are hereby reappropriated, for the construction and/or improvement of the Central School in the Municipality of Bato, Province of Leyte.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
