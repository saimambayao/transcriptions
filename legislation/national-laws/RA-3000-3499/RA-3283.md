> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3283**
>
> **An Act Amending Item (l), (j) and (m), Page Three Hundred Eighty-Eight, and (ad), Page Three Hundred Eighty-Nine, Section Two (f) of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen Hundred**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Items (l), (j) and (m), page three hundred eighty-eight, and (ad), page three hundred eighty-nine, Section two (f) of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred, are hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(l) San Isidro Health Center Building, Mauban Construction ₱2,500.00
> >
> > "(l-a) Balibago Health Center Building, Mauban Construction 2,500.00
> >
> > "(j) Maria Aurora Municipal Building Construction and/or repair 5,000.00
> >
> > "(m) Bagong Bayan-Sadsaran Road, Mauban Completion 4,500.00
> >
> > "(ad) Sabang Barrio School, Polillo Construction 5,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
