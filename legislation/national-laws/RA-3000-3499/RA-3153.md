> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3153**
>
> **An Act Converting the Calbayog City Emergency Hospital in the City of Calbayog into a General Hospital to be Known as the Calbayog City General Hospital**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1 T**he Calbayog City Emergency Hospital in the City of Calbayog is converted into a general hospital to be known as the Calbayog City General Hospital.
>
> **Section 2 T**he operation and maintenance of this hospital shall be governed by the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred thirty-nine, otherwise known as the Hospital Financing Act.
>
> **Section 3 T**his Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1961.
