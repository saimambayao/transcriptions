> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3287**
>
> **An Act Amending Item (f) on Page 440, Subparagraph 38, for the Province of Negros Oriental, Group I, Paragraph d, Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Three Hundred and One**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (f) on page 440, subparagraph 38, for the Province of Negros Oriental, Group I, paragraph d, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred and one, is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(f) La Libertad-Pacuan Road Construction and/or improvement ₱10,000.00."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
