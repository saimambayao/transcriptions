> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3371**
>
> **An Act Constituting Sitios Cabatuan and Atlas in the Municipality of Bontoc, Province of Leyte, into a Barrio to be Known as Barrio Talisay**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Sitios Cabatuan and Atlas in the Municipality of Bontoc, Province of Leyte, are hereby constituted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Talisay.1avvphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
