> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3163**
>
> **An Act Providing for the Establishment of an Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Carmen, Province of Bohol, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the supervision of the Director of Medical Services, an emergency hospital in the Municipality of Carmen, Province of Bohol, to be known as the Carmen Emergency Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-two. Thereafter, such sums as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Acts.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1961.
