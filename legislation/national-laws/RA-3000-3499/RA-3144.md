> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3144**
>
> **An Act Providing for the Establishment, Maintenance and Operation of a General Hospital in the Municipality of Himamaylan, Province of Negros Occidental, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the Bureau of Medical Services, a twenty-five-bed general hospital in the Municipality of Himamaylan, Province of Negros Occidental, to be known as the Occidental Negros South General Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Health shall promulgate such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry into effect the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The sum of seventy-five thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the maintenance and operation of said hospital: *Provided,* That no amount herein authorized to be appropriated will be released unless the Secretary of Health certifies that a suitable hospital building is available.1avvphi1 In subsequent years, the operation and maintenance of said hospital shall be financed in accordance with Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred and thirty-nine.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 17, 1961.
