> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 3367**
>
> **An Act Creating the Barrio of Quinale in the Municipality of Calabanga, Province of Camarines Sur**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Quinale in the Municipality of Calabanga, Province of Camarines Sur, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Quinale.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1961.
