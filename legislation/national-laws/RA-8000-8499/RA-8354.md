# Republic Act No. 8354

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY LIPIT, MUNICIPALITY OF CABUGAO, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS LIPIT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Tenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8354, October 09, 1997 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY LIPIT, MUNICIPALITY OF CABUGAO, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS LIPIT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a national high school in Barangay Lipit, Municipality of Cabugao, Province of Ilocos Sur, to be known as Lipit National High School.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue the rules and regulations to implement this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, Lapsed into law on October 9, 1997 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
