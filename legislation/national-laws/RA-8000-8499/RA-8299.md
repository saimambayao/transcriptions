# Republic Act No. 8299

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SIBULAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO POLILLO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLILLO, PROVINCE OF QUEZON**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 8299
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1997/ra_8299_1997.html)

> Tenth Congress
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8299, June 06, 1997 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SIBULAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO POLILLO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLILLO, PROVINCE OF QUEZON
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Sibulan National High School in the Municipality of Polillo, Province of Quezon, is hereby changed to Polillo National High School.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on June 6, 1997 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
