# Republic Act No. 8137

**AN ACT CREATING A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RAMON, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO BE KNOWN AS THE RAMON MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 8137, July 16, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CREATING A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RAMON, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO BE KNOWN AS THE RAMON MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a twenty-five-bed capacity municipal hospital in the Municipality of Ramon, Province of Isabela, to be known as the Ramon Municipal Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The Department of Health, in consultation and coordination with the local government officials concerned, shall promulgate the rules and regulations necessary for the effective implementation of this Act.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary for the establishment of the hospital shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law; thereafter, funds necessary for the maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be provided in accordance with the provisions of the Local Government Code or the applicable law at the time.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
