> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 8057, June 15, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF EASTERN VISAYAS SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL IN CANDAHUG, PALO, LEYTE, TO PHILIPPINE SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL-EASTERN VISAYAS CAMPUS
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Eastern Visayas Science High School in Candahug, Palo, Leyte, shall be changed to Philippine Science High School-Eastern Visayas Campus.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The students of the Philippine Science High School-Eastern Visayas Campus shall enjoy the same benefits and privileges as the students of the Philippine Science High School in Diliman, Quezon City.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on June 15, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
