# Republic Act No. 8242

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARANGAY PUG-OS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SINAIT, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BARANGAY DEAN LEOPOLDO YABES**

> Tenth Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8242, December 20, 1996 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARANGAY PUG-OS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SINAIT, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BARANGAY DEAN LEOPOLDO YABES
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barangay Pug-os in the Municipality of Sinait, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby changed to Barangay Dean Leopoldo Yabes.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, December 20, 1996.
