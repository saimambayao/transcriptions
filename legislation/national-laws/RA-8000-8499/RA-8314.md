# Republic Act No. 8314

**AN ACT DESIGNATING THE LUIS HORA MEMORIAL HOSPITAL IN BAUKO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, AS THE REGIONAL HOSPITAL OF THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Tenth Congress
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8314, June 20, 1997 ]**
>
> AN ACT DESIGNATING THE LUIS HORA MEMORIAL HOSPITAL IN BAUKO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, AS THE REGIONAL HOSPITAL OF THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Luis Hora Memorial Hospital in Abatan, Municipality of Bauko, Mountain Province, is hereby designated as the regional hospital of the Cordillera Administrative Region.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** Upon approval of this Act, all personnel, assets and liabilities of the Luis Hora Memorial Hospital shall be transferred to the Department of Health.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on June 20, 1997 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27 (1), Article VI of the Constitution.
