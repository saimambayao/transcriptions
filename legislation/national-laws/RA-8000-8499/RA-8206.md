# Republic Act No. 8206

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MABILA HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SARANGANI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, TO ALBERTO OLARTE, SR. NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> Tenth Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8206, September 05, 1996 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MABILA HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SARANGANI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, TO ALBERTO OLARTE, SR. NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Mabila High School in the Municipality of Sarangani, Province of Davao del Sur, is hereby changed to Alberto Olarte, Sr. National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, Lapsed into law on September 5, 1996, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.1aшphi1
