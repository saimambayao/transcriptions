# Republic Act No. 8326

**AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL IN SIGABOY, POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF GOVERNOR GENEROSO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SIGABOY AGRICULTURAL VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Tenth Congress
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8326, June 30, 1997 ]**
>
> AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL IN SIGABOY, POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF GOVERNOR GENEROSO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SIGABOY AGRICULTURAL VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established, under the direct supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, a national agricultural high school in Sigaboy, Poblacion, Municipality of Governor Generoso, Province of Davao Oriental, to be known as the Sigaboy Agricultural Vocational High School.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The school shall, in addition to the regular secondary school curricula, offer agricultural, technical and other vocational subjects.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, Lapsed into law on June 30, 1997, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
