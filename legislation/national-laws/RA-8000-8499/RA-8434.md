# Republic Act No. 8434

**AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 10 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN OBSERVANCE OF THE ARAW NG SIOCON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIOCON, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE**

> Tenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8434, December 15, 1997 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 10 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN OBSERVANCE OF THE ARAW NG SIOCON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIOCON, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** March 10 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Siocon, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, to celebrate the Araw ng Siocon commemorating its founding anniversary.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, Lapsed into law on December 15, 1997, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.1aшphi1
