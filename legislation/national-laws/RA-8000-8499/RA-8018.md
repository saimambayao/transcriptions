# Republic Act No. 8018

**AN ACT REVERTING THE NAME OF PHILIPPINE PEDIATRIC HOSPITAL AND DEVELOPMENT CENTER IN QUEZON CITY, TO PHILIPPINE CHILDREN'S MEDICAL CENTER**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 8018
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1995/ra_8018_1995.html)

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 8018, May 25, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT REVERTING THE NAME OF PHILIPPINE PEDIATRIC HOSPITAL AND DEVELOPMENT CENTER IN QUEZON CITY, TO PHILIPPINE CHILDREN'S MEDICAL CENTER
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Philippine Pediatric Hospital and Development Center in Quezon Boulevard, Quezon City, is hereby reverted to Philippine Children's Medical Center.
>
> **Section 2.** Any other law, decree, act, executive order, proclamation, rules and regulations or part thereof inconsistent with any of the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed, amended or modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on May 25, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.1aшphi1
