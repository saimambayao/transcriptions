# Republic Act No. 8417

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FISHERY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CABOTONAN, MUNICIPALITY OF LAGONOY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE LAGONOY FISHERY SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Tenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8417, November 27, 1997 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FISHERY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CABOTONAN, MUNICIPALITY OF LAGONOY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE LAGONOY FISHERY SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the supervision of the Technical Education Skills and Development Authority (TESDA), a fishery school in Barangay Cabotonan, Municipality of Lagonoy, Province of Camarines Sur, to be known as the Lagonoy Fishery School.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The Technical Education and Skills and Development Authority (TESDA) shall promulgate the necessary rules and regulations for the effective implementation of this Act, and shall determine the manner by which the said school shall be operational.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, Lapsed into law on November 27, 1997, without the signature of the President, in accordance Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
