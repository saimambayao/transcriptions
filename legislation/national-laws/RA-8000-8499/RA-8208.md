# Republic Act No. 8208

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LAPUYAN CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAPUYAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA, DEL SUR, TO ANTONIO V. APOSTOL, SR. MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL**

> Tenth Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8208, September 05, 1996 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LAPUYAN CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAPUYAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA, DEL SUR, TO ANTONIO V. APOSTOL, SR. MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Lapuyan Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Lapuyan, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, is hereby changed to Antonio V. Apostol, Sr., Memorial Elementary School
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon it approval.
>
> Approved, Lapsed into law on September 05, 1996, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.1aшphi1
