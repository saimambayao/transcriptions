# Republic Act No. 8205

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SIASI SCHOOL OF FISHERIES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JOLO, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO JOLO SCHOOL OF FISHERIES**

## Metadata

- **RA Number**: 8205
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1996/ra_8205_1996.html)

> Tenth Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8205, September 05, 1996 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SIASI SCHOOL OF FISHERIES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JOLO, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO JOLO SCHOOL OF FISHERIES
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Siasi School of Fisheries in the Municipality of Jolo, Province of Sulu, is hereby changed to Jolo School of Fisheries.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, Lapsed into law on September 5, 1996, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
