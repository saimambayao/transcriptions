# Republic Act No. 8017

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TALISAY SCHOOL OF FISHERIES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO INOCENCIO V. FERRER MEMORIAL SCHOOL OF FISHERIES**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 8017, May 25, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TALISAY SCHOOL OF FISHERIES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO INOCENCIO V. FERRER MEMORIAL SCHOOL OF FISHERIES
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Talisay School of Fisheries in the Municipality of Talisay, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Inocencio V. Ferrer Memorial School of Fisheries.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on May 25, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
