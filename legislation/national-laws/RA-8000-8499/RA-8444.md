# Republic Act No. 8444

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY LONOY, MUNICIPALITY OF JAGNA, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE LONOY BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Tenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **Republic Act No. 8444             December 29, 1997**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY LONOY, MUNICIPALITY OF JAGNA, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE LONOY BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a barangay high school in Barangay Lonoy, Municipality of Jagna, Province of Bohol, to be known as the Lonoy Barangay High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: **December 29, 1997**
