# Republic Act No. 8497

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL TO BE LOCATED AT BARANGAY HULO, CITY OF MANDALUYONG, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CITY OF MANDALUYONG SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Tenth Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8497, February 12, 1998 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL TO BE LOCATED AT BARANGAY HULO, CITY OF MANDALUYONG, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CITY OF MANDALUYONG SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a science high school to be located at Barangay Hulo, City of Mandaluyong, to be known as the City of Mandaluyong Science High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** All laws, presidential decrees, executive orders, rules and regulations contrary to or inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed, amended or modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.
>
> Approved, February 12, 1998.
