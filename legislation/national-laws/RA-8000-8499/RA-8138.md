# Republic Act No. 8138

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A VOCATIONAL SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CONCEPCION, PROVINCE OF TARLAC, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CONCEPCION VOCATIONAL SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 8138, July 16, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A VOCATIONAL SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CONCEPCION, PROVINCE OF TARLAC, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CONCEPCION VOCATIONAL SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a vocational school in the Municipality of Concepcion, Province of Tarlac, to be known as the Concepcion Vocational School, under the supervision of the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority.
>
> **Section 2.** The school curriculum shall give emphasis on agriculture, vocational and technical training, science and technology education.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
