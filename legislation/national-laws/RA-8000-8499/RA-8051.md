# Republic Act No. 8051

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE ALBAY PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN LEGAZPI CITY INTO A REGIONAL TRAINING AND TEACHING HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS THE BICOL REGIONAL TRAINING AND TEACHING HOSPITAL**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 8051, June 15, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE ALBAY PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN LEGAZPI CITY INTO A REGIONAL TRAINING AND TEACHING HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS THE BICOL REGIONAL TRAINING AND TEACHING HOSPITAL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The existing Albay Provincial Hospital in Legazpi City is hereby converted into a regional training and teaching hospital to be known as the Bicol Regional Training and Teaching Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Health shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on June 15, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
