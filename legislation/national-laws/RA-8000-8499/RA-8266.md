> Tenth Congress
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8266, February 20, 1997 ]**
>
> AN ACT TO GRANT FILIPINO CITIZENSHIP TO REV. FR. PAUL VAN PARIJS, CICM
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Rev. Fr. Paul Van Parijs, a Belgian citizen and a Roman Catholic Priest of the Congregation of the Immaculate Heart of Mary (CICM), is hereby granted Filipino citizenship: *Provided,* that he subscribe to the appropriate oath of allegiance as a Filipino citizen.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, February 20, 1997.
