# Republic Act No. 8253

**AN ACT DECLARING JULY ONE OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAHINOG, PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN, TO BE KNOWN AS "MAHINOG CHARTER DAY"**

> Tenth Congress
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8253, February 11, 1997 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING JULY ONE OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAHINOG, PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN, TO BE KNOWN AS "MAHINOG CHARTER DAY"
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** July one is the charter day anniversary of the Municipality of Mahinog, Province of Camiguin, and its celebration is highlighted with colorful parade and beauty pageant.
>
> **Section 2.** July one of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking public holiday in the Municipality of Mahinog.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, February 11, 1997.
