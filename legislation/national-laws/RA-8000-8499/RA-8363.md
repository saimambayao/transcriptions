# Republic Act No. 8363

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TUROD NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN CABUGAO, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO CABUGAO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL**

> Tenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8363, October 09, 1997 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TUROD NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN CABUGAO, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO CABUGAO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Turod National High School located at Barangay Turod, Municipality of Cabugao, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby changed to Cabugao National High School.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, Lapsed into law on October 9, 1997, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
