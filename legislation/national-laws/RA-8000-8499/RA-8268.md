**AN ACT CONVERTING THE NAVA BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY NAVA, MUNICIPALITY OF HINUNANGAN, PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE NAVA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Tenth Congress
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8268, March 01, 1997 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE NAVA BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY NAVA, MUNICIPALITY OF HINUNANGAN, PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE NAVA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Nava Barangay High School in Barangay Nava, Municipality of Hinunangan, Province of Southern Leyte, is hereby converted into a national high school, to be known as the Nava National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Nava Barangay High School shall be transferred to and absorbed by the Nava National High School.
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue the necessary rules and regulations for the conversion and operations of the school.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 4.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, Lapsed into law on March 1, 1997, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
