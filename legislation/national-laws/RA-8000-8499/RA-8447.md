# Republic Act No. 8447

**AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF LOS ARCOS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PROSPERIDAD, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Tenth Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8447, January 08, 1998 ]**
>
> **AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF LOS ARCOS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PROSPERIDAD, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established in the Municipality of Prosperidad, Province of Agusan del Sur, Los Arcos National High School, under the control and supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.
>
> **Section 2.** This school shall be known officially as the Los Arcos National High School.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, Lapsed into law on January 8, 1998, without the signature of the President, in accordance Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
