# Republic Act No. 8040

**AN ACT CONVERTING THE TAYTAY MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE TAYTAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYTAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 8040, June 05, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE TAYTAY MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE TAYTAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYTAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Taytay Municipal High School is hereby converted into a national high school to be known as the Taytay National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on June 5, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
