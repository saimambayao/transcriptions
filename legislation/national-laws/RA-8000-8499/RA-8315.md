> Tenth Congress
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8315, June 30, 1997 ]**
>
> AN ACT DECLARING DECEMBER EIGHT OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN ANGELES CITY IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDATION DAY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** December eight of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in Angeles City in commemoration of its foundation day.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Lapsed into law on June 30, 1997 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27 (1), Article VI of the Constitution.
