> Tenth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8400, November 22, 1997 ]**
>
> AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CONVERSION OF THE GUIUAN MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE GUIUAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The Guiuan Municipal High School in the Municipality of Guiuan, Province of Eastern Samar is hereby converted into a national high school to be known as the Guiuan National High School.
>
> **Section 2.** All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Guiuan Municipal High School are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Guiuan National High School.
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue the necessary rules and memorandum for the proper implementation of the provisions of this Act.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 4.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, Lapsed into law on November 22, 1997, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
