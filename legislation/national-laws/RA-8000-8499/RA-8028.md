# Republic Act No. 8028

**AN ACT NAMING THE ROAD FROM TIGBAO (TACLOBAN) TO STA. FE, PASTRANA, DAGAMI AND TANAUAN, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, AS FILOMENO MONTEJO HIGHWAY**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 8028, June 05, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT NAMING THE ROAD FROM TIGBAO (TACLOBAN) TO STA. FE, PASTRANA, DAGAMI AND TANAUAN, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, AS FILOMENO MONTEJO HIGHWAY
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The road starting from Tigbao (Tacloban) to Sta. Fe, Pastrana, Dagami and Tanauan (Junction of Pan-Philippine Highway), Province of Leyte, shall be known as the Filomeno Montejo Highway.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1aшphi1
>
> Lapsed into law on June 5, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
