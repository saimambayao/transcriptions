# Republic Act No. 8139

**AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL IN THE CAPITAL TOWN OF TANDAG, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE TANDAG NATIONAL SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 8139, July 16, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL IN THE CAPITAL TOWN OF TANDAG, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE TANDAG NATIONAL SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a national science high school in the capital town of Tandag, Province of Surigao del Sur, to be known as the Tandag National Science High School.
>
> **Section 2.** The school shall be situated within the eighth-hectare campus of the Surigao del Sur Polytechnic College in Barangay Governor Adela Serra Ty, Municipality of Tago, Surigao del Sur.
>
> **Section 3.** The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Act.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 4.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.
>
> **Section 5.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
