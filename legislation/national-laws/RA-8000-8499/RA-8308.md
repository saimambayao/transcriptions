# Republic Act No. 8308

**AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT, OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF A HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE CUYAPO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN POBLACION IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUYAPO, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR**

> Tenth Congress
> Second Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8308, June 13, 1997 ]**
>
> AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT, OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF A HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE CUYAPO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN POBLACION IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUYAPO, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established a municipal high school to be known as the Cuyapo National High School located in Poblacion in the Municipality of Cuyapo, Province of Nueva Ecija.
>
> **Section 2.** The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 3.** The Department of Education, Culture and Sports is directed to implement this Act. Thereafter, the operation and maintenance of said high school shall be the responsibility of the Municipal School Board.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 13, 1997.
