# Republic Act No. 8141

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE EASTERN SAMAR NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BORONGAN, EASTERN SAMAR, TO EASTERN SAMAR NATIONAL POLYTECHNIC SCHOOL**

> Nineth Congress
> Third Regular Session
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 8141, July 16, 1995 ]**
>
> AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE EASTERN SAMAR NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BORONGAN, EASTERN SAMAR, TO EASTERN SAMAR NATIONAL POLYTECHNIC SCHOOL
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Eastern Samar National Comprehensive High School in the Municipality of Borongan, Eastern Samar, is hereby changed to Eastern Samar National Polytechnic School.1aшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The Eastern Samar National Polytechnic School shall be under the supervision of the Commission on Higher Education (CHED).
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved,
