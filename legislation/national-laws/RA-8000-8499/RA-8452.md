> Tenth Congress
>
> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8452, January 11, 1998 ]**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING THE TWELFTH DAY OF JULY OF EACH YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAGUINDINGAN, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** Laguindingan, Misamis Oriental, was officially created as a municipality on July 12, 1963 by virtue of Republic Act No. 3824.Ꮮαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 2.** The Twelfth day of July of each year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Laguindingan, Province of Misamis Oriental, in celebration of its charter day to be known as Araw ng Laguindingan.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, Lapsed into law on January 11, 1998, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
