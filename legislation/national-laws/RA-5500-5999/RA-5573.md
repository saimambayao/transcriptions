> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5573, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO BATINGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GLORIA, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, TO BARRIO ANDRES BONIFACIO.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Batingan in the Municipality of Gloria, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is hereby changed to Barrio Andres Bonifacio.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
