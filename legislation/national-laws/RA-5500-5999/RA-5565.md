> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5565, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME "TABAO BARRIO SCHOOL" IN BARRIO TABAO, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ENRIQUE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO DON ESPIRIDION PRESBITERO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL,**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name "Tabao Barrio School" in Barrio Tabao, Municipality of San Enrique, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Don Espiridion Presbitero Elementary  School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
