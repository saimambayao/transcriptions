# Republic Act No. 5609

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MANHAYANG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF MANHAYANG, MUNICIPALITY OF STA. BARBARA, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO LAMAGUSA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5609, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MANHAYANG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF MANHAYANG, MUNICIPALITY OF STA. BARBARA, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO LAMAGUSA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Manhayang Elementary School in the barrio of Manhayang, Municipality of Sta. Barbara, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Lamagusa Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
