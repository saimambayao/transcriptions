> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5581, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO BUCANA IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIOCON, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, TO BARRIO SUHAILE ARABI.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives  of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Bucana, Municipality of Siocon, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Suhaile Arabi.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
