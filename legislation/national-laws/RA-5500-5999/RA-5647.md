> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5647, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF NULING IN THE PROVINCE OF COTABATO, TO SULTAN KUDARAT.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Nuling in the Province of Cotabato is hereby changed to Sultan Kudarat.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
