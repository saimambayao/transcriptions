**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ALABAAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARCOS PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO FORTUNATO DAQUIOAG MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5619, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ALABAAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARCOS PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO FORTUNATO DAQUIOAG MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Alabaan Elementary School in the Municipality of Marcos, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Fortunato Daquiaog Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
