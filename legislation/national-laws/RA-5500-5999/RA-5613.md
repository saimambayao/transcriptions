> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5613, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BADIANGAN PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PATNOGON, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, TO TELESFORO DE LA CRUZ COMMUNITY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Badiangan Primary School in the Municipality of Patnogon, Province of Antique, is hereby changed to Telesforo de la Cruz; Community School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
