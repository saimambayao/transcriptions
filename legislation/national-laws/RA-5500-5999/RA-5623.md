> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5623, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE COGON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARRIO SAN ISIDRO, MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA MARIA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO BEL SUR, SAN ISIDRO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Cogon Elementary School in Barrio San Isidro, Municipality of Santa Maria, Province of Davao del Sur, is hereby changed to San Isidro Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
