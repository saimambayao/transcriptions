> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5628, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SOUTH PILOT SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAOAY, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO EVANGELISTA PILOT SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of South Pilot School in the Municipality of Paoay, Province of Ilocos Norte is hereby changed to Evangelista Pilot School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
