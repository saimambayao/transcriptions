> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5824, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> AN ACT CONVERTING THE MUNICIPAL PORT OF PALOMPON IN THE PROVINCE OF LEYTE INTO A NATIONAL PORT.
>
> Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** The municipal port of Palompon in the Province of Leyte is hereby converted into a national port.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1969.
