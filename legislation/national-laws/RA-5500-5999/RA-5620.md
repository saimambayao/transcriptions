> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5620, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ISING ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARMEN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, TO CARMEN CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Ising Elementary School in the Municipality of Carmen, Province of Davao del Norte, is hereby changed to Carmen Central Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
