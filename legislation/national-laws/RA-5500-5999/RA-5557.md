> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5557, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CREATING BARRIOS POLO, OWAON, BURGOS, BA-AO, SIGAYAN, SINONOK, CANLUCANI, GUIMPUTLAN, SILINOG AND ALIGUAY IN THE CITY OF DAPITAN.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby created in the City of Dapitan, Barrios Polo, Owaon, Burgos, Ba-ao, Sigayan, Sinonok, Canlucani, Guimputlan, Silinog and Aliguay, their respective boundaries to be determined by the city council with the approval of the city mayor within six months after the approval of this Act.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
