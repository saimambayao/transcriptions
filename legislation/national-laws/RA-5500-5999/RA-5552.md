> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5552, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THIRTY-SIX HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE, ENTITLED "AN ACT CONVERTING THE LEON HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LEON, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, INTO A NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR."**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in  Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section one of Republic Act Numbered  Thirty-six hundred twenty-five is hereby amended to read  as follows:
>
> > "Section 1. Leon Regional High School in the Munic­ipality of Leon, Province of Iloilo, is hereby converted into a national agricultural high school and this school shall continue to be located at its present site in the Municipality of Leon, Province of Iloilo."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
