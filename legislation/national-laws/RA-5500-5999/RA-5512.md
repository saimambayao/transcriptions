> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5512, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CREATING A NEW POSITION OF A MUNICIPAL JUDGE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF OROQUIETA, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Any provision of law to the contrary not-withstanding, there is hereby created a new position of municipal judge in the Municipality of Oroquieta, Province of Misamis Occidental.
>
> Within thirty days from the approval of this Act, appointment to the new position herein created shall be made.
>
> **Section 2.** There is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, such sum as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
