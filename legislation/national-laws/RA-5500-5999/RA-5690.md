> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5690, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF LIPA CITY HOSPITAL AND CONVERTING THE SAME INTO A NATIONAL HOSPITAL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Lipa City Hospital in Lipa City is hereby converted into a national hospital with an authorized bed capacity of fifty beds.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** The additional sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated in the next General Appropriations Act for its maintenance and operation.
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
