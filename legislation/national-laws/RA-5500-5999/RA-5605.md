> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5605, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF TANQUE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF ROXAS TO PRESIDENT MANUEL ROXAS MEMORIAL SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Tanque Elementary School in the City of Roxas is hereby changed to President Manuel Roxas Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
