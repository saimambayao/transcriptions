> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5597, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO PANAPAAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BACOOR, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO BARRIO PEDRO ESPIRITU.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Panapaan in the Municipality of Bacoor, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to Barrio Pedro Espiritu.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
