> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5599, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF PUNDAQUIT IN BARRIO SAN MIGUEL, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ANTONIO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES, INTO A BARRIO.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Pundaquit in Barrio San Miguel, Municipality of San Antonio, Province of Zambales is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Pundaquit.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
