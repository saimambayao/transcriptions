> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5671, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LA RIOJA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PATNONGON, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, TO TEOFISTA M. SALVANI COMMUNITY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of La Rioja Elementary School in the Municipality of Patnongon, Province of Antique, is hereby changed to Teofista M. Salvani Community School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
