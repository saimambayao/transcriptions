> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5606, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CAPICHO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN ROXAS CITY TO GOVERNOR RAFAEL ACUNA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Capicho Elementary School in Roxas City is hereby changed to Governor Rafael Acuna  Elementary School.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
