> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5608, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ORION ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ORION, PROVINCE OF BATAAN, TO ARELLANO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Orion Elementary School in the Municipality of Orion, Province of Bataan, is hereby changed to Arellano Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
