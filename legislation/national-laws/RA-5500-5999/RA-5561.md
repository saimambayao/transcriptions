> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5561, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF OCO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIGA, PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES, TO SAN JOSE.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Barrio of Oco in the Municipality of Viga, Province of Catanduanes, is hereby changed to San Jose.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
