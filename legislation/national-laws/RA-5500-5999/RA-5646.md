> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5646, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN PEDRO IN THE PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of San Pedro in the Province of Oriental Mindoro is hereby changed to Bulalakao.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
