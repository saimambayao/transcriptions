> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5758, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE MISAMIS ORIENTAL SCHOOL OF FISHERIES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF INITAO, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL, INTO A NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE KNOWN AS INITAO NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Misamis Oriental School of Fisheries in the Municipality of Initao, Province of Misamis Oriental, created under Republic Act Numbered Thirty-six hundred thirty-seven, is hereby converted into a national comprehensive high school, to be known as Initao National Comprehensive High School. Said school shall be under the direct supervision of the Director of the Bureau of Public Schools.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred' seventy. Thereafter, such sum as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1969.
