>
> **AN ACT CREATING THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAP AD IN THE PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress -assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following territory is separated from the Municipality of Kapatagan, Province of Lanao del Norte, and constituted into a distinct and independent municipality, to be known as the Municipality of Sapad, same province. The seat of government of the new municipality shall be in the present site of the Barrio Sapad.
