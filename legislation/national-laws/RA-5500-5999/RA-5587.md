> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5587, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO BATULI, MUNICIPALITY OF PASUQUIN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BARRIO SAN ISIDRO.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Batuli in the Municipality of Pasuquin, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio San Isidro.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
