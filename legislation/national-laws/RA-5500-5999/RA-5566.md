> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5566, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONKAYO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Monkayo, Province of Davao, are constituted as barrios  of said municipality, as follows:
>
> > 1. The sitios of San Jose, Tagusab, Tigbawan, Totoy, Upper Tigbawan, Tawan, Upper Tina and Lower Totoy are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio San Jose;
> >
> > 2. The sitios of Tapia and New Calape are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Tapia;
> >
> > 3. The sitios of Rizal, Upper Gabanan, Patinay and Tabontabon are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio  Rizal;
> >
> > 4. The sitios of Casoon, Lagasi, Oling Dungga, Manakong and Tabonan are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Casoon;
> >
> > 5. The sitios of Macopa, Tandawan and San Miguel are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Macopa;The sitios of Hulip, Upper Mamunga, Matangad and Upper Liboton are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Hulip.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
