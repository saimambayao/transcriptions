# Republic Act No. 5618

**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CALUMPANG CERCA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARRIO CALUMPANG CERCA, MUNICIPALITY OF INDANG, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO DR. ALFREDO PIO DE RODA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5618, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CALUMPANG CERCA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARRIO CALUMPANG CERCA, MUNICIPALITY OF INDANG, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO DR. ALFREDO PIO DE RODA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Calumpang Cerca Elementary School in Barrio Calumpang Cerca, Municipality of Indang, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to Dr. Alfredo Pio de Roda Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
