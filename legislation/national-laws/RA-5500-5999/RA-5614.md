> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5614, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LOOC ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF DANAO TO BIBIANA MERCADO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Looc Elementary School in the City of Danao is hereby changed to Bibiana Mercado Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
