> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5650, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PROVINCE OF WESTERN SAMAR TO SAMAR**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Province of Western Samar  is hereby changed to Samar.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
