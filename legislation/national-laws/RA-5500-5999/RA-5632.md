> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5632, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF NAPAYONG ISLAND, LOCATED IN TAAL LAKE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, TO LAUREL ISLAND.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Napayong Island, located in Taal Lake, Province of Batangas, is hereby changed to Laurel Island.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
