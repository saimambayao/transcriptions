**AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME, OF THE TALOTAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CONCEPCION, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO ALONCIA ASIN MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**

> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5611, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME, OF THE TALOTAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CONCEPCION, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO ALONCIA ASIN MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Talotan Elementary School in the Municipality of Concepcion, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Aloncia Asin Memorial Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21,  1969.
