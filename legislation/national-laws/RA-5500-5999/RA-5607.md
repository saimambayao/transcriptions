> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5607, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF TALON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN ROXAS CITY TO ANTONIO VITERBO, SR. MEMORIAL SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Talon Elementary School in Roxas City is hereby changed to Antonio Viterbo, Sr. Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
