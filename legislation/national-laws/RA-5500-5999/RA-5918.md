> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5918, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES TO BE KNOWN AS BENGUET SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF THE NECESSARY FUNDS THEREFOR.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established a national school of arts and trades and to be established in the barrio of Daclan, Municipality of Bukod, Province of Benguet.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to organize the said school and to acquire by purchase, through grant or donation the school site,
>
> **Section 3.** The sum of five hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the acquisition of a school site, operation and maintenance of the said school during the school year nineteen hundred and sixty-nine to nineteen hundred seventy. Thereafter, the necessary funds for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
