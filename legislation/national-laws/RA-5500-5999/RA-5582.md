> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5582, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SERGIO OSMENA, SR., PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Sergio Osmena, Sr., Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are hereby  constituted as barrios thereof, to  wit:
>
> > 1. The sitios of Tanguban, Siasog, Matalom, Napuyan, Dacuyon, Sedimar, and Tulaban are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to  be known as Barrio Willben;
> >
> > 2. The sitios of Diwakat, Calican, Cabibit, Buburinga, and Solidio are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Antonino; and
> >
> > 3. The sitios of Upper Buburinga, Lanasan, Bayuhan, Lawaan, and Disoy are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Penacio.
>
> **Section 2.** THIS ACT SHALL TAKE EFFECT UPON ITS APPROVAL.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
