> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5869, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> AN ACT TRANSFERRING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MANUEL IN THE SAME PROVINCE.
>
> Be it enacted by the Semite and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:
>
> **Section 1.** Barrios Villanueva (Caloocan) and Sandiat in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Isabela, are hereby separated from said municipality, and are made part of the Municipality of San Manuel in the same province.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Enacted without Executive approval, June 21,1969.
