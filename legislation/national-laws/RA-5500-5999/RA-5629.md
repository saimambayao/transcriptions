> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5629, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BAUTISTA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF BAUTISTA, CITY OF SAN PABLO, TO FERNANDO QUISUMBING ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Bautista Elementary School in the Barrio of Bautista, City of San Pablo, is hereby changed to Fernando Quisumbing Elementary School.
>
