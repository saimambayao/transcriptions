> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5564, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF LUYO WAHIG, MUNICIPALITY OF SIERRA BULLONES, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, INTO A BARRIO.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in  Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Luyo Wahig in the Municipality of Sierra Bullones, Province of Bohol, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Santa Cruz of the same municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
