> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5641, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAPAO, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE, TO STA. MONICA.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Sapao, Province of Surigao del Norte, is hereby changed to Sta. Monica.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
