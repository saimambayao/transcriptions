> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5635, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT INCREASING THE BED QAPAC1TY OF BATAC GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF 1LOCOS NORTE, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of the Batac General Hospital in the Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby increased from twenty-five to fifty beds.
>
> **Section 2.** Such sums as may be needed for the operation of the additional facilities shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
