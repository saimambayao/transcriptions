> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5578, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CREATING BARRIO RABA IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SALUG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitio of Raba in the Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Raba. Said barrio shall be bounded as follows:
>
> > 1. NorthStarting from the corner of Lot No. 2904, thence to Lot No. 2903, 3362, 3363, 3409, 3405, and 3404;
> >
> > 2. Eastfollowing Guisapong Creek until it reaches  its source. Thence from its source draw a straight line going south until it reaches the bank of the  Mucas River;
> >
> > 3. SouthMucas River;
> >
> > 4. WestStarting from corner of Lot No, 2904, thence proceed going Southward, to Lot No. 2900, 2871, 2870, 2869, 2868, 2855, 2854, 285S. Thence draw a straight line until it reaches the bank of Mucas River. All areas within bounds of delineation, comprise the proposed barrio of Raba.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
