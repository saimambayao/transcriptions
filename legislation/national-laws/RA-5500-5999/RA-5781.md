> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (e), subparagraph 11, for the Province of Catanduanes, page 424, paragraph "a", Title E, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Fifty-one hundred eighty-seven is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(e) San Andres Seawall             110,000"
>
> **Section 2.** Item 42, page 1211, paragraph "a", Title J, Section five of the same Act is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "42. Port of  Virac, Catanduanes, including shore protection       500,000"
>
> **Section 3.** Item  107, page  1215, paragraph "b", *Title* J, Section five of the same Act is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "107. Port of Calolbon, Catanduanes, including shore protection      200,000"
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1969.
