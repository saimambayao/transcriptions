> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5535, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CREATING ADDITIONAL POSITIONS OF ASSISTANT CITY FISCAL FOR THE CITY OF BAGUIO, BY FURTHER AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF THE CHARTER OF SAID CITY.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section two thousand five hundred sixty-one of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended is further amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Section 2561. *The Assistant City Fiscals.*There shall be seven assistant city fiscals who shall assist the city fiscal as he shall direct. The assistant city fiscals shall receive the following compensation:
> >
> > > "(a) First Assistant, nine thousand pesos *per annum*.
> > >
> > > "(b) Second assistant, seven thousand four hundred pesos *per annum*.
> > >
> > > "(c) Third assistant, five thousand four hundred pesos *per annum*.
> > >
> > > "(d) Fourth assistant, eight thousand four hundred pesos *per annum*.
> > >
> > > "(e) Three fifth assistants, seven thousand two hundred pesos *per annum* each."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
