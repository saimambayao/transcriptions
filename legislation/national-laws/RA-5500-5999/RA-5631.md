> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5631, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT DECLARING PAOAY LAKE IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE A NATIONAL PARK**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Paoay Lake in the Province of IIocos Norte and its extremities within one kilometer from said lake is hereby declared a. national park.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
