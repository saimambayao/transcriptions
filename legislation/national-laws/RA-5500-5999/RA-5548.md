> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5548, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KINOGITAN, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established under the direct supervision of the Director of Vocational Education, a national agricultural high school in the Municipality of Kinogitan, Province of Misamis Oriental, to be known as Kinogitan National Agricultural High school.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized, to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said high school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred seventy. Thereafter, such sums as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
