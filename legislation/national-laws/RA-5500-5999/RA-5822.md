> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5822, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CREATING THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGOLOAN IN THE PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Barrios Kiasar, Darimbang, Dimayon, Ina-gongan, Malibato, Dalamas, and Panalawan, all of the Municipality of Baioi, Province of Lanao del Norte, are separated from said municipality and constituted into a distinct and independent municipality, to be known as the Municipality of Tagoloan, same province. The seat of government of the new municipality shall be in the poblacion of Barrio Darimbang.
>
> **Section 2.** The first mayor, vice-mayor and councilors of the new municipality shall be elected in the next general elections in November 1969.
>
> **Section 3.**. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1969.
