> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5977, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWENTY-ONE HUNDRED FORTY-ONE, ENTITLED "AN ACT CREATING THE SUB-PROVINCE OF BILIRAN."**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Section six of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-one hundred forty-one is amended to read as follows:
>
> > "**Section 6.** The governor of the Subprovince of Biliran shall exercise therein all the executive powers of a provincial governor. He shall not be a member of the Provincial Board of Leyte."
>
> **Section 2.** The same Act is amended by inserting between Sections seven and eight thereof the following sections:
>
> > "Section 7-A. The Office of the Deputy Provincial Treasurer, including its personnel, of the Province of Leyte, is hereby transferred to the Subprovince of Biliran, and the physical transfer of the said office to the capital of the said subprovince shall be effected within thirty days from the date of the approval of this Act.
> >
> > "Section 7-B. There shall be an auditor for the Subprovince of Biliran with permanent station at its capital. He shall exercise the functions of a provincial auditor in the said subprovince. He shall be assisted by such personnel as may be determined by the Provincial Board of Leyte. The operating expenses of the Office of the Auditor of the said subprovince shall be paid by it. The salaries of all the personnel in the said office shall be fixed by the governor.
> >
> > "Section 7-C. One hundred per centum of all provincial taxes, fines, or other revenue collected in the Subprovince of Biliran or by reason of any right originating therein shall accrue to the treasury of the said subprovince for the sole use and benefit thereof. All the proceeds of the road tax of the subprovince shall also accrue in their entirety to its treasury. There shall accrue to the treasury of the said subprovince, for the sole use and benefit thereof, one hundred per centum of the internal revenue and special allotments apportioned to the Province of Leyte in respect to the number of inhabitants in the said subprovince.1âшphi1
> >
> > "Section 7-D. All funds accruing to the Subprovince of Biliran shall be expended for its exclusive benefit."
>
> **Section 3.** Whenever the phrase "lieutenant-governor" appears in Republic Act Numbered Twenty-one hundred forty-one, it shall read "Governor."
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1969.
