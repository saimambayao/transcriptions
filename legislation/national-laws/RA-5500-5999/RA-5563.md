> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5563, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIOS OF LANTAWAN AND KALASAGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUPON, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, INTO A BARRIO, TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF LANTAWAN OF THE SAME MUNICIPALITY.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Lantawan and Kalasagan in the Municipality of Lupon, Province of Davao, are hereby converted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Lantawan of the same municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
