> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5590, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF FUENTES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANTUKAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Arog, Til-asan, Pintatagan and Lease in the barrio of Tambongon, Municipality of Pantukan, Province of Davao del Norte, are separated from said barrio, and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Fuentes.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
