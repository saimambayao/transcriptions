> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5627, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT NAMING THE PUBLIC PRIMARY SCHOOL IN BARRIO SANTA ELENA, SAN PABLO CITY, PLACIDO L. ESCUDERO MEMORIAL SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Hereafter, the public primary school in Barrio Santa Elena, San Pablo City, shall be named Placido L. Escudero Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
