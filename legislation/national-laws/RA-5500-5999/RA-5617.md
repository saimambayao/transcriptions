> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5617, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF AYUSAN PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TIAONG, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, TO BIENVENIDA AND ASINAS PRIMARY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Ayusan Primary School in the Municipality of Tiaong, Province of Quezon, is hereby changed to "Bienvenida and Asinas Primary School."
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
