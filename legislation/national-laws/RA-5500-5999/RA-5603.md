> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5603, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SIBAGUAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF ROXAS TO JUAN FUENTES ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Sibaguan Elementary School in the City of Roxas is hereby changed, to Juan Fuentes Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
