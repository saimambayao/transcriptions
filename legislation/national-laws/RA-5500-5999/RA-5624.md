> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5624, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF VALMA PRIMARY SCHOOL IN S1TIO STA. MARIA, BARRIO NGAN, MUNICIPALITY OF COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, TO TRANQUILINO H. VALDERRAMA PRIMARY SCHOOL.**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Valma Primary School in Sitio Sta. Maria, Barrio Ngan, Municipality of Compostela, Province of Davao del Norte, is hereby changed to Tranquilino H. Valderrama Primary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
