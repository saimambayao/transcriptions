> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5558, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANDA, PROVINCE OF BOHOL.**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The following sitios in the Municipality of Anda, Province of Eohol, are hereby constituted as barrios of said municipality, to wit:
>
> > 1. Sitios Pursenas and llawod are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Santa Cruz;
> >
> > 2. Sitios Abihid and Tayod are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Katipunan;
> >
> > 3. Sitios Almaria and Tinogdan are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Almaria;
> >
> > 4. Sitios Bagacay, Lutao, and Sa-ob are constituted into a  distinct and independent barrio  of said  municipality, to be known as Barrio Buenasuerte; and
> >
> > 5. Sitio Tawid is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Tawid.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969
