> **[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5622, June 21, 1969 ]**
>
> **AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF DALIAON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN TORIL DISTRICT, DAVAO CITY, TO JUAN DE LA CRUZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.**
>
> **Be it enacted by the Senate and, House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:**
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Daliaon Elementary School in Toril District, Davao City, is hereby changed to Juan de la Cruz Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> Approved, June 21, 1969.
