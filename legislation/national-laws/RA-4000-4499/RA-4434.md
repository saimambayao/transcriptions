> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4434**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Camp Murphy to Camp General Emilio Aguinaldo
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Camp Murphy shall hereafter be known and referred to as Camp General Emilio Aguinaldo.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
