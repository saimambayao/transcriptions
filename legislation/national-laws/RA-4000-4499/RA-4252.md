> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4252**
>
> AN ACT TO REPEAL SECTION ONE HUNDRED FORTY-FIVE, CHAPTER FOURTEEN OF THE CODE OF MINDANAO AND SULU
>
> **Section 1.** Section one hundred forty-five, Chapter Fourteen, of the Code of Mindanao and Sulu is hereby repealed.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965
