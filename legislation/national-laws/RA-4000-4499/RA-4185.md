> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4185**
>
> An Act Amending Item (v), Page 476, Subparagraph Thirty, for the Province of Leyte del Sur, Paragraph "c," Section Two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-Seven Hundred One
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Item (v), page four hundred seventy-six, subparagraph thirty, for the Province of Leyte del Sur, paragraph "c," Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred one is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > "(v) Aid to the Municipality of Hinundayan for construction, improvement and/or repair of municipal projects ₱5,000.00"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 21, 1965.
