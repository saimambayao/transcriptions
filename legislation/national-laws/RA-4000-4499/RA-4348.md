> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4348**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Tejero in the Municipality of Rosario, Province of Cavite, to Barrio Tejeros Convention
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Tejero in the Municipality of Rosario, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to Barrio Tejeros Convention.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
