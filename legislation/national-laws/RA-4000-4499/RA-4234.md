> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4234**
>
> An Act Converting the Siocon Puericulture Center in the Municipality of Siocon, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, into an Emergency Hospital, to be Known as Siocon Emergency Hospital, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Siocon Puericulture Center in the Municipality of Siocon, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is hereby converted into an emergency hospital to be known as Siocon Emergency Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The Director of Medical Services, who shall exercise direct supervision over said hospital, shall reorganize the same accordingly.
>
> **Section 3.** The sum of fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, operation and maintenance of the said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred and sixty-six. Such sums as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
