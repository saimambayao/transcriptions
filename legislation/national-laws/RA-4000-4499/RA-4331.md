> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4331**
>
> An Act Converting Doña Gregoria Memorial Hospital in the Municipality of Agoo, Province of La Union, into a National Hospital to be Known as Doña Gregoria National Hospital, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Doña Gregoria Memorial Hospital in the Municipality of Agoo, Province of La Union, is hereby converted into a national hospital to be known as Doña Gregoria National Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to carry out the purpose of this Act. Every year thereafter, the said sum must be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
