> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4238**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of San Jose Elementary School in the Barrio of San Jose, Municipality of San Vicente, Province of Camarines Norte, to Lucio Opedo Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the San Jose Elementary School in the barrio of San Jose, Municipality of San Vicente, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Lucio Opedo Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
