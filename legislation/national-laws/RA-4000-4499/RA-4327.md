> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4327**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Govantes Dike in the Municipality of Vigan, Province of Ilocos Sur, to Isabelo De Los Reyes Dike
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Govantes Dike in the Municipality of Vigan, Province of Ilocos Sur, is changed to Isabelo De Los Reyes Dike.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
