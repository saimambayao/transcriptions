> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4061**
>
> **An Act Granting Philippine Citizenship to Mrs. Gloria Padilla-Butt**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Mrs. Gloria Padilla-Butt is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives appurtenant thereto.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1964.
