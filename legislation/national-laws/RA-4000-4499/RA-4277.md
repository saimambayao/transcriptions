> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4277**
>
> An Act Naming the Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Ligao, Province of Albay, to Pio Duran Memorial Hospital
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The emergency hospital in the Municipality of Ligao, Province of Albay, shall hereafter be known as the Pio Duran Memorial Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
