> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4102**
>
> **An Act Increasing the Bed Capacity of Guihulngan Emergency Hospital in the Province of Negros Oriental, and Authorizing the Appropriations of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The bed capacity of Guihulngan Emergency Hospital is hereby increased from twenty-five to fifty.
>
> **Section 2.** The financing of this hospital shall be in accordance with the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred thirty-nine, otherwise known as the Hospital Financing Act. The necessary sums for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1964.
