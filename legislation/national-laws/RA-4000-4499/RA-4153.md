> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4153**
>
> **An Act to Standardize the Salaries of Officials in the Bureau of Public Highways**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Any existing law to the contrary notwithstanding, salaries of the following positions in the Bureau of Public Highways are hereby fixed as follows:
>
> (a) Chief Highway Engineer, thirteen thousand two hundred pesos per annum;
>
> (b) Highway Division Engineer, twelve thousand pesos per annum;
>
> (c) Six Staff Civil Engineers of the specialized divisions, twelve thousand pesos per annum.
>
> **Section 2.** In order to carry out the provisions of this Act for the fiscal year ending June thirty, nineteen hundred sixty-five, the Commissioner of Public Highways, with the approval of the Commissioner of the Budget is hereby authorized to charge the salary differentials of the officials whose salaries are herein standardized against the appropriations for "Maintenance and Other Operating Expenses" under the General Appropriations Act for the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-five for the Bureau of Public Highways.
>
