> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4218**
>
> An Act Creating Certain Barrios in the Municipality of San Policarpio, Province of Samar
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Sta. Cruz, Tan-awan, Bangon, Guin-osokan, Barras, Lipata and Libas in the Municipality of San Policarpio, Province of Samar, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the Barrios of Sta. Cruz, Tan-awan, Bangon, Guin-osakan, Barras, Lipata and Libas, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
