> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4447**
>
> An Act Converting Tagudin High School in the Municipality of Tagudin, Province of Ilocos Sur, into a National High School to be Known as Tagudin General Comprehensive High School, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Tagudin High School is hereby converted into a national high school, to be known as Tagudin General Comprehensive High School. Comprehensive curriculum which includes both academic and industrial arts and handicraft courses shall be offered. The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to reorganized the said school accordingly.
>
> **Section 2.** Upon organization of Tagudin General Comprehensive High School, the assets, liabilities, personnel, equipment and records of Tagudin High School shall stand transferred to said Tagudin General Comprehensive High School.
>
> **Section 3.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the said school in addition to its matriculation and tuition fees, and other revenues.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
