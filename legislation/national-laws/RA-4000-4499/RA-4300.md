> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4300**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Calasa Community School in the Municipality of Himamaylan, Province of Negros Occidental, to Don Jose T. Ramos Memorial School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Calasa Community School in the Municipality of Himamaylan, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Don Jose T. Ramos Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
