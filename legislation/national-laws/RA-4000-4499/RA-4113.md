> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4113**
>
> **An Act Repealing Proclamation Numbered Four Hundred Eleven, Dated September Eighteen, Nineteen Hundred and Fifty-Three, of the President of the Philippines**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Proclamation Numbered Four hundred eleven, dated September eighteen, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, of the President of the Philippines is hereby repealed.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 20, 1964.
