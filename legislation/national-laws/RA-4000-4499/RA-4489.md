> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4489**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Sambat Elementary School in the Municipality of Santa Teresita, Province of Batangas, to Paaralang Sentral ng Santa Teresita
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Sambat Elementary School in the Municipality of Santa Teresita, Province of Batangas, is hereby changed to Paaralang Sentral ng Santa Teresita.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
