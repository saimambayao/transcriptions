> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4032**
>
> **An Act Converting the Tria Tirona Memorial High School in the Municipality of Kawit, Province of Cavite, into a National High School, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor**
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Tria Tirona Memorial High School in the Municipality of Kawit, Province of Cavite, is hereby converted into a national high school.1âшphi1
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, maintenance and operation of the said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-five. Such sums as may be needed for the maintenance and operation of said school in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 18, 1964.
