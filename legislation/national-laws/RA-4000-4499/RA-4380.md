> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4380**
>
> AN ACT TO FURTHER AMEND SECTION NINE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWENTY-TWO HUNDRED SIXTY, AS AMENDED.
>
> **Section 1.** Section nine of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-two hundred and sixty as amended by Republic Act Numbered Thirty-six hundred and seventy-five is hereby further amended to read as follows:
>
> "Sec. 9. Wage and Position Classification Office. The Wage and Position Classification Office shall be responsible for the classification of positions in the civil service and shall standardize the salaries of the group or groups of positions to be classified: *Provided*, That the range of minimum and maximum salaries allowable for civil service eligibilities shall be:
>
> |  |  |  |
> | --- | --- | --- |
> | Eligibilities | Minimum | Maximum |
> | First Grade | P3,000 | No limit |
> | Second Grade | 2,400 | P7,200 |
> | Third Grade | 2,160 | 4,800 |
> | Senior Stenographer | 3,000 | No limit |
> | Junior Stenographer | 2,160 | 7,200 |
> | Senior Typist | 3,000 | 6,720 |
> | Junior Typist | 2,160 | 5,040" |
>
> **Section 2.** All Acts, executive orders, administrative orders, rules and regulations, or parts thereof inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965
