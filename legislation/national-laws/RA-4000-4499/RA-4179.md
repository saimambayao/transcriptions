> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4179**
>
> An Act Converting the Pototan General Hospital in the Municipality of Pototan, Province of Iloilo, into a National Hospital to be Known as Doña Trinidad Bolivar Memorial National Hospital, and Authorizing the Appropriation of Funds Therefor
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Pototan General Hospital in the Municipality of Pototan, Province of Iloilo, is hereby converted into a national hospital, to be known as Doña Trinidad Bolivar Memorial National Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The additional sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, operation and maintenance of said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-five.1âшphi1 Thereafter, such sums as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: March 26, 1965.
