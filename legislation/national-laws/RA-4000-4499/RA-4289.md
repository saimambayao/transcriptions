> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4289**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Municipal District of Carabao Island in the Province of Romblon to Municipal District of San Jose
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The Municipal District of Carabao Island in the Province of Romblon shall hereafter be known for all intents and purposes as the Municipal Districts of San Jose.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
