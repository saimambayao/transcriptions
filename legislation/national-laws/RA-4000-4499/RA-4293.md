> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4293**
>
> An Act to Create the Barrio of Malaya in the Municipality of Marikina, Province of Rizal
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The areas covering Paliparan Subdivision, Better Homes, Inc., and abandoned Manila Railroad tracks, within the Municipality of Marikina, Province of Rizal, are hereby constituted into a barrio in said municipality, to be known as Barrio of Malaya.
>
> The boundaries of said barrio shall be as follows:
>
> On the North, the 14-meter wide road and an imaginary line going West to East; on the East, the first creek near Barrio Parang; on the South, the Marikina townsite, including the hospital site and property of Roberto Tuason; and on the West, the Sto. Niño (Lazaro Estate, Manotok Subdivision), Lamuan (Malanday, Manotok, Inc.) Concepcion (Manotok Subdivision), and property of Gregorio San Diego, and others.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
