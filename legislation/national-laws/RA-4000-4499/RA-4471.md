> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4471**
>
> An Act Granting the Name of Calumpang Community School in the Municipality of Naval, Sub-Province of Biliran, Province of Leyte, to Father Sergio Eamiguel Community School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Calumpang Community School in the Municipality of Naval, Sub-Province of Biliran, Province of Leyte, is hereby changed to Father Sergio Eamiguel Community School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
