> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4294**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Pantalan Barrio School in the Municipality of Carles, Province of Iloilo, to Tiburcio Betita Memorial School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Pantalan Barrio School in the Municipality of Carles, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Tiburcio Betita Memorial School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
