> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4472**
>
> An Act Creating the Barrio of New Cagayancillo in the Municipality of Balabac, Province of Palawan
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sitios of Tarumbasi, Malanta, Baring-Baringin, Bonbon and Casayapan in the Municipality of Balabac, Province of Palawan, are hereby constituted into a district and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the Barrio of New Cagayancillo.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
