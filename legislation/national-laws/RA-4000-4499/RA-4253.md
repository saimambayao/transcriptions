> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4253**
>
> An Act Changing the Names of Mascarina Barrio School and Villa Gomez Barrio School in the Municipality of Quezon, Province of Quezon, to Pedro Cabangon Barrio School and Ulpiano Camacho Barrio School, Respectively
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The names of Mascarina Barrio School and Villa Gomez Barrio School in the Municipality of Quezon, Province of Quezon, are hereby changed to Pedro Cabangon Barrio School and Ulpiano Camacho Barrio School, respectively.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
