> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4184**
>
> An Act Authorizing the Appropriation of Two Hundred Thousand Pesos for the Establishment of an Emergency Hospital in Talim Island, Province of Rizal, to be Known as Talim Island Emergency Hospital
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There shall be established, under the direct supervision of the Director of Medical Services, an emergency hospital in Talim Island, Province of Rizal, to be known as Talim Island Emergency Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred and sixty-six. Thereafter, such sum as may be needed for the operation and maintenance of the hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 3.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 21, 1965.
