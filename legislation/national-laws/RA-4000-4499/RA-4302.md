> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4302**
>
> An Act to Establish a National School of Arts and Trades in the Municipality of Cabucgayan, Sub-Province of Biliran, Province of Leyte, to be Known as Cabucgayan National School of Arts and Trades, and to Authorize the Appropriation of Funds Therefor
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby established a national school of arts and trades in the Municipality of Cabucgayan, Sub-Province of Biliran, Province of Leyte, to be known as Cabucgayan National School of Arts and Trades.
>
> **Section 2.** The Secretary of Education is authorized to organize the curricular instruction in said school and he shall have the power to issue rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.
>
> **Section 3.** The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-six. Thereafter, the sums necessary therefor shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 4.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
