> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4365**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of the Municipality of Bugho in the Province of Leyte, to the Municipality of Javier
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of the Municipality of Bugho in the Province of Leyte is hereby changed to the Municipality of Javier.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
