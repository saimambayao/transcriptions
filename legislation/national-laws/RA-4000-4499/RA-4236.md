> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4236**
>
> An Act Providing for Four Additional Assistant Provincial Fiscals for the Province of Camarines Sur, Amending for This Purpose Section Sixteen Hundred Seventy-Four of the Administration Code, as Amended
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The provision of Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Administrative Code, as amended, relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals in the Province of Camarines Sur, is further amended to read as follows:
>
> > "Camarines Sur, eight assistant provincial fiscals;"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
