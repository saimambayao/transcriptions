> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4283**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Washington Street in Iligan City to General Emilio Aguinaldo Street
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Washington Street in Iligan City shall hereafter be known and referred to as General Emilio Aguinaldo Street.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
