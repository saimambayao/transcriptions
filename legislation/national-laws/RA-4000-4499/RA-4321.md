> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4321**
>
> An Act Declaring the Barrio of Kapunitan in the Municipality of Orion, Province of Bataan, a Duly Constituted Barrio Thereof
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The barrio of Kapunitan in the Municipality of Orion, Province of Bataan, is hereby declared a duly constituted barrio of said municipality.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
