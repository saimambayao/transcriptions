> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4418**
>
> An Act Condoning the Amount of One Hundred Eighteen Thousand Pesos Representing Privilege and Percentage Taxes Plus Twenty-Five Per Cent Surcharge Thereon, Allegedly Due from the Municipality of Olongapo, Province of Zambales, for the Operation of its Electric, Telephone and Ice Plants
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of one hundred eighteen thousand pesos representing privilege and percentage taxes plus twenty-five percent surcharge thereon, allegedly due from the Municipality of Olongapo, Province of Zambales, for the operation of its electric, telephone and ice plants which were turned over to said municipality by the United States Navy upon the relinquishment of the occupation and control of said municipality by the United States Navy, is hereby condoned.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
