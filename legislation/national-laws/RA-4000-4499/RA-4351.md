> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4351**
>
> An Act to Change the Name of Lucapon North Elementary School in Barrio Lucapon, Municipality of Santa Cruz, Province of Zambales, to Don Brigido Miraflor Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Lucapon North Elementary School in Barrio Lucapon, Municipality of Santa Cruz, Province of Zambales, is hereby changed to Don Brigido Miraflor Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
