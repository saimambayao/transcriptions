> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4260**
>
> An Act to Change the Name of Lake Lanao in the Province of Lanao del Sur, to Lake Sultan Alonto
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Lake Lanao in the Province of Lanao del Sur is changed to Lake Sultan Alonto.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
