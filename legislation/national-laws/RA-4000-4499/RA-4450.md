> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4450**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Balasan High School in the Municipality of Basalan, Province of Iloilo, to Leon Ganzon Memorial High School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Balasan High School in the Municipality of Basalan, Province of Iloilo, is hereby change to Leon Ganzon Memorial High School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
