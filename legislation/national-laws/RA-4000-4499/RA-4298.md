> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4298**
>
> An Act Authorizing the Appropriation of Three Hundred Thousand Pesos for the Operation and Maintenance of Cabalian National Vocational High School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The sum of three hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of Cabalian National Vocational High School during the fiscal year nineteen hundred and sixty-six. Thereafter, the necessary funds for the same purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
