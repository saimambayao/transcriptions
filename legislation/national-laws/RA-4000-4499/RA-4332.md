> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4332**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Barrio Walang Balahibo in the Municipality of Lemery, Province of Batangas, to Barrio San Isidro
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Barrio Walang Balahibo in the Municipality of Lemery, Province of Batangas, is hereby changed to Barrio San Isidro.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
