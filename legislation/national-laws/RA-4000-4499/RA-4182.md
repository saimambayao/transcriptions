> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4182**
>
> An Act to Change the Name of Eastern Pangasinan Emergency Hospital to Eastern Pangasinan General Hospital
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Eastern Pangasinan Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Tayug, Province of Pangasinan, is hereby changed to Eastern Pangasinan General Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 21, 1965.
