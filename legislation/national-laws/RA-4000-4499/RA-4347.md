> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4347**
>
> An Act to Modify the Name of Tejero Barrio School in Barrio Tejero, Municipality of Rosario, Province of Cavite to Tejeros Convention Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Tejero Barrio School in Barrio Tejero, Municipality of Rosario, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to Tejeros Convention Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
