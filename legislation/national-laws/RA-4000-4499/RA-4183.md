> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4183**
>
> An Act to Changing the Name of Inudaran Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Marantao, Province of Lanao del Sur, to Sultan Guro Central Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Inudaran Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Marantao, Province of Lanao del Sur, is changed to Sultan Guro Central Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: April 21, 1965.
