> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4328**
>
> AN ACT INCREASING THE NUMBER OF ASSISTANT PROVINCIAL FISCALS FOR THE PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION SIXTEEN HUNDRED SEVENTY-FOUR OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE CODE, AS AMENDED.
>
> **Section 1.** The provisions of Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Administrative Code, as amended, relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals in the Province of Oriental Mindoro, is further amended to read as follows:
>
> "Oriental Mindoro, five;"
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965
