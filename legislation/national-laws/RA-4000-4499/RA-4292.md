> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4292**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Infanta Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Infanta, Province of Quezon, to Claro M. Recto Memorial Hospital
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Infanta Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Infanta, Province of Quezon, is hereby changed to Claro M. Recto Memorial Hospital.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
