> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4494**
>
> An Act Creating Barrio Abaroan in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Palawan
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** Sitios Baututo and Egruroan in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Palawan are hereby constituted into a district and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Abaroan.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
