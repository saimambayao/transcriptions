> **REPUBLIC ACT No. 4404**
>
> An Act Changing the Name of Ticulon Elementary School in the Municipality of Malita, Province of Davao, to Toribio Limcaoco Elementary School
>
> *Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:*
>
> **Section 1.** The name of Ticulon Elementary School in the Municipality of Malita, Province of Davao, is hereby changed to Toribio Limcaoco Elementary School.
>
> **Section 2.** This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
>
> Approved: June 19, 1965.
