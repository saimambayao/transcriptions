“BANGSAMORO ELECTORAL CODE OF 2023”

EXPLANATORY NOTE

The Bangsamoro Autonomy Act (BAA) No. 35, otherwise known as the
Bangsamoro Electoral Code of 2023, was enacted by the Bangsamoro Parliament on
March 8, 2023. This law prescribes the structural, functional, and procedural
principles governing the conduct of elections, plebiscites, initiatives, referenda, and
recall proceedings, and promotes the formation of genuinely principled political
parties in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM).

Section 14, Article VII of the said law provides for the inclusion of the names
of all candidates, their photo/logo, and a “None of the Above” (NOTA) option in the
official ballot. However, if left unamended, this provision may result in the
possibility of unfilled seats in the Bangsamoro Government during the first
Parliamentary Elections in the Bangsamoro on October 13, 2025.

The Commission on Elections (COMELEC) has urged the Bangsamoro
Parliament to amend the Bangsamoro Electoral Code, particularly the NOTA
provision. Under the existing law, if the NOTA or abstain vote garners the majority,
the candidate placing second shall be proclaimed as the winner. Constitutionally,

This bill therefore seeks to amend Section 14, Article VII of the Bangsamoro

In view of the foregoing, the immediate passage of this bill is earnestly sought.

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament in session
assembled:

SECTION 1. Section 14, Article VII of Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 35,
otherwise known as “An Act Providing for the Bangsamoro Electoral Code in the
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” is hereby amended deleting the
clause “and a “None of the Above” option, to read as:

SEC. 14. Official Ballot. - As far as practicable, the official ballots for the
BARMM elections shall include the names of the candidates and their respective
photos or logos. The COMELEC, through the BEO, shall exert efforts to ensure that
the Bangsamoro people are adequately informed of all candidates running for election
in the BARMM.

SECTION 2. Revision of the Election Ballot. - The COMELEC, through the
Bangsamoro Electoral Office (BEO), shall cause the immediate revision of the official
ballots for the October 13, 2025 BARMM parliamentary elections, to ensure that no
ballot shall include the “None of the Above” (NOTA) option, pursuant to the
removal provided under this Act.
