EXPLANATORY NOTE

Republic Act No. 11054, the “Organic Law for Bangsamoro Autonomous
Region in Muslim Mindanao”, provides in Section 22 (1), Article IX thereof that:
“The Bangsamoro Government shall adopt a policy on health that provides for a
comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents. It shall
establish by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of
its people and ensure that thé individual basic right to life shall be attainable
through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services.”

Hospitals are one of the most important pillars of any society. It plays an
increasingly important role in people’s lives; with great pollution levels and
changing lifestyles, much more in this time of COVID-19 pandemic, more and
more people are falling prey to diseases and requiring adequate medical care.
Having no nearby hospitals within the locality would mean a lot of hassle for the
patient and their families to look for a proper specialist to diagnose particular
illness and its treatment.

Datu Piang is a 2nd class municipality in the Province of Maguindanao. It
is among the first municipalities of the old Cotabato Province. According to the

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in Parliament assembled.

SECTION 1. Short Title. — This Act shall be known as the “ESTABLISHMENT
OF DATU PIANG MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL 2023”.

SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy. - It is a policy of the Bangsamoro
Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article
IX of Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known as the “Organic Law for the
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to provide for a
comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and
establish by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of
its people and ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable
through the prompt intervention of excellence and affordable medical services.

SETION 3. The Establishment of Datu Piang Municipal Hospital. - To
implement the above-mentioned policy, a hospital facility is hereby established
to be known as the Datu Piang Municipal Hospital (DPMH) to be located in the
Municipality of Datu Piang, Maguindanao, Province of Maguindanao, BARMM.
The DPMH shall be constructed and start its operations under capacity and
capability as prescribed by the licensing standards of the Bureau of Health
Facilities and Services under the Department of Health (DOH). The standard
personnel, medical and office equipment and other facilities required shall

SECTION 4. Catchment area/coverage. — The Datu Piang Municipal Hospital
shall serve primarily, but not limited to, the patients coming from the
municipality of Datu Piang and may extend its services as an augmentation and
support to other existing medical facilities when such need arises to patients
coming from the adjacent municipalities of Maguindanao province.

SECTION 5. Objective/s - To provide a highly accessible, affordable,
comprehensive total health care and hospital services to the BARMM
constituents cited under Section 4 above, and to integrate in its system of
operations all pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic Act No. 11223
otherwise known as the Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

SECTION 6. Administration, Management and Organization. — The Datu
Piang Municipal Hospital shall be under the administrative supervision of the
Minister of Health who is hereby authorized to determine and approve the
appropriate organizational structure for the management and operations of the
facility in accordance with existing laws.

SECTION 7. Appropriation. - The amount necessary to carry out the provision
of this Act shall be included in the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act. Thereafter,
funds necessary for the maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be
provided in accordance with the provisions of the Bangsamoro Appropriations
Act and other pertinent laws.

Before the construction of the hospital, the Minister of Health shall issue a
written assurance that personnel of the hospital will be appointed and that funds
for its maintenance and operation have been appropriated.

SECTION 8. Implementing Rules and Regulations. - The BARMM-MOH shall
promulgate the Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of this Act within
sixty (60) days after the enactment of this act.

SECTION 9. Separability Clause. -— In the event any provision of this act or
the application of such provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this act
shall not be affected by such declaration and shall remain in full force and effect.

SECTION 10. Repealing Clause. - All laws and regulations under the
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) government
including those passed under the defunct Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao (ARMM) government regarding the construction, establishment and
operations of the Datu Piang Municipal Hospital not consistent with this Act are
hereby repealed.
