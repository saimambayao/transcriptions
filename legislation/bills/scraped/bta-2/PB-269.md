Bongo Island is comprised of eight (8) barangays under the jurisdiction of Municipality of
Parang, Maguindanao del Norte. Its population, as determined by the 2020 Census, was 4,000.
This represented 3.89% of the total population of Parang. Bongo Island is 19.9 kilometers away
from Barangay Poblacion, Parang, where some patients are being transferred in some cases.
However, in extreme cases, patients need to be transferred immediately in Cotabato City which
is twenty four (24) kilometers away.

In both cases, residents have to cross the sea in order to avail appropriate medical services.
Transporting patients using boats is subject to weather conditions. It poses a risk to the safety of
the patient during transit. When strong water current is experienced, there is no way that the

Be enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous
Region Muslim in Mindanao.

SECTION 1. Title. — This Act shall be known as the “Bongo Community Hospital of 2024”

SECTION. 2. Declaration of Policy. — It is the policy of the Bangsamoro Government to
protect and promote the right to health of the people and instill health consciousness among them
by making health services available, accessible and affordable for all. Further, the Bangsamoro
Government is mandated to uphold the people’s right to have access to essential good health. and
other social services that promote their well-being.

SECTION 3. Creation. — There is hereby established an Infirmary Hospital with ten (10) bed
capacity in Bongo Island, Municipality of Parang, Maguindanao del Norte, otherwise known as
the Bongo Community Hospital (BCH). All systems of operation of BCH shall be in accordance
with pertinent and relevant provisions of the Universal Health Care (UCH) Program under
Republic Act 11223.

SECTION 4. Site of the Infirmary Hospital. — The site of the Infirmary Hospital shall be
determined by the barangay local government units in coordination and with approval of the
Ministry of Health (MOH), and Ministry of Environment and Natural Resources and Energy
(MENRE).

SECTION 5. Administration. — The BCH shall be under the administrative supervision of the
MOH wihio is tereby authorized to determine and approve itie orgattizationai situciure for tire
effective and efficient management and operation of the hospital.

SECTION 6. Appropriation, - The amount necessary for the initial implementation of this Act
shall be charged against the present General Appropriation Act for the Bangsamoro (GAAB).
Thereafter, the funds necessary for the continued maintenance and operation of the hospital shall
be included in the GAAB.
