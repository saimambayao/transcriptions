EXPLANATORY NOTE

The creation of this Proposed BTA Parliament bill is in furtherance of
timely appropriation, comprehensive implementation and full compliance of the
adopted Republic Act No. 11330 entitled “ AN ACT CONVERTING THE DINAIG
MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DATU ODIN SINSUAT
PROVINCE OF MAGUINDANAO, INTO A FIFTY (50) BED CAPACITY DISTRICT
HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS THE DATU ODIN SINSUAT DISTRICT HOSPITAL,
UPGRADING ITS’ FACILITIES AND PROFESSIONAL HEALTH CARE,
AUTHORIZING THE INCREASE OF ITS MEDICAL PERSONNEL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR?’ signed and approved by no less than the
President of the Philippines, Rodrigo Roa Duterte on April 2019.

Upon inquiry with the hospital’s Officer in charge - Chief Nurse,
Laboratory Supervisor, Housekeeping Supervisor and Admin Assistant as of the
year 2022, it was found out that, even though the hospital has indeed

the people and instill health consciousness among them. It shall also uphold the
peoples’ right to have access to essential goods, health, and other social services
that promote their well-being. The Parliament shall enact appropriate legislation
towards this end.

The Bangsamoro Government is mandated to create and implement
legislation that would benefit the Bangsamoro People and the general Public.
Thus, this proposed BTA Parliament bill for the expansion of the DOS District

Be it enacted by Bangsamoro Transition Authority in Parliament assembled:

SEC. 1. Title. — This Act shall be known as the “UPGRADING DATU ODIN
SINSUAT DISTRICT HOSPITAL 2023”

SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy. - It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous
Region in Muslim Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article IX of the
Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known as the “Organic Law of the Bangsamoro
Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to provide for a comprehensive and
integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law a
general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure
that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt
intervention of excellence and affordable medical services.

SEC. 3. To upgrade the existing Datu Odin Sinsuat District Hospital. —-The
existing Datu Odin Sinsuat District Hospital is hereby upgraded from its present
status with a fifty (50) bed capacity into one hundred (100) Authorized Bed
Capacity in accordance with the national standards set by the Bureau of Health
Facilities and Services under the Department of Health (DOH). The existing
building including its landscaping, medical and office equipment and other
facilities, its operating funds and personnel are also hereby upgraded to include
the needed construction and procurement thereof in order to meet the
prescribed standards commensurate to the upgraded level.

SEC. 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program. -To
upgrade the existing Datu Odin Sinsuat District Hospital, it shall integrate into
its system of operations all the pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic
Act No. 11223 otherwise known as the Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

SEC. 5. Administration, Management, and Organization. —- The Datu Odin
Sinsuat District Hospital shall be under the administrative supervision of the
Minister of Health who is hereby authorized to determine and approve the
appropriate organizational structure for the management and operations of the
facility, and if necessary, shall cause the creation of the Hospital Board of
Directors, define its powers and functions, and appoint its officers and members,
in accordance with existing laws.

SEC. 6. Appropriation. - The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of
this Act shall be included in the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act. Thereafter,
funds necessary for the maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be
provided in accordance with the provisions of the Bangsamoro Appropriations
Act and other pertinent laws.

SEC. 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations. - The Bangsamoro
Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao — Ministry of Health (BARMM-MOH)
shall promulgate the Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of this Act
within sixty (60) days after the enactment of this act.

SEC. 8. Separability Clause. - In the event any provision of this act or the
application of such provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this act shall
not be affected by such declaration and shall remain in full force and effect.

SEC. 9. Repealing Clause. - All laws and regulations under the Bangsamoro
Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) government including those
passed under the defunct Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM)
government regarding the operations of the Datu Odin Sinsuat District Hospital
not consistent with this Act are hereby repealed.

SEC. 10. Effectivity. -This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following
the completion of its publication in a newspaper of regional circulation in the
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.
