AN ACT
FURTHER CONTINUING THE VALIDITY OF
THE 2020 TO 2022 SPECIAL DEVELOPMENT FUND, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

Section 1. Section 3 of the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act or Bangsamoro Act No.
22, as amended, is hereby further amended to read as follows:

"Section 3. Cash Budgeting System. All appropriations authorized in this
Act shall be available for release and disbursement for the purpose specified until
December 31, 2024.

Appropriations for infrastructure capital outlays shall be valid for
obligation until December 31, 2024, while the completion of construction,
inspection, and payment shall continue to be effective until fully released and
disbursed. On the other hand, appropriations for MOOE and other capital outlays
items shall likewise be valid for release, and obligation until December 31, 2024,
and shall continue to be available for disbursement until fully expended.

The MFBM is authorized to issue the necessary guidelines for the effective
implementation of the cash budgeting system."

Section 2. Section 48 of the Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 23 is amended to read as
follows:

“Sec. 48. All appropriations in this Act, except Personnel Services and
General Administration and Support under Maintenance and Other Operating
Expenses, shall be available for release and disbursement for the purpose specified,
and under the same general and special provisions applicable thereto, until the
periods hereunder specified. While the amount appropriated herein, as share of the
constituent LGUs on the income taxes collected within the BARMM shall be
available until fully expended or disbursed for the purpose.

For Fiscal Year 2022, the appropriations for infrastructure, capital outlays,
and Support to Operations and Operations under MOOE shall be valid for
obligation until December 31, 2023. The appropriation shall continue to be
effective until fully released and disbursed.

Appropriations under the Special Development Fund however, regardless
of allotment class, shall be valid for obligation until December 31, 2024 and shall
likewise be available until fully expended or disbursed for the purpose.

The appropriations for Personnel Services authorized in this Act shall be
valid for obligation and disbursement only until December 31, 2022.

The MFBM is authorized to issue the necessary guidelines for the effective
implementation of this Act.”

Section 3. Repealing Clause. - All laws, decrees, executive issuances, rules and
regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

Section 4. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect immediately after its publication in

a newspaper of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.

Approved,

HON.
Chief Minister
