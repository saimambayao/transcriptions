EXPLANATORY NOTE

Quality healthcare is a subjective, complex and multi-dimensional concept. According to an
Iranian Researcher, Ali Mohammad Mosadeghrad, quality healthcare as “consistently delighting
the patient by providing efficacious, effective and efficient healthcare services based on the latest

clinical guidelines and standards, which meet the patient’s needs and satisfies health providers”.

Delivery of quality and enhanced health care services requires an advanced medical equipment,
highly complex diagnostic and therapeutic approach including expert handling of capacitated
manpower that can provide efficient delivery of service and medical attention to patients in
need.

Dr. Serapio B. Montaner Jr. Al - Haj Memorial Hospital (DSBMMH) is a government run
hospital located in Malabang Municipality, Lanao del Sur, has been upgraded into level 2 with
100 authorized bed capacity since June 29, 2022 under Republic Act No. 11870. The DSBMMH
consistently has the highest number of patients served among the five (5) BARMM government -
run hospitals in the province of Lanao del Sur augmenting the hospitals in and around Lanao del
Sur, serving 1.2 Million people. According to the 2019 Hospital Statistical Report of the
Ministry of Health, the 75 bed DSBMMH provided care way above its bed capacity. In the last 3
years, the hospital bed occupancy rate was computed to be around 300-400% despite the absence
of any fund augmentation to the hospital Maintenance and Other Operating Expenses (MOOE)
and other financial support from the government except for Philhealth reimbursement, offering
setvices that include General Medical, Pediatric and Surgical services, Ophthalmology,
Obstetrical and Gynecological services and Anesthesiology.

Upgrading of Dr. Serapio B. Montaner Jr. Al Haj Memorial Hospital with its corresponding
provision of appropriate manpower is a timely and outstanding measure that this entails more
responsibilities, accountabilities and resource mobilization, both financial, infrastructure as
pledged by the national government and human resouce.

The Bangsamoro Transition Authority and the Office of the Chief Minister through the Ministry
of Health and the Ministry of Finance, and Budget Management play a crucial role to achieve a
better, efficient and quality health system.

To effectively respond to the present gaps and challenges of the DSBMMH this measure
becomes a matter of utmost consideration of the Government of the Day to approve and enact.
As such, this bill is deemed necessary to increase human resources and hospital logistics and
further enhance the delivery of improved health services attaining quality health care for all in
Lanao del Sur and nearby provinces.

In view of the foregoing, the passage of this bill is earnestly sought.

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament of the Bangsamoro
Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao:

Section 1. Title. - This act shall be known as the DR. SERAPIO B. MONTANER JR.
MEMORIAL HOSPITAL ACT OF 2025.

Section 2. Declaration of Policy. - It is hereby declared the policy of the Bangsamoro
Government to maintain and ensure the delivery of basic and responsive health
programs, promote the right to health of the Bangsamoro people, instill health consciousness
among them, and adopt a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its
constituents.

Section 3. Upgrading of the DSBMMH. - The DSBMMH is hereby upgraded from its present
Twenty-five (25) bed capacity to one hundred (100) bed capacity in accordance with the
National Level Classification Standards set by the Department of Health.

Section 4. Manpower Requirement. - The existing manpower of the DSBMMH, which
includes Doctors, Nurses, Midwives, and other Health Practitioners and support personnel shall
be increased correspondingly in accordance with Civil Service Commission rules and
regulations.

Section 5. Administration, Management, and Operation. - The DSBMMH shall remain
under the administrative supervision and control of the Ministry of Health which shall approve
the appropriate organizational structure and personnel, hospital plans, programs, and activities
for its effective management and operation of the herein-mentioned upgraded hospital.

Section 6. Appropriations. - The Ministry of Health shall immediately include in the
Ministry’s program the implementation of this Act, the necessary funding of which shall be
included in the Annual Bangsamoro Appropriations Act.

Section 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations. - Within sixty (60) days from the effectivity
of this Act, the Ministry of Health and Ministry of Finance, Budget, and Management shall
promulgate the appropriate Implementing Rules and Regulations for the effective
implementation of this Act.

Section 8. Separability Clause. - The provisions of this Act are hereby declared separable, in
the event one or more provisions or part thereof are declared unconstitutional, such declaration
of unconstitutionality shall not affect the validity of the other provisions thereof.

Section 9. Repealing Clause. - All regional laws, acts, decrees, executive orders, proclamations,
and/or administrative regulations inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby
amended, modified, superseded, or repealed accordingly.

Section 10. Effectivity. - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a
newspaper of general circulation in the region.
