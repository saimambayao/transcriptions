AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE SHARIFF KABUNSUAN SHRINE IN BARANGAY
KALANGANAN 2, COTABATO CITY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

EXPLANATORY NOTE

This Act seeks to establish the Shariff Kabunsuan Shrine in Barangay Kalanganan 2,
Cotabato City, in recognition of the historical and cultural significance of Shariff
Kabunsuan's arrival and his introduction of Islam to this part of Mindanao. The
Shrine will serve as a center for cultural preservation, historical education, and the
promotion of peace and understanding among diverse communities.

Shariff Kabunsuan's legacy is deeply intertwined with the history and identity of
the Bangsamoro people. The establishment of a dedicated shrine will not only
honor his contributions but also serve as a landmark for cultural tourism and a
venue for activities that celebrate and promote the rich heritage of the region.

This Act sets forth the objectives, powers, and functions of the Shariff Kabunsuan
Shrine and provides for the appropriation of funds necessary for its establishment
and initial operation. It is the sincere hope of this Body that the passage of this Act
will significantly contribute to the preservation and promotion of Bangsamoro
history and culture.

As the sponsor of this Bill, | seek to honor Shariff Kabunsuan for his historical
significance to the Bangsamoro people by establishing this shrine dedicated to his
legacy.

BE IT ENACTED by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in Parliament
assembled, as follows:

Section 1. Title. This Act shall be known as the “Shariff Kabunsuan Shrine Act”.

Section 2. Lega/ Bases. This Act is enacted pursuant to the inherent power of the
State to promote and preserve its cultural heritage, as enshrined in Section 2,
Article XIV of the 1987 Constitution, and in accordance with Republic Act No.
10066 (National Cultural Heritage Act of 2009), particularly Section 2 thereof
declaring the policy of the State to protect and preserve its cultural heritage, and
Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 (Bangsamoro Organic Law), which mandates
the preservation, protection, and promotion of the Bangsamoro's cultural and
historical heritage, recognizing the significant role of Islam and historical figures
like Shariff Kabunsuan in the region's history.

Section 3. Declaration of Policy. \t is hereby declared the policy of the Bangsamoro
Government to preserve, protect, and promote the rich cultural and historical
heritage of the Bangsamoro people. Recognizing the pivotal role of Shariff
Kabunsuan in the history of the region, the Bangsamoro Government shall
establish a shrine in his honor to serve as a beacon of cultural understanding,
historical awareness, and a symbol of unity.

‘Section 4. Establishment of the Shrine. There is hereby established a shrine in
honor of Shariff Kabunsuan in Barangay Kalanganan 2, Cotabato City, to be known
as the Shariff Kabunsuan Shrine.

Section 5. Purpose. The Shariff Kabunsuan Shrine shall serve as a historical and
cultural landmark, a center for Islamic studies and research, and a tourist
destination that showcases the rich cultural heritage of the Muslim community in
the Philippines.

Section 6. Definition of Terms. For the purpose of this Act, the following terms are
hereby defined as follows:

a) “Shrine” — A memorial honoring a well-known person or historical
event.

b) “Historical and Cultural landmark” — a place, structure, or site that
holds significant historical, cultural, or aesthetic value.

Section 7. Governance and Administration. The Bangsamoro Commission for the
Preservation of Cultural Heritage (BCPCH) shall be responsible for the overall
governance and administration of the Shrine. The specific structure and
composition of the governing body, as well as its detailed powers and functions,
shall be defined in the implementing rules and regulations of this Act.

Section 8. Powers and Functions of the Shrine. The Shrine, through its governing
body, shall have the following powers and functions:

a) To manage and administer the shrine and its facilities;

b) To develop and implement plans and programs for the promotion
and development of the shrine;

c) To enter into contracts and agreements with private entities and
organizations for the benefit of the shrine;

d) To receive and administer donations and grants for the shrine;

e) To establish rules and regulations for the use and maintenance of the
shrine.

Section 9. Funding and Appropriation. The necessary funds for the purpose of this
Act shall be provided for in the annual appropriations for public works and
disbursements shall be made by the Bangsamoro Commission on the Preservation
of Cultural Heritage.
