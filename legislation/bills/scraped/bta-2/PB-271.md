AN ACT CREATING THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILINTANGAN IN THE
PROVINCE LANAO DEL SUR, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR
OTHER PURPOSES

BE IT ENACTED by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament assembled, as follows;

SECTION 1. Declaration of Policy. In the exercise of genuine autonomy and self-governance,
the Bangsamoro Government is empowered to create, divide, merge, abolish, or substantially
alter boundaries of municipalities or barangays in accordance with a law enacted by the
Parliament. The municipalities or barangay created, divided or merged or whose boundaries
are substantially altered shall be entitled to their appropriate share in the National Taxes
Allocation (NTA), Provided, that it shall be approved by a majority of the votes cast in a
plebiscite in the political units directly affected.

SECTION 2. Creation of Municipality. The Barangays of Western (Poblacion), Pilintangan,
Kadingilan, Buntongan, Balatin, Panang, Mimbuaya, Park Area, Muslim Village, Buot, and
East Kili Kili are hereby separated from the Municipality of Wao, Lanao del Sur and constituted
into a district and independent municipality to be known as Municipality of Pilintangan, which
hereby created, in the province of Lanao del Sur.

SECTION 3. Seat of Government. The seat of government of the Municipality of Pilintangan
shall be established in Barangay Pilintangan, which shall be known hereafter as the "Bandara
Ingud".

SECTION 4. Territorial Boundary. The territorial boundary of the new municipality shall
follow the existing boundaries of the constituent barangays as above enumerated in Section 2
hereof.

SECTION 5. Grants and Donations. To accept local and foreign grants and donation.

SECTION 6. Plebiscite Requirement. The Municipality of Pilintangan shall acquire corporate
existence upon ratification of its creation by a majority of the votes cast by qualified voters in
a plebiscite to be conducted in the barangays comprising the municipality pursuant to Section
2 hereof within sixty (60) days after the approval of the Act.

SECTION 7. Conduct of Plebiscite. The Commission on Elections (COMELEC), through the
Bangsamoro Electoral Office, shall conduct and supervise the plebiscite for the purpose of
creation of Municipality of Pilintangan. The expenses in holding the plebiscite shall be taken
out of the available funds of the Commission on Election under the current fiscal year
appropriation.

SECTION 8. Appointment of Municipal Officials. The municipal government is composed
of a Municipal Mayor, Municipal Vice-Mayor and eight (8) members of its Sangguniang Bayan
who shall be appointed either by the President of the Republic of the Philippines or Chief
Minister of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM)
immediately after the ratification of this Act in a plebiscite. They shall continue to hold office
until their successors shall have been duly elected in the next regular election. Provided, That
the incumbent elective members of the Sangguniang Bayan of the Municipality of Wao, Lanao
del Sur, who are actual residents of the new municipality shall serve the remaining terms of the
elective offices in the mother municipality.

SECTION 9. Financial Assistance Pending Entitlement to National Tax Allocation. The
Bangsamoro Government shall appropriate and provide financial assistance for the
administration and operation of the Municipal Government of Pilintangan in the amount of not
less than THREE MILLION PESOS (PHP 3,000,000.00) every month until such time as the
new municipality shall have been allocated its share in the revenue allotment.

SECTION 10. Separability Clause. If, for any cause, any provision of this Act is declared
unconstitutional or contrary to the provisions of Republic Act No. 11054, the rest of the
provisions thereof which are not affected shall in full force and effect.

SECTION 11. Repealing Clause. That any provision of this Act which is contrary to existing
laws and declared unconstitutional shall be null and void.

SECTION 12. Effectivity of the Law. This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days from its
publication in the Bangsamoro Gazette or in (1) newspaper of regional circulation.

APPROVED.

This day of of at Cotabato City, Philippines.

PRINCIPAL AUTHOR
