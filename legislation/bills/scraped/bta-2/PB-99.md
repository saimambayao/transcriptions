Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in Parliament assembled:

SECTION 1. Creation. — There is hereby established the “South Ubian Municipal Hospital” in
the Municipality of South Ubian, Province of Tawi-Tawi to address the health and social

services of its constituents.


============================================================

SECTION 2. Purpose. - The purpose of this act is to establish a level 1 municipal hospital of
fifty (50) bed capacity to provide for a comprehensive and integrated health services to the
people of the Municipality of South Ubian and its neighboring islands. It is an essential
institution in the effective delivery of the health system making health services accessible
at any time to the people in need.

SECTION 3. Services. — The establishment of the hospital shall include required units and
services as the Ministry of Health may deem necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act
integrating into its system of operations the appropriate provisions of Republic Act No.
11223 otherwise known as the Universal Health Care Act.

SECTION 4. Organization; Administrative Supervision — The South Ubian Municipal
Hospital shall be under the direct supervision of the Ministry of Health which shall

determine and approve its appropriate organizationai structure and piantiiia positions.

SECTION 5. Appropriations. — The Ministry of Finance, Budget and Management shall
provide annual funding for the plantilla positions and the maintenance and other operating
expenses of the hospital and shall be made part of the annual General Appropriations Act
of the Bangsamoro

SECTION 6. Effectivity — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the
Official Gazette or a newspaper of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous
Region in Muslim Mindanao.
