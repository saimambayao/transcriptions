AN ACT RENAMING THE MUNICIPALITY OF SULTAN MASTURA,
MAGUINDANAO DEL NORTE TO MUNICIPALITY OF SUBPANGEN

BE IT ENACTED by the Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim

Mindanao: as follows:

SECTION 1. Title. The Municipality of Sultan Mastura in the Province of Maguindanao Del

Norte is hereby renamed as Municipality of Subpangen.

SECTION 2. Plebiscite Requirement. The renaming of municipality shall be effective upon
ratification by the majority of the votes cast by qualified voters in plebiscites to be conducted and
supervised by the Commission on Elections (COMELEC) in the present Municipality of Sultan
Mastura.

The expenses for the plebiscites shall be borne by the Bangsamoro Government.

SECTION 3. Effectivity of the law. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its

publication in the Bangsamoro Gazette or in one newspaper of regional circulation.
