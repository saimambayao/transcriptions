“AN ACT ESTABLISHING AND CONSTRUCTING A LEVEL — 1] MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL
WITH FIFTY (50) BED CAPACITY IN DATU ANGGAL MIDTIMBANG MUNICIPALITY,
PROVINCE OF MAGUINDANAO TO BE KNOWN AS DATU ANGGAL MIDTIMBANG
MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR FS FACILITIES AND
PROFESSIONAL HEALTH CARE, MEDICAL PERSONNEL AND FOR OTHER
PURPOSES”

Section I. Short Title. — This Act shall be known as the “DATU ANGGAL MUNICIPAL
HOSPITAL”

Section 2. Declaration of Policy. ~- \t is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao to provide for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its
constituents and establish by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its
people and ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt
intervention of excellent and affordable medical services. Towards this end, and in order to
adequately respond to emergencies, disasters and emerging diseases, the Bangsamoro
Government shall protect the interest and promote the well-being and the medical needs of the
Bangsamoro People.

Section 3. Datu Anggal Midtimbang Municipal Hospital. — There shall be established a level |
general hospital with fifty bed capacity in Datu Anggal Midtimbang Municipality in the Province
of Maguindanao to be known as the Datu Anggal Midtimbang Municipal Hospital.

Section 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program. -- The establishment of
the Datu Anggal Midtimbang Municipal Hospital shall integrate into its system of operations all
pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic Act No. 11223 otherwise known as the
Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

Section 3, Administration. — Upon the effectivity of this Act, the Datu Anggal Midtimbang
Municipal Hospital shall be under the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who is
hereby authorized to determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the
management and operations of the hospital.

Section 6. Appropriations. — The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall
be included in the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act. Thereafter, funds necessary for the
maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be provided in accordance with the provisions of
the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act and other pertinent laws.

Section 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations - The BARMM-MOH shall promulgate the
Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of this Act within sixty (60) days after the enactment
of this Act.

Section 8. Separability Clause. -- In the event any provision of this Act or the application of
such provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this Act shall not be affected by such
declaration.

Section 9. Effectivity - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a
newspaper of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
