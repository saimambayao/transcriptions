EXPLANATORY NOTE

Consistent with the policy of the State to promote the right to health of the people and
instill health consciousness among them, the delivery of essential health services at affordable
costs should be provided by the government and should remain unhampered at all times.
Likewise, the mandate of the State to adopt an integrated and comprehensive approach to health
development should be aligned with its responsibility to develop a healthy and alert citizenry.

Moreover, the Constitution emphasizes the responsibility of the State to enhance the
delivery of health services to the people. To further pursue these objectives, this Act seeks to
create a General Hospital in the Municipality of Maluso, Basilan.

The Municipality of Maluso is a BARMM component Local Government Unit within
the Province of Basilan which is classified as a 4" class municipality. It is located on the
western coast of Basilan and has a land area of 304.14 square kilometers, with twenty (20)
barangays and about 5,907 Households.

At present, the residents of Maluso can barely easily access essential health services
since the municipality only has one Rural Health Unit and fifteen (15) barangay health stations
manned by one MOA-doctor, twelve (12) nurses and fifteen (15) barangay health workers.
Thus, when the residents need essential hospital services, they have to travel to the neighboring
municipality of Sumisip.

Imagine the time it takes for these residents to travel to the hospital and their plight of
enduring long hours to avail of essential hospital services, which adds that much more to their

services denies these people the access to health care services that should be theirs by right,

It is for these reasons that the establishment of the Maluso General Hospital is

privileged Bangsamoro constituents, is vital to establishing a lasting peace, ensuring progress
and maintaining the progress and gains of the people and the government.

In view of the foregoing, the passage of this bill is earnestly sought.

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Parliament in session:

Section 1. There is hereby established a Level I general hospital with twenty-five (25)
bed capacity in the Municipality of Maluso, Province of Basilan, under the direct administrative
and technical supervision of the Basilan Integrated Provincial Health Office (IPHO).

Section 2. The BARMM Ministry of Health (MOH) shall formulate the necessary
guidelines for its operations as a level I hospital and ensure funding for the construction of the
hospital building and other complementary infrastructure, facilities, and medical equipment, as
well as the costs for the necessary maintenance and personnel services.

Section 3. The Chief of Hospital of Maluso General Hospital, in consultation and
coordination with the Basilan IPHO, shall submit to the MOH an annual development plan to
reflect the developmental goals of the hospital every year. The medical personnel, capital
outlays and budgetary needs for the operation of Maluso General Hospital shall be based on
the annual hospital development plan.

Any future increase in bed capacity and upgrading of health care services and the
facility shall be consistent with the annual hospital development plan.

Section 4. The provisions of this Act are hereby declared separable, and in the event
one or more such provisions or part thereof are declared unconstitutional, such declaration of
unconstitutionality shall not affect the validity of the other provisions thereof.
