EXPLANATORY NOTE

Tabuan-Lasa Municipality of Basilan Province is a coastal area outside
the Island of Basilan created through Muslim Mindanao Autonomy Act No.
187 and ratified on March 29, 2008. It has a total population of 29,327
according to Population Census 2020. It has an area which constituted
2.50% of Basilan’ s total area.

Since its creation, Tabuan-lasa has only few barangay health centers
to cater the health services of the whole municipality. Emergency cases
needs to transport the patient to nearest hospital by boat thus oftentimes
causes the loss of lives. Under this condition, the concomitant rights of the
Bangsamoro people vis-a-vis duties and responsibilities of the
Bangsamoro Government are stressed.

Article II, Section 15 of the Philippine Constitution provides that “ the
state shall protect and promote the right to health of the people and instill
health consciousness and in Section 11 of Article 12 states “ the state
shall adopt an integrated and comprehensive approach to health and
development which shall endeavor to make essential goods, health and
other social services available to the people at affordable costs” .

Section 22, Article IX of the Republic Act 11054 or otherwise known as
the Bangsamoro Organic Law (BOL) of the Bangsamoro Autonomous
Region in Muslim Mindanao states to provide for the comprehensive and
integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law
and general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people
and ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable
through the prompt intervention and excellent and affordable medical
services.

Hence, to give substance and spirits to the provision of the Philippine
Constitution and the Bangsamoro Organic Law , there is an urgent need
for the construction and establishment of Tabuan-Lasa level 1 General
Hospital, a measure that provides the Tabuan-Lasa constituents societal
rights and ensure their individual basic right to life.

On this premise, immediate passage of this bill is earnestly sought.

Section 1. Short Title. This act shall be known as the “ Establishing
Tabuan-Lasa Municipal Hospital Act of 2021” .

Section 2. Declaration of Principles and Policies. It is the policy of the
State to protect and promote the right to health of all Filipinos and instill
health consciousness among them as stipulated in the Republic Act 11223
The Universal Health Care Law. Towards this end, the State shall adopt:

(a) An integrated and comprehensive approach to ensure that all
Filipinos are health literate, provided with healthy living conditions, and
protected from hazards and risks that could affect their health;

(b) A health care model that provides all Filipinos access to a
comprehensive set of quality and cost-effective, promotive, preventive,
curative, rehabilitative and palliative health services without causing
financial hardship,, and prioritizes the needs of the population who cannot
afford such services;

(c) A framework that fosters a whole-of-system, whole-of-government,
and whole-of-society approach in the development, implementation,
monitoring, and evaluation of health policies, programs and plans; and

(d) A people-oriented approach for the delivery of health services that
is centered on people’ s needs and well-being, and cognizant of the

It is also the policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao to provide for the comprehensive and integrated health service
delivery for its constituents and establish by law and general hospital
system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the
individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt
intervention and excellent and affordable medical services of Section 22,
Article IX of the Republic Act 11054.

Section 3. General Objectives. This act shall seek out better health
system delivery and ensure that all Tabuan-Lasa constituents are
guaranteed equitable access to quality and affordable health care and
public health facilities. Health services that are devoted primarily to the
provision of services for health promotion, prevention, diagnosis,
treatment, rehabilitation and palliation of tabuan-lasa constituents
suffering from illness, disease, injury, disability, or deformity, or when
there is immediate danger and where delay in health support that may
cause loss of life.

Section 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program.
The Tabuan-Lasa Municipal Hospital shall integrate into its system of
operation all pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic Act No.
11223 otherwise known as the Universal Health Care Law.

Section 5. Organization and Human Resource System. The Ministry of
Health (MOH) with stakeholders, shall ensure the formulation and
implementation of a Municipal Health Human Resource Plan that will
provide policies and strategies for the appropriate generation, recruitment,
training, regulation, assessment of health workforce based on population
health needs. The MOH and if necessary with the Provincial Health
Boards shall exercise administrative and technical supervision over health
facilities and health human resources of the Tabuan Lasa Hospital which
shall include the creation of the Hospital Health Board, defining its power
and function and the appointment of the officers and members thereof.

Section 6. Appropriations for Construction and Administrative
Expenses. The Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, Office of the Chief
Minister together with the Minsitry of Finance, Budget and Managment
and the Ministry of Health shall appropriate for the funding of Hospital
Construction including the acquisition of medical and office equipment
and the budget needed for personal services, maintenance, operation and
other expenses for the impelementation of this Act.

Section 7. Safety and Quality. The MOH shall institute a licensing and
regulatory system for stand-alone health facilities, including those
providing ambulatory and primary care services, and other modes of
health services provisions. It shall set standard for health care provider
and health care facilities accessible for the people of Tabuan-Lasa. It shall
also coordinate with the Basilan Electric Company (BASELCO) for the
proper procedures and maintenance of its Electricity.

Section 8. Implementing Rules and regulation. The implementing
Rules and Regulations of this Act shall be formulgated within Sixty (60)
