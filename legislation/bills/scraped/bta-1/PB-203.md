Article |
PRELIMINARY PROVISIONS

Section 1. Short Title.— This act shall be known as the “Bangsamoro Science
High School Act of 2022.”

Section 2. Declaration of Policy. -— The Bangsamoro Government prioritizes
research and development, invention, innovation and their utilization; as well as
education, training and services in science and technology. It maintains and supports a
comprehensive, adequate and integrated high school science education system tailored
to the needs of learners in Bangsamoro.

Section 3. Objectives.— In implementing the foregoing policy, the Bangsamoro
Government, through the Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST) , is pursuing the
following objectives:

(a) Ensure that the Bangsamoro Science High School System is at par with the
standards of the existing Philippine Science High School System under the auspices
of the Department of Science and Technology (DOST); and

(b) Establish the Bangsamoro Science High School and integrate values
education as a way to strengthen the foundations of science and faith among
students in the region.

Section 4. Purpose.—The purpose of the Bangsamoro Science high School
shall be to offer on free scholarship to qualified Bangsamoro learners basis with the end
view of preparing its students for science career.

Section 5. Definition of Terms.— For purposes of this Act, the following terms
are hereby defined as follows:

(a) Bangsamoro Science High School (BSHS)— is a secondary school offering
scholarships to deserving Bangsamoro students who shall be admitted and trained

Article Il
SCOPE AND ADMINISTRATIVE AND ENFORCEMENT PROVISIONS

Section 6. Scope.— The Bangsamoro Science High School shall be established
in Parang Maguindanao for the Bangsamoro learners in the Bangsamoro Region.

Section 7. The Board of Trustees.— The Board of Trustees of the Bangsamoro
Science High School shall be composed of:

(a) The Minister of the Ministry of Science and Technology as ex-officio
chairperson;

(b) Minister of Ministry of Basic, Higher, and Technical Education as ex-
officio Vice-Chairperson;

(c) The president of Mindanao State University System;

(d) Campus Director of Bangsamoro Science High School;

(e) President of BSHS Alumni Association; and

(f) One representative from private sector

Section 8. Powers and Functions of the Board of Trustees.— The Board of
Trustees shall exercise the powers and privileges of a board of directors of a private
corporation. It shall also have the following powers and duties:

(a) to receive and allocate to the purposes provided by law such sums as
may be provided by law for the support of the Bangsamoro Science Secondary School;

(b) To approve appointments made and contracts entered into by the
director in connection with the employment of professors, instructors, lecturers, and
other employees of the school and to fix their compensation and other conditions of
employment and to remove them for cause after an investigation and hearing shall have
been had:

(c) To provide fellowship for faculty members and approve the grant of
scholarship, stipends and such other allowances to deserving students;

(d) To approve the issuance of certificates of diplomas to successful
candidates; ;

(e) To provide its own rules of procedure, and to prescribe for the
administration of the High School such general rules and regulations, not contrary to
law, as are consistent with the purpose of the High School, as defined in section 4 of
this Act;

(f) To receive in trust legacies, gifts, and donations of real and personal
property of all kinds and to administer the same in accordance with the directions and
instructions of the donor, and in the absence thereof, in such manner as the Board of
Trustees may in its discretion determine for the benefit of the High School; and

(g) To establish criteria for the selection and admission of students,
including competitive examinations

Section 9. Quorum. A quorum of the Board of Trustees shall consist of majority
of all the members holding office at the time of the Board is called. Any proceedings
against the Board of Directors shall be served on the Director or the Secretary of the
Board of Directors.

Section 10. The Campus Director.— The BSHS shall be headed by a Campus
Director who shall be appointed by the Board of Trustees through the merit and fitness
system. The Campus Director shall be the administrative and academic head of his or
her campus and is responsible for the campus’ operations.

The Campus Director shall not be removed from office except for a just cause.

Section 11. The Executive Committee.— The BSHS shall have an Executive
Committee consisting of the Campus Director, the heads of department, and such other
ranking faculty members or officials of the BSHS as may be determined by the Campus
Director.

It shall have the following powers and functions:

a. to screen and select the students for admission to the BSHS:;

b. to select the recipients of the scholarship, stipends and other allowances;

c. and to develop the curricula, course of study, and rules of discipline subject to
the approval of the Board of Trustees.

The Executive Committee shall implement and execute the policies established
by the Board of Trustees and shall exercise such other functions, powers and duties as
the Board may prescribe.

Section 12. Registrar.— There shall be a Registrar of the Bangsamoro Science
High School who shall also act as the Secretary of the Board. He or She shall be
appointed by the Director, subject to the approval of the Board of Trustees. He or she
shall Keep such records as may be prescribed by the Board.

Section 13. Treasurer.— There shall be a treasurer of the Bangsamoro Science
High School who shall be appointed by the Director and subject to the approval of the
Board of Trustees. All accounts and expenses shall be audited by the Auditor General or
his duly authorized representative.

Section 14. Exemption. Professors, instructors and other faculty members of
the BSHS are exempt from any civil service examination or regulation required for
appointment. They shall be selected in accordance with such criteria as may be
established by the Board of Trustees which shall include, if necessary and practicable,
screening and competitive examination.

Said professors, instructors and faculty members as well as the school’s
administrative staff shall be exempt from the provisions of the law, rules and regulations
on wage and position classification.

Section 15. Legacies, Gifts and Conditions. All legacies, gifts, and conditions
for the benefit of the Bangsamoro Science High School, or for its support and
maintenance, or for aid to any of its students, shall be tax-exempt and shall enjoy
allowable deduction from the gross income of the donors. For the purpose of the income
tax, the Bangsamoro Science High School is exempt from paying all taxes, fees,
assessments and other charges of the Government, its branches and subdivisions.

Section 16. Incomes and Donations. All revenues and donations to the
Bangsamoro Science High School created under this Act shall constitute a special fund
to be known as the Bangsamoro Science High School Fund. This Fund shall be
expended by the Board of Trustees exclusively for the purpose of this Act.

ARTICLE Ill
FINAL PROVISIONS

Section 18. Separability Clause.— If any provisions of this Act or the applica-
tion of such provision to any person or circumstance is held invalid, the remainder of
this Act or application of such provision of other persons or circumstances shall not be
affected thereby.

Section 19. Repealing Clause.— All other regional acts, decrees, instructions,
rules and regulations or parts thereof inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are
hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

Section 20. Effectivity.— This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its pub-

lication in a news paper of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
