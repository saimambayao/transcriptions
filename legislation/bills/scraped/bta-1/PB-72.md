SECTION 1. TITLE. This act shall be known and cited as an Act Extending Assistance
Service to the Badjao and other Bangsamoro Poorest Tribes by Implementing
Mechanism of Action, Proving Funds therefore and other Purposes and henceforth,
may referred to short as Badjao Act of 2020.

SECTION 2. DEFINITION OF TERMS. — When used in this act.

a. Badjao (variant Badjao, Bajau, and Badjiao) — are those referred as Badjao
People whether presently, originally or formally living in the seas, seashore,
and/or in land of Basilan, Tawi-Tawi and Sulu Archipelago, side by side with
Tausog, Yakan and Samal and who by historic right, lineage, Tetulag and
ancestry are subjects of the Sultanate of Sulu prior to Spanish occupation are
native tribe of this homeland and under our constitution are citizen of the

Philippines.

b. BDAC — Shall mean the Badjao and other Bangsamoro Poorest Tribe
Development Affairs Council.

programs as well as policies and strategies which are to be implemented by

L.

Resettlement Areas. The BDAC in coordination with the appropriate national
agencies and Local Government Units concerned shall designate a Special
Resettlement Areas for the Badjao and the like to serve as their home
communities upon implementation of the Balik Probinsya Program. It shall

. Balik Probinsya Program. To ensure that all Badjao and the like are included

in this program the BDAC shall takes steps and adopt a Balik Probinsya
Program for the concerned mass sending and returning back to their
communities in the provinces of Basilan, Sulu and Tawi-Tawi particularly to
designated Resettlement areas therein.

Inclusion in the 4P’s and other Applicable Programs. The BDAC shall
prepared the necessary complete data and list of these people covered
under this act and immediately submit the same to appropriate agencies for
inclusion in the Programang Pantawid ng Pamilyang Pilipino and other
National Beneficial Program;

. The BDAC shall work for the establishment by the Development of Education

of a free and complete (elementary, high school and college) public
education in the resettlement areas to ensures and enhance their
patriotism, literacy and competence in all aspects of life to become a fruitful
individual for nation building. In the furtherance of this objective, the TESDA
and other manpower skills Training Institute shall likewise be tapped to
introduce relevant training for development of their skills especially on
fishing, diving, boat making and marine activities as source of living.

The BDAC shall jointly work with the Department of Health the
establishment of infirmaries or health centers in the resettlement areas to
ensure that their health and physical conditions are properly addressed and
in so doing, a periodic medical outreach program shall be conducted
regularly;

V. PRIORITY RULE OF ACTION

The Badjao and other Bangsamoro Poorest Tribes shall enjoy priority and be
given preference over BARMM Government Employment opportunities and
BCOBAR Assistance Services and NGO’s charity Funded Projects as well as
Micro Finance institution Grants. The Bangsamoro Government shall grant
Tax credit for any institutions that offered financial or in kind assistance and
grants to BAdjao and the like as result of their partnership with the BDAC
related activities.

VI. OTHER ACTION
The BDAC may adopt and implement other programs and projects which are
beneficial to the Badjao and other Bangsamoro Poorest Tribes.

Section 6. Funds. The Bangsamoro Government shall appropriate funds under its
Regular Budget necessary to carry out this act.

Section 7. The BDAC shall render Annual report to the Parliament containing its
accomplishments, expenditures, and recommendations which requires legislative
measures and enactments.

Section 8. Repealing Clause. — All laws, executive orders, rules and regulations
which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed or
modified accordingly.

Section 9 Separability Clause. — If for any reason any section or prevision of this act
is declared invalid or unconstitutional the reminder hereof shall remain valid and
enforceable.
