“AN ACT ESTABLISHING AND CONSTRUCTING A LEVEL — I MUNICIPAL
HOSPITAL WITH FIFTY (50) BED CAPACITY IN DATU PIANG, PROVINCE OF
MAGUINDANAO TO BE KNOWN AS DATU PIANG MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL
APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR ITS FACILITIES AND PROFESSIONAL HEALTH
CARE, MEDICAL PERSONNEL AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES”

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, Bangsamoro
Transition Authority (BTA) Interim Parliament.

SECTION 1. Short Title. — This Act shall be known as the “THE DATU PIANG MUNICIPAL
HOSPITAL ESTABLISHMENT ACT OF 2021”.

SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy. — \t is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise
known as the “Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to
provide for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and
establish by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure
that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of
excellence and affordable medical services.

SETION 3. The Establishment of Datu Piang Municipal Hospital. — To implement the above-
mentioned policy, a hospital facility is hereby established to be known as the Datu Piang
Municipal Hospital (DPMH) to be located in the Municipality of Datu Piang, Maguindanao,
Province of Maguindanao, BARMM. The DPMH shall be constructed and start its operations

under capacity and capability as prescribed by the licensing standards of the Bureau of Health
Facilities and Services under the Department of Health (DOH). The standard personnel, medical
and office equipment and other facilities required shall likewise be provided to the hospital. The
DPMH may be upgraded thereafter into a higher facility with a large capacity upon the evaluation
and recommendation by the Ministry of Health.

SECTION 4. Catchment area/coverage. — The Datu Piang Municipal Hospital shall serve
primarily, but not limited to, the patients coming from the municipality of Datu Piang and may
extend its services as an augmentation and support to other existing medical facilities when such
need arises to patients coming from the adjacent municipalities of Maguindanao province.

SECTION 5. Objective/s — To provide a highly accessible, affordable, comprehensive total health
care and hospital services to the BARMM constituents cited under Section 4 above and integrating
in its system of operations all pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic Act No. 11223
otherwise known as the Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

SECTION 6. Administration, Management and Organization. — The Datu Piang Municipal
Hospital shall be under the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who is hereby
authorized to determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the management
and operations of the facility. :

SECTION 7. Appropriation. — The amount necessary to carry out the provision of this Act shall
be included in the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act. Thereafter, funds necessary for the
maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be provided in accordance with the provisions of
the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act and other pertinent laws.

SECTION 8. Implementing Rules and Regulations. - The BARMM-MOH shall promulgate the
Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of this Act within sixty (60) days after the enactment
of this act.

SECTION 9. Separability Clause. — In the event any provision of this act or the application of
such provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this act shall not be affected by such
declaration.

SECTION 10. Repealing Clause. — All laws and regulations under the Bangsamoro Autonomous
Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) government including those passed under the defunct
Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) government regarding the construction,
establishment and operations of the Datu Piang Municipal Hospital not consistent with this Act
are hereby repealed.
