Be it enacted by the Parliament of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority assembled:
ESTABLISHMENT AND ORGANIZATION

section 1. Short title. - This Act shall be known as "The Bangsamoro
Budgeting Act."

Section 2. Bangsamoro Budget Office. - There is hereby created a
Bangsamoro Budget Office under the control and supervision of the
Ministry of Finance, and Budget and Management (MFBM).

section 3. Officers and employees of the Bangsamoro Budget Office. — The
Bangsamoro Budget Office shall be under the direction of a Director
Ill who shall be appointed by the Chief Minister upon the
recommendation of the Minister, and whose qualifications shall be
in conformity with the prescribed Civil Service standards therefor.

To carry into effect the provisions of this Act, there shall also be in
said office such other officers and employees as may be appointed
by the Minister of Finance, and Budget and Management, subject
to existing laws and rules on the power to appoint.

Section 4. Mandate. - The BBO under the MFBM is primarily mandated to
promote the sound, efficient and effective management and
utilization of all resources of the Bangsamoro Government as
instrument in the achievement of its socioeconomic and political
development goals aligned with the national development goals

Powers and Functions of the Office. -The powers and functions of
the Bangsamoro Budget Office are as follows:

Formulate the annual regional budget in a way that ensures the
appropriate prioritization and allocation of funds to support the
annual program of the Bangsamoro government.

Define the form, content, and manner of preparation of the
Bangsamoro budget, consistent with existing laws, rules and
regulations of the National Government;

Formulate the overall resource application strategy to match the
government’s macro-economic policy;

Prepare the medium-term expenditure pian, indicating the
programming, prioritization, and financing of capital investment
and current operating expenditure requirements of medium-term
sectoral development plans;

Undertake the formulation of the annual Bangsamoro budget in a
way that ensures the appropriate prioritization and allocation of
funds to support the annual program of government;

Develop and administer an accounting system essential to fiscal
management and control;

Conduct a continuing study and assessment of the bureaucracy as
well as make policy recommendations on its role, size, composition,
structure and functions to establish a government bureaucracy
imbued with a spirit of public service;

Establish the rules and procedures for the management of
government organization resources i.e., physical, manpower and
other resources, formulates standards of organizational program
performance; and undertakes or provides services in work
simplification or streamlining of systems and procedures to improve
efficiency and effectiveness in government operations;

Conceptualize and administer the government's compensation and
position classification plan;

Monitor and assess the physical as well as the financial operations
of existing and to be created Government-Owned
and/or Controlled Corporations within the Bangsamoro region.
