Section 1. Short Title: This Law shall be known as an Act Creating Tipo-Tipo Hospital
in Tipo-Tipo Municipality, Basilan Province of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim

Mindanao.

Section 2. Mandate: There shall be established a hospital and named as Tipo-Tipo
Hospital in Tipo-Tipo Municipality, Basilan Province. Its mandate is to serve and cater into the

medical needs of the Tipo-Tipo

unicipality Constituents and other neighbouring towns by
rendering access to health care goods and services, responsive and appropriate measures and

actions for the health situation and| status of the patients who may come to it.

Section 3. Vision and Mission: As a medical institution, the Tipo-Tipo Hospital shall
endeavour to perform and deliver quality and holistic health services and take actions calling for

the real-time attention about health or medical services.

Its mission consists of affordable health services vis-a-vis health concerns, illness and
sickness that the residents of the Municipality may suffer and bring to their asylum for

consultation, medical advice or appropriate medical actions to be taken thereon.

Section 4. Objective: The establishment of the Tipo-Tipo Hospital shall aim at the

following objectives:

A. To improve delivery of|health services to the Tipo-Tipo Municipality constituents;

B. To develop a Health Responsive System among the personnel of the Tipo-Tipo

Hospital on par with its counterpart with other regions in the country;

C. To serve patients who may be financially incapable of bringing their patients to some
medical centre and hospital due to financial status or unavailability of the same in

those operating hospitals due to full accommodation in their centres or hospitals.

Section 5. Nature and Services: The Tipo-Tipo Hospital shall have fifteen (15) bed
capacityas an infirmary for its initial operation under the direct administrative and technical
supervision of the Ministry of Health, Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
(MOH-BARMM).

Section 6. Its operation: The MOH-BARMM shall formulate the necessary guidelines for
its operation as a newly established Municipal Hospital. Its personnel, both medical and
administrative, shall be appointed by the MOH-BARMM Minister or the BARMM CHIEF
MINISTER.

Section 7. Funds: The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be
included in the Budget Proposal of the MOH-BARMM Regional Office for its integration in the
General Appropriations Act of T year following its enactment into law and thereafter. The

funds shall include the salaries of its personnel, the capital outlay, and maintenance and other

operating expenses.

Section 8. This Law shall take effect fifteen days after its publication in newspapers of

general circulation.
