BE LOCATED AT THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUGAYA, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR, BARMM AND

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, Bangsamoro Transition
Authority (BTA) Interim Parliament

SECTION 1. Short Title. — This Act shall be known as the “THE LANAO DEL SUR PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL
ESTABLISHMENT ACT OF 2021”.

SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy. - It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known as the
“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to provide for a
comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law a general
hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the individual basic right
to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services.

SECTION 3. The Lanao del Sur Provincial Hospital (LSPH). — To implement the above-mentioned policy,
a hospital facility is hereby established to be known as the Lanao del Sur Provincial Hospital (LSPH) to
be located in the Municipality of Tugaya, province of Lanao Del Sur, BARMM. The LSPH shall be
constructed, initially, with at least 25 bed capacity and shall start its operations under such capacity and
capability as prescribed by the licensing standards of the Bureau of Health Facilities and Services under
the Department of Health (DOH). The standard personnel, medical and office equipment and other
facilities required shall likewise be provided to the hospital. The LSPH may be upgraded thereafter into a
higher facility with a larger capacity upon the evaluation and recommendation of the Ministry of Health.

SECTION 4. Catchment area/coverage. — The LSPH shall serve primarily, but not limited to, the
patients coming from the various parts of the province of Lanao Del Sur particulariy those who do not
have immediate and ready access to the existing related medical facilities in the province.

SECTION 5. Objective/s. — To provide a highly accessible, affordable, comprehensive total health care
and hospital services to the BARMM constituents cited under Section 4 above and integrating in its
system of operations all pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic Act No. 11223 otherwise
known as the Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

SECTION 6. Administration, Management and Organization. - The Lanao del Sur Provincial Hospital
shall be under the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who is hereby authorized to
determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the management and operations
of the facility.

SECTION 7. Appropriation. — The amount of Php 75 million pesos is hereby appropriated for the
procurement of equipment and initial operations of the Lanao dei Sur Provinciai Hospital to include
Pre-operating expenses, Personnel Services (PS) and Maintenance and Other Operating expenses
(MOOE) as augmentation to the available budget and appropriation of the Ministry of Health for the
construction of the LSPH facility structures. The BARMM- Ministry of Health shall incorporate in its
succeeding annual appropriations the amount required for the continued operations of the hospital
and its uograding ast may deem necessary.

SECTION 8. Separability Clause. - \n the event any provision of this Act or the application of such
provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this Act shail not be affected by such declaration.
