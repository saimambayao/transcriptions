AN ACT UPGRADING THE EXISTING DATU BLAH SINSUAT DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN MAGUINDANAO
FROM ITS PRESENT LEVEL I WITH A 10 BED CAPACITY INTO LEVEL I] WITH AN AUTHORIZED 50 BED
CAPACITY AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, Bangsamoro Transition
Authority (BTA) Interim Parliament

SECTION 1. Short Title. — This Act shall be known as the “DATU BLAH SINSUAT DISTICT HOSPITAL
UPGRADING ACT OF 2020”.

SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy. - It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054
otherwise known as the “Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao” to provide for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its
constituents and establish by law a general hospital system to serve the health
requirements of its people and ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be
attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services.

SECTION 3. Upgrading of the Datu Blah Sinsuat District Hospital. - The existing Datu Blah Sinsuat
Level | District Hospital is hereby upgraded from its present level with a Ten (10) bed capacity into a
Level Il hospital with a Fifty (50) bed authorized capacity in accordance with the national standards
set by the Bureau of Health Facilities and Services under the Department of Health (DOH). The
existing buildings including landscaping, medical and office equipment and other facilities, its
operating funds and personnel are also hereby upgraded to include the needed construction and
procurement thereof in order to meet the prescribed standards to the upgraded level.

SECTION 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program. - The upgrading of the Datu
Blah Sinsuat District Hospital shall integrate into its system of operations all pertinent and relevant
provisions under Republic Act No. 11223 otherwise known as the Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

SECTION 5. Administration, Management and Organization. - The Datu Blah Sinsuat Hospital shall
be under the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who is hereby authorized to

determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the management and
operations of the facility, and if necessary, shall include the creation of the Hospital Board of

SECTION 6. Appropriations. — The amount of Two hundred seventy eight million pesos (Php 278
Million) is hereby appropriated from the BARMM General Appropriations for the upgrading of the
Datu Blah Sinsuat Hospital including the construction of infrastructures, acquisition of medical and
office equipment and the annual budget needed for personnel services, maintenance, operations
and other expenses for the implementation of this Act, and, thereafter such amount as may be
needed for the continued operations of the hospital.

SEC. 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations. - The BARMM- MOH shall promulgate the
implementing rules and regulations of this Act within sixty (60) days after the enactment of this Act.

SEC. 8. Separability Clause. - In the event any provision of this Act or the application of such
provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this Act shall not be affected by such declaration.

SEC. 9. Repealing Clause. — All laws and regulations under the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) government including those passed under the defunct Autonomous
Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) government regarding the operations and upgrading of the
Datu Blah Sinsuat Hospital not consistent with this Act are hereby repealed.

SEC. 10. Effectivity. — This Act shail take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in at least two (2)
national newspapers of general circulation.
