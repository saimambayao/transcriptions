EXPLANATORY NOTE

Buluan District Hospital is one of the Eight (8) Government Hospitals in the
Province of Maguindanao. Health services and facilities development in the
Autonomous Region are not devolved to the Provincial and Municipal Local
Government Units making Buluan District Hospital an integral part of the Integrated
Provincial Hospital Office (IPHO) in Maguindanao, which in turn is under the
supervision of the Ministry of Health-BARMM. Buluan District Hospital belongs to
Cluster | of IPHO-Maguindanao and provides services to the people of the
municipalities of Buluan, Datu Paglas, Pandag, Mangudadatu, and General Salipada
K. Pendatun in Maguindanao and neighboring towns of North Cotabato and Sultan
Kudarat Province.

The facility begun operation as a 25-bed capacity Emergency Hospital in 1975
by virtue of Presidential Decree 503, issued by the late President Ferdinand E.
Marcos. It was again elevated to a District Hospital in 1982 by virtue of Executive Order
no. 851.

THEREFOR

Section 1. The classification of Buluan District Hospital in Buluan, Maguindanao is hereby
converted into Level II District Hospital and its bed capacity is hereby increased from seventy
five (75) bed to two hundred (200) bed capacity under the supervision and control of the
Ministry of Health (MOH), Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

Section 2. The existing hospital facilities and professional health care services of the Buluan
District Hospital are hereby upgraded and increased to conform with and be commensurate to the
increase in bed capacity and classification level. The number of medical personnel shall be
correspondingly increased.

Section 3. The increase in the bed capacity of Buluan District Hospital shall be implemented
incrementally for a period of not more than two (2) years from the approval of this Act.

Section 4. The construction and upgrading of the hospital facilities and services shall be under
the direct supervision and control of the Ministry of Health, Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao.

Section 5. The Ministry of Health shall immediately include in the Ministry's 2021 programs the
initial implementation of this Act, the funding of which may be charged) to’ available
