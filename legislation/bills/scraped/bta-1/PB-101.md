EXPLANATORY NOTE

The Bangsamoro government commits to adopt a policy on health that provides for a
comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and to establish by law a
general hospital system to serve the requirements of its people. This aims to ensure that the
individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and
affordable medical services.

This Bill seeks to upgrade Buluan District Hospital in province of Maguindanao from its
present 25 Bed Capacity Level 1 hospital to 100 Bed Capacity level 2 hospital. This hospital is the
only government-run hospital in southernmost part of Maguindanao. It caters to six municipalities
~ Buluan, Datu Paglas, Paglat, General Salipada K. Pendatun, Pandag and Mangudadatu — that are
home to no fewer than 350,000 residents.

Buluan District Hospital’s patients has been increasing and overcrowding, that is why it was
compelled to increase its bed capacity from 25 beds to now 69 implementing beds. However, despite
said increase, the hospital’s Bed Occupancy Rate (BOR) still gone up to 500% higher as shown in
the MOH-BARMM Annual Hospital Statistical Report in 2019. With this fivefold rise in its
occupancy rate, the hospital hardly sustains the increasing demand of its patients especially in terms
of hospital facilities and professionals.

Thus, it is imperative for this government to approve the immediate upgrade of Buluan
District Hospital from its present levell with 25 bed capacity to Level 2 with 100 bed capacity in
order to cater the necessary healthcare needs of the hospital’s increasing patients.

Hence, passage of this Bill is earnestly sought.

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao Bangsamoro Transition
Authority (BTA) Interim Parliament.

SECTION 1. Short Title. -- This Act shall be known as the “BULUAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL
UPGRADE ACT OF 2021”.

SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy. -- It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article IX of the Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known
as the “Organic Law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to provide for
a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law a
general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the individual
basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable
medical services.

SEC. 3. Upgrading of the Buluan District Hospital. - The existing Buluan District Hospital is
hereby upgraded from its present level 1 with 25 Authorized Bed Capacity into Level 2 with 100
Authorized Bed Capacity in accordance with the national standards set by the Bureau of Health
Facilities and Services under the Department of Health (DOH). The existing building including its
landscaping, medical and office equipment and other facilities, its operating funds and personnel
are also hereby upgraded to include the needed construction and procurement thereof in order to
meet the prescribed standards to the upgraded level.

SEC 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program. -- The upgrading of the Buluan
District Hospital shall integrate into its system of operations all pertinent and relevant provisions
under Republic Act No. 11223 otherwise known as the Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

SEC 5. Administration, Management, and Organization. — The Buluan District Hospital shall be
under the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who is hereby authorized to
determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the management and operations
of the hospital.

SEC. 6. Appropriations — The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be
included in the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act. Thereafter, funds necessary for the continued
operation and maintenance of the hospital shall be provided in accordance with the provisions of
the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act and other pertinent laws.

SEC. 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations - The BARMM-MOH shall promulgate the
Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of this Act within sixty (60) days after the enactment
of this Act.

SEC. 8. Separability Clause. -- In the event any provision of this Act or the application of such
provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this Act shall not be affected by such declaration.

SEC. 9. Repealing Clause. -- All laws and regulations under the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region
in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) government including those passed under the defunct
Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) government regarding the operations of the
Buluan District Hospital not consistent with this Act are hereby repealed.

SEC. 10. Effectivity - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper
of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
