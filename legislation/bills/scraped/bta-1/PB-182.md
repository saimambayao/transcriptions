AN ACT TO DECLARE THE BURIAL SITE OF THE LATE SHEIKH SALAMAT
HASHIM, MILF FOUNDING CHAIRMAN, AND THE LATE ALIM ABDUL AZIZ
MIMBANTAS, MILF VICE CHAIRMAN & VICE CHAIRMAN FOR MILITARY AFFAIRS,
AS “HASHIM & MIMBANTAS (H & M) HISTORICAL SHRINE”, TO PROVIDE FOR
THE PRESERVATION OF THE HISTORICAL SHRINE AND BURIAL SITE, AND FOR
OTHER PURPOSES

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known as the "Organic Law for the
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao" empowered the Bangsamoro
Government to exercise authority over libraries and museums, and historical, cultural
and archaeological sites in the Bangsamoro Region’;

WHEREAS, Section 24 of Article IX of RA 11054 mandated the creation of Bangsamoro
Commission for the Preservation of Cultural Heritage to preserve the history, culture,
arts, traditions, and the rich cultural heritage of the Bangsamoro people;

WHEREAS, Section 25 of Article IX of RA 11054 empowered the Bangsamoro
government to declare historical shrines and cultural sites to preserve the Bangsamoro
heritage for posterity;

WHEREAS, the late Sheikh Salamat Hashim, MILF Founding Chairman, and the late
Alim Abdul Aziz Mimbantas, MILF Vice Chairman and Vice Chairman for Military Affairs,
are among the Bangsamoro leaders, who, by virtue of their ideals, principles, works and
sacrifices, are worthy of commemoration;

WHEREAS, Sheikh Salamat Hashim and Alim Abdul Aziz Mimbantas were both buried
in Camp Bushra Somiorong in the Municipality of Butig, Lanao del Sur, BARMM,
Philippines, which is one of the major camps recognized by the GPH-MILF Peace
Panel;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED BY THE
Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, in session assembled:

Section 1. The burial site of Sheikh Salamat Hashim and Alim Abdul Aziz Mimbantas in
Camp Bushra Somiorong in the Municipality of Butig, Lanao del Sur, BARMM,
Philippines is hereby declared a Bangsamoro Historical Shrine.

Section 2. The historical shrine shall be preserved and/or reconstructed whenever
necessary as much as possible in their original form.

Section 3. The Bangsamoro Commission on the Preservation of Cultural Heritage shall
exercise supervision and control over the reconstruction and/or preservation of the
aforesaid historical shrine and shall issue rules and regulations to effectuate the
preceding sections of this Act.

Section 4. Necessary funds for the purposes of this Act shall be provided for in the
annual appropriations for public works and disbursements shall be made by the
Bangsamoro Commission on the Preservation of Cultural Heritage.

Section 5. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
