WHEREAS, there is a need to promote the development of the
fishing industry and improve efficiency in handling and distribution of fish
and fishery products through the establishment and operation of fish ports,

fish markets and other post harvest facilities;

WHEREAS, it is the objective of the Bangsamoro Government to
help improve the income of small fishermen, fish farmers and fish workers
through the provision of services and facilities which add value to their

product;

WHEREAS, as new fishery infrastructures and related industries are
established additional work opportunities is also open, made available thus

a source of additional revenue.

Section 1. There is hereby established a Quasi Government Agency called

“The Bangsamoro Fisheries Development Authority (BAFDA)”.

Section 2. MANDATES. The Bangsamoro Fisheries Development
Authority (BAFDA), is an attached agency of the Ministry of Agriculture
and Fisheries (MAFAR) mandated to provide adequate and essential post

harvest facilities for the fishery sector.

Section 3. POWERS AND FUNCTIONS. The BAFDA shall have the

following powers and functions.

a. Provide essential fisheries and related post harvest services that
would improve the quality of fish products that could compete in

the global market;

b. Management, operation and development of regional commercial

fish ports complexes;

c. Establishment of ice plants and cold storage, small scale fish

processing centers, Municipal fish ports, warehouse, factory

Section 4. The BAFDA shall be managed by a Board, composed of one (1)
chairman and six (6) members. The Minister of MAFAR will be the ex-

officio chair of the board.

Section 5. The Board will submit a mean and lean organizational structure
to be headed by an Executive Director with a salary grade of 26. The

Executive Director will serve as the Head of the Secretariat.

Section 6. The Ministry of Finance Budget and Management shall allocate
