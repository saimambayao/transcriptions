REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES
BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO
BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY
Cotabato City

Cabinet Bill No. .

Introduced by the Cabinet

AN ACT ADOPTING AN OFFICIAL BANGSAMORO EMBLEM OF THE
BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO.

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in session assembled:

SECTION 1. Adoption of the Official Bangsamoro Emblem. — For the
authentication in the exercise of its acts and other purposes, there is hereby adopted an
Official Bangsamoro Emblem of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao, hereinafter referred to as the Official Bangsamoro Seal.

SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy. — It is the declared policy of the Bangsamoro
Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to establish itself as a political entity that
recognizes the justness and legitimacy of the cause of the Bangsamoro people. To this
end, it shall adopt an Official Bangsamoro Seal that is reflective of its peoples struggles
and aspirations.

SECTION 3. Design, Symbolism, and Interpretation. The Official Bangsamoro
~ Seal shall be in circular design with the words “Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao” clearly printed at the outer portion and the term “Official Seal” printed
at the bottom between the words “Bangsamoro” and “Mindanao”

The outermost part of the Official Bangsamoro Seal is bordered by a rope while in its
center is a Crescent with a seven-rayed Siar to its right.

The Rope shall be golden yellow (HEX CODE: #FFD700) in color. The Crescent and
seven-rayed Star shall likewise be in golden yellow (HEX CODE: #FFD700). The
background of the Crescent and Star shall be in green (HEX CODE: #009966). The
words inscribed therein shall be written in green (HEX CODE: #009966) with white
(HEX CODE: #FFFFFF) as its background . All symbols within the Official Bangsamoro
Seal shall be interpreted as follows:

a. The Rope symbolizes unity as inspired by verse 3:103 of the Noble
Qur'an which says, “Hold tight to the rope of Allah, together. And be
not divided.”

b. The Crescent symbolizes the principles that guided the Bangsamoro
who struggled for self-determination

c. The seven-rayed Star represents the five (5) provinces of
Maguindanao, Lanao del Sur, Basilan, Sulu, Tawi-Tawi, the city of
Cotabato, and geographic areas that compose the Bangsamoro
territory.

An alternative version of the logo reflecting the Arabic translation of the texts shall
likewise be considered as official seal.

SECTION 4. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in a regional newspaper of general circulation in the Bangsamoro.

Approved,
