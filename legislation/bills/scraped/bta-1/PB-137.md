EXPLANATORY NOTE

The Municipality of Muhtamad was created out from the 10
barangays of Lantawan outside Basilan Island, in surrounding islets of
West Basilan. through Muslim Mindanao Autonomy Act No. 200 and
subsequently ratified on August 25, 2007 in a plebiscite. According to
Population Census 2020, it has a number of 3,921 household with total
population of 26,86.

The Haji Muhtamad Rural Health Unit is the only one facility that
cater to the basic health services of the whole municipality. In case of
emergency and other health services, it needs to transport the patient
to the nearest hospital) or health center in neighboring municipality
through sea transport. The difficulty in accessing the health facility in
the Municipality of Hji Muhtamad is not only troublesome but causes
anxiety to the famiiy and deprived them of the constitutionai right to
life which is inclusive in the right to health. And, the current principle
that has been recently adopted to 2030 Agenda for Sustainable
