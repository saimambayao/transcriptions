Explanatory Note

Among the many functions of the government is to provide and manage quality
infrastructure facilities and services that are responsive to the needs of the people.
Infrastructure is crucially important to foster the region's economic development and
prosperity. With this, it is necessary that government offices are readily accessible in

areas that need them most.

This Act seeks to establish a New Geographic Areas District Engineering Office
(DEO) in Cotabato City, covering all its thirty-seven (37) barangays and the 63
barangays in Cotabato province. Currently, there is no DEO in the City. The former
DEO had been abolished following the official inclusion of Cotabato City in the
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM). The residents of
Cotabato City and the 63 barangays of Cotabato province voted for their inclusion in the
BARMM through a plebiscite last February 5-6, 2019. Nearly two years later, Cotabato
City and the 63 barangays of Cotabato province have officially become a part of the

BARMM.

With the passage of this Bill, the New Geographic Areas DEO will be able to
provide quality public infrastructure and maintenance services in its area of jurisdiction.
It is expected to bring important benefits to the lives of those residing and those who are
doing business in Cotabato City and the 63 barangays of Cotabato province.
Improvements in the quality of infrastructure services will also help cut the cost of doing
business and will result in positive economic value that can stimulate job creation,
encourage private investments, improve productivity and ensure the well-being of the
Bangsamoro people.

In view of the foregoing, the passage of this bill is earnestly sought.

AN ACT CREATING THE NEW GEOGRAPHIC AREAS DISTRICT ENGINEERING
OFFICE, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament assembled:

SECTION 1. CREATION.- There shall be created under the supervision of the
Ministry of Public Works (MPW), a district engineering office for the City of Cotabato
and 63 barangays of Cotabato province to be known as New Geographic Areas District
Engineering Office (NGADEO).

SECTION 2. DECLARATION OF POLICY — The Bangsamoro Government shall
maintain an engineering and construction arm and continuously develop its technology,
for the purpose of ensuring the safety of all infrastructure facilities and securing for all
public works the highest efficiency and the most appropriate quality in construction.

SECTION 3. LOCATION AND COVERAGE- The District Engineering Office
created under this Act shall be located in the City of Cotabato, Province of
Maguindanao, covering thirty-seven (37) barangays and sixty-three (63) barangays in
Cotabato province. It shall assume jurisdiction over all infrastructure and public works in
these areas.

SECTION 4. FUNCTIONS -— The District Engineering Office shall be responsible
for local roads, flood control, water resources development system, and other public
works in the City of Cotabato and 63 barangays in Cotabato province and shall exercise
the following functions:

1) Implement policies, guidelines and procedures for the effective
implementation of construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects;

2) Inspect, verify, monitor, supervise and evaluate various infrastructure projects
in accordance with existing standards and guidelines;

3) Implement various infrastructure projects in their respective areas of
jurisdiction;

4) Conduct, inspect and assess the condition of roads, bridges, and other
infrastructure projects and submit a report thereon to the MPW;

5) Prepare, review and evaluate plans, programs of work, and estimate including
reports and submit a report thereon to the MPW;

6) Prepare, evaluate, and endorse requests for funding to the MPW for the
repair/rehabilitation of damaged infrastructure facilities brought about by
calamities;

7) Undertake the management, repair, maintenance and rehabilitation of
construction and maintenance property, plant and equipment;

8) Undertake regular monitoring of the progress of programs and _ project
implementation including implementation problems and issues for timely
resolutions; and

9) Perform such other duties and responsibilities as may be assigned by the
Director-General and/or the Minister of the MPW.

SECTION 5. DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE AND COMPOSITION — The
District Engineering Office shall be established at the City of Cotabato. It shall be
headed by a District Engineer who shall be responsible for the efficient and effective
discharge of the duties and functions of the District Office. Within his/her defined powers,
s/he shall exercise functional and administrative control and supervision over district
operations. S/he shall also perform such other related duties and responsibilities as may
be assigned or delegated by the Minister or as may be required by law. S/he shall be
appointed by the Chief Minister of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao.

SECTION 6. ORGANIZATION AND STAFFING — Pending the approval of the
plantilla and appointments of personnel, the City District Engineer shall propose
organizational structure and staffing of the office. New Geographic Areas District
Engineering Office shall be manned by former officials and employees of Cotabato City
District Engineering Office with employees from MPW Regional Office and other DEOs
in the BARMM as may be assigned by the Minister of the MPW, in order to effect a
gradual transition.

SECTION 7. APPROPRIATIONS. - The regular budget of the newly created New
Geographic Areas District Engineering Office shall be included in the total budget of
MPW and which shall be allotted to the district office immediately upon the release of
funds for the purpose.

SECTION 8. SEPARABILITY. — If any provisions of this Act or the application of
such provision to any person or circumstances be declared unconstitutional or contrary
to the provisions of RA 11054 or the Bangsamoro Organic Law, the remainder of this
Act or the application of such provision to other person or circumstances shall not be
affected by such declaration.

SECTION 9. EFFECTIVITY. — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days from the
date of its publication in a newspaper of general circulation in the Bangsamoro
Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.
