This bill pursues to create INFIRMARY Hospital in the municipality of Maluso to be known as
MALUSO INFIRMARY HOSPITAL with (15 Bed Capacity).

Basilan is one of the island provinces of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao (BARMM) and is separated from the mainland of Mindanao by a strait of about 17
miles at its narrowest margin. It is located across the southern tip of Zamboanga Peninsula
(Region 9) and is bounded on the north by Basilan Strait.

It has a total land area of 3,677.15 square kilometers comprising island and islets within
its territorial boundary including Isabela City. Basilan ranks third in terms of land area among
the provinces of the BARMM. The province is composed of two component cities and eleven
municipalities, 255 total numbers of Barangays with the population of 525,867 peoples
(estimates based on 2020 survey). Basilan Province under BARMM has 60.578% households and
likewise Isabela City has 20.127 % Households. The province in terms of poverty incidence
decreased in percentage of poor families from 32.5% in 2010 to 28.3% in 2015.

Hence, considering that the existing hospitals catering the Bangsamoro constituents in
the whole province are only confined in the two Cities of Isabela and Lamitan and none in the
different far flung rural and Island Municipalities where poor or indigent families cannot afford
to reach the hospital facilities and medical services in the two mentioned towns, the
establishment of infirmary hospital in Maluso Municipality is an urgent need to provide medical
services to the needy inhabitants of its neighbouring areas.

The municipality of Maluso with a population of 40,646 people as of 2015 census is a
fourth (4rth) class municipality and is geographically located at the western coast of Basilan
province. It has aland area of 304.14 square kilometers or 8.27% square miles with 20
barangays and about 5,907 Households. It has about 36.7% poverty incidence based in 2012
report.

Based on 2017 ratio report on Inventory of Health Personnel, Maluso has (1 MOA-
Doctor) 12 Nurses, 1 Rural Health Unit and 15 Barangay Health Stations wherein it is often
manned by Barangay Health Workers with voluntary services. Of the 5,907 households, about
3,962 H.H. are already energized or equivalence of 67%.

The nearest municipalities are Lantawan, Western part of mainland of Sumisip
Municipality, the Island Municipalities of Tabuan Lasa, and Hj. Muhtamad (Formerly known as
Pilas Island). People from these neighbouring municipalities have fluid movement in and out of
Maluso for various social and economic reasons. Even though Sumisip has hospital known as
Sumisip District Hospital, and with the rich cultural diversities among these municipalities, this
however makes Sumisip District hospital not easily accessible to them as it is located in the
town center. Furthermore, the constituents of Maluso and other neighbouring municipalities
will have to endure long hours of travel to avail essential hospital services in Isabela and
Zamboanga City which adds much more to their financial burden.

Therefore, the establishment of Maluso INFIRMARY Hospital as part of the pro-active
steps of the Bangsamoro government to self-governance and in pursuit to providing services
and protect the health of the people is a matter of great importance in delivering health
services that are easily accessible at an affordable cost in times of health emergencies
especially during this pandemic and for those under-privileged Bangsamoro constituents.

In view of the aforementioned, approval of this bill is earnestly sought.

GENERAL PROVISION

Section 1. Title- This shall be known as an Act creating Maluso Infirmary Hospital with
(15 Bed Capacity) in the Municipality of Maluso, Province of Basilan, defining its service
Capacity, organization, functions and operational responsibilities, appropriating funds
therefore and for other purposes.

Section 2. The Maluso Infirmary Hospital (MIH) shall be initially constructed as an
Infirmary/Hospital with at least 15 bed capacities and shall start its operation upon the
prescribed licensing standard issued by the Ministry of Health facilities and services (MOHFS).
Personnel structure, Medical, office equipment and other facilities required shall likewise be
provided to said Infirmary. Provided however, that MIH may be upgraded into a higher hospital
Capacity upon evaluation and recommendation of the Ministry of Health (MOH) (BARMM).

Section 3. Objectives- To cater highly accessible, low-cost and affordable health care
infirmary or hospital services and other relevant program and services provided for under R.A.
No. 11223, otherwise known as the Universal Health Care (UHC) Act where the Bangsamoro
constituents in BARMM may avail.

Section 9.The State shall promote a just and dynamic social order that will ensure the

ARTICLE Ill
CREATION AND GENERAL ORGANIZATION

Section 1. Creation- In undertaking the aforementioned policies, Maluso Infirmary
Hospital (with 15 bed capacity) is hereby created in the Province of Basilan of the Bangsamoro

Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM).

Section 2. Mandate- The Maluso Infirmary Hospital, shall deliver hospital care
effectively and efficiently. It shall provide appropriate hospital care services, promote and
protect the welfare of its’ clients and patients. Further, MIH shall have separate and
independent administrative functions from any existing General Hospital or district hospitals
operating within the province of Basilan EXCEPT patient referrals on emergency cases or

beyond the infirmary capacity.

Section 3. Administration, Management and Organization.- The Maluso
Infirmary/Hospital shall be under the administrative supervision of the Ministry of Health
(MOH) of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Regionin Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), who is hereby
authorized to determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the

management, operations and facilities herein provided.

Section 4. Appropriation.- The initial fund appropriated for the construction,
procurement of equipment, operation to include pre-operating expenses, annual personnel
services and maintenance of the Maluso Infirmary Hospital (MIH) as well as the necessary
amount for its continues operations and upgrading as may be deemed necessary by the MOH

shall be amounting to One Hundred Million Pesos (Php. 100,000,000.00).
