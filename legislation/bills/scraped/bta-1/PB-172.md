EXPLANATORY NOTE

This measure seeks to establish a level 1 municipal hospital in the municipality of
kapai in Lanao Del Sur to provide access to basic medical and healthcare services primarily to
the people of the municipality of Kapai, as well as of the municipality of Tagoloan, a
neighboring municipality.

It is most appropriate to establish a hospital in the area to serve and cater the basic
healthcare and medical needs of the more than thirty (30) thousand population of the two
neighboring municipalities of Kapai and Tagoloan. These two municipalities are among the
geographically isolated and disadvantaged areas in Lanao Del Sur whose population are
experiencing difficulties in accessing health services because of isolation and poor road
infrastructure.

In view of this, and pursuant to the bangsamoro government’s commitment to provide
for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by
law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people, immediate
approval of this bill is earnestly sought.

Section 1. Short Title. — This Act shall be known as the "KAPAI MUNICIPAL
HOSPITAL ACT OF 2022”

Section 2. Declaration of Policy. — It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao to provide for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for
its constituents and establish by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements
of its people and ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the
prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services. Towards this end, and in
order to adequately respond to emergencies, disasters and emerging diseases, the Bangsamoro
Government shall protect the interest and promote the well-being and the medical needs of the
Bangsamoro People.

Section 3. Kapai Municipal Hospital. — There shall be established a level 1 with twenty-five
(25) bed capacity municipal hospital in Kapai, Lanao Del Sur to be known as Kapai Municipal
Hospital.

Section 4, Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program. -- The establishment of
the Kapai Municipal Hospital shall integrate into its system of operations all pertinent and
relevant provisions under Republic Act No. 11223 otherwise known as the Universal Health
Care (UHC) Act.

Section 5. Administration. — Upon the effectivity of this Act, the Kapai Municipal Hospital
shall be under the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who is hereby
authorized to determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the
management and operations of the hospital.

Section 6. Appropriations. — The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act
shall be included in the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act. Thereafter, funds necessary for the
maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be provided in accordance with the provisions
of the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act and other pertinent laws.

Section 7, Implementing Rules and Regulations - The BARMM-MOH shall promulgate the
Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of this Act within sixty (60) days after the
enactment of this act.

Section 8. Separability Clause. -- In the event any provision of this Act or the application of
such provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this Act shall not be affected by such
declaration.

Section 9. Effectivity - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a
newspaper of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
