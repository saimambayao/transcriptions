EXPLANATORY NOTE

The Constitution, as well as the Bangsamoro Organic Law, mandate the adoption of an
integrated and comprehensive approach in making available essential and basic health care
services to the bangsamoro people at an affordable cost. This Bangsamoro government deems it
imperative that public health care must be affordable, effective and convenient.

Unayan Municipal Hospital located in Municipality of Binidayan is the only hospital found
in the Unayan area in Lanao Del Sur. It was established with only Ten (10) Authorized Bed
Capacity by virtue of Republic Act No. 6937 which was approved March 9, 1990.

Since its initial operationalization in 1995, Unayan Municipal Hospital has not been upgraded
as to facilities, manpower, equipment and budgetary allocation.

Although Unayan Municipal Hospital was created as Municipal Hospital, it is functioning as
district hospital. It has been catering the medical and health care needs not only of the residents
of Binidayan Municipality but also residents from neighbouring municipalities namely, Bayang,
Calanogas, Pualas and Pagayawan, Ganassi, Tubaran, Lumbaca-Unayan, Lumbatan and Sultan
Dumalondong.

MOH-BARMM Statistical Annual Report in 2019 shows that the hospital is rendering
services beyond the expected capacity. Its bed occupancy is higher than its authorized bed
capacity. For now the hospital Occupancy Rate has gone up to 400%, a four-fold increase of its
original 10 bed capacity. This mismatch in bed occupancy, compounded by lack of sufficient
manpower and health services, hinders the quality of health care for patients and inadequate to
sustain the growing health needs of the residents of Binidayan and other neighbouring
municipalities.

Cognizant to the above considerations, this bill seeks to authorize and expedite the process
of improving the quality of the delivery of health services of Unayan Municipal Hospital by
upgrading it from 10-bed Municipal Hospital to 50-Bed District Hospital to cater and sustain the
needs of its increasing patients.

In upholding Bangsamoro government’s mandate to adopt an integrated and comprehensive
approach to health development, approval of this bill is earnestly sought.

SECTION 1. Short Title. This Act shall be known as the “UNAYAN MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL
UPGRADING ACT OF 2020”.

SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy. It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article [X of the Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise
known as the “Organic Law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to
provide for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish
by law and general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that
the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent
and affordable medical services.

SEC. 3. Upgrading of the Unayan Municipal Hospital - The existing Unayan Municipal Hospital
is hereby upgraded from its present 10 Authorized Bed Capacity into a District Hospital with a 50
Authorized Bed Capacity to be now known as Unayan District Hospital. The existing buildings
including landscaping, medical and office equipment and other facilities, its operating funds and
personnel are also hereby upgraded to include the needed construction and procurement thereof in
order to meet the prescribed standards of the upgrade to a district hospital with 50 bed capacity.

SEC 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program - The upgrading of the Unayan
District Hospital shall integrate into its system of operations all pertinent and relevant provisions
under Republic Act No. 11223 otherwise known as the Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

SEC 5. Administration, Management, and Organization - The Unayan District Hospital shall be
under the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who is hereby authorized to
determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the management and operations
of the facility, and if necessary, shall include the creation of the Hospital Board of Directors,
defining its powers and functions and the appointment of the officers and members thereof,

SEC. 6. Appropriations - The amount of Two Hundred Nine Million Five Hundred Fourteen
Thousand Two Hundred Ninety Four and 64 Centavos (Php. 209, 514, 294.64) is hereby
appropriated from the BARMM General Appropriations for the upgrading of the Unayan District
Hospital including the construction of infrastructures, acquisition of medical and office equipment
and the annual budget needed for personal services, maintenance, operations and other expenses
for the implementation of this act, and thereafter, such amount as may be needed for the continued
operations of the hospital.

SEC. 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations - The BARMM-MOH shall promulgate the
Implementing Rules and Regulations of this Act within sixty (60) days after the enactment of this
act.

SEC. 8. Separability Clause - In the event any provision of this act or the application of such
provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this act shall not be affected by such declaration.

SEC. 9. Repealing Clause - All laws and regulations under the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region
in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) government including those passed under the defunct
Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) government regarding the operations of the
Unayan Municipal Hospital not consistent with this Act are hereby repealed.

SEC. 10. Effectivity - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a
newspapers of regional circulation.
