SECTION 1. Short Title — This act shall be known as the “Ramadan
Economic Assistance and Relief (REAR) Act of 2022 ”.

SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy — It is hereby declared the policy of the
Government of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
(BARMM) to establish a balanced society and assert its rights to conserve and
develop its patrimony, reflective of its system of life as prescribed by the
Bangsamoro people’s faith, in harmony with its customary laws, cultures and
traditions and affirm its distinct historical identity and birthright to its ancestral
homeland, and its nght to chart a political future that will secure the Bangsamoro
identity and posterity, and allow genuine and meaningful self-governance.

SECTION 3. Officials and Employees covered — All officials and
employees of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
(BARMM), whether permanent, co-terminus, job order or contractual, who have
rendered at least four months of service before the month of Ramadan, shall
receive Ramadan Economic Assistance and Relief (REAR) equivalent to one
month basic salary.

SECTION 4. Other employees covered — The above-mentioned officials
and employees of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
(BARMM), who are employed in the Bangsamoro Government for less than three
(3) months before the month of Ramadan shall be entitled solely with the following
grant:

SECTION 5. Source of fund — For CY 2022, the amount needed for the
implementation of this Act shall be taken from any available fund of the
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

For succeeding years, the amount shall be included in the annual
Bangsamoro Appropriations Act (BAA).

SECTION 6. Limit — No official and employee of the Bangsamoro
Government shall receive Ramadan Economic Relief Assistance from any and all
sources in excess of the one month basic salary.

SECTION 7. Implementing Ministry — The Office of the Chief Minister of
the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) through the
Office of the Ministry of Finance, Budget and Management (MFBM) shall
implement the provisions of this Act.

SECTION 8. Period of release — The grant shall be released a week prior
to the Month of Ramadan based on the Hijra Calendar but not later than the 15"
day of the same;

SECTION 9. Audit — All financial transactions and expenditures under this
Act shall be subject to existing accounting and auditing rules and regulations as
provided in the R.A NO. 11054, otherwise known as Bangsamoro Organic Law
(BOL);

SECTION 10. Separability Clause. If any section or provision of this Act
shall be declared invalid or unconstitutional, the other sections or provisions not
affected thereby shall remain in full force and effect.

SECTION 11. Repealing Clause. Any law, presidential decree or issuance,
executive order, letter of instruction, administrative order, rule or regulation
contrary to, or inconsistent with, the provisions of this Act is hereby repealed,
modified, or amended accordingly.

SECTION 12. Effectivity. This Act shall take effect immediately after its
publication.
