Article 1. Title - This Act shall be known as the "Bangsamoro Cooperative and
Social Enterprise Code (BCSEC) of 2021".

Article 2. Basis - Section 24 and 27 of Article XII! of Republic ACT no. 11054,
otherwise known as the Organic Law on the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao, provides that the Bangsamoro Government shall adopt and implement
cooperative development policies and programs through the enactment of this Code.
Further, the Bangsamoro Government shall recognize, protect and promote the
establishment of cooperative enterprises. It shall likewise encourage and promote social
entrepreneurship as a means to engage the private sector to assist in the development,
funding and implementation of solution to social, cultural and environmental issues.

Article 3. Purpose - This Code is enacted to govern the establishment and
organization of the Cooperatives and Social Enterprises in the Bangsamoro Autonomous
Region. It shall prescribe the registration requirements, duties and obligations of members,
provision of capacity building, monitoring of the same, prescribed fees and charges in
accordance with this Code and existing laws of the land.

The treasurer will be required to submit proof of bank deposits in the name
of the cooperative, showing the total subscribed capital.

Any newly organized |primary cooperative may be registered as multipurpose

cooperatives to be set by the Authority.

A single-purpose cooperative may transform into a multipurpose or may
create subsidiaries within two (2) to three (3) years of operations and with proof

extension as may be determined by the Authority.
