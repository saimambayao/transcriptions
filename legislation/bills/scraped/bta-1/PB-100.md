EXPLANATORY NOTE

Republic Act No. 11054, the “Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao”, provides in section 22, Article IX thereof that: “The Bangsamoro government
shall adopt a policy on health that provides for a comprehensive and integrated health service
delivery for its constituents. It shall establish by law a general hospital system to serve the
requirements of its people to ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through
the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services.” xxx

Datu Halun Sakilan Memorial Hospital is a government-run provincial hospital located in
Bongao, Tawi-Tawi. With the increasing population and demand, the hospital is constrained to
render services beyond its expected capacity. Based on the MOH-BARMM Annual Hospital
Statistical Report in 2019, the hospital’s Bed Occupancy Rate has gone up to 237% higher than its
Authorized Bed Capacity.

Further, Datu Halun Sakilan Memorial Hospital, since its creation in 1974, and despite its
conversion into a provincial hospital in 1982 by virtue of Batas Pambansa Blg. 257, it is still a Level
1 hospital with only 25-bed corresponding MOOE budget and limited health manpower status. The
DOH standard for a provincial hospital is at least Level 2 hospital with 100 beds.

The MOOE budget of a 25-bed capacity hospital is very small for the current operations,
medicines, diagnostics, equipment and health manpower complement of Datu Halun Sakilan
Memorial Hospital. In addition, it being a provincial hospital, in its budget is also included the
maintenance of almost all of the eleven (11) Rural Health Units in the province.

This proposed measure seeks to cater this problem by upgrading Datu Halun Sakilan
Memorial Hospital from its present Level 1 with 25 Authorized Bed Capacity into 100 Bed Capacity
Level 2 hospital. Upgrading the bed capacity of the hospital would also entail increase of the limited
budget for its operations as well as plantilla positions for nurses, doctors and other vital positions
to man the hospital.

Hence, in view of the foregoing, early approval of this bill is earnestly sought.

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao Bangsamoro Transition
Authority (BTA) Interim Parliament.

SECTION 1. Short Title. -- This Act shall be known as the “DATU HALUN SAKILAN
MEMORIAL HOSPITAL UPGRADE ACT OF 2021”.

SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy. -- It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article IX of the Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known
as the “Organic Law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to provide for
a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law a
general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the individual
basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable
medical services.

SEC. 3. Upgrading of the Datu Halun Sakilan Memorial Hospital. -- The existing Datu Halun
Sakilan Memorial Hospital is hereby upgraded from its present Level 1 with 25 bed capacity into
Level 2 with 100 Authorized Bed Capacity in accordance with the national standards set by the
Bureau of Health Facilities and Services under the Department of Health (DOH). The existing
building including its landscaping, medical and office equipment and other facilities, its operating
funds and personnel are also hereby upgraded to include the needed construction and procurement
thereof in order to meet the prescribed standards to the upgraded level.

SEC 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program. — The upgrading of the Datu
Halun Sakilan Memorial Hospital shall integrate into its system of operations all pertinent and
relevant provisions under Republic Act No. 11223 otherwise known as the Universal Health Care
(UHC) Act.

SEC 5. Administration, Management, and Organization. — The Datu Halun Sakilan Memorial
Hospital shall be under the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who is hereby
authorized to determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the management
and operations of the hospital.

SEC. 6. Appropriations — The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be
included in the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act. Thereafter, funds necessary for the continued
operation and maintenance of the hospital shall be provided in accordance with the provisions of
the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act and other pertinent laws.

SEC. 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations - The MOH-BARMM shall promulgate the
Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of this Act within sixty (60) days after the enactment
of this Act.

SEC. 8. Separability Clause. -- In the event any provision of this Act or the application of such
provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this Act shall not be affected by such declaration.

SEC. 9. Repealing Clause. -- All laws and regulations under the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region
in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) government including those passed under the defunct
Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) government regarding the operations of the
Datu Halun Sakilan Memorial Hospital not consistent with this Act are hereby repealed.

SEC. 10. Effectivity - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper
of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
