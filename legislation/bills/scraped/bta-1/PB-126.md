SECTION I. Short Title. -- This Act shall be known as the “DATU ODIN SINSUAT DISTRICT
HOSPITAL UPGRADE ACTIOF 2021”.

SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy. -- It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article IX of the Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise
known as the “Organic Law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to
provide for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish
by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the
individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellence and
affordable medical services.

SEC. 3. To upgrade the existing Datu Odin Sinsuat District Hospital. — The existing Datu Odin
Sinsuat District Hospital is herety upgraded from its present status with a fifty (50) bed capacity
into one hundred (100) Authorized Bed Capacity in accordance with the national standards set by
the Bureau of Health Facilities and Services under the Department of Health (DOH). The existing
building including its landscaping, medical and office equipment and other facilities, its operating
funds and personnel are also henbby upgraded to include the needed construction and procurement
thereof in order to meet the prescribed standards to the upgraded level.

SEC. 5. Administration, Management, and Organization. — The Datu Odin Sinsuat District
Hospital shall be under the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who is hereby
authorized to determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the management
and operations of the facility, and if necessary, shall include the creation of the Hospital Board of
Directors, defining its powers and functions and the appointment of the officers and members
thereof.

SEC. 6. Appropriation. — The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be
included in the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act. Thereafter, funds necessary for the maintenance
and operation of the hospital) shall be provided in accordance with the provisions of the
Bangsamoro Appropriations Act and other pertinent laws.

SEC. 7. Implementing Rules 'and Regulations. — The BARMM-MOH shall promulgate the
Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of this Act within sixty (60) days after the enactment
of this act.

SEC. 8. Separability Clause. —1n the event any provision of this act or the application of such
provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this act shall not be affected by such declaration.

SEC. 9. Repealing Clause. — All laws and regulations under the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region
in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) government including those passed under the defunct
Autonomous Region in Muslim| Mindanao (ARMM) government regarding the operations of the
Datu Odin Sinsuat District Hospital not consistent with this Act are hereby repealed.

SEC. 10. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following the completion
of its publication in a newspaper of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
