EXPLANATORY NOTE

Republic Act 11054, “The Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao” mandates this government to adopt a policy on health that provides for a comprehensive and
integrated health service delivery for its constituents. It shall establish by law a general hospital system
to serve the requirements of its people to ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable
through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services.

Wao District Hospital is a 25-Bed Capacity and Level 1 government hospital created by virtue of
Republic Act No. 3607 and commenced operations on December 8, 1979. It is strategically located and
caters to the health needs of the constituents of municipalities of Wao and Amai Manabilang (formerly
Bumbaran) in Lanao Del Sur, and even residents of nearby municipalities from other provinces such as,
Banisilan and some parts of Alamada in Cotabato and Kalilangan in Bukidnon.

It is Wao District Hospital’s mission to provide quality healthcare services to its constituents. It
also serves as a referral hospital of Rural Health Units within Wao and Amai Manabilang in Lanao Del
Sur and Banisilan in Cotabato.

As per 2015 census of population published by the PSA, Wao has a population of 45, 862 while
Amai Manabilang has 10, 401 population.

In addition, in the MOH Annual Hospital Statistical Report in 2019, it shows the increase in the
hospital’s bed occupancy rate to 151%. This statistical report indicates that the district hospital has been
serving patients beyond its expected capacity.

In view of the above-mentioned policy of this BARMM government to provide for a
comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents, it becomes a matter of utmost
consideration and necessity for this government to enact a law that will address the problem.

Thus, the early approval of this bill is earnestly sought.

AN ACT UPGRADING THE EXISTING WAO DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN LANAO DEL SUR
FROM ITS PRESENT LEVEL 1 WITH A 25 BED CAPACITY INTO LEVEL 2 WITH AN

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao Bangsamoro Transition
Authority (BTA) Interim Parliament.

SECTION 1. -- Short Title. This Act shall be known as the “WAO DISTRICT HOSPITAL
UPGRADE ACT OF 2021”.

SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy. -- It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article [IX of the Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known as
the “Organic Law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to provide for a
comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law and general
hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the individual basic right to
life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services.

SEC. 3. Upgrading of the Wao District Hospital. - The Wao District Hospital is hereby upgraded from
its present level with 25 bed capacity into Level 2 with 100 Authorized Bed Capacity in accordance with
the national standards set by the Bureau of Health Facilities and Services under the Department of Health
(DOH). The existing building including its landscaping, medical and office equipment and other
facilities, its operating funds and personnel are also hereby upgraded to include the needed construction
and procurement thereof in order to meet the prescribed standards to the upgraded level.

SEC 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program. — The upgrading of the Wao District
Hospital shall integrate into its system of operations all pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic
Act No. 11223 otherwise known as the Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

SEC 5. Administration, Management, and Organization. -- The Wao District Hospital shall be under
the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who is hereby authorized to determine and
approve the appropriate organizational structure for the management and operations of the facility, and
if necessary, shall include the creation of the Hospital Board of Directors, defining its powers and
functions and the appointment of the officers and members thereof.

SEC. 6. Appropriations — The amount of Two Hundred Six Million Pesos (Php. 206, 000,000. 00) is
hereby appropriated from the BARMM General Appropriations for the upgrading of the Wao District
Hospital, including the construction of infrastructures, acquisition of medical and office equipment and
the annual budget needed for personal services, maintenance, operations and other expenses for the
implementation of this act, and thereafter, such amount as may be needed for the continued operations of
the hospital.

SEC. 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations - The BARMM-MOH shall promulgate the Implementing
Rules and Regulations (IRR) of this Act within sixty (60) days after the enactment of this act.

SEC. 8. Separability Clause. -- In the event any provision of this act or the application of such provision
is declared invalid, the remainder of this act shall not be affected by such declaration.

SEC. 9. Repealing Clause. -- All laws and regulations under the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) government including those passed under the defunct Autonomous Region
in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) government regarding the operations of the Wao District Hospital not
consistent with this Act are hereby repealed.

SEC. 10. Effectivity - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of
regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
