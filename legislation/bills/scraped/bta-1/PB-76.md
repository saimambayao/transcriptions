AN ACT EXTENDING THE AVAILABILITY OF THE
2020 BANGSAMORO APPROPRIATIONS TO DECEMBER 31, 2021,
AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 47 OF THE GENERAL
PROVISIONS OF BANGSAMORO AUTONOMY ACT NO. 03, THE
BANGSAMORO APPROPRIATIONS ACT OF FISCAL YEAR 2020

Section1. Section 47 of the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act or Bangsamoro Act
No. 3 is hereby amended to read as follows:

“Sec. 47. Cash Budgeting System. All appropriations
authorized in this Act shall be available for release
and disbursement for the purpose specified, and
under the same general and special provisions
applicable thereto, until December 31, 2020.

For FY 2020, the appropriations for infrastructure and
other capital outlays shall be valid for obligation and
disbursement until December 31, 2021. Further,
appropriations for MOOE of __ Transitional
Development Impact Fund (TDIF), Tulong Alay sa
Bangsamorong Nangangailangan (TABANG), Quick
Response Fund, Ayudang Medikal Mula sa
Bangsamoro Government (AMBAG), Kapayapaan sa
Pamayanan (Kapyanan), Marawi Rehabilitation
Program, Family and Community Welfare Program,
Child and Youth Welfare Program, Women’s Welfare
Program, Older Person’s and Person with Disabilities
Welfare Program, Emergency Assistance Program,
Internal Affairs Program, Land Tenure Security
Program, Sustainable Development for Agrarian
Reform Beneficiaries in the Bangsamoro (SDAReBB),
Agrarian Justice Delivery Program and_ the
Contingent Fund shall likewise be valid for obligation
and disbursement until December 31, 2021. The
MOOE for all other programs not mentioned above
shall be valid for obligation until December 31, 2020,
while the delivery, inspection and payment shall be
made not later than June 30, 2021.

The MFBM is authorized to issue the necessary
guidelines for the effective implementation of the cash
budgeting system"

— Republic of the Philippines
By Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao

3) Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament

OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY GENERAL
