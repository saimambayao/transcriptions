Section 1. Short Title. This act shall be known asthe “ Establishing
Lantawan Municipal Hospital Act of 2021” .

Section 2. Deciaration of Principies and Policies. It is the policy of
the State to protect and promote the right to health of all Filipinos and
instill health consciousness among them as stipulated in the Republic
Act 11223 The Universal Health Care Law. Towards this end, the State
shall adopt:

(a) An integrated and comprehensive approach to ensure that all
Filipinos are health literate, provided with healthy living conditions, and
protected from hazards and risks that could affect their health;

(b) A health care model that provides all Filipinos access to a
comprehensive set of quality and cost-effective, promotive, preventive,
curative, rehabilitative and palliative health services without causing
financial hardship, and) prioritizes the needs of the population who
cannot afford such services;

(c) A framework that fosters a whole-of-system, whole-of-
government, and whole-of-society approach in the development,
implementation, monitoring, and evaluation of health policies,
programs and plans; and

(dj A peopie-oriented approach for the delivery of heaith services
that is centered on people’ s needs and well-being, and cognizant of the
differences in culture, values, and beliefs.

It is also the policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao to provide for the comprehensive and integrated
health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law and
general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people
and ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable
through the prompt intervention and excellent and affordable medical
services of Section 22, prticle IX of the Republic Act 11054.

Section 3. General Objectives. This act shall seek out better health
system delivery and ensure that ali Municipal Lantawan constituents
are guaranteed equitable access to quality and affordable health care
and public health facilities. Health services that are devoted primarily
to the provision of services for health promotion, prevention, diagnosis,
treatment, rehabilitation and palliation of Lantawan constituents
suffering from illness, disease, injury, disability, or deformity, or when
there is immediate danger and where delay in health support that may
cause loss of life.

Section 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program.
The Lantawan Municipal Hospital shall integrate into its system of
operation all pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic Act No.
11223 otherwise known! as the Universai Health Care Law.

Section 5. Organization and Human Resource System. The Ministry
of Health (MOH) with stakehoiders, shali ensure the formuiation and
implementation of a Municipal Health Human Resource Plan that will
provide policies and |strategies for the appropriate generation,
recruitment, training, regulation, assessment of health workforce based
on population health needs. The MOH and if necessary with the
Provincial Health Boards shall exercise administrative and technical
supervision over health) facilities and health human resources of the
Lantawan Hospital which shall include the creation of the Lantawan
Municipal Hospital Health Board, defining its power and function and
the appointment of the officers and members thereof.

Section 6. Appropriations for Construction and Administrative
Expenses. The Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, Office of the Chief
Minister together with the Ministry of Finance, Budget and
Management and the Ministry of Health shall appropriate for the
funding of Hospital Construction including the acquisition of medical
and office equipment and the budget needed for personal services,
maintenance, operation and other expenses for the implementation of
this Act.

Section 7. Safety and) Quality. The MOH shall institute a licensing
and regulatory system for stand-aione health facilities, including those
providing ambulatory and primary care services, and other modes of
health services provisions. It shall set standard for health care provider
and health care facilities accessible for the peopie of Lantawan. It shail
also coordinate with the| Basilan Electric Company (BASELCO) for the
proper procedures and maintenance of its Electricity.
