EXPLANATORY NOTES

This bill is requesting the members of the Parliament Bangsamoro Transition
Authority to adopt an official flag and emblem pursuant to Section 2, Article 1 of the
Oragnic Law for the BARMM.

Setting of an identity is one of the necessities of an operational government thus,
official flag and emblern and composition of hymn as the Bangsamore identities is
necessary to be adopted to reflect the pride and unity of the whole Bangsamoro
region,

Attached herewith is the proposed designed of the official flag, emblem and
composition of Bangsamoro Hymn for the BARMM for approval of this parliament
assembly and subject for improvements to be referred to specific committee.

in the light of the foregoing, | pray unto this Honorable Members of the
Parliament, Bangsamoro Transition Authority to give favorable considerations to the
