EXPLANATORY NOTE

This Bill seeks to convert the Tamparan District Hospital (TDH) in Tamparan Lanao Del
Sur into a Provincial Hospital to be known as the Lanao Del Sur Provincial Hospital and to upgrade
its level, bed capacity, personnel and facilities to cater the medical needs of the people of Lanao Del
Sur province.

Republic Act No. 11054, the “Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao”, provides in section 22, Article IX thereof that: “The bangsamoro government
shall adopt a policy on health that provides for a comprehensive and integrated health service
delivery for its constituents. It shall establish by law a general hospital system to serve the
requirements of its people to ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through
the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services.” Xxx

This provision of the Bangsamoro Organic Law highlights the fact that it is a basic
responsibility of this government to deliver quality health care to Bangsamoro people. In order to
attain this, apart from establishment of new hospitals, the existing ones must be upgraded especially
in areas where people largely depend on public hospitals for their health care needs and where
population has practically increased but health care services have not, such as in Lanao Del Sur.

In the last five (5) years, TDH has been catering to not less than 4000 numbers of patients
every year who are coming from different municipalities of Lanao Del Sur. With the increasing
population of the province, and TDH being the most accessible hospital and strategically located to
cater the medical needs of the people of the province, it is appropriate for it to be converted into a
provincial hospital. This conversion would also include the increase and upgrade of the hospital’s
bed capacity, facilities and personnel, among others, for more effective and efficient delivery of
medical care services.

TDH is presently a level 1 hospital with 100-bed capacity. It has been serving patients
needing medical attention coming not only from Tamparan but also from other municipalities in
Lanao Del Sur, like Taraka, Mulondo, Maguing, Lumba-Bayabao, Masiu, Poonabayabao,
Lumbayangue, Butig and other municipalities. The hospital’s patients are mostly indigents and
belonging to the low-income social groups in the province.

Considering the above, it is now high time to convert and upgrade the district hospital into
a provincial hospital with 150- bed capacity Level 2 hospital. With this, the hospital will be able to

address the health needs of the most marginalized sector in the province of Lanao Del Sur.

In view of the foregoing, the approval of this bill is earnestly sought.

“AN ACT CONVERTING THE TAMPARAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN LANAO DEL

AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES”

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao Bangsamoro Transition
Authority (BTA) Interim Parliament.

SECTION 1. Short Title. -- This Act shall be known as the “CONVERSION OF TAMPARAN
DISTRICT HOSPITAL TO PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL ACT OF 2021”.

SEC, 2. Declaration of Policy. -- It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article IX of the Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known
as the “Organic Law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to provide for
a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law a
general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the individual
basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable
medical services.

SEC. 3. Conversion of the Tamparan District Hospital. -- The existing Tamparan District Hospital
is hereby converted and upgraded into Level 2 with One Hundred Fifty (150) Bed Capacity
provincial hospital to be known as the Lanao Del Sur Provincial Hospital. The existing buildings
including its landscaping, medical and office equipment and other facilities, its operating funds and
personnel are also hereby upgraded to include the needed construction and procurement thereof to
conform with the prescribed standards to the upgraded level and bed capacity increase.

SEC 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program. -- The upgrading of the
Tamparan District Hospital shall integrate into its system of operations all pertinent and relevant
provisions under Republic Act No. 11223 otherwise known as the Universal Health Care (UHC)
Act.

SEC 5. Administration, Management, and Organization. -- The Lanao Del Sur Provincial Hospital
shall be under the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who is hereby authorized to
determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the management and operations
of the hospital.

SEC. 6. Appropriations — The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be
included in the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act. Thereafter, funds necessary for the maintenance
and operation of the hospital shall be provided in accordance with the provisions of the Bangsamoro
Appropriations Act and other pertinent laws.

SEC. 7. Implementing Rules and Regulations - The MOH-BARMM shall promulgate the
Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of this Act within sixty (60) days after the enactment
of this Act.

SEC. 8. Separability Clause. -- In the event any provision of this Act or the application of such
provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this Act shall not be affected by such declaration.

SEC. 9. Repealing Clause. -- All laws and regulations under the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region
in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) government including those passed under the defunct
Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) government regarding the operations of the
Lanao Del Sur Provincial Hospital not consistent with this Act are hereby repealed.

SEC. 10. Effectivity - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper
of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
