EXPLANATORY NOTE

The bill seeks to establish a level 1 general hospital in Barangay Poblacion in Pikit North
Cotabato primarily to improve access to medical services and modern health facility for the
people of Pikit North Cotabato and its neighboring municipalities within BARMM. It is most
appropriate to establish the hospital in the area it being the most populous municipality in the
province.

The Bangsamoro Government commits to adopt a policy to provide for a comprehensive
and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law a general hospital
system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the individual basic right
to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical
services.

This measure envisions to provide the people of Pikit and its neighboring towns access
to basic and quality of health care and medical services that will result to reduced morbidity and
savings in terms of medical expenses. Ensuring access to basic, quality and affordable medical
services for Bangsamoro people must be given priority by this government especially that
BARMM has long been left behind in terms of access to basic and quality healthcare services
than elsewhere in the country. The region falls well below the national targets and standards in
terms of the availability of primary healthcare infrastructure and health personnel.

Furthermore, the hospital shall be named as Ustadz Hashim Salamat Memorial Hospital
in honor of the memory of Ustadz Hashim Salamat, renowned as the Amir of the Bangsamoro
Mujahideen, the founding Chairman and leader of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF).
His profound contribution in leading and inspiring the Bangsamoro Mujahideen in its long-time
struggle to self-determination was significantly instrumental to what now being enjoyed by the
Bangsamoro People.

In view of the foregoing, the immediate approval of this bill is earnestly sought.

Section 1. Short Title. — This Act shall be known as the "USTADZ HASHIM SALAMAT
MEMORIAL HOSPITAL ACT OF 2021”

Section 2. Declaration of Policy. — It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao to provide for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its
constituents and establish by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of
its people and ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt
intervention of excellent and affordable medical services. Towards this end, and in order to
adequately respond to emergencies, disasters and emerging diseases, the Bangsamoro
Government shall protect the interest and promote the well-being and the medical needs of the
Bangsamoro People.

Section 3. Ustadz Hashim Salamat Memorial Hospital. — There shall be established a level 1
with fifty bed capacity general hospital in Barangay Poblacion in Pikit North Cotabato to be
known as the Ustadz Hashim Salamat Memorial Hospital.

Section 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program. - The establishment of
the Ustadz Hashim Salamat Memorial Hospital shall integrate into its system of operations all
pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic Act No. 11223 otherwise known as the
Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

Section 5. Administration. — Upon the effectivity of this Act, the Ustadz Hashim Salamat
Memorial Hospital shall be under the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who
is hereby authorized to determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the
management and operations of the hospital.

Section 6. Appropriations. — The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall
be included in the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act. Thereafter, funds necessary for the
maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be provided in accordance with the provisions
of the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act and other pertinent laws.
