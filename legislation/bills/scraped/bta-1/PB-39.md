SECTION 1. Adoption of the Official Bangsamoro Hymn. — Pursuant to Article Il,
Section 2 of Republic Act No. 11054, there is hereby adopted an Official Hymn of the
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, hereinafter referred to as the
Bangsamoro Hymn.

SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy.— In the exercise of its right to self-
governance, the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao is free to adopt
its Bangsamoro Hymn that conveys and preserves the peoples’ identity, history,
heritage, arid aspirations.

SECTION 4. Rendition of the Bangsamoro Hymn. — The rendition of the
Bangsamoro Hymn, whether played or sung, shall always be in accordance with the
official musical arrangement and composition.

All public and private schools, colleges and universities, public offices,
government-owned or controlled corporations, and other government instrumentalities
within the Bangsamoro are required to play and sing the Bangsamoro Hymn during flag-
raising and flag lowering ceremonies. Other offices of the Bangsamoro that are outside
its area of autonomy are likewise required play and sing the Bangsamoro Hymn.

Where necessary, the Bangsamoro Hymn may also be played and sung in
ceremonial and symbolic functions and aciivities.

Whenever the Bangsamoro Hymn is sung, it shall always be preceded by the
singing of the Philippine National Anthem pursuant to Article Il, Section 2 of Republic
Act No. 11054.

SECTION 5. Appropriations. — The amount of one million pesos (1,000,00U0.00
php) is hereby authorized to be charged to any funds of the Office of the Chief Minister
for the initial production and distribution of the Bangsamoro Hymn.

SECTION 6. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after sis
publication in a regional newspaper of general circulation in the Bangsamoro.
