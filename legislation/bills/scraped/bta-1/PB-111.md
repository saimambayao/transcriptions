AN ACT CONSTRUCTING AND ESTABLISHING THE BULDON MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL AT THE
MUNICIPALITY OF BULDON, MAGUINDANO PROVINCE, BARMM AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS
THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, Bangsamoro Transition
Authority (BTA) interim Parliament

SECTION 1. Short Title. — This Act shall be known as the “THE BULDON MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL
ESTABLISHMENT ACT OF 2021”.

SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy. - it is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known as the
“Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to provide for a
comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law a general
hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the individual basic right
to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services.

SECTION 3. The Buldon Municipal Hospital. — To implement the above-mentioned policy, a hospital
facility is hereby established to be known as the Buldon Municipal Hospital (BMH) to be located in the
Municipality of Buldon, Maguindanao Province, BARMM. The BMH shall be constructed, initially, as an
Infirmary with at least 10-15 bed capacity and shail start its operations under such capacity and
Capability as prescribed by the licensing standards of the Bureau of Health Facilities and Services under
the Department of Health (DOH). The standard personnel, medical and office equipment and other
facilities required shall likewise be provided to the hospital. The BMH may be upgraded thereafter into a
higher facility with a larger capacity upon the evaluation and recommendation by the Ministry of Health.

SECTION 4. Catchment area/coverage. — The BMH shall serve primarily, but not limited to, the patients
coming from the municipality of Buldon and may serve as an augmentation and support, when such

arises, to the existing medical facilities in what is popularly known as the lIranon municipalities consisting
of Parang, Buldon, Matanog and Barira including the adjacent areas of the municipalities of Suitan

SECTION 5. Objective/s. — To provide a highly accessible, affordable, comprehensive total health care and
hospital services to the BARMM constituents cited under Section 4 above and integrating in its system of
operations all pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic Act No. 11223 otherwise known as the
Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

SECTION 6. Administration, Management and Organization. - The Buldon Municipal Hospital shall be
under the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who is hereby authorized to determine and
approve the appropriate organizational structure for the management and operations of the facility.

SECTION 7. Appropriation. — The amount of Php 78 Million is hereby appropriated for the construction,
procurement of equipment and initial operations of the Buldon Municipal Hospital to include pre-
operating expenses, annual personal services and Maintenance and Operating expenses. The BARMM-
Ministry of Health shall include in its annual appropriations the amount necessary for the continued
operations of the hospital and its upgrading as may be deemed necessary by the MOH.

SECTION 8. Separability Clause. - |n the event any provision of this Act or the application of such
provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this Act shall not be affected by such declaration.

SECTION 9. Repealing Clause. — All laws and regulations under the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) government including those passed under the defunct Autonomous Region
in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) government regarding the construction, establishment and operations of
the Buldon Municipal Hospital not consistent with this Act are hereby repealed.
