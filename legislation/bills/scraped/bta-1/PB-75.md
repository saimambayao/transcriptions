EXPLANATORY NOTE

Republic Act No. 11054, the “Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao”, provides in section 22, Article LX thereof that: “Zhe bangsamoro government
shall adopt a policy on health that provides for a comprehensive and integrated health service
delivery for its constituents. It shall establish by law a general hospital system to serve the
requirements of its people to ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable
through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services.” xxx

Dr. Serapio B. Montaner Al-Haj Memorial Hospital (DSBMMH) is a government-run
hospital located in Malabang, Lanao Del Sur. It is a Level 1 hospital with 25 Authorized Bed
capacity (ABC).

Based on the MOH-BARMM Hospital Statistical Report in 2019, the DSBMMH has 75
Implementing Bed Capacity and about 417% Bed Occupancy Rate. The high discrepancy with the
authorized bed capacity of the hospital indicates that the hospital is rendering services that are
beyond its expected capacity.

Furthermore, DSBMMH is the only government district hospital in Lanao Del Sur that is
able to provide general surgery, obstetrical and gynecological services and trauma especially
during the tragic “Marawi siege” wherein internally displaced persons were referred and managed
at DSBMMH. In addition, the hospital has the highest number of patients served among the five
(5) BARMM government-run hospitals in the province of Lanao Del Sur. Based on the
abovementioned MOH Statistical Report in 2019, about 52.7% of the total number of admitted
patients in those hospitals are admitted in DSBMMH while 37% of the total OPD Patients in same
hospitals were served by DSBMMH.

In view of the policy of this government to provide for a comprehensive and integrated
health service delivery for its constituents, it becomes a matter of utmost consideration and
necessity for this government to enact a law that will adequately and effectively respond to the
problem.

This proposed measure seeks to answer this unfortunate circumstance by upgrading
DSBMMH from its present Level 1 with 25 Authorized Bed Capacity into 100 Bed Capacity
Level 2 hospital.

Hence, early approval of this bill is earnestly sought.

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao Bangsamoro
Transition Authority (BTA) Interim Parliament.

SECTION 1. Short Title. -- This Act shall be known as the “DR. SERAPIO B. MONTANER
MEMORIAL HOSPITAL UPGRADE ACT OF 2020”.

SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy. -- It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim
Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article IX of the Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise
known as the “Organic Law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to
provide for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and
establish by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and
ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of
excellent and affordable medical services.

SEC. 3. Upgrading of the Dr. Serapio B. Montaner Al-Haj Memorial Hospital. -- The existing
Dr. Serapio B. Montaner Al-Haj Memorial Hospital is hereby upgraded from its present level with
a 25 bed capacity into a Level 2 with 100 Authorized Bed Capacity in accordance with the
national standards set by the Bureau of Health Facilities and Services under the Department of
Health (DOH). The existing building including its landscaping, medical and office equipment and
other facilities, its operating funds and personnel are also hereby upgraded to include the needed
construction and procurement thereof in order to meet the prescribed standards to the upgraded
level.

SEC 4. Integration of the Universal Health Care (UHC) Program. -- The upgrading of the Dr.
Serapio B. Montaner Al-Haj Memorial Hospital shall integrate into its system of operations all
pertinent and relevant provisions under Republic Act No. 11223 otherwise known as the
Universal Health Care (UHC) Act.

SEC 5. Administration, Management, and Organization. -- The Dr. Serapio B. Montaner Al-Haj
Memorial Hospital shall be under the administrative supervision of the Minister of Health who is
hereby authorized to determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the
management and operations of the facility, and if necessary, shall include the creation of the
Hospital Board of Directors, defining its powers and functions and the appointment of the officers
and members thereof.

SEC. 6. Appropriations -The amount of Three Hundred Eighty One Million Pesos (Php 381,
000,000) is hereby appropriated from the BARMM General Appropriations for the upgrading of
the Dr. Serapio B. Montaner Al-Haj Memorial Hospital, including the construction of
