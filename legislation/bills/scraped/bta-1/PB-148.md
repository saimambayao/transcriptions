My high regard to all of the members of this Honorable Parliament and those in
attendance oftoday’s session!

It is a great honor and privilege for me to sponsor this Act which seeks your kind
approval toupgrade South Upi Municipal Hospital from its present ten (10) bed capacity into
licensed or authorized twenty-five (25) bed capacity in accordance with the national standards
set by the Bureau of Health Facilities and Services under the Department of Health (DOH).

It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao as provided
under Section 22, Article IX of RA 11054. otherwise known as the “Organic Law of the
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” to provide for a comprehensive and
integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law a general hospital
system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the individual basic
right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable
medical services.

South Upi Municipal Hospital is the only hospital in the Municipality of South Upi
Maguindanao. Majority of its clienteles are Indigenous Peoples belonging to the Teduray,
Lambangian and Manobo Dulangan tribes from nearby areas of Maguindanao and Sultan
Kudarat Provinces who prefer to be admitted in the facility. The next referral hospital is at
Cotabato Regional and Medical |Center (CRMC) which is 63 kilometers away and about 2-3
hours ride.

Based on its five (5) years (2016-2020) hospital statistical report, the Bed Occupancy
Rate of South Upi Municipal Hospital, a level is at 200% to 361.67%, average 20-36 patients
per day. With these statistics, there was overcrowding of patients and more workload on the
personnel contributing to mental and physical exhaustion of hospital personnel.

This year, 2021, the Regulations Licensing and Enforcement Cluster of the Ministry of
Health, BARMM, an accreditation body issued the hospital, a Level 1 25 bed license because it
was able to meet the standards/ |requirements for such accreditation. However, it needs legal
basis for the increase in the authorized bed capacity for the corresponding increase in budget
allocation and increase in human resources, hence, this Act.

My dear colleagues, it ismorally appropriate and necessary that this Act be approve by
this Honorable Parliament in response to the current Covid 19 pandemic.

As representative of the NMIPs to this honorable parliament, I humbly plea for your
support and approval to this Act. Fiyo bagi! MEUYAG!

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim MindanaoBangsamoro
Transition Authority BARMM-BTA) Interim Parliament

SECTION 1. Short Title. This Act shall be known as the “SOUTH UPI MUNICIPAL
HOSPITAL UPGRADE ACT”;

SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy. It is a policy of the Bangsamoro AutonomousRegion
in Muslim Mindanao as provided under Section 22, Article IX of RA 11054 otherwise known
as the Organic Law of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, to provide
for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish
by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that
the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of
excellent and affordable medical services;

SECTION 3. Upgrading of South Upi Municipal Hospital. The existing South Upi
Municipal Hospital is hereby upgraded from its present ten (10) bed capacity into licensed or
authorized twenty-five (25) bed capacity in accordance with the national standards set by the
Bureau of Health Facilities and Services under the Department of Health (DOH). The existing
building including its landscaping, medical and office equipment and other facilities, its
operating funds and personnel are also hereby upgraded to included the needed construction

SECTION 6. Appropriations. The necessary amount to carry out the provisions of this Act

SECTION 7. Implementing) Rules and Regulations. The BARMM MOH shall

SECTION 8. Separability Clause. In the event that any provision of this Act or the

by such declaration;
