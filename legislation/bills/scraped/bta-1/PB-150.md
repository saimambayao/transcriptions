“AN ACT ESTABLISHING AND CONSTRUCTING A BANGSAMORO MEDICAL

SCHOOL”

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, Bangsamoro
Transition Authority (BTA) Interim Parliament.

SECTION 1. Short Title. — This Act shall be known as the “BANGSAMORO MEDICAL

SCHOOL ACT OF 2021”.

SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy. — It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in
Muslim Mindanao as provided under Section 16, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise

known as Bangsamoro Organic

Law, that “It shall be a top priority of the Bangsamoro Government

to establish, maintain, and support a complete and integrated system of quality education, which
shall be a subsystem of the national education system. The Bangsamoro Government shall

SETION 3. The Establishment of Bangsamoro Medical School. — To implement the above-
mentioned policy, a medical school is hereby established to be known as the Bangsamoro
Medical School to be located in Cotabato City. The Bangsamoro Medical School shall be
constructed and start its operations under capacity and capability as prescribed by the licensing
standards and regulation of the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) of the Philippines, and
accredited by the Association of Philippine Medical Colleges. The Bangsamoro Medical School
may be upgraded thereafter into a higher facility for advance medical studies upon the evaluation
and recommendation by the Commission on Higher Education and by the Association of
Philippine Medical Colleges.

SECTION 4. Catchment area/coverage. — The Bangsamoro Medical School shall serve
primarily, but not limited to the Bangsamoro young people from different territories of BARMM
and may extend its services as additional to other existing medical schools in Mindanao.

SECTION 5. Objective/s — To provide the Bangsamoro young people with highly accessible,
affordable, comprehensive and| rewarding medical education and integrating in its system of
operations all pertinent and releyant provision under Republic Act No. 2382 otherwise known as
the Medical Act of 1959 as amended and CHED Memorandum Order No. 18, Series of 2016.

SECTION 6. Administration, Management and Organization. — The Bangsamoro Medical
School shall be under the administrative supervision of the Commission on Higher Education
(CHED) and by the Association of Medical Philippine Colleges who are hereby authorized to
determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the management and operations
of the facility pursuant to the “POLICIES, STANDARDS AND GUIDELINES FOR THE
DOCTOR OF MEDICINE (M.D.) PROGRAM” of CHED Memorandum Order No. 18, Series of
2016 and Medical Act of 1959 as amended.

SECTION 7. Appropriation. — The amount necessary to carry out the provision of this Act shall
be included in the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act. Thereafter, funds necessary for the
maintenance and operation of the medical school shall be provided in accordance with the
provisions of the Bangsamoro Appropriations Act and other pertinent laws.

SECTION 8. Implementing Rules and Regulations. — The Ministry on Basic, Higher and
Technical Education (MBHTE) shall promulgate the Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR)
of this Act within sixty (60) days after the enactment of this act, subject to the approval of the
Commission on Higher Education (CHED).

SECTION 9. Separability Clause. — In the event any provision of this act or the application of
such provision is declared invalid, the remainder of this act shall not be affected by such
declaration.

SECTION 10. Repealing Clause. — All laws and regulations under the Bangsamoro Autonomous
Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) government including those passed under the defunct
Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) government regarding the construction,
establishment and operations of medical school not consistent with this Act are hereby repealed.

SECTION 11. Effectivity. - This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following the
completion of its publication in a newspaper of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro
Autonomous Region.
