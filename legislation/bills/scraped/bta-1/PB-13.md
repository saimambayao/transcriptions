BTA BILL NO._|2

AN ACT DECLARING JULY THIRTEENTH AND EVERY YEAR THEREAFTER AS
HASHIM SALAMAT DAY, A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE AREAS OF
BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority

Section 1. HASHIM SALAMAT DAY. In order to remember the death anniversary of
Hashim Salamat and his teachings, principles and ideas be inculcated in the minds of the
Bangsamoro People, July thirteenth and every year thereafter be declared as Hashim Salamat
Day and be a special non-working holiday in the areas of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region
in Muslim Mindanao.

Section 2. Committee. The office of the Chief Minister may create a committee to plan and
implement the appropriate ceremonies or activities commemorating or in observance of the
Hashim Salamat Day.

Section 3. Dissemination. In order for the proper dissemination of this Law, the Office of
the Chief Minister shall instruct and make guidance to all agencies under the coverage of the
Bangsamoro Autonomous Government to make the proper information and guidance.

SECTION 4. Separability Clause. If any part of this law is held to be unconstitutional or
invalid, other parts which are not affected thereby shall remain valid.

SECTION 5. Repealing Clause. All Regional Laws, Rules or Regulations within the
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao which are inconsistent herewith are
deemed repealed, modified or amended accordingly.

SECTION 6. Effectivity. This law shall be effective within fifteen (15) from its publication in

a newspaper of regional or local circulation.

EXPLANATORY NOTE

Sheikh Hashim Salamat, the founding chairman of the Moro Islamic Liberation Front, the
Amir of the Mujahideen, a Bangsamoro, passed away and return to his Creator ALLAH swt on
July 13, 2003 (13 Jumada Al Awal 1324 Ab).

The Bill seeks to declare July 13 of every year as a special non-working holiday
throughout the Bangsamoro region, a Bangsamoro Holiday, to be known as Hashim Salamat

Day.

The Bill hopes to rightfully recognize the sacrifices, struggles, the immense contribution
of the late Sheikh Hashim Salamat in fighting for the Bangsamoro people’s right to self-
determination and for being an inspiration to the Bangsamoro people. Ustadj Salamat as he is
popularly known is a great thinker, peacemaker, philosopher and inspiration of the Bangsamoro
people in their quest for recognition, self-determination and fight for injustices. He is an icon not
only among the people in the Philippines but also in the International community. As a matter of
fact, he is a Muslim who successfully formed the first Islamic Liberation Front in Southeast Asia.

To honor the late Sheikh Hashim Salamat and to ensure that his legacy lives on, the
expedient passage of the Bill is strongly recommended. Hence, | am solemnly seeking your

support for this Bill.
