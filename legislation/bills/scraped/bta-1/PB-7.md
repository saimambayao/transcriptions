REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES
BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO
BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY
Cotabato City

Cabinet Bill No. |
a

Introduced by the Cabinet

AN ACT ADOPTING AN OFFICIAL FLAG OF THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS
REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO.

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority assembled:

SECTION 1. Adoption of the Official Flag. - Pursuant to Section 2 Article Il of RA
11054, there is hereby adopted an Official Flag of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region
in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), hereinafter referred to as the Bangsamoro Flag.

SECTION 2. Declaration of Policy. — |n the exercise of its right to self-governance,
the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao is free to adopt its
Bangsamoro Flag that is reflective of its peoples’ identity, history, heritage, struggles,
and aspirations.

SECTION 3. Design, Symbolism, and Interpretation. - The Bangsamoro Flag
shall be in the shape and dimension of a standard flag, three (3) feet by five-and-a-half
(5%) feet, and shall contain four (4) distinctive colors, a Crescent, a seven-rayed Star,

— and a Kris. The said colors and symbols shall be interpreted as follows:

a. The top side portion of the whole length of the Bangsamoro Flag shall
be in green (HEX CODE: #009966) and occupies one-third (1/3) of the
whole flag. It reflects the Islamic teachings and principles that majority
of the population subscribe to.

b. The middle portion of the length of the Bangsamoro Flag shall be in
white (HEX CODE: #FFFFFF), signifying peace, tranquility, and
righteousness. It shall likewise occupy one-third (1/3) of the whole flag.

c. The bottom side portion of the whole length of the Bangsamoro Flag
shall be in red (HEX CODE: #FF3333) and occupies the last one-third
(1/3) of the whole flag. It symbolizes the blood of the fallen mujahideen
who fought for the identity and self-determination of the Bangsamoro.

d. In the middle of the white portion shall be the Crescent and seven-
rayed Star in golden yellow (HEX CODE: #FFD700). The Crescent
symbolizes the principles that guided the Bangsamoro who struggled
for self-determination while the seven-rayed Star represents the five (5)
provinces of Maguindanao, Lanao del Sur, Basilan, Sulu, Tawi-Tawi,
the city of Cotabato, and geographic areas that compose the
Bangsamoro territory. Both the Crescent and Star shall be in golden
yellow to signify the bright future that awaits the Bangsamoro.

e. In the middle of the red portion shall be the Kris which symbolizes the
protection and resistance of the of the Bangsamoro against oppression,
tyranny, and injustice. The Kris shall be in white (HEX CODE:
#FFFFFF).

SECTION 4. Hoisting and Display of the Bangsamoro Flag. - The Bangsamoro
Flag shall always be regarded with the utmost respect and admiration and pursuant to
Section 2, Article Il of the RA No. 11054, the flag of the Republic shall always be
displayed alongside the Bangsamoro Flag.

All public and private schools, colleges and universities, public offices,
government-owned or controlled corporations, and other government instrumentalities
within the Bangsamoro are required to use and display the Bangsamoro Flag. Other
offices of the Bangsamoro that are outside its area of autonomy are likewise required to
use and display the Bangsamoro Flag.

Where necessary, the Bangsamoro Flag may also be used in ceremonial and
symbolic functions and activities, and the same may likewise be reproduced in small
sizes for purposes thereof.

SECTION 5. Effectivity. - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in a regional newspaper of general circulation in the Bangsamoro.

Approved,
