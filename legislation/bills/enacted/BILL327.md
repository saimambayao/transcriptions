**AN ACT DECLARING THE BURIAL SITE OF LATE HAJJI SALIK B. ABU “GHAZALI JAAFAR”, MORO ISLAMIC LIBERATION FRONT VICE CHAIR FOR POLITICAL AFFAIRS AND CHAIRMAN OF THE EXPANDED BANGSAMORO TRANSITION COMMISSION AS “GAZALI JAAFAR MEMORIAL SITE”, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES**

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in Parliament assembled:

**SECTION 1. Short Title.** This act shall be known as the *Ghazali Jaafar Memorial Site Act of 2024.*

**SECTION 2. Declaration as a Bangsamoro Memorial Site.** – The burial site of Hajji Salik B. Abu “Ghazali Jaafar” in Sitio Tapuken, Pigcalagan, Sultan Kudarat, Maguindanao del Norte, is hereby declared a Bangsamoro Memorial Site and shall be known as the “Ghazali Jaafar Memorial Site”.

**SECTION 3. Area.** The declared area shall be at least 1000 square meters, covering the memorial site in the Municipality of Sultan Kudarat, Maguindanao del Norte. The determination of the exact site shall be executed by a management board hereinafter created.

**SECTION 4. Preservation and Promotion.** The memorial site shall be preserved and reconstructed whenever necessary, to maintain its original form.

**SECTION 5. Recognition as a Historical Tourism and Heritage Site.** The *Ghazali Jaafar Memorial Site* shall form part of the tourism and heritage sites recognized by the relevant ministries, agencies, and offices in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

**SECTION 5. Creation of Memorial Site Management Board.** There shall be created a Ghazali Jaafar Memorial Site Management Board, hereinafter referred to as the “Board”.

The Board shall exercise supervision and control over the construction, preservation, and management of the aforesaid memorial site and shall issue the implementing rules and regulations to execute the provisions of this act within 90 days after its effectivity.

**SECTION 6. Composition of the Board.** The Board shall be composed of representatives of the following:

a. Bangsamoro Commission for the Preservation of Cultural Heritage (BCPCH) as the Chairperson;
b. Ministry of Basic, Higher, and Technical Education (MBHTE) as a member;
c. Ministry of Public Works (MPW) as a member;
d. Ministry of Trade, Investments and Tourism (MTIT) as a Member;
e. Bangsamoro Darul-Ifta’ (BDI) as a member; and
f. A representative from the family as a member.

**SECTION 7. Funding.** The necessary funds for the implementation of this Act shall be included in the annual appropriations of the concerned Bangsamoro Ministries, Offices, and Agencies.

An initial amount of Five Million Pesos (P5,000,000.00) is hereby appropriated for this purpose.

**SECTION 8. Repealing Clause.** All laws, decrees, orders, rules, and regulations, or any part thereof inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or amended accordingly.
