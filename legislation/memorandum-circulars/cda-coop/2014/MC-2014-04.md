SUBJECT : AMENDMENT TO MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2013-13, Series of

DATE : 16 AUGUST 2014

Upon the request of Secretary Joseph Abaya of the Department of Transportation and
Communication, Section 6 of Memorandum Circular No. 2013-12 as stated above is hereby
amended to read as follows:

“

XXX

Sec. 6. TRANSITORY PROVISION. A newly registered cooperative and those
intending to include transport services as one of its business activities, are given FIVE
(5) years from the date of registration/approval of amendment, within which to
comply with the minimum number of cooperatively owned units/vehicles as required
above.

The prescribed period for existing transportation service cooperative to
comply with the preceding Section is hereby extended to another three (3) years or
until February 7, 2018.

”

XXX.

Philippines.
