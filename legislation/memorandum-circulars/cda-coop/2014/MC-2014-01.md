Pursuant to Rule 8, Section 6 of the IRR and Article 53 of RA 9520 and due to the widespread
substantial damage and death in several areas brought about by Super Typhoon Yolanda,
the recent 7.2 magnitude earthquake, and public disorder in Zamboanga, there is a need to
provide regulatory relief to affected cooperatives.

Section 1. TITLE. This Memorandum Circular shall be known as the “Regulatory Relief for
Cooperatives Affected by 2013 Fortuitous Events”.

Section 2. COVERAGE. This Circular shall cover all affected cooperatives registered with the
Authority in the following areas:

Section 3. REGULATORY RELIEF. All affected cooperatives shall be relieved from th:
following:

a. Submission of Mandatory Reports

b. Payment of Fines and Penalties for failure to submit the required reports on
time except for those incurred prior to the occurrence of the fortuitous
event.

c. Submission of Requirements for the Issuance of Certificate of Good Standing
for Regular Purpose except payment of Certification Fee.

Section 4. REQUIREMENTS. The following are the requirements to be submitted to th2
Authority for the availment of Regulatory Relief and issuance of the Certificate of Good
Standing:

1. Letter of application for relief in the submission of regulatory requirements
signed by the Chairperson and/or the General Manager of the cooperative; _

2. Damage Assessment Report (Form 1); and

3. Board or General Assembly Resolution requesting availment of the
regulatory relief.

Section 5. FILING OF APPLICATION. All application for regulatory relief shall be filed with the
Authority where the affected cooperative is registered on or before April 30, 2014.

Section 6. ROLE OF EXTENSION OFFICE. The following shall be observed in the availment of
regulatory relief and securing of Certificate of Good Standing:

1. Upon receipt of the documentary requirements for the availment of
regulatory relief endorsed by the CDS in charge, the Supervision and
Examination Unit (SEU), within three (3) days, shall validate the documents.

2. Recommend to the Regional Director the approval/disapproval of the
application for regulatory relief.

3. Issuance of letter approving/denying the application for regulatory relief to
the cooperative (Form 2a or 2b) and Certificate of Good Standing.

Section7. APPLICABILITY PERIOD. The availment of the regulatory relief shall only cover the
operation of the affected cooperatives for calendar year 2013.
