CREDIT SURETY FUND COOPERATIVES (CSF COOPS)

DATE : 24 October 2019

To address the concern of the existing fifty-four (54) CSFs, particularly on the Board
Resolution required to be presented and submitted during the 2019 General Assembly meeting
for confirmation, sub-paragraph 2.a, paragraph 3, Section 3 of Memorandum Circular No.
2019-05 is hereby amended, to read as follows:

XXX
“If the authority to join or participate in the CSF Cooperative is by virtue only of a Board
Resolution, the applicant must execute an undertaking under oath, that said Board
Resolution shall be presented and submitted for confirmation in the immediately succeeding
General Assembly meeting. The original copy of the GA Resolution confirming the authority
of the cooperative to join or participate in the CSF Cooperative shall be submitted to the
Authority within thirty (30) days from the date of the GA meeting; ”

This Circular takes effect fifteen (15) days following the approval of the Board of
Administrators and the filing of the copy hereof to the Office of the National Administrative
Register (ONAR).

Approved by the CDA Board of Administrators on October 22, 2019 per BOA Resolution
No.491, S-2019.
