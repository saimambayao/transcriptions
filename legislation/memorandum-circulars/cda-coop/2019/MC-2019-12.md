DATE ; September 18, 2019

Pursuant to CDA Board Resolution No. 456, dated September 9, 2019, with
the objective of conferring back the temporary recalled jurisdiction of CDA
Extension Offices to register transport cooperatives to exercise again said
jurisdiction within their respective area, the Authority hereby amends
Memorandum Circular No. 2018-04 entitled as, “Registration of Transport
Cooperative/ Transportation Service Cooperatives by the CDA Central Office,
Registration Division” particularly as provided for in Section 5 thereof.

Section 5 shall be read as follows:
Section 5. After the verification /validation, said Extension Office shall do the

final evaluation and issuance of the Certificate of Registration. Should the
applicant completely satisfy the requirements needed.

This Circular shall take effect fifteen (15) days upon receipt by the Office of
the National Administrative Register (ONAR).

APPROVED per CDA Board of Administrators on October 22, 2019, per BOA
Resolution No. 464, Series of 2019.
