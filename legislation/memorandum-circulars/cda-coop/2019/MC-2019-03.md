SUBJECT : GUIDELINES GOVERNING THE ONLINE PAYMENT OF CDA
REGISTRATION FEES THROUGH THE LANDBANK
LINK.BIZPORTAL

In accordance with Republic Act (RA} No. 8972, otherwise known as the
"Electronic Commerce Act of 2000", and Republic Act 11032 or the Ease of
Doing Business and Efficient Government Service Delivery Act of 2018, the
Authority undertakes the modernization of its facility to provide fast and
efficient alternative payment collection services to its clientele. Thus, the
Authority hereby promulgates the following Guidelines.

Section 1. Purpose

This Guidelines is issued to prescribe the procedure on how to avail the online
payment facility for registration fees through LANDBANK Link.BizPortal that
would allow clients/cooperatives clients to transact business and to pay the
necessary fees due to the Authority via the internet using any of the payment
options being offered by LANDBANK automated teller machines, debit and
credit cards issued by Land Bank of the Philippines and any other banks
recognized by BancNet.

Section 2. Coverage

This Guidelines shall cover payment of the following:

Section 3. Definition of Terms
As used in this Guidelines, the following terms shall mean:
3.1. Authority-shall refer to the Cooperative Development Authority.

3.2. LANDBANK - a wholly-owned government financial institution and an
Authorized Government Depository Bank (AGDB) duly organized and
existing under and by virtue of R.A. 3844.

3.3. LANDBANK Link.BizPortal — is e-Payment facility that allows clients
to pay for products and services via the Internet from both the
government and private institutions/sectors.

3.4. Cooperative Name Reservation Fee- refers to the assessed amount
required to be paid on the reservation of proposed cooperative name.

3.5. Registration Fee -refers to the assessed amount required to be paid
for the registration of new cooperatives, consolidation of cooperatives,
establishment of branch/satellite offices and for recognition of
laboratory cooperatives.

3.6. Amendment Fee- refers to the assessed amount required to be paid
for the amendment of Articles of Cooperation and By-Laws and in case
of merger of cooperatives.

3.7. Legal Research Fund (LRF) Fee- an amount equivalent to one percent
(1%) of the fee imposed but in no case lower than ten (10) pesos shall
be collected on every fee for all applications for registration and
amendments, petitions and complaints filed which is imposed by the
Authority in the exercise and discharge of its regulatory functions.

3.8. CoopRIS — a web-based information system that provides CDA
registration officers a computerized platform for cooperative name
reservation, evaluation and approval of application for new registration,
amendments and recognition of branch/satellite/laboratory.

3.7. BancNet — a Philippine-based interbank network connecting the
Automated Teller Machine (ATM) networks of local and offshore banks.

3.8. Order of Payment — a document ordering the payment of money for
the assessed amount of fees due to the Authority.

3.9. Payment Confirmation Slip/Receipt -— an electronic generated
documents which serves as proof of successful payment.

Section 4. How and when to access the Online Payment System

The LANDBANK Link.Biz Portal may be accessed through the link located at
the CDA Website or the e-Banking menu of the LANDBANK website
via www.landbank.com.

The LANDBANK Link.Biz Portal may be accessed from 12:01AM to 11:00PM,
Philippine Standard Time (PST), 7 days a week including holidays, subject to
service periods, maintenance time, computer, telecommunication, electrical
or network failure, and/or any other reasons beyond the control of the Bank.

Section 5. Procedure for Online Payment
5.1 Cooperative Name Reservation

Step 1. The client/cooperative may reserve cooperative name on-line
through the www.cda.gov.ph. After successful reservation, the client
must take note of the system generated reservation number.

Step 2. The client/cooperative should advise the concerned
Extension/Central Office within 24 hours from the time the
cooperative name reservation was successfully made. The CDA
Extension /Central Office must process the same immediately.

Step 3. Once approved, the client may proceed to the CDA website
to verify the status of the name reservation or view/print the order
of payment.

Step 4. After the cooperative name has been reserved, the
client/cooperative then proceeds to LANDBANK Link.BizPortal
through the link located at the CDA website or the e-Banking menu
of the LANDBANK website via www.landbank.com. The client must
select the transaction type (Cooperative Name Reservation), payment
gateway option and fill-out the required mandatory fields such as
amount, reservation number, name of applicant, name of the
proposed cooperative and order of payment number. After the
successful transaction, a payment confirmation slip is generated.

Step 5. After verification of the payment, the CDA Extension/Central
Office shall send through e-mail the Cooperative Name Reservation
Notice (CNRN) to the client/cooperative.

5.2. New Registration and Consolidation

Step 1. Once the application is approved, the client may view/ print
the order of payment in the CoopRIS.

Step 2. The client/cooperative then proceeds to LANDBANK
Link.BizPortal through the link located at the CDA Website of the e-
Banking menu of the LANDBANK website via www.landbank.com.
The client must select the transaction type (Registration-New,
Registration-Consolidation), payment gateway option and fill-out the
required mandatory fields such as amount, reservation number,
name of applicant, name of the proposed cooperative and order of
payment number. After the successful transaction, a debit
confirmation slip is generated.

Step 3. After verification of the payment, the CDA Extension /Central
Office shall print the Certificate of Registration and notify the
client /cooperative.

5.3. Amendment of Articles of Cooperation and By-laws, Merger
and/or Division of Cooperatives and Recognition of
Branch/Satellite Offices/Laboratory Cooperatives

Step 1. Once the application is approved, the client may view/ print
the order of payment in the CoopRIS.

Step 2.The client/cooperative then proceeds to LANDBANK
Link.BizPortal through the link located at the CDA website or the e-
Banking menu of the LANDBANK website via www.landbank.com.
The client must select the transaction type {(Registration-Amendment,
Registration-Merger, Registration-Division, Registration-Branch,
Registration-Satellite, Registration-Laboratory,), payment gateway
option and fill-out the required mandatory fields such as amount,
registration number, name of applicant, name of the cooperative and
order of payment number. After the successful transaction, a debit
confirmation slip is generated.

Step 3. After verification of the payment, the CDA Extension /Central
Office shall print the Certificate of Registration of Amendments,
Merger and/or Division, Certificate of Authority of Branch, and
Letter of Authority of Satellite and Certificate of Recognition of
Laboratory Cooperative.

Section 6. Proof of Successful Transaction

A Payment Confirmation Receipt/Slip is generated for every successful
transaction. This may be printed by the client/cooperative.

Section 7. Transaction Fee

Transaction fee shall be charged/collected by LANDBANK from CDA clients
regardless of the amount of transactions:

1. Transaction fee of P10.00 per approved transaction shall be charged to
the Payor/Client; and

2. An additional P10.00 transaction fee will be charged by BancNet for the
use of its Internet Payment Gateway Facility.
Section 8. Status of Transaction.
The client/cooperative may verify the status of its application through
www.cda.gov.ph under the Reservation Cooperative Name Tab using its
Reservation / Registration Number as reference number.

Section 8. Repealing Clause

All circulars, regulations, issuances or parts thereof, inconsistent with any of
the provisions of this Circular are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

Section 9. Effectivity

This Guidelines shall become effective upon its approval by the Board of
Administrators and fifteen (15) days after registration with the office of the
National Administrative Register (ONAR).

Approved by the CDA Board of Administrators on December 18, 2018 per BOA
Resolution No. 627, S-2018.
