Republic of the Philippines
. OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT
COOPERATIVE DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY

M.C. NO. 03, S-1996
MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR

TO: ALL REGISTERED AND CONFIRMED COOPERATIVES
COOPERATIVE FEDERATIONS AND UNIONS
LABORATORY COOPERATIVES

SUBJECT: NOTICE OF SUBMISSION OF ANNUAL REPORTS
AND AUDITED FINANCIAL STATEMENTS

YOU ARE HEREBY NOTIFIED THAT UNDER ARTICLE 54, RA 6938,
YOU ARE MANDATED TO SUBMIT YOUR RESPECTIVE ANNUAL REPORTS
AND AUDITED FINANCIAL STATEMENTS WITHIN SIXTY (60) DAYS FROM
THE CLOSE OF YOUR COOPFRATIVE'S FISCAL YEAR.

YOUR ANNUAL REPORTS AND AUDITED FINANCIAL STATEMENTS
IN QUADRUPLICATE SHALL BE SUBMITTED TO THE COOPERATIVE
DEVELOPMENT SPECIALIST (CDS II) ASSIGNED IN YOUR RESPECTIVE
CONGRESSIONAL DISTRICT.

NON-SUBMISSION OF YOUR ANNUAL REPORT AND AUDITED
FINANCIAL STATEMENTS SHALL BE GROUNDS FOR REVOCATION OF
YOUR AUTHORITY TO OPERATE AS A COOPERATIVE.

FOR COMPLIANCE.

FEBRUARY 12, 1996, QUEZON CITY, METRO MANILA.

EDNA E. ABERILLA
Chairperson
