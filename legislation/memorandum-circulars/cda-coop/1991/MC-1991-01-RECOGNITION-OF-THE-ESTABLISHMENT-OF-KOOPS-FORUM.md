SUBJECT :

RECOGNITION OF THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE KOOPS FORUM

am PREFATORY STATEMENT

1.2

The Cooperative Leaders present at a meeting held last
March 7, 1991, at which existing national cooperative
federations and unions were represented, have, by a
consensus, agreed to the formation of a Cooperative
Advisory Group, henceforth to be known as "KOOPS
FORUM" .

These cooperative leaders, by the same consensus, have
agreed to meet once a month, to discuss issues and

concerns affecting cooperatives, to advise the
Cooperative Deve lopment Authority on sectoral
developments, to generally act as an unstructured
consultative body for cooperative development.

It was further agreed that the agenda for the said
meetings shall be prepared by the Cooperative
Development Authority on the basis of the following:

o CDA issuances, programs and projects

o Issues and concerns raised by the cooperatives

o Coop-related matters presented by other sectors.

II. STATEMENT OF UNDERTAKING

2.1

The Cooperative Development Authority, cognizant of:

o The need for a continuing partnership between the
cooperatives and the government agency tasked with
the promotion and development of this sector;

o The need for regular and substantive consultation
and exchange of information between the cooperatives
and the CDA.

o The need for an unstructured but representative
group to ventilate issues and concerns of the
cooperative sector;

hereby recognizes the establishment of the KOOPS FORUM.

The Cooperative Development Authority hereby undertakes:

o the consideration of the issues and concerns raised
during the meetings of said KOOPS FORUM.
