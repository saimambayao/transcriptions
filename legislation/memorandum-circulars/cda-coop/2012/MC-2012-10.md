stipulated in Section 7 of MC 2011-01.

Pool of Trainers

Any new member of the trainers’ pool must possess the minimum qualifications and

Evaluation of application

Approval of the renewal for accreditation shall be subject to the Authority's power to
review and evaluate their training activities and qualification on the assessment to be
provided by the CDA.

Grounds for non-approval of renewal

The following shall be considered:

1. Non-conduct of at least three (3) training within the accreditation period;
