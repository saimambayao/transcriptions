MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2022-_ 10

Series of 2022.
TO ALL REGIONAL DIRECTORS
SUBJECT : ORGANIZATION OF THE LOCAL MONITORING COMMITTEE FOR |

TRANSPORT COOPERATIVES

Pursuant to the provision of RA 9520, Rule V Transportation Service Cooperatives
particularly on Section 8 Monitoring Committee — All CDA Regional Extension Offices are
hereby ordered to organize/create their Local Monitoring Committee for Transport
Cooperatives.

Section 1. Legal Basis
Section 8. Monitoring Committee.

A monitoring committee has been created, composed of a representative
from the Authority, who shall be the Ex-Officio Chairperson thereof, and
representatives of the LTO, LTFRB, OTC, LGU, other concerned
government agencies as may be necessary, and the National Federation
of Transport Cooperatives as members.

Likewise, a Local Monitoring Committee has been created in all CDA extension to
facilitate the monitoring of these Transportation Service Cooperatives.

The permanent Secretariat shall come from the Authority.
Section 2. General Guidelines

The Monitoring Committee shall be composed of the following:
e CDA Regional Director as Chairperson
e OTC Representative
e LTFRB Regional Director or his duly Authorized Representative
e LTO Representative
e MARINA Representative

e DILG Regional Director or his duly authorized representative — representing the
LGUs of the region
