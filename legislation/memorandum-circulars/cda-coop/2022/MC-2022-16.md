Section 1. TITLE

This Memorandum Circular shall be known as the Revised Guidelines of the Gawad
Parangal of the Cooperative Development Authority.

Section 2. LEGAL BASIS

Republic Act 11364, Cooperative Development Authority Charter of 2019 Section 4 (1)
Grant awards, recognition, and incentives to cooperatives, cooperative leaders, and partners.

Section 3. . PROGRAM DESCRIPTION

The Gawad Parangal is the Cooperative Development Authority's (CDA) awards and
recognition program for exemplary cooperatives that showcase their best practices, programs,
and good governance.

Through this program, the CDA celebrates and honors cooperatives that have
contributed to the growth and development of their members and communities.

The awardees are chosen through a national search program and evaluated through
a multi-level screening process. Cooperative finalists are carefully selected and winners are
recognized in a very prestigious awarding ceremony.

Beyond the awards, winners will become models of cooperative best practices,
programs, and good governance that will serve as benchmarks and standards of cooperative
performance.

SECTION 6. PERIOD COVERED AS BASIS FOR ASSESSMENT

For purposes of this awards and recognition program, the basis for assessment shall
be the performance of the cooperatives in the previous year. Performance for the past years
of operation may also be subject for validation as deemed appropriate or necessary.

SECTION 7. PRE-QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS

The CDA Regional Extension Offices shall identify the candidates based on the
following pre-qualification criteria:

1. Must be a duly registered cooperative with the CDA;

2. Has been operating for at least three (3) years for Micro Cooperatives, and
five (5) years for Small, Medium, and Large Cooperatives. Further, the
cooperative should not have been in the same category for more than ten
(10) years;

3. Has a valid Certificate of Compliance issued for three (3) years, including
the current year, for Micro Cooperatives (e.g. 2020-2022) and for five (5)
years, including the current year, for Small, Medium, and Large
Cooperatives (e.g. 2018-2022);

4. All CY 2021 officers must have completed the mandatory trainings
prescribed under MC. No. 2015-09;
