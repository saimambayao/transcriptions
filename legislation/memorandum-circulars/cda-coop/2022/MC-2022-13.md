MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2022-__1_3__
Series of 2022.
SUBJECT: EXTENSION OF THE DEADLINE FOR THE SUBMISSION OF FY 2021
REQUIRED REPORTS BY THE COOPERATIVES AND EXTENSION OF
PERIOD OF VALIDITY OF CERTIFICATE OF COMPLIANCE (COC) ISSUED
FOR CY 2021
In view of the migration to a new system called the Cooperative Assessment
Information System (CAIS), in the on-line submission of the required reports, the CDA Board
of Directors in its Regular Board Meeting held on April 20, 2022 resolved to approve the
extension of the deadline in the submission of required reports by the cooperatives up to June
30, 2022 without the imposition of penalty charges.
In relation thereto, all Certificates of Compliance (COC) issued by the Authority which
will expire on April 30, 2022 shall still be valid and subsisting until June 30, 2022.
However, with regard to the submission of the Annual Tax Incentives Report (ATIR)
(Annex “A”) Form as mandated by the Tax Incentives Management and Transparency Act
(TIMTA) the same shall still be submitted on or before April 30, 2022.
All concerned CDA Extension Offices shall endeavor to inform and disseminate this
Circular to cooperatives concerned within their jurisdiction.
This Circular shall take effect fifteen (15) days following the completion of its
publication in the Official Gazette and the filing of the copy thereof with the Office of the
National Administrative Register (ONAR).
Approved by the CDA Board of Directors on April 20, 2022 per Board Resolution No.
3311, S-2022.
