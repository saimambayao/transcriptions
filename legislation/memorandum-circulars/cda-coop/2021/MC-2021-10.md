SUBJECT : SUSPENSION OF THE IMPOSITION OF MONETARY
PENALTIES FOR LATE SUBMISSION OF REQUIRED
REPORTS COVERING CALENDAR/FISCAL YEAR 2020

The IATF has issued Resolutions Nos. 121, S-2021, dated June 14, 2021 and
127-E, S- 2021, dated July 15, 2021 imposing community quarantines which
resulted in restricted mobility. Understandably, many cooperatives were not able
to submit to the Authority the required reports, hence, the Authority hereby
promulgates this Guidelines for late submission of required reports.

Section 1. Legal Basis

Section 4(j) of Republic Act No. 11364, also known as the "Cooperative
Development Authority Charter of 2019," to quote:

“Section 4. Powers, Functions and Responsibilities. — The Authority shall
have the following powers and functions:

@) Prescribe and collect reasonable fees, fines or charges in the
performance of its registration and regulatory functions,”

Section 2. Scope and Coverage

This Guidelines covers all cooperatives who failed to submit the required reports
for CY2020 last June 30, 2021.

Section 3. General Guidelines

1. The Authority shall not impose fines and other monetary penalties for non-
submission or late submission of required reports for CY 2020 until October 15,
2021.

2. For cooperatives which have been issued a Notice/Show Cause Order for
hon- submission of required reports, there shall be a moratorium on the
payment of penalties incurred during the previous years as well as those
incurred from August 4, 2020 to December 31, 2020. Payment of penalties shall
be in accordance with CDA Memorandum Circular No. 2020-27, S-2020. :
