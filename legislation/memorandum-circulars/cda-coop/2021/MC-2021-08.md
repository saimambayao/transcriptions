SUBJECT: EXTENSION OF DEADLINE FOR THE SUBMISSION OF
REQUIRED REPORTS BY THE COOPERATIVES FOR FY 2020

In view of the restricted movement of people nationwide due to the pandemic,
the CDA Board of Directors, in its Regular Board Meeting held on April 8, 2021, resolved
to extend the deadline for the submission of required reports by cooperatives up to
June 30, 2021.

However, with regard to the submission of the Annual Tax Incentives Report
(ATIR) (Annex “A”) Form as mandated by the Tax Incentives Management and
Transparency Act (TIMTA), the same must still be submitted on April 30, 2021
pursuant to Section 4 of CDA Memorandum Circular No. 2019-06, Series of 2019,
quoted as follows:
