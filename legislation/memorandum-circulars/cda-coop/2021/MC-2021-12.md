SUBJECT : AMENDMENT TO STEP 8, SECTION 5 OF MEMORANDUM
CIRCULAR NO. 2020-20, S-2020 :

To facilitate the issuance of the Certificates of Compliance to newly registered
cooperatives under the Electronic Cooperative Registration Information System (E-CoopRIS),
step 8 of Section 5 of Memorandum Circular No. 2020-20, Series of 2020, titled “Guidelines
Governing the Registration of Cooperatives Through the Electronic Registration Information
System (E-CoopRIS)’, is hereby amended to read as follows:

“Section 5. Procedure for Online Registration of New Cooperative
Step 8. Issuance of Certificate of Registration and Certificate of Compliance
The Authority, through the Registration Division, shall issue the Certificate of
Registration and the Certificate of Compliance upan contirmation, in case of online
payment, or upon the issuance of official receipt, if paynintt is through the CDA
treasury.”
This Circular shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following the completion of its

publication in the Official Gazette and the filing of a copy thereof with the Office of the National
Administrative Register (ONAR).

Approved per BOD Resolution No. 384, S-2021, dated August 25, 2021.
