RE: FREE LEGAL ASSISTANCE TO COOPERATIVES

DULY REGISTERED UNDER R.A. 6938

Article 63(10) of Republic Act No. 6938 (The

Cooperative Code of the Philippines) provides that
cooperatives duly registered under the Cooperative
Development Authority shall enjoy the privilege of being
represented by the provincial or city prosecutor or the‘
Office of the Solicitor General, free of charge, except
when the adverse party ia the Republic of the Philip-

pines.
In this connection, you are hereby directed:

. ‘

a. to represent the cooperative seeking
your services in all civil actions and spe-
cial proceedings wherein the cooperative
concerned or any official thereof, in his
official capacity, is a party, except when
such cooperative is already represented by
the Office of the Solicitor General.

b. to render opinion in writing on any
question of law when requested by the cooper-
ative, or by its responsible officer;

c. to extend legal assistance in cases
involving the internal affairs of the cooper-
ative which require the services of a lawyer;
