# MC 2024-18 | AMENDMENTS TO AGRI-MACHINERY GRANT GUIDELINES

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-18**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: AMENDMENT TO SECTIONS 3, 5, 6, AND 7 OF MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-13 RE: GUIDELINES ON THE GRANT OF AGRI-MACHINERY TO MICRO AND SMALL AGRICULTURAL COOPERATIVES**

To ensure the success of the mechanization program, the following sections of MC No. 2024-13 are hereby amended:

**SECTION 3. QUALIFIED BENEFICIARIES**
*   **Selection Process**: When there are more than ten (10) qualified applicants, a **first-come-first-serve** basis shall be applied. The first 10 to comply with minimum requirements shall be the beneficiaries.
*   **Replacement**: The Regional Director has the discretion to replace a disqualified cooperative from the tentative list with another qualified agricultural cooperative.

**SECTION 5. DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS**
*   **Business Classification**: Articles and Bylaws must show the cooperative's engagement in rice/corn production.
*   **Alternative Proof**: If classification is not in the Bylaws, a certification signed by the Secretary (under Board Resolution) must state that at least **thirty percent (30%) of its members** are engaged in rice/corn activities (production, processing, trading, or marketing).

**SECTION 6. PROCEDURES FOR RELEASE**
1. **Board Approval**: The CDA REO shall submit the list of eligible recipients to the CDA Board for **consideration and approval** (supported by a Board Resolution).
2. **MOA and DDA**: Once approved and documented, the CDA REO shall prepare both a **Memorandum of Agreement (MOA)** and a **Deed of Donation and Acceptance (DDA)**.

**SECTION 7. RECORDING OF THE RELEASE (Accounting Entries)**
*   To record acquisition: Debit `PPE-Agri Machineries` / Credit `Cash-NT-MDS`.
*   To record release: Debit `Subsidies-Others` / Credit `PPE-Agri Machineries`.

**SECTION 8. RESPONSIBILITIES AND RESTRICTIONS**
*   **Non-Disposal**: The cooperative shall not dispose of or sell the agri-machinery for a period of **five (5) years**.
*   **Misrepresentation**: Fraudulent applications will lead to the return of the machinery or payment of its equivalent value.
*   **Internal Policy**: Cooperatives must have an Internal Policy/Operation Policy covering the name of the operator, fees, and maintenance costs.

---

### **TEMPLATES INCLUDED IN THE CIRCULAR**
1. **Financial and Technical Evaluation Form**: Checklist for registration, COC, AFS (3 years), and non-relationship affidavit.
2. **Memorandum of Agreement (MOA)**: Detailing the responsibilities of CDA and the Cooperative.
3. **Acknowledgement Receipt**: For the physical transfer of machinery.
4. **Deed of Donation and Acceptance (DDA)**: Formal legal transfer of ownership from DONOR (CDA) to DONEE (Cooperative).
