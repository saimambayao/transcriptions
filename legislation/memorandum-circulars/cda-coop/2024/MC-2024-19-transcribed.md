# MC 2024-19 | REFERRAL SYSTEM FOR INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY (IP) DISPUTES

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-19**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: PROCEDURE FOR A REFERRAL SYSTEM OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY DISPUTES FROM THE COOPERATIVE DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY (CDA) TO THE INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY OFFICE – BUREAU OF LEGAL AFFAIRS’ ALTERNATIVE DISPUTE RESOLUTION SERVICES (BLA-ADRS)**

This Circular provides guidance to cooperatives and their members regarding the referral of intellectual property (IP) disputes to the IPOPHL for Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR).

**Section 2. Legal Bases**
*   **RA 11364 (CDA Charter)** — Section 4(a, u) on formulating regulations and adopting dispute resolution mechanisms.
*   **CDA-IPOPHL MOA (June 16, 2023)** — Agreement to collaborate on an ADR Referral system for IP disputes.

**Section 3. Scope and Coverage**
Applies to all cooperatives, their officers, and members with IP-related disputes.

**Section 4. Procedure**

1. **Receipt of Complaint (Rule 4.1)**: Filed with the Legal Division (Head Office) or Legal Section (Regional Extension Office).
2. **Initial Determination**: The Regional Legal Section determines if the matter involves IP. If yes, it is transmitted to the Legal Affairs Service (CDA Head Office) with the completed **Referral Form (Annex A)**.
3. **Evaluation (Rule 4.2)**: The Legal Division confirms if the dispute is IP-related.
4. **Reference Number (Rule 4.3)**: Assigned in the format: `Case No. 001-2024-IPM`.
5. **Briefing (Rule 4.4)**: CDA briefs parties on the benefits, voluntary nature, procedures, and fees of the IPOPHL-CDA ADR Referral System.
6. **Referral (Rule 4.5)**:
    *   If parties agree: CDA formally submits documentation to `blaadrs@ipophil.gov.ph`.
    *   If parties disagree: CDA evaluates if it has jurisdiction under the Omnibus Rules. If it's a purely IP dispute, parties are advised to file directly with IPOPHL.
7. **ADR Conference/Conduct (Rule 4.6 & 4.7)**: Handled by IPOPHL BLA-ADRS according to the parties' chosen ADR service.
8. **Closure (Rule 4.8)**: IPOPHL informs CDA of the outcome (Settlement Agreement or Arbitral Award).

**Section 6. Monitoring**
Legal affairs will maintain a database of IP-related matters referred to IPOPHL. Guidelines are subject to review every three (3) years.

**Section 7. Effectivity**
Takes effect fifteen (15) days after approval and filing with ONAR/Official Gazette.

### **ANNEX A: IPOPHL-CDA ADR REFERRAL FORM (Summary)**
*   **Data Privacy Consent**: Acknowledgment of processing personal data per RA 10173.
*   **Details Required**: Name of Parties, Cooperative, Business Address, Contact Details, and Competent ID.
*   **Dispute Details**: Narrative of the dispute and nature of the IP right involved.
*   **Evaluator Section**: Evaluation results and status of the referral.
