# MC 2024-14 | INCENTIVES FOR MICRO & SMALL COOP MERGERS

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-14**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: AMENDMENT TO SECTION 6, PARAGRAPH (2) OF MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2023-18: GUIDELINES GOVERNING THE IMPLEMENTATION OF A SPECIAL PROGRAM ON MERGER AND CONSOLIDATION FOR MICRO AND SMALL COOPERATIVES AND GRANTING INCENTIVES THEREOF**

To facilitate the success of organizational growth and financial capability through consolidation, Section 6, paragraph (2) of MC No. 2023-18 is hereby amended to provide a specific incentives matrix:

**INCENTIVES MATRIX**

| Description | Required Number of Constituent Cooperatives | Corresponding Incentives |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Merger or Consolidation (Tier 1) | Not less than Three (3) micro and small cooperatives | **Php 500,000.00** |
| Merger or Consolidation (Tier 2) | At least Five (5) micro and small cooperatives | **Php 750,000.00** |
| Merger or Consolidation (Tier 3) | More than five (5) micro and small cooperatives | **Php 1,000,000.00** |

**ADDITIONAL INCENTIVE**
In the case of a registered merged/consolidated cooperative with an initial three (3) constituent cooperatives, such cooperative may qualify for an **additional Php 300,000.00** if it enters again into a merger or consolidation with another two (2) micro and small cooperatives within the same year of its registration.

This Circular takes effect fifteen (15) days after publication in a newspaper of general circulation and filing with the ONAR.
