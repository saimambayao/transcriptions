# MC 2024-06 | REASSIGNMENT OF CASES DUE TO RD INHIBITION

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-06**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: GUIDELINES IN THE REASSIGNMENT OF CASES TO THE DEPUTY ADMINISTRATOR FOR LEGAL AFFAIRS SERVICE UPON INHIBITION OF THE REGIONAL DIRECTOR (RD) AS ADJUDICATOR IN THE ADJUDICATION OF CASES RELATIVE TO THE COOPERATIVE DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY (CDA/AUTHORITY) OMNIBUS RULES OF PROCEDURES**

These guidelines address the gap in the CDA Omnibus Rules regarding the reassignment of cases when a Regional Director inhibits themselves from a case within their original jurisdiction.

**SECTION 3. PURPOSE**
To avoid the appearance of impropriety or partiality due to conflicts of interest, cases where the RD inhibits (voluntarily or involuntarily) will be reassigned to the Deputy Administrator (DA) for Legal Affairs Service (LAS) to ensure operational efficiency and neutral adjudication.

**SECTION 6. AUTOMATIC REASSIGNMENT**
Since RDs from other regions lack jurisdiction over complaints outside their assigned area, and to prevent docket clogging, all cases involving the inhibition of an RD as adjudicator shall automatically be reassigned to the **DA for LAS**.

**SECTION 7. HIERARCHY IN HANDLING CASES**
1.  **LAS-AD**: Handles cases within the original jurisdiction of the REOs and the Head Office.
2.  **DA for LAS**: Handles re-assigned cases due to the **inhibition** of the original adjudicator/hearing officer.
3.  **CDA Board**: Handles all **appealed cases**.

**SECTION 8. MISCELLANEOUS PROVISION**
Parties retain their option to appeal the decisions of the DA for LAS to the CDA Board, ensuring that the Board’s power to handle appealed cases remains undiminished.
