# MC 2024-21 | ALLOCATION AND UTILIZATION OF CSF TRUST FUND INCOME

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-21**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: GUIDELINES ON THE ALLOCATION, UTILIZATION, AND ACCOUNTING TREATMENT OF A PORTION OF THE TRUST FUND INCOME OF THE CREDIT SURETY FUND (CSF) COOPERATIVES**

While operating expenses of CSF Cooperatives are generally sourced from service fees, some coops face insufficient funds for auditing, promotion, and training. This MC provides guidelines for using a portion of the trust fund income for such expenses.

**Section 6. Allocated Percentage of Utilization**
A maximum of **thirty percent (30%)** of the trust fund income generated in the immediately preceding year (as reflected in the AFS) may be allocated for administrative and operating expenses in the current year.

**Section 8. Period of Utilization**
CSF Cooperatives are authorized to implement this allocation for a period of **five (5) years** from the effectivity of this MC or from their date of registration.

**Section 9. Bylaws Requirement**
The allocation and its specific percentage must be explicitly stated in the **Bylaws**. If not present, the cooperative must amend its Bylaws and correspondingly amend its Trust Agreement (Section 10).

**Section 11. Accounting Treatment (Summary of Entries)**

1. **Recording Trust Income (Preceding Year-End)**:
    *   Debit: `Trust Deposit for Surety`
    *   Credit: `Imputed Income`
2. **Closing to Restricted Capital**:
    *   Debit: `Imputed Income`
    *   Credit: `Restricted Capital for Surety`
3. **Allocation for Subsidy (Beginning of Year)**:
    *   Debit: `Restricted Capital for Surety`
    *   Credit: `Restricted Capital for Surety-Subsidy` (Max 30%)
4. **Withdrawal (One-time)**:
    *   Debit: `Cash in Bank` (Regular Account)
    *   Credit: `Trust Deposit for Surety`
5. **Charging Expenses**: Standard debit to expense accounts (e.g., `Professional Fees`, `Trainings`) and credit to `Cash in Bank`.

**Section 12. Sanctions**
*   **1st Offense**: Fine of **Php 5,000.00** per erring officer.
*   **2nd Offense**: Non-issuance of COC for the succeeding year.
*   **3rd Offense**: Dissolution for willful violation.
