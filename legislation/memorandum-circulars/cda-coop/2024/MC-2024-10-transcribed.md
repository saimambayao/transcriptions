# MC 2024-10 | REVISED COOPERATIVE INSPECTION GUIDELINES

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-10**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: REVISED GUIDELINES ON THE CONDUCT OF INSPECTION OF COOPERATIVES**

Pursuant to RA 11364, the Cooperative Development Authority (CDA) establishes these revised guidelines to ensure the safe and sound operation of cooperatives through regular inspections.

**SECTION 3. SCOPE AND COVERAGE**
Covers all cooperatives EXCEPT:
a. Newly registered (less than 1 year of operation).
b. Cooperatives under dissolution, liquidation, or cancellation.
c. Delisted cooperatives.
d. Cooperatives with an active Show Cause Order (SCO) as of January 15.
e. Credit Surety Fund Cooperatives (governed by separate guidelines).

**SECTION 5. MANNER OF INSPECTION**
*   **Micro and Small Coops**: 50% are inspected **Onsite** and 50% **Off-site** annually, alternating each year.
*   **Medium and Large Coops**: **100% Onsite** inspection every year.

**SECTION 6. ONSITE INSPECTION PROCESS**
1. **Notice**: Sent at least **ten (10) calendar days** prior.
2. **Opening Conference**: Discussion of objectives and scope.
3. **Inspection Proper**: Officers must provide all required reports and documents.
4. **Exit Conference**: Findings are discussed; cooperatives may refute findings with evidence on the spot.
5. **Final Report**: Signed by the Chairperson and GM.
6. **Management Response**: Due within **fifteen (15) calendar days** from receipt of the report.

**SECTION 8. OFF-SITE INSPECTION PROCESS**
1. **Notice to Submit**: Cooperative has 15 days to submit proofs of compliance/documents.
2. **Validator Review**: CDA validates submissions within **seven (7) working days**.
3. **Report Issuance**: Final report sent via email/mail.
4. **Response**: 15 days for a management response.

**SECTION 6.5 & 8.4 POST-INSPECTION ACTIONS**
*   **Order to Comply**: Issued if compliance is partial or unacceptable.
*   **Show Cause Order (SCO)**: Issued for failure to comply with previous orders or for grave findings.
*   **Examination/Investigation**: Triggered by fraud or need for deeper scrutiny.

**SECTION 9. PROHIBITED ACTS**
As per Art. 140 of RA 9520, the following are punishable:
*   Refusal to furnish information or reports.
*   Providing false or misleading documents.
*   Hindering an authorized inspector or refusing inspection.
*   Failure to comply with written instructions or orders from the CDA.
