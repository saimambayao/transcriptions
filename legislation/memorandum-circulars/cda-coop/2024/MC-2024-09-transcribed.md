# MC 2024-09 | ONE-TIME AMNESTY FOR DISSOLVED & NON-COMPLIANT COOPS

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-09**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: ONE-TIME GRANT OF AMNESTY FOR DISSOLVED COOPERATIVES AND COOPERATIVES WITH PENDING INVOLUNTARY DISSOLUTION CASES OR NON-COMPLIANT COOPERATIVES AND THE REQUIREMENTS AND PROCEDURES FOR AVAILING OF THE SAME**

The CDA is committed to giving dissolved or non-compliant cooperatives a final chance to revive or revitalize their operations before leur Certificates of Registration are cancelled.

**SECTION 1. DEFINITIONS**
*   **Revival**: Regaining compliant status for a **dissolved** cooperative.
*   **Revitalization**: Reverting to compliant status for a **non-compliant** cooperative (2+ years of dormancy or pending SCO).
*   **Amnesty**: A one-time privilege giving cooperatives a fixed window to rehabilitate.

---

### **GUIDELINES FOR REVIVAL (DISSOLVED COOPS)**
**Section 2. Scope**: Applies to cooperatives with a Dissolution Order but NOT yet cancelled.
**Section 3. Pre-qualification**:
*   Willingness to undergo audit by a CDA-Accredited External Auditor.
*   Signified intent from at least 15 members (primary) or 5 member-coops (federation/union).
**Section 4. Documentary Requirements**:
1. Letter request for revival.
2. GA Minutes approving revival by **two-thirds (2/3) vote**.
3. **Three (3) year Revival Business Plan** with financial projections.
4. Audited Financial Statement of the preceding year.
5. Bond of accountable officers.

---

### **GUIDELINES FOR REVITALIZATION (NON-COMPLIANT COOPS)**
**Section 1. Scope**: Applies to coops tagged non-compliant for 2+ years for non-submission of reports or pending SCO for 2+ years.
**Section 2. Requirements**:
1. Affidavit of explanation/justification signed by the Chairman.
2. GA Resolution approving revitalization.
3. Latest Audited Financial Statement.
4. **Three (3) year Revitalization Business Plan**.

---

### **AMORTIZED PENALTY PAYMENTS**
**Section 3. Schedule**: Condonation is not possible, but penalties can be paid in equal monthly installments:
*   2 years non-submission: 24 months.
*   3 years non-submission: 30 months.
*   4 years non-submission: 36 months.
*   5 years non-submission: 40 months.
*   *Note*: Micro cooperatives are already exempt from monetary penalties for non-submission.

---

### **CRITICAL DEADLINES & PENALTIES**
*   **Application Deadline**: **November 30, 2024**.
*   **Failure to File**: Applications not filed by the deadline will result in an immediate **Order of Cancellation**.
*   **Failure to Comply with Plan**: Default in the 3-year plan leads to a Notice of Non-Completion and subsequent Cancellation within 15 days.
*   **Exclusions**: Already cancelled coops, those in litigation, or those failing to liquidate assets for 3+ years are excluded.
