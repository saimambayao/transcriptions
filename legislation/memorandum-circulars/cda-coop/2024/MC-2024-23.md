SUBJECT: AMENDING SECTION 6 (p1) OF MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR
NO. 2023-18: GUIDELINES GOVERNING THE
IMPLEMENTATION OF A SPECIAL PROGRAM ON MERGER
AND CONSOLIDATION FOR MICRO AND- SMALL
COOPERATIVES AND GRANTING INCENTIVES THEREOF

To facilitate further the overall success of the program for micro and small
cooperatives towards increased opportunities and benefits in terms of consolidated
capital, revenues, business opportunities, strengthened financial capabilities,
organizational growth, management development, and access to economic and
technical assistance, Section 6, paragraph one (1) of Memorandum Circular No.
2023-18 is hereby amended to read as follows:

Section 6 (p1). General Requirements

“To participate in the program on merger and consolidation for micro and
small cooperatives, the constituent cooperatives shall’ have the following
qualifications:

1. Active and operating not less than one year at the time of merger or
consolidation;

2. No existing intra/inter-cooperative disputes filed with the Authority; and

3. No pending cases filed with the Court and other Administrative
Agencies.

However, in the case of a merger, the surviving cooperative must have been
in operation for at least three (3) years.”

This Circular will take effect fifteen (15) days after it is published in the Official
Gazette or the newspaper of general circulation and a copy is filed with the Office of
the National Administrative Register (ONAR).
