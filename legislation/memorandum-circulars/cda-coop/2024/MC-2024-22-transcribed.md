# MC 2024-22 | GAP ANALYSIS AND ACTION PLAN ON FINANCIAL CONSUMER PROTECTION

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-22**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: GUIDELINES ON THE PREPARATION AND SUBMISSION OF GAP ANALYSIS AND BOARD-APPROVED ACTION PLAN IN COMPLIANCE WITH THE TRANSITORY CLAUSE OF MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2023-014 OR THE IMPLEMENTING RULES AND REGULATIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11765, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE “FINANCIAL PRODUCTS AND SERVICES CONSUMER PROTECTION ACT”**

Rule IX, Section 2 of Memorandum Circular (MC) No. 2023-014 requires all CDA-Regulated Entities (CDAREs) offering financial products or services to:
a. Conduct a gap analysis of their current consumer protection practices vis-a-vis the provisions of the MC.
b. Create an Action Plan duly approved by the Board of Directors within one (1) year from the effectivity of the MC (July 11, 2023).

**I. OPERATIONAL GUIDELINES**

1. **Gap Analysis**: CDAREs must perform a gap analysis of their current financial consumer protection practices, including their Consumer Protection Risk Management System (CPRMS) and Financial Consumer Protection Assistance Mechanism (FCPAM).
2. **Submission**: CDAREs must submit a **Summary Table of Observed Gaps and Corresponding Board-Approved Action Plan** (per Annex A) signed by the Chairperson.
3. **Deadlines**:
    *   **Target Completion Date for Activities**: No later than **July 11, 2025**.
    *   **Deadline for Submission of Summary Table**: On or before **December 27, 2024** (soft copy via email to the respective Regional Extension Office).
4. **Email Subject Format**: `CDARE_GapAnalysis_ActionPlan_(Date of Submission)`, e.g., `ABC Credit Cooperative_GapAnalysis_ActionPlan_07312024`.

**II. KEY PRINCIPLES UNDER MC 2023-014 (For Gap Analysis)**

*   **Fair and Equitable Treatment**: Non-discrimination based on race, age, financial capacity, gender, etc.
*   **Abusive Practices**: Prohibition of abusive collection or debt recovery practices.
*   **Transparency and Disclosure**: Ensuring financial consumers are informed of terms and conditions fairly.

**III. EFFECTIVITY**
This MC shall take effect fifteen (15) days after publication in the ONAR and Official Gazette.
