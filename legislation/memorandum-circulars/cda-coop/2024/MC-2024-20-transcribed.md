# MC 2024-20 | GENERAL GUIDELINES FOR FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE TO COOPERATIVES

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-20**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: GENERAL GUIDELINES FOR THE RELEASE OR GRANT OF FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE TO COOPERATIVES**

**SECTION 1. LEGAL BASIS**
*   **RA 9520 (Cooperative Code)** — Article 2 on fostering growth and self-reliance.
*   **RA 11364 (CDA Charter of 2019)** — Section 2 and 4(b, m) on providing financial assistance and administering grants.
*   **COA Circular No. 2012-001** — Rules on fund transfers to NGOs and social organizations.

**SECTION 2. ELIGIBILITY / WHO MAY AVAIL**
Duly registered and **CDA-accredited** cooperatives with a **valid Certificate of Compliance (COC)** and no unliquidated financial assistance with the CDA at the time of application.

**SECTION 3. PURPOSE**
Funds shall be utilized for maintenance and expansion of projects, development, or start-up capital.

**SECTION 4. APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS**
1. Application Form;
2. Board Resolution approving the request and the Project Proposal;
3. Approved Project Proposal with Budgetary Requirements;
4. Certified True Copy (CTC) of CDA Accreditation;
5. CTC of valid COC;
6. Audited Financial Statements (AFS) for the past three (3) years (or financial reports for newer coops).

**SECTION 6. EQUITY REQUIREMENT**
The cooperative must provide an equity share equivalent to at least **twenty percent (20%)** of the total project cost (in the form of labor, land, facilities, or equipment). This equity is not subject to CDA liquidation.

**SECTION 6.3 MEMORANDUM OF AGREEMENT (MOA)**
Approved grants require a MOA outlining the project name, cost estimates, time schedules, monitoring procedures, and a specific period for liquidation.
*   *Note*: A separate bank account is required for grants exceeding **PhP 100,000.00**.

**SECTION 7. LIQUIDATION**
The Fund Utilization Report (FUR) and supporting documents (photos, certificates of completion, official receipts) must be submitted within **thirty (30) calendar days** after project completion.

**SECTION 9. PROHIBITIONS**
Funds shall NOT be used for:
1. Money market placements or time deposits (unless in MOA);
2. Cash advances for officials (unless implementation-related);
3. Payment of salaries, honoraria, or allowances for cooperative personnel;
4. Asset acquisition (unless necessary for the project and in MOA);
5. Any purpose other than stated in the MOA.

**SECTION 10. SANCTIONS**
*   Refund of unused funds or disallowed amounts.
*   Administrative, civil, or criminal actions against defaulting officers.
*   **Disqualification** from all future grants/financial assistance from the CDA.

**SECTION 12. REPEALING CLAUSE**
Repeals MC 2022-05 and MC 2022-12.
