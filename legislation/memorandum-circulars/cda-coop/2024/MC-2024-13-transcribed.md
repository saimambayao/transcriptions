# MC 2024-13 | AMENDED AGRI-MACHINERY GRANT GUIDELINES (JUNE 2024)

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-13**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: AMENDED GUIDELINES ON THE GRANT OF AGRI-MACHINERY TO MICRO AND SMALL AGRICULTURAL COOPERATIVES**

These guidelines empower micro and small agricultural cooperatives through farm machinery/equipment grants, enhancing food security and sustainable agricultural growth.

**SECTION 3. QUALIFIED BENEFICIARIES**
Micro and small agricultural cooperatives that satisfy the following:
1. Duly registered with the CDA.
2. Hold a valid Certificate of Compliance (COC).
3. Production of rice and/or corn is provided in their Articles of Cooperation.
4. If there are more than 10 qualified applicants, a **first-come-first-serve** basis applies to the first 10 applicants.

**SECTION 5. DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS**
1. Letter of Intent from the Chairman/CEO.
2. Board Resolution approving the request.
3. Valid COC.
4. Certificate of Accreditation from CDA.
5. Notarized Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) and Deed of Donation and Acceptance (DDA).

**SECTION 6. PROCEDURES FOR RELEASE**
1. **Validation**: REO verifies eligibility using the Technical and Financial Evaluation form.
2. **Board Approval**: Eligible recipients are submitted to the CDA Board for consideration and approval.
3. **Release**: Once MOA and DDA are signed and notarized, the machinery is released.
4. **Orientation**: CDA REO conducts an orientation on the guidelines and machine maintenance.

**SECTION 7. ACCOUNTING TREATMENT**
*   **Acquisition**: Debit `PPE-Agri Machineries` / Credit `Cash-NT-MDS`.
*   **Release**: Debit `Subsidies-Others` / Credit `PPE-Agri Machineries`.

**SECTION 8. RESPONSIBILITIES AND RESTRICTIONS**
*   **Maintenance**: Cooperatives must keep the machinery in good condition.
*   **Non-Disposal**: A **five (5) year** prohibition on disposing of or selling the machinery.
*   **Liability**: Misrepresentation in applications leads to mandatory return of machinery or payment of its value.

---

### **ANNEXED TEMPLATES**
1. **Technical and Financial Evaluation Form**: Includes registration check, COP, and non-relationship sworn affidavit.
2. **Memorandum of Agreement (MOA)**: Formalizing the grant and monitoring terms.
3. **Deed of Donation and Acceptance (DDA)**: Legal transfer of ownership.
