# MC 2024-17 | SUPERVISED GENERAL ASSEMBLY MEETING GUIDELINES (AMENDMENTS)

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-17**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: AMENDMENTS AND SUPPLEMENTS TO MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2022-07 ON THE GUIDELINES IN THE CONDUCT OF A CDA SUPERVISED GENERAL/REPRESENTATIVE ASSEMBLY MEETING BY THE AUTHORITY**

To ensure effective regulatory oversight, the following amendments and supplements to MC No. 2022-07 are prescribed:

**Section 5. Review of Petition**
*   Petitions for a supervised meeting under Section 4(b) must be verified and signed by at least **ten percent (10%)** of all members entitled to vote.
*   If there are list discrepancies between the petitioners and the Board, a **clarificatory hearing** shall be conducted within **fifteen (15) days**.

**Section 8. Notice of Meeting**
*   Notice must be served at least **one (1) week** before the meeting.
*   If the Board refuses to issue notice, the Authority may issue it via publication, radio, television, social media, or other electronic means.
*   Attendance at the meeting constitutes a waiver of notice.

**Section 9. Mode of Conduct**
*   Meetings may be conducted **face-to-face, via videoconferencing, or hybrid**, regardless of whether this is expressly provided in the cooperative's bylaws.

**Section 10. Presiding Officer**
*   The Chairperson or Vice-Chairperson shall normally preside.
*   If they refuse, the Authority may order other officers or a representative from the petitioners to preside.
*   In cases of **legitimacy of leadership disputes**, a CDA representative may preside until a new officer is determined by the attendees.

**Section 12. Quorum**
*   **Regular**: At least **25%** of members entitled to vote.
*   **Electric Coops**: At least **5%** of members entitled to vote.
*   In case of non-cooperation by the Board, the most recent record of meeting shall be used as the basis for quorum.

**Section 13. Voting System**
*   **Primary Coops**: One member, one vote. **No proxy voting**.
*   **Secondary/Tertiary Coops**: One basic vote + incentive votes (max 5). Proxy voting is allowed if in bylaws.

**Section 16. Liability of Board and Officers**
Officers who refuse to furnish reports, provide false information, or fail to comply with CDA orders are liable for:
1. **Administrative Sanctions** via Notice of Charge.
2. **Direct or Indirect Contempt** under RA 9520 penal provisions.

**Section 17. Appeals**
*   Appeals to the CDA Board must be filed within **fifteen (15) days** of receipt of the final order.
*   The CDA Board shall render a final and executory decision within **thirty (30) days**.
