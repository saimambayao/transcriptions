**SUBJECT: GUIDELINES ON EXAMINATION OF CREDIT SURETY FUND COOPERATIVES**

Pursuant to RA 10744 (CSF Act) and RA 9520, these guidelines govern the examination of Credit Surety Fund (CSF) Cooperatives to ensure their sound operation and regulatory compliance.

**SECTION 3. DEFINITION OF EXAMINATION**
Probing specific aspects of a CSF Cooperative's operations or its members' CSF-related transactions through document scrutiny, interviews with officers, and ocular inspections.

**SECTION 4. PURPOSE**
1. Check for safe and sound operations.
2. Assess compliance with RA 10744 and its IRR.
3. Determine adequacy of internal policies and risk profile.
4. Advise on corrective actions.

**SECTION 5. COMMENCEMENT**
Examinations are triggered by:
*   Inspector recommendations or adverse monitoring results.
*   Letter-complaints or directives from other government agencies.
*   Risk to the **Restricted Capital for Surety (RCS)**.

**SECTION 7. TEAM COMPOSITION**
An authorized team of at least **three (3)** personnel (always an odd number) from the CSF Service or Regional Extension Office.

**SECTION 9. PREPARATION AND NOTICE**
*   **Special Order**: Issued by the CDA Administrator.
*   **Notice**: Sent at least **fourteen (14) days** before the examination, detailing names of examiners, scope, and required documents.

**SECTION 10. CONDUCT OF EXAMINATION**
1. **Opening Conference**: Discussing scope and duration.
2. **Examination Proper**: Guided by the Examination Plan.
3. **Documentation**: Notes, photos, recordings, and copies of records.
4. **Exit Conference**: Discussing findings with officers; allows the cooperative to refute findings before the final report.

**SECTION 11. EXAMINATION REPORT**
*   Submitted to the Administrator.
*   Shared with the CSF Cooperative within **30 days** of the exit conference.
*   The Cooperative has **30 business working days** to provide a written response.

**SECTION 12. POST-EXAMINATION REMEDIES**
Findings may lead to:
*   **Involuntary Dissolution** or administrative sanctions for the cooperative.
*   **Suspension or Removal** of directors/officers found in violation of the Omnibus Rules.

**SECTION 13. PROHIBITED ACTS**
Refusing to furnish info, providing false reports, hindering an examiner, or failing to comply with CDA orders are punishable under RA 10744 and RA 9520.
