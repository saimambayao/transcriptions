# MC 2024-08 | SUPPLEMENTAL RULES ON VOLUNTARY ARBITRATION

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-08**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: SUPPLEMENTAL RULES OF PROCEDURE GOVERNING VOLUNTARY ARBITRATION BEFORE THE COOPERATIVE DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY**

Promulgated pursuant to Article 137 of RA 9520 and the CDA Omnibus Rules of Procedure, these rules modernize and expedite voluntary arbitration proceedings.

**ARTICLE I: ELECTRONIC PROCEEDING**
1. **Mutual Agreement**: Videoconferencing (Google Meet) is allowed if agreed by parties or decided by the Arbitrator.
2. **Platform**: Must use official CDA Google accounts.
3. **Decorum**: Parties/witnesses must follow a dress code and online etiquette.
4. **Witness Verification**: Witnesses must scan their surrounding area with the camera to ensure no unauthorized persons are present for coaching.
5. **Recording**: Only the CDA may record (via Google Meet). Unauthorized recording by parties is considered **contempt**.
6. **Documentation**: Soft copies are submitted via email; hard copies must be transmitted to the CDA within **fifteen (15) working days**, or the documents will be deemed not filed.

**ARTICLE II: EMERGENCY ARBITRATION**
1. **Grounds**: For urgent issues causing grave and irreparable damage.
2. **Procedure**: Requires a **Joint Manifestation** under oath filed before the preliminary conference.
3. **Timeline**: The Emergency Arbitrator should endeavor to conclude the arbitration within **thirty (30) days** after the preliminary conference.
4. **Appointment**: Raffled among accredited arbitrators within 5 working days of recommendation.

**ARTICLE III: ACCREDITATION RENEWAL**
*   **Automatic Renewal**: Accreditation is automatically renewed for CDA Voluntary Arbitrators appointed before December 17, 2022, who have pending and unresolved cases.
*   **Duration**: Valid until the judgment/award becomes final and executory.

**ARTICLE IV: COMMON PROVISIONS**
*   **Retired/Resigned Arbitrators**: Cases previously handled by retired, resigned, or deceased arbitrators will proceed through the appointment of an Emergency Arbitrator.
