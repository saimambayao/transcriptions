# MC 2024-23 | AMENDING QUALIFICATIONS FOR SPECIAL PROGRAM ON MERGER AND CONSOLIDATION

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-23**
**Series of 2024**

**FOR: ALL CONCERNED**

**SUBJECT: AMENDING SECTION 6 (p1) OF MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2023-18: GUIDELINES GOVERNING THE IMPLEMENTATION OF A SPECIAL PROGRAM ON MERGER AND CONSOLIDATION FOR MICRO AND SMALL COOPERATIVES AND GRANTING INCENTIVES THEREOF**

To facilitate the success of the program for micro and small cooperatives toward increased opportunities in consolidated capital and strengthened financial capabilities, Section 6, paragraph one (1) of Memorandum Circular No. 2023-18 is hereby amended to read:

**Section 6 (p1). General Requirements**
"To participate in the program on merger and consolidation for micro and small cooperatives, the constituent cooperatives shall have the following qualifications:

1. Active and operating not less than one year at the time of merger or consolidation;
2. No existing intra/inter-cooperative disputes filed with the Authority; and
3. No pending cases filed with the Court and other Administrative Agencies.

**However, in the case of a merger, the surviving cooperative must have been in operation for at least three (3) years.**"

This Circular takes effect fifteen (15) days after publication in the Official Gazette or a newspaper of general circulation and filing with the Office of the National Administrative Register (ONAR).
