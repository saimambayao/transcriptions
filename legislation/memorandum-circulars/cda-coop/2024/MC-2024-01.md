SUBJECT: REVOCATION OF MC 2020-22 GUIDELINES ON THE SUBMISSION
OF A LETTER OF UNDERTAKING IN LIEU OF THE COOPERATIVE
EDUCATION AND TRANSPORT OPERATION SEMINAR (CETOS)
CERTIFICATION

The Authority issued Memorandum Circular No. 2020-22 on October 14, 2020,
in response to the request of the Office of Transport Cooperatives (OTC) and in
consideration of the national health emergency brought about by the COVID-19
pandemic, approving the submission of a Letter of Undertaking by applicants for
registration as transport service cooperatives who have not attended the Cooperative
Education and Transport Operations Seminar (CETOS) conducted by the OTC, in lieu
of the CETOS Certification required under. existing regulations.

On July 21, 2023, Proclamation No. 297 was issued, lifting the State of Public
Health Emergency throughout the Philippines due to COVID-19.

In relation to this and due consideration of the registration requirements for
transport service cooperatives under Memorandum Circular No. 2015-01 entitled,
Revised Guidelines for Registration of Cooperatives, Memorandum Circular No. 2020-
22, is hereby revoked, thereby reiterating the requirement of a CETOS Certification
for registration of transport service cooperatives.

Further, all registered transport service cooperatives who have submitted their
Letter of Undertakings but have not complied with the submission of the CETOS
Certification to the Authority are hereby given six (6) months to submit the requirement
mentioned above reckoned from the effectivity of this Memorandum Circular. Failure
to comply with this Memorandum Circular shall be grounds for dissolution of the
cooperative after due process.
