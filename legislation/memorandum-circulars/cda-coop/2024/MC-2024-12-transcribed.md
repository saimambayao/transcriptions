# MC 2024-12 | AGRI-MACHINERY GRANT GUIDELINES (INITIAL MAY 2024)

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-12**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: GUIDELINES ON THE GRANT OF AGRI-MACHINERY TO MICRO AND SMALL AGRICULTURE COOPERATIVES**

These guidelines establish the initial framework for granting agri-machinery to cooperatives to enhance food security and sustainable agricultural growth.

**SECTION 3. QUALIFIED BENEFICIARIES**
Micro cooperatives included in the tentative list. Qualifiers must:
1. Be registered with the CDA.
2. Hold a valid Certificate of Compliance (COC).
3. Belong to the **Micro cooperative category** (initial focus).
4. Have rice/corn production in their Articles of Cooperation and be CDA-accredited.
5. **First-come-first-serve** rule for the first 10 applicants if there are >10 qualifiers.

**SECTION 5. DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS**
1. Letter of Intent from the Chairman/CEO.
2. Board Resolution.
3. Valid COC.
4. Certificate of Accreditation.
5. Notarized MOA and Deed of Donation and Acceptance (DDA).

**SECTION 6. PROCEDURES FOR RELEASE**
1. REO verifies eligibility via Technical and Financial Evaluation.
2. CDA Board approves the list via Resolution.
3. REO prepares and notarizes MOA and DDA.
4. Physical release of machinery follows signing.
5. Orientation on machine maintenance and operation.

**SECTION 7. RECORDING**
Acquisition recorded as PPE; release recorded as `Subsidies-Others` / `PPE`.

**SECTION 10. EFFECTIVITY**
This Circular takes effect **fifteen (15) days** following the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or a newspaper of general circulation.
