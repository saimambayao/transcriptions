# MC 2024-15 | MANUAL PROCESSING OF ACBL AMENDMENTS

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2024-15**
**Series of 2024**

**SUBJECT: GUIDELINES ON THE MANUAL PROCESSING OF APPLICATIONS FOR THE REGISTRATION OF AMENDMENTS TO ARTICLES OF COOPERATION AND BY-LAWS OF COOPERATIVES UNDER SPECIFIC CIRCUMSTANCES**

These guidelines establish the basis for manual processing of amendments to the Articles of Cooperation and By-laws (ACBL) of CoopRIS and E-CoopRIS-registered cooperatives when system-related problems prevent electronic processing.

**SECTION 3. COVERAGE AND SPECIFIC CIRCUMSTANCES**
Applicable to all cooperatives (except CSF Cooperatives) under the following conditions:
1. Amending from **single-purpose to multipurpose**.
2. Adding specific business activities (e.g., transport, agriculture, agrarian reform).
3. Capitalization increase for cooperatives with **one hundred fifty (150) or more members**.
4. Amending registered address due to changes in political subdivisions.
5. Any other system-related condition preventing E-CoopRIS processing.

**SECTION 5. DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS**
Submit three (3) original copies of:
a. **General Assembly Resolution** approved by at least **two-thirds (2/3)** of all members with voting rights.
b. **Notarized Board and Secretary's Certificate** affirming the vote.
c. **Amended Articles and Bylaws**.
d. **Treasurer's Affidavit** (for capitalization changes).
e. **Regulatory Endorsement** from other agencies (if applicable).
*   *Note*: For Electric Cooperatives, Referendum results and CDA approval to hold the referendum are required.

**SECTION 6. PROCESS OF AMENDMENT**
1. **Submission**: Submit complete hard copies personally or via courier to the Head Office or Regional Extension Office. Concurrently, submit encoded data in an **editable Excel format** via email.
2. **Initial Evaluation**: Registration Section checks for completeness. If deficient, a checklist of deficiencies is issued.
3. **Validation**: On-site validation (or off-site if force majeure) is conducted. The validation report must be submitted within **two (2) working days** of receiving a complete application.
4. **Issuance**: Upon approval of the validation report, the **Certificate of Registration of Amendment (CRA)** is issued.

**SECTION 7. JURISDICTION**
*   **Regional/Head Office**: Based on the cooperative's current registration/supervision.
*   **Cooperative Banks and Insurance Coops**: Must file with the **CDA Head Office**.
