SUBJECT ©: AMENDMENT TO SECTION 5 OF MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO.

CIRCULAR NO. 2015-10, S-2015

To ensure the efficient delivery of services to cooperatives and to the public, and in
conformity with RA No. 9485, otherwise known as the Anti-Red Tape Act of 2007, Section 5
of MC No. 2019-10 and Section 8 of MC No. 2015-10, are hereby amended to read as follows: os

MC No. 2019-10 SUBJECT: REVISED GUIDELINES ON THE ACCREDITATION
OF COOPERATIVE EXTERNAL AUDITORS

“SECTION 5. Accreditation Process
Upon the recommendation of the CRITD/IDD of the CDA Head Office, through its
Department Director, the Executive Director may grant, revoke, cancel and/or deny

the accreditation of a Cooperative External Auditor.”

MC No. 2015-10 SUBJECT: REVISED GUIDELINES ON THE ACCREDITATION
OF COOPERATIVE TRAINING PROVIDERS

“SECTION 8. Accreditation Process
Upon the recommendation of the CRITD/IDD of the CDA Head Office, through its
Department Director, the Executive Director may grant, revoke, cancel and/or deny

the accreditation of a Cooperative Training Provider.”

This Circular shall take effect upon the approval of the Board of Directors and fifteen
(15) days after the filing thereof with the Office of the National Administrative Register (ONAR).
