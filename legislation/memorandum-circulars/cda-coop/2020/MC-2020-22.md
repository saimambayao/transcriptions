____________________________________________________________________
In response to the request of the Office of Transport Cooperatives (OTC) and in
consideration of the current national health emergency brought about by the COVID-
19 pandemic, the Cooperative Development Authority (Authority) hereby approves the
submission of a Letter of Undertaking by applicants for registration as transport service
cooperatives who have not attended the Cooperative Education and Transport
Operations Seminar (CETOS) conducted by the OTC, in lieu of the CETOS
Certification required under existing regulations.
1. Scope and Coverage. This Guidelines covers all applicants for registration as
transport service cooperatives who have not attended the CETOS conducted by
the OTC.
2. Guidelines.
2.1 The applicant transport service cooperative may submit a Letter of Undertaking
in lieu of the CETOS Certification;
2.2 Within six (6) months from the date of registration, the transport service
cooperative shall submit to the Authority the CETOS Certification;
2.3 Attendance to the CETOS by all interim officers and members is still a
mandatory requirement for registration; and
2.4 Failure to submit the CETOS certification within six (6) months from the date
of registration shall be a ground for the dissolution of the transport service
cooperative.
3. Modification Clause. This Memorandum Circular modifies subsection 4.a.4 of
Section 4 of Memorandum Circular 2015-01, Series of 2015, entitled Revised
Guidelines Governing the Registration of Cooperatives.

4. Effectivity. This Memorandum Circular takes effect immediately and shall remain
