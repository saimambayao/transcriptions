MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2020 -_1_6__
Series of 2020
T O : ALL CONCERNED
SUBJECT : EXTENSION OF VALIDITY OF ACCREDITATION OF
COOPERATIVE EXTERNAL AUDITORS
The Omnibus Guidelines on the Implementation of the Community Quarantine
in the Philippines, with Amendments as of June 25, 2020, by the Inter-Agency Task
Force (IATF) for the Management of Emerging Infectious Diseases, imposed
limitations on the movement of people and transportation, regulated the operations
of offices, establishments and industries, and required the presence of uniformed
personnel to enforce community quarantine protocols. Under such restrictions,
securing and completion of requirements such as certifications, trainings and other
documents from different agencies have become difficult to comply, including
accreditation as external auditors.
In view of this, the Cooperative Development Authority (CDA) hereby extends
the validity of accreditation of cooperative external auditors (CEAs), with expiration
date beginning March 2020, to December 31, 2020.
This Circular shall take effect immediately.
Approved per Board Resolution No. 176 S-2020 dated July 15, 2020.
FOR THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS
ORLANDO R. RAVANERA
Chairman
