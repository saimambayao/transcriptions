SUBJECT : TRAININGS FOR COOPERATIVE EXTERNAL AUDITORS (CEAs)
DURING COMMUNITY QUARANTINE
The Omnibus Guidelines on the Implementation of Community Quarantine in the Philippines,
issued by the Inter-Agency Task Force (IATF) for the Management of Emerging Infectious
Diseases, imposed limitations on the number of persons who may attend mass gatherings,
based on the type of community quarantine the area/region was placed. In view of this, the
following guidelines are hereby issued relative to the trainings required from applicants for
accreditation as Cooperative External Auditor:
a) The required trainings of applicants for accreditation as cooperative external auditors may
be complied with through webinars to be conducted by the Philippine Institute of Certified
Public Accountants (PICPA), in partnership with the Cooperative Development Authority
(CDA);
b) Training attendees may attend webinars to complete the required number of hours, which
is 24 hours for initial accreditation and the same number of hours for renewal. The 1.5
equivalent number of units credited thereof is in accordance with the issuance of the
Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) for every webinar applied for CPD units;
c) For the webinars to be conducted for the prospective cooperative external auditors, the
following topics may be offered:

The topic on Basics of Cooperatives is mandatory for initial accreditation, while the rest of
the topics may be applied for initial accreditation as CEA as well as for the renewal thereof;
d) Certificates of Training issued for trainings attended through webinars shall be valid
for five (5) years from the date of issue;
e) If the applicant opts to attend trainings by physical attendance, the required 24 hours
under MC 2019-10 shall be applied;
f) Webinars which covered the above topics conducted during the pandemic but prior to
the issuance of this Circular may be credited in order to give the CEAs a chance to
complete their required 24 units;
g) Conduct of trainings through webinar shall be valid and effective unless this Circular is
modified or revoked accordingly.
