SUBJECT : REVERSION OF THE REGISTRATION AND REGULATION

OF ALL COOPERATIVES EXCEPT COOPERATIVE BANKS,

COOPERATIVES TO THE REGIONAL OFFICES WHERE THE
PRINCIPAL OFFICE IS LOCATED

Pursuant to the powers vested in the Authority by Republic Act No. 11364, also
known as “An Act Reorganizing and Strengthening the Cooperative Development
Authority, Repealing for the Purpose Republic Act No. 6939, Creating the Cooperative
Development Authority’, this Circular is hereby issued.

l. BACKGROUND. In 2012, the Cooperative Development Authority (CDA)
issued Memorandum Circular No. 2012-08, Series of 2012, recalling
temporarily the delegated function of CDA Regional Offices to register
selected types of primary cooperatives whose applications for registration
were required to be filed with the CDA Central Office (now, CDA Head
Office) for their orderly registration and to ensure that they are not used as
tax shields of some misguided groups.

Il. POLICY. By virtue of CDA Board Resolution No. 332, Series of 2020 dated
November 5, 2020, the Board of Directors (BOD), approved the reversion
of the registration and regulation of all types of cooperatives to the Regional
Offices where the principal office is located except cooperative banks,
insurance cooperatives and Credit Surety Fund (CSF) cooperatives which
shall remain to be registered with the CDA Head Office. The existing
guidelines and/or requirements in the registration of cooperatives issued by
the Authority shall be followed.

Considering the reversion of the registration of abovementioned
cooperatives with the CDA Regional Offices, the latter shall exercise primary
jurisdiction over the same.

HII. REPEAL. All previous circulars and/or guidelines issued by the Authority

which are inconsistent with the ‘provisions of this Circular are hereby
repealed or modified accordingly.
