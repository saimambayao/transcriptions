SUBJECT : DECLARATION OF OCTOBER 20, 2018 AS NATIONAL
COOPERATIVE DAY

This Memorandum Circular is being issued to inform all stakeholders
in the cooperative movement, that the CDA Board of Administrators, in their
Regular Meeting held on August 29, 2018, has declared October 20, 2018
as National Cooperative Day in the Philippines thru Board Resolution No. 386
sernes 2018.

Relative to this, we enjoin the CDA and the cooperative sector in
every region to jointly undertake activities on said date geared towards
creating deeper public awareness and social consciousness on the vital role
of cooperatives as people centered, community-based organizations focused
on contributing to the solution of economic, social and environmental
problems in the country. Such activities should likewise highlight
the successes of cooperatives on poverty eradication and _ social
transformation in their areas of operations.

In view hereof, all concerned are directed to cause the widest possible
dissemination of this declaration through information campaign
and undertake preparatory activities to ensure a meaningful and
simultaneous celebration of “National Cooperative Day”, on October 20, 2018.
