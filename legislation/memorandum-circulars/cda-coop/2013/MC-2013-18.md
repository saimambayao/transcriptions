DATE : May 21, 2013

All cooperatives that have submitted to the Authority the required reportorial requirements
(such as the CAPR, Audited Financial Statements and list of Officer and Trainings
Undertaken) but without the Performance and Social Audit Reports within the period as
specified under Section 4 of Rule 8 of the Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of RA
9520, have been given an extended period from May 1, 2013 to July 31, 2013 to submit the
aforesaid Performance and Social Audit Reports without penalty.

In addition thereto, a Certificate of Good Standing (CGS) for regular purpose, valid and
effective up to July 31, 2013 shall be issued to them upon payment of the Certification fee.

Upon: submission of the aforesaid Performance and Social Audit Reports within the
extended period of time, a new CGS valid from August 1, 2013 to April 30, 2014 shall be
issued without paying the Certification fee for CGS. Should the cooperative fail to submit
the aforesaid reports within such period, the cooperative shall be considered “late” and any
request for CGS shall be imposed a penalty of P300.00 for CGS, in addition to the P100.00
penalty per day per report as mandated under Section 7, Rule 8 of the IRR of RA 9520.

Be it underscored that the extended period covers only the Performance and Social Audit
Report.

For information and guidance.

Approved per BOA Resolution No. 142 s-2013 dated May 21, 2013.
