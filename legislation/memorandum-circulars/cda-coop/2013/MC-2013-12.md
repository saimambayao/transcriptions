SUBJECT : POLICY ON THE REQUIRED OWNERSHIP OF UNITS BY
TRANSPORTATION SERVICE COOPERATIVES

DATE : 07 FEBRUARY 2013

Sec. 1. TITLE. This Memorandum Circular shall be known as the “Policy on the Required
Ownership of Units by Transportation Service Cooperatives.”

Sec. 2. LEGAL BASIS. The legal basis for this Memorandum Circular is Section 5 (2), Rule V of
the Rules and Regulations implementing the Special Provisions of the Philippine Cooperative
Code of 2008 (RA 9520).

Sec. 3. COVERAGE. This Circular shall cover the following:

a. Proposed cooperatives intending to engage in transport services;

_b. Cooperatives intending to include transport service in its business operations;
and

c. Existing transportation service cooperatives.

Sec. 4. OBJECTIVE. This Circular shall guide the Authority in the registration and regulation of
cooperatives which intend to engage in transport service and existing transportation service
cooperatives.

Seo 5. OWNERSHIP OF UNITS. Transportation Service Cooperatives shall own the minimum
units/vehides required to be used in the operation of transport services as prescribed in Section 5,
Rule V, of the Rules and Regulations Implementing the Special Provision of the Philippine
Cooperative Code of 2008 (RA 9520), as follows:

Sec. 6. TRANSITORY PROVISION. A newly registered cooperative and those intending to include
transport services as one of its business activities, are given FIVE (5) years from the date of
registration/approval of amendment, within which to comply with the minimum number of
cooperatively owned units/vehicles as required above.

Existing transportation service cooperatives are given TWO (2) years from effectivity of this
Memorandum Circular within which to comply with the preceding Section.

Sec. 7. APPLICABILITY. This Circular shall have suppletory application to existing regulations issued
by this Authority concerning the registration and regulation of transportation service cooperatives.

Sec. 8. SANCTION. Failure to comply with the provisions of this Memorandum Circular shail warrant
the cancellation of their certificate of registration after due process.

Sec. 9. EFFECTIVITY — This Circular shall take effect immediately.
