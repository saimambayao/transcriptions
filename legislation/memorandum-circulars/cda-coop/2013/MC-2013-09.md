OF R.A. 9520

DATE : 07 FEBRUARY 2013

Section 2 (2) of Rule Il, of Rules and Regulations Implementing Certain Provisions of
R.A. 9520, requires that in order for a parent cooperative to organize a subsidiary
cooperative, it must have a net worth of at least Ten Million (P10,000,000.00) Pesos as
shown in its latest audited financial statements;

in view of the foregoing premises, a parent cooperative may organize more than
one (1)subsidiary cooperatives provided that for every subsidiary cooperative, the parent
cooperative must have a net worth of Ten Million (P10,000,000.00) Pesos in its latest
audited financial statements [e.g. one subsidiary cooperative = Ten Million
(P0,000,000.00) Pesos, two subsidiary cooperatives = Twenty Million (P20,000,000.00)
Pesos , etc.], in addition to the other requirements as provided under Section 2, Rule 1! of
IRR of R.A. 9520.
