# MC 2025-01 | PROGRESSIVE INCREASE OF RESTRICTED CAPITAL FOR SURETY (RCS) LEVERAGE

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2025-01**
**Series of 2025**

**SUBJECT: GUIDELINES ON THE PROGRESSIVE INCREASE OF THE RESTRICTED CAPITAL FOR SURETY (RCS) LEVERAGE**

The Credit Surety Fund (CSF) Program is a credit enhancement scheme to provide support to micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) with no credit history or collateral. This is achieved through pooled contributions known as the Restricted Capital for Surety (RCS).

**Section 1. Title**
"Guidelines on the Progressive Increase of the Restricted Capital for Surety (RCS) Leverage."

**Section 2. Legal Basis**
*   **Section 27, Rule 20(G) of the IRR of RA No. 10744** — The aggregate outstanding balance of loans should not exceed **three (3) times** the RCS. This is subject to annual review for progressive increase up to a maximum of **five (5) times**.

**Section 5. Definition of Terms**
*   **Restricted Capital for Surety (RCS)** — Pooled contributions used solely to pay contingent liabilities from claims on past due loans.
*   **Leverage** — The extent to which the CSF Cooperative utilizes pooled contributions for surety-granting services.

**Section 6. Conditions for Increase**
Progressive increase is allowed if the CSF Cooperative:
a. Maintains a **20% Capital Adequacy Ratio** and a **20% Liquid Assets Ratio**.
b. Holds a valid **Certificate of Compliance (COC)**.
c. Shows at least a **10% increase** in the number of surety covers or loan amounts facilitated in each of the two (2) years prior to implementation.

**Section 7. Manner, Frequency, and Limit of Increase**
Increases must be progressive and approved annually by the Board. An abrupt jump from 3x to 5x is not allowed.

| Average % Increase (Surety Covers/Loan Amount) in last 2 years | Incremental RCS Increase | RCS Leverage Limit (e.g., on 1st increase) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 10-20% | Up to 0.20 | Up to 3.20 |
| 21-40% | Up to 0.40 | Up to 3.40 |
| 41-60% | Up to 0.60 | Up to 3.60 |
| 61-80% | Up to 0.80 | Up to 3.80 |
| 81-100% | Up to 1.00 | Up to 4.00 |

*The maximum leverage allowed is **5 times** the RCS.*

**Section 8. Procedures**
1. **BOD Review**: Annual review to determine performance-based need.
2. **GA Approval**: Increase and effective date must be approved by the General Assembly.
3. **Resolution Submission**: Submit the GA Resolution to the CDA Extension Office.

**Section 9. Restrictions**
Newly registered CSF Cooperatives (less than 2 years operation) or existing ones with no operation are **not allowed** to implement a leverage increase.

**Section 11. Sanctions**
*   **1st Offense**: Sanctions determined by the Authority for erring officers.
*   **2nd Offense**: Non-issuance of COC for the succeeding year.
*   **3rd Offense**: Dissolution for willful violation.

**Section 14. Effectivity**
Takes effect fifteen (15) days after publication in the Official Gazette and filing with ONAR.
