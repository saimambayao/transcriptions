# MC 2025-02 | REVISED GUIDELINES ON COMPLIANCE AND SUBMISSION OF REQUIRED REPORTS

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2025-02**
**Series of 2025**

**SUBJECT: REVISED GUIDELINES ON THE SUBMISSION OF REQUIRED REPORTS AND PRESCRIBING THE REQUIREMENTS FOR THE ISSUANCE OF A CERTIFICATE OF COMPLIANCE (COC) AND THE GROUNDS FOR THE NON-ISSUANCE, SUSPENSION AND REVOCATION OF COC**

Pursuant to the rule-making power of the Authority under RA 11364 (CDA Charter of 2019), the following Guidelines are hereby issued.

**SECTION 1. TITLE**
"Revised Guidelines on the Submission of Required Reports and Prescribing the Requirements for the Issuance of a Certificate of Compliance (COC)..."

**SECTION 2. LEGAL BASES**
Article 53 of RA No. 9520; Rule 8 of the Revised IRR; Section 4(a), (g), (h) of RA No. 11364; and DOF-DTI Joint Administrative Order 001-2023.

**SECTION 3. SCOPE**
Covers all cooperatives registered with the Authority except CSF Cooperatives.

**SECTION 6. PURPOSES OF A CERTIFICATE OF COMPLIANCE (COC)**
1. Affirm compliance with report submission.
2. Secure Certificate of Tax Exemption (CTE).
3. Avail local tax exemptions and secure business permits.
4. Essential for accreditation as a CDA training provider.

**SECTION 7. REQUIREMENTS FOR THE ISSUANCE OF COC**

**7.1 Primary Cooperatives, Federations, Cooperative Banks, and Insurance Coops**
1. **Cooperative Annual Progress Report (CAPR)** with:
    a. Audited Financial Statements (AFS) by CDA-accredited auditor.
    b. List of Officers and Mandatory Training Records.
    c. Social Audit Report.
    d. Performance Audit Report.
    e. Semi-annual Report on Mediation and Conciliation.
2. Annual Tax Incentive Report (ATIR) and Annual Benefits Report (ABR) (for CTE holders).
3. Sworn Statement of Officers (per Annex A).
4. COC Fee of P100.00.

**7.2 Electric Cooperatives**
*Same as 7.1 plus:*
f. List of Member-Consumer-Owners (MCO) issued with Share Capital Certificates.

**7.4 Newly Registered Cooperatives**
Initial COC is automatically issued with the Certificate of Registration (valid until April 30 of the succeeding year). For the next year, they must submit:
1. CAPR with Financial Status Reports signed by the Audit Committee and Chairman (Annex B & C).
2. ATIR/ABR (if applicable).
3. Sworn Statement and P100.00 Fee.

**SECTION 9. ONLINE SUBMISSION**
*   **CY 2024 Reports**: All cooperatives (except micro) via CAIS; Micro coops via Google Form.
*   **CY 2025 Reports Onwards**: Online through the Authority’s new Reporting System.

**SECTION 10. DEADLINES AND PENALTIES**
*   **Deadline**: Within **120 days** after the close of the calendar year.
*   **Penalty**: **Php 100.00 per day** of delay for accountable officers.

**SECTION 12. DEFICIENCIES**
Cooperatives have **15 calendar days** from receipt of a Notice of Deficiency to correct findings. Failure results in the same P100/day penalty.

**SECTION 17. GROUNDS FOR NON-ISSUANCE OF COC**
1. Non-submission of reports.
2. AFS discrepancies (Encoder vs PDF).
3. Unpaid monetary penalties/obligations (unliquidated grants/loans).
4. Failure to complete Mandatory Training for officers.
5. Leadership disputes (Double Board of Directors).
6. Undergoing dissolution or issued with a CDO/SCO.
7. Non-compliance with SCA, PFRF, CETF, or CDF utilization rules.

**SECTION 18. GROUNDS FOR SUSPENSION**
Includes leadership disputes, failure to comply with examination findings, or violation of Articles 45, 48, and 49 (Liability and Disloyalty of Directors).

**SECTION 23. EFFECTS OF NON-COMPLIANCE**
*   **To Cooperative**: Loss of tax exemptions and project endorsements.
*   **To Officers**: Barred/Disqualified from being officers for **two (2) consecutive years**; solidarily liable for the P100/day fine and BIR penalties.

---

### **ANNEX A: SWORN STATEMENT (Summary)**
Officers pledge adherence to Art. 45 (Liability), 48 (Disloyalty), and 49 (Confidential Information) of RA 9520, acknowledging administrative, civil, and criminal liabilities for breaches.

### **ANNEX B: STATEMENT OF FINANCIAL CONDITION (For New Coops)**
Includes Assets (Cash, Loans, PPE), Liabilities, and Equity (Paid-up Capital, Statutory Funds).

### **ANNEX C: STATEMENT OF OPERATION (For New Coops)**
Includes Revenues (Gross Sales, Credit Income), Costs, Expenses (Finance, Admin), and Allocation of Net Surplus (Reserve Fund, CETF, CDF).
