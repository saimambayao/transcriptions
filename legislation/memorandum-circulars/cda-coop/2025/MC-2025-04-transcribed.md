# MC 2025-04 | GUIDELINES GOVERNING THE REGISTRATION OF LOGISTICS SERVICE COOPERATIVES

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2025-04**
**Series of 2025**

**TO: ALL CONCERNED**

**SUBJECT: GUIDELINES GOVERNING THE REGISTRATION OF LOGISTICS SERVICE COOPERATIVES**

Pursuant to Republic Act (R.A.) No. 11364 (CDA Charter of 2019) and R.A. No. 9520 (Philippine Cooperative Code of 2008), the CDA hereby adopts and promulgates these guidelines.

**Article I. Title**
This Guidelines shall be known as the **"Guidelines Governing the Registration of Logistics Service Cooperatives."**

**Article III. Purpose**
To provide for the orderly registration of logistics service cooperatives in compliance with R.A. No. 9520 and relevant administrative issuances.

**Article V. Legal Bases**
*   **Article 6(4), RA 9520** — Purpose to provide goods and services to members.
*   **Article 23(1)(u), RA 9520** — Capability of the Authority to determine "other types" of cooperatives.

**Article VI. Definition of Terms**
*   **Logistics Service Cooperative** — A cooperative whose members are natural persons engaged in acquisition, storage (warehousing), transportation of items, packaging, freight forwarding, and end-to-end supply chain management.
*   **Area of Operation** — Can be municipal, city, provincial, regional (one or more regions in a major island), inter-regional (not more than two major islands), or national (all three major islands).

**Article VII. Number of Members**
Requires at least **fifteen (15) natural persons** who are Filipino Citizens.

**Article IX. Capital Requirements**
The minimum paid-up share capital requirement shall be:
*   **Php 5,000,000.00** — For City/Municipality or Provincial operations.
*   **Php 10,000,000.00** — For Regional, Inter-regional, or National operations.
*(Or the amount required in the business plan, whichever is higher.)*

**Article X. Cooperative Name**
Must include “Logistics Service Cooperative” and the word “Cooperative.” Prohibited words include "Integrated," "International," "Worldwide," "Federation," or "Union."

**Article XI. Bonding Requirements**
Accountable officers (Chairperson, Treasurer/Cashier, and Manager) must be bonded based on the cooperative's initial net worth.

**Article XII. Documentary Requirements**
1. Economic Survey;
2. Notarized Articles of Cooperation;
3. By-laws;
4. Notarized Treasurer's Affidavit;
5. Surety Bonds of Accountable Officers;
6. Certificate of Pre-Registration Seminar (PRS);
7. Business Plan;
8. Favorable endorsement from other agencies (if applicable).

**Article XIII. Registration Fee**
The fee is one-tenth (1/10) of 1% of the paid-up capital or **Php 5,000.00**, whichever is higher, plus a 1% Legal Research Fee (minimum Php 10.00).

**Article XVI. Validation Procedure**
The CDA Regional Extension Office will conduct an on-site validation/verification within **two (2) working days** of notice to verify the existence of the office and adherence to cooperative principles.

**Article XVIII. Prohibitions**
Logistics Service Cooperatives are prohibited from engaging in any business activity unrelated to logistics services or contrary to law/public policy.

**Article XXII. Effectivity**
This Circular shall take effect fifteen (15) days after publication in the Official Gazette or Newspaper of General Circulation.
