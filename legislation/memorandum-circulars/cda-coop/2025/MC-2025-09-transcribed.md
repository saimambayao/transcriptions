# MC 2025-09 | REQUIRED AND RECOMMENDED TRAININGS FOR MEMBERS, OFFICERS, AND STAFF OF COOPERATIVES

**MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2025-09**
**Series of 2025**

**TO: ALL CONCERNED**

**SUBJECT: REQUIRED AND RECOMMENDED TRAININGS FOR MEMBERS, OFFICERS, AND STAFF OF COOPERATIVES**

**Section I. Title**
This Memorandum Circular shall be known as the **Required and Recommended Trainings for Members, Officers, and Staff of Cooperatives**.

**Section II. Legal Bases.**
1. **Section 4 (o), RA 11364.** Develop and conduct management and training programs to provide members with entrepreneurial capabilities, managerial expertise, and technical skills.
2. **Section 4 (ii), RA 11364.** Formulate a standard training for cooperative officers or members focusing on principles, governance, business management, and technical skills.
3. **Section 17, RA 11364.** Partner with the cooperative sector to formulate standards of training requirements.
4. **Article 4 (5) RA 9520.** Education, Training and Information - cooperatives shall provide education and training for members, representatives, managers, and employees.
5. **Article 44 RA 9520.** Training requirements of Directors, Officers and Committee Members shall be in accordance with CDA rules.
6. **Section 3 (D) RA 10744.** Capability building in credit evaluation and risk management.
7. **Section 5 Rule 7 of the Revised IRR of RA 9520.** Officers mandatory training requirements: 16 hours of fundamentals and 16 hours of governance and management within the first half of their term.

**Section III. Scope.**
This Memorandum Circular covers the required and recommended trainings for all members, officers and staff of cooperatives registered with the Cooperative Development Authority (CDA).

**Section IV. Purposes.**
1. Prescribe the required and recommended trainings per category including a standard course outline.
2. Identify authorized providers of required trainings.
3. Prescribe sanctions for non-compliance.

**Section V. Definition of Terms**
*   **Required Trainings** — Refers to mandatory courses covering fundamentals, governance, management, and specialized financial/risk/credit management.
*   **Recommended Trainings** — Voluntary courses to enhance expertise and proficiencies in specific areas.
*   **Accredited Cooperative Training Providers (CTPros)** — Institutions accredited by the CDA to conduct training.
*   **Cooperative Development Officers (CDOs)** — Officers in Municipal, City, and Provincial levels per RA No. 11535.

**Section VI. Required Trainings**

**VI.1 Members.**
All cooperatives are required to ensure that all members undertake the **Fundamentals of Cooperatives Course**, **Financial Literacy** (including digital literacy), and **Leadership and Values Formation** within one (1) year from acceptance of membership. Existing members must comply within one (1) year from the effectivity of this Circular.

**VI.2. For Officers**

**VI.2.1 Primary Cooperatives (Small, Medium, and Large)**

| Category | Training Course | Number of Hours | Provider/s |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Micro** | Advanced Cooperative Course: 1. Cooperative Management and Governance; 2. Line of Business Trainings; 3. Financial Management (F.S. Interpretation/Ratios) | 8 Hours per topic | • CDA (Free)<br>• Certified CDOs/LCDO-LGU (Free)<br>• Unions/Federations<br>• Accredited CTPros |
| **Small, Medium, Large** | Advanced Cooperative Course: 1. Expanded Coop Mgmt, Governance, Relevant Laws, Parliamentary Procedure, Policy Formulation (16 hrs); 2. Financial Management (Basic Accounting for Non-Accountant & Internal Control) (8 hrs); 3. Line of Business Trainings (4 hrs). | 32 Hours Total | • Accredited CTPros<br>• Certified CDOs/LCDO-LGU (Free)<br>• Federations (for affiliates) |
| **Committees** | Role/Function specific trainings | Min. of 4 hours | Option of the cooperative |
| **Credit/Savings (>P5M Deposits)** | Specialized Financial Management: Basic Accounting; Internal Control; Risk Management; Credit Management | 16 Hours | • CDA (Free)<br>• CDOs/LCDO-LGU (Free)<br>• Accredited CTPros |

**VI.2.2. Officers of Registered Credit Surety Fund Cooperatives (CSF)**
*   **Startup/Refresher Course**: 16 hours (Covers RA 10744, Operations, Surety operations, Financial/Risk/Credit Management).
*   **Role/Function Specific**: Minimum of 4 hours.
*   **Providers**: CDA (Free), BSP, or Unions.

**VI.2.3. Officers of Cooperative Banks Supervised by BSP**
*   **Corporate Governance**: Completion of 6 parts.
*   **Provider**: BSP or BSP Accredited External Training Provider.

**VI.3. Staff**
Cooperatives are encouraged to have onboarding and continuous training (including role-specific) in the following areas:
1. Basic Cooperative Orientation; 2. Sales; 3. Fraud Detection; 4. Records Management; 5. Office Management; 6. Store Management; 7. Customer Service; 8. Bookkeeping; 9. Computer Literacy; 10. Data Privacy.
*   **Providers**: CDA (for Bookkeeping - Free), Accredited CTPros, CDOs (Free), Federations.

**Section VII. Recommended Trainings**
1. Organizational Development; 2. Project Development; 3. Computer Literacy; 4. Digitalization; 5. Succession Planning; 6. Franchising; 7. Business Continuity; 8. Conflict Management; 9. Strategic Planning; 10. Audit Management; 11. Records Management; 12. Entrepreneurial Management; 13. Performance Standards; 14. Risk Management; 15. Other trainings (e.g. IP Rights).

**Section VIII. Sanctions for Non-Compliance**
*   **Cooperative**: Non-issuance of Certificate of Compliance (COC) for the succeeding year.
*   **Officer**: Disqualification to hold any elective or appointive position.

**Section IX. Report on Trainings**
*   Submit list of officer trainings to Regional Office (or Head Office for Insurance/CSF) per SED form.
*   Records of member and staff training kept by cooperative for inspection.

**Section XIV. Effectivity.**
This Circular shall be effective fifteen (15) days after publication in the Official Gazette or any newspaper of general circulation.
