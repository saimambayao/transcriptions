SUBJECT: AMENDING Part IV (MISCELLANEOUS) [b] OF MEMORANDUM
CIRCULAR No. 2004-07, S. 2004 ON “AMENDED SCHEDULE OF

AUTHORITY.”

In order to rationalize and ensure a fair, just, and comparable collection of rates
of fees for the Authority, taking into account its socio-economic impact that is most
favorable for the cooperative stakeholders, PART IV (MISCELLANEOUS) sub [b] of
Memorandum Circular No. 2004-07, s. 2004 is hereby amended by reducing the
fee for “Certified True Copy” from the existing P10.00/page to P5.00/page.

This Circular will take effect fifteen (15) days after it is published in the Official
Gazette or the newspaper of general circulation and a copy is filed with the Office of

the National Administrative Register (OQNAR).
