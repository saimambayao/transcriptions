Process of Amendment

The amendments of Articies of Cooperation and By-Laws may’be

adopted in a special general assembly called for the purpose or
during annual general assembly through the process of :

i. Amendment by substitution —- wherein the whole old
articles of incorporation and, by-laws are amended and
new articles of cooperation and by-laws are adopted.

&. Amendment by specific provisions - only pertinent
provision/s of the articles of cooperation and/or bBy-
laws are amended. .
