SUBJECT : ADVISORY ON THE EXTENSION OF COMPLIANCE PERIOD
ON THE REQUIRED OWNERSHIP OF UNITS BY
TRANSPORTATION SERVICE COOPERATIVES ENGAGED IN
THE OPERATION OF PUBLIC UTILITY JEEPNEYS (PUJs)

DATE : September 15, 2017

Pursuant to the request of the Office of the Transport Cooperatives and in
order for the Transport Cooperatives engaged in the Public Utility Jeepneys (PUJs)
service to Cope with the implementation of Transportation Modernization Program of
the Government, this Authority hereby extend the period to comply with the
minimum number of cooperatively owned units for another three (3) years or until
February 7, 2021.

Approved by the CDA Board of Administrators on August 15, 2017 per BOA
Resolution No. 215, Series of 2017.
