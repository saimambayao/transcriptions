SUBJECT

DATE : June 22, 2010

Pursuant to BOA Resolution No. 102 dated June 17, 2010, you are hereby
notified that the Joint Congressional Oversight Committee on
Cooperatives (JCOCC) has approved the extension period for the issuance
of new certificate of registration under Article 144 of RA 9520. The
extension period shall start from July 1, 2010 and ends on September
30, 2010.

In this connection, the existing guidelines under MC 2009-02 and
pertinent Orders relative thereto shall still apply in so far as the general
guidelines and procedures in the application for the issuance of new
certificate of registration are concerned.

Accordingly, the system for registration and issuance of new certificate of
registration which started in July 2009 will be restored during such
period. No further extension shall be implemented after such
deadline.

Hence, all interested cooperatives are enjoined to please file your

application through the CDA website starting July 1, 2010 using your

Cooperative Identification Number (CIN): You may also approach any of

our provincial and district personnel fcr further information and technical

assistance.
