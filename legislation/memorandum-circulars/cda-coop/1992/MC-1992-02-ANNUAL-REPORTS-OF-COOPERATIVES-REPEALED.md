In line with the approved Rules and Regulations
Implementing Certain Provisions of the Cooperative Code of the
Philippines, "all cooperatives are required to submit a copy
of their Annual Report within sixty (60) days from the end
of their respective fiscal years". —

All cooperatives whose fiscal year ended or ends on or
before February 6, 1992 are required to file their Annual
Report within the sixty-day period computed from February 6,
1992, the date of effectivity of the Rules and Regulations
Implementing Certain Provisions of the Cooperative Code of the
Philippines. All other cooperatives whose fiscal year ends
after February 6, 1992 shall submit the Annual Report within
the reglamentary period.

The Extension Offices are also instructed to reproduce
and inform the cooperatives within their area of the contents

of this Circular.

This Cireular shall take effect immediately.

EDNA E. ABERILLA
