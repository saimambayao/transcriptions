SUBJECT .: ADDENDUM TO MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR
NO. 2011-17 DATED MAY 6, 2011

DATE : July 11, 2011

The Memorandum Circular 2011-17 dated May 6, 2011 entitled
the “Policy and Guidelines on the Establishment of Cooperative
Branch’ is hereby amended to include transitory provision under
Section 16 hereof, to wit:

Section 16. “Transitory Provisions. Cooperatives which have
already established branches prior to the effectivity of this Memorandum
Circular are hereby given six (6) months from the effectivity hereof to
comply with the prescribed requirements. Said cooperatives shall submit
a Statement of Management Responsibility duly signed by the Chairman
and General Manager firmly indicating their intent to comply.

Section 17”. Effectivity — This Circular shall take effect fifteen (15)
days following the approval of the Board of Administrators and the filing
of copy hereof to the Office of Administrative Register (ONAR).

Approved pursuant to BOA Resolution 217, S-2011 dated June
23, 2011.
