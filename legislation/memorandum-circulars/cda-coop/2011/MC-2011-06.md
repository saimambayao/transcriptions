As provided under Section 4 of Rule 8 of the Rules and Regulations Implementing
Certain Provisions of the Philippines Cooperative Code of 2008 ( RA9520) " allcooperative shall
file wHh the Authority a copy of the required reports either through xxx xxx or electronic
means within one hundred twenty (120) daysfrom the end of calendar days."
. .
'Electronic means' means using electronic equipment for the processing (including
digital compression) and storage of data which istransmitted, conveyed and received by wire,
by radio, byoptical means or by other electromagnetic meanS (Wikipedia).
However, for this purpose, electronic means shall refer only to fax or e-mail, sent in its
original form inthe format prescribed bythe Authority. Ife-mailed, it shall be to the Authority's
official e-mail address. It is deemed to have been received by the Authority from the time it is
retrieved by the Authority or by its authorized representative from the designated official
information system, who shall stamp on the said electronic documents the time and date of the
receipt. In establishing the delay or default, the date the electronic means was sent as
appearing on the copies of such electronic documents shall be considered the date of filing.
For purpose of verification, the original documents shall be sent to the Authority within
five (5) days from the receipt of the electronic documents.
Failure of the concerned cooperative to send the original copy shall be considered as if
the electronic documents have never been received, hence the cooperative shall be considered
in delay or default in the submission. Likewise, any alterations or tampering, which made the
electronic documents different from the original, ,sh<.lllbe considered <.ISif the electronic
documents have never been received.
