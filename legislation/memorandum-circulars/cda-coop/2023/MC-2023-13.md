SUBJECT : SUPPLEMENTAL GUIDELINES TO MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2022-
25, AMENDED PHILIPPINE FINANCIAL REPORTING FRAMEWORK FOR
COOPERATIVES

Memorandum Circular No. 2022-25 titled “Amended Philippine Financial Reporting
Frameworks for Cooperatives” (PFRF for Cooperatives) excludes cooperative banks, electric
cooperatives, and insurance cooperatives in the coverage of the said guidelines, instead shall
use the Philippine Financial Reporting Standards (PFRS) for Small and Medium Enterprises
(SMEs).

Taking into account the accounting policies and/or electronic systems of the these
cooperatives, shifting from the PFRF for Cooperatives to PFRS for SMEs would entail change
of its accounting system as well as restatement of its prior years’ comparative financial
information , thus, the Authority hereby issues this supplemental guidelines to read as follows:

“Electric and Insurance Cooperatives may use the Amended Philippine Financial
Reporting Framework (PFRF) for Cooperatives in the preparation of their financial
Statements for the year ended December 31, 2022, and the succeeding years”

This Circular takes effect upon approval of the Board of Directors and the filing of the copy
hereof to the Office of the National Administrative Register (ONAR) and the official gazette.

Approved by CDA Board of Directors per Board Resolution No. 129, S-2023, dated March 8,
