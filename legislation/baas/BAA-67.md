## BANGSAMORO AUTONOMY ACT NO. 67

Begun and held in Cotabato City, on Thursday, the 15th day of September 2022.

### AN ACT CREATING AND ESTABLISHING THE PURA MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DATU BLAH T. SINSUAT, PROVINCE OF MAGUINDANAO DEL NORTE, BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament assembled:

**SECTION 1. Short Title.** — This Act shall be known as the "The Pura Municipal Hospital Act of 2022."

**SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy.** — In accordance with Section 22, Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the "Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao", it is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM) to provide for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellence and affordable medical services.

**SEC. 3. Creation of Pura Municipal Hospital.** — To implement the abovementioned policy, a hospital facility is hereby created and established to be known as the "Pura Municipal Hospital (PMH)" located in Barangay Pura, Municipality of Datu Blah T. Sinsuat, Maguindanao del Norte, BARMM.

**SEC. 4. Hospital Bed Capacity.** — The PMH shall have at least twenty-five (25) beds, following the minimum national standards and guidelines set by the Department of Health (DOH). The hospital shall be equipped and operated in accordance with these standards. Future upgrades to expand the hospital's capacity shall be considered based on evaluations and recommendations by the Ministry of Health (MOH).

**SEC. 5. Catchment Area/Coverage.** — The PMH shall serve primarily, but not limited to, the patients from the municipality of Datu Blah T. Sinsuat. It may extend its services as an augmentation and support to other existing medical facilities, particularly for patients from the adjacent municipalities, especially the coastal areas of Maguindanao del Norte, when the need arises.

**SEC. 6. Objectives.** — The creation and establishment of PMH aims to:

(a) provide highly accessible, affordable, and comprehensive healthcare services, including hospital care, to the constituents of BARMM, as outlined in Section 5 above; and

(b) integrate all pertinent and relevant provisions of Republic Act No. 11223, otherwise known as the "Universal Health Care Act", in its system of operations.

**SEC. 7. Administration, Management, and Organization.** — The PMH shall be under the administrative supervision of the MOH, which is hereby authorized to determine and approve the appropriate organizational structure for the management and operations of the facility.

**SEC. 8. Appropriation.** — An initial amount of Fifty Million Pesos (P50,000,000.00), necessary to implement the provision of this Act, shall be charged against the Miscellaneous and Personnel Benefit Fund (MPBF) of the 2025 General Appropriation Act of the Bangsamoro (GAAB). Thereafter, funds necessary for the operations of the hospital shall be included in the annual appropriations.

**SEC. 9. Separability Clause.** — If, for any cause, any part of this Act is declared unconstitutional or in contrary to the provisions of Republic Act No. 11054, the rest of the provisions shall remain in force and effect.

**SEC. 10. Repealing Clause.** — All laws, orders, rules, and regulations under the BARMM Government, including those enacted by the defunct Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) Government, that are inconsistent with this Act regarding the construction, establishment, and operations of the PMH are hereby repealed, modified, superseded, or amended as necessary.

**SEC. 11. Effectivity.** — This Act shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following its full publication in a newspaper of general circulation in the BARMM.
