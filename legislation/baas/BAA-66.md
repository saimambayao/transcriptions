## BANGSAMORO AUTONOMY ACT NO. 66

Begun and held in Cotabato City, on Thursday, the 15th day of September 2022.

### AN ACT ESTABLISHING A LEVEL 1 GENERAL HOSPITAL WITH TWENTY-FIVE BED CAPACITY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOUTH UBIAN, PROVINCE OF TAWI-TAWI TO BE KNOWN AS THE SOUTH UBIAN GENERAL HOSPITAL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament assembled:

**SECTION 1. Creation of South Ubian General Hospital.** — There is hereby established the "South Ubian General Hospital" in the Municipality of South Ubian, Province of Tawi-Tawi to address the health and social services of its constituents.

**SEC. 2. Purpose.** — This Act aims to establish a level 1 general hospital of twenty-five (25) bed capacity to provide comprehensive and integrated health services to the people of the Municipality of South Ubian and its neighboring islands. It is an essential institution in the effective delivery of the health system. It ensures that health services are accessible at all times to the people in need.

**SEC. 3. Services.** — The establishment of the hospital shall include required units and services as the Ministry of Health (MOH) may deem necessary to fulfill the purpose of this Act, integrating into its system of operations the appropriate provisions of Republic Act No. 11223, also known as the "Universal Health Care Act."

**SEC. 4. Organization; Administrative Supervision.** — The South Ubian General Hospital shall be under the direct supervision of the MOH, which shall determine and approve its appropriate organizational structure and plantilla positions.

**SEC. 5. Appropriations.** — An initial amount of Fifty Million Pesos (PHP 50,000,000.00) necessary to implement the provision of this Act shall be charged against the Miscellaneous Personnel Benefits Fund of the 2025 General Appropriation Act of the Bangsamoro (GAAB). Thereafter, funds necessary for the operations of the hospital shall be included in the annual appropriations.

**SEC. 6. Effectivity.** — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.
