**AN ACT**
**AUTHORIZING THE USE OF FIFTY MILLION PESOS (PHP 50,000,000.00)**
**FOR THE ACQUISITION OF ADDITIONAL OXYGEN TANKS TO BE CHARGED**
**AGAINST THE 2021 QUICK RESPONSE FUND ON HEALTH OF THE**
**BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT**

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority in Parliament assembled:

**Section 1. Name.** – This Act shall be known as the “Oxygen Buffer Stock of 2021”.

**Section 2. Amount.** – The sum of Fifty Million Pesos (Php 50,000,000.00) is hereby authorized from the 2021 Quick Response Fund on Health for the acquisition of additional oxygen tanks in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region and Muslim Mindanao (BARMM).

**Section 3. Distribution.** – The additional oxygen tanks shall be equitably distributed in the different provinces of BARMM in order to ensure the sufficient supply of medical oxygen in the health facilities of the Region.

**Section 4. Fund Release.** – The fund shall be released by the Ministry of Finance, Budget and Management (MFBM) to the Ministry of Health (MOH) subject to usual accounting and auditing laws, rules and regulations.

**Section 5. Transparency.** – The MOH shall ensure the inclusion of the details of fund distribution in the information posted on its transparency seals.

**Section 6. Separability Clause.** – Should any part or provision of this Act be declared unconstitutional or invalid, the other parts not affected thereby shall remain in full force and effect.

**Section 7. Effectivity.** – This Act shall take effect immediately after its publication.
