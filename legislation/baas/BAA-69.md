## BANGSAMORO AUTONOMY ACT NO. 69

Begun and held in Cotabato City, on Thursday, the 15th day of September 2022.

### AN ACT CREATING AND ESTABLISHING A LEVEL 1 GENERAL HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS ALEEM ABDULAZIS S. MIMBANTAS MEMORIAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MASIU, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

Be it enacted by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament assembled:

**SECTION 1. Short Title.** — This Act shall be known as the "Aleem Abdulazis S. Mimbantas Memorial Hospital Act of 2023."

**SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy.** — It is a policy of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to provide for a comprehensive and integrated health service delivery for its constituents and establish by law a general hospital system to serve the health requirements of its people and ensure that the individual basic right to life shall be attainable through the prompt intervention of excellent and affordable medical services. Towards this end, and to adequately respond to emergencies, disasters and emerging diseases, the Bangsamoro Government shall protect the interest and promote the well-being and the medical needs of the Bangsamoro People.

**SEC. 3. Aleem Abdulazis S. Mimbantas Memorial Hospital.** — There shall be established a fifty-bed capacity general hospital in the Municipality of Masiu, Province of Lanao del Sur, to be known as "Aleem Abdulazis S. Mimbantas Memorial Hospital" under direct control and supervision of Ministry of Health.

Any further increase in bed capacity and upgrading of health care services and facilities shall be based on hospital development plan, which shall be prepared by the Ministry of Health (MOH) to reflect the development goals of the hospital.

**SEC. 4. Appropriations.** — An initial amount of Fifty Million Pesos (P50,000,000.00) necessary to carry out the provision of this Act shall be charged against the Miscellaneous and Personnel Benefit Fund (MPBF) of the 2025 General Appropriation Act of the Bangsamoro (GAAB). Thereafter, funds necessary for the operations of the hospital shall be included in the annual appropriations.

**SEC. 5. Implementing Rules and Regulations.** — The MOH shall promulgate the Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of this Act within sixty (60) days from its enactment.

**SEC. 6. Separability Clause.** — In the event any provision of this Act is declared invalid, the remainder of this Act shall not be affected by such declaration.

**SEC. 7. Effectivity.** — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of regional circulation in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.
