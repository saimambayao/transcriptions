# EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 401

**AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 359 ENTITLED ADJUSTING THE DIVIDEND RATES OF SELECTED GOVERNMENT OWNED AND/OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS ON THEIR 1995 EARNINGS PURSUANT TO SECTION 5 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7656**

## Metadata

- **EO Number**: 401
- **Date**: February 20, 1997
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/executive/execord/eo1997/eo_401_1997.html)

> **MALACAÑAN PALACE**
> MANILA
>
> **BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES**
>
> [ EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 401, February 20, 1997 ]
>
> AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 359 ENTITLED ADJUSTING THE DIVIDEND RATES OF SELECTED GOVERNMENT OWNED AND/OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS ON THEIR 1995 EARNINGS PURSUANT TO SECTION 5 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7656
>
> I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby amend Section 1 of Executive Order No. 359 dated 16 August 1996 entitled Adjusting the Dividend Rates of Selected Government Owned and/or Controlled Corporations on their 1995 Net Earnings Pursuant to Section 5 of Republic Act No. 7656 to include the National Power Corporation which shall declare cash dividends adjusted from fifty percent (50%) to ten percent (10%).
>
> This Executive Order shall take effect immediately.
>
> **DONE** in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Seven.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> (Sgd.) **FIDEL V. RAMOS**
> President of the Philippines
