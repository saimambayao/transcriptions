> DECLARING AND DELINEATING THE LOAY ZONE UNDER THE ADMINISTRATIVE JURISDICTION OF THE PHILIPPINE PORTS AUTHORITY.
>
> WHEREAS, the port of Loay is one of the major and busiest port in the country.
>
> **WHEREAS,** there is a need to expand the Port of Loay to accommodate projected increases in port traffic and to program the provision of the necessary port facilities to meet the demands of maritime commerce and trade within the region;

> **Section 2.** The Loay Port Zone, as expanded, is hereby placed under the administrative jurisdiction of the Philippine Ports Authority, which shall, consistent with the regional industrial plans of the Government, implement a program for a proper zoning, planning, development and utilization of the port pursuant to Presidential Decree No. 857, as amended.
>
> **Section 2.** The Loay Port Zone, as expanded, is hereby placed under the administrative jurisdiction of the Philippine Ports Authority, which shall, consistent with the regional industrial plans of the Government, implement a program for the proper zoning, planning, development and utilization of the port pursuant to Presidential Decree No. 857, as amended.
>
> **Section 3.** All other orders, proclamations and issuances or portions thereof which are inconsistent with this Executive Order are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 4.** This Executive Order shall take effect immediately.
