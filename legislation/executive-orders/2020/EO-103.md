# EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 103

**RECONSTITUTING AND STRENGTHENING THE NATIONAL QUINCENTENNIAL COMMITTEE, AND AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 55 (S. 2018) FOR THE PURPOS**

## Metadata

- **EO Number**: 103
- **Date**: January 28, 2020
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/executive/execord/eo2020/eo_103_2020.html)

> **MALACAÑAN PALACE**
> MANILA
>
> **BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES**
>
> [ EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 103, January 28, 2020 ]
>
> RECONSTITUTING AND STRENGTHENING THE NATIONAL QUINCENTENNIAL COMMITTEE, AND AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 55 (S. 2018) FOR THE PURPOSE
>
> WHEREAS, Article XIV, Section 15 of the Constitution declares it a policy of the State to conserve, promote and popularize the nation's historical and cultural heritage and resources, as well as its artistic creations;
>
> **WHEREAS,** pursuant to Republic Act No. 10086 or the "Strengthening People's Nationalism Through Philippine History Act," the State shall provide the means to strengthen the Filipino people's nationalism, love of country, respect for its heroes and pride for the people's accomplishments by reinforcing the importance of Philippine national and local history in daily life, with the end view of raising social consciousness;
>
> **WHEREAS,** Executive Order No. 55 (s. 2018) created the National Quincentennial Committee (Committee) to serve as the coordinator and clearing house of all government initiatives for the 500 th anniversary of the role of the Philippines in the first circumnavigation of the world, the victory of Lapulapu in the Battle of Mactan, and other related events, collectively known as the 2021 Quincentennial Commemorations in the Philippines (2021 QCP);
>
> **WHEREAS,** the Committee espouses a Filipino-centric point of view of the first circumnavigation of the world, by underscoring the magnanimity, compassion and humanity of our ancestors in helping the impoverished crew of the expedition that traversed the Pacific ocean, and the courage and bravery of the warriors in Mactan, which continue to serve as inspiration to our heroes and martyrs up to this day; and
>
> **WHEREAS,** the 2021 QCP aims to promote local and national tourism, reignite the nationalism of every Filipino, inculcate values for the common good, strengthen social cohesiveness of the community, intensify social awareness, and foster the Filipino creativity and innovation for our socioeconomic growth;
>
> **NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE**, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws, do hereby order:
>
> **Section 1.** *The National Quincentennial Committee.* The Committee is hereby reconstituted as follows:
>
> Chairperson: Executive Secretary
>
> Vice-Chairperson and Executive Director: Chairperson, National Historical Commission of the and Executive Philippines (NHCP)
>
> Members: Secretary, Department of Foreign Affairs;
>
> Secretary, Department of Tourism;
>
> Secretary, Department of Budget and Management;
>
> Secretary, Department of the Interior and Local Government;
>
> Secretary, Department of Public Works and Highways;
>
> Secretary, Department of Education;
>
> Secretary, Department of National Defense;
>
> Secretary, Presidential Communications Operations Office;
>
> Chairperson, National Commission for Culture and the Arts; and
>
> Presidential Adviser for the Visayas
>
> The Committee shall oversee the preparations for the 2021 QCP and perform the following functions:
>
> a. Undertake the overall study, conceptualization and implementation of relevant programs and projects on, but not limited to, diplomatic concerns, history, culture and the arts, academic affairs, science, environment and agriculture, socio-civic participations, volunteer and partner mobilization, military participation, and infrastructure;
>
> b. Act as the principal coordinator for all the activities related to the 2021 QCP;
>
> c. Serve as clearing house for the preparation and dissemination of all information on the programs, activities and projects for the 2021 QCP;
>
> d. Constitute sub-committees or working groups, as may be necessary, to undertake the implementation of programs, activities and projects;
>
> e. Undertake fund raising activities, as may be necessary, to attain the objectives of this Order, subject to applicable laws, as well as budgeting, accounting, and auditing rules and regulations;
>
> f. Call on any government agency or instrumentality, and invite private individuals and organizations, including overseas or international entities, to assist in the preparations and celebration of the 2021 QCP; and
>
> g. Carry-out such other functions as may be directed by the President.
>
> The Committee shall be provided with technical and administrative staff support by a Secretariat to be constituted by the NHCP.
>
> **Section 2.** *Powers and Functions of the Vice Chairperson and Executive Director.* The Vice Chairperson and Executive Director shall have the following powers and functions:
>
> a. Coordinate and monitor the implementation of the projects and activities of the Committee;
>
> b. Represent the Committee in meetings, as may be authorized or delegated by the Committee;
>
> c. Supervise and ensure that the Secretariat provides administrative support to the Committee and the working groups or subcommittees thereunder; and
>
> d. Perform such other functions as may be assigned by the Committee.
>
> **Section 3.** *The 2021 Quincentennial Commemorations of the Philippines (QCP).* The 2021 QCP is a historical event that seeks to reinvigorate the sense of nationalism of every Filipino through the commemoration of our ancestors' heroism, humanity and significant contribution to the first circumnavigation of the world. Towards this end, ali government initiatives related to the 2021 QCP must reflect the following principles:
>
> a. Filipino-centric - Ali activities relative to the 2021 QCP shall reflect Filipino viewpoints and highlight our cognizance and pride for our ancestors and rich cultural heritage through literature, languages, art and other cultural outlooks.
>
> Further, commemorative activities must emphasize the Filipino identity and the values of unity, magnanimity and sovereignty;
>
> b. Multidiscipiinary - Preparations for the 2021 QCP shail engage scholars from several academic disciplines and professional specializations to facilitate the contribution to the advancements of our historical understanding, as well as encourage historical research and discussion; and
>
> c. Communicative and Transformative - QCP commemorations shall endeavor to increase knowledge of historical education among Filipinos through traditional and new media, and further encourage promotion, restoration and conservation of historic sites associated with the 2021 QCP.
>
> Relative thereto, the Committee shall come up with an appropriate QCP branding or logo that will reflect and depict the heroism of Lapulapu in the Battle of Mactan, as well as highlight the 500 lh anniversary of the role of Filipinos in the first circumnavigation of the world.
>
> **Section 4.** *Funding.* The funding necessary for the implementation of this Order shall be sourced from the funds of the NHCP and, as may be necessary, the Contingent Fund, subject to existing budgeting, accounting and auditing laws, rules and regulations. Thereafter, subsequent funding requirements shail be included in the budget proposal of the NHCP, subject to the usual budget preparation process.
>
> **Section 5.** *Sunset Provision.* The Committee shall cease to exist after the liquidation and turnover of all documents and properties to the NHCP, or twelve (12) months from 31 December 2021, whichever is earlier.
>
> **Section 6.** *Separability.* If any part or provision of this Order is held unconstitutional or invalid, other parts not affected thereby shall continue to be in full force and effect.
>
> **Section 7.** *Repeal.* Except for the foregoing, all other provisions of EO No. 55 shall remain in full force and effect. All other orders, rules and regulations, or parts thereof, which are contrary to or inconsistent with the provisions of this Order are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 8.** *Effectivity.* This Executive Order shall take effect immediately.
>
> **DONE,** in the City of Manila, this 28thday of January , in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
>
> (SGD.) **RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE**
>
> By the President:
>
> (SGD.) **SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA**
