> **MALACAÑAN PALACE**
> MANILA
>
> **BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES**
>
> [ EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 876, March 10, 2010 ]
>
> AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 733 (s. 2008)
>
> WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 504 (s. 2006) created a Subic-Clark Area Development Council;
>
> **WHEREAS,** Executive Order No. 504-A (s. 2008) defined the powers and functions of the Subic-Clark Alliance for Development Council;
>
> **WHEREAS,** Executive Order No. 733 (s. 2008) recognized the Subic-Clark Alliance for Development Council;
>
> **WHEREAS,** under Section 31(2), Chapter 10, Title III of Executive Order No. 292, otherwise known as the Administrative Code of 1987 the President granted the authority to reorganize the executive branch of the government;
>
> **NOW, THEREFORE I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO**, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me, do hereby order:
>
> **Section 1.** *Reorganization.*  The Subic-Cterk AWiance for Development Council is hereby reorganized. It shall be henceforth called as the Subic-Clark Alliance for Development.
>
> **Section 2.** *Chairman.*  The Chairman of the Clark International Airport Corporation shall serve as the Chairman of the SCAD.ℒαwρhi৷
>
> **Section 3.** *Repealing Clause.*  Executive Order No. 733 (s. 2008) is hereby amended accordingly. All other executive issuances, orders, rules and regulations or parts thereof inconsistent with the provisions of this Executive Order are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.
>
> **Section 4.** This Executive Order shall take effect immediately.
>
> Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of March in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.
>
> (SGD.) **GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO**
> President of the Philippines
