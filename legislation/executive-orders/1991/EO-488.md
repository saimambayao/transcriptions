# EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 488

**CONSTITUTING THE PRESIDENTIAL COORDINATING COMMITTEE ON THE PRAWN INDUSTRY**

## Metadata

- **EO Number**: 488
- **Date**: November 22, 1991
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/executive/execord/eo1991/eo_488_1991.html)

> **MALACAÑAN PALACE**
> MANILA
>
> **BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES**
>
> [ EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 488, November 22, 1991 ]
>
> CONSTITUTING THE PRESIDENTIAL COORDINATING COMMITTEE ON THE PRAWN INDUSTRY
>
> WHEREAS, the prawn industry has become a major foreign exchange earner for the country;
>
> **WHEREAS,** there is an urgent need to unite all sectors of the Philippine prawn industry for it to remain competitive in the world market and
>
> **WHEREAS,** it is necessary for the Government and the private sector to work together in active partnership to realize the full potential of the industry;
>
> **NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO**, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order:
>
> **Section 1.** There is hereby constituted a Presidential Coordinating
>
> Committee on the Prawn Industry, hereinafter referred to as the Committee, composed of the following:
>
> a. Secretary, Department of Agriculture - Chairman
>
> b. Secretary, Department of Trade and Industry - Member
>
> c. Secretary, Department of Science and Technology - Member
>
> d. Secretary, Department of Finance - Member
>
> e. Chief, Aquaculture Department, South East Asian Fisheries Development Center - Member
>
> f. President, Philippine Federation of Prawn Growers, incorporated - Member
>
> g. President, Hatchery Operators Association of the Philippines - Member
>
> h. President, Feed Millers Association of the Philippines, Incorporated - Member
>
> i. President, Shrimp Exporters of the Philippines, Incorporated - Member
>
> Any member of the Committee may designate a duly authorized representative to attend committee meetings and other activities in his behalf.
>
> **Section 2.** The Committee shall coordinate all efforts, to develop and support the national prawn industry. The Committee shall have the following functions and responsibilities:
>
> a. Recommend directions/ programs and strategies for the industry in the areas of research, training, financing, production, processing and marketing;
>
> b. Recommend an appropriate mechanism that will provide the industry with timely market and other relevant information;
>
> c. Provide the forum for discussion of issues and concerns affecting the industry and recommend measures on such matters;
>
> d. Identify possible sources of funding to finance programs and projects that will contribute to the growth of the industry; and
>
> e. Assist in the generation and dissemination of pertinent information for the industry and the general public
>
> **Section 3.** The Committee may call upon other departments, agencies and instrumentalities of the Government to provide assistance in the performance of its functions and discharge of its responsibilities.
>
> **Section 4.** The Philippine Federation of Prawn Growers, Incorporated (PFPG) shall provide the secretariat support to the Committee.
>
> **Section 5.** The Committee shall submit to the President regularly on a quarterly basis a report of its activities and accomplishments.
>
> **Section 6.** This Executive Order shall take effect immediately.
>
> DONE in the City of Bacolod, this 22nd day of November, in the year Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.
>
> (Sgd.) **CORAZON C. AQUINO**
>
> By the President:
>
> (Sgd.) **FRANKLIN M. DRILON**
