# EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 329

**TRANSFERRING THE PUBLIC ESTATES AUTHORITY (PEA) FROM THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS AND HIGHWAYS TO THE DEPARTMENT OF FINANCE**

## Metadata

- **EO Number**: 329
- **Date**: July 19, 2004
- **Source**: [lawphil.net](https://lawphil.net/executive/execord/eo2004/eo_329_2004.html)

> **MALACAÑAN PALACE**
> MANILA
>
> **BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES**
>
> [ EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 329, July 19, 2004 ]
>
> TRANSFERRING THE PUBLIC ESTATES AUTHORITY (PEA) FROM THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS AND HIGHWAYS TO THE DEPARTMENT OF FINANCE
>
> WHEREAS, the Public Estates Authority is the government agency primarily responsible for integrating, directing and coordinating all reclamation projects for and behalf of the National Government;
>
> **WHEREAS,** Book IV, Chapter 1, Section 5 of the Administrative Code of 1987 empower the President to "indicate to which department a government corporation may be attached",
>
> **NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO**, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the power vested in me by law, do hereby order:
>
> **Section 1.** The Public Estates Authority is hereby transferred from the Department Public Works and Highways and attached to the Department of Finance.
>
> **Section 2.** All orders, issuances, rules and regulations or parts thereof inconsistent with this Executive Order are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.1a⍵⍴h!1
>
> **Section 3.** This Executive Order shall take effect immediately.
>
> DONE in the City of Manila, this 19th day of July in the year of our Lord, two thousand and four.
>
> (SGD.) **GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO**
> President of the Philippines
>
> By the President:
>
> (SGD.) **ALBERTO G. ROMULO**
