> **EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 411 July 13, 1990**
>
> RECONSTITUTING THE BAGONG KILUSANG KABUHAYAN AT KAUNLARAN (BKKK) COMMITTEE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT, NOW KNOWN AS THE NATIONAL LIVELIHOOD SUPPORT FUND (NLSF) COUNCIL
>
> Pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby order:
>
> Sec. 1. The Bagong Kilusang Kabuhayan at Kaunlaran (BKKK) Committee in the Office of the President, now known as the National Livelihood Support Fund (NLSF) Council, is hereby reconstituted as follows:
>
> The Secretary of the Presidential Management Staff - Chairman
>
> The Presidential Spokesman and Chief legal Counsel - Vice Chairman
>
> The Presidential Assistant for Legal and Judicial Affairs - Member
>
> The Deputy Executive Secretary - Member
>
> The Assistant Executive Secretary for Budget and Corporate Affairs - Member
>
> Sec. 2. Executive Order No. 314 dated December 17, 1987 is hereby amended accordingly.
>
> Sec. 3. This Executive Order shall take effect immediately.
>
> DONE in the City of Manila, this 13th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred ad ninety. lawphi1.net
