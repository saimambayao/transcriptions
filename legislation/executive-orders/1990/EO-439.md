> **MALACAÑAN PALACE**
> MANILA
>
> **BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES**
>
> [ Executive Order No. 439, December 7, 1990 ]
>
> MODIFYING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 429, SERIES OF 1990, ENTITLED PROVIDING FOR THE REORGANIZATION OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE REGIONS IN MINDANAO, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
>
> Pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, **I, CORAZON, C. AQUINO,** President of the Philippines, do hereby order:
>
> **Section 1.** Section 2 of Executive Order No. 429 dated 12 October 1990 is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> > **Section 2.** REGION X, to be known as NORTHERN MINDANAO, shall include the following provinces and cites:
> >
> > Provinces
> >
> > |  |  |
> > | --- | --- |
> > | Agusan del Norte | Camiguin |
> > | Agusan del Sur | Bukidnon |
> > | Misamis Oriental | Surigao del Norte |
> >
> > Cities
> >
> > Cagayan de Oro
> >
> > Gingoog
> >
> > Butuan
> >
> > Surigao
> >
> > The City of Cagayan de Oro shall serve as the regional center.
>
> **Section 2.** This Executive Order shall take effect immediately.1aшphi1
>
> DONE in the City of Manila, this 7th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.
>
> (Sgd.) **CORAZON C. AQUINO**
> President of the Philippines
