**PLACING THE COUNCIL OF INTERFAITH INITIATIVES UNDER THE NATIONAL SECURITY COUNCIL**

- **Date**: November 06, 2009

> **MALACAÑAN PALACE**
> MANILA
>
> **BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES**
>
> [ EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 844, November 06, 2009 ]
>
> PLACING THE COUNCIL OF INTERFAITH INITIATIVES UNDER THE NATIONAL SECURITY COUNCIL
>
> WHEREAS, Executive Order (EO) no. 626, s. 2007 created the National Committee on Interfaith Cooperation (NCIC) to consolidate, rationalize and ensure the consistency of the countrys policies and position in various interfaith initiatives and for a in and outside the country;
>
> **WHEREAS,** EO No. 714, s. 2008 renamed the NCIC as the Council of Interfaith Initiatives (CII);
>
> **WHEREAS,** interfaith dialogue is an integral component of the paradigm for peace and security of the Arroyo administration;
>
> **WHEREAS,** the President of the Philippines is the Chair of the National Security Council;
>
> **NOW THEREFORE I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO**, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me my law, do hereby order:
>
> **Section 1.** The Council of Interfaith Initiatives (CII) shall be placed under the oversight of the National Security Council;
>
> **Section 2.** The National Security Adviser shall be the Vice-Chair of the CII;
>
> **Section 3.** All issuances inconsistent with this order are hereby repealed or amended accordingly.
>
> **Section 4.** This Executive Order shall take effect immediately.
>
> DONE in the City of Manila, this 6th day of November in the Year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.
>
> (SGD.) **GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO**
>
> By the President:
>
> (SGD.) **EDUARDO R. ERMITA**
