> **Republic of the Philippines
> MALACAÑANG**
> Manila
>
> **BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES**
>
> **EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 794             April 10, 2009**
>
> **CREATING A PRESIDENTIAL TASK FORCE ON EMERGENCY EMPLOYMENT**
>
> **WHEREAS,** the generation of employment and livelihood opportunities for targeted income groups is part of the country's best preparation in thwarting the problems brought about by the global economic crisis;
>
> **NOW THEREFORE, I, GLORIA M. ARROYO,** President of the Philippines, by the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create a Presidential Task Force on Emergency Employment to be chaired by the President, with the following as members:
>
> 1. The Secretary of Labor and Employment
>
> 2. The Administrator of the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration
>
> 3. The Administrator of the Overseas Workers Welfare Administration
>
> 4. The President of the Employers' Confederation of the Philippines
>
> 5. The President of the Philippine Chamber of Commerce and Industry
>
> 6. The President of the organization of recruitment agencies
>
> 7. A representative from the Trade Union Congress of the Philippines.
>
> **DONE** on this 10th day of April in the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Nine.
