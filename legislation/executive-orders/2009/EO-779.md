> **Republic of the Philippines
> MALACAÑANG**
> Manila
>
> **BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES**
>
> **EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 779             January 21, 2009**
>
> **AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 634, DATED 03 JULY 2007, ENTITLED "CREATING THE BICOL CALAMITY ASSISTANCE AND REHABILITATION EFFORT COMMITTEE"**
>
> **WHEREAS,** the Bicol CARE Commission (B CARE C) was created in 2007 to integrated relief, rehabilitation, resettlement, recovery, livelihood and development efforts in the different areas of the Bicol Region particularly affected by typhoons Caloy, Milenyo and Reming;
>
> **WHEREAS,** the Executive Order creating the B CARE C provides that it shall operate for two (2) years;
>
> **WHEREAS,** due to some setback causing inevitable spill-over until 2009-2010 in the implementation of the programs and projects being monitored by the B CARE C, there is a need to extend the terms of the B CARE C until 2010.
>
> **NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO,** President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order:
>
> **Section 1.** - Section 9 of the Executive Order No 634, dated 03 July 2007, is hereby amended to read as follows:
>
> "**Section. 9 Term.** - The B CARE C shall exercise its powers and functions until 30 June 2010 unless otherwise directed by the President of the Philippines.
>
> **Section 2.** This Executive Order shall take effect immediately.
>
> Done in the City of Manila, this    21st   day of  January , in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.
