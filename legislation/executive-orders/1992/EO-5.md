# EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 5

**AMENDING SECTION 1 OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 2 DATED 01 JULY 1992**

> **MALACAÑAN PALACE**
> MANILA
>
> **BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES**
>
> [ EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 5, July 14, 1992 ]
>
> AMENDING SECTION 1 OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 2 DATED 01 JULY 1992
>
> I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order the amendment of Section 1, Executive Order No. 2 dated 01 July 1992 by shortening the period provided therein for continued operation of the 0% rate of import duty on the articles enumerated therein from 30 June 1995 to 30 June 1993.
>
> This Executive Order shall take effect immediately upon the completion of its publication in at least one (1) newspaper of general circulation.
>
> DONE in the City of Manila, this 14th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-two.
>
> (Sgd.) **FIDEL V. RAMOS**
> President of the Philippines
>
> By the President:
>
> (Sgd.) **PETER D. GARRUCHO, JR.**
> Acting Executive Secretary
