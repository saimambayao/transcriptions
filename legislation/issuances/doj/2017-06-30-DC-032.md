DEPARTMENT CIRCULAR NO.
To: The Director General and Penal Superintendents of the
Bureau of Corrections
All Concerned
Subject: Raw Rations Distribution and/or Catering by
Administration at the Bureau of Corrections pursuant
to Department Circular No. 31 dated 25 March 2013
Date: 30 June 2017
WHEREAS, Department Circular No. 31 dated 25 March 2013 provided for
a policy issuance wherein the Bureau of Corrections where directed to cease
and desist from engaging to self-administration and raw rations distribution
scheme and provided that food provisions for the inmates shall be
outsourced through the catering system scheme;
WHEREAS, the Bureau of Corrections has adopted the policy of the
Department and underwent outsourcing of catering system scheme in
compliance with the aforementioned Department Circular;
WHEREAS, the Bureau of Corrections has extended the contract of its
existing catering services for reasons beyond its control and has reached
the maximum allowable time to extend existing contracts pursuant to the
Revised Guidelines on the Extension of Contracts for General Support
Services;
WHEREAS, the present caterer of the Bureau of Corrections has already
pulled-out its equipment due to the expiration of its contract effective 30
June 2017 and the Bureau of Corrections is yet to award a new contract for
its caterer, for reasons beyond its control, to the great detriment of Persons
Deprived of Liberty;
NOW,T HEREFORE, the following directives are hereby issued:

### Page 2

1. Department Circular No. 31 dated 25 March 2013 is hereby revoked;
2. The Director General of the Bureau of Corrections is granted authority,
pursuant to the provisions of applicable laws, rules and regulations, to
undertake all necessary actions and means to provide food for Persons
Deprived of Liberty during the interim period following the
abovementioned revocation of D.C. No. 31;
3. The Director General shall expedite, subject to procurement laws, rules
and regulations, the public bidding of catering services for the Bureau
of Corrections and other penal colonies on or before 30 July 2017.
4. All other issuances inconsistent herewith are hereby revised, amended
or revoked accordingly, as the case may be.
For strict compliance.
VITALIANO N. AGUIRRE II
