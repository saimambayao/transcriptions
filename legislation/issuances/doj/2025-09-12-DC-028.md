conducted on April 28, 29 and 30, 2025 from 8:00 o’clock in the morning until 5:00

Elections (NLE), only the positions of Senators and Party-List Representatives shall
be voted for under the local absentee voting.

of the ECAD from whom a Head assisted by an Assistant Head and three (3)
Members shall be designated by the Chairman of the Commission.

applications coming from different regions and from the media.
