DEPARTMENT CIRCULAR NO.
TO ALL UNDERSECRETARIES AND ASSISTANT SECRETARIES
ALL HEADS OF SERVICES, OFFICES AND PROGRAMS
PROSECUTORS OF THE NATIONAL PROSECUTION SERVICE
ALL CONCERNED
SUBJECT : GUIDELINES FOR THE DURATION OF THE ENHANCED
COMMUNITY QUARANTINE( ECO)
DATE 28 March 2021
Pursuant to relevant resolutions of the Inter-Agency Task Force for the Management
of Emerging Infectious Diseases (lATF-MEID), and In view of the Memorandum of the
Executive Secretary dated 27 March 2021, placing the National Capital Region and the
provinces of Bulacan, Cavlte, Laguna and Rlzal (NCR+) under ECQ, and to ensure the
continuity of public service, Department of Justice (DOJ)e mployees In the subject area shall
adopt a work-from-home arrangement, except those Identified as skeletal staff at the frontline
services and the Administrative and Finance Services, from 29-31 March 2021.
Prosecutors and their support staff shall continue to conduct Inquest proceedings
online and/or In person, and attend to urgent matters that require Immediate action, as may
be determined by the heads of the prosecution offices concerned. Accordingly, all
prosecution offices are directed to ensure the availability at all times of Inquest prosecutors
who shall be on-call for the duration of the ECQ. The conduct of Inquest proceedings shall
proceed In accordance with the Rules of Court and existing rules and regulations of the
Department.
On-slte operational capacity of thirty percent( 30%) of the total workforce In the DOJ
Main Office and Annex Offices shall resume on 05 April 2021.
This Circular shall take effect Immediately and shall remain In force for the duration of
the ECO, subject to any such extension or amendment as may be declared or Issued by the
IATF-MEID or other competent authority.
For strict compliance.
MENARDO I. GUEVARRA
Secretary
