In the interest of the service, pursuant to the provisions of existing laws, and as
part of the implementation of austerity measures of the government, the conduct of Team
Building Activities (TBA) as prescribed under Department Circular No, 009, s. 2020, or the
"Guidelines for the Conduct of Team Building Activities in the Department Proper and Field
Offices", shall include activities for the Mid-Year Assessment.
Accordingly, the program of activities for the conduct of TBA shall include the
following agenda:
1(cid:9) Performance assessment for the first quarter or first semester of the current
year as the case may be; and
2. Strategies to achieve the targets set forth for the rest of the current year.
The TBA-cum-Mid-Year Assessment may be held anytime between April and June
of the current year for a maximum period of three (3) days, inclusive of travel time. The
same should be scheduled in such a way that the weekend or any non-working public
holiday is utilized for the purpose and thus work disruption is minimized.
The allowable budget for each participant shall remain at the rate of Eight Hundred
Pesos (P800) per day or a total of Two Thousand Four Hundred Pesos (P2,400) for three
(3) days to cover expenses for food, accommodation and other incidental expenses, or
such rates as may be provided by applicable laws and Department Circulars.
This Circular takes effect immediately, amends Department Circular No, 009, s.
2020, and shall remain in force unless amended or revoked.
For strict compliance.
