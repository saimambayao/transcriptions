In the exigency of the service, and pursuant to relevant resolutions of the Inter-
Agency Task Force for the Management of Emerging Infectious Diseases and the
proclamation by the President of a State of National Calamity on 16 March 2020, the
following Expanded Guidelines for the National Prosecution Service and for the filing
of Petitions for Review with the DOJ are hereby issued in conjunction with Department
Circular( DC) No. 008 and 008-A:
I. APPLICABILITY
These Expanded Guidelines shall be applicable in all prosecution service in
Regions I to V and in other jurisdictions where the province, city, and/or
municipality have imposed an enhanced community quarantine pursuant to
Inter-Agency Task Force for the Management of Emerging Infectious Disease
Resolution No. 13 dated 16 March 2020 and subsequent issuances.
II. INQUESTS AND PRELIMINARY INVESTIGATIONS
During the period of the enhanced community quarantine in the NCR and
Regions l-V, the conduct of all inquest proceedings shall continue and shall
proceed in accordance with the Rules of Court and existing rules and
regulations of the DOJ, including guidelines on the designation of inquest
prosecutors on duty and the necessary support staff therefor.
All preliminary investigation hearings, case conferences, and the like during
the period of the enhanced community quarantine are deemed cancelled and
shall be rescheduled after the lifting of the enhanced community quarantine.
Accordingly, the Prosecutor General, Regional Prosecutors, Provincial
Prosecutors, City Prosecutors, and prosecutors authorized and required to
approve resolutions/orders on inquest proceedings and act on other urgent

papers/matters, together with the necessary skeletal workforce, shall be on
stand-by at any given day during this period.
III. COURT HEARINGS
Subject to the provisions of Supreme Court Administrative Circular No. 31-
2020 dated 16 March 2020 and related orders that the Supreme Court may
subsequently issue on suspension of court hearings, trial prosecutors shall
continue to attend court hearings and prosecute pending cases.
IV. REGLEMENTARY PERIOD FOR FILING OF PETITIONS FOR REVIEW,
MOTIONS AND RENDITION OF RESOLUTIONS
The reglementary period for the filing of Petitions for Review before the Office
of the Secretary or the Office of the Regional Prosecutor, responsive
pleadings thereto, affidavits, motions, position papers, and/or other relevant
papers is deemed interrupted during the period of the enhanced community
quarantine and shall begin to run again after the enhanced community
quarantine is lifted in the regions or in the province, city, and/or municipality
concerned.
It is likewise understood that the reglementary period for the rendition of
decisions, resolutions, and/or other orders is deemed interrupted for the same
period and shall begin to run again after the enhanced community quarantine
has ended.
The foregoing notwithstanding, all Undersecretaries, Assistant Secretaries,
and state/field prosecutors, and all concerned or affected by the enhanced
community quarantine regulations are hereby directed to work from home and
reduce their pre-existing work backlog during the said period.
