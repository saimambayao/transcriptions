Attached is a copy of Memorandum Circular No. 43 signed by Executive
Secretary Salvador C. Medialdea on 27 March 2018 directing the suspension of
work in government offices including government-owned and -controlled
corporations and local government units, and classes in all public schools, state
universities and colleges on 28 March 2018, starting at 12:00 o'clock noon for
the preparation of government workers in observance of Holy Thursday and Good
Friday.
The Prosecutor General, the Regional, Provincial, and City Prosecutors, the
Heads of Bureaus, Commissions, and Offices attached to the DOJ, including the
Front-Line Service of the DOJ Proper, shall, however, leave a skeletal force at their
respective offices to ensure that the delivery of public service shall not be disrupted.
