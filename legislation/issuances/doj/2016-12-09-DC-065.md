In the interest of the service and pursuant to the Department's mission
towards the effective, efficient and equitable administration of justice, heads of
offices in the DOJ proper are directed to conduct Year-End Assessment to prepare
and/or consolidate, the following:
Accomplishment Reports for 2016;
Work and Financial Plan for 2017; and
Inventory of property, plant, equipment for procurement or disposal.
All officials are also directed to orient themselves and their employees of
new issuances which will be scheduled for full implementation starting 1 January
2017.
