You will be pleased to know that we are opening the 9*** batch of the Senior
Executives Class (SEC) under the Public Management Development Program
(PMDP). the National Government's Career Executive Service Development
Program, on March 27, 2020.
The new class is a sterling opportunity to send your 3^ level officials to a life-
changing training program that widens their perspectives, equips them with the
necessary skill set to tackle the increasingly challenging demands of the workplace,
and connects them to their peers for a collaborative mindset. Facilitated by a faculty
pool composed of today's top pracademics and seasoned bureaucrats, the SEC is
especially designed for leadership and governance in the Philippine public sector.
The program includes a Sensing Journey in a rural community and Foreign Study
Mission to an ASEAN country.
Qualified participants are your incumbents and OICs of executive positions with
Salary Grade 25 and up, aged 55 years and below who are in good health.
PMDP is an investment of the national government. Basically your agency's
counterpart is just the executive time of your officials at five (5) days a month for 10
months and the local transportation expenses. The personal reward for successful
participants is a graduate Diploma in Development Management to be awarded by
DAP.
