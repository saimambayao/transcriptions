VNA-DC-
DEPARTMENT CIRCULAR NO.0 04-A
TO All concerned
SUBJECT AMENDMENT TO D.C. NO. 004, s. 2018
DATE MAR 2 6 2018
In the interest of the service and pursuant to the provisions of existing laws,
Department Circular No. 004 dated January 17, 2018, granting Undersecretaries the
authority to issue Office Orders directing or authorizing local travels on official business,
is hereby amended to include:
"Notwithstanding the authority granted to the Undersecretaries,
should the request to attend a specific event shall come from four (4) or
more personnel of the Department, and expenses will be charged against
the funds thereof, the authority to issue an office order authorizing the
concerned local travel shall be with Undersecretary RAYMUND L.
MECATE."
This amendment takes effect immediately and shall remain in force until further
orders.
For strict compliance.
VITALIAN J N. AGUIRRE II
Secretary
CN: 0201S031S4

All concerned.
