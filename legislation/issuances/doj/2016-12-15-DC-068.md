SUBJECT AUTHORITY TO ATTEND THE 120th ANNIVERSARY
OF THE MARTYRDOM OF DR. JOSE RIZAL
In the interest of the service and pursuant to the invitation of Acting Executive
Director and Chairman Ludovico D. Badoy of the National Historical Commission of the
Philippines (NHCP), you are hereby directed to extend support and participate actively
in the commemoration of the 120th Anniversary of the Martyrdom of Dr. Jose Rizal for a
flag raising and wreath-laying rites at the Rizal National Monument, Rizal Park, Manila
at 7:00 a.m. on December 30, 2016.
Each office is authorized to send personnel to the said activity, on official time, to
wit:
Office of the Secretary (OSEC) - 10
Bureau of Immigration (BI) - 10
Bureau of Corrections (Bucor) - 10
National Bureau of Investigation (NBI) - 10
Parole and Probation Administration (PPA) - 10
Public Attorney's Office (PAO) - 10
Land Registration Authority (LRA) - 10
Office of the Solicitor General (OSG) - 10
Office of the Government Corporate Counsel (OGCC) - 10
Presidential Commission of Good Government (PCGG)
100
Likewise, you are requested to provide food and other incidental expenses,
which shall be charged against your respective offices.
For compliance.
