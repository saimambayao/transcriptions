Under Section 3, Chapter XI of the Unified Manual in the Investigation and
Prosecution of Illegal Drugs Cases (2022), the Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency
(PDEA) shall be responsible for the monitoring of drug cases filed and pending before
the prosecution offices, trial courts, Court of Appeals, and Supreme Court, The same
provision mandates all concerned government agencies involved in the prosecution of
illegal drug cases to extend assistance to the PDEA in the monitoring of drug cases.
This Circular is intended to provide more relevant data to PDEA and to
strengthen the coordination between PDEA and the National Prosecution Service
(NPS), wherein the former shall asist the latter in evidence gathering and case build-
UP
This Circular supersedes Department Circular No. 019 issued on 05 June 2014.
Henceforth, you are hereby directed to furnish the PDEA with copies of all
resolutions, orders, information, and other pleadings on drug cases, within five (5)
days from issuance or receipt of documents through the following addresses:
Office Address: Legal and Prosecution Service
