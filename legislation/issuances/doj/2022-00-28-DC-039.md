In the interest of the service, pursuant to the provisions of existing laws,
rules and regulations, and to efficiently and effectively streamline the processing
of financial expenses of the Department, all services/programs/offices in the
Department Proper, including all Regional, Provincial, City Prosecution Offices,
and other field offices of the Department, are hereby DIRECTED to strictly abide
by the following guidelines:
I. DOCUMENTARY CHECKLIST FORM (DCF)
All requests for: (i) payment, (ii) remittance, (iii) cash advance, and (iv)
reimbursement chargeable against the funds of the Department shall be
accompanied by a duly accomplished DCF, signed by the head of the relevant
service, program, office or unit.
1. PROCUREMENT EXPENSES – All requests for payment pursuant to
any of the modes of procurement under Republic Act No. 9184
(otherwise known as the Government Procurement Reform Act), shall
be submitted to the Procurement Management Division (PMD),
Administrative Service, this Department.
2. NON-PROCUREMENT EXPENSES – All other requests for payment,
remittance, trish advance or reimbursement not otherwise considered
as a procurement expense shall be submitted to the Accounting
Division (AD), Financial Service, this Department.
In the absence of a duly accomplished DCF, or in case of the incomplete
submission of the listed documents in the DCF, the PMD and AD shall nd be
bound to accept the request for payment, remittance, cash advance or
reimbursement, until compliance to the foregoing has been met by the requesting
service, program, office or unit.
The DCF shall include a certification by the head of the requesting service,
program, offIce or unit of the complete submission of the enumerated supporting

111. Certificate of Availability of Funds (CAF)
Pursuant to existing rules and regulations, all services/programs/offices of
the Department shall, before entering into a written contract or agreement in
implementing a program, activity or project (PAP), secure a CAF from the
Financial Service.
For this purpose, the Financial Service is hereby authorized to issue and
send via e-mail scanned copies of CAFs to all regional and other field offices of
the Department, with the original hard copies thereof to be maintained by the
Financial Service at the Department Proper.
This Circular amends previous Department Circulars insofar as they are
inconsistent herewith and takes effect for all requests submitted to the PMD,
Administrative Service, and AD, Financial Service, this Department, starting 01
December 2022 until further orders.
