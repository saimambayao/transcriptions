SUBJECT DISCONTINUANCE OF SUBMISSION OF AGENCY MONTHLY
SUMMARY PERFORMANCE MONITORING REPORTS (SPMRs)
1.0 Pursuant to Department of Budget and Management (DBM) Circular Letter No.
2015-91 dated June 30, 2015, the Full-time Delivery Units (FDUs) of
government entities receiving budget support from the national government,
i.e., National Government Agencies and State Universities and Colleges
(SUCs), including those under the Constitutional Fiscal Autonomous Group
(CFAG), are required to submit subject SPMRs to facilitate evaluation of
agency performance against their respective plans and targets. The SPMRs
were envisioned to ensure availability of indicative accountability reports in a
timely manner.
2.0
The SPMRs, which are partly based on available Budget and Financial
Accountability Reports (BFARs):
2.1 Summarize the agencies’ cumulative absorptive capacities (using
obligation and disbursement rates) by appropriation source and by
allotment class. However, such reports do not provide the full
information by program/activity/project nor by object class, which
details are contained in the BFARs in accordance with COA-DBM Joint
Circular (JC) No. 2014-12 dated July 2, 2014.
2.2 Include the following supporting documents:
2.2.1 Reasons for any underperformance; and

2.3 Shall be submitted to the DBM every 10th day of the following month,

(NEDA) and the Office of the Cabinet Secretary.
3.0 Agency compliance with the submission of the Budget and Financial
Accountability Reports (BFARs) has been enhanced since the initial
Implementation of DBM JC NO. 2014-1 dated July 2, 2014, which prescribed
the use of modified formats. Agency submission of BFARs was also facilitated
with better internet connectivity. Hence, higher quality and more detailed
performance information have become available in a timely manner to guide
implementation of measures to improve budget utilization.
4.0
Relative thereto and in view of the availability of more detailed performance
quarterly reports in a timely manner, for purposes of streamlining agency
reportorial requirements, agency submission of the monthly SPMRs to the
DBM and the provision of copies of the same to NEDA shall be discontinued.
