DIGITAL POSTER-MAKING CONTEST IN CELEBRATION OF
WORLD COMPETITION DAY
Theme: "Competition Policyforan Inclusive andResilientEconomy"
A. MECHANICS
1. The Digital Poster-Making Contest, as one of the activities for the annual
observance of World Competition Day, is open to all DOJ Employees
nationwide, including contractual, temporary, and job order personnel.
The Office for Competition (OFC) shall constitute the panel ofjudges who
will choose the winners ranking 1st, 24, and 3d place, with prizes of Eight
Thousand Pesos (Php8,000.00), Five Thousand Pesos (Php5,000.00),
and Three Thousand (Php3,000.00), respectively.
2. Participants may use photoshop, paint, or any digital drawing software or
application.
3. All entries must be consistent to the theme provided, original, and not have
been previously published.
4. An entry must follow the specifications below:
Size: A4 bond paper
Format: PDF or JPEG/JPG
5. Soft copies must be submitted to dojofc.ph@gmail.com.
6. Deadline for the e-mail submission of the entries for judging will be on or
before 03 December 2021, until 5:00pm only.
7. All entries will be posted in the Official Facebook Page ofthe DOJ.
8. Announcement of winners will be on 10 December 2021 and posted on
the DOJ Official Facebook page. The winners will also be informed
through e-mail.
B. CRITERIA FOR JUDGING
All entries will be judged based on the following criteria:
• Theme Relevance 40%
• Originality 30%
• Creativity 30%
Total 100%
