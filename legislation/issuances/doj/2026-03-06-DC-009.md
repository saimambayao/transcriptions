affairs, dealings and transactions, the official logos of the DOJ shall be exclusively as

The adoption of a single, uniform, and standardized logo for said particular
purpose is essential to eliminate inconsistencies, enhance the brand identities of the
Department and its attached/constituent agencies, and ensure conformity with
approved guidelines. You may access and download the e-copies of said logos at
https : //www .doj .gov. ph/doj-official-logo. html .
All other versions or variations of DOJ logos that do not conform to the above
specified logos are hereby suspended and must be discontinued.
This Circular takes effect immediately, supersedes all issuances inconsistent
herewith, and shall remain in force until further orders.
