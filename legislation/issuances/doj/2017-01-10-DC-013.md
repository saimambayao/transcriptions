DEPARTMENT CIRCULAR
SUBJECT : SUBMISSION OF MONTHLY ASSESSMENT AND
ACCOMPLISHMENT REPORT FROM THE OFFICE OF THE
SOLICITOR GENERAL AND THE PUBLIC ATTORNEY'S OFFICE
In the interest of the service, pursuant to existing laws, and considering that the
abovementioned agencies are attached to the Department for purposes of policy and
program coordination, the Solicitor General and the Chief Public Attorney are ENJOINED
to submit a MONTHLY ASSESSMENT AND ACCOMPLISHMENT REPORT.
The Monthly Report shall be brief and contain only the following:
1. Accomplishment
Monthly progress on annual projects;
Actual accomplishment/s (°/0 of performance targets);
Explanation on variance between targets and outputs, if any.
2. Assessment
Problems and issues in their respective offices, with identified
responsibility/responsible officer, if any;
Actions taken or recommended solutions;
Proposals to take a matter on the agenda.
The Monthly Report shall be submitted to the Planning and Management Service,
month. The PMS shall compile the same and provide copies thereof for the Office of the
Secretary and the Undersecretary-in-charge of the office concerned within one (1)
working day from receipt thereof.
All previous issuance are hereby amended or repealed as far as these are
inconsistent herewith.
This Circular shall take effect immediately.
For the Department's information and guidance.
