SUBJECT SUBMISSION OF REPORT FOR THE FIRST ONE HUNDRED

DATE 27 September 2022
In the interest of service, and relative to the upcoming 100th day in office of
President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr., all Heads of Offices/Services/Programs of the
Department and its Attached Agencies are hereby directed to submit a report
containing major highlights and tangible achievements, including available data in
support thereof, accomplished during the first one hundred (100) days of this
administration.
Please send your report to the Office of the undersigned through the Planning
and Management Service (PMS), this Department, at email address pms@}doi.gov ph
not later than 29 September 2022.
For strict compliance.
