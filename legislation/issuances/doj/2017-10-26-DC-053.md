In the interest of the service and pursuant to the provisions of existing laws,
the following are hereby delegated to Undersecretary DEO L. MARCO:
1. National Competitiveness Council;
2. Privatization Council;
3. Philippine National Aids Council;
4. Inter-Agency Committee on Tobacco; and
5. National Coordinating and Monitoring Board for Senior Citizens.
As such, Undersecretary Marco is hereby designated as Principal Representative
of the Secretary and Assistant Secretary MOSLEMEN T. MACARAMBON,S R. as
Alternate Representative.
This Circular amends Department Circular No. 44 dated 05 September 2016,
takes effect immediately and shall remain in force until further orders.
For strict compliance.
