SUBJECT Reconstitution of the Revision of Fees Committee
MAY 2 7 2021
DATE
In the interest of the service and pursuant to Administrative Order( AO) No. 31,
s. 2012 and Joint Circular No. 1-2013 of the Department of Finance, Department of
Budget and Management and National Economic Development Authority (DOF-DBM-
NEDA), the Revision of Fees Committee, this Department, is hereby reconstituted as
follows:
Chairperson Undersecretary for Finance
Co-Chairperson Undersecretary-in-Charge of the Office/Program/Unit
imposing fees and charges
Vice Chairperson Head of Financial Service
Permanent Members Chief of Accounting Division
Chief of Budget Division
Ad hoc lAembers Head of the Office/Program/Unit responsible for
collection/implementation
Planning Officer of the Office/Program/Unit imposing
fees and charges
The Committee shall have the following functions and responsibilities:
1. Review existing fees and charges and conduct a study on the necessity of
imposing new/revised ones, gathering data on the costs of delivery of
services in order to compute the appropriate rates that will recover these
costs;
2. Ensure that the Guiding Principles and Rules in the Determination of Rates
under DOF-DBM-NEDA Joint Circular No. 1-2013 are observed;
3. Submit to the Secretary of Justice report and recommendations regarding
proposed new/revised fees and charges containing the results of the
aforementioned study, including the legal and material bases and
justification for the proposed rates, as well as comments or feedback
received from the public, if any;

4. Coordinate with the Task Force on Fees and Charges on matters pertaining
to the implementation of AO No. 31, s. 2012 and DOF-DBM-NEDA Joint
Circular No. 1-2013; and
5. Keep and maintain all records pertaining to the implementation of AO 31, s.
2012 and DOF-DBM-NEDA Joint Circular No. 1-2013.
This Circular takes effect immediately, supersedes all circulars, orders, and
other issuances inconsistent herewith, and shall remain in force unless amended,
modified and/or revoked.
