1.0 BACKGROUND AND PURPOSE
This pertains to the clarifications on the requirements stated in Memorandum
Circular (MO) No. 2019-1, Guidelines for the Grant of FY 2019 Performance-
Based Bonus (PBB) issued on September 3, 2019, and MC No. 2019-2,
Supplemental Guidelines on the Grant of FY 2019 PBB issued on November 12,
2019.
2.0 CLARIFICATIONS ON THE FY 2019 PHILGEPS REQUIREMENT
2.1 In consideration of the global pandemic that transpired in the recent
months including the community quarantine lockdowns which seriously
affected the work operations, management, and compliance of
departments/agencies to PBB requirements, especially in the early
months, such as the updating of PhilGEPS transactions, adjustments in
the FY 2019 PBB validation process are taken into consideration.

2.2 The PhilGEPS requirement under Section 4.1b of MC No. 2019-1 has
been reclassified from a Good Governance Condition to a General
Administrative and Support Services requirement and shall not be
considered as a basis for a prequalifying condition of the entire
department/agency for the grant of FY 2019 PBB, and
2.3 Non-compliance to the FY 2019 PhilGEPS requirement shall be counted
as one deficiency, and provided that the department/agency does not
have any other deficiencies, the department/agency may be considered
eligible for the FY 2019 PBB. However, the whole procurement unit,
excluding the Bids and Awards Committee, shall be isolated from the
grant of the bonus.
3.0 CLARIFICATIONS ON THE STREAMLINING AND PROCESS IMPROVEMENT
REQUIREMENTS
3.1 For FY 2019 PBB, departments/agencies shall report their streamlining
and process improvements of all critical services covering all Government-
to-Citizens (G2C), Government-to-Businesses (G2B), and Government-to-
Government (G2G) transactions, dealing with external clients as declared
in the updated Citizen's Charter. Hence, the transactions made in behalf
of internal clients are not covered by the assessment.
4.0 EFFECTIVITY CLAUSE
This Memorandum Circular shall take effect immediately.
Certified true copies shall be posted on the RBPMS website and the Official
Gazette, and shall be filed at the University of the Philippines Law Center.
