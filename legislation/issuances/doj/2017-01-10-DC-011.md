SUBJECT :(cid:9) SUBMISSION OF ANNUAL PLAN OF ACTION AND
ACTIVITIES FOR FY 2017
In the interest of the service, pursuant to pertinent laws, and in accordance
with the power of supervision and control of the Secretary of Justice over attached
and constituent agencies of the Department, as the case may be, all attached and
constituent agencies of the Department of Justice are hereby DIRECTED to prepare
and submit an Annual Plan of Action and Activities for FY 2017.
The plan shall present each agency's official calendar of projects, programs,
and activities for FY 2017. It shall be subject to amendment should an agency make
changes thereon. It shall serve as a guide and reference for the Department in its
planning and coordination with the concerned agencies.
All agencies are enjoined to submit their respective plans to the Planning and
Management Service, this Department, on or before the next Management
Committee meeting on February 14, 2017.
For the Department's information and guidance.
