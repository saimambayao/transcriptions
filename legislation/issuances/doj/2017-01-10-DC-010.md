The Execom shall be the highest advisory council for the Department. It shall
assist the Secretary in the performance of the functions and powers of his office. It
shall have the following duties, in addition to their regular and official functions:
Advices the Secretary on all matters relating to the administration
of the Department Proper and its attached agencies and
constituent services, programs and projects;
Considers issues involving budgets, task forces, committees, and
other activities;
Provides general oversight of the department;

### Page 2

Keeps track of the Department's performance viz-a-viz its annual
performance targets;
Perform such other duties as may be assigned by the Secretary.
In addition, Undersecretaries-in-charge shall regularly report during the
Execom meetings on matters relating to their respective delineated responsibilities,
particularly on the Monthly Reports submitted by the offices under their jurisdictions,
as well as their actions taken thereon or recommendations therefor.
The Execom shall have a regular meeting every 2' Monday of the month
starting February 13, 2017, at 9:00 A.M. The regular meeting shall be held, as far as
practicable, at Room 206, 2/F De Las Alas Hall, Department of Justice.
For the foregoing purpose, the Planning and Management Service (PMS)
shall provide Secretariat and Logistical Support functions to the Execom. The
Director, PMS, shall serve as secretariat head and he shall assist the committee in its
regular meetings, agenda, record-keeping and other necessary assistance in the
performance of the aforementioned duties.
All previous issuance are hereby amended or repealed insofar as inconsistent
herewith.
This Circular shall take effect immediately.
For the Department's information and guidance.
