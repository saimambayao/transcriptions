RESPONSE
DATE Nov 1$ 2071
Pursuantto Resolution No. 149 dated 18 November2021 ofthe Inter-Agency Task
Force on the ManagementofEmerging Infectious Diseases (IATF-MEID), all offices ofthe
Department ofJustice in the National Capital Region shall be fully operational through an
onsite workforce of at least eighty percent (80%) and the rest under a work-from-home
arrangement. The following offices, however, shall operate onsite at one hundred percent
(100%) capacity:
1. Office ofthe Secretary Proper;
2. Offices ofthe Undersecretaries and Assistant Secretaries;
3. Administrative Service;
4. Financial Service;
5. Planning and Management Service;
6. Information and Communications Technology Service;
7. Internal Audit Service;
8. Technical Staff; and
9. All personnel performing administrative/clerical tasks stationed at the
Department Proper.
The heads of Regional Prosecution Offices shall adopt the onsite capacity
prescribed by the IATF, depending on the alert level in place in their respective areas.
This Circular takes effect immediately and shall remain in force until 30 November
2021, subject to any such extension or amendment as may be declared or issued by the
IATF-MEID or any other competent authority.
