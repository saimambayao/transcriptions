In line with the approval by President Rodrigo R. Duterte on 13 May 2021 of the
recommendation of the Inter-Agency Task Fcxce on the Management of Emerging
Infectious Diseases (lATF-MEID) to de-escalate the community quarantine classification
of Metro Manila and the provinces of Cavite, Laguna, Bulacan, and Rizal (NCR Plus) to
General Community Quarantine( GCQ)w ith heightened restrictions from 15 May 2021 to
31 May 2021, on-site work at the DOJ shall be adjusted to 30% of the personnel of each
office or unit. The rest shall continue to work from home. All otf^r existing rules and
regulations, insofar as they remain applicable under GCQ, shall be observed.
Prosecutors and their support staff shall continue to conduct inquest proceedings
online and/or in person, and attend to urgent matters that require immediate action, as
may be determined by the heads of the prosecution offices concerned. Accordingly, all
prosecution offices are directed to ensure the availability at all times of inquest prosecutors
who shall be on-call for the duration of the GCQ. The conduct of inquest proceedings shall
proceed in accordance with the Rules of Court and existing rules and regulations of the
Department.
This Circular takes effect immediately and shall remain in force for the duration of
the GCQ, subject to any such extension or amendment as may be declared or issued by
the lATF-MEID or other competent authority.
