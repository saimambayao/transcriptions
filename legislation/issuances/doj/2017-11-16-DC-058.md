Subject : Observance of the National Children's Month Act
Date : N0V16 2017
Pursuant to the National Children's Month Act known as Republic Act No. 10661
declaring every November as the National Children's Month, our support is calied upon
to support the Council for the Welfare of Children (CWC) in its celebration of the 25^
Silver Anniversary of the Children's Month Celebration.
Considering that this Department is a member of the Inter-agency Committee on
Anti-Illegal Drugs,( ICAD), and as partner of the CWC in our commitments and projects
for the welfare of children, we are requested to undertake activities which are geared
towards the attainment of the theme: "BATA: Iligtas sa Droga!". This theme is
aligned with the Administration's efforts to fight illegal Drugs.
Hence, in the interest of service, you are hereby required to promote the event
and the campaign message through:
1. Putting up streamers/logo design in the conspicuous places in your office using
the attached design;
2. Conducting of symposia, forum, media and/or online campaign;
3. Posting of the event and its theme in the official website.
You are hereby required to submit a report of actions taken (with pictures)
within five (5) days from receipt hereof through electronic submission to this email
address: yhs.lawl017@gmail.com.
