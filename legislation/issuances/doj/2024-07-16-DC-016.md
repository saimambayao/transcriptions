Dear Sec. Remulla:
Greetings from the Land Transportation Office (LTO) !
I hope this letter finds you in good health and high spirits. As we approach the Celebration
of the 127th Anniversary of the Proclamation of Philippine Independence on June 12,
2025, it is an opportune moment for us to reflect on our shared commitment to serving our
nation and its people. In line with this celebration, the LTO is pleased to announce a range of
services and initiatives designed to support employees/personnel from the National
Government Agencies (NGAs) and Local Government Units (LGUs), and enhance their
experience during this significant occasion.
Relative thereto, the LTO is inviting employees from your Office to visit our booth during the
Pampamahalaang Programa af Serbisyo on June 10-11, 2025 at the Liwasang Rizal,
Manila. The LTO will be offering services such as Free Medical Service to those who have
successfully completed the Theoretical Driving Course (TDC), Student Permit Application,
and Driver’s License (DL) Renewal to those who have successfully undertaken the
Comprehensive Driver’s Education (CDE) Examination. We understand that many
government employees may wish to take advantage of these services during the specified
period, and we are commit to accommodate as many as possible; however only those who
submitted the necqssqrygocy_mentary requirements will be processed.

public who also wish to avail the services,

responsible driving. Thus, we humbly seek your participation in the information dissemination
in promoting these services and initiatives.
We believe that through our collective efforts, we can create a more efficient and safer
environment for everyone as we celebrate our independence.
