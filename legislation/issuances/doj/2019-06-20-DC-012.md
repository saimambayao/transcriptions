In the interest of the service and upon the invitation of the Dangerous
Drugs Board (DDB), you are hereby enjoined to support the celebration of the
International Day Against Drug Abuse and Illicit Trafficking (IDADAIT) on
26 June 2019 with the theme, "Katarungan Para sa Kalusugan, Kalusugan
Para Sa Katarungan."
The program for the IDADAIT 2019 will be held at the Vista Mall Open
Parking, Balanga, Bataan on 22 June 2019, 2:00 p.m. onwards.
Relative thereto, you are encouraged to undertake activities to promote
the event and the campaign message through the following;
1. Putting up streamers/banners in strategic places in your agency /
office (see attached template);
2. Posting of the event and its theme on your electronic billboard, (if
any); and
3. Participation of your staff in the IDADAIT 2019 celebration (Please
note that food, transportation and other incidental expenses shall be
borne by the sending organization).
For other concerns on the IDADAIT, please contact Mr. Rex P.
Cammayo at 0939-2048258 or email rpcammavo.ddb@qmail.com. and Ms.
Liane Angelica M. Kalacas at 0923-4247443 or email
lamkalacas.ddb@qmail.com.
For information, guidance and appropriate action.
