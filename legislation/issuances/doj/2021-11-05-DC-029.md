DATE(cid:9) : 05 November 2021
In view of the approval by the Office of the President of the recommendation
of the Inter-Agency Task Force on the Management of Emerging Infectious
Diseases (IATF-MEID) to de-escalate the National Capital Region (NCR) to Alert
Level 2, all offices of the Department of Justice in the NCR shall be fully operational
for 05-21 November 2021 through an onsite skeletal workforce of at least fifty
percent (50%), on a rotational basis, and the rest under a work-from-home
arrangement, except the offices under the Financial and Administrative Services
which shall operate onsite at one hundred percent (100%) capacity.
The heads of the City Prosecution Offices in the NCR may increase the
onsite operational capacity of their workforce, when the exigency of the service so
requires.
This Circular takes effect immediately and shall remain in force until further
orders, subject to any such amendment as may be declared or issued by the IATF-
MEID or any other competent authority.
For strict compliance.
