Presidential Directive No. PBBM- 2024-1286
TO All Heads of Departments and Agencies
FROM The Executive Secretary
SUBJECT INSTITUTIONALIZATION OF SINGLE PASS RICE AND HALF-CUP
RICE OPTIONS IN GOVERNMENT INSTITUTIONS
DATE 20 November 2024
During the 8th Meeting with the Private Sector Advisory Council-Agriculture
Sector Group (PSAC-ASG) on 20 November 2024, the PSAC-ASG reported a
significant amount of rice wastage in a year. Thus, they prornoted the consumption of
half-cup rice and single pass rice as a healthier alternative to white rice to improve rice
self-sufficiency .
In the course of the discussion, the President directed all government agencies
to institutionalize the one-pass rice and half-cup rice option in all government
establishments and institutions.
Kindly submit a status report on this matter, citing the above-stated reference
number, to the email address: directives@pms.gov.ph within thirty (30) days from
receipt of this directive, and regular updates every thirty (30) days thereafter until the
same has been fully complied with.
For your appropriate action.
