Professionalization Program for Public Procurement
Practitioners
The Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila, together with the Department of
Budget and Management Is offering a three-level training program for procurement
officers and practitioners, entitled Professionalization Program for Procurement
Practitioners.
The training program, which was designed by the department of Budget and
Management, is divided into three levels: Basic, Intermediate, and Advanced. The
Basic Level consists of 52 hours, the Intermediate Level 60 hours, and the Advanced
Level 56 hours.
The detailed Information regarding the course topics, schedule, and fees for each
level are contained in the attached brochure.
For interested employees, submission of application shall be forwarded to the
Office of the Secretary thru the Training Section, Administrative Service, this
Department, not later than 18 September 2018.
For information, guidance and appropriate action.
