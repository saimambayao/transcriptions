Dear Secretary Guevarra,
Greetings!
This is to formally inform your good office on the recent reassignment of Bureau
Directors in the Budget Preparation and Execution Group( BPE)o f the Department.
As a matter of policy, DBM observes rotation of assignments especially of Officials
to ensure their exposure to a variety of budgetary issues relevant to the different
agencies and imbue them with an appreciation of the whole budget process.
Please also note of the renaming of our Performance Monitoring and Evaluation
Bureau (PMEB) to Budget and Management Bureau-F (BMB-F) which will handle
the University of the Philippines and Mindanao State University Systems, Department
of Education, Commission on Higher Education, including the overall supervision of
the State Universities and Colleges while maintaining its monitoring and evaluation
functions until the same are fully transferred to the BMB concerned.
In view of the abovementioned modifications, we are furnishing you herewith for
your guidance, the list of Directors in the BPE Group with their newly-assigned
Bureaus and covered agencies( Annexes "A" and "B").
We request that this information be shared to your respective attached agencies.
Thank you and best regards.
Very truly yours,
