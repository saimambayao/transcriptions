In the interest of service and pursuant to the provisions of existing laws, the
following areas of duties and responsibilities previously under the Assistant Secretary-
in-Charge of the Gender and Special Protections Sub Cluster are hereby delegated
and transferred to Senior Assistant State Prosecutor MA. NERISSA M. CARPIO;
1. Juvenile Justice and Welfare Council
2. Committee for the Special Protection of Children
3. Council for the Welfare of Children
4. National Child Labor Committee
5. Committee on Gender and Development
6. Inter-Agency Council on Violence Against Women and their Children
7. National Coordinating and Monitoring Board on the Expanded Senior
Citizens Act of 2010
8. Governing Board of the National Council on Disability Affairs
9. Indigenous Peoples Concerns
It is understood that Undersecretary Emmeline Aglipay-Villar shall continue to
have direct supervision and control over matters delegated above.
Further, Senior Assistant State Prosecutor Carpio is hereby designated to the
following positions vice former Assistant Secretary Cheryl L. Daytec-Yahgot:
1. Executive Head Technical Working Group to formulate the
rules and procedures integrating the
customary justice system and alternative
dispute resolution processes of indigenous
peoples and cultural communities in the
conduct of preliminary investigation in criminal
cases created under Department Order (DO)
No. 510, s. 2018
2. Vice Chairperson DOJ Committee for Women and Children
created under DO No. 717, s. 2016
