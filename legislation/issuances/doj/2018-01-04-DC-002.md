In the interest of service and to ensure that the technical nature of
cybercrime and its prevention is given focus, all Regional, Provincial, and
City Prosecutors, as well as the Chairpersons of the various Prosecution Task
Force in the Department proper are hereby enjoined to submit monthly
inventory report of cybercrime and cyber-related cases pending before their
respective offices, following the format indicated below:
DATE NPS COMPLAINANT RESPONDENT CRIME/ HANDLING STATUS
FILED NO. OFFENSE PROSECUTOR
XXX XXX XXX XXX XXX XXX XXX
For purposes of this Department Circular, cybercrime and cyber-
related cases shall pertain to cases filed before the National Prosecution
Service for violation of Republic Act (R.A.) No. 10175 or the Cybercrime
Prevention Act of 2012, as well as those crimes defined and penalized by
the Revised Penal Code, as amended, and special laws, if the same are filed
in relation to R.A. No. 10175, or where electronic evidence are involved.
The requisite Inventory Report shall be forwarded to the Department
of Justice - Office of Cybercrime every 10^ day of the succeeding month,
starting in January 2018.
