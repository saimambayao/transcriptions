TO: ALL PROSECUTORS
RE: REITERATING THE STATUTORY IMMUNITY IN FAVOR OF SOCIAL
WORKERS TAKING PROTECTIVE CUSTODY OF CHILDREN
DATE:
MAR2 2 2023
Anent the 24 February 2023-meeting of the undersigned with stakeholders to
further strengthen child protection vis-a-vis reports of social workers still
apprehensive of doing their duties albeit our Department Circular Nos. 70 (s.2006)
and 77 (s.2007), and consistent with Section 281 of Republic Act (RA) No. 7610, also
known as the “Special Protection of Children Against Abuse, Exploitation and
Discrimination Act, ” and Section 262 of RA No. 11930, otherwise known as the “Anti-
Online Sexual Abuse or Exploitation of Children (OSAEC) and Anti-Child Sexual
Abuse or Exploitation Materials (OSAEM) Act, ” all prosecutors are hereby
reminded and strictly enjoined, under pain of sanctions in case of non-
compliance, to not file criminal cases against social workers of the Department
of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) and local government units,
including social workers of duly accredited institutions, by reason of their
performance of official duty to take temporary protective custody of children
living in high risk and/or difficult circumstances.
This Department Circular shall take immediate effect until r/nodified.
For strict compliance.
