In order to facilitate settlement of valid government payables within the
current year and avoid recurrence of unpaid obligations, all money claims, such
as but not limited to salaries, allowances and other personal benefits,
transportation expenses for designated special disbursing and liaison officers,
should be filed, together with all supporting documents, to the Financial
Service, this Department, on or before the following non-extendlble
deadlines:
1. For payment and crediting in 2017 22 December 2017
-
2. For obligation as accounts payable 5 January 2018
-
Claims received after the 5 January 2018 deadline will be categorized as
unbooked obligations, and shall be processed or paid in accordance with the
applicable rules of the Commission on Audit and Department of Budget and
Management.
The Financial and Administrative Services are likewise directed to inform
the Department's creditors or suppliers to ensure timely processing of any
unpaid obligations within the current year.
