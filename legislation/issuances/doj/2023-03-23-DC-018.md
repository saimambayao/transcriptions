Pursuant to DICT Memorandum Circular No. 006 dated 1 August 2017, and DOJ Department
Circular No. 010 dated 13 February 2018, all programs, projects, activities and resources
necessary for CERT to fulfill its mandate, shall be included in the Information Systems Strategic
Plan (ISSP) submitted to and approved by DICT, and in the annual agency budget proposals and
expenditure programs.
All previous issuances contrary to this Circular are hereby amended. This Circular takes effect
immediately, and shall remain in force until further orders.
