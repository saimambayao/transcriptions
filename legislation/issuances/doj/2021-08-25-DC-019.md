DATE
AUG 2 5 2021
Attached, for information, is an invitation for nominations to the Middle
Managers Class (MMC) Batch 28 of the Public Management Development Program,
Development Academy of the Philippines, which will open on 11 October 2021 under
a flexible learning mode.
Applicants/nominees must be incumbents Division Chiefs (Salary Grade 24) or
those in equivalent positions, designated Officers-in-Charge (01C) Division Chiefs for
at least one (1) year, or high-performing and high-potential employees with salary
grade 18-23. All applicants/nominees must be fifty (50) years old or below, in good
health, and have no ongoing/pending completion of post-graduate studies at the time
of application.
Interested applicants may submit their applications to the Office of the
Secretary thru the Training Section, Administrative Service, this Department, not later
than 31 August 2021.
For any inquiries, you may directly contact Ms. Shellie Marie C. Dejan, PDMP
Recruitment and Admissions, at mobile number 0917-128-2732, or email at
pmdp.admissions@dap.edu.ph.
