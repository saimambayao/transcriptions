SUBJECT : GUIDELINES FOR THE DURATION OF ALERT LEVEL 3 FOR COVID-19
IN THE NATIONAL CAPITAL REGION (03-15 JANUARY 2022)

DATE : 02 JANUARY 2022

In line with the escalation of alert level system in the National Capital Region (NCR) to
Alert Level 3 for 03-15 January 2022, and pursuant to Memorandum Circular No. 93 dated 17
December 2021 of the Office of the President, all offices of the Department of Justice in the
NCR shall be fully operational through an onsite skeletal workforce of at least sixty percent
(60%) and the rest under a work-from-home arrangement.

This Circular takes effect immediately and shall remain in force until further orders,
subject to any such amendment as may be declared or issued by the Inter-Agency Task Force
for the Management of Emerging Infectious Diseases (IATF-MEID) or any other competent
authority.

For strict compliance.
