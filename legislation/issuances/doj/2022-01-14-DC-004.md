DEPARTMENT CIRCULAR NO. 0 0) | 4
TO : ALL CONCERNED
SUBJECT : EXTENSION OF DEADLINE FOR FILING AND SUBMISSION

OF PLEADINGS WITH THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE
AND THE NATIONAL PROSECUTION SERVICE

Date : JAN 14 2022

In the exigency of the service, in view of the devastating effects of typhoon
Odette in Bicol, Palawan, Visayas, and Mindanao, the recent surge of COVID-19
cases in the entire nation, and pursuant to the relevant resolutions of the Inter-Agency
Task Force for the Management of Emerging Infectious Diseases and Section 7,
Chapter 2 and Sections 1 and 3, Chapter 1, Title Ill, Book IV of Executive Order No.
292, series of 1987, or The Administrative Code of 1987, the filing and submission of
affidavits, motions, position papers, Petitions for Review, responsive pleadings
thereto, or other relevant papers before the Department of Justice, particularly the.
Office of the Secretary Appeals Management Office, and the National Prosecution
Service, whose due date falls within the period of 14 December 2021 to 01 February
2022, are hereby extended until 02 February 2022.

The heads of the Regional/Provincial/City Prosecution Offices shall strictly
observe this directive regardless of the existing alert level classification in their
respective areas.

This Circular takes effect immediately and shall remain in force unless sooner
modified or revoked.

For strict compliance.

MENARDO I. GUEVARRA
Secretary
