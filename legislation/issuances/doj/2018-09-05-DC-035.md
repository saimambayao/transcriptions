1. We would like to formaily invite your Office to nominate candidate/s for 2-year Master's
Degree program covering Japanese Academic Years (AY) 2019 and 2020, and 3-year
Ph.D. program covering AY2019 to AY2021 under the Project for Human Resource
Development Scholarship by Japanese Grant Aid (JDS). Since its Inception in 2003,
the JDS Project has dispatched 340 fellows from different Philippine Government
agencies and organizations to various prestigious Japanese universities.
2. The Framework of the JDS Project from 2019-2022 (copy attached as Annex A) will
be adopted for the 17'^ Batch of JDS Fellows who will be dispatched in 2019. Under
said framework, twenty (20) fellows from various Philippine Government agencies will
be dispatched each year from 2019 to 2022 to take master's degree programs from
respected Japanese Universities. On the other hand, two (2) Philippine civil servants
will be dispatched each year from 2019 to 2022 to take doctoral degree programs in
prestigious Japanese Universities of their choice.
3. Further information on the JDS Project will be provided during the promotion seminars
to be held from August to September 2018 in Metro Manila, Bicol, lloilo and Davao
(exact schedule and venue are being finalized). Interested HRD officers and personnel
are encouraged to participate in the seminars.
4. The deadline for submission of the candidates' nominations with their application
documents is on October 31. 2018. Kindly note the following important reminders;
a. The application documents should be submitted to the JICE JDS Project Office in
the Philippines, either personally or by mail/courier to the address below:
Address: JICE JDS Project Office in the Philippines

Salcedo Village, Makati City, Philippines 1227
