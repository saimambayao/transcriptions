SUBJECT : GUIDELINES FOR THE DURATION OF THE MODIFIED ENHANCED
COMMUNITY QUARANTINE (MECQ). 04-18 AUGUST 2020
DATE : 03 August 2020
Pursuant to relevant issuances of the Inter-Agency Task Force for the Management
of Emerging Infectious Diseases (lATF), in view of the proclamation of the President placing
the National Capital Region (NCR) and other high-risk areas under Modified Enhanced
Community Quarantine (MECQ) anew, and to safeguard the health and safety of all
concerned, DOJ employees shall adopt a work-from-home arrangement, except those
identified as skeleton staff at the frontline services and other offices, divisions, units and
programs of the Department, in accordance with Department Circular No. 008 dated 13
March 2020, particularly the provisions on the Skeletal Workforce on Rotation Basis, for the
duration of the MECQ in the region, province, city, municipality and/or area concerned.
This Circular shall take effect immediately and shall remain in force for the duration of
the MECQ, subject to any extension or such amendments as maybe necessitated by any
new or further guidelines issued by the lATF.
MENARDO I. GUEVARRA
Secretary
