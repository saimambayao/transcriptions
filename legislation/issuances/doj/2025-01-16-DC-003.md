Dear Secretary Remulla:
Assalamua’alaykum Warahmatullahi Wabarakatuh!
I am pleased to inform you that the Philippines will host the next 2025 Open
Government Partnership (OGP) Asia and Pacific Regional Meeting on February 5 – 7,
2025. The OGP is an international initiative launched in 2011 that promotes
transparent, participatory, inclusive, and accountable governance through
collaborations between the government and civil society.
As one of the founding member countries of the OGP, the Philippines has been
selected to host the conference, which will bring together high-level representatives
of government, civil society leaders, and policymakers from across the region, as well
as global and regional partners to discuss open government initiatives and
implementation of key issues.
In line with the OGP Asia and Pacific Regional Meeting, the Department of Budget and
Management, in partnership with the Metro Manila Development Authority (MMDA) is
organizing an OGP Fun Run on January 26, 2025, Sunday at the Quirino Grandstand.
This event is a precursor to the Regional Meeting, designed to raise awareness and
generate support. Additionally, all proceeds from the event will directly support the
humanitarian efforts of the Philippine Red Cross (PRC).

With this, we warmly invite the Department of Justice (DOJ) to join us in this
cause by participating in the Run and supporting our efforts to promote awareness of
the OGP Asia Pacific event.
The OGP Fun Run is scheduled for January 26, 2025, and will take place at Quirino
Grandstand in Manila, Philippines. It will feature 3KM, 5KM, and 10KM routes,
offering an engaging and inclusive activity for participants on varying fitness levels.
The event program will commence at 3:30 AM, with the gun start scheduled at 4:30
AM. We anticipate around 3,000 participants, including representatives from
government agencies, private organizations, and other sectors. Registration fees for
the event are set at PHP 700 for the 3KM route, PHP 800 for the 5KM route, and PHP
1,000 for the 10KM route.
