SUBJECT Renaming the Divisions of the Internal Audit Service
DATE $27 February 2026
In the interest of the service and in line with DOJ Memorandum Circular No
033 dated 13 November 2023, with subject Department of Budget and Management
(DBM) Circular Letter No. 2023-14 dated 27 October 2023, entitled, “Revised
Guidelines on the Organization and Staffing of an Internal Audit Unit (IAU) in
Departments, Agencies, SUCs, GOCCs/GFls, and LGUs,” which provides that the
“IAS shall have a maximum of two (2) divisions, namely, Internal Audit Division I and
Internal Audit Division II,” the IAS Divisions are hereby renamed, as follows:
Operations Audit Division (OAD) - Internal Audit Division I (IADI
)
Management Audit Division (MAD) - Internal Audit Division II (IAD2)
The renaming follows their order in the DOJ Plantilla.
This Circular supersedes all issuances inconsistent herewith and shall remain
in force until further orders.
