The Mancom shall serve as an advisory council for the Department. It shall
assist the Secretary in the performance of the functions and powers of his office,
particularly, on apprising him and the Execom on specific concerns of the attached
and constituent agencies of the Department.
The Mancom shall have a regular meeting every 2nd Tuesday of the month
starting February 14, 2017, at 9:00 A.M.' The regular meeting shall be hosted by the
attached agencies of the Department on a rotational basis, viz:
National Bureau of Investigation Feb. 14, 2017
Bureau of Immigration Mar. 14, 2017
Bureau of Corrections Apr. 11, 2017
Land Registration Authority May 9, 2017
Public Attorney's Office Jun. 13, 2017
Office of the Government Corporate Counsel Jul. 11, 2017
Office of the Solicitor General Aug. 8, 2017
Presidential Commission on Good Government Sep. 12, 2017
Office for Alternative Dispute Resolution Oct. 10, 2017
Parole and Probation Administration Nov. 14, 2017
In addition, the Undersecretary-in-charge shall regularly report during the
Mancom meetings on matters relating to their respective delineated responsibilities,
particularly on the Monthly Reports submitted by the attached and constituent
agencies under their jurisdictions, as well as their actions taken thereon or
recommendations therefor.
For the foregoing purpose, the Planning and Management Service shall
provide Secretariat and Logistical Support functions to the Mancom, except on the
logistics and venue of regular Mancom meetings which shall be hosted by the office
concerned.
The Director, PMS, shall serve as secretariat head and he shall assist the
committees in its regular meetings, agenda, record-keeping and other necessary
assistance in the performance of the aforementioned duties.
If the date falls on a holiday or a non-working day, the Mancom meeting concerned shall be
rescheduled on the immediately succeeding Tuesday.
