WHEREAS, Department Circular (D.C.) No. 041, dated 15 August 2017,
prescribing the guidelines on the issuance of Temporary Visitor's Visa Upon Arrival
(TV-VUA) to PROC nationals was issued pursuant to the mandate of the Bureau of
Immigration (81) under Commonwealth Act No. 613, otherwise known as the
"Philippine Immigration Act of 1940," as amended, and other relevant laws;
WHEREAS, the Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority (SBMA) has requested for
the amendment of D.C. No. 041 to include SBMA as one of the institutions that can
endorse TV-VUA to Chinese nationals;
WHEREAS, the Bl poses no objection to the request considering that the
amendment will contribute to increasing the number of Chinese tourists in the
Philippines and that the SBMA is the agency tasked to promote the Subic Bay
Freeport Zone;
WHEREAS, under Section 13(b)(2) and (3) of Republic Act (R.A.) No. 7227,^
as amended, otherwise known as the "Bases Conversion and Development Act of
1992," it is within the mandate of SBMA to, among others, accept any local or foreign
investment, business or enterprise; or undertake and regulate the establishment,
operation and maintenance of services in the Subic Special Economic Zone,
including shipping and related business and airport operations;
WHEREAS, Executive Order (E.G.) No. 72, dated 18 December 2018, was
issued amending E.G. No. 271 (s. 1995), governing the admission and stay of
foreign nationals in the Subic Bay Freeport Zone as temporary visitors, which
pertinently provides that SBMA, along with the Department of Foreign Affairs (DFA),
and the Bl, shall jointly implement the said E.G. No. 72 and are thereby authorized
