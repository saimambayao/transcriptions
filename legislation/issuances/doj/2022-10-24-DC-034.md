In the interest of the service and pursuant to the provisions of existing laws, the
following areas of duties and responsibilities under the Legal Services and Liaison Cluster
of Department Circular No. 029 dated 13 September 2022 are hereby delegated to
Assistant Secretary MAJKEN ANIKA S. GRAN-ONG and Assistant Secretary
RANDOLPH A. PASCASIO as Alternate Representatives:
1. Committee for the Special Protection of Children
2. Committee on Women and Children
3. Committee on Gender and Development
4. Juvenile Justice and Welfare Council
5. Council for the Welfare of Children
6. National Council Against Child Labor
7. Inter-Agency Council Against Child Pornography
8. Inter-Agency Committee on Children in Situations of Armed Conflict
9. Inter-Agency Council on Violence Against Women and their Children
10. Committee on Decorum and Investigation of Sexual Harassment Cases
It is understood that the Undersecretary on the Legal Services and Liaison Cluster
shall be the Primary Representative and continue to have direct supervision and control
over matters delegated above. This delegation shall be in addition to existing duties and
responsibilities of Assistant Secretaries Gran-Ong and Pascasio.
This Circular amends Department Circular No. 029 dated 13 September 2022, takeIS
effect immediately, and shall remain in force until furtrer orders
