DATE 11 April 2021
In view of the approval by President Rodrigo R. Duterte of the recommendation of
the Inter-Agency Task Force on the Management of Emerging and Re-emerging Diseases
(lATF-MEID)t o reclassify the level of community quarantine in Metro Manila and provinces
in Cavite, Laguna, Bulacan and Rizal (NCR Plus) to Mcxiified Enhanced Community
Quarantine (MECQ), work-from-home arrangement shall continue to be implemented in
the subject areas from 12-30 April 2021.
To ensure continuity of service in the Department of Justice, each office shall
maintain a skeletal staff at a twenty percent( 20%) maximum capacity. Prosecxitors and
their support staff shall continue to conduct inquest proceedings online and/or in person,
and attend to urgent matters that require immediate action, as may be determined by the
heads of the prosecution offices concerned. Accordingly, all prosecution offices are
directed to ensure the availability at all times of inquest prosecxitors for the duration of the
MECQ. The conduct of inquest proceedings shall proceed in accordance with the Rules
on Court and existing rules and regulations of the Department.
This Circular takes effect immediately and shall remain in force for the duration of
the MECQ, subject to any such extension or amendment as may be declared or issued
by the lATF-MEID or other competent authority.
For strict compliance.
