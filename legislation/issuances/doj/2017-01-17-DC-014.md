SUBJECT :(cid:9) Invitation for Executives and/or High-Ranking Officials to
Participate in the Upcoming Off Season of UNTV Cup
DATE
JAN 172011
Upon invitation of Mr. Atoy Co, Commissioner of UNTV Cup, this Department
shall join the upcoming Off Season of UN7V Basketball Cup to be held sometime April to
May 2017. A fifteen (15) player line up composed of executives and/or high ranking
officials of this Department shall be formed to join the said event. Accordingly, all DOJ
Officials, Heads of Agencies and their Officials (with a Salary Grade of 18 up) who are
interested to join the DOJ basketball team are hereby directed to submit their names to
the Office of the Secretary not later than January 20, 2017.
The following officials are hereby designated to organize the said team:
Undersecretary Erickson H. Blames —Coach;
Commissioner Reynaldo 0. Munsayac — Assistant Coach;
Atty. Jolas E. Brutas —Team Manager; and
ACP Laurence Joel M. Taliping —Assistant Team Manager.
