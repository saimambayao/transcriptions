SUBJECT : INTERIM WORK ARRANGEMENTS FOR 10 JANUARY 2022 AND
ONWARDS

KE JAN 07 2022

In view of the recent surge of COVID-19 cases in the National Capital Region
(NCR), all offices in the Department Proper shall be operational through an onsite
skeletal workforce of thirty percent (30%) and the rest under a work-from-home
arrangement. The following offices, however, shall maintain a sixty percent (60%)
onsite workforce, to wit: “

Office of the Secretary Proper;

Offices of the Undersecretaries and Assistant Secretaries;
Office of the Secretary Appeals Management Office;
Administrative Service;

Financial Service;

Technical Staff; and

Frontline Services.

SDF en fe

The heads of Regional/Provincial/City Prosecution Offices shall adopt the
onsite capacity prescribed by the Inter-Agency Task Force on the Management of
Emerging Infectious Diseases (IATF-MEID), depending on the alert level in their
respective areas.

This Circular takes effect immediately and shall remain in force until further
orders, subject to any such amendment as may be declared or issued by the IATF-
MEID or any other competent authority.

For strict compliance.
