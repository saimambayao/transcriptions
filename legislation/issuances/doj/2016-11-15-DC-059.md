In order to facilitate settlement of valid government payables of the
Department within the current year, everyone is advised to submit all claims,
together with the supporting documents as required under Department Circular
No. 27 dated 14 June 2016, to the Financial Service, this Department, on or
before 29 December 2016 (a non-extendible deadline).
Everyone is further advised that claims filed after the deadline shall be
considered either as Accounts Payable (if filed prior to 6 January 2017), or
Unbooked Obligations (if filed after 6 January 2017), and shall be processed in
accordance with the applicable rules of the Commission on Audit and
Department of Budget and Management.
