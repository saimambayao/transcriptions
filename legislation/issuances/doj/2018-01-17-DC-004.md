DEPARTMENT CIRCULAR NO
SUBJECT Authority of Undersecretaries to Issue Office Orders for
Local Travel on Official Business
DATE JAN 1 7 2018
In the interest of the service and pursuant to the provisions of existing laws,
All DOJ Undersecretaries are hereby designated and authorized to issue Office
Orders directing or authorizing local travel on official business including
payment of allowable travel expenses of personnel under immediate supervision
and in offices within respective delegated functional responsibilities per existing
Department issuances, except for Assistant Secretaries and Heads of constituent/
attached agencies which shall be acted upon and approved by the Secretary of Justice.
This authority is limited to official travel within the country that are necessary
for regular/day-to-day functions and operations of supervised personnel and
offices particularly field work, official representation and attendance in meetings, and
similar/related purposes.
All other official travels shall be authorized by the Secretary and Undersecretary
for Administration and Finance, subject to provisions of Department Circular No. 074,
s. 2013, re: Requirements and Internal Guidelines in the Submission and Processing
of Travel Authorities for the Approval of the Secretary of Justice, and other related
rules, regulations and issuances.
This Circular takes effect immediately and amends/supersedes the provisions
of earlier issuances that are inconsistent herewith, and remains in force until further
directives.
For compliance.
