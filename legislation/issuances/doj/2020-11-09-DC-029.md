As part of the Department's celebration of the 2020 National Children's Month
(NCM) and the 18-Day Campaign To End Violence Against Women and Children
(VAWC), the Department of Justice-Committee on Women and Children prepared
the following activities:
Online Exhibit on Online Sexual Exploitation of Children (OSEC)
Awareness to be uploaded and advertised on the official websites of the
Department and Inter-Agency Council Against Trafficking (IACAT) from 16
November 2020 up to 15 December 2020.
Wearing of National Children's Month Shirts by the employees of the
Department on four (4) Mondays until the end of the 18-Day Campaign To
End VAWC — 16, 23 and 30 November and 7 December 2020.
One hundred (100) official National Children's Month shirts will be printed and
distributed to the different offices within the Department.
Children's Webinar and Dialogue Consultation on OSEC Awareness in
partnership with SaferKids PH, Childs Rights Network, and IACAT, scheduled
on 21 November 2020, at 2:00 P.M. via Zoom Video Communications, Inc.
Forty (40) children from various SaferKids PH partner organizations will have
an OSEC awareness webinar and consultation dialogue with Undersecretary
Aglipay-Villar and Australian Ambassador Steven Robinson. This will be in a
form of an exclusive recorded talk show to be edited by a creative team, the
final output of which will be shared to the public via the official websites of the
Department a week after.

Cyber Parenting Webinar in partnership with the Department of
Justice-Office of Cybercrime (D0J-00C) for the employees of the
Department who are parents and/or legal guardians, scheduled on 26
November 2020, 9:00 A.M. to 4:00 P.M., via Zoom Video
Communications, Inc.
The webinar shall discuss the following topics:
the dangers of increased online presence of children as a
consequence of the COVID-19-related quarantine measures;
and
the Department of Education's Supplemental Policy on Child
Protection which aims to ensure the safety of learners from
online child protection risks such as cyberbullying, cybersex or
pornography, online sexual abuse and exploitation, and online
predator exposure.
Webinar on Recent Laws and Jurisprudence Concerning Violence
Against Women and Children to be conducted by Senior Assistant
State Prosecutor Lilian Doris S. Alejo for Prosecutors, scheduled on 4
December 2020, 1:00 P.M. to 4:00 P.M., via Zoom Video
Communications, Inc.
Inquiries regarding all the aforementioned activities may be directed to
Atty. Jacqueline E. Canlas or Ms. Desiree Lardizabal of the said Office.
