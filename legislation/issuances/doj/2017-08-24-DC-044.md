Greetings from the Philippine Institute for Development Studies (RIDS)!
Per Maiacanang Proclamation No. 247 dated September 02. 2002, the month of September of every year
is declared as Development Policy Research Month (DPRM)t o promote and draw nationwide awareness on
the importance of policy research in the formulation of national development plans, programs, and policies.
We shall be celebrating the DPRM once more this coming September. The theme of this year's
celebration is ''Pagpapatibay ng Desentralisasyon Tungo sa Kauniarang Panrehiyon^\ Its English version is
^^Strengthening Decentralization for Regional Development". We chose this theme in view of the ongoing
discourse on Federalism, and to highlight the need for in-depth reflections and analyses not just purely on
federalism but encompassing the bigger umbrella of decentralization reforms. Through the month-long
celebration, we wish to emphasize to our policymakers the importance of critical thinking and the formulation of
appropriate policy interventions to advance development in the regions.
PIDS, as the agency designated to lead and coordinate the activities for the annual observance of the
DPRM, hereby invites your agency/institution to participate in this year's celebration by;
1. Prominently displaying the DPRM banner/streamer within your institution's premises. Please see the
attached file for the design and specifications. The banner template may be accessed at this link:
bit.lv/dpnn2017. You are free to choose between the English version and the Filipino version depending
on your office's/locality's language preference. For inquiries regarding the banner, please contact Ms.
Gizelle Manuel at mnanuel@mail.pids.uov.ph).
2. Featuring announcements about the DPRM and its theme in your publications, website, social media
accounts, and other dissemination outlets.
3. Organizing activities or events that may be relevant to the theme or to policy research. If you have any
of such activities/events, please e-mail the details to Ms. Reynalyn Argueza at
RArguezawmail.pids.tiov.oh (copy to Ms. Viveka Miguel at VMiguel@mail.pids.gQv.ph)s o that we can
include them in the List of Activities/Events for the 2017 DPRM Celebration which will be posted on
the DPRM website at http://dprm.pids.gov.ph. The site will run throughout the months of September,
October, and November 2017.
We thank you for your continued support to PIDS and the DPRM. We look forward to hearing from you
soon.
With best regards.
Sincerely,
