DATE
1. Pursuant to Republic Act (RA) No. 11032 or "The Ease of Doing Business and
Efficient Government Service Delivery Act of 2018," all DOJ offices and agencies
concerned are hereby directed: (i) to conduct a review of existing policies and
operations, and (ii) to commence with the reengineering of their respective systems
and procedures, in accordance with the provisions of the said law which was earlier
disseminated through Department Circular No. 016 (s. 2018).
2. Furthermore, such reengineering should be in consonance with the following, as.
may be applicable at the agency and/or office levels, pending the issuance of the
implementing rules and regulation of RA 11032 and other applicable issuances by
the relevant agencies:
2.1. AO 25 Task Force Memorandum Circular No. 2018-1 on the streamlining and
process improvement of critical services as among the requirements for 2018
Performance Based Bonus( PBB);
2.2. Development/enhancement of systems, procedures, and manuals of the
Department and constituent/attached agencies pursuant to Department
Circular No. 024 (s. 2018) and the 2017-2022 DOJ Development Plan; and
2.3. Executive Order No. 605 (s. 2007) on Government Quality Management
System and ISO 9001 certification.
3. Management/support offices concerned shall facilitate the establishment of
appropriate internal controls for the foregoing, including operations planning and
performance management, as well as provision of necessary financial,
administrative and information technology requirements.
For strict and immediate compliance.
