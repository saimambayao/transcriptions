DATE 0CT 15 2021
In viewofthe de-escalation ofthe COVID-19 Alert Level System in the National
Capital Region (NCR) to Alert Level 3 and pursuant to the Guidelines on the Pilot
Implementation ofAlert Levels System for COVID-19 Response in the NCRdated 07
October 2021, Department ofJustice Offices and City Prosecution Offices in the NCR
shall remain fully operational for 16-31 October 2021 through an onsite skeletal
workforce offorty percent (40%) and the rest under work-from-home arrangement.
I. Skeletal Workforce on Rotation Basis
All officials, directors, and heads ofoffices/units/programs are hereby directed
to oversee and account for their respective personnel at all times.
The heads of offices/units/programs are further directed to submit to Director
IV Liza B. De Leon, Administrative Service, the list of personnel who will be
unable to physically reportforwork due to the imposition ofgranular lockdowns
in their respective residences.
II. Frontline Services
All officials, directors, and heads ofoffices/units/programs are hereby reminded
that frontline services shall be fully operational regardless of the alert level.
Accordingly, all heads ofoffices shall ensure the availability ofpersonnel atthe
Frontline Service who will receive documents, mail, and other papers.
Ill. Work-From-Home Arrangement
All officials, directors, and heads ofoffices/units/programs shall strictly observe
the relevant rules and guidelines of the Civil Service Commission (CSC),

### Page 2

particularly CSC Memorandum Circular No. 18,s. 2020,1 on the implementation
ofwork-from-home and/or other alternative work arrangements.
Department personnel under work-from-home arrangements are directed to
make themselves readily available during regular work hours for any urgent
work that may need their on-site presence.
•
IV. Inquest Proceedings
Prosecutors and their support staff shall continue to conduct inquest
proceedings online and/or in person, and attend to urgent matters that require
immediate action, as may be determined by the heads ofthe prosecution offices
concerned. Accordingly, all prosecution offices are directed to ensure the
availability at all times ofinquest prosecutors who shall be on-call. The conduct
ofinquest proceedings shall proceed in accordance with the Rules ofCourt and
existing rules and regulations ofthe Department.
This Circular shall take effect immediately and shall remain in force until further
orders, subject to any such amendment as may be declared or issued by the IATF
MEID or any other competent authority.
For strict compliance.
