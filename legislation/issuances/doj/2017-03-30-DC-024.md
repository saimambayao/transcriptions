3.1.1.2(cid:9) Posting of ISO Certification of at least one (1) core
process - Management improvement unit;
3.1.1.3(cid:9) Posting of system of ranking delivery units — Planning
unit;
3.1.2 Maintain/Update the posting of all invitations to bids and
awarded contracts in the Philippine Government Electronic
Procurement System (PhilGEPS) posting — BAC Secretariat;
