DEPARTMENT CIRCULAR NO.
SUBJECT : DELINEATION OF DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
DATE JUL 1 3 2020
In the interest of the service and pursuant to the provisions of existing laws, the
duties and responsibilities concerning the Inter-Agency Council Against Trafficking
(lACAT) Secretariat are hereby delegated to Assistant Secretary NICHOLAS
FELIX L. TY
It is understood that Undersecretary EMMELINE AGLIPAY-VILLAR shall
continue to have direct supervision and control over matters delegated to Assistant
Secretary Ty which fall under the special assignment given to her under Department
Circular( DC) No. 013 dated 01 August 2019, as amended.
The general provisions of DC No. 013, s. 2019 shall govern the implementation
of this Circular.
All Department orders, circulars, and issuances inconsistent herewith are
hereby revoked. This Order takes effect immediately and shall remain in force until
further orders.
MENARDO I. GUEVARRA
Secretary
Copy furnished;
All concerned.
