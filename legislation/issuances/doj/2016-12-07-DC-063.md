In line with the directive of the President to get rid of corruption and corrupt
practices in the bureaucracy, all D03 Officials and employees are hereby reminded to
strictly observe and adhere to the following provisions of applicable laws, rules and
regulations governing the acceptance of gifts, gratuity and benefits:
1. Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of Republic Act 6713,
otherwise known as the "Code of Conduct and Ethical Standards for
Public Officials and Employees" which provides:

unlawful or prohibited by the Code, shall constitute the grounds for
administrative disciplinary action, and without prejudice to criminal and
civil liabilities provided herein, such as:
