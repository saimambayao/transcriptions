In the interest ofjustice and pursuant to the provisions of existing
laws, all requests from other department, agencies and bureaus on
Information and Communications Technology (ICT)-related matters
addressed to the Department and all its attached agencies be coursed
through the Office of Assistant Secretary Jose Arturo R. Malvar. To
implement this, he must be copy furnished with all relevant letters,
communications and papers.
This shall be in addition to the existing duties and responsibilities
of Assistant Secretary Malvar.
