SUBJECT DONATION FOR CHILDREN'S BOOKS AND TOYS FOR

DATE OCT 312018
Pursuant to the provisions of existing laws, a lactation (breastfeeding) station
and Child-Minding Center will be established beside the Programs Building of this
Department. This Center will be inaugurated by the end of November 2018 in
celebration of the National Children's Month.
In view of the foregoing, all officials and employees of the DOJ are highly
encouraged to donate children's books and toys to the Child-Minding Center by 14
November 2018.
If you have any questions, please contact Nadine Bernardino at 0917-6264871
or at 526-2755.
