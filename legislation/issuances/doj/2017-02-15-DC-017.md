I.(cid:9) Policy Statement:
WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 10867 or otherwise known as the "National
Bureau of Investigation Reorganization and Modernization Act" provides
that the National Bureau of Investigation (NBI) shall act as a national
clearing house of criminal records and other related information for the
benefit of the government;
WHEREAS, the same law mandates the NBI to establish a modern NBI
Clearance and Identification Center containing all derogatory and criminal
records and civilian identification records;
WHEREAS, the NBI Clearance has been a forefront requirement for any
transaction in various government agency or private institutions;
WHEREAS, the present NBI Clearance is being issued for specific use or
purpose which entails additional cost for the public whenever they need to
transact on various matters and thus require various applications for NBI
Clearance;
NOW THEREFORE, to complement the directive of President Rodrigo
Roa Duterte for government services to be accessible to the public and
lessen the expense entailed by Filipinos to avail such service, the following
directives are hereby issued for the implementation of the NBI and
guidance of the general public.

II.(cid:9) Coverage:
These directives shall apply prospectively for all application of NBI
Clearances.
III.(cid:9) Directives to the NBI:
The National Bureau of Investigation is hereby directed to immediately
conduct the following:
Change the format of all NBI issued clearance for it to bear the phrase
"Issued for whatever legal purpose" instead of specific purpose;
Replace all existing format of NBI Clearances to the abovementioned
multi-purpose clearance;
Issue notices to the public through various platform, (ie. Newspaper,
social media, etc.) that NBI Clearances will be multi-purpose effective
fifteen days from the date of publication.
IV. Implementation:
Implementation of the multi-purpose NBI Clearance shall commence
fifteen (15) days after notices have been published in at least two (2)
newspapers of general circulation.
For strict compliance.
