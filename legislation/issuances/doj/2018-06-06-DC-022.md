SUBJECT: AUTHORIZED PARKING INSIDE THE DEPARTMENT COMPOUND
PURSUANT TO D.C. NO.0 02, s. 2017
Recently, parking spaces have become restrictive and limited due to the construction
activities inside the Department compound. Accordingly, there is a need to amend
Department Circular No. 002, s. 2017, which set the Guidelines for the Implementation of
the DOJ Gate Access Control System, in order to adequately address the said situation.
Thus, in the interest of the services, item III. A. ii. of the said Circular is hereby
amended as follows:
"ii. Main Gate for Vehicles
a. Only DOJ procured/issued vehicles and vehicles of the Secretary of
Justice, Prosecutor General and his Senior Deputies, Chief State
Counsel, Undersecretaries, Assistant Secretaries, and such other
officials and persons authorized by the Office of the Secretary shall be
allowed ingress, egress, and parking within the DOJ compound.
Vehicles of officials from other government offices or VIPs may
likewise be allowed access with the permission of the Office of the
Secretary.
Drivers of other DOJ vehicles and those issued to other DOJ officials,
as well as personal vehicles of DOJ employees are enjoined to park
their respective vehicles in parking spaces outside the Department
compound.
For the purpose of access and parking allotment under this item, refer
to Annex 'A' hereof.
XXX XXX XXX
Accordingly, the Security Section, Administrative Service, this Department, is hereby
DIRECTED to ensure that strict compliance of this Circular shall be implemented for the
security and order inside the Department Compound.
