DATE 10 December 2025
This is to direct all agencies to automatically repost on your official social media
accounts the announcements and activities of the President as posted on the official
social media pages of the President and the Presidential Communications Office
(PCO). The same directive should be issued to your attached agencies and bureaus.
This is to amplify government messaging, ensure consistent public information
dissemination, and maximize reach across all platforms.
For compliance. Thank you.
