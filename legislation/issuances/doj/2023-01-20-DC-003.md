FOR All Officials and Employees in the Department
SUBJECT Guidelines on Flexible Working Hours Schedule
DATE JAN 2 02023
In the interest of the service and pursuant to the provisions of the Civil Service
Commission (CSC) Memorandum Circular (MC) No. 25, s. 2019 and the CSC-MC No. 6,
s. 2022 in relation to Flexible Working Arrangements in the Government the following
guidelines shall be observed by the offi.pialq„and employees in the Department:
$
1. As a general rule, office hours shall be from 8:00 a.m. to 12:00 noon and from
1 :00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. on all days except Saturdays, Sundays, and Holidays;
2. Flexible working schedule outside of the official working hours and exclusive of
the one-hour lunch break shall be as follows: (a) 7:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.; and
(b) 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m., provided that the maximum number of employees
on flexible work schedule shall not exceed twenty percent (20%) of the
service/office total workforce;
3. All requests for a flexible working schedule shall be submitted to the Director
IV, Administrative Service on or before 27 January 2023 for consolidation
and validation of the (20%) total workforce of the said service/office, prior to its
submission to the Undersecretary-in-Charge for Finance, Administration
and Personnel Services for approval upon the favorable recommendation of
the concerned Head/Chief of Office; and
4. All services, programs, and offices with frontline service shall continue to
enforce the no noon-brealcpolicy.
The flexible working hour schedule once approved shall be deemed effective and
can only be changed in the event that the requester shall submit a new schedule, subject
for approval.
This Circular takes effect immediately and supersedes Section III of Department
Circular No. 019 dated 13 May 2022, and shall remain in force until further orders.
For strict compliance.
