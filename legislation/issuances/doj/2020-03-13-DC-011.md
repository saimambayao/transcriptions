1. PROCESSING OF 47(aM2) VISAS APPLICATIONS
The Legal Staff (Office of the Chief State Counsel) will temporarily
operate with a skeletal workforce to ensure the continuous processing of
applications for change of admission status from temporary visitor to, or for
extension of stay as, special non-Immigrants under Section 47(a)(2) of
Commonwealth Act No. 613, othenwise known as the "Philippine Immigration
Act of 194(T, as amended, received as of 13 March 2020.
In view of the community quarantine, the Legal Staff (Office of the
Chief State Counsel) will temporarily suspend the receipt of 47(a)(2) visa
applications from 16 March 2020 until 14 April 2020 or during the period
that community quarantine is enforced.
Applicants with no urgent need to process their 47(a)(2) visas are
requested to defer the filing of their applications until such time that the
community quarantine is lifted. Applicarrts whose 47(a)(2) visas will expire
