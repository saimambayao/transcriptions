Subject Scholarship Application for the Nanyang Fellows MBA
Nanyang Business School (NBS), Nanyang Technological

The Nanyang Business School (NBS), Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in
Singapore is accepting applications for the Nanyang Fellows MBA July 2018. This one
year full-time MBA programme is designed for exceptional mid-career executives. This
aims to develop the region's top business and government leaders and through a
rigorous classroom training.
Applicants should be a bachelor-degree holder with minimum 8 years of
management or senior professional experience, demonstrated fluency in English and
strong leadership potential in the civil service.
Further information and e-copy of the JDS brochure can be found online at
www.NanvanqFellows.com or contact Ms. Regine Wong, Nanyang Fellows MBA
Programme Manager at reaine.wona@ntu.edu.sq.
For interested employees, submission of application shall be forwarded to the
Office of the Secretary thru the Training Section, Administrative Service, this
Department, not later than 29 November 2017.
For information, guidance and appropriate action.
