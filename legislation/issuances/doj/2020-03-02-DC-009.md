DEPARTMENT CIRCULAR NO.
SUBJECT : Guidelines for the Conduct of Team Building Activities in the
Department Proper and Field Offices
DATE : 02 March 2020
Pursuant to Section 1, Rule VIII and Section 1, Rule XI of the Omnibus Rules
Implementing Book V of E.O. 292, which direct every department or agency to
establish a continuing program for career and development of its personnel; to create
an environment or work climate conducive to the development of personnel skills,
talents, and values for better public service and good supervisor-employee relations;
and to improve employee morale, the conduct of annual team-building and related
activities is hereby authorized.
1. Objectives. —
1.1. To afford every DOJ personnel an opportunity to participate in a staff
development activity that will enhance teamwork, foster interpersonal
relationships and improve team performance; and
1.2. To provide a more relevant, responsive, and rational set of parameters in the
implementation of Team Building Activities (TBA).
2. Participants. — All officers and employees of the Department holding regular
positions including Job Order (JO) and Contract of Service (COS) workers shall
participate in the TBA.
3. Requirements for the Conduct of TBA. — All proposals for the conduct of TBA shall
consider the following:
3.1. Objectives: the objectives of the proposed TBA shall indicate the need of the
requesting office to hold the TBA to promote workplace harmony and
organizational productivity.
3.2. Duration of the TBA: The TBA can only be allowed for a maximum of three (3)
days inclusive of travel time; two (2) days during weekdays and one (1) during
the weekends or public holidays.
3.3. Schedule of TBA: The TBA for every office shall be held once a year, on the
days where no critical functions may be affected. The approving authority shall

ensure that the TBA shall not be conducted simultaneously by the different
offices in the Department.
3.4. Budget; Funds for the conduct of TBA shall be guided by the following:
3.4.1. Each participant shall be entitled to a maximum of P800.00 budget per
day or a total of P2,400.00 for three (3) days to cover food,
accommodation, rent of facility, fees of facilitator and transportation, or
such rates as may be provided by applicable Department Circulars. No
funding allocation shall be provided to any official or employee excused
from participating in the scheduled activity of his/her office for justifiable
reasons. If an authorized official or employee fails to attend the activity,
his/her share in the office allocation shall be returned/refunded;
3.4.2. The designated Special Disbursing Officers are authorized to handle
cash advance to cover the expenses for the said activity which shall be
liquidated within thirty (30) calendar days after the completion thereof;
and
3.4.3. The disbursement of funds and corresponding liquidation reports shall
be subject to: (i) availability of funds; and (ii) existing budgeting,
accounting and auditing rules and regulations.
3.5. Transportation: DOJ vehicles may be utilized, on a 1®' come 1®' served basis,
in the conduct of TBA to transport participants to their place of destination,
provided that the travel time from the DOJ premises to the place of the TBA
and vice-versa shall not exceed five (5) hours, provided further that the related
gasoline and drivers' expenses shall be shouldered by the participants.
3.6. Venue and Security: The Offices shall ensure the safety of personnel
participating in the TBA. In choosing a venue, the following shall be considered:
3.6.1. A place that offers the most reasonable rates and facilities for the TBA;
and
3.6.2. The location must support the TBA's objectives.
3.7. Skeletal Force: For those offices with frontline services or critical functions, the
Head of Office shall maintain a skeletal force.
4. Procedure for Processing of Funds. — In the processing of funds for the TBA, the
following procedures shall be observed:
4.1. Upon approval of the concerned Undersecretary-in-Charge of the Order for the
conduct of TBA, the concerned office shall request from the Financial Service
(FS) the issuance of a corresponding Funds Availability based on the
submitted budget estimate of the TBA; and

4.2. Upon approval of the funding request, the concerned office shall coordinate
with the FS to process the release of the Cash Advance, subject to the usual
accounting and auditing rules and regulations.
Reporting. —The requesting office shall submit a post-activity report to the
Undersecretary-in-Charge, with copies furnished to the Training Section,
Personnel Division, within ten (10) days after the conduct of the TBA.
6. Repealing Clause. — All other Department issuances or parts thereof inconsistent
herewith are hereby repealed or amended accordingly.
MENARDO I. GUEVARRA
Secretary
