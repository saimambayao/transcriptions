In the interest of the service, pursuant to the provisions of existing laws, and
in order to promote efficient and effective administration of justice, the Department's
policy on pro-active involvement of prosecutors in case build-up shall likewise apply
to all violations of the following penal laws, namely:
1. PD No. 705 - Revised Forestry Code
2. RA No. 9175 - Chain Saw Act of 2002
3. RA No. 9147 - Wildlife Resources Conservation and Protection Act.
4. RA No. 7586 - National Integrated Protected Areas System (NIPAS) Act
5. RA No. 11038 - Expanded National Integrated Protected Areas System
Act of 2018
6. RA No. 7942 - Philippine Mining Act of 1995
7. RA No. 7076 - People's Small-Scale Mining Act.
8. PD No. 1152 - Philippine Environmental Code
9. RA No. 8550 - Philippine Fisheries Code of 1998
10. RA No. 4850 - Laguna Lake Development Authority (LLDA) Act
11. RA No. 6969 - Toxic Substances and Hazardous and Nuclear Wastes
Control Act of 1990
12. RA No. 8749 - Philippine Clean Air Act of 1999.
13. RA No. 9275 - Philippine Clean Water Act of 2004.
14. RA No. 9003 - Ecological Solid Waste Management Act
15. PD No. 1586 - Establishing an Environmental Impact Statement System
Including Other Environmental Management Related
Measures and for Other Purposes.
16. All other environmental crimes and violations of environmental laws and
regulations.
This Circular amends Department Circular No. 020 dated 31 March 2023 and
shall take effect after fifteen (15) days from the date of publication in the Official
