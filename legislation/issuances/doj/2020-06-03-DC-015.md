Date JUN 03 2020
In the exigency of the service, pursuant to Administrative Order No. 30 dated
21 April 2020 in relation to Executive Order No. 112 dated 30 April 2020, the
Omnibus Guidelines on the Implementation of Community Quarantine, as amended,
of the Inter-Agency Task Force for the Management of Emerging Infectious
Diseases, and the President's pronouncement placing the National Capital Region
(NCR) under General Community Quarantine (GCQ) and the rest of the country
under either GCQ or Modified GCQ, the reglementary period for the filing of
Affidavits, Petitions for Review before the Office of the Secretary or the Office of the
Regional Prosecutor, responsive pleadings thereto, motions, position papers, and/or
other relevant papers, shall no longer be deemed interrupted and shall begin to run
again starting 16 June 2020.
Likewise, the reglementary period for trie rendition of decisions, resolutions,
and/or other orders shall no longer be deemed interrupted and shall begin to run
again starting 16 June 2020.
This supersedes all previous guidelines inconsistent herewith, takes effect
immediately, and shall remain in force unless sooner modified or revoked.
