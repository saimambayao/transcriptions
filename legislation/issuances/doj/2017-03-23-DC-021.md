In the interest of the service and upon the invitation of the World Wide
Fund (WWF) for Nature Philippines, the Department is hereby invited to be a
partner for Earth Hour 2017.
Relative thereto, please support and promote Earth Hour 2017 by conducting
activities such as but not limited to the following:
Switch off or dim non-essential lights including streetlights, signages and
key monuments during their global 'lights out' event on Saturday, 25
March 2017 at 8:30 p.m. for one hour;
Release a memorandum inviting your employees to join and be part of
Earth Hour 2017;
Promote the Earth Hour 2017 to your officers, staff, employees and
constituents;
Pledge to go 'Beyond The Hour" and help "Shine A Light on Climate
Action" by publicly promoting renewed ways/practices/programs of
initiating change, which can include environmental projects/actions that
the offices and individuals are willing to undertake throughout the year;
and
Log in and register support at the Earth Hour Pilipinas website via
www.wwf.org.ph/earthhour.
For inquiries and more information, please get in touch at telephone numbers
920-7923/26/31 local 12 or 17 or email earthhour@iwwf.org.ph.
For information, guidance and appropriate action.
