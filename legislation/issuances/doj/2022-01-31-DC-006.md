DEPARTMENT CIRCULAR NO. 0 0 5
TO : ALL UNDERSECRETARIES AND ASSISTANT SECRETARIES

ALL HEADS OF SERVICES, OFFICES AND PROGRAMS
ALL REGIONAL PROSECUTORS

ALL CONCERNED

SUBJECT : INTERIM WORK ARRANGEMENTS FOR 01-15 FEBRUARY
2022

DATE : 31 January 2022

Pursuant to Memorandum Circular (MC) No. 93 dated 17 December 20211
and Resolution No. 159-A dated 29 January 2022, which downgraded the COVID
alert level classification of the National Capital Region (NCR) to Alert Level 2, all
DOJ offices in the NCR shall be operational through an onsite workforce of at least
eighty percent (80%) and the remainder under a work-from-home arrangement.

All heads of Regional/Provincial/City Prosecution Offices shall strictly
observe the onsite capacity prescribed in said MC, according to the prevailing alert
level classification in their respective areas.

This Circular takes effect immediately and shall remain in force until further
orders, subject to any such amendment as may be declared or issued by the Inter-
Agency Task Force for the Management of Emerging Infectious Diseases or any
other competent authority.

For strict compliance.
