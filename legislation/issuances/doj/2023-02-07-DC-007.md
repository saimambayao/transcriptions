MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 14
ENCOURAGING ALL GOVERNMENT LAWYERS TO PARTICIPATE
IN THE INTEGRATED BAR OF THE PHILIPPINES NATIONAL
CONVENTION OF LAWYERS
The Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP) will hold the 19*^ National
Convention of Lawyers from 16 to 18 February 2023 at the SMX Convention Center
in Davao City, with the theme 76P at 50; Looking Back, Moving Forward."
In this regard, all lawyers presently employed in departments, agencies, and
offices of the government, including government-owned or -controlled corporations,
government financial institutions, as well as state universities and colleges, are
encouraged to participate, and those interested may attend, on official time, provided
that they are issued the pertinent clearances or authorities by their respective heads of
office, consistent with Civil Service Commission Memorandum Circular No. 43
(s. 1993) and National Budget Circular No. 563 (s. 2016), and their participation in the
said Convention will not adversely affect the operations of their offices, as well as the
delivery of services to the public.
This Circular shall take effect immediately.
DONE, in the City of Manila, Philippines, this 20th day of January in
the Year of our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Three.
By authority of the President:
