In the interest of the service and pursuant to the directive of the President during
the 29^ Cabinet Meeting held on 11 September 2018 and Memorandum Circular No.
06 series of 2016 entitled: "ENJOINING ALL GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND
EMPLOYEES TO STRICTLY OBSERVE AND COMPLY WITH THE PROHIBITION
AGAINST GOING TO GAMBLING CASINOS", the following prohibition against all
government employees from entering and playing in gambling premises is hereby
reiterated:
Section 14 (4), Title IV of Presidential Decree No. 1869 entitled "Consolidating
and Amending Presidential Decree Nos. 1067-A, 1067-B, 1067-C, 1399 and 1632
Relative to the Franchise and Powers of the Philippine Amusement and Gaming
Corporation (PAGCOR)" enumerates persons who are not allowed to play In
casinos. It provides:
"(4) Persons not allowed to olav.
a) Government officials connected directly with the operation
of government or any of its agencies;
b) Members of the Armed Forces of the Philippines, including the
Army, Navy, Air Force or Constabulary; and
c) Persons under 21 years of age or students of any school, college
or university in the Philippines

### Page 2

From these are excepted the personnel employed by the
casinos, special guests, or those who at the discretion of the
Management may be allowed to stay in the premises."( Emphasis
supplied)
Accordingly, all officials and employees of the Department of Justice, including
all its constituent and attached agencies, are hereby strictly prohibited from entering,
staying or playing in casinos or other gambling premises. Further, all employees are
likewise prohibited from escorting or accompanying high-stake gamblers from the
airport to the casino or other gambling facilities.
Violation of this Circular shall be a ground for disciplinary action, in accordance
with existing laws and rules.
All Heads of Offices of constituent and attached agencies, the QIC Prosecutor
General, the QIC Chief State Counsel, and Directors of various services and Section
Heads are hereby directed to strictly implement and monitor compliance with this
directive.
For strict compliance.
MENARDO I. GUEVARRA
