DEPARTMENT CIRCULAR NO. 0
Subject : Delegation of Authority on Certain Matters in the
Bureau of Corrections (BUCOR), Amending for the
Purpose the Bureau of Corrections Operating Manual
Date JAN 2 7 2017
In the interest of the service and pursuant to the provisions of existing
laws, the following matters required to be approved/acted upon by the Secretary
of Justice are hereby delegated to be approved and/or acted upon by the
Director General of the Bureau of Corrections:
Requests for authority to allow an inmate to appear in court or other
government agency as directed by competent authority';
Requests for authority to allow an inmate to view the remains of a
deceased relative2;
Visits of Inmates to Relatives, Friends and Co-Accused at another
prison Facility under the Bureau of Corrections3;
Requests to interview inmates, employees or officials of the Bureau of
Corrections and its penal institutions as well as site visits to the
prison facilities by the media, students, civic groups, non-
governmental organizations and other similar groups, individuals or
associations;
Requests to visit inmates/prisoners at the New Bilibid Prison and other
prison facilities under the control of the Bureau of Corrections, by
investigators, public attorneys, probation and parole officers and law
enforcement officials4.
However, notwithstanding the foregoing delegation of authority, similar
requests for the above-enumerated undertakings authorities for HIGH-PROFILE
Section 1(a) Chapter 6 Part II Book I of the Bureau of Corrections Operating Manual
2 Section 1(c) Chapter 6 Part II Book I of the Bureau of Corrections Operating Manual
3 Department Circular No. 020 dated 11 January 2013
4 Department Circular No. 032 dated 22 August 2016

and HIGH-RISK INMATES shall still be subject to the approval or consideration
by the Secretary of Justice.
A Quarterly Periodic Report on documents approved/acted upon by
virtue of this delegated authority shall also be submitted by the Director General
of the BuCor to the SOJ, through Undersecretary Reynante B. Orceo, for
monitoring purposes.
All department orders, circulars and issuances inconsistent herewith are
hereby revoked. Hence, the provisions of Section 1 (a) to 1 (c) of Chapter 6, Part
2, Book I of the Bureau of Corrections Manual are hereby amended accordingly.
This Circular shall take effect immediately and shall remain in force until
further orders.
