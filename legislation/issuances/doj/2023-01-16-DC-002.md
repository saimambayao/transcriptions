DEPARTMENT CIRCULAR NO.
SUBJECT : DELINEATION OF DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
DATE JAN 1 6 2023
In the interest of the service and pursuant to the provisions of existing laws,
Department Circular (D.C.) No. 029 dated 13 September 2022 (Re.' Delineation of
Duties and Responsibilities) is hereby specifically amended as follows:
1. Assistant Secretary FREDDERICK A. VIDA is hereby designated as
the alternate representative of Undersecretary Brigido J. Dulay for the
Finance, Administration and Personnel Services Cluster, in lieu of
OIC-Assistant Secretary SAPP Gabriel Lorenzo L. Ignacio.
1
2. Assistant Secretary FRANCIS JOHN L. TEJANO is hereby designated
as the alternate representative of Undersecretary Deo L. Marco for the
Corrections Cluster, in lieu of Assistant Secretary Jose Dominic F.
Clavano IV.2
In addition to the foregoing, Assistant Secretary Vida and Assistant Secretary
Tejano may hereafter be designated by the Secretary to perform such other functions,
duties and responsibilities as will be required by the exigency of the service.
Further, the general provisions of D.C. No. 029 s. 2022 shall continue to govern
the implementation of this Circular.
All Department orders, circulars, and issuances inconsistent herewith are hereby
revoked. This Circular takes effect immediately and shall remain in force u ntb(lrther
