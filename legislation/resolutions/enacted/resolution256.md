**RESOLUTION NO. 256**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING THE DELEGATES OF THE PHILIPPINES IN THE RECENT 31ˢᵀ SOUTHEAST ASIAN GAMES IN HANOI, VIETNAM, THE PHILIPPINE SPORTS COMMISSION, AND THE TWO HUNDRED TWENTY-SEVEN (227) SUCCESSFUL MEDALISTS INCLUDING A BANGSAMORO PRIDE, MR. ALNAKRAN “RAN” ABDILLA**

**WHEREAS**, Section 19 (1), Article XIV of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides that the State shall promote physical education and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and amateur sports, including training for international competitions, to foster self-discipline, teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;

**WHEREAS**, as a Government’s effort to fulfill its constitutional mandate, the Philippine Sports Commission (PSC) was created on January 24, 1990, through Republic Act No. 6847;

**WHEREAS**, in order to further develop the athletic skills and talents of the students in world-class sports facilities which are at par with international standards and to be competitive in international competitions, the National Academy of Sports was created and established through Republic Act No. 11470;

**WHEREAS**, the approval of these meaningful legislations and the establishment of these government offices significantly contributed to the performance of the country’s delegates in the 31ˢᵗ Southeast Asian (SEA) Games in Hanoi, Vietnam;

**WHEREAS**, the 31ˢᵗ edition of the SEA Games features forty (40) sports across five hundred twenty-six (526) events and counts on the participation of over forty-five thousand four hundred sixty-seven (45,467) athletes from eleven (11) different nations which took place from May 12-23, 2022 in Hanoi, Vietnam;

**WHEREAS**, the Philippine Olympic Committee (POC) appointed PSC Commissioner Ramon Fernandez as the *Chef de Mission* of the Philippines for the 31ˢᵗ SEA Games in Hanoi, Vietnam;

**WHEREAS**, the Philippines finished firmly in fourth place among eleven (11) brother-countries in the SEA region with fifty-two (52) gold medals, seventy (70) silver medals, and one hundred five (105) bronze medals;

**WHEREAS**, Mr. Alnakran “Ran” Abdilla, a Bangsamoro pride and a native from the Province of Tawi-Tawi, is a bronze medalist for the men’s beach volleyball event in the 31ˢᵗ SEA Games;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government welcomes these successful athletes in the Bangsamoro Region:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED** by the BTA Parliament, as it is hereby resolved, To congratulate the delegates of the Philippines in the recent 31ˢᵗ Southeast Asian Games in Hanoi, Vietnam, the Philippine Sports Commission, and the two hundred twenty-seven (227) successful medalists including a Bangsamoro pride, Mr. Alnakran “Ran” Abdilla.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That a copy of this Resolution be furnished to the Philippine Sports Commission and Mr. Alnakran “Ran” Abdilla, for their information.

**ADOPTED**, Dhu Al-Hijjah 20, 1443/July 19, 2022.
