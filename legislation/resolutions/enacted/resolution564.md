PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 564

RESOLUTION RECOMMENDING THE IMMEDIATE DESIGNATION OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT CHAIRMAN OF THE NATIONAL INTERGOVERNMENTAL RELATIONS BODY

WHEREAS, Section 2, Article VI of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the "Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (OLBARMM)", created a National Government-Bangsamoro Government Intergovernmental Relations Body (IGRB) to coordinate and resolve issues on intergovernmental relations through regular consultation and continuing negotiations in a non-adversarial manner;

WHEREAS, Section 15, Article X of the 1987 Philippine Constitution provides for the creation of an Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao within the framework of the Constitution and the national sovereignty and territorial integrity of the Republic of the Philippines;

WHEREAS, Section 16, Article X of the same Constitution provides that the President shall exercise general supervision over autonomous regions to ensure that laws are faithfully executed;

WHEREAS, Section 1, Article VI of OLBARMM reiterates that the President shall exercise general supervision over the Bangsamoro Government to ensure the faithful execution of the laws;

WHEREAS, the Intergovernmental Relations Body has, for a time, implemented mechanisms to resolve intergovernmental issues, such as the creation of a Technical Working Group to effect the transition of Sulu from the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao to Region IX during the chairmanship of Secretary Amenah B. Pangandaman of the National Government-IGRB;

WHEREAS, the resignation of Secretary Amenah B. Pangandaman as Secretary of the Department of Budget and Management also resulted in the vacancy in the chairmanship of the National Government-IGRB;

WHEREAS, the vacancy in the chairmanship of the National Government-IGRB has unnecessarily caused the non-convening of the IGRB and delayed the resolution of many intergovernmental issues, including the transition of Sulu;

WHEREAS, Special Assistant to the President (SAP) Antonio Ernesto F. Lagdameo, Jr. possesses a thorough understanding of the complexities of the National Government's peace process and its implications for Moro communities;

WHEREAS, SAP Lagdameo, Jr. has consistently demonstrated competence, integrity, and credibility, enabling him to effectively articulate and implement the directives of His Excellency President Ferdinand "Bongbong" R. Marcos, Jr. at the intergovernmental level;

WHEREAS, the designation of SAP Lagdameo, Jr. as the Government of the Philippines Co-Chairman of the IGRB will further strengthen coordination and cooperation between the National Government and the Bangsamoro Government in addressing intergovernmental concerns;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, as the interim government of the Bangsamoro Government, deems it necessary to reinforce the National Government's commitment to the peace process and the effective implementation of the OLBARMM:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To recommend the immediate designation of the National Government Chairman of the National Intergovernmental Relations Body.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, To endorse SAP Secretary Antonio Ernesto F. Lagdameo, Jr., a Mindanaon and an active participant in the IGRB since the start of the Marcos Administration, as Co-Chairman of the IGRB for the National Government.

RESOLVED FINALLY, To forward this Resolution to the Office of the President through the Office of the Executive Secretary, Secretary Ralph Recto.

ADOPTED, Jumada Al-Akhirah 26, 1447/December 17, 2025.
