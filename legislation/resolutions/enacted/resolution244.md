**RESOLUTION NO. 244**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING DEEPEST SYMPATHY AND CONDOLENCES TO THE FAMILY OF THE FORMER AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (ARMM) REGIONAL LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SPEAKER DATU GUIMID "JIMMY" MATALAM, AND RECOGNIZING HIS CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, the former Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) Regional Legislative Assembly Speaker Datu Guimid "Jimmy" Matalam peacefully passed away in the early hours of Shawwal 17, 1443 AH/May 18, 2022;

**WHEREAS**, as a member of the first post-martial law Congress, he was best remembered as one of the two principal sponsors of the consolidated bills on the creation of the ARMM, and as the Chairperson of the Committee on Muslim Affairs, wherein the measure had been passed into law as Republic Act No. 6734, the Philippines’ first autonomous region law;

**WHEREAS**, he had been a Maguindanao Congressman of the Second District of Maguindanao for two terms (1987-1992; 2001-2004), Speaker of the ARMM Regional Legislative Assembly (RLA) (1993 to 1996), ARMM Vice-Governor (1996-2001), ARMM Department of Agrarian Reform Regional Secretary (1992-1993), and ARMM Department of the Interior and Local Government Regional Secretary (1996-2001);

**WHEREAS**, he was the son of the late Empire Governor Datu Udtog Matalam, also known as Bapa’y Zumbaga, of Maguindanao, North Cotabato, South Cotabato, and Sultan Kudarat province of Sarangani; he was also the older brother of the late former Maguindanao Governor and former Pagalungan Mayor Datu Norodin Matalam, also known as Tamagay;

**WHEREAS**, he was the nearest kin of the late Senator General Salipada K. Pendatun and late Moro Islamic Liberation Front founding Chairman Dr. Ustadz Hashim Salamat, *Allahu Yarhamo*;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Government condoles with the bereaved family and loved ones of the late Speaker Matalam, as well as expresses its admiration and gratitude for his invaluable contributions in public service for the people of Maguindanao and the Bangsamoro in general:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED** by the BTA Parliament, as it is hereby resolves, To express its deepest sympathy and condolences to the family of the former ARMM-Regional Legislative Assembly Speaker, Datu Guimid "Jimmy" Matalam, and to recognize his contributions to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To forward the foregoing Resolution to the bereaved family of Datu Guimid "Jimmy" Matalam, for their information.

**ADOPTED**, Dhu Al-Qa’dah 16, 1443/ June 15, 2022.
