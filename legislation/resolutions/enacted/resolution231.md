
Republic of the Philippines
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
**BANGSAMORO PARLIAMENT**
Bangsamoro Government Center, Cotabato City

**THIRD REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 231**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY’S HIGHEST SENSE OF GRATITUDE TO HIS EXCELLENCY PRESIDENT RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, FOR APPOINTING ATTY. SAIDAMEN B. PANGARUNGAN AS CHAIRMAN OF THE COMMISSION ON ELECTIONS, ATTY. ABDULLAH D. MAMA-O AS SECRETARY OF THE DEPARTMENT OF MIGRANT WORKERS, ATTY. GUILING A. MAMONDIONG AS SECRETARY OF THE NATIONAL COMMISSION ON MUSLIM FILIPINOS, ATTY. BASARI D. MAPUPUNO AS PRESIDENT OF THE MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY, AND ALL THE NEW APPOINTEES WHO HAIL FROM THE BANGSAMORO REGION, AS WELL AS CONGRATULATING THEM ALL ON THEIR RESPECTIVE NEW POSITIONS**

**WHEREAS,** His Excellency President Rodrigo Roa Duterte, on March 7, 2022 appointed Atty. Saidamen B. Pangarungan, former head of the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos (NCMF), as Chairman of the Commission on Elections;

**WHEREAS,** His Excellency President Rodrigo Roa Duterte, on March 4, 2022 appointed Atty. Abdullah D. Mama-o, presidential adviser on Overseas Filipino Workers (OFW), as Secretary of the Department of Migrant Workers;

**WHEREAS,** His Excellency President Rodrigo Roa Duterte, on March 8, 2022 appointed Atty. Guiling A. Mamondiong, administrator for the Local Water Utilities Administration (LWUA), as Secretary of the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos;

**WHEREAS,** His Excellency President Rodrigo Roa Duterte, on March 8, 2022 appointed Atty. Basari D. Mapupuno, Provincial Prosecutor of Lanao del Sur, as President of the Mindanao State University;

**WHEREAS,** His Excellency President Rodrigo Roa Duterte, also appointed several Moro professionals in key executive and judiciary positions;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, expresses the highest sense of gratitude to His Excellency President Rodrigo Roa Duterte for appointing these esteemed men and women, who all hail from the Bangsamoro Region;

**WHEREAS,** the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, with utmost pride, welcome and extends its warmest congratulations to all of the new appointees:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to express the highest sense of gratitude to His Excellency President Rodrigo Roa Duterte, for appointing Atty. Saidamen B. Pangarungan, Atty. Abdullah D. Mama-O, Atty. Guiling A. Mamondiong, Atty. Basari D. Mapupuno, and all the new appointees who hail from the Bangsamoro region, as well as congratulating them all on their respective new positions.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** to forward the foregoing resolution to His Excellency President Rodrigo Roa Duterte, President of the Philippines, and to the new appointees, for information.

**ADOPTED,** Sha’baan 12, 1443/March 16, 2022.
