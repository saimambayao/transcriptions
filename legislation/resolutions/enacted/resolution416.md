**RESOLUTION NO. 416**

**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING BAI REHANA MARAJAN LAUBAN FOR RANKING 9th IN THE OCTOBER 2023 FISHERIES TECHNOLOGIST LICENSURE EXAMINATION AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS FOR BRINGING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS**, Section 16 of Article IX of Republic Act No. 11054 otherwise known as the “Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao” states that education shall be a top priority of the Bangsamoro Government to establish, maintain, and support a complete and integrated system of quality education, which shall be a subsystem of the national education system;

**WHEREAS**, the Fisheries Technologist Licensure Examination (FTLE) was conducted from October 25 to 26, 2023 across sixteen (16) local testing centers throughout the Philippines administered by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC);

**WHEREAS**, on November 6, 2023, the PRC announced the results of the October 2023 FTLE, revealing that out of 2,124 examinees, 716 or 33.71% successfully passed the said examination;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban has displayed exceptional dedication and perseverance in passing the October 2023 FTLE and securing the remarkable position of 9th rank with an average score of 84.50% in the said rigorous examination administered by the PRC;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Lauban is a young 23-year-old Maguindanaon from Kabuntalan, Maguindanao Del Norte, an esteemed alumna of Mindanao State University (MSU) - Maguindanao and a proud daughter of Mr. Hadji Badrudin T. Taug and Mrs. Laguiabai L. Lauban;

**WHEREAS**, Ms. Lauban graduated Cum Laude at MSU-Maguindanao and received various accolades during her junior and senior high school and college education;

**WHEREAS**, the achievement of Ms. Lauban and all the successful Bangsamoro passers in the October 2023 FTLE has not only showcased the competitive spirit and excellence of the Bangsamoro people at a national level but also become a source of immense pride and inspiration for all young Filipinos, especially the Bangsamoro youth and children;

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, recognizing the remarkable accomplishments of Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban and all other Bangsamoro passers of the October 2023 FTLE, deems it fitting to commend their unwavering commitment to hard work and excellence:

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate and commend Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban for ranking 9th in the October 2023 Fisheries Technologist Licensure Examination (FTLE) and all Bangsamoro passers for bringing pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, To present this Resolution to Ms. Bai Rehana Marajan Lauban, for information.

**ADOPTED**, Jumada Al-Awwal 06, 1445/November 20, 2023.
