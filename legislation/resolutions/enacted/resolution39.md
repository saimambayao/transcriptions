**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 39**

**AN URGENT RESOLUTION URGING THE PHILIPPINE STATISTICS AUTHORITY TO PRIORITIZE THE IMPLEMENTATION OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11055 PHILIPPINE IDENTIFICATION SYSTEM IN THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority is mandated by Republic Act No. 11054 to govern the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao with emphasis on law and order, promoting seamless delivery of service, improve the efficiency, transparency and targeted delivery of public and social services, to reduce corruption and curtail bureaucratic red tape, to avert fraudulent transactions and misrepresentations, and, in general, to improve and enhance the governance, health and social services, economy and security of the region;

**WHEREAS**, the foremost objective of this initiative is primarily to establish an optimized identification system for all resident citizens and resident aliens of BARMM which can be used in public and private transactions and to fortify the internal security;

**WHEREAS**, the BARMM sees the need to simplify, streamline, and protect public and private transactions;

**WHEREAS**, the BARMM must take action to deter and prevent the occurrence of similar bombing incidents that recently happened in the Provinces of Sultan Kudarat and Sulu which according to the security sector were committed by foreign and local terrorists;

**WHEREAS**, a measure for such purposes was passed by the 17<sup>TH</sup> Philippine Congress entitled Philippine Identification System Act or R. A. No. 11055 and the Bangsamoro Transition Authority sees this as a land mark legislation beneficial to the political, socio-economic, and security needs of the BARMM.

**WHEREAS**, towards this end, R. A. No. 11055 or Philippine Identification System Act is to be implemented by the Philippine Statistics Authority;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, the BTA Parliament urges the Philippine Statistics Authority to prioritize the implementation of R.A. No. 11055 in the BARMM.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, the PSA and the BTA shall work together for the prioritization of implementation of Republic Act No. 11055 in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

**RESOLVED FINALLY**, that copies of this resolution be furnished to the Director-General of the PSA, the National Security Adviser, the Secretary of National Defense, the Secretary of Interior and Local Government, the Chairman of the Commission on Elections, and the Director General of the Philippine Health Insurance Corporation.

**Adopted**, September 25, 2019.
