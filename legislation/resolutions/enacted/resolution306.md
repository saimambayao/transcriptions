RESOLUTION NO. 306

RESOLUTION AMENDING BTA RESOLUTION NO. 97, RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING THE TOP-RANKED BANGSAMORO QUR'AN RECITERS AND MEMORIZERS WHO REPRESENTED THE COUNTRY IN VARIOUS INTERNATIONAL QUR'AN RECITING COMPETITIONS FOR AROUND HALF A CENTURY SINCE 1969

WHEREAS, BTA Resolution No. 97 provides that the Bangsamoro Qur'an reciters have been winning top placers in International Qur'an Reading Competitions for around half a century since 1969 and have brought great honor and pride to our nation and the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

WHEREAS, while formal participation of the delegates from Muslim Mindanao began in 1979 following the signing of the Government of the Republic of the Philippines - Moro National Liberation Front - Organization of Islamic Conference (GRP-MNLF-OIC) Tripoli Agreement of 1976, Muslim Filipinos have been participating in International Qur'an Reciting Competitions since 1969 and equally deserve recognition;

WHEREAS, to expedite the implementation of BTA Resolution No. 97, the Office of the Chief Minister recommended the creation of a technical working group (TWG);

WHEREAS, the TWG organized a validation committee to review the list of awardees mentioned in BTA Resolution No. 97;

WHEREAS, upon thorough review by the validation committee, the awardees are listed hereunder:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To amend BTA Resolution No. 97, Resolution recognizing the top-ranked Bangsamoro Qur'an reciters and memorizers who represented the country in various International Qur'an Reciting Competitions for around half a century since 1969.
