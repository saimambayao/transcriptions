**RESOLUTION NO. 360**

RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING MS. RAYHANAH NUESTRO DECAMPONG FOR PLACING 2ND IN THE MAY 2023 CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANT LICENSURE EXAMINATION AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS

WHEREAS, a total of 2,239 out of 7,376 or 30.36% successfully passed the Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination (CPALE) and Ms. Decampong placed second with a general average of 89.33%;

WHEREAS, Ms. Rayhanah Nuestro Decampong, a young Meranaw from Bayang, Lanao del Sur, in Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM), and an esteemed alumna of De La Salle University-Manila, has displayed exceptional dedication and perseverance for not only passing the CPALE but also achieving an impressive average score of 89.33%, securing the remarkable position of 2nd rank in this rigorous licensure examination administered by the Professional Regulation Commission;

WHEREAS, Ms. Decampong is the daughter of Mr. Norman Racman Decampong and Mrs. Sittie Sarah Nuestro;

WHEREAS, the same recognition also goes to all new Bangsamoro Certified Public Accountants who successfully scaled what time and again has proven to be a Herculean battery of exams;

WHEREAS, the achievements of Ms. Rayhanah Nuestro Decampong and all successful Bangsamoro candidates in professional licensure examinations have not only showcased the competitive spirit and excellence of the Bangsamoro people at a national level but have also become a source of inspiration and immense pride for everyone, especially for our younger generation:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To commend and congratulate Ms. Rayhanah Nuestro Decampong for placing 2nd in the May 2023 Certified Public Accountant Licensure Examination and all Bangsamoro CPA passers.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That a copy of this Resolution be presented to Ms. Rayhanah Nuestro Decampong, for information.

ADOPTED, Dhul Hijjah 08, 1444/June 26, 2023.
