**RESOLUTION NO. 228**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE PROFOUND SYMPATHIES AND DEEPEST CONDOLENCES OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT TO THE BEREAVED FAMILY OF FORMER MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY PRESIDENT DR. CAMAR AMPASO UMPA ON HIS DEMISE**

**WHEREAS**, Dr. Camar Ampaso Umpa was born on November 3, 1949, at Uyaan, Madamba, Lanao del Sur to parents Datu Montila “Oti” Umpa and Bae Macasimpan Ampaso;

**WHEREAS**, in 1965, at the age of 15, Dr. Umpa finished his secondary education with high honors at the Lanao del Norte National Comprehensive High School in Baroy, Lanao del Norte;

**WHEREAS**, in 1968, at the age of 18, through a full academic scholarship, Dr. Umpa was able to finish his Bachelor of Arts, major in Political Science within three years at the Mindanao State University, graduating with Latin Honors (Cum Laude) and a College Leadership Award;

**WHEREAS**, in 1973, he was awarded a scholarship at the University of Hawaii in Honolulu, Hawaii, United States of America, and he was able to finish the Master of Arts in Political Science program within 11 months;

**WHEREAS**, in 1977, he earned his Doctor of Philosophy degree from the University of Hawaii, receiving high marks in all his subjects;

**WHEREAS**, during his stint at the University of Hawaii, Dr. Harry Friedman, an international luminary in the field of education, cited Dr. Umpa as his “best ever student” in his twenty-one years of teaching, a move unprecedented in the history of the university;

**WHEREAS**, in 1977, upon his return to the Philippines, he was appointed as Assistant Dean of the College of Arts and Social Sciences, and also as a Senior Technical Assistant to the Vice President for Academic Affairs of the Mindanao State University;

**WHEREAS**, in 1978, he was designated as Assistant Vice President for Academic Affairs and continued to do so until 1986 when he served as Acting Vice President for Academic Affairs of the Mindanao State University;

**WHEREAS**, in 1988, at the age of 38, he was elected by the Board of Regents as the first Chancellor of the Mindanao State University – Iligan Institute of Technology, serving the prestigious academic institution for more than a decade, and leading them towards the path of sustained institutional growth and progress;

**WHEREAS**, from 1999-2005, he was appointed as the 5th president of the Mindanao State University, and during his term, Dr. Umpa largely contributed to the development of the University as a renowned world-class institute of higher learning;

**WHEREAS**, in 2008, he led the Ranao Council in the establishment of the Al Khwarizmi International College (AKIC), providing quality higher education in Lanao del Sur;

**WHEREAS**, on March 7, 2022, Dr. Camar Ampaso Umpa peacefully returned to the mercy of his Creator;

**NOW, THEREFORE, be it**

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to express the profound sympathies and deepest condolences of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament to the bereaved family of former President of Mindanao State University Dr. Camar Ampaso Umpa on his demise.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to forward this Resolution to the bereaved family members of Dr. Camar Ampaso Umpa.

**ADOPTED**, Sha’baan 11, 1443/March 15, 2022.
