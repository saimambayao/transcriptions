**RESOLUTION NO. 116**

**RESOLUTION CALLING ON THE OFFICE OF THE CHIEF MINISTER, THROUGH THE REGIONAL ACTION COMMITTEE, TO REGULARLY MONITOR AND REPORT ON THE STATUS OF COMPLIANCE AND IMPLEMENTATION OF THE PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11310 IN THE BARMM**

**WHEREAS**, Republic Act No. 11310, otherwise known as “An Act Institutionalizing the Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program” is being implemented in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

**WHEREAS**, the BARMM having the highest poverty incidents, there is a need for effective monitoring and compliance by the BARMM to realize the objectives of the said law;

**WHEREAS**, the BARMM is mandated to provide a just and dynamic social order that will ensure the prosperity and independence of the nation and free the people from poverty through policies that provide adequate social services and improve quality of life;

**WHEREAS**, the BARMM is also required by law to give priority to education and health of children; accelerate social progress and promote total human liberation and development; protect the life of mothers and their children; and promote gender equality and empower women;

**WHEREAS**, concerns have been raised in the implementation of the 4Ps program in various constituent LGUs within BARMM;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, to call for the Chief Minister, through the Regional Action Committee, to regularly monitor and report on the status of compliance and implementation of the provisions of Republic Act No. 11310 in the BARMM. It is still the Ministry of Social Services and Development shall render the report to the Regional Action Committee and other agencies.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, to forward the foregoing Resolution to the office of the Chief Minister Hon. Ahod Balawag Ebrahim and Ministry of Social Services and Development, for information and appropriate consideration.

**ADOPTED**, March 24, 2021.
