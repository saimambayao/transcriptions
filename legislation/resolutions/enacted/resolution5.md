**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 5**

**RESOLUTION CREATING AN AD HOC COMMITTEE ON FINANCE, BUDGET AND MANAGEMENT OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT**

**WHEREAS**, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament has performed its legislative and other functions following its inauguration on March 29, 2019;

**WHEREAS**, there is a need to determine all possible sources of funds that the BTA may utilize to finance its various tasks and programs as mandated by law;

**WHEREAS**, Section 14, Article XVI of Republic Act 11054, provides that “*. . . the current year's appropriations for the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao shall also be transferred to the Bangsamoro Transition Authority . . .*”;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved, To create an ad hoc Committee on Finance, Budget and Management to determine the remaining unexpended funds of the defunct Regional Legislative Assembly (RLA) and come up with an expenditure program of the said amount for its utilization by the BTA Parliament in the interest of public service.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, that the ad hoc committee as herein created render a report before this august Parliament in the soonest possible time.

**Adopted**, April 22, 2019.
