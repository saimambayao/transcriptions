FOURTH REGULAR SESSION

PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 567

RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING ALL PARLIAMENTARY AND STATUTORY COMMITTEES OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT TO CONDUCT MEETINGS, HEARINGS, AND CONSULTATIONS DURING THE MANDATORY ADJOURNMENT OF THE SECOND BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT

WHEREAS, the Fourth Regular Session of the Second Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament will adjourn sine die on Sha'ban 21, 1447/January 29, 2026, pursuant to Parliament Resolution No. 560;

WHEREAS, there are pending deliberations on priority codes such as the Bangsamoro Revenue Code, and other important bills and resolutions before the different committees of the BTA Parliament;

WHEREAS, the expeditious consideration of these pending measures is in the best interest of the Bangsamoro:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To authorize all Parliamentary and Statutory Committees of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament to conduct meetings, hearings, and consultations during the mandatory adjournment of the Second Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That this Resolution shall take effect on the date of its adoption and shall remain in force until amended or repealed.

RESOLVED, FINALLY, To furnish this Resolution to the Parliamentary and Statutory Committees of the BTA Parliament for their information and ready reference.

ADOPTED, Sha'ban 09, 1447/January 28, 2026.
