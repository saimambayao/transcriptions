**RESOLUTION NO. 300**

**RESOLUTION AMENDING THE CALENDAR OF SESSION OF THE FIRST REGULAR SESSION OF THE SECOND PARLIAMENT**

**WHEREAS**, Section 4, Rule VI of the Parliamentary Rules, Procedures, and Practices of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority – Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao states that:

> “**Section 4. Conduct of Sessions.** — Plenary sessions shall commence at one o’clock in the afternoon on Mondays through Thursdays of the third and fourth week of the month. xxx”

**WHEREAS**, on *Safar* 24, 1444/September 20, 2022, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, through Resolution No. 265, adopted a Calendar of Session to guide the Members of the Parliament in the performance of their legislative and constituency-serving functions;

**WHEREAS**, under Resolution 265, the session days of the third week of the month of March 2023 were not reflected in the adopted Calendar of Session;

**WHEREAS**, during its 4th Committee Meeting held on January 10, 2023, the Committee on Rules recognized the omission and thereafter approved the inclusion of the third week of the month of March 2023 in the Calendar of Session:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, as it is hereby resolved, To amend Resolution No. 265 to include the session days of the third week of March 2023 in the Calendar of Session, as follows:
