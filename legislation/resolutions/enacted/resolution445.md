**PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION NO. 445**

**RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE CONSENSUS OF THE BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY PARLIAMENT ON THE APPOINTMENT OF HONORABLE MUSLIM MASAGLANG GUIAMADEN AS THE WALI OF THE BANGSAMORO GOVERNMENT PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11054, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "ORGANIC LAW FOR THE BANGSAMORO AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO"**

**WHEREAS,** Section 1, Article VIII of Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the *"Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao"* provides for the appointment of the *Wali*, the ceremonial head of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS,** Paragraph 2, Section 1, Title II, Book III of Bangsamoro Autonomy Act No. 13, otherwise known as the *"Bangsamoro Administrative Code"* requires issuance of a resolution by the Parliament reflecting its consensus on the selection of the *Wali* from a list of eminent residents of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region submitted by the Council of Leaders;

**WHEREAS,** Section 2, Article VIII of Republic Act No. 11054 sets forth the qualifications of the *Wali* at the time of his nomination;

**WHEREAS,** the Council of the Leaders convened on May 9, 2024 / *Dhu Al-Qa 'dah 1, 1445* to deliberate over the selection of the next *Wali* of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS,** in its Council Resolution No. 01, Series of 2024, the Council of Leaders nominated and endorsed to the Parliament the names of Sheikh Muslim Masaglang Guiamaden and Dr. Muhammad Nadheer Ebil who are both eminent residents of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS,** upon careful deliberation and discussion, the Bangsamoro Parliament has reached a consensus in selecting Sheikh Guiamaden as the next *Wali* of the Bangsamoro Government;

**WHEREAS,** Sheikh Guiamaden served as the Chief Justice of Moro Islamic Liberation Front *Shari'ah* Court in Camp Abubakar;

**WHEREAS,** he was born in 1963 in Buluan, Maguindanao del Sur, and he has maintained his residence at his place of birth;

**WHEREAS,** he earned a bachelor's degree in *Shari'ah*, major in Islamic Jurisprudence and its Principles from the Islamic University of Madinah, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia in 1989;

**WHEREAS,** he was previously associated with the Ministry of Endowments and Islamic Affairs of the State of Qatar for 27 years, and he is one of the founders of Philippine Muslims Federation, an organization that aims to promote the welfare and interests of Filipino Muslims living in Qatar;

**WHEREAS,** Sheikh Guiamaden's morals and conduct are beyond reproach, and he has dedicated more than 60 years of his life in making significant contributions to the development of the religious and cultural identity of the Bangsamoro;

**WHEREAS,** given his qualifications and eminence in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region, Sheikh Muslim Masaglang Guiamaden is worthy of guiding the Bangsamoro Government as its ceremonial head:

**NOW, THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To express the consensus of the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament on the appointment of Honorable Muslim Masaglang Guiamaden as the *Wali* of the Bangsamoro Government pursuant to Republic Act No. 11054, otherwise known as the *"Organic Law for the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao."*

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** To present this Resolution to the Office of the Wali of the Bangsamoro Government, Hon. Muslim Masaglang Guiamaden, for information.

**ADOPTED,** *Dhu Al-Qa 'dah* 05, 1445/May 13, 2024.
