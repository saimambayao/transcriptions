SECOND REGULAR SESSION

RESOLUTION NO. 335

RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING CASMER BLAH DILANGALEN AND MOHAIMEN HADJI OMAR PASAWILAN FOR RANKING TOP 2 AND 10, RESPECTIVELY, AND ALL BANGSAMORO PASSERS IN THE APRIL 2023 CIVIL ENGINEERS LICENSURE EXAMINATION AND FOR GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE

WHEREAS, on April 23 and 24, 2023, the Civil Engineers Licensure Examination was conducted at several testing centers located in the cities of Manila, Baguio, Butuan, Cagayan de Oro, Cebu, Davao, Iloilo, Koronadal, Legazpi, Lucena, Pagadian, Pampanga, Rosales, Tacloban, Tuguegarao, Zamboanga, and Palawan;

WHEREAS, on April 28, 2023, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced the results of the April 2023 Civil Engineers Licensure Examination where only five thousand eight hundred eighty-seven (5,887) out of the sixteen thousand nine hundred thirty-six (16,936) aspirants passed it;

WHEREAS, Mr. Casmer Blah Dilangalen successfully passed and ranked TOP 2 with an impressive average score of 90.45%, while Mr. Mohaimen Hadji Omar Pasawilan ranked TOP 10 with an impressive average score of 89.30% in the April 2023 Civil Engineers Licensure Examination of the PRC;

WHEREAS, Mr. Casmer Blah Dilangalen, 22 years of age, is an honor student being a Magna Cum Laude graduate at the University of the Philippines - Diliman, and a proud Muslim Maguindanaon hailing from Kabacan, Special Geographic Area (SGA) of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM);

WHEREAS, Mr. Mohaimen Hadji Omar Pasawilan, 23 years of age, is a graduate of the University of Southern Mindanao-Main with a distinction of Cum Laude, and a proud Muslim Maguindanaon hailing from Matalam, North Cotabato;

WHEREAS, the distinguished Bangsamoro youth and all others who have passed the professional board examinations have demonstrated that the Bangsamoro people can compete and excel at the national level of competitiveness and give us greater hope, as they become the source of inspiration and pride for our young generation;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) is cognizant of Mr. Casmer Blah Dilangalen, Mr. Mohaimen Hadji Omar Pasawilan, and all other Bangsamoro passers of the Civil Engineers Licensure Examination for bringing honor to the Bangsamoro, and their achievements merit the Parliament's recognition and commendation:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the BTA Parliament, To congratulate and commend Mr. Casmer Blah Dilangalen and Mr. Mohaimen Hadji Omar Pasawilan for ranking Top 2 and 10, respectively, and all Bangsamoro passers in the April 2023 Civil Engineers Licensure Examination and for giving pride and honor to the Bangsamoro people.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, To present a copy of this resolution to Mr. Casmer Blah Dilangalen and Mr. Mohaimen Hadji Omar Pasawilan as a token of our recognition and appreciation of their achievements, and for their information and its necessary effects.

ADOPTED, Shawwal 27, 1444/May 18, 2023.
