**SECOND REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 444**

**RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING BRIGADIER GENERAL NASSER LIDASAN, DOCTOR OF PHILOSOPHY, FOR HIS APPOINTMENT AS THE FIRST BANGSAMORO MUSLIM CHAIRPERSON OF GOVERNMENT OF THE PHILIPPINES COORDINATING COMMITTEE ON THE CESSATION OF HOSTILITIES**

**WHEREAS,** it is with great pride and honor that we recognize the exemplary achievements of Brigadier General (BGen.) Nasser P. Lidasan, Doctor of Philosophy (PhD), who has brought distinction and honor to the Bangsamoro people through his dedicated service and outstanding accomplishments;

**WHEREAS,** BGen. Nasser P. Lidasan, PhD, has made history as the first Bangsamoro Muslim to ascend to the esteemed position of Assistant Commanding General of the 6th Infantry Division (6ID) of the Philippine Army, demonstrating exceptional leadership, commitment, and professionalism in his duties;

**WHEREAS,** BGen. Lidasan has also made and is making significant contributions to the peace process in the Bangsamoro region, having been appointed by the President of the Philippines, Ferdinand "Bongbong" R. Marcos, Jr., as Chairperson of the Government of the Philippine Coordinating Committee on the Cessation of Hostilities (GPH CCCH), a testament to his capacity for diplomacy, negotiation, and conflict resolution;

**WHEREAS,** he has exemplified the values of integrity, dedication, and service to both the Bangsamoro people and the nation as a whole, serving as a beacon of hope and inspiration for future generations:

**NOW THEREFORE,** be it

**RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA), To commend and congratulate Brigadier General Nasser Lidasan, Doctor of Philosophy, for his appointment as the first Bangsamoro Muslim chairperson of Government of the Philippines Coordinating Committee on the Cessation of Hostilities.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** commend and congratulate BGen. Nasser P. Lidasan, PhD, for his outstanding achievements and contributions to the Bangsamoro region and the Philippines as a whole.

**RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE,** To present a copy of this Resolution to BGen. Nasser P. Lidasan, PhD as a token of our deepest appreciation and admiration for his unwavering commitment to peace, progress, and prosperity in the Bangsamoro region.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY,** That this resolution be made part of the official records of the BTA to serve as a testament to the exemplary leadership and service of BGen. Nasser P. Lidasan, PhD.

**ADOPTED,** *Sha'ban 19, 1445/February 29, 2024.*
