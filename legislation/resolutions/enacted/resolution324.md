**FIRST REGULAR SESSION**

**RESOLUTION NO. 324**

**RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING ALL PARLIAMENTARY AND STATUTORY COMMITTEES OF THE BTA PARLIAMENT TO CONDUCT MEETINGS, HEARINGS, AND CONSULTATIONS DURING THE RECESS OF THE SECOND BTA PARLIAMENT**

**WHEREAS**, the First Regular Session of the Second Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament adjourns *sine die* on *Sha’aban* 29, 1444 AH/March 21, 2023 per Resolution No. 300 of the BTA Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, there are pending deliberations on priority codes such as the Bangsamoro Local Governance Code (BLGC), and other important bills and resolutions, as well as ongoing investigations in aid of legislation before the different committees of the BTA Parliament;

**WHEREAS**, the expeditious consideration of these pending measures which necessitates continued committee meetings during Ramadhan is in the best interest of the Bangsamoro;

**WHEREAS**, Zoom participation of the committee members during said meetings will be allowed subject to the discretion of the concerned committees:

**NOW, THEREFORE**, be it

**RESOLVED**, as it is hereby resolved by the BTA Parliament, To authorize all Parliamentary and Statutory Committees of the Parliament to conduct meetings, hearings and consultations during the recess of the Parliament.

**RESOLVED, FURTHER**, That the conduct of subject committee meetings, hearings and consultations shall not be done on Fridays.

**RESOLVED, FURTHERMORE**, That this Resolution shall take effect on the date of its adoption and shall remain in force until amended or repealed.

**RESOLVED, FINALLY**, To furnish copies of this Resolution to the Parliamentary and Statutory Committees of the Parliament for their information and ready reference.

**ADOPTED**, *Sha’ban* 29, 1444/March 21, 2023.
