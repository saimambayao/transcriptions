FIRST REGULAR SESSION

RESOLUTION NO. 309

RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING MR. ABDUL BATERBONIA LUMANGGAL, A BANGSAMORO GRADUATE OF UNIVERSITY OF SOUTHERN MINDANAO, FOR RANKING TOP 10 WITH AN IMPRESSIVE RATING OF 92.60%, AND ALL BANGSAMORO WHO PASSED IN THE OCTOBER 2022 LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR TEACHERS AT ELEMENTARY LEVEL, FOR GIVING PRIDE AND HONOR TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE

WHEREAS, on October 2, 2022, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and the Professional Regulatory Board for Professional Teachers (BPT) conducted the regular Licensure Examination for Teachers (LET) in various testing centers nationwide;

WHEREAS, the PRC announced on December 16, 2022 that out of 91,468 examinees at the elementary level, 49,783 or 54.43% of them passed. Of the total number of examinees, 29,183 are first timers while 20,600 are repeaters;

WHEREAS, among the 49,783 elementary teacher passers, Mr. Abdul Baterbonia Lumanggal, a Bangsamoro of Maguindanaon descent, admirably ranked as Top 10 with an average score of 92.60%;

WHEREAS, Mr. Lumanggal, who hails from Poblacion Carmen, Cotabato, is also a recipient of a Latin academic honor as Magna Cum Laude from the University of Southern Mindanao — Kabacan. He was born on November 3, 1994 and is now 28 years of age. He is the son of proud parents, the late Mr. Abdul Bolingceg Lumanggal and Mrs. Melda Dasal Baterbonia;

WHEREAS, his exemplary performance to become one of the topnotchers is a source of inspiration for the Bangsamoro children, youth, and young professionals not only in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region but also in the entire country; and elevated the Bangsamoro's educational profile among our younger generations inspiring them as the model of scholastic achievement;

WHEREAS, the Bangsamoro Transition Authority is cognizant of Mr. Abdul Baterbonia Lumanggal together with other LET Bangsamoro passers for bringing honor and pride to the Bangsamoro people. He is living proof that the youth are not only our future in as much as they are also our present:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, To congratulate and commend Mr. Abdul Baterbonia Lumanggal, a Bangsamoro graduate of University of Southern Mindanao, for ranking Top 10 with an impressive rating of 92.60%, and all Bangsamoro who passed the October 2022 Licensure Examination for Teachers at Elementary Level, for giving honor and pride to the Bangsamoro people.

RESOLVED, FURTHER, That a copy of this Resolution be presented to Mr. Abdul Baterbonia Lumanggal and all Bangsamoro who passed, for information.

ADOPTED, Rajab 03, 1444/January 25, 2023.
