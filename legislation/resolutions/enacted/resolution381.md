RESOLUTION NO. 381

RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING MS. FATIMA ALIAH QUIBRANZA DIMAPORO ON HER APPOINTMENT AS UNDERSECRETARY OF THE DEPARTMENT OF SOCIAL WELFARE AND DEVELOPMENT (DSWD)

WHEREAS, pursuant to Republic Act No. 5416, the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) was created to develop and implement a comprehensive social welfare program consisting of (1) preventive and remedial programs and services for individuals, families, and communities; (2) protective, remedial and developmental welfare services for children and youth; (3) vocational rehabilitation and related services for the physically handicapped, ex-convict and individuals with special needs; and (4) training and research and special projects;

WHEREAS, the Office of the Undersecretary for Operations shall be responsible for leading the implementation of the department's programs for social welfare and development, as mandated by laws, as well as directives of the President and of the Secretary. Particularly, the Undersecretary for Operations is concerned with the effective implementation of DSWD's statutory programs, flagship social protection programs, disaster response projects, as well as other timely and relevant social welfare concerns as they emerge. Finally, the Undersecretary for Operations oversees three (3) Sub-Clusters: (1) the Statutory Programs Sub-Cluster, (2) the Specialized Programs Sub-Cluster, and (3) the Disaster Response Management Sub-Cluster;

WHEREAS, on the 28th day of July 2023, the Malacanang Palace released the appointment paper, signed by President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr., of Fatima Aliah Quibranza Dimaporo as the Undersecretary of the DSWD;

WHEREAS, Dimaporo is a native of Lanao del Norte and Lanao del Sur, daughter of Lanao del Norte former governor Abdullah Dimaporo and incumbent governor Imelda "Angging" Quibranza Dimaporo;

WHEREAS, she was the former 2nd District Representative of Lanao del Norte during the 15th Congress as well as Executive Director of the World Youth Alliance Foundation in New York, which promotes the protection of human dignity;

WHEREAS, the appointment of Undersecretary Dimaporo is a clear indication of trust and confidence accorded to her by the President, and indicates her competence, dedication, and commitment to public service, signifying honor and pride to the Bangsamoro people and an inspiration for other Bangsamoro women;

WHEREAS, the national government's recognition of the capability and competency of Bangsamoro women through Undersecretary Dimaporo merits her commendation by the Bangsamoro Parliament:

NOW, THEREFORE, be it

RESOLVED, as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Transition Authority Parliament, To congratulate Ms. Fatima Aliah Quibranza Dimaporo on her appointment as Undersecretary of the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD).

RESOLVED, FURTHER, To present this Resolution to Undersecretary Fatima Aliah Quibranza Dimaporo, for information.

ADOPTED, Muharram 27, 1445/August 14, 2023.
