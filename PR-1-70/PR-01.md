**A RESOLUTION COMMENDING THE INVALUABLE CONTRIBUTION OF BTA MEMBERS NOT INCLUDED IN THE RE-APPOINTMENT LIST OF MARCOS’ ADMINISTRATION.**

**WHEREAS,** the Members of Bangsamoro Transition Authority (BTA) Parliament performs significant role in guaranteeing BTA’s success in realizing the aspirations and ideals of the Bangsamoro People for lasting peace and societal development embodied in the Bangsamoro Organic Law.

**WHEREAS,** underlying BTA success was the Parliament’s involvement in enacting essential legislations responding to the crises and problems of BARMM region, surpassing every critical piece of legislation in pursuit to the general welfare of the Bangsamoro People sustaining harmony in the diverse culture of Bangsamoro Autonomous Region.

**WHEREAS,** these Members of BTA Parliament not included in the re-appointment, to the Bangsamoro Transition Authority, deserves recognition and appreciation for their invaluable participation and contribution to the Bangsamoro Government quests and feats in the last three years.

**WHEREAS,** the BTA Parliament recognizes their utmost participation and contribution in rendering their legislative and executive powers lodged upon them during the transition cited in Section 3, Article XVI of R.A. No. 11054, otherwise known as the Bangsamoro Organic Law, to read;

ARTICLE XVI
BANGSAMORO TRANSITION AUTHORITY

Section 3. *Powers and Authorities.* - Legislative and executive powers in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region during transition shall be vested in the Bangsamoro Transition Authority.

All powers and functions of the Bangsamoro Government as provided in this Organic Law is vested in the Bangsamoro Transition Authority during the transition period.

**WHEREAS,** as Members of BTA Parliament they had shared collective efforts in carrying out their mandates in the accomplishment of various priorities set forth in Bangsamoro Organic Law, stated in Section 4 of the above Article:

Section 3. *Functions and Priorities.* – The Bangsamoro Transition shall ensure the accomplishment of the following priorities during the transition period:

(a) Enactment of priority legislations such as the Bangsamoro Administrative Code, Bangsamoro Revenue Code, Bangsamoro Electoral Code, Bangsamoro Local Government Code, and Bangsamoro Education Code consistent with powers and prerogatives vested in the Bangsamoro Government by this Organic Law. Xxx

The Bangsamoro Transition Authority may also enact a Bangsamoro Civil Service Code, as provided in this Organic Law, subject to the Constitutional mandate of the Civil Service Commission.

**WHEREAS,** for their indispensable contribution to and great participation in the crafting of R.A. No. 11054 otherwise known as Bangsamoro Organic Law, that the Bangsamoro Government is now implementing, and to them who tremendously exerted effort and strive to gain peace which we are now reaping, we honor your excellence and bravery.

**WHEREAS,** this Proposed Resolution aims to recognize their knowledge, efforts, competencies and proficiencies in the execution of their skills in the advancement and development of Bangsamoro Government.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION COMMENDING THE INVALUABLE CONTRIBUTION OF BTA MEMBERS NOT INCLUDED IN THE RE-APPOINTMENT LIST OF MARCOS’ ADMINISTRATION.**
