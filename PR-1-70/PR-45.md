**RESOLUTION AMENDING ADOPTED RESOLUTION NO. 97, RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING THE TOP RANKED BANGSAMORO QUR'AN RECITERS WHO REPRESENTED THE COUNTRY IN VARIOUS INTERNATIONAL QUR'AN RECITING COMPETITIONS FOR AROUND HALF A CENTURY SINCE 1969**

**WHEREAS,** Adopted Resolution No. 97 (A.R. No. 97) provides that the Bangsamoro Qur'an reciters have been winning top placers in international Qur'an reading competitions for around half a century since 1969, and have brought great honor and pride to our nation and Bangsamoro Autonomous Region;

**WHEREAS,** to expedite the implementation of A.R. No. 97, an evaluation committee was created for the purpose;

**WHEREAS,** in A.R. No. 97, there are sixty-nine (69) top-ranked Qur'an reciters from first place to eight places. It is deemed reasonable to award only the top three winners - first place, second place, and third place;

**WHEREAS,** the first place shall received three hundred thousand pesos (₱300,000.00); the second place, two hundred thousand pesos (₱250,000.00); and third place, two hundred thousand pesos (₱200,000.00); and plaque of recognition;

**WHEREAS,** upon thorough evaluation of the winners listed in A.R. No. 97 by the committee, of the sixty-nine (69) awardees, only thirty-seven (37) qualified for the entitlement as listed hereunder;
