
PROPOSED RESOLUTION 14

INTRODUCED BY:

**Author : MP DR. SUSANA S. ANAYATIN**

**A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING SYMPHATY AND CONDOLENCES TO THE BEREAVED FAMILY OF THE LATE DR. AMIREL S. USMAN, DiPHM, DPCHA, CHA RECOGNIZING AND HONORING HIS EXEMPLARY CONTRIBUTION AND STUPENDOUS DEDICATION AS PUBLIC SERVANT TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE**

**WHEREAS,** Dr. Amirel S. Usman fondly called as Doc Amz was the Director – General of the Ministry of Health of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao and was an Acting Chief of the Iranun District Hospital in Parang, Maguindanao.

**WHEREAS,** his incomparable dedication in delivering his commitment without reservation as public servant was truly recognized during the peak of the most inconceivable health crisis in modern history.

**WHEREAS,** his leadership as one of the prime movers of health in BARMM, facilitated the Bangsamoro region sustain a low (COVID-19) transmission, ensured that Bangsamoro communities are protected from the threat of COVID-19 and communities across the BARMM are vaccinated.

**WHEREAS,** his stupendous contribution to BARMM and great concern to the region’s health care made his young mind dream big for his fellow Bangsamoro.

**WHEREAS,** this proponent is privileged to be shared of Dr. Usman’s dream of establishing our very own Bangsamoro Medical School that would cater Bangsamoro students who desire to be professional doctors but do not have the means to pursue such dream.

**WHEREAS,** Dr. Amirel S. Usman was born in December 30, 1979, and was summoned and called by our Almighty Creator last September 17, 2022 at the age of 44.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao in **A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING SYMPHATY AND CONDOLENCES TO THE BEREAVED FAMILY OF THE LATE DR. AMIREL S. USMAN, DiPHM, DPCHA, CHA RECOGNIZING AND HONORING HIS EXEMPLARY CONTRIBUTION AND STUPENDOUS DEDICATION AS PUBLIC SERVANT TO THE BANGSAMORO PEOPLE.**

**RESOLVED, FURTHER,** that a copy of this Resolution be furnished to his bereaved family.
