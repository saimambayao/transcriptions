**RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING AND COMMENDING SECRETARY AMENAH F. PANGANDAMAN FOR THE CONFIRMATION OF HER APPOINTMENT AS SECRETARY OF THE DEPARTMENT OF BUDGET AND MANAGEMENT BY THE COMMITTEE ON APPOINTMENTS**

**WHEREAS,** Secretary Amenah F. Pangandaman is the first Muslim to be appointed Secretary of the Department of Budget and Management and the only Muslim in the cabinet of His Excellency President Ferdinand R. Marcos, Jr.;

**WHEREAS,** the appointment of Secretary Pangandaman was confirmed by the Committee on Appointments on September 28, 2022;

**WHEREAS,** Secretary Pangandaman has made the Bangsamoro people proud as this underscores the trust and confidence accorded her by the President and the Committee on Appointments and it manifests her integrity, competence, dedication, and commitment;

**WHEREAS,** the pride and honor brought by Secretary Pangandaman to the Bangsamoro merit her commendation by the Bangsamoro Parliament;

**NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED,** as it is hereby resolved by the Bangsamoro Parliament to congratulate and commend Secretary Amenah F. Pangandaman for the confirmation of her appointment as Secretary of the Department of Budget and management by the Committee on Appointments.

**RESOLVED FINALLY,** copy of this resolution be furnished Secretary Amenah F. Pangandaman, Department of Budget and Management.
